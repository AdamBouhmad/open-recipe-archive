## Ingredients

- cabbage head
- onion
- water
- salt
- pepper
- lard or butter
- tomatoes
- flour
- sour apple

## Directions

1. Shred a medium cabbage head and chop one large onion finely.
2. Pour boiling water and let stand for fifteen minutes, then press the cabbage out of the water.
3. Add to it a quarter pound of finely chopped and lightly fried lard, or a large tablespoon of butter.
4. Cover and braise on very low heat with a small amount of water.
5. When the cabbage has cooked this way for ten or fifteen minutes, add four medium tomatoes cut into pieces.
6. Cover and braise further until tender, frequently shaking the pan and stirring so it does not burn.
7. Just before serving season with a tablespoon of flour browned with a tablespoon of butter.
8. If the cabbage is not sufficiently sour, add a thinly sliced sour apple a quarter hour before serving.
