## Ingredients

- a few pounds stewing meat or first-quality cross-cut beef, thinly sliced
- salt
- pepper
- 3 roasted onions, minced finely
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon butter or some chopped kidney fat
- 1.5 cups white wine

## Directions

1. Slice the meat thinly, beat well with a mallet, and lightly salt.
2. Prepare a filling from the three roasted minced onions, two tablespoons of butter, salt, and pepper to taste; mix this thoroughly without cooking.
3. Place a tablespoon of butter or some chopped kidney fat in a pan and heat until browned.
4. Layer the beef rolls alternating with the filling until all are used, with filling on top.
5. Pour 1.5 cups of white wine over everything.
6. Cover tightly and place over high heat; once it comes to a full boil, reduce to low heat and braise until tender.
7. Shake the pan frequently during braising to prevent the meat from sticking.
