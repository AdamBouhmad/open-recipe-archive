## Ingredients

- two pounds lean raw beef
- one teaspoon salt
- one quarter teaspoon pepper
- one half teaspoon sage
- two tablespoons melted butter
- two heaping tablespoons finely grated bread
- two beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Chop the beef very finely
2. Add salt, pepper, sage, and melted butter
3. Grate bread very finely and take two heaping tablespoons
4. Add two beaten eggs
5. Mix everything well
6. Form the mixture into one long loaf and place on a baking sheet
7. Bake in the oven, basting frequently during baking
8. Remove from the oven and place in a cold location
9. When completely cooled, slice and serve as an appetizer
10. This meatloaf can also be served hot
