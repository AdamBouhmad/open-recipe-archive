## Ingredients

- one large lemon
- one gallon of hot water
- one ounce of ground ginger
- one pound of sugar
- one cup of yeast

## Directions

1. Slice one large lemon and pour one gallon of hot water over it
2. Add one ounce of ground ginger and one pound of sugar
3. Stir until sugar dissolves and then cool
4. Pour in one cup of yeast and set at room temperature for fermentation, which lasts about eighteen hours
5. When fermentation is complete, pour the clear liquid into bottles and cork
