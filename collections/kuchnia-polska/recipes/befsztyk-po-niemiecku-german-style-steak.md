## Ingredients

- a piece of tender meat for steak
- oil
- salt
- butter
- onion, sliced
- small potatoes, boiled in water
- mushrooms, sliced and stewed in butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Coat the meat on both sides with oil and lightly salt it
2. Immediately place it on a pan with browned butter and sliced onion; place the onion on the butter just before laying the meat on it
3. Fry on strong heat until the meat is well browned on one side, then flip to the other side
4. Arrange small potatoes that were previously boiled in water on one side of the pan next to the meat
5. On the other side, place mushrooms that have been sliced and stewed in butter
6. On opposite sides of the pan, near the edges, crack one egg on each side
7. When the eggs are well set, serve on the table
8. Prepare this steak on a round flat pan without a handle, something like a large plate, and after frying, place the pan on a plate and serve it together with the pan
