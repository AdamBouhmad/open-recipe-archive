## Ingredients

- four quarts of red raspberries
- one quart of yellow vinegar (cider vinegar)
- two pounds of sugar per quart of obtained liquid

## Directions

1. Pour four quarts of red raspberries with one quart of yellow vinegar (cider vinegar) and place in a cool place for four days
2. After this time strain, and for each quart of obtained liquid add two pounds of sugar, place on fire and boil for twenty minutes
3. Serve diluted with water and ice
4. In tightly corked bottles this juice can be kept for a quite long time
