## Ingredients

- canned green peas
- butter
- a little cream or milk
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Canned green peas should be rinsed several times with cold water after opening the can, poured into a pan, add butter, a little cream or milk, salt and pepper to taste, bring to a boil and serve on the table.
2. Some add a pinch of sugar.
