## Ingredients

- two oranges
- three or four bananas
- sugar
- one cup of cream
- one egg white
- half cup of fine sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and slice the oranges and bananas, mix together and sprinkle with sugar.
2. Arrange on a serving platter or individual plates.
3. Take one cup of cream, one egg white, and half cup of fine sugar; divide the sugar into two equal parts.
4. Beat one part of sugar with the egg white until foamy.
5. Beat the other part of sugar with the cream.
6. Mix the two mixtures together and pour the cream over the fruit.
