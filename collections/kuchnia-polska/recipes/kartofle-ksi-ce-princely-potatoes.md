## Ingredients

- leftover potato puree from the previous day
- egg
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Use leftover potato puree from the previous day - cooked mashed potatoes which have hardened so they can be cut into strips.
2. Cut the potatoes with a knife into strips two inches long, one inch wide and half an inch thick.
3. Beat one egg on one small plate and pour some melted butter on another.
4. Carefully take the cut potatoes with a knife, dip first in butter, then in egg.
5. Take the prepared potato strips with a knife and place on a butter-smeared sheet.
6. Place in a hot oven and after several minutes remove and serve immediately.
