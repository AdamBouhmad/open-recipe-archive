## Ingredients

- 6 large tart apples
- 3 cups water
- about 1/4 pound sugar
- 1 glass white sour wine
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- 1 large cup sour cream
- 2 egg yolks
- ice
- small fried bread cubes (grzaneczki)
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop finely 6 large tart apples, put in a pot, pour 3 cups of water and cook until the apples completely fall apart.
2. Strain through a loose sieve, add about 1/4 pound of sugar, 1 glass of white sour wine and zest from 1/2 lemon rubbed on sugar.
3. Whip 1 large cup of sour cream with 2 egg yolks, then slowly pour the hot soup onto this cream, stirring continuously.
4. Then place on ice to cool.
5. As a side, serve small fried bread cubes dipped in cream and then fried in butter and sprinkled with sugar in the pan.
6. Note: Egg yolks can be omitted. This soup can also be made with pears in this way with the addition of a few sour apples, otherwise it will be bland.
