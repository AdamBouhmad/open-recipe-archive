## Ingredients

- two dozen large ripe tomatoes
- two medium onions
- three large green chili peppers
- one quart of vinegar
- half a cup of sugar
- one tablespoon of Jamaican pepper (allspice)
- one tablespoon of cinnamon
- one tablespoon of mace
- one tablespoon of ground cloves
- one tablespoon of celery seed

## Directions

1. Chop all the above finely and add one quart of vinegar, half a cup of sugar, one tablespoon of Jamaican pepper (allspice), one tablespoon of cinnamon, one tablespoon of mace, one tablespoon of ground cloves and one tablespoon of celery seed
2. Cook everything for two hours, stirring frequently, then fill jars with this prepared mixture and store in a cool place
