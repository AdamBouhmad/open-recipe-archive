## Ingredients

- veal liver
- milk
- lard bacon for larding
- butter
- onion, chopped finely
- celery
- parsley
- flour
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Soak the veal liver in milk for an hour, remove the membrane and lard it with bacon.
2. Place a piece of butter, finely chopped onion, celery and parsley in a pan, put the liver on top, cover and braise on low heat.
3. When it is half braised, remove it from the pan, salt it, dust with flour, return it to the pan and continue braising until done, which can be recognized by the fact that no blood appears when pricked with a fork.
4. Be careful not to cook too long, as it hardens; it also hardens if salted too early.
5. After cooking, slice it, arrange on a platter and pour over the sauce remaining in the pan with the addition of several spoonfuls of sour cream.
6. Alternative method: Prepare the liver as above and slice it raw, dust with flour and braise on butter in a pan, but very briefly; toward the end of braising add sour cream; salt not immediately but just before adding the cream.
