## Ingredients

- equal measure cocoa and sugar
- two teaspoons of the mixture per cup
- boiling water
- cream or milk (to taste)

## Directions

1. Mix equal amounts of cocoa and sugar well
2. For each cup, take two teaspoons of this mixture and pour boiling water over it
3. Add some cream or milk to each cup
4. Can be prepared separately in cups or in one large vessel
5. Water must be boiling
