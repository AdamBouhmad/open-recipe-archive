## Ingredients

- lamb kidneys
- butter
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the kidneys of fat.
2. Cut each kidney in half.
3. Scald with boiling water.
4. Salt and place on a roasting pan.
5. Roast in a very hot oven, basting with butter.
6. Just before serving, place a small piece of butter in each cut.
7. Sprinkle with pepper and chopped parsley.
8. Serve.
