## Ingredients

- kohlrabi
- fat lamb
- bread soaked in milk
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste

## Directions

1. In late summer and in autumn, when kohlrabi is too hard to cook in the usual way, it is served stuffed in the following manner: Peel the kohlrabi, cut off the top, and hollow out the middle.
2. Finely chop raw fatty lamb, add bread soaked in milk and then squeezed out, salt and pepper to taste and fill the hollowed kohlrabi with this stuffing.
