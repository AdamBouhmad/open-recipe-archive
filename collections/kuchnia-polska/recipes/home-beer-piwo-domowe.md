## Ingredients

- two ounces of good hops
- two quarts of water
- half an ounce of ground ginger
- one and a half pounds of sugar
- one small cup of liquid yeast

## Directions

1. Pour two ounces of good hops into two quarts of water and boil for three hours
2. Then add half an ounce of ground ginger and boil for another half hour
3. Strain and add one and a half pounds of sugar, stirring until all sugar dissolves
4. Remove from heat
5. When the liquid has cooled almost completely, add one small cup of liquid yeast and set at room temperature for fermentation, which should last about twenty-four hours
6. When fermentation is complete, carefully pour the clear liquid into bottles and cork tightly
