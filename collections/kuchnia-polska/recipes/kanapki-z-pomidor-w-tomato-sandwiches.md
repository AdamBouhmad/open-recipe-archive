## Ingredients

- two medium tomatoes
- one small onion, finely chopped
- quarter cup cooked ham, finely chopped
- one tablespoon butter
- two eggs, well beaten
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- white bread, sliced
- butter for spreading
- optional: hot oven or pan-frying

## Directions

1. Scald tomatoes with boiling water, peel, and chop finely
2. Chop one small onion very finely
3. Chop one quarter cup of cooked ham finely
4. Put one tablespoon of butter in a saucepan
5. When melted, mix in tomatoes, ham, and onion
6. Stew covered for ten minutes
7. Remove from heat
8. Add two well-beaten eggs
9. Add salt and pepper to taste
10. Return to low heat and cook until the mixture sets
11. Remove from heat
12. Spread on slices of white bread that have been spread with a bit of butter and lightly browned in a hot oven or fried in a pan
