## Ingredients

- two cups flour
- three teaspoons baking powder
- one quarter teaspoon salt
- one and one half cups sweet milk
- one beaten egg
- one quarter cup melted butter

## Directions

1. Sift two cups of flour with three teaspoons of baking powder and one quarter teaspoon of salt several times through a sieve.
2. Pour in one and one half cups of sweet milk, one beaten egg, and one quarter cup of melted butter.
3. Beat well before adding the butter.
4. Pour by the spoonful onto a hot pan and fry.
5. When the bottom side is sufficiently browned, flip to the other side.
6. If the pancakes stick to the pan, do not grease the pan but instead put a teaspoon of butter into the batter so there will be no smoke.
