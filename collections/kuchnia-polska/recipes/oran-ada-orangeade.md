## Ingredients

- four large oranges
- one and a half quarts of water with ice
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Cut four large oranges in half and extract with a spoon all the pulp from seven halves, and grate the eighth with its zest; remove all seeds, add one and a half quarts of water with ice and add sugar to taste
