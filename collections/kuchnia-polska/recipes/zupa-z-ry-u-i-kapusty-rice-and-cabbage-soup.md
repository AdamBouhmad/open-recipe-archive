## Ingredients

- ordinary broth made on beef
- one head of cabbage, not too finely chopped
- some finely chopped bacon
- a little garlic
- green parsley
- one quarter pound of half-cooked rice
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cook ordinary broth on beef
2. Add to it one head of cabbage, not too finely chopped, and cook further for fifteen minutes
3. Fry some finely chopped bacon, add a little garlic and green parsley to it, then add one quarter pound of half-cooked rice
4. Put everything into the broth, mix well and cook further for half an hour
5. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve on the table
