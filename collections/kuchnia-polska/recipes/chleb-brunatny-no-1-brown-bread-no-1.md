## Ingredients

- half a cup of brown sugar
- one cup of wheat flour
- one heaping cup of graham flour
- one teaspoon of salt
- two heaping teaspoons of soda
- two cups of sour milk
- one tablespoon of molasses
- a handful of raisins

## Directions

1. Mix together the brown sugar, wheat flour, graham flour, salt and soda
2. Add two cups of sour milk, one tablespoon of molasses and a handful of raisins
3. Mix into dough and place in a moderately hot oven for one hour
