## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon Jamaican rum

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks, sugar, and rum into a smooth mass
2. If not thick enough, add more sugar
3. Spread on cake with a knife dipped in warm water
