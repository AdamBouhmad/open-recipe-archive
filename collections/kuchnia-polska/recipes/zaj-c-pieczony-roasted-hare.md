## Ingredients

- 1 hare, dressed
- bacon, finely chopped
- weak vinegar
- parsley, chopped
- celery, chopped
- carrots, chopped
- onion, chopped
- pepper
- English herbs
- salt
- sour cream
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Dress and lard the hare densely with finely chopped bacon
2. Take weak vinegar, add plenty of chopped parsley, celery, carrots, onion, as well as some pepper and English herbs, and boil it, then cool it
3. Pour this over the hare and let it marinate in this vinegar for thirty-six hours
4. One hour before roasting, remove the hare and salt it, then place in a roasting pan and roast slowly, continuously basting with sour cream
5. If you want a more sour sauce, you can add some of the vinegar in which the hare marinated to the sour cream
6. Roasted red beets are usually served alongside the hare
