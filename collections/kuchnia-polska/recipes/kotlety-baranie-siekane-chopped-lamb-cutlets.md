## Ingredients

- lamb meat
- salt
- pepper
- soaked roll
- one egg yolk per two pounds of meat
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Take lamb meat - these can be scraps left over from roasts or beaten cutlets - debone it, chop finely, add salt and pepper to taste, soaked roll, one egg yolk per two pounds of meat, mix well and form into flat cutlets.
2. Into these cutlets place small cubes, sprinkle with grated bread crumbs and fry in clarified butter.
3. Make these cutlets thin, as they shrink during frying and become considerably thicker.
