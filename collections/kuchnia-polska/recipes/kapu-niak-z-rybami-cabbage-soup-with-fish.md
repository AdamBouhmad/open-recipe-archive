## Ingredients

- sour cabbage
- salt
- pepper
- water
- fish
- butter
- flour
- sour cream (if available)

## Directions

1. Chop finely a sufficient amount of sour cabbage, add salt and pepper to taste, pour water over it and cook until the cabbage is soft
2. Then add fish and cook further until the fish is cooked through
3. Remove the fish, chop into small pieces and put back into the cabbage soup
4. Brown a spoonful of butter with flour and thicken the soup with it
5. If there is sour cream, use it to whiten the cabbage soup and serve
