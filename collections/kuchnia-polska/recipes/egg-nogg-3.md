## Ingredients

- six whole eggs
- half a cup of sugar
- one quart of fresh milk
- one-quarter quart of good brandy
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat six whole eggs with half a cup of sugar until thick like cream, then pour in one quart of fresh milk and one-quarter quart of good brandy, and some grated nutmeg
2. Beat everything thoroughly and mix, and at the end add three egg whites beaten until stiff peaks form
3. Serve in small cups or glasses with foam on top
