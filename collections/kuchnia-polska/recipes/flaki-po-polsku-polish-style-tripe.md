## Ingredients

- tripe, cleaned
- water
- meat for broth
- celery, parsley, carrots, thinly sliced
- salt and pepper to taste
- marjoram, nutmeg or ginger
- one tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Carefully clean the tripe and cover with soft water, cook while skimming like broth
2. Discard the water, rinse the tripe once more in cold water, add fresh water and continue cooking about four hours in whole pieces
3. Remove and slice into pasta-like strips
4. Separately cook a few pounds of meat for broth and use this broth to cover the sliced tripe
5. Chop some celery, parsley, and carrots into thin strips and cook until tender, then add to the tripe
6. Half an hour before serving, salt and pepper the tripe and add grated marjoram, nutmeg, or ginger to taste
7. Mix one tablespoon of butter and season the tripe, then bring to a boil, stir well, and pour into a dish
8. Meatballs can also be made with fat to add to the tripe, as described in the soup section
9. The vegetables can be braised in butter instead of boiling and added just before serving
10. The flour and butter can be browned in the pot first
11. Tripe is best made in an earthenware pot; if made in a metal pot it will be dark and less tasty
12. Tripe can also be served as a soup but should be made much thinner in that case
