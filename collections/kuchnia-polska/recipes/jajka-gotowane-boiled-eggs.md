## Ingredients

- eggs
- water

## Directions

1. To cook eggs soft-boiled, drop them into boiling water and cook for two and a half minutes in summer, or three minutes in winter since eggs are usually colder then
2. Hard-boiled eggs are usually cooked for five minutes, though in recent times in America the custom of cooking for fifteen minutes has spread; it is claimed that eggs cooked this way are both more tasty and easier to digest
