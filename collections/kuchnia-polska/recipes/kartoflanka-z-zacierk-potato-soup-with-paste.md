## Ingredients

- mixed vegetables (włoszczyzna)
- potatoes
- wheat flour
- one egg
- cold water
- butter
- onion
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook a broth from mixed vegetables, strain it and use it to pour over potatoes sliced into thin pieces and cook until soft
2. Make a mixture of wheat flour with one egg and cold water into a thin batter and use it to thicken the soup
3. Just before serving, fry in butter and nicely brown a spoonful of finely chopped onion and use it to season the soup
