## Ingredients

- floury potatoes
- one tablespoon of butter
- salt and pepper to taste
- one egg
- three tablespoons of sour cream or milk

## Directions

1. peel and cook the floury potatoes until tender
2. mash them thoroughly with the addition of butter, salt and pepper for half a quart of such potatoes
3. beat one egg well, add three tablespoons of sour cream or milk and also add to the potatoes
4. after proper mixing, form small cutlets half an inch thick from this mass
5. brush with egg white or milk and place on a baking sheet in the oven
6. keep them there until they are well browned
