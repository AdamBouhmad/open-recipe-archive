## Ingredients

- Veal breast
- Salt and pepper
- Marinated tomatoes, half a quart

## Directions

1. Wash the veal breast, wipe it with a damp cloth, place in a roasting pan, and put in a very hot oven to quickly brown the meat on top
2. Remove from the oven, add salt and pepper, and cover with half a quart of marinated tomatoes
3. Return to the oven until the meat is completely roasted
4. Place on a platter with the tomatoes and serve immediately
