## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- one-third cup of water
- one-eighth teaspoon of cream of tartar
- one egg

## Directions

1. Cook over low heat one cup of sugar with one-third cup of water and one-eighth teaspoon of cream of tartar until a small portion of this mass dropped in cold water forms a soft ball
2. Then beat one egg well and pour the above mass into it in the thinnest stream, constantly stirring
3. Spread on the cake with a knife dipped in warm water
