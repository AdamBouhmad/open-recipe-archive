## Ingredients

- 0.5 quart warm milk
- 1 cake of compressed yeast
- flour as needed
- 1 egg
- 0.5 cup butter
- sugar to taste
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Mix 0.5 quart of warm milk, 1 cake of compressed yeast, and enough flour (previously warmed) to make fairly soft dough.
2. Set to rise, then add 1 egg, 0.5 cup butter, some sugar, and salt and mix well.
3. Turn out onto a board, roll out, and cut flat rolls.
4. Dip the rolls in melted butter, fold in half, and place in the oven for twenty minutes.
