## Ingredients

- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 pinches pepper
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon good vinegar
- onion or onion juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Put 1/4 teaspoon salt and 2 pinches pepper in a cup
2. Pour in 1 tablespoon olive oil and mix well
3. Add 2 more tablespoons olive oil and 1 tablespoon good vinegar
4. If desired, add some onion to taste, preferably onion juice, of which use 3 or 4 drops
