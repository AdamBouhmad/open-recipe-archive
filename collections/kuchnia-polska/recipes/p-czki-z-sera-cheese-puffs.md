## Ingredients

- three ounces flour
- one ounce butter
- half teaspoon salt
- pinch of red pepper
- half cup water
- three ounces grated cheese
- one egg yolk
- one egg white

## Directions

1. Mix flour with butter in a bowl
2. Add salt and red pepper
3. Gradually pour in half cup water while stirring constantly
4. Mix in grated cheese combined with one egg yolk
5. Beat egg white until stiff and fold into the mixture at the very end
6. Scoop with a spoon and drop into boiling hot fat
7. Fry until golden brown like doughnuts
