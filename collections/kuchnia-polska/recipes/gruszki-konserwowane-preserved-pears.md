## Ingredients

- ripe and completely healthy pears
- sugar equal in weight to the pears
- water
- lemon

## Directions

1. Select ripe and completely healthy pears
2. Peel, cut in half and remove the core and seeds
3. Pour all the peelings with water and boil for fifteen minutes, then strain
4. For every four pounds of pears, take one quart of this juice; if there is not enough, add water
5. For each pound of pears, take one pound of sugar and make a syrup from it
6. Boil the syrup and skim it
7. Add one lemon cut into small dice to each quart of syrup
8. Then place the pears in the syrup and boil until they are transparent
9. Store in tightly sealed jars
