## Ingredients

- bread
- one clove of garlic, finely chopped, or onion
- salt and pepper to taste
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Chop bread into cubes
2. Add finely chopped garlic or onion and place in the oven to brown
3. Place on hot plates and pour boiling water over
4. Add salt and pepper to taste
