## Ingredients

- dried plums
- one cup plum puree
- one cup thin cream or half milk and half cream
- one teaspoon cornmeal
- cold milk
- two egg yolks
- one-third cup sugar
- fruit juice
- flour
- lard
- water
- two egg whites
- two tablespoons fine sugar

## Directions

1. Cook dried plums until soft
2. Press through a sieve and take one full cup of the puree
3. Bring one cup thin cream (or half milk and half cream) to a boil
4. When it begins to boil, pour in one teaspoon cornmeal mixed in a little cold milk
5. When it begins to thicken, pour into the plum puree
6. Add two beaten egg yolks and one-third cup sugar
7. It is also good to add a couple of tablespoons of some fruit juice
8. Line a baking pan with soft dough made from flour, lard and water
9. Spread the plum mixture on top and put in a hot oven
10. After baking, remove and cover the top with meringue made from two egg whites beaten with two tablespoons fine sugar
11. Put in a hot oven for one or two minutes
12. When browned, serve to the table
13. This charlotte can also be served cold
