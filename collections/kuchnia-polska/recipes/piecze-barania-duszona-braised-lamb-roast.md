## Ingredients

- lamb roast, prepared as for roasting
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the lamb roast as above, place in the oven and roast until half done.
2. Then remove, place in a pan and braise on low heat until tender.
3. Toward the end of braising, flavor with sour cream.
4. If the meat is fatty, excess fat should be removed from the roasting pan before placing the roast in the pan.
