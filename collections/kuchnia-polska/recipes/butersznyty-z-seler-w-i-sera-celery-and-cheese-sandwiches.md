## Ingredients

- half a cup heavy cream
- grated cheese (preferably Parmesan)
- celery stalks, white, finely chopped
- bread

## Directions

1. Whip half a cup of heavy cream
2. Add enough grated cheese (preferably Parmesan) to make a thick paste
3. Chop celery stalks very finely
4. Spread the cream and cheese mixture on bread
5. Sprinkle a generous amount of chopped celery on top
6. Prepare these sandwiches just before serving, as they lose flavor quickly
