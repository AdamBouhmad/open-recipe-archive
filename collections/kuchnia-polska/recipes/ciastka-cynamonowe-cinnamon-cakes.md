## Ingredients

- one-half cup butter
- one-half cup lard
- one heaping tablespoon sugar
- one egg white beaten to stiff peaks
- three-quarters cup sweet milk
- one heaping teaspoon baking powder
- flour as needed
- sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Mix one-half cup butter, one-half cup lard, one heaping tablespoon sugar, one egg white beaten to stiff peaks, three-quarters cup sweet milk, one heaping teaspoon baking powder, and enough flour to make a rollable dough
2. Mix thoroughly
3. Turn out onto a work surface and roll thin
4. Sprinkle thickly with sugar mixed with cinnamon
5. Cut into strips four inches long and two and one-half inches wide
6. Roll to form round cylinders
7. Arrange on a baking sheet and bake in a hot oven
