## Ingredients

- Veal from the hind quarter and shoulder
- One bread roll
- Milk
- Grated bread crumbs
- Butter
- Egg yolk
- Salt and pepper
- Bone for filling
- Optional: pork for mixture
- Sauce, spinach, or vegetables for serving

## Directions

1. Remove the sinews from the veal, cut into fine pieces, and chop into a uniform mass
2. For every pound and a half of chopped veal, take one bread roll soaked in milk and mix with a spoonful of grated bread crumbs and a small piece of butter
3. Add one beaten egg yolk
4. Mix all thoroughly with the meat into a smooth mass
5. Form into oblong cutlets, place a bone in each, coat in grated bread crumbs, and fry on hot butter
6. Serve with sauce, spinach, or other vegetables
7. The bones and sinews left from the meat can be cooked to flavor the sauce
