## Ingredients

- one quart of small cucumbers
- one quart of large cucumbers
- one quart of small onions
- two large cauliflowers
- four large green pepper pods
- four quarts of water
- half a quart of salt
- one tablespoon of ground mustard
- half a cup of flour
- one teaspoon of yellow ginger (turmeric powder)
- cold vinegar
- sugar
- red pepper (optional)

## Directions

1. Slice large cucumbers without peeling
2. Break cauliflowers into small pieces and chop green pepper pods finely
3. Pour four quarts of water in which half a quart of salt has been dissolved over all vegetables
4. Let stand for twenty-four hours
5. After twenty-four hours, put on fire and when it reaches boiling temperature, pour everything onto a colander or sieve to drain the water
6. Mix one tablespoon of ground mustard with half a cup of flour
7. Add one teaspoon of yellow ginger (turmeric powder) and enough cold vinegar to obtain a thin, smooth paste
8. Add almost one cup of sugar and enough vinegar so that everything together makes two quarts
9. Cook until thick, stirring constantly
10. Remove from fire and add the cucumbers and mix well
11. Put on fire again and let everything heat well
12. If desired, add red pepper to make the pickle sharper
