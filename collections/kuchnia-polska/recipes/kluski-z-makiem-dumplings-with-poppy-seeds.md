## Ingredients

- poppy seeds
- half pound poppy seeds
- quarter pound sugar or honey

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook dumplings as described above
2. Mix half pound of previously scalded poppy seeds in a bowl with quarter pound sugar or honey
3. Pour this over the dumplings, mix well and serve immediately
