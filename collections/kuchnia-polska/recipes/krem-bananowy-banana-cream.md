## Ingredients

- half of a box of gelatin
- half a cup cold water
- three large cups milk
- one cup sugar
- three bananas
- sour cream, whipped with sugar or plain

## Directions

1. Soak the contents of half a box of gelatin in half a cup of cold water.
2. Bring three large cups of milk to a boil with one cup of sugar, then pour off a sufficient amount of this milk to dissolve the gelatin.
3. When all the gelatin dissolves, pour it into the rest of the milk and boil for ten minutes, then remove from heat.
4. When it cools so that it is barely warm, but the gelatin has not yet set, peel three bananas and crumble them with a fork, then place in the milk and mix well, and then set in a cold place, preferably on ice.
5. This cream should be made in the evening for use the next day.
6. Serve with sour cream beaten with sugar, or with plain raw sour cream and sugar.
