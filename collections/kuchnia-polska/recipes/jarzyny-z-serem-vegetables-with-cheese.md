## Ingredients

- Italian cabbage in equal quantity
- young kohlrabi in equal quantity
- green peas in equal quantity
- butter
- grated parmesan cheese
- sugar
- water or milk for cooking

## Directions

1. Take equal quantities of Italian cabbage, young kohlrabi and green peas
2. First scald the cabbage and kohlrabi with boiling water, then cook all three vegetables separately in separate water or milk
3. Cut the cabbage and kohlrabi finely
4. When all three vegetables are tender, drain them and sprinkle with a small amount of sugar
5. Put some butter in a pot, place a layer of cabbage on it, then again some butter and sprinkle with grated parmesan
6. Then arrange a layer of kohlrabi, butter and parmesan
7. Finally peas, butter and parmesan
8. There should be six layers total, meaning two of each vegetable
9. Pour everything with butter, sprinkle with parmesan and put in a moderate oven for one hour
10. Then arrange on a serving dish, pour with butter browned with bread roll and serve
