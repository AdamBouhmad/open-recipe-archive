## Ingredients

- sandacz (pike-perch)
- salt
- oil
- white onion
- fresh parsley
- pepper
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the pike-perch, cut it into rings, salt it and let it sit for a couple of hours.
2. Pour plenty of oil onto a pan and place on heat; when it is well heated, carefully place the fish removed from salt and cook on very low heat, turning, until tender, being careful not to let it burn or brown.
3. When the fish is done, finely chop white onion and fresh parsley, mix together and sprinkle the platter; on the onion with parsley place the fried fish and sprinkle the top generously also with parsley and onion, and a pinch of pepper.
4. Finally pour lemon juice or good vinegar over it. Such fish is best the day after it is prepared.
