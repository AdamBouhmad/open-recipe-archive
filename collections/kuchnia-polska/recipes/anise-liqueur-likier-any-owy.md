## Ingredients

- one quart of spirit
- half a quart of boiled and cooled water
- one ounce of ground anise
- one and a half pounds of sugar
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Take one quart of spirit and add half a quart of boiled and cooled water
2. Add one ounce of ground anise and one and a half pounds of sugar and set in a cool place for two days
3. Then strain or filter and add sugar to taste
