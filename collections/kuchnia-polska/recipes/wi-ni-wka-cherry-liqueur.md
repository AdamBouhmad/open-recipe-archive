## Ingredients

- ten pounds of wild cherries
- one gallon of brandy
- two pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Remove pits from ten pounds of wild cherries and crush them, then place both the pits and cherries in a clay jar, cover with one gallon of brandy and let stand in a cool place for eight weeks
2. After this time add two pounds of sugar, strain and then filter through paper and pour into bottles
3. Do not use before six weeks after bottling
