## Ingredients

- kohlrabi
- fat mutton
- bread soaked in milk
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- burnt sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the kohlrabi, cut off the top, and hollow out the middle.
2. Mince raw fatty mutton, add bread soaked in milk and then squeezed out, salt and pepper to taste.
3. Stuff the hollowed kohlrabi with this filling and cover with the cut-off tops.
4. Place butter in a broad pan and arrange the kohlrabi one next to another.
5. Braise covered until tender, pouring water on it from time to time.
6. Just before serving, season with butter mixed with flour and add a little burnt sugar for color.
7. For six persons use twelve kohlrabi and one pound of mutton.
