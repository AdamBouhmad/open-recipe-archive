## Ingredients

- cabbage head
- water
- broth
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- fat or butter or lard
- sugar
- flour
- sour apple

## Directions

1. Shred a cabbage head, throw into boiling water and let stand about ten minutes, then drain the cabbage and press the water out of it.
2. For a medium head of cabbage pour into a pan half a quart of hot broth mixed half with water, a teaspoon of strong vinegar, two tablespoons of fat, butter or lard.
3. Add the cabbage, salt and pepper to taste and braise on low heat covered for one and a half or two hours.
4. Just before serving, when completely tender, sprinkle in a teaspoon of sugar and season with a tablespoon of flour browned with a tablespoon of butter.
5. Some use finely chopped sour apples instead of vinegar.
