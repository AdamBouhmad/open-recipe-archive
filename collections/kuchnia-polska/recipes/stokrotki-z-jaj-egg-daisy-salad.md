## Ingredients

- five hard-boiled eggs
- two tablespoons oil
- small teaspoon lemon juice
- salt
- red pepper
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Hard boil five eggs and place in cold water
2. When completely cooled, peel and slice crosswise into very thin slices, about ten slices per egg
3. Carefully remove the yolks, keeping the egg white rings intact
4. Arrange the egg white rings on a plate lined with lettuce leaves or on a half-dish in a circle like a wreath, with the rings overlapping at the edges
5. Place the yolks in a small bowl and mash them to a smooth paste with two tablespoons of oil and small teaspoon of lemon juice, adding salt and a bit of red pepper
6. Using a small spoon, place a little of this mixture in the center of each white ring
7. This is a decorative salad that looks like a wreath of daisies
