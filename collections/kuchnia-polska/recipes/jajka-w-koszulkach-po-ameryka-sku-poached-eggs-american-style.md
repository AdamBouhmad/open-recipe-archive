## Ingredients

- water
- one and a half tablespoons vinegar
- half a tablespoon salt
- eggs
- white bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Fill a deep frying pan halfway with water, add one and a half tablespoons of vinegar and half a tablespoon of salt
2. Place on heat, and when water boils, carefully pour cracked eggs onto the pan and cook until the whites set
3. Cut thin slices of white bread and dry them in a hot oven or with a toast-making machine until golden brown
4. While the toast is hot, spread with butter and place one egg on each slice and immediately serve on the table
5. For cooking eggs it is best to use special metal molds without a bottom, which are placed in the water; eggs cooked in them will not spread and will have a nice form
