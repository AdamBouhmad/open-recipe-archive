## Ingredients

- pheasant with tail feathers and head with neck
- pheasant wings
- thin slices of lard
- butter
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Cut out the entire tail with feathers without damaging them, also remove the head with neck and wings without plucking them
2. Pluck the body
3. Cover the entire pheasant with thin slices of lard and let rest for twenty-four hours
4. After this time, stuff the entire pheasant very densely with very thinly sliced lard
5. Roast, continually basting with butter
6. Just before serving, sprinkle with grated bread and pour over browned butter
7. To serve: cut a slice of bread the length of a whole loaf, brown it, place in the center of a platter
8. Place the roasted pheasant on the bread, fasten the tail on a wire from one end and the head from the other
9. Place the wings on the sides, spread as if in flight
10. The body can be served whole, or cut into thin slices and then arranged again so the pheasant appears whole
