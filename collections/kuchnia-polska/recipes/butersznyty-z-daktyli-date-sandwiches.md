## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound dates
- juice from 1/2 lemon
- butter
- bread
- chopped almonds (optional)

## Directions

1. Rub dates into a mass
2. Add lemon juice and mix
3. Cut bread into very thin slices and spread with butter
4. Place a layer of date mixture on the bread and cover with another slice
5. These sandwiches can be varied by sprinkling the date mixture with chopped almonds
6. Raisins may be used instead of dates
