## Ingredients

- chicken or 2 pounds veal
- vegetables
- barley or pearl groats
- small amount of butter
- 1 cup sour cream
- fried croutons
- fresh parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Cook broth with vegetables from chicken or veal, cooking until the meat is completely tender.
2. Separately cook barley or pearl groats with a small amount of butter until thick, making sure it does not burn and breaks down well, constantly collecting the formed starch in a bowl, as this is the best part.
3. When the groats are completely cooked, press through a fine sieve, pouring broth over it and mixing well.
4. Add 1 cup sour cream and bring to a boil.
5. Serve with croutons fried in butter and chopped fresh parsley.
