## Ingredients

- young or older carrots
- butter
- salt
- sugar
- flour
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Young carrots after scraping should be cut into quarters with the center intact; older carrots are best cut into thin strips, discarding the center.
2. Pour water in a pan and bring to a boil, then drain the water.
3. Pour in fresh hot water but little, add butter, salt to taste and a little sugar.
4. Braise covered; when half-cooked, mix some butter with flour and season the carrots with it.
5. Just before serving pour in a few spoonfuls of sweet cream, bring to a boil and serve immediately.
