## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- two ounces of cocoa or grated chocolate
- sugar
- vanilla or vanilla extract
- two egg yolks
- small croutons or white bread

## Directions

1. Bring one quart of milk to a boil with two ounces of cocoa or grated chocolate, add sugar and vanilla or vanilla extract and pour into a bowl into which two beaten egg yolks have been poured previously, and mix well
2. Serve with small croutons made from bread roll or white bread as an accompaniment to this soup
