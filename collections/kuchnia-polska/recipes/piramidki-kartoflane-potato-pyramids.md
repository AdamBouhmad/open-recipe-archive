## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- butter
- hot sour cream or milk
- salt
- pepper
- one beaten egg

## Directions

1. Mash boiled potatoes and add some butter, hot sour cream or milk, salt and pepper to taste
2. Beat with a spoon for about ten minutes
3. Add a beaten egg and mix well
4. Form small pyramids from this mixture
5. Bake in a hot oven until well browned
6. After removing from the oven, brush around with butter and serve on a hot serving dish
