## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds tender beef, minced finely
- half a pound minced pork
- two tablespoons minced beef suet from the kidney
- two rolls, skinned and soaked in cold water, or the same amount of white stale bread, pressed well of water
- salt and pepper to taste
- one roasted and finely minced onion
- two egg yolks
- a few tablespoons young bacon, cut into cubes
- flour
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Mince one and a half pounds tender beef very finely, add half a pound minced pork and two tablespoons minced beef suet from the kidney
2. Mix, then mince again
3. Add two rolls skinned and soaked in cold water, or the same amount of white stale bread pressed well of water
4. Add salt and pepper to taste, one roasted and finely minced onion, two egg yolks and a few tablespoons young bacon cut into cubes
5. Mix it all and form into an oblong loaf
6. Place on a buttered baking sheet and put in a hot oven for half an hour, basting frequently during roasting with the drippings from the meat
7. Before serving, thicken the sauce with flour and sour cream
