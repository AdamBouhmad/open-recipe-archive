## Ingredients

- ordinary broth from beef bones, veal, or offal
- vegetables for broth
- two large cauliflowers
- cold water
- salt
- flour
- butter
- egg yolks
- cold water

## Directions

1. Cook ordinary broth, which may be made from beef bones, veal, or offal.
2. After the broth is cooked and vegetables added in whole form, take two large cauliflowers, clean them, place in a pot, cover with cold water, salt, and bring to a boil once, then drain the water.
3. Break the cauliflowers into pieces and place in the broth, cooking until completely tender.
4. Remove all the vegetables from the broth.
5. Press the broth together with the cauliflower through a sieve or colander, then pour back into the pot and bring to a boil.
6. Brown a spoon of flour with a spoon of butter and thicken the boiling soup with this mixture.
7. Beat two egg yolks well with a spoon of cold water, pour into a serving bowl and onto this slowly pour the hot soup, stirring constantly with a spoon.
8. This soup is usually served with small pastries or croutons with cheese.
