## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- salt
- butter
- warm water

## Directions

1. Beat 2 whole eggs for five minutes, then add 1/4 teaspoon of salt and beat for three more minutes.
2. Pour the beaten eggs into a cup smeared with butter and place in a baking pan with warm water.
3. Cover with paper smeared with fat and place in a moderately hot oven for about half an hour.
4. When a knife inserted shows no mass remaining on it, this is a sign that the eggs have cooked, then remove from the oven.
