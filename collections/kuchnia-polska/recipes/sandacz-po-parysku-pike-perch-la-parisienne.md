## Ingredients

- sandacz (pike-perch)
- salt
- butter
- flour
- heavy cream
- egg yolks
- parmesan cheese, grated
- bread roll

## Directions

1. Clean the pike-perch, split it in half, remove the backbone, salt it and let it sit for an hour.
2. Then place the fish on hot but not browned butter and braise on very low heat until tender, then remove from heat and let it cool.
3. Make a roux by cooking a tablespoon of good butter with a tablespoon of flour on low heat; dilute with a quarter measure of thick cream and cook on very low heat until thickened.
4. Remove from heat, add two beaten egg yolks and one ounce of grated parmesan and mix well.
5. Break the cooled fish into small pieces and arrange half on a buttered porcelain platter or metal mold, pour the sauce over it, then add the other half of the fish and pour the remaining sauce over it.
6. Sprinkle one ounce of grated parmesan on top, pour browned butter with breadcrumb over it and place in the oven to brown around the edges, then serve on the table with the platter or mold.
