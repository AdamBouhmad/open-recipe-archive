## Ingredients

- three egg yolks
- salt
- half pound olive oil
- two tablespoons good vinegar
- one teaspoon sugar

## Directions

1. Beat three egg yolks in a bowl, salt and stir with a wooden spoon for twenty minutes.
2. When it thickens, add half a pound of olive oil, pouring very slowly, preferably in drops, constantly stirring with a spoon.
3. When it thickens, add about two tablespoons of good vinegar and one teaspoon of sugar and keep in a cool place until ready to use.
