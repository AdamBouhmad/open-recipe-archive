## Ingredients

- one cup rice
- two quarts boiling water
- one rounded teaspoon salt
- one tablespoon butter
- boiled eggs or fried eggs

## Directions

1. Rinse one cup of rice in cold water, drain, add to a saucepan and cover with two quarts of boiling water
2. Add one rounded teaspoon of salt and cook for half an hour
3. After cooking, pour onto a colander, and when all the water drains, return the rice to the saucepan and mix with one tablespoon of butter
4. Spread on a hot serving platter and arrange boiled or fried eggs on top
