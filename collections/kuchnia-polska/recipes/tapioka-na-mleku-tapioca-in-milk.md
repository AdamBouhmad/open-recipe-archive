## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup of tapioca
- 2 cups of cold water
- 1 quart of milk
- 2 tablespoons of butter
- 1 onion
- a few celery stalks
- 1 tablespoon of flour
- salt and pepper
- a little ground nutmeg

## Directions

1. Soak 1/2 cup of tapioca in 2 cups of cold water for 5-6 hours
2. Cook in a double boiler for 1 hour
3. In another pot, boil 1 quart of milk
4. Chop 2 tablespoons of butter, 1 onion, and a few celery stalks finely
5. Put them in a pan and fry for 10 minutes
6. Add 1 tablespoon of flour and mix well to obtain a smooth paste
7. Add this to the milk
8. Add salt, pepper, and a little ground nutmeg
9. Cook for another 10 minutes
10. Pour the milk into the tapioca and cook together for 1/2 hour
11. Serve immediately
