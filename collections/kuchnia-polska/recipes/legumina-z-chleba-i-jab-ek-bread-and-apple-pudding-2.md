## Ingredients

- half a small loaf of white bread
- butter
- several firm apples
- sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. Cut half a small loaf of white bread into thin slices and spread thinly with butter.
2. Peel, remove seeds and cut into small pieces several firm apples.
3. Take a tin baking mold and line it with bread, then place a layer of apples, then sprinkle with sugar and so on until the dish is full, then put into the oven and bake until the apples are completely soft.
4. Cream is usually served separately with this pudding.
