## Ingredients

- one pound Italian pasta
- salted water
- one-quarter pound cooked ham
- one tablespoon butter
- three tablespoons thick sour cream
- a few tablespoons grated cheese
- butter for greasing the mold

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of Italian pasta in salted water until tender
2. Drain the water and place the pasta in a salad bowl
3. Chop one-quarter pound of cooked ham into small pieces or mince it
4. Add one tablespoon of butter and three tablespoons of thick sour cream
5. Add a few tablespoons of grated cheese
6. Mix well and place the entire mixture in a buttered baking mold or baking pan
7. Place in the oven for twenty minutes
