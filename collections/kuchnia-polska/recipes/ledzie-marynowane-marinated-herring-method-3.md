## Ingredients

- herring
- water
- onion
- lemon
- herring roe
- sour cream
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare herring as in method two
2. Instead of oil, blend the roe with sweet sour cream, using half a quart for six herring
3. Dilute the roe blended with sour cream with strong vinegar, pass through a sieve
4. Pour this sauce over the herring
5. Herring prepared by any of these methods is ready to eat the next day
