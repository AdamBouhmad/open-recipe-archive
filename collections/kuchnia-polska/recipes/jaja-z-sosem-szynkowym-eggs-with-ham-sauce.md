## Ingredients

- one-eighth pound cooked ham, finely chopped
- one onion
- two sprigs green parsley
- water
- pepper
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Chop very finely one-eighth pound of cooked ham with one onion and two sprigs of green parsley, place in a small pot
2. Add half a cup of water, a pinch of pepper and braise covered for fifteen minutes
3. Hard-boil four eggs in their shells (it is best to use special metal molds without a bottom so the whites do not spread)
4. Place on a serving platter, sprinkle lightly with lemon juice, then pour over the sauce described above, which should first be strained through a sieve, and bring to a boil again
