## Ingredients

- leftover roasted chicken from previous day
- chicken wings, neck, back, giblets and all remains
- water
- onion slices
- celery seeds
- salt
- pepper
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Take the wings, neck, back, giblets and all remains from the roasted chicken and cover with a small amount of water.
2. Simmer covered with a few onion slices until the meat falls off the bones.
3. Remove all meat from the bones and chop fairly coarsely.
4. Add some celery seeds, salt and pepper to taste.
5. Take one-third of the chopped meat and spread on the bottom of a metal mold or bowl.
6. Lay a layer of hard-boiled eggs cut into thin slices on top.
7. Repeat with meat and eggs to make three layers total.
8. Place the remaining bones back in the pot and cook until only enough broth remains to well moisten the meat and egg mixture in the mold.
9. Pour the broth over the meat and eggs in the mold and place in a cold location.
10. Serve when well chilled.
