## Ingredients

- one watermelon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place the watermelon on ice and when very cold, cut into slices about an inch thick
2. Remove all the seeds and then from these slices cut out small diagonal squares, which are then arranged on a glass serving platter and sprinkled with sugar, or placed directly into small bowls
