## Ingredients

- two pounds of dates
- two cups of water
- one pound of sugar
- a piece of butter
- one quarter of grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pit two pounds of dates
2. Add two cups of water, one pound of sugar, a piece of butter and one quarter of grated nutmeg
3. Mix everything and place on the fire
4. Boil, stirring frequently, until a thick mass forms
5. Fill glasses and cover with buttered paper
