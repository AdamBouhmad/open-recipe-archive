## Ingredients

- three lemons
- three oranges
- three grapefruits
- three quarts water per quart of prepared fruit
- sugar, measure for measure with the mass

## Directions

1. Wash the fruits carefully, dry them, cut off the ends
2. Chop the fruits together with the skin into thin slices
3. Discard all veins and thick membranes
4. For each quart of chopped fruit, take three quarts of water
5. Pour everything into a pot, cover and let stand for twenty-four hours
6. After this time, place on heat and cook for one hour
7. Add sugar in the same measure as the mass
8. Cook on very low heat until a thick jelly forms
9. Remove from heat, fill jars or glasses, seal with paraffin and store
