## Ingredients

- 3 pounds mixed meat (1 pound each of beef, pork, and lamb), cut into pieces
- 1/2 pound young bacon, cut into cubes
- 6 onions, sliced thinly
- salt and paprika to taste
- 6 medium potatoes, cut into cubes
- beef broth or consommé, 1 tablespoon at a time

## Directions

1. Cut 3 pounds of various tender meat into walnut-sized pieces.
2. Cut 1/2 pound of young bacon into cubes, place in a pot, and fry it without letting it brown.
3. When the bacon begins to fry, add about 6 onions sliced thinly and braise for 5 minutes covered.
4. Add the meat, sprinkle with salt and plenty of paprika.
5. Braise on low heat covered for 1 hour, without stirring, but add 1 tablespoon of broth or consommé from time to time.
6. After the meat has braised for 1 hour in this manner, peel and cut 6 medium potatoes into cubes and add them to the meat.
7. Mix everything and continue cooking for about 15 minutes until the potatoes are completely soft.
