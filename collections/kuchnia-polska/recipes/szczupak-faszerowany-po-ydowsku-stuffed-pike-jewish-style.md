## Ingredients

- pike, at least three pounds
- one soaked roll per pound of meat
- one tablespoon butter
- one large onion
- salt and white pepper
- mixed vegetables

## Directions

1. Clean the pike carefully
2. Slit the belly and remove all meat, leaving only a little near the tail, without damaging the skin
3. Separate the meat carefully from bones and chop very finely
4. Add one soaked roll per pound of meat
5. Melt one tablespoon butter in a pan, add one large previously scalded and grated onion and fry, being careful not to brown
6. Add this to the fish, season with salt and white pepper to taste
7. Mix everything thoroughly into a uniform mass
8. Stuff the pike with this forcemeat, giving it a neat form
9. Place in an oblong pan with plenty of mixed vegetables on the bottom
10. Cover with cold water and boil for one hour
11. When stuffing, be careful not to add too much forcemeat as it swells during cooking and the skin may burst
12. Serve hot with white sauce or parsley sauce, or cold with vinegar and oil
13. Alternatively, cut the pike into large rings first, remove the meat without damaging the skin, chop and season as above, stuff each ring separately and cook as usual
