## Ingredients

- one pound chopped beef
- half a pound stale white bread
- water
- one or two onions, finely chopped
- salt and pepper to taste
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Take one pound chopped beef and half a pound stale white bread
2. Soak the bread in water, squeeze out, and mix well with the meat
3. Add one or two finely chopped onions, salt and pepper to taste
4. Mix the entire mass thoroughly and brown over gentle heat
5. Cool slightly and form into small round cutlets
6. Sprinkle with grated bread and fry on medium heat, ensuring they are well-fried but not dried out
7. Garlic may be added if desired
