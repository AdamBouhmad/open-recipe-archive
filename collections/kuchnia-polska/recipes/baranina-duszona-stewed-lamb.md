## Ingredients

- lamb roast
- vinegar
- roots and spices
- butter
- bacon slices
- celery
- parsley
- onion
- juniper berries
- flour
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean the lamb roast of excess fat and membranes and soak for several hours in vinegar with roots.
2. After removing from vinegar, place in a very hot oven for a few minutes to brown quickly on top.
3. Put some butter in a braising pan, several slices of bacon, chop celery, parsley, and onion, add a few juniper berries and place the browned meat on top.
4. Pour in about two tablespoons of the vinegar in which it was soaked and stew on low heat.
5. When almost tender, remove from the pan, dust with flour around it. Strain the sauce through a sieve, then place the meat back in the pan, pour the strained sauce over it and continue stewing until tender.
6. Shortly before serving, season with sour cream.
