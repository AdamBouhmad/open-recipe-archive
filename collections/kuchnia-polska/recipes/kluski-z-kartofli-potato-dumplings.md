## Ingredients

- one and a half quarts of floury potatoes
- salt to taste
- one grated roll
- four egg yolks
- one cup flour
- marjoram
- four egg whites

## Directions

1. Peel and cook one and a half quarts of floury potatoes in salted water
2. Drain and mash in the same pot or bowl
3. Transfer to a dough bowl and add one grated roll, four egg yolks, one cup flour, salt to taste and a pinch of marjoram
4. Mix well with a rolling pin
5. Add the foam from four beaten egg whites and mix slowly
6. Turn the prepared dough onto a flour-dusted table
7. Roll out to finger thickness and dust well with flour
8. Cut large dumplings into the shapes of lazy dumplings
9. Cook in a large amount of water in a large pot
10. When they float to the surface, remove with a slotted spoon and place directly on a platter
11. Pour rendered young bacon over and serve
