## Ingredients

- one cup corn meal
- one cup wheat flour
- half teaspoon salt
- half teaspoon soda
- half cup finely chopped beef suet
- two cups sour milk

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of corn meal, one cup of wheat flour, half teaspoon of salt, and half teaspoon of soda
2. Add half cup of finely chopped beef suet and two cups of sour milk and mix perfectly
3. Pour into a form and place in a moderately hot oven for half an hour
