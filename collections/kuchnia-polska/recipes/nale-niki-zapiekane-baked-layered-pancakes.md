## Ingredients

- regular pancakes
- four egg yolks
- quarter pound sugar
- cup sour cream
- vanilla or lemon extract
- butter
- grated bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Make regular pancakes as described previously
2. Beat four egg yolks until white with quarter pound sugar, add cup of sour cream, season with vanilla or lemon extract and mix well into a uniform mass
3. Take a baking dish about the width of the pancakes, grease well with butter and sprinkle with grated bread crumbs
4. Spread the egg yolk and cream mixture on the pancakes and layer in the dish as follows: spread one pancake and place on the bottom, then place another pancake unspread on top, then another spread pancake and cover with an unspread one, continue layering until the dish is full, with two pancakes per layer and filling between
5. Place in a hot oven for half an hour
6. Temperature is important, as if the oven is too cold or too hot, the filling will not bake but only melt
7. Alternatively, instead of the above filling, use fine sugar moistened with lemon juice and sprinkled with a little grated lemon zest
