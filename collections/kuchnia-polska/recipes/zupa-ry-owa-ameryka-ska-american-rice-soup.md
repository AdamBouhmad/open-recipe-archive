## Ingredients

- rice, washed
- broth from veal or chicken, or beef broth
- carrot
- celery
- onion
- butter
- flour
- nutmeg
- cloves
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Make this soup best on veal or chicken broth, though beef broth may also be used.
2. Pour half a small cup of washed rice into one and a half quarts of broth, bring to a boil, then add the following vegetables: three spoons of carrot, three spoons of celery, and three spoons of onion chopped finely.
3. In a small pot, melt three spoons of butter, add the vegetables and braise covered over very low heat for twenty minutes, then remove and place in the soup.
4. After adding these vegetables, rub two spoons of flour into the remaining butter in the pot, add some nutmeg, a few cloves, half a teaspoon of pepper and two teaspoons of salt and also pour into the soup, then cook everything for two hours over very low heat, then strain.
