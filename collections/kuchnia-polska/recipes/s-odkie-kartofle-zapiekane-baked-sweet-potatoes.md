## Ingredients

- sweet potatoes
- butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- milk

## Directions

1. Peel and slice sweet potatoes very thinly lengthwise
2. Butter a baking dish or baking form and place a thin layer of sliced potatoes on the bottom
3. Sprinkle with half a teaspoon flour and a little salt and pepper
4. Place another similar layer and continue until the dish is full
5. Scatter small pieces of butter on top
6. Pour in just enough milk to barely moisten everything
7. Put in a moderately hot oven for forty minutes
