## Ingredients

- one cup finely minced cooked ham
- one egg
- one cup any meat broth
- one-quarter teaspoon dry mustard (ground mustard)
- one teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- one tablespoon butter
- one teaspoon flour
- one egg (for coating)
- flour for the batter
- two tablespoons melted butter (for batter)
- one cup warm water (for batter)
- hot fat for frying

## Directions

1. Heat the broth and add flour mixed with butter
2. Add the minced ham and remaining ingredients and bring to a boil
3. Pour in one beaten egg, mix well and remove from heat to cool
4. When cooled completely, form small balls from the mixture
5. Dip each ball separately in thin batter
6. Fry in hot fat in a pan; use plenty of fat
7. For the batter: mix one cup of flour with two tablespoons of melted butter, add one egg and one cup of warm water
