## Ingredients

- potatoes
- good butter

## Directions

1. peel the potatoes, cut into slices and scald with boiling water
2. melt some good butter in a pan, add the potatoes and braise covered for about twenty minutes, shaking the pan frequently
3. to make them brown, finish cooking without the cover
4. alternatively, fry whole potatoes in a pan filled with hot fat, or use a small spoon to cut little balls from potatoes, shape them like small potatoes and fry in hot fat until browned
