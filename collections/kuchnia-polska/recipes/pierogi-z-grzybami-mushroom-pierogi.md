## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of dried mushrooms
- hot water
- two onions
- one tablespoon of butter
- a handful of grated bread
- salt and pepper
- flour
- egg
- water

## Directions

1. Pour hot water over one quarter pound of dried mushrooms, wash well, cook until tender, and chop very finely.
2. Peel and chop two onions, fry in one tablespoon of butter, and add to the mushrooms along with a handful of grated bread, salt, and pepper to taste, mixing everything well.
3. Knead dough from flour, egg, and water as usual for pierogi.
4. Place heaps of the mushroom mixture on the dough and cut out small pierogi with a glass, sealing the edges well.
5. Cook in a large pot of boiling water and arrange on a serving platter, pouring melted butter over them.
