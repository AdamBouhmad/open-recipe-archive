## Ingredients

- half a quart of milk
- half a cup of cornmeal
- half a quart of sliced apples
- half a cup of molasses
- quarter teaspoon of salt
- one quart of milk
- sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. Bring half a quart of milk to a boil, add half a cup of cornmeal and half a quart of apples sliced into pieces, mix and add half a cup of molasses and a quarter teaspoon of salt.
2. Remove from heat, add one quart of milk, mix well and pour all the mass into a baking mold, place in a gentle oven and let it bake for three hours.
3. Remove from the oven and cool.
4. Serve cold with sugar and cream.
