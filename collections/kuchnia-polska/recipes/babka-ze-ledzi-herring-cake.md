## Ingredients

- two large herring
- milk
- four medium potatoes
- one egg
- sour cream
- pepper to taste
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Soak two large herring in milk for eighteen hours
2. Remove from milk, clean, remove skin and bones and pound into a mass in a mortar
3. Bake four medium potatoes in their skins in the oven and then press through a sieve
4. Beat one egg strongly, add a few tablespoons of thick sour cream and pepper to taste
5. Mix all ingredients into a uniform mass
6. Butter and flour a small mold, fill with the herring and potato mass
7. Place in a moderately hot oven for twenty minutes, then serve immediately
