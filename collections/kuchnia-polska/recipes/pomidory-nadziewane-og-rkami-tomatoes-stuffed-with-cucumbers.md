## Ingredients

- tomatoes, one per person
- pair of cucumbers
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the tomatoes and peel off the skin
2. Scoop out the center of each tomato with a small spoon
3. Peel and chop the cucumbers not too finely
4. Make French sauce from oil, vinegar, salt and pepper
5. Mix the chopped cucumbers with the French sauce
6. Fill the hollowed tomatoes with this mixture
7. Pour the same French sauce over the top
8. Serve each tomato on its own small plate on a lettuce leaf
