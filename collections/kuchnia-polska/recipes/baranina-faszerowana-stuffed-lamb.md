## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- one cup of grated cooked potatoes
- one cup of grated roll
- one tablespoon of melted butter
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- one tablespoon of tomato sauce (catsup)
- salt
- pepper
- a pinch of powdered cloves
- bay leaf
- one tablespoon of orange juice
- one tablespoon of milk

## Directions

1. Butterfly the lamb shoulder on the inner side and remove the bones, which use for soup.
2. Make the following stuffing: Take one cup of grated cooked potatoes, one cup of grated roll, one tablespoon of melted butter, one tablespoon of vinegar, one tablespoon of tomato sauce (catsup), salt and pepper to taste, as well as a pinch of powdered cloves, a bay leaf, one tablespoon of orange juice and one tablespoon of milk; mix all of this well.
3. Fill the shoulder with this stuffing where the bone was removed, then roll the meat with the stuffing inside and roast on a roasting pan, basting frequently during roasting.
4. Roasting time is from fifteen to twenty minutes per pound of meat.
