## Ingredients

- veal feet
- vegetables
- flour, one tablespoon
- butter, one tablespoon
- sugar
- currants
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the veal feet the same way as for aspic and cut into pieces, arrange on a platter and pour with the following gray sauce:
2. Fry one tablespoon of flour with one tablespoon of butter until browned, dilute with broth or strong bouillon and bring to a boil.
3. Soak a few pieces of sugar in water or about one tablespoon of regular sugar, brown it in a pan, pour into the sauce and add a handful of cleaned dried currants without pits.
