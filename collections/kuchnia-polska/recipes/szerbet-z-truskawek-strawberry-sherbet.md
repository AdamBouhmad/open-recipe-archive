## Ingredients

- three-quarters pound ripe strawberries
- three glasses water
- several lemon slices
- one teaspoon orange flower water
- one pound sugar

## Directions

1. Crush three-quarters pound of ripe strawberries into a paste, add three glasses of water, add several slices of lemon and one teaspoon of orange flower water
2. Place in a cool place for two or three hours
3. Put one pound of sugar in another vessel, cover with cloth and strain the strawberry juice through this cloth onto the sugar, press out, stir until the sugar completely dissolves
4. Then place on ice
