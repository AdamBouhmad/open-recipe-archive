## Ingredients

- sandacz (pike-perch)
- vinegar
- roots and herbs
- salt
- butter
- crayfish butter
- lemon
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Wipe the cleaned pike-perch with cloth and place in cold vinegar boiled with roots and salt for an hour.
2. Half an hour before serving, butter an oblong baking dish, arrange the pike-perch, pour melted butter or oil over it and place in a moderately hot oven for half an hour, basting with melted fat at least every five minutes.
3. When cooked, carefully slide it onto a platter and coat with melted crayfish butter so that the entire fish is covered.
4. Then arrange with lemon slices and small boiled potatoes.
