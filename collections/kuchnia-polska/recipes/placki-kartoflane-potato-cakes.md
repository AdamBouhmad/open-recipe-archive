## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- half cup flour per heaping cup of potatoes
- one tablespoon milk
- half teaspoon salt
- butter for serving

## Directions

1. Mash cooked potatoes into a smooth mass.
2. For each heaping cup of potatoes, take half a cup of flour, one tablespoon of milk, and half a teaspoon of salt.
3. Mix everything well, place on a work surface, and roll out to the thickness of an inch.
4. Cut small circles from the dough, prick with a fork, and bake in a hot oven.
5. Serve hot with butter.
