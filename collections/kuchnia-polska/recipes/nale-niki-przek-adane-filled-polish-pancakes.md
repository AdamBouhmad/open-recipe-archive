## Ingredients

- regular pancakes
- jam, marmalade, preserves, or curd cheese beaten with butter, egg yolks and sugar

## Directions

1. Make regular pancakes as described above
2. After cooking, brush each one with jam, marmalade, preserves, or curd cheese beaten with butter, egg yolks and sugar
3. The jam should be thick
4. Fold and fry on both sides until browned
5. Serve to the table
