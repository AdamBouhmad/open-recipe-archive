## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked calf's liver
- 1/4 pound finely chopped beef suet from the tenderloin
- 1/2 quart grated roll
- two eggs
- salt and pepper to taste
- some chopped green parsley

## Directions

1. Grate the cooked calf's liver and press it through a sieve
2. Add to it 1/4 pound finely chopped beef suet from the tenderloin, 1/2 quart grated roll, two eggs, salt and pepper to taste and some chopped green parsley
3. Mix everything well, lay out on a board and dust your hands with flour
4. Make round dumplings the size of a walnut
5. Place them in boiling broth or water and cook for three minutes
6. Remove with a spoon and place in a bowl
