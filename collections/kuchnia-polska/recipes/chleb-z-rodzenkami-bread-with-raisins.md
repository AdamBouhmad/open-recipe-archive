## Ingredients

- half ounce or one tablet of yeast
- half quart of milk or water
- one teaspoon of salt
- two tablespoons of sugar
- two tablespoons of lard
- one and one half quarts of sifted flour
- one and one half cups of seedless raisins

## Directions

1. Dissolve half ounce or one tablet of yeast in half quart of milk or water
2. Add one teaspoon of salt, two tablespoons of sugar, two tablespoons of lard, and one and one half quarts of sifted flour mixed with one and one half cups of seedless raisins
3. The temperature of the prepared dough should be 82 to 84 degrees Fahrenheit
4. Cover the dough and let it rise well
5. Divide into three small loaves and let rise
6. Bake in a moderately hot oven like regular bread
