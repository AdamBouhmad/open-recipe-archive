## Ingredients

- handful of cooked asparagus
- small can of marinated green peas
- one onion
- a couple of potatoes
- half a quart of water
- salt and pepper to taste
- two tablespoons of butter
- three egg yolks
- milk if needed

## Directions

1. Take a handful of cooked asparagus (may be leftovers from the previous day), a small can of marinated green peas, one onion, and a couple of potatoes
2. Pour in half a quart of water and cook until everything is completely soft
3. Strain through a sieve; if the soup is too thick, add milk
4. Add salt and pepper to taste, two tablespoons of butter, and three egg yolks
5. Do not cook further after adding the yolks
6. Serve with croutons cut into cubes
