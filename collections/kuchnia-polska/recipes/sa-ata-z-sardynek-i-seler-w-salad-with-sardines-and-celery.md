## Ingredients

- small box of sardines in oil
- celery stalks, twice the amount of sardines
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- one cup of milk
- half a teaspoon of flour

## Directions

1. Remove sardines from oil and lay them on paper towels, turning them so all oil is absorbed
2. Dice the celery stalks and sprinkle with salt and pepper, then pour vinegar over them
3. Arrange the seasoned celery in the center of a glass plate in pyramid form
4. Arrange the sardines around the celery
5. Heat one cup of milk and season it with half a teaspoon of flour, pepper and salt
6. Remove from heat and cool completely
7. Pour this sauce over the arranged celery and sardines
