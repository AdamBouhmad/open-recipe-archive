## Ingredients

- 6 medium potatoes of uniform size
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- 1/2 cup sweet milk

## Directions

1. Choose six medium potatoes, trying to make them all the same size, of good shape and with smooth skin.
2. Wash them carefully, cut off both ends with a knife and bake in a moderately hot oven.
3. When the potatoes are completely soft, hollow them out with a spoon so that only the skin remains, being careful not to damage it.
4. To the scooped out mass add a teaspoon of butter, salt and pepper to taste and half a cup of sweet milk.
5. Beat everything with a spoon until it becomes a uniform mass, then fill the hollowed-out skins with it.
6. Arrange on a butter-smeared baking dish and bake in a hot oven until the ends brown.
