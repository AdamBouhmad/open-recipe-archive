## Ingredients

- six eggs
- one cup of milk
- salt and pepper
- one half teaspoon or more of chopped fresh parsley
- a dozen large oysters
- one tablespoon of melted butter

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs and separate the whites from the yolks
2. Beat the whites until stiff and the yolks separately
3. Pour one cup of milk into the beaten yolks, add salt, pepper and one half teaspoon or more of chopped fresh parsley
4. Chop a dozen large oysters finely and add to the milk, pouring slowly and mixing well
5. After mixing, add one tablespoon of melted butter
6. Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites gently
7. Melt one tablespoon of butter in a large skillet and pour in the egg and oyster mixture
8. Fry over gentle heat without stirring; when the whole mixture is well set, turn onto a hot plate and serve
