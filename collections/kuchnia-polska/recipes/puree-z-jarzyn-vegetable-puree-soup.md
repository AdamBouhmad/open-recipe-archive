## Ingredients

- one large onion
- two potatoes
- butter
- two tablespoons washed rice
- one carrot
- broth or water
- two tablespoons corn starch
- milk
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop finely one large onion and two potatoes.
2. Brown the onion in a pan with two spoons of butter and place in a pot.
3. Add the potatoes, two spoons of washed rice, one finely chopped carrot.
4. Pour in one quart of broth or water and simmer until all vegetables are tender.
5. Press everything through a sieve and pour back into the pot.
6. Mix two spoons of corn starch in half a cup of milk and pour into the soup.
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve.
