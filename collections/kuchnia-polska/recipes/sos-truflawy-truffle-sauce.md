## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- strong broth
- one tablespoon of preserved truffle flavor or three fresh truffles and red wine
- one black plum

## Directions

1. Brown one tablespoon of butter with one tablespoon of flour and dissolve in strong broth, pouring enough so the sauce is gravy-like
2. If truffles are preserved, pour in one tablespoon of their flavor; if fresh, cook three pieces in red wine and pour that wine into the sauce
3. Cook one black plum until soft, mash into a paste, mix with the sauce, and strain through a sieve
4. Slice the truffles into round slices, add to the sauce, and boil
