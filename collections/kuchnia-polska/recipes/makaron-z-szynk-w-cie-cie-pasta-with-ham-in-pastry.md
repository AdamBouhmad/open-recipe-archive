## Ingredients

- half pound of flour
- one egg yolk
- one quarter pound of butter
- one teaspoon of sugar
- half pound of Italian pasta
- salted water
- one quarter pound of cooked ham, sliced thin
- one quarter pound of good butter
- one tablespoon of grated cheese, preferably Parmesan

## Directions

1. Mix half pound of flour, one egg yolk, one quarter pound of butter, and one teaspoon of sugar into short pastry.
2. Grease a pot or mold with butter and line it with this pastry.
3. Break half pound of Italian pasta into pieces, cook in salted water, and mix with one quarter pound of cooked ham cut into thin strips, add one quarter pound of good butter and one tablespoon of grated Parmesan cheese, and mix everything perfectly.
4. Place this mixture in the lined pot, cover from the top with pastry, and put in a moderately hot oven.
5. When well browned, remove from the oven and arrange on a serving platter.
6. Serve as a main dish or as small pastries after soup.
