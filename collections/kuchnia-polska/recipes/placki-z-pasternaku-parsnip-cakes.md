## Ingredients

- parsnip roots
- one and a half cups cooked parsnip mass
- one egg
- one tablespoon flour
- salt
- pepper
- butter or fat for frying

## Directions

1. Cook parsnip roots until soft and then mash into a mass.
2. Take one and a half cups of this mass, add one egg, one tablespoon of flour, salt, and some pepper.
3. Mix well and form into thin cakes.
4. Fry on a pan in butter or fat, browning nicely on both sides.
