## Ingredients

- pike or sandacz fish, 6-8 pounds
- miscellaneous vegetables
- onion
- gelatin, 1 ounce
- cold broth, half a quarter
- oil, half a pound
- 1 whole lemon
- fine sugar, 1 teaspoon
- salt
- white gelatin leaves, a few
- red gelatin leaves, a few
- lemon juice
- carrot, sliced
- green peas
- capers
- cornichons
- olives
- mushrooms
- hard-boiled eggs
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Cook broth with miscellaneous vegetables and onion, then cool.
2. Fillet pike or sandacz, place in an oblong pan, cover with cold broth and cook until tender.
3. Remove fish and arrange on a platter.
4. Strain remaining broth through dense cloth and cool.
5. For a 6-8 pound pike, take 1 ounce gelatin and cover with half a quarter of cold broth.
6. When completely softened, pour off the broth and heat on fire.
7. When well heated, add the gelatin, which will dissolve immediately, then cool and beat with a whisk until white.
8. Add half a pound of oil very slowly while continuously beating.
9. When all oil is incorporated, squeeze juice from 1 whole lemon, add 1 teaspoon fine sugar and a pinch of salt.
10. Beat everything into a uniform mass and cover the fish arranged on the platter with this mayonnaise so it is completely covered.
11. Decorate the top with sliced carrot, green peas, capers, cornichons, olives, mushrooms, hard-boiled eggs and lettuce leaves.
12. Separately dissolve a few white gelatin leaves and as many red, add lemon juice and a pinch of sugar and salt, and pour onto plates.
13. When set, cut this aspic into triangles and decorate the platter with the fish with them.
