## Ingredients

- fat pigeons
- butter
- onions
- sour apples
- mushrooms
- lemon juice
- broth or bouillon
- butter
- flour
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean and dress the fat pigeons, cut them into quarters, and place them in a pot with a generous amount of butter, then braise on low heat covered until soft
2. Remove the pigeons, and in the remaining butter in the pot, place several onions cut into thin slices and braise until soft, then add some chopped sour apples, a few mushrooms, lemon juice from half a lemon, and dilute with broth or bouillon, then bring to a boil
3. In a separate pot, brown 1 tablespoon of butter with 1 tablespoon of flour and dilute with the sauce, then add the pigeons which should be previously soaked in sour cream, cook for about ten minutes and serve
