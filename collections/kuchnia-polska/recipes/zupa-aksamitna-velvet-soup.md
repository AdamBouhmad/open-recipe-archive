## Ingredients

- 1 quart clear broth
- 1 cup sour cream
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Add 1 cup sour cream to 1 quart of clear broth, season with salt and pepper to taste.
2. Beat 4 egg yolks with 4 tablespoons of sour cream and mix into the soup.
3. This soup is usually served in bouillon cups.
