## Ingredients

- one cup tapioca
- cold water
- half a quart of boiling water
- one and a half pounds of sour cherries
- one cup of sugar

## Directions

1. Soak one cup of tapioca overnight in cold water.
2. The next day, place in a pot, pour half a quart of boiling water over it, and cook on a low fire until completely transparent.
3. Remove the pits from one and a half pounds of sour cherries, add one cup of sugar, and mix with the tapioca.
4. Bring to a boil once, pour into a serving dish, and place in a cold place.
5. When completely cool, serve with sugar and cream served separately.
