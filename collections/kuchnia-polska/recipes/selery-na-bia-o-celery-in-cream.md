## Ingredients

- celery stalks (white parts only)
- salted water
- half quart milk
- one onion sliced thin
- one large cup grated bread or white bread
- one tablespoon butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and cut celery stalks into one-inch pieces, using only the white parts
2. Take two cups of cut celery and cook to tenderness in salted water
3. In a separate pot pour half quart of milk and throw in one onion cut into thin slices
4. Cook on low fire until the onion is completely tender
5. Strain the milk
6. Add to the milk one large cup of grated bread or white bread and the cooked celery
7. Also add one tablespoon butter and salt and pepper to taste
8. Mix, boil for about three minutes and serve
