## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked beef, minced
- 1/4 pound young, previously cooked bacon, minced
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 onions, finely minced (preferably roasted)
- a few mushrooms, minced (if available)
- 1 cup grated bread
- 1 or 2 egg yolks
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1/2 quart flour
- 1 egg
- water

## Directions

1. Put 1 tablespoon of butter in a small pan, and when it melts, add about 2 finely minced onions (preferably roasted), fry briefly and immediately remove from heat.
2. Mix with the minced meat; if there are mushrooms, mince a few and add them along with some of the broth in which they were cooked.
3. Add 1 cup of grated bread, 1 or 2 egg yolks, salt and plenty of pepper, and mix everything well.
4. Knead 1/2 quart flour and 1 egg into dough with enough water to make it sufficiently soft.
5. Roll out and cut into diagonal squares.
6. Place walnut-sized portions of meat filling (or slightly larger) on each square, fold and seal the edges.
7. Drop the prepared pierogi into boiling water.
8. When they float to the surface, they are done.
9. Arrange on a platter and pour melted butter with toasted bread, or young bacon, over them.
