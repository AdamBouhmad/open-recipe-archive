## Ingredients

- one calf's brain
- one tablespoon of butter
- four egg yolks
- salt and pepper to taste
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Take one calf's brain, remove the membrane and cook it in salted water with the addition of a few tablespoons of vinegar
2. Beat one tablespoon of butter in a mortar until creamy, then add the brain cut into pieces and stripped of veins and beat together well
3. Beat in the four egg yolks one at a time, beating well after each, along with salt and pepper to taste
4. Beat the remaining four egg whites into a stiff foam, mix with the beaten mass and beat everything again until thick
5. Heat a considerable amount of fat in a pan, take on a spoon as round pieces of mass as possible and throw them into the fat
6. When the dumplings brown, remove them with a spoon and place in a bowl already filled with soup
7. The dumplings should not be placed in an empty bowl because they will be squashed
