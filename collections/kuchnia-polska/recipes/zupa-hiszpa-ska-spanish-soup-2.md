## Ingredients

- One pound of white beans
- One small head of white cabbage
- Some smoked meat
- One red pepper pod
- One onion
- A clove of garlic
- Fat

## Directions

1. Soak one pound of white beans overnight
2. The next day, chop fine one small head of white cabbage, some smoked meat, one red pepper pod
3. Add to the beans and cook one hour
4. Add salt to taste
5. Melt some fat in a pan and fry one finely chopped onion on it and add slowly to the soup, constantly stirring
6. Add one minced clove of garlic, bring to a boil and serve
7. Garlic can be omitted
