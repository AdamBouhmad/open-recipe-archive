## Ingredients

- Half a pound rice
- Hot water
- Butter
- Strong broth or bouillon
- Poultry livers
- Salt

## Directions

1. Pour half a pound of rice with hot water and place on the stove, and when it comes to a boil, drain the water
2. Place one spoonful of butter in a pot, then the parboiled rice and add strong broth or bouillon, then place on low heat and boil
3. When the rice is half tender, place the poultry livers cut in half, cover the pot with a lid and stew on low heat until tender
4. Before serving at table one should salt it and add a little good butter
