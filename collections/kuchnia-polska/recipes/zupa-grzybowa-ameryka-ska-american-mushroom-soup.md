## Ingredients

- Half quart of fresh white mushrooms
- One quart of broth, preferably from veal or chicken
- One tablespoon of flour
- One tablespoon of butter
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Peel half quart of fresh white mushrooms and cut into cubes
2. Stew them in half quart of broth, preferably from veal or chicken
3. As soon as the mushrooms are cooked, add one quart of broth
4. Make a roux with one tablespoon of flour and one tablespoon of butter
5. Add to the soup, season with salt and pepper to taste and serve
