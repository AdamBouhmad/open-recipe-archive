## Ingredients

- leftover roasted or cooked chicken
- chicken meat
- butter
- flour
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from bones and chop finely.
2. For each cup of chicken meat, take a spoon of butter and mix on smooth mass with half a spoon of flour.
3. Heat half a cup of milk and thicken it with the butter and flour mixture.
4. Cook until thickened, then add the chopped chicken meat and season with salt and pepper to taste.
5. Mix well and form small flat cutlets.
6. Dip them in beaten egg and then in grated breadcrumbs.
7. Fry in fat or butter.
8. Note: turkey meat can be used in the same way instead of chicken.
