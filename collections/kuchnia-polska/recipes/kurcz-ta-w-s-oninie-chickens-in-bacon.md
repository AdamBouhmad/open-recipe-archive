## Ingredients

- young chickens
- salt
- thin but wide slices of young, unsalted bacon
- string
- butter
- grated bread
- lettuce or cucumber salad

## Directions

1. Prepare the chickens as usual, then salt them and cover completely with thin but wide slices of young, unsalted bacon and tie with string.
2. Place the prepared chickens in a pan and bake in a moderately hot oven.
3. After baking, unwrap the string and serve the chickens with the bacon, which should be browned.
4. If one does not like bacon, discard it and pour the chickens with butter browned with grated bread.
5. Serve such chickens with lettuce or cucumber salad.
