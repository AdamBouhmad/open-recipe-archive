## Ingredients

- half cup cornmeal
- half teaspoon salt
- half cup wheat flour
- one cup buckwheat flour
- half a cake of yeast dissolved in a small amount of warm water
- half cup milk
- quarter teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over half a cup of cornmeal, add half a teaspoon of salt, stir, and set in a cool place.
2. When cooled to lukewarm temperature, add half a cup of wheat flour and one cup of buckwheat flour, as well as half a cake of yeast dissolved in a small amount of warm water.
3. Make this mixture in the evening.
4. The next day, pour off the water that has collected on top, add half a cup of milk mixed with a quarter teaspoon of soda, and stir well.
5. Fry on a pan as usual for pancakes.
