## Ingredients

- one-half quart flour
- one and one-half teaspoons baking powder
- one teaspoon salt
- two tablespoons butter
- one egg
- two-thirds cup milk
- four large sour apples
- sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Mix one-half quart flour well with one and one-half teaspoons baking powder and one teaspoon salt
2. Rub in two tablespoons butter
3. Add one egg and two-thirds cup milk
4. Mix everything carefully and spread on a baking sheet to a thickness of one-half inch
5. Peel four large sour apples, cut into eight pieces, and arrange in rows on the dough
6. Sprinkle with sugar and a bit of powdered cinnamon
7. Bake in a hot oven for twenty-five minutes
8. Can be served with cream and sugar, or without
