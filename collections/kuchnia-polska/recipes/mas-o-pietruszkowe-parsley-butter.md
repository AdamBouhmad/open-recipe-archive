## Ingredients

- three tablespoons of butter
- one tablespoon of lemon juice
- one and a half tablespoons of finely chopped fresh parsley
- half a teaspoon of salt
- pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Work the butter in a bowl with sour cream
2. add lemon juice, finely chopped fresh parsley, salt and pepper
3. mix everything well
4. spread thickly over boiled fish on a serving platter
5. serve immediately to the table
