## Ingredients

- half pound strawberries or raspberries
- half pound sugar
- two egg whites
- four egg whites

## Directions

1. Press half pound strawberries or raspberries (which must be completely ripe and healthy) through a fine sieve
2. Add half pound sugar and two egg whites
3. Beat in a cool place until white
4. Separately, beat stiff meringue from four egg whites and mix with the fruit mixture
5. Arrange on a baking pan, sprinkle with sugar and put in a very slow oven for half an hour
