## Ingredients

- one gallon of ripe blackberries
- one quart of boiling water
- three quarts of cold water
- two pounds of brown sugar

## Directions

1. Crush one gallon of ripe blackberries
2. Pour one quart of boiling water over the crushed mass and set in a cool place for a whole day
3. After this time, strain through cloth and add three quarts of cold water
4. Add two pounds of brown sugar and stir until all sugar dissolves
5. Pour into a jug or clean barrel and set in a cool place
6. The vessel must be loosely closed or corked so that gas can escape
7. Fermentation should proceed very slowly and last from two to three months
8. When fermentation is complete, pour into bottles and cork well
