## Ingredients

- 2 pounds veal bones
- 2 quarts cold water
- 1/2 cup pearl groats

## Directions

1. Take 2 pounds of veal bones, cover with 2 quarts of cold water and cook on very low heat for three hours
2. Collect the scum and strain
3. Rinse 1/2 cup of pearl groats in cold water and add to the pot
