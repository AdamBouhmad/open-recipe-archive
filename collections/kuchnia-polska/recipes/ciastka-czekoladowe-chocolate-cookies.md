## Ingredients

- one cup of brown sugar
- half cup of melted butter
- one egg
- one and a half cups of flour
- half teaspoon of soda
- half cup of milk
- one cup of chopped currants and nuts
- three ounces of chocolate

## Directions

1. Cream together the sugar and melted butter
2. Add one egg
3. Sift together the flour with half a teaspoon of soda
4. Add the flour mixture and milk to the creamed mixture
5. Add one cup of chopped currants and nuts
6. Melt three ounces of chocolate and add it after mixing the other ingredients
7. Drop by spoonfuls of round cookies onto a greased baking sheet
8. Bake in a slow oven
