## Ingredients

- half a dozen apricots
- quarter pound of sugar
- five egg whites
- apricot preserves

## Directions

1. Peel half a dozen apricots, remove the pits and chop into small pieces, then cook into marmalade with a quarter pound of sugar and let cool
2. Beat five egg whites into stiff meringue and mix a couple of spoonfuls of this meringue with the marmalade, then add all the marmalade to the meringue, mix lightly and arrange on a baking sheet, leveling the top with a knife, and place in a slow oven for twenty minutes
3. After removing from the oven, cover with preserves, preferably also apricot, and serve at table
