## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon good butter
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- pepper
- lemon juice
- cooked potatoes
- flour
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Mix a tablespoon of good butter raw with a teaspoon of chopped parsley, add some pepper and lemon juice, then place in a pan.
2. When the butter dissolves, add cooked potatoes sliced into pieces, sprinkle some flour, add a couple of tablespoons of bouillon and braise briefly on low heat.
3. These potatoes cannot be made for more than two or three people at once; serve with beef steak.
