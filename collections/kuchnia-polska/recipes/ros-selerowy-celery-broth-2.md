## Ingredients

- beef for broth
- vegetables
- celery root, cut into small cubes

## Directions

1. Cook broth on beef with vegetables as usual
2. Shortly before serving, add celery root cut into small cubes and cook until completely tender
3. Serve the meat from this broth dressed with cooked vegetables as a meat course
