## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- water
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 tablespoon good butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- sweet sour cream
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the outer skin from mushrooms, wash carefully, cut larger ones into round slices and cook in a small amount of water
2. During cooking, press in juice of 1/2 lemon to keep mushrooms white
3. When completely tender, brown 1 tablespoon good butter with 1 tablespoon flour without browning
4. Dilute with sweet sour cream
5. Add the mushrooms along with their cooking liquid and boil together
6. Just before serving, add a pinch of sugar and press in some lemon juice to taste
