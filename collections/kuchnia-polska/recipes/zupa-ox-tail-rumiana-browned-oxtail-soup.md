## Ingredients

- one beef oxtail
- fat
- three onions
- two carrots
- parsley
- thyme
- one pound of beef
- salt
- pepper
- celery seeds
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop beef oxtail into pieces about an inch long and fry in fat in a pan until well browned.
2. Remove them and in the same fat fry three finely shredded onions and two carrots.
3. After frying, place these vegetables along with some parsley and thyme in a small cloth bag and put in the pot.
4. Take one pound of beef, cut into thin strips and add along with the previously fried oxtails to the pot with the vegetables.
5. Cover with three quarts of water and cook about four hours, or even longer, on a very low fire.
6. Grate two carrots and add to the soup salt and pepper for taste, and also a little celery seed.
7. Cook for another fifteen minutes, strain and thicken with a little flour browned in butter.
