## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- milk
- salt
- nutmeg
- cooked beets
- potato dumplings (pulpety z kartofli)

## Directions

1. In a pot, make a roux of one spoonful of flour with one spoonful of butter, being careful not to brown it, constantly stirring
2. When a uniform mass forms, slowly pour in one quart of milk while constantly stirring and bring to a boil
3. Add salt and some grated nutmeg
4. Just before serving, grate a cooked red beet, strain this mass through a sieve, take a full spoonful and mix it into the soup to give it color
5. The best addition to this soup is potato dumplings
