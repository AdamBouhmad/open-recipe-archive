## Ingredients

- beef or pork broth made from bones
- 1 small head of cabbage
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt to taste
- rice
- 1 clove garlic (optional)
- grated Parmesan or Swiss cheese (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook ordinary broth on beef or pork bones
2. Finely chop a small head of cabbage and cut one onion into thin slices
3. Place both with 1 tablespoon of butter in a pot, cover and braise for about twenty minutes
4. Then dilute with broth and add salt to taste
5. Cook some rice separately and add it to the soup
6. Those who like it can add one clove of garlic and grated Parmesan or Swiss cheese and serve
