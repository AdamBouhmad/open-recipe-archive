## Ingredients

- at least 1/2 pound veal per person
- water

## Directions

1. First scald the veal with boiling water and leave it in the water for a few minutes, then drain the water; otherwise the broth will become white
2. Proceed the same as with beef broth, except that veal broth cooks much faster, only about 1.5 hours
3. Salt it only just before serving, otherwise it will turn red
4. Serve only with rice, barley groats mixed with egg, or ribbon noodles
