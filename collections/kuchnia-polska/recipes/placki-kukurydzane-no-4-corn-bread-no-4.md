## Ingredients

- one cup corn meal
- one tablespoon sugar
- one large tablespoon butter
- three tablespoons cooked pumpkin
- two eggs
- pinch of salt
- one cup sour milk
- one teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Mix properly one cup of corn meal, one tablespoon of sugar, one large tablespoon of butter, and three tablespoons of cooked pumpkin
2. Add two beaten eggs, a pinch of salt, one cup of sour milk, one teaspoon of soda dissolved in a small amount of warm water
3. Mix everything properly and place in the oven for half an hour
