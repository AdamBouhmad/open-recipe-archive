## Ingredients

- five or six eggs
- salt
- water
- fried bacon

## Directions

1. Beat five or six eggs until the mixture is uniform, then pour into a saucepan with boiling salted water and cook for two minutes, stirring with a spoon
2. Pour everything onto a colander, drain the water, and arrange the scrambled eggs on a serving platter
3. Decorate with thin slices of fried bacon
4. The saucepan should contain plenty of water, at least two quarts per six eggs
