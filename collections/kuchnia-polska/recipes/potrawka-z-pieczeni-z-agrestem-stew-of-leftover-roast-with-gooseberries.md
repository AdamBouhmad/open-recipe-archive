## Ingredients

- leftover lamb roast from previous day
- butter
- flour
- sauce from remaining roast
- underripe gooseberries
- sugar
- or pickled cucumbers diced

## Directions

1. This stew is made from leftover lamb roast from the previous day. Melt a tablespoon of butter in a braising pan, brown it and thicken with a tablespoon of flour.
2. Add, stirring, the sauce from the remaining lamb roast, after collecting all the fat, bring to a boil and add a large cup of underripe gooseberries, cleaned of stems, and also a tablespoon of sugar and cook together.
3. Chop the lamb roast, place in the sauce, warm together and serve.
4. Instead of gooseberries one can equally well use pickled cucumbers cut into cubes.
