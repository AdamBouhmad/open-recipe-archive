## Ingredients

- twelve lemons (for juice)
- six lemons (for zest)
- six pounds sugar
- minimal amount of water

## Directions

1. Extract juice from twelve lemons, add grated zest from six lemons, strain
2. Add thick syrup made from six pounds of sugar with the smallest amount of water; the syrup should first be cooled
3. Pour into bottles and cork
4. These syrups can be served with water in summer instead of lemonade
