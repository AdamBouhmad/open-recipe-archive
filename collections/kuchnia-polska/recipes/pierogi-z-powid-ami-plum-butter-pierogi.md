## Ingredients

- flour
- egg
- water
- plum butter or jam
- water for sealing
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Knead dough from egg and water and roll out very thinly on a work surface.
2. Place small heaps of plum butter or jam on it, cut out small pierogi with a glass, brush the edges with water and seal firmly.
3. Cook in a large pot full of water, carefully remove with a slotted spoon onto a serving platter, pour butter over them, sprinkle with sugar mixed with cinnamon, and serve passing sour cream separately on the side.
