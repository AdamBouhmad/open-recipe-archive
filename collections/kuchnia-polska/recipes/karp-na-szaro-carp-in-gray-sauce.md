## Ingredients

- carp
- salt
- vinegar
- vegetables (miscellaneous)
- pepper
- English herbs

## Directions

1. If the carp is alive, kill it and let the blood drain into boiled and salted vinegar, as blood is very good for sauce.
2. Cut the cleaned carp into rings and salt it; do not discard the milt, but salt it together.
3. Cook stock from miscellaneous vegetables available, with the addition of pepper and English herbs, then strain and cool.
4. Arrange the cut fish in a pan and pour cold vegetable stock over it.
