## Ingredients

- half a quart of dried apricots
- six large dried figs
- one cup of sugar

## Directions

1. Cook half a quart of dried apricots until soft
2. Scald six large dried figs with hot water, chop and mix with the apricots
3. Add one cup of sugar and cook together for about fifteen minutes, then cool
4. Though this compote is usually served cold, it can also be served hot
