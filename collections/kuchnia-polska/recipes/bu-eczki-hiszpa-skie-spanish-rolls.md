## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 0.5 cup butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 2.5 cups wheat flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cloves
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Mix 2 cups sugar with 0.5 cup butter, 1 cup sweet milk, 2.5 cups wheat flour, 2 teaspoons baking powder, 1 teaspoon ground cloves, 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon, and a little nutmeg.
2. Make into dough, shape into rolls, and bake in a hot oven.
