## Ingredients

- two cups dried apples
- one cup molasses
- one cup sugar
- one cup butter
- one cup sweet milk
- one cup regular raisins
- one cup currant raisins
- two eggs
- four cups flour
- one heaping teaspoon baking soda
- one teaspoon cinnamon
- one teaspoon mace
- one teaspoon nutmeg
- one teaspoon ginger
- one teaspoon cloves
- one teaspoon allspice

## Directions

1. Soak two cups dried apples in water overnight
2. The next morning, chop them finely
3. Add one cup molasses and cook for one hour
4. Add one cup sugar, one cup butter, one cup sweet milk, one cup regular raisins, one cup currant raisins, two eggs, four cups flour, one heaping teaspoon baking soda, one teaspoon cinnamon, one teaspoon mace, one teaspoon nutmeg, one teaspoon ginger, one teaspoon cloves, and one teaspoon allspice
5. Mix into dough and bake as usual
6. The cake can be made different by adding candied citron, orange peel, nuts, etc.
