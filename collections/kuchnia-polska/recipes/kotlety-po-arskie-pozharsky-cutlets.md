## Ingredients

- Young hen
- One pound white veal
- One quarter pound beef marrow
- One egg yolk
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Clarified butter
- Mushroom sauce or lemon slices

## Directions

1. Prepare and clean a young hen as for roasting, remove the skin from the breast and cut from the white meat flat oblong strips in the form of cutlets
2. Mince one pound of white veal, add to it one quarter pound of beef marrow and one egg yolk and mix well
3. Spread a layer of this meat on a work surface, place on it a slice of chicken, on top again a layer of meat and form neat oblong cutlets, insert pieces of chicken wings, salt them
4. Dip in beaten egg and then in bread crumbs and fry on hot clarified butter over moderate heat
5. Serve at table with mushroom sauce, sharp sauce or with slices of lemon
