## Ingredients

- veal feet
- vegetables
- flour, one tablespoon
- three egg yolks
- cream, two tablespoons
- egg whites
- hot fat
- spinach
- sauce made from preserves or cherry juice

## Directions

1. Cook the carefully cleaned veal feet with various vegetables the same way as for aspic.
2. When completely soft, cut each in half between the hooves, salt them, sprinkle with flour and arrange on a platter, place in a warm place so they do not cool.
3. For four feet beat three egg yolks with one tablespoon of flour, add about two tablespoons of cream, and finally mix in the remaining whites beaten to a stiff foam.
4. Dip the feet in this batter and fry in hot fat until well browned.
5. Instead of batter, the feet can be dipped in beaten egg and then coated in grated bread and fried in butter.
6. The best vegetable to serve with such feet is spinach, and sauce is served made from preserves or cherry juice.
