## Ingredients

- one half tablespoon butter
- a bit of flour
- two tablespoons sour cream
- one tablespoon vinegar
- two tablespoons cold water
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put one half tablespoon of butter in a pan, and when it melts, sprinkle with a bit of flour
2. Pour in two tablespoons of sour cream, one tablespoon of vinegar and two tablespoons of cold water, add salt to taste and mix well over heat
3. When it comes to a boil, break in four to six eggs one by one, holding just above the pan
4. When the whites begin to set, cover and place the pan on low heat to cook gently
5. If the sauce is too sour, add a little sugar and serve at the table immediately
