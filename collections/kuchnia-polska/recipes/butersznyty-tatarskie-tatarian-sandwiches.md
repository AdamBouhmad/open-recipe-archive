## Ingredients

- 1 cup finely chopped cooked ham
- 4 sardines, chopped without bones
- 3 tablespoons finely chopped pickled cucumbers
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon tomato sauce
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- salt and red pepper to taste
- butter
- white bread

## Directions

1. Mix together the ham, sardines, pickled cucumbers, mustard, tomato sauce, vinegar, salt, and red pepper
2. Slice the bread thinly and butter each slice
3. Place the mixture on the butter
4. Cover with another slice of bread
5. Arrange on a platter or tray
6. For best appearance, use white bread in square loaves and cut each sandwich diagonally in half to form triangles
