## Ingredients

- one pound of ripe pineapple
- one quart of white arrack
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Slice one pound of ripe pineapple into thin slices
2. Pour one quart of white arrack over it and set in a cool place for two days
3. Then strain, add sugar to taste and store in a corked bottle
