## Ingredients

- half quart of wheat flour
- one cup of milk
- one packet of yeast
- two egg yolks
- one whole egg
- half tablespoon of butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix half quart of wheat flour with one cup of milk and one packet of yeast
2. When it rises, beat in two egg yolks and one whole egg
3. Add half tablespoon of butter and salt to taste
4. Mix well
5. When it begins to rise again, turn out onto a floured work surface
6. Stretch by hand to the thickness of an inch
7. Cut out round discs with a glass as if making doughnuts
8. Spread on the work surface and when they rise, drop into boiling water
9. Cook uncovered and when they float to the surface, remove with a slotted spoon
10. Pour melted butter or fried bacon over them and serve immediately on the table, as they will fall; when eating do not cut them with a knife as they will become tough, but tear them with a fork
