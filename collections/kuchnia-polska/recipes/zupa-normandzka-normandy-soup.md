## Ingredients

- one large calf's shank
- three quarts of cold water
- six white onions
- one quarter pound of stale white bread
- two tablespoons of flour
- one tablespoon of butter
- salt
- pepper
- a little grated nutmeg
- half a quart of boiled milk

## Directions

1. Cover one large calf's shank with three quarts of cold water and cook very slowly for three hours.
2. Slice six white onions thinly and crumble one quarter pound of stale white bread, add these to the soup and cook for another hour.
3. Remove the meat and press the soup through a sieve, trying to press through as much onion and bread as possible.
4. Pour back into the pot, blend two tablespoons of flour with one tablespoon of butter and add salt, pepper to taste, and also a little grated nutmeg.
5. Just before serving, add half a quart of boiled milk.
