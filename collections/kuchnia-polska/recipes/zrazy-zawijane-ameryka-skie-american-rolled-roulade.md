## Ingredients

- a large piece of steak, half an inch thick
- one onion, finely chopped
- butter
- one cup grated roll or white bread
- one tablespoon finely chopped onion
- one tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon butter
- one cup water

## Directions

1. Cut a large piece of steak half an inch thick and cut it into strips six inches long and three to four inches wide
2. Brown one finely chopped onion in a small amount of butter, and when it becomes slightly browned, remove it from heat
3. Add one cup of grated roll or white bread, one tablespoon of finely chopped onion, one tablespoon of chopped fresh parsley, salt and pepper to taste
4. Mix this well and spread this filling on the meat, putting about one tablespoon on each strip, then roll it up, secure with a toothpick and place in a baking dish with one tablespoon of butter and one cup of water for three quarters of an hour
5. After removing from the oven, thicken the sauce with flour and pour it over the roulade on a platter
6. During baking, frequently baste the meat with sauce so it does not dry out
