## Ingredients

- twaróg (curd cheese)
- cream, a little
- olives, finely chopped
- rye bread, sliced
- butter
- chives (optional)
- parsley (optional)
- dill (optional)

## Directions

1. Mash the twaróg with a little cream into a smooth paste
2. Add finely chopped olives and mix
3. Spread butter on rye bread slices
4. Spread the curd cheese and olive mixture on the bread
5. Alternatively, use chives, parsley, or dill instead of olives
