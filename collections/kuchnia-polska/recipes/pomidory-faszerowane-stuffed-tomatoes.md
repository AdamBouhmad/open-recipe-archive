## Ingredients

- one cup finely grated bread crumbs
- one tablespoon finely chopped fresh parsley
- one tablespoon melted butter
- half a teaspoon salt
- two beaten eggs
- red pepper
- six large tomatoes

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of finely grated bread crumbs, one tablespoon of finely chopped fresh parsley, one tablespoon of melted butter, half a teaspoon of salt, two beaten eggs, and a pinch of red pepper into a smooth mass
2. Hollow out six large tomatoes with a spoon
3. Fill with the above mass
4. Bake in a moderately hot oven for twenty minutes
