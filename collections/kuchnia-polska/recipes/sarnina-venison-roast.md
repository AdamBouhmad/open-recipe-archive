## Ingredients

- fresh venison
- vinegar with roots, vegetables and onion
- lard for larding
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Allow fresh venison to age several days at minimum to tenderize, or soak in vinegar that has been boiled with roots, vegetables and onion for several days
2. Remove from vinegar and remove sinews
3. Lard with lard
4. Roast in a roasting pan, initially basting with butter, then with sour cream
5. Roast no more than two hours, or less if the meat is small
6. Before serving, slice the meat into strips and then reassemble so the roast retains its original form
