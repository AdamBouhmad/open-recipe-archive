## Ingredients

- one cup of wheat flour
- half a cup of corn meal
- half a cup of graham flour
- one teaspoon of salt
- one teaspoon of soda
- one tablespoon of lard or butter
- half a cup of raisins
- half a cup of syrup
- one cup of sour milk

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of wheat flour, half a cup of corn meal, half a cup of graham flour, one teaspoon of salt, one teaspoon of soda, one tablespoon of lard or butter, half a cup of raisins and half a cup of syrup with one cup of sour milk into dough
2. Place in a form and bake for one hour
