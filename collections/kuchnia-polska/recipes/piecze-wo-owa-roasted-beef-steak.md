## Ingredients

- piece of beef suitable for roasting or cross-cut
- young lard, thinly sliced
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Lard the meat with thinly sliced young lard
2. Place on a roasting pan, put a piece of butter on top
3. Place in a very hot oven
4. During roasting, baste frequently with the drippings
5. When one side is sufficiently browned, turn over to the other side
6. Salt only fifteen minutes before serving
7. A four-pound piece of meat cooks for forty to forty-five minutes
8. For smaller pieces of two to three pounds, first sear in butter on a very high heat on the stove, then place in the oven on the roasting pan
