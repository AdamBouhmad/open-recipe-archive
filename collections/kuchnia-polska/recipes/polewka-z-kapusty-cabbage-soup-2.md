## Ingredients

- one small head of cabbage
- one quart of water
- half quart of good hot milk
- salt and pepper to taste
- two tablespoons of butter
- one tablespoon of flour

## Directions

1. Take a small head of cabbage and remove all the green leaves, chop the white leaves and pour one quart of water over them
2. Boil for half an hour or a little longer, then strain
3. The strained cabbage is used as a vegetable
4. Pour half of the strained water back into the pot
5. Add half a quart of good hot milk, salt and pepper to taste
6. Brown two tablespoons of butter with one tablespoon of flour in a pan, then add to the soup
7. Mix well, boil for about five minutes and serve
