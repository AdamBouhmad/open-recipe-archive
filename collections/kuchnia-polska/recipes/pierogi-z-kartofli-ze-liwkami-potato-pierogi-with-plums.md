## Ingredients

- one quart of floury potatoes
- one tablespoon of milk
- one whole egg
- one egg yolk
- half pound of sifted wheat flour
- ripe plums with pits

## Directions

1. Peel and cook one quart of floury potatoes in separate water, mash with a pestle in a bowl, pour in one tablespoon of milk, and beat in one whole egg and one egg yolk.
2. Mash to a uniform mixture and then add half pound of sifted wheat flour and knead into a not-very-thick dough.
3. Flour your hands, take small pieces of dough, stretch them out, and wrap one ripe plum with its pit in each piece, taking care that the layer of dough around the plum is thin and even.
4. Drop the prepared dumplings into a large pot filled with water and cook, then arrange on a serving platter, pour hot butter over them, and serve.
