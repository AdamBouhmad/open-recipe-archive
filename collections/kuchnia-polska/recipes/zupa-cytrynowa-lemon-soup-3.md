## Ingredients

- ordinary broth with vegetables
- beef, veal, or offal
- 1/4 pound rice
- butter
- 1/4 cup sour cream
- 1 lemon
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cook ordinary broth with vegetables, which may be from beef, veal, or offal.
2. Scald 1/4 pound of rice, then cook until it breaks down completely with butter.
3. Beat it well with 1/4 cup of sour cream.
4. Squeeze juice from one lemon, distribute with broth and bring to a boil with seasoning.
5. In the serving bowl, before pouring the soup, place lemon sliced thinly into thin slices, without seeds.
