## Ingredients

- 1 egg white
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 small cup sugar

## Directions

1. Mix egg white, lemon juice, and sugar into a mass
2. Beat strongly for five minutes
3. Spread on cake immediately after removing it from the oven while hot
