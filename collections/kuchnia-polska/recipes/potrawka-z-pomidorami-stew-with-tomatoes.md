## Ingredients

- lamb breast or shoulder
- water
- mixed vegetables
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. For this stew, lamb breast or shoulder is best. Chop the meat into pieces, pour water over, bring to a boil and skim.
2. Then drain this water, pour in fresh water, add various vegetables and cook until tender.
3. When the meat is completely tender, pour tomato sauce over it and stew for several more minutes on low heat.
