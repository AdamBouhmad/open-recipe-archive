## Ingredients

- calf's feet
- flour
- butter
- broth or strong bouillon
- sugar
- currants, washed and pitted
- lemon
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the calf's feet in the same manner as for aspic and cut into pieces
2. Arrange the pieces on a serving platter
3. To prepare the gray sauce: brown one tablespoon of flour with one tablespoon of butter until it colors
4. Dilute with broth or strong bouillon and bring to a boil
5. Soak a few pieces of sugar in water, or use one tablespoon of regular sugar, brown it in a pan, pour into the sauce and add a handful of washed currants without pits
6. Squeeze in half a lemon, or pour in one tablespoon of good vinegar and bring together to a boil
7. Before serving, place a few thin slices of lemon in the sauce
8. Pour the sauce over the arranged meat on the platter
