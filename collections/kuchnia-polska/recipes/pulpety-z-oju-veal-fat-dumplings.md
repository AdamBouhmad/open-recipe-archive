## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound fresh beef suet, preferably from the tenderloin
- 1/2 quart grated roll
- one tablespoon of broth
- two whole eggs
- finely chopped fresh parsley
- a pinch of nutmeg

## Directions

1. Chop the fresh beef suet very finely, remove all veins and membranes, then beat it in a mortar into a mass
2. Add 1/2 quart grated roll, one tablespoon of broth, two whole eggs, some finely chopped fresh parsley and a pinch of nutmeg and mix everything well
3. Dust your hands with flour and make round balls the size of a walnut from this mass
4. Cook them in broth
