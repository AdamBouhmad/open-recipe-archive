## Ingredients

- pure beet soup as prepared previously
- half a spoon of flour
- one large cup of good sour cream

## Directions

1. Prepare pure beet soup according to the previous recipe
2. Just before serving, carefully mix half a spoon of flour with one large cup of good sour cream
3. Add this mixture to the soup, pouring gradually and constantly stirring
4. Bring to a boil and serve
5. Serve with sliced kielbasa, or other cured meat, uszka, or hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters; grated potatoes may also be added
