## Ingredients

- young piglet, carefully cleaned
- mixed vegetables
- roots
- onion
- pepper
- several tablespoons of strong vinegar
- salt to taste
- egg whites
- caramel
- vinegar and oil for serving
- grated horseradish with sour cream for serving

## Directions

1. Carefully clean the young piglet and cut it into pieces as for a stew.
2. Place in a pot and bring to a boil.
3. After boiling and careful skimming, add mixed vegetables, some roots, onion, pepper, several tablespoons of strong vinegar, and salt to taste.
4. Cook until the meat is tender.
5. When the meat is tender, remove all pieces.
6. Clarify the remaining sauce with egg whites, cool slightly, and pour over the meat arranged in a salad bowl, making sure no bones protrude.
7. Cool completely.
8. To make the aspic a yellow color, tint it with caramel.
9. Serve at table with vinegar and oil, or with grated horseradish with sour cream.
