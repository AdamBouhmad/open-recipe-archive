## Ingredients

- two cups cooked rice
- two tablespoons melted butter
- half cup milk
- half cup wheat flour
- two beaten eggs
- baking powder or soda

## Directions

1. To two cups of cooked rice, add in order: two tablespoons of melted butter, half a cup of milk, half a cup of wheat flour, and two beaten eggs.
2. Beat everything well and fry on a hot pan.
3. You can also use baking powder or soda; if using soda, use sour milk instead of sweet.
