## Ingredients

- 1 pound of dried plums
- pieces of cinnamon
- 1/2 dozen dried mushrooms
- 1/2 quart of water
- a little flour
- cold water
- wheat groats for thickening
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Cook 1 pound of dried plums in a small amount of water with a few pieces of cinnamon
2. Separately cook 1/2 dozen dried mushrooms in 1/2 quart of water until completely soft
3. Strain the plums through a sieve, pouring the mushroom broth over them
4. Chop the mushrooms very finely and add to the soup
5. Thicken with a little flour mixed in cold water
6. Cook wheat groats separately in 1/2 quart of water and add to the soup
7. Sweeten to taste and serve
