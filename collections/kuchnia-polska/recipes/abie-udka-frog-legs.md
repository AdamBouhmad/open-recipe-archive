## Ingredients

- frog legs
- beaten egg
- grated bread
- butter or fat

## Directions

1. Remove the feet from the frog legs
2. Dip in beaten egg, then coat with grated bread
3. Fry in butter or fat, using a large amount of fat
4. In first-class restaurants they are fried in deep pans filled with fat
5. Serve on a platter dressed with lettuce leaves and fresh parsley
