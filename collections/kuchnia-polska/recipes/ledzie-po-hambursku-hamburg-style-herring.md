## Ingredients

- one good fat herring
- water
- mustard
- oil
- bay leaves
- pepper

## Directions

1. Soak one good fat herring in cold water for twenty-four hours
2. Remove from water, clean, remove skin, remove backbone, cut off head and tail
3. Flatten on a board and smear each half with mustard
4. Roll into a tube
5. Once all are rolled, arrange in a jar and cover with oil
6. Intersperse with bay leaves and coarsely ground pepper
7. When serving, cut each half in two on the plate, pour the same oil over and sprinkle with strong vinegar
