## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon good butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1/4 cup sweet sour cream
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- several small potatoes
- fish stock (optional)

## Directions

1. Brown 1 tablespoon good butter with 1 tablespoon flour
2. Dilute with 1/4 cup sweet sour cream
3. Add 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
4. Cook several small potatoes, cut them into thin slices while hot and add to the sauce
5. The amount of potatoes should be roughly equal to the amount of sour cream
6. If using this sauce for fish, use less sour cream and add some fish stock instead
