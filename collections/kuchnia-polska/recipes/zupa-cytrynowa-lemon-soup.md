## Ingredients

- one bread roll
- one and a half quarts of water
- a little cinnamon
- zest of one lemon
- three egg yolks
- sugar
- juice of one lemon
- one quarter litre of good white wine
- previously cooked rice

## Directions

1. Pour one and a half quarts of water over one bread roll, add a little cinnamon, then pass through a sieve
2. Grate the zest from one lemon; beat three egg yolks with sugar and press into them the juice of one lemon; add all this to the boiling soup, as well as one quarter litre of good white wine, stirring constantly, and immediately remove from fire and serve with previously cooked rice, which is poured into the bowl
