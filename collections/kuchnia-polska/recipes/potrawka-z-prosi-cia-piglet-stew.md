## Ingredients

- one piglet
- water
- one tablespoon flour
- one tablespoon butter
- vinegar
- caramel (for color)
- currants without pits (optional)
- sweet sliced almonds (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the piglet and cut into pieces
2. Split the head crosswise, leaving the brain undisturbed in the upper part
3. Place in a pan, cover with water and cook until tender
4. Remove the meat
5. Make a roux by browning one tablespoon of flour and one tablespoon of butter in a pan
6. Add a few tablespoons of vinegar, a little caramel for color, and if desired some large pitted currants and sweet sliced almonds
7. Boil everything together
8. Add the meat and adjust the seasoning
