## Ingredients

- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon boiling water

## Directions

1. Beat the powdered sugar, lemon juice, and boiling water strongly until a smooth, half-transparent mass forms
2. Cover the cake while hot, immediately after removing from the oven
