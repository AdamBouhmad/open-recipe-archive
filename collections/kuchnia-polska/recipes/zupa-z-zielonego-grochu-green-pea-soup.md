## Ingredients

- Shelled green peas
- Water
- One tablespoon of butter
- One teaspoon of sugar
- Beef broth with vegetables
- Empty pea pods (young ones)
- Flour
- Egg

## Directions

1. Pour shelled peas in a pot with a small amount of water, add one tablespoon of butter and cook until soft
2. Add one teaspoon of sugar
3. Separately cook broth with beef bones and various vegetables, and toward the end of cooking add some empty pods (husks) from the shelled peas, selecting only young ones
4. When the broth is cooked, remove the bones and vegetables and pass the cooked husks through a sieve
5. To the thus cooked broth, mix in a little paste made from flour and egg
6. Add the peas cooked with butter and serve
