## Ingredients

- one quart wheat flour
- half quart buckwheat flour
- one good quart sweet milk at lukewarm temperature
- one packet yeast dissolved in milk
- four egg yolks
- four egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix one quart of wheat flour and half quart of buckwheat flour with one good quart of sweet milk at lukewarm temperature.
2. Add one packet of yeast dissolved in milk and add more milk so the batter is thin.
3. Place in a warm location.
4. When it rises too much, stir it and repeat this mixing each time the batter rises too much.
5. It is best to prepare the batter in the morning so it is ready to use in a few hours.
6. Ten minutes before frying the pancakes, beat in four egg yolks.
7. Beat the whites into a foam and also add to the batter, mixing gently.
8. Fry on a small pan, pouring the batter with a spoon so it spreads over the whole pan.
9. When browned on one side, flip with a spatula or knife to the other side.
10. After frying, stack one on top of another on a platter which should stand on the stove or over steam.
11. Grease the pan with clarified butter.
12. Serve these pancakes with melted butter and sour cream.
