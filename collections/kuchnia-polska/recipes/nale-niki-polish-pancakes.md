## Ingredients

- butter
- salt
- flour
- milk
- water
- egg whites
- clarified butter or lard for cooking
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Add butter, salt, flour, and then milk mixed with water to make a thin batter that pours easily
2. Beat egg whites until stiff and fold into the batter
3. Heat a pan and brush with clarified butter or lard using a feather
4. Pour enough batter to cover the pan completely and quickly tilt to spread evenly
5. Keep pan on high heat
6. When pancake is cooked on one side, circle it with a knife and flip briefly to the other side, then remove to a plate
7. When all batter is cooked, fold pancakes into triangles by folding in half once, then folding through the middle again
8. Place four at a time back in the pan and brown on both sides in clarified butter
9. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon and serve
10. This proportion should yield sixteen to twenty pancakes
