## Ingredients

- 5 large white onions
- water
- 1 cup unboiled milk
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel 5 large white onions, cover with water and cook until completely tender
2. Drain water and mash the onions
3. Boil 1 cup unboiled milk and pour into the pot with onions
4. Add 1 tablespoon melted butter, 1/2 teaspoon salt and some pepper
5. Boil once and serve immediately
