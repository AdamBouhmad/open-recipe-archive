## Ingredients

- three cups of graham flour
- one teaspoon of salt
- one heaping teaspoon of soda
- two cups of sour milk
- one-third cup of molasses

## Directions

1. Mix together the graham flour, salt and soda
2. Add two cups of sour milk and one-third cup of molasses
3. Mix into dough, which should then be cooked on steam for three hours
4. Finally place in a hot oven for fifteen minutes to dry
