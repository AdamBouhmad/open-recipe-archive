## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup cornmeal
- 0.67 cup boiled milk
- 0.5 cup cooked rice
- 0.5 cup wheat flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 0.5 teaspoon salt
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Pour 0.67 cup of boiled milk over 0.5 cup cornmeal and let sit for five minutes, then add 0.5 cup cooked rice and mix.
2. Mix 0.5 cup wheat flour, 2 tablespoons sugar, 0.5 teaspoon salt, and 3 teaspoons baking powder, preferably sifting together several times to mix well, and add to the first mixture and mix.
3. Beat 1 egg yolk and add 1 tablespoon melted butter, then fold in 1 egg white beaten to stiff peaks and mix.
4. Fill forms and bake.
