## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups of flour
- half a teaspoon of salt
- half a cup of lard, or lard mixed half with butter
- ice water

## Directions

1. Sift one and one-half cups of flour, half a teaspoon of salt and add half a cup of lard, or lard mixed half with butter and mix thoroughly, then add enough ice water to obtain thick dough
