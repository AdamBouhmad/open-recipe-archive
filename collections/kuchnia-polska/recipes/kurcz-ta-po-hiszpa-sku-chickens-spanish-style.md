## Ingredients

- young chickens
- flour
- raw butter
- tomatoes
- sieve

## Directions

1. Prepare the chickens as usual for roasting, lightly sprinkle with flour, spread with raw butter and put in a hot oven, but for no longer than ten minutes, then remove immediately and let them cool.
2. Stew several raw tomatoes in a pan, allowing two large tomatoes for each chicken, then pass through a sieve so that all seeds and skins remain on it.
