## Ingredients

- half quart water
- one cup butter
- salt to taste
- half quart wheat flour
- four eggs
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. In half quart of water, add one cup butter and salt to taste and bring to a boil
2. Into the boiling water, add half quart wheat flour and mix well until it separates from the pot
3. Set aside in a cool place
4. When completely cooled, add four eggs one at a time, beating strongly after each addition until a uniform mass forms
5. In a large pot, bring a lot of water to a boil
6. Drop medium-sized dumplings into the boiling water with a spoon and cover with a lid
7. When they rise and grow well, remove with a slotted spoon onto a buttered platter
8. Pour melted butter over the top and sprinkle with grated cheese and drizzle with butter again
9. Place in a hot oven for twenty minutes until the top is well browned
