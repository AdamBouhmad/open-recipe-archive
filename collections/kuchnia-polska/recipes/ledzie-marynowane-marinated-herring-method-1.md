## Ingredients

- herring
- water
- onion
- pepper
- dill
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak herring in cold water for one and a half days
2. Remove water, wipe clean, clean without removing skin and arrange in a jar
3. Intersperse with sliced onion, pepper and dill
4. Pour boiled vinegar over them
