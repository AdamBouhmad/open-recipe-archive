## Ingredients

- two pounds calf meat, cut into small pieces
- one pound salted bacon, cut into slices
- water
- three tablespoons vinegar
- one or two onions, cut into slices
- one and a half pounds carrots, cut into small pieces
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Place in a pot two pounds of calf meat cut into small pieces and one pound of salted bacon cut into slices
2. Pour four quarts of water and add three tablespoons of vinegar
3. Place on fire and cook for one hour
4. Skim well and add one or two onions cut into slices, with one and a half pounds of carrots cut into small pieces, salt and pepper to taste
5. Pour the meat along with the broth onto a baking sheet and place in a moderately hot oven for two hours
