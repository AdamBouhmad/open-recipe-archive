## Ingredients

- partridges
- thin slices of lard
- butter
- grated bread
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean carefully, discard the offal
2. Stuff the breasts with thinly sliced lard
3. Roast, continually basting with butter
4. Shortly before removing from the oven, sprinkle with grated bread, brown and pour over browned butter
5. Can be stuffed with a forcemeat made from grated bread and butter, with a small addition of lemon juice
