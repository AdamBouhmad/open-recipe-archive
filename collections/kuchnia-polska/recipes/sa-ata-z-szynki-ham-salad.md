## Ingredients

- one cup cooked ham
- one cup small pickled cucumbers
- three hard-boiled eggs
- one tablespoon vinegar
- one tablespoon butter
- one teaspoon sugar
- one teaspoon mustard
- one egg yolk
- red pepper

## Directions

1. Trim all fat and chop the cooked ham into one cup of pieces
2. Slice the pickled cucumbers into thin slices
3. Slice the three hard-boiled eggs
4. Layer the ham and cucumbers in a bowl, arranging eggs on top
5. Heat one tablespoon of vinegar with one tablespoon of butter
6. Add one teaspoon of sugar, one teaspoon of mustard, one egg yolk, and some red pepper
7. Cook everything for about three minutes, then cool
8. Pour this sauce over the layered ham and vegetables
