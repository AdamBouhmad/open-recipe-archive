## Ingredients

- zest from four large lemons (yellow part)
- one quart of brandy or rum
- juice of four lemons
- one and a half pounds of sugar in pieces
- two grated nutmegs
- one quart of water
- one quart of fresh milk

## Directions

1. Scrape the yellow part of zest from four large lemons and pour one quart of brandy or rum over it, then place in a cool place for a whole day
2. Add juice of four lemons, one and a half pounds of sugar in pieces, two grated nutmegs and one quart of water; boil one quart of fresh milk and also pour it in, then strain everything through cloth, cool and serve
3. This punch can be kept in corked bottles for several months without spoiling
