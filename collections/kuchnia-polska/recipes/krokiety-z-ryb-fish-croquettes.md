## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon chopped green parsley
- 1/2 teaspoon onion juice
- a little nutmeg
- 2 cups cooked and cooled fish, finely chopped
- 1 beaten egg
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Bring 1 cup of milk to a boil and thicken with 2 tablespoons of flour mixed with 1 tablespoon of butter
2. Boil, stirring constantly, until thickened
3. Remove from heat and add 1 beaten egg yolk, 1 tablespoon of chopped green parsley, 1/2 teaspoon of onion juice, a little nutmeg and 2 cups of any cooked and cooled fish, finely chopped
4. Mix everything properly, spread on a board and form small cutlets from this mixture
5. Dip each cutlet first in beaten egg and then in breadcrumbs
6. Fry in very hot fat until browned
