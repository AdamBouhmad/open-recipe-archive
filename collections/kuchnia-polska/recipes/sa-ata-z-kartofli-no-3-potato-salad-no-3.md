## Ingredients

- one quart cooked cold potatoes
- one large onion
- three tablespoons oil
- one tablespoon vinegar
- half teaspoon salt
- three pinches pepper
- finely chopped fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Slice one quart cooked cold potatoes into uniform pieces
2. Chop one large onion very finely
3. Mix three tablespoons oil, one tablespoon vinegar, half teaspoon salt and three pinches pepper
4. Pour this sauce over the potatoes and mix everything well
5. May add some chopped fresh parsley
