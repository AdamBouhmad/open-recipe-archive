## Ingredients

- half a pound flour
- three eggs
- six medium apples
- one tablespoon butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- bread crumbs, grated

## Directions

1. Make pasta in the usual way with half a pound of flour and three eggs, roll out very thinly and cut very fine like thin threads and fairly short, then scatter in a thin layer on a baking sheet and place in a hot oven, and remove only when well browned.
2. Cook six medium-sized apples with one tablespoon of butter and such an amount of sugar to obtain a very sweet marmalade, but not too thick.
3. Grease a baking dish with butter and thickly sprinkle with grated bread crumbs, then place a layer of pasta on the bottom, then a layer of apple marmalade and so on, until all the pasta and marmalade are used; it is best to have three layers of pasta and three of marmalade.
4. Place the prepared dish in the oven for fifteen minutes, just to heat it well, then turn out onto a serving platter and generously sprinkle with sugar mixed with cinnamon.
5. This proportion is for six people.
