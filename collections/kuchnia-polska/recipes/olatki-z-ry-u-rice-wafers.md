## Ingredients

- two cups cold cooked rice
- one cup milk
- one cup flour
- one teaspoon baking powder
- half teaspoon salt
- two teaspoons sugar
- one egg
- fat for the pan

## Directions

1. Mash two cups of cold cooked rice into a mass, mix well with one cup of milk.
2. Add one cup of flour sifted with one teaspoon of baking powder, half a teaspoon of salt, two teaspoons of sugar, and one egg.
3. Mix to a loose batter and place spoonfuls on a greased pan.
4. Fry until nicely golden.
5. Make the wafers small, four or five on one pan.
6. Instead of sweet milk, you can use the same amount of sour milk, but then do not use baking powder, use half a teaspoon of soda instead.
