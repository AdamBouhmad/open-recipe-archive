## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- butter
- salt and pepper to taste
- chopped fresh dill or chives
- grated bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cool hard-boiled eggs and cut them in half lengthwise along with the shell
2. Carefully remove the yolk and white so as not to damage the shells
3. Chop the removed whites and yolks together
4. Add a tablespoon of butter, salt and pepper to taste, and some chopped dill or chives
5. Mix everything well and fill the shells with this mixture
6. Place butter on a pan and when well heated, place the stuffed shells on it sprinkled with grated bread crumbs, filling side down
7. Fry until well browned, then flip and serve in soup
