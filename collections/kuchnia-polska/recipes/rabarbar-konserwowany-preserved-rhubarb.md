## Ingredients

- young rhubarb stalks
- cold water

## Directions

1. Select young stalks and chop into half-inch pieces
2. Pack into jars nearly to the top
3. Cover with clean cold water and stir with a silver fork to bring up all air bubbles to the surface
4. Fill jars with water so it overflows
5. Apply new rubber rings and tighten the lids securely
6. Store in a dry, cool, and dark place, setting the jars upside down
7. If any jars leak, use them as soon as possible
