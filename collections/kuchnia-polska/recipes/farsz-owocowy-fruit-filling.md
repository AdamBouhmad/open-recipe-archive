## Ingredients

- one cup of currants without seeds
- half pound of blanched almonds
- half pound of figs
- half pound of citron
- royal frosting

## Directions

1. Chop finely one cup of currants without seeds, half pound of blanched almonds, half pound of figs and half pound of citron
2. Then add enough royal frosting to make a soft mass
