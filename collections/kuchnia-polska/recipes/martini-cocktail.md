## Ingredients

- one-third quart of Tom Gin
- one-third quart of Italian vermouth wine
- one-third quart of water
- two small liqueur glasses of orange bitters

## Directions

1. Add two small liqueur glasses of orange bitters, mix, pour into a bottle and cork
