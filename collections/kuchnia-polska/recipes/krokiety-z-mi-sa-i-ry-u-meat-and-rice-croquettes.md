## Ingredients

- any roasted or cooked meat, finely chopped
- one cup chopped meat
- one cup cooked rice
- half cup milk
- two spoonfuls butter
- salt
- pepper
- one beaten egg
- grated bread
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Chop any roasted or cooked meat very finely
2. Heat half cup milk and add chopped meat and rice, two spoonfuls butter, salt and pepper to taste
3. Mix well, and when mass boils, add one well-beaten egg, remove from heat and mix everything again
4. Place in cool location
5. When mass is completely cooled, place on work surface and form small cutlets
6. Dip in beaten egg and then in grated bread and fry in hot fat until browned
