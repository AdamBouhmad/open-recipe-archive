## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of rice
- water
- good butter
- water
- one half quart of fresh peas
- salt
- boiling water
- a few mushrooms
- one tablespoon young butter
- one tablespoon grated Parmesan
- salt to taste
- butter
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Cover rice with water and bring to a boil, then drain the water and braise the rice covered in good butter, pouring a little water so it does not burn.
2. Shell one half quart of fresh peas, throw into a large pan with salted boiling water and cook until tender.
3. Cook a few mushrooms and cut into thin strips, then mix everything together, add one tablespoon of young butter, one tablespoon of grated Parmesan, salt to taste and place in a deep buttered clay bowl and put in a hot oven for a few minutes just to heat through.
4. Then transfer to a plate and sprinkle generously with Parmesan on top. This is an Italian dish, but common in other countries as well.
