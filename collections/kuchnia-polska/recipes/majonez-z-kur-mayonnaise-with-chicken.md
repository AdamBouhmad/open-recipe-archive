## Ingredients

- cooked chicken
- good clarified butter
- flour
- chicken broth
- oil
- salt
- sugar
- lemon or vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken as for a stew.
2. For two chickens, take a quarter pound of good clarified butter and heat with two spoons of flour.
3. Mix with a quart of chicken broth (with all fat removed) and cook together until thickened.
4. Cool and beat in a bowl until it becomes like sour cream.
5. Slowly pour in half a pound of oil, adding it by the spoonful and constantly stirring with a whisk.
6. Add salt and continue whisking until the sauce becomes white and thick.
7. Add the juice of a whole lemon or enough vinegar to make the sauce sufficiently sour.
8. Add a teaspoon of fine sugar and pour over the chicken arranged on a platter.
