## Ingredients

- leftover hare pieces
- one tablespoon good butter
- half cup broth or bouillon
- flour
- one or two tablespoons sour cream
- a few mushrooms
- grated cheese, preferably Parmesan

## Directions

1. Remove all bones from leftover hare pieces and chop finely
2. Melt one tablespoon good butter in a saucepan
3. Thin out with half cup broth or bouillon and thicken with a little flour
4. Bring to a good boil
5. Add chopped meat, then add one or two tablespoons sour cream and a few mushrooms
6. Stew under cover until the sauce is reduced by half
7. Mix everything well
8. Place in small molds
9. Sprinkle grated cheese, preferably Parmesan, on top
10. Place in a hot oven until the top is browned
11. Serve
