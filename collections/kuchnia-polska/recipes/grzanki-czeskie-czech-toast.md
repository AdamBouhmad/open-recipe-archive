## Ingredients

- two eggs
- half cup milk
- one teaspoon onion juice
- one quarter teaspoon salt
- pinch of pepper
- white bread, preferably day-old
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs well.
2. Add half a cup of milk, one teaspoon of onion juice, one quarter teaspoon of salt, and a pinch of pepper.
3. Mix everything well.
4. Cut white bread, preferably day-old, into medium slices.
5. Dip both sides in the mixture.
6. Fry in butter on a pan until well browned on both sides.
