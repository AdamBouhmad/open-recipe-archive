## Ingredients

- leftover roast calf meat, cut into cubes
- one and a half cups calf meat
- three heaping tablespoons butter
- two heaping tablespoons flour
- one cup milk
- two hard-boiled eggs
- salt to taste
- white pepper
- red pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover roast calf meat from the previous day into cubes
2. In a pot melt three heaping tablespoons of butter, brown in it two heaping tablespoons of flour and slowly add one cup of milk, constantly stirring
3. When it comes to a boil, add the calf meat, as well as two hard-boiled eggs with yolks and whites finely chopped and cut into pieces
4. Cook for three minutes, then season with salt to taste, add a little white pepper and a pinch of red pepper
