## Ingredients

- three oranges
- two bananas
- two figs
- two tablespoons of sugar

## Directions

1. Peel three oranges, remove the seeds and chop into small cubes
2. Peel and slice two bananas
3. Rinse and chop two figs into small pieces
4. Mix everything together with two tablespoons of sugar and arrange on a salad bowl
