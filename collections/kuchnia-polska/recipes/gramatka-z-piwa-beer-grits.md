## Ingredients

- quarter pound of rye bread without crust
- one tablespoon of butter
- beer from a quart bottle
- a couple of pieces of cinnamon
- four tablespoons of sugar
- two tablespoons of raisins without seeds
- croutons from bread cut into cubes and dried
- a few thin slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Break into small pieces a quarter pound of rye bread without crust, add one tablespoon of butter, pour such an amount of beer so that it barely covers the bread, place on fire and let it cook down
2. Then rub in a pot with a spoon, pour in the rest of the beer from a quart bottle, add a couple of pieces of cinnamon, four tablespoons of sugar, and two tablespoons of raisins without seeds
3. Bring to a boil together and pour into a bowl in which there should be croutons from bread cut into cubes and dried, as well as a few thin slices of lemon
