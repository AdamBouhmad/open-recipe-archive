## Ingredients

- one carrot
- a couple of onions
- a couple of celery stalks
- parsley
- bay leaf
- whole pepper grains
- salt
- flour
- butter
- dumplings (kluski krajane)
- fish head and scraps

## Directions

1. Finely chop one carrot, a couple of onions, a couple of celery stalks, and some parsley
2. Add one bay leaf, several peppercorns and salt to taste
3. Cover everything with cold water and cook until all vegetables are completely soft
4. Strain through a sieve and pour this broth over the fish head and other scraps
5. Cook for one hour
6. Before serving, make a roux of flour and butter and thicken the strained soup with it
7. Serve with cut dumplings
8. The fish may be cooked separately and added to the soup twenty minutes before serving
