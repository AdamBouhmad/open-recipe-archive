## Ingredients

- one and a half cups grated bread
- two cups sweet milk
- one tablespoon butter
- two beaten egg yolks
- one cup flour
- two teaspoons baking powder
- less than one teaspoon salt
- egg whites beaten to stiff peaks

## Directions

1. Pour two cups of sweet milk over one and a half cups of grated bread.
2. Add one tablespoon of butter and cook in a double boiler for fifteen minutes.
3. Pass through a sieve and set in a cool place.
4. When completely cooled, add two beaten egg yolks and one cup of flour sifted with two teaspoons of baking powder and less than one teaspoon of salt.
5. Mix well and add the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Fry on a hot pan as usual for pancakes.
