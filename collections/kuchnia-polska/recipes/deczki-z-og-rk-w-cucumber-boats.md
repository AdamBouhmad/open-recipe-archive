## Ingredients

- two large cucumbers
- two medium onions
- one tablespoon celery seed
- half tablespoon mustard seed
- small teaspoon salt
- red pepper

## Directions

1. Cut two large cucumbers in half lengthwise
2. Carefully scoop out all the insides with a small spoon, leaving the skin intact so each half looks like a small boat
3. Place the boats in cold water
4. Finely chop the scooped-out cucumber flesh together with two medium onions
5. Spread the mixture on a colander to drain excess juice
6. After fifteen minutes, add one tablespoon of celery seed, half tablespoon of mustard seed, small teaspoon of salt, and some red pepper
7. Mix everything together and fill the hollowed cucumber boats
8. Serve on the table
