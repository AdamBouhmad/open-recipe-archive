## Ingredients

- veal brain
- salted water
- vinegar, one tablespoon
- cream, half a quart
- four egg yolks
- salt and pepper to taste
- nutmeg, a pinch
- egg whites
- butter
- flour
- sharp caper sauce or gherkin sauce

## Directions

1. Peel the raw brain of veins and membranes and blanch in salted water with the addition of one tablespoon of vinegar, place on a bowl and mash.
2. Add half a quart of cream, four egg yolks, salt and pepper to taste and a pinch of nutmeg; at the end beat the whites to a stiff foam and mix with the mass.
3. Grease a mold or form with butter, sprinkle with a little flour, place in another mold with hot water, put the brain mass in and cook on steam for about an hour.
4. After cooking carefully place on a platter and pour with melted butter; separately serve sharp caper or gherkin sauce.
