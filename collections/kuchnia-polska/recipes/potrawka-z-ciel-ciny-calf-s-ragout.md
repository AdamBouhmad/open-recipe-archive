## Ingredients

- calf meat (breast is best)
- water
- vegetables (carrots, celery, etc.)
- salt
- butter
- flour
- fresh parsley, finely chopped
- asparagus or cauliflower, cooked and cut into small pieces
- crayfish necks
- calf's brains
- butter for frying
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Take a whole piece of calf meat, pour boiling water over it and bring to a boil
2. Immediately remove, rinse in cold water and cut into pieces
3. Place the cut meat in a pot, pour fresh hot water, add vegetables and salt
4. Cook until the meat is tender
5. When the meat is cooked, arrange on a serving platter and cover to keep warm
6. Bring one tablespoon of butter with one tablespoon of flour to a boil, dilute with the broth, add finely chopped green parsley
7. You can also add cooked and cut into small pieces asparagus or cauliflower, or crayfish necks
8. Pour this sauce over the arranged meat on the platter
9. To serve the ragout elegantly, you can make small delicate cutlets from calf's brains, fry them in butter until brown and arrange them around the ragout
