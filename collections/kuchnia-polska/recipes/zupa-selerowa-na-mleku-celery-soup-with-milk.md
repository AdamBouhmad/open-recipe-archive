## Ingredients

- one heaping cup of celery cut into small pieces
- one and a half quarts of good milk
- one heaping spoonful of flour
- half a cup of cold milk
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take one heaping cup of celery cut into small pieces and cook in one and a half quarts of good milk until soft
2. Beat one heaping spoonful of flour in half a cup of cold milk and slowly pour into the soup while constantly stirring
3. Then add one spoonful of butter, salt and pepper to taste and serve
