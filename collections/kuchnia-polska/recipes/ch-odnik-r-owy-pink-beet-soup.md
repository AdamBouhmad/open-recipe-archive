## Ingredients

- 1 quart of pickle juice from cucumbers
- water
- fresh dill, chopped
- a couple of red beets
- 2 pickled cucumbers
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- several crayfish tails
- 1 cup sour cream
- ice

## Directions

1. Take 1 quart of pickle juice from cucumbers and add water if it is too sour. Add some chopped fresh dill and bring to a boil, then remove from heat.
2. Separately cook a couple of red beets, peel them, cut into thin strips and add to the warm pickle juice so it takes on color and sweetness.
3. Peel and slice 2 pickled cucumbers into slices; cut 3 hard-boiled eggs into quarters.
4. Add several crayfish tails. Whip 1 cup of sour cream well.
5. Mix the sour cream thoroughly with the soup first, then add the cucumbers, eggs and crayfish tails and place on ice. Serve on the table when completely cooled; you can place a piece of ice on each plate.
6. Note: This soup can also be served hot as borscht, in which case after adding the beets bring to a boil, remove from heat, then add sour cream and other additions and do not cook further, but serve immediately on the table.
