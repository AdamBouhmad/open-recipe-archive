## Ingredients

- pike, completely fresh
- salt, roasted
- parsley
- carrot
- onion
- other vegetables
- pepper, a few grains
- egg white
- water

## Directions

1. For good aspic, pike must be absolutely fresh.
2. Clean and cut the fish into bell-shapes and salt with roasted salt, letting it rest for a few hours.
3. Chop parsley, carrot, onion and other vegetables, add a few peppercorns and prepare, preferably in a clay pot.
4. Cook until the broth takes on the color of consommé, then set aside and let cool.
5. Place pike in a pan, cover with cooled vegetable broth and cook for half an hour, then add the rest of the fish and pour in some sauce that formed from the salt and cook until tender.
6. When the fish is cooked, arrange it on a platter and place in a cold location to cool.
7. Continue cooking the remaining broth in the pan over low heat, constantly skimming.
8. When the broth becomes sticky, add egg white beaten with water to clarify it, cook for a moment and strain through dense sieve or cloth.
9. Remove the back bone from each bell-shaped piece of fish, remove the skin and arrange nicely in a salad bowl.
10. Pour the cooked broth over it, pouring slowly through a napkin.
11. Do not decorate with anything, as the aspic flavor will be spoiled.
12. Serve at table with vinegar and oil.
