## Ingredients

- six large potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- one onion, grated
- two tablespoons milk
- one tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Boil six large potatoes
2. Mash them into a mass and add salt and pepper
3. Add one grated onion
4. Take two tablespoons milk and one tablespoon butter and mix everything well
5. Form small cones or pyramids from this mass
6. Place on a baking sheet and put in a hot oven for ten minutes
7. When they are properly browned, serve at table
