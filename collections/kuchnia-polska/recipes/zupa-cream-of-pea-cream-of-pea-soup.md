## Ingredients

- One cup of green peas (fresh or pickled)
- Water
- Onion, chopped fine
- Half quart of broth or water
- Two tablespoons of butter
- One tablespoon of flour
- Salt and pepper
- Several tablespoons of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Pour one cup of green peas in a pot with just enough water to barely cover them
2. Add finely chopped onion
3. Cook until completely soft
4. Mash to a smooth paste and pour in half quart of broth or water
5. Make a roux with two tablespoons of butter and one tablespoon of flour, being careful not to brown it
6. Add to the soup along with salt and pepper to taste
7. Bring to a boil, strain and serve
8. The quality of this soup can be improved by adding several tablespoons of whipped cream just before serving
