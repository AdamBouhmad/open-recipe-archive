## Ingredients

- one egg yolk
- half teaspoon butter
- hot water
- salt
- red pepper
- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Place one egg yolk and half teaspoon butter in a small pot and set the pot in hot water and constantly stir.
2. When it begins to thicken, add a little salt and a pinch of red pepper, then add another tablespoon of butter, adding slowly and continually stirring.
3. When a sufficiently thick and smooth mass forms, cool it, add one tablespoon of vinegar and the sauce is ready for use.
