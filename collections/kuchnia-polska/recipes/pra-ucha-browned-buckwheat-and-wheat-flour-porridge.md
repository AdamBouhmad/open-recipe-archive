## Ingredients

- half quart of buckwheat flour
- half quart of wheat flour
- one quart of milk
- salt
- lard

## Directions

1. Pour half quart of buckwheat flour and half quart of wheat flour onto a skillet
2. Place on very low heat and stir constantly with a spoon until the flour browns well
3. Then sift it and crush any lumps with a spoon
4. Bring one quart of milk to a boil in a small pot
5. Pour the browned flour into the boiling milk, salt it and cook briefly on low heat, stirring constantly, until the mass stops sticking to the pot
6. Remove from heat and shape on a platter using a spoon dipped in lard
7. Pour fried bacon over it
