## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- two or three eggs
- cream
- sugar
- fresh butter
- orange zest
- Method 2: four egg yolks
- quarter pound of sugar
- one tablespoon of butter

## Directions

1. Method 1: Knead one pound of flour with two or three eggs, roll out thin, and cut like regular noodles. Cook in abundant water. After draining, mix the noodles with cream, sugar, and fresh butter, adding finely chopped orange zest fried in sugar. Grease a pot or mold with butter, place the noodles in it, and bake for half an hour.
2. Method 2: Cook noodles made from one pound of flour as usual, drain, and add four egg yolks beaten with quarter pound of sugar and one tablespoon of butter, mixing well. Finally add the foam from beaten egg whites, mix lightly, and place in the oven for one hour. Both proportions above are for six people.
