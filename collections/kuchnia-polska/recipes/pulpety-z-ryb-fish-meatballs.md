## Ingredients

- any fish
- salt and pepper to taste
- a small spoon of butter
- one grated onion
- milk
- one bread roll
- flour for dusting
- water for cooking

## Directions

1. Remove skin from the fish and extract the meat
2. Chop the meat, add salt and pepper to taste
3. Melt a small spoon of butter in a pan, when it boils add one grated onion
4. Boil again and remove from heat
5. Soak one bread roll in milk and squeeze it out
6. Add to the chopped fish, chop everything again, mix well and place on a work surface
7. Dust with flour and shape into finger-length cylinders
8. Drop into boiling water and cook for about twenty minutes
9. After cooking, drain on a colander and cool
10. Cut into thick slices and place in a bowl before pouring soup over them
11. These same meatballs can also be served separately as a dish with small noodles and white cream sauce
