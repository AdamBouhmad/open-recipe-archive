## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 large tablespoon butter
- 0.5 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1.5 cups wheat flour

## Directions

1. Beat 1 egg, 1 large tablespoon butter, 0.5 teaspoon salt, 1 cup sour milk, and 1.5 cups wheat flour together well.
2. Pour into forms and bake.
