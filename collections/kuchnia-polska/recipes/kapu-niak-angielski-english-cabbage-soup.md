## Ingredients

- 1/2 head of cabbage
- 1 onion
- celery
- parsley
- dill
- ham bone or veal bone
- butter
- 1/2 quart tomatoes, cut into cubes
- beef broth
- flour or lard

## Directions

1. Cut not too finely 1/2 head of cabbage
2. Finely chop onion, celery, parsley and some dill
3. Place everything in a pot with a piece of ham or veal bone
4. Add some butter and braise on low heat for twenty minutes
5. Add 1/2 quart of tomatoes cut into cubes
6. Cover with previously cooked beef broth and cook for one hour
7. Just before serving, fry some flour with butter or lard and thicken the soup with it
