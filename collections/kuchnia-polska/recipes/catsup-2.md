## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- half a quart of vinegar per gallon of tomato liquid
- two tablespoons of salt
- two tablespoons of sugar
- two tablespoons of ground mustard
- one tablespoon of Jamaican pepper (allspice)
- one tablespoon of cinnamon
- one teaspoon of black pepper
- one teaspoon of cloves
- three-quarters teaspoon of red pepper

## Directions

1. Scald tomatoes with boiling water, peel and then cook until soft, then press through a sieve
2. For one gallon of thus obtained liquid add half a quart of vinegar, two tablespoons of salt, two tablespoons of sugar, two tablespoons of ground mustard, one tablespoon of Jamaican pepper (allspice), one tablespoon of cinnamon, one teaspoon of black pepper, one teaspoon of cloves, three-quarters teaspoon of red pepper; all these spices must naturally be ground
3. Mix well and cook on very low heat for one hour, then pour into bottles and cork tightly
