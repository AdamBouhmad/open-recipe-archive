## Ingredients

- milk
- celery stalks
- one onion
- potatoes
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook together in a quart of milk a couple of celery stalks and one onion
2. Separately cook in water a few potatoes until completely soft
3. Drain the water and mash the potatoes properly
4. Add to the soup along with a spoonful of fresh butter, salt and pepper to taste
5. Strain everything through a sieve and serve
