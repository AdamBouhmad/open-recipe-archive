## Ingredients

- one quart of water
- one large tablespoon of butter or one quarter pound of bacon cut into cubes
- half quart of buckwheat groats
- fried bacon

## Directions

1. Bring one quart of water to a boil and add one large tablespoon of butter or one quarter pound of bacon cut into cubes and fried
2. Pour in half quart of buckwheat groats, mix well, cover and place in the oven for two hours
3. When serving, pour fried bacon over it
