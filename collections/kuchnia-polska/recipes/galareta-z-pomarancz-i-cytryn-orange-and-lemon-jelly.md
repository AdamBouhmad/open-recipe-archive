## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of gelatin
- one cup of warm water
- half a cup of sugar
- juice of two oranges
- juice of one lemon
- zest of orange
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Dissolve one tablespoon of gelatin in one cup of warm water and then add half a cup of sugar
2. Squeeze juice from two oranges and one lemon and add to the gelatin, then strain
3. Rub some of the yellow part of the orange peel and mix with the liquid, then pour onto a large platter
4. When the jelly begins to set, beat a couple of egg whites to stiff peaks and mix with the jelly and arrange on a serving platter
5. This is usually served with whipped cream
