## Ingredients

- 1 head Italian cabbage
- butter or lard
- broth
- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- a small amount of flour
- 1/4 clove garlic, grated
- 2 pinches fine sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the head of Italian cabbage from outer leaves, wash and cut into quarters.
2. Pour cold water in a large pot and bring to boil several times.
3. Pour off the water a few minutes after boiling, remove the cabbage and place in a small pot with 1 tablespoon butter.
4. Pour a little broth over and braise on low heat for about three quarters of an hour, being careful not to let it burn.
5. When tender, make a roux with 1/2 tablespoon butter and a small amount of flour.
6. Add with 1/4 clove grated garlic, pour over the cabbage, sprinkle with 2 pinches fine sugar, mix well.
7. Bring to boil once and serve.
