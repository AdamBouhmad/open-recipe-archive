## Ingredients

- one pound tenderloin
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- onion

## Directions

1. Cut one pound tenderloin into thin slices, beat well and season with salt and pepper
2. Put one tablespoon butter in a pan, heat until browned and arrange a layer of rolls on it
3. Sprinkle with blanched onion cut into thin slices, then another layer of rolls, onion and a little butter on top, then cover tightly
