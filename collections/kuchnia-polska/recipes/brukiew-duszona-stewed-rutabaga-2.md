## Ingredients

- rutabaga
- cold water
- fresh warm water
- piece of young lard or pork
- salt to taste
- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Carefully peel the rutabaga and cover with cold water, bring to a boil once, drain the water, pour in fresh warm water, add a piece of young lard or pork, salt to taste and cook until tender.
2. When completely soft, cut into small cubes both the rutabaga and the lard.
3. Brown one tablespoon of butter with one tablespoon of flour on medium heat, dilute with the broth in which the rutabaga was cooked, add the rutabaga and lard, add a little sugar and braise on low heat for a while, then serve on the table.
