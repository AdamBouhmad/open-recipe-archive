## Ingredients

- several pounds of roast beef, preferably sirloin
- salt and pepper to taste
- fat for basting
- two medium onions
- butter
- white bread, grated
- sauce from roasting

## Directions

1. Pound a several-pound piece of beef strongly, salt it and roast in a hot oven, basting frequently with fat
2. When well browned and half cooked, remove and cut diagonally with a sharp knife
3. Prepare the stuffing: roast two medium onions, grate them, add butter, salt and pepper to taste, and enough grated white bread to make a fairly thick mass, mix well
4. Fill the cuts with this stuffing
5. Place the roast in a roasting pan, pour the sauce that formed during roasting over it, cover with a lid, and braise on low heat for half an hour
