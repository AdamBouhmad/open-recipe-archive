## Ingredients

- pair of large cucumbers
- sour cream
- vinegar
- white pepper
- lettuce, optional

## Directions

1. Prepare cucumbers the same way as in Mizerya po Francusku
2. Instead of oil, use sour cream
3. Instead of red pepper, use white pepper
4. Optionally mix the sliced cucumbers with lettuce in equal proportions
5. Pour the sour cream and vinegar sauce over everything
