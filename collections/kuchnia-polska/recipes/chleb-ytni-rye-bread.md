## Ingredients

- one tablet of yeast
- half quart of lukewarm water
- one quart of rye flour
- half quart of warm water
- half quart of sifted rye flour
- one and one half quarts of wheat flour
- two teaspoons of salt

## Directions

1. Dissolve one tablet of yeast in half quart of lukewarm water
2. Add one quart of rye flour and place in a warm location
3. When the mixture has risen enough to begin falling, add half quart of warm water, half quart of sifted rye flour, one and one half quarts of wheat flour and two teaspoons of salt
