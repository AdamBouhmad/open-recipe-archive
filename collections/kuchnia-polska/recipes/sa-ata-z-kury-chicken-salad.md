## Ingredients

- white meat of roasted chicken - two cups
- half teaspoon of salt
- quarter teaspoon of pepper
- four tablespoons of oil
- two tablespoons of vinegar
- two cups of celery
- one cup of mayonnaise sauce
- lettuce leaves
- olives or gherkins

## Directions

1. Chop the white meat of roasted chicken into half-inch cubes or smaller, about two cups of meat
2. Put half a teaspoon of salt and a quarter teaspoon of pepper into a bowl, mix and then pour in four tablespoons of oil very slowly, constantly stirring, and finally two tablespoons of vinegar
3. Place the meat in a salad bowl, pour this sauce over it and let it sit for an hour
4. Chop two cups of celery into cubes and add to the meat
5. Pour one cup of mayonnaise sauce over it, mix and place on a salad bowl lined with lettuce leaves
6. Pour about one tablespoon of mayonnaise sauce on top and garnish with olives or gherkins
7. The above proportion is for six people
