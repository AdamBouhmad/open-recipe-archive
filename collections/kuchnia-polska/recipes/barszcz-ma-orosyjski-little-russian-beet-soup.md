## Ingredients

- beef broth
- two red beets
- one carrot
- a few sprigs of parsley
- a few celery stems
- a small head of cabbage
- fat skimmed from broth
- flour
- a few chopped tomatoes
- bread kvass or beet kvass
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cook ordinary beef broth
2. Chop finely two red beets, one carrot, a few sprigs of parsley, a few celery stems, and chop into small pieces a small head of cabbage; place vegetables in a pot, add fat skimmed from the broth which should already be nearly done, and brown them
3. Add a spoon of flour to these vegetables and mix well
4. Add the browned vegetables and a few chopped tomatoes to the broth, pour in bread or beet kvass, cook for another half hour and thicken with flour
5. If sour cream is available, lighten the soup with it
6. Cut the meat cooked in the broth into cubes and place in the dish, then pour the soup over it
