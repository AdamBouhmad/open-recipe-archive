## Ingredients

- minced meat prepared as for pieczeń siekana
- hot mashed potatoes
- flour
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat exactly as for the roast stuffed with buckwheat
2. Instead of buckwheat, use hot mashed potatoes
3. Serve with a sauce made from flour and sour cream
