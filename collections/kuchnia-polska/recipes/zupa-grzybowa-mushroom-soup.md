## Ingredients

- Poultry offal (goose or duck) or beef
- Regular broth with vegetables
- Several mushrooms
- One cup of pearl barley
- One tablespoon of butter
- One quarter of cream

## Directions

1. This soup is usually made on goose or duck offal, though it can be made on beef
2. Cook regular broth with vegetables and several mushrooms
3. Separately, cook one cup of pearl barley, add one tablespoon of butter and beat to whiteness
4. Pour in one quarter of cream, dilute with broth
5. Cut the cooked mushrooms into thin strips
6. Serve on the table
