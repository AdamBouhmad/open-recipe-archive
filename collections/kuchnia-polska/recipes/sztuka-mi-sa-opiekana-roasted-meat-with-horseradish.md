## Ingredients

- cooked meat
- horseradish sauce

## Directions

1. Remove meat cooked to tenderness in broth.
2. Cut into oblong pieces and arrange on a glazed or ceramic plate.
3. Pour horseradish sauce prepared in the following manner over it.
