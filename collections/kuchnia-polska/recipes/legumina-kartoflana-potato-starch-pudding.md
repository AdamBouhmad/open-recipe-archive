## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- quarter pound of sugar
- half a lemon
- peel of one whole lemon
- sugar cubes
- remaining egg whites
- two ounces of potato starch
- butter for greasing
- half a quart of fresh sour cream
- fine sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat eight egg yolks with a quarter of sugar until white, then squeeze half a lemon, rub the peel of a whole lemon against three sugar cubes, which then pound and also add.
2. Beat the remaining egg whites into a stiff foam and add slowly to the yolks, while slowly sprinkling potato starch, of which use two ounces.
3. After mixing, grease a mold or form with butter, pour the mixed mass and put into a moderately hot oven for twenty minutes.
4. When it rises and browns, test with a toothpick or match: if the mass inside is dry and does not stick to the match, it is done.
5. Then turn out onto a platter and cut horizontally with a wide knife.
6. Beat half a quart of fresh sour cream with fine sugar and vanilla, spread this mixture thickly on the bottom half of the pudding, cover with the top half, and arrange the rest of the cream on top and serve to the table.
7. This proportion is for eight people.
