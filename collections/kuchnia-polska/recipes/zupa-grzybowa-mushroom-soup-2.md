## Ingredients

- a dozen medium-sized dried mushrooms
- a couple of whole onions
- various vegetables
- salt to taste
- butter
- a small amount of flour
- vinegar to taste
- small dumplings

## Directions

1. Take a dozen medium-sized dried mushrooms, wash carefully, pour over with water and cook for half an hour, then add a couple of whole onions and various vegetables and continue cooking until the mushrooms are completely soft
2. Remove the mushrooms and chop six of them finely, save the rest for the next day for ear-shaped pastries to beet soup or small pies; strain the soup through a sieve, add the chopped mushrooms, salt, thicken with butter mixed with a little flour, add vinegar to taste, bring to a boil and serve with small dumplings which are cooked in this soup
3. This soup can also be prepared without vinegar, and then it is called 'sweet'
