## Ingredients

- sandacz (pike-perch)
- onion
- vegetables (miscellaneous)
- butter
- flour
- fresh dill

## Directions

1. Clean and split the pike-perch, then fill the inside with onion sliced into strips and leave in a cool place for several hours.
2. Just before use, discard the onion, rinse the fish thoroughly, cut it into rings and pour cooled stock made from vegetables over it, then cook on strong heat until tender.
3. Carefully remove the fish and arrange on a platter.
4. Place in a pan a tablespoon of butter, rubbed raw with a tablespoon of flour, brown it lightly and then dilute with the stock in which the fish was cooked, cook for a couple of minutes.
5. Add plenty of chopped fresh dill and pour this sauce over the fish arranged on the platter.
