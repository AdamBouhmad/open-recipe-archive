## Ingredients

- 6 roots of salsify
- 1 teaspoon of vinegar
- 1 quart of water
- 1/2 quart of milk
- 1 heaping tablespoon of butter
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1/2 pod of red or green pepper (optional, instead of black pepper)
- a little onion (optional)

## Directions

1. Scrape 6 salsify roots and put them in cold water with 1 teaspoon of vinegar to prevent them from blackening
2. Wash them well and chop into small pieces
3. Cook in 1 quart of water
4. Add 1/2 quart of milk, 1 heaping tablespoon of butter, salt and pepper to taste
5. Boil and serve
6. If desired, use 1/2 pod of red or green pepper instead of black pepper
7. If desired, you can add a little onion for flavor
