## Ingredients

- several tart apples
- half a quart of water
- half a pound of sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Peel several tart apples, cut into quarters and remove the seeds, then place in cold water so they do not blacken
2. Boil half a quart of water with half a pound of sugar, add cinnamon and lemon zest and cook the apples in this syrup, taking care that they do not fall apart
3. This compote can be colored with the juice of any berries, or served naturally
