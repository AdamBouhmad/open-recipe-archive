## Ingredients

- quarter pound of white sago
- one quart of boiling water
- one bottle of white or red sour wine
- three quarters pound of sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- cloves
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Pour a quarter pound of white sago into one quart of boiling water and cook, constantly stirring, until soft
2. Pour one bottle of white or red sour wine over it
3. Add three quarters pound of sugar, some cinnamon pieces, lemon zest and a few cloves
4. Bring everything to a boil together and pour into a bowl into which you have first placed several lemon slices without seeds
5. The above proportion is for eight people
6. Tapioca may be prepared in the same way
