## Ingredients

- two cups milk
- one teaspoon baking soda
- half teaspoon salt
- two eggs
- half cup wheat flour
- one cup cornmeal
- fat for the pan

## Directions

1. Pour two cups of milk into a clay bowl.
2. Dissolve one teaspoon of baking soda and half a teaspoon of salt in half a cup of hot water and pour into the milk.
3. Lightly beat two eggs and also pour into the milk.
4. Mix half a cup of wheat flour with one cup of cornmeal, pour into the milk, and stir into smooth batter.
5. Pour this batter with a spoon onto a hot, greased pan and fry on high heat.
