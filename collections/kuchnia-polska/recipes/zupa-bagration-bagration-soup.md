## Ingredients

- 1 pound veal with bone
- water
- vegetables
- 1 cup cooked rice
- 1 cup milk
- 2 egg yolks
- flour dumplings or pasta

## Directions

1. Pour water over 1 pound of veal with bone and cook.
2. After skimming, add various vegetables and cook until the meat is completely tender.
3. Remove meat from bone and add 1 cup of previously cooked rice.
4. Pound together in a mortar into a paste, then press through a sieve, pouring broth over it.
5. Distribute the obtained paste with broth.
6. When serving, season with 1 cup of milk in which 2 egg yolks have been beaten.
7. Serve as an addition to this soup with sliced dumplings or pasta.
