## Ingredients

- calf's head
- hot water
- vegetables (carrots, celery, etc.)
- butter
- flour
- broth
- sugar
- caramel
- lemon
- Madeira wine
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. After carefully cleaning the calf's head of hair, split it in half
2. Remove the tongue and brains separately and cook them
3. Discard the eyes and jaws
4. Pour hot water and cook like any calf meat with the addition of vegetables
5. When the meat is tender, remove from broth, cut into nice pieces and arrange on a serving platter
6. Pour with the following sauce: brown one tablespoon of butter, brown with one tablespoon of flour, dilute with broth, add one teaspoon of sugar, a little caramel for color, squeeze half a lemon and pour one glass of Madeira wine
7. The brains should not be cooked, but right after scalding cut into small cutlets, dip in egg, sprinkle with grated breadcrumbs and fry in butter
8. Arrange these cutlets around the ragout and serve to the table
