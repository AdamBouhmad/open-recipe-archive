## Ingredients

- fine noodles (vermicelli)
- salted water
- sharp tomato sauce
- small chicken, about two pounds
- celery stalks
- large onion (preferably Bermuda)
- salt
- white pepper
- red pepper
- ginger powder
- cinnamon
- cloves
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the noodles in salted water, then drain.
2. Place the drained noodles in a small pot and add half a cup of sharp tomato sauce.
3. Roast or cook the chicken and shred both white and dark meat into match-stick sized pieces.
4. Chop four large celery stalks and finely chop one large onion, preferably Bermuda.
5. Add salt, white and red pepper, and a pinch of powdered ginger, cinnamon, and cloves.
6. Mix everything together, place in a buttered pot, and fry over heat, constantly stirring, until everything turns brown.
7. Transfer to a serving dish and serve immediately.
