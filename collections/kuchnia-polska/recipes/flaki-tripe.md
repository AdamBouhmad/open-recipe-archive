## Ingredients

- cleaned tripe
- water
- beef broth
- salt
- vegetables (celery and parsley)
- butter
- grated and sifted bread roll
- grated cheese or Parmesan
- ground white pepper
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Thoroughly clean the tripe and pour water over it, then bring to a boil. Pour out this water, add fresh water and cook the tripe until tender.
2. After cooking, slice thinly like noodles and pour over with warm beef broth prepared previously on beef meat. Add salt and vegetables and cook until it thickens well.
3. Separately, braise finely sliced celery and parsley in butter until soft, then add to the cooking tripe.
4. When the tripe has thickened, remove from heat and let cool.
5. After cooling, take a wide and low pot or stone jar or clay pot and arrange in layers in the following order: a layer of tripe, a layer of fresh unsalted butter in small pieces, a layer of grated and sifted bread roll, a layer of grated cheese or Parmesan, ground white pepper. Repeat these layers in the same order until all the tripe is used.
6. Mix everything well, add salt to taste, add good sour cream, mix gently and place in a slow oven for an hour.
7. Remove, wrap the pot in a napkin and serve at the table.
