## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound marrow
- two whole eggs
- 1/4 pound finely grated and sifted roll
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Beat the marrow well in a mortar until it is similar to sour cream
2. Beat in two whole eggs one at a time, beat everything well together
3. Then gradually sprinkle in 1/4 pound finely grated and sifted roll, add pepper and salt to taste and mix well
4. Lay this mass out on a board and roll out a long piece
5. Cut it into pieces the size of a walnut, then shape each piece until it is round
6. Throw into boiling water
7. When cooked, remove carefully with a spoon and place in a bowl filled with soup
8. These dumplings cannot be placed in an empty bowl because they will stick together
9. If the dumplings are to be served with borscht or sorrel soup, they can be cooked in the soup
