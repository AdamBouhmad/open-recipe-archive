## Ingredients

- kernels from several ears of green corn, one large cupful of the scraped kernels
- remaining corn cobs covered with water
- one tablespoon of flour
- one tablespoon of butter
- salt and pepper to taste
- half quart of good milk or cream

## Directions

1. Scrape the kernels from several ears of green corn with a knife and take one large cupful of this mass
2. Pour enough water over the remaining cobs to barely cover them and boil for half an hour, then strain carefully
3. The resulting liquid should yield three cupfuls and pour into a pot
4. Bring to a boil, then add the scraped corn mass and boil for fifteen to twenty minutes
5. Mix one tablespoon of flour with the liquid, add one tablespoon of butter, salt and pepper to taste
6. Add half a quart of good milk or cream
7. Boil for another five minutes and serve
