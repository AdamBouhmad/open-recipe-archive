## Ingredients

- one cup of figs
- one cup of dates
- water
- sugar as optional

## Directions

1. Wash a cup of figs and a cup of dates thoroughly in warm water
2. Remove the pits from the dates and chop the figs finely together with the dates
3. Put into a pot, add a little water and cook covered for about half an hour, adding only enough water to prevent burning
4. Usually sugar is not added as both dates and figs are sufficiently sweet, but if you wish the compote in sweet syrup, add one cup of sugar ten minutes after boiling and add more water
