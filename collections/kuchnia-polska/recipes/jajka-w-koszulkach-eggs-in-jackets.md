## Ingredients

- eggs
- water
- a tablespoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. If using special tin forms without a bottom, insert them in a pan with boiling water and slip one egg into each form, cook until the white sets, then remove with a slotted spoon
2. If forms are not available, pour at least an inch of water into a deep pan, add a tablespoon of vinegar and bring to boil
3. Slip eggs in very carefully so they do not spread across the pan
4. Before slipping in, hold the egg just above the water and lower it in slowly
5. When the white sets, remove with a slotted spoon and place on a plate in sorrel soup, borscht or broth
