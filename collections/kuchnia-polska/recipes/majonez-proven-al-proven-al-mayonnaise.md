## Ingredients

- chicken
- mixed vegetables (włoszczyzna)
- large bread loaf
- fat
- flour
- mayonnaise
- crayfish tails
- calf's foot jelly
- crayfish necks

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken with vegetables as usual and cut into equal-sized pieces; remove bones and skin as much as possible.
2. Take a large loaf of bread, trim the crust from all sides, and fry it on a large griddle until hardened.
3. Cool the bread, then mix a little flour with fat and coat the bread so the fat hardens on the bread but does not soak in, making it look like it is glazed.
4. Make mayonnaise following the method for 'Mayonnaise with Chicken' but make it thicker.
5. Dip each piece of chicken in the mayonnaise and arrange the pieces on the bread standing as vertically as possible.
6. Surround with crayfish tails, calf's foot jelly, crayfish necks, and similar garnishes.
