## Ingredients

- tender beef
- salt
- pepper
- sour cabbage
- lard or butter
- broth or water
- pork lard or pork rind
- tomatoes
- roots
- grated bread
- flour
- boiled potatoes
- fresh parsley or dill

## Directions

1. Cut tender beef into fairly large but thin slices, beat strongly and season with salt and pepper
2. Blanch some sour cabbage in a pan and spread it on the meat, using one large spoonful of cabbage per meat slice, also add a piece of lard or a teaspoon of butter
3. Roll up, tie with string, arrange in a pan and add broth or water
4. Add several slices of lard or pork rind and several chopped tomatoes and roots
5. Braise until tender
6. When the meat is completely tender, transfer to a serving platter, thicken the sauce with flour and pour it over the rolls
7. Around them arrange grated potatoes and sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley or dill
