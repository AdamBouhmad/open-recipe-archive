## Ingredients

- half pound flour
- one egg
- cold water
- butter
- six egg yolks
- quarter pound sugar
- vanilla
- half quart sweet cream
- burnt sugar for color

## Directions

1. Mix half pound flour and one egg with a little cold water to make fairly stiff dough
2. Turn out onto table, roll thin and cut first into strips, then into narrow noodles, then cook in plenty of salted water
3. When cooked, drain the water, add a tablespoon of good butter and two well-beaten egg yolks to the hot noodles, mix and spread on a buttered metal plate or pan and place in a hot oven for fifteen minutes
4. Flavor half quart sweet cream with vanilla, bring to boil and pour over six egg yolks that have been well beaten with quarter pound sugar, add a tablespoon of burnt sugar for color, shake briefly in the hot oven until thickened, then pour this sauce over the browned noodles, using no more than half the sauce, serving the rest separately at table
