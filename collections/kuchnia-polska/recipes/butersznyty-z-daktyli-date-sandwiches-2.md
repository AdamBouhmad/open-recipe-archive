## Ingredients

- half a pound of dates
- juice of half a lemon
- butter
- bread, sliced thinly
- almonds, chopped (optional)
- raisins (optional)

## Directions

1. Mash the dates into a paste
2. Add the juice from half a lemon and mix well
3. Slice bread into very thin slices and spread with butter
4. Spread a layer of date paste on the bread
5. Cover with another slice of bread
6. Optionally, sprinkle chopped almonds on the date paste before covering
7. Alternatively, raisins may be used instead of dates
