## Ingredients

- salad greens
- one thin but wide slice of bread
- garlic
- butter
- two hard-boiled egg yolks
- one teaspoon of strong mustard
- two tablespoons of oil
- one tablespoon of strong vinegar
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Sort, wash and drain the salad as usual
2. Cut a thin but wide slice of bread, rub it with garlic and fry in butter
3. Place this toast on a salad bowl and put the salad on top
4. Cover with a plate and let it stand for one hour
5. After one hour, take two hard-boiled egg yolks, add one teaspoon of strong mustard, two tablespoons of oil, one tablespoon of strong vinegar and a little white pepper
6. Crush all of this well into a uniform paste and pour this sauce over the salad
7. Mix and only then salt to taste, discarding the slice of toast beforehand
