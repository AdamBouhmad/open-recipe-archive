## Ingredients

- one quart of yellow vinegar (cider vinegar)
- one and three-quarter pounds of white sugar
- six whole cloves
- twelve grains of Jamaican pepper (allspice)
- one teaspoon of ground cinnamon
- one teaspoon of mace
- a small piece of ginger
- large and healthy pears

## Directions

1. Pour one quart of yellow vinegar (cider vinegar) into a pot and add one and three-quarter pounds of white sugar
2. Tie six whole cloves, twelve grains of Jamaican pepper (allspice), one teaspoon of ground cinnamon, one teaspoon of mace, and a small piece of ginger in a cloth and place in the vinegar
3. Cook all of this for fifteen minutes from the time it boils
4. Select large and healthy pears, peel and remove the stems
5. Place enough pears in the vinegar so that all are covered with liquid
6. Cook on a very low fire until pears are almost transparent
7. Remove pears and arrange in jars, then pour boiling vinegar over them
8. To preserve for a longer time, seal the jars tightly or pour paraffin over them
