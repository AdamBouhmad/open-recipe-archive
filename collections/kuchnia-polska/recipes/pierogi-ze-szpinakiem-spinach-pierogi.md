## Ingredients

- flour
- egg
- water
- spinach
- salted water
- butter
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Knead ordinary dough from egg and water as above.
2. Place heaps of spinach that has been cooked in salted water and then carefully mashed in a bowl or passed through a sieve on the dough.
3. Wrap and cook the same way as other wrapped pierogi, and when serving pour hot butter over them and sprinkle with grated cheese.
