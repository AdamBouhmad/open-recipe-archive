## Ingredients

- one quart of tomatoes
- half a quart of beef broth
- one onion
- one bay leaf
- one celery stalk
- a few sprigs of parsley
- one teaspoon of sugar
- one quarter teaspoon of baking soda
- one tablespoon of butter
- two tablespoons of flour
- salt
- pepper
- croutons

## Directions

1. Stew one quart of tomatoes—they may be fresh or preserved—in a pot, add half a quart of beef broth, one onion, one bay leaf, one celery stalk and a few sprigs of parsley, one teaspoon of sugar, one quarter teaspoon of baking soda, one tablespoon of butter, two tablespoons of flour, salt and pepper to taste.
2. Place the tomatoes in the pot with the celery, parsley and onion; cook about ten minutes, then press through a colander or sieve to remove the seeds.
3. Pour into a clean pot, place on the fire and bring to a boil.
4. Rub butter with flour and work into the soup, then stir until the soup is uniform.
5. Add salt, pepper, sugar and soda just before serving. May be served with croutons.
