## Ingredients

- several raw potatoes
- broth or bulion
- one large tablespoon of dried mushrooms
- butter
- butter and flour

## Directions

1. Slice several raw potatoes into slices or strips
2. Pour broth or bulion over them and cook
3. Scald one large tablespoon of dried mushrooms and stew in butter
4. Add to the cooked potatoes
5. Bind with a rounded teaspoon of butter mixed with a bit of flour
6. Mix together and pour over meat
