## Ingredients

- half dozen young leeks
- butter
- potatoes
- bay leaf
- peppercorns
- salt
- flour
- parsley or dill

## Directions

1. Chop half dozen young leeks into cubes and stew in butter
2. Separately cook several peeled and sliced potatoes, adding to them a bay leaf, a few peppercorns and salt to taste
3. When the potatoes are soft, add the stewed leeks
4. On the remaining butter brown half spoonful of flour, pour also into the soup, bring to boil and pour into a bowl, into which sprinkle chopped parsley or dill
