## Ingredients

- 1 large hare
- cold water
- salt to taste
- warm water
- salt to taste
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1.5 cups milk
- 2 onions, finely chopped
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Clean a large hare, cover with cold water and let soak for twenty minutes
2. Remove from water, sew or wrap in a piece of thin cloth, place in a pot, cover with warm water, salt to taste, cook on low heat for one and a half hours, then remove from cloth, place on a platter, and pour the following sauce over it: Brown 1 tablespoon butter with 1 tablespoon flour in a pot, add one and a half cups milk and bring to a boil
3. Chop very finely two onions previously cooked in water, add them to the sauce, as well as salt and pepper to taste, and bring to a boil several times
