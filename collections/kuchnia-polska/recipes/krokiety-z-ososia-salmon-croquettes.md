## Ingredients

- 1 small can of salmon
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 large tablespoon butter
- salt to taste
- 2 well-beaten eggs
- 1 beaten egg
- breadcrumbs
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Remove salmon from 1 small can, discard bones and chop finely
2. Bring 1 cup of milk to a boil and thicken with 1 tablespoon of flour mixed with 1 large tablespoon of butter
3. Add the chopped salmon to the milk, salt, stir and bring to a boil
4. Then beat in 2 well-beaten eggs and immediately remove from heat and place in a cold location until the mixture cools
5. When completely cooled, form small cutlets, dip each in beaten egg and then in breadcrumbs
6. Fry in butter in a pan
