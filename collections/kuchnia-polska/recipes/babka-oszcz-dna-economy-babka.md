## Ingredients

- one egg
- one tablespoon butter
- four tablespoons cream
- water
- one and a half cups flour
- one cup sugar
- pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Break one egg into a cup, add one tablespoon of butter, four tablespoons of cream—which may be canned—and fill to the top with water
2. Sift together one and a half cups of flour, one cup of sugar, and a pinch of salt
3. Add the egg with butter, beat everything well, beating at least five minutes
4. Fill a form with this batter and bake
