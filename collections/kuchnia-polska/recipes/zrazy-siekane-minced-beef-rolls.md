## Ingredients

- two pounds good beef, first cross-cut
- fat
- one soaked and wrung-out roll
- two medium roasted and finely minced onions
- salt and pepper to taste
- two egg yolks or one whole egg
- quarter pound young lard
- a few tablespoons broth
- a few dried mushrooms
- two small onions
- one or two tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Mince two pounds good beef with fat, add one soaked and wrung-out roll, two medium roasted and finely minced onions, salt and pepper to taste, and two egg yolks or one whole egg
2. Mix everything well and form eight elongated rolls from this mixture
3. Finely mince a quarter pound young lard and fry in a pan until lightly browned, then add the rolls
4. Brown them well on all sides, then add a few tablespoons broth, a few dried mushrooms which should be soaked in hot water for an hour and finely chopped, and two small onions
5. Braise covered on low heat for fifteen to twenty minutes, then add one or two tablespoons sour cream and braise again for a quarter hour, then transfer to a serving platter
