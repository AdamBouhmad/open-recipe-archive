## Ingredients

- beef broth
- cabbage, one quarter head per person
- small potatoes, finely diced
- water for cooking cabbage

## Directions

1. Prepare ordinary clear broth. Separately, cook cabbage that has been cut into quarters and previously parboiled, allowing one quarter head per person. Cook the cabbage until completely soft and without a lid so the bitterness can evaporate. When the cabbage is cooked, drain it and place in a serving bowl along with a portion of finely diced cooked potatoes and pour broth over it. Some also use sour cream with this broth, which is served separately and added to the plate at the table.
