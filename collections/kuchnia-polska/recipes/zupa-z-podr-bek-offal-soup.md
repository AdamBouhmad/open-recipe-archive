## Ingredients

- offal from two pieces of poultry
- various vegetables for broth
- semolina or fine Cracow barley
- egg
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. For four or five persons, take offal from two pieces of poultry, clean carefully, add plenty of various vegetables and cook like broth.
2. After skimming, sprinkle in semolina and cook until tender, then beat two egg yolks in a bowl and pour the soup over them.
3. Instead of semolina, fine Cracow barley mixed with egg may be used.
