## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of fresh butter
- one teaspoon of sifted potato starch or cornstarch
- half a quarter of white wine
- quarter pound of fine sugar
- juice from half a lemon
- a good glass of good rum

## Directions

1. Rub one tablespoon of fresh butter with one teaspoon of sifted potato starch or cornstarch
2. Dissolve in half a quarter of white wine
3. Place on fire and cook slowly, constantly stirring, until thickened
4. When the sauce is thick, add quarter pound of fine sugar
5. Squeeze in juice from half a lemon
6. Pour in a good glass of good rum
7. Place again on fire and warm well, stirring thoroughly
8. This sauce is served with legumes
