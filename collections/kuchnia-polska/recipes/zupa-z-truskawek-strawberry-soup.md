## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 cup sweet cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 pound fine sugar
- 1 quart ripe strawberries
- 1 glass light sour wine
- whole strawberries
- ice
- biscuits (biszkopciki), cut into cubes

## Directions

1. Mix together 1 cup of sour cream, 1 cup of sweet cream and 3 egg yolks. Add 1/2 pound of fine sugar and whip everything together into a foam.
2. Place in a pot or pan on hot heat and stir with a spoon until it thickens slightly, being careful that the yolks do not curdle.
3. Strain 1 quart of ripe strawberries through a sieve so that only the seeds remain on the sieve. Pour the obtained liquid into the hot cream, mix well.
4. Then add, while constantly stirring, 1 glass of light sour wine. Put a handful of whole nice strawberries into a bowl, pour the soup over and place on ice.
5. When completely cooled, serve on the table, providing separately biscuits cut into cubes.
