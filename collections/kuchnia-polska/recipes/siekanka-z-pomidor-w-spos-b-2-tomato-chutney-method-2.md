## Ingredients

- a bunch of green tomatoes
- half a bunch of peeled and chopped apples
- one pound of common raisins
- one pound of currants
- two cups of finely chopped beef suet
- one chopped lemon
- three pounds of sugar
- one cup of vinegar
- two tablespoons of cinnamon
- half a teaspoon of cloves
- half a teaspoon of nutmeg

## Directions

1. Scald a bunch of green tomatoes with boiling water, peel and chop
2. Scald with boiling salted water and drain the water
3. Add half a bunch of peeled and chopped apples, one pound of common raisins, one pound of currants
4. Add two cups of finely chopped beef suet, one chopped lemon, three pounds of sugar, one cup of vinegar
5. Add cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg
6. Cook for half an hour
7. Fill jars, seal tightly and store
