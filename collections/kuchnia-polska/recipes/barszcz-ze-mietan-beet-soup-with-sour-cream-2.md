## Ingredients

- a few mushrooms
- a few onions
- various vegetables to taste
- a few red beets
- acid

## Directions

1. Cook a few mushrooms, a few onions and various vegetables to taste separately; cook or bake a few red beets separately, then chop them finely and pour with acid
