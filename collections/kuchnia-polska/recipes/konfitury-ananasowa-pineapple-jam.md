## Ingredients

- ripe pineapples
- sugar equal in weight to the chopped fruit
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel ripe pineapples and cut into dice; do this in the evening
2. For each pound of chopped fruit, take one pound of sugar, sprinkle this sugar over the pineapples arranged in a bowl and let it stand overnight
3. The next morning, pour everything into a pot and boil until the pineapple is soft and clear, then add some lemon juice
4. Boil once and fill jars
5. Do not add water at all
