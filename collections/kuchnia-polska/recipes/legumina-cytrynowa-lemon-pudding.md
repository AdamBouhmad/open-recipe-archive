## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one cup sugar
- juice of one large lemon
- grated lemon zest
- one quart milk
- half quart grated buns or white bread
- preserves or jam
- two whole eggs
- two tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Beat three eggs with one cup sugar
2. Add juice from one large lemon and grated zest
3. Add one quart milk and half quart grated buns or white bread
4. Mix everything together and bake in a baking tin in a moderately hot oven
5. Remove from oven and cover the top with preserves or jam
6. Beat strongly two whole eggs with two tablespoons sugar
7. Arrange the beaten eggs and sugar on top of the jam
8. Put in the oven for a moment until browned
9. Serve to the table
