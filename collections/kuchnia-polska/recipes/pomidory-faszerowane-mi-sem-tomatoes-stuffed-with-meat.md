## Ingredients

- large tomatoes
- cooked beef, pork, or both
- grated bread crumbs
- egg yolk
- salt
- pepper
- tomato pulp
- garlic, grated, optional
- butter or oil

## Directions

1. Cut off the tops of large tomatoes with a sharp knife
2. Remove the center with a spoon
3. Pass the removed mass through a sieve so that all the seeds remain on the sieve
4. Take cooked meat (which may be beef, pork, or both)
5. Add grated bread crumbs, egg yolk, salt and pepper to taste, the sieved tomato mass, and a pinch of grated garlic if desired
6. Fill the hollowed tomatoes with this mixture
7. Cover with the tops cut from the tomatoes
8. Place in a baking dish or form generously buttered or oiled
9. When well baked, serve at table
