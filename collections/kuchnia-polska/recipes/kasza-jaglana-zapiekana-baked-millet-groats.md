## Ingredients

- half quart of millet groats
- one quart of whole milk
- one tablespoon of butter
- fresh bacon slices
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Scald half quart of millet groats
2. Then cook until tender in one quart of whole milk to which one tablespoon of butter has been added before boiling
3. When the groats are completely tender, line a baking dish with thin slices of fresh bacon
4. Place the cooked groats in the middle
5. Place in the oven for two hours
6. Then turn out onto a serving platter or bowl and serve, offering fine sugar separately
