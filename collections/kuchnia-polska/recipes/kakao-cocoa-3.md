## Ingredients

- three tablespoons cocoa
- two tablespoons sugar
- one cup boiling water
- three cups hot milk
- half cup cream
- vanilla extract (optional)
- one egg (optional)
- half cup hot water (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix three tablespoons cocoa with two tablespoons sugar, pour into a saucepan and add one cup of boiling water, pouring slowly
2. Cook for five minutes from the moment it boils
3. Then add three cups of hot milk and half cup cream, mix and serve
4. If desired, add some vanilla extract; some also add one egg, beaten with half cup of hot water
