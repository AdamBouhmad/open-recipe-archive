## Ingredients

- two cups flour
- one-half cup sugar
- one-half cup molasses
- one-third cup butter
- one teaspoon baking soda
- one teaspoon powdered ginger
- one teaspoon cinnamon
- one teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Mix two cups flour, one-half cup sugar, one-half cup molasses, one-third cup butter, one teaspoon baking soda, one teaspoon powdered ginger, one teaspoon cinnamon, and one teaspoon salt together (first dissolve the soda in the molasses)
2. Bake on a flat sheet so the layer is no thicker than three-quarters inch, at most one inch
3. After baking, cut with a hot knife into square pieces and serve hot
