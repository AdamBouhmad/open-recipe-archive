## Ingredients

- three cups strong beef broth
- three tomatoes
- one cup coarse rice
- one teaspoon salt
- quarter teaspoon pepper
- one tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Pour three cups of strong beef broth into a pot and boil
2. Cut three tomatoes into small pieces and throw into the broth
3. Cook for five minutes, then strain and pour the broth back into the pot
4. Rinse one cup of coarse rice and spread on a tableboard or paper to dry
5. Throw it into the broth and cook on low fire until it absorbs all the broth
6. Add one teaspoon salt, quarter teaspoon pepper and one tablespoon butter
7. Mix well and serve on the table
