## Ingredients

- 1 cup cornmeal
- 0.5 cup wheat flour
- 1.5 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 egg
- 1.5 cups sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup cornmeal and 0.5 cup wheat flour and add 1.5 teaspoons baking powder and sift together to mix well.
2. Beat 1 egg, add 1.5 cups sweet milk and mix all into dough.
3. Place in forms and bake in a hot oven.
