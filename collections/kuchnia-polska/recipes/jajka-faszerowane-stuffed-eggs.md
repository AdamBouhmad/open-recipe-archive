## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- two tablespoons cooked ham, chopped very finely
- two tablespoons vinegar
- salt and pepper to taste
- one teaspoon mustard

## Directions

1. Hard boil eight eggs for fifteen minutes.
2. Shell the eggs and cut each one in half crosswise.
3. Remove the yolks without damaging the whites and mash them into a paste.
4. Mix the yolks with two tablespoons of finely chopped cooked ham, two tablespoons of vinegar, salt, pepper to taste, and one teaspoon of mustard.
5. Mix everything well and fill the cavities in the whites left by the removed yolks.
6. Cut one end of the egg flat so it can stand.
7. Arrange on a platter and pour over a sauce made from the remaining filling thinned with vinegar.
