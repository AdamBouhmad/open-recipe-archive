## Ingredients

- quarter pound of chocolate
- one cup of sugar
- three-quarters cup of water
- two tablespoons of butter
- two tablespoons of cornstarch
- quarter cup of milk
- one teaspoon of vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Cook together for five minutes: quarter pound of chocolate, one cup of sugar, three-quarters cup of water and two tablespoons of butter
2. Mix two tablespoons of cornstarch in quarter cup of milk, pour into the chocolate mixture and cook another three minutes
3. Beat with a spoon until it cools, then add one teaspoon of vanilla extract
