## Ingredients

- 1 cup prunes
- water
- 1/2 package gelatine
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 lemon juice
- sherry wine or orange juice
- cream for serving
- ground almonds (optional)

## Directions

1. Soak 1 cup prunes in cold water
2. Cook until tender
3. Drain, pass through a sieve and grind finely or through a meat grinder
4. Add to the prune liquid 1/2 package dissolved gelatine, 1 cup sugar, 1 lemon juice and a little sherry wine or orange juice
5. Stir over the fire until the gelatine is dissolved, then strain
6. Add the prunes and let set, stirring occasionally until it begins to jelly so that the fruit does not settle to the bottom
7. Serve with cream
8. Ground almonds used in the mixture give a good flavor
