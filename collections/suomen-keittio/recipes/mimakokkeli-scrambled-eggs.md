## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons cream, milk or water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Stir eggs, add milk, salt and pepper.
2. Melt butter in a pan and turn the mixture in.
3. Let cook slowly stirring constantly.
4. When it begins to thicken, remove from the pan carefully with a spoon.
5. If water is used instead of milk, the eggs will become bright yellow.
6. Alternatively, melt 1 tablespoon of butter in a clean hot pan, break the eggs into the pan, and stir with fork until the whites become firm, then season with salt and pepper.
