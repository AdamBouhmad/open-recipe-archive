## Ingredients

- 11 whites of eggs
- 1 cupful of granulated sugar
- 1 cupful of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoonful of sherry or vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat whites of eggs to a stiff froth
2. Sift sugar many times and add slowly into the beaten whites
3. Mix with flour, sifted six times with cream of tartar, and seasonings
4. Pour the mixture into an unbuttered domeshaped mold and bake in a moderate oven about 40-50 minutes
5. The cake may be covered with following mixture: 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar, 2 tablespoonfuls of sherry
