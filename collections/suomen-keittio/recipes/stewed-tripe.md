## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 cupful of milk
- salt
- pepper
- 1 teaspoonful of onion juice
- 1 cupful of boiled tripe

## Directions

1. Make a sauce of 1 tablespoonful of butter, 1 tablespoonful of flour and 1 cupful of milk
2. Season with salt, pepper and 1 teaspoonful of onion juice
3. Cut boiled tripe in dice and mix with sauce
4. Stir over the fire until the pieces of tripe are thoroughly done
