## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 cup cream or spinach liquor
- cooked chopped spinach
- hard boiled eggs
- cheese
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Make a sauce of butter, flour and cream or spinach liquor
2. Add boiled chopped spinach
3. Split hard boiled eggs and remove the yolks
4. Chop yolks and mix with the spinach and bring to boil
5. Fill the halves of the whites with the spinach mixture
6. Sprinkle with grated cheese and bread crumbs
7. Brown in oven
