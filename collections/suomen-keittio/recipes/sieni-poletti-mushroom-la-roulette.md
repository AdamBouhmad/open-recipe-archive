## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- butter
- beef stock
- salt and pepper
- cream
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms
2. Cook in butter for 5 minutes
3. Add a little beef stock, salt and pepper
4. When ready to serve, add cream and egg yolks
