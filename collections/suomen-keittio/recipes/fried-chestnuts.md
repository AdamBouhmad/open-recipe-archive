## Ingredients

- skinned chestnuts
- butter
- fine salt
- chicken stock

## Directions

1. Cut the skinned chestnuts in two or four pieces
2. Fry quickly in best butter
3. Sprinkle with fine salt
4. Pour chicken stock on
5. Let stand in pan, covered, two minutes
