## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked sweetbreads
- 1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs soaked in cream
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- 3 egg whites
- butter for molds
- milk sauce

## Directions

1. Have 1/2 cup of chopped mushrooms to 1 cup of sweetbreads.
2. Add 1/2 cup of bread crumbs soaked in cream, 3 yolks of egg, salt, pepper and some onion juice.
3. Last of all add 3 whites of egg beaten firm.
4. Put into buttered timbale molds and bake in oven in a pan of water covered with buttered paper for 30 or 40 minutes.
5. Serve with milk sauce.
