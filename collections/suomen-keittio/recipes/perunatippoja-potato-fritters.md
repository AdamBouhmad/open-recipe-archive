## Ingredients

- 1 cup mashed potatoes
- 1 tablespoon cream
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- nutmeg and pepper
- 1 yolk and 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Mix the potato with the cream, salt, nutmeg and pepper.
2. Add the whites beaten stiff last of all.
3. Drop by the spoonful into boiling lard.
4. Cook until light brown.
