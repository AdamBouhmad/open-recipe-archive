## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup cream mixed with milk
- 1 pint cooked shrimps
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- 2 egg yolks
- cream

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of 1 tablespoon butter, 1 tablespoon flour and 1 cup of cream mixed with milk
2. Add 1 pint of cooked shrimps to the sauce
3. Heat through
4. Season with salt, pepper and onion juice
5. Beat 2 egg yolks with some cream
6. Add the beaten egg yolks to the sauce
7. Serve either baked in molds with bread crumbs sprinkled on top, or in another form
