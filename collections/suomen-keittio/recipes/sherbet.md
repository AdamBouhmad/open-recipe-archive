## Ingredients

- 1 quart of half-frozen lemon ice
- wine to taste
- 1 white of egg

## Directions

1. Mix half-frozen lemon ice with wine to taste
2. Add white of egg
3. Freeze until the mixture is hard
4. Serve as entree
