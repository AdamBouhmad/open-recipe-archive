## Ingredients

- 6 large artichokes
- water
- salt
- 4 ozs. of butter
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Strip off outer leaves and remove stalks
2. Trim off tops of inner leaves
3. Rub the bottom with lemon
4. Immerse the artichokes with stalk ends uppermost in boiling water mixed with a little salt
5. Cook until tender
6. Take out quickly
7. Arrange on a serving dish with the top up
8. Serve with Hollandaise sauce or melted butter
