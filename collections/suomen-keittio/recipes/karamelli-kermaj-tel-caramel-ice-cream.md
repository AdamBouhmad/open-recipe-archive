## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup water

## Directions

1. Prepare as given for vanilla ice cream.
2. Instead of sugar use caramel made by browning 1 cup of sugar in a pan and 1/2 cup of water added to it.
3. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
4. The sugar must not be burnt, only delicately browned.
