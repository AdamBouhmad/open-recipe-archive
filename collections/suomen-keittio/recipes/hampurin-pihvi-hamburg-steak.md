## Ingredients

- 1 pound of raw lean meat
- onion juice
- salt and pepper
- 1 egg
- flour

## Directions

1. Chop the meat very finely, removing all membranes as carefully as possible
2. Mix in onion juice, salt, pepper and 1 egg
3. Form the mixture into small flat cakes
4. Dust in flour
5. Fry in butter
6. Make a sauce by adding flour and meat stock to the butter in the pan
7. Season with salt, pepper and lemon juice
