## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of butter
- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- 3 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 10 eggs
- 2 pounds of currants
- 1/4 pound of blanched and chopped almonds
- 1/4 pound of grated lemon rind
- 1/4 pound of grated orange peel
- 1 pound of raisins, cut in pieces
- 2 teaspoonfuls of cinnamon
- 2 teaspoonfuls of spices
- 1/2 grated nutmeg
- 1/2 teaspoonful of mace
- 1 cupful of brandy
- 1/4 teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Work butter and sugar to a cream, add well beaten yolks of eggs, spices, fruits dipped in flour, brandy, soda, flour and hard beaten whites.
2. Bake in oven about 3 — 4 hours.
3. If it becomes brown cover with paper.
