## Ingredients

- 3-4 cupfuls of boiling water
- 1/2 quart of rhubarb, cut in pieces
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of tapioca
- 2 teaspoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Soak tapioca in boiling water 10-15 minutes.
2. Place a saucepan on the fire, adding rhubarb, sugar and butter to tapioca.
3. Let simmer 1/2 hour, stirring often, so as not to burn the mixture.
4. Mold, serve cold, unmolded and garnished with whipped cream.
