## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of sour cream
- 2 cupfuls of sweet cream
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Whip 4 yolks and 1 whole egg with sugar to a froth.
2. Pour cream into it, add flour and salt.
3. Lastly stir in whites, beaten stiff.
4. Fry thin pancakes in fryingpan or bake in oven.
