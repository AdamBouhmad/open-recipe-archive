## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 3/4 cup boiling water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3/4 cup jelly

## Directions

1. Mix sugar with cornstarch
2. Add slowly the boiling water
3. Let boil for 5 minutes
4. Remove from the fire, add butter and jelly
5. Keep in a pan of hot water until served
