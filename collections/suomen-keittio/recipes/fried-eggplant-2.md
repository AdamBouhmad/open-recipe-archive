## Ingredients

- 1 eggplant
- salt
- 1 egg
- bread crumbs
- hot fat
- cheese crumbs (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel an eggplant and cut in thin slices
2. Lay the slices on a dish and sprinkle with salt between the slices
3. Press with a slight weight for 1/2 to 1 hour
4. Wipe the slices
5. Dip in egg and bread crumbs
6. Fry in hot fat
7. Sprinkle with cheese crumbs if desired
