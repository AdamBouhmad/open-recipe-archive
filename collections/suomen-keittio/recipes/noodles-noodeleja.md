## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/2 tablespoonful water
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Whisk 2 eggs into foam; add 1/2 tablespoonful of water, a little salt, and flour enough to make a thick dough.
2. Knead the dough well.
3. Roll out thin, like thin paper.
4. Cover with a cloth and let stand for a little while.
5. Cut into nice pieces with a sharp knife.
6. Let dry on the table.
7. Boil 15 minutes before putting in the soup.
