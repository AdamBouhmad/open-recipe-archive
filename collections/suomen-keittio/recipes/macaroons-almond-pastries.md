## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound of almond-paste
- 1/2 pound of sugar
- 3 whites of eggs

## Directions

1. Work almond-paste and sugar together on board.
2. Add 3 whites of eggs slowly and rub until smooth and even.
3. Drop with spoon on paper small cookies.
4. Put paper in a slow oven.
5. If not thick enough to hold together, the batter may be mixed with 1 tablespoonful of flour.
