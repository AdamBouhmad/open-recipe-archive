## Ingredients

- Calf heart and lungs
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 onion
- Butter
- 2 tablespoons wheat flour or bread crumbs
- 2 cups milk or bouillon
- Lemon juice
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Split the heart and lungs of calf
2. Soak in cold water to remove the blood
3. Put on the fire in cold water with salt and pepper
4. Cook for 1 hour
5. Remove the skin and the throat, chop the meat
6. Brown the onion in butter, mix with the meat and fry until brown
7. Add 2 tablespoons of flour or bread crumbs and 2 cups of milk or bouillon
8. Season with salt and pepper, flavor with lemon juice
9. Serve with potatoes
