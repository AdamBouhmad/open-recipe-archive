## Ingredients

- Veal
- Salt and pepper
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Butter
- Lemon
- Anchovies
- Capers

## Directions

1. Cut veal crosswise into slices 1/2 inch thick
2. Pound lightly and trim into oval-shaped cakes
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, or dip in salted water
4. Dip in beaten egg and bread crumbs
5. Brown butter in a frying pan
6. Fry the slices of meat on both sides
7. Cover and leave on the range for a little while
8. Put a bit of butter under each slice
9. Place a slice of lemon on top
10. Spread boned anchovies with caper, roll together, and lay the rolls on the slices of lemon
