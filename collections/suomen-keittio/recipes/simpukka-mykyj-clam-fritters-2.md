## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder
- lemon juice
- 1 pint of clams
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Make batter of 2 eggs, 1/2 cupful of milk, 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour, 1 teaspoonful of baking powder and a little lemon juice
2. Have one pint of clams and chop them fine
3. Season with salt and pepper and add the batter into the mixture
4. Drop by spoonfuls into boiling lard, cooking 3 or 4 at a time
5. When browned drain on brown paper
6. Serve on a folded napkin
