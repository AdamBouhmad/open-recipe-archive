## Ingredients

- lima beans
- a piece of pork
- 2 tablespoons butter or lard
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup bean cooking liquid

## Directions

1. Rinse the beans, let stand in lukewarm water until they begin to crack.
2. Put on the fire in cold water with a piece of pork.
3. Cook slowly until tender.
4. Make a brown sauce of 2 tablespoons butter or lard browned with 2 tablespoons flour and diluted with 1 cup liquor of the beans.
5. Add the beans to the sauce.
6. Bring to boiling.
