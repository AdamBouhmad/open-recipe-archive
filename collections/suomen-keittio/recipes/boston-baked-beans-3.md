## Ingredients

- 1½ quarts of beans
- cold water
- piece of pork
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar or molasses
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1 chopped onion
- ½ cupful of chili sauce
- 3 cupfuls of water

## Directions

1. Rinse and soak 1½ quarts of beans in cold water
2. Cook in the same water slowly about 1 hour having a little piece of pork in the kettle
3. Drain off the water, put the beans with rind of pork into an earthen bowl
4. Prepare a sauce of 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar or molasses, 1 tablespoonful of salt, 1 chopped onion, ½ cupful of chili sauce and 3 cupfuls of water
5. Pour the sauce over the beans
6. Cover and bake in a slow oven 6—7 hours
7. Add a little boiling water if necessary
