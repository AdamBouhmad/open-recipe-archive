## Ingredients

- 2 cups cottage cheese
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons milk or cream
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3 eggs
- Vanilla and currants if desired
- Salt

## Directions

1. Force the cheese through a sieve
2. Add the seasonings, flour and cream
3. Beat the yolks with sugar
4. Add to the cheese
5. Mix with the beaten whites
6. Bake in an open crust until firm and the crust is brown on the bottom
