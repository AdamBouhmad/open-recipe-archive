## Ingredients

- 2 cups whipping cream
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs to a froth.
2. Add the whipped cream and flour.
3. Bake in a buttered mold; sprinkle the top with sugar.
4. Serve with jam.
