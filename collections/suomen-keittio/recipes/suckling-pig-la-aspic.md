## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of suckling pig
- 2 quarts of water
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 10 peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- 3 leaves of gelatine
- knuckles of veal
- 2 eggs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat and the knuckles with cloth wrung out from hot water.
2. Put into hot water, place over the fire.
3. Skin carefully, season, let simmer on a slow fire until tender.
4. Remove the skin, cut the meat into nice cubes.
5. Beat the whites lightly, mix with gelatine dissolved in water and 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice.
6. Add the liquor of meat carefully skimming.
7. Whisk over the fire until it begins to boil.
8. Move the kettle to the side of the stove and let it simmer gently for 5 minutes.
9. Strain through a fine cloth.
10. Pour a little clear aspic into the bottom of the mold, decorate with hard-boiled eggs and parsley.
11. Lay the meat pieces into the mold gently, so that there is room for plenty of liquid in between.
12. Place the mold in a cold place and let the liquid set.
13. Serve with potato salad.
