## Ingredients

- 1½ cupfuls of mashed chestnuts
- 4 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Mix chestnuts with eggs, butter, and cream
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Form into fillets and prepare as directed for Fillets of Potatoes with Chestnuts
