## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Cream butter with sugar
2. add yolks of eggs rubbing well
3. Add little by little milk and flour mixed with soda
4. Last whites beaten to a froth
5. Bake in a buttered dome-shaped mold, covered, about 25 minutes
6. Turn out upon a serving dish and serve with hard, chocolate or fruit sauce
