## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of butter
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cupful of milk
- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 2 1/2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 3/4 teaspoonful of nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put all ingredients into a bowl, beat well five minutes.
2. Bake in a buttered baking pan 30 - 40 minutes.
