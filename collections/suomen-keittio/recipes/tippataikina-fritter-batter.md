## Ingredients

- 1½ cups flour
- 1 cup milk or water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon sugar for sweet fritters
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- ⅜ teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks
2. Add milk or water, sugar, butter, flour and salt
3. Last of all fold in the beaten whites
4. Test if the batter is thick enough
5. If too thick, dilute with an egg white
6. Flavor with lemon juice and vanilla
