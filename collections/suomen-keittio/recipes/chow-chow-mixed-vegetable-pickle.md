## Ingredients

- 10 pints of cucumbers
- 1 quart of green tomatoes
- 1 red pepper
- 1 cauliflower
- 1 pint of small white onions
- 1 quart of string beans
- 1 quart of peas
- 1 pint of carrots, cut in pieces
- 2 quarts of vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of pepper
- few bay leaves
- mace
- 2 ozs. of mustard seed
- a little salt
- 1/2 oz. of allspice
- different kinds of mushrooms

## Directions

1. Clean vegetables and cut in pieces
2. Let stand in salted water for 24 hours
3. Boil up vinegar with sugar and seasonings, put the vegetables into it and cook until soft
4. Arrange the vegetables nicely into jars, sprinkle with white pepper, mace and bay leaf between the layers
5. Fill the jars with drained vinegar mixture
6. Sprinkle the top with mustard seeds
7. Cover and keep in a cool place
