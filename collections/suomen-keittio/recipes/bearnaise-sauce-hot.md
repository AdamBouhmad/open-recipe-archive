## Ingredients

- yolks of 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of salad oil
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice or vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful of hot water
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Whip the yolks; add gradually the oil, water and lemon juice.
2. Put the saucepan in boiling water, stir until thick.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Serve with fish, chops and broiled meat.
