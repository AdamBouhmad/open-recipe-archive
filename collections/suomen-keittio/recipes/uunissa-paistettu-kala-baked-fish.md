## Ingredients

- Fish
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg
- Cracker crumbs or flour
- Bacon or butter
- Chopped onion
- Vegetables
- Water or cream
- Flour
- Parsley
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean and wipe.
2. Rub with salt and pepper.
3. Spread with egg and dip in cracker crumbs or flour.
4. Lay bacon or butter in a pan, place the fish upright on it.
5. Place chopped onion and vegetables around.
6. Bake basting often.
7. When done, place on a platter.
8. Make a gravy of the drippings in the pan by adding a little water or cream and flour.
9. Pour the gravy over the fish.
10. Garnish with parsley and lemon.
