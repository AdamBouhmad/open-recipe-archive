## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 6 ozs. of sugar
- 1/2 quart of thin cream
- 6 ozs. of butter
- 1 lb. of plums
- 4 ozs. of sweet almonds
- rind and juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Beat yolks and sugar to a froth
2. add cream and butter and cook to a thick batter
3. Stir until cool
4. then add grated yellow rind and juice of lemon
5. Lastly mix carefully the beaten white into it
6. Cook plums in a little water until soft, then stone them
7. Butter a mold and cover inside with buttered paper
8. Put a layer of plums into it
9. then minced almonds
10. lastly pour the egg mixture in
11. Bake in a slow oven about 1 hour
12. Turn out upon a serving dish and serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce
