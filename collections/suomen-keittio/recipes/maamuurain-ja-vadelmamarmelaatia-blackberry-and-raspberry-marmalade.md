## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 quarts of blackberries
- 2 quarts of raspberries
- 2 1/2 lbs. of granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Place all the berries in kettle on fire.
2. Cook until it gets firm when tested by dropping on plate.
3. Pour in jars and preserve as usually.
