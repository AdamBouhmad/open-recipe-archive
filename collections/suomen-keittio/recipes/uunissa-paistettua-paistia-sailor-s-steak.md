## Ingredients

- Sirloin steak or other beef steak
- 1 carrot, chopped
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 tomato or small amount of catsup
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Water

## Directions

1. Broil the steak for a few minutes or brown in a pan.
2. Place in a covered pan or casserole with the chopped carrot, onion, tomato or catsup, salt, pepper and parsley.
3. Pour a little water into the pan in which the meat was browned and pour over the meat.
4. Let cook until the vegetables are tender.
5. Alternatively, cook without vegetables by placing small pieces of butter on top instead.
