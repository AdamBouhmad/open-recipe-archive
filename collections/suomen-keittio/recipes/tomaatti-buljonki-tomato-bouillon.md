## Ingredients

- Six medium sized tomatoes
- 5 cupfuls of vegetable stock
- Salt and pepper
- Whipped cream

## Directions

1. Peel six medium sized tomatoes, split, remove the seeds.
2. Cut in pieces, cook with 5 cupfuls of vegetable stock for 15-20 minutes.
3. Strain through a fine cloth, season with salt and pepper.
4. Serve with whipped cream.
