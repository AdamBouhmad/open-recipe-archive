## Ingredients

- mackerel
- butter or salad oil
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak the fish, wipe and spread with butter or salad oil and lemon juice.
2. Let stand for a while.
3. Then broil on a slow fire spreading often with butter.
4. Serve with tomato or milk sauce.
