## Ingredients

- 1 quart water
- Juice of 3 lemons
- Grated yellow rind of 3 lemons
- 3 tablespoons cornstarch
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the water with lemon rind and juice for a few minutes
2. Thicken with cornstarch
3. Beat egg yolks with sugar
4. Add the beaten yolks to the mixture
5. Stir over fire, not allowing to boil
6. Optional: fold in 2 egg whites beaten stiff after removing from fire
