## Ingredients

- Onions
- Turnips
- Celery
- Boiled rice
- Bread crumbs
- Eggs
- Butter
- Rich milk
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the vegetables until nearly done, peel and scoop.
2. Grind the insides.
3. Mix with boiled rice and onion, add bread crumbs, eggs, butter and rich milk, according to taste.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Stuff the vegetables with the mixture.
6. Bake in a buttered pan.
7. Serve with sauce.
