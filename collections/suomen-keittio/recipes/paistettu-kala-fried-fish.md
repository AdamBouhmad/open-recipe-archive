## Ingredients

- fish
- egg
- bread crumbs
- flour
- salt
- butter or lard
- water or cream

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, rinse, wipe and cut into pieces.
2. Dip in egg and bread crumbs or in flour mixed with salt.
3. Melt butter in a hot pan, fry the fish until light brown.
4. Cover, place the pan in a hot oven for a short while.
5. Place the fish on a platter, make a gravy of the drippings in the pan by adding cream. Pour over the fish.
6. If cooked in lard, dip fish in bread crumbs, in egg and in bread crumbs again, taking care not to leave any opening for the lard to get in.
