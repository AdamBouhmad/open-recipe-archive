## Ingredients

- kidneys
- salt
- pepper
- butter, melted
- lemon juice
- bread crumbs
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cut kidneys crosswise into slices.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Dip in melted butter mixed with some lemon juice.
4. Roll in bread crumbs.
5. Broil over a clear fire, turning often so as not to burn them.
6. Serve very hot on toast basted with melted butter.
