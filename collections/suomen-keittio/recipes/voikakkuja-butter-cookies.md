## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 egg yolks or 1 whole egg
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 6 or 7 cups flour

## Directions

1. Rub until creamy butter with sugar
2. Work gradually into the butter eggs, lemon juice and flour mixed with baking powder
3. Roll out
4. Cut in small cookies
5. Brush with egg white
6. Add a sprinkling of sugar and almonds
7. Bake until light brown
