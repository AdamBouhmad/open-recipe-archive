## Ingredients

- 2 quarts bouillon, water or milk
- 2 cups potatoes cut in cubes
- 2 stalks celery
- 2 slices onion
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and celery and cut in pieces.
2. Boil in 1 quart of bouillon or water until tender.
3. Press through a sieve.
4. Cook the onion with the butter in a saucepan.
5. Thicken with the flour and add gradually the soup to the onion.
6. Mix with the rest of the bouillon or with milk.
7. Cook for 10 minutes.
8. Season and add the yolks and cream, if preferred.
9. Serve with croutons.
