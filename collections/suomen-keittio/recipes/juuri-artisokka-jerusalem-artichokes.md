## Ingredients

- Jerusalem artichokes
- cold water
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Let Jerusalem artichokes stand in cold water for 1 or 2 hours and then peel.
2. Put on the fire in salted water mixed with lemon juice.
3. When tender, drain quickly, otherwise they will become tough.
4. Serve hot with milk sauce or cold as salad prepared the same as potato salad.
