## Ingredients

- 2½ pounds beef removed from bone
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon pepper
- 1 onion
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3 tablespoons tomato
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 4 cups bouillon or water
- cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the meat in cubes.
2. Fry in butter until brown.
3. Add chopped onion, seasonings and flour, and continue frying for a little while.
4. Pour the bouillon over the meat and let simmer with lid on until the meat is tender.
5. Add the strained tomato and a little cream.
6. Let it come to a boil and serve.
