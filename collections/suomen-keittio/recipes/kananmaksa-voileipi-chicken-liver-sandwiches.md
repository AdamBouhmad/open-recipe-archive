## Ingredients

- boiled chicken livers
- mushrooms
- butter
- onion juice
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- toasted bread slices
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Chop boiled chicken livers and mushrooms.
2. Heat in a pan with a lump of butter.
3. Season with onion juice, salt, pepper and lemon juice.
4. Spread on toasted bread slices.
5. Sprinkle with hard boiled chopped egg on top.
