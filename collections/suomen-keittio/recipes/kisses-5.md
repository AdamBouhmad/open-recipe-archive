## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of almonds
- 1 lb. of powdered sugar
- 2 whites of eggs

## Directions

1. Peel almonds, grate and pound with sugar in mortar or in wooden bowl.
2. Beat whites of eggs to a hard froth and stir almonds into it.
3. Drop almond-mixture with spoon on baking sheet.
4. Press lightly with finger in centre of each cookie, put jelly in.
5. Bake in a slow oven.
