## Ingredients

- cooked slices of onion
- carrots
- turnip
- celery cut in dice
- layer of meat cut in cubes, either fried or boiled
- salt
- pepper
- brown sauce
- paste for cover or slices of tomato
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Line pie plate with cooked slices of onion, carrots, turnip, and celery cut in dice.
2. Place a layer of the meat cut in cubes on the vegetables.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Fill the plate alternating the layers.
5. Pour brown sauce over.
6. Cover with paste or lay slices of tomato on and sprinkle with bread crumbs.
7. Bake in oven about 1 hour.
