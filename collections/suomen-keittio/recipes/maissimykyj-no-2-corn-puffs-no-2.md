## Ingredients

- 1 cup corn canned or fresh corn grated
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 2 eggs, whites and yolks beaten separately

## Directions

1. Take 1 cup of corn canned or fresh corn grated, add 4 tablespoons flour, 1/4 teaspoon baking powder, 2 eggs, the whites and yolks beaten separately.
2. Drop by the spoonful in boiling lard, cook until brown.
3. Or fry in a pan until brown on both sides.
