## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 cupfuls rich milk
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- Onion juice
- Salt and pepper
- Milk sauce and vegetable balls for serving

## Directions

1. Make a sauce of butter, flour and milk.
2. Add the yolks beaten well and the seasonings.
3. Last of all mix in the whites beaten stiff.
4. Bake in a soufflé dish with a hole in the center, placed in a pan of water, until it sets.
5. Serve with milk sauce and vegetable balls.
6. When served unmolded, the soufflé may be made without flour to make it fluffy.
