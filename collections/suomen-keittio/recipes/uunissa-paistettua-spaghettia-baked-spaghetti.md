## Ingredients

- boiled spaghetti
- butter
- onion
- green pepper
- strained tomato
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Fill a buttered mold with boiled spaghetti
2. Place chopped onion and green pepper fried or raw between the layers of spaghetti
3. Cover with strained tomato
4. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese
5. Bake in the oven
