## Ingredients

- cabbage
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt
- sugar
- onion
- mushrooms
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage in strips and parboil, then drain.
2. Fry with 2 tablespoons of butter, some salt and sugar until tender.
3. Fry in butter a chopped onion and mushrooms.
4. Fill a buttered mold with alternating layers of cabbage and mushrooms, dot with butter, and sprinkle with bread crumbs.
5. Bake for 1.5 hours or until brown.
