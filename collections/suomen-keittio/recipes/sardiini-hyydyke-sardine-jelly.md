## Ingredients

- small fish molds
- sardine
- aspic
- toast
- egg
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Garnish small fish molds
2. Place a sardine on the bottom of each
3. Fill with aspic
4. Turn on toast
5. Decorate with egg and mayonnaise
