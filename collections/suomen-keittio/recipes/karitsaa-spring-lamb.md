## Ingredients

- Spring lamb
- Water
- Mint sauce
- Butter
- Salt
- Lemon juice
- Stuffing

## Directions

1. Roast lamb in a hot oven with a little water in the pan.
2. Allow 10-18 minutes to a pound.
3. Serve with mint sauce.
4. When using the forequarter, have the bones well cracked or removed.
5. Rub the space with butter, salt and lemon juice.
6. Stuff.
