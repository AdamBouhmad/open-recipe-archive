## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 quart water
- raspberries, strawberries, gooseberries, or blackberries

## Directions

1. All kinds of berries, like raspberries, strawberries, gooseberries, blackberries may be preserved in the same way as directed for apples, but do not let them boil as long a time as apples.
