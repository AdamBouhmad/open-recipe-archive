## Ingredients

- rice
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash the rice carefully in several waters
2. Put in salted boiling water on the fire to cook slowly 20 minutes or until the rice is tender but not mashed
3. Drain off the water and rinse well with hot water
4. Sprinkle with salt, dot with small bits of butter
5. Place on the range stirring now and then to evaporate the moisture
6. Or turn the rice into a strainer and put in oven to dry
