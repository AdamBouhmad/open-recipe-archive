## Ingredients

- 1 pound cabbage
- 3/4 pound butter
- 5 tablespoonfuls cooked rice
- 1/2 pound wheat flour
- 3 cupfuls milk or vegetable broth
- 4 eggs
- pepper
- nutmeg
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook cabbage in salted water and grind in a meat grinder with the rice
2. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and milk
3. Mix the ground cabbage with the sauce, add seasonings and beaten egg yolks
4. Finally fold in egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
5. Bake either in small timbale molds or in a mold with a hole in the center, placed in a pan of water
6. May also be baked as balls
7. Serve with tomato or milk sauce
