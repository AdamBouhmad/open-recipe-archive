## Ingredients

- Boiled and fried sweetbreads
- Fritter batter
- Fat for frying
- Tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cut boiled and fried sweetbreads into egg shaped pieces.
2. Dip in fritter batter.
3. Fry in deep fat.
4. Serve on a folded napkin with tomato sauce.
