## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons milk, cream or water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and add milk or water, salt and pepper.
2. Melt the butter in a hot omelet pan.
3. Turn the eggs into the pan.
4. Fry shaking the pan over the fire until firm.
5. Place the omelet on one side of the pan and brown quickly underneath.
6. Turn onto a hot platter and serve with or without sauce.
