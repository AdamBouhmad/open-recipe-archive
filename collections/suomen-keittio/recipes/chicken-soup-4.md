## Ingredients

- remains of 2 chickens
- 2 1/2 quarts of water
- 1 leek
- 2 stalks or 1/2 root of celery
- 2 sprigs of parsley
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- pepper and salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of wine or 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from the bones and cut it nicely into pieces.
2. Place the bones into boiling water to boil together with the vegetables washed and cut into pieces.
3. Let boil slowly for a few hours.
4. Thicken with butter and flour.
5. Season with salt and pepper, flavor with wine or lemon.
6. Strain and serve with the meat cut into pieces and fried bread dice.
