## Ingredients

- chicken liver
- butter
- flour
- brown sauce
- Madeira wine
- salt
- pepper
- Kitchen Bouquet (optional)

## Directions

1. Split liver and wipe.
2. Place 1 tablespoonful of butter in a frying-pan and fry the liver in it about 5 minutes, turning often so it does not burn.
3. Dredge with flour and add brown sauce and a little Madeira wine into the pan.
4. Season with salt and pepper and cook gently 10 minutes.
5. Add a dash of Kitchen Bouquet if desired.
