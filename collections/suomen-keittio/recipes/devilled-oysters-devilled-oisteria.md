## Ingredients

- 1 pint of oysters
- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 3/4 cupful of milk
- lemon juice
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- yolk of 1 egg
- cleaned oyster shells

## Directions

1. Strain and mince oysters
2. Prepare sauce with butter, flour and milk
3. Add the beaten egg yolk to the sauce
4. Add oysters, lemon juice, parsley and seasonings
5. Put mixture into cleaned buttered oyster shells
6. Dredge with bread crumbs
7. Bake in a hot oven 15 minutes
8. Serve on plates about 3 on each
9. Garnish with lemon slices and parsley
