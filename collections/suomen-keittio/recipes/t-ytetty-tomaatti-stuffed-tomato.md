## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- finely cut celery
- cooked and minced chicken or veal
- mayonnaise
- cucumber or celery

## Directions

1. Peel the tomatoes and scoop out the inside
2. Sprinkle the inside with salt
3. Let stand on ice until they have to be served
4. Fill the tomatoes with finely cut celery mixed with equal parts of chicken or veal and mayonnaise
5. Alternatively, the tomatoes may be filled with cucumber or celery mixed with mayonnaise
