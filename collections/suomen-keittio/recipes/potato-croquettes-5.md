## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of mashed potatoes
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- salt and pepper
- yolks of 3 eggs
- egg
- bread crumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. Mix mashed potatoes with butter, salt, pepper and yolks.
2. Form into balls or oval-shaped croquettes.
3. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
4. Cook in lard.
