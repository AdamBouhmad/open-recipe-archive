## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salted water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- milk sauce
- cream

## Directions

1. Use ripe cucumbers.
2. Peel, cut lengthwise in big pieces.
3. Cook in salted water until tender.
4. Drain carefully.
5. Mix with butter, salt and pepper.
6. Or make a sauce of the liquor of the cucumbers.
7. Serve in a vegetable dish or on toast.
8. May be served in milk sauce.
9. Or mash the cucumbers, mix with butter, salt and pepper.
10. Add cream, if preferred.
