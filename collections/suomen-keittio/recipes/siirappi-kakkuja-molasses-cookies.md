## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cupfuls of molasses
- 3/4 cupful of cream
- 9 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1/2 lb. of fine sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tablespoonful of cardamom
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 tablespoonful of brandy
- 4 eggs
- 5 1/2 lbs. of flour

## Directions

1. Molasses, cream, butter, sugar and spices are brought to a boil.
2. Flour is sifted.
3. Half of the flour is placed in a bowl and the cooled molasses mixture is gradually mixed into it.
4. Eggs are beaten, brandy and soda with the remaining flour are added and well mixed.
5. Batter is allowed to stand until the next day.
6. Roll thin and shape into small cookies with a cutter.
7. Bake in a moderate oven.
