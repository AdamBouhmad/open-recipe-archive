## Ingredients

- 1 to 1½ salt herrings
- 2 cupfuls of boiled or roast meat
- 2 cupfuls of boiled potatoes
- 2 cupfuls of boiled carrots
- 2½ cupfuls of boiled beets
- 2 pickled cucumbers
- White pepper
- French dressing
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Lettuce leaves
- 2 apples (optional)

## Directions

1. Soak fish in water for a few hours
2. Bone, skin and chop the fish
3. Cut vegetables, pickles and meat in small pieces
4. Mix all well together and pour French dressing on
5. Place in bowl
6. Garnish the surface with hard-boiled eggs and beets
7. Arrange lettuce leaves around
8. The salad may be mixed also with two apples, cut in small pieces
