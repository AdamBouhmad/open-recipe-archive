## Ingredients

- salmon, 1 inch thick pieces
- olive oil
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- lemon juice
- Bearnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the fish in one inch thick pieces.
2. Lay in olive oil for an hour.
3. Wipe and fry on a slow fire basting with butter now and then.
4. Cook for about 20 minutes, carefully watching that it does not scorch.
5. Lay on a platter.
6. Sprinkle each piece with salt, pepper and parsley.
7. Dot with butter and squeeze some lemon juice on each.
8. Serve with Bearnaise sauce.
