## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- toast
- salt
- pepper
- red pepper

## Directions

1. Separate whites from yolks.
2. Put the yolks aside leaving them in the shells.
3. Beat the whites to a firm froth.
4. Press through a pastry bag upon buttered toast close to the edge.
5. In centre of these put the yolks, one on each.
6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
7. Garnish with pieces of red pepper.
8. Bake in oven until lightly brown.
