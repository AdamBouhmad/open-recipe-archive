## Ingredients

- 1 egg white
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice or other flavoring

## Directions

1. Put the white of an egg into a bowl
2. Mix gradually with 1 cup of powdered sugar
3. Add 1 tablespoon lemon juice or any other flavoring preferred
4. Beat until the mixture is smooth
5. Place the icing in the center of the cake when the latter is yet a little warm
6. Spread even over the cake with a knife dipped in water
7. If the icing is prepared before the cake is done, cover it with a damp cloth so that it will not harden
8. By using more sugar and a little water, the icing will be made thinner
9. Use fruit or vegetable juice or saffron for coloring
