## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of plums
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of cream
- 5 leaves or 4 tablesp. of gelatine
- 1/4 lb. of sugar

## Directions

1. Cook plums until tender in a small quantity of water
2. Stone and press through a sieve
3. Add sugar and gelatine dissolved in 1/2 cupful of plum liquor
4. Add lastly cream whipped to a froth
5. Set to firm in a mold
6. Turn on a serving dish and garnish with whipped cream
