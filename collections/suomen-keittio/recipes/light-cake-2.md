## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of butter
- 3 cupfuls of flour
- 9 whites of eggs
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Work butter to a froth
2. Add soda sifted many times with flour and continue rubbing a little while
3. Whip whites to a stiff froth and add sugar slowly into it
4. Combine with the above mixture
5. Bake in small forms or thin cakes in layer cake molds
6. Shape into small cakes and cover with Royal icing
