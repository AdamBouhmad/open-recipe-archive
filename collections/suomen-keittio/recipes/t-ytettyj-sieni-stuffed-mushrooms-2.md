## Ingredients

- large mushrooms of equal size
- cooked chicken
- mushrooms
- cream
- White Chaufroid sauce
- bread crumbs
- eggs
- bacon

## Directions

1. Peel large mushrooms of equal size and remove stems
2. Prepare a forcemeat of cooked chicken and finely chopped mushrooms, softened slightly with cream
3. Place this mixture between the mushroom caps
4. Coat the mushrooms with White Chaufroid sauce
5. Let cool, then roll in bread crumbs, dip in egg, and roll again in bread crumbs
6. Fry in butter, or arrange in a baking pan with a small piece of smoked bacon on each, and bake covered for 15 minutes
