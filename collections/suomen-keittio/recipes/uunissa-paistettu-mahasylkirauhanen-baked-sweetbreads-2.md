## Ingredients

- pork
- carrots
- celery
- turnip
- boiled sweetbreads
- meat stock
- brown sauce or mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Line a pan with pork, carrots, celery and turnip
2. Place boiled sweetbreads on the pork
3. Pour meat stock over them so as to cover the bottom of the pan
4. Bake for 20 minutes, basting often
5. Serve with brown or mushroom sauce
