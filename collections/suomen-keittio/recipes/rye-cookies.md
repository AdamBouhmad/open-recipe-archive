## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 3/4 lb. of powdered sugar
- 7 lbs. of flour
- 6 ozs. of rye flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Wash butter and work to a froth
2. Sift flour with baking powder
3. Mix all ingredients well together and put the batter in a cold place for a little while
