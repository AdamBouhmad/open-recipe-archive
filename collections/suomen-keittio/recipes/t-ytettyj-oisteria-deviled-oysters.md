## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 3/4 cup milk
- Lemon juice
- Chopped parsley
- Salt, pepper, mustard
- 1 egg yolk
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Drain and chop the oysters
2. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and milk
3. Add the beaten egg yolk to the sauce
4. Add oysters, lemon juice, parsley and spices to the sauce
5. Put the mixture into clean buttered oyster shells
6. Dredge with bread crumbs
7. Bake in a hot oven for 15 minutes
8. Serve on plates with 3 shells each, garnished with lemon and parsley
