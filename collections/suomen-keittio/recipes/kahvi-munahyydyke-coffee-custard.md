## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cupful of sugar
- a pinch of salt
- a pinch of nutmeg
- 2 tablespoonfuls of ground coffee

## Directions

1. Boil the milk with 2 tablespoonfuls of ground coffee
2. Strain the milk
3. Beat eggs, add sugar, salt and nutmeg
4. Pour the scalded milk gradually into it
5. Put in a buttered mold and steam in oven until firm
