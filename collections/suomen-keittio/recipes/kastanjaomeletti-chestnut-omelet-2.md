## Ingredients

- ½ pound chestnuts
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons water or milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- Cream

## Directions

1. Peel the chestnuts, chop fine and boil in a small amount of water until soft.
2. Mix 1 tablespoon of butter and 1 tablespoon of flour in a pan.
3. Add the chestnuts, mix well and let simmer.
4. Add a little cream and season.
5. Prepare a French omelet, pour the mixture over one half and fold the other half over.
