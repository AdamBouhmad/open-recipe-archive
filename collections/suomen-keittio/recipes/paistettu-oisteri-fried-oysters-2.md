## Ingredients

- oysters
- lemon juice
- chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- butter
- flour
- milk
- yolk of one egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Drain and chop the oysters
2. Prepare the sauce of butter, flour and milk
3. Add beaten yolk of one egg, oysters, lemon juice, parsley and seasonings to the sauce
4. Put the mixture into clean buttered oyster shells
5. Dredge with bread crumbs
6. Bake in a hot oven 15 minutes
7. Arrange on plates, 3 on each, garnish with slices of lemon and parsley
