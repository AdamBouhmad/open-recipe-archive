## Ingredients

- 1 cup grated cheese
- 1/2 tablespoon flour
- salt
- pepper
- 1 egg white, beaten firm

## Directions

1. Mix grated cheese with flour, salt, pepper and the beaten egg white.
2. Cook in deep lard.
