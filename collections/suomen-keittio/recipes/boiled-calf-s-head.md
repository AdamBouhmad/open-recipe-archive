## Ingredients

- Calf's head
- Lemon
- Vinegar
- Peppercorns
- Bay leaf
- Onion
- Carrot
- Parsley
- Butter
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Have the head split open and cleaned
2. Wash carefully and remove the tongue and brains
3. Boil the brains quickly and put aside with the tongue for later use
4. Soak the head in cold water, place to boil
5. Bring to boiling point, pour off the water, and let stand until cool
6. Rub the head with lemon, return into boiling water to cook slowly
7. Mix with 2 tablespoonfuls of vinegar, 10 peppercorns, 1 bay leaf, 1 onion, 1 carrot and a sprig of parsley
8. Cover, let cook covered until the meat is tender, taking care not to break it by boiling
9. Remove the bones carefully, place the meat in an equal-sized pan
10. Spread with butter, sprinkle with bread crumbs, dot with butter
11. Brown in oven
12. Serve with Poulette or Allemande Sauce
