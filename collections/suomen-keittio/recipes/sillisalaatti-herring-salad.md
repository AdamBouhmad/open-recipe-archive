## Ingredients

- 1½ salted herrings
- 2 cups boiled potatoes
- 2 cups boiled carrots
- 2½ cups boiled beets
- 2 dill pickles
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 hard boiled eggs
- 2 cups boiled or fried meat
- lettuce leaves
- French dressing or cream dressing

## Directions

1. Soak the herrings in water for a few hours
2. Cut herrings into small pieces
3. Cut vegetables and pickle into small pieces
4. Mix all ingredients together well
5. Put on a platter
6. Garnish the top with hard boiled eggs and beets
7. Garnish around with lettuce leaves
8. Serve with French or cream dressing
