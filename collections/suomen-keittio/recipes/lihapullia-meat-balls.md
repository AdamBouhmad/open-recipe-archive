## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- 1/2 or 3/4 cup water or cream
- 1/2 onion, fried
- 2 tablespoons butter or lard
- 1 tomato, cut in pieces
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the bread crumbs in water or cream.
2. Add egg, tomato, meat, salt, pepper and onion.
3. Stir until smooth.
4. Roll into round balls.
5. Fry in butter or lard.
6. Make a gravy of the drippings in the pan by adding some butter, flour and water until moderately thick.
7. May be made without tomato if preferred.
