## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Vegetables or chicken salad
- Goose liver pie or chopped sweetbreads
- White Chaudfroid Sauce
- Lettuce
- Chicken jelly

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens whole.
2. Remove carefully the fillets, the breastbone and the legs.
3. Stuff with vegetables or chicken salad.
4. Fill the breast hole with goose liver pie or chopped sweetbreads.
5. Pour upon the fillets white Chaudfroid Sauce.
6. Place nicely on the chicken and around it upon a serving dish.
7. Garnish with lettuce and chicken jelly.
