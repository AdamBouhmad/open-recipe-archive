## Ingredients

- rice grains
- milk mixed with cream
- vanilla
- sugar
- lemon juice
- 2 leaves of gelatine or 1 tablespoon of granulated gelatine
- 2 egg yolks
- cooked fruit
- salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- chopped almonds
- fruit juice

## Directions

1. Cook rice in milk until tender, add vanilla, sugar and lemon juice
2. Dissolve 2 leaves of gelatine or 1 tablespoon of granulated gelatine, mix with the rice
3. Add last 2 egg yolks
4. Place the rice on a platter, garnish around with cooked fruit
5. Force through a strainer a few fruits, pour over the rice
6. Alternatively, cook the rice in slightly salted water for 20 minutes
7. Drain, rinse in cold water, let stand for a while
8. Baste with sweetened water mixed with 1 teaspoon of vanilla
9. Mix with chopped almonds, place into oven for a while
10. Arrange on a platter, baste with fruit juice
