## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of potato mash
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 1 cupful of cream or milk
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix butter, cream and yolks with potato mash.
2. Add the stiffly beaten whites last.
3. Bake in a buttered mold.
