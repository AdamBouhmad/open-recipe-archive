## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 6 tablespoons sugar
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons cream
- Yellow rind of 1 lemon
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 4 cups flour

## Directions

1. Beat until frothy eggs with sugar
2. Add flavoring, cream, butter and flour mixed with baking powder
3. Form into cookies in the shape of an elk horn
4. Bake on a buttered baking sheet in a moderately hot oven
