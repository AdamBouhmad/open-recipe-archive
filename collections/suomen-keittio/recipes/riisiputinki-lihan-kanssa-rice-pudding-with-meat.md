## Ingredients

- 1 pint boiled chopped meat
- 1 onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt
- pepper
- 1 cup rice
- water
- 1 quart milk
- 1 egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Chop the onion and fry in butter with the meat
2. Add seasonings
3. Cook a batter of rice with some water and milk
4. When cool, add the beaten egg and salt
5. Combine the meat mixture with the rice mixture
6. Put into a buttered mold
7. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
8. Bake in oven
