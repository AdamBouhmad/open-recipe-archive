## Ingredients

- hard boiled eggs
- spinach
- melted butter
- cream
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- milk sauce

## Directions

1. Cut hard boiled eggs lengthwise in halves
2. Rub the yolks through a sieve
3. Mix chopped boiled spinach with melted butter, cream, onion juice, salt and pepper
4. Fill the halves of the whites with this mixture
5. Arrange them in a buttered mold
6. Cover with a rich milk sauce
7. Sprinkle with grated yolk of egg
8. Bake until light brown
9. Serve in mold
