## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of sour milk
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Prepare and bake in the same way as the preceding griddle cakes.
