## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- lettuce leaves
- mayonnaise sauce
- minced parsley

## Directions

1. Peel tomatoes, putting them first in boiling water for a few minutes.
2. Split them in halves or quarters and arrange on lettuce leaves and pour mayonnaise sauce on.
3. Or place tomatoes whole in a bowl on a bed of lettuce leaves, cover with mayonnaise sauce and sprinkle with minced parsley.
