## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Rub until creamy the butter with the sugar
2. Add the egg yolks beaten until frothy
3. Add walnuts, milk, baking powder and flour sifted together
4. Add vanilla or lemon juice
5. Last of all fold in the whites of the eggs beaten until firm
6. Bake in a tub tin
