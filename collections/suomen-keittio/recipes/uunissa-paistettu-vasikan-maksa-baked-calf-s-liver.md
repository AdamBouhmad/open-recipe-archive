## Ingredients

- Calf's liver
- Flour
- Salt
- Onion slices
- Bacon slices or lard
- Carrots, celery, potatoes, and onions (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the liver.
2. Dip in flour mixed with salt.
3. Place in a buttered pan.
4. Line with slices of onion and bacon around, or dot with lard.
5. Vegetables such as carrots, celery, potatoes and onions cut in pieces may be baked with the liver.
6. Bake for 1 hour.
7. When brown, cover to keep it juicy.
8. Serve plain or with fried bacon.
9. Make a gravy of the drippings.
