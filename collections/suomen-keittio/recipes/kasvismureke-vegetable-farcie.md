## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of cooked vegetables
- 2 cupfuls of bread crumbs
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of corn starch
- 3/4 lb. of butter
- 2 onions
- 3/4 cupful of cream

## Directions

1. Grind vegetables in meat mill
2. Brown chopped onions in butter
3. Add browned onions to ground vegetables
4. Soak bread crumbs in cream and mix with beaten eggs
5. Combine with vegetable mixture
6. Season with salt and pepper
7. Turn into a buttered mold and form into a loaf
8. Spread with egg
9. Bake in oven 1 hour
10. Serve with potatoes and brown vegetable sauce
