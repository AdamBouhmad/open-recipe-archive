## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- caviare
- anchovy slices
- mayonnaise sauce
- toast

## Directions

1. Remove a slice from the side of hard-boiled eggs.
2. Scoop out the yolks and fill the eggs with caviare.
3. Score both ends near the top, fill the scores with a slice of anchovy.
4. Make a hole in center of the surface, put a piece of anchovy into it.
5. Place the eggs on toast the hollowed side down so as to resemble barrels.
6. Garnish the toast around the barrel with mayonnaise sauce.
