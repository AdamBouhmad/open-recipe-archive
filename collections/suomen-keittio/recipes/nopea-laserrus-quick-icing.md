## Ingredients

- 1 egg white
- a small cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons cold water
- vanilla for flavoring
- coffee or chocolate (optional)
- 1 1/2 ounces chocolate dissolved (optional)

## Directions

1. Put into a double boiler 1 egg white, a small cup sugar and 3 tablespoons cold water
2. Cook for 7 minutes and beat while cooking
3. Flavor with vanilla
4. Instead of water, coffee or chocolate may be used
5. 1 1/2 ounces of chocolate dissolved may be added after removing the icing from the fire
