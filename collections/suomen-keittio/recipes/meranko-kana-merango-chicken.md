## Ingredients

- Breast and leg pieces of chicken
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Butter
- Mushrooms

## Directions

1. Take breast and leg pieces of chicken, trim nicely leaving a bone in each.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Roll in flour and fry in butter.
4. Lay in a pan, pour the drippings over.
5. Let simmer until tender.
6. Place on a platter, garnish with triangular toast.
7. Add chopped mushrooms to the gravy.
8. Cook for a few minutes and pour over the chicken.
