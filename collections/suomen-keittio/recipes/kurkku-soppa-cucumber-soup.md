## Ingredients

- 3 large cucumbers
- 1 1/2 quarts of white stock
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 onion or 1 slice of onion
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 cupful of cream
- salt, pepper, a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, cut into slices and remove the seeds.
2. Simmer the onion and cucumber slices in butter, add the flour and meat stock.
3. Let boil.
