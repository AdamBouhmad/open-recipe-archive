## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- egg

## Directions

1. Prepare potatoes same as for potato croquettes.
2. Force through a pastry bag in a buttered pan forming it into roses, leaves, pyramids or baskets.
3. Spread with egg.
4. Bake in oven to a light brown.
