## Ingredients

- potatoes
- lard or deep frying fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Pare the potatoes, rinse and wipe dry.
2. Cut into pieces of equal size, strips, balls, slices or any preferred shape.
3. Cook in deep lard until light brown.
4. Sprinkle with salt and serve on a folded napkin.
