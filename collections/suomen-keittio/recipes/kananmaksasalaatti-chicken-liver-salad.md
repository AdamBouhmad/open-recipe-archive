## Ingredients

- 1 cup fried chicken liver
- 4 hard boiled eggs
- 2-3 pickled onions
- French dressing

## Directions

1. Cut fried chicken liver in pieces.
2. Mix with 4 hard boiled eggs cut in pieces and 2 or 3 pickled onions.
3. Pour French dressing over.
