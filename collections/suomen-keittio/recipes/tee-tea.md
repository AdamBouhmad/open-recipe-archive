## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons tea
- 2 cups water

## Directions

1. Heat the teapot
2. Put the tea in
3. Pour 3/4 cup boiling water over
4. Let stand for 5 minutes in a warm place
5. Add the remaining water just before serving
