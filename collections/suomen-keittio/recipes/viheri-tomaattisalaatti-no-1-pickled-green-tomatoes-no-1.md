## Ingredients

- 3 pounds green tomatoes
- 1 quart small onions
- 2 cups salt
- 2 quarts cider vinegar
- 1 or 2 pounds brown sugar
- 1/4 pound mustard seed
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons cloves
- 1 tablespoon celery seed
- 1 tablespoon allspice

## Directions

1. Cut in pieces the tomatoes, peel the onions, sprinkle the salt over them
2. Let stand for 24 hours
3. Drain, place into cold water
4. Put into a kettle the onions and tomatoes with sugar and spices tied in a bag
5. Simmer for a few hours
6. Note: A few cucumbers cut in pieces may be added to the tomatoes
