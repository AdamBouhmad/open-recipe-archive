## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful of grated horseradish
- 4 tablespoonfuls of lemon juice
- 3 tablespoonfuls of tomato catsup
- Tabasco sauce
- salt
- paprika
- ice
- small oysters

## Directions

1. Mix vinegar, grated horseradish, lemon juice, tomato catsup, Tabasco sauce, salt and paprika
2. Set on ice to cool
3. Wash small oysters and set on ice
4. Serve in small glasses with five oysters in each glass and sauce poured over
