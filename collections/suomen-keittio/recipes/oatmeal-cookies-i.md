## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of oatmeal
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 1/2 cupful of granulated sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of cream
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 cupful of butter

## Directions

1. Cream butter with sugar
2. Add beaten eggs and cream
3. Mix in oatmeal, flour mixed with baking powder and salt
4. Rub well
5. Roll into a thin sheet, take with cutter small round cakes
6. Bake in a hot oven
