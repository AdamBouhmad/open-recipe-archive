## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of mayonnaise sauce prepared with mustard
- 1/2 tablespoonful of capers
- 1/2 tablespoonful of olives
- 1/2 tablespoonful of pickles
- 1/2 tablespoonful of minced parsley

## Directions

1. Mix mayonnaise sauce prepared with mustard with capers, olives, pickles and minced parsley
2. The sauce should be made just before serving
3. If served hot, mix with 1 cupful of white sauce and keep on the fire until thoroughly hot
