## Ingredients

- 1 pound of macaroni
- 1 1/2 quarts of water
- salt
- pepper
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3-4 tablespoonfuls of cheese crumbs
- milk, tomato or Béchamel sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the macaroni in pieces 2 inches long.
2. Rinse and put to cook in boiling water with salt and a few grains of pepper.
3. Let boil 20 minutes or until soft.
4. Drain off the water carefully and mix the macaroni with 2 tablespoonfuls of butter.
5. Arrange on a dish and sprinkle with 3-4 tablespoonfuls of cheese crumbs.
6. May be served with milk, tomato or Béchamel sauce.
