## Ingredients

- mutton
- onion
- carrots
- salt
- pepper
- 1 cup water
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 tablespoon tomato
- mustard

## Directions

1. Cut the bone short and wipe the meat with a cloth wrung out of hot water.
2. Place in a dripping pan, skin-side down.
3. Lay onion and carrots around.
4. Roast in a hot oven until brown.
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
6. Pour 1 cupful of hot water into the pan.
7. Lower the heat and let roast slowly, basting often.
8. Allow 10 minutes to a pound.
9. Remove some of the fat.
10. Mix the drippings with 3 tablespoons of flour.
11. Add boiling water until the gravy becomes moderately thick.
12. Flavor with a tablespoon of tomato and some mustard.
13. Strain.
