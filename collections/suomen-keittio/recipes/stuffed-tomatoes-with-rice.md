## Ingredients

- 6 tomatoes
- 2 ounces of bread
- 1/2 cupful of rich milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- chopped parsley
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the tops of 6 tomatoes
2. Scoop out the insides
3. Soak 2 ounces of bread in 1/2 cupful of rich milk
4. Mix with the tomatoes
5. Add yolks of 2 eggs
6. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
7. Add salt, pepper, onion juice, chopped parsley and a little sugar
8. Add hard-beaten whites of eggs at the last
9. Sprinkle the insides of the tomatoes with salt and stuff
10. Place in a buttered pan
11. Bake, covered, 30-40 minutes
