## Ingredients

- 1 cup prunes
- 1/2 package gelatine, dissolved
- 1 cup sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- some sherry or orange juice
- chopped almonds (optional)

## Directions

1. Soak 1 cup prunes in cold water.
2. Cook until tender.
3. Drain, chop or grind.
4. Add to the liquid of the prunes, 1/2 package of dissolved gelatine, 1 cup sugar, the juice of 1 lemon, some sherry or orange juice.
5. Stir over the fire until the gelatine is dissolved.
6. Strain; add the prunes.
7. Let set, stirring now and then until it begins to thicken, so the fruit does not settle to the bottom.
8. Chopped almonds will greatly improve the taste.
9. Serve with cream.
