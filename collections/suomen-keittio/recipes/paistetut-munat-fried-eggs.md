## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Melt some butter in a hot pan.
2. Break the eggs into the pan one at a time.
3. Sprinkle with salt and fry until the whites are firm.
4. If preferred, fry the eggs on both sides.
5. Serve plain or on slices of tomato cut crosswise, rolled in bread crumbs and fried in butter.
