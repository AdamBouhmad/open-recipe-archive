## Ingredients

- 6 green peppers
- 2 tablespoons rice
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoon chopped mushrooms
- 1 tablespoon chopped green pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons tomato
- Pepper
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut a piece off the top of the pepper.
2. Scoop carefully to remove seeds.
3. Place in cold water for 1/2 hour, or boil in hot fat for a few minutes.
4. Cook the rice until tender.
5. Mix in the butter, onion, mushrooms, chopped green pepper, salt, tomato, and pepper.
6. Fill the peppers with the mixture.
7. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top.
8. Bake in a buttered pan, basting occasionally or cover with a lid.
