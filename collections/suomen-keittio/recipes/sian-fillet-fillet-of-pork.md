## Ingredients

- Pork fillet
- Butter
- Onion
- Salt
- Pepper
- Water or cream

## Directions

1. Place the fillet in a pan, fry in butter with an onion.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Fry until brown.
4. Add some water or cream to the drippings, pour over the meat.
5. The fillet may be cut in pieces 2 inches thick, pound and fry.
