## Ingredients

- 2 cups mashed potato
- 1 1/2 cups mashed chestnuts
- 2 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 3 eggs
- 4 tablespoons butter
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Mix together all the ingredients
2. If too thick, dilute with cream or milk
3. Form into patties
4. Fry in butter, or dip in egg and turn in bread crumbs and cook in lard
