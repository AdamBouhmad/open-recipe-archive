## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of milk
- 1/2 cupful of farina
- 3 eggs
- 3 ruokalus. of sugar
- raisins

## Directions

1. Cook milk and farina to a porridge, add egg yolks beaten with sugar, a little salt and raisins.
2. Put in a buttered mold.
3. Lastly place stiffly beaten whites with a little sugar mixed in on top of the mold.
4. Bake in oven until brown.
