## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2/3 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 yeast cake
- 1/3 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- flour

## Directions

1. Warm the milk mixed with salt and sugar
2. Add yeast and 1 1/2 cups flour, cover and let rise
3. Work in the eggs beaten well, melted butter and flour enough to make a moderately thick dough
4. Let rise again
5. Form into rolls and place on a buttered baking sheet to rise
6. Bake in a hot oven
