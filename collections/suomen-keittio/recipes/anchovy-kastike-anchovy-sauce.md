## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon anchovy paste
- ½ lemon juice
- 1 egg yolk
- cream
- salt and pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Make a gravy of butter, flour and milk.
2. Add anchovy paste and lemon juice.
3. Mix in egg yolk added to some cream.
4. Stir over the fire until hot.
5. Season with salt and pepper and add a sprinkling of parsley.
6. If used with fish, prepare with fish stock; if with meat, use meat stock and milk; if with eggs, use milk.
