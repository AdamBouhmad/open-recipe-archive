## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of cracker crumbs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 small onion chopped
- chopped parsley
- pickles
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the onion in butter in a frying pan.
2. Add to it the cracker crumbs soaked in a small quantity of water or fish stock, parsley, salt and pepper.
3. Fry for a little while on a slow fire.
