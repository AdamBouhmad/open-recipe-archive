## Ingredients

- small molds with a hole in center
- red pepper
- string-beans
- fish jelly
- caviare
- round thin toast
- minced lettuce

## Directions

1. Garnish small molds, having a hole in center, with red pepper and string-beans.
2. Fill with fish jelly.
3. Lay caviare between round thin toast, turn the fish jelly on them and fill the hole with caviare.
4. Serve on minced lettuce before soup.
