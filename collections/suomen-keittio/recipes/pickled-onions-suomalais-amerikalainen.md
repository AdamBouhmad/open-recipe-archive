## Ingredients

- 1 quart of small white onions
- 1 quart of vinegar
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of white pepper
- mace, a little
- cloves, a little
- ginger, a little
- a handful of salt to a quart of boiling water

## Directions

1. Wash onions, pour boiling water on and peel when water gets cool
2. Prepare a brine allowing a handful of salt to a quart of boiling water
3. Cover the onions with the brine
4. Let stand for 24 hours
5. Boil 10 minutes vinegar, sugar and spices, wrapped in a linen cloth
6. Remove the spices, place the onions in and let cook nearly tender
7. Put the onions in jar, drain the vinegar on, cork and keep in a cool place
