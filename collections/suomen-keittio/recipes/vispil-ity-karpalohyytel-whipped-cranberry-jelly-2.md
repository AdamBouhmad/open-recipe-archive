## Ingredients

- 1½ cupfuls of cranberry liquor without sugar
- 2½ cupfuls of water
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 8 leaves of gelatine

## Directions

1. Cook cranberry liquor, water, sugar and gelatine together.
2. Pour in a bowl stirring well until it gets cold, light and firm.
3. Turn in a serving dish and serve in small glasses.
