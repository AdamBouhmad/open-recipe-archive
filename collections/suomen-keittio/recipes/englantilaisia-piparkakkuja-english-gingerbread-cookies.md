## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cupfuls of molasses
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 7 ozs. of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of ginger
- 1/2 grated lemon rind
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of thick cream
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 3 1/2 lbs. of flour

## Directions

1. Work molasses, butter and spices together to a smooth paste.
2. Whip cream to a froth and mix with the above paste.
3. Sift flour with soda and stir into the molasses paste.
4. Let stand over night.
5. Roll out and shape into small round cookies.
6. Wet with cold water and bake to a delicate brown.
