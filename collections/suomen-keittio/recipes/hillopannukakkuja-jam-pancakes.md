## Ingredients

- batter as for sweet pancakes
- jam
- cream
- sugar
- vanilla
- preserved fruit

## Directions

1. Prepare the batter as directed for sweet pancakes
2. Fry thin large cakes
3. Pile on a serving platter having jam between the cakes
4. Whip cream, add sugar and vanilla
5. Cover the top of the pile with the cream
6. Garnish around with preserved fruit
7. Serve cold
