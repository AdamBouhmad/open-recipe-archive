## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut with knife a cross or a little hole in the top
2. Put the chestnuts with salt in a saucepan on a quick fire to cook without water
3. Stir carefully
4. After 30 minutes boiling they are easy to skin
5. May be served unsinned on a folded napkin with cold butter
6. Alternatively bake the chestnuts in oven covered with a thick layer of salt on them
7. In moderate oven they will be done in 30 minutes
8. Or boil the chestnuts in salted water, covered, 30 minutes to remove the skins easily
