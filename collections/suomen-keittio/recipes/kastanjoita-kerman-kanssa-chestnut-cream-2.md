## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- sugar
- vanilla
- salt
- cream
- whipped cream
- preserved apples

## Directions

1. Cook chestnuts, shell and rub through a sieve
2. Add sugar, vanilla and salt, dilute with cream
3. Heat the mixture
4. Arrange on a platter in form of a ring or a pyramid
5. Let cool
6. Serve garnished with whipped cream and preserved apples
