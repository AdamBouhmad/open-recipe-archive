## Ingredients

- Sweetbreads
- Melted butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fried mushrooms

## Directions

1. Prepare the sweetbreads as directed.
2. Wipe, dip in melted butter and broil over a clear fire turning often so as not to scorch them.
3. When done, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Baste with melted butter.
5. Line with fried mushrooms.
