## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 3/4 cup sugar
- Grated rind of 1 orange
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 1/4 package of gelatine
- 2 cups cream

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks with the sugar in a saucepan
2. Add all the ingredients except the cream
3. Place on the fire and beat until thick
4. Let cool beating continuously
5. Add the whipped cream
6. Put into a mold to set
7. Serve with fruit sauce or cream
