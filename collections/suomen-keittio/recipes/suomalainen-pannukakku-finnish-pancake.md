## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1½ cups milk
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup flour
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar, add salt, melted butter, milk and flour.
2. Bake in buttered pan about an hour.
3. The pancake will be lighter if the batter is left to stand for a while after the flour has been mixed in milk.
4. Add yolks beaten with sugar.
5. Last fold in the egg whites beaten until firm.
6. May be fried on the stove.
7. The batter may be mixed with very thin slices of apples.
