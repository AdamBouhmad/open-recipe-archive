## Ingredients

- peas
- salt
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- butter
- pepper
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Put the peas on the fire in boiling water with a dash of salt and 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2. Cook until tender but not mushy
3. Drain the water
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Add butter and some sugar if desired
6. When using canned peas rinse them well in cold water before cooking
