## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. of green tomatoes
- 5 lbs. of sugar
- 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Wash tomatoes and put in salted water for three or four days.
2. Rinse in cold water and let stand in cold water until next day.
3. Cook in a small quantity of water until stewed, force through a sieve.
4. Place to simmer with lemon juice and sugar.
5. Cook until of firm consistency skimming carefully.
6. Pour into jar and seal when cooled.
