## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of milk or cream
- a little salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- brandy or rum

## Directions

1. Whip eggs, add milk, salt and sugar.
2. Put hot butter in a pan, pour the mixture in and stir on heat until it sets but remains soft.
3. Push quickly to the edge of the pan with a knife, let the bottom brown slightly and turn onto a serving dish.
4. Put fine sugar on top and pour a few tablespoonfuls of brandy or rum on the serving dish and set on fire.
