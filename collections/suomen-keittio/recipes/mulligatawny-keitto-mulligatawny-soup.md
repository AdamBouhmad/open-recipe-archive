## Ingredients

- 3 quarts vegetable stock
- 4 large onions
- 2 stalks celery
- 2 carrots
- 2 sour apples
- 1/2 cup tomato
- Some parsley
- Butter and flour for thickening

## Directions

1. Peel and cut in pieces the onions, the carrots, and the apples.
2. Brown with a little lump of butter.
3. Add the vegetable stock and the parsley.
4. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
5. Press through a sieve.
6. Thicken with butter and flour, or serve with boiled rice.
