## Ingredients

- rice
- tomato juice
- salt
- sugar
- pepper
- butter
- onion juice
- egg yolks
- cheese crumbs
- egg
- bread crumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. When rice is half-done, drain off the water
2. Add tomato juice, salt, sugar and pepper to season
3. Cook 10 minutes more, or until rice is tender
4. Remove from fire and add butter, onion juice, egg yolks and cheese crumbs
5. Let cool completely
6. Form into croquettes
7. Dip in egg and bread crumbs
8. Fry in lard
