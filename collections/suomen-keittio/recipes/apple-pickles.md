## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of small apples
- 3 ozs. of sugar
- 5 cloves
- 1 1/4 cupfuls of water
- 3/4 cupful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe apples carefully.
2. Boil up sugar, water and vinegar.
3. Place the apples in and let simmer until half cooked.
4. Place apples in a glass jar and put the cloves on.
5. Cook the liquor for a little while and pour it on the apples.
6. Seal the jar and keep in a cool dry place.
7. Pears and peaches may be pickled in the same way.
