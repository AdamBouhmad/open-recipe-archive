## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- Yellow rind and juice of 1 lemon
- 1/2 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar together until creamy.
2. Beat the yolks until frothy.
3. Add cream of tartar and beat until stiff froth.
4. Combine this well with the butter mixture.
5. Add salt, lemon rind, juice, milk and flour sifted with soda gradually.
6. Last of all fold in the egg whites beaten until firm.
7. Bake for 1 hour.
