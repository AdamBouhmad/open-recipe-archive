## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 lbs. of mushrooms
- 10 ozs. of bread crumbs
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 2 cupfuls of rich milk
- 2 small onions
- 3 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Clean mushrooms and grind in meat mill
2. Chop onions and fry in butter
3. Add fried onions to mushrooms
4. Add bread crumbs soaked in milk
5. Add seasonings and beaten eggs
6. Rub mixture thoroughly together
7. Bake in a buttered mold 1 hour
8. Serve with vegetables and sauce made of melted butter or vegetable stock
