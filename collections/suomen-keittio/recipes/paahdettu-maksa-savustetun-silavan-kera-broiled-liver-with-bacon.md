## Ingredients

- Liver
- Hot water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bacon
- Butter or lard (optional)

## Directions

1. Slice the liver 1/2 inch thick.
2. Pour hot water over and let stand 5 minutes, so the blood water runs off.
3. Wipe the liver slices and remove the skin from the surface.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
5. Cut bacon slices of the same size as the liver slices and fasten with a skewer on top of the liver.
6. Broil over bright fire; put in the oven for a few minutes.
7. Dish.
8. If bacon is not used, the liver slices may be dipped in butter or lard before broiling.
