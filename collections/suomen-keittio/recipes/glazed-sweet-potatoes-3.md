## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- sugar
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes, peel and cut lengthwise into halves.
2. Put 2 tablespoonfuls of butter in a frying pan.
3. When the butter is boiling put the potatoes into the pan.
4. Sprinkle with 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar.
5. Fry until brown.
6. Season with lemon juice or vinegar if required.
