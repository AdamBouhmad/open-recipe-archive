## Ingredients

- veal roast
- butter or fat
- salt
- pepper
- carrots
- celery
- onion
- peppercorns
- boiling water
- flour
- cream

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat.
2. Heat butter or fat in a dripping-pan, place the meat into the pan skin side down.
3. Put in a hot oven.
4. When brown sprinkle with salt and pepper and baste with the drippings.
5. Add carrots, celery, onion and a few peppercorns.
6. When half done, turn the roast over, sprinkle with salt, add boiling water to the drippings.
7. Roast about 2 hours basting often.
8. Make a gravy of the drippings by adding some flour and cream.
