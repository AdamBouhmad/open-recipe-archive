## Ingredients

- equal parts of dates
- plums
- figs
- raisins
- chopped walnuts
- lemon juice
- sugar
- water
- brown bread

## Directions

1. Stone and chop all dried fruit
2. Mix with chopped walnuts and lemon juice
3. Serve with brown bread
4. Alternatively, cook chopped fruit with sugar in small quantity of water into a thick marmalade, boiling slowly to prevent it from burning
5. Use approximately 1 pound of fruit to 1 cup of water and 1 cup of sugar
