## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup rice
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 hard-cooked eggs, minced finely
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 8 whole cooked prunes

## Directions

1. Cook rice in salted water
2. Strain off the broth
3. Mix melted butter, chopped eggs, prunes and salt with the rice
4. Use this mixture to stuff the fish
