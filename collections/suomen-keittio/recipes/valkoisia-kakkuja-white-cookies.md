## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 3/4 cupful of cream
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 1/4 lb. of melted butter
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice
- a little cinnamon and cardamom
- 1/2 lb. of corn starch
- flour

## Directions

1. Whip eggs and sugar to a froth
2. Mix in cream, cream of tartar, butter, spices, and corn starch
3. Add flour enough to make a dough tolerable to handle
4. Roll out and shape into cakes with a form
5. Bake in a slow oven
