## Ingredients

- 5 pounds pike
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- crackerdust
- 2 egg yolks
- cream
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Scale, clean and rinse the pike and remove the backbone
2. Rub with salt and pepper
3. Moisten with lemon juice and let rest for a while
4. Dry the fish
5. Brush with butter
6. Sprinkle crackerdust on top
7. Fry in a buttered pan in the oven until light brown
8. Place the fish on a serving platter
9. Make sauce from the drippings in the pan by adding butter, flour, fish stock, lemon juice, egg yolks and cream
10. Fry mushrooms in butter in a pan and place on top of the fish or dice them and mix into the sauce
11. Pour the sauce around the fish
