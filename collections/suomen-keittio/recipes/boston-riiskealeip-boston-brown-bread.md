## Ingredients

- 2 cups rye flour
- 2 cups granulated cornmeal
- 2 cups graham flour
- 1 1/2 cups molasses
- 2 1/2 cups water or milk, fresh or sour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons soda
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt

## Directions

1. Mix together all the dry ingredients, add molasses and milk.
2. Pour the mixture into a buttered tin with cover.
3. Fill about 3/4 of the tin's height.
4. Steam for 3 hours in a water bath, keeping the water level about halfway up the tin.
5. May be baked also and instead of soda yeast may be used.
