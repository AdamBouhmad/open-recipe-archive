## Ingredients

- cold potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut cold potatoes into small cubes.
2. Fry in butter until thoroughly heated, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Brown underneath, double on a dish like omelet.
