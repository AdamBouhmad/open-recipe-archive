## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- water
- 1 cupful of mushrooms
- 1 cupful of chicken mousse
- sherry wine
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare mushrooms
2. Place on heat in a little boiling water
3. Let simmer 10 minutes
4. Press through a sieve
5. Mix 1 cupful of strained mushrooms, 1 cupful of chicken mousse, a little sherry wine, salt and pepper
6. Butter small timbale molds, decorate and fill with this mixture
7. Bake in oven in a water bath with buttered paper on top for about 15 minutes, or until firm
8. Serve with mushroom sauce
