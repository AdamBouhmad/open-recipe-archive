## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- Some salt

## Directions

1. Rub butter with sugar, add eggs beaten well.
2. Add milk, baking powder mixed with flour, ginger and some salt.
3. Bake in individual tins.
