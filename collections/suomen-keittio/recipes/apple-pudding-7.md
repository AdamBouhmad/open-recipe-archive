## Ingredients

- 6 large sour apples
- 1/2 quart of water
- 6 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 3/4 quart of bread crumbs
- 1/2 cupful of almonds
- 2 eggs
- yolks of 2 eggs
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Pare and core apples, cut in slices and cook in water with sugar and vanilla to a sauce.
2. Let cool.
3. Beat yolks until light and whites until stiff and dry.
4. Blanch and chop almonds finely.
5. Mix bread crumbs, almonds, yolks and lastly whites with apple sauce.
6. Pour the mixture in a buttered mold, sprinkled with bread; bake in oven about 3/4 hour.
7. Let cool.
8. Turn out of the mold, cover with whipped cream.
