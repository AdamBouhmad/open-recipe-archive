## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 1 yeast cake
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 teaspoonful of grated lemon rind
- a dash of nutmeg
- 1/2 cupful of currants, washed and dried

## Directions

1. Heat milk and mix in yeast, salt and half a cupful of flour.
2. Let rise.
3. Beat eggs with sugar and add to the mixture.
4. Add creamed butter, seasonings, currants and remaining flour.
5. Let rise.
6. Drop by spoonful into hot fat and cook through until golden brown.
7. Place on paper to drain.
8. Sprinkle with fine sugar.
