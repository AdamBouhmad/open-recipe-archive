## Ingredients

- 1 egg white
- 1 cup powdered sugar, finely sifted
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice or other flavoring

## Directions

1. Put one egg white in a bowl, rub into it 1 cup finely powdered sugar, added gradually.
2. Then work in 1 tablespoon lemon juice or other flavoring and beat until the mixture is smooth and of an even consistency.
3. When the cake is still somewhat warm, put the icing in the center of the cake and smooth it with a wet knife over the top and sides of the cake.
4. If the icing is prepared before the cake is finished, it should be covered with a damp cloth, otherwise it will harden.
5. If less water and more sugar are used in the mixture, the icing will be smoother.
