## Ingredients

- fish
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- crackerdust
- onion
- cream

## Directions

1. Scale the fish and remove the backbone
2. Scrape the flesh from the skin and remove all small bones
3. Grind the flesh through a meat grinder several times
4. Mix butter pieces into the flesh while grinding
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Form into round, flat cakes
7. Dip in egg and roll in crackerdust
8. Fry in butter or lard
9. Place the fish cakes on a serving dish
10. Line with fried slices of onion
11. Add a little cream to the drippings
12. Pour the sauce over the fish
