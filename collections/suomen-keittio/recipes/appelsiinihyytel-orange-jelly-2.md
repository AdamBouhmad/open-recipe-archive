## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- 3 cupfuls of water
- 1 lemon
- 6 ozs. of sugar
- 2 ozs. of gelatine
- few drops of carmine

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same way as lemon jelly without whites of eggs.
