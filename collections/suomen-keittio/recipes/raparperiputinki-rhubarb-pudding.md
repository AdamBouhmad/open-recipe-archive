## Ingredients

- Bread soaked in milk
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- Stewed rhubarb
- Bread crumbs
- Butter for dotting

## Directions

1. Have bread soaked in milk, mix with the juice of 1 lemon, 3/4 cup butter and 1/2 cup sugar; drain through a sieve.
2. Lay alternately bread and stewed rhubarb into a mold.
3. Sprinkle with bread crumbs, dot with butter.
4. Bake for 40 minutes covered with buttered paper.
5. When nearly done remove the paper so as to brown the surface.
6. Serve with hard sauce.
