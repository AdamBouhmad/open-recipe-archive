## Ingredients

- chicken and veal, ground several times
- salt
- pepper
- finely chopped parsley
- bread crumbs
- milk
- 1 egg
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove the bones carefully, leaving only one inch of bone in the legs.
2. Fill the space with stuffing made of chicken and veal ground several times.
3. Season with salt, pepper and finely chopped parsley.
4. Soak a little bread crumbs in milk, mix with 1 egg and a bit butter.
5. Combine the mixtures rubbing well.
6. Put a spoonful of mixture into each leg, truss and cook in water or chicken stock mixed with salt, celery, onion and parsley about 1 hour.
7. Remove from the pan, keep under a weight until cool.
8. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dip in egg and bread crumbs, fry in butter or in lard until brown.
9. Arrange on a platter same as chops.
10. Serve with Bearnaise or Tartar Sauce.
