## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of corn
- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. Butter an individual mold
2. Mix corn with milk, eggs, butter, salt and sugar
3. Pour the mixture into the mold
4. Bake in a moderate oven until thick
5. Serve as a vegetable or dessert
