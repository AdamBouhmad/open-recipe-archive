## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of nuts
- 1 lb. of potatoes
- 3/4 lb. of bread crumbs
- 2 ozs. of corn starch
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of milk
- 3 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes and mash.
2. Grind shelled nuts.
3. Combine both the mixtures.
4. Add bread crumbs soaked in milk, whipped eggs, corn starch and seasonings.
5. Put in a buttered mold and bake in a pan of water.
