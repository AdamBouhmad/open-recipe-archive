## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of butter
- 1 cupful of brown sugar
- 3/4 cupful of molasses
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 cupful of milk
- 2 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 1/2 cupful of seeded raisins, cut in pieces
- 1/2 cupful of currants
- 3/4 cupful of chopped lemon rind
- 1/2 teaspoonful of soda
- 1/4 cupful of chopped almonds
- 1/2 cupful of chopped walnuts
- 1/4 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoonful of nutmeg
- 1/2 teaspoonful of mace
- 1/8 teaspoonful of cloves
- corn starch

## Directions

1. Work butter and sugar to a froth, add molasses rubbing a little while.
2. Mix with well-beaten yolks of eggs, milk, spices, fruit, dipped in flour, soda and cream of tartar, mixed and sifted with flour.
3. Lastly whites of eggs, whipped to a froth.
4. Bake in a deep mold 1 1/4 hours.
