## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- pork
- vegetables
- meat stock
- tomato or allemande sauce
- rice
- artichokes

## Directions

1. Cook sweetbreads and put under weight for a short time
2. Cut in half lengthwise
3. Place a small slice of pork on each piece
4. Mince some vegetables and place in a pan
5. Pour meat stock over to cover the bottom
6. Let braise slowly in the oven, basting often, about 15 minutes
7. Pour tomato or allemande sauce over the top
8. Arrange on a rice base with boiled artichokes
