## Ingredients

- 1 quart of strawberries
- 5 yolks of eggs
- 1/4 pound of sugar
- 1 cupful of bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Mash 1 quart of strawberries
2. Beat 5 yolks of eggs with sugar
3. mix with the mashed strawberries
4. Add 1 cupful of bread crumbs
5. At the last mix with the beaten whites of eggs
6. Bake in an individual mold
7. Serve cold with cream
