## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 quart water
- peaches

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same way as preserved apples.
2. Skin peaches putting them first quickly in boiling water.
3. Cut them into pieces lengthwise or cook whole.
4. Boil peaches rapidly in syrup before you place them into jars.
5. All kinds of hard fruit are quickly cooked in boiling water before placing them into jars.
