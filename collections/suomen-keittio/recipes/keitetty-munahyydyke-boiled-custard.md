## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- Salt

## Directions

1. Heat the milk and bring to a boil
2. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar
3. Pour gradually the boiling milk into the yolks stirring well
4. Stir over the fire in a pan of water until thick
5. Season and let cool
6. Two egg-whites beaten until firm may be added shortly before serving if desired
