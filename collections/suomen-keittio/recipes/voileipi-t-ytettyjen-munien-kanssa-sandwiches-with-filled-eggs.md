## Ingredients

- eggs
- anchovy paste
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- toasted bread slices
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Cut a slice off both ends of the eggs.
2. Cut the eggs in two, take the yolks.
3. Mix yolks with anchovy paste and butter, season with salt and pepper.
4. Fill the whites to a heap with this mixture.
5. Sprinkle with parsley.
6. Place on toast.
7. Serve having mayonnaise around, if desired.
