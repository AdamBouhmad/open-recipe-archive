## Ingredients

- baked sweet potatoes
- 3/4 cupful of syrup
- 1 tablespoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Take baked sweet potatoes, split in halves lengthwise.
2. Scoop out the insides and mash.
3. Return the mash into the empty potato skins.
4. Cook 3/4 cupful of syrup with 1 tablespoonful of butter.
5. Spread the tops with the syrup and bake in oven until brown.
