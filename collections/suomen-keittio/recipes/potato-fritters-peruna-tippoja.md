## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of mashed potatoes
- 1 tablespoonful of cream
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- nutmeg and pepper
- 1 egg yolk and 2 egg whites
- lard

## Directions

1. Mix the potatoes with cream, salt, nutmeg and pepper.
2. Add the egg whites last.
3. Drop with spoon into boiling lard.
4. Cook until brown.
5. Fritters of sweet potatoes may be made in the same way.
