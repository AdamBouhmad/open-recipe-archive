## Ingredients

- 2 cups corn canned or fresh corn grated
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups milk
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 3 eggs beaten

## Directions

1. Mix 2 cups of corn canned or fresh corn grated, 1 tablespoon butter, 2 tablespoons flour, 2 cups milk, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1 tablespoon sugar, and 3 eggs beaten in order given.
2. Bake in oven in a pan of water.
3. The flour can be left out of the mixture and less milk used, if less eggs are used.
