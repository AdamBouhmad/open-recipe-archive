## Ingredients

- 3 fresh herrings
- 6 anchovies
- 4 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons milk
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Split the herrings in back, bone.
2. Rinse the fish, wipe, rub with salt.
3. Bone the anchovies and chop.
4. Soak the bread crumbs in milk, mix with the anchovies, season with salt and pepper, flavor with onion juice and chopped parsley.
5. Stuff the herrings with the mixture.
6. Place in a buttered pan, bake for half hour.
7. Serve with currant or any tart sauce.
