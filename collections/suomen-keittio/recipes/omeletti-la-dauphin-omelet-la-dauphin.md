## Ingredients

- Apples or other fruit
- Sugar
- A little butter
- Whipped cream

## Directions

1. Cook apples or any other kind of fruit with sugar and a little lump of butter.
2. Prepare an omelet as directed.
3. Fold the cooked fruit into the omelet.
4. Garnish with whipped cream.
