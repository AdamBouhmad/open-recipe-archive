## Ingredients

- yolks of 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 2 cupfuls of cream
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat yolks, cream, sugar and vanilla over the fire until thick
2. Mix with small dots of butter
3. Let cool
