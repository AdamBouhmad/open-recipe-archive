## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound of chicken or veal
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons milk or bouillon
- 1/2 onion
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Grind the meat finely.
2. Cook the chopped onion in butter.
3. Add eggs, bread crumbs soaked in bouillon, salt and pepper.
4. Knead thoroughly.
5. Form into small balls and roll in flour.
6. Boil in salt water.
