## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean beef
- 1 pound veal
- lard
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- 1 cup strained tomato
- small potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut beef and veal into 1 inch thick pieces
2. Fry in lard with onion, season with salt and pepper
3. When brown, add a cupful of strained tomato
4. Cook slowly covered
5. Add small potatoes to the stew
6. Serve when done
