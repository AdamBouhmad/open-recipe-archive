## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 10 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of almonds, blanched and chopped
- 2 lbs. of currants
- 2 lbs. of seeded and chopped raisins
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoonful of mace
- 1/2 teaspoonful of nutmeg
- 1/2 cupful of brandy
- 1 teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar to a cream, mix with yolks of eggs, spices, fruit, dredged with flour, soda and flour.
2. Then whites of eggs, beaten until stiff.
3. Put in a buttered mold and bake in a moderate oven 1 1/2 — 2 hours.
4. If it begins to get too brown, cover with paper.
