## Ingredients

- 1 cup shelled walnuts
- 2 cups bread crumbs
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 2.5 cup milk or tomato liquor
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Grind the nuts.
2. Mix bread crumbs soaked in milk with the ground nuts.
3. Add melted butter rubbed until creamy.
4. Add beaten egg yolks.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Mix in the beaten egg whites last of all.
7. Pour the mixture into a buttered mold.
8. Bake for 1 hour.
