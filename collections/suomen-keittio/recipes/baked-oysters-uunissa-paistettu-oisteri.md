## Ingredients

- oyster shells
- oysters
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the shells carefully changing the water several times
2. Keep in oven until the shells open
3. Remove carefully the upper shells
4. Put three oysters in each deep half of the shells
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
6. Put a little dot of butter on each
7. Dredge with bread crumbs
8. Bake in oven until bread crumbs are brown
