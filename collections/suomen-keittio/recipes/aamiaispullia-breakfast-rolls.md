## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 yeast cake
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 1/2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Warm the milk mixed with sugar and salt
2. Add the yeast and a part of the flour and let rise
3. Mix with the melted butter and the rest of the flour, kneading well
4. Form into rolls and let rise
5. Brush with egg and bake for 15-20 minutes
