## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- slices of pork or butter
- melted butter
- water

## Directions

1. Clean and stuff the chicken with one of the provided stuffings
2. Tie with cord and place in a buttered pan, lined with slices of pork or dotted with butter
3. Put in hot oven and baste often with melted butter and water
4. Roast for 1½ hours until done
5. Prepare sauce from the liquor of the chicken by adding a little cream
