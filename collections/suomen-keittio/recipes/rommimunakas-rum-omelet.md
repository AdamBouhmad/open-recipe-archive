## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons milk or cream
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- Some salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- Fine sugar for sprinkling
- A few tablespoons rum or brandy

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs, add milk, salt and sugar.
2. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a hot pan, add the mixture, shake the pan over the fire until the mixture is firm but not hard.
3. Heap quickly with a knife on one side of the pan, brown underneath and turn onto a platter.
4. Sprinkle with sugar, pour a few spoonfuls of rum or brandy around on the platter.
5. Light the brandy and serve immediately.
