## Ingredients

- 6 large roots of celery
- 1/4 lb. of veal
- 3/8 lb. of beef
- 2 ozs. of suet
- 1 tablespoonful of bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped onion
- salt and pepper
- salted water
- butter
- beef stock
- cream

## Directions

1. Scrape celery and wash well; cook in salted water until half done.
2. Drain, remove tops nicely and scoop out the insides.
3. Stuff the celery with mixture made of the described ingredients as usually.
4. Replace the tops.
5. Butter a baking pan, and bake the celery slowly in it, covered, basting often with beef stock until the celery is soft and the stuffing done.
6. Place in a dish, prepare the sauce of the liquor in the pan by adding a little cream.
