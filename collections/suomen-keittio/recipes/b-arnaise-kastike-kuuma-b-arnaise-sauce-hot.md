## Ingredients

- 2 yolks of egg
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice or vinegar
- 1 tablespoon hot water
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of egg
2. Add olive oil gradually, then water and lemon juice
3. Place the bowl in a pot of boiling water and whisk until thickened
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Serve with all kinds of fish, cutlets and roasted meat
