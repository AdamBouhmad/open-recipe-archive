## Ingredients

- celery roots
- salted water
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs
- butter
- lard
- melted butter
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cook roots of celery in salted water until tender.
2. Cut in thin slices, dip in beaten egg and bread crumbs.
3. Fry with butter in a frying pan or cook in lard.
4. Pour melted butter over and sprinkle with chopped parsley.
