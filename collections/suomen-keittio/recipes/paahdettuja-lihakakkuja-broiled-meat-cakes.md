## Ingredients

- 3 pounds ground meat
- 3 tablespoons butter
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Mix the meat with butter rubbed to a cream and season.
2. Stir until smooth.
3. Form into round, thin cakes.
4. Broil or fry in a pan.
5. If desired, mix in two whites of eggs to make the cakes fluffy.
6. Serve with a brown gravy having sliced onions fried in butter on top of the cakes, or may be served with parsley butter.
