## Ingredients

- 1 cupful rice
- 3 cupfuls water
- 1 pint milk
- 1 pound carrots
- 1 tablespoonful almonds
- 1 dessertspoonful salt
- 1/2 tablespoonful sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter

## Directions

1. Cook rice, water and milk until thick.
2. Peel, wash and cook carrots until nearly tender.
3. Grate the carrots and almonds together.
4. Mix the grated carrots and almonds with sugar and salt into the rice.
5. Whip the egg and stir into the mixture.
6. Pour into a buttered mould and bake in the oven for about 1 hour.
