## Ingredients

- 1 quart cream
- 4 egg yolks
- ½ pound sugar
- ½ pound almonds
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Blanch and grate or pound the almonds.
2. Cook in 1 pint of cream.
3. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar, add gradually the scalded cream, beat in a double boiler over the fire until thick.
4. When cool add the remaining cream and freeze.
5. The almonds may be browned with sugar in a frying-pan and chopped, if brown colored ice cream is preferred.
