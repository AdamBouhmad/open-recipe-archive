## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- salt
- water
- cream or milk
- pepper

## Directions

1. Skin the chestnuts
2. Cook in salted water until tender
3. Drain
4. Chop
5. Moisten with cream or milk
6. Season with salt and pepper
7. Stir until smooth
