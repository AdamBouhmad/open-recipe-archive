## Ingredients

- Slices of smoked ham
- Butter or olive oil

## Directions

1. Place ham slices on the fire in cold water.
2. Cook slowly until almost done.
3. Remove from the water and wipe dry.
4. Spread with butter or olive oil.
5. Broil until done.
