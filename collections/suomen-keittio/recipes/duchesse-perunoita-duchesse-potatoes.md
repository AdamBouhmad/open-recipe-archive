## Ingredients

- mashed potatoes
- butter
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Prepare as directed for potato croquettes.
2. Force through a pastry bag forming roses, leaves, pyramids or baskets into a buttered pan.
3. Brush with egg and bake until light brown.
