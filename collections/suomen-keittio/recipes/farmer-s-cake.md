## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1/4 lb. of melted butter
- 4 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 1 cupful of currants
- 1 teaspoonful of cardamom
- 1/2 lb. of flour

## Directions

1. Whip yolks of eggs with sugar to a froth
2. Mix with melted butter, cream, flavoring and flour
3. Add last beaten whites
4. Bake in a buttered mold
