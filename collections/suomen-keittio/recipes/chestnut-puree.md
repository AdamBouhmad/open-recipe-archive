## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of chestnuts
- 3 quarts of vegetable stock
- 1 leek
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 cupful of cream

## Directions

1. Soak the chestnuts in boiling water.
2. Remove the shells and cook in vegetable stock until tender.
3. Cook the onion in butter, add the flour and put it into the soup.
4. Strain and lastly add the yolks of eggs and cream.
5. Mushrooms cut in pieces and cooked in a small quantity of water for 10 minutes, may be served in the soup.
