## Ingredients

- salt fish or fresh fish
- water
- mashed potatoes
- butter
- eggs
- flour
- bread crumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. If very salty, soak the fish in water.
2. Boil, let cool.
3. Mix equal parts of fish and mashed potatoes.
4. Add two tablespoons of butter and 1 or 2 eggs, rubbing well.
5. Form into balls or patties.
6. Sprinkle with flour, let stand in a cold place.
7. Fry in butter or lard.
8. If dipped in egg and rolled in bread crumbs, they will look nicer.
9. Potatoes and fish may be boiled together if preferred.
