## Ingredients

- 2 herrings
- 1 cup of finely chopped potatoes
- 2 eggs
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cream

## Directions

1. Soak the herrings, skin and bone.
2. Grind in a meat grinder.
3. Chop the onion and fry in butter.
4. Soak the breadcrumbs in cream, to which add beaten eggs.
5. Mix the fried onion, and egg and bread mixture with the herring.
6. Shape into cakes or patties, dip in egg and breadcrumbs and fry in lard.
7. Serve with a sharp sauce.
