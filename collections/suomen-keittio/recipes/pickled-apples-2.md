## Ingredients

- 8 cups sugar
- 2 cups water
- 4 cups vinegar
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon and cloves
- apples

## Directions

1. Pare the apples, place into a jar alternate layers of apples and sugar
2. Pour water and vinegar over
3. Let stand covered overnight
4. Drain the juice, put into a kettle with the spices tied in a bag
5. When the juice is coming to a boil and looks clear, add the apples
6. Cook until nearly done
7. Place the apples into glass jars, pour the boiling hot juice over
8. Cover airtight
9. Note: Crab apples must not be pared before cooking. Alternatively, boil the sugar in water mixed with vinegar, add the apples and cook
