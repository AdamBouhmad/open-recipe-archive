## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 3 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and sugar to a froth
2. Add melted butter and flour enough to make a moderately thick dough
3. Roll the dough 1/4 inch thick
4. Cut into pieces three inches long and two inches broad
5. Make three one-inch gashes crosswise on one side and twist the ends slightly
6. Cook in fat like doughnuts
