## Ingredients

- 2 pounds carrots
- 2 cupfuls bread crumbs
- 1 1/2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 2 cupfuls cream or milk
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- 3/4 cupful almonds
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Clean and cook carrots until tender in salted water.
2. Mash the cooked carrots.
3. Add all ingredients to the mashed carrots, adding the beaten eggs last.
4. Bake in a slow oven or in a pan of water.
5. Serve in the same dish or turn it out onto a serving dish.
