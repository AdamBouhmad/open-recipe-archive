## Ingredients

- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 whole egg
- 1/2 cupful beef stock or milk
- salt and pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs.
2. Add beef stock and seasonings.
3. Pour in a well buttered mold.
4. Bake in oven in a pan of water until firm.
5. Turn on a dish, cut into nice pieces and serve with meat soups.
