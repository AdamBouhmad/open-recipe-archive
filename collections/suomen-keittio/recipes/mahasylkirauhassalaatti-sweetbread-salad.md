## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- onion
- carrot
- celery
- French dressing
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Boil sweetbreads with onion, carrot and celery.
2. Let cool.
3. Cut in small pieces.
4. Mix with celery.
5. Mix with French dressing.
6. Garnish with mayonnaise.
