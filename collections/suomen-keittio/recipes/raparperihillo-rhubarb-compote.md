## Ingredients

- Rhubarb
- Sugar equal in weight to rhubarb

## Directions

1. Rinse rhubarb, skin and cut in pieces.
2. Weigh.
3. Have rhubarb and sugar in equal parts.
4. Lay alternately in a jar.
5. Let stand until following day.
6. Prepare a thick syrup of the liquor, put the rhubarb into it and simmer slowly until clarified.
7. Pour into a dry jar.
