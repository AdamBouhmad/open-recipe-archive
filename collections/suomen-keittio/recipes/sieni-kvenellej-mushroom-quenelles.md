## Ingredients

- boiled chopped mushrooms
- 2 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of milk or meat stock
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare like meat quenelles, but instead of meat use boiled chopped mushrooms
2. Serve in mushroom and meat soups
