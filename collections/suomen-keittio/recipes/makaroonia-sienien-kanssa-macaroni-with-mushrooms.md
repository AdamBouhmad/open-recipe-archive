## Ingredients

- boiled macaroni
- butter
- mushrooms
- tomato
- cream or milk
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a well buttered mold with a layer of cooked macaroni
2. Add a thick layer of boiled or raw mushrooms
3. Fill the mold with layers of macaroni and mushrooms
4. Have a layer of tomato on top
5. Pour over milk or cream mixed with an egg
6. Bake in a pan of water covered
7. Remove the cover to brown the top of the dish
