## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon gelatine
- cold water
- 1 cup chicken breast
- salt
- pepper
- 2 cups whipped cream

## Directions

1. Beat 2 yolks of egg to a froth, add 1/2 cup milk and 1 tablespoon gelatine dissolved in cold water.
2. Heat, beating well until thick, not allowing to boil.
3. Mix with 1 cup of chicken breast ground or cut in tiny pieces, season with salt and pepper.
4. Last of all add 2 cups of whipped cream.
5. Put into mold to set.
6. Serve with fruit salad. Instead of cream mayonnaise may be used.
