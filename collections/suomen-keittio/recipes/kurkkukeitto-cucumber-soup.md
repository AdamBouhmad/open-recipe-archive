## Ingredients

- 3 large cucumbers
- 1½ quarts white stock
- 1 red onion or a slice of onion
- 2 stalks celery
- 1 sprig parsley
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup cream
- butter
- flour
- salt and pepper
- lemon or Sherry

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, cut in slices, remove the seeds.
2. Boil quickly in butter the onion and the slices of cucumber, add flour and stock.
3. Continue to boil for a few minutes.
4. Force through a sieve and season.
5. Whip the yolks with the cream, add the soup slowly and beat well.
6. Some lemon or Sherry will improve the taste of the soup.
7. May be made by using vegetable stock or water and some tomato.
