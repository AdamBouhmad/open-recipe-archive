## Ingredients

- Calf's heart
- Salted water
- Ground meat or bread
- Onion
- Pepper
- Parsley
- 1 tablespoon tomato
- 1 egg
- Bacon slices
- Carrots
- Celery
- Onion
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. Clean the heart and place in salted water for an hour to draw out the blood.
2. Fill the cavity with a forcemeat made of ground meat or bread mixed with finely chopped onion fried in butter, pepper, fine parsley, 1 tablespoon tomato and 1 egg.
3. Sew the cavity closed.
4. Place in a baking pan with bacon slices, carrots, celery and onion.
5. Bake in the oven for about 2 hours, frequently basting with the broth in the pan.
6. If it begins to dry, add meat broth.
7. Keep covered so it does not burn.
