## Ingredients

- 1 quart of cream
- 1/2 cupful of orange juice
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 6-8 yolks of eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same way as vanilla ice cream.
2. Instead of vanilla use orange juice and a little grated orange rind.
3. Last of all add the remaining cream without cooking.
