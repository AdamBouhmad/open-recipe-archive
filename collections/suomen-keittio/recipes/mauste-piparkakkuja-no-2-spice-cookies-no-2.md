## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 or 2 eggs
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon mace
- 1/4 teaspoon cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 or 1 cup raisins
- 1 1/2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar until creamy
2. Add beaten egg
3. Add spices, milk, and flour mixed with baking powder and raisins
4. Drop by the spoonful onto a buttered baking sheet
5. Bake in a hot oven
