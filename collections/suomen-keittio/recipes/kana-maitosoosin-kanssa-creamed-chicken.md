## Ingredients

- cold boiled chicken
- 1 cup white sauce
- milk or chicken bouillon
- ½ cup chicken pieces
- egg yolk
- cream
- chopped mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut cold boiled chicken into nice pieces.
2. Mix with 1 cup of white sauce, made of milk or chicken bouillon.
3. Heat.
4. Add one yolk of egg and cream.
5. May be mixed with chopped mushrooms, if desired.
