## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- 1/2 pound powdered sugar
- vanilla ice cream or blanc mange for filling
- whipped cream for garnish

## Directions

1. Beat 6 egg whites until firm for about 20-30 minutes.
2. Sift 1/2 pound of powdered sugar several times and mix carefully with the egg whites.
3. Force through a pastry bag on a floured baking sheet forming larger and smaller rings.
4. Dry in a slow oven.
5. Pile on a serving dish so as to give the appearance of a pyramid.
6. Fill the center with vanilla ice cream or blanc mange.
7. Garnish the sides and the top with whipped cream.
