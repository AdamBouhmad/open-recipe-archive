## Ingredients

- Eel
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion
- Milk sauce
- Parsley
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, cut in pieces 1 inch thick.
2. Place on fire in cold water with salt, pepper and onion.
3. Let cook until tender.
4. Drain off the water, pour rich milk sauce over the fish, season, add some chopped parsley, return to fire.
5. If mixed with 2 yolks of egg, the gravy will be delicious.
