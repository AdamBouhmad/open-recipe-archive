## Ingredients

- mackerel
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- green pepper
- mushrooms
- tomato sauce
- lemon juice
- parsley

## Directions

1. Split the fish in back and remove the backbone
2. Wipe and spread with butter
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Bake covered about 15 minutes
5. Chop onion and green pepper, cook in butter
6. Add 1 cup of chopped mushrooms and cover the fish with the mixture
7. Continue baking for 10 minutes
8. Remove the fish on a platter
9. Add tomato sauce and lemon juice to the drippings in the pan
10. Pour on the platter around the fish
11. Strew with chopped parsley
