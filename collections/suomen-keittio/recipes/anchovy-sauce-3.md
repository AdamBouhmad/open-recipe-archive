## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 teaspoonful of anchovy paste
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- yolk of 1 egg
- a little cream
- minced parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and milk, add anchovy paste, lemon juice, yolk, a little cream and minced parsley.
2. Stir over the fire until hot.
