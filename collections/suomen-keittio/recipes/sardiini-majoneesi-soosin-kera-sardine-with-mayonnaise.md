## Ingredients

- bread pieces
- anchovy paste
- sardines
- 5 tablespoonfuls of milk
- 5 gelatine leaves
- 4 tablespoonfuls of mayonnaise sauce
- salt
- whipped cream
- pink color

## Directions

1. Spread pieces of bread with anchovy paste, place the sardine on it.
2. Prepare a sauce: dissolve 5 gelatine leaves in 5 tablespoonfuls of milk.
3. Let cool until firm.
4. Add 4 tablespoonfuls of mayonnaise sauce stirring gradually into it.
5. Season with salt.
6. Mix with the whipped cream.
7. Put a layer of this mixture on the bread.
8. Mix the rest of it with a little gelatine and pink color to garnish the top with.
