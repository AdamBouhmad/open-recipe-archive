## Ingredients

- ¾ cupful of butter
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 6 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 4 eggs
- 1 cupful of flour
- ¼ lb. of almonds
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder

## Directions

1. Shell almonds and grind them finely.
2. Beat egg yolks and sugar to a froth.
3. Add cream, butter, flour, baking powder, almonds and finally the stiffly beaten egg whites.
4. Pour the mixture in a buttered mold or small molds and bake in a moderate oven.
