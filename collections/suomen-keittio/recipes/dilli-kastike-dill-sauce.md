## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups bouillon
- 1 tablespoon vinegar or lemon juice
- 1 or 2 tablespoons chopped dill
- Salt and sugar

## Directions

1. Make a gravy of butter, flour and bouillon, add dill, vinegar and sugar.
2. Last of all mix with the yolk of an egg, if desired.
3. Serve with boiled mutton.
