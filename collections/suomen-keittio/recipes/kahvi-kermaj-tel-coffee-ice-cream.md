## Ingredients

- 1 quart cream
- ½ cup black coffee
- 1½ cups sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- Vanilla
- 1 cup ground coffee
- 2½ cups water

## Directions

1. Make black coffee by boiling 1 cup coffee in 2½ cups water for 10 minutes.
2. Strain through a fine cloth and let cool.
3. Make a mixture of one half of the cream, yolks and sugar as directed.
4. When cool, add the rest of the cream, vanilla and coffee.
5. Freeze.
