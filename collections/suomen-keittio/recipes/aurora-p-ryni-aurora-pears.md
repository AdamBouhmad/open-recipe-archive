## Ingredients

- pears
- sugar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons chopped almonds
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons gelatine or 2 leaves
- 1 cup whipped cream
- 1 pound chocolate
- 1/2 cup hot water
- 1 cup sugar
- small lump of butter
- sponge cake

## Directions

1. Pare and core the pears leaving the stems on
2. Cook with sugar until tender, let cool
3. Mix together all the ingredients except the cream, stir over the fire until the gelatine is dissolved
4. Let cool, add the whipped cream
5. Dissolve the chocolate, cook the sugar in the water, add gradually the chocolate
6. Fill the pear cores with the mixture
7. Baste the pears with chocolate caramel
8. Place the pears on and around a foundation of sponge cake
9. Have whipped cream in the center
