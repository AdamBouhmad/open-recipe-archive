## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiled rice
- 1 cup thick tomato sauce
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Mix 2 cups of boiled rice with 1 cup of thick tomato sauce with fork so as not to mash the rice
2. Add butter, salt and pepper
3. Serve as a vegetable
