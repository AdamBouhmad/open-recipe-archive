## Ingredients

- mutton
- boiling water
- vegetables
- pepper
- salt
- Caper sauce

## Directions

1. Wipe mutton, put into a kettle
2. Pour over it boiling water and let quickly boil
3. Add vegetables, pepper and salt
4. Let simmer covered, until tender
5. Arrange on a serving dish, having the vegetables around the meat
6. Serve with Caper sauce
