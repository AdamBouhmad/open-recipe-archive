## Ingredients

- 1 quart clams
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup cream
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 egg yolks
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the clams fine, put on the fire in their own liquor diluted with some water or fish stock.
2. Simmer for 1 hour.
3. Thicken with butter and flour.
4. Beat the yolks, add slowly the scalded milk.
5. Remove the soup from the fire, add some soda and pour slowly the milk into the soup.
