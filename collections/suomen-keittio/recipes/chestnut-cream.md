## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- sugar
- vanilla
- salt
- cream
- whipped cream
- cooked apples

## Directions

1. Cook chestnuts, skin and press through a sieve.
2. Season with sugar, vanilla and salt, moisten with cream.
3. Heat the mixture, arrange on a serving dish in form of a ring or pyramid.
4. Let cool.
5. Garnish with whipped cream and cooked apples.
