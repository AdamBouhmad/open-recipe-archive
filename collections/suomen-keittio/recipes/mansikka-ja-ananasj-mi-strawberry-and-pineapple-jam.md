## Ingredients

- Equal parts of strawberries and pineapples
- 8 cups sugar to 10 cups fruit

## Directions

1. Pare and core pineapples, grind coarse.
2. Have equal parts of strawberries and pineapples.
3. Put into a kettle in layers, let stand for a few hours.
4. Simmer until clear and thick.
5. Keep in glass jars.
