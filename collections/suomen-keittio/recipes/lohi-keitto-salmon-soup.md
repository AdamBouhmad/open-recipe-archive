## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of salmon- or fish stock
- 2½ lbs. of fresh salmon or ½ can of canned salmon
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter or crab-butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- salt and pepper
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 cupful of cream

## Directions

1. Cook the salmon in a small quantity of salted water until tender.
2. Remove the skin and the bones, and cut from the fish nice pieces to be served in the soup.
3. Chop the rest of the fish finely, cook it in fish stock and force through a sieve.
4. Thicken the soup with butter and flour.
5. Beat the yolks, add the cream to it, pour the soup slowly in.
6. Serve with pieces of fish, hard boiled yolks or fish balls.
7. Remains of crab meat may be served in salmon soup, giving it a good taste.
