## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of brown sauce
- 3 tablespoonfuls of currant jelly
- a little sherry

## Directions

1. Make a cupful of brown sauce.
2. Add 3 tablespoonfuls of currant jelly and a little sherry.
3. Serve with game and mutton.
