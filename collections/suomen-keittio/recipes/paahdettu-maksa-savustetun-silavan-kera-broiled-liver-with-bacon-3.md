## Ingredients

- liver
- boiling water
- salt
- pepper
- pork
- butter (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut liver in half an inch thick slices.
2. Cover with boiling water and let stand for 5 minutes to draw out the blood.
3. Wipe the slices and remove carefully the skin.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
5. Cut pork into thin slices as big as the slices of liver and fasten with sticks on the liver.
6. Broil over a clear fire and push into the oven for a few minutes.
7. Dish on a plate.
