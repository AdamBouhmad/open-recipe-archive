## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 carrot
- 1 slice of onion
- 1 1/2 cups white stock
- 1/2 cup grated cheese
- 1 cup cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the stock with the onion and the carrot for 15 minutes
2. Strain and prepare a sauce of butter and flour by adding the stock
3. Mix with the cheese, cream and seasonings
4. Let boil quickly
