## Ingredients

- kidneys
- cold water
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter
- onion, sliced
- 1 tablespoon flour
- water or bouillon
- 1/2 cup mushrooms, cleaned
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Soak kidneys in cold water for about an hour.
2. Wipe and cut into pieces.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dip in egg and bread crumbs.
4. Cook in butter with a slice of onion.
5. Add 1 tablespoon of flour and water or bouillon, continue cooking for 5 minutes.
6. Mix in 1/2 cup of mushrooms cut into quarters.
7. Season with salt and pepper and cook for a few more minutes.
8. Serve hot.
