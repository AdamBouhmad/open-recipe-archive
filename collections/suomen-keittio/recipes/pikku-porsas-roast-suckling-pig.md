## Ingredients

- A suckling pig, about 4 weeks old
- Salt and pepper
- Apple slices
- Prunes with stones removed
- Boiled rice or a mixture made of soaked bread, egg, chopped liver and heart of the pig, ham, and parsley

## Directions

1. Rub the inside of the suckling pig with salt and pepper.
2. Stuff with slices of apple and prunes (having the stones removed from the prunes) and boiled rice, or with a mixture made of soaked bread, egg, chopped liver and heart of the pig, ham and parsley.
3. Sew up the pig.
4. Spread the surface while roasting with butter or baste with the drippings.
5. Cover with paper if it becomes too brown.
6. Roast for 2 or 3 hours.
7. When done, lift onto a platter. If very small, cut the pig in quarters. If larger, cut in pieces having the pieces replaced around the backbone.
8. Make a gravy of the drippings in the pan.
9. It is best to tie the pig into the required shape before roasting so the meat is easier to handle.
