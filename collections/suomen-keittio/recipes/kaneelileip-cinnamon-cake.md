## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- 4 cupfuls of flour
- 2 cupfuls of lukewarm milk
- 1 cupful of melted butter
- 1 cake of yeast
- cinnamon
- chopped almonds
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as for tea-ring.
2. When risen until light, toss in a shallow baking pan.
3. Let rise again.
4. Spread the surface with egg, sprinkle with cinnamon, sugar and chopped almonds.
5. Bake in oven nearly 20 minutes.
6. The dough may be mixed with almonds and spices, if preferred.
7. Shape with knife.
