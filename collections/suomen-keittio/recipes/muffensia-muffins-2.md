## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of flour
- ¾ cupfuls of milk
- ¼ cupful of butter
- 2 eggs
- ½ teaspoonful of salt
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Sift salt and baking powder with flour
2. Beat eggs with sugar until light
3. Add milk, butter and flour gradually into it
4. Put the batter in buttered molds
5. Bake in a moderate oven
