## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 pint rich milk
- 2 boiled sweetbreads
- Salt and pepper
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Make a milk sauce of flour, butter and rich milk.
2. Cut boiled sweetbreads in pieces, mix with milk sauce.
3. Season with salt and pepper, add chopped parsley.
4. Serve.
