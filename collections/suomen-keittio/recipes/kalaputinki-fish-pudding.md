## Ingredients

- 2 cups remains of fish
- 1½ cups rich milk
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon bread crumbs
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- ⅔ onion
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 3 eggs
- Chopped parsley and pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the bones and force the fish through a meat grinder
2. Fry the onion in butter, add bread crumbs and milk, mix with fish rubbing well
3. Add the eggs beaten to a froth
4. Force the mixture through a strainer, add salt, pepper, lemon juice and some chopped parsley
5. Pour into a buttered mold
6. Bake in oven in a pan of water
