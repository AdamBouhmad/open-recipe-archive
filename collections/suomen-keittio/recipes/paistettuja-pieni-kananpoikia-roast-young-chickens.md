## Ingredients

- young chickens
- butter
- parsley
- salt
- bread crumbs
- water
- cream

## Directions

1. Clean the chicks.
2. Mix a few spoonfuls of butter with some chopped parsley.
3. Fill the chicks with the butter.
4. Tie with string.
5. Roast in oven having a little butter in the roasting pan.
6. When lightly brown, sprinkle with salt and bread crumbs.
7. Pour some water into the pan to prevent from burning and baste often.
8. Arrange on a platter and garnish with vegetables.
9. Make a gravy of the drippings by adding some cream.
