## Ingredients

- 10 ozs. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of powdered sugar
- 1 egg
- 3 ozs. of grated almonds
- 1 lb. of flour

## Directions

1. Wash butter, work to a froth and mix with remaining ingredients.
2. When thoroughly mixed, force it through a pastry bag on baking sheet into rings.
3. Bake in a slow oven.
