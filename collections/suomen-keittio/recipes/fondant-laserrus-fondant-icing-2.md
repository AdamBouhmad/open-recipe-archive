## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of granulated sugar
- 1/2 cupful of water
- 1/4 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- oil for coating

## Directions

1. Put sugar with water and cream of tartar in a saucepan
2. Stir until sugar is dissolved, no longer
3. Let boil until it threads when dropped from spoon
4. Oil a marble table or large baking pan
5. Pour sugar mixture on it
6. Let cool a few minutes
7. Stir with a knife or wooden spoon, keeping the mixture in the center as much as possible, until it is a smooth white mass
8. Store in a container for long keeping and use as needed for small cakes
