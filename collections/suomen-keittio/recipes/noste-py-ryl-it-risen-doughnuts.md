## Ingredients

- 2 cups warmed milk
- 3/4 cups sugar
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 yeast cake
- 2 eggs
- Some nutmeg
- Flour

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm milk, add salt and flour enough to make a thick batter, let rise over night.
2. In the morning add melted butter and eggs beaten with sugar.
3. Work more flour in, if necessary.
4. Let rise again.
5. Roll out to 1/2 inch thickness, shape into rings or round balls.
6. Let rise, cook in boiling fat until light brown.
7. Dip in powdered sugar.
