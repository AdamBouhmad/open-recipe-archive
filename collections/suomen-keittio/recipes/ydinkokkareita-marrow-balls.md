## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of marrow
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- Wheat bread flour
- 2 eggs
- Salt and pepper
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Melt 2 tablespoonfuls of marrow and strain through a cloth.
2. Let cool for a little while, then rub to a cream.
3. Scald 1/2 cupful of milk and add bread crumbs until absorbed.
4. Mix with 2 eggs thickened with bread crumbs.
5. Season with salt and pepper and sprinkle with parsley.
6. Add the bread mixture slowly to the marrow, rubbing well.
7. Test the mixture; if too thin, thicken with bread crumbs.
8. Form into tiny balls and roll in flour.
9. Cook in salt water.
10. Serve with meat soup.
