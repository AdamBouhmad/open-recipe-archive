## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cupful of cream or milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of grated cheese
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat yolks and whites separately
2. Mix the cheese, salt and cream beaten to a froth with the yolks
3. At the last mix in the beaten whites
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered omelet mold and bake in the oven
5. Serve hot
