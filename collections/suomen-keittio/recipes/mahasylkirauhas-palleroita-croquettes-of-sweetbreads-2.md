## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of sweetbreads
- 2 ozs. of salted tongue
- 3 ozs. of mushrooms
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1/2 cupful of meat stock
- 1/2 cupful of cream
- yolks of 2 eggs
- salt and pepper
- bread crumbs
- egg

## Directions

1. Chop boiled sweetbreads fried in butter, tongue and boiled mushrooms
2. Make a sauce of butter, flour and meat stock
3. Add cream and combine both mixtures
4. Mix with yolks of eggs and seasonings
5. Let cool and form into oblong croquettes
6. Dip in egg and bread crumbs, then in egg and bread crumbs again
7. Fry in fat
