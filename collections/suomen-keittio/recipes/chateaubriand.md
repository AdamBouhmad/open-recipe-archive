## Ingredients

- center of the fillet
- melted butter or salad oil
- salt and pepper
- parsley butter

## Directions

1. Cut the center of the fillet in pieces 1½ inch thick
2. Pound lightly
3. Dip in melted butter or salad oil
4. Fry the pieces quickly in a pan on both sides
5. Sprinkle salt and pepper on top
6. Place on a heated serving platter with a small piece of parsley butter for each piece
7. Add a little meat stock to the pan and pour this sauce on the platter
