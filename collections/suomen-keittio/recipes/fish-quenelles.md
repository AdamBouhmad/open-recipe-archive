## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of boned fish
- 2 ozs. of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of cream
- 1 egg
- a little flour

## Directions

1. Grind boned fish, pound well with butter, rub through a sieve.
2. Mix with other ingredients and work to a smooth paste.
3. Finally add enough flour to make a batter.
4. Form into quenelles, cook in salted water or fish stock.
5. Serve in fish or crab soup.
