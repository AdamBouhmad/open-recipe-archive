## Ingredients

- powdered sugar
- white of egg
- cleaned, wiped cranberries

## Directions

1. Warm sifted powdered sugar on paper or in a clean pan.
2. Whip white of egg.
3. Dip cleaned, wiped cranberries into it.
4. Drain in a colander.
5. Roll in heated sugar until white and covered with sugar.
6. Keep in a warm place until the sugar is fastened on berries.
