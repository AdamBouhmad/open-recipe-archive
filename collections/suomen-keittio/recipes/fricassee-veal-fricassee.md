## Ingredients

- 2 pounds veal fillet
- boiling water
- 2 stalks celery
- 1 small carrot
- salt
- 1 slice onion
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- strained veal bouillon
- pepper
- lemon juice
- 2 egg yolks
- cream
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Wipe and cut the veal fillet into pieces, place in a kettle and pour boiling water over.
2. Add the celery stalks, carrot, salt and onion slice.
3. Cook slowly covered until the meat is tender.
4. Make a gravy of 3 tablespoons butter, 2 tablespoons flour, strained veal bouillon, salt, pepper and lemon juice.
5. Beat 2 egg yolks with a little cream and add to the gravy.
6. Pour the gravy over the meat and strew with chopped parsley.
