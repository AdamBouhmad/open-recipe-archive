## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- eggs
- remains of fish

## Directions

1. Make the milk sauce of 1 tablespoonful of butter, 1 tablespoonful of flour and 1½ cupful of milk; season with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
2. Add yolks of 2 eggs to it.
3. Mix with 2 cupfuls of remains of fish boned and flaked into bits.
4. Put in a buttered mold, dredge with breadcrumbs.
5. Bake in a moderate oven until brown.
