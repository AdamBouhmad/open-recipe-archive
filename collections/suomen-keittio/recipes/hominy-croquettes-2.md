## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of boiled hominy
- 1 tablespoonful of melted butter
- salt and pepper
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Season hominy with salt and pepper
2. Add well beaten egg
3. Rub until smooth
4. Form into croquettes
5. Dip in egg and bread crumbs
6. Fry in lard
