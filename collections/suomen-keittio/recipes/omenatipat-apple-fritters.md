## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 2 apples
- 5 tablespoonfuls of milk
- 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 3 tablespoonfuls of whipped cream
- 3 eggs
- a little salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of baking powder

## Directions

1. Peel and split the apples lengthwise in six or eight parts, slice the parts crosswise thinly.
2. Beat yolks with sugar, add milk, butter, flour, cinnamon, salt and cream.
3. Mix the paste with sliced apples and stir stiff beaten whites into it.
4. Drop with a spoon into boiling lard and cook until brown.
5. Serve with hard sauce.
