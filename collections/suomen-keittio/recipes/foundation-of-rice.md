## Ingredients

- boiled rice
- butter
- egg yolk (optional)

## Directions

1. Put the boiled rice in well buttered shallow pan and bake a few minutes in a hot oven.
2. Turn on a serving platter.
3. Mix with a lump of butter and yolk of egg, if desired.
4. Used as foundation for several dishes.
