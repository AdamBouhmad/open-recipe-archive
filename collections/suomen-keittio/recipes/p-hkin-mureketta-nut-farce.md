## Ingredients

- 1 pound nuts
- 1 pound potatoes
- 1/2 pound bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 1/2 cups milk or strained tomato
- 3 eggs
- salt and pepper
- 1/4 pound butter

## Directions

1. Cook and mash the potatoes.
2. Put the shelled nuts through a meat grinder.
3. Combine the potatoes with the nuts.
4. Add the bread crumbs soaked in milk or tomato juice, beaten egg, cornstarch and the seasonings.
5. Bake in a buttered mold in a pan of water.
6. May be made into balls, croquettes, etc., and fried in butter.
7. Make a sauce of vegetable stock and cream or serve with tomato sauce.
