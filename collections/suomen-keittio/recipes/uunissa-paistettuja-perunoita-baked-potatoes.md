## Ingredients

- 6 medium potatoes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt
- pepper
- 2 tablespoons milk or cream
- egg whites, optional
- grated cheese, optional
- paprika, optional

## Directions

1. Rinse well 6 potatoes of uniform, moderately large size.
2. Bake for 1 hour or until tender.
3. Serve on a folded napkin.
4. Alternatively, cut the potatoes in half lengthwise and scoop out the insides.
5. Mash the insides and add 2 tablespoons of butter, some salt and pepper, and 2 tablespoons of milk or cream.
6. Fill the potato halves with this mixture and bake in a hot oven until they brown.
7. Two stiffly beaten egg whites added to the mashed potato mixture will make it fluffy.
8. Grated cheese may be sprinkled on top if desired, or paprika may be mixed into the mashed potato to make paprika potatoes.
