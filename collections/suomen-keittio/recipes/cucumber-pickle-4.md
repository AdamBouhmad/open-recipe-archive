## Ingredients

- small cucumbers
- water
- salt
- grape leaves, cherry leaves, or oak leaves
- dill
- cloves
- white pepper
- horse-radish
- 1 1/2 quarts of vinegar
- 1 cupful of salt

## Directions

1. Mix water with salt and put small cucumbers into it to soak for one day.
2. Lay them in glass jar alternately with layers of grape-, cherry and oak leaves so as not to touch each other.
3. Put seasonings, such as dill, cloves, white pepper and horse-radish between the layers.
4. Cook 1 1/2 quarts of vinegar with 1 cupful of salt and pour it boiling on the cucumbers.
5. Boil up once more and pour again over the pickles.
