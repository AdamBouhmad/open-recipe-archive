## Ingredients

- marrow bones
- thick dough (flour and water)
- salt
- pepper
- toast

## Directions

1. Spread a thick dough (made of flour and water) on each end of marrow bone to keep the marrow in while cooking.
2. Put the marrow bones on the fire in boiling water; let boil 1-2 hours.
3. Spread the toast with the marrow, dust with salt and pepper.
4. Put the toast in oven for two minutes, to heat them.
