## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 3/4 cup cream or milk
- lemon juice or wine

## Directions

1. Rub until creamy 2 tablespoons butter with 1 tablespoon sugar
2. Add 2 egg yolks beaten until frothy and 3/4 cup cream or milk
3. Beat in a double boiler until thick
4. Remove from the fire, add lemon juice or wine
