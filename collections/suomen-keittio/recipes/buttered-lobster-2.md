## Ingredients

- lobster
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from the shells taking care not to break the shells.
2. Cut the meat into small dice.
3. Season with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
4. Fill the shells with the meat.
5. Pour a little melted butter over it.
6. Heat in oven.
7. Serve garnished with lobster claws.
