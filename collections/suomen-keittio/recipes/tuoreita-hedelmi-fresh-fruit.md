## Ingredients

- pineapple
- oranges
- grapes
- grapefruit
- bananas
- sugar
- lemon juice
- walnuts

## Directions

1. Cut pineapple in cubes.
2. Split grapes and remove stones.
3. Mix all kinds of fresh fruit either plain or combined as desired.
4. Serve in glasses or in half orange peels.
5. Sweeten with sugar and flavor with lemon juice.
6. Serve ice cold.
7. Add chopped walnuts just before serving if desired.
8. Mix bananas just before serving, otherwise they will turn black.
