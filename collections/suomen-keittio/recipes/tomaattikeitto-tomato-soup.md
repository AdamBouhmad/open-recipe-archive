## Ingredients

- 2 cups tomato
- 1 quart meat stock or water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 slice onion
- 1 stalk celery
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the onion with butter or bacon, add flour and tomato, stirring well.
2. Add gradually the meat stock and the celery cut in pieces.
3. Let boil until the tomato is tender.
4. Strain, add stock and season.
5. Serve with tiny meat balls or croutons.
