## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 1 cake of yeast
- 1¼ cupfuls of melted butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 cupful of sugar
- blanched almonds, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Prepare dough the same way as for tea-rings.
2. Form into long-shaped cakes.
3. Before putting into oven, brush with egg and sprinkle finely chopped blanched almonds on top.
