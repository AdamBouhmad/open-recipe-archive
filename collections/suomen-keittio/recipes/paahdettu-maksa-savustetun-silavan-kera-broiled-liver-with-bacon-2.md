## Ingredients

- Liver
- Bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter or lard (if bacon is not used)

## Directions

1. Cut the liver in slices 1/2 inch thick.
2. Cover with boiling water and let stand for 5 minutes to draw out the blood.
3. Wipe the slices and remove the skin carefully.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
5. Cut slices of bacon the same size as the liver slices.
6. Fasten the bacon slices with toothpicks on the liver.
7. Broil over a clear fire and place in oven for a few minutes.
8. Dish and serve.
9. If bacon is not used, dip the liver in melted butter or lard before broiling.
