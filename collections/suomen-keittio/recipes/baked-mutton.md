## Ingredients

- Mutton
- Bone
- Hot water
- Carrots
- Onions
- Salt
- 1 cupful water
- 3 tablespoonfuls flour
- Boiling water
- 1 tablespoonful tomato
- Mustard

## Directions

1. Cut off the bone and wipe the meat with a cloth wrung out from hot water.
2. Put in a dripping pan skin side down.
3. Lay carrots and onions around it.
4. Bake in hot oven until it browns.
5. Sprinkle with salt and pour 1 cupful of water into the pan.
6. Mix the drippings with 3 tablespoonfuls of flour and add boiling water until moderately thick.
7. Add 1 tablespoonful of tomato and a little mustard.
8. Strain the sauce.
