## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- 3 cupfuls of water
- 2 ozs. of gelatine
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice
- 1/2 cupful of sugar

## Directions

1. Dilute gelatine with 1 cupful of cold water.
2. Add 2 cupfuls of boiling water.
3. Strain and mix with sugar, orange- and lemon juice.
4. Mold and put aside to set.
5. Serve with any kind of salad.
