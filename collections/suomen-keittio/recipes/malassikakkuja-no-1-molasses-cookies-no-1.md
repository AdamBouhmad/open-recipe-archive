## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups molasses
- 3/4 cup cream
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1/2 tablespoon cardamom
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 4 eggs
- 2 1/2 pounds flour

## Directions

1. Boil up molasses with butter, sugar, cream and spices
2. Sift flour
3. Put one-half of flour into a bowl, add gradually to the flour the cooled molasses mixture
4. Beat eggs, add to the mixture above
5. Mix in stirring well lemon juice and soda mixed with the remaining flour
6. Let stand over night
7. Roll thin, cut with a round cutter in small cookies
8. Bake in a moderate oven
