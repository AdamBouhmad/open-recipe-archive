## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of brown sugar
- 1 cupful of molasses
- 1 cupful of boiling water
- 1/4 cupful of butter
- 1/4 cupful of lard
- 1 heaping cupful of flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 heaping teaspoonful of soda
- 1/4 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoonful of ginger
- 1/4 teaspoonful of mace

## Directions

1. Beat well together molasses, sugar and water
2. Add melted butter and lard
3. Add beaten eggs, spices, soda and flour
4. Bake in a moderately hot oven
