## Ingredients

- Red cabbage
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Mushrooms
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage in strips, put into cold water for 1/2 hour.
2. Let simmer with plenty of butter covered until tender, season.
3. Arrange in a serving dish.
4. Place mushrooms sprinkled with some lemon juice on top of or around the cabbage.
