## Ingredients

- squab
- bacon
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- cream

## Directions

1. Clean a squab, lay on it a thin slice of bacon, put a little lump of butter in.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Roast 20-30 minutes.
4. Thicken the gravy with a little cream.
5. Divide the squab lengthwise in two, remove the backbone.
6. Arrange on a platter and strain over it the gravy.
