## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 cup mashed chestnuts
- 1/2 cup milk
- 4 egg whites
- 2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Mix together gently the sugar, the flour, the chestnuts and the milk
2. Cook for a few minutes
3. Beat the whites until firm, add to the mixture
4. Bake in a well-buttered mold in a pan of water until firm or about 30 minutes
5. Serve in the mold with vanilla or cream sauce
