## Ingredients

- Parsnips
- Salt
- Butter
- Cream
- Milk
- Flour
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and cut parsnips into pieces, boil in salted water until tender.
2. Drain, add some butter and cream and let boil.
3. Alternatively, boil in milk, mix with butter and flour and season with salt and pepper.
