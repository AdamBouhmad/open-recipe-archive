## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Beat until frothy 2 eggs with 3 tablespoons sugar, add 3 tablespoons melted butter and flour enough to make a moderately thick dough.
2. Roll the dough 1/4 inch thick, divide in parts 3 inches long and 2 inches wide.
3. Make three 1 inch gashes on one side, give both ends a twist.
4. Cook in fat.
