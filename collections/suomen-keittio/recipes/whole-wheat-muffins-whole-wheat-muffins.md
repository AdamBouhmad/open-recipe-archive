## Ingredients

- 1½ cups whole wheat flour
- ½ cup white flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 tablespoons butter or shortening
- 1 cup milk
- 1 or 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix together the dry ingredients.
2. Beat the eggs with the sugar, add the milk, mix in the flour and the melted butter.
3. Stir well, bake in buttered muffin tins in a hot oven for 30 minutes.
