## Ingredients

- boiled rice
- butter
- cold chopped meat
- 1/4 cup bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- bouillon
- salt
- pepper
- 1 onion

## Directions

1. Line a well buttered mold with boiled rice
2. Fry the chopped onion in butter
3. Mix cold chopped meat with bread crumbs, beaten egg, and bouillon to achieve desired consistency
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Add the fried onion to the mixture
6. Stir well
7. Fill the mold with the meat mixture
8. Add a layer of rice on top of the dish
9. Dot with butter
10. Bake in oven covered with buttered paper for 40 minutes
