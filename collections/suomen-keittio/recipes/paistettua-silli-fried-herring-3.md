## Ingredients

- herrings
- salt
- pepper
- flour or crackerdust

## Directions

1. Clean the herrings, wash and wipe.
2. Rub with salt and pepper, roll in flour or crackerdust, and fry in a pan on the stove until browned on both sides.
3. Cover and bake in oven until done.
