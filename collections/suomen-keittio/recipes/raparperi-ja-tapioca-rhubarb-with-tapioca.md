## Ingredients

- 3-4 cupfuls of boiling water
- 1/2 quart of rhubarb, cut in pieces
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of tapioca
- 2 teaspoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Soak tapioca in boiling water 10-15 minutes
2. Put pot on the fire, add rhubarb, sugar and butter
3. Let simmer gently 1/2 hour, stirring often, so that the mixture does not burn
4. Pour in a mold
5. Serve cold on a plate, garnished with whipped cream
