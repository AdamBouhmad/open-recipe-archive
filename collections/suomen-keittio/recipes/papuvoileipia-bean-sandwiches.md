## Ingredients

- 1 cup baked beans
- 1 teaspoon onion juice
- 1 teaspoon chopped celery
- catsup
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Force baked beans through a sieve.
2. Mix 1 cup of beans with 1 teaspoon onion juice, 1 teaspoon chopped celery, a little catsup and chopped parsley.
