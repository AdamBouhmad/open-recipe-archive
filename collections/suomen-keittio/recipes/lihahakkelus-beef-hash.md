## Ingredients

- 2 parts chopped cold steak or boiled corned beef
- 1 part boiled chopped potatoes
- Salt and pepper
- Onion
- Butter
- Egg
- Flour
- Lard

## Directions

1. Fry onion in butter.
2. To two parts of chopped cold steak or boiled corned beef allow one part of boiled chopped potatoes, some salt and pepper.
3. Turn all into a pan, stir over a fire until light brown.
4. Continue frying until a brown crust has formed underneath.
5. Turn onto a platter having the brown side uppermost.
6. Or form into patties, dip in egg and flour, fry in lard.
7. May be made without potatoes. Chop the meat, cook a few slices of onion in butter, remove the onion, add 1 tablespoon of flour, 1 cup of bouillon, 1 1/2 cups of chopped meat, salt and pepper.
8. If veal or poultry are used for hash, use milk instead of bouillon.
9. Serve on buttered toast having poached egg on top.
