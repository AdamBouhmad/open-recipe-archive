## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound macaroni
- 1 quart milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 tablespoon sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon salt
- salted water

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into 2 inch long pieces
2. Parboil in salted water
3. Drain
4. Put on the fire in boiling milk
5. Cook until tender or 20 minutes
6. Add the butter, salt and the sugar
7. Let cool some
8. Beat the egg and mix with the macaroni
9. Bake in a buttered mold until light brown for 20 to 30 minutes
10. Sprinkle with cheese, if preferred
