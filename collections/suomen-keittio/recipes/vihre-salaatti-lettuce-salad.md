## Ingredients

- fresh crisp lettuce
- cold water
- French dressing
- minced parsley

## Directions

1. Use light colored fresh crisp lettuce
2. Let stand in cold water for awhile
3. Remove the leaves from the stalks
4. wash quickly one by one in cold water
5. Place on a clean towel so the water may drip from the leaves
6. Arrange the lettuce in a circle into a salad bowl, having the hearts in the center
7. Pour French dressing on
8. sprinkle with minced parsley
