## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful grated horseradish
- 4 tablespoonfuls lemon juice
- 3 tablespoonfuls tomato catsup
- a few drops of Tabasco
- salt
- paprika
- small oysters

## Directions

1. Mix vinegar with grated horseradish, lemon juice, tomato catsup, Tabasco, salt and paprika.
2. Place on ice to get cool.
3. Wash small oysters and place on ice.
4. Serve the cocktail in small glasses, with five oysters in each glass.
5. Pour the sauce over the oysters.
