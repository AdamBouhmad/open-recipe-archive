## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 pint of beef or fish stock
- 1 to 2 tablespoonfuls of capers
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the sauce from butter, flour and stock
2. Season with pepper, salt, sugar, lemon juice and capers
3. You can also add a couple of egg yolks and a little cream at the end
4. Serve with boiled fish and meat
