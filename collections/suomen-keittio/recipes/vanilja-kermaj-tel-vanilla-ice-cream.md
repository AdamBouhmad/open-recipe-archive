## Ingredients

- 1 pint of rich milk
- 1 pint of cream
- 3/4 cupful of sugar
- 6-8 yolks of eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla extract or 1 vanilla bean

## Directions

1. Put milk on the fire and let boil up.
2. Whip yolks of eggs with sugar.
3. Pour the boiling milk slowly in it.
4. Put in a double-boiler on the fire and beat until thick, but do not let it boil, because it will curdle.
5. Remove from the fire and let cool down.
6. Add vanilla and cream, then freeze.
7. When using vanilla bean, it has to be cut in pieces and boiled with milk.
8. Care should be taken when using whites of eggs, that they do not curdle.
