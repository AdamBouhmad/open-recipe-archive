## Ingredients

- chickens
- vegetable or chicken salad
- goose liver paste or chopped sweetbreads
- white chaudfroid sauce
- lettuce leaves
- chicken jelly

## Directions

1. Boil the chickens whole.
2. Remove carefully the fillets, breastbone and the legs.
3. Stuff with vegetable or chicken salad.
4. Fill the breast space with goose liver paste or chopped sweetbreads.
5. Baste the fillets with white chaudfroid sauce.
6. Arrange nicely on top and around the chicken on the platter.
7. Garnish with lettuce leaves and chicken jelly.
