## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of mushrooms
- 4 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 2 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of milk or beef stock
- pepper and salt
- few drops of onion juice

## Directions

1. Grind mushrooms
2. Mix bread crumbs, milk and eggs together
3. Add the mushrooms and seasonings
4. Stir thoroughly together
5. Form into croquettes
6. Let simmer in salted water 15 minutes
7. Dip in egg, dredge with bread crumbs, fry rapidly in butter
8. Serve with brown sauce and vegetables
