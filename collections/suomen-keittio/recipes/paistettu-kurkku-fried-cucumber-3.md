## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt and pepper
- bread crumbs
- egg
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Peel cucumbers
2. Cut lengthwise
3. Dry with cloth
4. Sprinkle salt and pepper on top
5. Dredge in bread crumbs
6. Dip in egg
7. Dredge again in bread crumbs
8. Fry in fat
