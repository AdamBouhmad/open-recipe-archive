## Ingredients

- marrow
- pickled onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- toast

## Directions

1. Mix the marrow, before spreading it on the toast, with a little pickled onion and parsley, finely minced.
2. Season with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
3. Spread on toast and serve very hot.
