## Ingredients

- boiled pared potatoes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt
- milk or cream
- pepper

## Directions

1. Mash the pared boiled potatoes.
2. Add 2 tablespoons of butter, salt, milk or cream and a pinch of pepper.
3. Rub to a smooth paste and heat.
4. Heap on a serving dish, trim nicely with a knife, or work the mashed potatoes through a potato ricer into the center of the dish.
5. Chopped onion browned in butter or lard may be placed on top if desired.
