## Ingredients

- pineapples
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Pare and core pineapples and remove the black eyes.
2. Cut in slices.
3. Let stand in sugar over night (allow 3 or 4 cups pineapple to 1 cup sugar).
4. Add 1 cup water.
5. Bring slowly to boil.
6. When tender, put into jars.
7. Cover as given for jams.
