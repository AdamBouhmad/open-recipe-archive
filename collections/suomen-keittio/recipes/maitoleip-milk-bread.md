## Ingredients

- milk or equal parts of water and milk
- 1 tablespoon butter or lard
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 cake of yeast
- 6 or 6 1/2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Prepare as directed for water bread. Instead of water use milk or equal parts of water and milk.
