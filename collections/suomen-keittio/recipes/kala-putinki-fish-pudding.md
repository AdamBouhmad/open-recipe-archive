## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of remains of fish
- 1½ cupful of rich milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of crackercrumbs
- 1½ teaspoonful of salt
- ½ onion
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice
- chopped parsley
- pepper

## Directions

1. Bone the fish, chop with the meat chopper.
2. Fry onion in butter, add crackercrumbs and milk, add gently to the fish, mixing thoroughly.
3. Mix with beaten yolks, rub through a sieve.
4. Add salt, pepper, lemon juice and chopped parsley.
5. Put in a buttered mold.
6. Bake in oven.
