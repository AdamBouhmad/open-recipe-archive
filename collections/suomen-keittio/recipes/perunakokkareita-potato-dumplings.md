## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of mashed potato
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 3 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs
- 2 beaten eggs
- Flour, approximately 1 cupful
- Salt

## Directions

1. Mix mashed potato with butter, cream, bread crumbs and beaten eggs.
2. Add flour enough to make a firm batter.
3. Test first by dropping a little of it into boiling water.
4. Drop by the spoonful into boiling salted water or into bouillon shortly before serving.
