## Ingredients

- pears
- walnuts
- celery
- apples
- mayonnaise
- whipped cream
- maraschino cherries
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Cut pears in two and scoop a little in the center.
2. Fill with chopped walnuts, celery and apples.
3. Cover with mayonnaise diluted with cream.
4. Garnish with chopped maraschino cherries.
5. Serve on lettuce leaves.
