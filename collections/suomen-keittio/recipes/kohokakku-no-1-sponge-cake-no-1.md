## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 4 tablespoons cornstarch
- 4 tablespoons water
- 1 tablespoon brandy or lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks with the sugar, add water, brandy, flour sifted with baking powder.
2. Last fold in the egg whites beaten to a stiff froth.
