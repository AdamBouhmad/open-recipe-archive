## Ingredients

- strawberries
- sugar
- lemon juice
- pastry for two crusts

## Directions

1. Cut strawberries in halves, let stand mixed with sugar and lemon juice for 1 hour.
2. Cover a pie plate with pastry, fill with the strawberries.
3. Lay the upper crust on.
4. Bake.
