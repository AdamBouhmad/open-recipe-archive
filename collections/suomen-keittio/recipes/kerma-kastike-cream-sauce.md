## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 2 beaten yolks
- 3/4 cupful of cream or milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of wine

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add 2 beaten yolks and 3/4 cupful of cream or milk
3. Whip in double-boiler until thick
4. Remove from the fire
5. Mix with 3 tablespoonfuls of wine
