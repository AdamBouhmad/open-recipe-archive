## Ingredients

- 3 pounds shoulder of mutton
- carrots
- 4 onions
- celery
- 1 tomato
- salt
- pepper
- potatoes
- flour
- cold water
- butter

## Directions

1. Wipe and cut the mutton into pieces.
2. Put in a kettle in cold water and bring quickly to boiling.
3. Skim off the foam.
4. Add carrots cut in pieces, onions, celery cut in pieces, tomato, salt and pepper.
5. Let simmer covered until the meat is tender.
6. Half an hour before the stew is done, add potatoes to it.
7. Thicken with flour diluted in cold water.
8. Serve with dumplings.
