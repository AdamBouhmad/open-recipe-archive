## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of coffee
- 6 cupfuls of water
- 1 white of egg
- egg shells

## Directions

1. Wash egg and put the white in a cup, beat it and add cold water to fill the cup
2. Pour this onto the coffee grounds in a pot with the egg shells and stir well
3. Pour boiling water on, stir and let boil 3—5 minutes
4. Add a little cold water and let settle
