## Ingredients

- ½ pound of rice
- fruit syrup or ½ cupful of sugar, ½ cupful of water, and few drops of vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook ½ pound of rice 15 minutes in deep water.
2. Drain and put the rice in a dish.
3. Pour fruit syrup over it or mixture made of ½ cupful of sugar, ½ cupful of water and few drops of vanilla.
4. Let stand covered 2–3 hours.
5. Mix with half-frozen vanilla ice cream.
6. Freeze.
7. Rice ice cream may be mixed with a little fruit or garnished with stewed fruit.
