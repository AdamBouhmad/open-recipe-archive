## Ingredients

- fresh tomatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- cornstarch
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. If using fresh tomatoes, they must first be peeled.
2. Keep them in boiling water for a few seconds so as to remove the skin easier.
3. Cut in pieces, boil until tender with butter, salt and pepper.
4. Thicken with cornstarch dissolved in cold water or with 1 cup of bread crumbs.
5. Or serve plain.
