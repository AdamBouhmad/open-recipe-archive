## Ingredients

- Cold cooked tongue
- Boiled string beans
- Egg yolk
- Strained tomato
- Cornstarch
- Hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. Cut cold tongue in slices
2. Place a few boiled string beans on top of each slice
3. Roll together and fasten the edges with egg yolk
4. Place on a platter, cover, and heat in oven
5. Make a sauce of strained tomato and cornstarch to baste the rolls
6. Pour Hollandaise sauce on the platter and place the rolls on top
