## Ingredients

- 1 cupful tomatoes, fresh or canned
- 1/2 onion
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- salt
- sugar
- paprika
- 6 eggs
- cream
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Cook 1 cupful of tomatoes, onion, 2 tablespoonfuls of butter, salt, some sugar and paprika for 10 minutes.
2. Beat lightly 6 eggs and add some cream.
3. Mix the eggs slowly with tomatoes and stir until thick.
4. Serve with buttered toast, cut in triangles and placed around the tomato.
