## Ingredients

- carrots
- potatoes
- peas
- cauliflower
- celery
- turnips
- salted water
- butter
- tomato slices
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- pie-crust (optional)

## Directions

1. Use carrots, potatoes, peas, cauliflower, celery, turnips or whatever you may have.
2. Boil the vegetables in salted water.
3. Drain and place into a mold.
4. Add dots of butter.
5. Cover the top with slices of tomato.
6. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and bread crumbs.
7. May be covered with pie-crust leaving off the bread crumbs.
8. Bake for 1 hour.
9. Serve with melted butter or sauce.
