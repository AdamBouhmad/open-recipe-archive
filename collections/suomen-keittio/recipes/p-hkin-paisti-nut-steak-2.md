## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of shelled nuts
- 1/2 lb. of bread crumbs
- 10 ozs. of rice
- 3 cupfuls of vegetable stock
- 1 cupful of cream
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 4 eggs
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Grind boiled rice together with shelled nuts
2. Soak bread crumbs in vegetable stock mixed with cream
3. Mix soaked bread crumbs with beaten eggs and melted butter
4. Combine all mixtures
5. Season with salt
6. Rub mixture well
7. Form into a loaf on a buttered baking pan
8. Spread with egg and sprinkle with bread crumbs
9. Bake in oven 3/4 hour
10. Prepare a sauce by adding flour, vegetable stock and cream to drippings in pan
11. Serve with vegetables
