## Ingredients

- Calf's tongue
- Fresh tomatoes or toast or hominy for foundation
- Carrots
- Cooked currants
- Parsley
- Hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. Cook calf's tongue and cut in thin slices while hot
2. Make a foundation of fresh tomatoes, toast, or hominy arranged in a ring on the platter
3. Place a slice of tongue on each item
4. On top of each tongue slice place a cup made of carrots filled with cooked currants
5. Place a sprig of parsley in the center of the ring
6. Pour Hollandaise sauce mixed with currants around the platter
