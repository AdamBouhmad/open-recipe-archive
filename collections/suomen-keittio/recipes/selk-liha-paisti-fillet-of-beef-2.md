## Ingredients

- Fillet of beef
- Pork slices
- Onion slices
- Carrots
- Turnip
- Celery
- Bouillon
- Bay leaf
- Parsley
- Salt
- Mushrooms
- Kitchen Bouquet
- Cream
- Sherry or Madeira

## Directions

1. Remove the fat carefully and trim off skin and fibres, taking care not to make the meat ragged.
2. If the fillet is thin, fold it once and tie with cord.
3. Lay thin slices of pork in a buttered dripping-pan and put a few slices of onion, carrots, turnip and celery on the pork.
4. Place the fillet on them and a few slices of pork on the fillet.
5. Bake in a hot oven.
6. When browned, sprinkle with salt and pour 1/2 cupful of bouillon into the pan.
7. Add 1 bay leaf and a little parsley.
8. Allow 10 minutes to each pound.
9. Baste often with drippings.
10. Place the meat on a platter and make the sauce of drippings by skimming the fat off carefully.
11. Add 1/2 cupful of mushrooms to the pan and cook 5 minutes.
12. If not brown enough, mix with 1 teaspoonful of Kitchen Bouquet and a little cream.
13. Pour the sauce over the meat and garnish with mushrooms.
14. Instead of mushrooms sherry or madeira may be used.
