## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/4 cake of yeast
- 2 eggs
- 3-4 cupfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Warm milk, add sugar, salt, butter, yeast dissolved in a small quantity of milk, and flour enough to make a thin batter.
2. Cover with towel and let rise over night.
3. Next morning beat eggs to a froth and stir into the batter.
4. Half fill buttered muffin rings with the batter.
5. Let rise until the rings are full and bake about 30 minutes.
6. If desired, use 1/3 cupful of corn meal and 2/3 cupfuls of flour to prepare the batter.
7. Muffins may be made also of hominy and rice in the same way, adding 2 beaten eggs into the batter in the morning.
