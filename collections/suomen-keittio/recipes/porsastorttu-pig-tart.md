## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- Juice and yellow rind of 1 lemon
- 1 cup potato flour or cornstarch
- 1/2 cup almonds

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks with the sugar
2. Add the lemon and flour
3. Mix in the beaten egg whites last of all
4. Bake for 3/4 hour
5. When done, baste with 3/4 cup of white wine in which 2 tablespoons of sugar is dissolved, or with fruit juice
6. Blanch and cut lengthwise the almonds
7. Stick the almonds into the tart
