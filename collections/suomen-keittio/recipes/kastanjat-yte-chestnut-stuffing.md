## Ingredients

- 1 quart chestnuts
- Water or bouillon
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon parsley
- 1 egg
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Place a quart of chestnuts in boiling water until the skins come off, sieve and peel.
2. Place the peeled chestnuts in water or bouillon and cook until soft.
3. Press through a sieve, add 2 tablespoons butter, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon parsley, 1 egg, and some bouillon if needed.
