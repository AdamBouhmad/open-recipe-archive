## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salted water
- lettuce leaves
- mayonnaise sauce
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cook asparagus or only tips in salted water until tender.
2. Dish on lettuce leaves.
3. Line with mayonnaise sauce.
4. Mix with hard-boiled eggs if desired.
