## Ingredients

- ½ pound figs
- ½ cup boiling water
- ½ cup sugar
- lemon juice or vanilla
- 1 egg white
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Chop ½ pound figs exceedingly fine
2. Add ½ cup boiling water, ½ cup sugar and some lemon juice or vanilla
3. Let simmer to a soft paste
4. Let cool before using
5. The stiffly beaten white of an egg mixed with 2 tablespoons powdered sugar may be added to this filling
