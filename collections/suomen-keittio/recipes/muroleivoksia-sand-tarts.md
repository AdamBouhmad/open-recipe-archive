## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound butter
- 3/4 pound sugar
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons cream
- 1/2 cup almonds
- 3/4 cup flour
- 2 tablespoons cracker dust

## Directions

1. Blanch and chop the almonds
2. Wash the butter and rub to a cream
3. Add all remaining ingredients to the butter and work well together
4. Wipe small molds
5. Line with a thin layer of batter
6. Place the molds on a baking sheet
7. Bake in a quick oven until light brown
8. Unmold carefully
