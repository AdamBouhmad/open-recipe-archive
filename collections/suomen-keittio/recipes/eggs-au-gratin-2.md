## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- grated cheese
- anchovy essence
- melted butter
- butter for dish
- milksauce
- cheese and breadcrumbs for topping

## Directions

1. Shell 4 hard-boiled eggs and cut lengthwise into halves.
2. Take out the yolks and rub to a paste with a little grated cheese, anchovy essence and melted butter.
3. Fill the halved whites with this mixture.
4. Butter a dish, put a layer of the mixture on the bottom of it, line with the filled eggs and pour over it a rich milksauce.
5. Dredge with cheese and breadcrumbs.
6. Bake in oven until the surface is browned.
