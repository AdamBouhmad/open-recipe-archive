## Ingredients

- 1/2 onion
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 lb. of fresh or 1 cupful of canned tomatoes
- salt
- paprika
- a little sugar
- 6 eggs
- a little cream

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 onion with 2 tablespoonfuls of butter.
2. Mix with 1 lb. of fresh or 1 cupful of canned tomatoes, salt, paprika and a little sugar.
3. Let boil 10 min.
4. Whip 6 eggs, add a little cream, pour slowly into it the mixture of tomatoes strained or as it is, and stir until firm.
