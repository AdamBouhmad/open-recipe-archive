## Ingredients

- 2 cups molasses
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup lard
- 1 cup cold coffee
- 3 teaspoons ginger
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 10 cups flour
- Some salt

## Directions

1. Warm up molasses
2. Add lard, butter and sugar
3. Rub well
4. Mix in coffee, spices and flour
5. Roll thin, cut in cookies with a round cutter
6. Bake in a hot oven
