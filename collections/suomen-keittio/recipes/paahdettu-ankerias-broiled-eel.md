## Ingredients

- Eel
- Water or fish stock
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the fish in 2 or 3 inch thick pieces.
2. Parboil with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
3. Wipe, dip in melted butter, broil on a slow fire.
