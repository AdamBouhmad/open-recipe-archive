## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Beat 2 egg yolks and 1 white with sugar
2. Add butter rubbed until creamy and flour, beating well
3. Roll thin, shape into round cookies
4. Place on a buttered baking sheet
5. Spread with egg white
6. Sprinkle with chopped almonds and granulated sugar
7. Bake until light brown
