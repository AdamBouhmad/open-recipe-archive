## Ingredients

- 2 cups cold boiled or fried fish (salmon, haddock, sole, or similar firm-fleshed fish)
- 1 cup mashed potatoes
- 2 egg yolks
- cream or milk
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- egg
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Mix cold boiled or fried fish with mashed potatoes.
2. Add two egg yolks, some cream or milk, season with salt and pepper.
3. Form into cakes or balls.
4. Roll in bread crumbs, dip in egg, and roll in bread crumbs again.
5. Fry with butter or cook in lard, or if frying in a pan, roll in flour only.
6. Serve with Bearnaise, Hollandaise, or tartar sauce.
