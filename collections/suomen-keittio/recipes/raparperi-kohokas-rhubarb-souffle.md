## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of rhubarb
- 5 ozs. of granulated sugar
- 1/2 cupful of water
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of cream
- 5 tablesp. of powdered sugar
- 1 teasp. of grated lemon rind

## Directions

1. Skin the stalks, cut in pieces and cook with granulated sugar.
2. Whip yolks with sugar, add cream and lemon rind.
3. Beat whites until stiff and stir into the mixture.
4. Put one half of the mixture into a buttered mold, lay the cooked and strained rhubarb on it.
5. Pour the remaining mixture in.
6. Steam in oven about 3/4 of an hour.
7. Serve hot with whipped cream.
