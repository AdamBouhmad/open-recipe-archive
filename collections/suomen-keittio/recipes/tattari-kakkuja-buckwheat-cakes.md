## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 cupful of water
- 2 tablespoonfuls of molasses
- ½ cake of yeast
- ½ teaspoonful of salt
- 1½ cupfuls of buckwheat flour
- ½ cupful of flour

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm water.
2. Add all other ingredients stirring well.
3. Set to rise.
4. Bake like griddle cakes.
