## Ingredients

- 4 squabs
- 3 ozs. of marrow
- 4 ozs. of butter
- 2 ozs. of bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- salt and pepper
- chopped parsley
- fried slices of artichokes
- cream

## Directions

1. Clean squabs.
2. Mince the marrow, mixed with butter and rub well.
3. Add the other ingredients.
4. Stuff the squabs, and bake 30 minutes, basting often.
5. Arrange on fried slices of artichokes.
6. Skim the gravy and thicken with a little cream.
