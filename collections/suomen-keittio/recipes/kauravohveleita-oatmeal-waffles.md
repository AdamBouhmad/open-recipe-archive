## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked oatmeal
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- Some salt

## Directions

1. Sift together all the dry ingredients
2. Add the milk with beaten eggs
3. Combine with the oatmeal
4. Add the flour
5. Fry in hot greased waffle-iron until brown on both sides
6. Rice waffles may be made in the same way
