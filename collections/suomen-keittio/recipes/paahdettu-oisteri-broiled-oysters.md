## Ingredients

- equal-sized oysters
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- bread crumbs or cracker crumbs
- lemon juice
- lemon butter

## Directions

1. Rinse and wipe equal-sized oysters
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Dip in butter and lay on a buttered broiler
4. Broil for a few minutes over a clear fire on both sides
5. Serve on buttered toast having 3 or 4 oysters on each
6. Baste with melted butter and lemon juice
7. Serve with lemon butter
