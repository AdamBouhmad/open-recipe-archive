## Ingredients

- 1 pound spinach
- 3/4 cup rice
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 cup water
- 3 cups milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Clean and boil the spinach in salt water with a little soda. Drain and chop.
2. Make a batter of rice, water and milk.
3. Mix with the spinach; add butter, sugar, salt and eggs.
4. Put in a buttered mold; bake for 30 or 40 minutes.
5. Serve with or without meat.
