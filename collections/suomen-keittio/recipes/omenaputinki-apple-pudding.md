## Ingredients

- 2 cups apple sauce
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 0.25 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 0.5 teaspoon cinnamon
- 0.25 teaspoon nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix the apple sauce with the spices, sugar, butter and the egg yolks, beat until frothy.
2. Bake in a buttered mold in a hot oven for 10-15 minutes.
3. Remove from the oven, coat with meringue made of the egg whites beaten stiff and 0.25 cup of powdered sugar and vanilla.
4. Return to the oven so as to brown the surface.
5. Serve cold with whipped cream.
