## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of milk or cream
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs to a froth; add milk and seasonings
2. Put butter in hot omelet pan and pour the mixture in it
3. Shake the pan over the fire until the omelet forms a creamy mass
4. Brown quickly on the bottom, fold once and serve hot with any kind of sauce
