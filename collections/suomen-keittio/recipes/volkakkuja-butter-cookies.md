## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 glassful of sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 lbs. of flour

## Directions

1. Wash butter and work it to a cream
2. Mix with sugar and continue rubbing a little while
3. Stir slowly egg and flour into the butter
4. Roll paste and shape into small cookies
5. Spread with egg, sprinkle with sugar and chopped almonds
6. Bake in oven to a light yellow
