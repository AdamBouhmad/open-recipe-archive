## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 yeast cake
- 1 whole egg and 1 yolk
- 1 1/2 teaspoon grated lemon rind
- Dash of nutmeg
- 1/2 cup currants washed and wiped

## Directions

1. Warm the milk, add yeast, salt and 1/2 cup flour, let rise.
2. Beat the eggs with the sugar, combine with the risen sponge, add butter rubbed until creamy, spices, currants and the rest of the flour, let rise.
3. Drop by the spoonful into boiling fat, cook until thoroughly done and nice brown.
4. Drain on paper, sprinkle with powdered sugar.
