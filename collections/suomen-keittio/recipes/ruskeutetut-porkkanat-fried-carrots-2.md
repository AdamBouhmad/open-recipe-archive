## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Butter or lard
- Salt
- Sugar (optional)
- Lemon (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel boiled carrots, cut lengthwise in strips, brown in butter or lard on both sides.
2. Or peel raw carrots, cut into cubes, cook in salted water until half done, drain carefully, and brown the carrots in butter in a pan.
3. If desired, season with sugar and lemon.
