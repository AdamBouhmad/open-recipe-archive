## Ingredients

- ½ lb. of pork
- ½ lb. of veal
- 6 tablespoonfuls of milk sauce
- 4 tablespoonfuls of cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Grind the meat several times
2. Mix with milk sauce, cream, pepper and salt
3. Stir until smooth
