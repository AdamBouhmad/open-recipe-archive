## Ingredients

- 1 can of peas
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- a pinch of pepper
- few drops of onion juice

## Directions

1. Wash one can of peas and rub through a strainer
2. Add 3 eggs, 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter, 1/2 teaspoonful of salt, a pinch of pepper and few drops of onion juice
3. Pour the mixture in a buttered mould
4. Bake in oven in a pan of water, covered with a buttered paper, until firm
