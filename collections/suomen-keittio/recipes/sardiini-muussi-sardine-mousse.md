## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sardines
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 1 1/2 teaspoons anchovy paste
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 egg whites
- milk
- hard boiled egg white
- parsley
- toast
- anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Soak bread crumbs in milk.
2. Add finely chopped sardine, anchovy paste, and egg yolk.
3. Mix in egg whites last.
4. Garnish individual fish molds with hard boiled egg white and parsley.
5. Fill with sardine mousse.
6. Bake in oven in a pan of water until firm.
7. Serve on toast with anchovy sauce.
