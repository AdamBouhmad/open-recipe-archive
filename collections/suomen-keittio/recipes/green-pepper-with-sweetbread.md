## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 cupful of chicken stock
- 1 sweetbread
- salt
- pepper
- Worcestershire sauce
- cream
- a few boiled mushrooms
- green peppers
- lard
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and chicken stock.
2. Cook 1 sweetbread as usually and cut in pieces, add to the sauce.
3. Season with salt, pepper and Worcestershire sauce.
4. Add a little cream and if wanted, a few boiled mushrooms cut in pieces.
5. Cook green peppers quickly in lard, so as to remove the bitter skin easily.
6. Let cool, fill with the mixture, and sprinkle with bread crumbs.
7. Bake in oven until the surface is browned.
8. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and chicken stock.
9. Season with salt and pepper.
10. Add lastly one yolk and cream.
