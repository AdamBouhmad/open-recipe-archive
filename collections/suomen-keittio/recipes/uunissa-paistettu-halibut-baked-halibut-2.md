## Ingredients

- chicken halibut, 2 or 3 pounds
- salt
- pepper
- bacon or salt pork slices
- onion, sliced
- butter
- flour
- bread crumbs
- lemon juice
- water or cream

## Directions

1. Take a 2 or 3 pound chicken halibut.
2. Clean, wipe and rub with salt and pepper.
3. Place in a pan on bacon or salt pork slices with onion slices on the edges.
4. Cover the fish with a paste made from equal parts butter and flour mixed well.
5. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top.
6. Place a few slices of salt pork or lumps of butter on top.
7. Bake in the oven frequently basting.
8. If it becomes too brown on top, cover with buttered paper.
9. Make a sauce from the drippings by adding a little flour, lemon juice, water or cream.
