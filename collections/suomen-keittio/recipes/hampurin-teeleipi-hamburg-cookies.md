## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 5 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 1 cup of finely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and sugar well to a froth
2. Mix with almonds, butter and flour
3. Form the batter into strings to resemble fingers
4. Bake on a greased tin sheet until light brown
