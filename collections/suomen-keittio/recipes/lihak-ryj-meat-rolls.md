## Ingredients

- 2 pounds beef removed from bone
- 1.5 pounds pork
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- 1 onion
- Salt and pepper
- Boiling water or meat stock

## Directions

1. Cut beef into thin slices and pound, taking care not to break the slices.
2. Put onion juice and chopped parsley on the meat and add the pork cut in slices.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Roll the slices tightly together and tie with string.
5. Fry until all sides are browned.
6. Pour boiling water or meat stock over.
7. Let boil from 1 to 1.5 hours.
8. Make a gravy of the bouillon in the pan.
