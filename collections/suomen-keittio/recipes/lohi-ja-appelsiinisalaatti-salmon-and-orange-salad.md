## Ingredients

- 1 can of salmon
- 1 sour orange
- 1 cup minced lettuce leaves
- 1 orange
- 1 hard boiled egg
- salt
- pepper
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Rinse the salmon well
2. Cut in cubes and squeeze the juice of a sour orange over the salmon
3. To 1 cup of salmon, add 1 cup of minced lettuce leaves
4. Add 1 orange cut in pieces and 1 hard boiled egg chopped
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Mix in 3 tablespoons of mayonnaise
