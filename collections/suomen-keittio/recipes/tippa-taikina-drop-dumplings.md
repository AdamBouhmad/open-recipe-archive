## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 cupful of milk or water
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar for sweet dumplings
- 1 tablespoonful of melted butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful of baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks and add milk, butter, flour and salt.
2. Finally fold in stiffly beaten egg whites.
3. The batter should be of firm consistency; it is best to test first.
4. If it is too stiff, add egg white.
5. Cognac and a little lemon juice may be used as flavoring.
