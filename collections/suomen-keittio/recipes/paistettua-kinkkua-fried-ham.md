## Ingredients

- Slices of smoked ham
- Butter or lard

## Directions

1. Prepare as directed for broiled ham (place in cold water and cook slowly until almost done, then remove and wipe dry).
2. Heat the pan.
3. Fry quickly in butter or lard until brown on both sides.
4. Serve with fried eggs or with potatoes.
5. Alternatively, the ham may be fried without boiling first, if preferred.
