## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- salted water
- 1 quart of vinegar
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- white pepper, 1 teaspoonful
- cinnamon, 1 teaspoonful
- cloves, 1 teaspoonful
- ginger, 1 teaspoonful

## Directions

1. Clean mushrooms and let boil up in salted water
2. Place into jar
3. Boil up vinegar with sugar and spices
4. Drain over the mushrooms when it gets cool
5. Seal the jars and keep in a cold place
