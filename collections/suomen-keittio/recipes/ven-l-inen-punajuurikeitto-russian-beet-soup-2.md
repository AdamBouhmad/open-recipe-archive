## Ingredients

- 3 quarts of bouillon
- 6–8 beets
- 2–3 tomatoes
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- lemon juice or vinegar
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the beets and bake them in the oven until tender.
2. Peel and cut into slices and fry in butter (a few drops of lemon juice or vinegar on them will keep them nicely colored).
3. Sprinkle the flour on the beets, then slowly pour the boiling stock on them.
4. Let it boil about 10 minutes.
5. Peel the tomatoes, mash and add to the beets.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. The color can be improved with raw grated beet.
8. Serve with sour cream.
