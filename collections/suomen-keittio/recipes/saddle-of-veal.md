## Ingredients

- veal
- butter
- paper
- salt
- pork slices
- egg
- bread crumbs
- meat stock
- cream

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat and trim into a good shape.
2. Roll into a buttered paper and bake in oven same as roast veal.
3. Remove the paper 1/2 hour before you send the meat to the table.
4. Sprinkle with salt and let brown.
5. May be prepared also with pork by laying thinly sliced pork on the veal.
6. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
7. Bake as usually, basting often and adding meat stock into the pan.
8. Prepare the sauce of its own liquor and cream.
