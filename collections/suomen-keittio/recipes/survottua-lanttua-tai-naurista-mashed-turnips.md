## Ingredients

- 2 medium-sized turnips
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup rich milk
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons cream

## Directions

1. Cook 2 medium-sized turnips in salted water until tender.
2. Mash.
3. Make a sauce of 2 tablespoons butter, 1 tablespoon flour and 1 cup rich milk.
4. Season with salt and pepper, sweeten with sugar.
5. Mix with the mashed turnips.
6. Let boil.
7. Last of all add 2 yolks of egg mixed with 2 tablespoons cream.
