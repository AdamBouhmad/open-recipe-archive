## Ingredients

- 1 quart of cream
- 4 yolks
- ½ lbs. of sugar
- ½ lb. of almonds
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Peel and grate or pound almonds.
2. Cook in ½ quart of cream.
3. Beat sugar with yolks.
4. Pour cream slowly in it and whip in double-boiler until it is thickened.
5. Add the rest of the cream, when the mixture is cold.
6. Freeze.
7. The almonds may be browned with sugar in a fryingpan, cooled and chopped, if brown ice cream is desired.
