## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons milk or water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Separate the yolks from the whites.
2. Beat the whites stiff.
3. Set aside until the yolks are beaten.
4. Mix the yolks with milk or water and season.
5. Add the beaten whites to the yolk mixture.
6. Melt butter in an omelet pan and turn the eggs into the pan.
7. Place on the stove to fry slowly, turning the pan now and then so the omelet browns evenly.
8. If the surface feels soft, place the pan in the oven for a moment.
9. Fold once and turn onto a hot platter.
10. Serve plain or with sauce.
