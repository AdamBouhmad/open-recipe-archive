## Ingredients

- 1 squab
- thin slice of bacon
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- cream

## Directions

1. Clean a squab, lay on it a thin slice of bacon, put a little lump of butter in; sprinkle with salt and pepper.
2. Roast 20-30 minutes.
3. Thicken the gravy with a little cream.
4. Divide the squab lengthwise in two, remove the backbone.
5. Arrange on a platter and strain over it the gravy.
