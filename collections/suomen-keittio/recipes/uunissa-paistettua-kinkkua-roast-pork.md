## Ingredients

- fresh pork
- water
- salt
- pepper
- ginger
- mustard powder
- bread crumbs
- tomato

## Directions

1. Place the pork skin side down in a dripping pan
2. Pour a cupful of water over and put in a hot oven
3. When the surface is brown, sprinkle with salt and baste with drippings
4. Turn over and remove the skin when it begins to loosen
5. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, ginger, some mustard and bread crumbs
6. Return to the oven and roast until done, basting often
7. Place on a platter, remove the fat from the drippings
8. Thicken with flour and season with mustard, pepper and tomato
9. Serve with vegetables, brown beans, apple sauce or stewed prunes
