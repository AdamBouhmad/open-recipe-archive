## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons of chopped carrot and celery
- 2 slices of onion
- Some parsley
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 cloves
- 2 tablespoons tomato
- 2 teaspoons beef extract
- 2 1/2 cups water
- 1 package of gelatine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 1 egg white for clarification

## Directions

1. Place into a saucepan 2 tablespoons of chopped carrot and celery, 2 slices of onion, some parsley, 1 bay leaf, 2 cloves, 2 tablespoons tomato, 2 teaspoons beef extract and 2 1/2 cups water.
2. Cover let cook for 1/2 hour.
3. Add 1 package of gelatine, stir until dissolved.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Flavor with the juice of 2 lemons.
6. Clarify with an egg white.
