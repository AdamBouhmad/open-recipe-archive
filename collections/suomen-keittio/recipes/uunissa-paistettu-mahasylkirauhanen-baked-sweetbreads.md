## Ingredients

- Slices of bacon
- Carrots
- Celery
- Turnip
- Boiled sweetbreads
- Bouillon
- Brown sauce or mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Place slices of bacon, carrots, celery and turnip in a pan.
2. Cover the bacon with big pieces of boiled sweetbreads.
3. Add some bouillon so that the bottom of the pan is covered.
4. Bake for 20 minutes basting often.
5. Serve with brown or mushroom sauce.
