## Ingredients

- 8 crabs
- 1 cupful of cream or milk
- 1½ tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- ½ teaspoonful of lemon juice
- ¼ cupful of finely chopped mushrooms
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- mustard

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and cream.
2. Add the yolks and the chopped crab meat to it.
3. Flavor with a dash of mustard.
4. Mix well.
5. Wash the shells, fill with the above mixture.
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and dot with butter.
7. Brown in oven.
8. Serve on a folded napkin.
