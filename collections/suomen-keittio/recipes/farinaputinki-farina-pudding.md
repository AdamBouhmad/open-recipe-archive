## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup farina
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- Raisins
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook a porridge of milk and farina
2. Add the egg yolks beaten with sugar, salt and raisins
3. Beat the egg whites until firm
4. Mix the beaten egg whites with the porridge or place on top as meringue
5. Bake in a buttered mold until brown
