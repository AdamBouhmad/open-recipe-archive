## Ingredients

- 10 smelts
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- 1/2 tablespoonful of butter
- chopped parsley
- 1/2 cupful of chopped fried mushrooms
- 1/2 cupful of fish mousse
- cream
- thick milk sauce
- flour
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- Bearnaise or lobster sauce

## Directions

1. Clean 10 smelts taking out the backbones with a sharp knife.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and lemon juice and put aside until you have the stuffing ready.
3. Cook 1/2 tablespoonful of butter with a little chopped parsley.
4. Add 1/2 cupful of chopped fried mushrooms and 1/2 cupful of fish mousse.
5. If the mixture is too thick, add a little cream; if too thin, thicken with a little thick milk sauce.
6. Fill the smelts with this mixture.
7. Roll in flour, dip in egg and breadcrumbs.
8. Fry on both sides until brown in the oven.
9. If they begin to dry out, cover and let braise until done.
10. Serve with Bearnaise or lobster sauce.
