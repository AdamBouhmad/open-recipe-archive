## Ingredients

- 3 pints of fish stock or water
- 1 quart of clams
- 1 middle-sized onion
- 6 ozs. of salted pork
- 1 potato
- 1 carrot
- 1 tomato
- 1 teaspoonful of chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of pepper
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 cupfuls of milk or cream
- mace
- crackers

## Directions

1. Place clams on fire, in their own liquor, let boil for a few minutes.
2. Cut pork into cubes, and fry with chopped onion until brown.
3. Mix with flour and pour clam liquor and fish stock gently in it.
4. Put potatoes, carrot and tomato cut in cubes in it.
5. Boil until the vegetables are soft.
6. Add clams cut in pieces, parsley, crackers and milk into it.
7. Bring to the boiling point and send to the table.
