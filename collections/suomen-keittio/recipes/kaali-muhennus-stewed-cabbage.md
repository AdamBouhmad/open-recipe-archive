## Ingredients

- 1 (2 1/2 lbs.) cabbage
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 3 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1/2 quart of boiling water
- 1 cupful of water

## Directions

1. Cut cabbage into pieces
2. Parboil in salted water
3. Pour the water out
4. Stir butter, sugar, salt and 1/2 quart of boiling water into it
5. Let the cabbage simmer covered until tender (about 1 hour)
6. Mix one cupful of water with flour
7. Thicken the cabbage with it
8. Pour vinegar in
9. Let the stew boil 10 minutes
10. Serve with mutton or tongue
