## Ingredients

- 2 quarts meat stock
- 1 carrot, 1 onion
- 2 stalks celery, 1 head lettuce
- 1 1/2 cups string beans
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the vegetables and cut into long thin strips and brown in butter.
2. Add the hot meat stock and string beans.
