## Ingredients

- spring lamb
- butter
- salt
- lemon juice
- mint sauce

## Directions

1. Roast in a hot oven, having a little water in dripping-pan
2. Allow 15-18 minutes to a pound
3. When using the fore quarter have the bones well cracked or removed
4. Rub the space with butter, salt and lemon juice
5. Stuff
6. Serve with mint sauce
