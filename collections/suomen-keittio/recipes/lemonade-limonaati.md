## Ingredients

- 1 quart of water
- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- 1 cupful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook 1 quart of water and 2 cupfuls of sugar for 10 minutes
2. Mix with 1 cupful of lemon juice
3. Let it get cold, bottle up or serve immediately
