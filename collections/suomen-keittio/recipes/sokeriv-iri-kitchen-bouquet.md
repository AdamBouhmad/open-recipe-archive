## Ingredients

- 1 heaping tablespoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon water

## Directions

1. Cook sugar and water together on a quick fire until dark brown
2. Test it by cooling some of it
3. If it becomes firm, add some water, continue to boil
4. Bottle up
5. Use to color soups and sauces with
