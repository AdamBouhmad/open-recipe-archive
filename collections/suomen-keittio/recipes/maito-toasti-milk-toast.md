## Ingredients

- Toast bread
- Butter
- Salt
- Hot milk

## Directions

1. Toast bread
2. Spread with butter and a dash of salt
3. Pile in a serving dish
4. Pour some scalded milk over
5. Let stand covered in a hot place or in oven for a few minutes
6. Pour boiling milk on the soaked toast just before serving
