## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of macaroni
- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1/2 onion
- 2 cupfuls of meat stock or sauce
- 1 lb. of cooked chopped meat
- 3—4 tablespoonfuls of grated cheese
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni until tender in salted water; drain the water off.
2. Fry onion in butter.
3. Add meat and fry a little while.
4. Pour meat stock or sauce over.
5. Add cheese.
6. Layer in baking dish alternating macaroni and meat mixture, or mix macaroni with meat.
7. Brown in oven. Bread crumbs may be used instead of macaroni.
