## Ingredients

- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 teaspoonful minced parsley
- 1 teaspoonful anchovy paste
- 1 teaspoonful butter
- salt and pepper
- toast

## Directions

1. Remove the tops from the eggs.
2. Split in halves, remove the yolk.
3. Mix yolk well with anchovy paste and butter; season with salt and pepper.
4. Fill the whites with this mixture, rounding the top of the filling.
5. Garnish with parsley.
6. Place the filled halves of eggs on toast.
7. Serve hot.
