## Ingredients

- orange peels
- equal weight of sugar
- water, 1 cupful per pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Soak orange peels in cold water for 6 or 7 days, changing the water every day
2. Cook until tender
3. Drain
4. Have equal weights of orange peels and sugar, and allow 1 cupful of water to 1 pound of sugar
5. Cook the sugar in water to a thick syrup
6. Add to the syrup the orange peels and cook until the liquid has evaporated
7. Use for puddings and to garnish cakes with
