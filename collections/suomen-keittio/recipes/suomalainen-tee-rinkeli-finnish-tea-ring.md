## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 2 lbs. of flour
- 3 eggs
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 3/4 lb. of butter
- 1 cake of yeast
- melted butter
- chopped blanched almonds
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm milk, mix with 3 tablespoonfuls of flour and set in a warm place to rise.
2. Heat milk. Work butter to a cream.
3. Whip eggs and sugar in a bowl, add milk and flour into it to make a smooth paste.
4. Mix thoroughly with risen sponge and with the rest of the flour, lastly the butter.
5. Knead briskly and long.
6. Set in a warm place to rise and cover with a towel.
7. Knead the dough again on board.
8. Roll into a thin sheet, spread with melted butter, sprinkle with sugar and chopped blanched almonds.
9. Roll together, cut even from each end and join the ends to form a ring.
10. Place on a buttered baking sheet and cut with scissors into equal sized slices and turn them crosswise on both sides.
11. Set to rise. Brush over with egg, sprinkle with granulated sugar and peeled, chopped almonds.
12. Bake in a moderate oven.
