## Ingredients

- 4 cups rye flour
- boiling water or milk
- 1 yeast cake
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 1/2 cup lard or butter
- yellow rind of 4 oranges, chopped or grated
- salt
- white flour

## Directions

1. Mix 4 cups rye flour with boiling water just enough to soak the flour.
2. When cool add the risen sponge of 1 yeast cake, 3/4 cup molasses, 1/2 cup lard or butter, the yellow rind of 4 oranges chopped or grated and salt.
3. Add white flour enough to make a moderately thick dough.
4. Let rise.
5. Bake longer than white bread.
6. Brush the crust with water.
