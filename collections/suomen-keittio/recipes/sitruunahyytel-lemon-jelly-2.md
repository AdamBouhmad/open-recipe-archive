## Ingredients

- 3 cupfuls of water
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 4 lemons
- 1½ ozs. of gelatine (½ box)
- 2 whites of eggs and shells

## Directions

1. Put water, grated yellow lemon rind, lemon juice, sugar, gelatine, half beaten whites of eggs and shells in a saucepan.
2. Place the saucepan on the fire and stir until it begins to boil.
3. Simmer 5 minutes.
4. Strain through a cloth, wrung out of hot water.
5. Bring the unclear first juice back into the draining-cloth.
6. If you do not want to clarify the jelly, dissolve gelatine in ½ cupful of water.
7. Put the rest of the water with sugar and few thin slices of lemon rind on the fire.
8. Stir until the sugar has dissolved.
9. Mix with gelatine stirring until the gelatine has dissolved.
10. Remove from fire, add lemon juice, drain, pour into a mold to get firm.
