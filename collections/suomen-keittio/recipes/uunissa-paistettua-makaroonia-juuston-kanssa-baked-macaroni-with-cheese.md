## Ingredients

- boiled macaroni
- butter
- grated cheese
- milk sauce
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Put boiled macaroni into a buttered mold
2. Sprinkle with grated cheese
3. Cover with milk sauce
4. Add a sprinkling of bread crumbs
5. Dot with butter
6. If more cheese is preferred, put two layers of macaroni into the mold
7. Sprinkle both layers with cheese and cover with milk sauce
