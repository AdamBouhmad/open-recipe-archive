## Ingredients

- 1 cupful mashed potato
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls cream
- 3 tablespoonfuls bread crumbs
- 2 eggs, beaten
- flour enough to make a firm batter, about 1 cupful

## Directions

1. Mix mashed potato with butter, cream, bread crumbs, beaten eggs and flour enough to make a firm batter
2. Test first by dropping a little into boiling water
3. Drop by the spoonful into boiling salted water or into bouillon shortly before serving
