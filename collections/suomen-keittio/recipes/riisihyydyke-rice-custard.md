## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup rice
- 3 cups milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3 eggs
- salt
- lemon juice or vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in milk until soft, add butter and salt
2. Let cool some
3. Beat the yolks with sugar, add to the porridge
4. Flavor with lemon juice or vanilla according to taste
5. Beat the whites until firm, mix with the porridge
6. Bake in a buttered mold in a pan of water
7. When half baked, coat the top of the pudding with whites of egg beaten with sugar, if preferred
