## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Mix all above ingredients.
2. Heat timbale iron in hot fat.
3. Lower in batter to 3/4 of its height.
4. Immerse in hot fat.
5. When the timbale is light brown lift on paper and remove from the iron.
