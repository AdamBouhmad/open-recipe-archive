## Ingredients

- sour skimmed milk
- boiling water
- salt
- pepper
- cream
- chopped green pepper (optional)

## Directions

1. Have some sour skimmed milk in a bowl.
2. Pour boiling water carefully around the edges having the water come over the top of the milk.
3. Let stand for an hour.
4. Strain through a cloth, drain carefully.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Dilute with cream.
7. May be mixed with chopped green pepper according to taste.
