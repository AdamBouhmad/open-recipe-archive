## Ingredients

- young chickens
- butter
- chopped parsley
- salt
- bread crumbs
- water
- cream
- vegetables for garnish

## Directions

1. Clean young chickens
2. Mix butter and fine parsley
3. Place the butter mixture inside the chickens
4. Tie and roast in oven with a little butter in the pan
5. When they turn light brown, sprinkle salt and bread crumbs on top
6. Pour water into the pan so the chickens do not burn
7. Baste often with the liquid
8. Arrange on serving dish and garnish with vegetables around the sides
9. Prepare sauce from the liquid by adding a little cream
