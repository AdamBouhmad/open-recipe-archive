## Ingredients

- Liver
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Bacon
- Bouillon or water
- Onion (optional)

## Directions

1. Slice the liver, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
2. Dip in flour.
3. Fry thin slices of bacon, twice as many as you have slices of liver.
4. Remove the bacon from the pan.
5. Fry the liver in drippings slowly and turning often until done.
6. Add some bouillon, bring to boiling.
7. Dish, having the liver in center and strain the gravy over.
8. Liver may be fried with onion; have the onion removed before serving.
