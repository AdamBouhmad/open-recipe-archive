## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup milk
- 1/4 cup sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Rub the butter with the sugar until creamy
2. Add the yolks stirring briskly
3. Mix gradually with milk and flour sifted with baking powder
4. Last of all add the egg whites beaten firm
5. Bake for 1/2 hour in a buttered dome-shaped mold covered
6. Unmold, serve with hard, chocolate or fruit sauce
