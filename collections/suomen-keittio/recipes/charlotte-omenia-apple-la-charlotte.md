## Ingredients

- 3 large apples
- 1/2 teaspoon butter
- 2 tablespoons raisins
- 1 orange cut in slices
- 1 cup sugar
- Lemon juice and grated rind
- 1 tablespoon currant jelly
- Small round slices of bread
- Whipped cream

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cut in thin slices.
2. Put into a saucepan with sugar, butter, raisins and slices of orange.
3. Keep on the fire until hot.
4. Add lemon juice, rind and currant jelly.
5. Cover the bottom and the edges of a mold with small round slices of bread dipped in butter.
6. Fill with the apples.
7. Cover the top with a few slices of bread so as to prevent from burning.
8. Bake for 1 hour.
9. Serve unmoulded with whipped cream.
