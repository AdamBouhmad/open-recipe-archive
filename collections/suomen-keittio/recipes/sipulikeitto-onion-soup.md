## Ingredients

- more onions than in potato soup
- less potatoes than in potato soup
- vegetable stock or meat stock
- several kinds of vegetables

## Directions

1. Prepare as stated for potato soup by using more onions and less potatoes.
2. May be made of vegetable stock or having several kinds of vegetables cooked with the onion.
