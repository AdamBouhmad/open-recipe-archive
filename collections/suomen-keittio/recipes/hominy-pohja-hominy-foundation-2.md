## Ingredients

- 1 cup hominy
- 3 cups water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil hominy with water and salt for 5 to 10 minutes
2. Pack into a buttered shallow mold
3. Turn onto a suitable platter
4. Use as a foundation for various dishes
