## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- butter
- bread
- poached eggs
- milk sauce
- onion juice
- grated cheese
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Fry fresh mushrooms in butter.
2. Place the fried mushrooms on buttered toast.
3. Lay the toast in a heat-resistant mould.
4. Place poached eggs carefully on top.
5. Pour milk sauce mixed with onion juice and grated cheese over the eggs.
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and bake until browned.
7. Alternatively, use cooked finely chopped spinach seasoned with cream, salt and pepper, or fried tomato slices in place of mushrooms.
