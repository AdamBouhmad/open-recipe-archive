## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 5 cupfuls of water
- 5 eggs
- sugar
- whipped cream
- vanilla
- jam

## Directions

1. Boil water with butter.
2. Mix quickly with flour.
3. Stir over the fire until thick enough to easily remove from the pan.
4. Let cool.
5. Mix with eggs one by one.
6. Drop the mixture by heaping spoonfuls on a baking sheet.
7. Sprinkle thinly with sugar.
8. Bake in a moderate oven.
9. Serve when cold filled with whipped cream mixed with sugar, vanilla and jam.
