## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of cranberries
- 1/2 box of gelatine
- 1 cupful of sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- a little sherry wine

## Directions

1. Soak 1 cupful of cranberries in cold water
2. Cook until tender
3. Strain and chop fine
4. Add into the liquor of cranberries 1/2 box of gelatine, soaked in water
5. Add 1 cupful of sugar, juice of 1 lemon and a little sherry wine
6. Stir over the fire until the gelatine is thoroughly dissolved
7. Strain and mix with the cranberries
8. Let set, stirring now and then until it begins to thicken so as to mix the fruit evenly with the liquid jelly
9. Serve with cream
10. Chopped almonds make a fine flavor to it
