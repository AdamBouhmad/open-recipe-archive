## Ingredients

- string beans
- salted water
- lettuce leaves
- mayonnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Cut stringbeans lengthwise into shreds, cook in salted water until tender.
2. Let cool, arrange on lettuce leaves and cover with mayonnaise sauce.
