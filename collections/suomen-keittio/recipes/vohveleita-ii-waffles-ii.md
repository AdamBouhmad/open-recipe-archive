## Ingredients

- 3 cupfuls of cream
- 2 eggs
- 6 heaping tablespoonfuls of flour
- 4 tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Beat cream to a stiff froth.
2. Whip eggs with sugar to a froth, mix with cream.
3. Add flour and let stand for 1 hour.
4. Heat waffle-iron, butter it and fry waffles to a delicate brown on both sides.
5. Sprinkle with sugar and serve with jam.
