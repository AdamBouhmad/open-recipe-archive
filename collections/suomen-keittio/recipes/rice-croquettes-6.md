## Ingredients

- boiled rice
- yolks of two eggs
- a little bread crumbs
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Mix boiled rice with yolks of two eggs and a little bread crumbs
2. Let stand a little while in a cold place
3. Form the mixture into round or oval balls
4. Dip in egg and bread crumbs
5. Fry in butter or in lard
