## Ingredients

- 1 lobster
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare for broiling as directed.
2. Place on a buttered broiler.
3. Broil for 8 minutes on meat side.
4. Turn over and continue broiling for 6 minutes on shell side.
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and dot with butter.
6. Put in oven for a few minutes.
7. Open the claws.
