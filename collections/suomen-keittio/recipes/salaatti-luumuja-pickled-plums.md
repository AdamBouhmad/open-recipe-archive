## Ingredients

- 6 pounds plums
- 7 cups sugar
- 2 cups vinegar
- 1 tablespoon cloves
- Some cinnamon

## Directions

1. Remove the stones from the plums
2. Boil the sugar with vinegar and spices
3. Add the plums and simmer until tender, about 1/2 hour
4. Put into glass jars
5. Cover airtight
