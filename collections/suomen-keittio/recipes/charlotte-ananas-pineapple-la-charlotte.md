## Ingredients

- 1 pineapple
- 7 leaves of gelatine
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1 pint of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Remove skin and eyes from pineapple and chop
2. Dissolve 7 leaves of gelatine in pineapple juice by stirring on the fire
3. Add 1 cupful of sugar into it and mix it with the chopped pineapple
4. When cool, add 1 pint of whipped cream
5. Put in a wet mold to form
