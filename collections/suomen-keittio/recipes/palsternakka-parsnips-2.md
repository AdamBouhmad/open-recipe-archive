## Ingredients

- parsnips
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Wash parsnips carefully.
2. Cook them in salted water 1 hour or until tender.
3. Drain off the water and peel the parsnips.
4. Cut lengthwise in 1/4 inch thick pieces.
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
6. Dip in melted butter and flour and brown in butter on both sides.
