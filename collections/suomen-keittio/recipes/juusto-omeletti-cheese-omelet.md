## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup cream or milk
- 3 tablespoons grated cheese
- Salt and pepper
- Butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks and whites separately.
2. Mix the whipped cream or milk, cheese and salt with the yolks.
3. Add the whites.
4. Turn into a buttered omelet mold.
5. Bake.
6. Serve hot.
