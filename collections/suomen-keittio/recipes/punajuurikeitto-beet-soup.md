## Ingredients

- 3 quarts bouillon
- 5 or 8 beets
- 2 or 3 tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- lemon juice or vinegar
- salt and pepper
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Wash the beets and bake in oven until tender.
2. Peel and cut into small pieces.
3. Cook in butter, dropping some lemon juice or vinegar on them so as to keep the color.
4. Dust the beets with the flour and add gradually the bouillon.
5. Cook for 10 minutes.
6. Peel the tomatoes, grind, and add to the beets.
7. Season with salt and pepper.
8. The color may be improved by adding some raw grated beet to the soup.
9. Serve with sour cream.
10. Alternatively, the beets may be cooked until tender by simmering them in butter for a few hours covered.
11. Beet soup may be made by using vegetable stock instead of bouillon.
12. Last of all add the yolks of eggs and the cream.
