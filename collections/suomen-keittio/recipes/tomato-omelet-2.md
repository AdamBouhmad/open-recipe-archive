## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 3 tomatoes
- 3/4 cupful of milk
- chopped parsley
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Butter the omelet dish, lay the skinned tomato-slices on the bottom of it.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Beat the eggs to a froth, add milk, melted butter, pepper, parsley and salt.
4. Pour the mixture over the tomatoes and bake until firm in a moderate oven.
