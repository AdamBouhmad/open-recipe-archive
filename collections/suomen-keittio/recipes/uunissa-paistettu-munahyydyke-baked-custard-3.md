## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 cupful of sugar
- a little salt and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat eggs, add sugar, salt and nutmeg, pour gradually heated milk into mixture.
2. Put into a buttered mold and place in oven in a water bath until it sets.
