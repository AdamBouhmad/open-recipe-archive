## Ingredients

- apple sauce
- brandy
- carmine coloring
- pears
- sugar
- 1 cup apple juice
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- cake or puff paste
- cream

## Directions

1. Mix apple sauce with brandy and carmine coloring, beat well
2. Pare the pears, split and cook with sugar
3. Let cool
4. Baste with a sauce made of 1 cup apple juice, 3 tablespoons lemon juice, 2 tablespoons cornstarch, 4 tablespoons sugar, carmine coloring
5. Place the pears on a cake or puff paste having the apple sauce in the center
6. Garnish with cream
