## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
- 2 cups flour
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 12 egg whites
- Some salt

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks with sugar, add lemon juice, egg whites beaten until firm and last of all mix in lightly the flour.
2. Bake for 1 hour in a buttered tin.
