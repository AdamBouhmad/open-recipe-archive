## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of spinach leaves
- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 cupful of cream or milk
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of soda
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the roots and spoiled leaves
2. Cook in water mixed with salt and 1/2 teaspoonful of soda
3. Drain off the water and chop the spinach fine
4. Put the butter and flour in kettle and add the chopped spinach
5. Add spinach stock, milk, salt and sugar
6. Let the stew boil 10 minutes
7. Pour in the dish and garnish with hard boiled eggs
8. Serve with meat or fish
