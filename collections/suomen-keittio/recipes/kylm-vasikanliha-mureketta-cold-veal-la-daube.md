## Ingredients

- 3 pounds veal with bones
- 1 pound pork
- salt
- whole peppercorns
- onion
- carrot
- celery
- parsley
- hard boiled eggs
- mayonnaise or tart sauces

## Directions

1. Place veal and pork on fire in cold water with salt, pepper, onion, carrot, celery and parsley
2. Cook until done
3. Remove from bouillon and force through the meat chopper
4. Garnish the bottom of a mold with hard boiled eggs
5. Fill the mold with meat
6. Pour some bouillon over
7. Place in a cold place to set
8. Unmold and cut into slices
9. Serve with mayonnaise or any kind of tart sauces
