## Ingredients

- half-cooked eggs
- warm meat stuffing
- spinach or mashed potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut a slice off the tops of half-cooked eggs
2. Take out the yolks
3. Stuff the eggs with warm meat stuffing
4. Replace the tops
5. Serve with spinach or mashed potatoes
