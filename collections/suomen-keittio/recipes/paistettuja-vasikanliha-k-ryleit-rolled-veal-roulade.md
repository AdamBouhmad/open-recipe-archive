## Ingredients

- veal steak
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- bread
- mushrooms
- liver or boiled ham
- flour
- bouillon or water

## Directions

1. Cut veal steak into thin slices
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and flavor with lemon juice
3. Spread with soaked bread mixed with mushrooms, liver or boiled ham
4. Roll together and tie with string
5. Roll in flour and fry until brown
6. Cover with bouillon or water and cook until done
7. Make a gravy of the bouillon in the pan
