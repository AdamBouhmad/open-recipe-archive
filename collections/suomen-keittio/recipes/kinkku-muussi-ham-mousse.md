## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound ham
- 1/2 pound chicken breast
- 2 egg whites
- 1/2 pint cream

## Directions

1. Force ham with chicken breast through meat chopper several times.
2. Add egg whites and pound well.
3. Press through a sieve.
4. Dilute gradually with 1/2 pint of cream or until soft enough.
5. Test consistency by dropping 1/2 spoonful of mixture into boiling salted water.
6. If too thick, dilute with cream; if too soft, add an egg white.
