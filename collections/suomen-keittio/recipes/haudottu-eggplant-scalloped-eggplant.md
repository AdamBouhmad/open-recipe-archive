## Ingredients

- Eggplant
- Water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 onion
- 1 green pepper
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 cup tomato

## Directions

1. Peel the eggplant and cut in cubes 2/3 inch thick.
2. Cook in scanty water until tender.
3. Drain.
4. Cook 2 tablespoons butter with 1/2 onion and 1 green pepper chopped.
5. Add the eggplant, 1/2 cup bread crumbs and 1 egg.
6. Put the mixture into a buttered mold.
7. Fill the mold with a cup of tomato.
8. Bake from 1/2 to 1 hour.
