## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of rice
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 hard boiled chopped eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 8 prunes

## Directions

1. Boil the rice in salted water and drain off the water.
2. Mix with melted butter, chopped eggs, prunes and salt.
3. Stuff the fish with this mixture.
