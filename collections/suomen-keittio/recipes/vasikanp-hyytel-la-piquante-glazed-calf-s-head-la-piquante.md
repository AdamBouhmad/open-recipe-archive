## Ingredients

- boiled calf's head
- milk sauce
- toasted bread
- tomato jelly
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup brown sauce
- 1 cup tomato
- chopped parsley
- fresh chopped cucumber

## Directions

1. Cut the boiled calf's head into pieces
2. Cover with milk sauce
3. Place on toast
4. Line with liquid jelly of tomato
5. Prepare Piquante sauce by cooking together 1 tablespoon of butter, 1 tablespoon of flour, 1 cup of brown sauce, 1 cup of tomato, chopped parsley, and fresh chopped cucumber
6. Serve with Piquante sauce poured around the meat on the platter
