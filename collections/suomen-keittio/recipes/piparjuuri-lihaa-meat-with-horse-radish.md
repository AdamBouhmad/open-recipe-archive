## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of lean meat
- 3 quarts of water
- 2 tablespoonfuls of salt
- 1 onion
- 10 peppercorns
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 3 cupfuls of meat stock or milk
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1/2 tablespoonful of vinegar
- 1/2 tablespoonful of sugar
- 4—5 tablespoonfuls of grated horse-radish

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat with cloth wrung out from hot water.
2. Put on fire in boiling water.
3. Skim and add salt, pepper and onion.
4. Cook covered until tender on a slow fire.
5. For the sauce: Mix butter with flour in saucepan.
6. Add meat stock and seasonings.
7. Let boil.
8. Remove from the fire.
9. Stir beaten egg yolks into it.
10. At the last mix with horse-radish.
