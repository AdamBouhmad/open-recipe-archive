## Ingredients

- fresh tomatoes
- boiling water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- cornstarch or bread crumbs

## Directions

1. If using fresh tomatoes, they must first be peeled.
2. Keep them in boiling water for a few seconds so as to remove the skin.
3. Peel them.
4. Slice them.
5. Put in a pot and cook until tender with butter, salt and pepper.
6. Thicken with cornstarch mixed with water or add bread crumbs.
7. May be served without thickening.
