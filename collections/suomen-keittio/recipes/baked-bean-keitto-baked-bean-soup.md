## Ingredients

- 3 cups baked beans
- 2 quarts water or meat stock
- 1 onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- ¾ cup tomato

## Directions

1. Cook the beans with onion and tomato until tender.
2. Press through a strainer.
3. Cook the butter and the flour in a saucepan, add gradually the soup, bring to boil.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
