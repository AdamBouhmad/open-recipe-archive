## Ingredients

- fish
- 1 cupful of flour
- 1 1/2 tablespoonfuls of salt
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, wash, wipe dry and cut in pieces.
2. Mix 1 cupful of flour with 1 1/2 tablespoonfuls of salt.
3. Roll the pieces of fish in it.
4. Brown the butter in pan and fry the fishes until lightly browned.
5. Cover and put the pan into oven.
6. Bake until done.
7. Pour a little cream into the pan, let boil up and pour on the fish.
8. For fish fried in lard, roll in cracker dust, in eggs and again in cracker-dust.
9. Take care that the fish is completely covered with egg and crackerdust, so that no opening is left for the lard to enter.
