## Ingredients

- Boiled sweetbreads
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Butter
- Tomato sauce or creamed asparagus

## Directions

1. Cut boiled sweetbreads into round slices.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
4. Cook in butter not allowing to brown.
5. Serve with tomato sauce or creamed asparagus.
