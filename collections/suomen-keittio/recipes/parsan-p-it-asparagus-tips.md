## Ingredients

- asparagus tips
- salt
- water
- butter
- flour
- milk
- egg yolks
- rolls or bread

## Directions

1. Cut the tips off the asparagus and put on the fire in boiling salted water.
2. Cook until tender.
3. Prepare a milk sauce mixed with 1 or 2 egg yolks.
4. Add the asparagus tips to the sauce carefully and heat but do not bring to boiling.
5. Serve on toast or in cups made of rolls (cut off the end of each roll, hollow out the cavity, and dry a little in the oven).
