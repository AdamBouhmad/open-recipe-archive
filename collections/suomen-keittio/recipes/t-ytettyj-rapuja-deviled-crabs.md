## Ingredients

- 8 crabs
- 1 cup cream or milk
- 1 1/4 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 1/2 cups chopped mushrooms
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 hard boiled yolks of egg

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and milk or cream
2. Add the yolks chopped and the meat
3. Season with mustard according to taste
4. Stir well
5. Wash the shells, fill with the mixture, sprinkle with bread crumbs, dot with butter
6. Brown in oven
7. Serve on a platter
