## Ingredients

- salmon
- mayonnaise sauce
- lettuce
- boiled potatoes
- artichokes
- beets
- carrots
- cauliflower
- string beans
- green peas
- tomatoes
- mushrooms
- lemon halves or hard-boiled eggs
- French dressing

## Directions

1. Boil the salmon as directed, skin while hot; and place on a platter to cool.
2. Cover with thick mayonnaise sauce.
3. Garnish the dish with lettuce.
4. Arrange neatly on the leaves boiled vegetables, as potatoes, artichokes, beets, carrots, cauliflower, string beans, green peas, tomatoes and mushrooms cut in dice.
5. Fill cups made of halves of lemons or whites of hard-boiled eggs with mayonnaise sauce or salmon jelly and place around the fish.
6. Sprinkle the fish with chopped yolks of eggs and pour French dressing over the lettuce leaves.
