## Ingredients

- middle part of fish
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- onion juice
- melted butter
- cracker dust
- eggs, whipped

## Directions

1. Cut the middle part of fish into pieces two inches long
2. Split the pieces
3. Remove the bones and skin with a sharp knife
4. Rub with salt, pepper, lemon and onion juice and let stand about 1/2 hour
5. Wipe and dip in melted butter, cracker dust, whipped egg and again in cracker dust
6. Fry using enough butter for frying
7. Serve with Tartar or tomato sauce
