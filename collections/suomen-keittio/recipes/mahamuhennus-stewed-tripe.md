## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup milk
- salt
- pepper
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 cup boiled tripe, cut in cubes

## Directions

1. Prepare a gravy of 1 tablespoon of butter, 1 tablespoon of flour and 1 cup of milk.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Flavor with 1 teaspoon of lemon juice.
4. Mix with 1 cup of boiled tripe cut in cubes.
5. Stir over the fire until thoroughly hot.
6. Serve immediately.
