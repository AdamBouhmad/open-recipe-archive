## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 cup water
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as voitaikina (puff paste).
2. Roll the paste thin into a round cake.
3. Place outside the pie mold, perforate with holes around and moisten the edges with water.
4. Cut a narrow long strip from the paste, which you place along the moistened edge.
5. Brush with egg.
6. Place the mold on some vessel in the oven and bake until light brown.
7. Remove the crust neatly from the mold and place inside the mold to cool.
8. Alternatively: place the rolled paste in the mold and let the paste hang over the edge about one inch wide.
9. Turn the paste inside the edge, perforate a few holes in the bottom, place thin paper on the crust and fill the mold with rice or dry peas so that the paste cannot rise unevenly.
10. Brush the edge with egg and bake at regular oven heat.
11. When the crust is done remove the rice and paper.
