## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt
- water
- baking soda
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Scrape asparagus and rinse in cold water.
2. Tie in bunches so that they will not break while boiling.
3. Put on the fire in boiling salted water.
4. Cook for 15 or 20 minutes.
5. When done, drain quickly and untie the bunches.
6. Arrange on a serving platter or on toast.
7. Serve with melted butter mixed with lemon juice or baste with melted butter and garnish with lemon.
