## Ingredients

- ½ lb. of butter
- ½ lb. of flour
- ½ lb. of sugar
- 5 eggs
- juice of ½ lemon
- ½ cupful of currants
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder

## Directions

1. Work butter to a froth, add eggs, one at a time, alternately with 2 tablespoonfuls of flour and 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar.
2. Lastly add the rest of the flour and sugar, mixed with baking powder.
3. Stir lemon juice, a little grated lemon rind and currants into the mixture.
4. Pour in a buttered mold and bake in oven about ¾—1 hour.
