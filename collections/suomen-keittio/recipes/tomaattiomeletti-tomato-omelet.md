## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 3 tomatoes
- 1 cup milk
- Chopped parsley
- Salt and pepper
- Butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Lay the skinned sliced tomatoes in a buttered pan.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, add a little melted butter to it.
3. Beat the eggs, add milk, salt and parsley.
4. Pour over the tomatoes and bake until firm.
