## Ingredients

- 1 wild duck
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 small onion sliced
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 cupful of water
- 3 cupfuls of tomatoes
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the wild duck in nice pieces, dip in flour and fry in butter with onion.
2. Add water and tomatoes, cook tightly covered about 1/2 hour.
3. Remove the duck, strain the gravy, season with salt and pepper.
4. Arrange the duck on a serving dish, pour over it the gravy and serve with rice.
