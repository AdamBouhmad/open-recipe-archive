## Ingredients

- boiled macaroni
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- cheese
- strained tomato sauce
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Put a layer of boiled macaroni into a buttered mold
2. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, onion juice and cheese
3. Cover with strained tomato sauce
4. Add another layer of macaroni and continue until the mold is full
5. Have a layer of tomato on top
6. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top and dot with butter
7. Bake in the oven until the surface is brown
