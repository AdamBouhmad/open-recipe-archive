## Ingredients

- 10 smelts
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- onion, chopped
- 1/2 cup fried mushrooms, chopped
- 1/2 cup fish mousse
- cream
- milk sauce
- flour
- egg
- bread crumbs
- Bearnaise sauce or lobster sauce

## Directions

1. Clean about 10 smelts and remove the backbones with a sharp thin knife.
2. Starting from the head, slip the knife along the backbone loosening the flesh from the bone on both sides.
3. Break off the bone carefully to prevent the tails from breaking.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, flavor with lemon juice.
5. Lay aside until the stuffing is made.
6. Cook 1/2 tablespoon of butter with some chopped onion.
7. Add 1/2 cup of chopped fried mushrooms and 1/2 cup of fish mousse.
8. If too thick, dilute with cream or thicken with milk sauce, if too thin.
9. Stuff the smelts.
10. Roll in flour, dip in egg and bread crumbs.
11. Bake until brown on both sides.
12. If they become too dry, cover and leave until done.
13. Serve with Bearnaise or lobster sauce.
