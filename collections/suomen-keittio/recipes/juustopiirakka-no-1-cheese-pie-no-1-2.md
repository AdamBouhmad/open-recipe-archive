## Ingredients

- 3 cups grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- Grated yellow rind of a lemon
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup whipped cream or plain milk
- 3 tablespoons currants
- 3 tablespoons chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Mix the cornstarch and sugar together with the cheese.
2. Add cream or milk.
3. Add the seasonings and whipped eggs separately.
4. Bake in an open crust.
