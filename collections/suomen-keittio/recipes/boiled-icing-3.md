## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of water
- 3/4 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- white of an egg
- lemon or vanilla

## Directions

1. Put 1 cupful of sugar, 1/2 cupful of water and 3/4 teaspoonful of cream of tartar in a sauce-pan, let boil until syrup will thread when dropped from tip of spoon.
2. Beat white of an egg to a stiff froth, pour syrup gradually on it, whipping well.
3. Flavor with lemon or vanilla.
