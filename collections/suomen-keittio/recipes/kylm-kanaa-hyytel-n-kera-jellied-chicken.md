## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- 2 stalks of celery
- 1 quart chicken stock
- 1/2 cup tomato
- 2 teaspoons meat extract
- 7 tablespoons gelatine dissolved in water, or 10 leaves of gelatine
- 2 egg whites
- hard boiled eggs
- beets
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the chicken and place on the fire to cook with salt, pepper, onion and 2 stalks of celery.
2. Let simmer until tender.
3. Remove the skin and bones and cut into nice pieces.
4. Take 1 quart of chicken stock, 1/2 cup of tomato, 2 teaspoons of meat extract, 7 tablespoons of gelatine dissolved in water or 10 leaves of gelatine and 2 whites of egg.
5. Whip over the fire until boiling.
6. Let simmer for 5 minutes.
7. Strain through a fine cloth.
8. Cover the bottom of a jelly mold with cleared liquid jelly.
9. Let thicken.
10. Garnish the mold with hard boiled eggs, beets and parsley.
11. Lay the pieces of chicken into mold and fill the mold with liquid jelly.
12. Put in a cold place to set.
