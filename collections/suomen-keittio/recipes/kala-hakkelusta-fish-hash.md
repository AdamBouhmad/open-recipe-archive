## Ingredients

- cold prepared fish
- cold boiled potatoes
- salt and pepper
- lard or butter
- 1 onion

## Directions

1. Take cold, prepared fish and the same amount of cold boiled potatoes and chop them separately into fine pieces.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Melt a piece of lard or put a little butter in a pan, cook one finely chopped onion in it.
4. Add the fish and potato hash to it and stir until it is heated.
5. Then fry it brown on the bottom.
6. Turn like an omelette and place on a serving-dish.
