## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 tablespoon grated horseradish
- 4 tablespoons lemon juice
- 3 tablespoons tomato catsup
- A few drops of tobasco
- Salt
- Paprika
- Small oysters

## Directions

1. Make a dressing of vinegar, grated horseradish, lemon juice, tomato catsup, a few drops of tobasco, salt and paprika.
2. Place on ice to cool.
3. Wash small oysters and chill on ice.
4. Serve ice cold in small glasses, five oysters in each glass and the dressing poured over the oysters.
