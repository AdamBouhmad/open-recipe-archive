## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons cardamom
- 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 10 teaspoons cream
- 1/2 cup melted butter
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 7 or 8 cups flour

## Directions

1. Work eggs and sugar to a froth
2. Add spices and cream
3. Mix soda well with some flour and add
4. Mix in flour enough to make a soft batter
5. Work in the melted butter and the rest of the flour
6. Form the dough into small balls
7. Bake on a buttered baking sheet in oven until light brown
