## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup each raisins, currants, walnuts and almonds cut in pieces
- 1 cup yellow rind of lemon cut in pieces
- 1/2 teaspoon each cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Rub until creamy the butter with the sugar, add molasses, continue rubbing the mixture for a while.
2. Mix in the egg yolks beaten well, milk, spices, currants and raisins rolled in flour, cream of tartar and soda sifted with flour.
3. Last of all fold in the egg whites beaten until firm.
4. Bake 1 1/2 hours.
