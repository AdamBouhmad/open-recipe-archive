## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Mix together all the ingredients.
2. Heat a timbale iron in hot lard.
3. Lower into the batter 3/4 of its height.
4. Immerse into hot lard.
5. When light brown lift onto paper and remove from the iron.
