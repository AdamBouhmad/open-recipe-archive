## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 8 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 8 tablespoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs and sugar to a thick froth
2. Add flour slowly into it
3. Lastly mix with stiffly beaten whites of eggs
4. Bake in a buttered mold in a moderate oven nearly 3/4-1 hour
5. Garnish, when cold, with white icing
