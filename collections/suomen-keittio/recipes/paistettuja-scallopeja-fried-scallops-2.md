## Ingredients

- scallops
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- melted butter
- flour
- egg
- crackerdust
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Bring the fish to the boiling point.
2. Drain off the water.
3. Wipe and put in a dish with salt, pepper, lemon juice and a little melted butter.
4. Put aside for 10 min.
5. Roll in flour and fry in butter or lard.
6. Alternatively, dip first in flour, then in egg, then in crackerdust and cook in lard.
