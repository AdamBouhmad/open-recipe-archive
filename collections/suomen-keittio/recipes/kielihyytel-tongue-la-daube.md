## Ingredients

- 1 cup ground tongue
- 1/2 cup whipped cream
- 3 tablespoons gelatine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mix the tongue with salt, pepper, lemon juice, gelatine and cream.
2. Line the mold with aspic.
3. Garnish with red pepper and slices of hard boiled egg.
4. Fill the mold with the tongue mixture.
5. Let set.
6. Serve unmolded with lettuce leaves.
