## Ingredients

- 1 head of cabbage
- 4 or 5 tablespoons brown sugar
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Pepper
- Butter
- Water

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves, scoop out the stem end.
2. Fill the cavity with brown sugar, salt, nutmeg and pepper.
3. Brown some butter in a pan, place the head of cabbage in, cover.
4. Fry slowly until tender.
5. Baste with salted water when becoming dry and turn now and then.
6. May be baked in oven covered.
