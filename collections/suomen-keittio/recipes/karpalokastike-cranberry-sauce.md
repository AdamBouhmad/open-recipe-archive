## Ingredients

- 1 quart cranberries
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups water

## Directions

1. Rinse 1 quart of cranberries
2. Boil with 2 cups of sugar and 2 cups of water
3. Cook slowly to a smooth pulp
4. Put into a mold to set strained or unstrained
5. If strained, mix the sugar with the strained pulp and heat until the sugar is dissolved
