## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 yeast cake

## Directions

1. Whip eggs with sugar
2. Mix with warmed milk
3. Add yeast and flour
4. Let rise
5. Force through a pastry bag with a very narrow socket into boiling lard forming into half an inch thick and two inches wide zig-zags
6. Turn with a perforated spoon
7. When lightly browned remove from the fat
8. Place on brown paper to drain
9. Sprinkle with sugar
