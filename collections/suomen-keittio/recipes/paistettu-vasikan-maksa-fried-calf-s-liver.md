## Ingredients

- Calf's liver
- Salt
- Pepper
- Water
- Cream

## Directions

1. Cut in slices.
2. Rinse and wipe.
3. Fry on both sides until brown.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
5. Add some water so as not to scorch.
6. Bring to boiling.
7. Add a little cream.
