## Ingredients

- ½ pound almond paste
- ½ pound sugar
- 3 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon flour (if needed)

## Directions

1. Work almond paste and sugar together on the baking board.
2. Add slowly 3 egg whites.
3. Rub until the mixture is smooth.
4. Drop by the tiny spoonful on paper.
5. Place the paper on a baking sheet.
6. Bake in a slow oven.
7. If not thick enough to hold together, the batter may be mixed with 1 tablespoon flour.
8. Instead of almonds, chopped walnuts, hickory nuts, cocoanut, dates, raisins, figs and prunes may be used plain or mixed.
