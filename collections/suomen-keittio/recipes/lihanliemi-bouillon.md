## Ingredients

- 5 pounds lean beef with bones
- 2 pounds veal and bones
- 1 pound chicken or some other fowl
- 8 quarts water
- 1/2 cup each: carrot, onion, and celery
- 1 parsnip, beet and turnip
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 12 peppercorns
- 5 cloves
- 2 sprigs parsley
- 1 pound lean beef, chopped
- 2 egg whites, beaten
- egg shells

## Directions

1. Prepare as directed for soup stock.
2. To clarify the stock, pour it into the kettle after skimming the fat.
3. Add 1 pound of chopped lean beef, 2 beaten egg whites and the shells of the eggs.
4. Stir until it starts to boil.
5. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
6. Place aside, let stand for 5 minutes.
7. Strain through a fine cloth.
8. Serve.
