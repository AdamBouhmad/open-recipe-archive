## Ingredients

- oysters
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- aspic
- toast
- red pepper
- chopped jelly

## Directions

1. Place the oysters on the fire
2. Mix with salt, pepper and lemon juice
3. Bring to the boil
4. Wipe and put in two rows on a platter
5. Prepare the aspic of the liquor
6. Baste the oysters with the aspic
7. Arrange on toast
8. Garnish with red pepper, lemon and chopped jelly
