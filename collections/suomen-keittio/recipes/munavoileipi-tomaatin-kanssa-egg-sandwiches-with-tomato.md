## Ingredients

- salad leaf
- tomato
- chopped egg
- salt
- paprika
- mayonnaise
- mustard

## Directions

1. Place a tomato slice on a salad leaf.
2. Add finely chopped egg, season with salt and paprika.
3. Neatly set with a knife on top of the tomato.
4. Cover with mayonnaise prepared with mustard.
