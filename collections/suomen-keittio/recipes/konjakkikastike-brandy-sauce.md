## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup brandy
- 1 1/4 cup water

## Directions

1. Cook a light brown syrup of the sugar and 1/2 cup of water
2. Add the rest of the water
3. Let cool until not quite cold
4. Pour over the pudding shortly before serving
5. Add carefully the brandy and light it
6. Serve with plum and marrow puddings
