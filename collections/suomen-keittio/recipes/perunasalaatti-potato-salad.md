## Ingredients

- potatoes
- 1 small onion
- salt
- 1 cup celery
- 1 dill pickle or beet
- mayonnaise dressing
- lettuce
- hard boiled eggs
- beets

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes in jackets.
2. Let cool.
3. Peel and cut in thin slices.
4. Have 4 cups of potato, mix with 1 small onion grated and some salt.
5. Shortly before serving add 1 cup of celery, 1 dill pickle or beet.
6. Mix with boiled mayonnaise dressing.
7. Serve on a bed of lettuce garnished around with hard boiled egg and beets.
