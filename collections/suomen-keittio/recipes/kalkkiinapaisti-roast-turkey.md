## Ingredients

- 1 turkey
- ¼ pound bacon
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 tablespoons butter
- water
- flour
- cream

## Directions

1. Clean the turkey, rub with salt inside and out.
2. Line with slices of bacon and place in a buttered pan.
3. Put in a hot oven.
4. When brown sprinkle with salt, add hot water to the drippings.
5. Baste often.
6. If the oven is too hot, cover the turkey with a buttered paper.
7. Allow 2 or 3 hours for roasting.
8. Make a gravy of the drippings by adding some flour and cream.
9. Skim off the fat carefully.
