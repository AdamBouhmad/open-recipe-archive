## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- chicken breast for mousse
- truffles
- red pepper

## Directions

1. Bone the chicken and remove the flesh carefully so the skin does not tear
2. Make mousse from the chicken breast
3. Place the mousse mixture inside the skin
4. Intersperse with truffles and red pepper cut into long slices
5. Sew closed
6. Wrap in cloth and steam until firm to the touch
7. Remove cloth and place in oven to dry briefly without browning
8. Place on a serving dish
9. Cut lengthwise down the middle and then crosswise into neat slices
10. Serve with Holland sauce
