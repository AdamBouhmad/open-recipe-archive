## Ingredients

- remains of roast mutton
- 3 quarts of water
- 2 stalks of celery
- 1 small pickle onion
- 1 small parsnip
- 2 sprigs of parsley
- salt and pepper
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 yolks
- 1/2 cupful of cream

## Directions

1. Cut the bones and put into cold water.
2. Place on the fire.
3. When coming to boiling point add the browned vegetables and seasonings into it.
4. Let cook slowly for a few hours.
5. Thicken with butter and flour.
6. Strain and season.
7. Whip the yolks with cream.
8. Pour the soup slowly into it whipping well.
