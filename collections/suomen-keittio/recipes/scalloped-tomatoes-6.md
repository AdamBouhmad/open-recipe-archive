## Ingredients

- 1 can of tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- sugar
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Take 1 can of tomatoes and drain
2. Season the tomatoes with salt, pepper, a small quantity of onion juice and sugar
3. Butter a mould
4. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
5. Lay the tomatoes in it
6. Sprinkle with buttered bread crumbs
7. Bake in oven about 15 minutes
8. Serve in the same dish
