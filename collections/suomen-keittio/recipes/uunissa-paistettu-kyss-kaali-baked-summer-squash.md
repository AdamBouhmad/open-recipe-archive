## Ingredients

- summer squash
- butter
- lemon juice or syrup
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the squash in nice pieces
2. Remove the seeds
3. Bake in the lower part of oven for an hour or until tender
4. Baste with melted butter flavored with lemon juice or syrup, according to taste
5. Serve in shells garnished with parsley
