## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 pounds of steak
- 4 ounces of veal
- 3 ounces of pork
- 5 tablespoonfuls of breadcrumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 cupful of meat stock
- 1/2 onion
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter

## Directions

1. Take a 12-inch long, 7-inch wide, and 1-inch thick piece of steak and pound it well.
2. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper on top.
3. Grind the veal and pork finely.
4. Mix the breadcrumbs with the meat stock and add the onion fried, egg, parsley, salt and pepper.
5. Mix this mixture with the ground meat and knead it well.
6. Spread the filling on the meat, roll it up and tie it with cord.
7. Put butter in a pan, place the meat in it and roast in the oven until brown.
8. Add meat stock or water and roast for 3/4 to 1 hour, basting often.
9. Take the meat on a platter and remove the cord.
10. Make the sauce from the same stock by adding flour, meat stock and cream.
