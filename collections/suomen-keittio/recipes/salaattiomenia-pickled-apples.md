## Ingredients

- 9 pounds small apples
- 8 cups sugar
- 2 cups water
- 4 cups vinegar
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon and cloves

## Directions

1. Pare the apples.
2. Place them in layers with sugar into a jar, pour vinegar and water over and let stand covered over night.
3. Drain the juice, put into a kettle over the fire with the spices, which are tied in a bag.
4. When the juice begins to boil and appears clear, add the apples and cook almost tender.
5. Place the apples in glass jars and pour the boiling juice over.
6. Cover the jars.
