## Ingredients

- 1½ cupfuls of boiled fowl
- ½ cupful of milk sauce
- ¾ teaspoonful of salt
- lemon juice
- ½ teaspoonful of chopped parsley
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- lard
- chopped mushrooms

## Directions

1. Grind 1½ cupful of boiled fowl, add ½ cupful of milk sauce, ¾ teaspoonful of salt, a few drops of lemon juice, ½ teaspoonful of chopped parsley and a dash of pepper.
2. Let cool.
3. Form into croquettes, dip in egg and bread crumbs, cook in lard.
4. Chopped mushrooms mixed with the croquettes make them taste better.
