## Ingredients

- 6 hard shelled crabs
- 3.5 cups fish stock or veal stock
- 1 leek
- 2 sprigs parsley
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup cream
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from boiled crabs and chop finely.
2. Mix with the fish stock, leek and parsley.
3. Simmer covered for 0.5 hour.
4. Press through a sieve, thicken with butter and flour.
5. Season with salt and pepper; last of all add the cream.
6. Serve with crab balls or hard boiled yolks of eggs.
