## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 4 tablespoons milk or cream
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Rub until creamy the butter with the sugar
2. Add beaten eggs, milk, flavoring, flour mixed with baking powder
3. Use flour to make thick enough batter
4. Roll out
5. Cut in small cookies with a cutter
6. Sprinkle with sugar and chopped almonds
7. Bake in a hot oven
