## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup tomato
- 1 slice onion
- Celery and parsley
- 1/3 teaspoon soda
- Salt and pepper
- 2 1/3 cups milk or cream

## Directions

1. Cook onion and celery with butter in a saucepan, add flour, cook a few minutes longer.
2. Mix with tomato and seasonings, continue to cook until the tomato is tender.
3. Add soda and scalded milk gradually and stirring well.
4. Strain and season.
