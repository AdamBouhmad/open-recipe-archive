## Ingredients

- Pork chops
- Salt and pepper
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Butter or lard

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the chops with salt and pepper.
2. Place in a hot frying pan.
3. Fry on both sides until brown and done.
4. If desired, dip the chops in egg and roll in bread crumbs before frying.
5. Fry in butter or lard.
