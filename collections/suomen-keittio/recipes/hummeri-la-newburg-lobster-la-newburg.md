## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiled lobster
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt
- pepper
- some sherry or lemon juice
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup hot cream

## Directions

1. Cut 2 cups of boiled lobster in dices 1 inch thick.
2. Put 2 tablespoons of butter in a saucepan and add the lobster pieces.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Cover and let simmer about 5 minutes.
5. Add some sherry or lemon juice.
6. Beat 2 egg yolks and mix with 1 cup of hot cream, then add to the pan.
7. Shake the pan over the fire until the mixture becomes thick.
8. Alternatively, boiled egg yolks rubbed through a sieve may be used instead of raw yolks to prevent curdling.
