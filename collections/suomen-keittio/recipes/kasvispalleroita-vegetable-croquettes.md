## Ingredients

- cooked vegetables of all kinds
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Grind the vegetables
2. Add eggs, bread crumbs, flour and season with salt
3. Form into croquettes
4. Dip in egg and turn in flour
5. Fry in butter or deep fat
6. Serve with brown sauce and vegetables
