## Ingredients

- 1 glassful of eggs
- 1 glassful of sugar
- 3/4 glassful of melted butter
- 1 1/2 glassfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Whip eggs and sugar to a froth, mix with butter and flour.
2. Pour the mixture into a buttered mold and bake 1/2 - 3/4 hour.
3. May be used for fruit layer cakes.
