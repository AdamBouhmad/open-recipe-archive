## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon butter, melted
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 3 egg whites
- lemon juice
- large oysters
- butter or lard
- lemon
- parsley
- tartare sauce or lemon butter

## Directions

1. Beat 3 egg yolks
2. Add 1 cup of milk, 1 tablespoon of butter melted, 1 teaspoon of salt
3. Add 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder mixed with 1 1/2 cups of flour
4. Last of all add 3 egg whites beaten stiff
5. Have the batter thick
6. Use some lemon juice for flavor
7. Rinse and wipe large oysters
8. Dip in batter and fry in butter or lard
9. Serve on a napkin garnished with lemon and parsley
10. Serve with tartare sauce or lemon butter
