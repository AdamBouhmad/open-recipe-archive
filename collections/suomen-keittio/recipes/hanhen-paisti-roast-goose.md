## Ingredients

- 1 goose
- 1 lemon
- 2 tablespoonfuls of salt
- ½ teaspoonful of pepper
- ½ lb. of prunes
- 2 quarts of apples
- butter or lard
- meat stock
- cream

## Directions

1. Clean the goose, wash and wipe with a cloth.
2. Rub inside and outside with lemon.
3. Rub a little salt inside.
4. Wash the fruit and stuff the goose with them.
5. Truss and tie the wing tips and legs together at the back.
6. Spread a piece of butter or lard on the goose, place in a pan and roast in a hot oven, basting frequently with the fat in the pan and adding meat stock to the pan as needed.
7. When the goose is browned, sprinkle salt on top and cover with buttered paper.
8. Allow 2-3 hours for roasting.
9. Place on a platter, arrange the prunes and apples around it.
10. Strain the broth, skim off fat, and add a little cream.
