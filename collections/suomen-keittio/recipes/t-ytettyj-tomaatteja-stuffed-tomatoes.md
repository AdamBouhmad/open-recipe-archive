## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- butter
- onion
- chopped cooked chicken or meat, 1/2 cupful
- bread crumbs, 1/2 cupful
- tomato insides from scooped tomatoes
- minced parsley, 1 teaspoonful
- egg
- salt

## Directions

1. Wipe tomatoes and remove a slice from top
2. Do not peel
3. Remove soft inside portion
4. Sprinkle salt inside
5. Melt 1 tablespoonful butter with 1 tablespoonful onion
6. Add 1/2 cupful chopped cooked chicken or meat
7. Add 1/2 cupful bread crumbs
8. Add tomato insides removed from tomatoes
9. Add 1 teaspoonful minced parsley
10. Bring mixture to boil
11. Add one egg
12. Fill tomatoes with mixture
13. Place in buttered pan
14. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top
15. Bake in oven about 20 minutes
16. Serve with brown sauce
