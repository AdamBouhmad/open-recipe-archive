## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of water
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 cake of yeast
- 6-7 cupfuls of flour
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm water, add flour enough to make a very soft paste. Let rise.
2. Warm the rest of the water, pour in a bowl, mix with salt, butter and flour to prepare a soft paste.
3. Work the risen sponge into it and add the rest of the flour gradually, kneading until the dough is even.
4. Throw a towel over it and set in a warm place to rise.
5. When risen until light, toss the dough on a board, knead briskly and long, divide into loaves and put in buttered molds.
6. Cover with towel and let rise again.
7. Bake in a moderate oven 1 hour. If preferred, the sugar may be left out.
