## Ingredients

- 1/2 can of tomatoes
- 1 small onion
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- a little sugar
- 3/8 box or 8 leaves of gelatine

## Directions

1. Cook seasonings with tomato until tomato is soft.
2. Add gelatine and strain.
3. Pour into an individual mold to set.
4. Serve with salad.
5. If desired, the jelly may be cleared with white of an egg.
