## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 1/2 pound butter
- 3/4 pound flour
- 6 eggs
- sugar for sprinkling
- whipped cream
- vanilla
- jam

## Directions

1. Boil 4 cups water
2. Add 1/2 pound butter
3. Reheat to boiling point
4. Add 3/4 pound flour
5. Cook beating the mixture until it leaves the edges of the saucepan
6. Pour into a bowl to cool
7. Mix in 6 eggs, 2 at a time, beating well
8. Form into small round cakes on a baking sheet
9. Bake until light brown
10. Sprinkle with sugar when done
11. May be filled when cool with whipped cream mixed with sugar, vanilla and jam
