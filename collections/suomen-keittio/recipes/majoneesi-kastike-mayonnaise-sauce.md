## Ingredients

- 1 egg yolk
- 3/4 cup salad oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/2 tablespoon tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- a little cayenne
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard (dry)

## Directions

1. Put the mustard in a bowl and mix with the egg yolk
2. Add the oil very gradually at first
3. When it begins to thicken, add a little lemon juice and vinegar
4. Finally season with salt and pepper
5. If it curdles, take one egg yolk and gradually mix in the curdled mayonnaise
6. To make white mayonnaise, add 1/2 cup of well-whipped cream just before serving
7. To color green, pound spinach leaves, watercress, and parsley leaves with a little lemon juice in a mortar, press and add the juice to the mayonnaise
