## Ingredients

- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 chicken liver, cooked and minced
- 1 tablespoonful of milksauce
- a few drops of sherry
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- lard
- Hollandaise sauce, tomato sauce, or milksauce for serving

## Directions

1. Cut 3 hard-boiled eggs neatly in half.
2. Separate the yolks and mix with them one chicken liver cooked and minced, 1 tablespoonful of milksauce, a few drops of sherry, salt and pepper.
3. Fill the halves with this mixture, put together to give the appearance of a whole egg.
4. Dip in egg, roll in bread crumbs, dip in egg again and once more roll in bread crumbs.
5. Cook quickly in hot lard.
6. Serve with Hollandaise, tomato or milksauce.
