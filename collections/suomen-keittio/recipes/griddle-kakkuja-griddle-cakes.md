## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 2 cups milk
- 1 or 2 eggs
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 1½ teaspoons baking powder
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Sift together all the dry ingredients.
2. Beat the eggs, add the milk and pour gradually in the flour, stirring until smooth.
3. If the batter is too thick add some milk to it.
4. Drop by the spoonful into a hot buttered griddle pan and fry until brown on both sides.
5. Serve with maple syrup or honey.
