## Ingredients

- liver
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- bacon
- meat stock

## Directions

1. Cut liver in slices, sprinkle with salt and pepper and dip in flour.
2. Fry thin slices of bacon twice as many as you have slices of liver.
3. Remove the bacon from the pan.
4. Fry the liver slowly in drippings, turning often until done.
5. Pour meat stock into the pan and let boil.
6. Arrange with liver in the center and strain the gravy over the meat.
