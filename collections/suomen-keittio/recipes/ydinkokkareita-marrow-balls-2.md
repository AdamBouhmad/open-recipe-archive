## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of marrow
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- bread crumbs
- 2 eggs
- salt, pepper and minced parsley

## Directions

1. Melt the marrow and strain it through a cloth
2. Let cool a little while, then cream it
3. Boil 1/2 cupful of milk and add bread crumbs until absorbed
4. Mix 2 eggs with bread crumbs and stir into the milk mixture
5. Season with salt, pepper and minced parsley
6. Add this mixture gradually to the creamed marrow, rubbing well
7. Test the mixture and add more bread crumbs if too soft
8. Form into round balls
9. Dip in flour
10. Cook in salted water
11. Serve with meat soup
