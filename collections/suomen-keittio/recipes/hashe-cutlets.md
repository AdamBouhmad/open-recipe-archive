## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 cupfuls of boiled meat
- 1/2 cupful of bacon
- 2 cupfuls of boiled potatoes
- 1 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 red onion
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Force beef, bacon and potatoes through a meat chopper.
2. Cook chopped onion in butter, mix with meat.
3. Add seasonings, stirring well.
4. Form into cutlets, fry in butter until lightly browned.
5. For sauce: Cook 1 chopped onion in butter, add flour, bouillon, cream, 2 tablespoonfuls of tomato stew, salt and pepper, color with Kitchen Bouquet, and strain.
