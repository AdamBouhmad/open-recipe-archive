## Ingredients

- beans
- pork
- bay leaf
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- cream or bouillon

## Directions

1. Rinse the beans, let stand in cold water over night.
2. Put on the fire in cold water, let boil slowly changing the water once when the beans are half done.
3. Add a few pieces of pork, 1 bay leaf and 1 small onion.
4. If the beans are to be baked, they have to be removed from the fire immediately when they begin to crack.
5. For puree, cook the beans until tender, rub through a sieve or force through a meat chopper.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Add a lump of butter and dilute with cream or bouillon.
