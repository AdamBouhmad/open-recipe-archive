## Ingredients

- 2 quarts meat stock
- several kinds of vegetables, parboiled and cut in pieces
- salt and pepper
- soup meat and bones (optional)
- tomato (optional)

## Directions

1. To 2 quarts of meat stock add several kinds of vegetables parboiled and cut in pieces.
2. Let simmer until tender but not stewed.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Instead of using meat stock the vegetables may be cooked with soup meat.
5. Tomato will improve the taste of the soup.
