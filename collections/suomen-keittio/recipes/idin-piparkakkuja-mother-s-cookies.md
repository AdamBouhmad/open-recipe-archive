## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cupful of molasses
- 6 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful of pepper
- 1 teaspoonful of ginger
- flour to make a moderately thick batter

## Directions

1. Whip eggs add molasses, butter and spices, beating well, into it.
2. Mix soda with a little flour and stir it into the batter.
3. Add flour enough to make a moderately thick batter.
4. Roll thin, cut with cutter into small cookies.
5. Bake on greased sheet in a slow oven.
