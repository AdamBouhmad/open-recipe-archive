## Ingredients

- 2 pounds veal
- 1 cup rich milk
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Grind the meat several times.
2. Add the milk gradually, rubbing well, and season with salt, pepper and mustard.
3. Form into cutlets, dip in egg and bread crumbs.
4. Brown in butter.
5. Cover and continue frying until done.
6. Make a gravy of the drippings by adding flour and bouillon, or serve with tomato, milk or Bearnaise sauce.
