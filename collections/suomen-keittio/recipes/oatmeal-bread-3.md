## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of oatmeal
- 1/2 cupful of molasses
- 1/2 cupful of hot water
- 1 yeast cake
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Cook oatmeal to a batter
2. When cool add salt, molasses and yeast dissolved in lukewarm water
3. Add flour enough to make a thin batter
4. Put in buttered molds to rise
5. Bake in a moderate oven 1-1 1/2 hours
6. Serve on following day
