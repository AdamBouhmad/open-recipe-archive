## Ingredients

- 1 quart of sweetened berry- or fruit juice
- 1½ ozs. of gelatine
- water
- Berries and all kinds of fruit, skinned and cut in pieces

## Directions

1. Mix 1 quart of sweetened berry- or fruit juice with 1½ ozs. of gelatine, dissolved in water.
2. Mold to get firm.
3. Berries and all kinds of fruit, skinned and cut in pieces may be used in the mold alternately with layers of jelly.
