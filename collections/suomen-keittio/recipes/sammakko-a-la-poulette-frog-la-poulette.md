## Ingredients

- frog legs
- water
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- milk
- flour
- chopped parsley
- 2 egg yolks
- cream
- fried mushrooms

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the frog legs
2. Place in boiling water mixed with a tablespoon of lemon juice
3. Parboil covered for a few minutes
4. Remove from the water and wipe the legs
5. Immerse in hot butter seasoned with salt and pepper
6. Cook covered until tender (may alternatively be boiled in milk)
7. Prepare a gravy of butter, flour and milk, seasoned with salt and pepper
8. Flavor the gravy with lemon juice and chopped parsley
9. Beat 2 egg yolks with some cream
10. Stir the beaten egg yolks into the gravy over the fire until thick, but do not allow it to boil
11. Arrange the frog legs on a platter
12. Arrange fried mushrooms around the legs
13. Pour the gravy over
