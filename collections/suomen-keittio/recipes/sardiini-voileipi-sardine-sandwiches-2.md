## Ingredients

- 6 large sardines
- 1 desert spoon flour
- 3/4 cup water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/4 cup cream
- 1 teaspoon anchovy paste
- toasted bread slices
- hard boiled egg

## Directions

1. Bone and skin sardines
2. Mix with butter and anchovy paste, rubbing well
3. Put flour, water and cream into a saucepan
4. Let boil stirring well
5. Add sardine mixture to it
6. Spread on toast
7. Garnish with hard boiled egg
8. Serve hot
