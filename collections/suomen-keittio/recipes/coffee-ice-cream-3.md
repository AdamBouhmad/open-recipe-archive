## Ingredients

- 1 quart of cream
- ½ cupful of black coffee
- 1½ cupfuls of sugar
- 6 yolks
- 1 cupful of ground coffee
- 2 cupfuls of water

## Directions

1. Cook 1 cupful of ground coffee with 2 cupfuls of water ten minutes.
2. Strain through a tight cloth and let cool down.
3. Prepare cream mixture as usually of one half of the cream and yolks.
4. When cool, add the rest of the cream and the black coffee.
5. Then freeze.
