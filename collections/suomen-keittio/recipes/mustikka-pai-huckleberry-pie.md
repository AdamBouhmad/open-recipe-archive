## Ingredients

- pastry
- huckleberries
- a little corn starch or flour
- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Cover pieplate with pastry
2. Fill with huckleberries, mixed with a little corn starch or flour
3. Pour 1/2 cupful of sugar on and dust with a little salt
4. Place the upper crust on
5. Bake in a moderate oven
