## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- ½ teaspoonful of lemon juice
- nutmeg
- ½ teaspoonfuls of salt
- 1 cake of yeast
- flour
- finely chopped almonds
- crystal sugar

## Directions

1. Heat milk, add yeast and 1 cupful of flour, cover and let rise.
2. Add eggs beaten with sugar, butter rubbed into foam, salt, lemon juice and nutmeg and enough flour to make ordinary thick dough.
3. Stir mixture well, cover and let rise.
4. Roll dough ¾ inch thick, brush with melted butter, sprinkle with finely chopped almonds and crystal sugar.
5. Fold dough in three layers and cut cakes with round form.
6. Place cakes on buttered pan.
