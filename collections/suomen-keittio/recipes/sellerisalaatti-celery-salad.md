## Ingredients

- celery stalks
- cold water
- French dressing
- mayonnaise dressing
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Clean and scrape celery stalks, cut in 1/4 inch long pieces.
2. Keep in cold water for a while.
3. Wipe, mix with French or mayonnaise dressing.
4. Serve on a bed of lettuce.
