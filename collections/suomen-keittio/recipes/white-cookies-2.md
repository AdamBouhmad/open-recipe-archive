## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 3/4 cupful of cream
- 1 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 1/2 lb. of melted butter
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice
- a little cinnamon and cardamom
- 1/2 lb. of corn starch
- flour

## Directions

1. Whip eggs with sugar to a froth
2. Mix with cream, cream of tartar, butter, spices, corn starch and lastly flour enough to make a dough tolerable to handle
3. Roll and shape small cookies
4. Bake in a slow oven
