## Ingredients

- washed unpared and uncored apples
- cold water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut apples in pieces lengthwise.
2. Place on fire in cold water, having water just enough to cover the apples.
3. Cook to a mash.
4. Place to drain on a cloth without stirring.
5. Weigh the juice and cook slowly with equal amount of sugar, stirring often, so as not to burn the mixture.
6. Cook about half an hour, place in jars, cover as directed, preserve.
