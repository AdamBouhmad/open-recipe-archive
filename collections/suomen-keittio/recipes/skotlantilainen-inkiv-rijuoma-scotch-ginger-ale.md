## Ingredients

- 3 pounds granulated sugar
- 2 lemons
- 1 ounce wine acid
- 1.5 ounces powdered ginger
- 10 quarts water
- 1 yeast cake

## Directions

1. Grate the yellow rind of the lemons
2. Cut the lemons in slices
3. Put into a covered bowl with the sugar, wine acid, ginger and the yellow rind
4. Pour 1.5 quarts boiling water over
5. Cover and let stand over night
6. Bring to boil the remaining water and add to the mixture in the bowl
7. When cool, mix in the yeast
8. Cover and let stand over night
9. Strain carefully leaving the yeast on the bottom of the bowl
10. Bottle up and seal
11. The drink is ready to be used after 8 or 10 days
