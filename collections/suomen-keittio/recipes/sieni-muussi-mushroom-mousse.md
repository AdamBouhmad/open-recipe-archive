## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- 1 cup of finely chopped mushrooms
- 1 cup of chicken mousse
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- butter for molds

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms, put on fire in boiling water.
2. Cook for 10 minutes.
3. Press through a sieve.
4. Mix 1 cup of mushrooms with 1 cup of chicken mousse, some lemon juice, salt and pepper.
5. Butter and garnish small timbale molds, fill with the mixture.
6. Bake in oven in a pan of water covered with buttered paper for 15 minutes or until firm.
7. Serve with mushroom sauce.
