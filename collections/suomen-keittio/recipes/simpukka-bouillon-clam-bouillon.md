## Ingredients

- clams
- hot water
- cream

## Directions

1. Wash the clams well with brush, changing water.
2. Put into the kettle with a small quantity of hot water.
3. Cook on a slow fire, covered, until the shells open.
4. Strain the bouillon through a sieve-cloth and put aside to cool and clear.
5. Serve in cups as it is or mix with a little scalded cream.
6. It may be served also mixed with clarified rich meat-stock or chicken stock, 2 cupfuls of clam bouillon allowed to 1 cupful of meat stock.
