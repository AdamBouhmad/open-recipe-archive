## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 salted herrings
- 3 cups bread crumbs
- 2 cups milk
- 1/2 cup melted butter
- pepper
- sugar if desired
- 3 egg yolks beaten to a froth
- 3 egg whites beaten until firm

## Directions

1. Soak over night 2 or 3 salted herrings
2. Remove the skin and the bones
3. Force through a meat grinder
4. Soak 3 cups bread crumbs in 2 cups milk or use stale bread instead of crumbs
5. Mix in the fish
6. Add to the mixture 1/2 cup melted butter, a dash of pepper and sugar if desired and 3 egg yolks beaten to a froth
7. Last of all fold in 3 egg whites beaten until firm
8. Steam in a buttered mold in oven for 3/4 or 1 hour
9. Serve with egg sauce
