## Ingredients

- 2 heads red cabbage
- 1 onion
- 3 apples or tomatoes
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon parsley
- 4 tablespoons butter
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- Some pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage in strips, remove the strongest ribs.
2. Cook the onion in butter, add the cabbage, the apples cut in pieces, sugar, salt, parsley and pepper.
3. Let boil tightly covered for 1 or 2 hours.
4. Add lemon juice, the rest of the butter and 1 tablespoon of currant jelly, continue boiling for 5 minutes.
5. Serve with meat.
