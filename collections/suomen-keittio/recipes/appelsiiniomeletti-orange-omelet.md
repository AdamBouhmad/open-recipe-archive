## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
- rind of 1/2 orange, grated
- butter
- sugar-coated orange slices

## Directions

1. Beat separately whites and yolks of 3 eggs.
2. Mix the yolks with 4 tablespoons of sugar, 1 1/2 tablespoon of lemon juice and grated yellow rind of 1 orange.
3. Last of all add the whites beaten lightly.
4. Melt some butter in an omelet pan, turn the mixture into the pan, let cook.
5. Fold once and serve on a hot platter with slices of orange thoroughly sugared.
