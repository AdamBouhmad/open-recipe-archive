## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 cupful of rich milk
- 5 eggs
- 4 ozs. of sugar
- 2 ozs. of cocoa

## Directions

1. Cook a batter of flour and milk; mix cocoa with the hot batter.
2. Whip well the yolks and sugar and add into the cocoa mixture.
3. Lastly stir stiffly beaten whites into it.
4. Pour the mixture immediately in a well buttered mold.
5. Cover and steam in oven or on the stove.
6. Instead of cocoa coffee may be used if preferred.
