## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- Grated yellow rind of a lemon
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup whipped cream or plain
- 3 tablespoons currants
- 3 tablespoons chopped almonds
- cheese
- sugar
- spices

## Directions

1. Mix the cornstarch and flour with sugar and cheese
2. Add the cream, the spices and the eggs beaten separately
3. Bake in an open crust
4. May be made without currants, almonds and lemon and instead of cream milk may be used, if plain cheese pie is preferred
