## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiled spinach
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Take 2 cups of boiled spinach rubbed through a sieve.
2. Add butter, salt, sugar and egg whites beaten stiff.
3. Bake in buttered timbale molds in a pan of water.
4. May be flavored with onion juice if desired.
