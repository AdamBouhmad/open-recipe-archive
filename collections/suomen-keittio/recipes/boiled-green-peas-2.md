## Ingredients

- peas
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put peas on fire in boiling water mixed with a little salt
2. Boil until tender, but not broken
3. Pour off the water and season with salt, pepper and butter
4. A little sugar may be used
5. Canned peas must be well washed in cold water before using
