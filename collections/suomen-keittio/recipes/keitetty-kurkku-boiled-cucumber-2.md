## Ingredients

- older cucumbers
- salt
- water
- cream sauce or minced parsley
- butter and pepper (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel cucumbers
2. Cut lengthwise into four parts and then across the middle
3. Boil in salted water until tender
4. Strain water carefully
5. Mix with cream sauce and minced parsley
6. Alternatively, mince the boiled cucumbers and season with salt, pepper and butter
