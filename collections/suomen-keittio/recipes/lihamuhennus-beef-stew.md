## Ingredients

- Meat, cut in 1 to 1.5 inch pieces
- Butter
- Onion, chopped
- 2 carrots, cut in pieces
- 1 turnip, cut in pieces
- 1 tomato, cut in pieces
- Salt and pepper
- Water or bouillon
- 2 potatoes, cut in pieces
- 2 celery stalks
- Flour

## Directions

1. Brown the meat in butter with chopped onion.
2. Place into a kettle with carrots, turnip, tomato, salt and pepper.
3. Rinse the frying pan with water or bouillon and pour over the meat.
4. Let simmer slowly, covered.
5. About 0.5 hour before the meat is done, add the potatoes cut in pieces and celery stalks.
6. Thicken with flour mixed with cold water if desired.
7. Arrange vegetables around the meat when serving.
