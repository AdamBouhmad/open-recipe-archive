## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 3/4 cup cream
- 3/4 cup melted butter
- 1/4 pound almonds
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cardamom
- 1/2 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 6 cups flour

## Directions

1. Beat to a froth eggs with sugar
2. Add cream, butter, spices, almonds blanched and cut in pieces lengthwise, soda mixed with flour
3. Form into flat loaves on a buttered baking sheet
4. Bake in a moderate oven
5. Cut crosswise in pieces the loaves while warm
6. Dry the cookies in a lukewarm oven
