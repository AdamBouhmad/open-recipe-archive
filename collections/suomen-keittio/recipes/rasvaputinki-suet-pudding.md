## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped suet
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup syrup
- 1/2 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 cups flour
- Cinnamon and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix with finger tips salt, flour and suet
2. Add soda dissolved in water, milk, syrup and spices
3. Rub to an even paste
4. Put into a mold
5. Steam for 3 hours
6. Serve with lemon or wine sauce
