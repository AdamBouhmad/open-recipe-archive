## Ingredients

- 3 cups mashed cooked turnips
- Salt
- Sugar
- Pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter or lard
- 1/2 cup rich milk
- 1 egg (if desired)

## Directions

1. Add to the 3 cups of mashed turnips some salt, sugar, pepper.
2. Add 2 tablespoons butter or lard and 1/2 cup rich milk (and 1 egg, if desired).
3. Turn into a buttered mold.
4. Bake slowly for 1.5 to 2 hours.
