## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 small onion, chopped
- Chopped parsley
- Pickles
- Salt
- Pepper
- Water or fish stock
- Egg (optional)
- Oyster soft parts (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the onion in butter in a frying-pan.
2. Add the bread crumbs soaked in water or fish stock, parsley, salt and pepper.
3. Fry on a slow fire.
4. Add an egg if desired.
5. Oyster may be used for stuffing also.
