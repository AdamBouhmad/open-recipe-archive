## Ingredients

- 1 cup lentils
- 1/4 cup brown beans
- 1/2 onion
- celery
- carrot
- a sprig of parsley
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup milk sauce
- salt and pepper
- butter or lard for cooking

## Directions

1. Wash the lentils and brown beans
2. Put over the fire in cold water with onion, celery, carrot and a sprig of parsley
3. Cook until tender and the water has evaporated
4. Force through a sieve
5. Add bread crumbs, egg, milk sauce, salt and pepper
6. Stir until smooth
7. Form into any shape preferred
8. Dip in egg and turn in bread crumbs
9. Cook or fry in butter
