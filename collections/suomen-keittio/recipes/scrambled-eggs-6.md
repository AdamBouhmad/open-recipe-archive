## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of milk or water
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Whip the eggs, add milk, salt and pepper.
2. Melt the butter in a frying pan, not allowing it to brown.
3. Slip the whipped eggs into the pan, stir constantly over a slow fire.
4. When the mixture is a creamy mass remove carefully with a spoon.
5. By using water instead of milk the eggs get a delicate brown color.
