## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 1 tablespoon washed rice
- salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- stewed fruit or jam

## Directions

1. Pour 2 cups of milk into a mold, add 1 tablespoon of washed rice and a dash of salt
2. Bake in oven for 2 hours stirring often
3. Add 1 tablespoon sugar and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla when the pudding is half done
4. If the pudding becomes hard, add scalded milk
5. Serve hot or cold with stewed fruit or jam
