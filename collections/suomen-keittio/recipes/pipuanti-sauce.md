## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 cupful of beef stock
- 1 cupful of tomato
- 1 tablespoonful of minced pickles
- 1 tablespoonful of minced fresh cucumber
- 1/2 teaspoonful of minced onion
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Make a sauce from butter, flour and beef stock
2. Add tomato, minced cucumbers, onion, salt and pepper
3. Cook for a few minutes
