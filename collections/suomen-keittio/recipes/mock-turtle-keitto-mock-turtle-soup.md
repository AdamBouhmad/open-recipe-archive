## Ingredients

- 3 quarts vegetable stock or water
- 2 onions
- 2 small carrots
- 2 small turnips or 2 potatoes
- 1 stalk celery
- 1/2 cup tomato
- 1 cup mushrooms
- 2 tablespoons chopped chives
- Butter and flour for thickening
- Lemon
- Salt and pepper
- Hard boiled egg yolks or egg balls

## Directions

1. Clean the vegetables and cut in pieces.
2. Cook in vegetable stock with the mushrooms, the tomato and the chives.
3. Add liquor of boiled rice or vegetable stock as needed.
4. Force through a sieve.
5. Brown 2 tablespoonfuls of butter with 1 tablespoonful of flour.
6. Add the soup to the butter.
7. Bring to boil.
8. Season with salt and pepper.
9. Flavor with lemon.
10. Serve with hard boiled yolks of eggs or with egg balls.
