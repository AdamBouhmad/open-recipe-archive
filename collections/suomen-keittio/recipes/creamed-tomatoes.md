## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter
- thin milk sauce

## Directions

1. Cut tomatoes into slices
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Dip in flour
4. Fry in butter
5. Place in a hot serving dish
6. Pour thin milk sauce on them
