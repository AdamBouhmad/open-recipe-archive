## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- 1 cupful of water
- 4 ounces of chocolate
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Cook sugar with water until the syrup will thread when dropped from spoon
2. Melt chocolate in a dry pan
3. Pour the syrup gradually into the chocolate
4. Stir until the mixture becomes even
5. Add flavoring
6. Spread cake with the icing
