## Ingredients

- beans
- cold water
- pieces of pork
- bay leaf
- small onion

## Directions

1. Wash the beans, soak over night in cold water
2. Put on the fire in cold water, let simmer, changing the water once when the beans are half done
3. Add a few pieces of pork, 1 bay leaf and 1 small onion
4. If the beans are to be baked, they have to be removed from the fire immediately when they begin to boil
