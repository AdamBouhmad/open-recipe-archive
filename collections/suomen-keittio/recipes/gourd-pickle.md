## Ingredients

- gourd
- vinegar
- sugar (as much as the weight of gourd)
- cloves
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel gourd, split in two and remove seeds.
2. Slice or cut into dice.
3. Cook liquor of vinegar and as much sugar as the weight of gourd.
4. Season with cloves and pepper.
5. Put the gourd in and cook until clear and soft.
6. Place in a glass jar, pour the liquor over, cork and keep in a cold place.
