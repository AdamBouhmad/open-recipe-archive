## Ingredients

- celery stalks
- cold water
- Mayonnaise or French dressing
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Clean and scrape celery stalks, cut in pieces 1/4 inch long.
2. Put in cold water for a few minutes.
3. Mix celery with Mayonnaise or French dressing.
4. Garnish with lettuce leaves.
