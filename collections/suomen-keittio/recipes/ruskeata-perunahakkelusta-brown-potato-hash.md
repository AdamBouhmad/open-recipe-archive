## Ingredients

- Cold potatoes
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut cold potatoes in small cubes
2. Fry in butter until thoroughly hot
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Continue frying until brown underneath
5. Double on a serving dish like an omelet
