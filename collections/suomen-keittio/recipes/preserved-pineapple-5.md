## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 quart water
- pineapples

## Directions

1. Pare pineapples and remove the black eyes.
2. Slice or cut into dice.
3. Cook in syrup before you place them in jars.
4. Prepare like preserved apples.
