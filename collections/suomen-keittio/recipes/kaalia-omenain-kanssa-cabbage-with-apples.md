## Ingredients

- 1 head of cabbage
- 4 or 6 apples
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1/2 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage in strips, put in a saucepan.
2. Add apples pared and cut in pieces, butter, lemon juice and sugar.
3. Cook slowly tightly covered until tender, about 2 hours.
