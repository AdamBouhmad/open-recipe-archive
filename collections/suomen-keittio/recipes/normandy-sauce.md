## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of fish stock
- white wine
- 2 egg yolks
- a little cream

## Directions

1. Mix butter with flour
2. Add fish stock
3. Flavor with a dash of white wine
4. Mix with yolks of 2 eggs and a little cream
