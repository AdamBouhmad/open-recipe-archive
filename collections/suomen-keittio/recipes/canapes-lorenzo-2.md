## Ingredients

- 2 thin slices of onion
- butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups rich milk
- salt
- pepper
- 1/2 pound lobster meat, chopped fine
- round shaped toast

## Directions

1. Brown 2 thin slices of onion in butter
2. Add 2 tablespoons flour and 2 cups rich milk, stirring the whole to a smooth gravy
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Mix in 1/2 pound of lobster meat chopped fine
5. Let cool
6. Place on round shaped toast, cover with following mixture: bread crumbs, butter and grated cheese, equal amounts of each
7. Brown in oven
8. Serve hot
