## Ingredients

- loin of venison
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- bread crumbs
- fried slices of onion
- meat stock
- cream
- wine sauce

## Directions

1. Wipe loin of venison with cloth wrung out from hot water, cut into thick pieces, pound on both sides, trim with a knife.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, spread with melted butter, and dip in bread crumbs.
3. Broil over a clear fire or fry with butter in a fryingpan until done.
4. Arrange on a platter, lay fried slices of onion on.
5. Mix the gravy with meat stock and cream, or serve with wine sauce.
