## Ingredients

- 4 pounds of beef
- Butter or pork
- 1 onion
- 1 carrot
- 1 turnip
- Boiling water or meat stock
- Flour
- Cream
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Take a four-pound piece of beef and brown with butter or pork.
2. When well-browned, add 1 onion, 1 carrot, and 1 turnip cut in pieces.
3. Half cover with boiling water and let simmer, tightly covered, until tender.
4. Remove the fat and thicken with flour.
5. Strain the stock and rub the vegetables through a sieve.
6. Add water and a little cream until moderately thick.
