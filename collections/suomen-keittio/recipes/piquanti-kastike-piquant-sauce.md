## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup bouillon
- 1 cup tomato
- some onion juice
- 1 tablespoon chopped pickles
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cucumber
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Make a gravy of butter, flour and bouillon.
2. Add tomato, chopped cucumber, pickles, onion juice, salt and pepper.
3. Cook for a few minutes.
4. Serve with meat or fish.
