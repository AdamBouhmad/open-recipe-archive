## Ingredients

- 1 boiled lobster
- bread crumbs
- egg
- lard

## Directions

1. Clean 1 boiled lobster.
2. Cut the tail meat in quarters and the meat of claws in halves.
3. Roll in bread crumbs, dip in egg and roll in bread crumbs again.
4. Fry in lard.
5. Serve with tartare sauce.
