## Ingredients

- trout
- salt
- vinegar
- butter
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Parboil the fish with some salt and vinegar
2. Wipe and put in a buttered pan
3. Pour 3/4 cup of tomato sauce over
4. Bake in hot oven for 20 minutes basting often
5. Remove to a platter and strain tomato sauce over
