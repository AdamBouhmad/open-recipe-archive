## Ingredients

- boiled lobsters
- 1/4 cup milk
- 4 tablespoons gelatine
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 4 tablespoons cream
- truffle

## Directions

1. Shell boiled lobsters and cut the meat in cubes
2. Stir gelatine with milk
3. When it is cool add the mayonnaise and the whipped cream
4. Decorate small molds with truffle
5. Cover the bottom of molds with the mixture
6. When firm, lay lobster meat on it, and a layer of the mixture on top
7. When firm turn on lettuce leaves
