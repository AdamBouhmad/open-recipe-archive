## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 cupfuls of fish stock
- 2 - 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of minced parsley
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and fish stock
2. Add finely chopped egg
3. Season with salt, pepper, parsley and chives
4. Alternatively: Cook 1/2 pound of butter with 1 cupful of fish stock
5. Chop 4 hard-boiled eggs and pour the sauce over
