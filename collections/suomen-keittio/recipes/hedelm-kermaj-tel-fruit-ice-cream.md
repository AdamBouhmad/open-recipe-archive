## Ingredients

- Vanilla ice cream
- Fruit such as cherries, strawberries, apples or apricots
- Preserved fruit for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare as directed for vanilla ice cream.
2. When half frozen pack in a freezer alternately with fruit such as cherries, strawberries, apples or apricots.
3. Freeze completely.
4. Garnish around with preserved fruit.
