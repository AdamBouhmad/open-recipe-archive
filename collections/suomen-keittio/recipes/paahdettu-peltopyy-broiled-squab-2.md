## Ingredients

- squabs
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove bones from squabs, sprinkle outside with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
2. Let stand for a little while.
3. Broil on a slow fire, dot with butter, push into the oven for a few minutes.
4. May be broiled leaving the bones in, same as directed for broiled chicken.
