## Ingredients

- 2 cups ground ham
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup bread soaked in milk
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/2 tablespoon chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Soak the bread crumbs in milk.
2. Add butter and cook for 5 minutes.
3. Stir into the bread crumbs the ground ham, parsley and the beaten eggs.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Put into buttered molds.
6. Bake in oven in a pan of water for 25 minutes.
7. Serve with mushroom sauce.
