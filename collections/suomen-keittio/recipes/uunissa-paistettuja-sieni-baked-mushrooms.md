## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Cream

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned mushrooms in a pan stem side down
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Dot with butter and add some cream
4. Bake in a hot oven for 10 minutes
5. Serve on toast with the sauce poured over the mushrooms
