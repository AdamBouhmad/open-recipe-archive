## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- chicken or any meat
- ham
- bread crumbs
- bouillon
- egg
- butter
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Split lengthwise, remove the insides.
2. Fill with mixture of chicken or any other kind of meat and ham with bread crumbs, half of the amount of meat, diluted with bouillon and one egg.
3. Rub well.
4. Sprinkle the top with bread crumbs.
5. Arrange in a buttered pan with 1 cup of bouillon or water and lumps of butter.
6. Cover with buttered paper.
7. Bake for an hour or until done.
8. Arrange on a platter, make a sauce of the drippings.
9. Cucumbers may be stuffed with chicken mousse and baked covered with buttered paper for 40 minutes.
10. Serve on toast with Béchamel sauce.
11. Instead of meat, mushrooms may be used for stuffing.
