## Ingredients

- 3 lemons
- 2½ pounds of powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of wine acid powder

## Directions

1. Grate yellow rind of three lemons
2. Rub together with 2½ pounds of powdered sugar
3. Press juice of the lemons, drain and mix with the above sugar
4. Stir 1 teaspoonful of wine acid powder into it
5. Mix thoroughly together, lay on paper to get dry and keep in a covered glass jar
6. When serving allow 2 teaspoonfuls of powder to 1 glassful of ice water
