## Ingredients

- Peels of 5 oranges
- 5 cups sugar
- 2 teaspoons lemon acid
- 1 quart water

## Directions

1. Remove the white part from the peels of 5 oranges
2. Cut the peels into strips
3. Put the peels with sugar and lemon acid into a large bottle
4. Add water
5. Let stand on a window sill in the sun
6. Shake the bottle every day for about a week
7. Strain and bottle up
8. Seal
9. When served add water according to taste
