## Ingredients

- oysters, equal-sized
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- buttered toast
- lemon juice
- lemon butter

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe equal-sized oysters
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Dip in melted butter
4. Lay them on the broiler
5. Broil over a clear fire on both sides for a few minutes
6. Serve on buttered toast laying 3-4 oysters on each
7. Baste with a little melted butter and lemon juice
8. Serve with lemon butter
