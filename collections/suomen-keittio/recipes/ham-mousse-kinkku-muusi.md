## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of ham
- 1/2 lb. of chicken fillet
- whites of 2 eggs
- cream, about 1/2 pint

## Directions

1. Grind the ham with chicken fillet many times.
2. Pound well and add the whites.
3. Pound well again.
4. Force through a sieve.
5. Add gradually about 1/2 pint of cream, or until the mixture is soft enough.
6. Test it by dropping a little dumpling of the mixture in salted boiling water.
7. If too thick, add a little cream; if too thin it may be thickened with white of an egg.
