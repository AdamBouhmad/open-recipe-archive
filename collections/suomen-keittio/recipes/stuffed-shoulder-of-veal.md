## Ingredients

- shoulder of veal
- few tablespoonfuls of suet
- 1 egg
- 3 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs
- 1/2 cupful of cream or milk
- 1/2 tablespoonful of salt
- 1/2 tablespoonful of parsley
- chopped mushrooms or pork
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the bones and the blade carefully; scrape the meat from the blade.
2. Grind and mix with chopped suet, egg, bread crumbs, cream, salt, parsley and white pepper.
3. Rub the space with salt, stuff and sew up.
4. Bake same as roast veal.
