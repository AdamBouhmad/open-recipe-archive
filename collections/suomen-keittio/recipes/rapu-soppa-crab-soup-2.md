## Ingredients

- 6 hard-shelled crabs
- 3½ cupfuls of fish- or veal stock
- 1 small pickle onion
- 2 sprigs of parsley
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 cupful of cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from the cooked crabs and chop it finely.
2. Mix the fish stock, onion and parsley together and let simmer gently covered for half an hour.
3. Force through a sieve.
4. Thicken with butter and flour, season with salt and pepper and finally add cream.
5. Serve with crab balls or hard boiled yolks.
