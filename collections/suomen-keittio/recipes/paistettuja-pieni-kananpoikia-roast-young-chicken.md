## Ingredients

- young chickens
- butter
- minced parsley
- salt
- bread crumbs
- water
- cream

## Directions

1. Clean young chickens.
2. Mix a few tablespoonfuls of butter with minced parsley and stuff the chickens with the butter.
3. Tie up and roast in oven having a little butter in your roaster.
4. Sprinkle with salt and bread crumbs when the chickens are slightly browned.
5. Pour water into the pan so as not to burn the chickens.
6. Baste frequently.
7. Dish and decorate with vegetables.
8. Thicken the gravy with cream.
