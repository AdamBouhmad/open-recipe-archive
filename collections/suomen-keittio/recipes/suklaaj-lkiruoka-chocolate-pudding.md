## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 2.5 cups milk
- 3 or 4 tablespoons grated chocolate
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 0.5 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 or 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. Melt the chocolate in a hot pan, add gradually the scalded milk, bread crumbs, butter, vanilla and salt.
2. Beat the eggs with the sugar, add to the mixture.
3. Bake in a buttered mold in a pan of water until firm.
4. Serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce. Instead of chocolate cocoa may be used.
