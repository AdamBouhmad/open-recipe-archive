## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Stuffing
- Bacon slices or butter
- Melted butter
- Water
- Kitchen Bouquet
- Cream

## Directions

1. Clean and stuff the chicken.
2. Tie with string, put in a pan, line with bacon, or dot with butter.
3. Place in a hot oven, baste often with melted butter and water.
4. Bake until done or about 1½ hours.
5. Make a gravy of the drippings by adding some Kitchen Bouquet and cream.
