## Ingredients

- boiled macaroni
- cheese crumbs
- milk sauce
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Put the boiled macaroni in a buttered mold.
2. Sprinkle with cheese crumbs and pour milk sauce over.
3. Cover with bread crumbs and dot here and there with bits of butter.
4. If more cheese is wanted, put two layers of macaroni into the mold and sprinkle the layers with cheese crumbs.
