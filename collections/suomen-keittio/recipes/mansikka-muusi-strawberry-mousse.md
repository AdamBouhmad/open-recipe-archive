## Ingredients

- 3 cups cream
- 1 1/2 cups strawberries, juice and pulp
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/2 package gelatine
- Vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar with the strawberries, let stand for 1/2 hour.
2. Add the lemon juice and the dissolved gelatine.
3. When the mixture begins to thicken, add the whipped cream.
4. Pour into a mold to set.
5. Serve unmolded garnished with cream and strawberries.
