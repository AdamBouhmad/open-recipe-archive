## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1/4 lb. of flour
- yellow rind of lemon

## Directions

1. Beat yolks and sugar until light.
2. Mix with lemon rind, flour and lastly whites, beaten stiff.
3. Butter a paper and cover a baking pan inside with it.
4. Pour the paste evenly on the paper and bake in oven 15-20 minutes.
5. Turn on a paper sprinkled with sugar, spread with jelly, roll together.
