## Ingredients

- meat of boiled lobster
- mayonnaise sauce
- gelatine
- caper
- pickled onions
- hard-boiled eggs
- truffle
- slices of bread

## Directions

1. Cut meat of boiled lobster into dice.
2. Mix with mayonnaise sauce diluted with gelatine.
3. Season with caper and a few finely chopped pickled onions.
4. Line individual molds with liquid jelly, garnish with egg and truffle.
5. Turn the mixture of lobster into the molds, put aside to set.
6. Unmold on slices of bread.
