## Ingredients

- sardines
- yolks of hard boiled eggs
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- lemon juice
- olive oil
- mayonnaise
- whites of egg
- parsley

## Directions

1. Bone and skin sardines
2. Mix equal parts of yolks of hard boiled eggs and sardines
3. Season with salt, pepper and mustard
4. Rub until smooth
5. Dilute with lemon juice and olive oil or with mayonnaise
6. Make sandwiches
7. Sprinkle the top with whites of egg chopped fine and parsley
