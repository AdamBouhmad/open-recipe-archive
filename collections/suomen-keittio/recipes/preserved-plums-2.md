## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 quart water
- plums

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same way as preserved apples.
2. Remove the skins or leave them on.
3. When pricked in several places with a fork before cooking, the skins will not crack open or come off if boiled too long.
4. Do not boil plums as long a time as apples.
