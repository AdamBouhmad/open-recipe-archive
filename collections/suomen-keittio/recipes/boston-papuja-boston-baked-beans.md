## Ingredients

- 1½ quarts of beans
- pork
- 2 tablespoons sugar or syrup
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 onion
- ½ cup Chili sauce
- 3 cups water

## Directions

1. Rinse and soak 1½ quarts of beans in cold water over night.
2. Put on the fire in cold water.
3. Cook slowly with a piece of pork about an hour.
4. Drain.
5. Place the beans with a rind of pork into an earthen bowl.
6. Prepare a sauce of 2 tablespoons sugar or syrup, 1 tablespoon salt, 1 chopped onion, ½ cup Chili sauce and 3 cups water.
7. Pour the sauce over the beans.
8. Have plenty of water to cover the beans.
9. Bake in a slow oven for 6—7 hours adding water, if the beans are getting dry.
