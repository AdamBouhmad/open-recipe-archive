## Ingredients

- Melon
- Lettuce
- Mayonnaise
- Cream

## Directions

1. Cut the melon in small parts, remove the seeds and the peels, cut in pieces
2. Serve on a bed of lettuce mixed in mayonnaise diluted with cream
