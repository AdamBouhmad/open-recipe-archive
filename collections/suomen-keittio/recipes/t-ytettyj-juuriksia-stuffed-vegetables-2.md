## Ingredients

- onion, turnip, celery root, or rutabaga
- butter
- flour
- rich milk
- boiled rice
- cooked onion
- egg
- salt

## Directions

1. Half cook the vegetables
2. Peel and scoop out the insides
3. Grind the insides with boiled rice and cooked onion in a meat grinder
4. Prepare a stuffing of butter, flour and rich milk
5. Mix the ground vegetable insides with the butter mixture, add egg and salt
6. Stuff the scooped vegetables with this mixture
7. Put in a buttered pan and bake in oven
8. Serve with any kind of sauce
