## Ingredients

- 1 cup whipped cream
- 2 tablespoons caviare
- 2 tablespoons melted gelatine
- red pepper
- sandwiches

## Directions

1. Mix the caviare with gelatine and dilute with cream.
2. Garnish small molds with red pepper.
3. Fill the molds alternately with layers of the mixture and plain caviare having the mixture on the bottom and on top.
4. Serve on sandwiches.
