## Ingredients

- Fresh apricots, pears and peaches
- Fritter batter
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Pare the fruit into sections
2. Cut in pieces
3. Mix in the batter
4. When made of canned fruit, drain the liquid and cut the fruit in pieces
5. Drop into boiling lard by the spoonful
6. Cook until brown
