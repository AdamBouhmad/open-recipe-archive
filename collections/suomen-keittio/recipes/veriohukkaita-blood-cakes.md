## Ingredients

- 2 cups blood
- 2 cups milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 onion
- 4 tablespoons suet or butter
- ½ tablespoon salt
- Some thyme
- 4 tablespoons rye flour
- 4 tablespoons white flour
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Strain the blood and beat
2. Add milk, flour, eggs beaten, onion fried in butter, salt and some pepper
3. Heat the frying pan and spread with butter
4. Fry pancakes of the batter
5. Serve with lingon or cranberries and melted butter
