## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of apple sauce
- whites of 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Beat whites to a firm froth, mix with apple sauce.
2. Pour the mixture into a buttered mold.
3. Bake in a pan of water.
4. Serve with vanilla sauce or cream.
