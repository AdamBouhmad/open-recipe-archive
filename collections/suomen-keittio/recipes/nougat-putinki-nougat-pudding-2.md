## Ingredients

- 3 whites of eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of finely chopped almonds
- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 3 yolks of eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1/2 tablespoonful of corn starch
- 8 gelatine leaves
- a little brandy
- 5 tablespoonfuls of whipped cream
- 1 package lemon jelly
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of water

## Directions

1. Beat 3 whites of eggs until stiff.
2. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar and 2 tablespoonfuls of finely chopped almonds.
3. Boil 2 cupfuls of milk.
4. Drop the beaten whites with spoon into it and let boil for a few minutes.
5. Take out the drops and set to drain.
6. Add 3 yolks, whipped with 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar and 1/2 tablespoonful of corn starch into the milk.
7. Stir the mixture over the fire until it thickens.
8. Add 8 gelatine leaves, dissolved, and let cool.
9. When cold, mix with a little brandy and 5 tablespoonfuls of whipped cream.
10. Lay the white drops on the bottom of the mold.
11. Pour on them slowly 1 cupful of jelly made from 1 package lemon jelly and 1 1/2 cupfuls of water.
12. When firm, lay the cream mixture on it and set to harden.
