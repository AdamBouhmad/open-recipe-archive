## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- salted water
- milk
- onion juice
- 3 egg yolks
- bread crumbs
- 3 egg whites
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Parboil 1 cup of rice in salted water
2. Drain and add milk
3. Cook until the rice is very tender
4. Mix with onion juice and 3 egg yolks
5. If thin, add bread crumbs
6. Last of all mix with the whites of 3 eggs beaten until firm
7. Drop by spoonful into a pan and fry until brown on both sides
8. Serve with tomato sauce
