## Ingredients

- 1 quart of chestnuts
- water or meat stock
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- a dash of pepper
- 1 teaspoonful of parsley
- meat stock if needed

## Directions

1. Soak chestnuts in boiling water to remove the shells
2. Drain from the water and remove the shells
3. Cook the shelled chestnuts in water or meat stock until soft
4. Press through a sieve
5. Add 1 tablespoonful of butter, 1 teaspoonful of salt, a dash of pepper, and 1 teaspoonful of parsley
6. Dilute with meat stock if necessary
