## Ingredients

- 4 cupfuls of water
- 2 1/2 cupfuls of sugar
- 4-5 lemons

## Directions

1. Cook sugar with water 15 minutes
2. Let cool and squeeze juice of the lemons in it
3. Let chill, strain and freeze
