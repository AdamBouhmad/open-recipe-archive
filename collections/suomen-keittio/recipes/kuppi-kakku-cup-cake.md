## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of butter
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 1/2 teaspoonful of vanilla
- 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar to a froth, add yolks of eggs, milk, vanilla, baking powder with flour.
2. Lastly add whites beaten stiff to a froth.
3. Bake in a buttered mold in a moderate oven.
