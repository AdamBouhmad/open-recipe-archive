## Ingredients

- 3 cups boiled meat
- 1/2 cup bacon
- 2 cups boiled potatoes
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 red onion
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Force meat, bacon and potatoes through a meat chopper.
2. Cook onion, chopped, in butter, add to the meat.
3. Season and beat well.
4. Form into cutlets, fry in butter until light brown.
5. For gravy: Cook 1 onion, chopped, in butter, add flour, bouillon, cream, 2 tablespoons stewed tomato, salt and pepper.
6. Color with Kitchen Bouquet; strain.
