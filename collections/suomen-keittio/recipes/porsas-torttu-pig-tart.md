## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 6 ozs. of sugar
- 1/2 lemon rind and juice
- 4 ozs. of corn starch
- 2 ozs. of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of white wine
- 2 ozs. of almonds

## Directions

1. Beat yolks and sugar well, add lemon rind, juice and corn starch.
2. Lastly mix with whites, whipped stiff.
3. Bake in oven.
4. When done, baste with following frosting: 2 ozs. of sugar, 1/2 cupful of white wine.
5. Peel almonds, cut into long thin pieces and stick on top of cake.
