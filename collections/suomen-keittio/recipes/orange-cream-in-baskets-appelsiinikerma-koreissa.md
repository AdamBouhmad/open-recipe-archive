## Ingredients

- 12 oranges
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 ounces of sugar
- 1 cupful of thin cream
- 1/2 grated yellow lemon rind
- 1 grated orange rind
- juice of 5 oranges
- 6 leaves or 4 tablespoonfuls of gelatine
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of rich cream

## Directions

1. Put thin cream with orange and lemon rinds in a saucepan and place on fire to boil
2. Whip yolks and sugar together
3. Add the boiling cream gradually into it, stirring over the fire until it thickens
4. Set to cool
5. Mix with orange juice and gelatine
6. Add the whipped rich cream
7. For baskets: take oranges of equal size and remove one half from each one, leaving remaining part in shape of basket with a handle
8. Remove pulp and notch the edges of the peel in points
9. Fill baskets with the mixture
