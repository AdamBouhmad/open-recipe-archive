## Ingredients

- 4 cups carrots
- 3 onions
- salt
- water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- butter
- flour
- liquor from vegetables

## Directions

1. Clean and cut carrots in cubes
2. Peel and cut onions in pieces
3. Boil together in salted water until tender
4. Drain and reserve liquor
5. Make a sauce of butter, flour and liquor from vegetables
6. Mix with the vegetables, bring to boiling and serve
7. Alternatively, cook onions in butter, add flour and liquor of carrots, mix with cooked carrots
8. Carrots may be cut in long strips and boiled together with white part of green onions
9. Make sauce of butter, flour and liquor of vegetables
10. Serve with mashed potatoes in the center and carrots around
