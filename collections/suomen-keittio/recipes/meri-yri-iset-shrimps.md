## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup rich milk
- 1 pint boiled shrimps
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- 2 egg yolks
- cream

## Directions

1. Prepare a gravy of 1 tablespoon of butter, 1 tablespoon of flour and 1 cup of rich milk.
2. Add 1 pint of boiled shrimps and heat.
3. Season with salt, pepper and onion juice.
4. Last of all add two yolks of eggs beaten with some cream.
5. Serve either baked in a mold with breadcrumbs sprinkled on top, or fill scooped tomatoes with the mixture.
6. If using tomatoes instead of milk, use bouillon and add the scooped inside of tomato to the mixture.
7. Stir in the shrimps cut in pieces last of all.
