## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 1/2 to 2 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1/4 teaspoon soda
- 3 egg whites
- whipped cream
- sugar
- vanilla
- strawberries

## Directions

1. Rub 1 cup of sugar with 1/2 cup of butter until creamy.
2. Add 3 egg yolks one by one, stirring well.
3. Add 1/2 cup milk, 1 1/2 to 2 cups flour, 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar, and 1/4 teaspoon soda (cream of tartar and soda dissolved in milk).
4. Last mix with 3 egg whites beaten until firm.
5. Butter 3 layer cake tins, pour the mixture into the tins and bake.
6. When cool, spread with whipped cream mixed with some sugar and vanilla.
7. Lay strawberries cut in halves on top and sprinkle with sugar.
8. Pile onto a platter.
9. Serve immediately.
