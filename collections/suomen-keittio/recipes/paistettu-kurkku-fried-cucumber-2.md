## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- egg
- lard

## Directions

1. Peel cucumbers and cut lengthwise into pieces
2. Wipe with a cloth
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Dip in bread crumbs, then egg, then bread crumbs again
5. Fry in lard
