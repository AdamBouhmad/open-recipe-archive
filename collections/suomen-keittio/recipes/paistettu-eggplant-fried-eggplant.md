## Ingredients

- Eggplant
- Salt
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Butter or lard
- Grated cheese (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the eggplant and cut in thin slices.
2. Pile the slices on top of each other and sprinkle salt between.
3. Place under a light weight for 1/2 to 1 hour.
4. Wipe the slices.
5. Turn in egg and bread crumbs.
6. Fry in butter or lard.
7. Add a sprinkling of grated cheese, if preferred.
