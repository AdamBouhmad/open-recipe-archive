## Ingredients

- 1 quart clams
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup cream
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 egg yolks
- Salt and pepper
- Water or fish stock

## Directions

1. Chop the clams finely and put on a gentle fire in their own liquid, with a small amount of water or fish stock added.
2. Simmer gently for about one hour.
3. Thicken with butter and flour.
4. Beat the egg yolks, add the gradually heated milk.
