## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of rice
- 6 cupfuls of milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1/2 lemon
- 3 tablespoonfuls or 4 leaves of gelatine
- 3 cupfuls of cream

## Directions

1. Rinse rice and cook with milk until tender
2. Season with sugar
3. Let cool
4. Add rind and juice of 1/2 lemon
5. Add gelatine dissolved in water
6. Mix with cream whipped to a froth
7. Serve with fruit sauce
