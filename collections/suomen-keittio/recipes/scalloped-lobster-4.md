## Ingredients

- boiled lobster
- milk sauce
- 1 hard-boiled egg yolk
- mace
- lemon juice
- pepper
- lobster shells
- bread crumbs
- butter
- chicken stock
- cream

## Directions

1. Mix boiled lobster cut into cubes with a little milk sauce.
2. Add yolk of one hard-boiled egg, a little mace, lemon juice and pepper.
3. Fill the shells with this mixture.
4. Dredge with bread crumbs.
5. Dot with butter.
6. Bake in oven until brown.
7. Serve with sauce prepared of chicken stock and cream.
