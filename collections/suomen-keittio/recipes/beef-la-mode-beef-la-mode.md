## Ingredients

- 4 pound piece of beef chuck
- Pork for larding, seasoned with salt and pepper
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1 onion
- 2 carrots
- 3/4 cup brandy
- 1 cup white wine (or some lemon juice as substitute for brandy and wine)
- 2 cups veal stock or water

## Directions

1. Smear the beef with pork, dip in salt and pepper
2. Fasten with cord and brown in lard
3. When brown, add salt, pepper, onion, carrots, brandy, white wine, and veal stock or water
4. Cover tightly and let simmer for a few hours until tender, adding stock if necessary
5. Lift the meat onto a platter and remove the cord
6. Prepare a gravy from the drippings, skimming off the fat first
