## Ingredients

- boiled rice
- butter
- egg yolk (optional)

## Directions

1. Put boiled rice in a well buttered flat-bottomed mold
2. Bake for a few minutes in a hot oven
3. Pour onto a suitable dish
4. Use as a foundation on which to set various dishes
5. A little butter and egg yolk may be added
