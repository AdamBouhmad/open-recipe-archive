## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- ½ teaspoonful of salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of milk or bouillon
- ½ cupful of flour

## Directions

1. Beat 2 eggs, add salt, milk or bouillon and flour.
2. Pour slowly through a strainer into boiling lard.
3. When brown drain on paper.
4. Serve in hot bouillon.
