## Ingredients

- 3 yolks of egg
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1/4 cup boiling water
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Mix the oil drop by drop with the yolks, stirring well
2. Add gradually the vinegar and the water
3. Beat in a pan of boiling water until thick
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Add chopped parsley, if desired
