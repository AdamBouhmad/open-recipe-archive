## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of water
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Whip the eggs, add water, pepper and salt
2. Melt butter evenly in a hot omelet pan, and turn the omelet onto it
3. Fry moving the pan over the fire; place the omelet on one side of the pan and brown it quickly on the bottom
4. Turn on a hot platter and serve with tomato sauce
