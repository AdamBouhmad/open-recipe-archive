## Ingredients

- 2 hard boiled egg yolks
- 1 raw yolk
- ½ teaspoon butter
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Force the yolks through a sieve, add seasonings and raw yolk of an egg.
2. Form into small balls, roll in flour.
3. Boil in salted water or in butter.
4. Serve with soup.
