## Ingredients

- 8 whole eggs
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 6-8 ozs. of flour
- 4-8 ozs. of butter

## Directions

1. Whip eggs and sugar in a pan of hot water on the fire until the mixture is thick and light, about 20-30 minutes
2. Add melted butter and sifted flour
3. Pour in a buttered mold and bake in a moderate oven about 3/4 hour
