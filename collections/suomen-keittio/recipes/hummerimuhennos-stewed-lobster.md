## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiled lobster, cut in cubes
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 slice of onion
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup rich milk
- salt
- paprika
- some sherry or lemon juice
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 cup cream

## Directions

1. Cook 1 tablespoon of butter with a slice of onion.
2. Add 1 tablespoon of flour, 1 cup of rich milk and the lobster cubes.
3. Season with salt and paprika.
4. Bring to boiling.
5. Before serving, add some sherry or lemon juice.
6. Beat 2 egg yolks with 1/2 cup of cream and add to the mixture.
7. Shake over the fire for a minute.
