## Ingredients

- apples, pears, peaches or plums
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 cups water

## Directions

1. Cut the fruit lengthwise in two, pare and core.
2. Pack tight in glass jars having airtight covers.
3. Cook 1 cup sugar in 2 cups water for 10 minutes and pour over fruit while hot.
4. Screw covers on as tight as you can get them with the thumb and the little finger.
5. Place the jars on a foundation in a kettle.
6. Pour lukewarm water into the kettle up to 2/3 of the height of the jars.
7. Steam slowly with the kettle covered.
8. Remove the covers of the jars one at a time and fill the jars with hot syrup made of sugar and water.
9. Replace the covers and let cool.
10. Tighten the covers until airtight.
11. The apples may be flavored with vanilla and cinnamon.
