## Ingredients

- 1 cup boiling milk
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- ½ teaspoon vanilla
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Add to 1 cup boiling milk 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 4 tablespoons sugar
2. Boil up
3. Flavor with vanilla and thicken with 2 egg yolks
4. Reheat, not allowing it to boil
5. Let cool
6. May be flavored with lemon, orange or any fruit juice preferred, dissolved chocolate, chopped almonds, or black coffee
