## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 5 eggs
- 1/2 pound raisins
- 1/4 pound lemon rind cut in pieces
- 1/4 pound chopped almonds
- 1/4 cup brandy or lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Rub the butter with the sugar until creamy
2. Add the yolks of the eggs beaten to a froth
3. Add raisins, lemon rind, almonds, brandy
4. Add soda and flour sifted together
5. Last of all fold in the whites of the eggs beaten stiff
6. Bake in a tub tin
