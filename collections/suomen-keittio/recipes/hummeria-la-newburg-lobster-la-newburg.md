## Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked lobster
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sherry wine or lemon juice
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup heated cream

## Directions

1. Cut the lobster into cubes about an inch in size.
2. Put the butter in a saucepan and add the lobster pieces; season with salt and pepper.
3. Cover and let simmer gently about 5 minutes.
4. Add a little Sherry wine or lemon juice, two egg yolks beaten and mixed with one cup of heated cream.
5. Shake the pan over the fire until the mixture is blended.
6. Alternatively, cooked egg yolks pressed through a sieve may be used instead of raw yolks, which prevents the mixture from curdling when heated.
