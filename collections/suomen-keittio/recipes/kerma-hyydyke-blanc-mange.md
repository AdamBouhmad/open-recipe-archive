## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cupfuls of cream
- 3 teaspoonfuls of powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla
- 4 leaves or 3 tablespoons of gelatine
- macaroons and fruit jelly for garnish

## Directions

1. Whip cream with sugar to a froth.
2. Dissolve gelatine in water and add carefully to the cream.
3. Add vanilla.
4. Pour in a serving dish to stiffen.
5. Garnish with macaroons and fruit jelly.
