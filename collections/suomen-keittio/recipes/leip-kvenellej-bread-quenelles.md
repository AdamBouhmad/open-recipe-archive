## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of cream
- 1 teaspoonful of chopped parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Beat 2 eggs, mix with butter, cream, chopped parsley, salt, pepper and nutmeg.
2. Add bread crumbs until thick enough to be formed into small rolls.
3. Turn in flour and cook in salted water.
4. May be mixed with chopped almonds, if desired.
