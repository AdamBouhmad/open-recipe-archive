## Ingredients

- Beets
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 2 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. Boil the beets until tender, cut in slices
2. Make a sauce of 3/4 cup of sugar, 1/2 tablespoon of cornstarch, 1/2 cup of vinegar and 2 tablespoons of water
3. Cook for 5 minutes
4. Pour the sauce over the beets let simmer for 1/2 hour
5. Serve hot or cold
