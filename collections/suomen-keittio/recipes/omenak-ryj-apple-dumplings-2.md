## Ingredients

- puff paste
- apples
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter
- egg white

## Directions

1. Roll puff paste into a thin sheet, cut into squares, big enough to enclose an apple.
2. Pare and core apples.
3. Bake them in oven a little while.
4. Put in the centre of each square an apple, fill the core with sugar, mixed with a little cinnamon and dot with a little piece of butter.
5. Dampen the edges of the paste with white of egg, close the paste, bringing the corners together.
6. Turn the edges nicely in.
7. Spread with egg, bake in a moderate oven until the apples are softened.
8. Serve hot with hard sauce.
