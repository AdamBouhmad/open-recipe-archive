## Ingredients

- 3 cups of meat or fish stock well seasoned
- 4 tablespoons tomato
- 1/2 onion
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 1/2 package or 12 leaves of gelatine
- 2 eggs, whites and shells, lightly beaten
- Kitchen Bouquet (optional)

## Directions

1. Place all the ingredients into a saucepan with the lightly beaten whites and shells of 2 eggs.
2. Put on the fire, stir until it begins to boil.
3. Let simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Strain through a fine cloth.
5. If not clear, strain once more without pressing the cloth.
6. If darker color is preferred, add a few drops of Kitchen Bouquet.
