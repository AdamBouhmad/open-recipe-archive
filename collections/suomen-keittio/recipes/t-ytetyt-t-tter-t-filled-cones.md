## Ingredients

- batter as for roll tart
- whipped cream
- sugar
- jam

## Directions

1. Prepare a batter as for roll tart.
2. Spread on a buttered baking sheet shaping it into round cakes.
3. Bake.
4. Roll immediately into cones.
5. Just before serving fill the cones with whipped cream mixed with sugar and jam.
