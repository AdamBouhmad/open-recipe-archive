## Ingredients

- 2.5 pounds steak, 12 inches long, 7 inches wide, 1 inch thick
- 4 ounces veal
- 3 ounces pork
- 5 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 cup meat stock
- 1/2 onion
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- Salt and pepper
- Butter

## Directions

1. Pound the steak well and sprinkle with salt and pepper
2. Grind the veal and pork fine
3. Mix the bread crumbs with the meat stock, add the onion fried, egg, parsley, salt and pepper
4. Add this mixture to the ground meat and beat well
5. Spread the mixture over the steak, roll together and tie with string
6. Melt butter in a dripping pan and place the steak in it
7. Bake in oven until brown
8. Add meat stock or water and continue baking for 3/4 or 1 hour, basting often
9. Prepare a gravy from the drippings by adding flour, stock and cream
