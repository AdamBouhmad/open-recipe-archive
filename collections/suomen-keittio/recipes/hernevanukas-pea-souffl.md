## Ingredients

- 1 cup canned peas or fresh ones
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- pepper
- a few drops of onion juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the peas
2. Rub through a sieve
3. Add eggs, melted butter, salt, pepper and a few drops of onion juice
4. Place mixture in a buttered mold
5. Bake in a pan of water covered with buttered paper until it feels firm
