## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of onion
- 1/2 cupful of boiled, chopped chicken or any kind of meat
- 1/2 cupful of bread crumbs
- 1 teaspoonful of minced parsley
- 1 egg
- bread crumbs for topping
- brown sauce

## Directions

1. Wipe and cut a slice from top of tomatoes, leave skins on
2. Scoop out the inside part and sprinkle the insides with salt
3. Cook 1 tablespoonful of butter with 1 tablespoonful of onion
4. Add 1/2 cupful of boiled, chopped chicken or any kind of meat
5. Add 1/2 cupful of bread crumbs
6. Add the insides of tomatoes
7. Add 1 teaspoonful of minced parsley
8. Let the mixture come to boiling point
9. Add 1 egg
10. Put the filled tomatoes in a buttered baking pan
11. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
12. Bake in oven nearly 20 minutes
13. Serve with brown sauce
