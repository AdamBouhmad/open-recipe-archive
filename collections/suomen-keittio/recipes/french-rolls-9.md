## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 1 cake of yeast
- flour

## Directions

1. Scald milk
2. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm milk and mix with 1 cupful of flour
3. Cover, set to rise
4. Add eggs beaten with sugar, salt, butter and flour enough to make a moderately thick dough
5. Knead well, set to rise
6. Form into rolls
7. Let rise again and bake in oven
