## Ingredients

- peanut butter
- cream
- salt
- buttered bread
- raisins
- walnuts

## Directions

1. Dilute peanut butter with a little cream.
2. Season with salt.
3. Spread on buttered bread.
4. Mix with chopped raisins and walnuts if preferred.
