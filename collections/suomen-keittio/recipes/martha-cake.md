## Ingredients

- 9 ozs. of flour
- 2 ozs. of almonds
- 1/4 pound of butter
- 4 ozs. of seeded raisins
- 1 cake of yeast
- 1/4 pound of sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 cupfuls of lukewarm milk
- 1/4 yellow lemon rind

## Directions

1. Peel and chop almonds.
2. Mix flour, salt, raisins and almonds thoroughly together in a bowl.
3. Add grated lemon rind.
4. Rub yeast to a powder, mix with sugar, stir into milk, then add mixture of the dry ingredients into it.
5. Beat eggs and sugar until light, work butter to a cream.
6. Combine all the above mixtures.
7. Knead the dough well and put in a buttered mold to rise to double its size.
8. Bake in the oven for about an hour.
9. Turn out from mold, brush with butter and sprinkle a little sugar on top.
