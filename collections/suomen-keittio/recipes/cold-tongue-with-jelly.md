## Ingredients

- 1 beef tongue
- Cold water
- Hot salted water
- 1 quart of tongue liquor
- 8 tablespoonfuls of dissolved gelatine or 10 leaves of gelatine
- 1/2 cupful of tomato
- Whites of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Soak a beef tongue in cold water.
2. Put it into hot water and scrape well.
3. Put in hot salted water, let simmer until tender; allow 2-3 hours for boiling.
4. Skin the tongue.
5. Strain the liquor, skim, and prepare an aspic jelly by allowing 8 tablespoonfuls of dissolved gelatine (or 10 leaves of gelatine), 1/2 cupful of tomato and whites of 2 eggs to 1 quart of liquor of tongue.
6. Boil stirring well.
7. Let simmer 5 minutes.
8. Strain through a fine cloth.
9. Cover bottom of a mold with clarified aspic jelly.
10. Cut the tongue in slices.
11. Arrange the slices into the mold so as to give the appearance of a whole tongue.
12. Pour gradually the aspic jelly over it and put aside to set.
