## Ingredients

- chicken livers
- butter
- tomato slices
- salt
- pepper
- chicken mousse
- brown paper
- truffle sauce

## Directions

1. Fry the livers with butter in a pan
2. Place slices of tomatoes, sprinkled first with salt and pepper
3. Cover with chicken mousse
4. Bake in oven with brown paper on for 10-12 minutes
5. Serve with truffle sauce
