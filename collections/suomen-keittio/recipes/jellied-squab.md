## Ingredients

- Squab
- Stuffing for stuffed chicken
- Thin cloth

## Directions

1. Remove the bones as directed.
2. Fill with the same stuffing as directed for stuffed chicken.
3. Sew up and wrap in a thin cloth.
4. Cook about 3/4 hour, or until done.
5. Remove from the liquor and put under a tiny weight until cold and even.
6. Prepare an aspic jelly of the liquor.
7. Line with it the bottom of a mold.
8. Cut the squab crosswise into slices.
9. Arrange nicely into the mold.
10. Pour the jelly over it and set aside to get firm.
