## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of pineapple
- 2 cupfuls of water
- 1 cupful of sugar
- juice of ½ lemon
- 2 ozs. of gelatine
- 2 whites of eggs and shells for clarifying

## Directions

1. Put pineapple liquor, water, sugar, lemon, gelatine and lightly whipped whites of eggs in a saucepan and stir on the fire until the mixture comes to boiling point.
2. Let simmer 5 minutes.
3. Drain through a cloth.
4. Pour clear jelly in the mold, let it get firm.
5. Fill the mold with cut pineapple, pour the rest of the jelly on.
6. Let get quite firm.
