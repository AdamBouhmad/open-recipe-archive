## Ingredients

- onions
- salted water
- butter
- salt
- sugar
- meat stock
- water
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Peel onions and cook in salted water until half done.
2. Drain off the water, brown in butter, sprinkled with salt and sugar.
3. Mix with one cupful of meat stock or water and let simmer, covered, until tender.
