## Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked and mashed pumpkin
- 2 egg yolks and 1 whole egg
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 2 cups milk
- 1/4 teaspoon each cinnamon, nutmeg, and mace
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
- Some lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mix the pumpkin with the dry ingredients.
2. Add milk or cream and whipped eggs.
3. Bake in an open crust.
