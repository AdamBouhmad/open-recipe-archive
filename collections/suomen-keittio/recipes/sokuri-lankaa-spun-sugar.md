## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- 1 cupful of water
- 1/4 teaspoonful of cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Put 2 cupfuls of sugar in a saucepan with 1 cupful of water and 1/4 teaspoonful of cream of tartar.
2. Place on fire stirring until the sugar has dissolved.
3. Boil without stirring until the sugar gets firm when dipped in cold water, and cracks when broken.
4. Put the saucepan in cold water to stop boiling.
5. Put dipper or two forks in syrup and spin sugar from them over a stick, shaking them quickly back and forth.
6. Remove the spun sugar carefully from the stick and use to garnish desserts.
7. Keep well when covered with a bowl.
