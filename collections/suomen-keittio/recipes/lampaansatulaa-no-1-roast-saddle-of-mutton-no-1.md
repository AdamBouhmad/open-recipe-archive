## Ingredients

- Saddle of mutton
- Salt
- Pepper
- Thin brown gravy
- Currant jelly
- Parsley
- Radishes

## Directions

1. Separate the back part before splitting the mutton in two.
2. Remove the kidneys, the fat and the skin.
3. Crack the ribs and trim into a nice shape.
4. Tie with string.
5. Roast in a hot oven, basting often and allowing 9 minutes to each pound.
6. Before taking to the table, remove the meat from the backbone and slice crosswise.
7. Replace the slices around the backbone.
8. Serve with thin brown gravy and currant jelly, garnished with parsley and radishes.
