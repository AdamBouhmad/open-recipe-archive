## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2 cups rhubarb
- water

## Directions

1. Wash rhubarb and cut in pieces without peeling if young.
2. Put rhubarb and sugar into a bowl in layers and let stand over night.
3. Reduce the juice by boiling to a thick syrup.
4. Add the rhubarb and simmer until clear.
5. Pour into jars while hot and cover as given for jams.
