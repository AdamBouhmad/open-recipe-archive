## Ingredients

- Fillet of beef
- Ham
- Eggs
- Hollandaise sauce or milk sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare as mignon fillets
2. Place a slice of fried ham on each piece
3. Place a poached egg on top
4. Cover with a rich milk or Hollandaise sauce
