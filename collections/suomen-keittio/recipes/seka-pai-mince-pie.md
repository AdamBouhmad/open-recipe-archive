## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of lean beef, boiled and chopped
- 1 lb. of suet, chopped
- 2 quarts of apples, chopped
- 1 lb. of cleaned currants
- 2 lbs. of raisins, stones removed
- 1/2 lb. of lemon peel, cut in pieces
- 1 cupful of molasses
- 1 1/2 cups of brown sugar
- 2 lemon juice
- 2 orange juice
- 1/2 teaspoonful of nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful each of cinnamon, cloves and mixed spices
- 1 nutmeg, chopped
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of brandy
- 1 cupful of sherry wine
- 1 cupful of cider vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix meat and suet
2. Mix dry ingredients and then liquids
3. Let simmer gently
4. Add spices after boiling
5. Pack in a jar for a few days before use
6. If mixture looks too firm, add broth in which the meat was boiled
7. Bake between two crusts in a moderate oven 1 hour
