## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup water
- 1½ teaspoons cream of tartar
- a smooth stick

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan 2 cups sugar, 1 cup water and 1½ teaspoons cream of tartar.
2. Place over the fire and stir until the sugar is dissolved.
3. Boil without stirring until the sugar becomes firm when dipped in cold water and cracks when broken.
4. Place the pan in cold water to stop the boiling.
5. Dip two forks into the syrup and spin the sugar from them by shaking them rapidly over a smooth stick.
6. Remove the spun sugar carefully from the stick and use to garnish desserts.
7. Keeps best when covered with a bowl.
