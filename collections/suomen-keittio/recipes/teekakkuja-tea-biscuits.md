## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 yeast cake

## Directions

1. Warm the milk, add butter, sugar and salt.
2. Mix with the yeast and a part of the flour, let rise.
3. Add the rest of the flour and let rise again.
4. Form into rolls, place on a buttered baking sheet, let rise and bake in a hot oven.
5. May be baked in gem pans by having the batter very thin.
