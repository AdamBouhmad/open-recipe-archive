## Ingredients

- Rabbit
- Water
- Vinegar or lemon juice
- Salt
- Flour
- Lard or butter
- Cream
- Jelly

## Directions

1. Clean the rabbit, rinse and remove the fine skin.
2. Soak in water mixed with salt and vinegar or lemon juice for 2 hours.
3. Wipe the pieces of meat, dip in flour.
4. Cook in butter or lard covered until tender.
5. Skim off the fat, add some water or bouillon.
6. Let boil quickly.
7. Season, add some cream and serve with jelly.
