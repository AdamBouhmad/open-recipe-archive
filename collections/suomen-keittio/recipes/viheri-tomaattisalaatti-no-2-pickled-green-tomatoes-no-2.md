## Ingredients

- 8 quarts tomatoes
- 1 cup onions cut in pieces
- 3 red peppers chopped
- 4 quarts vinegar
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 1 cup prepared mustard
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon and cloves

## Directions

1. Let stand in salt water the tomatoes, onion and pepper
2. Or add a little vinegar to the vegetables, parboil, drain off the vinegar
3. Add to the vegetables vinegar, sugar, salt, mustard and spices
4. Let simmer, put into glass jars, cover airtight
