## Ingredients

- 6 pounds of apples
- 4 pounds of sugar
- 3 cupfuls of water
- a piece of cinnamon
- lemon rind

## Directions

1. Pare and core 6 pounds of apples.
2. Prepare them whole or cut in nice pieces.
3. Cook 4 pounds of sugar, 3 cupfuls of water, a piece of cinnamon and lemon rind until thick syrup.
4. Place the apples in and let simmer until quite done and clear.
5. Put in jar and seal as directed.
