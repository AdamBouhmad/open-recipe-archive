## Ingredients

- 1 pint of clams
- butter
- flour
- clam liquor
- scalded cream
- soda
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Have 1 pint of clams, remove the hard parts and chop them fine
2. Cook one heaping tablespoonful of butter with the chopped clams
3. Add one tablespoonful of flour and 1/2 cupful of clam liquor
4. Cook for a few minutes
5. Mix with soft parts of clams and 1 cupful of scalded cream mixed with a pinch of soda
6. Let cook for 2 minutes
7. Pour gradually in it beaten yolks of 2 eggs mixed with a little cream
8. Season with salt and pepper
9. Serve immediately on buttered toast
