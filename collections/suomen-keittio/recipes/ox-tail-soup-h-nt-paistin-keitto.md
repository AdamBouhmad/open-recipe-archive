## Ingredients

- 2 ox-tails
- 1 onion
- 1 carrot
- 1 root or stalk of celery
- 1 sprig of parsley
- 5 peppercorns
- 2 cloves
- few grains of cayenne
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 5 quarts of brown stock
- a little wine and lemon
- 3 tablesp. of butter or 1 tablesp. of raw ham cut into pieces
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Scrape the vegetables and cut into small cubes.
2. Cut the ox-tails separating them at the joints.
3. Saute the pieces with vegetables, flour and seasonings in butter in the soup-pot on a slow fire.
4. Add brown stock and salt into it, let simmer, covered, for 3 hours.
5. Remove the pieces of ox-tail, when the meat is tender, strain the stock, season with cayenne, white wine or sherry.
6. Serve with the pieces of ox-tail, hard boiled yolks of egg, fried bread dice and two slices of lemon.
7. Color with Kitchen Bouquet if wanted.
