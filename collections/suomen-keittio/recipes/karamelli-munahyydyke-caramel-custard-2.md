## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of water
- 1 pint of milk
- 3 yolks of eggs
- 2 whole eggs
- 1/2 teaspoonful of vanilla
- 1/4 cupful of sugar
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Melt 1 cupful of sugar in a pan, stirring well, to a syrup of light brown color
2. Add 1 tablespoonful of water
3. Pour a part of syrup in a buttered mold, covering the bottom and the sides
4. Add equal part of water to the remaining syrup and set aside
5. Beat the eggs, add sugar, salt, vanilla and scalded milk
6. Pour the mixture into the mold on the hardened syrup
7. Steam in a slow oven in a water bath until firm
8. Serve cold, unmolded, with caramel sauce
