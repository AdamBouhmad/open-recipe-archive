## Ingredients

- 2 herrings
- 4 cups boiled or raw potatoes
- ½ onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 cups milk
- 3 eggs
- Chopped parsley and pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the fish, skin and bone
2. Spread a mold with butter
3. Fill alternately with layers of potatoes and herring having potatoes on the bottom and on the top of the dish
4. Place the chopped onion on the layers of fish and pour melted butter over the top
5. Beat the eggs, add milk, pepper and parsley, pour into the mold
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
7. Bake
