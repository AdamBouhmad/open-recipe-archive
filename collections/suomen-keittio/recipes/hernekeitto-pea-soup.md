## Ingredients

- 1½ cups dried peas
- 1½ quarts bone stock or bouillon
- 2½ quarts cold water
- 1 small onion
- 2 stalks celery
- ½ pound pork
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the peas, soak in lukewarm water for a few hours.
2. Drain, put into cold water with the ham.
3. Cook on a slow fire.
4. Cook the onion in butter, add flour, combine with the soup.
5. Strain, rubbing the peas through the strainer, or serve unstrained.
6. Cut the pork in pieces, serve in the soup.
7. Instead of meat stock, milk or water may be used.
