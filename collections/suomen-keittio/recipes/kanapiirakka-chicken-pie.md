## Ingredients

- pieces of chicken or remains of chicken
- butter
- 1 onion
- 1 carrot
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1½ cups water or chicken stock
- 1 tablespoon tomato
- pie crust
- 1 egg
- vegetables (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil pieces of chicken or use remains of chicken.
2. Prepare a brown gravy by frying butter with onion and carrot.
3. Add 1 tablespoon of flour, 1½ cups of water or chicken stock and tomato; boil quickly.
4. Lay the pieces of chicken into a mold and strain the gravy over.
5. Cover with pie crust, spread with egg, and perforate with fork.
6. Bake in oven until the crust is brown.
7. Vegetables may be used in the pie if desired.
