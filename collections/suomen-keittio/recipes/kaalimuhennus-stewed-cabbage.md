## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 or 2 tablespoons sugar
- Salt
- 2 cups boiling water
- 1 tablespoon flour
- Juice of 1 or 2 lemons or 3 tablespoons vinegar
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage in strips, parboil in salted water and drain.
2. Mix with 1 tablespoon of butter, 1 or 2 tablespoons of sugar, salt and 2 cups of boiling water.
3. Let simmer covered until tender.
4. Thicken with 1 tablespoon of flour mixed in cold water.
5. Add the juice of 1 or 2 lemons or 3 tablespoons of vinegar, season and continue boiling for 10 minutes.
