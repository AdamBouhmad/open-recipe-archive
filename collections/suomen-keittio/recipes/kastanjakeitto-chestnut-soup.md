## Ingredients

- 2 quarts white stock
- 1½ cups boiled and chopped chestnuts
- 1 slice onion
- salt and pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup cream
- Madeira or tomato

## Directions

1. Parboil the chestnuts so as to remove the shells easily.
2. The shells must be removed while the chestnuts are hot.
3. Cook until tender in meat stock, press through a sieve.
4. Cook the onion in butter, add flour, chestnuts and dilute with meat stock.
5. Cook for 5 minutes longer.
6. Season, add two spoonfuls of Madeira or tomato.
7. May be made by using vegetable stock or water instead of meat stock.
