## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 2 ounces of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of finely ground cardamom
- 1 cake of yeast
- 12 tablespoonfuls of milk
- 6 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- wheat flour as needed

## Directions

1. Prepare dough as for wheat dough.
2. Let it rise and shape small rings from it.
3. Bake them on buttered baking sheets in the oven immediately after shaping.
