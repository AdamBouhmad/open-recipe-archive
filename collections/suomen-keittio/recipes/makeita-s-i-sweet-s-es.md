## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 cupful of almonds
- 1/2 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 lb. of flour

## Directions

1. Prepare batter
2. Mix the batter with peeled and chopped almonds and soda mixed with flour
3. Form into cookies and bake like the corn cookies
