## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1 cupful of molasses
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 egg
- 1 heaping tablespoonful of butter
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking soda
- 1 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 3/4 teaspoonful of ginger
- flour enough to make a thin batter, a little thicker than for cakes

## Directions

1. Warm molasses, butter and sugar, rub to a froth.
2. Add beaten egg, milk and flour mixed with cream of tartar and soda.
3. Bake on a shallow pan and cut into squares.
