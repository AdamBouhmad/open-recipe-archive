## Ingredients

- 4 ozs. macaroni
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 onion or some grated cheese
- 1 cup milk
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni in pieces, boil with a dash of salt, drain.
2. Butter an omelet pan, lay the macaroni on the bottom.
3. Spread the chopped onion fried in butter or the grated cheese on the macaroni.
4. Beat the eggs, season and add the milk.
5. Pour over the macaroni.
6. Bake until set.
7. May be served with or without vegetables.
