## Ingredients

- Scallops
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup cream
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Prepare same as oyster stew by using scallops instead of oysters.
2. Boil the scallops for 2 minutes.
