## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons gelatine
- 0.5 cup water
- 1 cup fish stock
- 0.5 cup mayonnaise
- 1.5 cups whipped cream
- 2 cups cold fish, cut in cubes
- Lettuce leaves
- Cucumbers
- French dressing

## Directions

1. Melt 2 tablespoons of gelatine in 0.5 cup of water.
2. Add 1 cup of fish stock, heat until the gelatine is melted, let cool.
3. When the mixture begins to thicken, add 0.5 cup of mayonnaise, whipping it until firm.
4. Mix with 1.5 cups of whipped cream and last of all add 2 cups of cold fish cut in cubes.
5. Moisten the fish mold with water mixed with gelatine, put the mixture in, let thicken.
6. Serve on a platter having lettuce leaves and cucumber around the fish.
7. Pour French dressing over.
