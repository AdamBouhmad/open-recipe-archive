## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cupful of cream
- 2½ cupfuls of water
- 2 teaspoonfuls of salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- ½ pound of buckwheat flour
- 10 ounces of wheat flour
- 2-3 cupfuls of lukewarm milk
- ½ cake of yeast

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm water.
2. Whip eggs, add cream, water, salt, flours and lastly butter, whipping well.
3. Set batter to rise over night.
4. In the morning stir in lukewarm milk until batter is of moderate thickness.
5. Bake like griddle cakes.
6. Serve with melted butter and syrup.
