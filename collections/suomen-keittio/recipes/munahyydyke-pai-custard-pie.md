## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- a dash of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla
- pastry

## Directions

1. Mix milk with 2 yolks and 1 whole egg, beaten with 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar
2. Season with a dash of salt and 1 teaspoonful of vanilla
3. Lay pastry in a pieplate
4. Spread with white of egg
5. Pour the custard on
6. Bake until it gets firm
