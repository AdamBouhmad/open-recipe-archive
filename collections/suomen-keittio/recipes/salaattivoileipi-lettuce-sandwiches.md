## Ingredients

- lettuce leaves
- bread slices
- mayonnaise
- walnuts

## Directions

1. Rinse and wipe lettuce leaves.
2. Place on slices of bread spread with mayonnaise.
3. Sprinkle with chopped walnuts if desired.
4. Cover with another slice of bread.
