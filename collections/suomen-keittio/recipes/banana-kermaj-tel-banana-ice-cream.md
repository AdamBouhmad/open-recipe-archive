## Ingredients

- 1 quart of cream
- 4 bananas
- 2 tablespoonfuls of lemon juice
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 3 yolks of eggs

## Directions

1. Put one half of the cream on the fire.
2. Beat yolks with sugar.
3. Pour boiling cream slowly in it and stir in double-boiler on the fire until thickened.
4. Let cool.
5. Add bananas forced through a sieve, lemon juice and the remaining cream.
6. Freeze as usually.
