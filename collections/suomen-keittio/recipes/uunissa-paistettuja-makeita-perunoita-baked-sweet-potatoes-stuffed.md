## Ingredients

- Baked sweet potatoes
- Butter
- Salt and spices
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 cup water
- 3 1/2 tablespoons butter for syrup

## Directions

1. Halve baked sweet potatoes lengthwise, scoop out the insides and mash.
2. Add some butter and spices, return to the peels.
3. Cook 1/2 cup of sugar in 1 cup of water for 5 minutes, add 3 1/2 tablespoons of butter to the syrup.
4. Spread the tops of potatoes with the syrup.
5. Bake until nice brown basting often with the syrup.
