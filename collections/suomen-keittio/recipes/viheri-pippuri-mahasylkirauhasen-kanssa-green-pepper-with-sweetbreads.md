## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 1 sweetbread
- salt
- pepper
- Worcestershire sauce
- boiled chopped mushrooms
- green peppers
- lard
- bread crumbs
- 1 egg yolk
- cream

## Directions

1. Prepare a gravy of 1 tablespoon butter, 1 tablespoon flour and 1 cup chicken stock.
2. Cook sweetbreads as directed, cut in pieces, add to the gravy.
3. Season with salt, pepper and Worcestershire sauce.
4. Last of all add boiled chopped mushrooms.
5. Boil green pepper quickly in lard, so as to easily remove the bitter skin.
6. Cool, fill with sweetbreads, sprinkle with bread crumbs.
7. Bake until the surface is brown.
8. Make the gravy of butter, flour and chicken stock, season with salt and pepper.
9. Last of all add 1 yolk of egg and some cream.
