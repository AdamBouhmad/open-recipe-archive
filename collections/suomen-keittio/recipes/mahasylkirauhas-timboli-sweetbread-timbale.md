## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked sweetbreads
- 1/2 cup chopped chicken breast
- 2 egg whites
- 1 1/2 cups whipping cream
- butter for molds
- butter
- flour
- chicken stock
- cream

## Directions

1. Rub the boiled sweetbreads through a sieve.
2. Have 1 cup of sweetbreads and 1/2 cup of chopped chicken breast, add the whites of 2 eggs and rub well.
3. Finally add 1 1/2 cups of whipping cream slowly to the mixture, stirring briskly.
4. Butter timbale molds and fill with the mousse.
5. Bake in oven in a pan of water covered with buttered paper until firm.
6. Serve with a sauce made of butter, flour, chicken stock and cream.
