## Ingredients

- black coffee
- cracked ice
- sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. Prepare black coffee
2. Strain and cool
3. Half fill glasses with cracked ice
4. Fill with coffee
5. Sweeten with sugar
6. Add cream if desired
