## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- mushrooms
- salt and pepper
- rich milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Put 2 tablespoonfuls of butter in a frying pan
2. Peel the mushrooms, cut into pieces and fry in butter 5 minutes
3. Take out the mushrooms
4. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and rich milk in the pan
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Stir the mushrooms into the sauce, heat thoroughly
