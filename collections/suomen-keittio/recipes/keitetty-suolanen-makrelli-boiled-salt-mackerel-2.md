## Ingredients

- salt mackerel
- warm water
- a little vinegar
- onion
- milk
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Soak the fish in warm water.
2. Cook with a little vinegar and onion until tender.
3. Place on a serving-dish, pour milksauce, hard-boiled chopped eggs or two yolks upon it.
4. Or flake the fish in pieces, mix with sauce, serve on toast.
5. When milk is used for cooking instead of water, the fish will taste better.
