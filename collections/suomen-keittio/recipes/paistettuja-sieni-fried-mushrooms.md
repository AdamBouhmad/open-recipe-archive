## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms and cut off the stems
2. Heat the butter in a frying pan
3. Fry the mushrooms rapidly, turning often
4. For larger mushrooms, dip in egg and bread crumbs, then brown in butter
5. Serve on toast garnished with lemon
