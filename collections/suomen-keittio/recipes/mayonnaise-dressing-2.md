## Ingredients

- yolk of 1 egg
- 3/4 cupful of salad oil
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice
- 1/2 tablesp. of Tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- a little cayenne
- 1/2 teaspoonful of mustard powder

## Directions

1. Mix mustard with yolk, add gradually the oil, at first drop by drop, stirring constantly.
2. As mixture thickens, dilute it with a little lemon juice and vinegar.
3. Lastly season with salt and pepper.
4. If dressing curdles, take yolk of another egg and add curdled mixture slowly to it.
