## Ingredients

- 1 bunch of mint
- 1/2 cupful of vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the mint, chop the leaves finely
2. Mix sugar with vinegar
3. When sugar has melted, mix with the mint
4. Let stand one hour
5. If the vinegar is too strong dilute with a little water
6. Serve with roast lamb
