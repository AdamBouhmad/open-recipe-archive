## Ingredients

- onions
- cold water
- salted water
- butter
- salt
- sugar
- vinegar
- milk sauce
- Bechamel sauce
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Put onions in cold water and remove outer skins.
2. Place in a saucepan and pour boiling water over.
3. Let boil 10 minutes.
4. Drain off the water, and pour a little cold water on the onions.
5. Melt 2 tablespoonfuls of butter in a saucepan, place the onions in it, sprinkle with a little salt and sugar.
6. Pour 1 cupful of water mixed with vinegar on them, let simmer, covered, until tender.
7. Serve with milk sauce or Bechamel sauce or with melted butter.
