## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/2 cup flour
- 2 eggs
- Fine sugar for sprinkling
- Whipped cream mixed with fruit juice for serving

## Directions

1. Place butter and milk into a saucepan on the fire; when coming to boil, add the flour stirring well so as to have a smooth paste.
2. Cook until brown underneath.
3. Remove from the fire, let cool.
4. Beat the yolks, work into the paste, at the last add the whites beaten firm.
5. Force through a pastry bag or drop by the spoonful into boiling lard.
6. Sprinkle with sugar.
7. Serve with whipped cream mixed with fruit juice.
