## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of ground tongue
- 1/2 cupful of whipped cream
- 3 tablespoonfuls of gelatine
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sherry
- Pepper and salt
- Liquid jelly
- Red pepper
- Whites of hard-boiled eggs
- Lettuce salad

## Directions

1. Mix tongue with salt, pepper, sherry, gelatine and cream.
2. Line a mold with liquid jelly.
3. Garnish with red pepper and whites of hard-boiled eggs cut in slices.
4. Put the tongue into the mold.
5. Let thicken.
6. Unmold; serve with lettuce salad.
