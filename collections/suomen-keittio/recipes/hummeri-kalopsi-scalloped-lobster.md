## Ingredients

- 1 boiled lobster, cut in cubes
- milk sauce
- hard boiled egg yolk
- mace
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice
- bread crumbs
- butter
- fish stock
- cream

## Directions

1. Cut the boiled lobster meat in cubes.
2. Mix with some milk sauce, yolk of a hard boiled egg, some mace, pepper, salt and lemon juice.
3. Fill the lobster shell with this mixture.
4. Dredge with bread crumbs and dot with butter.
5. Brown in oven.
6. Serve with sauce made of fish stock and cream.
7. Alternatively, the mixture may be baked in a mold and made thinner by using less milk sauce.
8. Instead of milk sauce, bread crumbs may be used.
9. Fill the mold with layers of lobster having bread crumbs, salt and pepper between the layers.
10. Cover with milk mixed with 2 eggs and dot with butter.
11. Bake.
