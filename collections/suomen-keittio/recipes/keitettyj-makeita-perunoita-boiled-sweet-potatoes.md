## Ingredients

- Sweet potatoes
- Salt
- Water
- Syrup (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the potatoes well
2. Place on the fire in boiling water slightly salted
3. Boil until tender
4. Peel and place into a hot oven for a few minutes if too watery
5. Cover with syrup and brown quickly in oven if desired
