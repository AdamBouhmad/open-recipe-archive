## Ingredients

- Eel
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Butter or lard
- Lemon juice
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, cut in pieces two inches thick (if small, use them whole).
2. Parboil with salt and pepper.
3. Wipe, roll in flour.
4. Fry in butter or lard until nice and brown.
5. Arrange on a platter, pour the drippings over, add some lemon juice.
6. Strew with chopped parsley.
7. Or: Dip the fish in egg, roll in bread crumbs, cook in lard.
8. Serve with tomato or tartar sauce.
