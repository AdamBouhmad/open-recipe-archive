## Ingredients

- 6 potatoes
- 3 leeks
- 3 quarts of vegetable stock
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 cupful of cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and cut in pieces.
2. Cook in vegetable stock until tender.
3. Cut the leeks in pieces and cook in butter, add flour and the soup, and press through a strainer.
4. Return to the fire, stir until thick.
5. Finely chopped parsley gives it a good flavor.
