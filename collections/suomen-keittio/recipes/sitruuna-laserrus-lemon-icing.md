## Ingredients

- 1 cup confectioner's sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon water
- grated lemon rind

## Directions

1. Have 1 cup confectioner's sugar
2. Mix in slowly 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 1 tablespoon water and some grated lemon rind
3. More water and less lemon juice may be used, or other flavoring may be substituted for lemon
