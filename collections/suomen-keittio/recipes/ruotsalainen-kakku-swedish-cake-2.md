## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of sour cream
- 1 1/4 cupfuls of brown sugar
- 1 lb. of flour
- 4 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful of cardamom

## Directions

1. Whip eggs and sugar to a froth
2. Add cream and spices
3. Mix soda with flour, stir it and the rest of the flour into the egg mixture
4. Add lastly butter to it
5. Pour the mixture in a buttered mold and bake in a moderate oven 3/4 — 1 hour
