## Ingredients

- 1 pint of apple sauce
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 3 whites of eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice
- whipped cream for serving

## Directions

1. Mix apple sauce with sugar and lemon juice.
2. Add whites of eggs, beaten stiff.
3. Serve in small glasses, covered with whipped cream.
