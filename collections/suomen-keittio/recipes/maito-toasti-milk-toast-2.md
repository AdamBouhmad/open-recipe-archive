## Ingredients

- bread
- butter
- salt
- hot milk

## Directions

1. Toast bread, spread with butter, dust with salt.
2. Pile in a serving dish, pour a little hot milk over it.
3. Cover and let stand in a hot place or in oven for a few minutes.
4. Pour boiling milk on the soaked toast just before you send it to the table.
