## Ingredients

- 3 cups water
- 1.5 cups berries
- 1 pound sugar per 1.5 pounds berry juice

## Directions

1. Crush the berries and put on the fire in cold water
2. Stir while heating and let boil for a few minutes
3. Strain the juice and weigh it
4. Allow 1 pound sugar to 1.5 pounds juice
5. Put sugar and juice into a kettle and bring quickly to boil
6. Cook for 10-15 minutes
7. Let cool and pour into dry bottles
8. Cork carefully
