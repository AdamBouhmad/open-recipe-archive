## Ingredients

- eggs
- buttered toast
- salt
- pepper
- red pepper

## Directions

1. Separate whites from yolks and leave the yolks in shells
2. Beat the whites to a firm froth
3. Press through a pastry bag a layer of it about an inch thick on buttered toast, close to the edge
4. Place the yolks in center one on each toast
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
6. Garnish with red pepper
7. Bake until the whites are light brown
