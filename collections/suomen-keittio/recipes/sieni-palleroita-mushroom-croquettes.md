## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- butter
- milk sauce
- bread crumbs
- egg

## Directions

1. Fry and chop mushrooms
2. Mix fried chopped mushrooms with milk sauce to a moderately thick mixture
3. Form into croquettes
4. Roll in bread crumbs, dip in egg, roll again in bread crumbs
5. Fry in butter
