## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of rice
- 1 quart of milk
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- ½ onion
- salt
- pepper
- sugar
- ½ lb. of salmon

## Directions

1. Boil the rice in water until half-cooked.
2. Drain the water off and mix the rice with boiled milk.
3. Cook until thin batter.
4. Let cool for a while.
5. Mix with butter and whipped eggs.
6. Season.
7. Add the salmon flaked into bits.
8. When too salty the salmon has to be soaked.
9. Bake in a buttered mold.
10. Serve with melted butter.
