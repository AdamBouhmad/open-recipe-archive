## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of oatmeal porridge
- ½ cupful of flour
- 1 cupful of milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Beat eggs, add milk, porridge, flour mixed with baking powder and salt.
2. Fry in well buttered waffle iron until brown on both sides.
