## Ingredients

- beans
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- juice of half a lemon
- some onion juice
- 1 cup beef stock or bean cooking liquid

## Directions

1. Soak beans in cold water over night.
2. Put on the fire in cold water.
3. Cook for an hour.
4. Drain.
5. Pour beef stock or water over the beans, cook until tender.
6. Make a sauce of 3 tablespoons butter, 2 tablespoons flour, 2 tablespoons sugar, juice of half a lemon, some onion juice and 1 cup of beef stock or liquor of the beans.
7. Cover the beans with the sauce, continue to boil for 10 minutes.
