## Ingredients

- pike
- butter
- parsley
- fish stock
- flour
- horseradish
- cream

## Directions

1. Boil the fish as directed
2. Remove carefully onto a platter
3. Pour some melted butter over and sprinkle with parsley
4. Make a sauce of fish stock, butter, and flour, mixed with grated horseradish when done
5. Serve sauce in a separate dish
6. Or make a sauce of whipped cream and grated horseradish and serve cold
