## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 cups boiling water
- 2 cups rhubarb cut in pieces
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup tapioca
- 2 teaspoons butter
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Soak the tapioca in boiling water for 10-15 minutes
2. Place on the fire, add rhubarb, sugar and butter
3. Simmer for 1/2 hour, stirring often
4. Pour into a serving dish
5. Serve cold, garnished with whipped cream
