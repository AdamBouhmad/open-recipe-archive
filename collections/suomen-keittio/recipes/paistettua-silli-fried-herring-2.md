## Ingredients

- herrings
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, wash quickly, drain
2. Sprinkle the inside with salt
3. Roll in flour mixed with salt
4. Fry in butter or fat until brown on both sides
5. Cover and let cook until thoroughly done
6. For salted herring, soak over night first before preparing in the same way
