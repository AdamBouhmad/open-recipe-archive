## Ingredients

- fresh or canned fruit
- 1 ounce of gelatine to 2 cupfuls of fruit
- sugar
- lemon juice
- orange juice
- wine
- 2 1/2 cupfuls of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Pass the fruit through a sieve or cut them in pieces.
2. Dissolve gelatine in fruit juice.
3. Season the mixture of fruit with sugar, lemon juice, orange juice and wine.
4. Stir the mixture until cool.
5. Add gradually 2 1/2 cupfuls of whipped cream.
6. Put in a wet mold to harden.
