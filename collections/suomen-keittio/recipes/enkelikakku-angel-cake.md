## Ingredients

- 9 egg whites
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 7/8 cup flour
- 3/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla and lemon juice

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites until stiff.
2. Sift sugar several times, add gradually to egg whites.
3. Add flour gradually sifted six times with cream of tartar and flavorings.
4. Pour mixture into an unbuttered tube tin and bake in a moderate oven for 40 to 50 minutes.
