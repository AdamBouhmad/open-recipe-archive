## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls of cinnamon
- 10 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls of cardamom
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 2 lbs. of flour
- 1/2 cupful of melted butter

## Directions

1. Work eggs with sugar to a froth
2. Mix with spices and cream
3. Mix soda with a little flour and stir into the above mixture
4. Add flour enough to make a soft batter
5. Mix with melted butter and the rest of the flour
6. Form the batter into small nuts
7. Bake on greased baking sheet to a light brown, about 10-15 minutes
