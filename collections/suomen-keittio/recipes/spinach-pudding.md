## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of spinach leaves
- 3/4 cupful of rice
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 pint of water
- 1 quart of milk
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Clean and cook spinach in salted water with a little soda
2. Drain the water and chop the spinach
3. Cook rice, water and milk until a thick paste
4. Stir the chopped spinach into it and mix with butter, sugar, salt and eggs
5. Pour in a buttered form and bake in oven about 30-40 minutes
6. Serve with meat
