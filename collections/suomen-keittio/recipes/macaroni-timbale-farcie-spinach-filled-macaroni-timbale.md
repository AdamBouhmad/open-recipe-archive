## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful of onion
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 tablespoonful of mushrooms, chopped
- 1/2 cupful of beef stock
- salt
- nutmeg
- 1 cupful of chopped spinach
- 1 egg yolk
- boiled macaroni
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cook and chop the spinach.
2. Cook 1 tablespoonful of butter and 1/2 teaspoonful of onion together.
3. Add 1 tablespoonful of flour, 1 tablespoonful of mushrooms chopped, and 1/2 cupful of beef stock.
4. Season with salt and nutmeg.
5. Add 1 cupful of chopped spinach and let come to boiling point.
6. Add one yolk.
7. Butter small timbales or a big mold.
8. Cut the boiled macaroni into rings, 3/8 inch long, and line the mold with them.
9. Fill with mixture of spinach.
10. Bake in oven in a pan of water until firm.
11. Turn on a serving dish and serve with tomato sauce.
