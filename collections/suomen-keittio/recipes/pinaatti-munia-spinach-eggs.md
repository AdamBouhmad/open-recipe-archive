## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1/2 cupful of cream or spinach stock
- boiled chopped spinach
- hard-boiled eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and cream or spinach stock
2. Mix with boiled chopped spinach
3. Cut hard-boiled eggs in halves
4. Rub the yolks to a paste and add to the spinach mixture
5. Let boil up
6. Fill the halves of whites with the spinach mixture
7. Serve with vegetables and fish
