## Ingredients

- 1 bunch mint
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 1 tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse mint, chop the leaves fine.
2. Mix sugar with vinegar.
3. When the sugar is dissolved, add the mint to the vinegar.
4. Let stand about an hour.
5. If too strong the vinegar may be diluted with water.
6. Serve with roast mutton.
