## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- 1 quart of small onions
- 1 cupful of salt
- vinegar
- 1 1/2 pounds of brown sugar
- 1/4 pound of mustard seeds
- pepper
- cloves
- allspice
- 1 tablespoonful of each spice

## Directions

1. Cut tomatoes in pieces, peel 1 quart of small onions, sprinkle with 1 cupful of salt, let stand over night.
2. Drain in the morning and put in cold water for a little while.
3. Alternatively, tomatoes may be boiled quickly and strained.
4. Place in boiler and cover with vinegar.
5. Add 1 1/2 pounds of brown sugar, 1/4 pound of mustard seeds, pepper, cloves and allspice, 1 tablespoonful of each.
6. Cook 1/2 hour or until the pickles get soft.
7. Place in jars and preserve as usually.
