## Ingredients

- toasted bread
- anchovy butter
- eggs
- olives
- pickles

## Directions

1. Spread toast with anchovy butter.
2. Boil eggs.
3. Separate the yolks from the whites.
4. Chop.
5. Spread the chopped whites on the anchovy butter.
6. Sprinkle with chopped yolks, olives and pickles.
