## Ingredients

- cold boiled tripe
- melted butter
- lemon juice
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter or lard
- Bearnaise or hot Mayonnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Take pieces of cold boiled tripe
2. Pour over them melted butter mixed with a little lemon juice
3. Let stand for an hour
4. Dip in egg and bread crumbs
5. Fry in butter or lard to a light brown
6. Serve with Bearnaise or hot Mayonnaise sauce
