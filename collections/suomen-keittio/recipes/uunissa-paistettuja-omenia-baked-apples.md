## Ingredients

- Apples
- Sugar
- Water
- Butter

## Directions

1. Have apples of equal size, wipe and core.
2. Butter a pan, lay the apples in the pan, fill the hollowed cores with sugar.
3. Pour some water into the pan to prevent the apples from burning.
4. Bake until tender.
5. Serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce. May be baked without sugar.
