## Ingredients

- 3 yolks of egg
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 cups milk or cream
- salt and pepper
- lemon juice
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Beat 3 yolks of egg in a saucepan
2. Add 3 tablespoons of butter and 2 cups of milk or cream
3. Continue to beat until thick
4. Season with salt, pepper, lemon juice and chopped parsley
5. Serve with fish
