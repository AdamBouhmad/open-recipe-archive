## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of sugar
- 1/4 cupful of melted butter
- 3 cupfuls of cream
- 4 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of baking soda
- 1 teaspoonful of cardamom
- 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful of ginger
- 1 teaspoonful of orange rind

## Directions

1. Whip eggs with sugar
2. Mix with butter, flavoring, cream and flour mixed with baking soda
3. Bake in a buttered mold 1 hour
