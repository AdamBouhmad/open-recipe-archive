## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 cake of yeast
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 whole egg
- 1 yolk of egg
- 1/2 teaspoonful of grated lemon rind
- a dash of nutmeg
- 1/2 cupful of currants, washed and dried

## Directions

1. Warm milk, mix with yeast, salt and cupful of flour, set to rise
2. Beat eggs with sugar and mix with risen sponge
3. Add creamed butter, spices, currants and the rest of the flour
4. Set to rise again
5. Drop with spoon in boiling lard and cook until quite done and delicate brown
6. Drain on paper
7. Sprinkle with powdered sugar
