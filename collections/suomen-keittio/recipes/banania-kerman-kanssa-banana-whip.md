## Ingredients

- 3 bananas
- 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
- salt
- lemon juice
- 1 cup cream

## Directions

1. Rub the bananas through a sieve.
2. Add sugar, lemon juice and salt.
3. Heat.
4. Let cool, add cream and flavor with vanilla.
