## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1/3 cupful of butter
- 1 egg
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of vanilla
- raisins to garnish

## Directions

1. Beat butter with sugar to a froth
2. Add beaten egg, vanilla and flour
3. Drop with spoon on a baking sheet
4. Fit edges with knife
5. Place a raisin or an almond on each one
6. Bake to a light brown
