## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of boiled lobster
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 slice of onion, chopped
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 cupful of milk
- salt
- paprika
- sherry
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 cupful of cream

## Directions

1. Cook chopped onion in butter.
2. Add flour.
3. Add lobster.
4. Season with salt and paprika and bring slowly to boiling point.
5. Before serving add a little sherry and yolks of 2 eggs mixed with 1/2 cupful of cream to it.
6. Shake on the fire for a little while.
