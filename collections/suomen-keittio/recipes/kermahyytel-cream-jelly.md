## Ingredients

- 2 cups whipping cream
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla or lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon gelatine dissolved in 1/4 cup water

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 tablespoon gelatine in 1/4 cup water.
2. Add vanilla and one part of the sugar.
3. Heat until gelatine is dissolved.
4. Whip the cream with the remaining sugar.
5. Add cream mixture gradually to the gelatine mixture when it has cooled somewhat.
6. Pour into a mold to set.
7. Unmold and garnish with preserved fruit and macaroons.
