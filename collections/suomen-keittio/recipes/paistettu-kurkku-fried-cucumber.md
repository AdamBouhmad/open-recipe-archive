## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- egg
- lard or butter

## Directions

1. Peel and cut lengthwise the cucumbers.
2. Wipe with a cloth.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Turn in bread crumbs, dip in egg and in bread crumbs again.
5. Fry in lard.
6. Or roll in flour, fry in a frying-pan with butter or lard.
