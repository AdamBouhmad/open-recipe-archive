## Ingredients

- 2 wild ducks
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1½ quarts bouillon or water
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the wild ducks, let stand in water mixed with vinegar, or parboil.
2. Wipe and rub with salt, brown in butter in a dripping-pan.
3. Add boiling bouillon to it when necessary.
4. Roast for an hour basting often.
5. Serve very hot.
6. Strain the drippings, skim off the fat and flavor with lemon.
