## Ingredients

- 4-5 pound chine of pork
- lemon
- salt
- pepper
- apples
- prunes
- cord
- butter
- bread crumbs
- hot water
- flour
- Kitchen Bouquet

## Directions

1. Wipe the 4 or 5 pound chine of pork with a cloth wrung out of hot water
2. Rub with lemon, salt and pepper
3. Fill with pared apples cut in pieces and with stewed stoned prunes
4. Roll together and tie with cord
5. Place in a hot buttered pan and sprinkle with bread crumbs
6. Roast in a hot oven
7. Add hot water to the drippings when necessary and baste often
8. When done, place on a platter skin-side up
9. Remove the meat from the backbone and slice crosswise
10. Replace the slices and lay the apples and prunes around
11. Skim off the fat and prepare a gravy
12. Thicken the gravy with flour and color with Kitchen Bouquet
