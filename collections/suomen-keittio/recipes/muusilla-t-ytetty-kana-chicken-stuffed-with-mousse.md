## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- chicken breast for mousse
- truffles
- red pepper
- Hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. Bone the chicken carefully so as not to break the skin.
2. Make a mousse of chicken breast.
3. Stuff, adding truffles and red pepper cut into long strips between the layers of stuffing.
4. Sew up.
5. Wrap into a cloth and steam until tender.
6. Remove the cloth and put in the oven to evaporate but not to brown.
7. Place on a platter.
8. Cut lengthwise in the middle, then slice crosswise.
9. Serve with Hollandaise sauce.
