## Ingredients

- 5 or 6 apples
- Sugar
- Salt
- 1 cup tapioca
- 1 quart water

## Directions

1. Peel and core 5 or 6 apples and fill with sugar
2. Lay into a buttered mold
3. Cook 1 cup of tapioca in 1 quart of water, season with salt and sugar
4. Cover the apples with tapioca and bake until the apples are done
5. Alternatively, cook the apples with sugar in water; when tender, lay the apples into a mold, cook the tapioca in the liquor of the apples, pour over the apples, and brown quickly in oven
6. Or cook the tapioca in a saucepan with 2 cups of apples and 3 or 4 cups of water; add sugar and salt according to taste
