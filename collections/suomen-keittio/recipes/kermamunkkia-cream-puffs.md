## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 level tablespoon butter
- 1/2 cup flour
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 white beaten stiff
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 square chocolate
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 egg yolks
- Fondant icing

## Directions

1. Boil the milk with the butter, add the flour, cook stirring well until the bottom begins to brown.
2. When cool, add the yolks and at the last the whites beaten stiff.
3. Shape through a pastry bag or drop by the spoonful on the baking sheet.
4. Brush with egg; bake.
5. When done fill with the chocolate mixture: Dissolve the chocolate, add half of the sugar and the milk. Whip the rest of the sugar with the yolks, stir on the fire until it thickens. Flavor and let cool.
6. When filled, coat the puffs with fondant icing.
