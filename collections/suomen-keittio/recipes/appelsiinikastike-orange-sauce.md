## Ingredients

- juice and yellow rind of 2 oranges
- 2 teaspoons cornstarch
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon currant jelly or juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Mix the cornstarch with the sugar
2. Add the orange juice, the yellow rind and the lemon juice
3. Stir over the fire until thick
