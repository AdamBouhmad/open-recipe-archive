## Ingredients

- Ham
- Salt
- Mustard
- Bread crumbs
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the ham in cold water over night.
2. Rinse carefully and place on the fire in cold water.
3. When it has come to boil, lower the heat and let simmer, allowing 20 minutes to a pound.
4. When done, lift it out of the liquor and remove the skin.
5. Spread the ham with mustard.
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and sugar.
7. Bake in the oven until brown.
