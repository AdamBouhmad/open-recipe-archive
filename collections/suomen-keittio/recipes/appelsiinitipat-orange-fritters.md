## Ingredients

- Oranges
- Fritter batter
- Lard for frying
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges
2. Separate the sections
3. Remove the seeds carefully
4. Dip the pieces of the fruit into the batter
5. Cook in lard until light brown
6. Sprinkle with sugar
7. Serve with fruit sauce or plain
