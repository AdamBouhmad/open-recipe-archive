## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of flour

## Directions

1. Whip 2 yolks of eggs and 1 white with sugar to a froth
2. Mix with creamed butter and flour, kneading the batter well
3. Roll thin and shape into round cakes
4. Place on a buttered baking sheet
5. Brush over with the rest of the whites
6. Sprinkle with chopped almonds and granulated sugar
7. Bake in oven to a light brown
