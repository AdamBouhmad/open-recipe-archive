## Ingredients

- Fish fillets
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Butter
- Fish stock
- Tomato slices
- Hard boiled eggs
- Mayonnaise
- Lettuce leaves
- Crab tails
- Lemon slices
- Fish aspic

## Directions

1. Raise the fillets of the fish, remove the skin and bones and cut in pieces of equal size.
2. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and lemon juice, let stand for 10 minutes.
3. Place the pieces of fish in a pan with a dot of butter on each, add some fish stock.
4. Bake covered with buttered paper 15 minutes.
5. Remove from oven, let cool.
6. Arrange the fish on slices of tomato on a platter having a slice of egg on each piece.
7. Cover with rich mayonnaise.
8. Garnish around with leaves of lettuce, crab-tails, slices of lemon and aspic made of liquor of the fish.
