## Ingredients

- 3 pounds of shoulder of mutton
- boiling water
- carrots
- onions
- celery
- salt
- pepper
- potatoes
- flour
- dumplings

## Directions

1. Wipe and cut in pieces 3 pounds of shoulder of mutton
2. Put into a kettle and pour over it boiling water
3. Let quickly come to boil, then skim off the foam
4. Add carrots cut into nice pieces, onions, celery cut into pieces, salt and pepper
5. Let simmer slowly covered until the meat is tender
6. About half an hour before serving add potatoes
7. Thicken with flour mixed in cold water and stir in
8. Serve with dumplings
9. The meat may also be browned in a little butter before adding the water
