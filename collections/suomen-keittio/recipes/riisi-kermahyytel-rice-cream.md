## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- 6 cups milk
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- Yellow rind of 1/2 lemon
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 3/4 package of gelatine
- 3 cups cream
- Vanilla (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash 1 cup of rice
2. Cook until tender in 6 cups of milk
3. Add 3 tablespoons of sugar
4. Let cool
5. Mix with the yellow rind and juice of 1/2 lemon and 3/4 package of dissolved gelatine
6. Mix with 3 cups of whipped cream
7. May be flavored with vanilla
