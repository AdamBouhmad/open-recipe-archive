## Ingredients

- 3 cupfuls of chopped sweetbread
- 3 tablespoonfuls of dissolved gelatine
- 5 tablespoonfuls of whipped cream
- 1/2 cupful of sherry
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Garnish small molds or a big one
2. Mix all the above ingredients, adding cream last
3. Fill the molds with the mixture
4. Let thicken
5. Turn upon a serving dish
6. Serve with lettuce salad
