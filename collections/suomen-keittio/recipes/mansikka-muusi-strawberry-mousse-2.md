## Ingredients

- 3 cupfuls of cream
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of strawberries, juice and pulp
- 1 3/4 cupful of sugar
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 7 leaves or 5 tablespoonfuls of gelatine
- whole strawberries for garnish

## Directions

1. Mix strawberries with sugar, let stand 1/2 hour
2. Add lemon juice and gelatine, dissolved
3. When the mixture begins to thicken add whipped cream into it
4. Pour in a mold to get firm
5. Serve unmolded, garnished around with whole strawberries
