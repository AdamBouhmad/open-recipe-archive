## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Bouillon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cream
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms
2. Cook with a lump of butter for 5 minutes
3. Add some bouillon, salt and pepper
4. Shortly before serving, add some cream and 2 yolks of egg
