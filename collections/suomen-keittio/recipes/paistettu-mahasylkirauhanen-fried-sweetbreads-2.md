## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- egg
- bread crumbs
- lard or butter
- Bechamel sauce or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare sweetbreads as directed above
2. Divide lengthwise through the center
3. Dip in egg and bread crumbs
4. Fry in lard or butter
5. Serve with Bechamel or tomato sauce
