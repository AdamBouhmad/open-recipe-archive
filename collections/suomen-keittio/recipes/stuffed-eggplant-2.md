## Ingredients

- 1 eggplant
- salted water
- 1 cupful of bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1/2 tablespoonful of chopped onion
- beef stock or water
- salt and pepper
- 1 to 2 eggs
- bread crumbs for topping

## Directions

1. Cook eggplant 10 minutes in salted water
2. Cool thoroughly
3. Cut a piece from the top or split the eggplant in two
4. Scoop the insides out and chop it
5. Add 1 cupful of bread crumbs, 2 tablespoonfuls of butter, and 1/2 tablespoonful of chopped onion
6. Cook the mixture for a few minutes
7. Pour a little beef stock or water over, add salt and pepper
8. Stir in 1 to 2 whipped eggs
9. Fill the eggplant with this mixture
10. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
11. Bake in oven till brown
