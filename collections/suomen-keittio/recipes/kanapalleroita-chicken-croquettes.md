## Ingredients

- 1½ cups boiled fowl
- ½ cup rich milk sauce
- ⅜ teaspoon salt
- lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon chopped parsley
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- lard
- chopped mushrooms

## Directions

1. Grind 1½ cups of boiled fowl, add ½ cup of rich milk sauce, ⅜ teaspoon of salt, a few drops of lemon juice, ½ teaspoon of chopped parsley and some pepper.
2. Let cool.
3. Form into croquettes, dip in egg and bread crumbs.
4. Cook in lard.
5. If chopped mushrooms are added to the croquettes, they will be delicious.
