## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 small onion, minced
- parsley, minced
- pickles
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Put butter in frying pan and cook the onion in it
2. Add bread soaked in a little water or fish broth
3. Add parsley, salt and pepper
4. Fry on gentle heat for a while
5. If desired, soft oyster meat can also be used in the stuffing, mixed in last
