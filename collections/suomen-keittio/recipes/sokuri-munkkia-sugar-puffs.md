## Ingredients

- 4 cupfuls of water
- 1/2 pound of butter
- 1/2 pound of wheat flour

## Directions

1. Boil up 4 cupfuls of water
2. Add 1/2 pound of butter into it
3. Reheat to boiling
4. Add 1/2 pound of wheat flour
5. Beat the mixture well until it leaves the sides of the pot
