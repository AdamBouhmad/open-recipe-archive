## Ingredients

- kidneys
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- lemon juice
- bread crumbs
- toast

## Directions

1. Cut kidneys crosswise into slices.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Dip into melted butter mixed with a dash of lemon juice.
4. Roll in bread crumbs.
5. Broil over a clear fire turning often so as not to burn them after they are done.
6. Arrange on toast, pour melted butter over.
7. Serve very hot.
