## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Pare potatoes and cut into balls of equal size, or use a potato ball cutter, and rinse well.
2. Place on the fire in boiling water with a dash of salt and let simmer gently until tender.
3. Drain off the water and let stand near the fire uncovered for a while so the water vapor evaporates.
4. For old potatoes, place on the fire in cold salted water to boil.
