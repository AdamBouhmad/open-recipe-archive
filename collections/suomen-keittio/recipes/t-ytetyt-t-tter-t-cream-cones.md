## Ingredients

- batter for roll tart
- whipped cream
- sugar
- jam

## Directions

1. Prepare a batter same as for roll tart.
2. Spread on a buttered baking sheet shaping into round cakes.
3. Roll immediately into cones.
4. Shortly before serving, fill the cones with whipped cream mixed with sugar and jam.
