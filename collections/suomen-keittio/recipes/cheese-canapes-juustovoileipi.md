## Ingredients

- bread slices
- grated cheese
- pepper

## Directions

1. Brown slices of bread on one side.
2. Cover the browned side with grated cheese.
3. Sprinkle with pepper.
4. Put in a baking pan in a quick oven.
5. When the cheese begins to melt, place the toast on a napkin and serve hot.
