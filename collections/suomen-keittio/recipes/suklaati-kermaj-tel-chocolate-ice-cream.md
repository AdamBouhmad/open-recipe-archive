## Ingredients

- Vanilla ice cream
- 2 to 4 ounces dissolved chocolate
- Milk or cream
- Sugar
- Vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix vanilla ice cream with 2 or 4 ounces of dissolved chocolate dissolved in milk or cream, and freeze.
2. Alternatively, when made without eggs mix the cream with the dissolved chocolate, add sugar and vanilla, and freeze.
