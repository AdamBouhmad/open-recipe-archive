## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of tapioca
- 5 cupfuls of milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Cook a batter of milk and tapioca
2. Work butter to a cream, beat yolks well with sugar
3. Mix with creamed butter
4. Combine the mixtures when the tapioca batter is cold
5. Add lastly the hard-beaten whites of eggs into it
6. Put in a buttered mold and steam in oven about 3/4 hour
