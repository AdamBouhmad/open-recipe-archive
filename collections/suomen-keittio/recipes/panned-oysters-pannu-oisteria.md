## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of oysters
- 1/2 cupful of butter
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- yolks of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe oysters
2. Brown the butter in pan
3. Place oysters on it and cook for a few minutes
4. Add flour and beaten yolks mixed with milk
5. Shake on the fire until firm
6. Serve on toast
