## Ingredients

- 1 eggplant
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1/2 onion, finely chopped
- 1 green pepper, chopped
- 3/4 tablespoonful of finely chopped parsley
- butter for baking dish
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Pare an eggplant and cut in 3/4 inch cubes
2. Cook in a small quantity of boiling water until soft
3. Drain
4. Cook butter with onion, green pepper, parsley and eggplant
5. Turn into a buttered baking dish
6. Cover with buttered crumbs
7. Bake until the crumbs are brown
