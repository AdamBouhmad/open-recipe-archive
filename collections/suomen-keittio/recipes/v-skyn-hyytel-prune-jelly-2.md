## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of prunes
- cold water
- ½ box of dissolved gelatine
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1 lemon juice
- sherry wine

## Directions

1. Soak 1 cupful of prunes in cold water.
2. Cook until soft, strain and chop fine.
3. Add to the prune liquor ½ box of dissolved gelatine, 1 cupful of sugar, 1 lemon juice and a little sherry wine.
