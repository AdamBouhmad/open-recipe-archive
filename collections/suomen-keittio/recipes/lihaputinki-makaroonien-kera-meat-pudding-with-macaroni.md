## Ingredients

- 1 pound macaroni
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 0.5 onion
- 2 cups bouillon or sauce
- 1 pound boiled meat, ground
- 3 or 4 tablespoons grated cheese
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni in pieces and boil until tender in salted water; drain.
2. Fry the onion in butter and add the meat to it; fry for awhile.
3. Pour the bouillon or sauce into the pan and add the cheese.
4. Fill a mold with layers of meat and macaroni or with macaroni and meat mixed together.
5. Bake until browned.
6. Instead of macaroni, mashed potatoes may be used.
