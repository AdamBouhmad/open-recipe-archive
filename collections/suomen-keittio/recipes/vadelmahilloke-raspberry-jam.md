## Ingredients

- 5 pounds raspberries
- 3 or 4 pounds sugar
- 2 cups water

## Directions

1. Cook sugar in water until clear.
2. Add raspberries to the syrup.
3. Continue to simmer, shaking the kettle.
4. Skim carefully.
5. Let cook until the scum ceases to rise, taking care not to crush the berries.
6. Pour into glass jars while hot.
7. Cover airtight immediately.
