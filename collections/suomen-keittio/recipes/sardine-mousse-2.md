## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of sardines
- 1/2 cupful of bread crumbs
- 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls of anchovy paste
- yolk of 1 egg
- whites of 2 eggs
- milk

## Directions

1. Soak the bread crumbs in milk, add the minced sardines, anchovy paste and the yolk.
2. At the last mix with the whites.
3. Garnish small fish-molds with hard-boiled white of egg and parsley, fill with the sardine mixture and bake in oven in a pan of water until firm.
4. Serve on toast with anchovy sauce.
