## Ingredients

- 1 quart of clams
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 cupful of cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 2 egg yolks
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the clams finely and put over gentle heat in their own liquid and a little water or fish stock added.
2. Simmer gently for about an hour.
3. Thicken with butter and flour.
4. Beat the egg yolks and gradually add the scalded milk, to which a little soda has been added.
5. Remove the soup from heat and gradually pour in the egg and milk mixture.
