## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- parsley
- mayonnaise or Béarnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Cut tomatoes with peelings on crosswise in two or in pieces 1/2 inch thick.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Broil until brown, first having the skin side down.
4. Arrange on a hot platter with a dot of butter on each.
5. Sprinkle with parsley.
6. May be served with hot mayonnaise or Béarnaise sauce.
