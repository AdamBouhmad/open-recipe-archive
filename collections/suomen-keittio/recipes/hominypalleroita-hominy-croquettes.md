## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiled hominy
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- salt and pepper
- 1 egg, well beaten
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Rub the hominy, butter, salt, pepper, and egg until all lumps are smooth.
2. Form into croquettes.
3. Roll in egg and bread crumbs.
4. Fry in butter or deep lard.
