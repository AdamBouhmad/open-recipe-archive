## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of butter
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- yolks of 7 eggs
- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- juice and grated rind of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Work butter and sugar to a froth
2. Add beaten yolks of eggs, lemon juice and rind, milk and baking powder mixed with flour
3. Bake in a buttered mold in a moderate oven
4. Cover with white frosting
