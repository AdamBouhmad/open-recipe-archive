## Ingredients

- Corned beef
- Cold water
- Seasonings and vegetables according to taste
- Cabbage

## Directions

1. Put the meat on fire in plenty of cold water.
2. Bring slowly to boiling, simmer on steady fire allowing 30 minutes to a pound.
3. Cabbage and other kinds of vegetables may be boiled with the meat or separately and served with the meat.
