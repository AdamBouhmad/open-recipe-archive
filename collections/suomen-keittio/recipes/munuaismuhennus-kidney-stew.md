## Ingredients

- kidneys
- flour
- 1 tablespoon butter
- onion, chopped
- 1 cup bouillon or water
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice or Worcestershire sauce
- mushrooms, chopped

## Directions

1. Prepare kidneys same as for broiling.
2. Cut in slices approximately 3/8 inch thick.
3. Roll in flour and brown in butter with a chopped onion.
4. Add 1 cup of bouillon or water and simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Just before serving, flavor with lemon juice or Worcestershire sauce and add chopped mushrooms.
7. Serve hot.
