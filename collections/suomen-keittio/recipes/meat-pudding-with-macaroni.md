## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of macaroni
- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1/2 onion
- 2 cupfuls of meat stock or sauce
- 1 lb. of cooked chopped meat
- 3-4 tablespoonfuls of grated cheese
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni until tender in salted water; drain the water off.
2. Fry onion in butter, mix with meat and continue frying for a little while.
3. Stir meat stock or sauce and cheese into it.
4. Fill a mold with mixture of macaroni and meat, or put layers of macaroni and meat alternately in it.
5. Bake until brown.
6. In place of macaroni mashed potatoes may be used.
