## Ingredients

- macaroni
- salted water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil macaroni in salted water; drain, chop.
2. Make a sauce of 2 tablespoons butter, 1 tablespoon flour and 1/2 cup milk.
3. Add 2 cups of macaroni, 1 tablespoon grated cheese, 2 yolks of egg and 1 whole egg, salt and pepper.
4. Stuff the tomatoes, sprinkle with bread crumbs.
5. Bake for 3/4 hour.
6. Serve with tomato, milk or brown sauce.
