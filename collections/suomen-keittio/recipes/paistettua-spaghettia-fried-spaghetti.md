## Ingredients

- spaghetti
- salted water
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Break spaghetti into pieces
2. Boil in salted water for 15 to 20 minutes
3. Drain water carefully
4. Brown in butter
5. Sprinkle with fried bread crumbs
