## Ingredients

- 1 ounce chocolate
- 6 tablespoons boiling water
- vanilla
- sifted confectioner's powdered sugar, about 1 1/2 cups

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 ounce chocolate in a dry pan
2. Add about 6 tablespoons boiling water
3. Add a little vanilla and stir well
4. Add sifted confectioner's powdered sugar gradually, enough to make the mixture reasonably thick, about 1 1/2 cups
5. Spread immediately on the cakes
6. If cocoa is used it must be dissolved in hot water
