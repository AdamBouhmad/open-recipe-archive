## Ingredients

- boiled cauliflower
- butter
- milk sauce
- cheese crumbs
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut boiled cauliflower into small pieces
2. Put in a buttered form
3. Pour milk sauce over
4. Sprinkle with cheese crumbs and bread crumbs
5. Dot with bits of butter
6. Bake in oven until brown
