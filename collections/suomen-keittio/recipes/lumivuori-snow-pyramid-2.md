## Ingredients

- 6 whites of eggs
- 1/2 pound of powdered sugar
- vanilla ice cream or cream jelly for filling
- whipped cream for garnish

## Directions

1. Beat 6 whites to a firm froth, about 20 minutes.
2. Sift 1/2 pound of powdered sugar several times and mix carefully with the froth of eggs.
3. Force through a pastry bag upon a floured baking sheet, forming into larger and smaller rings.
4. Dry in a slow oven.
5. Pile on a serving dish so as to give the appearance of a pyramid.
6. Fill with vanilla ice cream or cream jelly.
7. Garnish the sides and the top with whipped cream.
