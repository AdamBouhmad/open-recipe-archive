## Ingredients

- apples
- sugar
- sherry
- apple liquor
- pink color
- corn starch
- 1/2 cupful of thin cream
- 4 leaves of gelatine
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of sherry
- 5 tablesp. of whipped cream
- sponge cake

## Directions

1. Pare and core apples, cut into slices crosswise
2. Cook with sugar and a little sherry
3. When soft, set to cool
4. Baste with a sauce made of apple liquor, colored to a fine pink and thickened with corn starch
5. Scald cream, sugar and gelatine until the gelatine is dissolved
6. When cool, add sherry and whipped cream
7. Wet small molds with cold water and fill them with the mixture
8. Let harden
9. Cut foundations of sponge cake, place the apples on them
10. Unmold the mixture of cream on the apples
11. Arrange them in circle on a serving dish
12. Pour the whipped cream in centre of it
