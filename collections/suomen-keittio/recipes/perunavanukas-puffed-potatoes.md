## Ingredients

- 3 cups mashed potato
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 or 4 eggs
- 8 tablespoons cream or milk
- 2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Add to the potato butter, salt, cream, flour and yolks of eggs.
2. Last of all add the whites of eggs beaten firm.
3. Bake in oven until light brown.
4. May be baked in muffin molds; when half done spread with white of egg beaten firm and brown the top in oven.
