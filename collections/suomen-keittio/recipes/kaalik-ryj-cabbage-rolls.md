## Ingredients

- Ground meat
- Bread crumbs
- Milk
- Eggs
- Onion
- Pepper
- Salt
- Butter
- 1/2 cup rice
- 1 head of cabbage
- 3 or 4 tablespoons butter
- 4 teaspoons sugar
- 4 teaspoons salt
- Bouillon
- Flour
- Cream

## Directions

1. Make a mixture of ground meat, bread crumbs, milk, egg, onion, pepper, salt and butter
2. Boil the cabbage whole and remove the outside leaves and tough stalk
3. Boil the rice in plenty of water and strain; mix with the meat
4. Add the small leaves of cabbage chopped and fried in butter
5. Place this mixture on leaves, one spoonful on each, and roll together
6. Brown on both sides
7. Add a sprinkling of sugar, salt and pepper
8. Pour some bouillon into the pan and place the pan into oven
9. Bake from 1 to 1 1/2 hours, turning often
10. Arrange on a platter
11. Make a gravy of the stock in the pan by adding flour and cream
12. Strain over the rolls
