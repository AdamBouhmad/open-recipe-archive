## Ingredients

- 1 glassful of eggs
- 1 glassful of sugar
- 1 glassful of milk
- 1 glassful of melted butter
- 1 cake of yeast
- enough flour to make a moderately thick dough
- granulated sugar
- chopped almonds
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as for usual tea-ring
2. Set to rise and form it into small rings
3. When risen, brush over with egg
4. Sprinkle with granulated sugar and chopped almonds
5. Bake in oven about 10 minutes
