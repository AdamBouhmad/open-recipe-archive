## Ingredients

- 5 pounds apples
- 2.5 pounds sugar
- 3 cups water

## Directions

1. Wash and cut the apples in pieces.
2. Cook with water to a sauce.
3. Force the apple sauce through a strainer and return to the fire.
4. Add the sugar and simmer stirring continuously until of firm consistency.
5. Spread a platter with oil or butter and pour marmalade over, spreading with a knife.
6. When cold, cut in squares, dip in granulated sugar and dry.
