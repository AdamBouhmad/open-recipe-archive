## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups warm milk
- 2 egg yolks and 1 whole egg
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- Salt, nutmeg or vanilla to taste

## Directions

1. Whip the eggs and add to the milk.
2. Mix with the spices.
3. Cover a pie plate with pastry and brush with egg white.
4. Fill with the mixture.
5. Bake until it becomes firm, first having the oven hot and reducing the heat when nearly done.
