## Ingredients

- 2 cups mashed potatoes
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- egg for dipping
- bread crumbs
- lard or butter for frying

## Directions

1. Mix the mashed potatoes with salt, pepper, butter and egg yolks.
2. Form into balls or oval-shaped croquettes.
3. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
4. Fry in butter or cook in deep lard.
