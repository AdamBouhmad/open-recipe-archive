## Ingredients

- lunghi pezzi di fini maccheroni o spaghetti (long pieces of fine macaroni or spaghetti)
- brodo salato (salted water)
- carne di pollo cotta e finemente tritata oppure carne di vitello (cooked chicken or veal, finely minced)
- salsa di latte (milk sauce)
- 3 o 4 uova ben montate (3 or 4 eggs, well beaten)

## Directions

1. Cook long pieces of fine macaroni or spaghetti in salted water.
2. Place in cold water so they remain whole.
3. Set on a clean cloth to cool.
4. Butter a mold with a hole in the center well.
5. Place macaroni lengthwise around the mold.
6. Fill the mold with cooked and minced chicken or veal mixed with milk sauce.
7. Just before putting in the oven, add 3 or 4 well beaten eggs.
8. Bake in oven in a pan of water, covered with buttered paper, about 30 minutes.
