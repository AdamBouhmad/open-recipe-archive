## Ingredients

- 1 goose
- 1 lemon
- 2 tablespoonfuls of salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of pepper
- 1/2 lb. of prunes
- 2 quarts of apples
- meat stock

## Directions

1. Clean the goose, wash and wipe with a cloth.
2. Rub inside with a little salt.
3. Wash the fruit and stuff the goose with them.
4. Truss and spread with butter or line with a slice of pork.
5. Place in a pan and roast in a hot oven basting often with its gravy.
6. Add meat stock if necessary.
7. When browned sprinkle with salt, and cover with buttered paper.
8. Allow 2-3 hours for roasting a goose.
9. Arrange on a platter, cut in pieces and garnish with prunes and apples.
10. Strain the gravy, skim off the fat, add a little cream.
