## Ingredients

- frog legs
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- bread crumbs
- egg
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Skin, clean and wipe the frog legs
2. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and lemon juice
3. Let stand for a while
4. Wipe the frog legs
5. Roll in cracker crumbs, dip in egg, and roll in cracker crumbs again
6. Fry in butter or lard
7. Place the pan in oven covered for a few minutes
