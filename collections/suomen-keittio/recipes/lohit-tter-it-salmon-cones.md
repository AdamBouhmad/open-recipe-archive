## Ingredients

- thinly sliced smoked salmon
- 1 cupful of fish jelly
- 1/2 pound of caviare
- whipped cream
- gelatine

## Directions

1. Roll thinly sliced smoked salmon into cones.
2. Beat 1 cupful of fish jelly to a froth, mix with 1/2 pound of caviare.
3. Fill the cones with the mixture of jelly and caviare.
4. Set on ice to get firm.
5. Dish, garnish the top with whipped cream mixed with gelatine and caviare.
