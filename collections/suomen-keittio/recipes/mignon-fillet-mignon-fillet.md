## Ingredients

- Tenderloin (selkäliha/fillet)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Bearnaise sauce
- Fried bananas

## Directions

1. Cut fillets into pieces 1 inch thick.
2. Pound the pieces and form into round cakes of equal size.
3. Add a sprinkling of salt and pepper.
4. Fry in butter in a pan.
5. Pour Bearnaise sauce on a serving platter and arrange the cakes on top.
6. Garnish around with fried bananas.
7. Alternatively, the fillets may be broiled for 4 or 5 minutes.
8. Serve with parsley, mushroom or sherry sauce.
