## Ingredients

- 3 quarts of liquor of ham
- 1 cupful of pearl-barley or barley
- 1 carrot
- 1 small parsnip
- 1/2 root of celery
- 1 onion
- a piece of turnip
- 3 potatoes

## Directions

1. Wash and soak the barley and boil in the liquor of ham until half done.
2. Add the vegetables cut into dice.
3. Then add the potatoes a little later.
4. The soup is done when the vegetables and barley are tender.
5. Add the seasonings and chopped parsley into it and serve with ham or meat cut into cubes in it.
6. If the soup is too salty dilute it with milk.
