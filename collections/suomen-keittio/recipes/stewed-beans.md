## Ingredients

- beans
- cold water
- beef stock
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- onion juice
- juice of half a lemon
- ½ cupful of beef stock

## Directions

1. Soak the beans in cold water over night
2. Put cold water on the fire, strain off the water after an hour
3. Pour beef stock over the beans, cook until tender
4. Prepare a sauce of 3 tablespoonfuls of butter, 2 tablespoonfuls of flour, 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar, a little onion juice and juice of half a lemon, ½ cupful of beef stock
5. Pour the sauce over the beans
6. Let still rest on the fire 10 minutes
