## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of almonds
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of vanilla
- water

## Directions

1. Choose nice whole almonds, rub them between a cloth.
2. Moisten sugar with water and mix with 1/2 teaspoonful of vanilla.
3. Put almonds into it and let boil, stirring carefully until they begin to crack.
4. Remove the pan from the fire and stir the almonds until they are dry.
5. Place the pan again on slow fire, add a few drops of water stirring until almonds are covered with syrup.
6. Remove the pan from fire and let stand covered for 20 minutes.
