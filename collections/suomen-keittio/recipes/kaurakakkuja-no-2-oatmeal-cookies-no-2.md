## Ingredients

- 2 cups rolled oats
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat separately whites and yolks.
2. Rub butter with sugar until creamy, add salt, vanilla, rolled oats and baking powder; beat well.
3. Fold in the yolks and last of all the whites.
4. Drop by 1/2 teaspoon on a buttered baking sheet leaving room for the cookies to rise.
5. Bake in a moderate oven.
