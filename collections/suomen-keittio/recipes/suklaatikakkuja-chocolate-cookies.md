## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1 egg
- 2 squares chocolate
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- Cinnamon, cloves, chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Work until frothy butter with sugar
2. Add eggs beaten well, melted chocolate, milk and flour sifted with baking powder
3. Work all the ingredients well together
4. Roll thin
5. Cut in cookies with a small cutter
6. Brush with egg white
7. Sprinkle with sugar
8. Bake in a moderate oven
9. May be prepared without spices
