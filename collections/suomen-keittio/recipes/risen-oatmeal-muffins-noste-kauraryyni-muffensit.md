## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1/2 cake of yeast
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 cupful of cold cooked oatmeal
- 1 tablespoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Scald milk, add salt, sugar, butter and yeast dissolved in a small quantity of milk, oatmeal and flour.
2. Cover and let rise over night.
3. In the morning grease small muffin rings and fill two-thirds with the batter.
4. Let rise until full.
5. Bake nearly 30 minutes in a moderate oven.
