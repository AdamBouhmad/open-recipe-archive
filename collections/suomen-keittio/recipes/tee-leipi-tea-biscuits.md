## Ingredients

- 4 cupfuls of flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 eggs
- 1½ cupfuls of milk
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Sift flour and baking powder together
2. Work butter in flour
3. Beat eggs until light
4. Add sugar, salt and milk into the eggs
5. Mix gradually with flour
6. Add milk if the batter is too hard
7. Roll into a sheet ½ inch thick
8. Shape into round cakes
9. Bake in a hot oven for 20 minutes
