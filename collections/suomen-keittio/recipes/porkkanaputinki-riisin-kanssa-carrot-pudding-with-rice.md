## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- 3 cups water
- 1 pint milk
- 1 pound carrots
- 1 tablespoon almonds
- 1 dessert-spoon salt
- 1½ tablespoon sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Make a porridge of rice, water and milk
2. Peel, rinse and boil carrots until almost tender
3. Grate the carrots and almonds
4. Add carrots, sugar and butter to the porridge
5. Mix in the egg beaten
6. Pour into a buttered mold
7. Bake in oven about 1 hour
