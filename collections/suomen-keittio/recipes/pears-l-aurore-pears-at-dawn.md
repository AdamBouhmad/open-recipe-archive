## Ingredients

- pears
- sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of sherry
- 1 tablespoonful of brandy
- 2 tablesp. of chopped almonds
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of dissolved gelatine or 3 leaves
- 1 cupful of whipped cream
- 1/4 lb. of chocolate
- 1/2 cupful of hot water
- 1 cupful of sugar
- butter
- sponge cake

## Directions

1. Pare and core pears leaving the stems on
2. Cook with sugar until tender, let cool
3. Mix sherry, brandy, chopped almonds, sugar, and gelatine together, except cream, and keep on the fire until the gelatine is dissolved
4. Let cool and add lastly the whipped cream
5. Fill the pears with this mixture
6. Melt chocolate, cook sugar and water together, add gradually into the chocolate
7. Baste the pears with chocolate caramel
8. Place prepared pears on a foundation of sponge cake
9. Line the serving dish with them
10. Pour the whipped cream in centre of cake
