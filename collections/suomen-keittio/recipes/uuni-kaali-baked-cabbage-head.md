## Ingredients

- 1 (2 1/2 lb.) cabbage head
- 4-5 tablespoonfuls of brown sugar
- 4-5 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- a little white pepper and nutmeg
- water

## Directions

1. Remove the outermost leaves from the cabbage head
2. Hollow it from the center
3. Place sugar, nutmeg and pepper in the hollow
4. Brown the butter in a pan
5. Place the cabbage head in it
6. Cover and let it gradually cook through
7. Pour water mixed with salt over the cabbage
8. Let cook about two hours, basting often to prevent burning on top
