## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1/2 cup chopped suet
- 1/4 cup syrup or sugar
- 1/4 cup milk
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1/2 cup currants
- 1/2 cup raisins

## Directions

1. Mix the dry ingredients
2. Add the spices and the milk
3. Stir well
4. Steam in a buttered mold for 2 1/2 hours
