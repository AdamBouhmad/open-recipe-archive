## Ingredients

- Squab
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Butter

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the squabs, sprinkle with salt and pepper, flavor with lemon juice.
2. Let stand for awhile.
3. Broil on a slow fire, place in oven for a few minutes dotted with butter.
4. May be broiled leaving the bones in, same as directed for chicken.
