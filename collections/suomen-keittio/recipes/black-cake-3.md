## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cupfuls of molasses
- 1/2 cupful of butter
- 1/2 cupful of cream
- 3 eggs
- 3 cupfuls of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of ginger
- 1/2 teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Beat molasses, add butter, beaten eggs, cream, ginger, soda and flour.
2. Bake in a hot oven.
