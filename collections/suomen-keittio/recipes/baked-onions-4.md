## Ingredients

- onions
- salted water
- salt
- sugar
- butter
- meat stock
- flour

## Directions

1. Parboil onions in salted water, drain off the water and lay in a pan.
2. Sprinkle with salt and sugar.
3. Melt 1 tablespoonfuls of butter in 1 cupful of meat stock and pour over the onions.
4. Bake in oven until brown.
5. Thicken the gravy with flour and pour over the onions.
