## Ingredients

- 1 cup thin cream
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 yolks of egg
- 2 cups whipping cream
- 2 ounces macaroons

## Directions

1. Scald the thin cream.
2. Beat the yolks with the sugar.
3. Add gradually the scalded cream.
4. Stir over the fire in a double boiler until thick.
5. Remove from the fire, let cool.
6. Add the whipped cream.
7. Dip a mold into cold water.
8. Fill with alternate layers of macaroons and cream mixture.
9. Cover tightly.
10. Place the mold into a pail of cracked ice and salt.
11. Let stand for 6 or 7 hours.
12. Unmold and garnish with macaroons.
