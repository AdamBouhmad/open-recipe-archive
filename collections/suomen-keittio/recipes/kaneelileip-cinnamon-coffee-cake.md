## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 4 cups flour
- 2 cups lukewarm milk
- 1 cup melted butter
- 1 yeast cake
- Cinnamon
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as given for coffee bread.
2. When risen until light, toss the dough into a shallow baking tin.
3. Let rise some more.
4. Brush the top with egg, sprinkle with cinnamon, sugar and almonds.
5. Bake for 20 or 30 minutes.
6. The dough may be mixed with almonds and spices, according to taste.
