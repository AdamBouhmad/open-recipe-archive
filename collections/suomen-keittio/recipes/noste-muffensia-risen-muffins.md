## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ yeast cake
- 2 eggs
- 3 or 4 cups flour

## Directions

1. Warm the milk, add sugar, salt and yeast dissolved in a small quantity of milk.
2. Make a moderately thick dough by adding flour to the milk.
3. Let rise over night.
4. In the morning mix the dough with the beaten eggs.
5. Half fill the buttered muffin tins, let rise until full and bake for 30 minutes.
