## Ingredients

- cabbage
- 2 teaspoons mustard
- salt
- pepper
- sugar
- 5 tablespoons salad oil
- 1 cup vinegar
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage in very fine shreds.
2. Mix 2 teaspoons of mustard, salt, pepper, sugar and 5 tablespoons of salad oil.
3. Add this mixture to a cup of vinegar.
4. Pour over the cabbage and place under a weight for a few hours.
5. Put the cabbage into a salad bowl, sprinkle with minced parsley.
6. Serve with steak.
