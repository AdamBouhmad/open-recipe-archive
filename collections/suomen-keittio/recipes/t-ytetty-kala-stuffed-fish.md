## Ingredients

- 1 fish weighing about 3 pounds
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 2 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- 1 onion
- fish stock or water
- 1 stalk of celery
- butter

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish. Split in the back, remove the bones and scrape the meat off carefully with a knife to prevent the skin from breaking. Force through a meat chopper.
2. Soak 1/2 cup of bread crumbs in water, add 2 eggs, salt, pepper, 1 onion grated or ground with the fish and ground fish. Rub the mixture well.
3. Stuff the fish and sew together.
4. Put some fish stock or water in the pan, lay 1 onion, 1 stalk of celery finely cut and few dots of butter around the fish in the pan. Add pepper and salt.
5. Cover and bake until tender. May be cooked on the stove also.
6. Lift carefully onto a platter, garnish and serve.
