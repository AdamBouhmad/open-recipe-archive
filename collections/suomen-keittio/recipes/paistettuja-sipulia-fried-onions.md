## Ingredients

- onions
- salt
- water
- butter
- sugar
- bouillon or water
- egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and cook in salted water until half done
2. Drain and brown in butter
3. Sprinkle with salt and sugar
4. Pour 1 cup of bouillon or water over the onions, cover
5. Let simmer until tender
6. For raw fried onions: slice thinly and fry on slow fire on both sides until brown
7. Alternatively, sliced onions may be turned in egg and bread crumbs and browned in butter
