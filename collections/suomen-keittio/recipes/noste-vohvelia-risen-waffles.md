## Ingredients

- 1½ cupfuls of milk
- ½ teaspoonful of salt
- 1½ cupfuls of flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 eggs
- ¼ cake of yeast

## Directions

1. Scald milk, add yeast dissolved, butter, salt and flour, stirring well.
2. Set to rise over night.
3. In the morning mix with beaten eggs and fry like waffles usually.
