## Ingredients

- pork tenderloin
- lemon juice
- salt
- ginger
- pepper
- water
- bread crumbs
- apple slices

## Directions

1. Crack the ribs
2. Rub the pork with lemon juice, salt, ginger and pepper
3. Place it in the roaster having the skin side downward
4. Lay slices of apple on the ribs
5. Pour a little water into the roaster and roast in a hot oven
6. Turn over, sprinkle with bread crumbs and continue roasting until brown
7. When the pork is tender, place it on a platter, remove from the bone and cut into thin slices
8. Strain over it the sauce
