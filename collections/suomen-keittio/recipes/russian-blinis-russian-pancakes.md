## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cupful of cream
- 2½ cupfuls of water
- 2 teaspoonfuls of salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- ½ lb. of buckwheat flour
- 10 ozs. of wheat flour
- 2—3 cupfuls of scalded milk
- ½ cake of yeast

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm water.
2. Whip eggs, mix with cream, water, salt and flour and lastly butter, stirring carefully.
3. Put to rise over night.
4. In the morning mix it with lukewarm milk until a moderately thick batter.
5. Fry like griddle cakes.
6. Serve with melted butter and maple syrup.
