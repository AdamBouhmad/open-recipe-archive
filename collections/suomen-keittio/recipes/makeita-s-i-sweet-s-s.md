## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound butter
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup almonds
- 3/4 teaspoon soda
- 1 pound flour

## Directions

1. Prepare a batter from the ingredients in order given
2. Mix in blanched and chopped almonds
3. Mix soda with flour and combine with batter
4. Force through a pastry bag onto a buttered baking sheet, forming cookies in the shape of an S
5. Bake
