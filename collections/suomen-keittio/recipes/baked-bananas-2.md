## Ingredients

- bananas
- sugar
- lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel bananas, lay in a fryingpan, sprinkle with a little sugar, squeeze lemon juice on it and dot with butter.
2. Bake in oven about 20 minutes.
