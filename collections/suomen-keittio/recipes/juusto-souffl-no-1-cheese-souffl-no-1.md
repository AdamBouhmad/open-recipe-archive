## Ingredients

- 1 heaping tablespoon butter
- 3 heaping tablespoons American cheese
- 2 tablespoons whipped cream
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 egg whites
- some paprika
- some mustard

## Directions

1. Stir the yolks of egg with the butter on the fire until thick.
2. Add cheese and spices.
3. Last of all mix with the whites beaten stiff.
