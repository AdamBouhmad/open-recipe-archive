## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of mashed potatoes
- 1½ cupfuls of mashed chestnuts
- 2 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs
- 3 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- salt and pepper to taste
- cream if needed

## Directions

1. Mix together all ingredients
2. If too thick, add cream
3. Form into round cakes, fry in butter; or dip in egg and bread crumbs, cook in lard
