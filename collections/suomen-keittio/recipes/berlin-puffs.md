## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 3 ozs. of butter
- 1 1/2 eggs
- yolk of 1 egg
- 1 oz. of sugar
- juice and rind of 1/2 lemon
- 1 yeast cake
- 1 lb. of flour
- applesauce or currant jelly

## Directions

1. Warm milk, put butter into it.
2. Mix with the eggs beaten with sugar, lemon rind and juice, then risen sponge of yeast.
3. Add flour, beat well and let rise.
4. Knead and roll into a sheet.
