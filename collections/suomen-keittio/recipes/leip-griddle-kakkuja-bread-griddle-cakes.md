## Ingredients

- 1½ cups bread crumbs
- 1½ cups hot milk
- ½ cup flour
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Dissolve the butter in the hot milk, pour over the bread crumbs, let stand until the bread crumbs are thoroughly soaked.
2. Add the beaten eggs, and the flour sifted with baking powder.
3. Fry.
