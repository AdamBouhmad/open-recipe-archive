## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of brown sauce
- 3 tablespoonfuls of currant jelly
- a little sherry wine

## Directions

1. Prepare 1 cupful of brown sauce
2. Add currant jelly and a little sherry wine
3. Serve with game and boiled mutton
