## Ingredients

- 7 pounds of upper round of beef
- Pork for larding
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1/4 teaspoonful of pepper
- 1 onion
- 2 carrots
- 3/4 cupful of brandy
- 1 cupful of white wine
- 2 cupfuls of veal stock or water
- Lard

## Directions

1. Use about 7 pounds of the upper round of beef.
2. Trim the meat with pork, dipped in salt and pepper.
3. Fasten with cord and brown in lard.
4. When browned add 1 tablespoonful of salt, 1/4 teaspoonful of pepper, 1 onion, 2 carrots, 3/4 cupful of brandy, 1 cupful of white wine, and 2 cupfuls of veal stock or water.
5. Cover tightly and let simmer for a few hours, until the meat is tender, adding stock if necessary.
6. Lift the meat on a platter and cut the cords.
7. Prepare the sauce of drippings by skimming the fat off first.
