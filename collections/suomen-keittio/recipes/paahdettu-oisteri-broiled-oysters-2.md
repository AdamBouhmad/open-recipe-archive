## Ingredients

- oysters
- salt and pepper
- butter
- toast
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe oysters of uniform size.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Sprinkle with butter sauce and place on broiler.
4. Broil on both sides over bright fire for a few minutes.
5. Serve on toast spread with butter, about 3-4 for each serving.
6. Brush with a little butter sauce and lemon juice.
7. Serve with lemon butter.
