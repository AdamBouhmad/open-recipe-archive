## Ingredients

- 2 cups oysters
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt
- pepper
- cream

## Directions

1. Put the butter in a pan with a cover
2. When melted add the oysters, salt and pepper
3. Shake over the fire until hot
4. Drain the oysters
5. Add some cream to the drippings
6. Pour the gravy over the oysters and serve
