## Ingredients

- 2.5 pounds boiled or fried meat
- 1 onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups bouillon or gravy
- 2 tablespoons tomato
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the meat in cubes.
2. Chop the onion and brown in butter with the meat and flour.
3. Add the bouillon, tomato and seasonings.
4. Boil quickly.
5. When fried meat is used it will become tough if boiled too long.
