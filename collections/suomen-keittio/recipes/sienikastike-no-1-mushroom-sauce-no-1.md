## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 can mushrooms with the liquor
- 2 cups bouillon or fish stock
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Make a gravy of butter, flour, bouillon and the liquor of mushrooms
2. Add small mushrooms whole or cut in pieces
3. Cook the gravy for 10 minutes
4. Season with salt, pepper and lemon juice
5. Some cream and yolk of an egg may be added to the sauce shortly before serving
