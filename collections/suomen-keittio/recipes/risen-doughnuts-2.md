## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of scalded milk
- 3/4 cupfuls of sugar
- 1/4 cupful of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/3 cake of yeast
- 2 eggs
- a dash of nutmeg
- flour

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast, mix with milk, salt and flour to a thick batter and let rise over night
2. In the morning add melted butter and eggs whipped with sugar
3. Work more flour in if desired
4. Set to rise again
5. Roll out to 1/2 inch thickness, shape into rings or round cakes
6. Let rise, cook in boiling fat to a light brown
7. Dip in powdered sugar while hot
