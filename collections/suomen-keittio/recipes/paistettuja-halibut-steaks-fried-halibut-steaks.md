## Ingredients

- halibut steaks
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- melted butter
- flour
- eggs
- butter or lard for frying

## Directions

1. Rub the pieces of fish with salt, pepper and lemon juice on both sides
2. Let stand about half an hour
3. Wipe and spread with melted butter, roll in flour, egg and again in flour
4. Fry in butter or lard
