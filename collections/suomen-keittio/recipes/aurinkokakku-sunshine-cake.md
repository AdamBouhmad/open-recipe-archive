## Ingredients

- 9 egg whites
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice or vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites to a firm froth, add cream of tartar, continue to beat for 10 minutes.
2. Beat the yolks with the sugar until frothy, add lemon juice and the egg whites.
3. Last of all gradually mix in the flour sifted several times.
4. Bake in an unbuttered tub tin for 30 or 40 minutes.
5. After removing from the oven let cool in the tin upside down.
