## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Make a sauce of butter, flour and milk.
2. Stir until smooth and cook for a few minutes.
3. Remove from the fire.
4. Add seasonings, cheese and yolks of egg.
5. Drop by the spoonful into boiling bouillon or boil in salted water about 5 minutes, or until done.
6. If too soft, mix with beaten white of an egg.
