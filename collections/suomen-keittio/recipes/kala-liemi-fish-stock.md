## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. small fishes
- 3 quarts of water
- 1 carrot
- 1 root of celery
- 2 sprigs of parsley
- 1 leek
- 6 peppercorns
- salt
- bit of bay leaf
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the fishes but do not scale them.
2. Cut into pieces and put in cold salted water.
3. Place on the fire.
4. When coming to a boil add the vegetables into it.
5. Season and add a little bit of butter.
6. Let cook covered for an hour.
7. Strain through a fine cloth.
8. Fish stock may be used for different kinds of fish soups prepared with vegetables (spinach, cauliflower, asparagus, potatoes, rice etc.) in the same way as meat stock.
9. If brown fish stock is wanted, brown the vegetables in a small quantity of butter before putting them into the soup.
10. Fish stock may be made richer by adding eggs and cream.
