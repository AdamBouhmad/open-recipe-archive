## Ingredients

- 1 cabbage head
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1 cupful of chopped mushrooms
- 2 slices of onion
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut and boil cabbage
2. Drain the water
3. Mix with butter, salt and sugar
4. Bake covered until tender
5. Cook chopped onion and mushrooms with 1 tablespoonful of butter in a pan
6. Butter a mould
7. Lay alternately the cabbage and mushrooms in it
8. Leave small dots of butter on the surface
9. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
10. Bake the pudding in a slow oven for ½ hour
