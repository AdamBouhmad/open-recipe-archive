## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of roast bones
- 3 quarts of water
- 1 tablespoon of pearl-barley or rice
- 1 carrot
- 1 onion
- a piece of turnip
- 2 cupfuls of potatoes
- 10 peppercorns
- salt
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the bones and put them into cold water.
2. Place on the fire.
3. Cut the vegetables into small cubes and cook with the barley until tender in 1 quart of water.
4. Remains of the vegetables have to be cooked with the bones for a few hours.
5. Strain the soup.
