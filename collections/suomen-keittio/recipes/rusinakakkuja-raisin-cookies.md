## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 6 tablespoons butter
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- Salt
- 1 1/2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar until creamy
2. Add beaten egg, vanilla, and milk
3. Mix in flour sifted with baking powder and raisins washed and wiped
4. Drop by the spoonful on a buttered baking sheet
5. Bake in a hot oven
6. May be made without milk and baking powder, placing a few raisins on top of each one as decoration
