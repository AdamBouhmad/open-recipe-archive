## Ingredients

- 1 quart of pears
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1 pint of milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- grated yellow rind of 1 lemon
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 2 cupfuls of water

## Directions

1. Pare and core pears and cook them with 1 cupful of sugar and 2 cupfuls of water until tender
2. Prepare a batter of milk and flour
3. Stir butter, yolks whipped with sugar, and lemon rind into it
4. Mix with stiffly beaten whites of eggs
5. Butter a mold and lay the cooked pears on the bottom
6. Pour the batter in
7. Bake in oven 3/4 hour
