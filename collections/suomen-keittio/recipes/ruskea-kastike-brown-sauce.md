## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 slices carrot
- 1 slice onion
- 8 peppercorns
- 2 cups brown beef bouillon
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 sprig parsley
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Brown the carrot and the onion in butter
2. Add flour, bouillon and seasonings
3. Let boil
4. Strain and season with salt and pepper
