## Ingredients

- Rice porridge
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 beaten eggs
- Or 1 cup bread crumbs
- Boiled or baked beets
- Mushrooms if desired

## Directions

1. Make a porridge of rice, add 2 tablespoons butter and 2 eggs, beaten
2. Instead of rice 1 cupful of bread crumbs may be used
3. Cut beets in slices or chop fine, and add boiled or baked mushrooms if desired
4. Fill a buttered mold with alternate layers of rice and beets or bread crumbs and beets, having rice on the bottom and on the top
5. When using bread crumbs, beat the eggs, mix with 2 cups milk and 2 tablespoons butter
6. Pour on top of the mold
7. Bake in oven in a pan of water
