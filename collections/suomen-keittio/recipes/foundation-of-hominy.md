## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of hominy
- 3 cupfuls of water
- 1 teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Cook 1 cupful of hominy, 3 cupfuls of water, 1 teaspoonful of salt, about 5-10 minutes.
2. Pour in greased shallow baking pan.
3. Turn on a suitable platter and use as foundation for various dishes.
