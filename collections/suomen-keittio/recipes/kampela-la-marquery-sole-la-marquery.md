## Ingredients

- Two soles
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Fish stock or water
- Butter
- Normandy sauce
- Bread, toasted or fried tomato slices
- Parsley or truffle, chopped
- Crab tails
- Oysters

## Directions

1. Raise the fillets of two soles, skin with a sharp knife.
2. Cut nicely in pieces; if too thin, fold in two.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, flavor with lemon juice.
4. Let stand for 10 minutes.
5. Lay the fillets in a pan, pour 1/2 cup of fish stock or water over, add some more lemon juice.
6. Bake in oven for 10-12 minutes covered with buttered paper.
7. Baste with Normandy sauce, lay on toast or fried slices of tomato, arrange on a warm platter.
8. Sprinkle with chopped parsley or truffle, garnish around with crab-tails, oysters, etc.
