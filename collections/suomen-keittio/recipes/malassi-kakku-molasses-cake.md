## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup boiling water or milk
- 1/4 cup butter
- 3/4 cup lard
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 heaping teaspoon soda
- Cinnamon, ginger and mace, 1/2 teaspoon each

## Directions

1. Beat well together molasses, sugar and water
2. Add butter and lard melted
3. Add eggs beaten to a froth
4. Add spices
5. Add soda dissolved in hot water and flour
6. Bake in a hot oven
