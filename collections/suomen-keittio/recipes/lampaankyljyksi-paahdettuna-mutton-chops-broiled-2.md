## Ingredients

- Mutton chops
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Potatoes or peas
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the fat and scrape the meat from the bone.
2. Broil carefully on both sides.
3. Chops one inch thick take 8-10 minutes.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and place small pieces of butter on top.
5. Arrange on hot platter either around potato puree or peas.
6. Garnish with parsley.
