## Ingredients

- codfish
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- Hollandaise sauce
- parsley
- truffels

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and rub with salt and pepper
2. Put in a buttered pan with cover
3. Bake for half an hour
4. Lift onto a platter and remove the skin carefully
5. Cover with Hollandaise sauce
6. Garnish with chopped parsley and truffels
