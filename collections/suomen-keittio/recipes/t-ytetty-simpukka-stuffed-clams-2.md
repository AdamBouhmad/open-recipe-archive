## Ingredients

- 1 pint of clams
- 2 cupfuls of crackers
- clam liquor
- milk
- salt
- paprika
- lemon juice
- eggs
- cheese
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop 1 pint of clams
2. Soak 2 cupfuls of crackers in clam liquor and milk mixed in equal parts
3. Season with salt, paprika and lemon juice
4. Add chopped clams and three egg yolks
5. Fill clam shells with this mixture
6. Sprinkle with cheese and bread crumbs and small pieces of butter
7. Bake in a hot oven until the bread crumbs brown
