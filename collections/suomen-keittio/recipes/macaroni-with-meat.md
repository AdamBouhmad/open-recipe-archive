## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 pint of chicken or beef stock
- 1 cupful of boiled chopped meat
- 1/2 pkg. of boiled spaghetti
- 1 beaten egg
- salt and pepper
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of 1 tablespoonful of butter, 1 tablespoonful of flour and 1 pint of chicken or beef stock.
2. Stir into the sauce 1 cupful of boiled chopped meat and 1/2 pkg. of boiled spaghetti cut in pieces.
3. Add 1 beaten egg and season with salt and pepper.
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered mould, sprinkle with bread crumbs and bake in oven 1/2 hour.
