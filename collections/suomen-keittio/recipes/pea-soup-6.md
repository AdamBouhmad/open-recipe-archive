## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of green peas
- 3 quarts of water
- several kinds of vegetables
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the peas in 1 1/2 quarts of water.
2. Boil separately the finely cut vegetables and add to the soup with their liquor.
3. Thicken with butter and flour.
4. Small artichokes may be served in soup and finely chopped parsley will give it a good flavor.
5. If desired, the peas may be strained out before adding the other vegetables.
