## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sauce
- 1/2 cup Champagne
- A few peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- Some sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare brown sauce, add Champagne, peppercorns, bay leaf and sugar.
2. Cook for 10 minutes and strain.
