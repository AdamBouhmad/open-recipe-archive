## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of lentils
- 1/2 cupful of brown beans
- 1/2 onion
- a branch of celery
- a sprig of parsley
- 1 carrot
- 1 cupful of bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- pepper and salt
- 1/2 cupful of milk sauce

## Directions

1. Wash 1 cupful of lentils and 1/2 cupful of brown beans.
2. Put on the fire in cold water with 1/2 onion, a branch of celery, a sprig of parsley and 1 carrot until soft and all the water has evaporated.
3. Mash through a sieve, add 1 cupful of bread crumbs, 1 egg, pepper and salt and 1/2 cupful of milk sauce.
4. Work to a smooth paste.
5. Form into balls or croquettes.
6. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
7. Cook in deep fat.
