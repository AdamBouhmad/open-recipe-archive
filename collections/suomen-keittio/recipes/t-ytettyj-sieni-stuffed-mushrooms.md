## Ingredients

- large mushrooms of equal size
- cooked chicken
- mushrooms
- cream
- white chaufroid sauce
- bread crumbs
- egg
- bacon

## Directions

1. Peel large mushrooms of equal size, remove the stems.
2. Make a mousse of chopped chicken and chopped mushrooms diluted with cream.
3. Lay the mousse between the mushrooms, two and two.
4. Baste with white chaufroid sauce; let cool.
5. Turn in bread crumbs, dip in egg and turn in bread crumbs again.
6. Fry in butter or bake in oven having a thin slice of bacon on top of each.
7. Bake covered for 15 minutes.
