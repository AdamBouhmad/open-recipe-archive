## Ingredients

- 1 cup grated pineapple
- 2 cups water
- 3/4 cup sugar
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 2 tablespoons or 1/2 package gelatine
- white of 1 egg (optional, for clarifying)

## Directions

1. Dissolve the gelatine in 1/4 cup cold water
2. Put into a boiling double boiler, add the boiling water and sugar, stir until the sugar is dissolved
3. When half jellied add the pineapple and the lemon juice
4. Pour into a wet mold to set
5. May be prepared of the juice of pineapple having the mold filled with alternate layers of pineapple and the jelly
6. If clarified with the white of an egg, the jelly will look nicer
