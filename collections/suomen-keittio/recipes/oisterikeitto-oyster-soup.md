## Ingredients

- 2 dozen oysters
- 3 cups liquor of oysters
- 1 stalk celery
- 1 sprig of parsley
- Slice of onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3 cups milk
- 1 cup cream
- Salt and pepper
- Soda

## Directions

1. Scald the oysters in their own liquor until they ruffle.
2. Strain, add onion, celery and parsley and cook for 10 minutes.
3. Thicken with butter and flour, remove the vegetables from the soup, add some soda so as not to curdle.
4. Pour the scalded milk and cream slowly into the soup.
5. Add the oysters, season, scald and serve.
6. May be made by using equal parts of clams and oysters.
