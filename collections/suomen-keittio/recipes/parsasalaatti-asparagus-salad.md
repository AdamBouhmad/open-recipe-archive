## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salted water
- lettuce leaves
- mayonnaise
- hard cooked eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the asparagus tips in salted water until tender
2. Arrange on a bed of lettuce leaves
3. Cover with mayonnaise
4. Hard cooked eggs may be used in asparagus salad
