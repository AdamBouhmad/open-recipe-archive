## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon vinegar
- 1 teaspoon tomato catsup
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoon grated horseradish
- Salt
- A few drops of tobasco
- Clams

## Directions

1. Make a dressing of lemon juice, vinegar, tomato catsup, Worcestershire sauce, grated horseradish, salt and a few drops of tobasco.
2. Wash the clams.
3. Mix with the dressing.
4. Keep on ice until served.
