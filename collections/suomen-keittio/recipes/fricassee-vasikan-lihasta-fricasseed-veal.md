## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of veal fillet
- 2 stalks of celery
- 1 small carrot
- salt
- 1 slice of onion
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- strained veal stock
- pepper
- lemon juice
- yolks of two eggs
- a little cream
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Wipe and cut veal.
2. Put in a pan, pour boiling water over.
3. Add celery, carrot, salt and onion slice.
4. Cover, cook slowly until the meat is tender.
5. Make sauce of butter, flour, strained veal stock, salt, pepper and lemon juice.
6. Beat yolks of two eggs, add a little cream, mix with the sauce.
7. Pour the sauce over the veal.
8. Garnish with chopped parsley.
