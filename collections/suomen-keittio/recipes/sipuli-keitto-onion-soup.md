## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. of onions
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 quarts of meat stock
- 3 potatoes
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cupful of cream or milk
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and cook them in water about 10 minutes. Drain well.
2. Cut in pieces and fry in butter on a slow fire until tender; add the flour stirring thoroughly.
3. Mix with the boiling meat stock and cooked and mashed potatoes. Let boil 10 minutes.
4. Pass through a sieve; season with salt and pepper.
5. Whip the yolks and cream, pour it slowly into the soup whipping well; sprinkle with finely chopped parsley.
6. Instead of potatoes tapioca may be used. Before using the tapioca has to be cooked in a small quantity of water until tender.
