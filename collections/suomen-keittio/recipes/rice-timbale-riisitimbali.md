## Ingredients

- boiled rice
- butter
- strained tomato (optional)

## Directions

1. Fill individual buttered molds with boiled rice
2. Pack tightly
3. Dot with butter
4. Let stand for a while in a pan of boiling water
5. Use for garnishing meat dishes
6. If mixed with some strained tomato, the rice will have a nice color
