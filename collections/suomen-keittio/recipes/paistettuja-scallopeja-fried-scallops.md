## Ingredients

- Scallops
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Melted butter
- Flour
- Butter or lard

## Directions

1. Bring to the boiling point, strain, place in a dish with pepper, salt, lemon juice and some melted butter.
2. Let stand 10 minutes.
3. Roll in bread crumbs, fry in butter or lard.
4. If dipped in flour, egg and bread crumbs, the scallops will look nicer.
5. Cook in lard.
