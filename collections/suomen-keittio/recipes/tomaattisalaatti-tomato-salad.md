## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- boiling water
- lettuce
- mayonnaise dressing
- parsley
- French dressing

## Directions

1. Peel the tomatoes. If kept in boiling water for a minute the skin is easy to remove.
2. Cut in halves or in quarters, arrange on lettuce leaves, coat with mayonnaise.
3. Or place the tomatoes whole on a bed of lettuce, coat with mayonnaise add a sprinkling of parsley.
4. Tomatoes cut in thin slices may be served with French dressing.
