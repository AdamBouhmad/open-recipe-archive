## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. of soupmeat
- 2 lbs. of veal
- 1 chicken
- 7 quarts of cold water
- 1 parsnip
- 1 root or 4 stalks of celery
- 1 beet
- 1 carrot
- 1 turnip
- 1 leek
- 2 sprigs of parsley
- 12 peppercorns
- 5 cloves
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into cubes.
2. Put the butter into kettle and brown the meat in it.
3. Add the chicken and the bones into the kettle, pour cold water in and let it stand covered on side of range about half an hour.
4. Place it on a slow fire to heat it.
5. When boiled 5 minutes add salt, seasonings and vegetables cut and browned with a tablespoonful of butter.
6. Let simmer for 5-6 hours.
7. Strain the bouillon.
8. It will keep well by leaving the cover of fat on.
