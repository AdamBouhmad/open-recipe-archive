## Ingredients

- 3/4 cupful of sugar
- 1/4 cupful of lemon juice
- 1 teaspoonful of butter
- 3/4 cupful of water
- 1 tablespoonful of corn starch
- 3/4 cupful of white wine

## Directions

1. Mix sugar with corn starch.
2. Pour slowly boiling water into it, stirring well.
3. Cook until corn starch is clear.
4. Remove from the fire and add lemon juice, butter and white wine.
