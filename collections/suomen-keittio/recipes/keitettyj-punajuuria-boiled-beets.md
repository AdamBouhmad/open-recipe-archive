## Ingredients

- Beets
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Vinegar (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the beets.
2. Place on the fire in boiling salted water and cook until tender.
3. Young beets will be done in an hour, old ones require about 3 hours.
4. Put into cold water so as to remove the peels easier.
5. Cut the young beets in quarters, the old ones in slices.
6. Serve sprinkled with salt and pepper and butter, or with vinegar mixed in melted butter.
