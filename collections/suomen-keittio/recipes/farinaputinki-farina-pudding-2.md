## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup farina
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- raisins
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook a porridge of milk and farina
2. Add the yolks beaten with the sugar, salt and raisins
3. Place in a buttered mold
4. Beat the whites stiff with some sugar, place on top of the mold or mix into the porridge
5. Bake in the oven until brown
