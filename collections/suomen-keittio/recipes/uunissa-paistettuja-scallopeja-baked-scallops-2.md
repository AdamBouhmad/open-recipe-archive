## Ingredients

- 3 cupfuls of fish
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1/2 cupful of liquid of scallops
- 3 tablespoonfuls of cream
- salt
- pepper
- crackerdust

## Directions

1. Bring the fish to the boiling point.
2. Strain and chop fine.
3. Cream the butter.
4. Add the chopped fish, liquid, pepper and salt to it.
5. Put in a buttered dish.
6. Sprinkle with crackerdust and bake in oven.
7. Alternatively, put the scallops whole into a buttered dish.
8. Prepare Bechamel sauce using liquid of scallops for it.
9. Pour it over the fish.
10. Sprinkle with crackerdust and dot with butter.
