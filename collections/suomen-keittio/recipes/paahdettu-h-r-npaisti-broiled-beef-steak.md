## Ingredients

- porterhouse, sirloin or round steak
- fat or grease
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- onions
- mushrooms (optional)
- brown gravy
- tomato, mushroom or Béarnaise sauce or parsley butter (optional)

## Directions

1. Wipe the steak with a cloth wrung out of boiling water.
2. Spread with grease and place on a larded broiler with the fat edge next to the handle.
3. Broil over a clear fire until brown on both sides.
4. Place on a hot platter, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and dot with butter.
5. Put in oven for a few minutes.
6. Serve with brown gravy having slices of fried onions on top. If mushrooms are used, place them on the steak and pour the gravy around.
7. May be served also with tomato, mushroom or Béarnaise sauce or parsley butter.
