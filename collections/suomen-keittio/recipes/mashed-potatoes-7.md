## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- milk
- cream

## Directions

1. Mash pared boiled potatoes.
2. Add butter, salt, pepper, milk and cream.
3. Rub to a smooth paste.
4. Heap in a serving dish, trim nicely with knife.
5. Or work through a potato press into a serving dish.
