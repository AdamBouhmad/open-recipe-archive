## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cupfuls of molasses
- 3/4 cupful of butter
- 4 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 teaspoonful of ginger
- 1 teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Work molasses and butter to a froth
2. Add spices and flour
3. Rub the mixture to a smooth paste
4. Roll thin
5. Shape small cookies
6. Bake in a moderate oven
