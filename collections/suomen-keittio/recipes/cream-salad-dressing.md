## Ingredients

- yolks of 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 1/2 teaspoonful of mustard
- 2 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- 1 cupful of cream
- a little salt, pepper and sugar

## Directions

1. Mix powdered yolks well with mustard and seasonings, add vinegar.
2. Rub to a smooth paste.
3. Add the cream gradually.
