## Ingredients

- Pig's head
- Pork from various parts
- 4 eggs
- Pepper
- Salt
- Lemon juice
- Slices of carrots
- Truffles
- Cucumbers
- Cloth for wrapping

## Directions

1. Split the head and remove the tongue.
2. Saw the bone leaving the nose whole.
3. Grind all kinds of pork and pound well.
4. Mix with 4 eggs, pepper, salt and lemon juice.
5. Put one half of this mixture into the head.
6. Lay slices of carrots, truffles, cucumbers, etc. on the meat.
7. Fill the head with the remaining mixture.
8. Sew up the head and the eyes.
9. Put a slice of carrot in the ears and fasten them down.
10. Tie the nose with string.
11. Wrap the head in a cloth and cook.
12. Place under a heavy weight.
13. Serve cold with Cumberland sauce.
