## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of flour
- 1½ cupfuls of milk
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- ½ teaspoonful of salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Sift flour with baking powder and salt.
2. Beat yolks of eggs, add milk and pour flour slowly into it.
3. Mix with melted butter.
4. Lastly mix with hard-beaten whites of eggs.
5. Fry on hot greased waffle iron to a nice brown on both sides.
