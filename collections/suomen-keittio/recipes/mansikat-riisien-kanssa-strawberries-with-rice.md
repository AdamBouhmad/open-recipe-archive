## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound of rice
- 1 lemon
- 1 orange
- sugar
- water
- small strawberries
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Wash 3/4 pound of rice and cook until soft in deep water, mixed with juice of 1 lemon
2. Turn in a dish and pour liquor, made of sugar and water, on them
3. Mix with lemon- and orange rinds
4. When cold, put layers of rice and small strawberries alternately in a mold
5. Pour syrup on them and set to harden
6. Serve garnished with whipped cream
