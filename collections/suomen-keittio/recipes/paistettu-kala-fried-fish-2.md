## Ingredients

- fish
- 1 cup flour
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, rinse, dry and slice
2. Mix 1 1/2 tablespoons salt into 1 cup flour
3. Coat the pieces in this flour mixture
4. Brown butter in a pan
5. Fry the fish in the pan until light brown
6. Cover the pan
7. Place the pan in the oven to braise until done
8. Pour a little cream into the pan
9. Let it come to a boil
10. Pour over the fish
