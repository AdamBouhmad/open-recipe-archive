## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. of fish meat
- 2 ounces of butter
- 1 tablespoon of cream
- 1 egg
- flour

## Directions

1. Remove bones from fish and grind the meat in a meat grinder
2. Mix well with butter
3. Press through a sieve
4. Add the other ingredients and blend to a smooth paste
5. Add just enough flour to hold the mixture together
6. Form into small balls
7. Cook in salted water or fish stock
8. Serve in fish or crab soup
