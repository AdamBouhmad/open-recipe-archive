## Ingredients

- 2 kupillista hienoksi jauhettua kinkkua (2 cupfuls of ground ham)
- 3 munaa (3 eggs)
- 1 kupillista maidossa lioitettua leipää (1 cupful of bread, soaked in milk)
- 2 ruokalusikallista sherry viiniä (2 tablespoonfuls of sherry)
- ½ ruokalusikallista hienonnettua persiljaa (½ tablespoonful of minced parsley)
- suolaa ja pippuria (salt and pepper)

## Directions

1. Soak the bread crumbs in milk.
2. Add the butter and cook 5 minutes.
3. Stir into it the ground ham, parsley, and eggs beaten well.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Pour into a buttered mold.
6. Bake in oven in a pan of water for 25 minutes.
7. Serve with mushroom sauce.
