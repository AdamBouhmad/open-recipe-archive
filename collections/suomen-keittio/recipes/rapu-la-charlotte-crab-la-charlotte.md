## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup milk
- 3/8 package gelatine
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise sauce
- 4 tablespoons cream
- Boiled crab
- Truffle

## Directions

1. Dissolve the gelatine in milk
2. When cool, add gradually the mayonnaise and the whipped cream
3. Garnish individual molds with truffle, cover the bottom with some of the mixture
4. When firm, cover with a layer of crab meat
5. Have a layer of the mixture on the top
6. Serve unmolded, garnish with lettuce leaves
