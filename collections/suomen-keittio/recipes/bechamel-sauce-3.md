## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 carrot
- 1 slice of onion
- 1 1/4 cupfuls of white stock
- 1/2 cupful of grated cheese
- 1 cupful of sweet cream
- a pinch of white pepper
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cook stock with onion and carrot 15 minutes
2. Strain
3. Prepare a sauce by adding butter and flour
4. Mix with cheese, cream and seasonings
5. Let boil
