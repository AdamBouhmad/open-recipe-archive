## Ingredients

- 4 cups cranberries
- 1 cup water
- 2 cups sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the cranberries, put on the fire in 1 cup of water
2. Let boil for 20 minutes
3. Rub through a sieve
4. Add 2 cups sugar, continue to cook slowly for a few minutes
5. If boiled too fast, it will not jelly
6. Put into a wet mold to set
