## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- lemon juice
- olive oil
- egg whites
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Mix the yolks with a few drops of lemon juice and olive oil
2. Form into tiny balls
3. place inside of the rings of whites if preferred
