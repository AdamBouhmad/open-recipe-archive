## Ingredients

- 2 onions
- 1/2 pound butter
- 1/4 pound boiled rice
- 1 tablespoon flour
- Scalded milk
- 5 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon chopped dill
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg whites
- Hollandaise or lemon sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Chop 2 onions
2. Cook with 1/2 pound of butter
3. Add 1/4 pound of boiled ground rice and 1 tablespoon flour
4. Pour slowly scalded milk into the mixture until it becomes a thick porridge
5. Stir briskly
6. Remove from the fire and let cool
7. Add 5 egg yolks one by one and 1 tablespoon of chopped dill
8. Season with salt and pepper
9. Mix in the whites beaten until firm
10. Bake in timbale molds in a pan of water covered with buttered paper
11. Serve with Hollandaise or lemon sauce
