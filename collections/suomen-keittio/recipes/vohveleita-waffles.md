## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1½ cups milk
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 2 or 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Sift together flour, baking powder and salt.
2. Beat yolks, add milk and pour gradually in the flour; mix with butter.
3. Last of all fold in the beaten egg whites.
4. Fry in hot greased waffle iron until brown on both sides.
