## Ingredients

- 2 herrings
- 4 cupfuls of boiled potatoes
- 1/2 onion
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 cupfuls of milk
- 3 eggs
- chopped parsley and pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the herrings, bone and skin.
2. Butter a mold.
3. Fill it with alternating layers of potatoes and herring, having potatoes on the bottom and on the top.
4. Sprinkle the layers of herring with chopped onion and pour the melted butter over.
5. Beat the eggs, add milk, pepper and parsley into it, pour into the mold.
6. Sprinkle with crackercrumbs and bake in oven.
