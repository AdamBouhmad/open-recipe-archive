## Ingredients

- kidneys
- cold water
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- butter
- onion
- flour
- 1/2 cupful of meat stock
- 1/2 cupful of mushrooms

## Directions

1. Soak kidneys in cold water for one hour.
2. Wipe and cut into pieces.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Dip in bread crumbs and cook in butter with onion slices.
5. Add flour, 1/2 cupful of meat stock and let boil 5 minutes.
6. Strain, add 1/2 cupful of mushrooms, cleaned and cut into four pieces.
7. Season with salt and pepper and let boil a few minutes more.
