## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Salt
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- Butter or milk sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the head of cabbage in four or eight parts.
2. Put on the fire in boiling salt water with 1/4 teaspoon baking soda.
3. Cook until tender, about 1/2 hour.
4. Drain carefully.
5. Serve with melted butter or rich milk sauce.
