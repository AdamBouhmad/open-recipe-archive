## Ingredients

- macaroni
- salted water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- cheese crumbs

## Directions

1. Break macaroni to pieces 1 inch long
2. Rinse
3. Put on the fire in plenty of salted water
4. Cook for 20 minutes or until soft
5. Drain and cover with cold water to loosen the starch
6. Reheat
7. Season with salt and pepper
8. Dilute with butter
9. Serve in a vegetable dish with the top sprinkled with cheese crumbs
