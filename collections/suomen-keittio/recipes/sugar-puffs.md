## Ingredients

- 4 cupfuls of water
- 1/2 pound of butter
- 1/2 pound of flour
- 6 eggs
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Boil up 4 cupfuls of water, add 1/2 pound of butter into it, reheat to boiling point, mix with 1/2 pound of flour.
2. Then whip the mixture well until you can easily remove it from the saucepan.
3. Pour in a bowl to cool.
4. Then mix with 6 eggs, two at a time, beating well.
5. Form into small round cakes on baking sheet, bake to a light brown.
6. When done, sprinkle with sugar.
