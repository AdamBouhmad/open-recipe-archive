## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 7/8 cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla and lemon juice

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites until stiff.
2. Sift sugar several times and add gradually to the whites.
3. Work into the whites little by little flour sifted six times with cream of tartar and flavorings.
4. Pour into unbuttered tube tin.
5. Bake in a moderately hot oven for 40 or 50 minutes.
