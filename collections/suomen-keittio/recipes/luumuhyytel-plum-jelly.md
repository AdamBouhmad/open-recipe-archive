## Ingredients

- plums
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash plums, remove the stems, put into a saucepan
2. Add a little water and simmer on a slow fire until the plums have broken apart
3. Strain the liquid
4. Weigh the strained liquid and take an equal amount of sugar
5. Prepare as given for other jellies
