## Ingredients

- 4 teaspoonfuls of tea
- 2 cupfuls of water
- cracked ice
- lemon slices
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare tea as directed for regular tea but allow 4 teaspoonfuls of tea to 2 cupfuls of water
2. Half fill glasses with cracked ice, put a slice of lemon in
3. Strain the tea on it, filling the glasses
4. Add granulated sugar to taste
