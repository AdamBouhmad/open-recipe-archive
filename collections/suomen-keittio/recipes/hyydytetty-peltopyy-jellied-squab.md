## Ingredients

- squab
- stuffing for stuffed chicken
- thin cloth
- aspic jelly

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the squab as directed.
2. Stuff the same as stuffed chicken.
3. Sew up and wrap into a thin cloth.
4. Cook for 3/4 hour or until tender.
5. Remove from the stock and place under a small weight, until cold and even.
6. Make an aspic jelly of the stock.
7. Lay some of the aspic on the bottom of the mold.
8. Slice the squab crosswise and arrange nicely into the mold.
9. Fill the mold with aspic and place aside to set.
