## Ingredients

- center of fillets, cut 1½ inches thick
- melted butter or olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- parsley butter
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Cut the center of fillets in pieces 1½ inches thick and pound lightly.
2. Dip in melted butter or olive oil.
3. Fry quickly on both sides in a pan.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and place on a hot platter with a little dot of parsley butter on top of each piece.
5. Add a little bouillon to the drippings in the pan and pour this over the meat.
