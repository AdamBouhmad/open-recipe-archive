## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread crumbs
- ¾ cup bouillon or water
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon salt
- Chicken liver and heart
- Some nutmeg and pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the bread crumbs, squeeze dry, add the egg, liver and heart chopped fine and spices.
