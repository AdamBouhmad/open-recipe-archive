## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of baking powder
- 1/4 lb. of flour
- 1/4 lb. of corn starch
- juice of 1 lemon or 10 drops of lemon extract

## Directions

1. Beat yolks with sugar until light
2. Add corn starch mixed with flour, lemon, butter washed and rubbed to a cream
3. Stir lastly the whites beaten stiff and dry into it
4. Half fill small molds with the above mixture
5. Bake in a moderate oven
