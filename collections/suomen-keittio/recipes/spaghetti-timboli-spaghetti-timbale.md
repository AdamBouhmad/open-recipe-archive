## Ingredients

- long pieces of macaroni or spaghetti
- salt
- cooked chicken or veal
- milk sauce
- 3 or 4 eggs
- butter for molds

## Directions

1. Parboil in salted water long pieces of macaroni or spaghetti.
2. Put into cold water to keep them whole.
3. Place on a straight cloth to cool.
4. Butter a ring-shaped mold and line with the macaroni having the pieces lengthwise in the mold.
5. Fill the mold with cooked chicken or veal chopped and mixed with milk sauce.
6. Add 3 or 4 well beaten eggs shortly before putting the mold into the oven.
7. Bake for 30 minutes in oven in a pan of water covered with buttered paper.
