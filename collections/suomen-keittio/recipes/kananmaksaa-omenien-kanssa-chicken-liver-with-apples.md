## Ingredients

- apples
- butter
- sugar
- chicken livers
- salt
- pepper
- bouillon
- bread for toast points
- fruit sauce

## Directions

1. Pare apples and cut lengthwise in six parts.
2. Cook in butter and sugar until tender.
3. Fry chicken livers in butter, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Add some bouillon and let come to boil.
5. Arrange the apples on a serving dish and place the livers on top.
6. Line the dish around with toast points.
7. Serve with fruit sauce.
