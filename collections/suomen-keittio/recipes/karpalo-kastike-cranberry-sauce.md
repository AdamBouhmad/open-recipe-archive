## Ingredients

- 1 quart of cranberries
- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Rinse the cranberries
2. Place on fire to boil with 2 cupfuls of sugar and water so that it reaches the level of the berries
3. Cook on a slow fire until the berries break
4. Put in a mold to set either strained or unstrained
