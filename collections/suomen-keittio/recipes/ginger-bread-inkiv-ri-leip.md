## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of molasses
- 1/2 cupful of sour cream or milk
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 teaspoonful of ginger
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Melt molasses and butter, work to a froth.
2. Add seasonings, cream and flour mixed with baking soda.
3. Bake about 1/2 hour.
