## Ingredients

- ½ cupful of butter
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- ½ cupful of milk
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla or lemon juice
- egg whites

## Directions

1. For white part: Rub ½ cupful of butter and 1 cupful of sugar to a cream, add 4 well-beaten yolks of eggs, ½ cupful of milk, 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder mixed with 2 cupfuls of flour and 1 teaspoonful of vanilla or lemon juice. Lastly stir the stiffly beaten whites of eggs into the mixture. If white batter is preferred, leave out the yolks of eggs.
2. For dark part: Work ½ cupful of butter and 1 cupful of brown sugar to a cream, add well-beaten yolks of eggs, ½ cupfuls of molasses, ½ cupful of milk, 1 teaspoonful of soda, cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg, ½ teaspoonful of each, and 2 cupfuls of flour; lastly whites, beaten to a froth. Or use for the dark part the same mixture as for the white part mixed with 1½ ozs. of melted chocolate.
3. Butter a mold and lay a spoonful of both mixtures alternately in it to resemble marble.
4. Bake in a buttered mold in a moderate oven.
