## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 8 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon cardamom
- 1/2 teaspoon cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon lemon rind
- 8 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and sugar
2. Mix in butter, spices and flour
3. Roll dough thin
4. Cut in small cookies
5. Bake in a moderate oven
6. If allowed to stand in a cold place for a while, the dough is easier to handle
