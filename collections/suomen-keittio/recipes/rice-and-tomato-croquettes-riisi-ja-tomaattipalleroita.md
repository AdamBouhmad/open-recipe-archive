## Ingredients

- rice (half cooked and strained)
- tomato
- salt
- sugar
- pepper
- 1 large tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon onion juice
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- egg for coating
- bread crumbs
- butter or lard for frying

## Directions

1. To half cooked strained rice add tomato, salt, sugar and pepper
2. Continue to boil for 10 minutes
3. Remove from the fire and add 1 large tablespoon butter, 1 teaspoon onion juice, 3 egg yolks and 2 tablespoons grated cheese
4. Cool thoroughly
5. Form into croquettes
6. Roll in egg and bread crumbs
7. Fry in butter or cook in deep lard
