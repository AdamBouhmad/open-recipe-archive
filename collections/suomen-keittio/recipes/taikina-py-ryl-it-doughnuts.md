## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 4 cups flour

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar to a froth.
2. Add butter rubbed until creamy.
3. Add milk with soda and cream of tartar dissolved in it.
4. Add salt, cinnamon and flour.
5. Roll out to 1/2 inch thickness.
6. Cut in rings with a round cutter (doughnut cutter).
7. Cook in lard until light brown on both sides.
8. Drain on brown paper and dip in powdered sugar.
9. Cook 2 or 3 at a time.
10. Ensure lard is at moderate temperature - if not hot enough, doughnuts will absorb lard; if too hot, they will brown before sufficiently risen.
