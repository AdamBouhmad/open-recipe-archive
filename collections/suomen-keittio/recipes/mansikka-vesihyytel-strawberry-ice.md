## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 quart strawberries
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook sugar and water for 15 minutes
2. Press strawberries through a cloth into the syrup
3. Flavor with lemon juice
4. Freeze
