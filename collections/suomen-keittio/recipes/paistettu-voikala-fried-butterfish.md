## Ingredients

- Fish
- Milk
- Flour
- Salt
- Butter
- Lemon juice
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Clean, rinse and wipe the fish.
2. Dip in milk, roll in flour mixed with salt.
3. Brown some butter in frying-pan, fry the fish until brown on both sides.
4. Cover, place in oven until well done.
5. Arrange on the platter, pour browned butter mixed with some lemon juice over the fish, strew with chopped parsley.
6. Garnish with parsley and lemon.
