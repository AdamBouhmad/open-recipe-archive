## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1½ cupfuls of milk
- 1-2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- ½ teaspoonful of baking powder
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- ½ teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Sift flour with salt and baking powder.
2. Beat eggs with sugar, add milk into it.
3. Pour milk slowly into flour.
4. Lastly mix with melted butter.
5. Drop with spoon on a hot griddle and fry on both sides.
6. Serve with melted butter and maple syrup.
