## Ingredients

- Sweetbreads
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Butter or lard
- Bechamel sauce, tomato sauce, or milk sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the sweetbreads as directed.
2. Split lengthwise.
3. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
4. Fry in butter or lard.
5. Serve with Bechamel, tomato or milk sauce.
