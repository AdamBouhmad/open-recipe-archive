## Ingredients

- veal
- salt
- pepper
- salted water
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter
- lemon
- anchovy
- capers

## Directions

1. Cut the veal into 1/2 inch thick slices, pound and form into oval-shaped cakes.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper or dip in salted water.
3. Dip in beaten egg and roll in bread crumbs.
4. Brown in butter on both sides.
5. Cover and leave on the heat for a while.
6. Arrange on a serving platter with a dot of butter under each piece and a slice of lemon on top.
7. Make rings of anchovies with capers inside and place on top of the lemon.
