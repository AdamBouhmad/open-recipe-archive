## Ingredients

- rice, half-done
- tomato juice
- salt
- sugar
- pepper
- 1 big tablespoonful of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of onion juice
- 3 yolks of eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cheese crumbs
- egg
- bread crumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. Drain off the water when the rice is half-done, add tomato juice, salt, sugar and pepper to season.
2. Cook 10 minutes or until the rice is tender.
3. Remove from fire, add 1 big tablespoonful of butter, 1 teaspoonful of onion juice, 3 yolks of eggs, 2 tablespoonfuls of cheese crumbs.
4. Cool thoroughly.
5. Form into croquettes, dip in egg and bread crumbs.
6. Fry in lard.
