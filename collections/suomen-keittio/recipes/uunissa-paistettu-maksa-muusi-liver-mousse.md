## Ingredients

- 2 pounds beef liver
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1 onion
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup water or cream
- 1 or 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Soak the liver in cold water.
2. The liver may be boiled in salted water, or ground fresh with an onion.
3. Soak the bread crumbs in water or fry in lard.
4. Add egg and combine with liver stirring briskly.
5. Form into patties and fry in butter, or bake in a mold lined with bacon slices on top.
6. Bake until nice brown or about 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
7. Cover with buttered paper to keep it juicy.
8. If the onion is fried in butter before added to the liver, it will taste better.
