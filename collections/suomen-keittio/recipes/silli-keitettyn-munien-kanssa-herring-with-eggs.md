## Ingredients

- 4 salt herrings
- 3 hard boiled eggs
- Butter sauce
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Soak the herrings, skin and bone.
2. Cover with boiling water, let boil slowly for a few minutes.
3. If very salty, place the fish on a fire in cold water.
4. Chop the hard boiled eggs.
5. Arrange the fish on a platter alternately with layers of egg.
6. Serve with hot potatoes.
7. The fish may be covered with thin milk sauce flavored with onion juice.
