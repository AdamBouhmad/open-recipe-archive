## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- 1 cupful of water
- strawberries

## Directions

1. Cook 2 cupfuls of sugar with 1 cupful of water 15 minutes
2. Put strawberries in and let boil up
3. Pour in a bowl and let cool down
4. Serve in a glass dish, with a small quantity of their own liquor
