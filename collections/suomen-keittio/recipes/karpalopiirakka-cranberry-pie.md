## Ingredients

- cranberries
- sugar
- pastry for one open crust and pastry strips

## Directions

1. Cook cranberries with sugar.
2. Let cool.
3. Cover a pie plate with pastry, fill with cranberries.
4. Lay strips cut of pastry across the top.
5. Brush with egg.
6. Bake in a moderate oven.
