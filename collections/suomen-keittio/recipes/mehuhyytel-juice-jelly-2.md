## Ingredients

- 1 quart fruit or berry juice
- 2 tablespoons or 1/2 package gelatine
- water
- berries and fruits, peeled and cut up (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix 1 quart of fruit juice with 2 tablespoons or 1/2 package gelatine dissolved in water
2. Pour into a mold to set
3. The mold may be filled with alternate layers of jelly and peeled and cut up fruit
