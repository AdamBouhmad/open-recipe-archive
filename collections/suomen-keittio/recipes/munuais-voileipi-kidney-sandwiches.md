## Ingredients

- roasted calf or lamb kidneys
- 2 ounces butter
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolk
- toast

## Directions

1. Mince the roasted kidneys very fine.
2. Put 2 ounces of butter in a saucepan.
3. Mix with minced kidneys, a little lemon juice, salt and pepper.
4. Let boil a few minutes.
5. Remove the saucepan from the fire, stir an egg yolk into the mixture.
6. Spread on toast and serve very hot.
