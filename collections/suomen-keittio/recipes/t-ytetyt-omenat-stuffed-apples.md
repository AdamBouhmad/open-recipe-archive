## Ingredients

- apples
- lemon juice
- celery
- chopped walnuts
- mayonnaise
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Scoop the apples and peel them
2. Moisten with lemon juice to prevent browning
3. Mix pieces of the scooped apple with celery and some chopped walnuts
4. Mix this mixture with mayonnaise
5. Fill the apples with the mixture
6. Place whipped cream on top
