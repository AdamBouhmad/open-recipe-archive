## Ingredients

- 3 pounds suckling pig meat
- 2 quarts water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 10 peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- 3 leaves of gelatine
- Knuckles of veal
- Egg whites
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- Tomato

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat and the knuckles with a cloth wrung out of hot water.
2. Place the knuckles on the fire in cold water; when coming to a boil add the meat.
3. Skim, spice and let boil slowly until tender.
4. Remove the skin, cut the meat into nice cubes.
5. Beat the whites of egg lightly, mix with the gelatine dissolved, 1 tablespoon of lemon juice and the liquor of meat carefully removing the fat.
6. Add some tomato.
7. Stir on the fire until it begins to boil, place the saucepan on the side of the range, cook slowly.
8. Strain through a fine cloth.
9. Line a mold thinly with clarified liquid jelly, garnish with hard boiled eggs and parsley.
10. Lay the pieces of meat into the mold lightly so as to fill it with plenty of liquid.
11. Put in a cold place to set.
