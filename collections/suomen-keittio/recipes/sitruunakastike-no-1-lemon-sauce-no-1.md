## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 cup water
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar with the cornstarch
2. Add gradually the boiling water, stirring well
3. Cook until clear
4. Remove from the fire, add the lemon juice and butter
