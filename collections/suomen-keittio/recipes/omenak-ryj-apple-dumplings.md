## Ingredients

- Puff paste
- Apples
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Butter
- Egg white
- Hard sauce

## Directions

1. Roll puff paste into a thin sheet and cut into squares large enough to enclose an apple.
2. Peel and core the apples, then bake for a while.
3. Place an apple in the center of each square.
4. Fill the hollowed core with sugar mixed with cinnamon.
5. Dot with butter.
6. Moisten the edges of the paste with egg white and close the paste by bringing the corners together.
7. Turn the edges in and spread with egg.
8. Bake in a moderate oven until the apples feel tender.
9. Serve hot with hard sauce.
