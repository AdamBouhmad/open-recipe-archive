## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup confectioner's sugar
- cream, milk or water
- lemon, orange, chocolate or vanilla for flavoring

## Directions

1. Rub 2 tablespoons butter with 1 cup confectioner's sugar until creamy
2. Add cream, milk or water
3. Flavor with lemon, orange, chocolate or vanilla
