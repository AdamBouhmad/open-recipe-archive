## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 3 cupfuls of brown sugar
- 1 cupful of melted butter
- 4 cupfuls of flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls of soda
- 1 cupful of sour cream
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of currants
- 1 cupful of sweet almonds
- 1 yellow lemon rind
- 10 cardamoms

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks with sugar until light
2. Mix with butter, cream and soda dissolved in cream, currants, almonds, lemon rind, cardamom and flour
3. Fold in the stiffly beaten whites
4. Bake 1 hour
