## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup mushrooms, chopped
- butter
- 1/2 cup cooked shrimps
- 1 to 3 eggs
- cream
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Fry 1/4 cup of chopped mushrooms in butter for 5 minutes.
2. Add shrimps and eggs mixed with cream.
3. Use 1 or 2 eggs more than in the basic recipe.
4. Shake over the fire until firm.
5. Serve on a platter.
