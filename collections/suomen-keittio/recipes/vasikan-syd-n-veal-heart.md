## Ingredients

- veal heart
- salt water
- ground meat or bread
- butter
- onion
- pepper
- egg

## Directions

1. Clean heart and place in salted water for one hour to remove blood.
2. Fill the opening with stuffing made from ground meat or bread mixed with finely chopped onion fried in butter, pepper and 1 egg.
3. Sew the opening closed and roast in the oven.
