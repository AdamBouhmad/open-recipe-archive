## Ingredients

- 1 can of tomato or 8-9 fresh tomatoes
- 1 small onion
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 teaspoon of salt
- Some lemon juice
- Sugar according to taste
- 2 tablespoons or 1/2 package of gelatine
- Lettuce leaves
- Mayonnaise (for serving)
- Egg white (optional for clarification)

## Directions

1. Cook the tomato with seasonings, add the gelatine and strain.
2. Put into a jelly mold or individual molds to set.
3. Serve on a bed of lettuce garnished with mayonnaise.
4. Or cut in pieces and serve with cold meat.
5. May be clarified with the white of an egg, if desired.
