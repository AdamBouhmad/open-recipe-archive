## Ingredients

- 3/4 cupful of rice grain
- 3 cupfuls of milk
- 2 ruokalus. of butter
- cinnamon
- 2 ruokalus. of sugar flour
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook grains and milk to a porridge, mix in butter, cinnamon and eggs beaten with sugar to the porridge.
2. Put in a buttered mold.
3. Bake in oven set in a pan of water about 30-40 minutes.
4. Raisins and almonds can be used if desired.
