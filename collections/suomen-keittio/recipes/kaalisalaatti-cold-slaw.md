## Ingredients

- cabbage
- ice water
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 cup salad oil
- 1/2 cup vinegar or juice of one lemon
- salt
- pepper
- mustard

## Directions

1. Shred the cabbage, rinse and let stand in ice water.
2. Drain and wrap into a towel.
3. Make a dressing of 3 egg yolks mixed with 1/2 cup of salad oil, 1/2 cup vinegar or the juice of a lemon.
4. Season with salt, pepper and mustard.
5. Add dressing to the cabbage.
