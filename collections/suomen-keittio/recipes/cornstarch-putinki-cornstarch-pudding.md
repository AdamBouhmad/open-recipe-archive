## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 3 tablespoons cornstarch
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- Some vanilla
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Scald the milk
2. Add part of the sugar, vanilla and cornstarch dissolved in cold milk
3. Bring to boil
4. Remove from fire
5. Add egg yolks beaten with remaining sugar
6. Reheat over fire, not allowing to come to boiling point
7. Serve cold with vanilla or fruit sauce
8. May be served with whipped cream mixed with jam
9. Optional: use egg whites instead of yolks if white color is preferred
10. Optional: melt chocolate with milk and prepare as cornstarch pudding using 2 cups milk to 5-6 tablespoons finely chopped chocolate
11. Optional: mix in various canned fruits or berries
