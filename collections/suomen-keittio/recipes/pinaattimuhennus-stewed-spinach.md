## Ingredients

- 2 pounds spinach
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup milk or cream
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the spinach carefully.
2. Boil in salted water with 1/4 teaspoonful of soda for 20 minutes.
3. Drain and chop.
4. Put butter and flour into a saucepan, add spinach, some liquor of spinach or milk, salt and some sugar according to taste.
5. Heat to boiling.
6. Place in a vegetable dish, garnish with hard boiled eggs.
7. Serve with meat or fish.
