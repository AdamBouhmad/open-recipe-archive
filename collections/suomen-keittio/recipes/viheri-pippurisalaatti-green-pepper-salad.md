## Ingredients

- 3 green peppers
- 4 tomatoes
- 4 apples
- lard
- French dressing
- mayonnaise dressing
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Cook quickly in lard the green peppers so as to remove the skin.
2. Skin and cut in fine strips.
3. Peel the apples and tomatoes, cut in pieces.
4. Mix together and pour French dressing over.
5. Place into a salted bowl on a bed of lettuce, garnish around with mayonnaise.
