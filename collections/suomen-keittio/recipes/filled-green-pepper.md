## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of rice
- 1/2 tablespoonful of butter
- 1/2 tablespoonful of onion
- 1/2 tablespoonful of minced green pepper
- 1/2 tablespoonful of mushrooms
- 1 tablespoonful of tomatoes
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- a little pepper
- bread crumbs
- melted butter for basting

## Directions

1. Cook rice until tender
2. Mix rice with other ingredients
3. Fill scooped green peppers with this mixture
4. Bake in a buttered pan
5. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
6. Baste with a little melted butter
