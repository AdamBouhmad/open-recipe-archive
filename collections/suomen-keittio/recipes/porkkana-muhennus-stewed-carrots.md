## Ingredients

- carrots
- salted water
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls flour
- cream
- parsley, chopped (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel, wash and cut carrots into dice or strips.
2. Cook in salted water until tender.
3. Prepare a sauce of butter and flour mixed with the cooking liquid from the carrots.
4. Add a little cream to the sauce.
5. Put the carrots in the sauce and let boil.
6. Sprinkle with chopped parsley if desired.
