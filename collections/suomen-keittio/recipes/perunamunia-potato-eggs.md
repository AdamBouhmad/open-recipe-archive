## Ingredients

- equal-sized potatoes
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil equal sized potatoes with some salt.
2. Scoop carefully and peel.
3. Fill each one with an egg.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
5. Bake until the eggs are firm.
6. Serve with browned butter.
