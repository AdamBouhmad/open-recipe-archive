## Ingredients

- 2 herrings
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice
- 4 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the herring, skin and bone.
2. Chop the herring.
3. Cook the chopped onion in butter, add the herring into it.
4. Mix with lemon juice, cream and seasonings.
5. Let quickly boil.
6. At the last mix with chopped eggs.
7. Arrange on a serving-dish.
8. Serve hot or cold covered with Mayonnaise sauce.
