## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 2 cups cream
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup orange juice
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/2 package or 8 leaves gelatine

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar
2. Add the lemon and the orange juice
3. Beat over the fire until thick
4. Add the dissolved gelatine
5. Let cool
6. Mix in the whipped cream and the whites beaten stiff
7. Put into a wet mold
8. Serve unmolded
