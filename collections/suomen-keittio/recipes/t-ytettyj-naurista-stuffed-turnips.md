## Ingredients

- 12 medium-sized turnips
- 1 cup almonds
- 3/4 cup bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- Pinch of nutmeg and pepper
- Water and butter for basting

## Directions

1. Rinse the turnips, boil with salt and pepper until almost tender.
2. When cool, peel them.
3. Scoop, mash the interior.
4. Add sugar, blanched and grated almonds, butter, salt, pepper, nutmeg, bread crumbs and eggs beaten.
5. Stuff the hollowed turnips with this mixture.
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs.
7. Bake until light brown, basting often with water mixed with butter.
