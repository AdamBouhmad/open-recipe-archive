## Ingredients

- Salmon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bay leaf
- 2 cups fish stock, cold
- 1 cup tomato
- 0.5 lemon, juice of
- 2 tablespoons gelatine
- 2 egg whites, lightly beaten
- Hard boiled eggs
- Parsley
- Lettuce leaves
- Tomato slices
- Lemon slices
- Mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Boil the fish with salt, pepper and a bay leaf.
2. When done lift off to cool.
3. Prepare a jelly of 2 cups of fish stock, 1 cup of tomato, juice of 0.5 lemon, 2 tablespoons of gelatine and 2 whites of egg lightly beaten.
4. Put all the ingredients mentioned above into a saucepan, stir over the fire until boiling.
5. Lift aside, let simmer 5 minutes or until clear.
6. Strain through a fine cloth.
7. Pour some of the jelly into a mold, garnish with hard boiled egg and parsley.
8. Lay the fish nicely on top of it in the mold and fill the mold slowly with the jelly.
9. Place in cold place for a few hours.
10. Unmold onto a platter, garnish with lettuce, slices of tomato, lemon and parsley.
11. Serve with mayonnaise.
