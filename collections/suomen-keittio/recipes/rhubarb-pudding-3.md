## Ingredients

- bread soaked in milk
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/4 cupful of butter
- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- rhubarb
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Put bread, soaked in milk, into a saucepan, with juice of 1 lemon, 1/4 cupful of butter and 1/2 cupful of sugar and let come to boiling point.
2. Skin, cut and cook rhubarb with sugar and drain on a sieve.
3. Put layers of bread mixture and cooked rhubarb alternately in a mold.
4. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and dot with butter.
5. Bake in oven 40 minutes, first covered with paper; then uncovered so as to brown it.
6. Serve with hard sauce.
