## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 1 lb. of flour
- 10 eggs
- 3/4 cupful of brandy
- a dash of mace

## Directions

1. Work butter and sugar to a froth, add beaten yolks of eggs into it, then season and mix with flour.
2. At the last stir the whites of eggs, beaten to a stiff froth, into it.
3. Bake in a moderate oven.
