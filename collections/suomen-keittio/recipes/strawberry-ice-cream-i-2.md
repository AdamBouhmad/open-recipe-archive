## Ingredients

- 1 quart of cream
- 1 basket of strawberries
- 1½ cupfuls of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash strawberries and let stand in sugar 1 hour.
2. Mash and force through a cloth.
3. Add cream and lemon juice.
4. Freeze.
