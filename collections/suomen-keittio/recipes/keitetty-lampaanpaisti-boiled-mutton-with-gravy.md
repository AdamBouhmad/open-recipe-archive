## Ingredients

- Roasted mutton
- Fat from drippings
- 3 tablespoons flour
- Boiling water
- 1 tablespoon tomato
- Mustard

## Directions

1. Remove some of the fat from the roasted meat.
2. Mix the drippings with 3 tablespoons of flour.
3. Add boiling water until the gravy becomes moderately thick.
4. Flavor with a tablespoon of tomato and some mustard.
5. Strain.
