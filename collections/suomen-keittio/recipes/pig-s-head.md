## Ingredients

- pork
- 4 eggs
- pepper
- salt
- sherry
- slices of carrots
- truffles
- cucumbers

## Directions

1. Split the head, remove the tongue, saw the bone leaving the nose whole.
2. Grind all kinds of pork, pound well.
3. Mix with 4 eggs, pepper, salt and sherry.
4. Put one half of the pork forcemeat into the head, lay on it slices of carrots, truffles, cucumbers, etc.
5. Fill with the remaining forcemeat.
6. Sew up the head and the eyes.
7. Put a slice of carrot in the ears and fasten them down.
8. Tie the nose with cord.
9. Wrap the head in a cloth and cook.
10. Place under a heavy weight.
11. Serve cold with Cumberland sauce.
