## Ingredients

- 1 pound spinach
- 1 cup milk
- 4 eggs
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the spinach, boil and chop.
2. Mix with butter, soften with cream.
3. Put in a buttered pan.
4. Beat the yolks and whites separately.
5. Mix the yolks with milk and season.
6. Add the whites.
7. Pour the mixture over the spinach.
8. Bake until firm and light brown.
