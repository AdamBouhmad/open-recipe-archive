## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of almonds
- 8 tablesp. of powdered sugar
- 1 white of egg

## Directions

1. Peel almonds and cut in pieces.
2. Beat whites of eggs to a stiff froth, mix with sugar and almonds.
3. Drop with spoon on a baking sheet, bake in a slow oven until lightly browned and dry.
