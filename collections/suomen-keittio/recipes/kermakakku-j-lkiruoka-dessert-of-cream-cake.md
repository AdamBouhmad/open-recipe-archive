## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of rich cream
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Whip eggs to a froth
2. Add beaten cream and flour
3. Bake in a buttered mold sprinkled with sugar
4. Serve as dessert with jam
