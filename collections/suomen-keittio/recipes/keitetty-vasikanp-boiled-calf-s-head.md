## Ingredients

- 1 calf's head, split and cleaned
- cold water
- lemon
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 10 peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 onion
- 1 carrot
- parsley
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the calf's head well and remove the tongue and brains.
2. Boil the brains quickly.
3. Soak the head in cold water and place in boiling cold water.
4. When it comes to a boil, drain the water and cool in cold water.
5. Rub the head with lemon and place in hot water to simmer slowly.
6. Add vinegar, peppercorns, bay leaf, onion, carrot and parsley sprigs.
7. Cover and simmer until the meat is tender but not falling apart.
8. Remove the bones carefully and place the meat in a pan shaped to the original form.
9. Brush with egg, sprinkle with bread crumbs and dot with small pieces of butter.
10. Brown in the oven.
11. Serve with roulade or allemande sauce.
