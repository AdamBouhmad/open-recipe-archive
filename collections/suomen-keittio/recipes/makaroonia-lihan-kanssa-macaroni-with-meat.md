## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 cups chicken or meat stock
- 1 cup cooked ground meat
- 3/4 pound boiled macaroni
- 1 egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and stock
2. Add to the sauce the cooked ground meat and boiled macaroni
3. Mix in the beaten egg
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered mold
5. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
6. Bake in the oven for about 3/4 hour
