## Ingredients

- 1 chicken, turkey, or fowl
- stuffing of choice
- cream

## Directions

1. Bone the chicken, turkey or fowl
2. Stuff with one of the provided stuffings
3. Sew up
4. Roast in oven as directed for stuffed chicken
5. Prepare a sauce of its own liquor by adding cream
