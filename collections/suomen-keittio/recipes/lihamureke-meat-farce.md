## Ingredients

- 1 pound beef
- 1/4 pound pork
- 1/2 pound veal
- 1 cup cream or milk
- 2 cups bouillon or water
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 2 eggs
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Force the meat twice through a meat chopper.
2. Soak the bread crumbs in bouillon and cream.
3. Add the beaten eggs and mix with meat, pounding well.
4. Chop the onion, fry in butter, and strain the butter into the mixture.
5. Add seasonings last of all.
6. Put in a buttered pan, forming into a nice loaf.
7. Add a sprinkling of bread crumbs on top.
8. Bake from 1 to 1 1/2 hours, basting often and adding bouillon or water as needed.
9. Serve with a brown gravy.
