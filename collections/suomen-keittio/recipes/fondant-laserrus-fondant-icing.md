## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
- lemon juice for flavoring
- oil for brushing

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan 1 cup granulated sugar, 1/2 cup water and 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
2. Place over the fire
3. Cook stirring constantly until the sugar is dissolved
4. Continue to cook without stirring until it threads when dropped from a spoon
5. Brush with oil a marble slab or a big platter
6. Pour the mixture on and let cool for a few minutes
7. Stir with a knife or a wooden spoon until white and smooth, keeping the mixture as much as possible in the center of the slab
8. This icing may be kept for a long time in an earthen jar and used when needed for cookies
9. Put the icing into a double boiler, flavor with lemon juice, stir until dissolved
10. Coat the cookies with it
11. By adding some sugar and 1 egg white the icing may be used for garnishing
