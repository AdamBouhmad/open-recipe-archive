## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- Salt and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs
2. Add sugar, salt and nutmeg
3. Pour gradually the scalded milk into the eggs
4. Bake in a buttered mold in a pan of water until firm
