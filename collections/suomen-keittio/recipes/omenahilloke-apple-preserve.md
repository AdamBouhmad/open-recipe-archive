## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of sugar per 1 quart of water
- apples

## Directions

1. Make a sugar syrup of 2 lbs. of sugar to 1 quart of water.
2. Peel apples, remove seeds and cut in pieces or keep whole.
3. Carefully place them in glass jars with airtight covers and fill jars with sugar syrup.
4. Place covers on and set jars in kettle on stands so they do not break when boiling.
5. Pour lukewarm water so it covers 3/4 of the jars.
6. Set kettle on fire, let water heat slowly and then boil 20-30 minutes, depending on the size and type of fruit.
7. Leave jars in water until they cool.
