## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of water
- 3 ozs. of melted chocolate
- a little vanilla
- a little cream

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 cupful of sugar and 1/2 cupful of water 5 minutes
2. Add 3 ozs. of melted chocolate and a little vanilla
3. Let stand in double-boiler until served
4. Add a little cream
5. When too smooth the sauce may be thickened with arrowroot
