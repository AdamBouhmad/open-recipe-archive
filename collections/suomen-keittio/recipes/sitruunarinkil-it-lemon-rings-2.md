## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 3 ozs. of sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cream
- grated lemon rind
- wheat flour as needed

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks and sugar to a froth
2. Add cream, butter, and lemon rind
3. Add flour enough to make a dough easy to handle
4. Form into small rings
5. Bake on a buttered baking sheet until light brown
