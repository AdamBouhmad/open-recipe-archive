## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoonfuls of salad oil
- 2 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped pickles
- 1/2 tablesp. of chopped green pepper
- 1 teaspoonful of parsley
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Pour gradually oil into vinegar
2. Add pickles, green pepper, parsley, salt and pepper
