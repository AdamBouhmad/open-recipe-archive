## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of oranges
- 2 lemons
- 5 lbs. of crystal sugar
- 1.5 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Wipe oranges and lemons well.
2. Peel them.
3. Cut away the white part from the inside of the peels, then cut the peels into thin strips and put 1.5 cupfuls of water to simmer slowly for half an hour.
4. Cut the fruit in pieces and remove the seeds.
5. Put to simmer slowly in 1.5 quarts of water for about half an hour.
6. Pour the mixture into a fine straining cloth to drip by itself.
7. Cook the juice obtained and the strips of fruit peel with its liquid and sugar for 15 minutes stirring constantly.
8. Pour the marmalade into small jars and preserve as usual.
9. Serve with tea and toast.
