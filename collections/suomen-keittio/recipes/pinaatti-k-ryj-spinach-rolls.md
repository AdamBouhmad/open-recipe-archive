## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs. of spinach
- 1/2 lb. of white bread
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 2 eggs
- salt, sugar and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Soak bread in milk
2. Clean spinach and boil
3. Separate the nicest leaves and grind the rest of leaves
4. Add melted butter, ground spinach, eggs and seasonings to the soaked bread, stirring well
5. Drop the mixture on the nice whole leaves and roll together
6. Bake in oven, covered, about 30 minutes
7. Arrange on a serving platter and serve with a sauce made of melted butter and hard-boiled egg
