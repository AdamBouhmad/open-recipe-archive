## Ingredients

- 1 cup graham flour
- ½ cup white flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- ¾ cup milk
- 1 or 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Sift together the flour, the baking powder and the salt.
2. Beat the eggs with the sugar, add gradually the milk.
3. Last of all mix in the melted butter.
4. Bake for 20 or 30 minutes in buttered muffin tins.
