## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of bone- or meat-stock
- 3 tablespoonfuls of tapioca
- salt and pepper
- 2 tablespoonfuls of chopped parsley
- 2 yolks
- 1 cupful of cream

## Directions

1. Wash the tapioca and cook in stock until tender.
2. Beat the yolks and add the cream into it.
3. Pour the soup slowly in.
4. Place on the fire for a little while; stir, not allowing it to boil.
5. Serve with cheese-balls, nicely cut artichokes or small potatoes.
