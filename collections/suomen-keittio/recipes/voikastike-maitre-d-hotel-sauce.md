## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cream well 2 tablespoons of butter
2. Add the juice of half a lemon and 1 tablespoon of chopped parsley
3. Serve with broiled meat or fish having the sauce on top
4. The sauce may be made without parsley and served with asparagus and artichokes
