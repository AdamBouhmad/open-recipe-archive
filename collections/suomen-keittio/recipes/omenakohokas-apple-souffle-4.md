## Ingredients

- 6 ripe apples
- 3 eggs
- 2 ozs. of butter
- 2 ozs. of flour
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of milk
- 3 ozs. of sugar
- a dash of cinnamon
- jam

## Directions

1. Pare and core the apples and cook with 1 cupful of sugar and cinnamon.
2. Lay them on bottom of a souffle mold and fill the hollowed cores with jam.
3. Cook half of the butter with flour and milk until paste.
4. Whip yolks with sugar and stir it into the paste, add remaining butter.
5. Lastly mix with the stiff beaten whites.
6. Pour the mixture on the apples.
7. Bake in oven nearly 30 minutes.
