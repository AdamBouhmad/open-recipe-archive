## Ingredients

- marrow bone
- salt water
- thick paste
- clean cloth
- bouillon
- toast
- parsley
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Saw the marrowbone into pieces 2-3 inches long.
2. Scrape the meat off carefully.
3. Leave the bones in cold water for 1/2 hour.
4. Close both ends with thick paste and tie a clean cloth around.
5. Place the bones in a saucepan in bouillon.
6. Cook slowly for 1 1/2 hours.
7. Remove the cloth and the paste.
8. Place the bones on toast.
9. Garnish around with parsley and lettuce leaves.
10. Serve very hot.
