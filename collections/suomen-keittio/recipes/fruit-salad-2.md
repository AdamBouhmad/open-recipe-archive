## Ingredients

- Oranges
- Bananas
- Grapefruit
- Strawberries
- Cherries
- Melons
- Currants
- Pineapples
- Figs
- Walnuts
- Apples
- Salad oil
- Lemon juice
- Salt
- Paprika

## Directions

1. Use one or several kinds of fruit to prepare a salad
2. Oranges, bananas, grapefruit, strawberries and cherries may be mixed together for salad
3. A fancy salad may be made of melons, currants, strawberries, pineapples, cherries and figs
4. Oranges, bananas, currants and walnuts mixed together; oranges mixed with bananas and apples make a delicious fruit salad
5. Prepare a sauce of 3 tablespoonfuls of salad oil and 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice seasoned with salt and paprika
