## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of water
- 3/4 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- white of an egg
- lemon or vanilla for flavoring

## Directions

1. Put sugar, water and cream of tartar in a sauce-pan
2. Let boil until syrup will thread when dropped from tip of spoon
3. Beat white of an egg to a stiff froth
4. Pour syrup gradually on it, whipping well
5. Flavor with lemon or vanilla
6. If using two whites of eggs in the syrup, the icing will be smoother
