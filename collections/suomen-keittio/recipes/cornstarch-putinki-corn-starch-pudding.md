## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 3/4 cupful of corn starch
- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- yolks of 2 eggs
- a little vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil milk
2. Stir in a part of sugar, vanilla and corn starch which has been mixed with cold milk
3. Let boil up
4. Add yolks whipped with sugar until light
5. Serve cold with fruit sauce, whipped cream or jam, or mixed with fruit or chocolate
