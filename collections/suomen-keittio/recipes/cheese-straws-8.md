## Ingredients

- puff paste
- grated cheese
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Roll puff paste into a sheet, 3/8 inch thick.
2. Sprinkle one half with grated cheese and a little cayenne.
3. Double in half and press lightly together.
4. Cut with knife into straws, 4 inches long and 1/2 inch wide.
5. Bake in hot oven 10 minutes.
6. Serve with salad.
