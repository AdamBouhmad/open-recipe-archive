## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons chopped almonds
- 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
- egg yolks
- sugar
- scalded milk
- butter
- egg whites
- 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks with the sugar, add the scalded milk and butter, stir over the fire until it becomes thick
2. Cool, mix with flour, almonds and the egg whites beaten firm
3. Brown 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar in a pan, add 2 tablespoons water and some lemon juice
4. Coat the bottom and the edges of a mold with the syrup
5. Fill the mold with the mixture
6. Bake in oven in a pan of water or steam on the stove for 1 hour
7. Serve with vanilla sauce
