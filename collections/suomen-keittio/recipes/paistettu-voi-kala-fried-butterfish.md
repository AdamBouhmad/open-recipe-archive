## Ingredients

- fish
- milk
- flour
- salt
- butter
- lemon juice
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and wipe fishes.
2. Dip in milk, roll in flour mixed with salt.
3. Brown butter in the frying-pan, fry fishes until brown on both sides.
4. Bake covered in oven until thoroughly done.
5. Arrange the fish on a serving-dish, baste with browned butter mixed with a dash of lemon juice.
6. Sprinkle with chopped parsley.
7. Garnish with parsley and sliced lemon.
