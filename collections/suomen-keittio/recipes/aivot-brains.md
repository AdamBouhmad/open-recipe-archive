## Ingredients

- brains
- cold water
- salt
- pepper
- onion

## Directions

1. Soak in cold water.
2. Put on fire in boiling water seasoned with salt, pepper and onion.
3. Cook 10 minutes.
4. Put in cold water to set.
5. Remove fibres and skin.
6. Prepare as sweetbreads and serve with any kind of sauce given for sweetbreads.
