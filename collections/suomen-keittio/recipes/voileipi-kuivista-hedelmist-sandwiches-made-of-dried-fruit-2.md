## Ingredients

- dates
- prunes
- figs
- raisins
- lemon juice
- nuts
- brown bread

## Directions

1. Take equal amounts of dates, prunes, figs and raisins.
2. Remove the stones and chop the fruit.
3. Dilute with lemon juice.
4. Chop nuts and mix in if desired.
5. Serve with brown bread.
6. Alternatively, the chopped fruit may be cooked in a little water with sugar to make marmalade, using about 1 pound of fruit to 1 cupful of water and 1 cupful of sugar, cooking gently.
