## Ingredients

- small fishmolds
- sardines
- aspic
- eggs or mayonnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Garnish small fishmolds, place the sardines in and fill with aspic.
2. Turn on toast.
3. Garnish with egg or mayonnaise sauce.
