## Ingredients

- gooseberries
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take half ripe gooseberries, wash, pick.
2. Place over the fire in cold water.
3. When coming to boil, strain.
4. Let cool.
5. Put into boiling syrup.
6. Heat slowly.
7. The gooseberries do not crush and are easy to keep.
