## Ingredients

- 3 large cucumbers
- 1 1/2 quarts of white stock
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 red onion or 1 slice of onion
- 2 yolks of eggs
- 1/2 cupful of cream
- salt, pepper and a little mace

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, cut into slices and remove the seeds.
2. Cook the onion with the slices of cucumbers in butter, add flour and white stock into it.
3. Let boil for a few minutes; pass through a strainer and season.
4. Whip the yolks and cream, pour the soup slowly into it beating well.
5. Add a spoonful of white wine to flavor the soup.
