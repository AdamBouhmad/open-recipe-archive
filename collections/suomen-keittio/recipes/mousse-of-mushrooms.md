## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- 1 cupful of pressed mushrooms
- 1½ cupfuls of chicken mousse
- a little sherry
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel the mushrooms, put on fire in boiling water, let cook 10 minutes
2. Press through a strainer
3. Mix with 1 cupful of pressed mushrooms with 1½ cupfuls of chicken mousse, a little sherry, salt and pepper
4. Butter small timbale moulds, garnish and fill with the mixture of mushrooms
5. Bake in oven in a pan of water, covered with a buttered paper, about 15 minutes or until it is firm
6. Serve with mushroom sauce
