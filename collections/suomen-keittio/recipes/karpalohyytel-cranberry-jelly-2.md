## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of cranberries
- ½ cupful of water
- 1 cupful of sugar

## Directions

1. Clean 2 cupfuls of cranberries, put on the fire, mixed with ½ cupful of water.
2. Let boil 20 minutes.
3. Force through a colander, add 1 cupful of sugar and cook a few minutes.
4. Pour into a mold to get firm.
