## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 3/4 teaspoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat whites of eggs to a froth.
2. Whip yolks with sugar until light.
3. Mix yolks with vanilla and flour.
4. Lastly stir the beaten whites into it.
5. Drop with spoon on greased baking sheet and sprinkle thinly with sugar.
6. Bake in a slow oven to a light yellow.
