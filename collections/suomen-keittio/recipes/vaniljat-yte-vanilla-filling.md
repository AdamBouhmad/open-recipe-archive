## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 cups cream
- 1 tablespoon butter
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Stir egg yolks, cream, sugar and vanilla until thick in a double boiler
2. Add butter in small dots
3. Let cool
4. Instead of cream lemon juice with water may be used
5. Chocolate or cocoa may be used in this filling
