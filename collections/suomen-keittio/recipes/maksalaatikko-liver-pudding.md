## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups rice
- 1 pint water
- 1 quart milk
- 1 pound liver
- 1 or 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar or raisins
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 onion
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook a porridge of rice, water and milk.
2. Put aside to cool.
3. Mix with ground liver, beaten eggs, onion fried in butter, sugar and spices.
4. If the mixture appears stiff, add more milk.
5. Pour into a buttered mold.
6. Bake for an hour.
7. The liver may be parboiled before grinding to make the grinding easier.
8. Add raisins according to taste for flavor.
