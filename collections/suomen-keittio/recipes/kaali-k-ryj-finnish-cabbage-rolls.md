## Ingredients

- Chopped meat, bread crumbs, milk, eggs, onion, pepper, salt and butter mixed together
- 1 cupful of rice
- one cabbage
- 3-4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 4 teaspoonfuls of sugar
- 4 teaspoonfuls of salt

## Directions

1. Cook one cabbage and take off outside leaves
2. Remove tough stalk carefully
3. Boil rice until tender in a large quantity of water
4. Drain off the water
5. Mix rice with the meat and chopped and fried small inside leaves
6. Put this mixture by spoonfuls on the leaves
7. Roll together
8. Brown on both sides
9. Sprinkle with salt, sugar and pepper
10. Pour bouillon over and put in oven
11. Bake 1-1½ hours, turning often
12. Place on a serving dish
13. Prepare sauce of liquor in pan by adding flour and cream
14. Strain sauce over the rolls
