## Ingredients

- rye flour or white flour and rye flour mixed
- caraway seeds
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare same as water bread. Instead of white flour use rye flour or 1 part white flour mixed with 3 parts rye flour. Caraway seeds may be used in rye bread.
