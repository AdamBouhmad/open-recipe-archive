## Ingredients

- yolks of 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 raw yolk
- 1/2 teaspoonful butter
- salt and pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Rub the yolks through a strainer.
2. Add seasonings and the raw yolk.
3. Form into balls.
4. Dip in flour.
5. Cook in salted water or in butter.
6. Serve with soups.
