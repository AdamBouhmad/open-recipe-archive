## Ingredients

- eggs
- toast
- salt
- pepper
- red pepper
- Hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. Boil eggs 5 minutes
2. Shell and place on scooped toast
3. Beat a few whites of egg to firm froth
4. Force through a pastry bag covering the edges of toast and the eggs
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
6. Garnish with pieces of red pepper
7. Bake in oven until light brown
8. Serve with Hollandaise sauce on a platter with eggs on top
