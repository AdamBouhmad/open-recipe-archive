## Ingredients

- 2 cups whipping cream
- 1 cup flour
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

## Directions

1. Whip the cream
2. Add the beaten eggs, flour and butter
3. Fry in hot greased waffle-iron until brown on both sides
4. Serve with jam, maple syrup or honey
