## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 cups milk
- 1 pound flour
- 1 yeast cake

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs
2. Add warmed milk, yeast and flour
3. Let rise
4. Force through a pastry bag having a very narrow opening into boiling fat, forming into zig-zags 1 inch thick and 2 inches wide
5. Turn with a perforated spoon
6. Cook until light brown on both sides
7. Drain on brown paper
8. Sprinkle with powdered sugar
