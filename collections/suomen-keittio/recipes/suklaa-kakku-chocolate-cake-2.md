## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- 4 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls of finely chopped chocolate
- 2½ cupfuls of wheat flour
- ¼ cupful of almonds
- ½ teaspoonful of soda
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar together, add cream with soda dissolved in it, blanched and finely chopped almonds, chocolate, wheat flour and lastly egg whites beaten to a froth.
2. Place in a buttered mold and bake in a moderate oven.
