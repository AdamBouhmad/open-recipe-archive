## Ingredients

- eggplant
- salt
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs
- fat
- cheese

## Directions

1. Peel eggplant and cut into thin slices.
2. Place slices overlapping, sprinkle a little salt between and let stand under light weight 1/2 to 1 hour.
3. Wipe slices, dip in beaten egg and bread crumbs; fry in hot fat.
4. Cheese may be sprinkled on top if desired.
