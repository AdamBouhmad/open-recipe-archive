## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of rye flour
- 2 cupfuls of granulated corn meal
- 2 cupfuls of graham flour
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of molasses
- 3 1/2 cupfuls of milk, water or sour milk
- 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls of soda
- 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls of salt

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients, add molasses and milk.
2. Pour the mixture in a buttered mold with a closely-fitted cover, filling 3/4 of the mold.
3. Cook for 3 hours in a vessel of boiling water, taking care that the vessel is filled with water to half the height of the mold.
