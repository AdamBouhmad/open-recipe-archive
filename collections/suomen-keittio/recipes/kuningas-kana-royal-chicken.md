## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon chopped onion
- Fillets of roast or boiled chicken, diced
- 1/2 cup tomato
- 1 tablespoon chopped mushrooms
- 2 tablespoons mushroom liquor
- 1/4 cup Sherry wine or lemon juice
- Pepper and salt
- 1/2 teaspoon chopped green pepper
- Chopped truffles
- 1/3 teaspoon meat extract
- A few scallops

## Directions

1. Cook the onion in butter for a few minutes
2. Add the chicken cut in cubes, tomato, and mushrooms
3. Let boil for 10 minutes
4. Add the scallops, lemon juice, and spices
5. Bring to boiling and serve as an entree or substitute for fish
6. May be mixed with peas and asparagus tips if desired
7. Alternative: use meat stock instead of tomato, thicken with butter and flour, add 2 egg yolks diluted with cream, heat but do not boil
