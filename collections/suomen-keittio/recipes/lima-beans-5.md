## Ingredients

- beans
- salted boiling water
- butter
- salt and pepper
- hot cream or white sauce

## Directions

1. Put beans into salted boiling water and boil until tender.
2. Drain off the water.
3. Moisten them with butter, season with salt and pepper.
4. If wanted, add a little hot cream or cover with white sauce.
