## Ingredients

- rhubarb
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Wash rhubarb and cut in 1/2 inch long pieces without peeling
2. Allow 1 cup rhubarb to 3/4 cup sugar
3. Put into a bowl
4. Pour 2 cups boiling water over
5. Cover and let cool
6. Strain and serve immediately
