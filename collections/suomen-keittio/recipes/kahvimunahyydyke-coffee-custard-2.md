## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons ground coffee
- Milk
- Eggs
- Sugar
- Salt
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Prepare as given for baked custard
2. Boil 2 tablespoons of ground coffee in milk
3. Strain the milk
4. Use the coffee-infused milk to prepare custard
5. Alternatively, use 1 cup of black coffee and 1 cup of cream instead of milk
