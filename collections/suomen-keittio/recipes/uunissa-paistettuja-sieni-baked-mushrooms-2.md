## Ingredients

- cleaned mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned mushrooms in a pan stem side down.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dot with butter and add some cream.
3. Bake in a hot oven for 10 minutes.
4. Serve on toast having the sauce poured over the mushrooms on the dish.
