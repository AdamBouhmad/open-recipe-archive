## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound chicken breast
- 2 egg whites
- 1 1/2 cups cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Remove all fiber from chicken, pound well.
2. Add egg whites gradually and continue to pound.
3. Force through a sieve.
4. Add cream slowly.
5. Bake in oven in a pan of water covered with buttered paper, or use as stuffing for different kinds of vegetables.
