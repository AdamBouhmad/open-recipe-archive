## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- 1 tablespoon butter or lard
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 cake of yeast
- 6 or 6 1/2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in lukewarm water, add flour enough to make a very thin paste. Let rise.
2. Warm the rest of the water, pour into a baking pan, add salt, sugar and butter, also flour so as to make a soft paste.
3. Work in the risen sponge and the rest of the flour, kneading until the dough is smooth.
4. Cover with a towel and put into a warm place to rise.
5. When risen until light, toss the dough on the baking board, knead briskly, divide into loaves and place them into buttered pans.
6. Cover, let rise again.
7. Bake in a moderate oven for an hour. If the whole yeast cake is used, the dough will rise quicker.
