## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Ice water
- Egg yolks
- Salad oil
- Vinegar or lemon juice
- Salt
- Pepper
- Mustard
- Cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut cabbage into shreds
2. Wash and let stand in ice water
3. Drain and wrap in a cloth to draw out the moisture
4. Pour over it a sauce made of 3 yolks of eggs mixed gradually with ½ cupful of salad oil, ½ cupful of vinegar or juice of one lemon
5. Season with salt, pepper and mustard
6. Dressing for salad may be made also of cream
7. For hot slaw: Cook cabbage until tender and pour over it Bearnaise sauce or chill the cabbage and serve cold with French dressing
