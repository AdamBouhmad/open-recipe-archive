## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 6 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of cream
- yellow rind of 1 lemon
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 4 cupfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Whip eggs with sugar to a froth
2. Add spices, cream, butter and baking powder mixed with flour
3. Form into cookies to look like elk horns
4. Bake on a buttered baking sheet in a slow oven
