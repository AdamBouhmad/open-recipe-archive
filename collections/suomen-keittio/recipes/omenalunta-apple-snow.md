## Ingredients

- 1 cup thick apple sauce
- sugar
- 4 egg whites
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Have 1 cup of thick apple sauce, sweeten with sugar according to taste
2. Add 4 egg whites beaten stiff
3. Serve in sherbet glasses with whipped cream on top
