## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of veal
- 1 cupful of rich milk
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter
- veal stock

## Directions

1. Grind veal several times.
2. Mix with milk gradually, stirring thoroughly.
3. Add seasonings.
4. Form into cutlets.
5. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
6. Fry in butter until brown.
7. Pour a little veal stock over and let boil 10-15 minutes.
