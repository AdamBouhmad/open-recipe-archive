## Ingredients

- 3 bananas
- 4 egg whites
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel the bananas, cut in cubes, sprinkle with 2 tablespoons of sugar and with the juice of 1 lemon.
2. Let stand for a while.
3. Beat the egg whites to a firm froth, add slowly the rest of the sugar and the liquor of the fruit.
4. Combine with the fruit.
5. Bake in a well-buttered mold for 10-15 minutes.
6. Serve with lemon sauce.
