## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh mushrooms or 1 can
- 4 eggs
- 1 onion
- 4 tablespoons cream or milk
- Salt and pepper
- Butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Cut mushrooms fine, fry with onion.
2. Beat the yolks and whites of eggs separately.
3. Mix the yolks with the milk or cream and the mushrooms.
4. Add the whites beaten stiff.
5. Pour in a hot buttered omelet pan.
6. Bake until firm.
