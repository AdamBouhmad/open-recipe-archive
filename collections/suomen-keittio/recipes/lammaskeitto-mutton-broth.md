## Ingredients

- Remains of mutton
- 1 onion, 1 parsnip
- 2 stalks celery
- 2 sprigs parsley
- 2 egg yolks
- Salt and pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 cup cream

## Directions

1. Crush the bones, put on the fire in cold water.
2. When coming to boil, add the vegetables browned and the spices, let simmer for a few hours.
3. Thicken with butter and flour, strain and season.
4. Whip the yolks with the cream, add the soup slowly, heat.
5. Serve with rice.
6. May be made without yolks and cream also.
