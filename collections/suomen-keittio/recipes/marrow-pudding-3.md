## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of bread crumbs
- 4 eggs
- 1 pint of rich milk
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 6 ozs. of marrow
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1/4 lb. of almonds
- 1 cupful of raisins
- 1/2 pint of cream

## Directions

1. Soak bread crumbs in a mixture of egg and milk.
2. Flavor.
3. Chop marrow fine, mix with flour, add into the bread mixture, then put raisins, almonds and sugar into it.
4. Pour the mixture in a buttered mold, sprinkle with bread.
5. Bake in oven about one hour.
6. Serve with wine sauce.
