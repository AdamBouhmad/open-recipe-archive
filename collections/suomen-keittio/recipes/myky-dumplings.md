## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup milk
- 3 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix all the dry ingredients.
2. Rub the butter into it with fingers.
3. Add the milk slowly.
4. Roll into a sheet 1/2 inch thick.
5. Cut into small circles with a round cutter.
6. Place into the kettle to cook with the meat.
7. Boil slowly so as not to break the dumplings.
8. Serve immediately because the dumplings will fall quickly.
