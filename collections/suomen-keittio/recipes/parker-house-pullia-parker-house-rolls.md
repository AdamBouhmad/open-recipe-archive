## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 4 or 5 cups flour
- 1/2 yeast cake

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in 1 cup lukewarm milk, add 1 1/2 cups flour and let rise.
2. Warm the rest of the milk, add the butter, sugar, salt, the risen sponge of the yeast and flour enough to make a moderately thick dough.
3. Let rise in a warm place.
4. Knead on the baking board, and roll into 3/8 inch thick sheet.
5. Cut with biscuit cutter into round cakes.
6. Brush with melted butter one half of the surface of the cake.
7. Fold the other half on.
8. Let rise and bake in a hot oven for 15 minutes.
