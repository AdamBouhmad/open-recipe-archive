## Ingredients

- 3 cupfuls water
- 1 1/2 cupfuls berries
- 1 pound sugar to 1 1/2 pounds liquor

## Directions

1. Allow 3 cupfuls of water to 1 1/2 cupfuls of berries
2. Mash berries and put in cold water
3. Place on fire stirring all the while the mixture heats up
4. Let boil for a few minutes
5. Drain the liquor
6. Allow 1 pound of sugar to 1 1/2 pounds of liquor
7. Put in kettle, boil quickly 10-15 minutes
8. Let cool down, pour in dry bottles, cork well and keep in a cold place
