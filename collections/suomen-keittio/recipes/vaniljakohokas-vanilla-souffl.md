## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup rich milk
- 5 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- Fruit cooked with sugar

## Directions

1. Cook a paste of butter, flour and milk.
2. Beat the yolks of 5 eggs with sugar, add to the paste, flavor with vanilla.
3. Last of all add the egg whites beaten firm.
4. Butter a soufflé mold, line the bottom and the sides with fruit, fill the mold with the mixture.
5. Cover carefully with a lid or buttered paper.
6. Bake in oven in a pan of water or steam on the stove for 1/2 or 3/4 hour.
7. Unmold onto a platter; serve hot.
