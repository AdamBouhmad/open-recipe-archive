## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups orange juice
- 3/4 cup lemon juice
- Yellow rind of 2 oranges and 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Grate the yellow rind of 2 oranges and 1 lemon
2. Cook rind with sugar and water for 15 minutes
3. Let cool
4. Add orange juice and lemon juice
5. Strain and freeze
