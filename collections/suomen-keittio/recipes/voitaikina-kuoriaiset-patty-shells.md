## Ingredients

- puff paste
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Roll puff paste to a 1/2 inch thick sheet.
2. Cut in round cakes with a patty cutter.
3. Remove the center of half of the cakes with a smaller cutter.
4. Moisten the edges of the whole cakes, place the hollowed cakes on top, press together.
5. Keep in a cold place until they become hard.
6. Brush with egg (not the edges).
7. Bake in a hot oven for 20 minutes.
8. Bake the small circles cut out of the center of the cakes to be used as covers for the filled shells.
9. Remove the soft paste from the inside of the shells.
