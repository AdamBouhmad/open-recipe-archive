## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 1 scant tablespoonful of butter
- 1/2 cupful of flour
- yolks of 3 eggs
- beaten white of 1 egg
- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- 1 square of chocolate
- 2 tablespoonfuls of milk
- 1/2 teaspoonful of vanilla
- yolks of 2 eggs
- Fondant icing

## Directions

1. Put milk and butter in a saucepan; when it boils, add flour and stir well until it begins to brown on the bottom.
2. Mix with yolks when cool and lastly stir whites, beaten stiff, into it.
3. Force through a pastry bag on a baking dish.
4. Spread with egg and bake in oven.
5. Melt chocolate, add one half of the sugar and milk.
6. Beat the remaining sugar and yolks, stir on the fire until it thickens.
7. Flavor and let cool.
8. When the puffs are done and filled, cover them with Fondant icing.
