## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of perches
- 5 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls of salt
- 3 tablespoonf. of chopped parsley
- 1/2 chopped onion
- 4 cupfuls of water
- pepper

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean and wash.
2. Rub inside with salt, put the roes into the fish.
3. Lay in a buttered kettle with butter, salt, pepper, flour, onion and parsley.
4. Pour warm water on, and let simmer 20 — 30 min.
5. Place on a serving-dish, pour over it the sauce and arrange small potatoes around it.
