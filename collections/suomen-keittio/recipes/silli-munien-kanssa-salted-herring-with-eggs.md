## Ingredients

- 2 salted herrings
- 1 onion
- pepper
- 2 hard boiled eggs
- cream
- beets
- chives

## Directions

1. Soak 2 salted herrings over night
2. Clean and remove the skin and bones
3. Chop fine with a knife
4. Add 1 chopped onion, some pepper and 2 hard boiled eggs chopped
5. Dilute with cream
6. Place on a serving dish
7. Garnish with hard boiled eggs, beets and chives
