## Ingredients

- 2½ pounds fresh bass
- ½ pound butter
- 5 eggs
- 1 cup sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, cut in pieces.
2. Sprinkle with salt.
3. Place in a casserole having the melted butter in it before putting the fish in.
4. Bake covered.
5. When half done, add eggs beaten and cream.
6. Cover.
7. Continue baking.
8. Serve in the baking dish.
