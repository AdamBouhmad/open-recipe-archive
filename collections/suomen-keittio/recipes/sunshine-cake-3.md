## Ingredients

- 9 whites of eggs
- 1 cupful of powdered sugar
- 1 cupful of flour
- 6 yolks of eggs
- ½ teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Whip whites of eggs to a firm froth, continue whipping 10 minutes
2. Add slowly sugar and lemon juice
3. Mix the beaten whites of eggs with beaten yolks
4. Add gradually flour mixed with cream of tartar and sifted several times
5. Bake in a domeshaped mold
