## Ingredients

- ½ lb. of fowl and liver
- ½ lb. of veal
- a little pork or suet
- ¾ cupful of bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- ¾ cupful of cream or milk
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Soak bread crumbs in milk
2. Mix with beaten egg and seasonings
3. Grind fowl several times
4. Pound well
5. Combine little by little both mixtures
6. Work until smooth
