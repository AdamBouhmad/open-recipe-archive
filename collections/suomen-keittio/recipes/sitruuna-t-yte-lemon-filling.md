## Ingredients

- yolks of 4 eggs
- 1 cupful of sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 2 tablespoonfuls of corn starch
- a little water

## Directions

1. Rub yolks with sugar in a kettle
2. Add lemon juice, water and corn starch
3. Thicken on the fire
