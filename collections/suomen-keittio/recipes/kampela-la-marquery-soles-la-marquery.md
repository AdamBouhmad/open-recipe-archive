## Ingredients

- two soles, skinned
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- fish stock
- sherry wine
- Normandi sauce
- toast or fried tomato slices
- truffle or parsley
- crayfish tails
- oysters

## Directions

1. Raise the fillets of two skinned soles
2. Cut nicely in pieces; if too thin fold double
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and lemon juice and let stand 10 minutes
4. Place the fish pieces in a pan
5. Pour 1/2 cupful of fish stock and 1/4 cupful of sherry wine over
6. Place in oven with buttered paper on top for 10-12 minutes
7. Coat the fish pieces with Normandi sauce
8. Arrange on toast or fried tomato slices on a warm serving dish
9. Sprinkle with finely chopped truffle or parsley
10. Garnish around with crayfish tails, oysters, etc.
