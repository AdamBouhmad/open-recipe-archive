## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of veal
- 3/4 lb. of suet or butter
- 2 ozs. of bread crumbs
- 1 cupful of milk
- 2 eggs
- salt and pepper
- macaroni

## Directions

1. Grind veal.
2. Soak bread crumbs in milk, add egg, and seasonings, and mix with the meat.
3. Rub well.
4. Cook macaroni in salted water, drain off the water, add a lump of butter and grated cheese.
5. Line a buttered mold with macaroni.
6. Fill with farcie, and bake in oven one hour.
