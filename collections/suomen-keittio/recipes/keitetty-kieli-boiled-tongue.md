## Ingredients

- Tongue
- Hot salted water
- Peppercorns
- Vegetables
- Tomato or horseradish sauce
- Tartar sauce

## Directions

1. Rinse the tongue and scrape carefully.
2. Put on fire in boiling salt water with peppercorns and vegetables.
3. Boil until tender.
4. Skin immediately.
5. Serve with tomato or horseradish sauce, having the sauce on the platter and the sliced tongue on the top.
6. If served with tartar sauce have the sauce poured over the tongue.
7. Salted tongue has to be put on the fire in cold water and boiled a longer time.
