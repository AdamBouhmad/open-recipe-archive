## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of boiled lobster
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- salt and pepper
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sherry
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 cupful of cream

## Directions

1. Cut lobster into one-inch cubes.
2. Put butter with pieces of lobster into a kettle; season with salt and pepper.
3. Cover and let simmer about 5 min.
4. Add sherry, beaten yolks of 2 eggs mixed with 1 cupful of cream into it.
5. Shake the kettle on the fire until the mixture is firm.
6. Instead of raw eggs boiled yolks of eggs rubbed through a sieve may be used.
