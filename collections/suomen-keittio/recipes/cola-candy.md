## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cupfuls of cream
- 3/4 cupful of brown sugar
- 8 tablespoonfuls of brown sugar
- 7 tablespoonfuls of cocoa
- 3/4 cupful of molasses
- a little vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix all the ingredients together in a saucepan and cook until the mixture becomes firm when cooled.
2. Pour on a buttered paper and cut into squares.
