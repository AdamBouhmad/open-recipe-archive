## Ingredients

- 1 chicken, turkey or fowl
- stuffing
- cream

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from chicken, turkey or any kind of fowl.
2. Stuff the bird.
3. Sew closed.
4. Roast in the oven as directed for stuffed chicken.
5. Prepare the gravy of the drippings by adding some cream.
