## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 2 1/2 cups milk
- 3 or 4 tablespoons grated chocolate
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 3/4 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 or 3 eggs
- Salt
- 3 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Melt the chocolate in a dry pan
2. Gradually add the boiling milk, bread crumbs, butter, vanilla and salt
3. Beat the eggs with the sugar and add last to the mixture
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered mold
5. Bake in a pan of water until firm
