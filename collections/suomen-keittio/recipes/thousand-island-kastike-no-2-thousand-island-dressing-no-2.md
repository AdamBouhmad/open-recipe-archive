## Ingredients

- 1 yolk of a hard boiled egg
- 1 raw yolk
- 1 1/4 cups salad oil
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons chopped chives
- 1 pimento
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 3 tablespoons chopped olives
- 1 cup whipped cream

## Directions

1. Rub well the yolk of a hard boiled egg
2. Add 1 raw yolk and stir well
3. Pour slowly 1 1/4 cups salad oil into the yolks
4. Add 3 tablespoons lemon juice, 2 tablespoons chopped chives, 1 pimento and 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
5. Add 3 tablespoons chopped olives
6. Add 1 cup of whipped cream before serving
