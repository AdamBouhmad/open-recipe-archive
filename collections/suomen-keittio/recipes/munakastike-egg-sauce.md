## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups fish stock
- 2 or 3 hard boiled eggs
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Make a gravy of butter, flour and fish stock.
2. Add finely chopped egg and season with salt, pepper, parsley and chives.
