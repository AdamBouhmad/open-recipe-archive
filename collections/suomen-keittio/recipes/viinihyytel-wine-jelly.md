## Ingredients

- 1 cup wine (madeira, sherry, claret or port)
- 1/2 cup white wine
- 2 cups water
- 2 tablespoons or 1/2 package gelatine
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- whites and shells of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare as stated for lemon jelly, clarified or unclarified
2. If desired, less water may be used or orange juice instead of water
3. May be prepared without white wine
