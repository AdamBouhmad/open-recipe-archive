## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of boiled hominy
- 1 tablespoonful of melted butter
- salt and pepper
- 1 egg
- bread crumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. Combine 2 cupfuls of boiled hominy and 1 tablespoonful of melted butter.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Add an egg well beaten.
4. Rub until smooth.
5. Form into croquettes, dip in egg and bread crumbs and fry in lard.
