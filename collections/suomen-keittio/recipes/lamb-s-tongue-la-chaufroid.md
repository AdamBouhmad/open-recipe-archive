## Ingredients

- lamb tongues
- seasonings
- Chaufroid sauce
- white of an egg
- salad

## Directions

1. Cook the lamb tongues with seasonings until tender
2. When cool, cover with Chaufroid
3. Garnish the surface with white of an egg
4. Serve with salad
