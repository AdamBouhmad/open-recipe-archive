## Ingredients

- Clams
- Water
- Cream

## Directions

1. Wash the clams well with a brush changing water.
2. Put into a kettle with a small quantity of hot water.
3. Cook on a slow fire covered until the shells open.
4. Strain the bouillon through a cheese cloth, put aside to cool and clarify.
5. Serve in cups as it is or mixed with some scalded cream.
6. May be served mixed with clarified rich meat stock or chicken stock, allowing 2 cups of clam bouillon to 1 cup of meat stock.
