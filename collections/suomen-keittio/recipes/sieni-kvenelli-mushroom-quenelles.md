## Ingredients

- Boiled chopped mushrooms
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons milk or bouillon
- 1/2 onion
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare as stated for meat quenelles.
2. Instead of meat use boiled chopped mushrooms.
3. Use in mushroom and meat soups.
