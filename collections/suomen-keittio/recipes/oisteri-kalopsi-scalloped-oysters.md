## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 1/2 cups bread crumbs
- Equal parts oyster liquor and milk or cream
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and drain 1 pint of oysters
2. Mix 1/2 cup of butter and 1 1/2 cups of bread crumbs
3. Butter a mold and dredge with bread crumbs
4. Lay alternately oysters and bread crumbs into the mold, sprinkling with salt and pepper
5. Have a layer of bread crumbs on the top
6. Mix equal parts of oyster liquor and milk or cream to fill the mold
7. Bake in a hot oven for 20 or 30 minutes
8. Serve in the mold
