## Ingredients

- large red onions
- salted water
- 1/4 pound of beef
- 1/4 pound of veal
- 2 ozs. of suet
- 1 tablespoonful of bread crumbs
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 egg
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- mushrooms
- melted butter
- mushroom sauce
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cook large red onions 15 minutes in salted water.
2. Scoop them carefully.
3. Stuff with meat mixture made of 1/4 pound of beef, 1/4 pound of veal, 2 ozs. of suet, 1 tablespoonful of bread crumbs, 1 cupful of milk, 1 egg, salt and pepper to taste, a little onion juice and minced mushrooms.
4. Brush onions with melted butter, sprinkle with bread crumbs and bake in oven, using plenty of butter in the pan and baste often so they do not burn.
5. Serve as an entree, with mushroom sauce or tomato sauce.
