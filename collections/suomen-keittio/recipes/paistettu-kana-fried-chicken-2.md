## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter or lard
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. Joint a chicken and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
2. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
3. Fry in butter or lard until brown.
4. Place in oven with cover for 15 minutes to finish cooking.
5. Serve with a milk sauce made from either milk or cream.
6. Pour the sauce on a serving dish and arrange the chicken pieces on top.
7. May also be served with tomato sauce.
