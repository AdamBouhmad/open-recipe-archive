## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons tea
- 1 cup water
- cracked ice
- 1 slice of lemon
- granulated sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Prepare tea allowing 2 teaspoons tea to 1 cup water
2. Half fill glasses with cracked ice
3. Put a slice of lemon in each glass
4. Strain the tea and fill the glasses with it
5. Add granulated sugar according to taste
