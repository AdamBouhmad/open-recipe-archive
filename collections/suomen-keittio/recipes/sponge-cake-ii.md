## Ingredients

- yolks of 12 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice
- whites of 12 eggs
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs with sugar until light
2. Add lemon juice and stiffly beaten whites of eggs
3. Lastly stir flour slightly into it
4. Put in a buttered mold and bake about one hour
