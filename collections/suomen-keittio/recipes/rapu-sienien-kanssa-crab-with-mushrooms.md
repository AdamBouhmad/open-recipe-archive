## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of boiled chopped crab meat
- 1½ cupfuls of chopped mushrooms
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- salt, pepper and lemon juice
- 1 slice of onion
- 1 cupful of milk
- sherry
- cream

## Directions

1. Cook chopped onion with butter.
2. Add flour, mushrooms, crab meat, seasonings and milk into it.
3. Bring to the boiling point.
4. Remove from the range, flavor with a little sherry and cream.
5. Serve hot.
6. Alternatively, fill the shells with the mixture.
7. Sprinkle with bread-crumbs, dot with butter and bake in oven until brown.
