## Ingredients

- bananas
- salt
- bread crumbs
- egg
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Peel bananas and split in two, sprinkle with a little salt.
2. Dip in bread crumbs, egg and again in bread crumbs.
3. Fry in butter or in lard.
4. Serve very hot.
