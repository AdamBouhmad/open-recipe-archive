## Ingredients

- Lemon juice
- Orange juice
- Olive oil
- Sugar
- Onion juice
- Salt
- Minced parsley
- Cooked egg yolk
- Cream or sour cream
- Roqueford cheese

## Directions

1. Lemon and orange juice may be used with salad plain or mixed with olive oil and some sugar
2. Lemon juice mixed with oil may be seasoned with salt and flavored with onion juice and minced parsley
3. Rub well the cooked yolk of an egg, add gradually the juice of a lemon, season with salt, pepper and sugar
4. Last of all mix with ¾ cup of cream
5. Sour cream may be used
6. Grated cheese may be used in salad dressing, Roquefort is the best
7. Mix the grated cheese with lemon juice and seasonings
8. Last of all add cream
