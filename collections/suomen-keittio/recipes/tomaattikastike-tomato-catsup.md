## Ingredients

- 4 quarts tomatoes
- a few white onions
- 2 bay leaves
- mace, 1 teaspoon
- celery salt, 1 teaspoon
- cloves, 1 teaspoon
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 4 cups sugar
- 2 cups vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut in pieces 4 quarts tomatoes and a few white onions
2. Cook together until tender
3. Force through a sieve, return to the fire
4. Add 2 bay leaves, mace, celery salt, cloves, 1 teaspoon each tied in a bag, 2 tablespoons salt, 4 cups sugar, or according to taste
5. Simmer for 4 or 5 hours stirring now and then
6. Remove the spices, add 2 cups vinegar, bring to boil
7. Cool, put into bottles, cork and keep in a cool place
