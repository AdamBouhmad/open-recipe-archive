## Ingredients

- Fish stock
- Egg whites for clarification
- Hard boiled eggs
- Parsley
- Halibut fillets
- Carrots
- Beets
- Mayonnaise
- Whipped cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish and the aspic as directed for salmon aspic.
2. Put a layer of aspic on the bottom of the mold.
3. On top of it arrange the fish nicely with carrots and beets cut in cubes.
4. Add a layer of mayonnaise plain or mixed with whipped cream.
5. Continue filling the mold alternately with layers of aspic, fish and mayonnaise.
6. Lay aspic on the top.
7. Let stand for a few hours.
8. Serve garnished with lettuce leaves, tomato and cucumber, etc., having plenty of dressing on.
9. May be prepared without using whites of egg to make the aspic clear.
