## Ingredients

- large oysters
- salt and pepper
- cracker crumbs
- eggs
- butter or fat
- lemon butter

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe large oysters, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
2. Roll in cracker crumbs, then dip in egg and roll again in cracker crumbs.
3. Fry quickly in deep butter or fat, a few oysters at a time.
4. Serve with lemon butter.
