## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean beef
- onion juice
- salt
- pepper
- 1 egg
- flour
- butter
- bouillon
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Chop the beef very fine and remove all the fiber possible.
2. Mix with onion juice, salt, pepper and egg.
3. Form into small patties and roll in flour.
4. Fry in butter in a pan.
5. Prepare a gravy by adding flour and bouillon to the drippings in the pan.
6. Season with salt and pepper and flavor with lemon juice.
7. The patties may also be baked in the oven.
