## Ingredients

- boiled cauliflower
- butter
- milk sauce
- grated cheese
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the boiled cauliflower in pieces
2. Lay the pieces into a buttered mold
3. Cover with milk sauce
4. Sprinkle with grated cheese
5. Dot with butter
6. Add a sprinkling of bread crumbs
7. Bake until the top is brown
