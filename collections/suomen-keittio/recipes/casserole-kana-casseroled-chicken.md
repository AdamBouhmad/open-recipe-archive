## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- yellow turnips
- potatoes
- green beans
- onions
- bouillon from cooking the chicken
- Kitchen Bouquet
- 1 cup tomato

## Directions

1. Clean the chicken and cook whole until half done.
2. Cut in nice pieces and place into a casserole, arranging it in form of a whole.
3. Cut yellow turnips and potatoes into cubes.
4. Cut green beans and onions into pieces and cook until tender.
5. Mix these vegetables with sauce and fill inside and around the chicken as much as will fit.
6. Prepare sauce from the bouillon in which the chicken was cooked.
7. Color brown with Kitchen Bouquet.
8. Add 1 cup tomato and pour over the chicken.
9. Roast in the oven for 1 hour without a lid and the rest of the time with lid on until tender.
