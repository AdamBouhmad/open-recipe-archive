## Ingredients

- 1 pound tomatoes
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1 cup milk
- 4 eggs
- salt, pepper and sugar
- onion juice (if desired)

## Directions

1. Mix the butter with the flour in a saucepan.
2. Add the tomatoes peeled and ground.
3. Boil quickly and let cool.
4. Mix bread crumbs soaked in rich milk.
5. Add to the egg yolks.
6. Season and add the egg whites beaten until firm.
7. Bake in a buttered mold in a pan of water for 1 or 1.5 hours.
8. If made of canned tomatoes, use 2 cups of tomatoes.
