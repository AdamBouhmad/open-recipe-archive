## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups sour cream
- 2 cups fresh cream
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Beat 4 egg yolks and 1 whole egg with the sugar until frothy
2. Pour in the cream, add salt and flour
3. Last of all beat the egg whites until firm
4. Fry into cakes or bake in a buttered pan
