## Ingredients

- cold boiled chicken
- 1 cupful of white sauce
- chicken stock
- milk
- 1½ cupfuls of chopped chicken
- 1 egg yolk
- cream
- chopped mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut cold boiled chicken into nice pieces.
2. Mix with 1 cupful of white sauce made of chicken stock and milk.
3. Add 1½ cupfuls of chopped chicken.
4. Heat.
5. At the last add yolk of 1 egg and a little cream.
6. May be mixed also with chopped mushrooms.
