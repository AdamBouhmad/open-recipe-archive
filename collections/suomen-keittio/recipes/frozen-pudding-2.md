## Ingredients

- 1 pint of rich cream
- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- stewed fruit
- lady fingers

## Directions

1. Whip cream and sugar to a froth
2. Mix with fruit and macaroons
3. Pour into an individual mold
4. Put to freeze in cracked ice and salt for a few hours
5. Unmold and serve garnished with fruit and macaroons
