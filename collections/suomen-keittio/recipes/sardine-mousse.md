## Ingredients

- ½ cupful of sardines
- ½ cupful of bread crumbs
- 1½ teaspoonfuls of anchovy paste
- yolk of 1 egg
- whites of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Soak bread crumbs in milk.
2. Add the minced sardine, anchovy paste and egg yolk.
3. Add lastly the egg whites.
4. Decorate small fish molds with hard boiled egg yolk and parsley, fill with sardine mixture and bake in a pan of water until firm.
5. Serve on toast with anchovy sauce.
