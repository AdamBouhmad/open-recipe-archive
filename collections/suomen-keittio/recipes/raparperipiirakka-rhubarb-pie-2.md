## Ingredients

- 2 cups rhubarb cut in pieces
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- cornstarch or flour as needed

## Directions

1. Skin the rhubarb and cut in pieces inch long.
2. Mix with the sugar and let stand for 1 hour.
3. Add some cornstarch or flour if necessary.
4. Bake between two crusts.
