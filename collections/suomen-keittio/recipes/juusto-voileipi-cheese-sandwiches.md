## Ingredients

- bread slices
- grated cheese
- white pepper or cayenne pepper
- American cheese
- butter
- anchovy paste
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- Swiss cheese
- lettuce leaves
- cottage cheese
- green pepper
- nuts
- olives
- cream

## Directions

1. Fry slices of bread light brown on one side.
2. Spread the fried side with grated cheese.
3. Sprinkle with white pepper or cayenne.
4. Lay the slices in a pan and place in hot oven.
5. When the cheese starts to melt, place the sandwiches on a napkin and serve hot.
6. Alternatively, place a thin slice of American cheese between buttered slices of bread and toast in a hot oven.
7. Or mix grated American cheese with a little butter, anchovy paste, lemon juice, salt, pepper and mustard, rub well, and spread between thin slices of bread.
8. For another variation, cut American or Swiss cheese into thin slices, place between buttered slices of bread, and use lettuce leaves if desired.
9. For cottage cheese variation, mash the cottage cheese and mix with chopped green pepper, nuts or olives, dilute with cream, and season with salt and pepper.
