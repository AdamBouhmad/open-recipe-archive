## Ingredients

- uniform-sized potatoes
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- browned butter

## Directions

1. Boil uniform-sized potatoes in salted water.
2. Peel and carefully hollow out the inside.
3. Fill the potato with egg, sprinkle with salt and pepper and bake in the oven.
4. Serve with browned butter as sauce.
