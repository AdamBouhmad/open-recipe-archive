## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cups boiling water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons gelatine
- 1/2 cup cold water
- Lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Dissolve the gelatine in 1/2 cup of cold water.
2. Add 1 1/2 cups of boiling water, sugar and lemon juice.
3. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
4. Strain through a cloth.
5. Pour into a mold to set.
6. Serve with lettuce.
