## Ingredients

- lettuce leaf
- tomato slice
- fresh cucumber slices
- chopped onion
- mayonnaise
- fried smoked herring slices

## Directions

1. Put a slice of tomato on a lettuce leaf.
2. Add a few slices of cucumber and chopped onion.
3. Spread thinly with mayonnaise.
4. Add 2 slices of fried smoked herring if desired.
