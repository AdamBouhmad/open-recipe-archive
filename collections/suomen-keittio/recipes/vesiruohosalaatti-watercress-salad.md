## Ingredients

- watercress
- French dressing
- lettuce leaves
- oranges
- pears

## Directions

1. Wash and drain the watercress
2. Mix with plenty of French dressing
3. Serve on a bed of lettuce garnished with fruit such as oranges cut in pieces, pears, etc.
