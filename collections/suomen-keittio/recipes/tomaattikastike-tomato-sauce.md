## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 slice onion
- 1 stalk celery
- 1 cup bouillon
- 1 cup canned tomatoes or 5-6 fresh tomatoes
- Parsley, salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Put butter into the saucepan, cook with onion, add flour, continue to cook for a few minutes.
2. Add tomato, bouillon and seasonings, cook slowly for half hour or until the tomatoes are tender.
3. Strain if preferred.
