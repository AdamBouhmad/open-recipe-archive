## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs of fish boned and skinned
- 3 cupfuls of cream
- 5 eggs
- 1/2 lb of butter
- 1/4 lb of white bread
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Bone and skin the fish.
2. Chop several times with the meat chopper.
3. Put into a wooden bowl, rub well, add gradually the butter rubbed to a froth and the yolks one by one.
4. Soak the bread in cream, add slowly to the mixture of fish, continuing rubbing until frothy.
5. At the last mix with beaten whites of eggs and seasonings.
6. Pour into a buttered mold, cover, bake in a pan of water 1 to 1 1/4 hours.
7. Serve with mushroom-, crab-, caper- or lemon sauce.
