## Ingredients

- 2 cups dried lima beans
- water
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 4 tomatoes
- 1/4 pound butter
- 2 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Soak 2 cups of dried lima beans in water.
2. Cook and mash the beans.
3. Add 1 cup chopped walnuts, 1 cup bread crumbs, 4 tomatoes, and 1/4 pound butter.
4. Add 2 eggs.
5. Rub the mixture until smooth.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Form into a loaf.
8. Bake, basting often.
