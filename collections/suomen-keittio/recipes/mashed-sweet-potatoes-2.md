## Ingredients

- sweet potatoes
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- salt
- pepper
- hot milk

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes, mash.
2. Add 3 tablespoonfuls of butter, salt, pepper and hot milk.
3. Stir until smooth dough.
