## Ingredients

- apples
- 1 pint of apple sauce
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 3 whites of eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook apples until tender.
2. Force through a sieve and set to cool.
3. Mix apple sauce with sugar and lemon juice.
4. Add whites, beaten stiff.
5. Serve in small glasses, covered with whipped cream.
