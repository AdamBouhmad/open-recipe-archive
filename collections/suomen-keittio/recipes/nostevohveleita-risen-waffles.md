## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 yeast cake

## Directions

1. Warm the milk
2. Add the dissolved yeast, butter, salt and flour
3. Stir well
4. Let rise over night
5. In the morning add the eggs beaten until frothy
6. Fry in hot greased waffle-iron until brown on both sides
