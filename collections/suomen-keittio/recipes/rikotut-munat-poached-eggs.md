## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- water or milk
- butter
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Half fill a frying pan with boiling water and add a dash of salt
2. Break the egg carefully into a wet cup
3. Let it slip slowly from the cup into the boiling water
4. When the white is firm the egg is done
5. Remove the egg carefully with a perforated spoon
6. Alternatively, cook eggs in milk instead of water for better flavor
7. When eggs are poached, remove pan from fire and let stand covered for 5 minutes
8. Serve on toasted bread with butter or with sauces such as milk sauce or tomato sauce
