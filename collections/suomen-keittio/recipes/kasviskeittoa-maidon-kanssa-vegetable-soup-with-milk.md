## Ingredients

- 1 carrot
- 1 celery
- 1/4 cauliflower
- 3/4 pound spinach
- 1 potato
- 3/4 cup peas
- 3 quarts milk
- Chopped parsley
- Butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cut the vegetables in cubes, boil with butter in a small quantity of water.
2. Cook 2 tablespoonfuls of butter with 2 tablespoonfuls of flour in a saucepan, add the scalded milk and the vegetables.
3. Season, bring to boil.
