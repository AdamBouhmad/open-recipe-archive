## Ingredients

- 1 quart of cranberries
- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- water enough to cover the cranberries

## Directions

1. Rinse 1 quart of cranberries.
2. Put on the fire to boil with 2 cupfuls of sugar and water enough to cover them.
3. Cook slowly until a smooth pulp.
4. Pour in a mold to get firm, strain or leave unstrained.
