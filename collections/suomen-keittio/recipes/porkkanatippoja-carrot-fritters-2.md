## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiled carrots
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 yolks of egg beaten
- 2 whites of egg beaten firm
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Rub boiled carrots through a sieve
2. Add flour
3. Add beaten egg yolks and last of all beaten egg whites
4. Drop by spoonful into a well buttered frying pan
5. Fry on both sides until brown
6. Serve immediately
