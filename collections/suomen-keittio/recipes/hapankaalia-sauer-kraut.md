## Ingredients

- Medium-sized heads of white cabbage
- Salt
- Small apples
- Sprigs of dill

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage in shreds and pound well.
2. Lay into a wooden vessel having salt, small apples and sprigs of dill between the layers.
3. Press tightly with a cover fitting inside the vessel.
4. Place a heavy weight on the top.
5. Keep in a warm room for a week, carefully skimming off the mildew.
6. When ready, keep in a cool place.
7. Allow 1 pound of salt to 50 heads of cabbage.
