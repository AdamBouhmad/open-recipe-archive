## Ingredients

- boiled mushrooms
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- butter
- lemon juice
- parsley
- paprika
- caper
- grated cheese
- buttered toast
- caviare
- pickled cucumbers
- lobster tails

## Directions

1. Mix boiled chopped mushrooms with yolks of hard-boiled eggs, creamed butter, a dash of lemon juice, chopped parsley, paprika, caper and grated cheese.
2. Force through a sieve.
3. Spread on buttered toast.
4. Decorate with caviare, pickled cucumbers and lobster tails.
