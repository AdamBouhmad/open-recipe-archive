## Ingredients

- 1 quart fresh mushrooms
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- 1½ cups cream or milk
- 2 egg yolks
- ½ cup cream

## Directions

1. Clean 1 quart of fresh mushrooms and cut in pieces.
2. Place on the fire to simmer with butter, salt and pepper for 15-20 minutes.
3. Add 1½ cups of cream or milk and bring to boiling.
4. Mix 2 yolks of egg with ½ cup of cream.
5. Add the egg yolk mixture to the sauce and heat.
