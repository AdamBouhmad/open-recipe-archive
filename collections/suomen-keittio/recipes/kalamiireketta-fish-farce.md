## Ingredients

- 1½ pounds fish, pike or bass
- 3 cups cream
- 5 eggs
- ½ pound butter
- ¾ pound white bread
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Scrape the fish off the skin, remove bones, force through a meat chopper several times
2. Place the fish into a wooden bowl or mortar and pound well, adding gradually foamed whipped butter and egg yolks one at a time
3. Soak bread in cream and add gradually to the fish mixture, continue pounding until the mixture looks fluffy
4. Last of all add foamed whipped egg whites and seasonings
5. Place the mixture in a buttered mold, cover with lid in a pan of water in the oven and bake 1-1½ hours
6. Serve with mushroom, crayfish, caper or lemon sauce
