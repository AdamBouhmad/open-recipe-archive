## Ingredients

- 1 turkey
- 3/4 lb. of pork
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the turkey, rub with salt inside and out.
2. Line with thin slices of pork and place in a buttered pan.
3. Roast in a hot oven.
4. When browned, sprinkle with salt, and pour hot water in the pan.
5. Baste often.
6. If the oven is too hot, cover the turkey with buttered paper.
7. Allow 2-3 hours for roasting.
8. Thicken the gravy with a little flour and cream.
9. Skim off the fat carefully.
