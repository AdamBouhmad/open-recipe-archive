## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of onions
- 3 quarts of vegetable stock or water
- 2 stalks or 1 root of celery
- 1 small parsnip
- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 cupfuls of cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cut vegetables and onions into pieces and brown in butter
2. Add flour and scalded vegetable stock and let cook for an hour
3. Force through a sieve and season with salt and pepper
4. Whip egg yolks, add cream and pour the soup gradually into it
5. Stir on the fire until hot
6. Thicken with fried bread dice boiled in soup
