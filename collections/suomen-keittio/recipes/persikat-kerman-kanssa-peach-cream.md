## Ingredients

- 1 quart peaches
- 1/2 package gelatine
- Sugar
- Lemon juice
- 2 cups whipped cream

## Directions

1. Peel 1 quart of peaches, remove the stones, rub through a sieve.
2. Add 1/2 package of gelatine, dissolve by heating.
3. Mix with sugar and lemon juice, according to taste.
4. When cool, add 2 cups of whipped cream.
5. Put into a mold to set.
