## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup cream
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 cups potato flour
- 4 cups white flour
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Beat to a froth eggs with sugar
2. Add cream, potato flour, butter rubbed until creamy and flour mixed with soda
3. Mix all well together
4. Roll out thin
5. Prick with a fork and cut small round cookies
6. Bake on a buttered baking sheet in the oven until light brown
