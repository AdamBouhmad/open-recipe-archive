## Ingredients

- 1¼ pounds of veal (without bones)
- 2 ounces of butter
- 2 eggs
- 1⅜ cupfuls of cream
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- salt and pepper
- macaroni

## Directions

1. Wipe meat with cloth wrung out from hot water
2. Remove fibres and bits of skin, cut into dice and grind fine
3. Mix gradually with butter and yolks of eggs
4. Add cream, flour, salt and pepper
5. Butter a mold, line the bottom and the sides with cooked macaroni cut in pieces ¼ inch long
6. Beat the whites of eggs to a firm froth, mix with the meat
7. Turn the mixture into the mold
8. Cover with buttered paper, cook in a pan of water on the range or in oven 1—1½ hours
