## Ingredients

- fish
- mashed potato
- melted butter
- parsley
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish as for broiling. Broil on a plank, skin side down without turning.
2. When brown place the fish in oven to finish the cooking.
3. Lay mashed potato around the fish on the plank, brown.
4. Pour melted butter over, sprinkle with parsley.
5. Place the plank on a platter, serve garnished with parsley.
