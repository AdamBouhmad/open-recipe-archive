## Ingredients

- Lamb tongue
- Seasonings
- Hard boiled egg white for garnish
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- Pepper
- 1/4 cup butter
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- Cream
- Soya (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue with seasonings until tender
2. When cool, cover with sauce and garnish with hard boiled egg white
3. For the sauce: Mix 4 beaten egg yolks with 1/2 teaspoon mustard, 1/4 teaspoon salt and pepper
4. Add 1/4 cup butter and 2 tablespoons lemon juice
5. Stir on the fire until it becomes thick
6. Dilute with cream
7. Add some soya if brown sauce is preferred
