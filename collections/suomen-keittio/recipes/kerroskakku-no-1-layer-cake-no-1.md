## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1½ cups sugar
- 2½ cups flour
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Rub the butter with the sugar until creamy.
2. Add the yolks one by one, beating the mixture well.
3. Mix in milk, vanilla and flour sifted together with baking powder.
4. Last of all fold in the egg whites beaten until firm.
5. Bake in three layer cake tins.
6. Have filling between layers.
7. Coat with icing.
