## Ingredients

- 1 rabbit
- ½ lemon
- ¼ pound bacon
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 cups bouillon or water

## Directions

1. Clean the rabbit, rinse and soak in water for 2 hours to remove the fiber more easily.
2. Rub with lemon and line with thin slices of bacon.
3. Melt the butter in a pan and place the rabbit in it.
4. Sprinkle with salt, baste often and add bouillon when necessary.
5. Roast for 1 to 1½ hours.
6. Prepare a gravy of the drippings by adding some flour, bouillon and cream.
7. Alternatively, the rabbit may be cut in three parts: hind-legs and fore-legs trimmed and tied together, and the middle part cut in two and tied around for easier roasting.
