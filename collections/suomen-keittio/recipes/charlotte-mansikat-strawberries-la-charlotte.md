## Ingredients

- Large strawberries of equal size
- Red colored liquid lemon jelly
- 1/4 cup thin cream
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 1/2 cups whipping cream
- 1/4 package gelatine
- Macaroons
- Strawberries cut in pieces

## Directions

1. Have large strawberries of equal size. Split lengthwise, dip in red colored liquid lemon jelly.
2. Line the bottom and the sides of a mold with the strawberries laying them flat side out.
3. Dissolve the gelatine with the sugar in the thin cream, add vanilla, let cool.
4. Mix with the whipped cream, macaroons and strawberries.
5. Pour into a wet mold, let set.
6. Instead of macaroons boiled rice may be used.
7. Serve unmolded.
