## Ingredients

- 3 Jerusalem artichokes
- 6 potatoes
- 2 parsnips
- 1 stalk of celery
- 1 onion
- 1/4 pound butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 3/4 cup milk or cream
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 3 eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Tomato sauce or Hollandaise sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Cook the artichokes, potatoes, parsnips, celery, and onion
2. Force through a meat grinder
3. Make a sauce of 1/4 pound butter, 3 tablespoons flour and 3/4 cup milk or cream
4. Add 1 cup bread crumbs, 3 eggs, salt and pepper
5. Combine the mixtures
6. Stir until smooth
7. If too thick, dilute with milk
8. Bake in a buttered mold in a pan of water covered with buttered paper
9. Serve with tomato or Hollandaise sauce
