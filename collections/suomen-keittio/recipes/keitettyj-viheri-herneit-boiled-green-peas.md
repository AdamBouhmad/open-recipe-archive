## Ingredients

- green peas
- salt
- 1/4 teaspoon soda
- pepper
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put the peas on the fire in boiling water with a dash of salt and 1/4 teaspoon soda.
2. Cook until tender but not mushy.
3. Drain, season with salt and pepper.
4. Add butter and some sugar if desired.
