## Ingredients

- summer squash
- flour
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel the squash and cut in pieces
2. Bake in oven covered for 1/2 hour
3. Dip in egg and bread crumbs or turn in flour
4. Fry in butter
