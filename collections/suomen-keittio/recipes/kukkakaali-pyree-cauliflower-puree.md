## Ingredients

- 2 cauliflowers
- 3 quarts of vegetable stock
- 1 leek
- 1 parsnip
- 1 carrot
- salt and pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cupful of cream

## Directions

1. Cut the thick stalks of cauliflower into thin slices
2. Peel the remaining vegetables and cut in pieces and cook in a small quantity of water until soft
3. Press through a strainer and mix with the stock
4. Use the nicest pieces of cauliflower in the soup and let cook until tender
5. Thicken with butter and flour and last of all add the yolks of eggs and cream
