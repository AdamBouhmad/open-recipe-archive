## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- 3 cups water
- 2 tablespoons gelatine
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/2 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve the gelatine in 1/2 cup of cold water, add 2 cups of boiling water.
2. Mix with sugar, orange and lemon juice, stir until the sugar is dissolved.
3. Strain through a cloth and pour into a mold to set.
