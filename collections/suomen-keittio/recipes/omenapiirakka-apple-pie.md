## Ingredients

- pastry for pie crust
- apples, thinly sliced
- 1/2 to 2/3 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- salt
- cinnamon
- butter
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Cover a pie plate with pastry.
2. Fill with thin slices of apples.
3. Mix 1/2 to 2/3 cup of sugar, 1 teaspoon lemon juice, some salt and cinnamon, add to the apples.
4. Dot with butter.
5. Moisten the edges of the pastry with water, place the upper crust on, press the edges together and fold the upper edge under the lower.
6. Brush with egg, bake in a moderate oven for 1 hour.
