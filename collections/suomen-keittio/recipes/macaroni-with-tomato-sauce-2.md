## Ingredients

- boiled macaroni
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- cheese crumbs
- strained tomato sauce
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Put a layer of boiled macaroni in a buttered mold.
2. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, onion juice and cheese crumbs.
3. Pour strained tomato sauce over.
4. Lay macaroni on tomato and fill the dish in this way having tomato on top.
5. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and dot with butter.
6. Bake in oven until the surface is browned.
