## Ingredients

- 2 quarts bone stock or bouillon
- 3 tablespoons tapioca
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup cream
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the tapioca, cook in bouillon until tender.
2. Beat the yolks of eggs with the cream, add gradually the soup to it, heat but not to boiling.
3. Serve with tiny potatoes or cheese balls.
