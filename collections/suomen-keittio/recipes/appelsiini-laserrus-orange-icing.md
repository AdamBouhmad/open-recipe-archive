## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of granulated sugar
- 1/2 cupful of water
- one or two whites of eggs
- juice of an orange
- grated rind of an orange
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook sugar with water until the syrup threads when dropped from spoon
2. Beat one or two whites of eggs to a froth
3. Pour the syrup slowly into it stirring well
4. Add juice and grated rind of an orange
5. Add 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice
6. Stir until the sugar has thickened and holds its shape
