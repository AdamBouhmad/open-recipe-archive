## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 ounces chocolate
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1 cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Dissolve chocolate in double boiler
2. Add 3 tablespoons sugar and pour gradually boiling water over, stirring well
3. Add 2 cups sugar, let boil for a while, stirring well
4. Flavor with vanilla
5. Serve cold
