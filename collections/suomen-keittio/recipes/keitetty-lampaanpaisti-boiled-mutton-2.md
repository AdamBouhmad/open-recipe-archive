## Ingredients

- Mutton
- Vegetables
- Salt
- Pepper
- Caper sauce

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat and place in a kettle.
2. Cover with boiling water.
3. Bring quickly to boiling.
4. Cook with vegetables, salt and pepper.
5. Let simmer slowly covered until tender.
6. Serve with caper sauce, placing the vegetables around the meat on the platter.
