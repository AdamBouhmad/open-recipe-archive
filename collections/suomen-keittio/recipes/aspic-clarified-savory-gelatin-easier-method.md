## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of minced carrot and celery
- 2 slices of onion
- a little parsley
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 cloves
- 2 tablespoonfuls of tomato
- 2 teaspoonfuls of beef extract
- 2½ cupfuls of water
- 1 box or 20 leaves of gelatine
- salt
- pepper
- juice of 2 lemons
- white of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan 2 tablespoonfuls of minced carrot and celery, 2 slices of onion, a little parsley, 1 bay leaf, 2 cloves, 2 tablespoonfuls of tomato, 2 teaspoonfuls of beef extract and 2½ cupfuls of water.
2. Cover and let boil ½ hour.
3. Add 1 box or 20 leaves of gelatine; stir until the gelatine is dissolved.
4. Season with salt, pepper and juice of 2 lemons.
5. Clarify with white of an egg.
