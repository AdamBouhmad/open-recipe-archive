## Ingredients

- 1 pauna vasikanlihaa (1 lb. of veal)
- 2 unssia voita (2 ozs. of butter)
- 2 munaa (2 eggs)
- 1½ kupillista kermaa (1½ cupfuls of cream)
- 3 ruokalusikallista leipäjauhoa (3 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs)
- suolaa ja pippuria (salt and pepper)

## Directions

1. Remove the fibres and force the meat through a meat chopper three times.
2. Then pound the meat well.
3. Add gradually the butter into the meat and rub the mixture until smooth.
4. Soak the bread crumbs in milk and stir into the meat.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Finally add the whites, beaten stiff and dry.
7. Butter the mold, garnish with macaroni and fill with the mixture.
8. Bake in oven in a pan of water, covered with buttered paper, about 1 hour.
