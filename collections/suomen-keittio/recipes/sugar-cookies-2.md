## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of butter
- 1/2 cupfuls of lard
- 1 egg
- 5 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 3 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 1 cupful of milk
- lemon or vanilla to flavor

## Directions

1. Rub sugar, butter and lard to a froth
2. Mix with egg, milk and dry ingredients
3. Roll into a thin sheet
4. Cut with a cutter into cakes
5. Bake to a light brown
