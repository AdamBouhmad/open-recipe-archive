## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 cupful of cream
- 6 ozs. of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder
- flour enough to make a dough easy to handle

## Directions

1. Whip eggs and sugar to a froth
2. Add cream and creamed butter
3. Mix baking powder with flour and add to mixture
4. Add flour enough to make the dough thick enough to handle
5. Form into small rings
6. Dip them in sugar mixed with cinnamon
7. Bake until lightly browned
