## Ingredients

- 2 slices of onion, chopped
- butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 cupfuls of rich milk
- salt and pepper
- ½ pound of chopped crab meat
- toast
- bread crumbs
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Fry two chopped slices of onion in butter.
2. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of flour and 2 cupfuls of rich milk into it.
3. Stir thoroughly; season with salt and pepper.
4. Mix with ½ pound of chopped crab meat; put aside to get cool.
5. Heap the mixture on toast.
6. Spread thinly with a mixture of bread crumbs, butter and grated cheese mixed together in equal parts.
7. Bake until brown.
8. Serve hot.
