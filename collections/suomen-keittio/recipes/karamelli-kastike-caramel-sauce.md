## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1 cupful of water
- a little wine or vanilla

## Directions

1. Put 1 cupful of sugar in a saucepan
2. Stir until it is browned
3. Add 1 cupful of water
4. Let boil 5 minutes
5. When cool, add a little wine or vanilla
