## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of thin cream
- 2 ounces of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 2 cupfuls of rich cream
- 2 ounces of macaroons

## Directions

1. Boil up the thin cream.
2. Beat yolks with sugar and pour the boiling cream gradually into it.
3. Stir on the fire until it thickens.
4. Remove the saucepan from the fire and let cool.
5. Add the rich cream, beaten to a froth, into the mixture.
6. Rinse a mold with cold water.
7. Put in it layers of macaroons and cream alternately.
8. Cover closely.
9. Place the mold in a pail of cracked ice and salt.
10. Let stand 6 to 7 hours.
11. Turn on a serving dish and garnish with macaroons.
