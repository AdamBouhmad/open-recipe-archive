## Ingredients

- washed unpared crab apples
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut washed unpared and uncored apples or crab apples in halves
2. Cook until soft, allowing 1 part of water to 2 parts of apples (10 quarts apples to 5 quarts water)
3. Strain through a cheese cloth without pressing the cloth
4. Weigh the juice and have an equal amount of sugar
5. Place the juice on the fire, when coming to boil, add the sugar
6. Simmer stirring often, until it becomes firm when dropped from a spoon on a cold plate
7. Skim carefully and pour into hot jelly glasses
8. When cooled down, cover the top with melted wax
