## Ingredients

- lentils
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- cream
- fried chopped onion

## Directions

1. Cook lentils until soft.
2. Force through a sieve, season with salt, pepper and butter.
3. If the mass is too thick add a little cream.
4. Heap upon a platter.
5. Make a little cavity on top and fill it with 3 tablespoonfuls of fried chopped onion.
