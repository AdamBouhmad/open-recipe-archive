## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of boiled lobster
- 2 cupfuls of fried mushrooms
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 slice of onion
- 1 cupful of milk or cream
- sherry or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook finely chopped onion in butter.
2. Add flour, mushrooms, lobster and seasonings.
3. Add scalded milk or cream and heat the mixture.
4. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of sherry or lemon juice.
5. Serve having toast laid around it, or put the mixture in lobster shells, sprinkle with bread crumbs, dot with butter and bake in oven until brown.
