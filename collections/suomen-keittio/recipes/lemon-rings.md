## Ingredients

- 4 yolks of eggs
- 3 ozs. of sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cream
- grated lemon rind
- enough flour to make a paste

## Directions

1. Work yolks of eggs with sugar to a froth
2. Mix with cream, butter, lemon rind and flour to make a paste thick enough to handle
3. Form into small rings
4. Bake on greased baking sheet until lightly browned
