## Ingredients

- rich milk
- ginger
- sugar

## Directions

1. Have rich milk and let stand covered in a warm room until sour (solid but not watery)
2. Cool
3. Serve, which may be whipped and served in glasses
4. Mix with sugar and ginger according to taste
