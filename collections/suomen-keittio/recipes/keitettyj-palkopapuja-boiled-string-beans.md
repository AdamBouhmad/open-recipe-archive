## Ingredients

- string beans
- salt
- baking soda
- pepper
- melted butter
- parsley (optional)
- apples (optional)
- flour (optional)
- sugar (optional)
- lemon juice or vinegar (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove all the strings from the beans
2. Cut the beans in pieces
3. Put on the fire in boiling salt water with baking soda
4. Cook until tender
5. Drain
6. Season with salt and pepper and add melted butter
7. A tiny bunch of parsley added to the beans while boiling will give good flavor
8. Alternatively, cook the beans with two pared and sliced apples, make a sauce of butter flour and liquor of the beans, season with salt, sugar, lemon juice or vinegar and pour over the beans
9. Or serve without sauce seasoned with salt and sugar and mixed with melted butter
