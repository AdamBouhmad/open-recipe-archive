## Ingredients

- 3 large cucumbers
- 3 quarts of vegetable stock or water
- 2 leeks
- 1 celery
- 1 sprig of parsley
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cupful of cream
- salt and pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, split, remove the seeds and cut in pieces
2. Clean the remaining vegetables and brown with onion in butter, adding a little sugar and salt to it
3. Pour the boiling stock on the vegetables and cook until stew
4. Press through a strainer, thicken with butter and flour
5. Beat the eggs, add the cream and soup into it slowly, stirring on the fire until hot
6. Cucumber cut in dice, peas, spinach, salad and carrots may be served in soup
