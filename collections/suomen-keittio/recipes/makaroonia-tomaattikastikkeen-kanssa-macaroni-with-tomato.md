## Ingredients

- boiled macaroni
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- grated cheese
- strained tomato sauce
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Put a layer of boiled macaroni into a buttered mold
2. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, onion juice and cheese
3. Cover with strained tomato sauce
4. Put another layer of macaroni and continue until the mold is full
5. The top layer should be tomato
6. Sprinkle bread crumbs and butter pieces on top
7. Bake in oven until the top is brown
