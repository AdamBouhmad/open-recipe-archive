## Ingredients

- 2 dozen ripe tomatoes
- 4 medium-sized onions
- 2 green peppers
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons salt
- cinnamon, 2 teaspoons
- cloves, 2 teaspoons
- nutmeg, 2 teaspoons
- allspice, 2 teaspoons
- 1 quart vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and cut in pieces 2 dozen ripe tomatoes and 4 medium-sized onions
2. Remove the seeds from 2 green peppers and chop the peppers
3. Put the vegetables into a kettle, add 1 cup sugar, 2 tablespoons salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg and allspice, 2 teaspoons each, 1 quart vinegar
4. Simmer for 3 hours or until moderately thick
