## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 cupfuls of fish stock
- 1 can (1/2 quart) or 1 pound of fresh lobster
- 1-2 yolks of eggs
- salt and pepper
- a little lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and fish stock.
2. Add the lobster cut in pieces, then salt and pepper.
3. Lastly add beaten yolks and a little lemon juice.
