## Ingredients

- 1½ quarts bouillon
- 1 quart Jerusalem artichokes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- salt and pepper
- 1 cucumber
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup cream
- vinegar and water

## Directions

1. Scrape the artichokes, rinse and put for a while into water mixed with vinegar so as to prevent them from turning dark.
2. Cook in meat stock until tender.
3. Pass through a sieve.
4. Thicken with butter and flour and cook for a few minutes longer.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Peel the cucumber, remove the seeds, and cut in cubes.
7. Cook with a little butter until tender.
8. Add the cucumbers to the soup.
9. Last of all mix with the eggs and cream if desired.
