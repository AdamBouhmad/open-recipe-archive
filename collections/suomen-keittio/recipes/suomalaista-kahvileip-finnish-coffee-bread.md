## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 pound butter
- 1 yeast cake
- 1/2 teaspoon cardamom
- 2 pounds flour

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm milk
2. Mix in some flour and let rise in a warm place
3. Warm the milk and heat or whip the butter
4. Beat eggs and sugar
5. Add the milk, cardamom and enough flour to make a thick batter
6. Add the risen yeast
7. Mix in the butter and remaining flour
8. Knead the dough well until it becomes soft and elastic
9. Cover and let rise in a warm place
10. Knead the dough on a table and form into long loaves or roll thin and spread with melted butter
