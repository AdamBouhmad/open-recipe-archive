## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. of apples
- 2 1/2 lbs. of sugar
- 3 cupfuls of water

## Directions

1. Wash apples, cut in pieces and cook in 3 cupfuls of water until it becomes a sauce.
2. Force through a sieve and return to the fire.
3. Simmer with sugar stirring all the time until of firm consistency.
4. Spread on an oiled or buttered platter.
5. Cut in pieces, dip in granulated sugar and dry.
