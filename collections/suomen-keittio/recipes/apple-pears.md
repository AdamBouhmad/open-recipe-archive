## Ingredients

- apple sauce
- brandy
- pink color
- apples
- sugar
- 1 cupful of apple liquor
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sherry
- 2 tablespoonfuls of brandy
- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of corn starch
- pink color
- cake or puff paste shell
- cream

## Directions

1. Mix apple sauce with a little brandy and pink color; beat well
2. Pare apples, split in two and cook in sugar
3. When cold, prepare sauce of apple liquor, sherry, brandy, sugar, corn starch and a little pink color
4. Place the apples on cake or shell of puff paste
5. Put the apple sauce in the center of serving dish
6. Lay the apple cakes around
7. Garnish with cream
