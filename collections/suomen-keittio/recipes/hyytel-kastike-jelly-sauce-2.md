## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sauce
- 3 tablespoons currant jelly
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare a cupful of brown sauce
2. Add 3 tablespoons of currant jelly and some lemon juice
3. Serve with game or mutton
