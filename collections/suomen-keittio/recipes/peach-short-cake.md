## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1 lb. of flour
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Wash butter and work to a cream with sugar
2. Mix with flour and lastly with beaten eggs
3. Bake in two molds to light brown
4. Lay fruit between and on the cakes
5. Serve with whipped cream
