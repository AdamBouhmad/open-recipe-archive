## Ingredients

- 1 pound mushrooms
- 4 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 eggs
- 4 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 4 tablespoons milk or vegetable stock
- white pepper and salt to taste
- onion juice

## Directions

1. Grind the mushrooms
2. Add bread crumbs, milk and eggs mixed together
3. Season and stir until smooth
4. Form into balls
5. Boil slowly in salted water for 15 minutes
6. Dip in egg and turn in bread crumbs
7. Fry quickly in butter
8. Serve with brown sauce
