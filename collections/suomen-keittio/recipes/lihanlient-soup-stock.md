## Ingredients

- 5 pounds lean meat with bones
- 6 quarts cold water
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 1 cup each: onions, carrots and celery
- 10 peppercorns
- 1/2 cup tomato, fresh or canned
- 2 sprigs parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the fat and cut the meat in pieces.
2. Put the meat and the bones with the salt and cold water into a soup kettle; cover, let stand for about half an hour.
3. Place on a slow fire.
4. When coming to boil, skim.
5. Continue to cook for 3 hours or more.
6. Add the vegetables and spices to the soup.
7. Continue to boil for 1 hour or longer, adding boiling water when necessary.
8. Strain, let cool and skim the fat.
9. If the vegetables are browned before added to the soup, it will improve the color of the soup.
10. If the meat is to be eaten, remove it from the soup immediately when it is done and serve with a sauce such as horseradish, sweet sour, mustard, etc.
