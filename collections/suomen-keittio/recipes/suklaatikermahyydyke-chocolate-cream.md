## Ingredients

- 2 cups whipping cream
- 1 ounce chocolate
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1/4 package gelatine
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Dissolve the chocolate in a saucepan
2. Add sugar, water, gelatine and vanilla
3. When the gelatine is all dissolved, let cool
4. Whip the cream
5. Pass through a sieve the cold chocolate mixture into the cream
6. Put into a wet mold to set
