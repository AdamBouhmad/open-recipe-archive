## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls of salad oil
- 1 tablespoonful of vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 3/4 teaspoonful of pepper

## Directions

1. Stir salt and pepper in oil, pour oil gradually into vinegar.
