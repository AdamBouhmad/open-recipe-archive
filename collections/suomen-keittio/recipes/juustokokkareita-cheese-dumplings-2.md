## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 3 tablespoonfuls of cheese crumbs
- yolks of 2 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook a sauce of butter, flour and milk, stirring well to make a smooth paste
2. Let boil a few minutes
3. Remove the saucepan from the fire
4. Stir the seasonings, cheese and egg yolks into it
5. Drop the mixture with a teaspoon into boiling beef stock
6. Cook slowly about 5 minutes
