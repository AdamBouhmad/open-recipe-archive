## Ingredients

- Parsnips
- Salt
- Pepper
- Melted butter
- Flour
- Butter for frying

## Directions

1. Rinse parsnips carefully, boil in salted water about 1 hour or until tender.
2. Drain and peel.
3. Cut lengthwise in pieces 3/4 inches thick, sprinkle with salt and pepper, dip in melted butter, roll in flour and fry in butter until brown on both sides.
