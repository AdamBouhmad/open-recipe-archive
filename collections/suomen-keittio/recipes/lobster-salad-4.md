## Ingredients

- Boiled lobster
- Mayonnaise sauce
- Lettuce leaves
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Celery (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut boiled lobster in small pieces
2. Set in cold place until ready to serve
3. Mix with a small quantity of mayonnaise sauce
4. Garnish around with lettuce leaves, lobster claws and hard-boiled eggs
5. The lobster may be mixed also with celery cut in small dice
