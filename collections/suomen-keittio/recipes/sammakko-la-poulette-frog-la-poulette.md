## Ingredients

- frogs' legs
- butter
- flour
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- parsley
- egg yolks
- cream
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the frogs' legs.
2. Put into boiling water and place on the fire.
3. Cook for a few minutes, wipe dry and immerse in hot butter.
4. Cook covered until tender. (Milk may be used also in cooking.)
5. Make the sauce of butter, flour and milk, seasoned with salt, pepper, lemon juice and chopped parsley.
6. Add last into it beaten yolks of two eggs, mixed with a little cream.
7. Stir over the fire until thick.
8. Arrange the legs on a serving platter, garnish with fried mushrooms and pour the sauce over.
