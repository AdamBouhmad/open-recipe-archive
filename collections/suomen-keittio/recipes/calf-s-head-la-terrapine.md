## Ingredients

- Cooked calf's head
- Butter
- Flour
- White stock
- Salt and pepper
- Cream
- Eggs
- Madeira

## Directions

1. Cut cooked calf's head into small cubes
2. Mix 2 cupfuls of meat with 1 cupful of sauce made of 2 tablespoonfuls of butter, 2 tablespoonfuls of flour, 1 cupful of white stock
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Add 2 cupfuls of cream
5. Add yolks of 2 eggs whipped
6. Flavor with 2 tablespoonfuls of Madeira
