## Ingredients

- 1½ cups rice groats
- 1 pint water
- 1 quart milk
- 1 pound liver
- 1 egg
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1½ tablespoonfuls sugar
- 1 tablespoonful salt
- ½ onion
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook rice groats, water and milk into porridge and pour to cool.
2. Mix ground liver with the porridge.
3. Add beaten egg, finely chopped onion fried in butter, sugar, salt and pepper.
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered mold and bake 1 hour.
5. A few raisins added to the mixture gives good flavor.
6. Serve with melted butter sauce.
