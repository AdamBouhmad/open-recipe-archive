## Ingredients

- 5 cold boiled sweet potatoes
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut sweet potatoes in cubes
2. Place into a buttered mold
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and a tablespoon of brown sugar
4. Dot with butter, dredge with bread crumbs
5. Bake covered for 20 minutes
6. Uncover and continue baking until brown on top
