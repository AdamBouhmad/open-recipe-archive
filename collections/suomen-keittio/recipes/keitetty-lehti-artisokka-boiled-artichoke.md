## Ingredients

- artichoke
- lemon
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Remove the bottom leaves and stem of the artichoke and trim the tips of the upper leaves slightly.
2. Rub the bottom with a little lemon.
3. Place the artichoke stem side up in boiling water with a little salt.
4. Cook until tender.
5. Transfer immediately to a serving platter and serve with Hollandaise sauce or melted butter.
