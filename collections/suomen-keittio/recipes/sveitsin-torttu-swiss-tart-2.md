## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 1/4 cupfuls of melted butter
- 1 1/4 cupfuls of sugar
- 1 1/4 cupfuls of flour
- few tablespoonfuls of fruit juice
- few tablespoonfuls of apple sauce
- 1/2 quart of rich cream
- 3-4 tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Work butter to a cream, add eggs one at a time, then sugar and flour.
2. Beat well the mixture.
3. Put the paste in two equal-sized hot, buttered baking dishes and bake to a light brown.
4. Pile the cakes on a serving dish placing jam between them.
5. Let get cold and cover with whipped cream.
