## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 4 tablespoons cream
- 1 cup currants
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon cardamom
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar until creamy
2. Add the melted butter, cream, spices and flour sifted with soda
3. At the last fold in the whites beaten to a firm froth
