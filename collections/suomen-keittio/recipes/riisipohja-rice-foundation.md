## Ingredients

- boiled rice
- butter

## Directions

1. Pack boiled rice into a buttered shallow mold
2. Bake for a few minutes in a hot oven
3. Unmold on a serving platter
4. Use as a foundation for several kinds of dishes
5. The rice may be mixed with some butter and yolk of egg
