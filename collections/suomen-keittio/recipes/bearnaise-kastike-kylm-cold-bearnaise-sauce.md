## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon caper
- 1/4 tablespoon sour pickle
- 1 teaspoon parsley
- 2 yolks of egg
- 2 tablespoons butter
- some white pepper

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of egg in a saucepan
2. Add slowly the oil, 2 tablespoons of hot water and 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
3. Beat until thick having the pan in boiling water
4. Mix with the seasonings and the butter
5. Serve with fish, chops, roast and cutlets
