## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 cupful of milk
- salt
- pepper
- 1 teaspoonful of onion juice
- 1 cupful of cooked, cut stomach

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce with butter, flour and milk.
2. Season with salt, pepper and 1 teaspoonful of onion juice.
3. Add 1 cupful of cooked, cut stomach.
4. Heat over fire until the stomach pieces are warmed through.
