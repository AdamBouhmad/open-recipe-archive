## Ingredients

- 1 pint of oysters
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 cupful of bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- oyster liquor
- milk

## Directions

1. Clean and drain 1 pint of oysters
2. Mix 2 tablespoonfuls of butter with 1 cupful of bread crumbs
3. Butter a mold and sprinkle with bread crumbs
4. Lay oysters in it alternating with bread crumbs sprinkled with salt and pepper
5. Fill mold with oyster liquor and milk mixed in equal parts
6. Dredge with bread crumbs and dot with butter
7. Bake in a hot oven 20-30 minutes
8. Serve in the same mold
