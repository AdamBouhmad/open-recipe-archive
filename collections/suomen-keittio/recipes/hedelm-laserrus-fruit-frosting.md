## Ingredients

- 1 cup confectioner's sugar
- 2 tablespoons fruit juice
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mix slowly 1 cup confectioner's sugar with 2 tablespoons fruit juice and some lemon juice
2. Stir until smooth
