## Ingredients

- 5 ozs. of cracker dust
- 2 ozs. of flour
- 18 ozs. of suet
- 1/2 lb. of currants
- 3/4 lb. of sugar
- 2 ozs. of chopped almonds
- 1 teaspoonful of nutmeg
- 2 leaves of mace chopped
- 1 small spoonful of salt
- a dash of cinnamon
- yellow rind of 2 lemons
- juice of 4 lemons
- 8 eggs
- 1 glassful of brandy

## Directions

1. Prepare same as directed for last recipe.
