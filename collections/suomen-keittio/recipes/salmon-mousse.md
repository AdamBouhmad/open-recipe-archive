## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of salmon
- whites of 2 eggs
- 2 cupfuls of rich cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Skin and bone the fish.
2. Grind several times.
3. Rub well in a wooden bowl.
4. Add the whites of eggs one by one to it.
5. Force through a sieve.
6. Put the mixture in a kettle or bowl, place it in ice water.
7. Mix with cream whipping well.
8. Pour the cream very carefully into it; if added too quickly the mixture will curdle.
9. Season with salt and pepper.
10. Decorate an individual mold with truffles, chopped parsley, turn the mixture carefully into it.
11. Cover with buttered paper.
12. Bake in a pan of water about 15 min. or until firm when touched with fingertips.
13. Turn on a serving-dish, serve with crab- or Hollandaise sauce.
14. Test the mixture before baking by dropping a teaspoonful of it into boiling water.
15. If too thick, a little cream may be added, if too thin, harden with white of egg.
16. May be prepared earlier and kept in ice box until baking.
