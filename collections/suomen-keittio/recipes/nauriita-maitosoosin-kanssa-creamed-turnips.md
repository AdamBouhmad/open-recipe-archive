## Ingredients

- turnips
- salt
- water
- milk sauce
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel and rinse turnips and cut in cubes
2. Boil in salted water until tender
3. Drain and mix with milk sauce moderately thick
4. If used without milk sauce, add some butter to turnips
5. Shake over the fire until butter is mixed with turnips
