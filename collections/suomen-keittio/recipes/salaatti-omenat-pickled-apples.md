## Ingredients

- 1 pound small apples
- 3 ounces sugar
- 5 cloves
- 1.5 cups water
- 0.5 cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Rinse and peel the apples well.
2. Boil the sugar, water and vinegar.
3. Put the apples into the mixture and let simmer slowly until half soft.
4. Place carefully in glass jars with air-tight covers and fill the jars with the syrup.
