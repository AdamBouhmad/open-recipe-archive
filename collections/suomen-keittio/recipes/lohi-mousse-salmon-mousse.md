## Ingredients

- 1 pound salmon
- 2 egg whites
- 2 cups whipping cream
- Salt and pepper
- Truffles
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Scrape the fish off the skin and remove the bones.
2. Grind several times and pound well in a wooden bowl.
3. Add the whites of egg slowly and rub the mixture through a sieve.
4. Place the fish into a saucepan or a jar and set the jar in ice water.
5. Add gradually the cream to the fish and beat well.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Garnish a souffle mold with truffles and chopped parsley and fill with the mixture.
8. Bake in a pan of water covered with buttered paper about 15 minutes or until firm when touched with finger tips.
9. Unmold onto a platter and serve with Hollandaise or lobster sauce.
10. Test the mixture before baking by dropping some of it with a spoon into boiling water. If it feels hard, add a little cream or if too soft, harden with a white of egg.
11. May be prepared earlier and kept in icebox until baking time.
