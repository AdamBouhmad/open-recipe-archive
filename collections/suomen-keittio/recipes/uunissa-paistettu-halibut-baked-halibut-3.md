## Ingredients

- 2-3 pounds Chicken Halibut
- salt
- pepper
- pork slices
- onion slices
- butter
- flour
- crackerdust
- lemon juice
- cream

## Directions

1. Take a 2-3 pound Chicken Halibut, wipe and rub with salt and pepper
2. Place in a pan lined with pork slices
3. Put onion slices around the edges
4. Cover the fish with batter made from equal parts butter and flour mixed well
5. Sprinkle crackerdust on top
6. Place slices of smoked pork or dots of butter on top
7. Bake in oven, basting often
8. If browning too quickly, cover with buttered paper
9. Make sauce from the drippings by adding lemon juice and cream
