## Ingredients

- Currant jelly
- Lemon slices, boiled
- Orange slices, boiled
- Mustard
- Orange juice
- Cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Melt currant jelly.
2. Mix with boiled slices of lemon and orange.
3. Add some mustard, orange juice and cayenne pepper.
