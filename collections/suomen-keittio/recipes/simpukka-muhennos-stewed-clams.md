## Ingredients

- 1 pint clams
- 1 large tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 3/4 cup clam liquor
- 1 cup heated cream
- Pinch of baking soda

## Directions

1. Take 1 pint clams and separate the soft part from the hard part
2. Chop the hard part finely
3. Heat a large tablespoon of butter with the chopped clams
4. Add 1 tablespoon flour
5. Add 3/4 cup clam liquor and boil for a few minutes
6. Add the soft part of the clams
7. Add 1 cup heated cream with a pinch of baking soda stirred in
8. Let boil for a couple of minutes
9. Just before serving, gradually stir in two beaten egg yolks
