## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 1/4 cups sugar
- 4 eggs
- 3/4 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup grated chocolate
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Rub butter with sugar until creamy
2. Add egg yolks beaten until frothy
3. Mix in chocolate diluted with milk, vanilla, flour and baking powder
4. Last of all fold in the whites of the eggs beaten to firm froth
5. Bake for 1 hour in a buttered tin in a moderately hot oven
