## Ingredients

- 2 middle-sized turnips
- salted water
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- 3/4 cupful rich milk
- salt
- pepper
- sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoonfuls cream

## Directions

1. Cook the turnips in salted water until tender.
2. Mash the turnips.
3. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and rich milk.
4. Season the sauce with salt, pepper and sugar.
5. Mix the mashed turnip into the sauce and let it come to a boil.
6. Beat 2 egg yolks with 2 tablespoonfuls cream and stir in last.
