## Ingredients

- 1 quart oysters
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup cream
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Strain the liquor of the oysters through a fine cloth, put on the fire.
2. Thicken with butter and flour.
3. Wash the oysters, add to the hot liquor and bring quickly to a boil.
4. Mix with teaspoonful of soda.
5. Scald the milk with the cream, pour slowly into the soup.
6. Yolks of 2 hard boiled eggs rubbed through a sieve and added to the soup will improve the color and taste.
7. Serve with slices of lemon and toast.
