## Ingredients

- 4 cups cherries
- ½ cup sugar
- cornstarch
- pastry for two crusts
- alternative: 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Wash the cherries, remove the stems and the stones.
2. Cover a pie plate with pastry, place the cherries over, sprinkle with sugar mixed with cornstarch or use 1 egg yolk diluted with liquor of cherries.
3. Pour the yolk over the cherries.
4. Cover with the crust.
5. Bake in a moderate oven.
