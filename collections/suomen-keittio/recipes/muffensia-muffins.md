## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Sift the flour with salt and baking powder.
2. Beat the eggs with the sugar, add milk, butter and gradually flour.
3. Drop by the spoonful into buttered biscuit tins and bake in a moderate oven.
4. The muffins will be made lighter by adding beaten egg whites to the batter last.
