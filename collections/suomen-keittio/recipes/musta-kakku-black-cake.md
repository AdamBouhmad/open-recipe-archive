## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups molasses
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup cream
- 3 eggs
- 3 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger

## Directions

1. Rub molasses
2. Add butter
3. Add beaten eggs
4. Add cream, ginger, soda and flour
5. Bake in a hot oven
