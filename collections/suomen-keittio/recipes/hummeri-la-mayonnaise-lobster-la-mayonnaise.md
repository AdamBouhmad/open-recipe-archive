## Ingredients

- lobster shells
- lobster meat
- mayonnaise
- red pepper
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse lobster shells.
2. Fill with lobster meat mixed with mayonnaise.
3. Garnish the top with red pepper.
4. Serve with lettuce leaves on a napkin.
