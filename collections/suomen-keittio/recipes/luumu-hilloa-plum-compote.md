## Ingredients

- 5 pounds of plums (not too ripe)
- 4 pounds of sugar
- 3 cupfuls of water

## Directions

1. Halve the plums enough to remove the pits.
2. Cook sugar and water to a thick syrup, into which put the plums and cook until they have become clear.
3. Put plums in jar and pour the syrup over the fruit.
4. If the syrup seems loose, cook it a little while longer and pour it hot over the plums.
5. Seal the jar when cooled.
6. If the plums are hard-skinned, pour boiling water over them so the skins will separate more easily.
