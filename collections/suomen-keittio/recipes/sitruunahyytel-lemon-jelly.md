## Ingredients

- 3 cups water
- 1 cup sugar
- 4 lemons
- 2 tablespoons or 1/2 package gelatine
- Whites and shells of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan water, the grated yellow rind and the juice of the lemons, sugar, gelatine, egg whites beaten frothy and the shells.
2. Place on the fire, stir until coming to boil.
3. Simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Strain through a cloth wrung out of hot water.
5. Bring the unclear first liquor back into the straining cloth.
6. If not clarified jelly desired, prepare as follows: Dissolve gelatine in 1/2 cup water, add the remaining water, juice, sugar and the yellow rind of 1 lemon.
7. Stir until the gelatine is dissolved.
8. Remove from the fire, add lemon juice, strain and mold.
