## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. shoulder of mutton
- boiling water
- carrots
- 4 onions
- 2 stalks of celery
- salt
- pepper
- potatoes
- flour
- cold water
- butter
- dumplings

## Directions

1. Wipe and cut the mutton in pieces.
2. Put into a kettle and pour boiling water over.
3. Let quickly come to boil and skim.
4. Add carrots cut in nice pieces, onions, celery cut in pieces, salt and pepper.
5. Let simmer, covered, until tender.
6. About half an hour before taking the meat out add potatoes into it.
7. Thicken with flour mixed with cold water.
8. Serve with dumplings.
9. Meat may be browned with a little butter before putting water in.
