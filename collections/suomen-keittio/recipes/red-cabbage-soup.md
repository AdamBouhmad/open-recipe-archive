## Ingredients

- 1 red cabbage
- 3 quarts of vegetable stock
- 3 large apples
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoonful of parsley
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage into shreds.
2. Cook the butter with the chopped onion.
3. Add the cabbage and cut apples, sugar, salt, parsley and pepper.
4. Let boil, closely covered, 2 hours, taking care not to burn it.
5. Add the scalded vegetable stock into it and let it boil for a while.
6. Flavor with wine and currant jelly.
