## Ingredients

- Calf's brain
- Cold water
- Salt
- Lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak in cold water for about an hour.
2. Put on fire in boiling water, cook with salt, some lemon juice or vinegar.
3. Place into cold water to cool, remove the skin and fiber.
4. Prepare the same as sweetbreads and serve with any kind of sauce.
