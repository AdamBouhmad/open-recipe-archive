## Ingredients

- White grapes
- Peaches
- Pineapples
- Cantaloupes
- Bananas
- Strawberries
- Cherries
- Figs
- Oranges
- Walnuts
- Grapefruit
- Celery
- Apples
- Lettuce
- French dressing
- Mayonnaise
- Cream
- Dates

## Directions

1. All kinds of fruit plain or mixed may be used for salad
2. Have 1 cup of each: white grapes split and seeded, peaches and pineapples cut in cubes
3. Cantaloupes, grapes, peaches, bananas, strawberries, pineapples, cherries and figs mixed make a delicious salad
4. Oranges, bananas, grapes and walnuts may be mixed together
5. Equal parts of oranges, grapefruit and celery mixed make a nice salad
6. For pineapple, celery and apples: Take 2 cups apple, 1 cup celery and 1 cup pineapple
7. Or pile slices of pineapple on lettuce leaves, fill the center with fruit moistened with French dressing
8. Cover the whole with mayonnaise mixed with cream
9. Sprinkle with chopped walnuts
10. Mix together 1 grapefruit, 2 oranges, 1 cup grapes split and seeded and 2 bananas
11. Mix with cream dressing and serve in cups made of orange peels
12. Grapefruit, apples, bananas, celery, figs and dates cut in pieces make a nice fruit salad
13. Serve in mayonnaise mixed with cream
