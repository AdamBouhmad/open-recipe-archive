## Ingredients

- Eel
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Tartar sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, split in back, remove the backbone.
2. Rub with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
3. Dip in egg, roll in bread crumbs, bake for ½ hour.
4. Serve with any kind of tartar sauce.
