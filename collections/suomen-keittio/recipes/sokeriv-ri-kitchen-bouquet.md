## Ingredients

- 1 heaping tablespoonful sugar
- 1 tablespoonful water

## Directions

1. Have a heaping tablespoonful of sugar and 1 tablespoonful of water
2. Boil on a quick fire until dark brown syrup
3. Test it by cooling a little of it
4. If it gets firm, add a small quantity of water and return over the fire
5. Bottle and use to color soups and sauces
