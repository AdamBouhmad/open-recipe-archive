## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1½ cupfuls of milk
- 9 tablespoonfuls of grated cheese
- 3-6 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs
- 3 yolks

## Directions

1. Cook butter and flour in a saucepan, mix with milk and cheese, stir until a smooth paste.
2. Let boil a few minutes.
3. Set to cool, and thicken with yolks and bread crumbs so as to make a batter thick enough to be formed into small balls.
4. Roll the balls in flour.
5. Cook in milk or in deep lard, or fry in butter.
