## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cupfuls of rice
- 1 pint of water
- 1 quart of milk
- 1 lb. of liver
- 1 egg
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 3/4 onion
- a pinch of pepper

## Directions

1. Cook a batter of rice, water and milk.
2. Put aside to cool.
3. Mix ground liver with batter, add beaten egg, chopped onion fried in butter, sugar, salt and pepper.
4. Turn the mixture in a buttered mold.
5. Bake 1 hour.
6. Flavor with raisins if desired.
7. Serve with melted butter.
