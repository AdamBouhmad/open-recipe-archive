## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 eggs
- Butter, sugar and flour in equal quantities, each as much as the weight of the eggs
- 1/4 cup chopped almonds
- Yellow rind of a lemon
- 1/2 cup brandy
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Rub until creamy the sugar with the butter
2. Add the yolks beaten until frothy
3. Add almonds and grated lemon rind
4. Add brandy and flour sifted with baking powder
5. Last fold in the whites beaten to a firm froth
6. Pour into a buttered tin
7. Bake for 1 hour in a moderate oven
