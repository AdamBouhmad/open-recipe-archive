## Ingredients

- 6 good apples
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 5 tablespoons flour
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 3 eggs
- Dash of cinnamon
- Few teaspoons jam

## Directions

1. Pare and core the apples, cook with 1 cup of sugar and a stick of cinnamon.
2. Lay the apples into a buttered mold, fill the hollowed cores with jam.
3. Cook a batter of 2 tablespoons butter, flour and milk.
4. Beat the yolks of the eggs with the sugar, add to the batter with the rest of the butter.
5. Last of all mix with the whites whipped to a firm froth.
6. Cover the apples with the mixture.
7. Bake in oven in a pan of water for 30 minutes.
8. When the surface is becoming brown, cover with buttered paper.
