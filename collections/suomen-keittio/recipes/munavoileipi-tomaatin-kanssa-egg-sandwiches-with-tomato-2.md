## Ingredients

- lettuce leaf
- tomato slice
- eggs
- salt
- paprika
- mayonnaise
- mustard
- pepper
- onion juice

## Directions

1. Place a slice of tomato on a lettuce leaf.
2. Lay chopped egg seasoned with salt and paprika carefully with a knife on the tomato.
3. Cover with mayonnaise mixed with mustard.
4. Alternatively, chopped egg seasoned with salt, pepper and onion juice may be shaped in different ways and served on lettuce leaf garnished with mayonnaise.
