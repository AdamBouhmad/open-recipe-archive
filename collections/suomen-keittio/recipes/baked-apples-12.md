## Ingredients

- equal sized apples
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Take some equal sized apples, wash and wipe them.
2. Core.
3. Butter a baking pan, lay the apples in it.
4. Fill the cores with sugar, pour a little water into the pan so as not to burn the apples.
5. Bake in oven, until tender.
6. Serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
