## Ingredients

- cold boned fish
- cold boiled potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter or lard
- onion

## Directions

1. Chop separately cold boned fish and equal amount of cold boiled potato, season with salt and pepper.
2. Melt some butter or lard in a frying pan, fry one chopped onion in it.
3. Put the fish and potato in the pan, stirring until hot.
4. Let fry until brown underneath.
5. Fold as an omelet and turn onto a platter.
