## Ingredients

- Strawberries
- Sugar equal in weight to strawberries

## Directions

1. Clean strawberries and weigh.
2. Have sugar and berries in equal parts.
3. Lay sugar and berries alternately into a jar.
4. Put aside until next day.
5. Place into a kettle.
6. Simmer on a slow fire skimming carefully and shaking the kettle.
7. When the berries are cleared and the scum no longer rises to the surface pour the compote into a dry jar.
8. Seal when cooled.
