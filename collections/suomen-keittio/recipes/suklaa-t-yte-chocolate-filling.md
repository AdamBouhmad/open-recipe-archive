## Ingredients

- yolks of 4 eggs
- 8 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 1/2 lb. of sweet butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 2 teaspoonfuls of vanilla
- 3 tablespoonfuls of cocoa
- a little water

## Directions

1. Put yolks, cream and sugar into a kettle
2. Beat over the fire until thick
3. When cool add creamed butter
4. Add vanilla and cocoa melted in a small quantity of water
