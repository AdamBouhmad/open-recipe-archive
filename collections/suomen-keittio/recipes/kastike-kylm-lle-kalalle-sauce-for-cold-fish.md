## Ingredients

- 1.5 cups fish stock
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 lemon, juice of
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley, chopped finely
- Mayonnaise (optional)
- Olives
- Pickles, finely chopped
- Capers
- Hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Boil 1.5 cups of fish stock.
2. Thicken with 1 tablespoon of cornstarch.
3. Beat 3 yolks of egg, add 1 tablespoon sugar and juice of 1 lemon.
4. Pour gradually mixing well into the fish stock.
5. Heat, taking care not to boil it.
6. Season with salt, pepper and chopped parsley.
7. Pour over the fish.
8. When cold the sauce may be mixed with mayonnaise according to taste.
