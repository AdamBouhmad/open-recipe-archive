## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1/4 cupful of milk
- 1 cupful of flour
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- Salt, pepper and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook a porridge of butter, milk and flour.
2. Remove from the fire.
3. Add 3 yolks of egg one by one to the porridge stirring well.
4. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg.
5. Beat 3 whites of egg until firm and add last of all.
6. Drop by the spoonful into boiling salt water.
7. Boil for 5 minutes or until done.
