## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt
- water
- butter
- flour
- milk
- parsley
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean asparagus and cut the tender part in pieces an inch long.
2. Cook in salted water until tender.
3. Make a milk sauce of 1 tablespoon butter, 1 tablespoon flour and 1 cup milk.
4. Mix the asparagus with the sauce and bring to boiling.
5. Alternatively, asparagus may be cooked in scanty water with butter and salt until tender, dusted with flour or sprinkled with parsley when ready to be served.
6. Boiled asparagus cut in pieces may be mixed with carrots or peas in equal parts and served in a sauce made of the liquor of the asparagus or without sauce, sprinkled with salt and pepper and basted with butter.
