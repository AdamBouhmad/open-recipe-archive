## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 whole egg and yolk of 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- Juice and yellow rind of 1/2 lemon
- 2 cups flour
- 1/2 yeast cake
- Apple sauce or currant jelly

## Directions

1. Warm the milk, add butter, lemon juice and rind, eggs beaten with sugar and the risen sponge of the yeast.
2. Add flour, beat well and let rise.
3. Knead and roll into a sheet 1/8 inch thick.
4. Cut in cakes with a round cutter, have some apple sauce or currant jelly in the center of each cake.
5. Place two together by fastening the edges with egg white.
6. Cook in lard, turn in sugar.
7. Serve hot.
