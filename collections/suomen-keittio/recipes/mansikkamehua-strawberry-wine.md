## Ingredients

- 6 pounds strawberries
- 2 quarts water
- 2 ounces wine acid

## Directions

1. Dissolve 2 ounces wine acid in 1 quart water
2. Put the strawberries into a glass jar, pour the water mixed with wine acid over
3. Cover tight
4. Let stand in a cold place for 24 hours
5. Strain through a cloth without pressing the cloth
6. Weigh the juice, allow an equal amount of sugar, mix in the juice
7. Bottle up when the sugar is dissolved
