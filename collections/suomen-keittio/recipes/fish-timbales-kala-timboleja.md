## Ingredients

- 1 lb of boned fish
- 6 ozs of butter
- 2 ozs of bread
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of cream
- 4 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the boned fish with meat chopper several times.
2. Rub well in a wooden bowl, add gradually the butter rubbed to a froth and the yolks one by one.
3. Mix with the bread soaked in cream.
4. Continue rubbing until frothy.
5. Add the beaten whites of eggs to it.
6. Put in timbale molds, cover with buttered paper.
7. Bake in a pan of water.
8. Turn on a serving dish.
9. Serve with sauce.
