## Ingredients

- Chestnuts
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut with a knife across or make a little hole in the top of each chestnut
2. Put the chestnuts in a sauce pan with salt and cook without water on high heat, stirring briskly
3. After 30 minutes cooking they are easy to shell
4. Serve in the shells on a folded napkin with cold butter
5. Alternatively, bake chestnuts in oven covered with a thick layer of salt on top; in a moderate oven they will be done in 30 minutes
6. Or boil the chestnuts in salted water; when boiled 20 minutes the shells will be easy to remove
