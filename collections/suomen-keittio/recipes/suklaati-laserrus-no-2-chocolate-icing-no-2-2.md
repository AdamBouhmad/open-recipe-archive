## Ingredients

- 1 ounce chocolate
- 6 tablespoons boiling water
- vanilla
- 1½ cups confectioner's sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 ounce chocolate in a dry pan
2. Add 6 tablespoons boiling water and some vanilla
3. Stir well
4. Mix in 1½ cups confectioner's sugar gradually until the mixture is moderately thick
5. Spread immediately on cakes
6. If cocoa is used instead, it must be dissolved in hot water
