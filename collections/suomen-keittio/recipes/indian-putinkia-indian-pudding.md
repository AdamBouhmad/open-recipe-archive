## Ingredients

- 4 cups milk
- 1/4 cup corn meal
- 1/2 cup syrup
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon soda
- Lemon rind or ginger

## Directions

1. Cook a mush of corn meal and milk for about 15 minutes
2. Add the rest of the ingredients
3. Bake in a slow oven for 2 or 3 hours
4. Serve with hard sauce or cream
