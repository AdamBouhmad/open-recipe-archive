## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 pint bouillon or fish stock
- 1 or 2 tablespoons caper
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- salt, pepper and sugar
- 1 egg, finely chopped
- 2 egg yolks (optional)
- cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare a gravy of butter, flour and bouillon.
2. Add finely chopped egg, salt, pepper, sugar, lemon juice and caper.
3. Season to taste.
4. 2 yolks of egg and some cream may be added to the sauce at the last.
5. Serve with mutton or fish.
