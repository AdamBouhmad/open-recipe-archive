## Ingredients

- blueberries
- sugar
- ½ tablespoon cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- cinnamon
- salt
- pastry for two crusts
- egg white or bread crumbs
- alternative: 1 egg yolk and 3 tablespoons cream

## Directions

1. Cover a pie plate with pastry, brush with egg white or sprinkle with bread crumbs.
2. Fill with blueberries mixed with sugar, ½ tablespoon cornstarch, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, cinnamon and salt.
3. Cover with the crust, bake in a moderate oven.
4. Instead of cornstarch, an egg yolk beaten with 3 tablespoons of cream may be used and poured over the berries.
