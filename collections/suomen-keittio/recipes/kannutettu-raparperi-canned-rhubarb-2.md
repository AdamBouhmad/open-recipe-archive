## Ingredients

- rhubarb
- sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse rhubarb when young and cut in pieces without peeling.
2. Take 1 cup sugar to 2 cups rhubarb.
3. Lay in a kettle in layers and let stand until the next day.
4. Bring the juice to a boil and cook down until thick.
5. Add the rhubarb pieces and cook slowly until they appear clear.
6. Pour into jars while hot and seal.
