## Ingredients

- 2 pounds fresh or 1 can mushrooms
- 2 quarts white stock
- 1 small red onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 tablespoon tomato
- salt and pepper
- 1 cup cream
- green beans (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the onion in butter for 5 minutes.
2. Add flour, white stock, mushrooms cleaned and chopped, and tomato.
3. Cook for 10 minutes.
4. Force through a colander.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Last of all add the cream.
7. Finely cut green beans, cooked first in salted water until tender, may be used in the soup.
