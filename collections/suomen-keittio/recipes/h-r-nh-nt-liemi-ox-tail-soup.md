## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 2 red onions
- White part of celery
- 1 quart of Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 cup fresh or dried mushrooms
- Salt and pepper
- Butter and flour for thickening
- Lemon
- Cayenne pepper
- Kitchen Bouquet for coloring
- Hardboiled egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the vegetables.
2. Cut in pieces and brown in butter.
3. When brown cover with boiling water.
4. Add the artichokes, the mushrooms, the green part of leek, a sprig of parsley, a bay leaf, salt and pepper.
5. Let simmer covered until the vegetables are tender.
6. Strain through a fine cloth.
7. Thicken with butter and flour.
8. Season with salt and cayenne pepper.
9. Flavor with lemon.
10. Color with Kitchen Bouquet.
11. Serve with hardboiled egg yolks cut in pieces or with egg balls.
12. May be made without artichokes and mushrooms, if desired.
