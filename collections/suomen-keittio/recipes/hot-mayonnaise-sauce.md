## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoonfuls of salad oil
- 1 tablespoonful of vinegar
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice
- 1/4 cupful of hot water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Mix the oil drop by drop with the yolks, stirring well
2. Add vinegar and water gradually into it
3. Cook in a pot of boiling water until thick
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Add minced parsley, if you like
