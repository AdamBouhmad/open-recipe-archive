## Ingredients

- 1 hard boiled egg yolk
- 1 raw egg yolk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard
- sugar
- 1 1/2 cups salad oil
- 4 tablespoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Rub hard boiled egg yolk well.
2. Add salt, mustard, and a little sugar.
3. Mix in raw egg yolk and beat well.
4. Pour salad oil gradually, at first drop by drop while stirring well, about 1 1/2 cups total.
5. Add lemon juice or to taste (4 tablespoons).
