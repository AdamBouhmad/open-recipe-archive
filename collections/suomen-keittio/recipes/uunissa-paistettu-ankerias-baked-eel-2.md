## Ingredients

- eels
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- egg
- crackerdust

## Directions

1. Clean the eels, split down the back.
2. Remove the backbone.
3. Rub with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
4. Dip in egg and crackerdust, and bake about half an hour.
5. Serve with sour sauce.
