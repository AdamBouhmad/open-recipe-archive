## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 3/4 cupful of brandy
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of water

## Directions

1. Cook sugar in 1/2 cupful of water to a light brown
2. Mix it with the remaining water
3. Let cool, not quite cold
4. Turn the caramel over dessert when serving it
5. Pour carefully brandy on and light it
6. Serve with plum and marrow puddings
