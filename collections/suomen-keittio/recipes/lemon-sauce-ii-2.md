## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- juice and rind of 1 lemon
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of white wine or water
- yolks of 4 eggs
- 2 cupfuls of cream

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar to a cream, add beaten yolks and water.
2. Stir it in a saucepan on the fire until thick.
3. Remove from the fire, add lemon juice, grated rind and wine.
4. When cool mix with the whipped cream.
