## Ingredients

- boiled shrimps
- cucumber
- salt
- pepper
- mayonnaise
- lettuce leaves
- French dressing

## Directions

1. Take equal parts of boiled shrimps cut in pieces and cucumber
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Mix with mayonnaise
4. Serve on a bed of lettuce
5. Garnish with whole shrimps and cucumber
6. Pour French dressing over
