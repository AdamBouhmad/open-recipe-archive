## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks (boiled)
- 1½ teaspoons mustard (prepared)
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 cup cream
- Salt, pepper and sugar

## Directions

1. Rub well the chopped yolks with mustard and seasonings
2. Add slowly vinegar and lemon juice, rub until a smooth paste
3. Last of all add gradually the whipped cream
4. Or mix ½ cup of oil with scant ½ cupful of lemon juice
5. Add salt and sugar according to taste
6. Mix last with 1 cup of cream
