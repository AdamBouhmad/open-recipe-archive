## Ingredients

- strawberries
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Choose nice berries, wash and remove the stems.
2. Allow 1 pound sugar to 1 quart berries.
3. Lay into a kettle, crush lightly or let stand in the kettle over night.
4. Simmer until clear, skimming carefully.
5. Pour into glass jars while hot.
6. Seal as directed.
