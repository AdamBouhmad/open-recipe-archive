## Ingredients

- boiled lobster meat
- mayonnaise
- gelatine
- capers
- pickles
- egg
- truffle
- bread

## Directions

1. Cut meat of boiled lobsters in cubes.
2. Stir into mayonnaise mixed with gelatine.
3. Flavor with finely chopped capers and pickles.
4. Fill small molds and let cool.
5. Serve on thin slices of bread.
