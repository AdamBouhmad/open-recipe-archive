## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- celery
- boiled chicken or veal
- mayonnaise sauce
- cucumber
- pineapple
- apple

## Directions

1. Peel tomatoes and scoop mellow out; sprinkle inside with salt.
2. Keep on ice until they have to be served.
3. Stuff the tomatoes with celery cut in very small pieces and mixed with boiled, chopped chicken or veal and mayonnaise sauce.
4. Tomato may be filled with cucumber and pineapple, both separately, celery and apple (together) cut in small pieces, mixed with mayonnaise sauce.
