## Ingredients

- beef
- butter
- salt
- boiling water
- tomato
- pepper
- flour (optional)

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat carefully with a cloth wrung out of warm water.
2. Place the meat in a buttered dripping pan skin-side down.
3. Put into a hot oven.
4. When brown, sprinkle with salt and pour a little boiling water into the pan.
5. Baste often with the drippings.
6. When half done turn it over and continue baking slowly.
7. If getting too brown, cover the roast with buttered paper.
8. Cook 10 to 15 minutes per pound.
9. Place the roast on a platter skin-side up.
10. Remove some fat from the pan.
11. Prepare a gravy by adding flour and some water to the drippings (or use the drippings without flour).
12. Season with tomato, pepper and salt.
