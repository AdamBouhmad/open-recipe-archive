## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup water
- Strawberries

## Directions

1. Cook 2 cups of sugar with 1 cup of water for 15 minutes.
2. Add strawberries, bring to boil.
3. Pour into a dish, let cool.
4. Serve in a glass dish with some of their own liquid.
