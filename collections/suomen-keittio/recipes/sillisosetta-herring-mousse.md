## Ingredients

- 2 herrings
- 2 hard boiled eggs
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 4 tablespoons cream
- 1 tablespoon butter
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the fish, skin and bone.
2. Chop the fish.
3. Chop the onion, fry in butter.
4. Add the fish, lemon juice, cream and seasonings.
5. Let boil quickly.
6. Add the chopped eggs.
7. Place on a platter.
8. Serve hot or cold covered with mayonnaise.
