## Ingredients

- duck
- lemon juice or sherry wine
- 4 tablespoons gelatine dissolved in water
- 1 cup cream
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 3 tablespoons gelatine dissolved in water
- cornstarch
- Kitchen Bouquet
- whipped cream
- truffles
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Clean and roast the duck as directed.
2. When cold, remove the fillets and grind several times.
3. Mix with some lemon juice or sherry wine, 4 tablespoons of gelatine dissolved in water and 1 cup of cream.
4. Fill the breast space with this mixture.
5. Baste the duck with liquid jelly prepared of 1 cup of chicken stock, 3 tablespoons of gelatine dissolved in water and some cornstarch.
6. Color dark with Kitchen Bouquet.
7. Garnish the top with whipped cream, truffles and hard boiled eggs.
