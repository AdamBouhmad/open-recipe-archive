## Ingredients

- 3 pounds lean meat
- 3 quarts water
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 1 onion
- 10 peppercorns
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3 cups bouillon or milk
- 2 yolks of egg
- 1.5 tablespoon vinegar
- 0.5 tablespoon sugar
- 4 or 5 tablespoons grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat with a cloth wrung out of hot water.
2. Put into boiling water.
3. Cook, skimming off foam, and add salt, pepper and onion.
4. Continue boiling on a slow fire until tender.
5. For the sauce: Mix the butter and flour in a saucepan.
6. Add bouillon or milk, season and bring to boiling.
7. Remove from the fire and mix with yolks of egg.
8. Last of all add the grated horseradish.
