## Ingredients

- 3 quarts of stock
- 2 lbs. of tomatoes or 2 cupfuls of canned tomatoes
- 1/4 lb. of ham
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 slice of onion
- 1 sprig of parsley
- 1 stalk of celery
- 6 peppercorns

## Directions

1. Cut the ham into pieces, brown it in butter, add the onion into it and cook for a few minutes.
2. Add flour and tomatoes stirring thoroughly.
3. Pour the boiling stock gently on, add parsley, celery and pepper; let cook until the tomatoes are stewed.
4. Strain and season.
5. Serve with small meatballs.
