## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 cupfuls of milk
- 3 1/2 cupfuls of cream
- 4 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- yolks of 6 eggs
- 10 leaves or 8 tablespoonfuls of gelatine
- vanilla to flavor

## Directions

1. Stir all the ingredients, except cream, in a saucepan until the mixture is thick.
2. Beat the cream to a froth and mix with the cold mass.
3. Divide into three parts.
4. Color one part red, one chocolate colored, and leave one white.
5. Pile the parts in layers in a mold.
