## Ingredients

- Yellow rind of 3 lemons
- 2.5 pounds sugar
- lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon wine acid powder

## Directions

1. Grate the yellow rind of 3 lemons
2. Rub together with 2.5 pounds sugar
3. Press the juice of the lemons and strain it
4. Add the strained juice and 1 teaspoon wine acid powder to the sugar
5. Mix thoroughly together
6. Spread on paper to dry
7. Keep in a covered glass jar
8. Allow 2 teaspoons powder to 1 glass of ice water when serving
