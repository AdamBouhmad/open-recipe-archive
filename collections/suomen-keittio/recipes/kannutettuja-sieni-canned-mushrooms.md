## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- rich milk or mushroom liquor and milk
- mushrooms
- onion juice
- parsley
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and rich milk or of liquor of the mushrooms and milk equal parts.
2. Cut the mushrooms in pieces, add to the sauce.
3. Heat slowly to boiling.
4. May be mixed with onion juice, if desired.
5. Serve sprinkled with parsley.
6. Or put the mushrooms with the sauce into a mold, sprinkle the top with bread crumbs.
7. Heat in oven in a pan of water so as not to curdle.
