## Ingredients

- 1 lobster
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare for baking as directed.
2. Put in a frying pan.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dot with butter, and add a sprinkling of bread crumbs.
4. Bake in a hot oven for about 15 minutes, basting with drippings in pan.
