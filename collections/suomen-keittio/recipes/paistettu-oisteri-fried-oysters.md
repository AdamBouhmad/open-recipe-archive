## Ingredients

- large oysters
- salt
- pepper
- cracker crumbs
- eggs
- butter or lard
- lemon butter

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe large oysters
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Roll in cracker crumbs, dip in egg, roll again in cracker crumbs
4. Fry quickly in deep butter or lard a few oysters at a time
5. Serve with lemon butter
