## Ingredients

- 5 pounds breast of mutton
- 1 or 2 heads of cabbage
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 10 peppercorns
- 1 tablespoon flour

## Directions

1. Cut the meat and bones into pieces.
2. Chop the cabbage coarsely.
3. Place the meat into a kettle and sprinkle with salt.
4. Add the cabbage with a sprinkling of salt and pepper.
5. Put the butter on top in dots.
6. Cover the whole with boiling water.
7. Cook on a slow fire covered for 2 or 3 hours.
