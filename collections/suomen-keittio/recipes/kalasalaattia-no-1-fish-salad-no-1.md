## Ingredients

- Mayonnaise
- Cold fish, any kind, cut in pieces
- Hard boiled eggs, chopped
- Pickles or cucumbers, chopped and cut in pieces
- Lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Mix any kind of cold fish cut in pieces with mayonnaise.
2. Add hard boiled eggs, chopped cucumber cut in pieces and chopped pickles.
3. Serve on lettuce leaves.
