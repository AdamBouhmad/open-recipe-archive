## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of rich cream
- 3/4 cupful of powdered sugar
- 5-6 leaves of gelatine
- 1/4 teaspoonful of vanilla or lemon juice
- 1/2 cupful of water

## Directions

1. Dissolve gelatine in 1/2 cupful of water
2. Whip cream and sugar to a stiff froth and mix with seasonings
3. Stir the dissolved gelatine into the whipped cream, when it is cool, but not firm
4. Pour in a mold to get quite firm
5. Garnish the cream jelly with stewed fruit and macaroons
