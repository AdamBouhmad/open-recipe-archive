## Ingredients

- milk bread dough or wheat bread dough
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as directed for milk bread and let rise
2. Form into a loaf and divide into equal parts
3. Form into rolls and place on a baking sheet to rise
4. Brush with egg and bake in a hot oven
