## Ingredients

- Lobster
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from the shells taking care so as not to break the shells
2. Cut the meat in small cubes, season with salt, pepper and lemon juice
3. Fill the shells, pour some melted butter over
4. Heat in oven
5. Serve garnished with lobster claws
