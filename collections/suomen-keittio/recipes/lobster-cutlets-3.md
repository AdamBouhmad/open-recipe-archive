## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of boiled chopped lobster
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- chopped parsley
- 1 cupful of milk sauce
- 2 hard-boiled egg yolks or 1 raw yolk
- breadcrumbs
- egg
- lard

## Directions

1. Season lobster with salt, pepper, lemon juice and chopped parsley.
2. Mix with 1 cupful of milk sauce to make it thick enough.
3. Add 2 hard-boiled or 1 raw yolk.
4. Let it stand on ice for a while.
5. Form into cutlets.
6. Roll in breadcrumbs, egg and again in breadcrumbs.
7. Cook in lard.
8. Serve with Tartar or tomato sauce.
