## Ingredients

- 5 cups milk
- 3 1/2 tablespoons cocoa
- 3/4 cup sugar
- a pinch of salt
- 1 cup boiling water
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Scald 5 cups milk
2. Dissolve 3 1/2 tablespoons cocoa in water
3. Add 3/4 cup sugar and a pinch of salt
4. Pour 1 cup boiling water over
5. Bring to boil
6. Pour in the scalded milk
7. Serve with whipped cream or plain
