## Ingredients

- sirloin steak or other steak
- 1 carrot
- 1 onion
- 1 tomato or tomato catsup
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- water
- butter or lard (optional)
- mushrooms (optional)

## Directions

1. Broil or fry the steak for a few minutes.
2. Place in a casserole with the carrot and onion cut in pieces, tomato or catsup, salt, pepper and parsley.
3. Add a little water to the drippings in the frying pan and pour over the meat.
4. Bake until the vegetables are tender.
5. May be made without vegetables and dotted with butter. If mushrooms are added, they will give fine flavor to the meat.
