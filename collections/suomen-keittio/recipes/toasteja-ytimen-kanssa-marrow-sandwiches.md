## Ingredients

- marrow bones
- flour
- water
- salt
- pepper
- pickled onions
- parsley
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Spread a thick dough (made of flour and water) on each end of marrow bone to keep the marrow in while cooking.
2. Put the marrow bones on the fire in boiling water; let boil 1-2 hours.
3. Spread the marrow on toasts, sprinkle salt and pepper on top.
4. Set the bread pieces in the oven for a pair of minutes, so they can be served very hot.
5. Alternative way: Before spreading the marrow on the toasts, mix with it a little pickled onions and parsley, finely chopped, season with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
