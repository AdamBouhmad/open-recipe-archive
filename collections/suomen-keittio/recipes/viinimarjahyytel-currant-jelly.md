## Ingredients

- currants
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash currants, put into a saucepan
2. Extract the juice by crushing the berries
3. Simmer until the berries turn almost white
4. Strain through a cloth
5. Weigh the juice, have an equal amount of sugar
6. Cook the juice for 10 minutes
7. Add the heated sugar, continue to simmer until the sugar is dissolved and the juice becomes clear
8. Skim, pour into jelly glasses
9. Seal as directed and keep in cool place
