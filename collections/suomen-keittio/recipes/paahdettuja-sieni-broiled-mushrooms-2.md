## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the mushrooms and cut off the stems
2. Dip in melted butter
3. Place on the broiler and broil over clear coals on both sides to a nice brown
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and pour a little melted butter on
