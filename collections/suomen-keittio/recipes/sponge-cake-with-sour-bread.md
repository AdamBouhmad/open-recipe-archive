## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of finely ground sour bread
- 1/2 cupful of flour
- 1/4 teaspoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Rub the yolks of eggs and sugar until light
2. Add seasonings, ground bread, flour and whites of eggs beaten to a stiff froth
3. Pour the mixture in a buttered mold and bake in a moderate oven 3/4-1 hour
