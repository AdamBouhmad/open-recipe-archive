## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- mace
- allspice
- 1 teaspoonful each of pepper, mace and allspice to 2 cupfuls of liquor
- bay leaf
- onion

## Directions

1. Clean fresh mushrooms and cut in pieces
2. Lay in jar and sprinkle with salt between layers
3. Cover and let stand in a cold place 3-4 days, stirring now and then with spoon
4. Cook mushrooms, chop and press the liquor through a sieve
5. Put the liquor on fire
6. Season with pepper, mace and allspice, allowing 1 teaspoonful of each to two cupfuls of liquor
7. Add one bay leaf and a little onion
8. Let boil until it gets thick
9. Strain, let cool down and bottle
