## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiled and chopped vegetables
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1 cup beans
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 onions
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup cream or tomato juice
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Force through a meat grinder the boiled vegetables such as carrots, potatoes, celery, parsnips, or whatever is available.
2. Brown the chopped onions in butter and add to the vegetables.
3. Add the bread crumbs soaked in cream and mixed with cornstarch and the eggs.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Place into a buttered pan and shape into a loaf.
6. Brush with egg and bake for 1 hour.
7. Serve with brown sauce.
8. May be baked in a mold and served unmoulded.
