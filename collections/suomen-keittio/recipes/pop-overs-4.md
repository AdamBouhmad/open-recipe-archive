## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of flour
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoonful of melted butter

## Directions

1. Put all ingredients in a bowl and whip well for 5 minutes.
2. Pour the batter in hot, buttered, deep iron molds.
3. Bake for 30 minutes, first in hot oven.
4. When risen, reduce heat.
5. Do not touch them before they are quite hard.
