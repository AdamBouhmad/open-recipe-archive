## Ingredients

- hard boiled eggs
- caviare
- anchovy
- toast
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Cut a thin slice of the sides of hard boiled eggs.
2. Scoop out the yolks, filling the eggs with caviare.
3. Cut off a fine strip near both ends and place a slice of anchovy into the cuts.
4. Make a little hole between the strips and fill the hole with a piece of anchovy.
5. Place the eggs on toast with the sliced side down so as to make the egg look like a barrel.
6. Garnish the toast with mayonnaise.
