## Ingredients

- 2 dozen oysters
- 3 cupfuls of oyster liquor
- 1 stalk of celery
- 1 sprig of parsley
- slice of onion
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 3 cupfuls of milk
- 1 cupful of cream
- salt, pepper and a little mace

## Directions

1. Scald the oysters in their own liquor until they ruffle.
2. Strain the liquor and thicken with butter and flour.
3. Add onion, celery and parsley into it and let boil for 10 minutes.
4. Remove the vegetables from the soup.
5. Mix the soup with a pinch of soda before pouring the cream in.
6. Pour the scalded cream and milk gently into it.
7. Season with salt and pepper.
8. Put the oysters into the soup, scald it and serve.
