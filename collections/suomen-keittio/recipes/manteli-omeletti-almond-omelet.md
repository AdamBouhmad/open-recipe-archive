## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons milk
- 1 tablespoon flour
- sugar
- vanilla
- chopped almonds
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Beat separately whites and yolks of 4 eggs.
2. Mix the yolks with 2 tablespoons of butter, 4 tablespoons of milk, 1 tablespoon of flour, some sugar and vanilla.
3. Whisk together.
4. Last of all stir in the whites beaten stiff.
5. Fry on a slow fire or bake.
6. Lay chopped almonds on one half, fold once.
7. The chopped almonds will look nice if fried in butter.
8. Sprinkle with almonds and sugar.
9. Serve with fruit sauce hot or cold.
