## Ingredients

- 1½ cupfuls of mashed chestnuts
- 1 cupful of rich milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Beat eggs with sugar
2. Add milk, flour and chestnuts
3. Bake in oven 30-40 minutes
