## Ingredients

- 1 pint of oysters
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1/2 cupful of cream
- yolks of 2 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- lemon
- toast or patty shells
- mushrooms (optional)

## Directions

1. Put 1 pint of oysters into kettle and parboil in their own liquor
2. Cook 2 tablespoonfuls of butter with 1 tablespoonful of flour
3. Add 1/2 cupful of oyster liquor and 1/2 cupful of cream
4. Put the oysters into it and place to heat
5. Add 2 beaten yolks of eggs
6. Season with salt, pepper and lemon
7. Serve on toast or in patty shells
8. Stewed oysters may be mixed with chopped mushrooms
