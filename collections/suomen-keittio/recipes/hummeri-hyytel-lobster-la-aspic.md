## Ingredients

- fresh boiled lobsters or canned lobsters
- fish stock

## Directions

1. Prepare fresh boiled lobsters or canned lobsters.
2. Make jelly of fish stock in the same way as for Salmon à la Aspic.
3. Place the lobsters cut in pieces into an individual mold, laying the nicest pieces on the bottom and on the sides.
4. Pour the jelly over and let get firm.
