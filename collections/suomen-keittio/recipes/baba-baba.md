## Ingredients

- 1 cup scalded milk
- 1 cake yeast
- 4 cups flour
- 1/2 grated rind of lemon
- 6 or 8 eggs
- 1 cup butter
- Salt and sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar
2. Add the milk, lemon rind, yeast, flour and butter rubbed until creamy or melted
3. Beat hard, set to rise
4. Knead on a board and put into mold to rise again
5. Bake in a moderate oven
6. When done, baste the top with mixture made of sugar, brandy and water
7. When cool, coat with 1 cup of wine mixed with 1/2 pound of sugar
