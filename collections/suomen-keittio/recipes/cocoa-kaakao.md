## Ingredients

- 5 cupfuls of milk
- 3½ tablespoonfuls of cocoa
- ¾ cupful of sugar
- a pinch of salt
- 1 cupful of boiling water
- whipped cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Heat 5 cupfuls of milk
2. Mix 3½ tablespoonfuls of cocoa, ¾ cupful of sugar and a pinch of salt
3. Pour 1 cupful of boiling water into the mixture and let boil
4. Add the boiling milk and let boil 1 minute
5. If desired, it may be whisked over hot water for 5 minutes
6. Serve with whipped cream or without
