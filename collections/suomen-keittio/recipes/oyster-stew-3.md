## Ingredients

- 1 quart of oysters
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 cupful of cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Strain the oyster liquor through a fine sieve and put on heat.
2. Thicken with butter and flour.
3. Rinse the oysters and put into the hot liquor and let come to a quick boil.
4. Pour the scalded milk and cream gradually into it and season.
