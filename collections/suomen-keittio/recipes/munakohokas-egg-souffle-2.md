## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 2 cupfuls of rich milk
- 4 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 tablesp. of chopped almonds
- 1/2 tablespoonful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs with sugar, add scalded milk and butter and stir over the fire until thick.
2. When cool mix with flour, almonds and whites of eggs, beaten to a firm froth.
3. Brown 2 cupfuls of granulated sugar in a fryingpan, add 2 tablespoonfuls of water and a dash of lemon juice.
4. Pour into a mold turning the mold so as to cover the bottom and the sides of it with the syrup.
5. Pour the mixture into the mold and bake in a pan of water 1 hour.
6. Serve with vanilla sauce.
