## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup flour
- 4 tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 1/2 cups cream
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 pound pineapple

## Directions

1. Cook a paste of the cream and the flour.
2. Add the butter and the yolks beaten with sugar.
3. Cut the pineapple in cubes and add to the paste.
4. Last of all add the egg whites beaten firm.
5. Bake in a buttered mold in a pan of water for 1 hour.
6. Prepare the sauce from the liquid of pineapple by adding lemon juice, sugar and cornstarch.
