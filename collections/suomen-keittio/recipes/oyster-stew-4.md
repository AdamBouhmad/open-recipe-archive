## Ingredients

- 1 quart of oysters
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 cupful of cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- yolks of 2 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Strain the oyster liquor through a fine cloth and place on the fire
2. Thicken with butter and flour
3. Wash the oysters and put into the hot liquor and bring quickly to boil
4. Pour the scalded milk and cream gradually in and season
5. Rub the yolks of two hard boiled eggs through a sieve and add to the soup to improve the taste
6. Serve with sliced lemon and toast
