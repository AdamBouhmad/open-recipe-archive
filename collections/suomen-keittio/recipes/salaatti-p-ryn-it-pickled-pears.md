## Ingredients

- 8 pounds pears
- 7 cups sugar
- ¾ cup water
- 3¾ cups vinegar
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons cloves, cut in pieces

## Directions

1. Pare the pears, leave the stems on.
2. Place alternate layers of pears and sugar into a jar, pour water and vinegar over, let stand over night.
3. Drain the juice, put into a kettle with the spices tied in a bag.
4. Bring slowly to boiling point.
5. Add to the juice the pears, simmer, not allowing them to become too tender.
6. Place the pears carefully into glass jars, pour the juice over.
7. Cover airtight.
