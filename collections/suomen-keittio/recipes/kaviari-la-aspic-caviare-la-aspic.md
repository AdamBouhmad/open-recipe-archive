## Ingredients

- small molds with hole in center
- red pepper
- lentils
- jellied caviare
- toast
- caviare
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of small molds thinly with red pepper and lentils.
2. Fill with jellied caviare.
3. Spread caviare on toast and turn the molds on top of it.
4. Fill the holes with caviare.
5. Serve on lettuce leaves before soup.
