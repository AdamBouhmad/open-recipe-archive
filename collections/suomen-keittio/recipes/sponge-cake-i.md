## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1 cupful of flour
- 4 tablespoonfuls of corn starch
- 4 tablespoonfuls of water
- 1 tablespoonful of brandy
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with sugar until light
2. Add water, brandy, baking powder mixed and sifted with flour and corn starch
3. At the last add the stiff beaten whites of eggs
