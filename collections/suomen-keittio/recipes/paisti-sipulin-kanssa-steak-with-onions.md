## Ingredients

- steak
- salt
- pepper
- butter or lard
- onions

## Directions

1. Heat the frying pan and place the steak in it.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dot with butter or spread with lard.
3. When brown, turn the meat over and continue frying until done.
4. Fry onions separately or with the steak and serve on top of the steak on the serving platter.
