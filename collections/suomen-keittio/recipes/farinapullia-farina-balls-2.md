## Ingredients

- 1 cup farina
- 3 cups milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Cook a porridge of farina and milk
2. Add butter and season with salt and pepper
3. Mix in the egg yolks
4. Let cool
5. Form into balls
6. Roll in bread crumbs
7. Fry in lard
