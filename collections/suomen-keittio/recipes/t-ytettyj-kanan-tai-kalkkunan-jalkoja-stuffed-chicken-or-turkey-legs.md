## Ingredients

- Chicken or turkey legs
- Chicken and veal
- Pork
- Chicken liver
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Bread crumbs
- Milk
- Egg
- Butter
- Water or bouillon
- Celery
- Onion

## Directions

1. Remove the bones carefully leaving only 1 inch of bone in the legs.
2. Make stuffing of chicken and veal, some pork and chicken liver added to it and ground several times.
3. Season with salt, pepper and chopped parsley.
4. Soak some bread crumbs in milk, add 1 egg and some butter.
5. Combine the mixtures rubbing well.
6. Put a spoonful of mixture into each leg, truss.
7. Cook in water or in bouillon with salt, celery, onion and parsley for 1 hour.
8. Remove from the pan, keep under a weight until cool.
9. Fry dipped in egg and bread crumbs or serve cold.
