## Ingredients

- 4 cupfuls of water
- 3 cupfuls of sugar
- 1 grated pineapple
- juice of 2 oranges
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Cook water and sugar 15 minutes
2. When cool, add pineapple and juice of fruits
3. Freeze
