## Ingredients

- remains of roast rabbits
- 3 quarts of water
- 1 onion
- 2 stalks of celery
- 2 sprigs of parsley
- 1 small parsnip
- 2 tomatoes
- 10 peppercorns
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sherry or lemon juice
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the bones and put them in boiling water on fire.
2. Scrape the vegetables, cut into pieces and brown in butter.
3. Then put them to boil with the bones.
4. Add the seasonings parsley and the tomatoes.
5. Let boil slowly for a few hours.
6. Thicken with butter and flour; season and strain.
7. Serve with the meat cut into cubes.
8. The soup may also be served with small potatoes or rice in it.
