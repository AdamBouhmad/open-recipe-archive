## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- egg yolks
- fish
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Make a milk sauce of 1 tablespoon of butter, 1 tablespoon flour and 1½ cups of milk.
2. Season with salt, pepper and onion juice.
3. Add 2 egg yolks last of all.
4. Mix with 2 cups of fish, cut finely with bones removed.
5. Bake in a buttered mold, sprinkled with bread crumbs in a moderate oven until brown.
