## Ingredients

- 1 cabbage
- 1 cupful of rice
- 2 cupfuls of water
- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 eggs
- salt, pepper, a dash of sugar

## Directions

1. Cook rice in water mixed with milk until tender
2. Boil cabbage in salted water
3. Chop the cabbage
4. Put into a kettle to cook with butter until thoroughly done
5. Mix all above ingredients with the batter of rice
6. Add eggs at the last
7. Pour in a buttered mold
8. Bake about 1 hour
