## Ingredients

- Squab
- Thin slice of bacon
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cream

## Directions

1. Clean the squab, line with a thin slice of bacon and place a little lump of butter in.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, roast for 20 or 30 minutes.
3. Make a gravy of the drippings by adding some cream.
4. Divide lengthwise in two, remove the backbone.
5. Place on a platter and strain the gravy over.
