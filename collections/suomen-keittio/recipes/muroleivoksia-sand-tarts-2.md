## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 1/2 cupful of almonds
- 12 ozs. of flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Peel almonds and chop fine
2. Wash butter and rub to a cream
3. Mix with remaining ingredients stirring well
4. Wipe small molds, cover them inside with a thin layer of batter
5. Put the molds on a baking sheet and bake in a quick oven to light brown
6. Turn the tarts carefully out
