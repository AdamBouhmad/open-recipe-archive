## Ingredients

- 2½ cupfuls of water
- 1 oz. of gelatine
- 1 cupful of sugar
- ½–¾ cupful of strong coffee

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same manner as lemon jelly.
2. Pour into a mold to get firm.
3. Serve with whipped cream.
