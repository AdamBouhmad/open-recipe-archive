## Ingredients

- 2 cups of rhubarb, cut in pieces
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- a little corn starch or flour

## Directions

1. Skin rhubarb and cut in 1/2 inch long pieces
2. Mix with sugar and let stand 1 hour
3. Add a little corn starch or flour, when too smooth
4. Bake between two crusts
