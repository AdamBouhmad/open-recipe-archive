## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- Vanilla or lemon rind
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 1/2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Rub butter with sugar until creamy
2. Add eggs one at a time rubbing the mixture well
3. Mix with vanilla or lemon rind, milk and baking powder sifted with flour
4. Bake in two tins
5. For a plainer cake, use less butter, 1 egg and water instead of milk
6. For a quick cake, beat all the ingredients together in a bowl for 5 minutes
