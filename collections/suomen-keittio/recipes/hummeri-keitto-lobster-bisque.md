## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of fish stock
- 1 lobster or 1 small can of lobster
- 1 tablespoonful of butter or lobster-butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- yolks of 3 eggs
- 1 cupful of cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the tail meat and cook it in fish stock about half an hour and rub through a sieve.
2. Cook the shells separately in a small quantity of water and add then into the soup.
3. Thicken with butter and flour and season with salt and pepper.
4. Beat the yolks, add cream into it, pour the soup slowly in and stir on the fire for a little while.
5. Serve with the meat of claws in it or with lobster balls and tips of asparagus cooked in fish stock until tender.
