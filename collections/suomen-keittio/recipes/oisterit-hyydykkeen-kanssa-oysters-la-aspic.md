## Ingredients

- Oysters
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Aspic made from oyster liquor
- Red pepper
- Jelly

## Directions

1. Boil the oysters quickly with salt, pepper and lemon juice
2. Wipe and put on a platter in two rows
3. Prepare an aspic from the liquor in the pan
4. Baste the oysters with the aspic, having a moderately thick layer covering them
5. Garnish with red pepper, lemon and whipped jelly
