## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Rich milk sauce
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion
- 3 eggs
- Flour
- Butter

## Directions

1. Boil the cabbage, force through a meat chopper.
2. Add rich milk sauce until it becomes a thick porridge.
3. Season with salt and pepper, flavor with chopped fried onion.
4. Mix in 3 beaten eggs.
5. Form into croquettes or patties, roll in flour, brown in butter.
