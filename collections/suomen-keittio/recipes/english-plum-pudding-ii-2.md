## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. of flour
- 3/4 lb. of bread crumbs
- 1 lb. of suet
- 3/4 lb. of raisins
- 3/4 lb. of currants
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1 cupful of molasses
- 1/4 lb. of candied orange rind
- yellow rind of 2 lemons
- 1/2 grated nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful of allspice
- 1 cupful of brandy

## Directions

1. Prepare the mixture as above, cook 6 hours in a napkin.
2. Set aside.
3. Cook 2 hours more just before serving.
4. Serve with wine sauce.
