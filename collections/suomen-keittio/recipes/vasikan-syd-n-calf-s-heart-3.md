## Ingredients

- 1 calf heart
- salted water
- ground meat or bread
- onion
- butter
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- 1 egg
- pork slices
- carrots
- celery
- meat stock
- lemon

## Directions

1. Clean heart and soak in salted water 1 hour to draw out the blood.
2. Fill the heart with mixture made of ground meat or bread mixed with chopped onion cooked in butter, chopped parsley, salt, pepper and 1 egg.
3. Sew up the opening.
4. Place a few slices of pork, carrots, celery and onion in a baking pan.
5. Bake in oven for 2 hours.
6. Baste often with the drippings.
7. Add meat stock if necessary.
8. Cover to prevent burning.
9. Prepare sauce of the gravy in the pan.
10. Season with salt, pepper and lemon.
