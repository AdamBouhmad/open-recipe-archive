## Ingredients

- 1 calf's head
- 1 red onion
- 1 leek
- 1 carrot
- 2 stalks celery
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 10 peppercorns
- 1 cup stewed strained tomato
- 1/2 lemon juice
- 2 cups brown stock

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the split calf's head
2. Place on the fire in boiling water and cook tender, with roots, salt and pepper
3. Remove the head from boiling; cut the meat away and place the bones back to boil again
4. Cut the meat and the tongue, peeled into nice pieces, separating the ugly pieces away and warm in a little strained broth
5. Mince the leek, brown it in butter, add the flour, meat broth and strained calf's head broth and let the mixture come to boil
6. Add the tomato and meat pieces, color with Kitchen Bouquet, season with cayenne pepper, salt, lemon and wine
7. Serve with hard boiled egg yolks, or custard (Royal Custard) cut in cubes, or egg balls (Egg Balls)
