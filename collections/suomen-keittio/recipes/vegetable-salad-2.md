## Ingredients

- 1/2 quart of fresh peas
- 1 cauliflower
- 1/2 quart of string beans
- 3/8 lb. of asparagus
- caper
- cucumber slices
- salted water
- mayonnaise sauce
- lettuce leaves
- parsley leaves

## Directions

1. Clean vegetables and cook in salted water, all separately.
2. Pour off the water and let cool.
3. Arrange the vegetables nicely in a bowl and pour mayonnaise sauce on.
4. Sprinkle with caper and thinly sliced cucumber.
5. Garnish with lettuce and parsley leaves.
