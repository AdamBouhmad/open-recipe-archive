## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salted water
- lettuce leaves
- mayonnaise
- hard cooked eggs
- minced parsley
- olives

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower in salted water until tender
2. Place whole or in parts on a bed of lettuce
3. Cover with mayonnaise
4. Garnish with hard cooked eggs, minced parsley and olives
