## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of spinach leaves
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 cupfuls of milk
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 3 eggs
- a pinch of nutmeg

## Directions

1. Clean and cook spinach as directed above and chop them
2. Mash with butter, flour, milk and spinach stock
3. Add bread crumbs soaked in milk, sugar, salt and nutmeg
4. Lastly add eggs well beaten
5. Butter a mould and pour the mixture in
6. Bake in oven about 1 hour in a pan of boiling water
7. Serve with meat and fish
