## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 4 cups flour
- 1/4 cup brandy

## Directions

1. Rub butter with sugar until creamy
2. Add brandy and flour
3. Form into tiny rings
4. Bake in a hot oven
