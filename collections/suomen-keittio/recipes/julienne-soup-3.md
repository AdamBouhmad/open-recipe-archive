## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of rich bouillon
- 1 small celery
- 1 carrot
- 1 leek
- 1 head of lettuce
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of string beans
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the vegetables and cut them into long strips.
2. Brown in butter.
3. Add the hot bouillon and the string beans into the vegetables and let it boil for a while.
4. When the vegetables are half cooked add the head of lettuce cut into shreds.
5. When all the vegetables are tender season the soup and serve while very hot.
