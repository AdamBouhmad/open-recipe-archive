## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup whipping cream
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 10 cardamom seeds
- 3 cups flour

## Directions

1. Beat to a thick froth the yolks with sugar and cream
2. Add cardamom, soda and flour enough to make a paste thick enough to be rolled on the board
3. Roll thin
4. Cut into strips
5. Form them into tiny flat rings
6. Bake in an oven or cook in fat
7. Sprinkle with sugar
