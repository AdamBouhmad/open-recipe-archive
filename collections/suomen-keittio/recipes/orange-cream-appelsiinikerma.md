## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 2 ounces of sugar
- juice of 1 orange
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice
- 8 leaves or 6 tablespoonfuls of gelatine
- 2 cupfuls of cream
- whipped cream
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Whip yolks and sugar together
2. Add orange and lemon juice and stir on the fire until thick
3. Add dissolved gelatine and let cool
4. Pour whipped cream and whites, beaten stiff and dry, into the mixture
5. Turn in a wet mold
6. Unmold on a serving dish
