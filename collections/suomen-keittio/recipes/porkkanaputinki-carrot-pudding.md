## Ingredients

- 2 pounds carrots
- 2 cups bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 cups cream or milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/4 cup almonds
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Clean and cook carrots in salted water until tender
2. Grate carrots
3. Mix with all ingredients
4. Add eggs beaten last of all
5. Bake in oven in a pan of water
6. Serve in mold or unmolded
