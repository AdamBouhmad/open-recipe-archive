## Ingredients

- Herrings
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour or bread crumbs
- Butter or lard

## Directions

1. Clean the herrings, rinse and wipe.
2. Rub with salt and pepper.
3. Roll in flour or bread crumbs.
4. Fry in butter or lard until brown on both sides.
5. Cover, place the pan in hot oven for a few minutes until the fish is thoroughly done.
6. For salt herring: Soak the fish, wrap in butter paper, then fry.
