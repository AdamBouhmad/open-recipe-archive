## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1½ tablespoons flour
- ½ cup milk or cream
- Chopped parsley or chives
- Salt and pepper
- Hollandaise or tomato sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and milk.
2. Add the yolks beaten well and seasonings.
3. Last of all add the whites beaten stiff.
4. Put into buttered timbale molds.
5. Bake in a pan of water until firm.
6. Serve with Hollandaise or tomato sauce.
7. Chopped parsley may be mixed into the mixture or used to garnish the molds.
