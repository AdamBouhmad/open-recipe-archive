## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- 1 cup vinegar
- 3/4 cup olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Have the cucumbers fresh and green.
2. Peel, cut in thin slices.
3. Make a French dressing of 1 cup vinegar, 3/4 cup of olive oil, salt and pepper.
4. Add to the cucumbers.
5. Sprinkle with parsley.
