## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- cream
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Butter the small dishes.
2. Break the eggs carefully, one into each dish.
3. Sprinkle with salt and bake in oven until the eggs are firm.
4. Alternatively, put a little cream on the bottom of the cups, break the eggs in the cream, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and when half done, sprinkle with grated cheese and bake until firm.
