## Ingredients

- 3 cups boiled mashed potatoes
- 2 salted, skinned and boned herrings
- 1 onion, chopped and fried in butter
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- pepper
- 2 or 3 beaten eggs
- cream if necessary

## Directions

1. Have 3 cups boiled mashed potatoes
2. Chop 2 salted, skinned and boned herrings and add to the potatoes
3. Mix in 1 onion chopped and fried in butter, 3 tablespoons butter, 1/2 cup bread crumbs and a dash of pepper
4. Last of all add to the mixture 2 or 3 beaten eggs
5. Dilute with cream if necessary
6. Form into croquettes
7. Fry or cook in deep fat
