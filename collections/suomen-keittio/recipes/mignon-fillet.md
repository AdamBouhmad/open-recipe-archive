## Ingredients

- fillet beef slices
- salt and pepper
- butter
- Bearnaise sauce
- fried bananas

## Directions

1. Cut fillets into one inch thick slices
2. Pound the slices and trim into round equal-sized cakes
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Fry in butter in a pan
5. Pour Bearnaise Sauce on a serving platter
6. Arrange the fillets on it
7. Garnish with fried bananas
8. Alternatively, the fillets may be broiled about 4-5 minutes
9. Serve with parsley, mushroom or sherry sauce
