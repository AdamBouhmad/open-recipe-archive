## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salted water
- butter
- sugar, 1 teaspoon
- mushroom croquette paste
- cabbage broth

## Directions

1. Cook cabbage in salted water until soft
2. Cut into pieces and fry in butter until browned, adding 1 teaspoon sugar
3. Prepare mushroom croquette paste and form into small balls
4. Cook the balls in deep lard or fry in butter
5. Layer cabbage and mushroom balls in a buttered mold, ending with a cabbage layer
6. Pour some cabbage broth over the top
7. Bake in oven for 1 hour
