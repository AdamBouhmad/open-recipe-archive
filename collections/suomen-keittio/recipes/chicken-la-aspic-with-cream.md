## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of chicken
- ½ tablespoonful or 5 leaves of gelatine
- milk or white beef stock
- 2 cupfuls of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Cut 1 cupful of chicken, pound it well and force through a sieve.
2. Dissolve ½ tablespoonful or 5 leaves of gelatine in milk or white beef stock, stir gradually into the chicken.
3. Add lastly 2 cupfuls of whipped cream.
4. Line the mold inside with aspic, as that will make it easier to remove from the mold.
5. When the aspic is firm fill the mold with chicken cream.
6. Turn onto a serving dish when firm, and serve with salad.
