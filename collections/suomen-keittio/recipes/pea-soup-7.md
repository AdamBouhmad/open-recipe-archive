## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cupfuls of dried peas
- 1 1/2 quarts of bouillon
- 2 1/2 quarts of cold water
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1/2 pound of ham

## Directions

1. Wash the peas and put them into warm water to soak for a few hours.
2. Drain and put the peas and ham into cold water to simmer until the peas are thoroughly soft.
3. Thicken the soup with butter and flour.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Rub through a sieve and add bouillon into it as much as required.
6. Cut the ham into pieces.
