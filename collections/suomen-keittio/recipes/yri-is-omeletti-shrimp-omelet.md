## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup cooked shrimp meat
- butter
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons milk or cream
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Heat 1/2 cup of cooked shrimps cut in pieces with butter in a saucepan.
2. Add 3 eggs beaten with 3 tablespoons of milk or cream.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Shake over the fire until firm.
5. Serve on a platter.
