## Ingredients

- thin slices of smoked salmon
- 1 cup fish aspic
- 1/2 pound caviare
- whipped cream
- gelatine

## Directions

1. Roll into cones thin slices of smoked salmon
2. Work 1 cup of fish aspic to a froth
3. Mix with 1/2 pound of caviare
4. Fill the cones with the caviare mixture
5. Lay on ice to thicken
6. Arrange on a platter
7. Decorate the top with whipped cream mixed with gelatine and caviare
