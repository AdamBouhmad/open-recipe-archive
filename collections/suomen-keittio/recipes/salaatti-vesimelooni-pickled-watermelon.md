## Ingredients

- watermelon (rind removed)
- salt (1 cup per 2 pounds melon)
- 4 quarts water per 2 pounds melon
- 8 cups vinegar
- 4 cups sugar
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons cloves

## Directions

1. Remove the red part of melon
2. Cut the remaining part in squares
3. Weigh the melon, allow 1 cup salt and 4 quarts water to 2 pounds melon
4. Let stand in salt water overnight
5. In the morning put into cold water, let stand for 2 hours
6. Boil 8 cups vinegar with 4 cups sugar, 2 tablespoons cinnamon, 2 tablespoons cloves (the spices tied in a bag) until the liquid becomes clear
7. Add the pieces of the melon, cook until tender
8. Place carefully into glass jars
9. Continue to cook the liquid until reduced some
10. Pour over the melon
11. Cover airtight
