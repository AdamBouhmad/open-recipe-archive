## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 egg
- 1 cupful of water
- 1 teaspoonful of alcohol
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Wash butter in icewater and force the water out by wrapping tightly the butter into a towel.
2. Whip egg, mix with water, lemon juice, alcohol and sifted flour, mixed with 1 teaspoonful of butter.
3. Knead with fingers to an even paste.
4. Roll, place butter, pressed into a flat cake, on the paste.
5. Fold up the sheet of paste to inclose the butter.
6. Put on ice for 3/4 hour.
7. Roll into an oblong square and triple.
8. Turn over and reroll.
9. Triple again.
10. Repeat this operation three times, rolling out twice and folding up three times each time.
11. Set the paste on ice for 15 minutes between each operation.
12. Rerolling has to be made in the opposite direction.
13. Bake in hot oven.
