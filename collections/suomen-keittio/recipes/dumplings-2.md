## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls of butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 3 teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix all dry ingredients and rub butter with fingers into it.
2. Add milk gradually.
3. Roll into a sheet 1/2 inch thick.
4. Cut it into small circles with a round cutter.
5. Place into the kettle to boil on the meat so as not to break them.
6. Serve immediately because the dumplings will fall quickly.
7. Instead of butter, 1/2 cupful of chopped suet may be used, and water instead of milk.
