## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup rich milk
- 5 eggs
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 or 3/4 cups grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Cook a batter of butter, flour and milk.
2. Dissolve the chocolate in a hot pan, add to the hot batter.
3. Mix with the yolks beaten with sugar.
4. At the last add the whites whipped to a firm froth.
5. Fill a well-buttered mold with the mixture.
6. Cover carefully.
7. Bake in oven in a pan of water, or steam on the stove.
8. Instead of chocolate, cocoa or strong coffee may be used.
