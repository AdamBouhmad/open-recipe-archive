## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound macaroni
- Oysters
- Salt and pepper
- Grated cheese
- Butter

## Directions

1. Boil 1/2 pound of macaroni broken in pieces 1 inch long in salted water until tender
2. Strain and rinse well in cold water
3. Fill a buttered mold with alternating layers of macaroni and oysters
4. Sprinkle oysters with salt and pepper
5. Lay macaroni on the top
6. Sprinkle with grated cheese and dot with butter
7. Bake for 20 or 30 minutes
8. Instead of macaroni, spinach or rice may be used
