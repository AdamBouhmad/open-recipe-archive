## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of lean beef boiled and chopped
- 1 pound suet chopped
- 2 quarts apples cut in pieces
- 1 pound currants
- 2 pounds seeded raisins
- 1/2 pound lemon rind cut in pieces
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Juice of 2 oranges
- 1/2 teaspoon mace
- 1 teaspoon each cinnamon, cloves, and allspice
- 1 nutmeg grated
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/4 cups brandy
- 1 cup Sherry wine
- 1 cup old cider

## Directions

1. Mix beef with suet.
2. Add the dry ingredients, then the liquid ones.
3. Simmer until the ingredients become tender.
4. Add the spices.
5. Pack into a jar to stand for a few days before using.
6. If too dry, dilute the mixture with beef stock.
7. Bake in a double crust in a moderate oven.
