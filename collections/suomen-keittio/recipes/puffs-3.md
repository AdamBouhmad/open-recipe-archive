## Ingredients

- 3 ozs. of butter
- 12 tablespoonfuls of milk, mixed with cream
- 5 ozs. of flour
- 1 1/2 ozs. of corn starch
- 6 eggs
- sugar
- jam
- cream

## Directions

1. Boil up butter and milk, mix thoroughly with flour.
2. Let boil until it begins to brown on the bottom.
3. Work one yolk immediately into it, and when the batter is cold, add the remaining egg yolks.
4. Lastly add whites, beaten stiff.
5. Drop with a spoon into boiling lard and cook until well done.
6. Sprinkle with sugar immediately and serve with jam and cream.
