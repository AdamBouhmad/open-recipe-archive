## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1½ cups flour
- 2 large apples
- 4 tablespoons milk
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 3 tablespoons whipped cream
- 3 eggs
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- Some salt
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Pare the apples
2. Cut lengthwise in six or eight parts
3. Slice thinly the parts crosswise
4. Beat the yolks with sugar
5. Add milk, butter, flour, cinnamon, salt and cream
6. Add the sliced apples to the batter
7. Last, fold in the beaten whites
8. Drop by the spoonful into boiling lard
9. Fry until brown
10. Serve with hard sauce
