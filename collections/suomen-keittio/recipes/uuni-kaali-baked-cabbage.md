## Ingredients

- 1 (2½ lb.) cabbage head
- 4–5 tablespoonfuls of brown sugar
- 4–5 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- pinch of white pepper and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Remove old leaves from the cabbage head and scoop it a little from the stem end
2. Put sugar, nutmeg and pepper into the hole
3. Brown butter in a baking dish
4. Put the cabbage head into it and cover
5. Let cook slowly
6. Pour salted water on top
7. Let cook for 2 hours, basting often so as not to burn the surface
