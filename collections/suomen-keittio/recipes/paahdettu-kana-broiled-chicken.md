## Ingredients

- Spring chicken or young chicken
- Melted butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon
- Watercress

## Directions

1. Use only spring chickens for broiling.
2. Divide from the back, draw and remove the backbone.
3. Wipe and rub with melted butter.
4. Broil over a slow fire skin side up until brown.
5. Turn over, brown on the other side.
6. Put in a pan, spread with melted butter, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
7. Place in oven for 15-20 minutes.
8. Arrange on the platter having small chickens cut in halves and the larger ones quartered.
9. Garnish around with lemon and watercress.
