## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 cup flour
- yellow rind of 1 lemon
- butter
- jelly

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks with the sugar until frothy.
2. Add the yellow rind and the flour.
3. Last of all mix with the whites beaten until firm.
4. Cover the inside of a baking pan with buttered paper.
5. Pour the paste evenly on the paper, bake for 15-20 minutes.
6. Unmold on paper sprinkled with sugar.
7. Spread with jelly, roll up.
