## Ingredients

- chicken breast fillets
- 2 scant tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 egg whites
- 1 cup whipped cream
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the fillets from chicken, discard skin and fat
2. Remove fiber with a knife and pound well in a mortar
3. Add gradually the butter and egg whites
4. Rub through a sieve
5. Add slowly the whipped cream to the paste
6. Season with salt and pepper
7. Put into buttered timbale molds
8. Bake in oven in a pan of water covered with buttered paper for 12 to 15 minutes
