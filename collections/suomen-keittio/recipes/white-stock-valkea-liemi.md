## Ingredients

- 1 fowl
- 3 lbs. of knuckles of veal
- 1 quart of water to each pound of meat and bones
- vegetables and seasonings

## Directions

1. Wash a fowl and knuckles of veal.
2. Allow 1 quart of water to each pound of meat and bones.
3. Place all in a kettle, cover and let it slowly come to the boiling point.
4. Add the vegetables and seasonings and let it slowly boil for 5 hours.
5. Strain and let cool uncovered.
