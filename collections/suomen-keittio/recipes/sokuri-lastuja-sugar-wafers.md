## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 1/2 lb. of eggs

## Directions

1. Melt butter and mix with sugar.
2. Rub in a bowl until white.
3. Stir slowly eggs alternately with flour into it, whipping well.
4. Place a thin layer of batter on a well greased sheet and bake in a moderate oven.
5. Cut into pieces 3 inches long and 1 inch wide and roll them hot around a stick.
6. Alternatively, form the batter into round thin cookies and roll together while hot.
