## Ingredients

- bread slices
- butter
- steak
- ham
- tongue
- cheese
- mustard
- pepper
- mayonnaise
- capers
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Fry slices of bread in butter on both sides.
2. Force equal amounts of steak, ham and tongue through the meat chopper.
3. Mix with equal amount of cheese, add a little butter, season with mustard and pepper.
4. Rub until smooth.
5. Spread on the fried slices of bread.
6. Decorate the top with mayonnaise, caper and hard boiled egg.
