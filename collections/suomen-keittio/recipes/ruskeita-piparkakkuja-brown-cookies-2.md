## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 lb. of brown sugar
- 3/4 cupful of cream
- 3/4 cupful of melted butter
- 1/4 lb. of almonds
- 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful of cardamom
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cloves
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 1/2 lbs. of flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs with sugar to a froth, add cream, spices, almonds, blanched and cut in pieces lengthwise and soda mixed with flour.
2. Form the batter into flat strips and bake on greased baking sheet in a moderate oven.
3. Cut the strips hot crosswise about 1 inch long pieces and dry in a cool oven.
