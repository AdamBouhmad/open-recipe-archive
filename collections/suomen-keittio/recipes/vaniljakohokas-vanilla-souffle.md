## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 cupful of rich milk
- 5 eggs
- 2 ozs. of powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla
- fruit cooked with sugar

## Directions

1. Cook a batter of butter, flour and milk.
2. Whip yolks of eggs with sugar to froth, stir into the batter; flavor with vanilla.
3. Lastly add the whites, beaten stiff.
4. Butter a mold, lay fruit on the bottom and sides.
5. Pour the souffle mixture on the fruit.
6. Steam in oven 1/2 hour.
7. Serve hot.
8. The souffle may be cooked also on the stove.
