## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salted water
- lettuce leaves
- mayonnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Cook cauliflower in salted water until tender.
2. Arrange on a serving dish in its original shape.
3. Garnish around with lettuce leaves.
4. Pour mayonnaise sauce over.
