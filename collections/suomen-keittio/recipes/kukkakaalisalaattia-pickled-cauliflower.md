## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt water
- 1 quart vinegar
- 1/2 cup sugar
- cloves
- white pepper, 1 teaspoon each
- ginger
- mace

## Directions

1. Cut cauliflower in small parts
2. Cook in salt water for 10-15 minutes
3. Lay carefully into jars
4. Boil 1 quart vinegar with 1/2 cup sugar
5. Add cloves and white pepper, 1 teaspoon each, some ginger and mace, having the spices tied in a bag
6. Strain the liquid over the cauliflower
7. Cover
