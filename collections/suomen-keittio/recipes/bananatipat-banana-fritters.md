## Ingredients

- Bananas
- Fritter batter
- Lard for frying
- Sugar
- Lemon sauce

## Directions

1. Peel and cut in pieces the bananas
2. Mix in the batter
3. Cook in lard to a light brown
4. Serve with lemon sauce or plain sprinkled with sugar
