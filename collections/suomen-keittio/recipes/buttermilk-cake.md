## Ingredients

- 3 cupfuls of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1 cupful of buttermilk
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 tablespoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Mix all the dry ingredients with butter in a bowl
2. Turn the batter on a floured board
3. Knead diligently
4. Divide the dough into two parts
5. Roll into cakes 1/3 inch thick
6. Flour them
7. Cut the cakes into six equal parts
8. Fry them or bake in oven about 10-15 minutes
9. When frying on the stove the cakes have to be turned over so as to be evenly done on both sides
10. Do not make the batter hard
11. Serve hot
