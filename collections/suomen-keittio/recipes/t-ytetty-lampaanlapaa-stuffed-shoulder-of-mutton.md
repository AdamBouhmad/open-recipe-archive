## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon chopped onion
- Chopped mushrooms
- 1 egg
- Melted butter
- Bouillon to moisten the mixture
- Salt and pepper
- Shoulder of mutton

## Directions

1. Remove carefully the bone from the shoulder and the ribs, taking care not to break the skin side.
2. Rub with salt inside.
3. Stuff the space with the stuffing.
4. Sew up.
5. Roast in oven same as directed for roast mutton.
