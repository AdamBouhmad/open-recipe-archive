## Ingredients

- fresh tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- corn starch
- water

## Directions

1. Put tomatoes in boiling water for few minutes
2. Peel tomatoes
3. Cut in pieces
4. Put in kettle and cook until soft
5. Season with salt, pepper, and butter
6. Thicken with corn starch diluted with cold water
