## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 cupful of butter
- yolk of 1 egg
- 1/2 cupful of water
- a pinch of salt and sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare as puff paste
2. Roll the paste into a crust 1/8 inch thick
3. Lay over the bottom of a pie-plate
4. Perforate and moisten the edges
5. Cut a string of the paste to fasten the crust around the wet pie-plate
6. Spread with egg and bake, placing the plate on a dish, to a light brown
7. Remove the plate carefully and put the pastry into the plate to get cold
