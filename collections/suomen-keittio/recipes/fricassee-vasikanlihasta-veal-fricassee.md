## Ingredients

- 2 pounds veal backstrap
- boiling water
- 2 stalks celery
- 1 small carrot
- salt
- onion slices
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- veal broth
- pepper
- lemon juice
- 2 egg yolks
- cream
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wipe and cut 2 pounds veal backstrap into pieces and pour boiling water over them into a kettle.
2. Add 2 stalks celery, 1 small carrot, salt and an onion slice.
3. Simmer gently covered until the meat is tender.
4. Make a sauce from 3 tablespoons butter, 2 tablespoons flour, strained veal broth, salt, pepper and lemon juice.
5. Beat 2 egg yolks, add some cream and add last to the sauce.
6. Pour the sauce over the meat and garnish with fine parsley.
