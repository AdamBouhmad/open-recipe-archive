## Ingredients

- 4 pounds of mutton
- vegetables
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut mutton into pieces
2. Wipe
3. Put into a kettle and pour over it boiling water
4. Cook slowly with vegetables until the meat is tender
5. Strain
6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
7. Dip in flour and fry in butter
8. Arrange on a platter
9. Make sauce of the liquor of mutton, by carefully removing the fat first
10. Pour the brown sauce upon it
