## Ingredients

- eggs
- grated cheese
- lettuce leaves
- chopped pickles
- tomato
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Cut the eggs in slices
2. sprinkle thickly with grated cheese
3. Arrange on lettuce leaves
4. add a sprinkling of chopped pickles
5. garnish with tomato and mayonnaise
