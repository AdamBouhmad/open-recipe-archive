## Ingredients

- 2 cups grated carrots
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup cream or milk
- 6 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 6 tablespoons flour
- 4 tablespoons melted butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. Mix together the grated carrot, cream, whipped egg yolks, bread crumbs, flour, melted butter and salt
2. Stir well
3. Beat the egg whites to a firm froth and add last
4. Drop by spoonful into boiling fat
5. Cook until brown on both sides
