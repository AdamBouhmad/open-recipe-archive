## Ingredients

- 1 pound rhubarb
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoon cream
- 5 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon grated lemon rind

## Directions

1. Cut the rhubarb in pieces, cook with the sugar.
2. Beat the yolks with powdered sugar, add cream and lemon rind.
3. Beat the whites to a firm froth, add to the yolks.
4. Put one half of the mixture into a buttered mold, lay the cooked and strained rhubarb on the top, cover the whole with the remaining mixture.
5. Bake in oven in a pan of water for 3/4 hour.
