## Ingredients

- Sweetbreads
- Bacon
- Vegetables
- Bouillon
- Tomato or allemande sauce
- Boiled artichokes
- Rice
- Mushroom sauce
- Green peas

## Directions

1. Boil sweetbreads, put under a weight for awhile.
2. Cut lengthwise in two, place in a pan having a slice of bacon on each.
3. Cut some vegetables in pieces, add to the sweetbreads, pour some bouillon over.
4. Bake slowly basting often about 15 minutes.
5. Remove from the pan, cover with tomato or allemande sauce.
6. Arrange boiled artichokes on a foundation of rice.
7. Place the sweetbreads on the top.
8. Serve with mushroom sauce having the sauce in the center of the ring of rice and green peas around on the platter.
