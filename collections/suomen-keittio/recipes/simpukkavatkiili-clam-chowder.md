## Ingredients

- 3 pints fish stock or water
- 1 quart clams
- 1 middle sized onion
- 6 ozs. salt pork
- 1 potato
- 1 carrot
- 1 tomato
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups milk or cream
- Mace
- Crackers

## Directions

1. Place clams on fire in their own liquor, let boil a few minutes.
2. Cut the pork in cubes, fry with chopped onion.
3. Stir flour in it and add the liquor and the fish stock slowly in the pan.
4. Put potato, carrot and tomato cut in cubes in the mixture, boil until they are tender.
5. Add the clams cut in pieces, seasonings, parsley, crackers and the milk.
6. Boil quickly and serve.
