## Ingredients

- 2 cups mashed sweet potato
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 cup cream or milk
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix the mashed potatoes with butter, cream, and yolks of eggs
2. Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
3. Bake in a buttered mold
