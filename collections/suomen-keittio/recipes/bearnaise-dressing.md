## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of olive oil
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice
- 1/2 tablespoonful of caper
- 1/2 tablespoonful of sour pickle
- 1 teaspoonful of parsley
- yolks of 2 eggs
- a pinch of white pepper
- a pinch of salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of hot water
- butter

## Directions

1. Whip the yolks in a saucepan, add gradually the oil, hot water and lemon juice.
2. Whip in a saucepan set in boiling water until it thickens, add seasonings and butter.
3. Serve with fish, chops, roasted meat and cutlets.
