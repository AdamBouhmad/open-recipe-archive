## Ingredients

- boiled chopped mushrooms
- 3 hard boiled eggs
- butter
- lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon anchovy paste
- paprika
- capers
- grated cheese
- buttered toasted bread slices
- caviar
- cucumber

## Directions

1. Make a mixture of boiled chopped mushrooms, yolks of hard boiled eggs, butter worked to a froth, lemon juice, chopped parsley, paprika, caper and grated cheese.
2. Force the mixture through a sieve.
3. Spread on buttered toast.
4. Garnish with caviar and cucumber.
