## Ingredients

- Leeks
- Salted water
- Toast
- Melted butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse and clean the leeks.
2. Cook in salted water until tender.
3. Lay on toast.
4. Cover with melted butter.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
