## Ingredients

- 2 cups whipping cream
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3/4 teaspoon vanilla or some lemon juice
- 1/4 package gelatine
- 1/2 cup water
- Lady fingers or cake slices
- Wine jelly for decoration

## Directions

1. Dissolve the gelatine in 1/2 cup of water
2. Whip the cream to foam
3. Add sugar and vanilla, and gelatine when it is slightly cooled
4. Line the bottom and sides of the mold with long lady fingers or with slices cut from sponge cake, arranged according to the size of the mold
5. Fill the mold with the cream mixture
6. Let it set
7. Turn out of the mold
8. Decorate on top with wine jelly and whipped cream
9. The mold may be filled with various fruit jellies
