## Ingredients

- 2 cups apple sauce
- 4 egg whites
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Prepare as directed for apple snow
2. Allow 2 cups of apple sauce to 4 egg whites
3. Bake in a slow oven for 25 minutes
4. Serve with whipped cream
