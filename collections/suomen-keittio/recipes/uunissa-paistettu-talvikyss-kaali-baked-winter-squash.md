## Ingredients

- winter squash
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut squash in two
2. Remove the mellow insides
3. Bake the shells in oven, covered, for two hours
4. Scrape the insides from the shells
5. Chop
6. Season with salt, pepper and butter
