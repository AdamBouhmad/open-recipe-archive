## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 lbs. of cooked or fried meat
- 1 onion
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 cupfuls of meat stock or sauce
- salt and pepper
- 2 tablespoonfuls of tomato

## Directions

1. Cut meat into dice, chop onion, brown in butter.
2. Mix with meat and flour and continue frying.
3. Add meat stock, seasonings and tomato.
4. Bring to the boiling point.
5. When fried meat is used, it will get tough if boiled too long a time.
