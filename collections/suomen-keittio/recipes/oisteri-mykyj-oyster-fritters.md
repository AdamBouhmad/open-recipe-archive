## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup flour
- 1 pint oysters
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs
2. Add milk, flour, baking powder and finely chopped oysters
3. Add salt
4. Drop by the spoonful into boiling lard
5. Cook until brown on both sides
6. Serve hot garnished with slices of lemon and parsley
