## Ingredients

- 3 cupfuls of white wine
- 2 cupfuls of madeira or sherry
- 2 cupfuls of water
- 1½ ozs. of gelatine
- 7 ozs. of sugar
- 2 lemons
- 2 whites of eggs and shells

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same manner as lemon jelly.
2. If desired, you may use less wine and sugar and more water, so that the taste is milder.
