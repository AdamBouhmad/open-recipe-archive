## Ingredients

- 1 pint mashed potatoes
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg and 1 yolk of egg
- 1/4 cup flour
- 3/4 cup bread crumbs
- nutmeg and pepper
- chopped onion (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix mashed potatoes with butter, salt, beaten eggs and flour.
2. Form into balls and roll in flour.
3. Cook in salted water for 15 minutes.
4. Serve as is, or roll the boiled dumplings in egg and bread crumbs and cook in deep lard.
5. When boiling the dumplings take care not to stop boiling before they are done, otherwise they will fall apart.
