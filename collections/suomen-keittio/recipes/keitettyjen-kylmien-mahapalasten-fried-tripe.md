## Ingredients

- Cold boiled tripe
- Melted butter
- Lemon juice
- Egg
- Bread crumbs or flour
- Butter or lard

## Directions

1. Pour melted butter mixed with some lemon juice over pieces of cold boiled tripe
2. Let stand for an hour
3. Dip in egg and roll in bread crumbs or flour
4. Fry in butter or lard until light brown
5. Serve with Béarnaise or hot mayonnaise sauce
