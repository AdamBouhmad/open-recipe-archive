## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salted water
- butter
- sugar
- mushroom ball mixture
- cabbage broth

## Directions

1. Cook the cabbage in salted water until tender
2. Cut in pieces and brown in butter with 1 teaspoon sugar
3. Prepare mushroom ball paste and form into tiny balls
4. Fry the balls in butter
5. Put into a buttered mold in alternate layers of cabbage and mushroom balls with cabbage layer last
6. Pour some cabbage broth on top
7. Bake in oven for 1 hour
