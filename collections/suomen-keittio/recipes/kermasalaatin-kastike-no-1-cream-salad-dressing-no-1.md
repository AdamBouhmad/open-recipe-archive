## Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup cream or milk
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Mix together all the dry ingredients
2. Add the beaten eggs, lemon juice and cream
3. Beat in a double boiler until thick
4. Remove from the fire, add butter, beat well
5. Let cool
