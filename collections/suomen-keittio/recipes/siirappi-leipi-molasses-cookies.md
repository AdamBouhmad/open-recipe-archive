## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of molasses
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of butter
- 1 cupful of lard
- 1 cupful of cold coffee
- 3 teaspoonfuls of ginger
- 3 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 10 cupfuls of flour
- a dash of salt

## Directions

1. Warm molasses, add lard, butter and sugar, rubbing well.
2. Mix with coffee, spices and flour.
3. Roll into a thin sheet and cut with a round cutter into small cakes.
4. Bake in a quick oven.
