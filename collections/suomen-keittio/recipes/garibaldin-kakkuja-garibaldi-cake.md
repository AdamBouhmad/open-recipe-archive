## Ingredients

- 4 cupfuls of flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1¼ cupfuls of milk
- 1½ teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix butter well with flour in a bowl
2. Add sugar and baking powder
3. Pour milk slowly into it, stirring carefully
4. Knead the batter on a board
5. Form into round cakes
6. Divide into six parts
7. Brush over with egg
8. Bake in oven for 15–20 minutes
9. Serve hot
