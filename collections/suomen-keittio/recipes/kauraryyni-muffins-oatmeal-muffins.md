## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup boiled oatmeal
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ¾ cup milk
- 2 eggs
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Sift salt, sugar and baking powder with flour.
2. Mix the oatmeal with the milk, beat the eggs until frothy and add to the oatmeal.
3. Add gradually all this to the flour stirring well.
4. Bake in buttered muffin tins in a moderate oven.
