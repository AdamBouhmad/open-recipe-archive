## Ingredients

- 1 pound veal
- 1 egg
- 4 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- water or bouillon
- cream

## Directions

1. Cut the veal crosswise into slices and pound them.
2. Rub the butter until foamy and mix with parsley and pepper.
3. Spread the mixture on the veal slices, roll together and tie with string.
4. Dip in egg and bread crumbs or in flour only.
5. Fry in butter or lard until brown.
6. Add water or bouillon and cook for about 1/2 hour until done.
7. Make a gravy of the drippings by adding cream.
