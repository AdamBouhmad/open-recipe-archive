## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of bread crumbs
- ½ cupful of chicken stock
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- a dash of nutmeg and pepper
- chicken liver and heart

## Directions

1. Soak breadcrumbs in chicken stock
2. Add parsley, eggs finely chopped, liver and heart
3. Add a dash of nutmeg, pepper and salt
4. Mix well
