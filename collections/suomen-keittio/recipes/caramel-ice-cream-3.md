## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of granulated sugar
- ½ cupful of water

## Directions

1. Brown 1 cupful of granulated sugar in a pan.
2. Mix with ½ cupful of water stirring until the sugar is dissolved.
3. Take care not to burn the sugar only let it get delicate brown.
4. Use this caramel instead of vanilla and sugar in vanilla ice cream base.
