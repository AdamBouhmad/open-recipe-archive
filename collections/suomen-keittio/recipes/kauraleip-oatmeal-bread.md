## Ingredients

- 2 cups oatmeal
- 1 quart boiling water
- 1/4 cup molasses
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 yeast cakes
- 2 quarts flour

## Directions

1. Bring the water to boil and add the oatmeal
2. When cool, add salt, molasses and the risen sponge of yeast
3. Work in white flour enough to make a dough
4. Keep in a warm place until risen
5. Knead on the table and put into tins to rise
6. Bake for an hour in a hot oven
