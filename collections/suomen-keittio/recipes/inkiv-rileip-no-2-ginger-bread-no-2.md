## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1/4 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- Cinnamon and mace

## Directions

1. Rub until creamy the butter with the sugar, add egg, milk and the dry ingredients sifted with flour.
2. Bake in a shallow tin in a moderate oven.
