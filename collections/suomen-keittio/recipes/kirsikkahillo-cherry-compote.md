## Ingredients

- Cherries
- Sugar equal in weight to cherries
- 2 cupfuls of water to 5 lbs. of sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse cherries and stone.
2. Allow 2 cupfuls of water to 5 lbs. of sugar.
3. Simmer sugar with water until clear syrup threading when tested by dropping.
4. Skim carefully.
5. Place the cherries into the syrup.
6. Heat slowly.
7. Simmer on a slow fire, shaking the kettle and skimming until the scum ceases to rise.
8. When the cherries are clear and the scum no longer rises, pour the compote into jars.
9. If the syrup is not of right consistency, continue cooking for a while.
10. Pour over the berries when cooled.
