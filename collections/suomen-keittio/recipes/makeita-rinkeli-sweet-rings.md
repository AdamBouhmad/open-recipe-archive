## Ingredients

- 1 glass of egg
- 1 glass of sugar
- 1 glass of milk
- 1 glass of melted butter
- 1 cake of yeast
- wheat flour to make a moderately thick mixture
- egg for brushing
- coarse sugar
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as for ordinary tea-ring.
2. Let it rise and shape small rings from it.
3. When risen, brush them with egg.
4. Sprinkle coarse sugar and chopped almonds on top.
5. Bake in the oven for about 10 minutes.
