## Ingredients

- 1 pint clams
- 2 cups crackers
- clam liquor
- milk
- salt
- paprika
- lemon juice
- 3 egg yolks
- clam shells
- grated cheese
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop a pint of clams
2. Soak 2 cups of crackers in clam liquor and milk mixed in equal parts
3. Season the soaked crackers with salt, paprika and lemon juice
4. Add the chopped clams and 3 egg yolks to the mixture
5. Stuff the clam shells with this mixture
6. Sprinkle with grated cheese and bread crumbs
7. Dot with butter
8. Bake in a hot oven until the bread crumbs are brown
9. Alternatively, the mixture may be baked in a buttered serving mold and served in the same dish
