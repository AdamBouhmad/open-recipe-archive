## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1 cup thin cream
- Yellow rind of 1 lemon
- Juice of 5 oranges
- 3/4 package of gelatine
- 1 1/2 cups whipping cream

## Directions

1. Bring to boil the thin cream with the lemon rind
2. Beat the yolks with the sugar
3. Add gradually the scalded cream
4. Beat over the fire until thick
5. Let cool
6. Mix with the orange juice and the gelatine
7. Fold in the whipped cream
8. Fill the baskets with this mixture
9. Have oranges of equal size
10. Remove the peel of half an orange leaving 1/4 inch wide strip in the middle for the handle of the basket
11. Remove the pulp
12. Notch in points the edges of the baskets
