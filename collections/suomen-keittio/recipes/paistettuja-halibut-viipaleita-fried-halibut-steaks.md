## Ingredients

- Halibut steaks
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Melted butter
- Flour
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Butter or lard for frying
- Béarnaise sauce, lemon sauce, or tomato sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Rub the pieces of fish with salt, pepper and lemon juice on both sides.
2. Let stand for half an hour.
3. Wipe, spread with melted butter, roll in flour, egg and bread crumbs.
4. Fry in butter or lard.
5. Alternatively, roll the pieces in flour mixed with salt and pepper and fry.
6. For broiling: prepare the fish as directed above. Broil over clear fire, sprinkle with salt and pepper, place in a pan, dot with butter, bake for a few minutes.
7. Serve with Béarnaise, lemon or tomato sauce.
8. For steamed halibut: rub the pieces of fish with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
9. Place in a buttered pan, sprinkle with fried chopped onion, cover.
10. Cook in oven for 20-30 minutes.
11. Lift onto a platter and cover with rich milk sauce.
