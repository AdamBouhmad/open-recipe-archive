## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1½ cupfuls of thick cream
- 1½ cupfuls of water
- ¾ cupful of melted butter
- ½ cupful of sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. Whip cream to a stiff froth.
2. Beat eggs with sugar until light.
3. Combine both mixtures.
4. Mix with water, butter and last flour enough to make a thick batter.
5. Bake as directed.
