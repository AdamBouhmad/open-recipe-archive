## Ingredients

- 1 ounce chocolate
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 7 leaves or 1/2 package of gelatine
- 1 cup milk
- 2 cups cream

## Directions

1. Scald the milk with the grated chocolate.
2. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar, add gradually the scalded milk.
3. Beat over the fire until thick.
4. Add the dissolved gelatine, let cool.
5. When the mixture begins to harden (it must be free from lumps) add the whipped cream.
6. Mold.
7. Serve unmolded with whipped cream.
