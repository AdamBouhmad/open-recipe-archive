## Ingredients

- lentils
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- cream (optional)
- onion
- parsley

## Directions

1. Boil the lentils until tender
2. Press through a sieve
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Mix with butter
5. Dilute with cream if desired
6. Make a little cavity on top of the mound
7. Fill with fried chopped onions
