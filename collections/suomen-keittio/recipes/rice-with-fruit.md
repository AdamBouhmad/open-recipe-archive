## Ingredients

- rice
- milk
- cream
- vanilla
- sugar
- wine
- two leaves of gelatine
- two egg yolks
- cooked fruit
- pounded almonds
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook rice in milk mixed with cream until tender, flavor with vanilla, sugar and wine
2. Dissolve two leaves of gelatine in milk and add it to the rice
3. At the last mix it with two yolks of eggs
4. Put the rice on a serving dish, garnishing around with cooked fruit
5. Force a few fruits through a sieve and pour this mash on the serving dish
6. Alternatively, cook the rice in water, basting with sweetened boiled water, flavored with 1 teaspoonful of vanilla
7. Stir pounded almonds slightly into it
8. Put in oven for a few minutes until the sweetened water is absorbed
9. Place on a serving dish and baste with fruit sauce
