## Ingredients

- 3 quarts bouillon
- 5 or 8 beets
- 2 or 3 tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Rinse beets and roast them in the oven until tender.
2. Peel and cut them into small pieces and sauté in butter.
3. Add a few drops of lemon juice or vinegar to preserve the color.
4. Add flour to the beets and pour boiling bouillon gradually into them.
5. Let the soup boil for about 10 minutes.
6. Peel tomatoes, chop them finely and add to the beets.
7. Season with salt and pepper.
