## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of sugar
- 1/2 cake of yeast
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 3 cupfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Warm milk mixed with salt and sugar
2. Add yeast and a part of the flour into it
3. Let rise
4. Mix with melted butter and the rest of the flour, kneading well
5. Put to rise and form into rolls
6. Let rise again and bake about 15-20 minutes
