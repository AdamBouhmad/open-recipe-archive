## Ingredients

- 3/4 cupful of butter
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 4 eggs, beaten until light
- 3 cupfuls of flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 1 1/2 tablespoonfuls of ginger

## Directions

1. Work butter with sugar to a cream.
2. Add beaten eggs.
3. Mix in flour combined with baking powder and ginger.
4. Bake in small molds.
