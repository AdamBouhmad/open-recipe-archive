## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- 2 cups milk
- 1/2 yeast cake
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 1/2 cups buckwheat flour
- 2 tablespoons molasses

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in lukewarm water
2. Add salt and buckwheat flour
3. Let rise over night
4. In the morning stir in slowly the scalded milk and molasses, so as to make a thin batter
5. 1/2 teaspoon of soda may be added to the batter to make them rise better
6. Fry on griddle until brown on both sides
