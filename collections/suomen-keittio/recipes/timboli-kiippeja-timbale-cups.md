## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon melted butter or olive oil
- Fat for heating the timbale iron

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup flour, 1 teaspoon salt, and 1 teaspoon sugar
2. Beat 1 egg and add 1 cup milk
3. Pour into the flour mixture and beat well
4. Add 1 tablespoon melted butter or olive oil last
5. Heat the timbale iron in fat until hot
6. Dip the iron into the batter, leaving a little of the top edge bare
7. Place the iron into the hot fat and fry until the batter is brown and comes away easily
8. If the cups do not look crispy, the batter is too thick and needs more milk added
