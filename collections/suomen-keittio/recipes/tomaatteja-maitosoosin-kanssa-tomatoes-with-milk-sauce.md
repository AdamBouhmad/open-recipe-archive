## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter
- milk sauce

## Directions

1. Cut tomatoes into slices
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Dip in flour
4. Fry in butter
5. Place in hot dish
6. Pour loose milk sauce over top
