## Ingredients

- 2 cups lobster cut in pieces
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 small onion
- 4 cups milk
- 2 tablespoons cracker crumbs
- Salt
- Paprika

## Directions

1. Cook the butter with onion (chopped) in a saucepan for 5 minutes.
2. Strain the butter, add flour and cracker crumbs, milk, lobster meat and season.
3. Boil quickly and serve.
