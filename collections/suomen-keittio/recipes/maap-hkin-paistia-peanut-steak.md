## Ingredients

- 1 cup shelled peanuts
- 1 cup boiled lentils
- 1 cup lima beans or brown beans
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- milk or tomato liquor
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Have 1 cup of shelled peanuts, 1 cup of boiled lentils, 1 cup of lima beans or brown beans.
2. Boil and force through a meat grinder.
3. Add 1 cup of bread crumbs soaked in milk or liquor of tomato.
4. Add 4 tablespoons butter and 2 beaten eggs.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Form into a loaf.
7. Bake, basting often.
