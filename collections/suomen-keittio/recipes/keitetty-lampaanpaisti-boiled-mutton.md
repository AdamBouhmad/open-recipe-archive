## Ingredients

- mutton
- water
- vegetables
- salt
- pepper
- caper sauce

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat and place in a pot.
2. Pour boiling water over it and let it come to a boil quickly.
3. Add vegetables, salt and pepper.
4. Let simmer slowly under cover until tender.
5. Serve with caper sauce and vegetables.
