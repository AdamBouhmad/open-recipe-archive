## Ingredients

- 4 hard boiled eggs
- grated cheese
- anchovy essence
- melted butter
- milk sauce
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Shell and split lengthwise 4 hard boiled eggs
2. Remove the yolks and mix them with grated cheese, anchovy essence and melted butter
3. Fill the halves of whites with this mixture
4. Butter a baking dish and spread some of the paste on the bottom
5. Arrange the eggs on top
6. Cover with milk sauce
7. Sprinkle with cheese and bread crumbs
8. Brown in oven
