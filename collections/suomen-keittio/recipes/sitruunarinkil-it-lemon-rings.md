## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 5 tablespoons sugar
- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 tablespoons cream
- Grated yellow rind of 1 lemon
- Flour enough to make a paste

## Directions

1. Beat yolks with sugar
2. Add cream, butter, lemon rind and flour to make a paste thick enough to handle
3. Form into small rings
4. Bake on a buttered baking sheet until light brown
