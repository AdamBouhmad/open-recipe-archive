## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups milk
- ½ cup cream
- Salt and white pepper

## Directions

1. Cook butter, flour and milk together
2. Add cream, pepper and salt
3. For use as Hollandaise sauce: mix with 1 teaspoon of lemon juice and yolk of egg
