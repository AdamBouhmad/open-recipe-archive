## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- salt water
- 1 quart vinegar
- 3/4 pound sugar
- white pepper, 1 teaspoon
- cinnamon, 1 teaspoon
- cloves, 1 teaspoon
- ginger, 1 teaspoon

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms
2. Parboil in salt water
3. Lay into jars
4. Bring to boil vinegar with sugar and spices
5. Cool
6. Strain over the mushrooms
7. Cover
