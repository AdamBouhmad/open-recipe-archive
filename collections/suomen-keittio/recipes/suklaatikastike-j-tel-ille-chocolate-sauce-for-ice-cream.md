## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 ounces chocolate
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1 cup boiling water
- 2 cups sugar
- Vanilla

## Directions

1. Melt 1 or 2 ounces of chocolate in a dry pan set in a double boiler.
2. Add 3 tablespoons of sugar and 1 cup of boiling water gradually stirring well.
3. Add 2 cups more of sugar and let boil for a little while stirring well.
4. Flavor with vanilla.
5. Serve cold.
