## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- Butter, lard or chicken fat

## Directions

1. Place the chicken on the fire in boiling water, add salt, pepper and a tablespoon of vinegar.
2. When half done, lift into the roasting pan, spread with butter, lard or chicken fat and brown.
3. If becoming too brown, cover and add water to the drippings when necessary.
4. Baste often.
5. Make a gravy of the drippings in the pan.
