## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Milk sauce
- Bread crumbs
- Egg
- Butter or lard for frying

## Directions

1. Mix fried chopped mushrooms with milk sauce until a moderately thick batter forms
2. Form into croquettes
3. Turn in bread crumbs, dip in egg and turn in bread crumbs again
4. Fry in butter or lard
