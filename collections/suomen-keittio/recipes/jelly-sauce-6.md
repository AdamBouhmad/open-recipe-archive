## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of corn starch
- 1 cupful of boiling water
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 3/4 cupful of jelly

## Directions

1. Mix sugar with corn starch.
2. Add slowly 1 cupful of boiling water and let boil 5 minutes.
3. Remove from fire and add butter and jelly.
4. Keep in double-boiler until it has to be served.
