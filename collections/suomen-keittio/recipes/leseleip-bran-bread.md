## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 2 cups bran
- 2 cups white flour
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons soda

## Directions

1. Mix together bran, white flour, soda and salt
2. Add milk and molasses
3. Pour the mixture into a buttered pan having the pan filled 4 inches high
4. Bake for 1 hour
