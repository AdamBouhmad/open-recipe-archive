## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of cooked, mashed peas
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cupful of milk
- 1/2 teaspoonful of baking powder
- 5 tablespoonfuls of flour
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Mix butter, seasonings, milk, beaten eggs and flour with mashed peas
2. Form into thin cakes
3. Bake in oven
4. Serve instead of vegetables with meat or fish
