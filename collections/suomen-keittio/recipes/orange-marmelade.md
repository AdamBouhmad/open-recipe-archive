## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of oranges
- 2 lemons
- 5 lbs. of granulated sugar
- 1 1/2 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Wipe oranges and lemons carefully.
2. Peel and remove the white part from inside of peels.
3. Slice yellow rinds and put to simmer for 1/2 hour with 1 1/2 cupfuls of water.
4. Cut the fruit in pieces, remove seeds.
5. Place in kettle to simmer for 1/2 hour with 1 1/2 cupfuls of water.
6. Pour on a tight cloth to drain without stirring.
7. Simmer the drained juice mixed with sugar, sliced and cooked rinds of fruit and their own liquor for 15 minutes stirring constantly.
8. Place the marmalade in small jars and preserve as directed.
9. Serve with tea and toast.
