## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 1/4 cupfuls of sugar
- 1 1/4 cupfuls of melted butter
- 1 lemon
- 2 tablespoonfuls of brandy
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 1/4 cupfuls of corn starch

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs with sugar to a froth
2. Mix with melted creamed butter
3. Add lemon rind and juice, brandy and corn starch
4. Lastly add the beaten whites
5. Bake in a buttered mold
