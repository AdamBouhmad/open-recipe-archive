## Ingredients

- long pieces of fine macaroni or spaghetti
- salted water
- butter
- cooked chicken or veal, chopped
- milk sauce
- 3 or 4 beaten eggs
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Cook long pieces of fine macaroni or spaghetti in salted water.
2. Put in cold water to keep them whole.
3. Place on a straight cloth to cool.
4. Butter well a dome shaped mold.
5. Put the macaroni lengthwise around the inside of the mold.
6. Fill the mold with cooked chicken or veal chopped and mixed with milk sauce.
7. Add 3 or 4 beaten eggs just before putting into the oven.
8. Bake in a pan of water, covered with a buttered paper, about 30 minutes.
