## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 cupfuls of rich milk
- salt and pepper
- 2 tablespoonfuls of chopped parsley
- a little onion juice

## Directions

1. Cook a sauce of butter, flour and milk
2. Add well beaten yolks and seasonings
3. At the last mix with the whites beaten to a firm froth
4. Bake in a souffle dish until set
5. Serve with milk sauce and vegetable balls
