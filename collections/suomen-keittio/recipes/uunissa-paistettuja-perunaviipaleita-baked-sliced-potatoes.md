## Ingredients

- 1½ quarts potatoes
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 tablespoons cheese crumbs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Pepper
- Water

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes, cut into slices
2. Lay into a buttered mold having salt, pepper, dots of butter and cheese crumbs between the layers of potato
3. Add a little water
4. Bake covered until done
5. Instead of cheese, slices of onion or tomato may be used
