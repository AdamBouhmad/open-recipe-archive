## Ingredients

- 2 cups lobster, cut in fine pieces
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- chopped parsley
- 1 cup milk sauce
- 2 hard boiled egg yolks or 1 raw egg yolk
- bread crumbs
- egg
- lard or butter

## Directions

1. Season 2 cups of lobster, cut in fine pieces, with salt, pepper, some lemon juice and chopped parsley.
2. Mix with 1 cup of milk sauce and 2 hard boiled egg yolks or one raw egg yolk.
3. Place on ice for a while.
4. Form into cutlets.
5. Roll in bread crumbs, dip in egg and roll in bread crumbs again.
6. Fry in deep lard.
7. Serve with tartare or tomato sauce.
8. Alternatively, the cutlets may be rolled in flour and fried in butter.
