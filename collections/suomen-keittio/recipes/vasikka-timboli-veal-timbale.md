## Ingredients

- 1 pound veal
- 2 ounces butter
- 2 eggs
- 1.5 cups cream
- 3 tablespoons bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- macaroni

## Directions

1. Remove fibers from meat and put through meat chopper three times
2. Pound well
3. Add gradually the butter and egg yolks, rub until smooth
4. Soak bread crumbs in milk and mix with the meat
5. Last of all add the egg whites beaten firm
6. Butter the molds and garnish with macaroni
7. Fill with the mousse
8. Bake in oven in a pan of water covered with buttered paper for an hour
9. Serve with tomato or brown sauce
