## Ingredients

- 1 cupful rice
- Water
- 2 quarts bouillon
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup cream
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Parboil 1 cupful of rice.
2. Drain and cook in 2 quarts of bouillon until tender.
3. Force through a sieve, return to the fire, dilute with meat stock if necessary.
4. Beat 2 egg yolks with ½ cup of cream, add the bouillon gradually to the yolks.
5. Reheat, season with salt and pepper.
6. May be prepared without yolks and cream.
