## Ingredients

- timbale molds
- butter
- chopped parsley or truffle
- eggs
- salt
- tomato sauce, bechamel sauce, or rich milk-sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Butter carefully timbale molds.
2. Sprinkle with chopped parsley or truffle on the sides and bottom.
3. Break one egg into each mold.
4. Sprinkle with a little salt and put a little dot of butter on top.
5. Bake in a pan of water until moderately firm.
6. Turn out upon a platter.
7. Serve with tomato, bechamel or rich milk-sauce.
