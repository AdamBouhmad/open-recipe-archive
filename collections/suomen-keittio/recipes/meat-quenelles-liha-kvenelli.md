## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound chicken or veal meat
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter sauce
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls breadcrumb
- 2 tablespoonfuls milk or beef stock
- 1/2 onion
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Grind the meat very fine.
2. Cook the finely minced onion in butter.
3. Add to the mixture an egg, breadcrumb soaked in beef stock, salt and pepper.
4. Stir the mixture well until smooth.
5. Form into small balls.
