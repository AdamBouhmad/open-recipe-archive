## Ingredients

- Hard boiled eggs
- Fried chopped mushrooms
- Thick milk sauce
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bread crumbs
- Egg for dipping
- Butter for frying

## Directions

1. Chop hard boiled eggs fine.
2. Add a few fried chopped mushrooms, thick milk sauce and season with salt and pepper.
3. When cold, form into balls.
4. Roll in bread crumbs and dip in egg, then roll in bread crumbs again.
5. Fry in butter in a pan or bake in the oven.
