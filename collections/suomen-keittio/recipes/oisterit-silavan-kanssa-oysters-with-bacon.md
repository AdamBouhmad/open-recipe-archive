## Ingredients

- Large oysters
- Thin slices of bacon
- Flour

## Directions

1. Choose large oysters and wipe dry
2. Roll each oyster into a thin slice of bacon
3. Fasten with a toothpick
4. Dredge with flour
5. Fry quickly in a pan
6. Serve very hot
