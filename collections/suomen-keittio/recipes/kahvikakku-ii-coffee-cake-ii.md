## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of butter
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of sugar
- 1 cupful of molasses
- yolks of 5 eggs
- 1 cupful of coffee
- 1/2 cupful of raisins, cut in pieces
- 1/2 cupful of currants
- 1/4 cupful of lemon rind, finely chopped
- 3 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 3 cupfuls of flour
- 3/4 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 3/4 teaspoonful of cloves
- 3/4 teaspoonful of mace
- 3/4 teaspoonful of nutmeg

## Directions

1. Work sugar and butter to a froth
2. Add beaten yolks of eggs, molasses, coffee, spices, floured fruit and baking powder mixed with flour
3. Bake in a deep mold
