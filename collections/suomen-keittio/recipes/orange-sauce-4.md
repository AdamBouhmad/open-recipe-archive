## Ingredients

- juice and rind of 2 oranges
- 2 teaspoonfuls of corn starch
- 3/4 cupful of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of currant jelly or juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Mix sugar with corn starch.
2. Add orange juice and grated rind, then currant jelly.
3. Stir on the fire until it is thick.
