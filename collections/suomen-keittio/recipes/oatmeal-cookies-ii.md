## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of oatmeal
- 3/4 lb. of flour
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of milk
- 1 1/4 lb. of butter
- 2 teaspoonfuls of salt
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Boil milk with oatmeal
2. Add salt and sugar
3. When cool mix with melted butter and flour sifted with baking soda and cream of tartar
4. Roll into a thin sheet
5. Take with cutter small round cakes
6. Lay on a buttered mold
7. Bake to a light brown
