## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 6 eggs
- 1 1/2 lbs. of flour

## Directions

1. Wash butter and work to a cream with sugar
2. Add slowly eggs into it, whipping well
3. Lastly mix with flour
4. Force through a pastry bag on baking sheet cookies in shape of S
5. Bake about 10 minutes in a moderate oven
