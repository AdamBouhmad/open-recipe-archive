## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 3 ozs. of almonds
- whites of 2 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of flour

## Directions

1. Wash butter and sift flour onto a pastry board
2. Place butter in small pieces on the flour
3. Add almonds and egg whites unbeaten
4. Mix all ingredients well together into a smooth batter
5. Roll out batter
6. Cut small cookies with a cutter
7. Brush with egg white
8. Sprinkle chopped almonds and crystal sugar on top
9. Bake to a light brown in a moderate oven
