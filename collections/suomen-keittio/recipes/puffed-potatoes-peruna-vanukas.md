## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of potatoes
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 1 small tablespoonful of salt
- 4 eggs
- 8 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Peel potatoes, cook and mash.
2. Add butter, salt, cream, flour and egg yolks.
3. Beat egg whites and add last.
4. Bake in oven to light brown.
