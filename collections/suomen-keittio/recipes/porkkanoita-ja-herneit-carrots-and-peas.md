## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Water or bouillon
- Salt
- Cooked green peas (equal amount to carrots)
- Pepper
- Butter
- Chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel and rinse the carrots, cut into tiny cubes.
2. Cook in slightly salted water until tender, or use bouillon instead of water.
3. Drain, mix with equal part of green peas, season with salt and pepper, add butter and a sprinkling of chopped parsley.
