## Ingredients

- Tomatoes of equal size
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion juice
- Chopped cucumber (if preferred)
- Mayonnaise
- Cream
- Dissolved gelatine (optional)
- Lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Stick tomatoes into boiling water so as to remove the skin easier.
2. Cut off the tops and scoop the insides.
3. Place the tomatoes top down on ice.
4. Mix tomato with salt, pepper and onion juice.
5. Add chopped cucumber if preferred.
6. Mix with a dressing made of equal parts of mayonnaise and cream.
7. Put into a mold, pack in ice and salt.
8. Fill the tomatoes with the frozen mixture, arrange on a bed of lettuce.
9. If some dissolved gelatine is used in the mixture, it will freeze quicker.
10. Can be served on a bed of lettuce having the tomatoes chopped and added to the mixture.
