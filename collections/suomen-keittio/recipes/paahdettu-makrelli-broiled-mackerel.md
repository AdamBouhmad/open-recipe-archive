## Ingredients

- mackerel
- butter or olive oil
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak the fish, wipe and spread with butter or olive oil and lemon juice.
2. Let stand for a while.
3. Broil on a slow fire, basting with butter often.
4. Serve with tomato or milk sauce.
