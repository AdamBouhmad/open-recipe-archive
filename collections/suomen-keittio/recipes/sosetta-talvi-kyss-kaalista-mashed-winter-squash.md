## Ingredients

- winter squash
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the squash, cut in large pieces and remove the seeds
2. Put in oven in a frying-pan covered for 2 hours, or if uncovered add a dot of butter to prevent from drying
3. Remove the inside with a spoon, mash
4. Add butter, salt, pepper, milk or cream
5. Rub well
6. Alternatively: peel, remove the seeds, cut in pieces, put in boiling salt water, cook until tender, mash and prepare as stated above
