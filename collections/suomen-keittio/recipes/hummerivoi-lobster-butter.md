## Ingredients

- Shells, fat and small claws of boiled lobster
- 1.5 pounds butter
- 2 quarts boiling water

## Directions

1. Pound shells, fat and small claws of boiled lobster
2. Mix with 1.5 pounds of butter
3. Cook on a slow fire for 1 hour
4. Add 2 quarts of boiling water to the butter
5. Continue to cook for 1/2 hour
6. Place to cool
7. Next day skin the fat
8. Melt it again and pour into a small jar for future use
