## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiled mashed turnips
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons almonds
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Add to the mashed turnips butter, sugar, salt, pepper, blanched and grated almonds, bread crumbs and eggs beaten.
2. Put into a buttered mold, sprinkle with bread crumbs.
3. Bake until the top becomes brown.
4. Serve with mutton or pork.
