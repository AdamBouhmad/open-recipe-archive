## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon cardamom pounded fine
- 1 yeast cake
- ¾ cup milk
- 6 tablespoons melted butter
- Flour

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as given for coffee cake.
2. Let rise, knead and form into small rings.
3. Bake at once on a buttered baking sheet.
