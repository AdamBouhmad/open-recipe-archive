## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons orange juice
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- grated yellow orange rind
- 1 cup confectioner's sugar

## Directions

1. Have 2 tablespoons orange juice and 1 teaspoon lemon juice and some grated yellow orange rind
2. Add 1 cup confectioner's sugar little by little
3. Stir until smooth
