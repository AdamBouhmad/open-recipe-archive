## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 quarts of potatoes
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 3 tablespoonfuls of grated cheese
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- pepper
- tomato slices (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel potatoes and cut in slices.
2. Butter a baking pan.
3. Lay the slices into the pan, sprinkle with salt, pepper, small pieces of butter and cheese between the layers.
4. Dot the surface with bits of butter.
5. Bake in oven until done.
6. Between the layers of potato slices tomato may be added.
