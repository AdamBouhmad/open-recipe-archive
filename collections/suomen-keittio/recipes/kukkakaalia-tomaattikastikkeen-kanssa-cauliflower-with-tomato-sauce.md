## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salted water
- tomatoes
- flour
- water
- butter
- salt
- sugar
- pepper
- cream
- baking soda

## Directions

1. Boil the cauliflower in salted water until tender
2. Cut tomatoes in pieces
3. Boil tomatoes in a saucepan until tender
4. Thicken with flour mixed with some water
5. Add butter, salt, sugar and pepper
6. May be mixed with cream and a pinch of baking soda
