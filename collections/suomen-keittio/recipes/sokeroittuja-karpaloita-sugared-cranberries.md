## Ingredients

- powdered sugar
- white of an egg
- cleaned dried cranberries

## Directions

1. Warm sifted powdered sugar on paper or in a pan
2. Beat the white of an egg until firm
3. Dip cleaned wiped cranberries in the egg white
4. Drain in a colander
5. Roll in heated sugar until all are white and coated with sugar
6. Keep in a warm place until the sugar has stuck to the cranberries
