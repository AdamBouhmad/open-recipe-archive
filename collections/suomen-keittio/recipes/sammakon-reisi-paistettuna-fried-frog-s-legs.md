## Ingredients

- frog's legs
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- egg
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Skin, clean and wipe the frog's legs.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Dip in bread crumbs, whipped egg and again in bread crumbs.
4. Fry in butter or cook in lard.
