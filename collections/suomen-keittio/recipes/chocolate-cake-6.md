## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- 4 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls of grated chocolate
- 2½ cupfuls of flour
- ¼ cupful of almonds
- ½ teaspoonful of baking soda
- whites of 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Cream butter with sugar
2. Add soda dissolved in cream
3. Add blanched and chopped almonds
4. Add chocolate and flour
5. Fold in whites beaten to a froth
6. Turn in a buttered mold and bake in a moderate oven
