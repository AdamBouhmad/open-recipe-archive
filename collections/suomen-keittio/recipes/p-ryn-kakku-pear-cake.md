## Ingredients

- 1 quart pears
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- yellow grated rind of 1 lemon
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, core and cook until tender with 1 cup of sugar.
2. Cook a paste of milk, flour and butter.
3. Add the yolks beaten with sugar and lemon rind.
4. Finally mix with the beaten egg whites.
5. Lay the pears on the bottom of a buttered mold.
6. Pour the paste over and bake for 3/4 hour.
