## Ingredients

- 2 cups mashed sweet potatoes
- 3 tablespoons butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 or 2 yolks of egg
- Chopped parsley (optional)
- Bread crumbs
- Egg for dipping
- Lard for frying
- Syrup for basting (optional)

## Directions

1. Add butter, salt, pepper and yolks of egg to mashed sweet potatoes.
2. Beat well.
3. Chopped parsley may be mixed last of all, if preferred.
4. Form into balls or croquettes, roll in bread crumbs, dip in egg and roll in bread crumbs again.
5. Cook in deep lard.
