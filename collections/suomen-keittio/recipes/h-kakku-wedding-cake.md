## Ingredients

- 2 cups butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 4 cups flour
- 12 eggs
- 2 or 4 pounds currants
- 2 or 4 pounds raisins cut in pieces
- 1 pound almonds blanched and chopped
- 1/2 pound each candied lemon and orange peel
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon cloves and cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 cup brandy

## Directions

1. Rub the butter with the sugar until creamy
2. Add the yolks of the eggs beaten to a froth
3. Add spices, raisins, currants and candied peel chopped and all rolled in flour
4. Add brandy, soda and flour
5. Fold in the egg whites beaten until firm
6. Bake for 3 or 4 hours
7. If the surface is becoming too brown, cover with paper
