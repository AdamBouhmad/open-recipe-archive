## Ingredients

- Sweetbreads
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Soak the sweetbreads in cold water for an hour, changing the water several times so as to draw the blood.
2. Put on the fire in cold water, cook for 20 minutes.
3. Return to cold water, remove the fiber and the skin.
4. When half cold, place under a light weight to make them even.
