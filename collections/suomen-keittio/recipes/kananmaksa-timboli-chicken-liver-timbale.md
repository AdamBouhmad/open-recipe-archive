## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked chopped chicken liver
- 2 tablespoons bread crumbs soaked in milk or cream
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- 3 egg whites
- butter for molds

## Directions

1. Have 1 cup of cooked chopped chicken liver.
2. Add 2 tablespoons bread crumbs soaked in milk or cream, 1 tablespoon butter, 3 yolks of egg, salt, pepper and some onion juice.
3. Last of all mix with 3 whites of egg beaten firm.
4. Put into buttered molds.
5. Bake in oven in a pan of water covered with buttered paper for 15 minutes or until firm.
