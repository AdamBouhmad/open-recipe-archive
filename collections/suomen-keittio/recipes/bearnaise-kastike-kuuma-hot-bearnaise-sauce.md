## Ingredients

- 2 yolks of egg
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice or vinegar
- 1 tablespoon hot water
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of egg
2. Add gradually the oil, the water and the lemon juice
3. Put the bowl into boiling water and continue beating the sauce until it thickens
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Serve with fish, chops or broiled meats
