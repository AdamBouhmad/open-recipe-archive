## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound macaroni cut into pieces
- salted water
- 1/2 onion
- 1/2 green pepper
- 1 small carrot
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 quart fresh tomatoes or 2 cups canned tomatoes
- 1/2 cup water or bouillon
- salt
- 4 tablespoons chopped mushrooms
- cheese

## Directions

1. Boil macaroni in salted water for 20 minutes
2. Drain and rinse with cold water
3. Brown butter with onion, green pepper and carrot cut in pieces
4. Thicken with flour and stir well
5. Add fresh tomatoes and water or bouillon
6. Season with salt
7. Let simmer for 30 to 40 minutes
8. Strain the sauce
9. Add chopped mushrooms to the sauce and cook for 5 minutes
10. Mix with the macaroni
11. Sprinkle with cheese
