## Ingredients

- 1 salt herring or 2 fresh ones
- 1 cup chopped potato
- 2 eggs
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons cream

## Directions

1. Soak the fish, skin and bone, force through a meat chopper.
2. Chop the onion, fry in butter.
3. Soak the bread crumbs in cream, add the beaten eggs.
4. Mix the herring with onion, eggs and bread crumbs.
5. Form into cakes or balls, dip in egg and bread crumbs, or roll in flour only.
6. Fry in butter or lard.
7. Serve with any tart sauce.
