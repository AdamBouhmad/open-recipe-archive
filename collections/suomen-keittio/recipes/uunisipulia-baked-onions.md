## Ingredients

- Spanish onions
- salt
- water
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Boil Spanish onions in salt water for 15 minutes or until half done
2. Lay in a pan, split the tops
3. Stick a dot of butter in the split; sprinkle with salt
4. Pour some water into the pan and add a dot of butter or lard
5. Bake until tender
6. Ordinary larger onions of equal size may be prepared the same way
