## Ingredients

- 4 sardines
- 1 dessertspoonful of flour
- 1/2 cupful of water
- 2 ozs. of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of anchovy paste
- milk
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cut 6 thin slices of bread, spread with butter and brown in oven.
2. Bone and skin the sardines.
3. Mix the sardines, butter and anchovy to a smooth paste.
4. Put flour, water and milk in a saucepan on the fire, stir until it comes to a boil.
5. Add the sardine butter to it.
6. Lay on the pieces of bread, garnish with hard-boiled eggs, and serve hot on a folded napkin.
