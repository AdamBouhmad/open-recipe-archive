## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- fried chopped mushrooms
- thick milksauce
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- egg
- lard

## Directions

1. Chop hard-boiled eggs fine.
2. Add a little fried chopped mushrooms and thick milksauce.
3. Mix with a little salt and pepper.
4. When cold form into balls.
5. Dip in breadcrumbs and egg and again in breadcrumbs.
6. Cook in lard.
