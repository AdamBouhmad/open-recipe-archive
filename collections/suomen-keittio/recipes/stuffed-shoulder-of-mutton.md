## Ingredients

- shoulder of mutton with bone and ribs
- salt
- 1 cupful of bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoonful of chopped onion
- 6 chopped mushrooms
- melted butter
- 1 egg
- meat stock to moisten the mixture
- pepper

## Directions

1. Remove carefully the bone from the shoulder and the ribs taking care not to break the skinside
2. Rub inside with salt
3. Mix together bread crumbs, chopped parsley, chopped onion, chopped mushrooms, melted butter, egg, meat stock, salt and pepper
4. Fill the space with the mixture
5. Sew up
6. Bake in oven same as baked mutton usually
7. Instead of mushrooms oysters may be used for stuffing
