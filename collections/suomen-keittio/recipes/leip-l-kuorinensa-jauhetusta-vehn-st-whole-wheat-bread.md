## Ingredients

- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 yeast cake
- 3 cups whole wheat flour
- white flour

## Directions

1. Mix together milk, water, salt and sugar, when lukewarm add the risen sponge of yeast and flour.
2. Let rise in a warm place for 3 hours.
3. Work in white flour enough to make a moderately thick dough, form into a loaf, place in a baking tin to rise for an hour.
4. Bake in a moderate oven for 1 1/2 hours.
5. May be made by using whole wheat flour only.
