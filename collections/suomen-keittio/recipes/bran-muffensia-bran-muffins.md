## Ingredients

- 2 cups bran
- 1 cup white or graham flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 tablespoons molasses
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix together all the dry ingredients
2. Add the egg beaten to a froth with milk and the molasses
3. Mix well
4. Bake for 50 or 60 minutes in buttered muffin tins
