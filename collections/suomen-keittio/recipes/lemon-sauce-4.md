## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 cupfuls of milk or cream
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- minced parsley

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of 3 eggs in a saucepan
2. Add butter
3. Add milk or cream
4. Place in a pan of hot water and whip until thick
5. Season with salt, pepper, lemon juice and minced parsley
6. Serve with fish
