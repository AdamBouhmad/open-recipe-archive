## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 1/2 cupful of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- large oysters
- butter or lard
- lemon or parsley
- Tartar or lemon sauce

## Directions

1. Mix 1 egg with 1/2 cupful of milk and 1/2 cupful of flour mixed with 1/2 teaspoonful of baking powder and 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
2. Wash and wipe large oysters
3. Dip in batter
4. Cook in butter or lard
5. Serve on a folded napkin garnished with lemon or parsley
6. Serve with Tartar or lemon sauce
