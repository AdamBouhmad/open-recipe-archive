## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of water
- 2 cupfuls of strawberry puree pressed through a sieve
- a little vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water 10 minutes.
2. When it has cooled, add strawberry puree pressed through a sieve and a little vanilla.
