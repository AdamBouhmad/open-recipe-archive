## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat until firm egg whites
2. Beat yolks with sugar
3. Add to the yolks vanilla and flour
4. Fold in the whites last of all
5. Drop by the spoonful on a buttered baking sheet
6. Sprinkle lightly with sugar
7. Bake in a slow oven until light brown
