## Ingredients

- 1 lemon
- 1 tablespoonful of bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Beat yolks and sugar to a froth.
2. Stir grated yellow lemon rind, juice and bread crumbs into it.
3. Lastly add the stiff beaten whites.
4. Pour the mixture in a well buttered individual mold.
5. Steam in oven about 20-30 minutes.
6. Serve hot in the mold.
