## Ingredients

- summer squash
- chopped onion
- butter or lard
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- parsley
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. Cut the squash in nice pieces
2. Bake in oven until tender
3. Remove the inside with a spoon and mash
4. Fry a tablespoonful of chopped onion in butter or lard
5. Add 1/2 cup of soaked bread crumbs, salt, pepper and the squash
6. Cook for a few minutes
7. Add 2 beaten eggs and rub well
8. Fill the shells with this mixture
9. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
10. Brown in oven
11. Serve garnished with parsley
12. The mixture may be baked in a mold, some milk or cream added to it
