## Ingredients

- chicken livers
- butter
- tomato slices
- salt
- pepper
- chicken mousse
- truffle sauce

## Directions

1. Fry the livers with butter.
2. Place on slices of tomato sprinkled with salt and pepper.
3. Cover with chicken mousse.
4. Bake in oven covered with buttered paper for 10-12 minutes.
5. Serve with truffle sauce.
