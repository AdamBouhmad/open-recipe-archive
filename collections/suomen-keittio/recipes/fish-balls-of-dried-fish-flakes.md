## Ingredients

- dried fish flakes
- boiled potatoes
- butter
- eggs
- flour
- lard or butter for frying
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Soak the fish in water if it is too salty.
2. Cook and let cool.
3. Make a mixture of equal quantities of boiled fish and hot mashed potatoes.
4. Mix with 2 tablespoonfuls of butter.
5. Add 1-2 eggs to it.
6. Form into balls or cakes.
7. Roll in flour, put in the icebox until the next morning.
8. Fry in lard or in butter.
9. For nicer appearance, first dip in egg and roll in breadcrumbs.
