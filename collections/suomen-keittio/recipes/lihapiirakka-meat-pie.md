## Ingredients

- Boiled slices of onion
- Carrots
- Turnip
- Celery
- Cold meat, fried or boiled
- Salt
- Pepper
- Brown gravy
- Pie crust or tomato slices
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Line a pie plate with boiled slices of onion, carrots, turnip and celery cut in pieces
2. Place a layer of cold meat fried or boiled cut in cubes on the vegetables
3. Fill the dish with alternate layers of vegetables and meat
4. Pour a brown gravy over
5. Cover with pie crust or lay slices of tomato on top
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
7. Bake for 1 hour
