## Ingredients

- 1 pound small carrots of uniform size
- 1 tablespoonful salt
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- butter
- 1 tablespoonful minced parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the carrots and place in boiling water to cook.
2. Add salt and sugar and let simmer until tender.
3. Pour off the water and place butter in a saucepan.
4. Return the saucepan to the stove to heat it up for a few minutes.
5. Add minced parsley if desired.
