## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound pork
- 1/4 pound veal
- 6 tablespoons milk sauce
- 4 tablespoons cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Grind the meat several times.
2. Add the milk sauce, cream, pepper and salt to it, rubbing well.
