## Ingredients

- 2½ lbs. of beef removed from bone
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- ¼ teaspoonful of pepper
- 1 onion
- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 3 tablespoonfuls of tomato
- 1 teaspoonful of mustard
- 4 cupfuls of bouillon or water

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into cubes and fry in butter until brown
2. Add chopped onion, seasonings and flour
3. Continue frying for a little while
4. Pour the stock over
5. Let simmer covered until the meat is tender
6. Mix with strained tomato and a little cream
7. Bring to the boiling point
8. Serve
