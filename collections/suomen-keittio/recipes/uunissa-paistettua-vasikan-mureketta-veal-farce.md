## Ingredients

- 2 pounds veal
- 1/4 pound suet or butter
- 2 ounces bread crumbs
- 1 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- salt and pepper
- macaroni
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Grind the veal.
2. Soak the bread crumbs in the milk, add egg and seasonings.
3. Mix with the veal.
4. Rub well.
5. Boil the macaroni in salted water, drain; add some butter and grated cheese.
6. Line the bottom and edges of a buttered mold with the macaroni.
7. Fill with meat.
8. Bake for 1 hour.
