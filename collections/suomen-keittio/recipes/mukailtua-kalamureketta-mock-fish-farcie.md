## Ingredients

- 3 artichokes
- 6 potatoes
- 2 parsnips
- 1 celery
- 1 onion
- 3 tablespoonfuls flour
- 3/4 cupful cream
- 6 ounces butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 cupful bread crumbs
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil all vegetables and grind them together
2. Add beaten eggs, bread crumbs soaked in cream, butter, flour and seasonings
3. Rub the mixture well
4. Bake in a buttered mold placed in a pan of water, covered with buttered paper
5. Serve with tomato or hollandaise sauce
