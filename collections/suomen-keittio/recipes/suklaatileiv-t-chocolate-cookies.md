## Ingredients

- 3 egg whites
- 6 tablespoons sugar
- 1½ tablespoons grated chocolate
- Vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites to a firm froth.
2. Continue to beat for 15 minutes.
3. Sift slowly the powdered sugar and chocolate into the whites.
4. Beat together.
5. Form into small cookies on a buttered and floured baking sheet.
6. Let harden in a slow oven.
7. Grated cocoanut may be used instead of chocolate.
