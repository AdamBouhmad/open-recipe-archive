## Ingredients

- pie crust
- split strawberries
- sugar
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Cover the piedish with crust
2. Fill with split strawberries, soaked an hour in sugar and juice of half a lemon
3. Place the upper crust on
4. Bake in oven
