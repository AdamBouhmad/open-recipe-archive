## Ingredients

- 5-6 apples
- 1 cupful of tapioca
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare and core 5-6 apples and fill the cores with sugar
2. Put in a buttered mold
3. Cook 1 cupful of tapioca and season with a little salt and sugar
4. Pour tapioca on the apples and bake in oven until the apples are softened
5. Serve with whipped cream
