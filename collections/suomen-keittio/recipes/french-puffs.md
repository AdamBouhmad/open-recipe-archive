## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1/2 cupful of flour
- 2 eggs
- powdered sugar
- whipped cream
- fruit juice

## Directions

1. Put butter and milk into a saucepan, place on the fire; when boiling add flour stirring well to an even paste.
2. When it begins to brown underneath remove the saucepan from the fire and set to cool.
3. Add first into the paste the beaten yolks, then whites, beaten stiff.
4. Form into balls by forcing through a pastry bag on a buttered paper.
5. Cook in lard.
6. Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
7. Serve with whipped cream, mixed with fruit juice.
