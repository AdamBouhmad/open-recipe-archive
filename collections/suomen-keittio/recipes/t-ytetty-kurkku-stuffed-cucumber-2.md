## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- chopped chicken or any meat
- boiled ham in equal parts to meat
- bread crumbs, half as much as meat
- egg
- beef stock
- butter for pan
- 1 cupful beef stock

## Directions

1. Split cucumbers lengthwise in two
2. Scoop out the insides
3. Make filling of chopped chicken or meat and boiled ham in equal parts
4. Add bread crumbs, half the amount of meat, moistened with egg and beef stock
5. Fill cucumber halves with mixture
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
7. Place in buttered pan
8. Pour 1 cupful of beef stock over them
9. Bake covered with buttered paper for one hour or until done
10. Arrange on serving dish
11. Prepare sauce from the cooking liquid
