## Ingredients

- 1½ pound spinach
- ¾ pound white bread
- ¼ pound butter
- 2 cups milk
- 2 eggs
- Salt
- Sugar
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Soak the bread in milk.
2. Clean the spinach and boil.
3. Separate the nicest leaves and grind the remaining leaves.
4. Add to the bread the melted butter, chopped spinach, eggs and spices.
5. Stir the mixture well.
6. Place some of the mixture on the spinach leaves, roll up and bake covered in a buttered pan for 30 minutes.
7. Arrange on a platter, serve with a sauce made of melted butter and hard boiled eggs chopped.
