## Ingredients

- 1 pint of boiled chopped meat
- 1 red onion
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1/2 tablespoonful of pepper
- 1 cupful of rice
- 1 quart of milk
- 1 egg
- 1 cupful of beef stock

## Directions

1. Chop onion and fry in butter with meat.
2. Add seasonings.
3. Cook rice and milk until a smooth paste and let cool.
4. Mix with the beaten egg and salt.
5. Stir browned meat and beef stock into the mixture.
6. Turn in a buttered mould and sprinkle with bread crumbs.
7. Bake in oven.
