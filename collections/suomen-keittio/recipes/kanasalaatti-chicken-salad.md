## Ingredients

- boiled or roast chicken
- celery
- salt
- pepper
- mayonnaise
- lettuce
- hard boiled eggs
- pickles
- capers
- olives

## Directions

1. Cut boiled or roast chicken in small pieces.
2. Add half as much celery cut in pieces.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Mix with mayonnaise.
5. Place into a salad bowl on a bed of lettuce.
6. Cover with mayonnaise.
7. Garnish with hard boiled egg, pickles, caper and chopped olives.
