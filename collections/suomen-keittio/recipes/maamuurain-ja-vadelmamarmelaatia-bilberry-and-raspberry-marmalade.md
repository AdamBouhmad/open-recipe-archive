## Ingredients

- 2.5 quarts of bilberries
- 2 quarts of raspberries
- 2.5 lbs. of crystal sugar

## Directions

1. Put all berries into a kettle on the fire and cook so long that a little tested on a plate becomes firm.
2. Put marmalade into jars and preserve as usual.
