## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cupfuls of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of butter
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cupful of milk
- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 1/4 teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar to a froth, add yolks of eggs and continue rubbing a little while.
2. Mix with milk, flour, soda and cream of tartar.
3. At the last add whites beaten stiff.
4. Bake in small molds or form into layer cakes.
