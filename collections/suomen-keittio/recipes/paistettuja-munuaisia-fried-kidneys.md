## Ingredients

- beef, mutton, or veal kidneys
- salted water
- bacon slices, smoked
- flour
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoon catsup
- toasted bread
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove the fat and white center from the kidneys carefully.
2. Soak in salted water for an hour.
3. Cut kidneys in halves.
4. Fry a few slices of smoked bacon in a pan, then remove from the pan.
5. Fry the kidneys in the bacon drippings for 10 minutes.
6. Add some flour, 1 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce and 1 teaspoon of catsup to the drippings.
7. Place the kidneys on toast upon a platter, pour the gravy over and lay the slices of bacon around the toast.
8. Alternatively, cut kidneys crosswise into slices, dip in egg and bread crumbs and fry in butter.
