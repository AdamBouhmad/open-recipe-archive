## Ingredients

- fish
- melted butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley butter
- watercress
- lemon

## Directions

1. Split the fish in back, rinse, wipe dry, spread with melted butter.
2. Heat the broiler and grease.
3. Have the fire clear and hot for small fish, more moderate for large fish so as to have the inside done before the outside will scorch.
4. The fish should be first broiled on flesh side.
5. If there is danger of burning, lay the broiler on a pan in oven to finish the cooking.
6. Put the fish on a warm platter, spread with butter or parsley butter, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
7. Garnish with watercress and lemon.
