## Ingredients

- pinaatti (spinach)
- 1 ruokalusikallista voita (1 tablespoonful of butter)
- ½ teelusikas sipulia (½ teaspoonful of onion)
- ½ ruokalusikallista jauhoja (½ tablespoonful of flour)
- 1 ruokalusikallista sieniä hienonnettuna (1 tablespoonful of minced mushrooms)
- ½ kupillista lihalientä (½ cupful of meat stock)
- suolaa, muskottipähkinää (salt, nutmeg)
- 1 kupillista hienonnettua pinaattia (1 cupful of minced spinach)
- 1 munanruskuainen (1 egg yolk)
- keitetyt makaroonit (cooked macaroni)

## Directions

1. Cook the spinach and mince it.
2. Heat 1 tablespoonful of butter with ½ teaspoonful of onion.
3. Add ½ tablespoonful of flour and 1 tablespoonful of minced mushrooms.
4. Add ½ cupful of meat stock.
5. Season with salt and nutmeg.
6. Add 1 cupful of minced spinach and let it boil.
7. Add 1 egg yolk.
8. Butter small timbale molds or one large mold.
9. Cut cooked macaroni into ¾ inch pieces, shaped into rings to line the edge of the mold.
10. Fill the mold with the spinach mixture.
11. Bake in oven in a pan of water until it feels firm.
12. Turn out onto a plate and serve with tomato sauce.
