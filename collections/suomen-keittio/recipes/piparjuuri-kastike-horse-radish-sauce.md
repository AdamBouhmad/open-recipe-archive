## Ingredients

- 2 tablesp. of grated horse-radish
- 1/2 tablespoonful of vinegar
- 4 tablespoonfuls of rich cream
- a little salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Mix horse-radish with vinegar, salt and pepper
2. Add the whipped cream last
3. Alternatively: Prepare a sauce of 2 tablespoonfuls of butter, 2 tablespoonfuls of flour and 2 cupfuls of fish stock
4. Add 3 - 4 tablespoonfuls of grated horse-radish, a dash of lemon juice, sugar and salt
5. At the last mix with yolk of 1 egg, if preferred
6. Hollandaise sauce may be mixed with horse-radish if desired
