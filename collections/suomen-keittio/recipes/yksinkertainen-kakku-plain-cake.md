## Ingredients

- 1½ cups butter
- ¾ cup sugar
- ½ cup milk
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1½ cups flour

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar together until creamy.
2. Add egg yolks beaten to a froth.
3. Add milk, baking powder and flour sifted together.
4. Add vanilla.
5. Last, fold in stiffly beaten egg whites.
6. Beat the mixture well.
7. Bake for 30 minutes.
8. Dissolved chocolate mixed with milk or 2 tablespoons cocoa sifted with flour may be used in this cake.
