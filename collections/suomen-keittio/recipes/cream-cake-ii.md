## Ingredients

- 3 3/4 cupfuls of thick cream
- 6 eggs
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 10 ozs. of flour
- a little grated lemon rind

## Directions

1. Beat cream and half of the sugar to a froth
2. Whip yolks of eggs and the rest of the sugar until light
3. Combine both mixtures
4. Stir grated lemon rind, flour and lastly the whites, beaten to a stiff froth, into it
5. Pour the batter in a carefully buttered mold and bake in a slow oven about 3/4 hour
