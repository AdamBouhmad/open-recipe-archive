## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 1 beaten egg
- Flour
- Salt

## Directions

1. Mix melted butter with beaten egg.
2. Add enough flour to keep together.
3. Season with salt.
4. Drop by the spoonful into boiling bouillon.
5. The consistency should be moderately thin, about that of a thin porridge.
