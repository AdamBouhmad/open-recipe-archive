## Ingredients

- new potatoes or cold boiled potatoes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 pint scalded milk
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- bread crumbs and grated cheese (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut new potatoes into dice or boil whole potatoes in salted water until done.
2. Drain and cover with milk sauce made of 2 tablespoons of butter, 2 tablespoons of flour and 1 pint of scalded milk.
3. Season with salt, pepper and parsley.
4. If cold boiled potatoes are used, cut the potatoes in pieces and boil slowly in rich milk.
5. Add 1 tablespoon of butter, salt and pepper and just before serving sprinkle with minced parsley.
6. Alternatively, bake potatoes in a buttered mold sprinkled with bread crumbs and grated cheese, using half the amount of flour in the sauce, covering until potatoes are nearly done (about 40 minutes), then remove cover, sprinkle cheese and bread crumbs or 2 beaten eggs on top, and bake until browned.
