## Ingredients

- ham
- rye flour
- water
- bread crumbs
- brown sugar
- egg yolk
- cloves
- cider

## Directions

1. Soak the ham and clean well with a brush
2. Make a thick paste of rye flour and water
3. Spread the paste over the ham taking care not to leave any openings
4. Place in a roasting pan in a hot oven
5. Roast slowly for 5 hours
6. Shortly before removing from oven, make a little hole in the paste and pour some of the liquor from the pan into the hole, repeating several times
7. When done, remove the paste and the skin
8. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and brown sugar
9. Place in the oven until brown
10. Alternatively, spread the ham with yolk of egg, sprinkle with sugar and bread crumbs and dot with cloves
