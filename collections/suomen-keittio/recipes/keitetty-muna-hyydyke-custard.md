## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/4 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of vanilla
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Put milk on heat and let boil.
2. Beat egg yolks with sugar, pour boiling milk gradually into eggs, beating well.
3. Beat still over water until it thickens, flavor and let cool.
4. If desired, two stiffly beaten egg whites may be added just before serving.
5. Boiled custard may also be flavored with wine and almonds.
