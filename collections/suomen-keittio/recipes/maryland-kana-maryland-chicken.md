## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter
- milk sauce
- smoked bacon slices
- corn fritters

## Directions

1. Clean 2 chickens and cut in pieces.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
4. Fry in butter or roast for 1/2 hour.
5. Pour milk sauce on a serving dish.
6. Arrange the pieces of chickens on the sauce.
7. Have slices of fried bacon on top of the meat and corn fritters around.
