## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of veal
- 1 egg
- 4 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- veal stock or butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Cut veal crosswise into slices; pound them.
2. Cream the butter, and mix with parsley and a little pepper.
3. Lay this mixture on the slices of veal, roll together, fasten with cord.
4. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
5. Fry in butter until brown.
6. Pour veal stock or butter over and let boil 1/2 hour.
7. Make sauce from the stock by adding a little cream.
