## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 1/2 lemon rind and juice
- 4 tablespoonfuls of water
- 3/4 cupful of sugar

## Directions

1. Beat yolks with sugar
2. Add lemon juice and rind, then water
3. Stir on the fire in double-boiler until thick
4. Let cool
5. Mix with stiff beaten whites
6. Bake on an open pie
7. When firm, cover with Meringue, if desired
