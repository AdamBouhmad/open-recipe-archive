## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 cups rich milk
- 3/4 cup flour
- salt
- sugar
- yellow rind of 1 lemon, grated
- 1/2 pound asparagus, cut in pieces and cooked in salted water
- egg whites beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Rub butter until creamy
2. Add egg yolks one by one
3. Add rich milk, flour, salt, sugar, and grated lemon rind
4. Add asparagus cut in pieces and cooked in salted water
5. Last of all add the egg whites beaten stiff
6. Bake in a buttered mold in a pan of water. Chopped asparagus may be used for soufflé, if preferred.
