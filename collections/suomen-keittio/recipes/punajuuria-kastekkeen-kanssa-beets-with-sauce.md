## Ingredients

- beets
- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 tablespoonful of corn starch
- 1/2 cupful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook beets until tender and cut into slices.
2. Prepare a sauce of 1/2 cupful of sugar, 1/2 tablespoonful of corn starch, 1/2 cupful of vinegar and let boil 5 minutes.
3. Pour the sauce over the beets.
4. Place the saucepan aside on the stove.
5. Let stand 1/2 hour.
