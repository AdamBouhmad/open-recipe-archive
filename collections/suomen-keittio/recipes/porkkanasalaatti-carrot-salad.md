## Ingredients

- raw carrots
- chopped almonds or nuts
- lettuce leaves
- mayonnaise or cream dressing

## Directions

1. Grate raw carrots
2. Mix with chopped almonds or nuts
3. Place on a bed of lettuce
4. Cover with mayonnaise or cream dressing
