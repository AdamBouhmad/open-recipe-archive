## Ingredients

- 6 potatoes of uniform size
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- cream or milk
- eggs

## Directions

1. Wash well 6 potatoes of uniform size.
2. Bake in oven 1 hour or until tender.
3. Serve on a folded napkin.
4. Or split the potatoes lengthwise in halves.
5. Scoop out the insides and mix this with 2 tablespoonfuls of butter, a pinch of salt and pepper and 2 tablespoonfuls of cream or milk.
6. Fill the potatoes with the mixture and bake in a quick oven.
7. Beaten whites of 2 eggs may be added into the mixture to make it smooth.
8. For paprika potatoes, the filling may be mixed with paprika.
