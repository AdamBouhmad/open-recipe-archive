## Ingredients

- artichokes
- lemon
- salted water
- Hollandaise sauce or melted butter

## Directions

1. Strip off outer leaves, remove stalks and trim off tops of inner leaves.
2. Rub the bottom with lemon.
3. Immerse into boiling salted water, having the stalk ends uppermost.
4. Cook until tender.
5. Remove from the water quickly.
6. Serve with Hollandaise sauce or melted butter.
7. Artichokes may be served cold with salad using French dressing or tartare sauce.
