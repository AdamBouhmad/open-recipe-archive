## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup water
- 4 ounces chocolate
- vanilla for flavoring
- 1/2 cup water or milk (for filling)
- 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch (for filling)
- salt (for filling)
- butter (for filling)

## Directions

1. Boil 2 cups of sugar in 1 cup of water until it threads when dropped from a spoon
2. Dissolve in a dry pan 4 ounces chocolate
3. Add gradually the syrup to the chocolate
4. Stir until smooth
5. Flavor with vanilla
6. Spread on cakes
7. For chocolate filling: Cook 1/2 cup water or milk with 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch
8. Add chocolate according to taste, salt, and a dot of butter
9. Instead of cornstarch the yolk of an egg may be used
