## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 quarts of ripe raspberries
- 1 cupful of water
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Mash raspberries, mix with 1 cupful of water and cook on a slow fire for 20 minutes, stirring constantly.
2. Pour on a cloth to drain without stirring.
3. Weigh the juice and mix with equal part of granulated sugar.
4. Place in kettle and let simmer nearly 1/2 hour.
5. Preserve in glass jars.
6. Cover jars only when jelly has cooled completely.
