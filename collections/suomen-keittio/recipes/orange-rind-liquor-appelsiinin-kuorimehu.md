## Ingredients

- 5 big oranges
- 5 cupfuls of sugar
- 2 teaspoonfuls of lemon acid
- 1 quart of water

## Directions

1. Remove the white part from peels of five big oranges
2. Cut the rinds into strips and bottle with five cupfuls of sugar and 2 teaspoonfuls of lemon acid
3. Add 1 quart of water and keep the bottle in the sun for one week shaking it every day
4. Drain the liquor and bottle up
5. Cork and keep in a cold place
6. Mix with water when served
