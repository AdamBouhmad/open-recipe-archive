## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of white beef stock
- 1/4 teaspoonful of salt
- a little pepper
- a little lemon juice
- yolk of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Prepare as usually and add a little lemon juice, lastly one yolk.
