## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups water
- 1/2 or 3/4 cup black coffee
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons or 1/2 package gelatine
- whipped cream for serving

## Directions

1. Prepare as given for lemon jelly, put into a mold to set
2. Serve with whipped cream
