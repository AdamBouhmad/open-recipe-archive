## Ingredients

- Frozen ice cream
- 5 egg whites
- 5 tablespoons sifted powdered sugar
- Sponge cake slices

## Directions

1. Fill individual molds with ice cream and pack tight in cracked ice and salt
2. Beat egg whites until stiff, add sifted powdered sugar and continue beating
3. Cut thin slices of sponge cake and place on dessert plates with wet cloth strips on the edges
4. Unmold ice cream onto cake slices
5. Cover with beaten egg whites, taking care not to leave any openings
6. Place plates in a pan and brown meringue quickly in a very hot oven
