## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- Salt and nutmeg
- 2 tablespoons ground coffee

## Directions

1. Add 2 tablespoons of ground coffee to the milk
2. Bring to a boil and strain
3. Prepare the same way as baked custard, using the coffee-infused milk instead of plain milk
4. Beat the eggs and add sugar, salt and nutmeg
5. Pour gradually the milk mixture into the eggs
6. Bake in a buttered mold in a pan of water until firm
