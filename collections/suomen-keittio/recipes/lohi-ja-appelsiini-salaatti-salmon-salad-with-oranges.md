## Ingredients

- 1 can of salmon
- 1 sour orange
- 1 cupful of lobster
- 1 cupful of minced lettuce leaves
- 1 orange
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- Salt
- Pepper
- 3 tablespoonfuls of mayonnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Rinse the salmon well
2. Cut in dice
3. Squeeze juice of one sour orange over the salmon
4. Allow 1 cupful of minced lettuce leaves, 1 orange cut in pieces and 1 hard-boiled chopped egg to one cupful of lobster
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Mix with 3 tablespoonfuls of mayonnaise sauce
