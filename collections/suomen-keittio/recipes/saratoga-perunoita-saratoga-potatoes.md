## Ingredients

- potatoes
- lard or deep frying fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut potatoes into thin slices.
2. Rinse and wipe dry.
3. Cook in deep lard until light brown.
4. Sprinkle with salt and serve hot or cold.
