## Ingredients

- Eggplant
- Salted water
- 3/4 cup bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 tablespoon chopped onion
- Bouillon or water
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 or 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Parboil the eggplant in salted water for 10 minutes.
2. Let cool thoroughly.
3. Cut a slice off the top or split lengthwise.
4. Scoop out the mellow and chop.
5. Add 3/4 cup bread crumbs, 2 tablespoons butter, and 1/2 tablespoon chopped onion.
6. Cook for a few minutes.
7. Dilute with some bouillon or water.
8. Season with salt and pepper.
9. Add 1 or 2 eggs beaten.
10. Stuff the eggplant with the mixture.
11. Sprinkle with bread crumbs.
12. Brown in oven.
