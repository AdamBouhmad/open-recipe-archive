## Ingredients

- onion
- butter
- 2 parts chopped cold roast or boiled corned beef
- 1 part chopped potatoes
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Fry onion with butter in frying pan.
2. To two parts of chopped cold roast or boiled corned beef allow one part of chopped potatoes and a little salt and pepper.
3. Turn all into the pan, stir over the fire until lightly browned.
4. Continue frying until a brown crust has formed on the under side.
5. Turn out whole into a serving dish the brown side uppermost.
