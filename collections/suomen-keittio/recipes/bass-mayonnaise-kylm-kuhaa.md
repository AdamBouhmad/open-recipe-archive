## Ingredients

- bass fillets
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- butter
- fish stock
- tomatoes
- hard-boiled egg
- mayonnaise sauce
- lettuce
- crab-tails
- lemon slices
- aspic made of bass liquor

## Directions

1. Remove the fillets, skin and bone the fish.
2. Cut in pieces of equal size.
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
4. Let stand 10 min.
5. Put the fish in a pan, dot a little bit of butter on each piece, pour a little fish stock over.
6. Bake in oven, covered with buttered paper, about 15 min.
7. Remove, place to cool.
8. Arrange the fish on slices of tomatoes on a platter.
9. Place a slice of egg on each piece.
10. Cover with thick mayonnaise sauce.
11. Garnish with leaves of lettuce, crab-tails, slices of lemon and aspic made of liquor of bass.
