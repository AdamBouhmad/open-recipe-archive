## Ingredients

- 7 pounds peaches
- 10 cups sugar
- 2 cups cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons whole cloves
- Some stick cinnamon

## Directions

1. Pare the peaches
2. Boil until clear sugar with vinegar and spices tied in a bag
3. Add the peaches, cook until half done
4. Place the peaches into jars, cover up until all the peaches are cooked, cooking a few peaches at a time so that they will not crush
5. Pour the boiling hot liquid over
6. Cover airtight
