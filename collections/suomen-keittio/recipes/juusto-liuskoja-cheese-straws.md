## Ingredients

- Puff paste
- Grated cheese
- Cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Roll puff paste into a sheet, ¾ inch thick
2. Sprinkle one half with grated cheese and a little cayenne
3. Fold in half and press lightly so the layers stick together
4. Cut with knife into strips 4 inches long and ½ inch wide
5. Bake 10 minutes in a hot oven
6. Serve with salad
