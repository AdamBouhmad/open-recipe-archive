## Ingredients

- Shelled chestnuts
- Butter
- Salt
- Chicken stock

## Directions

1. Cut the shelled chestnuts in two or four pieces
2. Fry quickly in best butter
3. Sprinkle with salt
4. Cover with chicken stock
5. Let stand in pan covered for 2 minutes
