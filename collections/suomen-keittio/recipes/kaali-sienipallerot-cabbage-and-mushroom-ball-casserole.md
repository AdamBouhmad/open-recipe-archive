## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Salted water
- Sugar
- 1 teaspoon
- Butter
- Mushroom ball paste
- Cabbage broth

## Directions

1. Cook the cabbage in salted water until tender
2. Cut the cabbage in pieces
3. Brown in butter with 1 teaspoon sugar
4. Prepare a mushroom ball paste
5. Form the paste into tiny balls
6. Fry the balls in butter
7. Put into a buttered mold alternate layers of cabbage and mushroom balls, having a layer of cabbage on top
8. Surround with cabbage broth
9. Bake for 1 hour
