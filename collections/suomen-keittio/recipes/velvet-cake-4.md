## Ingredients

- 3/4 cupful of butter
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of sugar
- 6 eggs
- 1/2 cupful of water
- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1/2 cupful of corn starch
- 3 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 1/2 cupful of chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Work butter with sugar to a froth
2. Add yolks of eggs beaten well, water, flour sifted with baking powder, and one half of the almonds
3. Stir lastly stiff beaten whites into it
4. Put in a buttered mold and sprinkle with the rest of the almonds and powdered sugar
5. Bake 40 minutes in a moderate oven
