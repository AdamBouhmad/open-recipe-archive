## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound of almonds
- oil or fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Blanch almonds and wipe them with a cloth.
2. Cook in oil or fat until delicately browned.
3. Place on brown paper and sprinkle with salt.
4. Alternatively, when browning in oven place almonds in dry pan stirring often until evenly browned; sprinkle with salt when they are done.
