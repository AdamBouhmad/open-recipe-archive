## Ingredients

- 1 pound fish, boned
- 6 ounces butter
- 2 ounces bread
- 1.5 cups cream
- 4 eggs
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Force the fish through a meat chopper several times.
2. Pound well in a wooden bowl.
3. Add gradually the creamed butter, yolks of egg one by one and the bread soaked in cream.
4. Continue rubbing until the mixture becomes very fluffy.
5. Last of all add the whites of egg beaten stiff and seasonings.
6. Bake in timbale molds in a pan of water covered with buttered paper.
7. Unmold and serve with any kind of sauce given for fish.
