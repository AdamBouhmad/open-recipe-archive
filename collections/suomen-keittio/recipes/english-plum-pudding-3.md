## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of chopped suet
- 1/2 lb. of seeded raisins
- 1/2 lb. of chopped currants
- 1/4 lb. of sifted sugar
- 2 ozs. of candied orange rind
- 2 ozs. blanched, chopped almonds
- 1/2 teaspoonful of nutmeg
- 1/2 teaspoonful of mace
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- yellow rind of 2 lemons
- juice of 4 lemons
- 6-8 eggs
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 glassful of brandy

## Directions

1. Mix all the dry ingredients thoroughly with suet.
2. Soak bread crumbs in milk, add yolks, beaten with sugar.
3. Combine mixtures with hands and add brandy into it.
4. At the last mix with stiffly beaten whites.
5. Pour the mixture in a buttered mold, cover, steam 6 hours.
