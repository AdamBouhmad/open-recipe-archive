## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound flour
- 3/4 pound bread crumbs
- 1 pound suet
- 3/4 pound seeded raisins
- 3/4 pound currants
- 1/4 pound sugar
- 1 cup syrup
- 1/4 pound candied orange peel
- 1/2 grated nutmeg
- Yellow rind of 2 lemons
- 1 teaspoon mixed spices
- 1 cup brandy

## Directions

1. Prepare as directed in the previous plum pudding recipe.
2. Steam 6 hours in a napkin.
3. Place aside.
4. Steam 2 hours more before serving.
5. Serve with wine or lemon sauce.
