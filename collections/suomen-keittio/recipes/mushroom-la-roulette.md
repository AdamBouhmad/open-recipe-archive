## Ingredients

- cleaned mushrooms
- butter
- beef stock
- salt
- pepper
- cream
- yolks of two eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned mushrooms 5 minutes with a little butter
2. Add beef stock, salt and pepper
3. When ready to send to table, mix with a little cream and yolks of two eggs
