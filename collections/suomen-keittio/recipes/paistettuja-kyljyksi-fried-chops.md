## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- bouillon
- tomato

## Directions

1. Trim as directed for broiling.
2. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
3. Fry in butter until brown on both sides, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Put the pan in hot oven for a few minutes.
5. Make a gravy of the drippings in the pan by adding some flour, bouillon, pepper, salt and tomato.
6. Strain and serve with the chops.
