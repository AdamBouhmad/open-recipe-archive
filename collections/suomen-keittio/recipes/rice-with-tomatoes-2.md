## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of boiled rice
- 1 cupful of thick strained tomato sauce
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Stir boiled rice into tomato sauce with fork carefully so as not to break the rice
2. Season with butter, salt and pepper
3. Serve as vegetables
