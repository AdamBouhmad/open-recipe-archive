## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound plums
- 1 1/2 cups cream
- 5 leaves or 3/4 package gelatine
- 3/4 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the plums in scanty water until tender.
2. Remove the stones, force the plums through a sieve or a meat grinder.
3. Add sugar, gelatine and last the whipped cream.
4. Mold.
5. Serve unmolded garnished with whipped cream.
6. Prune pudding may be prepared in the same way.
