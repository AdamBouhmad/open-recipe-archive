## Ingredients

- crab shells
- crab meat
- mayonnaise sauce
- red pepper
- truffles
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the crab shells.
2. Fill with crab meat mixed with mayonnaise sauce.
3. Garnish with red pepper and truffles.
4. Serve on a folded napkin garnished with lettuce.
