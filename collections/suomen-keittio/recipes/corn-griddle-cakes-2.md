## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 cupful of cold corn meal
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder

## Directions

1. Sift flour with salt, sugar and baking powder.
2. Stir milk into cold cornmeal.
3. Mix with flour and butter.
4. Fry as griddle cakes.
