## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1 lb. of molasses
- 8 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful of ginger
- 1 teaspoonful of cardamom
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cloves
- 1/2 teaspoonful of pepper
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon rind
- 2 lbs. of flour

## Directions

1. Whip eggs with sugar.
2. Mix with molasses, butter, spices and flour.
3. Roll thinly and shape into round cookies.
4. Bake in a moderate oven.
5. If allowed to stand in cold place a little while, the dough is better to handle.
