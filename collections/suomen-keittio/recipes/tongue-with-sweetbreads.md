## Ingredients

- butter
- thin slices of tongue
- small pieces of sweetbread
- white of an egg
- buttered paper
- spinach
- truffle sauce

## Directions

1. Butter the small molds
2. Lay thin slices of tongue on the sides
3. Fill with small, fried pieces of sweetbread
4. Fasten the tongue and the sweetbread together with white of an egg
5. Bake in oven in a pan of water, covered with a buttered paper
6. Serve on a foundation of spinach with truffle sauce
