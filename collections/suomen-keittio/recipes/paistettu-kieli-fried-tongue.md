## Ingredients

- tongue
- salt
- water
- 3 tablespoons butter
- onion
- flour
- carrots
- celery
- turnips
- parsley
- 3 cups broth
- tomato
- mushrooms
- pepper
- lemon

## Directions

1. Rinse and scrape the tongue.
2. Put on the fire in hot salt water and boil slowly for 1 hour.
3. Remove the tongue from water, skin and remove the roots.
4. Cook 3 tablespoons of butter with a sliced onion in a pan.
5. Place the tongue in and fry on both sides.
6. Add a sprinkling of flour, place carrots, turnips and celery cut in pieces and chopped parsley around.
7. Pour 3 cups of liquor over the tongue.
8. Cover tightly and cook for 2 or 3 hours.
9. Lift the tongue onto a platter.
10. Prepare gravy of the liquor in the pan by adding flour, bouillon and 1/2 cup of tomato.
11. Strain the gravy and add chopped mushrooms to it.
12. Cook for 5 minutes.
13. Season with salt and pepper, flavor with lemon.
14. Place the tongue on a serving dish and pour the gravy over.
