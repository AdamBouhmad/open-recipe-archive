## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup flour
- 3/4 cup milk
- 4 tablespoons cornstarch or potato flour
- 6 eggs
- Sugar for sprinkling
- Jam and cream for serving

## Directions

1. Scald the milk with the butter, mix with the flour.
2. Cook stirring carefully until it begins to brown on the bottom.
3. Work immediately an egg yolk into the paste, adding the rest of the yolks and the beaten whites when it begins to cool.
4. Drop by the spoonful into boiling lard.
5. Cook until done, sprinkle immediately with sugar.
6. Serve with jam and cream.
