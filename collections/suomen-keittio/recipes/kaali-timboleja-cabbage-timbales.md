## Ingredients

- 1 pound cabbage
- 1/2 pound butter
- 5 tablespoons boiled rice
- 3/4 cup flour
- 3 cups milk or vegetable stock
- 4 eggs
- Pepper
- Mace
- Salt
- Tomato sauce or milk sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Cook the cabbage in salted water
2. Force through a meat grinder with the boiled rice
3. Make a sauce of butter, flour and milk
4. Add the cabbage and rice to the sauce
5. Add spices and the whipped egg yolks
6. Mix in the whites of the eggs beaten firm
7. Bake in timbale molds or in a dome-shaped mold
8. Serve with tomato or milk sauce
9. The cabbage may be mixed with tomato, if preferred
