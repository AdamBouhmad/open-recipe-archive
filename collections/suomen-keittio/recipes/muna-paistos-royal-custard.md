## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 1 egg
- ⅓ cup bouillon or milk
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs, add bouillon and seasonings.
2. Pour into a well buttered mold.
3. Bake in oven in a pan of water until firm.
4. Unmold, cut into nice pieces.
5. Serve with bouillon.
