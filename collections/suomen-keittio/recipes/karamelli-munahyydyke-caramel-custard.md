## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1 pint milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Brown 1 cup of sugar in a pan stirring constantly
2. Add 1 tablespoon of water
3. Pour a part of the syrup into a buttered mold
4. Turn the mold so as to line the edges and bottom with the syrup
5. Add to the remaining syrup equal part of water to make a sauce
6. Prepare custard: beat eggs with sugar, salt and vanilla
7. Pour the custard into the caramel-lined mold
8. Bake in oven in a pan of water until firm
9. Unmold when cold and serve with the caramel sauce
10. Alternatively, the caramel may be dissolved in milk by stirring over the fire
