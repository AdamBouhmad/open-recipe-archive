## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 cupful of cream
- 6 ozs. of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder
- wheat flour as much as needed to make a dough that can be kneaded

## Directions

1. Whip eggs and sugar to a froth
2. Add cream and butter worked to a froth
3. Add baking powder mixed with flour
4. Finally add as much flour as needed so the dough can be kneaded
