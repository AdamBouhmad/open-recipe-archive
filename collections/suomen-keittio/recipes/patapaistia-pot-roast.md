## Ingredients

- 4 pound piece of beef
- Butter or pork
- 1 onion
- 1 carrot
- 1 turnip
- Water or meat stock
- Flour
- Cream

## Directions

1. Brown the beef with butter or pork
2. When well browned, add onion, carrot and turnip cut in pieces
3. Half cover with boiling water or meat stock
4. Cook tightly covered until tender
5. Remove the fat and thicken with flour
6. Strain the stock, rubbing the vegetables through the strainer
7. Add meat stock and cream until moderately thick
