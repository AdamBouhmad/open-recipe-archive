## Ingredients

- 1 kupillinen hienonnettua mahasylkirauhasta (1 cupful of minced sweetbread)
- ½ kupillista hienonnettua ja hakattua kanan rintalihaa (½ cupful of chopped chicken breast)
- 2 munanvalkuaista (2 whites of eggs)
- 1½ kupillista paksua kermaa (1½ cupfuls of rich cream)

## Directions

1. Rub the cooked sweetbreads through a sieve.
2. Mix well together the minced sweetbread and chopped chicken breast.
3. Rub the whites of eggs into the mixture.
4. Finally add the rich cream.
5. Butter the timbales and fill with this mixture.
6. Bake in oven in a pan of water, covered with a buttered paper, until firm.
7. Serve with a sauce made of butter, flour, chicken stock and cream.
