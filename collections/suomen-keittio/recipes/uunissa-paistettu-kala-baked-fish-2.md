## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- cracker dust
- butter or salt pork
- chopped onion
- vegetables
- cream

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean and wipe dry
2. Rub with salt and pepper
3. Spread with eggs on both sides
4. Roll in cracker dust
5. Put butter in pan or lay slices of salt pork in it
6. Place the fish upright on it
7. Put chopped onion and vegetables around the fish
8. Bake in oven basting often
9. When done place the fish on serving-dish
10. Add a little cream into the pan
11. Pour the sauce on the fish
