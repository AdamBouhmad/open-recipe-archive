## Ingredients

- beef, cut in 1-1 1/2 inch pieces
- butter
- chopped onion
- 2 carrots
- 1 turnip
- salt and pepper
- water or bouillon
- 2 potatoes
- 2 stalks of celery
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut beef in 1-1 1/2 inch pieces, brown with butter and chopped onion.
2. Place in kettle with 2 carrots, 1 turnip cut in pieces, salt and pepper.
3. Put a little water or bouillon in the frying pan in which the onions were browned, pour over the meat.
4. Let simmer covered.
5. About 1/2 hour before the meat is done, add 2 potatoes cut in pieces and 2 stalks of celery.
6. Thicken with flour mixed with cold water.
7. Place on a serving dish, arrange the vegetables around the meat.
