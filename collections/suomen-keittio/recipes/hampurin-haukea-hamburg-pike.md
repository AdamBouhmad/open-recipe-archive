## Ingredients

- 5 pounds pike
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- 2 egg yolks
- cream
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean, rinse, wipe and remove the backbone
2. Rub with salt and pepper
3. Moisten with lemon juice, let stand for a while
4. Wipe the fish and spread with melted butter
5. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
6. Bake in a buttered pan to a light brown
7. Remove the fish to a platter
8. Make a gravy of drippings by adding some butter, flour, fish stock, lemon juice
9. Mix with yolks of egg and cream
10. Clean mushrooms and fry in butter, arrange on the fish
11. Or chop the mushrooms, stir into the gravy, pour the gravy over the fish
