## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- warm ground meat
- spinach or potato puree

## Directions

1. Cut a piece from the top of hard-boiled eggs and remove the yolk.
2. Fill the eggs with warm ground meat.
3. Place the caps on top and serve set into spinach or potato puree.
