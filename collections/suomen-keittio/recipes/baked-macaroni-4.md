## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of macaroni
- 1 quart of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1/2 tablesp. of powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into 2 inch pieces, wash and cook in salted water 15 minutes.
2. Drain off the water and put the macaroni in boiling milk and cook them 20-30 minutes.
3. Mix with sugar, butter and salt and let cool.
4. Whip the egg and stir into the mixture.
5. Pour in a buttered mold and bake to a light brown, 20-30 minutes.
