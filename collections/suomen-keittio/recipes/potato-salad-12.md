## Ingredients

- potatoes
- onion juice
- parsley
- French dressing
- mayonnaise dressing
- bacon
- pickled cucumber
- beet
- lettuce leaves
- hard-boiled eggs
- capers

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes with skins on.
2. Let cool down, peel and cut in very thin slices.
3. Drop a little onion juice on and mix with minced parsley.
4. Pour French dressing on.
5. Mayonnaise dressing may also be used with potato salad.
6. Potato slices mixed with fried bacon cut in very small cubes, 1 pickled cucumber and thinly sliced beet, make a delicate salad; has to be served with French dressing.
7. Arrange lettuce leaves around and garnish with hard-boiled egg yolk and capers.
