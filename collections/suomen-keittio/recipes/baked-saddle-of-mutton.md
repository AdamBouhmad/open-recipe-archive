## Ingredients

- saddle of mutton
- cord

## Directions

1. Separate the back part before splitting the mutton in two
2. Remove the skin, the fat and the kidneys
3. Crack the ribs, trim into a nice shape
4. Fasten with cord
5. Bake in a hot oven same as baked mutton basting often
6. Allow about 9 minutes to a pound
7. Before serving, remove the meat from the backbone, cut crosswise into slices
8. Replace around the backbone
9. Arrange on a serving dish
10. Serve with thin brown sauce or with currant jelly
11. Garnish with parsley, radishes, etc.
