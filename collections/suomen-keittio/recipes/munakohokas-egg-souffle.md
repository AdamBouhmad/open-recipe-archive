## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 2 cups rich milk
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons chopped almonds
- 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks with the sugar, add the warmed milk and butter; beat over heat until it thickens.
2. When the mixture has cooled, mix in the flour, almonds and the stiffly beaten egg whites.
3. Caramelize 2 tablespoons of sugar in a baking pan, add 2 tablespoons of water and a little lemon juice, pour into the mold and turn to coat the bottom and sides.
