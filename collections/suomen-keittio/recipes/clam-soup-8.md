## Ingredients

- 1 quart of clams
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 cupful of cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- yolks of 2 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the clams fine and put on a slow fire to cook in their own liquor
2. Add a little water or fish stock and let simmer about an hour
3. Thicken with butter and flour
4. Beat the yolks and add gradually into it the scalded milk mixed with a pinch of soda
5. Remove from the fire and stir the milk mixed with eggs into it
