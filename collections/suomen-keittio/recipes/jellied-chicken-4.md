## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- celery stalks
- 1 quart of chicken stock
- ½ cupful of tomato
- 2 teaspoonfuls of meat extract
- 7 tablespoonfuls of gelatine dissolved in water or 10 leaves of gelatine
- 2 egg whites
- hard-boiled eggs
- beets
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the chicken, put on the fire to cook with salt, pepper, onion and two stalks of celery.
2. Let simmer until tender.
3. Skin, remove the bones and cut the meat into neat pieces.
4. Take 1 quart of chicken stock, ½ cupful of tomato, 2 teaspoonfuls of meat extract, 7 tablespoonfuls of gelatine dissolved in water or 10 leaves of gelatine and whites of 2 eggs.
5. Whip over the fire until boiling; let simmer 5 minutes.
6. Strain through a fine cloth.
7. Line an individual mold with cleared liquid jelly.
8. Let thicken.
9. Garnish the mold with hard-boiled eggs, beets and parsley.
10. Lay the pieces of chicken into the mold, and pour over them the jelly.
11. Set in a cold place to stiffen.
