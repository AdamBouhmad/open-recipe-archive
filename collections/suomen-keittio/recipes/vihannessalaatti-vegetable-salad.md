## Ingredients

- 1 cauliflower
- 1/2 cup peas
- 1/2 cup string beans cut in 3/4 inch pieces
- 1/4 cup asparagus cut in pieces
- 1/4 cup carrots cut in pieces
- 1/2 cup turnip or potato cut in pieces
- capers
- cucumber slices
- salt
- French dressing
- boiled mayonnaise
- lettuce leaves
- parsley
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Wash the vegetables
2. Cook each type separately in salted boiling water
3. Drain and let cool
4. Pour French dressing over and arrange on a platter
5. Coat with boiled mayonnaise
6. Sprinkle the top with chopped capers and cucumber
7. Place lettuce leaves around the edges
8. Garnish with parsley and hard boiled eggs
9. If not all types of vegetables are available, combine those that are on hand
