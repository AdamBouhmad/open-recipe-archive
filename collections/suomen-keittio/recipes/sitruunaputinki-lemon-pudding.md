## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups cream
- 6 eggs
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1 lemon
- 2 tablespoons potato flour
- 1/2 pound sugar for caramel
- 3/4 cup hot water
- Lemon juice
- Lemon or fruit sauce

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar in a saucepan.
2. Add the yellow rind and the juice of a lemon, cream and flour.
3. Place the saucepan on the fire and cook beating well until thick.
4. Let cool, stirring now and then.
5. Beat egg whites until frothy.
6. Add to the mixture.
7. Prepare caramel by dissolving 1/2 pound sugar in 3/4 cup hot water with lemon juice.
8. Coat a mold with caramel and fill with the mixture.
9. Bake in oven in a pan of water for 3/4 hour.
10. Serve hot with lemon or fruit sauce.
