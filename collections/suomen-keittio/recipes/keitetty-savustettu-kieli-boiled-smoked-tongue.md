## Ingredients

- Smoked tongue
- Cold water
- Pepper
- Onion
- Bay leaves
- Sweet or tart sauce

## Directions

1. Wash the tongue.
2. If too salty, soak in cold water over night.
3. Put on the fire in cold water with pepper, onion and bay leaves.
4. Cook slowly until tender.
5. Skin immediately, remove the bone.
6. Serve hot or cold with sweet or tart sauce.
