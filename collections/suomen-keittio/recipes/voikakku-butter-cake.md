## Ingredients

- 1 glassful eggs
- 1 glassful powdered sugar
- 1/2 glassful melted butter
- 1 glassful flour
- Vanilla or lemon flavor

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar for about 1/2 hour
2. Add gradually butter and flour
3. Pour the mixture into a buttered mold
4. Bake 3/4 hour
