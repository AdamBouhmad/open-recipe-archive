## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 3/8 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 cake of yeast
- 1/4 cupful of butter
- 2 eggs
- flour

## Directions

1. Scald milk with salt and sugar
2. Add yeast and 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour to it
3. Cover and let rise
4. Mix with well beaten eggs, melted butter and flour enough to make a moderately thick dough
5. Let rise again
6. Roll into a sheet 1/2 inch thick
7. Shape with a round cutter
8. Place tightly on a buttered baking sheet
9. Let rise
10. Bake for 15 minutes
