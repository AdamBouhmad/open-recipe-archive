## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1/4 cupful of lard
- 3/4 cupful of butter
- 1/2 cupful of cold water

## Directions

1. Work thoroughly together flour, salt and butter
2. Pour gradually water into it
3. Knead to an even paste
4. Put in a cold place for a while
5. Roll the paste into 1/2 inch thick crust, a little larger than the pie-plate
6. Place the crust on the floured pie plate
7. Spread the edges of the crust with water or white of egg
8. Have the upper crust a little larger than the under crust
9. Perforate the upper crust so that the steam may escape
10. Cover the filling with the upper crust pressing the edges well together
11. Cut the edges to fit evenly and fold down the edges of the upper crust
12. Spread with egg and tie around the edges with a wet cloth, 1 inch wide
13. Bake until lightly browned, first in hot oven then reducing the heat, 1 hour
