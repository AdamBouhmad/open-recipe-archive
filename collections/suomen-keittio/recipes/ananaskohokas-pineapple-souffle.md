## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound pineapple, grated or cut in cubes
- 4 egg whites
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare as directed for banana souffle by using 3/4 pound of pineapple grated or cut in cubes instead of bananas.
