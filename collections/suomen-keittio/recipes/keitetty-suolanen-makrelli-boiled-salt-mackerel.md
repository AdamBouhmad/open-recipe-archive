## Ingredients

- salt mackerel
- warm water
- vinegar
- onion
- milk
- eggs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the fish in warm water skin side up.
2. Boil until tender with some vinegar and onion.
3. Place on a platter, cover with milk sauce mixed with hard boiled chopped eggs or two raw egg yolks according to taste.
4. The fish may be cut into fine pieces, mixed with milk sauce and served on toast.
5. The fish will taste better if boiled in milk instead of water.
