## Ingredients

- eggs
- fried chopped chicken liver
- onion juice
- salt
- pepper
- lettuce leaves
- chopped pickles
- tomato
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Cut the eggs in slices
2. remove carefully the yolks
3. place the rings of whites on lettuce leaves
4. Mix the yolks with fried chopped chicken liver, some onion juice, salt and pepper
5. rub the mixture well
6. Place into the rings of whites
7. Garnish with chopped pickles, tomato and mayonnaise
