## Ingredients

- boiled rice
- 2 egg yolks
- bread crumbs
- butter or lard for frying
- egg for coating

## Directions

1. Mix boiled rice with 2 egg yolks and bread crumbs
2. Let stand in cold place for a while
3. Form into balls or croquettes
4. Coat in egg and bread crumbs
5. Fry in butter or cook in deep lard
