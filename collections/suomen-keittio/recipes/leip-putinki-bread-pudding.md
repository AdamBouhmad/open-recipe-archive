## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of bread, cut in pieces
- 2 1/2 cupfuls of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoonful of vanilla
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter

## Directions

1. Soak bread in milk
2. Lay the pieces of bread on bottom of a buttered mold
3. Mix milk with egg, whipped with sugar, vanilla, butter and salt
4. Pour the mixture on the bread
5. Steam in oven until firm
