## Ingredients

- 3 cups chopped sweetbreads
- 3 tablespoons dissolved gelatine
- 5 tablespoons whipped cream
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Garnish small molds or a jelly mold.
2. Mix together the sweetbreads, gelatine, and lemon juice, adding the cream last of all.
3. Fill the molds with this mixture.
4. Serve unmolded with lettuce leaves.
