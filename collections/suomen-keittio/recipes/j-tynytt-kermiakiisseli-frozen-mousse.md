## Ingredients

- 2 cups whipped cream
- 1/2 cup macaroons cut in pieces
- Stewed fruit cut in pieces
- Sugar
- Vanilla and maraschino

## Directions

1. Mix together all the ingredients
2. Pour mixture into ice cream mold
3. Pack in a pail of cracked ice and salt
4. Let stand for 4-5 hours
5. Serve unmolded garnished with fruit and macaroons
