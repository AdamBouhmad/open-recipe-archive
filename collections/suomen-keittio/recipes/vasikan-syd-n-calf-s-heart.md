## Ingredients

- 1 calf's heart
- salted water
- ground meat or bread
- onion, fried and chopped
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- 1 tablespoon tomato
- 1 egg
- a few slices of bacon
- carrots
- celery
- onion
- bouillon
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the heart and soak in salted water for an hour to draw out the blood.
2. Fill the heart with a mixture made of ground meat or bread, chopped fried onion, pepper, chopped parsley, 1 tablespoon of tomato and 1 egg.
3. Sew up the opening.
4. Place a few slices of bacon, carrots, celery and onion in a roasting pan.
5. Bake in the oven for about 2 hours, basting often with the drippings in the pan.
6. If the meat begins to dry, add some bouillon.
7. Cover with a lid so as not to burn.
8. Make a gravy from the drippings, seasoning with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
9. Serve hot.
