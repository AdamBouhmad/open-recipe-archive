## Ingredients

- 2 wild ducks
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 teaspoonfuls of salt
- 1/2 quart of meat stock
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the ducks, let stand in water mixed with vinegar.
2. Boil quickly.
3. Wipe dry, rub with salt, brown in a pan with a lump of butter.
4. Pour over it little by little the boiling meat stock.
5. Roast about an hour basting often.
6. Serve very hot.
7. Strain the gravy, skim off the fat and season with lemon.
8. Thicken with flour if wanted.
