## Ingredients

- middlesized cabbages
- salt
- small apples
- sprigs of dill

## Directions

1. Cut cabbages into strips
2. Pound well
3. Lay into a wooden vessel, having salt, small apples and sprigs of dill between the layers
4. Press tightly with cover, having a weight on top of it
5. Keep in a warm room for a week, carefully skimming off the mildew
6. Keep in a cool place
7. Allow 1 pound of salt to 50 heads of cabbage
