## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- salt
- pepper, 1 teaspoon per 2 cups liquid
- mace, 1 teaspoon per 2 cups liquid
- allspice, 1 teaspoon per 2 cups liquid
- 1 bay leaf
- some onion

## Directions

1. Clean and cut in pieces fresh mushrooms
2. Lay into a bowl, having a sprinkling of salt between the layers
3. Cover and let stand in a cold place for 3 or 4 days, stirring now and then with a spoon
4. Bring to boil the mushrooms, force the liquid through a sieve
5. Put the liquid on the fire, allow to 2 cups liquid, pepper, mace and allspice, 1 teaspoon each, 1 bay leaf and some onion
6. Simmer until thick, strain, cool and bottle up
