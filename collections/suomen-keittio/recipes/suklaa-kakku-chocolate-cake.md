## Ingredients

- ½ cupful of butter
- 1¼ cupfuls of sugar
- 4 eggs
- ¾ cupful of milk
- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 2½ ozs. of chocolate
- ½ teaspoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar to a cream, add beaten yolks, chocolate dissolved in milk, vanilla, flour and baking powder.
2. Add the stiff beaten whites of eggs.
3. Pour in a buttered mold and bake in a moderate oven ¾—1 hour.
