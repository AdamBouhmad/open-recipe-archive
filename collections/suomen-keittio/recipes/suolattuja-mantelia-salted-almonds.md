## Ingredients

- ¼ pound almonds
- Salt
- Oil or fat

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and wipe with a cloth.
2. Cook in oil or fat until nice brown.
3. Drain on brown paper and sprinkle with salt.
4. Alternatively, place the almonds in a dry baking tin, stir often until evenly browned, and sprinkle with salt when browned.
