## Ingredients

- 8 oranges
- 8 lbs. granulated sugar
- 2 ozs. lemon acid powder
- 2 1/2 quarts cold water

## Directions

1. Grate yellow rind of oranges
2. Peel oranges and mash with sugar in a big bowl
3. Add the grated yellow rind into it
4. Mix with boiled cooled water
5. Keep in a cold place 2-3 days stirring now and then until the sugar has quite dissolved
6. Put lemon acid into it stirring well
7. Drain through a tight cloth
8. Bottle, cork and keep in a cool place
9. When serving add water to taste
