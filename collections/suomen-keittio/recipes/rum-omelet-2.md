## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of milk or cream
- a little salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- a few tablespoonfuls of brandy or rum
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat eggs, add milk, salt and sugar.
2. Put 1 tablespoonful of butter in a hot fryingpan, pour the mixture in and fry, shaking the pan over the fire until omelet is thick, but not hard.
3. Heap quickly on one side and let brown underneath.
4. Turn on a serving-plate.
5. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and pour a few tablespoonfuls of brandy or rum over and light it.
