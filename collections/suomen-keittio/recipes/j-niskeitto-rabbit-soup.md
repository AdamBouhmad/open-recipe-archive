## Ingredients

- Remains of roast rabbit
- 3 quarts water
- 1 onion
- 2 stalks celery
- 1 small parsnip
- 2 sprigs parsley
- ¼ cup tomato
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 10 peppercorns
- Salt and lemon juice

## Directions

1. Crack the bones, put on the fire in boiling water.
2. Peel the vegetables, cut in pieces and brown in butter.
3. Add to the bone stock, season; add the parsley and the tomato.
4. Simmer for a few hours.
5. Thicken with butter and flour.
6. Serve with tiny potatoes or rice.
