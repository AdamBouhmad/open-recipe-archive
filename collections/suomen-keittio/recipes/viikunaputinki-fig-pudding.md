## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound chopped figs
- 1 cup chopped suet
- 2 1/2 cups bread crumbs
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Soak the figs in lukewarm water, chop and mix thoroughly with the suet.
2. Soak the bread crumbs in milk; beat the eggs with sugar, add to the bread crumbs.
3. Combine the mixtures by stirring well.
4. Steam for 3 hours in a buttered mold.
5. Serve with vanilla, wine or brandy sauce.
