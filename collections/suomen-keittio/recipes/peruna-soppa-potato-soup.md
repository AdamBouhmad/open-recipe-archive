## Ingredients

- 3 quarts of meat stock
- 3/4 quart of potatoes
- 1/2 root or 2 stalks of celery
- 1 leek or 2 slices of onion
- a little finely chopped parsley
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cupful of cream or milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and celery, cut in pieces and cook in 1 quart of meat stock until tender; force it through a sieve.
2. Cook the butter with the onion in a kettle, add the flour and pour the soup and the meat stock slowly into it; boil 10 minutes.
3. Season and at last add the yolks and cream, whipping well.
4. Serve with fried bread dice.
