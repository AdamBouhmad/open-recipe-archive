## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3/4 lb. of butter
- 1 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 3 1/2 cupfuls of oatmeal
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Whip eggs with sugar to a froth
2. Mix with melted butter, oatmeal and flour
3. Drop from tip of spoon in small portions on a buttered baking sheet
4. Bake until brown
