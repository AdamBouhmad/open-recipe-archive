## Ingredients

- lettuce leaves
- hard cooked eggs
- French dressing
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Wash the lettuce and wipe
2. Arrange on a platter
3. place hard cooked eggs split lengthwise on the lettuce
4. Pour French dressing over
5. Boiled potatoes cut in nice pieces may be used in egg salad
