## Ingredients

- 4 squabs
- 3 ounces marrow
- 4 ounces butter
- 2 ounces bread
- 1 egg
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- artichoke slices
- cream

## Directions

1. Clean the squabs.
2. Mince the marrow mixed with butter and rub well.
3. Add the other ingredients (bread, egg, salt, pepper, and chopped parsley).
4. Stuff the squabs with this mixture.
5. Bake for 30 minutes, basting often.
6. Arrange on fried slices of artichokes.
7. Skim the drippings and add a little cream to make the gravy.
