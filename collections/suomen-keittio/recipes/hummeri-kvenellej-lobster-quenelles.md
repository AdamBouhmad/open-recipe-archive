## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls chopped lobster meat
- 4 tablespoonfuls bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls lobster butter or butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls cream
- 2 eggs
- hard boiled egg yolk
- anchovy paste
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Chop or grind lobster meat fine with hard boiled yolk of an egg and anchovy paste
2. Add seasonings, butter, raw yolk of an egg and cream, stirring well
3. Mix with white of an egg beaten stiff
4. Form into small balls
5. Roll in flour
6. Boil in salt water
