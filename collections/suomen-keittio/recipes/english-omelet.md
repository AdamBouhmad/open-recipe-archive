## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of milk or cream
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Separate the yolks from the whites
2. Beat the whites to firm froth and put aside until the yolks are beaten
3. Mix the yolks with milk and seasonings, and at the last with the beaten whites lightly stirring
4. Melt butter in an omelet pan, pour the mixture onto it and cook slowly, turning the pan now and then to make the omelet evenly brown
5. If the surface is too soft, place the pan on the shelf of the oven for a little while
6. Fold once and turn on a hot platter
7. Serve with sauce
