## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- juice and yellow rind of 1 lemon
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 cups cream
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Rub the butter with the sugar until creamy
2. Add the beaten egg yolks and the water
3. Stir in a double boiler over the fire until thick
4. Remove from the fire, add the juice and grated yellow rind of a lemon and some Sherry
5. When cool, mix with whipped cream
