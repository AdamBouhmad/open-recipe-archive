## Ingredients

- 1 peck of unskinned tomatoes
- few white onions
- two bay leaves
- mace, 2 teaspoonfuls
- pepper, 2 teaspoonfuls
- celery-salt, 2 teaspoonfuls
- cloves, 2 teaspoonfuls
- sugar, to taste
- salt, to taste
- 2 cupfuls of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut 1 peck of unskinned tomatoes and few white onions in pieces
2. Cook together until tender, force through a sieve
3. Place on fire and mix with two bay leaves, mace, pepper, celery-salt, cloves, 2 teaspoonfuls of each, boiled in a bag
4. Add sugar and salt to taste
5. Cook 4-5 hours, stirring occasionally
6. Remove the spices, add 2 cupfuls of vinegar and let boil up
7. Cool and put in bottles, seal and store
