## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 ozs. of chocolate
- 4 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- yolks of 5 eggs
- 7 leaves or 5 tablespoonfuls of gelatine
- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 2 cupfuls of cream

## Directions

1. Boil up milk and chocolate.
2. Whip yolk with sugar and pour the boiling milk gradually into it.
3. Stir well on the fire until it thickens.
4. Mix with gelatine and set to cool.
5. When the mixture begins to harden it must be free from lumps.
6. Add the whipped cream and pour in a mold to stiffen.
7. Unmold and serve with whipped cream.
