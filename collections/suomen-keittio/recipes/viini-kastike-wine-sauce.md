## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of water
- 1/2 cupful of sherry
- 1/2 cupful of white wine or madeira
- yellow rind of 1 lemon
- 1-2 tablespoonfuls of arrowroot
- 1/2 cupful of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix sugar with arrowroot
2. Pour boiling water slowly into it stirring well
3. Cook until the mixture is clear
4. Add wines and yellow lemon rind
5. The sauce may be thickened also with yolk of an egg
