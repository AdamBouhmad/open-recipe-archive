## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 6 ozs. of sugar
- ½ cupful of butter
- ¾ cupful of milk
- 1 cupful of walnut meat, chopped with the thin brown skin
- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Work butter and sugar to a froth
2. Add beaten yolks
3. Add milk, walnuts, baking powder, flour and then whites of eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
