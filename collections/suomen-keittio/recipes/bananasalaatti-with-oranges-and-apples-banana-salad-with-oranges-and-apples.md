## Ingredients

- 2 bananas
- 2 oranges
- 2 apples
- mayonnaise
- cream
- nuts

## Directions

1. Mix the bananas with oranges and apples equal parts of each
2. Add mayonnaise diluted with cream
3. Sprinkle with nuts
