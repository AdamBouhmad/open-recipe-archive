## Ingredients

- new potatoes
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 pint of boiling milk
- salt
- pepper
- minced parsley

## Directions

1. Cut new potatoes into dice, cook in salted water until tender.
2. Drain, pour milk sauce over.
3. Put 2 tablespoonfuls of butter in a saucepan, melt, add 2 tablespoonfuls of flour.
4. Add 1 pint of boiling milk, season with salt and pepper.
5. Lastly sprinkle with minced parsley.
6. If cold potatoes are used, cut them first in pieces, parboil in milk or cream.
7. Add 1 tablespoonful of butter, salt, pepper and just before serving sprinkle with 1/2 teaspoonful of minced parsley.
8. May be baked in oven in buttered mold, sprinkled with bread crumbs and cheese.
