## Ingredients

- 1 kupillista jauhettua kieltä (1 cupful of ground tongue)
- ½ kupillista vispattua kermaa (½ cupful of whipped cream)
- 3 ruokalusikallista tai 4 lehteä liivakkoa (3 tablesp. or 4 leaves of gelatine)
- 2 ruokalusikallista sherry viiniä (2 tablespoonfuls of sherry)
- pippuria ja suolaa (pepper and salt)

## Directions

1. Dissolve the gelatine in a little water.
2. Add the sherry and pour this mixture gradually into the tongue.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Finally add the whipped cream.
5. Moisten the small timbales with the mixture of gelatine.
6. Garnish with red pepper and fill with the mixture of tongue.
7. Let it get firm.
8. Serve with salad.
