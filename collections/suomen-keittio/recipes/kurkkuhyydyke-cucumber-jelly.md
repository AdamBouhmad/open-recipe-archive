## Ingredients

- 2 medium-sized cucumbers
- 2 tablespoons tomato
- 1 tablespoon gelatine or 6 leaves
- 1 cup chicken or veal stock
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Place on the fire the stock with the cucumber and the tomato. Cook until the cucumber is tender.
2. Strain, add the gelatine, clarify with the whites of 2 eggs.
3. Pour into a mold to set; mix with pieces of fresh cucumber, if desired.
