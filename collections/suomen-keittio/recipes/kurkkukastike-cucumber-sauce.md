## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups bouillon
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 pickled cucumber
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Make a gravy of butter, flour and bouillon, add lemon juice, chopped cucumber, sugar, pepper and salt.
2. Serve with fillet of beef, duck or veal.
