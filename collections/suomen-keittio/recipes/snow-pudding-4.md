## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of lemon or wine jelly
- 3 whites of eggs
- whipped cream or custard for serving

## Directions

1. Prepare lemon or wine jelly and let cool.
2. Before it is firm, whip it to a froth.
3. Beat whites of eggs until stiff and add to the jelly.
4. Continue beating until it is stiff enough to hold its shape.
5. Put in a mold or drop with spoon into a glass dish.
6. Serve with whipped cream or custard.
