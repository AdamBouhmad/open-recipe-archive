## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of whipped cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls of gelatine
- 2 tablespoonfuls of caviare
- red pepper
- buttered slices of bread

## Directions

1. Stir cream, gelatine and caviare all together.
2. Garnish small molds with red pepper.
3. Cover the bottom with creamed caviare, then put a layer of caviare in, and cover that with creamed caviare.
4. Serve on buttered slices of bread.
