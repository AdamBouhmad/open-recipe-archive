## Ingredients

- fresh cucumbers
- 1 cupful of vinegar
- ⅓ cupful of salad oil
- salt and pepper
- minced parsley

## Directions

1. See to it that the cucumbers are fresh
2. Peel and cut in thin slices
3. Make French dressing of vinegar and salad oil with salt and pepper to taste
4. Pour over the sliced cucumbers
5. Sprinkle with minced parsley
