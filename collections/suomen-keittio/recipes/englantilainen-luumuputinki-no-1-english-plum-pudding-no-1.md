## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 pound chopped suet
- 3/4 pound seeded raisins
- 1/2 pound currants
- 1/4 pound sifted sugar
- 2 ounces candied orange peel
- 2 ounces chopped almonds
- 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1/4 teaspoon mace
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- Yellow rind of 2 lemons
- Juice of 4 lemons
- 6 or 8 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup brandy

## Directions

1. Soak the bread crumbs in milk, add the yolks beaten with sugar.
2. Rub the suet with fingers until creamy, add the dry ingredients, raisins rolled in flour and currants.
3. Combine the mixtures, add brandy or lemon juice.
4. Last of all mix in the whites beaten until firm.
5. Pour into a buttered mold, cover tightly.
6. Steam for 6 hours.
