## Ingredients

- whites of 3 eggs
- 6 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 1/2 tablesp. of grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Whip whites of eggs to a firm froth, continue whipping about 15 minutes.
2. Sift powdered sugar little by little into the whites.
3. Form into small cakes on a buttered sheet, dry in a slow oven.
