## Ingredients

- 100 medium-sized cucumbers
- 1/2 cup salt
- 4 quarts water
- dill
- black or white peppercorns
- horseradish
- cherry, currant or oak leaves
- 10 quarts water
- 1 quart vinegar
- 2 cups salt

## Directions

1. Let cucumbers stand in salt water over night (1/2 cup salt to 4 quarts water)
2. Lay into an earthen jar having dill, black or white peppercorns and horseradish between the layers
3. Place cherry, currant or oak leaves between the cucumbers for better preservation
4. Boil 10 quarts water with 1 quart vinegar and 2 cups salt
5. Let cool
6. Pour over the cucumbers
7. Cover with a cloth, place a cover on having the cover to fit inside the jar
8. Put a weight on top
