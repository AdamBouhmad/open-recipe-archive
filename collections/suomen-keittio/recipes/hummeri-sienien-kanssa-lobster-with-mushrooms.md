## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiled lobster, diced
- 2 cups fried mushrooms, cut in pieces
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 slice of onion
- 1 cup milk or cream

## Directions

1. Cook the chopped slice of onion in butter
2. Add flour, mushrooms, lobster, spices and milk or cream hot, bring to boiling
3. Remove from fire, add some lemon juice or sherry
4. Serve on a platter having toast around, or fill lobster shells with the mixture, sprinkle with bread crumbs, dot with butter
5. Brown in oven
