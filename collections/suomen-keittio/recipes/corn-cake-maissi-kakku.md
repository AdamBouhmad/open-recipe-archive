## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cupfuls of corn meal
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 3/4 cupful of sugar
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of milk
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cupful of butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 4 teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix flour and corn meal with salt and baking powder.
2. Whip eggs with sugar, add milk and melted butter to it.
3. Mix with flour, stirring thoroughly together.
4. Bake in a shallow pan in hot oven.
