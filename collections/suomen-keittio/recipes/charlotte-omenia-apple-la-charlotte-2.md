## Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoonful of butter
- 3 big apples
- 2 tablespoonfuls of raisins
- 1 sliced orange
- a little grated lemon rind
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of sherry
- 1 tablespoonful of currant jelly
- bread
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Pare apples, cut into thin slices, put in a saucepan with butter, sugar, raisins and orange slices.
2. Keep on the fire until hot, add sherry and currant jelly.
3. Cover the bottom and the sides of a mold with small, round slices of bread, dipped in melted butter.
4. Pour the apple mixture into the mold, cover with a few slices of bread, so as not to burn the surface.
5. Bake in oven 1 hour.
6. Turn out of the mold; serve with whipped cream.
