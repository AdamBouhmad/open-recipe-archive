## Ingredients

- Onions of uniform size
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Select onions of uniform size
2. Peel and rinse
3. Place in boiling water
4. Cook 5 minutes, drain off water and pour fresh boiling salted water over
5. Cook until tender
6. If placing onions in cold water no need to change the water
7. Serve either with milk sauce, or with a sauce made of butter, flour and water in which the onions have cooked
8. Season with salt
