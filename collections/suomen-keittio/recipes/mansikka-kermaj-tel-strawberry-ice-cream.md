## Ingredients

- 1 quart cream
- 1 box strawberries
- 1½ cups sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- Juice of 1½ lemon
- 4 egg yolks
- Salt

## Directions

1. Scald 1½ pint of cream or milk.
2. Beat ¾ cup of sugar with the egg yolks, add slowly the scalded milk, stir in a double boiler until thick.
3. When cooled down, add the strawberries (that have to be washed, let stand in the rest of the sugar and rubbed through a sieve).
4. Mix with vanilla and lemon juice.
5. Freeze.
6. May be prepared without eggs.
