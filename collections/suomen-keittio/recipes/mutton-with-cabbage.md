## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. of breast of mutton
- 1-2 cabbages
- 2 tablespoonfuls of salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 10 peppercorns
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 quart of boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces and crack the bones.
2. Chop the cabbage coarsely.
3. Put the butter into a kettle and lay meat and cabbage alternately in.
4. Sprinkle with salt, flour and pepper.
5. Pour 1 quart of boiling water over.
6. Cook tightly covered on a slow fire 2-3 hours, or until the meat and cabbage are tender.
7. Meat may also be browned first and a little water put into the pan, then pour the water over the meat and cabbage.
