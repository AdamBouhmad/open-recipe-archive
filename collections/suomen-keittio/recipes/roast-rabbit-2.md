## Ingredients

- 1 rabbit
- ½ lemon
- ¼ lb. of pork
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 2 cupfuls of meat stock or water
- flour
- cream

## Directions

1. Clean the rabbit, wash and soak in water 2 hours to remove the skin easier.
2. Rub with lemon, line with thin slices of pork.
3. Melt butter in a dripping-pan, place the rabbit in it, sprinkle with salt and add gradually the meat stock.
4. Roast 1-1½ hours.
5. Prepare a sauce from the gravy in pan by adding a little flour and cream.
