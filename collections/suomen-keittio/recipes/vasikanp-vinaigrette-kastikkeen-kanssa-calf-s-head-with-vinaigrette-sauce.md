## Ingredients

- calf's head
- spices
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 10 peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 onion
- 1 carrot
- sprig of parsley
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter
- 3 tablespoons salad oil
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 teaspoon grated onion
- chopped parsley
- caper
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the calf's head with spices
2. Cut in nice pieces
3. Baste the pieces with tomato sauce made of tomato and cornstarch
4. Serve with vinaigrette sauce made of 3 tablespoons of salad oil, 1 tablespoon of vinegar, 1 teaspoon of grated onion, chopped parsley, caper, salt and pepper mixed
