## Ingredients

- apples
- butter
- sugar
- chicken livers
- salt
- pepper
- beef stock
- toast points
- Madeira sauce

## Directions

1. Pare the apples and cut lengthwise in six pieces
2. Cook in butter with a little sugar until softened
3. Fry the livers in butter
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
5. Add a little beef stock and let come to boiling point
6. Arrange the apples on a serving dish
7. Lay the livers on them
8. Line the dish with toast points
9. Serve with Madeira sauce
