## Ingredients

- slices of bread
- butter
- steak
- ham
- tongue
- grated cheese
- mustard
- pepper
- pickled cucumbers
- capers
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Brown slices of bread on both sides in butter.
2. Chop or grind steak, ham and tongue.
3. Mix with grated cheese, butter, a little mustard and pepper.
4. Stir until smooth.
5. Lay on the toast.
6. Garnish with chopped pickles, capers and hard-boiled egg.
