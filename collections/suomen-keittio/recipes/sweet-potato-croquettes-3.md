## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of potato mash
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- beaten yolks of 2 eggs
- parsley
- pepper
- egg for dipping
- bread crumbs
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Form into round or oval balls.
2. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
3. Fry in lard.
