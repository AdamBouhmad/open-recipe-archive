## Ingredients

- 1 cup ground nuts
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 2 cups boiled rice
- 1 cup cream
- 1 cup vegetable stock or water
- 1/4 pound butter
- 3 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Add the ground nuts to the rice or grind the rice together with the nuts.
2. Mix with bread crumbs soaked in vegetable stock or tomato juice.
3. Add beaten eggs and melted butter.
4. Combine with the mixture of rice and nuts.
5. Dilute with cream if necessary.
6. Season and stir well.
7. Place onto a buttered pan and form into a loaf.
8. Bake for 1 hour.
9. If brushed with egg, the steak will look nicer.
10. Prepare a gravy of the drippings.
