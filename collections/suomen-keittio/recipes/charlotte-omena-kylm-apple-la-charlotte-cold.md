## Ingredients

- apples
- sugar
- lemon juice
- carmine coloring
- cornstarch
- 1 cup cream
- 4 tablespoons dissolved gelatine
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1/2 cup whipped cream
- vanilla
- sponge cake

## Directions

1. Pare and core the apples, cut crosswise in slices
2. Cook with sugar and some lemon juice
3. When tender, place to cool
4. Baste with a sauce made of the juice of the apples colored with carmine and thickened with cornstarch
5. Scald the cream with sugar and gelatine
6. When cool, add lemon juice, vanilla and whipped cream
7. Dip small molds into cold water, fill with this mixture and let set
8. Cut foundations from sponge cake
9. First place basted apple slices on the foundations
10. Pour the cream mixture from the molds over the apple slices
11. Arrange in a circle on a platter
12. Place whipped cream in the center
