## Ingredients

- 1 medium head of cabbage
- Salted water
- Mushroom ball paste

## Directions

1. Cook the cabbage in salted water until tender
2. Drain and cool
3. Prepare a paste of mushrooms as for mushroom balls
4. Place the cabbage on a towel wrung out of water
5. Open the leaves carefully
6. Fill the spaces between the leaves with the paste
7. Close the cabbage upon itself
8. Tie a towel around
9. Cook in the liquor of the cabbage for 1 hour
10. Serve with milk sauce or make a sauce of the liquor of the cabbage
