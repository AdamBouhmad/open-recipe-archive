## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 10 ozs. of sugar
- 1/4 cupful of cream
- 3/4 teaspoonful of soda
- 3/4 lb. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of corn starch
- 1 1/4 lbs. of flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs with sugar until light
2. Add cream, soda and corn starch
3. Wash butter, work to a cream and stir into the above mixture
4. Lastly mix with flour
5. Knead thoroughly
6. Roll into a thin sheet, perforate with fork and shape into round cookies
7. Bake on greased sheet in oven to a light brown
