## Ingredients

- 3 quarts of meat stock
- 1/2 head of cabbage
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- pepper and salt
- meatballs

## Directions

1. Cook the head of cabbage in salted water.
2. Drain well.
3. Cut into narrow strips and fry in butter in a pan until brown, turning often.
4. Pour hot meat stock into it and boil until the cabbage is soft.
5. Season with salt and pepper and serve with small meatballs.
