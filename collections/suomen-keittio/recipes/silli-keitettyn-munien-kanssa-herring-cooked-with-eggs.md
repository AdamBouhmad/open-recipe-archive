## Ingredients

- 4 salted herrings
- water
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak 4 salted herrings, split, skin and bone.
2. Pour boiling water over and let simmer for a little while.
3. Chop 3 hard-boiled eggs.
4. Arrange the fish in layers with the eggs on a serving-dish and pour browned butter over.
