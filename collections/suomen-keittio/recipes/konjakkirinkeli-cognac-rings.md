## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 lbs. of butter
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1 lb. of flour
- 1/4 cupful of cognac

## Directions

1. Beat butter to a froth
2. Add sugar, cognac and flour
3. Form the batter into small rings
4. Bake in a hot oven
