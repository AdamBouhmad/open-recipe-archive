## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/2 teaspoon onion
- 3/4 tablespoon flour
- 1 tablespoon chopped mushrooms
- 1/2 cup bouillon
- salt
- nutmeg
- 1 cup chopped spinach
- 1 egg yolk
- boiled macaroni

## Directions

1. Cook and chop spinach.
2. Cook 1 tablespoon of butter with 1/2 teaspoon onion, add 3/4 tablespoon flour, 1 tablespoon chopped mushrooms, 1/2 cup bouillon, salt, nutmeg and 1 cup chopped spinach; bring quickly to boiling, add 1 yolk of egg.
3. Butter small molds or a large one, cut boiled macaroni in 1/4 inch long rings; line the mold with macaroni.
4. Fill with spinach mixture.
5. Bake in oven in a pan of water until firm.
6. Unmold onto a platter.
7. Serve with tomato sauce.
