## Ingredients

- eel
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter or lard
- lemon juice
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the eel and cut in pieces two inches long.
2. If small use the fishes whole.
3. Boil with salt and pepper.
4. Dry and roll in flour.
5. Fry in butter or lard until nicely browned.
6. Arrange on a platter and pour over the butter in which they were cooked, mixed with a little lemon juice.
7. Garnish with fine parsley.
