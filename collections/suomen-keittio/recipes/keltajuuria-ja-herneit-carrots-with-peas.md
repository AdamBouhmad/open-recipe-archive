## Ingredients

- small carrots
- salted water or beef stock
- boiled green peas in equal part to carrots
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape small carrots and cut into small dice.
2. Cook in salted water or beef stock until tender.
3. Strain and add equal part of boiled green peas.
4. Season with salt, pepper, butter and parsley.
