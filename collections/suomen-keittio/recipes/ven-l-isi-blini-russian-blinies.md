## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup cream
- 2 cups water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 4 tablespoons melted butter
- 3 1/2 cups buckwheat flour
- 1 cup white flour
- 2 cups warmed milk
- 1/2 yeast cake

## Directions

1. Dilute the yeast in lukewarm water
2. Beat the eggs
3. Add cream, water, salt, butter and flour
4. Let rise over night
5. In the morning pour into the batter lukewarm milk enough to make a thin batter
6. Fry as directed for pancakes
7. Serve with melted butter and maple syrup
