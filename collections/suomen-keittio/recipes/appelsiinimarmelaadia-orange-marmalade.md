## Ingredients

- 4 pounds oranges
- 2 lemons
- 5 pounds granulated sugar
- 1½ quarts water

## Directions

1. Wipe carefully oranges and lemons.
2. Peel and remove the white part of the peel.
3. Cut the peel in strips, simmer in 1½ cups water for ½ hour.
4. Divide the fruit sections, remove the seeds and the fibre.
5. Let simmer in 1½ quarts water for ½ hour.
6. Drain without stirring through a fine cheese cloth.
7. Cook stirring constantly the drained liquor with sugar and the cooked yellow rind in its liquor for 15 minutes.
8. Fill the glass jars, cover as directed.
9. Serve with tea and toast.
