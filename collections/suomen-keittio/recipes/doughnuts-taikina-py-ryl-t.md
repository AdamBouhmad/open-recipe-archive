## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cupful of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of soda
- 3/4 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs with sugar to a froth.
2. Add creamed butter, milk, salt, cinnamon, cream of tartar and soda mixed with flour.
3. Work in flour enough to make the batter tolerable to handle.
4. Roll out to 1/2 inch thickness, shape into rings.
5. Cook in fat on both sides until light brown.
6. Put on brown paper to drain.
7. Dip in powdered sugar.
8. Ensure fat is moderately hot - if not hot enough, doughnuts will absorb it; if too hot, they will brown before sufficiently risen.
9. Avoid turning too often. Brown on one side before turning.
