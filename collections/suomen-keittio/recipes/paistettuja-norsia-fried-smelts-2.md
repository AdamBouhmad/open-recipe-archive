## Ingredients

- smelts
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- lard
- crackerdust
- egg
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of lemon juice
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- lemon halves

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and wipe smelts
2. Roll in flour mixed with salt and pepper
3. Fry in butter
4. When lard is used for frying, roll the smelts in crackerdust, dip in egg and roll in crackerdust again
5. Fry 3-4 fishes at a time
6. When done lay them on brown paper to drain
7. Prepare sauce of 3 tablespoonfuls of butter rubbed to a froth mixed with 3 tablespoonfuls of lemon juice and 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
8. Serve with the sauce in cups made of halves of lemons
