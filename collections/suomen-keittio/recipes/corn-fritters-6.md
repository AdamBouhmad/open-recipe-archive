## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of fresh corn, grated from ears, or canned corn pressed through a colander
- 1/2 cupful of cracker crumbs
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 2 eggs, yolks and whites beaten separately
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Mix corn with cracker crumbs soaked in milk
2. Add eggs (yolks and whites beaten separately), salt and pepper
3. Fry in a fryingpan, dropping the batter by spoonfuls into the lard
4. Brown on both sides
5. Or cook the fritters in deep lard
