## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups fish stock
- 1 can (1/4 quart) or 1 pound of fresh lobster
- 1 or 2 egg yolks
- Salt and pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make a gravy of butter, flour and fish stock
2. Add lobster cut in pieces, salt and pepper
3. Last of all mix with yolks of egg beaten
4. Flavor with lemon juice
5. Lobster sauce may be made of Hollandaise sauce by adding lobster to it
