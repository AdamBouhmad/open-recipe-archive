## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup rice
- 3 cups milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- dash of cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3 eggs
- raisins and almonds (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook a porridge of the milk and the rice
2. Add the butter, the cinnamon and the eggs beaten with sugar
3. Bake in a buttered mold in a pan of water for 30 or 40 minutes
4. Raisins and chopped almonds may be used in the pudding, if desired
