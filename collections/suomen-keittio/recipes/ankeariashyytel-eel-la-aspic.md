## Ingredients

- Eel
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice
- Water
- Onion
- Fish stock
- Gelatine

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and remove the backbone.
2. Cut larger fishes in pieces 3 inches thick.
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and lemon juice and put aside for 10 minutes.
4. Roll together and fasten with string.
5. Boil slowly with salt, pepper and onion until tender.
6. Lift off to cool.
7. Make aspic of the fish stock as directed for salmon.
8. Cut the rolls crosswise in slices and place in a mold to set with the aspic.
9. Or pour some aspic on a platter, lay the slices of fish over it, and cover with aspic.
10. Arrange the jellied pieces on a platter in a ring and place bits of jelly in the center.
11. Garnish around with tomato, lemon and lettuce leaves.
