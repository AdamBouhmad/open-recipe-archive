## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of water
- 2 quarts of bouillon
- 1 celery
- 1 onion
- bone of a boiled ham
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the beans and soak them over night in cold water
2. Put to boil in the same water with the bone of ham, celery and onion
3. Let cook until soft
4. Rub through a colander
5. Thicken with butter and flour
6. Add the boiling bouillon into it
7. Continue cooking for 10 minutes
8. Season with salt and pepper
9. Serve with rice, eggs boiled hard and cut into slices and fried bread dice
