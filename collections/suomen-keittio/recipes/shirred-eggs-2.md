## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- salt
- cream (optional)
- pepper (optional)
- cheese or chopped tongue (optional)

## Directions

1. Butter the eggshirrers.
2. Break the eggs carefully into them, one in each, sprinkle with a little salt and bake in oven until firm.
3. Before breaking the eggs into the cups a little cream may be put in them.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and when half firm, sprinkle with cheese or chopped tongue.
