## Ingredients

- 2 quarts salmon or fish stock
- 2.5 pounds fresh salmon or 0.5 can of salmon
- 2 tablespoons butter or lobster butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup cream
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the salmon in a small quantity of salt water until tender.
2. Remove the skin and the bones and cut some nice pieces from the fish to be served in the soup.
3. Chop finely the rest of the fish, cook in fish stock, force through a sieve.
4. Thicken the soup with butter and flour.
5. Beat the yolks, add to the cream and pour the soup gradually into the cream.
6. Serve with pieces of salmon, hard boiled yolks or fish quenelles.
7. Remains of lobster or crab may be served in the soup.
