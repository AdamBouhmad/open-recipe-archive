## Ingredients

- eel
- water or fish stock
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Clean and cut eel into 2-3 inch pieces.
2. Boil in water or fish stock with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
3. Dry them and roll in melted butter.
4. Roast over a gentle fire.
