## Ingredients

- 1 pint clams
- 1 heaping tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 cup clam liquor
- 1 cup scalded cream
- a pinch of soda
- 2 egg yolks
- some cream
- salt
- pepper
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Remove the hard parts of the clams and chop fine
2. Cook the heaping tablespoon of butter with the chopped clams
3. Add the tablespoon of flour and 1/2 cup of clam liquor
4. Continue cooking for a few minutes
5. Mix the cup of scalded cream with the soft parts of clams
6. Add a pinch of soda to the cream mixture
7. Boil for 2 minutes
8. Shortly before serving, beat 2 egg yolks with some cream
9. Add the beaten egg yolks gradually to the mixture
10. Season with salt and pepper
11. Serve on buttered toast
