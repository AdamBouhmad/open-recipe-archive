## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- 2 cups cream
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook sugar in water for 8 minutes
2. Let cool
3. Add 2 cups cream and 1 teaspoon vanilla when served
4. Serve with chocolate and strawberry ice cream
