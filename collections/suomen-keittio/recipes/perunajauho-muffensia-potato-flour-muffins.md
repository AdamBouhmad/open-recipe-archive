## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- ¾ cup potato flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons ice water
- lemon juice for flavoring

## Directions

1. Sift several times the potato flour with the baking powder.
2. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar, add ice water and the flour.
3. Flavor with lemon juice.
4. Beat until firm the egg whites, fold in the batter.
5. Bake in buttered muffin tins for 15 minutes.
