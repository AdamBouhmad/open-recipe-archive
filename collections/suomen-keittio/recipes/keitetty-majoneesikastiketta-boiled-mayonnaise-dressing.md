## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons salad oil or butter
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- Some pepper and sugar

## Directions

1. Put spices into a small double boiler
2. Mix in the yolks, vinegar and lemon juice
3. Cook stirring well until thick
4. Remove from the fire, add the oil or butter, stirring thoroughly
5. Dilute with cream or lemon juice before serving
