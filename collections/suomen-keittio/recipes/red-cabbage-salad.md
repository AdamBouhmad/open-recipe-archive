## Ingredients

- cabbage
- mustard
- pepper
- sugar
- oil
- vinegar
- parsley
- steak

## Directions

1. Cut cabbage into very thin shreds.
2. Mix together 2 teaspoonfuls of mustard, a little pepper and sugar, 5 tablespoonfuls of oil.
3. Take 1 cupful of vinegar and pour the oil gradually into the vinegar.
4. Pour this dressing on the cabbage and press with a weight for few hours.
5. Place the cabbage in a salad bowl, sprinkle with minced parsley.
6. Serve with steak.
