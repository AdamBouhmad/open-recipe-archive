## Ingredients

- peaches
- sugar
- lemon juice
- pastry for two crusts

## Directions

1. Peel the peaches, cut in pieces.
2. Let stand mixed with sugar and lemon juice for 1 hour.
3. Bake between the crusts in a moderate oven for 1 hour.
