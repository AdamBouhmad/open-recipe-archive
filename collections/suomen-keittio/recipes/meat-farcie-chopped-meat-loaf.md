## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of meat stock or water
- 1 cupful of bread crumbs
- 2 eggs
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1/4 teaspoonful of pepper
- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 cupful of cream

## Directions

1. Soak the bread crumbs in meat stock mixed with cream and add the beaten eggs into it
2. Chop the onion, cook in butter and strain the butter into the mixture
3. Mix with chopped meat and work until smooth
4. At the last add seasonings
5. Turn in a buttered pan and trim into a nice loaf
6. Spread with beaten egg and sprinkle with bread crumbs
7. Bake in oven 1-1½ hours, basting often and adding water or meat stock
8. Serve with brown sauce
