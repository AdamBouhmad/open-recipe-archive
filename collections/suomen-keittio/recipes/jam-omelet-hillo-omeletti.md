## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- sugar
- 3 tablespoonfuls of milk or cream
- jam
- fruit-sauce

## Directions

1. Beat separately the whites and yolks of three eggs.
2. Mix the yolks with sugar, 3 tablespoonfuls of milk or cream and the beaten whites.
3. Fry as directed; spread with jam, fold once, and serve at once with fruit-sauce.
