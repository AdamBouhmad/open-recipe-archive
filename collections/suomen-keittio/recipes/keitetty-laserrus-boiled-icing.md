## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup water
- 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1 egg white
- lemon juice or vanilla for flavoring

## Directions

1. Put 1 cup sugar, 3/4 cup water and 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar in a saucepan over the fire
2. Cook until the syrup will thread when dropped from the tip of a spoon
3. Beat until frothy the white of an egg
4. Pour gradually the syrup in stirring well
5. Flavor with lemon juice or vanilla
6. By using 2 egg whites with 1 cup of sugar, the icing will be made thinner
