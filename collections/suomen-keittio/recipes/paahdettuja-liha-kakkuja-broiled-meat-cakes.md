## Ingredients

- 3 pounds of chopped meat
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Mix the meat with rubbed butter, salt and pepper
2. Stir until smooth
3. Form into round thin cakes
4. Broil or fry in a pan
5. Arrange fried slices of onion on the cakes
6. Serve with brown sauce or parsley butter
