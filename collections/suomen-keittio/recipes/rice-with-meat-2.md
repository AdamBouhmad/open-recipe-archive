## Ingredients

- boiled rice
- butter
- cold chopped meat
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- lemon juice
- 1/4 cupful of bread crumbs
- 1 beaten egg
- beef stock

## Directions

1. Put a layer of boiled rice on bottom and sides of a well buttered pudding dish.
2. Fill the dish with cold chopped meat, seasoned with salt, pepper, onion and lemon juice.
3. Add 1/4 cupful of bread crumbs, 1 beaten egg and beef stock to moisten the mixture to a soft paste.
4. Put a layer of rice on the meat and leave a few dots of butter on the rice.
5. Bake in oven about 40 minutes, covered with buttered paper.
