## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of scalded milk
- 3/4 cupful of sugar
- 1/4 cupful of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/3 cake of yeast
- 2 eggs
- a dash of nutmeg
- flour

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast and mix with milk, salt and flour to a thick batter.
2. Let rise over night.
3. In the morning add melted butter and eggs whipped with sugar.
4. Work more flour in if desired.
5. Set to rise again.
6. Roll out to 1/2 inch thickness, shape into rings or round cakes.
7. Let rise and cook in hot fat until light brown.
8. Dip in powdered sugar while still hot.
