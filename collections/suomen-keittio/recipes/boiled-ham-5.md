## Ingredients

- ham
- mustard
- bread crumbs
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the ham in cold water over night.
2. Wash carefully and put into cold water.
3. Place over the fire.
4. Let come to boil, simmer, allowing 20 minutes to each pound.
5. When done, lift it out of the liquor, remove the skin and spread the ham with mustard.
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and powdered sugar.
7. Bake in the oven until brown.
