## Ingredients

- 8 egg whites
- ½ pound granulated sugar
- ½ pound powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the whites to a stiff froth.
2. Add gradually the sugar sifted several times.
3. May be flavored with vanilla, lemon or orange.
4. Drop by the spoonful or force through a pastry bag on paper.
5. Place the paper on a baking sheet.
6. Bake in a slow oven.
7. When light brown and hard, remove from the oven and let cool.
8. Remove from the paper with a knife.
9. Put two together filling them with whipped cream flavored with vanilla, lemon, chocolate, jam etc.
