## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 cupful of oatmeal
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 cupful of water or milk
- 1 egg
- 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix flour with sugar, salt and baking powder, work butter into it
2. Whip egg with sugar, add milk into it and pour it slowly into the flour
3. Roll the batter thin, cut with cutter into small cookies
4. Lay on greased baking-sheet and bake in a hot oven to light brown
