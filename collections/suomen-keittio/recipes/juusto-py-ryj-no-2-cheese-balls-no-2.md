## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons flour
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 9 tablespoons grated cheese
- 3 or 6 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook butter and flour in a saucepan.
2. Add milk and cheese, stirring until smooth, and let boil for a few minutes.
3. Place to cool.
4. Mix with yolks of egg and bread crumbs so as to make a batter thick enough to be formed into small balls.
5. Roll the balls in flour.
6. Cook in milk or lard or fry in butter.
