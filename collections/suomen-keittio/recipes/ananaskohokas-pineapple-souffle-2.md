## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound of pineapples cut in dice
- whites of 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel fruit and cut into cubes.
2. Sprinkle with 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar and juice of a lemon.
3. Let stand for a while.
4. Beat whites of eggs to a froth, add little by little the remaining sugar and fruit stirring gently.
5. Butter a mold, turn the mixture into it.
6. Bake 10—15 min.
7. Serve with lemon sauce.
