## Ingredients

- White part: 1/2 cup butter, 1 cup sugar, 4 egg yolks, 1/2 cup milk, 2 teaspoons baking powder, 1 1/2 cups flour, 1 teaspoon vanilla or lemon juice
- Dark part: 1/2 cup butter, 1 cup brown sugar, 4 egg yolks, 1/2 cup molasses, 1/2 cup milk, 1 teaspoon soda, 1/2 teaspoon each cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg, 2 cups flour

## Directions

1. For the white part: Rub butter with sugar until creamy, add egg yolks beaten to a froth, milk, baking powder sifted with flour, and vanilla or lemon juice, then fold in beaten egg whites
2. For the dark part: Rub butter with brown sugar until creamy, add egg yolks beaten to a froth, molasses, milk, soda, and spices mixed with flour, then fold in beaten egg whites
3. Alternatively, the dark part may be made by adding 1 1/2 ounces melted chocolate to the white part
4. Butter a tin and lay in both mixtures by the spoonful alternately so as to resemble marble
