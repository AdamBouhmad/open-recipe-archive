## Ingredients

- salt
- pepper
- ginger
- slices of apple
- stoned prunes
- or paste made of bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Rub the inside of pig with salt, pepper and ginger.
2. Stuff with slices of apple and stoned prunes, or with paste made of bread crumbs as for chicken and turkey.
3. Sew up and place, back upward, into a buttered roaster.
4. Spread the skin with butter, add water for basting into the pan.
5. If getting too brown, cover with paper.
6. Allow 2-3 hours for baking.
7. When done lift on a platter.
8. Remove the meat from the backbone, cut into slices and replace around the backbone.
9. Prepare a sauce of the drippings.
10. Remove the grease carefully.
