## Ingredients

- 1 quart of small white onions
- 1 quart of vinegar
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of white pepper
- mace, cloves and ginger, a little of each

## Directions

1. Wash onions, pour boiling water on and peel when water gets cool.
