## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups bread crumbs
- 2 1/2 cups milk
- 3/4 cup currants
- 1/2 lemon juice and rind
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Scald the milk with the butter and add the bread crumbs
2. When cool, mix with the washed currants, the lemon juice and rind
3. Add the egg yolks beaten with sugar
4. Last of all add the egg whites beaten firm
5. Pour the mixture into a buttered mold
6. Bake in a pan of water
7. Serve with fruit sauce
