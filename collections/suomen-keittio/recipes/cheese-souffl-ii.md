## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of thick milk sauce
- 1 cupful of grated American cheese
- 3 eggs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Mix milk sauce and cheese well together
2. Add gradually yolks, pepper and salt
3. Fold in whites, beaten stiff, lastly
4. Butter small molds and fill with this mixture
5. Bake in oven in a pan of water for 15 minutes
6. Serve on a folded napkin
