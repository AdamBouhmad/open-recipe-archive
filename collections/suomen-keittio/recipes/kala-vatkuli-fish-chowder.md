## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of fresh fish
- 4 cupfuls of potatoes
- 1 onion
- 1/2 lb. of pork
- 3 cupfuls of milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- pepper and salt
- crackers

## Directions

1. Bone the fish and cut in pieces 1 inch long.
2. Fry onion in butter, strain and put the butter again into the frying pan to fry the fish in.
3. Cut pork in cubes, roll in flour, and fry with a lump of butter, taking care not to burn it.
4. Drain and fry the boiled potatoes in the drippings, cooking them for a little while.
5. Put into the kettle layers of potatoes, fish, pork and onion.
6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
7. Pour on it a little fish stock, let boil 10 min.
8. Add scalded milk to it, mix with a little butter, chopped parsley and crackers soaked in milk, and let come to a boil.
9. Turn the fish chowder on a serving dish, cover with soaked crackers and pour the sauce over.
