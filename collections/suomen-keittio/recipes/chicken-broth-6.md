## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of chicken cut into pieces
- 4 quarts of water
- 1 onion
- 2 stalks or 1 root of celery
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the fat, put the meat in cold water on a slow fire.
2. When boiled 5 minutes add salt and vegetables cut into pieces, and let cook until the meat is tender.
3. Remove the breast and when boiled a few hours more strain the soup.
4. Remove the fat carefully.
5. Serve with the breastmeat cut into cubes and boiled rice.
