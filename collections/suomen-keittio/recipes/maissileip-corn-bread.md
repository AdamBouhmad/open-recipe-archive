## Ingredients

- 1 cup cornmeal
- 1 cup flour
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 or 2 eggs
- 4 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix and sift together all the dry ingredients
2. Add the eggs beaten with sugar, milk and butter stirring well
3. Bake in a buttered shallow tin in a very hot oven for 20 or 30 minutes
