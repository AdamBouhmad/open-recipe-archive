## Ingredients

- cold chicken
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- mayonnaise
- chicken gravy or cream
- green pepper (optional)
- buttered bread slices

## Directions

1. Force cold chicken through the meat chopper.
2. Season with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
3. Soften with mayonnaise, chicken gravy or cream.
4. Mix with chopped green pepper, if desired.
5. Use for double sandwiches.
