## Ingredients

- 1 duck
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 teaspoonfuls of salt
- 2 cupfuls of meat stock

## Directions

1. Clean the duck.
2. Rub with salt inside and out.
3. Melt butter in a dripping-pan, place the duck in it and let brown well on every side.
4. Sprinkle with salt, and pour into the pan hot meat stock for basting.
5. Baste frequently.
6. Roast slowly 1 1/2 - 2 hours.
7. Strain the gravy, skim and season as usually.
