## Ingredients

- apples
- sugar
- water
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pare and core apples
2. Fill the cores with sugar
3. Bake until tender
4. Mash the apples
5. Add water, allowing 1 cup water and 1 teaspoon lemon juice to a large apple
6. Let stand for 1/2 hour
7. Strain
