## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of Brussels sprouts
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Clean Brussels sprouts
2. Soak 1/2 hour in water mixed with vinegar
3. Put in slightly salted boiling water
4. Cook until tender
5. Remove quickly from the water
6. Place on a serving dish
7. Baste with melted butter
