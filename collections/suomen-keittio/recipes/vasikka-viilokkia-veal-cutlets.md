## Ingredients

- veal cutlets
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- pork fat or butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. The cutlets may be fried whole or cut in nice pieces.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
4. Fry in pork fat or butter to a golden brown on both sides.
5. Arrange on a hot platter, sprinkle with lemon juice.
6. Serve with brown-, tomato- or Bearnaise sauce.
