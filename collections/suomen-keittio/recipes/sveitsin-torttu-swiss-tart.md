## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- equal weight of butter
- equal weight of sugar
- equal weight of flour

## Directions

1. Rub the butter with sugar until creamy
2. Add the eggs one by one and the flour
3. Beat hard
4. Divide into two parts and bake separately
5. Pile the cakes on a serving platter having jam between
6. Let cool
7. Cover with whipped cream and serve
