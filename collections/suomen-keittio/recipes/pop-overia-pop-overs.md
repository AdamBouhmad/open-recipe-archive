## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 or 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Sift flour with salt
2. Beat eggs, add milk and pour the mixture gradually into the flour
3. Beat well for 3 minutes
4. Heat and butter muffin tins, fill to 3/4 of their height
5. Bake for 30 or 40 minutes in a hot oven
6. Do not touch them before they are thoroughly done
