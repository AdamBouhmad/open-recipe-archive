## Ingredients

- 1 quart of peaches
- 1 ounce of gelatine
- sugar to taste
- a little lemon juice
- 2 cupfuls of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Skin peaches, stone and force through a sieve.
2. Dissolve gelatine into the fruit juice by heating.
3. Add sugar to taste and a little lemon juice.
4. When cool, add two cupfuls of whipped cream and pour in a mold to form.
