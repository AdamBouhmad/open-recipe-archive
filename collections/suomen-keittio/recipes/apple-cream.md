## Ingredients

- 1 pint of cream
- cupfuls of apple sauce (cooked with sugar)
- 1/2 grated lemon rind
- 7 leaves or 5 tablespoons of gelatine
- fruit and macaroons for garnish

## Directions

1. Mix apple sauce with lemon rind and gelatine.
2. Add cream, whipped to a froth.
3. Wet a mold with cold water and pour the cream mixture into it.
4. Let get firm.
5. Turn on a serving dish and garnish with fruit and macaroons.
