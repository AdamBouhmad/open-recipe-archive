## Ingredients

- 2 quarts bouillon
- 1 cup carrots cut in pieces
- 1 small onion
- 2 stalks celery
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup cream
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse, peel and cut in pieces the vegetables.
2. Put into a saucepan with 2 tablespoons of butter and some salt, cook slowly until lightly brown, stirring continually.
3. Add the bouillon and simmer until the vegetables are tender.
4. Press through a strainer, thicken with butter and flour.
5. Last of all add the egg yolks whipped with cream.
6. May be prepared without yolks and cream.
