## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of scalded milk
- 1 yeast cake
- 1 lb. of flour
- 1/2 grated lemon rind
- 6-8 eggs
- 12 ozs. of butter
- a little salt and sugar

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and sugar until light, add milk, salt, lemon, yeast and flour, mixed with melted or softened butter.
2. Mix the batter well and let it rise.
3. Knead on a pastry board and put in a mold to rise.
4. Bake in a moderately hot oven.
5. When done, brush over with a warm mixture made of sugar, liqueur and water.
6. Let cool and brush with the following mixture: 1 cupful of wine, 1/2 lb. of sifted sugar.
7. You may also add currants and raisins to this.
