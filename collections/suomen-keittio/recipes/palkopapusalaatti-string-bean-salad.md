## Ingredients

- string beans
- salt
- lettuce leaves
- mayonnaise
- green peas
- beets
- tomatoes
- radishes

## Directions

1. Cut the string beans lengthwise
2. Boil in salted water until tender
3. Let cool
4. Arrange on a bed of lettuce
5. Pour mayonnaise over
6. String beans may be mixed with green peas and served with mayonnaise
7. Garnish with beets, tomatoes or radishes
