## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of nuts
- 1 cupful of peas
- 1 cupful of boiled potatoes
- 3/4 lb. of butter
- 2 cupfuls of bread crumbs
- 2 cupfuls of cream
- 4 eggs
- salt
- 1 stalk of celery
- 1 onion

## Directions

1. Grind boiled vegetables and nuts in meat mill
2. Add bread crumbs soaked in cream
3. Add beaten eggs and seasonings
4. Rub briskly
5. Form into a loaf and bake in oven, or form into croquettes or fillets
6. Dip formed pieces in egg and roll in bread crumbs
7. Fry in butter
8. Prepare a sauce from the drippings in the pan
9. Serve with vegetables
