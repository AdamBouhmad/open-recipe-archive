## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of granulated sugar
- 1/2 lb. of powdered sugar
- 8 whites of eggs

## Directions

1. Whip eggs to a stiff froth, add slowly sugar, sifted many times, into it.
2. May be flavored with vanilla, lemon or orange.
3. Put the mixture on paper with spoon or press it through a pastry bag.
4. Place the paper on board or in baking pan.
5. Put in oven to bake slowly.
6. When light brown and dry, take them out, let cool and remove the paper with knife.
7. Put the meringues together in pairs combining them with the same mixture or fill with whipped cream, flavored with vanilla, lemon, coffee, chocolate, etc.
