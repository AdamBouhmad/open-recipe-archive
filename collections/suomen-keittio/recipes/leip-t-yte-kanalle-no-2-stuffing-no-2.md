## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- ½ cup bouillon
- 1 egg
- Salt
- Pepper
- Chicken liver and heart

## Directions

1. Soak the bread, squeeze dry.
2. Fry in a pan with onion, add parsley and bread crumbs, continue frying for a while, dilute with bouillon, add 1 egg, salt, pepper and the chicken liver and heart chopped.
3. The same stuffing may be used for duck and goose. Add ½ cup of strained tomato instead of bouillon and some grated celery. May be mixed with mashed sweet potatoes also.
