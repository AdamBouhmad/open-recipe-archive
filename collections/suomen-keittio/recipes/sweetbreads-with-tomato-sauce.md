## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- pork
- vegetables
- meat stock
- tomato sauce or Allemande sauce
- boiled artichokes
- rice
- mushroom sauce
- french peas

## Directions

1. Boil sweetbreads and place under a weight for a little while.
2. Cut in two lengthwise and place in a pan.
3. Put a thin slice of pork on each.
4. Cut vegetables into dice, put in the pan with the sweetbreads and pour meat stock over them until the bottom is covered.
5. Bake in a slow oven about 15 minutes, basting often.
6. Mask with tomato or Allemande sauce.
7. Arrange boiled artichokes on a foundation of rice.
8. Place the sweetbreads on top.
9. Serve with mushroom sauce in the center of the foundation.
10. Garnish around with french peas.
