## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of farina
- 4 cupfuls of milk
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of pepper
- yolks of 3 eggs
- bread crumbs
- egg
- lard

## Directions

1. Cook a porridge of farina and milk.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Add yolks of eggs and cool.
4. Form into balls.
5. Dip in bread crumbs, in egg and bread crumbs again.
6. Fry in lard.
