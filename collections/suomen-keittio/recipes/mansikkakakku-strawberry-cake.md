## Ingredients

- 1 quart strawberries
- 5 egg yolks
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Mash 1 quart of strawberries.
2. Beat 5 egg yolks with 3/4 cup of sugar, add to the strawberries.
3. Mix in 1 cup of bread crumbs.
4. Last of all add the whites beaten stiff.
5. Bake in a crockery mold.
6. Serve cold with cream.
