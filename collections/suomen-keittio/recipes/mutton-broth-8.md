## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of lean mutton
- 3 quarts of cold water
- 6 peppercorns
- 1 tablespoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces and remove the fat.
2. Put the meat into the kettle with bones, pour cold water on them.
3. Place on a slow fire to boil for a few hours.
4. Strain and remove the fat.
5. Serve with rice or barley.
