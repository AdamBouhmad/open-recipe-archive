## Ingredients

- 2 ozs. of almonds
- 2 ozs. of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 2 eggs
- 6 leaves or 4 tablespoonfuls of gelatine
- 1 pint of rich cream

## Directions

1. Blanch almonds and pound with sugar to a paste.
2. Stir the almond paste, milk and eggs on the fire to a thick mixture.
3. Add gelatine leaves, dissolved in a small quantity of water.
4. When cool, add the whipped cream into it.
5. Wet a mold with cold water and pour the mixture into it.
6. Set to stiffen.
7. Unmold and garnish with macaroons and berries.
