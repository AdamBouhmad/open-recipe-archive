## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- butter
- toast
- eggs
- milksauce
- onion juice
- grated cheese
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Fry fresh mushrooms in butter.
2. Place fried fresh mushrooms on buttered toast.
3. Arrange them upon a dish, and line with poached eggs.
4. Pour over the eggs milksauce mixed with onion juice and grated cheese.
5. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and brown in oven.
