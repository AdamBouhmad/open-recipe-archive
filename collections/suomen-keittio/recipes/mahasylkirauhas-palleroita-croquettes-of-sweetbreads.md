## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sweetbreads
- 2 ounces salted tongue
- 3 ounces mushrooms
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 cup bouillon
- 1/2 cup cream
- 2 yolks of egg
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare boiled and butter-fried sweetbreads, tongue and mushrooms as indicated in the recipe.
