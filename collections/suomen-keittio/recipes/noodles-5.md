## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/2 tablespoonful water
- salt
- flour enough to make a dough

## Directions

1. Beat eggs to a froth
2. Add water, salt and flour enough to make a dough
3. Knead the dough thoroughly
4. Roll as thin as paper
5. Cover with a damp towel and let stand for a while
6. Cut with a sharp knife into nice strips
7. Let dry on the table
8. Cook the noodles for 15 minutes before adding to the soup
9. They may also be rolled together and cut with a knife into very thin slices
