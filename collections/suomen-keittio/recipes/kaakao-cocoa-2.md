## Ingredients

- 5 cupfuls of milk
- 3½ tablespoonfuls of cocoa
- ¼ cupful of sugar
- a dash of salt
- 1 cupful of boiling water

## Directions

1. Scald 5 cupfuls of milk
2. Mix with 3½ tablespoonfuls of cocoa, ¼ cupful of sugar and a dash of salt
3. Add 1 cupful of boiling water into it
4. Cook for 1 minute
5. Cocoa may be also whipped in double boiler for 5 minutes
6. Serve with whipped cream or as it is
