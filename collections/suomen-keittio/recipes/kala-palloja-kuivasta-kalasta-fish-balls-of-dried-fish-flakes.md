## Ingredients

- salt dried fish
- cold boiled fish
- hot mashed potatoes
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1-2 eggs
- flour

## Directions

1. If kala is too salty, soak it in water.
2. Cook and let cool.
3. Make a mixture of equal quantities of boiled fish and hot mashed potatoes.
4. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of butter and stir well.
5. Add 1-2 eggs.
6. Form into balls or cakes.
7. Dip in flour and place in refrigerator.
8. Can be kept overnight.
9. Fry either in lard or butter.
10. For a nicer appearance, dip in egg and breadcrumbs.
