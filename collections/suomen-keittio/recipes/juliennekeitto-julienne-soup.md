## Ingredients

- 2 quarts meat stock
- 1 carrot
- 1 onion
- 2 stalks celery
- 1 head lettuce
- 1½ cups string-beans
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the vegetables, cut in fine strips and brown in butter.
2. Add to the vegetables the hot meat stock and the string-beans, let boil.
3. When the vegetables are half done, add the head of lettuce cut in strips.
4. When the vegetables are tender, season the soup.
5. Serve very hot.
