## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of chicken fillet
- white of 1 egg
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of cream

## Directions

1. Remove the fibres and pound the fillet to a paste.
2. Add the white of egg and pound again.
3. Rub through a sieve.
4. Add the cream gradually.
