## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of vanilla
- butter
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Beat separately the whites and yolks of 4 eggs.
2. Mix the yolks with 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar and 1 tablespoonful of vanilla and whip well.
3. At the last stir gently into it the hard beaten whites.
4. Turn it into a buttered omelet dish and sprinkle with sugar.
5. Bake in a hot oven until lightly browned.
