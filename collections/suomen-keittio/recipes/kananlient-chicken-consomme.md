## Ingredients

- 4 pound chicken
- 4 quarts water
- 2 stalks celery or 1 1/2 cup celery root
- 1 onion or leek
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 6 peppercorns

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken in pieces, put on the fire in cold water
2. When coming to boil, skim and let simmer for 2 hours
3. Add the vegetables and the spices, continue to boil for 1 hour
4. Strain, let cool
5. The breast and the leg meat may be removed from the kettle when done and used for croquettes, salad, etc.
6. If old chickens are used, the soup will be stronger, but the chicken has to cook longer
