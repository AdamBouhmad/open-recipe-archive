## Ingredients

- smelts
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- lard
- egg
- bread crumbs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon parsley, chopped
- lemon halves

## Directions

1. Clean, rinse and wipe the smelts.
2. Roll in flour mixed with salt and pepper.
3. Fry in butter.
4. If cooked in lard, roll in flour, dip in egg and roll in bread crumbs.
5. Fry three or four fishes at a time.
6. When done, lay them in a hot strainer or on brown paper to drain.
7. Make sauce of 3 tablespoons of creamed butter, 3 tablespoons of lemon juice and 1 tablespoon of chopped parsley.
8. Serve the sauce on the same platter in cups made of halves of lemon or place it in dots around the fish.
