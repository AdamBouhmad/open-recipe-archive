## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1/2 cupful of milk or cream
- 3 eggs
- chopped parsley or chives
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare sauce of butter, flour and milk.
2. Mix with whipped yolks and seasonings.
3. Last of all mix with the hard beaten whites.
4. Put in buttered timbale molds and bake in a pan of water until firm.
5. Serve with Hollandaise or tomato sauce.
6. Garnish the molds with parsley or mix parsley with the mixture of eggs.
