## Ingredients

- 2 middlesized cucumbers
- 2 tablespoonfuls of tomato
- 5 leaves of gelatine
- 1 cupful of chicken stock

## Directions

1. Put chicken stock, cucumbers and tomato on the fire.
2. Let boil until cucumber is soft.
3. Strain and stir gelatine leaves into it.
4. Clarify with two whites of eggs.
5. Line a mold with pieces of cucumber.
6. Pour the jelly in.
