## Ingredients

- salmon
- salad oil
- butter
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- Bearnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the fish in one inch thick pieces.
2. Lay in olive oil for an hour.
3. Wipe, fry on a slow fire basting with butter now and then. The fish needs about 20 minutes to cook.
4. Place on a platter.
5. Put a small piece of butter, a little lemon juice, salt, pepper and fine parsley on each piece.
6. Serve with Bearnaise sauce.
