## Ingredients

- oysters in shells
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the oysters in shells changing the water several times
2. Keep in oven until the shells open
3. Remove carefully the upper shells
4. Put 3 oysters in each deep half of the shells
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
6. Place a little dot of butter on each
7. Dredge with bread crumbs
8. Bake in oven until the bread crumbs are brown
