## Ingredients

- Timbale molds
- Chopped parsley
- Eggs
- Salt
- Butter
- Tomato, mushroom or milk sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Butter carefully timbale molds and sprinkle with chopped parsley on the sides and bottom.
2. Break one egg into each mold.
3. Sprinkle with salt and put a little lump of butter on top.
4. Bake in a pan of water in the oven until firm.
5. Turn out onto a platter and serve with tomato, mushroom or milk sauce.
