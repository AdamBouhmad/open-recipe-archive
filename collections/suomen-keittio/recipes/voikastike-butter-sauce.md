## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 tablespoonful of minced parsley

## Directions

1. Cream well the butter
2. Add juice of 1/2 lemon and minced parsley
3. Pour on broiled meat or fish
4. The sauce may be served without parsley with boiled asparagus and artichokes
