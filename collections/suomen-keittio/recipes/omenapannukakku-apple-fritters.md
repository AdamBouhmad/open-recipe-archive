## Ingredients

- 1/2 tablespoon baking powder
- Some salt
- Apples
- Egg yolks
- Sugar
- Milk
- Butter
- Flour
- Cinnamon
- Cream
- Egg whites

## Directions

1. Pare the apples, cut lengthwise in six or eight parts and slice thinly the parts crosswise.
2. Beat the yolks with sugar, add milk, butter, flour, cinnamon, salt and cream.
3. Add to the batter the slices of apples and last of all the whites beaten stiff.
4. Drop by the spoonful into boiling lard.
5. Cook until brown.
6. Serve with hard sauce.
