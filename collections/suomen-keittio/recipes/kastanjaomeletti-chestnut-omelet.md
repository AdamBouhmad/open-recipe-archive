## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound chestnuts
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons water or milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- cream
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Shell the chestnuts, chop and boil in a small quantity of water until soft.
2. Stir 1 tablespoon of butter with 1 tablespoon of flour in a saucepan.
3. Put the chestnuts in, stirring briskly, let boil quickly.
4. Add some cream and season.
5. Make a French omelet.
6. Pour the mixture on one half and turn the other half over it.
