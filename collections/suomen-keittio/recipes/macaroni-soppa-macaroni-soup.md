## Ingredients

- 1 ounce of macaroni
- 5 cupfuls of bouillon
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook 1 ounce of macaroni for 20 minutes.
2. Drain, cut into small rings.
3. Heat 5 cupfuls of bouillon to the boiling-point.
4. Add the macaroni into it and let boil slowly for a few minutes.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
