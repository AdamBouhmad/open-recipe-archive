## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoonfuls of tea
- 2 cupfuls of water

## Directions

1. Heat the tea-pot, put tea in and pour ¾ cupful of boiling water on
2. Let stand in a warm place for 5 minutes
3. Pour the rest of the water into the pot just before serving
