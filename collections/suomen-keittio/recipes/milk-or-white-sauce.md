## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 1/2 cupful of cream
- white pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cook butter, flour and milk; add cream, pepper and salt.
2. Ready to be served when it comes to boiling-point.
