## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- Salt

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks to a firm froth
2. Add sifted sugar and flavoring, continue to beat for a while
3. Beat egg whites until firm
4. Add gradually sifted flour to whites
5. Combine the mixtures
6. Force through a pastry bag on a baking sheet lined with paper, forming cookies 1 inch wide and 4 inches long
7. Sprinkle with powdered sugar
8. Bake in a slow oven so as not to brown the top
9. May spread with egg white and put two together if preferred, or drop by spoonful on baking sheet
