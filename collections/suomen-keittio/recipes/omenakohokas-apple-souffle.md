## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups apple sauce
- juice of 1 lemon
- 4 egg whites
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Add to the apple sauce the lemon juice, sugar according to taste and the whites beaten stiff.
2. Bake in a buttered mold as directed for souffles.
3. Serve with vanilla sauce or cream.
