## Ingredients

- Tenderloin (selkäliha)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter or olive oil
- Bearnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Cut tenderloin into pieces two inches thick.
2. Pound lightly.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Dip in butter or olive oil.
5. Broil.
6. Serve with Bearnaise sauce.
