## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- hot salted water
- 1 quart liquor
- 8 tablespoons dissolved gelatine, or 10 leaves of gelatine
- 1 1/2 cup tomato
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Soak the tongue in cold water.
2. Scrape carefully in hot water.
3. Place in hot salted water to simmer until tender.
4. Allow 2 or 3 hours for boiling.
5. Skin the tongue.
6. Strain the liquor and skim.
7. Mix 8 tablespoons of dissolved gelatine with 1 quart of liquor (or 10 leaves of gelatine), add 1 1/2 cup of tomato and 2 egg whites.
8. Boil the mixture well while stirring, then let simmer gently for 5 minutes.
9. Strain through a fine cloth.
10. Pour cleared liquor on the bottom of a mold.
11. Cut the tongue into slices and arrange in the mold in the whole form.
12. Gradually pour the jelly on top and let set.
