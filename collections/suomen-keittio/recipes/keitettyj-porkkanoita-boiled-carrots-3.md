## Ingredients

- 1 pound of small uniform carrots
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- butter
- minced parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel carrots and put in boiling water.
2. Add 1 tablespoonful of salt and 1 tablespoonful of sugar.
3. Let simmer gently until tender.
4. Drain water and put butter in the saucepan and place by the fire for a few minutes.
5. Large carrots may also be used, but should be cut into dice or strips.
6. May add 1 tablespoonful of minced parsley.
