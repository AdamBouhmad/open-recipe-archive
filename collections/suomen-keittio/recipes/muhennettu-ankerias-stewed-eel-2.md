## Ingredients

- eel
- water
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- fish stock
- milk sauce
- parsley
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the eel and cut into pieces 1 inch long.
2. Put in cold water, place on range with salt and pepper.
3. Cook until soft.
4. Drain off the water and pour on the fish thin milk sauce seasoned and mixed with parsley.
5. Return to the fire for a little while.
6. Yolks of two eggs mixed with the sauce give a good taste to it.
