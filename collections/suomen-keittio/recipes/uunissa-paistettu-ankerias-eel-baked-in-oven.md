## Ingredients

- eel
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- egg
- crackerdust

## Directions

1. Clean the eel.
2. Split along the back and remove the backbone.
3. Rub with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
4. Roll in egg and crackerdust.
5. Bake in oven for about half an hour.
6. Serve with a sour sauce.
