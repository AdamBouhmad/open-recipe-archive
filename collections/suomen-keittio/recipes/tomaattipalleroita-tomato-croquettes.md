## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiled potatoes
- 2 cups tomatoes
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 2 eggs
- 1 onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Force through a meat grinder the potatoes with the tomatoes
2. Fry in butter the chopped onion
3. Add to the ground potatoes
4. Mix with the eggs beaten until frothy and the bread crumbs
5. Form into any shape preferred
6. Dip in egg, roll in bread crumbs
7. Fry in butter
8. Serve with tomato or rich milk sauce
