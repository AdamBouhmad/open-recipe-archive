## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 3/4 lb. of sugar
- 1 lb. of flour
- 1/4 cupful of brandy

## Directions

1. Work butter to a cream
2. Add sugar, brandy and flour
3. Form into small rings
4. Bake in a hot oven
