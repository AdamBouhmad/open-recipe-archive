## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of butter
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1½ cupfuls of flour
- 5 eggs
- ½ lb. of raisins
- ¼ lb. of peeled and chopped almonds
- ¼ lb. of chopped lemon rind
- ¼ cupful of brandy
- 1 teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Work butter and sugar to a froth
2. Add beaten yolks of eggs
3. Add spices and fruit
4. Mix soda with flour
5. Fold in yolks of eggs beaten to a stiff froth
