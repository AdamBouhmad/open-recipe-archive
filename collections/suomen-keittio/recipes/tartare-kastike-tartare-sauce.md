## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup mayonnaise prepared with mustard
- 1/2 tablespoon capers
- 1/2 tablespoon olives
- 1/2 tablespoon pickles
- 1/2 tablespoon parsley, all chopped
- 1 cup milk sauce (for serving hot)

## Directions

1. Mix 1/2 cup of mayonnaise prepared with mustard with 1/2 tablespoon of capers, 1/2 tablespoon of olives, 1/2 tablespoon of pickles and 1/2 tablespoon of parsley having all the ingredients chopped
2. When served hot the sauce must be mixed with 1 cup of milk sauce and kept on the fire until thoroughly hot
