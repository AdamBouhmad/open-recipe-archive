## Ingredients

- cold boiled cauliflower
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Cut cold boiled cauliflower in small pieces
2. Dip in egg and bread crumbs
3. Fry carefully in butter or lard
4. May be fried without egg
