## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of flour
- 1/2 cupful of butter
- yolk of 1 egg
- 3/4 cupful of water
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice
- a little sugar and salt

## Directions

1. Mix salt and sugar with flour
2. Add butter to the flour same as directed in last recipe
3. Mix egg yolk with water
4. Work with fingers into the flour
5. Put in a cold place for a little while
