## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Salt
- Water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- Carrot liquor or milk
- Cream
- Chopped parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the carrots, rinse, cut into cubes or strips.
2. Cook until tender in a small quantity of salted water.
3. Make a sauce of 2 tablespoons butter, 2 tablespoons flour, liquor of carrots or milk.
4. Add some cream to the sauce.
5. Stir the carrots in.
6. Boil quickly.
7. May be sprinkled with chopped parsley if desired.
