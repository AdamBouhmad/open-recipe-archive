## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- ⅓ cupful of farina
- Salt and pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- Egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook a porridge of milk, butter and farina.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. When cool mix with 2 yolks and whites of eggs beaten firm.
4. Form into tiny balls or roll flat and cut in cubes.
5. Roll in flour and cook in salt water.
