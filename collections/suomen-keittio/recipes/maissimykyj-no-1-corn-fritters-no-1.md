## Ingredients

- 1 cup grated fresh corn or canned corn rubbed through a sieve
- 2 tablespoons flour or 1 1/2 cup cracker crumbs diluted with milk
- 2 eggs, whites and yolks beaten separately
- salt
- pepper
- milk if needed

## Directions

1. Mix 1 cup of grated fresh corn or canned corn rubbed through a sieve with 2 tablespoons flour or 1 1/2 cup cracker crumbs diluted with milk, 2 eggs, whites and the yolks beaten separately, salt and pepper.
2. If too thick dilute with milk.
3. The whites of eggs added last of all.
4. Brown in a pan dropping the batter by the spoonful into the pan or cook in deep lard.
