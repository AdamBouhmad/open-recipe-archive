## Ingredients

- 1 lb of salmon
- whites of 2 eggs
- 2 cupfuls of rich cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Skin and bone the fish.
2. Grind several times.
3. Rub well in a wooden bowl.
4. Add the whites of eggs one by one to it.
5. Force through a sieve.
6. Put the mixture in a kettle or bowl, place it in ice water.
7. Mix with cream whipping well, pouring the cream very carefully to avoid curdling.
8. Season with salt and pepper.
9. Decorate an individual mold with truffles or chopped parsley, turn the mixture carefully into it.
10. Cover with buttered paper.
11. Bake in a pan of water for about 15 minutes or until firm to the touch when pressed with a finger.
12. Turn onto a serving dish.
13. Serve with crab or hollandaise sauce.
