## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- 2 cups crushed strawberries

## Directions

1. Cook sugar in water for 5-8 minutes
2. Let cool
3. Add 2 cups crushed strawberries
4. Serve with vanilla ice cream
