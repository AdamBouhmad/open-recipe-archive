## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup milk
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Sift together flour, salt and baking powder
2. Beat the eggs with sugar, add milk and melted butter
3. Pour gradually the flour in, stirring well
4. Bake in buttered muffin tins
