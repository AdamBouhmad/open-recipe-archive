## Ingredients

- 4 pounds small fish
- 3 quarts water
- 1 carrot
- 1 stalk celery
- 2 sprigs parsley
- 1 leek
- 5 peppercorns
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Clean the fish leaving the scales on.
2. Place on the fire in cold salted water, having the fish cut in pieces.
3. When coming to boil add the vegetables, spices and a small lump of butter.
4. Cook covered for an hour.
5. Strain through a fine cloth.
