## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3/4 lb. of sugar
- 2 ozs. of flour
- 2 ozs. of corn starch
- 2 1/2 cupfuls of thin cream
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of pineapple

## Directions

1. Cook cream and flour to a paste.
2. Add butter and whipped yolks gradually.
3. Then stir pineapple, cut in dice, into it.
4. Lastly whites, beaten stiff.
5. Pour in a buttered mold and bake in a moderate oven 1 hour.
6. Prepare a sauce of pineapple liquor, a little white wine, corn starch and sugar.
