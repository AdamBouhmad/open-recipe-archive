## Ingredients

- 1 cup fresh grated or canned corn
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 2 or 3 egg yolks, beaten
- salt
- pepper
- some sugar
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- egg whites, beaten firm
- milk or cream (if using fresh corn)

## Directions

1. Have 1 cup of fresh grated or canned corn, add 1/2 cup bread crumbs, 2 or 3 yolks of egg beaten, salt, pepper, some sugar and 1 tablespoon melted butter.
2. At the last add the whites of egg beaten firm.
3. When using fresh corn dilute with milk or cream or if necessary thicken with bread crumbs.
4. Bake in a pan of water covered with buttered paper until firm.
5. Serve unmolded.
