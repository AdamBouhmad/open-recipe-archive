## Ingredients

- veal liver
- salt
- pepper
- water
- cream

## Directions

1. Cut veal liver in slices.
2. Rinse, dry and fry on both sides until brown.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and pour enough water into the pan so it does not burn.
4. Let simmer gently for a while.
5. Pour cream on top and let boil.
