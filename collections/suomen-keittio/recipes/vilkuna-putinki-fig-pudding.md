## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of figs
- 1/4 lb. of chopped suet
- 3 ozs. of flour
- 1/4 lb. of bread crumbs
- 2 ozs. of sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 cupful of milk
- 1 teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Soak figs in lukewarm water, chop and mix with suet.
2. Add flour, bread crumbs, soaked in milk, eggs beaten with sugar.
3. Rub the mixture well.
4. Put in a buttered mold and cook in a pan of water about 3 hours.
