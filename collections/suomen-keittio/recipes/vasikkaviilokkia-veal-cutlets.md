## Ingredients

- veal cutlets
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- flour
- butter or lard
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the cutlets into nice pieces or leave whole.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Dip in egg and roll in bread crumbs or flour only.
4. Fry in butter or lard until brown on both sides.
5. When done, place on a hot platter and sprinkle with lemon juice.
6. Serve with brown, tomato or Bearnaise sauce.
