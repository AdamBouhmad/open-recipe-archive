## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of dried peas
- 1 cupful of bread crumbs
- 1 cupful of milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 onion
- 2 eggs
- salt, pepper and chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cook peas until tender and drain
