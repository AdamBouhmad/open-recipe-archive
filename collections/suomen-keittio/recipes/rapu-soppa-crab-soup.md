## Ingredients

- 6 hard-shelled crabs
- 3½ cupfuls of fish or veal stock
- 1 small leek
- 2 sprigs of parsley
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 cupful of cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Remove meat from cooked crabs and chop it finely.
2. Mix with fish stock, leek and parsley and let simmer, covered, for half an hour.
3. Rub through a sieve.
4. Thicken with butter and flour.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Lastly add the cream.
7. Serve with crab balls or hard boiled egg yolks.
