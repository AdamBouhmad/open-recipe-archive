## Ingredients

- veal chops
- salt and pepper
- egg
- melted butter
- cream
- bread crumbs
- lard
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Wipe the chops, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
2. Dip in a mixture made of 1 egg, 1 tablespoon of melted butter and 1 tablespoon of cream.
3. Roll in bread crumbs.
4. Fry in deep lard on both sides.
5. Cover, place in a hot oven for a few minutes.
6. Serve with tomato sauce or add some cream to the drippings and strain over the chops.
7. Or the chops may be sprinkled with salt and pepper, dipped in flour and fried in butter or lard until brown on both sides.
