## Ingredients

- 5 cupfuls of milk
- 1/2 lb. of grated bread
- 1/2 cupful of currants
- 1 lemon
- 6 ozs. of butter
- 6 eggs
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Cook butter with milk until boiling
2. Mix with grated bread
3. When cool mix with rinsed currants, lemon rind and juice and yolks of eggs beaten with sugar
4. At the last add beaten whites into the mixture
5. Turn on a buttered mold and bake in a pan of water
6. Serve with fruit sauce
