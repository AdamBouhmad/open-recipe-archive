## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 1 tablespoonful of rice
- salt
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Pour 2 cupfuls of milk in a mold, mix with 1 tablespoonful of rice and a little salt.
2. Bake in oven 2 hours, stirring often.
3. Add 1 tablespoonful of sugar and 1/2 teaspoonful of vanilla when pudding has been in oven one hour.
4. Add boiling milk if pudding appears stiff.
