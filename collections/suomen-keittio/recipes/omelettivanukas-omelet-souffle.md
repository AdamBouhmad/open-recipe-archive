## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon vanilla
- butter
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Beat separately whites and yolks of 4 eggs.
2. Mix the yolks with 3 tablespoons of sugar, 1 tablespoon of vanilla, beating well.
3. Add carefully the whites beaten stiff.
4. Turn into a buttered mold and sprinkle with sugar.
5. Bake in a hot oven until light brown.
