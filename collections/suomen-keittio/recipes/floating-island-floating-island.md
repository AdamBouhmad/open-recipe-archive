## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 3 or 4 eggs
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla or chopped almonds
- Salt

## Directions

1. Separate egg yolks from whites
2. Beat the whites until firm
3. Add gradually 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar to the whites
4. Drop the meringue by spoonfuls into boiling milk
5. Cook for 2 minutes until firm
6. Remove from milk with a perforated spoon and place on a plate
7. Beat the egg yolks and add milk gradually
8. Beat the mixture in a pan of water until firm
9. Add vanilla or chopped almonds and salt
10. Let cool
11. Place the cooked egg whites on top
12. Garnish with bits of jelly or berries
13. Optional: while custard is still warm, add 1 1/2 cups cream and whip until cold and firm before serving
