## Ingredients

- 1 duck
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 cups meat stock

## Directions

1. Clean the duck.
2. Rub inside and out with salt.
3. Melt the butter in a roasting pan.
4. Place the duck in the pan, brown well on every side.
5. Sprinkle with salt, add hot meat stock to the drippings.
6. Baste often.
7. Roast slowly from 1½ - 2 hours.
8. Strain the drippings, skim and prepare the gravy as directed.
