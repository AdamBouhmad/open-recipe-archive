## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of butter
- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- 4 tablespoonfuls of coffee
- 4 tablespoonfuls of cocoa
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream butter with sugar
2. Add cocoa, coffee and vanilla
3. Rub to a smooth paste
