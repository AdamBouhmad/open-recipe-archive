## Ingredients

- summer squash
- salted water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- milk sauce
- cream

## Directions

1. Peel and remove the seeds
2. Cut in cubes
3. Put on fire in boiling salted water
4. Cook until tender
5. Drain carefully
6. Add butter, salt and pepper, or prepare a thin milk sauce and pour over the squash
7. May be served mashed, seasoned with salt and pepper, diluted with butter and cream
