## Ingredients

- Fresh boiled lobsters or canned lobsters
- Fish stock
- Gelatine

## Directions

1. Make an aspic of fish stock, same as directed for salmon.
2. Lay the lobster cut in pieces in a mold, having the nicest pieces on the bottom and on the sides.
3. Fill the mold with the aspic.
4. Let set.
