## Ingredients

- veal
- butter or fat
- salt
- flour
- carrots
- celery
- peppercorns
- boiling water
- cream

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat.
2. Melt butter or fat in a dripping-pan and place the meat into it skinside down.
3. Put in a hot oven.
4. When lightly browned, sprinkle with salt and flour and baste with drippings.
5. Add carrots, celery and a few peppercorns.
6. When delicately browned, turn over.
7. Sprinkle with salt and flour and pour boiling water over.
8. Bake about 2 hours.
9. Prepare the sauce of drippings by adding a little cream.
