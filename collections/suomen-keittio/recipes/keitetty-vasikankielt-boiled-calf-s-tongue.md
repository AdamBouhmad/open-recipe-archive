## Ingredients

- calf tongues
- salt
- peppercorns
- vegetables
- butter
- flour
- broth
- horseradish
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Wash the tongues and scrape.
2. Place on the fire in boiling water with salt, peppercorns and vegetables.
3. Cook on a slow fire until tender, about 1 hour.
4. Remove the skin.
5. If the tongues are small, split them lengthwise in the middle.
6. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and liquor of the tongue.
7. Add grated horseradish or tomato.
