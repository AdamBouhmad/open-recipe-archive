## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 8 whites of eggs
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of butter
- 2 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 3 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- vanilla or almond extract to taste

## Directions

1. Work butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add milk, spices, baking powder mixed with flour and whites of eggs beaten until stiff and dry
