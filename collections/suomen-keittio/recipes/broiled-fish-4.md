## Ingredients

- fish
- melted butter
- salt
- pepper
- watercresses or lemon

## Directions

1. Split the fish to be broiled down the back, wash and wipe dry.
2. Spread with melted butter.
3. Make the broiler hot and butter it.
4. The fire must be clear and hot for small fish, more moderate for larger fish, because the outside should not be too scorched before the inside is done.
5. The fish should be first done on flesh side.
6. When there is danger of burning, the broiler may be laid on a pan in oven to finish the cooking.
7. Turn the fish onto a hot dish, spread with butter, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
8. Garnish with watercresses or lemon.
