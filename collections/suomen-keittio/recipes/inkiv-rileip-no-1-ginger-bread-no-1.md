## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup molasses (or sugar syrup)
- 1 cup sour milk
- 1 egg
- 1 large tablespoon butter
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- Some cinnamon and cloves
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 3 cups flour

## Directions

1. Warm molasses, rub to a froth with sugar and butter.
2. Add soda dissolved in sour milk, egg beaten until frothy, spices and flour.
3. Bake in a shallow tin in a moderately hot oven.
