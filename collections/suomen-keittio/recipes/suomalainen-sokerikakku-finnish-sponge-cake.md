## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 8 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 8 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar to a thick froth.
2. Add some vanilla or lemon juice.
3. Stir in gradually the sifted flour.
4. Last of all fold in the egg whites beaten until firm.
5. Bake in a buttered tin in a moderate oven for 3/4 hour.
