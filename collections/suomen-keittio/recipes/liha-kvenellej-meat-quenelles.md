## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of chicken or veal
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of milk or meat stock
- 1/2 onion
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Grind the meat very fine
2. Cook the chopped onion in butter
3. Add to the meat the egg, bread crumbs soaked in meat stock, salt, pepper, and the cooked onion
4. Knead thoroughly to combine
5. Form into small balls
6. Dip in flour
7. Cook in salted water
