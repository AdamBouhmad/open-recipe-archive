## Ingredients

- orange peels
- sugar equal in weight to orange peels
- 1 cupful water to 1 pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Soak peels 6-7 days in cold water changing the water every day
2. Cook until tender, place to drain
3. Have equal weights of orange peels and sugar, and allow 1 cupful of water to 1 pound of sugar
4. Cook to a thick syrup
5. Mix with orange peels and continue cooking until the peels are clear and the liquid is cooked away
6. Use for puddings and to garnish cakes
