## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 cupful of flour
- 1 pint of oysters
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs
2. Add milk, flour and baking powder, salt and chopped oysters
3. Drop by the spoonful into boiling lard
4. Cook until brown on both sides
5. Serve hot garnished with slices of lemon
