## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of nuts
- 1 lb. of potatoes
- 3/4 lb. of bread crumbs
- 2 ozs. of corn starch
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of milk
- 3 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes and mash
2. Grind shelled nuts
3. Combine both mixtures
4. Add bread crumbs soaked in milk
5. Add whipped eggs, corn starch and seasonings
6. Put in a buttered mold
7. Bake in a pan of water
8. Alternatively, form into croquettes and fry in butter
9. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and vegetable stock
10. Mix sauce with cream
