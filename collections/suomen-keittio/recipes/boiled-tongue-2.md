## Ingredients

- Tongue
- Salt
- Water
- Tomato, horseradish, or Tartar sauce

## Directions

1. Wash tongue and scrape carefully.
2. Place on the fire in hot salted water.
3. Cook slowly until tender.
4. Skin immediately and cut in slices.
5. Pour tomato, horseradish or Tartar sauce upon the tongue.
6. Serve.
