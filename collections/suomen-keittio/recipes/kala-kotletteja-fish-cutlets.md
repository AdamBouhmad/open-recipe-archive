## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of cold boiled or fried fish (salmon, haddock, halibut, etc.)
- 1 cupful of mashed potatoes
- yolks of 2 eggs
- a little cream
- salt and pepper
- breadcrumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. Mix cold boiled or fried fish with mashed potatoes.
2. Mix with yolks of 2 eggs and a little cream, season with salt and pepper.
3. Form into cakes or balls.
4. Dip in breadcrumbs, egg and again in breadcrumbs.
5. Cook in boiling lard.
6. Serve with Bearnaise, Hollandaise or Tartar sauce.
