## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Pare potatoes, form into balls of equal size
2. Wash thoroughly
3. Put over the fire in boiling water mixed with a dash of salt
4. Let simmer slowly until tender
5. Drain off the water and let stand on the side of the range, uncovered to evaporate all the water
6. Put old potatoes to cook in cold water
