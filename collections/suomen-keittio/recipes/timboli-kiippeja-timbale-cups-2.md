## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon melted butter or olive oil
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Mix flour with salt and sugar.
2. Beat egg and dilute with milk.
3. Add to flour and beat well.
4. Stir in melted butter or olive oil last.
5. Heat a timbale iron in lard.
6. Dip iron into batter, leaving upper edge out.
7. Stick into boiling lard and cook until brown and it loosens easily.
8. If cups are tough, add some milk as batter may be too thick.
9. Fill the cups with creamed fish or meat of various kinds.
