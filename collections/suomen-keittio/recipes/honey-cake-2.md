## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 cupful of honey
- 6 ozs. of sugar
- 8 ozs. of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoonful of ginger
- 1/2 teaspoonful of baking soda

## Directions

1. Beat yolks with sugar
2. Mix with warm honey beaten to a froth
3. Continue rubbing for a while
4. Stir into it spices and flour mixed with baking soda
5. Add last whites of eggs beaten to a froth
6. Bake in a quick oven 1 to 1 1/2 hours
