## Ingredients

- 1½ cups mashed chestnuts
- 1 cup rich milk
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar
2. Add milk, flour and chestnuts
3. Bake for 30 or 40 minutes
