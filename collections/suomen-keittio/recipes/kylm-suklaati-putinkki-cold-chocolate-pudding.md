## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 ozs. of chocolate
- 4 tablesp. of sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- 7 leaves or 5 tablesp. of gelatine
- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 2 cupfuls of cream

## Directions

1. Heat milk and chocolate together
2. Whip egg yolks with sugar
3. Pour the hot milk gradually into the egg yolks and sugar
4. Whip on the fire until it reaches a sabayon consistency
5. Add gelatine to the mixture and let cool
6. When the mixture begins to thicken (before it sets into lumps), add whipped cream
7. Put in a mold to set firm
8. Serve turned out of the mold with whipped cream
