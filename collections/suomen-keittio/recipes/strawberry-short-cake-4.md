## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 3 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/4 cupful of sugar
- 3/8 cupful of milk
- 1/2 cupful of butter
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl
2. Work butter in with fingers
3. then add milk into the mixture
4. Put the paste in two molds bake in oven
5. Clean strawberries and sweeten with sugar
6. Put a layer of strawberries between and on the cakes
7. Cover with whipped cream
