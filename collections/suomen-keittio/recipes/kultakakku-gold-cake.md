## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup milk
- 5 or 7 egg yolks
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 lemon juice and rind

## Directions

1. Rub the butter with the sugar until creamy
2. Add the well beaten egg yolks
3. Add the juice and the grated yellow rind of a lemon
4. Add milk, baking powder and flour sifted together
5. Rub the mixture well
