## Ingredients

- veal chops
- salt
- pepper
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoonful of melted butter
- 1 tablespoonful of cream
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Wipe chops, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
2. Dip in a mixture made of 1 egg, 1 tablespoonful of melted butter and 1 tablespoonful of cream.
3. Roll in bread crumbs.
4. Fry in deep fat on both sides.
5. Cover, bake in oven for a few minutes.
6. Serve with tomato sauce, or mix the drippings with a little cream, strain over the chops.
