## Ingredients

- 1 quart cream
- 3/4 cup mashed banana
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place one-half of the cream on the fire.
2. Beat the yolks with the sugar.
3. Add gradually the scalded cream.
4. Stir in a double boiler until thick.
5. Let cool.
6. Add the lemon juice, banana and the rest of the cream.
7. Freeze.
8. May be made without eggs, if desired.
