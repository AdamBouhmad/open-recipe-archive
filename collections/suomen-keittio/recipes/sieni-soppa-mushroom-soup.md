## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. or 1 small jar of mushrooms
- 2 quarts of veal stock
- 1 small onion
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls of tomatoes
- 1 cupful of cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the onion in butter for 5 minutes; add the flour, the stock, the mushrooms cleaned and chopped, and the tomatoes.
2. Cook for 10 minutes and force through a sieve.
3. Season with salt and pepper and at last add the cream.
4. Finely chopped split peas, cooked first in water until soft, may be used in the soup.
