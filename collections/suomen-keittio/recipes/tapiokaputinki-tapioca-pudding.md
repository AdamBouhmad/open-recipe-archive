## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup tapioca
- 3 cups milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 or 3 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Cook a porridge of milk and tapioca
2. Rub the butter until creamy
3. Beat the egg yolks with sugar and add to the butter
4. Combine with the porridge when it is cold
5. Add the egg whites beaten firm
6. Pour the mixture into a buttered mold
7. Bake in a pan of water for 3/4 hour
8. Optional additions: juice of 1 lemon, 1/2 cup raisins, 1/4 cup chopped almonds, or vanilla to taste
9. Serve with cream or fruit sauce
