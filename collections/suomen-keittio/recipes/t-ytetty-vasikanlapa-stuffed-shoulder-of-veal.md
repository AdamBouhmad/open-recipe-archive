## Ingredients

- shoulder of veal
- suet
- egg
- bread crumbs
- cream or milk
- salt and pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the bones and the blade carefully.
2. Scrape the meat from the blade and grind finely.
3. Mix with chopped suet, egg, bread crumbs, cream, salt, parsley and pepper.
4. Rub the space with salt, stuff and sew up.
5. Roast as directed for veal.
