## Ingredients

- middle-sized cabbage
- salted water
- mushroom mixture
- towel

## Directions

1. Cook cabbage in salted water until tender
2. Lift out to cool
3. Prepare mushroom mixture same as for croquettes
4. Place cabbage on a towel wrung from water and carefully open the leaves
5. Fill the spaces between the leaves with mushroom mixture
6. Close the cabbage again and tie it up in the wet towel
7. Place in boiling cabbage broth and cook for 1 hour
8. Serve with milk sauce or a sauce made from the cabbage broth
9. May also be prepared like stuffed cabbage with meat and baked in oven
