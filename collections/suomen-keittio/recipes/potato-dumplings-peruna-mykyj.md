## Ingredients

- 1 pint of mashed potatoes
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 whole egg and 1 yolk
- 3/4 cupful of flour
- nutmeg and pepper
- bread crumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. Mix mashed potatoes with butter, salt, beaten egg and flour.
2. Form into croquettes and roll in flour.
3. Cook in salted water 15 minutes.
4. Serve as they are or dip the boiled rolls in egg, dredge with bread crumbs and fry in lard.
