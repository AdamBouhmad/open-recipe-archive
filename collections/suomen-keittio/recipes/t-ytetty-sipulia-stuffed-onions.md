## Ingredients

- large red onions
- beef
- veal
- melted lard or butter
- bread crumbs
- milk
- egg
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- chopped onion
- mushrooms (alternative)
- cream (alternative)
- parsley (alternative)
- tomato (alternative)
- boiled rice (alternative)

## Directions

1. Cook large red onions for 15 minutes in salted water
2. Scoop carefully
3. Make a stuffing of 1/4 pound beef and 1/4 pound veal ground, 2 tablespoons melted lard or butter, 1 tablespoon bread crumbs, 1/4 cup milk, 1 egg, salt, pepper, some onion juice and chopped onion
4. Prepare the stuffing as directed
5. Spread the onions with melted butter and sprinkle with bread crumbs
6. Bake in oven with plenty of butter and some water in the pan
7. Baste often so as not to burn the onions
8. Serve as an entree with mushroom or tomato sauce
9. Alternative stuffing: Make of 1/2 pound chopped mushrooms, 4 tablespoons bread crumbs soaked in 1/2 cup of cream, 1 egg and the insides of the scooped onions chopped and fried
10. Simple stuffing: Fry the insides of the onions in butter with bread crumbs, add chopped parsley, 2 tablespoons tomato, salt and pepper. Boiled rice plain may be used instead of bread crumbs
