## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- 1/2 teaspoon soda
- 1 1/2 cups flour

## Directions

1. Rub butter, sugar and molasses to a froth
2. Add spices and eggs beaten until frothy
3. Mix in flour sifted with soda
4. Roll thin on a floured baking board
5. Cut in small cakes with a cutter
6. Bake in a hot oven
