## Ingredients

- Saddle of mutton
- Buttered paper
- Mashed potatoes
- Peas
- Stewed mushrooms
- Chestnut puree

## Directions

1. Split the saddle of mutton in the middle.
2. Remove the bones, scraping carefully.
3. Form into rolls leaving the bones outside and taking care that they are of equal length.
4. Sew up.
5. Wrap into buttered paper.
6. Roast for 1 hour or until done.
7. Place on a serving platter having the meat in a ring with mashed potatoes, peas, stewed mushrooms and chestnut puree inside the ring.
