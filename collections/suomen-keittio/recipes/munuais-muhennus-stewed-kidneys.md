## Ingredients

- kidneys
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- tablespoonful of butter
- chopped onion
- 1 cupful of meat stock or water
- sherry or Worcestershire sauce
- chopped mushrooms

## Directions

1. Prepare kidneys as for frying.
2. Cut in half an inch thick pieces.
3. Dip in flour and fry with chopped onion in a tablespoonful of butter until brown.
4. Add 1 cupful of meat stock or water and let boil 10 minutes.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Flavor with sherry or Worcestershire sauce and chopped mushrooms when sending it to the table.
