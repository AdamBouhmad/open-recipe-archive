## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of bread
- 1/2 lb. of shelled nuts
- 3/4 lb. of butter
- 4 eggs
- 3/4 cupful of milk
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- a dash of pepper

## Directions

1. Grind nuts
2. Soak bread in milk
3. Mix bread with nuts
4. Add melted butter creamed with egg yolks
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites
7. Pour into buttered mold
8. Bake about 1 hour
