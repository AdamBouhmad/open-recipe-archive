## Ingredients

- slices of bread
- anchovy paste
- butter
- sardine
- 5 tablespoons milk
- 5 tablespoons gelatine
- 4 tablespoons mayonnaise
- salt
- 2 or 3 tablespoons whipped cream
- red coloring

## Directions

1. Spread slices of bread with anchovy mixed with butter
2. Place a sardine on top
3. Stir 5 tablespoons of gelatine into 5 tablespoons milk
4. Let cool until thick
5. Add slowly 4 tablespoons of mayonnaise
6. Season with salt
7. Mix with 2 or 3 tablespoons whipped cream
8. Spread a layer of this mixture on the bread
9. Add a little more gelatine and red coloring to the mixture left over
10. Use for decorating the sandwiches
