## Ingredients

- 1/4 cupful of butter
- 3/4 cupful of sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar to a cream, add well beaten eggs, milk, vanilla, baking powder mixed with flour into it.
2. Pour the mixture in a buttered mold and bake in a moderate oven.
