## Ingredients

- Salmon
- Mayonnaise
- Lettuce leaves
- Boiled vegetables: potatoes, artichokes, beets, carrots, cauliflower, string beans, green peas, tomatoes and mushrooms
- Lemon halves or hard-boiled egg white halves
- Hard-boiled egg yolks, chopped
- French dressing

## Directions

1. Cook the salmon as directed.
2. Skin while it is hot and place on a platter to cool down.
3. Cover with rich mayonnaise.
4. Garnish the platter with lettuce leaves.
5. Arrange neatly on the leaves boiled vegetables cut in cubes.
6. Fill cups made of halves of lemon or halves of whites of hard-boiled eggs with mayonnaise or salmon jelly and place around the fish.
7. Sprinkle the fish with hard boiled yolks of egg chopped.
8. Moisten the lettuce leaves and vegetables with French dressing.
