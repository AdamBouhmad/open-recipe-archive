## Ingredients

- 3 fresh herrings
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1/2 chopped onion
- 1/2 cupful of chopped mushrooms
- 1/2 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- a little lemon juice
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook 1 tablespoonful of butter with 1/2 chopped onion.
2. Add to it 1/2 cupful of chopped mushrooms, 1/2 tablespoonful of chopped parsley and a little lemon juice.
3. Let cook on the stove 10 min.
4. Mix with 2 tablespoonfuls of cream, season with salt and pepper.
5. Stuff the herrings with this mixture.
6. Wrap in a buttered paper, bake in oven.
7. Serve with tomato sauce.
