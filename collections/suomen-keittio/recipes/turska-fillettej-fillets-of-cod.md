## Ingredients

- cod
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- onion juice
- butter
- bread crumbs
- egg
- tartare sauce or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the middle part of fish into pieces two inches thick and cut these pieces in half
2. Remove skin and bones with a sharp knife
3. Rub the fish pieces with salt, pepper, lemon juice and onion juice
4. Let stand for about half an hour
5. Wipe dry
6. Coat in melted butter, then bread crumbs, then beaten egg, then bread crumbs again
7. Fry in plenty of butter or fat
8. Serve with tartare or tomato sauce
9. For a simpler preparation: coat fish pieces in flour mixed with salt and pepper, and fry
