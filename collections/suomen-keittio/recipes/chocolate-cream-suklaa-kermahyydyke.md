## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of rich cream
- 2 ounces of chocolate
- 2 tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
- 6 leaves or 4 tablespoonfuls of gelatine
- 1/2 cupful of water
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Melt chocolate in a saucepan
2. Add sugar, water, gelatine and vanilla into it
3. Set the mixture to cool when gelatine is all dissolved
4. Beat cream to a froth
5. Pass the cold chocolate mixture through a sieve into it
6. Put in a wet mold to stiffen
