## Ingredients

- salmon steaks cut nearly one inch thick
- salad oil
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- chopped parsley
- Bearnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Have the steaks cut nearly one inch thick.
2. Wipe and lay in salad oil for an hour.
3. Drain and put upon a gridiron over a slow fire basting with butter now and then.
4. They will take at least twenty minutes to cook and must be carefully watched that they do not scorch.
5. Turn on serving-dish and put on each steak a generous lump of butter.
6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and a little lemon juice.
7. Sprinkle with chopped parsley.
8. Serve with Bearnaise sauce.
