## Ingredients

- 2 lobsters
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 tablespoon grated horseradish
- 4 tablespoons lemon juice
- 3 tablespoons tomato catsup
- A few drops of tobasco
- Salt
- Paprika

## Directions

1. Prepare the dressing as for oyster cocktail: mix vinegar, grated horseradish, lemon juice, tomato catsup, a few drops of tobasco, salt and paprika.
2. Boil two lobsters.
3. Cut in fine pieces.
4. Serve in glasses same as oysters.
