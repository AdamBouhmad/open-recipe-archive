## Ingredients

- 1/2 cupful of butter
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of sugar
- 3 beaten yolks
- 1/2 cupful of milk
- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 1/4 teaspoonful of soda
- 3 hard beaten whites

## Directions

1. Work 1/2 cupful of butter, mixed with 1 1/2 cupfuls of sugar, to a cream
2. add 3 beaten yolks, 1/2 cupful of milk, 2 cupfuls of flour, 1/2 teaspoonful of cream of tartar, 1/4 teaspoonful of soda, and lastly 3 hard beaten whites
3. Butter 3 low pans, pour the batter into them and bake in oven
4. When cold, cover cakes with whipped cream, mixed with a little sugar and vanilla
5. place strawberries, split in two, on and sprinkle with sugar
6. Pile the layers on a dish and serve immediately
