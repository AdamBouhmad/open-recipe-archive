## Ingredients

- boiled carrots
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Peel the boiled carrots and cut lengthwise in strips
2. Brown in butter or lard on both sides
3. Alternatively, peel raw carrots and cut in cubes
4. Boil in salted water until half done
5. Drain carefully and brown with butter in a pan
6. If desired, season with sugar and lemon juice
