## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound prunes
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook the prunes until tender, stone and grind or force through a sieve.
2. Add the lemon, sugar and the egg whites beaten until firm.
3. Bake in a well-buttered mold for 20-30 minutes, having the mold in a pan of water.
4. Serve immediately with whipped cream.
