## Ingredients

- herrings
- sliced onion
- whole peppercorns
- cloves
- bay leaves
- sugar
- vinegar
- water
- chives

## Directions

1. Soak the herring over night
2. Remove the skin and bones
3. Lay into an earthen jar having sliced onions, peppercorns, cloves, bay leaves and some sugar between the layers
4. Pour strong vinegar over (the vinegar may be diluted with an equal amount of water)
5. Let stand in a cold place for 24 hours
6. Serve in pieces with a little liquid, garnished with chopped chives
