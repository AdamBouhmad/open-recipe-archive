## Ingredients

- 3 cupfuls of well seasoned chicken or beef stock
- 4 tablespoonfuls of tomato
- ½ onion
- juice of 2 lemons
- ½ box or 12 leaves of gelatine
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Put all the ingredients into a saucepan, add two whites (beaten until half stiff) and the shells.
2. Set on the fire and whip until it begins to boil.
3. Let simmer 5 minutes.
4. Strain through a tight cloth.
5. If not thoroughly clear, strain once more, without pressing the cloth.
6. If a darker color is desired, add a few drops of kitchen bouquet.
