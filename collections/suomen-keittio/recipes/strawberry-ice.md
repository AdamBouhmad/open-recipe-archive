## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of water
- 2 1/2 cupfuls of sugar
- 1 quart of strawberries
- 2 tablespoonfuls of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook water and sugar
2. Press strawberries through a cloth
3. Mix with syrup, flavor with lemon juice and freeze
