## Ingredients

- 1 yolk of a hard cooked egg
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard
- a dash of sugar
- 1 raw egg yolk
- 1 1/2 cups salad oil
- 4 tablespoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Rub well the yolk of a hard cooked egg
2. Add 1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon mustard and a dash of sugar
3. Mix with 1 raw egg yolk and stir well
4. Pour gradually into the mixture 1 1/2 cups of salad oil, drop by drop at first, stir thoroughly
5. Add 4 tablespoons of lemon juice
6. Mix with whipped cream when served
