## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 6 ozs. of sugar
- ½ cupful of butter
- ½ cupful of milk
- 1 cupful of walnut meats chopped with shell
- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar to a froth, add well-beaten egg yolks and continue with the remaining ingredients as directed.
