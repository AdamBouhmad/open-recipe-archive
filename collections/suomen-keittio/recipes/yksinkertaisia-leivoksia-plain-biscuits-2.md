## Ingredients

- 1½ cupfuls of milk
- 4 cupfuls of flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter or lard
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 3 teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Sift flour mixed with salt and baking powder
2. Mix butter with flour carefully
3. Add milk slowly with knife or hand
4. Roll the dough into a sheet ½ inch thick
5. Shape into round cakes
6. Place on a buttered baking pan
7. Bake in a hot oven for 15 minutes
