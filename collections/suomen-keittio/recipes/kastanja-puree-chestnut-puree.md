## Ingredients

- Chestnuts
- Salt
- Cream or milk
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Shell the chestnuts
2. Cook in salted water and drain
3. Mash and dilute with cream or milk
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Stir until smooth
