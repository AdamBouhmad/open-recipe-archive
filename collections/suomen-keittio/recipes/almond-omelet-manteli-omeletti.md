## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- sugar
- vanilla
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat separately whites and yolks of 2 eggs.
2. Mix the yolks with 2 tablespoonfuls of butter, 2 tablespoonfuls of milk, 2 tablespoonfuls of flour, a little sugar and vanilla.
3. Stir thoroughly.
4. At the last add the beaten whites into it.
5. Fry on a slow fire.
6. Sprinkle one half of it with almonds, then fold.
7. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and chopped almonds, browned in butter.
8. Serve cold with fruit sauce or caramel sauce.
