## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 or 2 squares of chocolate
- vanilla
- cream
- arrowroot

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 cup sugar in 1/2 cup water for 5 minutes
2. Add 1 or 2 squares of chocolate dissolved and some vanilla
3. Add some cream
4. Serve very hot
5. If too thin, thicken the sauce with arrowroot
