## Ingredients

- 1 pound of beef
- 1/2 pound of pork
- 1/2 pound of veal
- 1 cupful of cream
- 2 cupfuls of meat stock or water
- 1 cupful of breadcrumbs
- 2 eggs
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of pepper
- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Grind the meat twice.
2. Soak the breadcrumbs in meat stock and cream.
3. Add beaten eggs to the mixture and combine with the meat, which is beaten until light.
4. Finely chop the onion, cook it in butter and strain the butter into the mixture.
5. Add the seasoning last.
6. Pour the mixture into a buttered pan and shape into a long loaf, smooth the surface well and brush with beaten egg.
7. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and bake in a moderate oven until brown.
