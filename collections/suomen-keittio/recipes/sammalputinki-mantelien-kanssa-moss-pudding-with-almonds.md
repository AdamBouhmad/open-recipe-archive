## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons almonds
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 package gelatine
- 1 pint whipping cream

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, grate and rub with the sugar to a paste.
2. Add the milk and the eggs, stir over the fire until thick.
3. Mix in the gelatine dissolved in water.
4. When cool add the whipped cream.
5. Dip a mold into cold water, pour the mixture in, garnish with macaroons and berries.
