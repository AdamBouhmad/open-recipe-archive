## Ingredients

- shells, fat and all small claws of the lobster
- 1/4 pound of butter
- 2 quarts of boiling water

## Directions

1. Pound shells, fat and all small claws of the lobster and mix with 1/4 pound of butter.
2. Cook it on a slow fire 15 minutes.
3. Pour 2 quarts of boiling water into it and let boil half an hour.
4. Set to cool.
5. Next day skim the fat.
6. Melt it again, pour in a small jar for future use.
