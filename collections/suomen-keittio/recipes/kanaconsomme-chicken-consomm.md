## Ingredients

- 1-2 chickens
- 4 quarts of cold water
- 1/2 root or 2 stalks of celery
- 1 leek or 1/2 onion
- 2 sprigs of parsley
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 6 peppercorns

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into pieces and place it into the soupkettle in cold water.
2. Let it slowly come to boiling.
3. Add vegetables and seasonings and let simmer slowly for 4 hours.
4. Strain to cool and clarify.
5. Serve in cups for lunch.
