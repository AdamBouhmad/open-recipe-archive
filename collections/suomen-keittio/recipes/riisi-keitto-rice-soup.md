## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of well-washed rice
- water
- 2 quarts of stock
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cupful of cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Boil a cupful of well-washed rice in water for ten minutes.
2. Strain and cook the rice in 2 quarts of stock until tender.
3. Rub through a strainer and return to the fire.
4. Beat the yolks of two eggs, add a cupful of cream.
5. Pour the soup slowly into it and stir on the fire for a while, not allowing it to boil.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
