## Ingredients

- 2 ox-tails
- 1/4 cup onion
- 1/4 cup carrot
- 1/4 cup celery
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 sprig parsley
- 5 peppercorns
- 2 cloves
- Some cayenne pepper
- 1 tablespoon flour
- Some wine and lemon
- 2 tablespoons butter or 1 tablespoon pork fat
- 5 quarts brown stock or 3 pounds lean beef

## Directions

1. Scrape the vegetables and cut in small cubes
2. Cut the ox-tails at the joints
3. Brown the butter in a soup kettle
4. Add the meat, the vegetables and the flour, continue frying until all is browned
5. Add the brown stock or water and the meat, let simmer covered for 3 hours
6. Remove the pieces of ox-tail when the meat is tender
7. Strain the stock
8. Add cayenne, white wine or Sherry
9. Serve with hard boiled egg yolks or tiny cubes of meat and a few slices of lemon, or with croutons flavored with lemon or plain
