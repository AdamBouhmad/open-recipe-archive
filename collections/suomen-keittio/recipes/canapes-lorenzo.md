## Ingredients

- butter
- 2 sliced onions
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups cream and milk mixture
- salt
- pepper
- ½ pound finely chopped lobster meat
- round toasted bread slices
- breadcrumbs
- cheese

## Directions

1. Fry 2 sliced onions in butter.
2. Add 2 tablespoons flour.
3. Add 2 cups cream and milk mixture to make a smooth sauce.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Mix in ½ pound finely chopped lobster meat.
6. Let cool.
7. Place on round toasted bread slices.
8. Spread on top a thin layer of mixture made of breadcrumbs, butter and cheese mixed in equal amounts.
9. Place in oven until browned.
10. Serve.
