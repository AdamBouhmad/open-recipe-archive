## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- mashed potatoes or string beans

## Directions

1. Trim off the fat
2. Scrape the bones
3. Broil on both sides
4. One inch thick chops have to be broiled 8-10 minutes
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
6. Dot with small bits of butter
7. Arrange the chops on a hot platter having mashed potatoes or string beans in centre of it
