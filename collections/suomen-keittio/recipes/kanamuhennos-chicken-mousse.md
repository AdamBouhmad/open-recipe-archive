## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- Onion
- Celery stalk
- Flour
- Butter or lard

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken in pieces, place on the fire in cold water.
2. Or brown the pieces in butter or lard, add boiling water to the drippings.
3. When coming to boiling add salt, pepper, 1 tablespoon of vinegar, onion and a stalk of celery.
4. Cook until tender.
5. Remove the onion and the celery from the drippings, thicken with flour.
