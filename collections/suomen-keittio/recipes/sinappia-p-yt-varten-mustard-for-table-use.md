## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons fine mustard
- 4 tablespoons vinegar
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- a pinch of salt
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Mix well the mustard and vinegar
2. Add the sugar, salt and olive oil
3. Mix until smooth
4. If too thick, dilute with some boiling water
