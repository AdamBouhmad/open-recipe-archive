## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 1/2 cup powdered sugar
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 5 eggs
- Juice of half a lemon
- 1/2 cup currants
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Rub the butter until creamy
2. Add the eggs one by one
3. After each egg work into the mixture 2 tablespoons flour and 2 tablespoons sugar
4. Last mix in the rest of the flour and sugar with the flour sifted with the baking powder
5. Stir in the lemon juice and the currants rolled in flour
6. Pour into a buttered tin
7. Bake for about 1 hour
