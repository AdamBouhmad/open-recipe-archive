## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of plums
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice
- whites of 5 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook plums until soft, stone and press through a sieve.
2. Mix with sugar and lastly with stiff beaten whites.
3. Bake in oven 20-30 minutes.
4. Serve hot with whipped cream.
