## Ingredients

- 1 cup walnuts
- 1 cup peas
- 1 cup boiled potatoes
- 4 tomatoes
- 1/4 pound butter
- 2 cups bread crumbs
- 4 eggs
- 1 onion
- 1 stalk celery
- salt
- cream

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes, peel the tomatoes, shell the nuts and clean the celery.
2. Force all the vegetables through a meat grinder.
3. Add the bread crumbs, beaten eggs and seasonings.
4. If the mixture appears too thin, add cornstarch; if too thick, dilute with cream.
5. Form into small cakes or bake in a mold.
6. May be formed into balls, croquettes, etc. and fried in butter or lard.
