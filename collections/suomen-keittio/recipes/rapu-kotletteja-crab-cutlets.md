## Ingredients

- 2 cups crab meat boiled and cut in pieces
- 1 cup rich milk sauce
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- Some onion juice and pepper
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- 1 raw yolk of egg

## Directions

1. Stir all the ingredients with the milk sauce, add 1 raw yolk of egg (may be made without egg)
2. Let stand for an hour in a cold place
3. Form into cutlets, roll in bread crumbs, dip in egg, roll in bread crumbs again
4. Cook in deep lard
5. Serve with tartare or Hollandaise sauce
