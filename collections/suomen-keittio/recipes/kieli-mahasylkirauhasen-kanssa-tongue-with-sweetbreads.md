## Ingredients

- Tongue, sliced thin
- Sweetbreads, fried and diced
- Egg white
- Butter
- Spinach
- Truffled sauce

## Directions

1. Butter individual molds
2. Line the edges with thin slices of tongue
3. Fill the molds with small pieces of fried sweetbreads
4. Fasten the tongue and sweetbread together with white of egg
5. Bake in oven in a water bath covered with buttered paper
6. Serve on a foundation of spinach with truffled sauce
