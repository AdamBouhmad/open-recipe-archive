## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- butter
- chopped onion
- chopped boiled chicken or other meat
- bread crumbs
- parsley
- pepper
- egg

## Directions

1. Wipe and cut a slice off top of tomatoes; leave skins on.
2. Scoop out the insides, sprinkle the cavities with salt.
3. Cook 1 tablespoon of butter with 1 tablespoon of chopped onion.
4. Add 1 cup chopped boiled chicken or any other kind of meat, 1/2 cup bread crumbs, the insides of tomatoes, 1 teaspoon chopped parsley, salt and pepper.
5. Boil quickly, add 1 egg.
6. Place into a buttered pan, sprinkle with bread crumbs.
7. Bake about 20 minutes.
8. Serve with brown sauce.
