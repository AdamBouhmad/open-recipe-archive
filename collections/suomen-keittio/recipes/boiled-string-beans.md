## Ingredients

- string beans
- boiling water
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- parsley
- sour apples
- butter
- flour
- sugar
- vinegar or lemon juice
- milk sauce

## Directions

1. Remove all the strings, cut the beans into pieces.
2. Put on the fire in boiling water mixed with a little salt.
3. Let boil until soft.
4. Drain, season with salt, pepper and melted butter.
5. A tiny bunch of parsley added to the beans while boiling will give a good flavor.
6. String beans may be boiled with two sour apples cut in pieces.
7. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and of liquor of string beans.
8. Pour over the beans.
9. Season with salt, sugar and vinegar or lemon juice.
10. String beans may also be served with milk sauce.
