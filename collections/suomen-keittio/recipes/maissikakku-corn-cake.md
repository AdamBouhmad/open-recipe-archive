## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups cornmeal
- 1 1/2 cups white flour
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 4 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Sift together flour, salt and baking powder
2. Beat eggs with sugar, add milk and melted butter
3. Pour in the flour stirring well
4. Bake in a shallow tin in a hot oven
