## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of boiled spinach, rubbed through a sieve
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of sugar
- whites of 2 hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Add melted butter, salt, sugar and last of all whites of 2 hard boiled eggs to the spinach
2. Bake in a buttered mould in a pan of water
