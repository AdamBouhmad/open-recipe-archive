## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter
- poached eggs

## Directions

1. Slice the tomatoes crosswise.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Turn in flour or in egg and bread crumbs.
4. Brown on both sides in butter.
5. Serve on a platter or on toast.
6. Poached eggs may be served on top, if preferred.
