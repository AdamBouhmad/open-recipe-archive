## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of marrowbones
- 7 quarts of cold water
- 1 pound of soupmeat
- 1 pound of veal
- fat from marrowbones
- carrot
- turnip
- onion
- celery
- parsley
- clove
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut 4 lbs. of marrowbones into pieces and put them in a soupkettle.
2. Pour 7 quarts of cold water on them and let it stand for an hour.
3. Cut 1 pound of soupmeat and 1 pound of veal into small squares and brown the meat in a pan using the fat from marrowbones for browning.
4. Add the browned meat into the soupkettle, place it on a slow fire.
5. When boiling add the cleaned and cut vegetables into it.
6. Season with cloves, salt and pepper.
7. Let simmer covered for 6 hours.
8. When ready, strain through a fine cloth into an earthen bowl.
9. Let it cool uncovered.
