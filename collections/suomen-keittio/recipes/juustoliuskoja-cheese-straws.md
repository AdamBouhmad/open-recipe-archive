## Ingredients

- puff paste
- grated cheese
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Roll puff paste into a sheet 1/8 inch thick, sprinkle with grated cheese and some cayenne pepper.
2. Double up and press lightly together.
3. Cut with knife in straws 1/2 inch wide and 4 inches long.
4. Bake in a hot oven for 10 minutes.
5. Serve with salad.
