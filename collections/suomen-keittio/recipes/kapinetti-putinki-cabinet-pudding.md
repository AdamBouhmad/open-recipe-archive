## Ingredients

- Almonds
- Sugar
- Prunes
- Sponge cake
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3/4 lemon
- 1/2 cup prune juice
- Hard sauce

## Directions

1. Blanch and chop almonds and mix with sugar.
2. Cook prunes, split and fill the halves with almonds.
3. Line a mold with filled prunes.
4. Fill with sponge cake cut in cubes.
5. Cover with the remaining prunes crushed.
6. Prepare a mixture by whipping 2 eggs with 2 tablespoons of sugar, 3/4 lemon and 1/2 cup of prune juice.
7. Cover the top with this mixture.
8. Bake in oven in a pan of water.
9. Serve with hard sauce.
