## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 5 eggs
- 1 cup coffee
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1/2 cup currants
- 1/2 cup lemon rind chopped
- 4 cups flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon cloves
- 1/4 teaspoon mace
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

## Directions

1. Rub until creamy the butter with the sugar
2. Add the beaten yolks of the eggs
3. Add molasses, coffee, spices
4. Add raisins and currants rolled in flour, baking powder and flour sifted together
5. Last of all add the whites beaten to a firm froth
6. Bake in a tub tin
