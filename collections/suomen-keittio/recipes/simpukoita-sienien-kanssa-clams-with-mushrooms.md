## Ingredients

- 1 onion
- butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 pint clams
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- 3 egg yolks
- water
- 10 medium-sized mushrooms

## Directions

1. Fry 1 chopped onion in butter
2. Add 1 tablespoon of flour and 1 pint of clams chopped
3. Season with salt, pepper and some mustard
4. Cook for 20 or 30 minutes
5. Remove from fire and add 3 beaten egg yolks
6. Add some water if desired
7. Chop and fry 10 medium-sized mushrooms and add to the mixture
8. Shake over the fire until firm
9. Serve, optionally garnished with whole mushrooms around the edges
