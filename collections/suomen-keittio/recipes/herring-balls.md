## Ingredients

- 2 herrings
- 1 cupful of chopped potato
- 2 eggs
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cream

## Directions

1. Soak the herrings, skin and bone.
2. Force through the meatchopper.
3. Chop the onion, fry in butter.
4. Soak the breadcrumbs in cream, add the beaten eggs into it, mix with the fried onion.
5. Form into cakes or balls, roll in egg and breadcrumbs, cook in fat.
6. Serve with any kind of sour sauce.
