## Ingredients

- carrots
- salt
- water
- butter
- pepper
- cream

## Directions

1. Peel the carrots and cook in salted water until tender
2. Force through a meat chopper, sieve, or grate them
3. Add butter, salt, pepper and some cream
4. Heat to boiling
