## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Trim off the fat and scrape the bones.
2. Broil on both sides. Allow 8 to 10 minutes to chops 1 inch thick.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and dot with butter.
4. Place on a hot platter having mashed potatoes or string-beans in the center of the dish, or arrange the chops nicely on the platter and garnish with parsley.
