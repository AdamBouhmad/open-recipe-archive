## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of chopped onion
- 1 tablespoonful of skinned and cut red and green pepper
- 1½ cupfuls of tomatoes
- 2 tablespoonfuls of chopped mushrooms
- 1 tablespoonful of caper
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook onion and pepper in butter, add tomato, and let simmer gently, covered, 15 minutes
2. Mix with mushrooms, caper and salt
3. Prepare the plain omelet, pour the mixture on it, and serve immediately
