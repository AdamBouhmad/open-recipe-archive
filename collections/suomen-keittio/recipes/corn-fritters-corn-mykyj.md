## Ingredients

- ½ cupful of corn
- 4 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 eggs
- ¼ teaspoonful of baking powder

## Directions

1. Rub corn through a strainer
2. Add salt, flour, baking powder, egg yolks and lastly egg whites
3. Place by spoonfuls into boiling fat and fry brown
