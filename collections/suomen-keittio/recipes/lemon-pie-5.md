## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of water
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 3 eggs
- juice and grated rind of 3/4 lemon
- 1/2 teaspoonful of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of corn starch

## Directions

1. Put water, lemon juice and rind and butter on the fire
2. Add sugar and corn starch
3. Pour the boiling water slowly into the corn starch
4. Cook few minutes
5. Stir yolks into it
6. Turn the mixture on the open baked pie crust
7. Bake in oven until firm
8. Cover with meringue
9. Let stand in oven until light brown
