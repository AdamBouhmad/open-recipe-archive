## Ingredients

- 4 pounds mutton
- Water
- Vegetables
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Butter
- Brown gravy

## Directions

1. Cut mutton into pieces and wipe.
2. Place in a saucepan and cover with boiling water.
3. Cook slowly with vegetables until tender.
4. Drain and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
5. Roll in flour and brown in butter.
6. Arrange on the platter.
7. Cover with brown gravy made of the bouillon, removing the fat carefully.
