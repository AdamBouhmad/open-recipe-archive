## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of milk
- 1/2 cupful of farina
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- some raisins
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Cook milk and farina to a paste
2. Add yolks, beaten with sugar, a little salt and raisins
3. Pour in a buttered mold
4. Cover with whites, beaten with sugar until stiff
5. Brown in oven
