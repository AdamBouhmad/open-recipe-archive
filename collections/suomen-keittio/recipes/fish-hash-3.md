## Ingredients

- remains of fish bone
- cold chopped potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- pork or butter
- onion

## Directions

1. Take remains of fish bone and chop them.
2. Mix with equal quantity of cold chopped potatoes.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Melt a slice of pork or a bit of butter in a frying pan, cook one chopped onion in it.
5. Put the mixture of fish and potatoes in the pan and stir until hot.
6. Fry until brown underneath.
7. Fold as omelet and turn on a platter.
