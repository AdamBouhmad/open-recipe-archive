## Ingredients

- boiled lobster
- cracker crumbs
- egg
- lard
- Tartar sauce

## Directions

1. Shell and clean boiled lobster.
2. Cut the tailmeat in quarters and the meat of claws in halves.
3. Roll in cracker crumbs, in egg and again in cracker crumbs.
4. Cook in lard.
5. Serve with Tartar sauce.
