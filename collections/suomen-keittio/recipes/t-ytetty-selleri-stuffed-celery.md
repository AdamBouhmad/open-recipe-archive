## Ingredients

- 6 celery roots
- 1/4 pound beef
- 1/4 pound veal
- 2 ounces suet or lard
- 1 tablespoon bread crumbs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon chopped onion
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Scrape the celery and rinse well
2. Parboil in salted water
3. Remove the top of the roots and scoop the insides
4. Stuff the celery with a mixture made of the described ingredients as usually
5. Replace the tops, place in a buttered pan
6. Bake slowly, basting often with bouillon
7. When the celery is tender and the stuffing done, remove onto a platter
8. Make a gravy of the drippings
