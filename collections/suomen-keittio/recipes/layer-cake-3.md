## Ingredients

- 1/4 cupful of butter
- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- 2 1/2 cupfuls of flour
- 4 eggs
- 1 cupful of milk
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Work butter and sugar to a froth, add yolks, one at a time, rubbing well.
2. Stir milk, baking powder, mixed and sifted with flour, into it.
3. Lastly mix with stiffly beaten eggs.
4. Butter three shallow baking pans, put a thin layer of the batter in each one and bake in a moderate oven about 20 minutes.
5. Place filling on two layers, pile the cakes nicely.
6. Cover with Royal or chocolate icing.
