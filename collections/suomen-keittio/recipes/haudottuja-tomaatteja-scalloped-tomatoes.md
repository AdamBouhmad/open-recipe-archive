## Ingredients

- 1 can of tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- sugar
- chopped onion
- green pepper (optional)
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Take 1 can of tomatoes, drain off the juice.
2. Season the tomato with salt and pepper.
3. Add butter, sugar and chopped onion (green pepper chopped may be added, if desired).
4. Put into a buttered mold, sprinkle with bread crumbs mixed with butter.
5. Bake for 15 minutes.
6. Serve in the mold.
