## Ingredients

- 2½ quarts blackberries
- 2 quarts raspberries
- 2½ pounds granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Put the berries with the sugar into a kettle.
2. Place over the fire and simmer until it becomes firm when dropped from a spoon on a plate.
3. Put into jelly glasses, cover as directed.
