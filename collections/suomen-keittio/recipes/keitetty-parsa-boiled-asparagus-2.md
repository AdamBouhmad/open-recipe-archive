## Ingredients

- 60 asparagus stalks
- 1 oz. of salt
- 1½ quarts of water
- 4 ozs. of butter
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Scrape asparagus
2. Wash in cold water
3. Tie up in bunches
4. Put in boiling water
5. Cook 15–20 minutes
6. When done, take them out from water
7. Unbind the bundles
8. Place on a serving dish or on toast
9. Serve with melted butter mixed with a little lemon juice or with Hollandaise sauce
