## Ingredients

- 1 cup canned or fresh corn grated
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt
- pepper
- some sugar
- 2 egg whites
- some flour or bread crumbs (if canned corn is used)
- onion or chopped green pepper (optional for flavoring)

## Directions

1. Mix grated corn with 2 egg yolks, 1 tablespoon butter, salt, pepper and some sugar
2. Beat 2 egg whites firm and add last of all
3. If canned corn is used, add some flour or bread crumbs
4. Bake in buttered timbale molds in a pan of water covered for 20 minutes or until firm
5. Serve with milk sauce
6. Flavor with onion or chopped green pepper according to taste
