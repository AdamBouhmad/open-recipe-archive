## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- salt
- celery
- onion
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- chicken stock
- 2 egg yolks
- cream
- lemon juice
- rice

## Directions

1. Clean and joint the chicken.
2. Put into a kettle, pour over it boiling water.
3. When boiled about 5 minutes, add salt, a little celery, onion and pepper to season.
4. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and chicken stock, add yolks of two eggs and a little cream.
5. Arrange on a serving dish having the chicken upon a foundation or molded ring of rice.
6. Or place rice timbales around the chicken.
7. Flavor with a dash of lemon juice.
