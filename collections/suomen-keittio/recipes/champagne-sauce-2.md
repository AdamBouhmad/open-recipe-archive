## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of brown sauce
- 1/2 cupful of champagne
- few grains of pepper
- 1 bay leaf
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Make a cupful of brown sauce.
2. Add 1/2 cupful of champagne, few grains of pepper, 1 bay leaf and a little sugar.
3. Cook 10 minutes and strain.
