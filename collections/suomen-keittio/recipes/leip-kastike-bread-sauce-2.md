## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 slice of onion
- 1/2 cupful of sifted bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook milk with butter, slice of onion and sifted bread crumbs
2. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg
3. Remove the onion
4. Bake in oven in a pan of water for 30 minutes
5. Brown bread crumbs in butter
6. Sprinkle the sauce with it
7. Serve with boiled chicken, the sauce poured around the meat
