## Ingredients

- 4 pounds perch
- 5 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 3 tablespoons chopped parsley
- 1 chopped onion
- 4 cups water
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean and rinse.
2. Rub inside with salt and place the roe back into the fish.
3. Lay the fish in a buttered saucepan having butter, salt, pepper, flour, parsley and onion between the layers of fish.
4. Cover with hot water, let simmer 20 to 30 minutes.
5. Place on a platter, pour the sauce over and lay small potatoes around the fish.
