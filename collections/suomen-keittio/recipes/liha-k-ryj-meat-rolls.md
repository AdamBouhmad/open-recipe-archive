## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of meat removed from bone
- 3/4 lbs. of pork
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablesp. of chopped parsley
- 1 onion
- salt and pepper
- boiling water or bouillon

## Directions

1. Cut beef into thin slices and pound it taking care not to break it.
2. Spread with onion juice and chopped parsley.
3. Lay slices of pork on it.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
5. Roll tightly together and fasten with cord.
6. Fry until all browned.
7. Pour boiling water or bouillon over and let boil 1—1 1/2 hours.
8. Prepare sauce of the same liquor.
