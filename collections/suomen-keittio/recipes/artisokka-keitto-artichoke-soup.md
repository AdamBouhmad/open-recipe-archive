## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 quarts of bouillon
- 1 quart of artichokes
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- salt and pepper
- 2 yolks of eggs
- 1 cupful of cream
- 1 cucumber

## Directions

1. Scrape the artichokes, rinse and put them for a little while in water mixed with vinegar.
2. Cook them in bouillon until soft and pass through the strainer.
3. Thicken with butter and flour, let boil for a few minutes more.
4. Whip the yolks and cream, pour the soup slowly in it, whipping well, season with salt and pepper.
5. Peel the cucumber, remove the seeds and cut it into pieces. Cook until tender with a little bit of butter; add to the soup.
6. Serve with fried bread dice.
