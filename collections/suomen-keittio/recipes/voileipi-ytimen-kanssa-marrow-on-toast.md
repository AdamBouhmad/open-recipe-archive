## Ingredients

- marrow bone
- flour
- water
- salt
- pepper
- preserved onions
- parsley
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cover the holes of the marrow-bone with hard dough made of flour and water so as to prevent the marrow running out while boiling.
2. Put the bone into boiling water and let boil 1 or 2 hours.
3. Spread marrow on toast and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Keep the toast in oven for 2 or 3 minutes and serve very hot.
5. Alternatively, mix the marrow with preserved onions and chopped parsley, season with salt and pepper, and flavor with lemon juice before spreading on toast.
