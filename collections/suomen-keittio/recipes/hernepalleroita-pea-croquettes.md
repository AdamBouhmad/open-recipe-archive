## Ingredients

- 2 cups dried peas
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1 cup milk
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 onion
- 2 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- minced parsley

## Directions

1. Boil the peas until tender
2. Drain and let cool
3. Force through a meat grinder
4. Add the chopped onion fried in butter
5. Add bread crumbs soaked in milk
6. Add salt, pepper and minced parsley
7. Mix well
8. Form into croquettes
9. Dip in egg and roll in bread crumbs
10. Fry in butter or deep fat
