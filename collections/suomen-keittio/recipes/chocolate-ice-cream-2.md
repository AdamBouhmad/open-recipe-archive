## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 1 pint of cream
- 3 ozs. of chocolate
- 6 yolks
- 1½ cupfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Melt chocolate and pour boiling milk on it.
2. Beat yolks with sugar.
3. Cook one half of cream.
4. Pour the boiling cream and chocolate mixture slowly into the yolks, whipping well.
5. Put in double-boiler on the fire and beat until thick.
6. When cool, add the rest of the cream and freeze.
