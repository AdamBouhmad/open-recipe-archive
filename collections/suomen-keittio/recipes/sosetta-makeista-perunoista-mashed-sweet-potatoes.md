## Ingredients

- Sweet potatoes
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Scalded milk or cream

## Directions

1. Boil or bake the potatoes
2. Scoop out the insides and mash
3. Add pepper, salt, butter and scalded milk or cream until of right consistency
4. Rub to a smooth paste
