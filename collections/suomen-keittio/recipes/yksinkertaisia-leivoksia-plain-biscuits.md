## Ingredients

- 1 or 3/4 cup milk or water
- 2 cups flour
- 2 tablespoons butter or shortening
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Sift the flour with salt and baking powder.
2. Work well with fingers the butter into the flour.
3. Add gradually milk enough to make a batter thick enough to handle.
4. Roll into 3/8 inch thick sheet, cut with a biscuit cutter into round cakes.
5. Place on a buttered baking sheet and bake in a hot oven for 15 minutes.
6. 1 egg may be added to the milk, if preferred.
