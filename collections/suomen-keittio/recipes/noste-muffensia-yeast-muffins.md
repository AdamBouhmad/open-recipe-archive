## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- ½ teaspoonful of salt
- ¾ cake of yeast
- 2 eggs
- 3–4 cupfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Heat milk
2. Add sugar, salt, butter and yeast dissolved in a small amount of milk
3. Add enough flour to make a loose batter
4. Cover and let rise overnight
5. In the morning beat eggs well
6. Mix into the batter
7. Fill buttered muffin molds about halfway
8. Let rise until full
9. Bake about 30 minutes
10. Alternatively, use ⅓ cupful of cornmeal and ¾ cupful of wheat flour
