## Ingredients

- fresh ham
- water
- salt
- pepper
- ginger
- mustard powder
- bread crumbs
- vegetables
- rice
- baked beans
- apple sauce or boiled prunes

## Directions

1. Place ham skin side down in a dripping-pan
2. Pour 1 cupful of water into the pan and put in a hot oven
3. When the surface is lightly browned sprinkle with salt and baste with the drippings
4. Turn over and remove the skin when it begins to loosen from the meat
5. Sprinkle the ham with salt, pepper, ginger, a little mustard powder and bread crumbs
6. Return to the oven and bake until done, basting often
7. Lift on a serving platter, skim the fat from the sauce, season with mustard, pepper and tomato
8. Send the ham to the table with vegetables, rice, baked beans, apple sauce or boiled prunes
