## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour whipping cream
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- Dash of salt

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar until frothy.
2. Add cream.
3. Add flour sifted with soda.
4. Flavor with yellow rind of a lemon or cinnamon.
5. Last of all fold in the egg whites beaten until firm.
6. Bake in a shallow tin.
7. May be mixed with melted chocolate.
