## Ingredients

- kidneys
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- butter
- onion
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1/2 cupful of meat stock
- 1/2 cupful of mushrooms

## Directions

1. Soak kidneys in cold water for an hour
2. Wipe and cut in pieces
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, roll in bread crumbs and fry in butter with a slice of onion
4. Mix with 1 tablespoonful of flour, 1/2 cupful of meat stock and let boil 5 minutes
5. Strain, add 1/2 cupful of mushrooms cleaned and cut into quarters
6. Season with salt and pepper, and let boil for a few minutes
