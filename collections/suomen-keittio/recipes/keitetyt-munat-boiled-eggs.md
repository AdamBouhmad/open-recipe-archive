## Ingredients

- eggs
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash eggs with lukewarm water
2. Put them carefully into boiling water and cook 3-4 minutes, uncovered
3. Alternatively, put the eggs into cold water and place on the range
4. When coming to boil, remove the eggs, put them in cold water and wipe
5. For hardboiled eggs, put the eggs into boiling water and cook 10 minutes uncovered
6. Put into cold water to remove the shells easier
