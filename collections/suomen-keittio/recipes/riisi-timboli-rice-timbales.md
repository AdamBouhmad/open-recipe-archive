## Ingredients

- boiled rice
- butter
- strained tomato (optional)

## Directions

1. Press the boiled rice well in small buttered timbales.
2. Dot with butter and let stand in boiling water a few minutes.
3. Use to garnish meat dishes.
4. The rice timbales may be mixed also with a little strained tomato to color them.
