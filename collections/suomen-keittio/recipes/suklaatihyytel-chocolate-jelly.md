## Ingredients

- 1 pint rich milk
- 2 ounces chocolate
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon (1/4 package) gelatine
- 1 cup cream (may be made without)
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Scald the milk.
2. Add vanilla, chocolate and gelatine dissolved and a part of the sugar.
3. Stir until the gelatine is dissolved.
4. Let cool.
5. Whip the cream with the rest of the sugar.
6. Add to the chocolate mixture just when it begins to set.
7. Pour into a mold dipped in water and let set.
8. Serve unmolded garnished with whipped cream.
