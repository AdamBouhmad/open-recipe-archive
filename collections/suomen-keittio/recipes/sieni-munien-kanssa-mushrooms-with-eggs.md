## Ingredients

- 1 pound mushrooms
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 onion
- salt and pepper
- 5 eggs
- 5 cups milk

## Directions

1. Clean and chop the mushrooms
2. Brown the chopped onion with butter
3. Add the mushrooms, salt and pepper and fry until brown
4. Whip 5 eggs and add 5 cups of milk and the mushrooms
5. Pour into a buttered mold
6. Bake in oven in a pan of water until firm, about 20 minutes
