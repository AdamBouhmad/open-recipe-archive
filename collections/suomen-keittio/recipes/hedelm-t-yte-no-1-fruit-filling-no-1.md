## Ingredients

- juice of 1 orange
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- chopped nuts or almonds

## Directions

1. Stir over the fire until thick the juices of an orange and a lemon with 1 cup sugar and 2 egg yolks
2. Mix with chopped nuts and almonds, if preferred
