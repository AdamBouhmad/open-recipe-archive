## Ingredients

- 1 cup thick milk sauce
- 1 cup grated American cheese
- 3 eggs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Stir the cheese into the milk sauce.
2. Add gradually the yolks of egg, salt and pepper.
3. At the last mix with the whites of egg beaten to a firm froth.
4. Butter individual molds and fill with the mixture.
5. Bake in oven in a pan of water for 15 minutes.
6. Serve in the mold.
