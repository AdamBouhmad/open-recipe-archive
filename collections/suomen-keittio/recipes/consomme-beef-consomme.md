## Ingredients

- 5 pounds soup meat
- 3 pounds bone
- 3 pounds chicken or some other fowl
- 6 quarts water
- 1/4 cup carrot
- 1/4 cup onion
- 1/4 cup celery
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Cut the meat in cubes, brown in a soup kettle with 1 tablespoonful of butter
2. Add the bones and the fowl
3. Cover with cold water, let stand covered on the side of the range for 1/2 hour
4. Cook on a slow fire for 3 hours or longer
5. Brown the vegetables, add to the soup, continue to boil for 1 hour
6. Strain the soup
7. If keeping for a long time, do not remove the cover of fat
