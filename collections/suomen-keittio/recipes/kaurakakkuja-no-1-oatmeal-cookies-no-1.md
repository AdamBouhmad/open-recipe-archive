## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup oatmeal
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 3/4 cup butter
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- Cinnamon and raisins

## Directions

1. Sift together flour, salt and baking powder, work butter in the flour.
2. Beat eggs with sugar, add milk and pour the mixture gradually into the flour.
3. Roll the dough thin, cut in round small cookies.
4. Lay on a buttered baking sheet, bake in a hot oven until light brown.
5. The raisins may be left out, if desired.
