## Ingredients

- 3 egg whites
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped almonds
- 2 cups milk
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1/2 package gelatine
- lemon juice
- 5 tablespoons whipped cream
- 1 package lemon jelly powder
- 1/2 cup water

## Directions

1. Beat until firm 3 egg whites, add 2 tablespoons sugar.
2. Scald 2 cups milk with 2 tablespoons finely chopped almonds.
3. Drop the beaten egg whites by the spoonful into the milk, let boil for a few minutes.
4. Take out the whites, drain.
5. Add to the milk 3 egg yolks beaten with 2 tablespoons sugar and 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch.
6. Beat over the fire until thick, add 1/2 package of gelatine, let cool.
7. When cold, add some lemon juice and 5 tablespoons whipped cream.
8. Lay on the bottom of a mold the whites.
9. Pour slowly over the whites 1 cup of liquid jelly made of 1 package of lemon jelly powder and 1/2 cup water.
10. When firm, pour the cream mixture over, put aside to set.
