## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Thick milk sauce
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sole fillets
- Fish stock or water
- Butter
- Grated cheese
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil spinach, grind or force through a sieve, add some thick milk sauce, salt and pepper.
2. Butter a baking dish, place the spinach in the center of dish.
3. Raise the fillets of a sole, lay in a pan, sprinkle with salt and pepper, add 1/2 cup of fish stock or water and small dots of butter.
4. Bake covered with buttered paper for 10 minutes.
5. Arrange the fillets over the spinach, cover the whole with milk sauce mixed with grated cheese.
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs, bake until brown.
7. Serve in the baking dish.
