## Ingredients

- Mashed chestnuts
- ½ cup rice, boiled with milk
- Butter
- Salt
- Sugar or raisins

## Directions

1. Mix the mashed chestnuts with ½ cup rice boiled with milk
2. Add some butter, salt and sugar, or raisins instead of sugar
3. Serve as a substitute for vegetables or as a dessert with fruit sauce
