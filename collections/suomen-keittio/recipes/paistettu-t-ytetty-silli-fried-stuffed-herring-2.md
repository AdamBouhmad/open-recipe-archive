## Ingredients

- 3 fresh herrings
- 6 anchovies
- 4 tablespoonfuls of breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of milk
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Open the herrings at the back, remove the backbone and small bones.
2. Wash the fish, wipe and rub with salt.
3. Remove the bones from the anchovies and chop finely.
4. Soak the breadcrumbs in milk, add the chopped anchovies.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
