## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of graham flour
- 1 cupful of wheat flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 cupful of milk
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix and sift graham flour and wheat flour with baking powder and salt
2. Beat eggs with sugar and milk
3. Pour slowly into the mixture of flour whipping hard
4. Mix with melted butter at the last
5. Put in greased muffin tins
6. Bake nearly 25–30 minutes
