## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound of macaroni
- salted water
- 1/2 onion
- 1/2 green pepper
- 1 small carrot
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 quart of fresh tomatoes
- 1/2 cupful of water
- salt
- 4 tablespoonfuls of mushrooms
- 2 tablespoonfuls of grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 pound of macaroni cut in pieces in salted water and drain.
2. Fry 1/2 onion, 1/2 green pepper and 1 small carrot paloiteltuna in 2 tablespoonfuls of butter until brown.
3. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of flour and stir thoroughly.
4. Add 1 quart of fresh tomatoes paloiteltuna and 1/2 cupful of water.
5. Season with salt and let simmer slowly 30-40 minutes.
6. Strain and add 4 tablespoonfuls of chopped mushrooms and let boil five minutes.
7. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of grated cheese and pour over the macaroni.
