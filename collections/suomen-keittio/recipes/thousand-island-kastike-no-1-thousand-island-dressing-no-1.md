## Ingredients

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1/2 cup whipped cream
- 1 tablespoon catsup
- 1 tablespoon Chili sauce
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 or 2 tablespoons chopped green peppers
- 2 tablespoons chopped pimento
- 2 tablespoons chives
- 1 teaspoon onion juice
- Salt and paprika

## Directions

1. Put all the ingredients into a bowl
2. Add the mayonnaise stirring well
3. Last mix slowly with the whipped cream
4. Add a hard boiled chopped egg, if preferred
5. Dilute with lemon juice, if necessary
