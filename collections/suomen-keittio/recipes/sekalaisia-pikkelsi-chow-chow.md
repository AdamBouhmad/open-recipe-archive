## Ingredients

- 5 quarts cucumbers
- 1 quart green tomatoes
- 1 red pepper
- 1 cauliflower
- 1 pint small onions
- 1 quart string beans
- 1 quart peas
- 1 pint carrots cut in pieces
- 2 quarts vinegar
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon pepper
- a few bay leaves
- mace
- 2 ounces mustard seed
- salt
- 1/2 ounce allspice
- different kinds of mushrooms
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and cut in pieces the vegetables
2. Let stand in salt water for 24 hours
3. Boil vinegar with sugar and spices
4. Add vegetables and cook until tender
5. Arrange the vegetables nicely into jars
6. Add white pepper, mace and bay leaves
7. Strain the liquid, pour over the vegetables
8. Sprinkle the top with mustard seeds
9. Cover as directed
