## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 4 egg yolks
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1/4 teaspoonful of salt
- a little pepper
- 3/4 cupful of hot water

## Directions

1. Cream butter in a saucepan
2. Add yolks one by one whipping well between each one
3. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper
4. Add hot water
5. Stir the sauce in a pot of boiling water on the fire until it is thick
6. Remove from the fire and stir a little while
7. Serve with fish, asparagus and cauliflower
