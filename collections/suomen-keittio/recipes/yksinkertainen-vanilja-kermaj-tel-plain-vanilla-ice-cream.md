## Ingredients

- 1 quart cream
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 vanilla bean or 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Scald the cream with the sugar.
2. When cool add vanilla and freeze.
3. If whipping cream is used, dilute it with some milk.
