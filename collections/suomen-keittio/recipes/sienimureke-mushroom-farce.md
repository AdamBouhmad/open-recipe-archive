## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 pounds mushrooms
- 10 ounces bread crumbs
- 1/4 pound butter
- 2 small onions
- 2 cups rich milk
- 3 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms and force through a meat grinder.
2. Add the onions chopped and fried in butter.
3. Add the bread crumbs soaked in milk, seasonings and eggs.
4. Rub the mixture well.
5. Bake in a buttered mold for 1 hour.
6. Serve with melted butter or make a sauce of vegetable stock.
