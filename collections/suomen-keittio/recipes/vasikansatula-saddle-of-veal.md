## Ingredients

- veal saddle
- butter
- paper
- salt
- pepper
- pork slices
- egg
- bread crumbs
- water
- cream

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat and trim into a good shape.
2. Wrap into a buttered paper and roast in oven as directed for roast veal.
3. Remove the paper 3/4 hour before serving, add a sprinkling of salt, let brown.
4. Line the meat with thin slices of pork, dip in egg, roll in bread crumbs and roast as directed for veal, basting often and adding water to the drippings.
5. Make gravy of the drippings and cream.
