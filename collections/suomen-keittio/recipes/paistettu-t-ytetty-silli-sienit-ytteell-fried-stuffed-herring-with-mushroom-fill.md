## Ingredients

- 3 fresh herrings
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/2 onion
- 1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
- 1/2 tablespoon chopped parsley
- Lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons cream
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook butter with chopped onion.
2. Add chopped mushrooms, chopped parsley and some lemon juice.
3. Let simmer on the stove for 10 minutes.
4. Add cream, season with salt and pepper.
5. Stuff the herrings with the mixture.
6. Wrap in buttered paper and bake in oven until done.
7. Serve with tomato sauce.
