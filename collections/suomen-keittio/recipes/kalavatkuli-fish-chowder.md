## Ingredients

- 3 pounds fresh fish
- 4 cups potatoes
- 1 onion
- 1½ pounds salt pork
- 3 cups milk
- 3 tablespoons butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Crackers

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the fish and cut in pieces 1 inch thick.
2. Fry onion in butter and strain.
3. Put the butter back in the frying pan to fry the fish in.
4. Cut the pork in cubes, roll in flour, and fry in a little butter taking care not to brown the pork.
5. Drain the pork.
6. Put the boiled potatoes in the drippings and fry for a while.
7. Place layers of potato, fish and pork alternately into a kettle.
8. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
9. Add a little fish stock into the kettle.
10. Cook for 10 minutes.
11. Add heated milk, some butter, chopped parsley and crackers soaked in milk.
12. Boil quickly.
13. Put the chowder on a serving dish, cover with soaked crackers, and pour the sauce over.
14. Instead of milk, stewed strained tomatoes may be used.
