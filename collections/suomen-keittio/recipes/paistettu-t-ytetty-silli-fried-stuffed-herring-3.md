## Ingredients

- 3 fresh herrings
- 6 anchovies
- 4 tablespoonfuls of breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of milk
- salt and pepper
- onion juice
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Split the herring down the back; remove the backbone and small bones.
2. Wash the fish, wipe and rub with salt.
3. Bone the anchovies and chop them.
4. Soak the breadcrumbs in milk, mix with the chopped anchovies.
5. Season with salt, pepper, onion juice and chopped parsley.
6. Stuff the herrings with this mixture.
7. Place in a buttered pan, bake about 1/2 hour.
8. Serve with currant- or any kind of sour sauce.
