## Ingredients

- bream
- salted water
- vinegar
- tomato sauce
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil bream in salted water with a little vinegar
2. Skin the fish
3. Place in a buttered pan
4. Pour 3/4 cupful of tomato sauce over the top
5. Bake for 20 minutes in a hot oven
