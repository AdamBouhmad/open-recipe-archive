## Ingredients

- batter from sweet pancakes recipe
- jam
- cream
- sugar
- vanilla
- cooked fruit

## Directions

1. Fry thin pancakes and pile them nicely on a serving dish, putting jam between the cakes.
2. Whip cream until stiff, mix with a little sugar and vanilla, place on the pancakes.
3. Garnish with cooked fruit.
4. Serve cold.
