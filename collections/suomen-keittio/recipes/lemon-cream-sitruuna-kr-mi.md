## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 1 orange rind
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 cupful of sherry
- 5 leaves or 3 tablespoonfuls of gelatine
- 1 pint of cream

## Directions

1. Whip the yolks and sugar in a saucepan
2. Add all other ingredients except cream, which has to be whipped to a froth separately
3. Place the saucepan on the fire stirring mixture until it thickens
4. Set to cool whipping continually
5. Pour the beaten cream into it
6. Put the mixture in a mold to harden
7. Serve with fruit sauce or cream
