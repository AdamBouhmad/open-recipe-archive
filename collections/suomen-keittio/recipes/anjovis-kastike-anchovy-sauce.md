## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- anchovy essence
- 3 cupfuls of beef stock or milk
- 2 - 3 tablespoonfuls of sour cream

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and beef stock
2. Add anchovy essence to taste and sour cream
3. Serve with fish or meat
4. When served with fish, use fish stock instead of beef stock
5. When served with eggs, use milk instead of beef stock
