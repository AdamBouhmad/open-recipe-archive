## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound chicken meat and liver
- 1/4 pound veal
- some pork or suet
- 1/4 cup bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup milk or cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the bread crumbs in milk.
2. Add the egg beaten and spices.
3. Grind the meat several times.
4. Pound well.
5. Add slowly the bread mixture to the meat, rubbing well.
