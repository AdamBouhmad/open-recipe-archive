## Ingredients

- 10 ozs. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 lb. of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of vanilla
- 1/2 lb. of almonds

## Directions

1. Work butter to a froth.
2. Beat egg with sugar until light.
3. Combine the butter and egg mixtures.
4. Mix in finely chopped almonds, vanilla and flour, stirring well.
5. Force through a pastry bag onto a baking sheet into small rings.
6. Bake in a slow oven.
