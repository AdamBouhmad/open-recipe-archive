## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice
- 1/2 teaspoonful of vinegar
- 1 teaspoonful of tomato catsup
- 1 teaspoonful of Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoonful of grated horseradish
- salt
- Tabasco sauce
- clams
- ice

## Directions

1. Prepare sauce with lemon juice, vinegar, tomato catsup, Worcestershire sauce, grated horseradish, salt and a few drops of Tabasco
2. Wash clams
3. Mix clams into the sauce
4. Set on ice until serving
