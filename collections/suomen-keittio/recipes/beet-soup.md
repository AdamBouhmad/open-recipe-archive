## Ingredients

- 5 beets
- 3 quarts of vegetable stock
- 2 leeks
- 2 tablespoonfuls of lemon juice
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 cupful of cream

## Directions

1. Bake the beets in oven, peel and cut into thin strips.
2. Moisten with lemon juice.
3. Cook the leek in butter, add the flour and scalded vegetable stock and bring to a boil.
4. Add the yolks of eggs and cream beaten together, at the last add the slices of beet into it.
5. Raw grated beet will give it a better color.
