## Ingredients

- onions of equal size
- salt
- butter
- flour
- water
- pepper
- lemon juice
- egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. Select onions of equal size; peel and rinse
2. Place on the fire in boiling water; parboil for 5 minutes
3. Drain the water and pour boiling water over again; season with salt
4. Cook until tender. If put on the fire in cold water, you do not need to change the water
5. Serve either with milk sauce, or prepare a sauce of butter, flour and the water in which the onions cooked
6. Season with salt, pepper and lemon juice
7. If desired, add 2 egg yolks at the end; do not boil after that
8. Alternatively, serve boiled onions with butter, salt and pepper
