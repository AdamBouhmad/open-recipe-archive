## Ingredients

- cucumber
- cold water
- lettuce
- French dressing
- tomato

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumber, place in cold water.
2. Cut in thin slices, arrange on a bed of lettuce.
3. Pour French dressing over.
4. Serve very cold with fish.
5. Cucumber and tomato together make a nice salad.
