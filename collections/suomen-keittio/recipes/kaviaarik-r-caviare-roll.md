## Ingredients

- fresh bread
- caviare
- cream
- red pepper

## Directions

1. Cut fresh bread in thin slices.
2. Spread with caviare mixed with cream.
3. Roll together.
4. Lay caviare on the rolls.
5. Decorate with whipped cream and red pepper.
