## Ingredients

- currants
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Wash currants.
2. Mash few berries on the bottom of kettle to moisten it so as not to burn the berries.
3. Let simmer until tender.
4. Drain through a cloth, weigh and place into kettle, let simmer for 15 minutes.
5. Put an equal amount of sugar in oven to heat, but take care not to burn it.
6. Place sugar in kettle to simmer with currant juice until it has dissolved and the mixture gets clear.
7. Pour it in jelly glasses, cover and keep in a cold place.
