## Ingredients

- ¼ pound rice
- ½ cup sugar
- ½ cup water
- Vanilla
- Fruit (optional)
- Preserved fruit for garnish

## Directions

1. Cook ¼ pound rice for 20 minutes in deep water.
2. Drain and pour cold water over.
3. Put into a dish, baste with liquid of fruit or with syrup made of ½ cup of sugar boiled in ½ cup of water and flavored with vanilla.
4. Cover and let stand for 2 or 3 hours.
5. Mix into half frozen vanilla ice cream.
6. Continue to freeze until done.
7. May be mixed with some fruit or garnished around with fruit.
