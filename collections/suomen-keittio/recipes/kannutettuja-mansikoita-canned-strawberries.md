## Ingredients

- strawberries
- 1/2 to 3/4 sugar to weight of strawberries

## Directions

1. Clean strawberries and weigh them.
2. Put the berries with the sugar in layers into a kettle and let stand overnight.
3. Place on low heat, skim carefully, stir slowly or shake the kettle often.
4. Continue to cook until clear and the skum ceases to rise to the surface.
5. Fill the jars immediately and cover airtight with rubber rings and covers.
