## Ingredients

- Ripe peaches
- Figs
- Dates
- Mayonnaise
- Lettuce
- Raspberries
- Whipped cream
- Cream cheese
- Chopped nuts

## Directions

1. Peel and split ripe peaches, fill the cavity in the center with chopped figs and dates, coat with mayonnaise
2. Serve on a bed of lettuce
3. Peaches may be filled with raspberries and covered with whipped cream
4. Form tiny balls of cream cheese, fill the cavity in peaches with the cheese balls
5. Add a sprinkling of chopped nuts
