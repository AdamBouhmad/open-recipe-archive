## Ingredients

- 1 pint of cream
- 3/4 cupful of molasses
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook cream with sugar and vanilla to a thick mass.
2. Put aside to cool.
3. Boil molasses, mix gradually with the cold creamy mass.
4. Cook stirring well, until when tested in cold water mixture will become brittle.
5. Spread platter with salad oil.
6. Pour the mixture on.
7. Let cool.
8. Cut in pieces and wrap in paper.
