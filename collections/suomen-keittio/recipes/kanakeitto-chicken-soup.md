## Ingredients

- Remains of chicken
- Water enough to cover them
- 1 onion
- 2 stalks celery and a small carrot
- 1 tablespoon salt, pepper
- 2 sprigs parsley

## Directions

1. Put the remains of chicken with the bones into a kettle, add the vegetables.
2. Cover with boiling water.
3. Let simmer for a few hours.
4. Thicken with butter and flour, season with salt and pepper.
5. Flavor with lemon.
6. Strain and serve with pieces of meat and croutons.
7. The remains of duck and turkey may be used for soup same as given above.
