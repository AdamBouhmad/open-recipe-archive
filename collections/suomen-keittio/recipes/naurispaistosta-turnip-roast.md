## Ingredients

- 2 pounds turnips
- 1/2 teaspoonful salt
- 1/8 teaspoonful pepper
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls almonds
- 1 cupful bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Peel, wash and cook turnips until tender.
2. Mash the turnips.
3. Add butter, sugar, peeled and grated almonds, bread crumbs and beaten eggs.
4. Pour into a buttered mould, sprinkle bread crumbs on top and bake in the oven until the top is brown.
5. Serve with lamb or pork.
