## Ingredients

- 1 pint rich milk
- egg yolks
- sugar
- vanilla
- cream

## Directions

1. Scald the milk.
2. Beat the egg-yolks with the sugar.
3. Pour gradually the scalded milk into the yolks.
4. Beat in a double boiler over the fire until thick, not allowing it to boil as it will curdle.
5. Remove from the fire, let cool.
6. Add the vanilla and the cream.
7. Freeze.
8. If vanilla bean is used instead of vanilla extract, cut the bean in pieces and scald with milk.
