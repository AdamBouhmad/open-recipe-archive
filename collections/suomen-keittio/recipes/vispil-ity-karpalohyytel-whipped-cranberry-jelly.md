## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups cranberry liquor without sugar
- 2 1/2 cups water
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons or 1/2 package gelatine

## Directions

1. Boil together the cranberry liquid, water, sugar and gelatine
2. Pour into a large bowl
3. When beginning to jelly whip until it becomes frothy
4. Serve in a glass dish or small glasses
