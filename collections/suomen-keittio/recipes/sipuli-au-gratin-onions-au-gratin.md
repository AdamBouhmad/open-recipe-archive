## Ingredients

- onions
- salt
- water
- butter
- milk sauce
- bread crumbs
- cream
- milk
- tomato slices (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel and cook onions in salted water until tender
2. Drain carefully and lay in a buttered mold
3. Cover with milk sauce
4. Sprinkle with bread crumbs mixed with butter; bake until brown on top
5. Alternatively, lay onions in a mold alternately with bread crumbs, having a layer of bread crumbs on top
6. Sprinkle the layers with salt and dot with butter
7. Fill the mold with rich milk
8. Bake for 1/2 hour
9. Slices of tomato may be laid on top of the bread crumbs, but no milk should be used then
