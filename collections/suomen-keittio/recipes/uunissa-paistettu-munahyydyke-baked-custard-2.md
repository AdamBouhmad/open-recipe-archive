## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cupful of sugar
- a pinch of salt
- a pinch of nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat eggs, add sugar, salt and nutmeg
2. Pour the scalded milk gradually into it
3. Put in a buttered mold and steam in oven until firm
