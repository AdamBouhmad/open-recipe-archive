## Ingredients

- 1/2 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped onion
- breast meat of fried chicken, cut in pieces
- 1/2 cupful of tomato
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped mushrooms
- 2 tablespoonfuls of mushroom liquor
- 1/4 cupful of sherry
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- a little paprika
- 1/2 teaspoonful of chopped green pepper
- a little minced truffle
- 1/2 teaspoonful of meat extract
- a few scallops

## Directions

1. Cook the butter and the onion a few minutes.
2. Add the chicken cut in cubes, tomato and mushrooms.
3. Let boil 10 minutes.
4. Add the scallops, sherry and seasonings.
5. Let boil.
6. Serve instead of fish or as entrée.
