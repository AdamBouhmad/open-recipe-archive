## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 salted herrings
- 1 tablespoon chopped onion
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 or 5 cloves
- juice of 1 lemon or 1 cup vinegar
- 1/2 cup tomato
- pepper
- salt if using fresh herrings
- sugar
- sour apples, sliced thin
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak over night 4 or 5 salted herrings
2. Remove the skin and the bones
3. Lay the herrings in a buttered baking dish with the cover on
4. Sprinkle with 1 tablespoon chopped onion, 2 bay leaves, 4 or 5 cloves, the juice of 1 lemon or 1 cup vinegar, 1/2 cup tomato, some pepper, and if the herrings are fresh, some salt and some sugar
5. Cover with a layer of sliced sour apples
6. Dot with butter if preferred
7. Bake for 3/4 hour
8. Serve hot or cold
