## Ingredients

- Boiled sweet potatoes
- Butter or lard
- Sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel boiled potatoes
2. Cut into thick slices
3. Fry in butter or lard
4. Sprinkle with a little sugar while frying for better appearance
