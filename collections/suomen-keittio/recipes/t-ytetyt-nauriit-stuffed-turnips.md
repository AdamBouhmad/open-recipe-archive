## Ingredients

- 12 middle-sized turnips
- 1/2 cupful of almonds
- 1/2 cupful of bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 eggs
- a pinch of nutmeg and pepper

## Directions

1. Wash turnips and cook in water with salt and pepper until nearly tender.
2. When cool, peel them.
3. Scoop the turnips and mash the interior.
4. Add grated almonds, bread crumbs, butter, salt and pepper, nutmeg and egg.
5. Stuff the turnips with this mixture.
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs.
7. Place in a buttered baking pan and bake in oven to a light brown.
8. Baste often with melted butter.
