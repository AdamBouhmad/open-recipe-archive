## Ingredients

- mashed potatoes
- milk
- 2 eggs
- mushrooms
- butter
- onion
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare mashed potatoes with milk and add 2 eggs
2. Chop the mushrooms
3. Brown mushrooms in butter with onion and season with salt and pepper
4. Put into a buttered mold in alternate layers of potato and mushrooms
5. Bake in oven to a light brown
6. Serve with melted butter or brown sauce
