## Ingredients

- crab shells
- crab meat
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Scrape and rinse the crab shells
2. Fill with crab meat mixed with mayonnaise
3. Garnish the top with red pepper and truffel
4. Serve on a platter garnished with lettuce leaves
