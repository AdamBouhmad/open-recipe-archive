## Ingredients

- 4 salted or fresh herrings
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion, fresh or pickled
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 or 5 cloves
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/4 cup tomato
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Soak over night 4 salted or fresh herrings
2. Lay into a buttered baking dish with the cover on
3. Sprinkle with 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion fresh or pickled, 2 bay leaves, 4 or 5 cloves, the juice of 1 lemon, 1/4 cup tomato, some salt and sugar
4. Bake covered until done
