## Ingredients

- salmon
- salt
- pepper
- 4 bay leaves
- 2 cupfuls of fish-stock
- 1 cupful of tomatoes
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 box of gelatine
- whites of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil the fish with salt, pepper and bay leaf.
2. When done lift off to cool.
3. Put all the above ingredients into a kettle and stir over the fire until boiling.
4. Let simmer without stirring on a slow fire 5 min. until clear.
5. Strain through a fine cloth.
6. Cover the bottom of an individual mold with the jelly, garnish with hard-boiled egg and parsley.
7. Lay the fish pieces carefully into the mold and pour the jelly over gradually.
8. Turn out onto a platter, garnish with tomato slices, lemon and parsley.
9. Serve with mayonnaise sauce.
