## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 cup buttermilk or milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Mix in a bowl all the dry ingredients with the butter.
2. Add gradually the milk stirring well.
3. Turn onto the baking board, knead well.
4. Divide the dough in two parts, roll into 3/8 inch cakes, sprinkle with flour.
5. Divide both cakes in 6 parts, fry on both sides or bake for 10-15 minutes.
6. Serve hot.
