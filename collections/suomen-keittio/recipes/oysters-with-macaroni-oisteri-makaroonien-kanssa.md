## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. of macaroni
- oysters
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Break 3/4 lb. of macaroni in pieces 1 inch long
2. Put into boiling water mixed with a little salt
3. Cook until tender
4. Strain and wash well in cold water
5. Fill a buttered mold with layers of macaroni and oysters sprinkled with salt and pepper
6. Dot with butter
7. Have the last layer of macaroni
8. Dredge with grated cheese
9. Bake for 20-30 minutes
10. Instead of macaroni spinach may be used
