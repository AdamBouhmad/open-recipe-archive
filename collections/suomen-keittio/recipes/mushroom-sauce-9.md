## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1/2 can of mushrooms with liquor
- 2 cupfuls of beef or fish stock
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice
- a pinch of white pepper
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Cook the butter with the flour, the beef stock and the mushroom liquor
2. Put small mushrooms into the mixture
3. Let boil 10 minutes
4. Season with lemon juice, pepper and salt
5. Serve hot
6. Before serving mix one yolk and cream into the sauce, if you want to thicken it
7. Color with Kitchen Bouquet
8. For white sauce use white stock or milk and prepare the sauce without coloring
