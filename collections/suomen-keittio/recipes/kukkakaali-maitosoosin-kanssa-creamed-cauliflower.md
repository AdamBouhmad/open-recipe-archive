## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt water
- milk sauce
- egg yolks (optional)
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil the cauliflower in salt water for 20 minutes
2. Drain and place in a serving dish
3. Cover with milk sauce
4. The sauce may be mixed with 2 yolks of egg and some lemon juice, if preferred
