## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of oysters
- ½ cupful of butter
- ½ cupful of milk
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Wash and dry oysters.
2. Brown butter in pan, put oysters into it and cook for a few minutes.
3. Add flour and egg yolks beaten and mixed with milk.
4. Stir gently.
