## Ingredients

- hard boiled eggs
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- mayonnaise
- buttered toast
- olives

## Directions

1. Chop hard boiled eggs.
2. Season with salt, pepper and onion juice if desired.
3. Soften with mayonnaise.
4. Serve on buttered toast.
5. Mix with chopped olives if preferred.
