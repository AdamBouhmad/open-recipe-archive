## Ingredients

- 4 quarts small white onions
- 2 quarts vinegar
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- mace
- cloves
- ginger, a dash of each
- 1/2 cup salt
- 1 quart water

## Directions

1. Wash the onions, pour boiling water over
2. Peel when cool
3. Let stand in salt water (1/2 cup salt to 1 quart water) for 24 hours
4. Boil for 10 minutes vinegar with sugar and spices tied in a bag
5. Remove the spices
6. Add the onions, cook until nearly done
7. Place the onions into jars
8. Strain the liquid over
9. Cover and keep in cool place
