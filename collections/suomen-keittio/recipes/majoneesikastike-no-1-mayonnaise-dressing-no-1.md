## Ingredients

- 1 egg yolk
- 3/4 cup salad oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/2 tablespoon Tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Put mustard in a bowl and mix with egg yolk.
2. Add oil very gradually at first, drop by drop.
3. When it begins to thicken, add lemon juice and vinegar.
4. Season with salt and pepper at the end.
5. If dressing curdles, add it slowly to an egg yolk.
6. For white mayonnaise, add 1/2 cup whipped cream shortly before serving.
7. For green mayonnaise, pound spinach leaves, parsley, watercress and lemon juice in a mortar, extract juice and add to dressing.
8. For red mayonnaise, add strained tomato puree.
