## Ingredients

- 2 quarts fish stock
- 1 lobster or 1 can of lobsters
- 1 tablespoon butter or lobster butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 cup cream
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the tail meat fine.
2. Cook in fish stock for about half an hour.
3. Force through a sieve.
4. Boil the shells separately in small quantity of water and add to the broth if desired.
5. Thicken with butter and flour.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Beat the egg yolks, add the cream.
8. Gradually add the broth to the egg yolk and cream mixture and stir over heat for a short time.
9. Serve with pieces of lobster meat or with lobster balls and asparagus tips cooked in fish stock until tender.
