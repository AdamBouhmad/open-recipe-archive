## Ingredients

- oranges
- fritter batter

## Directions

1. Peel oranges, cut lengthwise into six parts.
2. Take out seeds carefully and dip the pieces of fruit in fritter batter.
3. Cook in boiling lard.
4. Serve with sauce or sprinkled with sugar.
