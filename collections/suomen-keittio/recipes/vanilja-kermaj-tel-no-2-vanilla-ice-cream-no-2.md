## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 or 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- 1 quart cream
- Salt

## Directions

1. Scald the milk.
2. Mix together sugar, salt and cornstarch, pour gradually the scalded milk in, cook for a few minutes stirring well.
3. Remove from the fire, add the beaten eggs.
4. When cool, add vanilla and cream.
5. Strain and freeze.
6. Use more eggs if cream is not used.
