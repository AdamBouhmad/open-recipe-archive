## Ingredients

- 2 cups tomatoes, canned or fresh
- 1 quart milk
- 1 slice of onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/4 teaspoon soda
- Some sugar
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the onion in butter, add flour and tomato, stirring well, let boil, stirring often until the tomato is tender.
2. Remove from the fire, add 1/4 teaspoonful of soda and the scalded milk gradually and stirring well.
3. Strain, season with salt and pepper.
