## Ingredients

- mackerel
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- green pepper
- mushrooms
- tomato sauce
- wine
- parsley

## Directions

1. Split the fish and remove the backbone
2. Wipe and spread with butter
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Bake in oven covered for about 15 minutes
5. Chop onion and green pepper and cook in butter
6. Add 1 cupful of chopped mushrooms to the mixture
7. Lay the mixture over the fish
8. Continue baking for 10 minutes
9. Remove the fish to a platter
10. Add tomato sauce and wine to the pan
11. Strain the sauce and pour around the fish
12. Strew with chopped parsley
