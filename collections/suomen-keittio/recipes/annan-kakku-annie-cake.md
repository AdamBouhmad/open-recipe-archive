## Ingredients

- 4-5 eggs
- butter, sugar and flour, in equal quantities, as much as the weight of the eggs
- ¼ cupful of almonds
- 1 yellow lemon rind
- ½ cupful of brandy
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder

## Directions

1. Wash butter and rub to a cream.
2. Separate yolks from whites.
3. Beat yolks with sugar, add blanched and chopped almonds, grated lemon rind, butter, flour, brandy and lastly the stiff beaten eggs.
4. Pour the mixture in a buttered mold and bake in a moderate oven about 1 hour.
