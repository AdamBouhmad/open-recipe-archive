## Ingredients

- young rhubarb
- cold water

## Directions

1. Skin young rhubarb and cut into pieces one inch long.
2. Place them in clean jars and pour cold water on filling the jars until three-fourths of their depth.
3. Place the jars in a boiler, fill it with water and put on fire to heat slowly.
4. Cook for 3/4 hour.
5. Keep the jars in water, in which they are cooked until it gets cool.
6. Seal the jars and keep in a cold place.
7. Alternatively, rhubarb may be preserved without boiling: Place rhubarb in jars, pour cold water on, seal the jars and keep in a cool place.
