## Ingredients

- 2 cups crab meat
- 3 hard boiled egg whites
- salt
- pepper
- boiled mayonnaise
- whipped cream
- lettuce leaves
- chopped almonds
- green pepper

## Directions

1. Take 2 cups of crab meat and remove the bones and cut into pieces
2. Add 3 hard boiled egg whites chopped
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Mix boiled mayonnaise with whipped cream
5. Combine the mixture with the mayonnaise and cream
6. Place on a bed of lettuce leaves
7. Garnish with chopped almonds and green pepper
