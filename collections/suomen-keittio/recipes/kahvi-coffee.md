## Ingredients

- 1 cup ground coffee
- 6 cups water
- egg white or egg shells
- cold water

## Directions

1. Put into a coffee pot the ground coffee with the white or the shells of an egg
2. Mix with cold water
3. Pour boiling water over
4. Boil for 5 minutes
5. Place on the side of the range and let stand for 10 minutes to set
