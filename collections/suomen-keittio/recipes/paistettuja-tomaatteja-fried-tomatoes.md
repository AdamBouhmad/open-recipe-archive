## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- browned butter

## Directions

1. Cut tomatoes crosswise into slices
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Dip in egg and bread crumbs
4. Fry on both sides in browned butter
