## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 cupfuls of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1/2 to 2/3 cupful of flour
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Whip yolks and sugar until light, add flour, salt and melted butter.
2. Lastly add whites, beaten stiff.
3. Fry thin pancakes, cover with jelly or jam, roll together and serve hot.
4. The batter may be made thicker or thinner if preferred.
5. Pancakes may be made of cream without butter.
