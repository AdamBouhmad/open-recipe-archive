## Ingredients

- fillet of beef
- butter
- salt
- slices of lard
- onion slices
- carrots
- turnips
- celery
- 1/2 cup beef bouillon
- 1 bay leaf
- parsley
- mushrooms, 1/2 cup
- cream
- sugar coloring (Kitchen Bouquet) - 1 teaspoon (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the fat carefully so the meat does not tear, along with the surface skin and tendons.
2. If the fillet is thin and flat, tie it with string folded in half.
3. Place thin slices of lard in a buttered pan.
4. Arrange a few slices of onion, carrots, turnips and celery on top of the lard.
5. Place the fillet on top, and arrange a few slices of lard over the meat.
6. Put into a hot oven.
7. When browned, sprinkle salt on top and pour 1/2 cup of beef bouillon into the pan.
8. Add 1 bay leaf and some parsley.
9. Cook 10 minutes per pound and baste often with the drippings in the pan.
10. Place the meat on a platter.
11. Prepare gravy from the drippings by carefully skimming off fat.
12. Add 1/2 cup of mushrooms and simmer 5 minutes.
13. If not sufficiently colored, add 1 teaspoon of sugar coloring (Kitchen Bouquet) and some cream.
14. Pour the gravy over the meat.
