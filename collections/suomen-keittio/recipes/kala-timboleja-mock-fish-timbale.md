## Ingredients

- 2 onions
- 3/4 pound butter
- 1/2 pound wheat flour
- 3 cupfuls boiling milk
- 5 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoonful chopped dill
- pepper
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Chop 2 onions and cook in 3/4 pound butter without browning
2. Add 1/2 pound wheat flour and stir in 3 cupfuls boiling milk, whisking well until it pulls away from the sides of the pan
3. Allow to cool
4. Add 5 egg yolks one at a time, along with 1 tablespoonful chopped dill and pepper
5. Finally fold in egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
6. Bake in a fish mold or in timbale molds placed in a pan of water, covered with buttered paper
7. Serve with lemon or hollandaise sauce and boiled rice
