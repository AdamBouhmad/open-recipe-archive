## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Water
- Salt
- Celery
- Onion
- Pepper
- Butter
- Flour
- Egg yolks
- Cream

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the chicken in pieces.
2. Place in cold water and bring quickly to a boil.
3. When chicken has boiled for about five minutes, add salt, celery, onion and pepper for seasoning.
4. Cook until chicken is tender.
5. Make sauce of butter, flour and chicken broth.
6. Add a few egg yolks and some cream.
7. Heat over fire until ready to serve.
