## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 pint of cream or milk
- two pairs of boiled sweetbreads
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Make a cream sauce with butter, flour and cream or milk
2. Cut the boiled sweetbreads into nice pieces
3. Add sweetbreads to the sauce
4. Season with salt and pepper and fine parsley
5. May also be prepared with chicken, using half boiled chicken and half sweetbreads
6. Mushrooms may be used with sweetbreads instead of chicken
