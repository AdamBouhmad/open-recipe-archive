## Ingredients

- 4 cupfuls of water
- 2 1/2 cupfuls of sugar
- juice and rind of 5 oranges
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Grate yellow rind of 2 oranges and of 1 lemon
2. Cook in water, mixed with sugar, 15 minutes
3. Let cool, mix with juice of fruits and freeze
