## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup rice
- water
- milk
- cabbage
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- sugar
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil 1/2 cup of rice in water and milk to a batter.
2. Parboil the cabbage in salt water, drain, chop, and cook with butter in a saucepan until tender.
3. Mix the cabbage with rice and season with salt and pepper, sweeten with some sugar.
4. Add 2 eggs beaten at the last.
5. Turn into a buttered mold and bake about an hour.
