## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls of finely chopped lobster
- 4 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of crab butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 2 eggs
- a little anchovy paste
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Boil one egg until hard
2. Chop or pound the lobster, hard-boiled egg yolk and anchovy paste fine
3. Add seasonings and crab butter (melted butter may be used instead)
4. Mix in raw egg yolk and cream
5. Mix thoroughly
6. Beat the egg whites to a froth and stir into the mixture
7. Form into small balls
8. Dip in flour
9. Cook in salted water
