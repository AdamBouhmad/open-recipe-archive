## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- ½ cup milk or water
- ½ cup flour
- Salt
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- Lard for frying
- Jam

## Directions

1. Bring to boiling the milk with the butter
2. Add the flour beating well
3. Cook until it loosens from the edges of the saucepan
4. Remove from the fire
5. Add 2 eggs, one at a time, 2 tablespoons sugar and beat well
6. Drop by the spoonful into boiling lard
7. When browned and risen, remove from the lard
8. Make a little cavity in the center
9. Fill with jam
