## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup molasses
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 1/4 cup each raisins, currants, walnuts and almonds cut in pieces
- 1/4 cup yellow rind of lemon cut in pieces
- Cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg, 1/2 teaspoon each
- 1/4 teaspoon soda
- 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar until creamy
2. Add molasses and rub again
3. Add egg yolks beaten well
4. Add milk, spices, fruits rolled in flour, soda and cream of tartar mixed with flour
5. Fold in egg whites beaten
