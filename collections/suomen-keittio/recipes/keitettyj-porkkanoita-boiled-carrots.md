## Ingredients

- Small carrots of equal size or larger carrots
- Salt
- Water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- Chopped parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel equal-sized carrots, cut the bigger carrots in pieces.
2. Place on the fire in boiling water with some salt, cook slowly until tender.
3. Drain.
4. Add a tablespoon of butter to the carrots, shake over the fire until the butter is mixed with the carrots.
5. Sprinkle with parsley, if desired.
