## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups bread crumbs
- 1/2 cup flour
- 3/4 cup suet or 1/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup currants
- 1/2 cup raisins
- Yellow rind of 2 and juice of 4 lemons
- 1 tablespoon chopped almonds
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 2 leaves mace chopped
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 cup sugar or syrup
- 1 cup milk or brandy
- 8 eggs or 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Prepare as directed for fruit pudding
