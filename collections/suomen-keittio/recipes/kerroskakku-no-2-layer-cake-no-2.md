## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1/2 cup cornstarch
- 5 eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Rub the butter with the sugar until creamy
2. Add the egg yolks beaten to a froth
3. Mix in cornstarch and baking powder sifted with flour and vanilla
4. Last of all fold in the firmly beaten egg whites
5. Bake in two layer cake tins
