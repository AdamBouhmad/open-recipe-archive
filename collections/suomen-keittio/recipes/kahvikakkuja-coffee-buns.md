## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 whole egg and 1 yolk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1½ teaspoon lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Dash of mace
- 1 yeast cake
- Flour
- Sugar
- Chopped almonds
- Vanilla

## Directions

1. Warm the milk, dissolve the yeast in it, add 1 cup flour; cover, let rise.
2. Work in the eggs beaten with sugar, the butter rubbed until creamy, salt, lemon juice, mace and flour enough to make a moderately thick dough.
3. Knead well, cover, let rise.
4. Roll the dough in a 1/4 inch thick sheet, brush with melted butter, sprinkle with chopped almonds and granulated sugar.
5. Triple up and cut with a round cutter in cakes.
6. Place the cakes to rise on a buttered baking sheet.
7. Brush over with egg and drop with a spoon in the center of each bun the following mixture: 1 egg yolk, 1 tablespoon sugar, 2 tablespoons chopped almonds and some vanilla beaten together.
8. Sprinkle with granulated sugar.
9. Bake in a moderate oven.
