## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salted water
- French dressing
- vinaigrette dressing
- mayonnaise dressing

## Directions

1. Cook the artichoke in salted water until the leaves are easy to part.
2. Let cool.
3. Serve with French, vinaigrette or mayonnaise dressing.
