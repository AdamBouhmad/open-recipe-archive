## Ingredients

- 1 pound spinach
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 cups milk
- 1 1/2 cups bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 eggs
- Some nutmeg

## Directions

1. Clean and boil the spinach and chop.
2. Add the bread crumbs soaked in milk, sugar, salt and nutmeg.
3. Add last the eggs beaten well.
4. Put into a buttered mold.
5. Bake in oven in a pan of water for 1 hour.
6. Serve with meat or fish.
