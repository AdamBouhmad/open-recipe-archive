## Ingredients

- 1 square vanilla chocolate, grated
- 5 egg whites
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare as directed for prune souffle. Instead of prunes use one square of vanilla chocolate grated.
