## Ingredients

- 1 cup boiled rice
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs, add the milk and pour the mixture in the rice.
2. Mix all the dry ingredients with the flour.
3. Last of all fold in the egg whites beaten until firm.
4. Fry in a griddle pan.
