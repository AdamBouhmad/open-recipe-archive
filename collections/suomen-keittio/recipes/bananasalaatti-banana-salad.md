## Ingredients

- bananas
- lemon juice
- grapes
- mayonnaise
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Peel the bananas
2. cut in cubes
3. moisten with lemon juice to keep their color
4. May be mixed with grapes
5. Serve in mayonnaise mixed with plenty of whipped cream
