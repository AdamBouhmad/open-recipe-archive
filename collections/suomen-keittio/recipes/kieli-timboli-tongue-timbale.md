## Ingredients

- 1 cup ground tongue
- 1/2 cup whipped cream
- 3 tablespoons dissolved gelatine or 4 leaves of gelatine
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- liquid jelly
- red pepper
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Dissolve the gelatine in scanty water.
2. Add the lemon juice and pour gradually into the meat.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Add the whipped cream at the last.
5. Moisten the timbales with liquid jelly.
6. Garnish with red pepper and vegetables and fill with the mousse.
7. Put aside to set.
8. Serve with salad.
