## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 3/4 cup lard
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup cold water
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- salt
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Work with fingers thoroughly together the flour, salt, baking powder, butter and lard.
2. Add gradually the water. Knead to an even paste. Put in a cold place for a while.
3. Roll the paste in 1/2 inch thick sheet a little larger than the pie plate.
4. Place in the slightly buttered and with flour dusted plate, moisten the edges with water or egg white.
5. Fill, cover with the upper crust pressing the edges together.
6. Trim the edges even and fold down the edges of the top crust.
7. Perforate with a fork so as to let the steam escape.
8. Spread with egg, tie a strip of wet cloth around the edge.
9. Bake for 1 hour in a hot oven reducing the heat after baking a while.
