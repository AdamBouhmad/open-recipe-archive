## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups flour
- 1/2 cup butter or lard
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 cup cold water
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- sugar and salt

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with butter, sugar, salt and baking powder. Work thoroughly with fingers.
2. Dilute the egg with the water, add to the flour stirring well.
3. Keep in a cold place.
