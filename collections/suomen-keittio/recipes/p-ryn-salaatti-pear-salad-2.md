## Ingredients

- 2 pears
- lemon juice
- equal parts cucumber
- equal parts melon
- French dressing
- lettuce leaves
- string beans or asparagus tips

## Directions

1. Cut in pieces 2 pears
2. moisten with lemon juice
3. add equal parts of cucumber and melon
4. Mix with French dressing
5. Serve on lettuce leaves garnished with string beans or asparagus tips
