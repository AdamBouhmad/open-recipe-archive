## Ingredients

- 3 quarts bouillon
- 1 small head of cabbage
- 1 carrot
- 1 onion
- 1 stalk of celery
- salt and pepper
- meat balls

## Directions

1. Parboil the cabbage in salted water, drain well.
2. Cut the cabbage in fine strips and fry in butter constantly stirring.
3. Add the hot meat stock and continue cooking until the cabbage is tender.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Serve with small meat balls or plain.
6. The soup may be made without browning the cabbage and using water boiled with lean meat instead of bouillon.
