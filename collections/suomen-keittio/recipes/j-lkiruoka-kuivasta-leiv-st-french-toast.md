## Ingredients

- Stale bread
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 cup milk
- Butter
- Jam or jelly

## Directions

1. Cut stale bread in slices.
2. Beat an egg with 1 tablespoon of sugar, add 1 cup milk.
3. Dip the bread into the milk, fry in butter until brown on both sides.
4. Spread with jam or jelly.
