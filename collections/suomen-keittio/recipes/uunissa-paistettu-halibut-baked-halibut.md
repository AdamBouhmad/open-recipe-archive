## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 pound halibut
- salt
- pepper
- bacon slices
- onion slices
- butter
- flour
- bread crumbs
- lemon juice
- water or cream

## Directions

1. Take a 2 or 3 pound halibut, clean and wipe
2. Rub with salt and pepper
3. Lay in a pan lined with slices of bacon and place a few slices of onion around the fish
4. Cover with batter made of equal parts of butter and flour
5. Sprinkle with bread crumbs, and line with slices of bacon or dot with butter
6. Bake basting often
7. If the surface is becoming too brown, cover with paper
8. Make a sauce of drippings by adding some flour, lemon juice, water or cream
