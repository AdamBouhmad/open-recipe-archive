## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1/4 cupful of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 4-5 cupfuls of flour
- 1/2 cake of yeast

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast in 1 cupful of lukewarm milk
2. Mix with 4 tablespoonfuls of flour and let rise
3. Scald the rest of the milk
4. Mix with butter, sugar, salt and flour enough to make a soft paste
5. Work the risen sponge into it
6. Add flour until it becomes a moderately thick dough
7. Set to rise
8. Roll on the baking board into a sheet 1/2 inch thick
9. Cut with biscuit cutter into round cakes
10. Moisten one side of the surface with melted butter and double the other side over it
11. Put to rise and bake in a hot oven 15 minutes
