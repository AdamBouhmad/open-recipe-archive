## Ingredients

- Breast meats and legs of chicken
- Butter
- Bread for triangular pieces of toast
- Small potatoes
- Green pepper
- Tomato
- Cream

## Directions

1. Fry breast meats and legs in butter covered, taking care so as not to brown the meat.
2. Lay on a platter.
3. Garnish around with triangular pieces of toast and small potatoes.
4. Make a gravy of the drippings by adding green pepper cut in strips and tomato cooked with a lump of butter.
5. Last of all add some cream to the gravy.
