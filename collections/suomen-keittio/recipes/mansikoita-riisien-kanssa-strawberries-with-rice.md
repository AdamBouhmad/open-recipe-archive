## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound rice
- 1 lemon juice
- Sugar
- Water
- Rind of 1 lemon
- Rind of 1 orange
- Small strawberries
- Whipped cream

## Directions

1. Cook 1/4 pound rice until tender in deep water mixed with the juice of 1 lemon.
2. Turn into a dish, pour syrup made of sugar and water over, add the rinds of a lemon and an orange.
3. When cool lay alternately rice and small strawberries into a mold.
4. Pour thick syrup over, let harden.
5. Garnish with whipped cream.
