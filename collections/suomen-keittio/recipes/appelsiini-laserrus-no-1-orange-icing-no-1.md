## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup water
- 1 or 2 egg whites
- juice of 1 orange
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- grated yellow rind of 1 orange

## Directions

1. Boil 1 cup sugar in ½ cup water until it threads when dropped from a spoon
2. Beat 1 or 2 egg whites to a firm froth
3. Add the syrup gradually to the eggs, stirring well
4. Dilute with the juice of an orange and 1 teaspoon of lemon juice
5. Mix in the grated yellow rind of an orange
6. Stir until thick and holds its shape
