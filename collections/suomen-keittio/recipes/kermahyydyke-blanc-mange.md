## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups cream
- 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/4 package gelatine, dissolved

## Directions

1. Whip the cream.
2. Add the sugar, vanilla and the dissolved gelatine.
3. Pour into a serving dish to set.
4. Garnish with macaroons and fruit jelly.
