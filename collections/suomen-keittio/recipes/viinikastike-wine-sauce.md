## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 1/2 cup sherry
- 1 cup white wine or madeira
- Yellow rind of 1 lemon
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 or 2 tablespoons arrowroot

## Directions

1. Sift together the sugar and the arrowroot
2. Add slowly boiling water, stirring well
3. Cook until clear
4. Add the wines and the yellow rind of a lemon
5. May be thickened with egg yolks instead of arrowroot
