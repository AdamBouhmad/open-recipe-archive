## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 or 2 eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Scald the milk
2. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar
3. Pour gradually the milk into the egg yolks
4. Beat over the fire until thick
5. Season with salt, flavor with vanilla
6. Last of all fold in the beaten egg whites
