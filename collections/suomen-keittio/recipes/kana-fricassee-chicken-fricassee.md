## Ingredients

- chicken
- cold water
- salt
- celery
- onion
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- chicken bouillon
- egg yolks
- cream
- rice
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and joint the chicken. Place on the fire in cold water.
2. Bring quickly to boiling. When boiled about 5 minutes, add salt, some celery, onion and pepper.
3. Cook until tender.
4. Make a gravy of butter, flour and bouillon, add 2 yolks of egg and some cream.
5. Heat up shaking over the fire.
6. Arrange on a platter upon a foundation of molded rice or place the chicken in a ring of rice.
7. Rice timbales may be placed around the chicken.
8. Flavor with a dash of lemon juice, according to taste.
