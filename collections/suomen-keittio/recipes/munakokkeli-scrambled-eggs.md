## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of milk or water
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Whip the eggs and add milk, salt and pepper
2. Melt the butter in a frying pan but do not let it brown
3. Pour the egg mixture into the butter and stir gently over a slow fire
4. When the mixture begins to thicken, remove carefully with a spoon
5. Use water instead of milk for a lighter yellow color
