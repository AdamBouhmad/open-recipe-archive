## Ingredients

- 1 pound almonds
- 1 pound sugar
- ½ teaspoon vanilla
- Water

## Directions

1. Select nice whole almonds and rub them between a cloth.
2. Moisten with syrup mixed with ½ teaspoon vanilla.
3. Cook the almonds stirring constantly until they begin to crack.
4. Remove from the fire and continue to stir until all the moisture has evaporated.
5. Place the pan again on a slow fire and add a few drops of water.
6. Stir until the almonds become coated with the syrup.
7. Remove from the fire and cover for 20 minutes with a clean cloth.
