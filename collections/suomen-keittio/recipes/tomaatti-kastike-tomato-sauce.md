## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 slice of onion
- 1 piece of celery
- 1 cupful of beef stock
- 1 cupful of canned or 5 - 6 fresh tomatoes
- parsley
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Put butter in a saucepan and cook onion and celery in it
2. Add flour and cook a few minutes
3. Stir in tomato, beef stock and seasonings
4. Let simmer 1/2 hour, or until tomato is soft
5. Strain and season with salt and pepper if wanted
