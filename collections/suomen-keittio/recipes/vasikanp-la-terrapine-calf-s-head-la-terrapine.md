## Ingredients

- boiled calf's head
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup white meat broth
- salt
- pepper
- 2 cups cream
- 2 egg yolks
- lemon juice or Madeira

## Directions

1. Cut the boiled calf's head into cubes
2. Make gravy of 2 tablespoons butter and 2 tablespoons flour mixed together
3. Add 1 cup of white meat broth and season with salt and pepper
4. Add 2 cups of cream
5. Add 2 beaten egg yolks
6. Add lemon juice or Madeira at the end
