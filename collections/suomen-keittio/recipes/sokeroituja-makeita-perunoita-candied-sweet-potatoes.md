## Ingredients

- potatoes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- lemon juice or vinegar (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil potatoes, peel, cut in halves lengthwise.
2. Heat 2 tablespoons of butter in a frying pan.
3. Brown the potatoes sprinkled with 2 tablespoons of sugar.
4. Sprinkle with lemon juice or vinegar if preferred.
