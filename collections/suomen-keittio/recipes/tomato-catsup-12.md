## Ingredients

- 1 peck unskinned tomatoes
- few white onions
- 2 bay leaves
- mace
- pepper
- celery-salt
- cloves
- 2 teaspoonfuls of each spice
- sugar to taste
- salt to taste
- 2 cupfuls vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut 1 peck of unskinned tomatoes and few white onions in pieces
2. Cook together until tender
3. Force through a sieve
4. Place on fire and mix with two bay leaves, mace, pepper, celery-salt, cloves, 2 teaspoonfuls of each, boiled in a bag
5. Add sugar and salt to taste
6. Cook for 4-5 hours, stirring now and then
7. Remove the bag of spices
8. Pour 2 cupfuls of vinegar into the mixture and let boil up
9. When cooled, bottle, cork and keep in a cool place
