## Ingredients

- 3 cups water per 1.5 cups berries
- raspberries
- 1 pound sugar per pound of raspberry juice

## Directions

1. Crush raspberries and put them in cold water
2. Stir while heating and then boil for a few minutes
3. Strain the juice, weigh it
4. Allow 1 pound sugar per pound of raspberry juice
5. Put sugar and juice in a kettle and bring to boil quickly for 10-15 minutes
6. Allow juice to cool
