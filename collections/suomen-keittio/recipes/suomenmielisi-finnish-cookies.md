## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 10 ozs. of flour

## Directions

1. Whip eggs and sugar to a froth
2. Add creamed butter and flour
3. Form the batter into cookies of various shapes
4. Bake in oven to a light brown
