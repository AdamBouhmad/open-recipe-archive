## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- salt
- lemon juice
- 1 pint clams
- pepper
- hot lard
- lemon
- parsley

## Directions

1. Make a batter of 2 eggs, 1/2 cup of milk, 1 1/2 cups of flour, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, salt and lemon juice
2. Chop the 1 pint of clams fine
3. Season the clams with salt and pepper
4. Add the chopped clams to the batter
5. Drop the batter by spoonfuls into boiling lard, cooking 3 or 4 at a time
6. When brown, drain on gray paper
7. Serve on a folded napkin garnished with lemon and parsley
