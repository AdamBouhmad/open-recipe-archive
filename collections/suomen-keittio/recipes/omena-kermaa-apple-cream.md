## Ingredients

- 1 cup cream
- 1 cup apple sauce
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1/2 yellow rind of a lemon, grated
- 1/2 package gelatine

## Directions

1. Add to the apple sauce the lemon rind and the gelatine dissolved in water.
2. Last of all mix with the whipped cream.
3. Pour into a wet mold to set.
4. Unmold onto a serving dish.
5. Garnish with fruit and macaroons.
