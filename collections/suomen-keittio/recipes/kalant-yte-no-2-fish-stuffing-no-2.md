## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup rice
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 8 plums

## Directions

1. Boil the rice in salt water.
2. Drain.
3. Mix with melted butter, chopped eggs, plums and salt.
4. Use for stuffing. Leave out the plums if desired.
