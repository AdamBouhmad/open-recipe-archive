## Ingredients

- 1 goose
- 1 lemon
- 2 tablespoons salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- ½ pound prunes
- 2 quarts apples
- butter or bacon
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Clean the goose, rub with lemon inside and out.
2. Rub inside with salt.
3. Rinse the fruit and stuff the goose with the fruit.
4. Truss.
5. Spread with butter or line with bacon.
6. Roast in a hot oven basting often.
7. Add some water to the drippings, if necessary.
8. When brown sprinkle with salt and cover with buttered paper.
9. Allow 2 or 3 hours for roasting a goose.
10. Slice onto a platter, arrange the apples and prunes around.
11. Strain the drippings, skim off the fat and add some flour and cream.
