## Ingredients

- cherries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash cherries and remove the stones.
2. Weigh the berries.
3. Have an equal amount of sugar.
4. Lay in a kettle and let stand over night.
5. Bring slowly to boiling point.
6. Simmer until clear and thick.
7. Put into jars while hot.
