## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- ½ yeast cake
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup cold oatmeal porridge
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Warm the milk, add salt, sugar, butter melted and yeast dissolved in milk, oatmeal and flour.
2. Cover and let rise over night.
3. In the morning butter muffin tins, fill to ¾ of their height, let rise until full.
4. Bake in a moderate oven for 30 minutes.
