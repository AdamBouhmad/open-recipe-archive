## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 lemon
- 1 tablespoonful of bread flour

## Directions

1. Whip the yolks and sugar to froth.
2. Add the yellow rind and juice of lemon and bread flour.
3. Whip whites to froth and mix in lastly.
4. Pour the mixture in a well buttered souffle mold.
5. Bake in oven in a water bath nearly 20—30 min.
6. Serve hot in its own dish.
