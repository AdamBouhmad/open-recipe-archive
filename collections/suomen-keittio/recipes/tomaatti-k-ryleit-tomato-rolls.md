## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls mashed potatoes
- 1 cupful ground tomatoes
- 1 cupful bread crumbs
- 3 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls butter
- salt, pepper, and onion juice

## Directions

1. Grind tomatoes
2. Add potatoes, bread crumbs, seasonings, and eggs
3. Form into rolls
4. Dip in egg and bread crumbs
5. Fry in butter or cook in deep fat
6. Serve with milk or egg sauce
