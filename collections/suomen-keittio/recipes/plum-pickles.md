## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. of plums
- 1 1/2 lbs. of sugar
- 1 pint of vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful of cloves
- a little cinnamon

## Directions

1. Wash plums and drain.
2. Prick skins in several places with a silver fork or a pin.
3. Cook sugar with vinegar and spices for a few minutes.
4. Pour the liquor on the plums into jars.
5. Cover tightly and let stand for two days.
6. Then boil up the liquor, place the plums in it and let simmer for a little while.
7. Place the plums into the jar and cook the liquor until it gets thick.
8. Pour the liquor on the plums, seal and keep in a cool place.
