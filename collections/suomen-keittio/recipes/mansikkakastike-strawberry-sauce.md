## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- 2 cups strawberries pressed through a sieve
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook for 10 minutes 1 cup sugar in 1/2 cup water
2. When cool, add 2 cups of strawberries pressed through a sieve
3. Flavor with vanilla
4. Whipped cream may be added, if desired
