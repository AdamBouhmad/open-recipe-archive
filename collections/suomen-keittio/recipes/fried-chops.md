## Ingredients

- chops
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- meat stock
- tomato

## Directions

1. Trim as for frying.
2. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
3. Fry in butter until brown on both sides.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
5. Put the pan in oven and bake covered for a few minutes.
6. Prepare sauce of the drippings in pan by adding a little flour, meat stock, pepper, salt and tomato.
7. Strain and serve with chops.
