## Ingredients

- apples
- currants
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare apples and cut in pieces.
2. Put over the fire in cold water, having water just enough to cover the apples.
3. When tender, add currants, allowing 1 part of currants to 2 parts of apples.
4. Cook until tender and strain.
5. Prepare the jelly as directed.
