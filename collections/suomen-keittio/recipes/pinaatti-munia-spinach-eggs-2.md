## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- 1/2 cupful cream or spinach broth
- cooked chopped spinach
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce with butter, flour and cream or spinach broth
2. Mix in cooked chopped spinach
3. Cut hard-boiled eggs in half and chop the yolks finely
4. Mix the yolks with the spinach mixture and bring to a boil
5. Fill the egg white halves with the spinach mixture
6. Serve as a vegetable side dish with fish dishes
