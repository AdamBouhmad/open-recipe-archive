## Ingredients

- 3 green peppers
- 4 tomatoes
- 4 apples
- lard
- French dressing
- mayonnaise dressing
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Cook green peppers quickly in lard to remove the bitter skin.
2. Skin and cut in very thin, 1 1/2 inch long slices.
3. Mix all together and pour French dressing on.
4. Arrange in a salad bowl.
5. Garnish with lettuce leaves and put mayonnaise dressing with spoon on the leaves.
