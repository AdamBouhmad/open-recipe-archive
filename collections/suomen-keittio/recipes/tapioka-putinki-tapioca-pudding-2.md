## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of tapioca grain
- 5 cupfuls of milk
- 3 ruokalus. of sugar
- 2 ruokalus. of butter
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 teelus. of salt

## Directions

1. Cook milk and grain to a porridge.
2. Cream butter, whip egg yolks with sugar and mix with the porridge.
