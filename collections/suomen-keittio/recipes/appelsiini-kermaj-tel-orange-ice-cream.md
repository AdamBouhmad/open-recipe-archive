## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup orange juice
- yellow rind of 2 oranges
- rind of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare as directed for vanilla ice cream.
2. Before freezing add 3/4 cup of orange juice, the yellow rind of 2 oranges and the same of lemon.
