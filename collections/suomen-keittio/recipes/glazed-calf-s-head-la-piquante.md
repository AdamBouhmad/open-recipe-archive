## Ingredients

- Cooked calf's head
- Milk sauce
- Toast
- Glazed tomatoes
- Butter
- Flour
- Brown sauce
- Tomato
- Parsley
- Cucumber

## Directions

1. Cut cooked calf's head into pieces
2. Pour a milk sauce over them
3. Place on toast
4. Line with glazed tomatoes
5. Serve with Piquante sauce poured around the meat onto a serving dish
6. Piquante sauce is made of 1 tablespoonful of butter, 1 tablespoonful of flour, 1 cupful of brown sauce, 1 cupful of tomato, chopped parsley, fresh chopped cucumber
