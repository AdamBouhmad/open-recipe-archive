## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup cream
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 cups potato flour
- 4 cups white flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- eggs

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and sugar to a froth
2. Add cream, soda and potato flour
3. Add butter rubbed until creamy and flour mixed with soda
4. Mix all ingredients well together
5. Roll dough thin
6. Perforate with a fork
7. Cut into small round cookies
8. Bake on a buttered baking sheet in an oven until light brown
