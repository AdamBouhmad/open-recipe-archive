## Ingredients

- 2 cups crushed berries
- grated apple, peach or pineapple
- some lemon juice or rind
- 1½ cups sugar
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Take 2 cups crushed berries, grated apple, peach or pineapple, some lemon juice or rind, 1½ cups sugar and 3 egg whites.
2. Beat together all the ingredients until firm.
