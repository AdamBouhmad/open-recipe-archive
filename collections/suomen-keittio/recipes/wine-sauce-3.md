## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of water
- 1/2 cupful of sherry
- 1/2 cupful of white wine or madeira
- 1 lemon's yellow rind
- 1-2 tablespoonfuls of arrowroot flour
- 1/2 cupful of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and arrowroot.
2. Pour boiling water slowly into it, stirring well.
3. Boil until the mixture is clear.
4. Add wines and lemon yellow rind.
5. The sauce can also be thickened with a few egg yolks.
