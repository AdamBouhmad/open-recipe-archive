## Ingredients

- 8 oranges
- 8 pounds granulated sugar
- 2 ounces lemon acid powder
- 2.5 quarts cold water

## Directions

1. Grate the yellow rind of the oranges
2. Peel the oranges and crush them in the sugar in a large bowl
3. Add the grated yellow rind
4. Mix in water that has been boiled and cooled
5. Let stand in a cold place for 2 or 3 days, stirring now and then until the sugar is dissolved
6. Add the lemon acid, stirring constantly
7. Strain through a fine cloth and bottle up
8. Seal
9. Dilute with water when served according to taste
