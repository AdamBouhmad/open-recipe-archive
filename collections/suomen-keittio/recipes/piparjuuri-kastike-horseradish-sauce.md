## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons grated horseradish
- ½ tablespoon lemon juice
- 4 tablespoons whipping cream
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Mix horseradish with lemon juice, salt and pepper.
2. Add whipped cream last.
3. Serve as desired or mix with Hollandaise sauce.
