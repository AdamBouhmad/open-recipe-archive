## Ingredients

- Tongue
- Salt
- Water
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- Sliced onion
- Flour
- Carrots
- Celery
- Chopped parsley
- 3 cupfuls tongue liquor
- Chopped mushrooms
- Tomato
- Pepper
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape the tongue carefully.
2. Put in hot salted water and let boil about one hour.
3. Remove skin and roots.
4. Cook 3 tablespoonfuls of butter with sliced onion in a frying pan.
5. Place the tongue in it and fry on both sides.
6. Sprinkle with flour and lay around it carrots and celery cut in pieces and chopped parsley.
7. Pour over it 3 cupfuls of liquor of tongue.
8. Cover tightly and bake 2-3 hours.
9. Lift the tongue on a platter and prepare sauce of liquor by adding flour, stock and 1/2 cupful of tomato.
10. Strain and mix with chopped mushrooms.
11. Return to the fire and cook 5 minutes.
12. Season with salt, pepper and lemon.
13. Place the tongue on a serving dish and pour the sauce over.
