## Ingredients

- 1/4 cupful of thin cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of vanilla
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of cream
- 6 leaves or 4 tablespoonfuls of gelatine
- a few macaroons
- strawberries cut in pieces
- big strawberries of equal size
- red-colored lemon juice

## Directions

1. Split lengthwise big strawberries of equal size
2. Dip in red-colored lemon juice and cover the bottom and the sides of the mold with the strawberries, laying them with the flat side down
3. Dissolve gelatine with sugar in thin cream, add vanilla and let cool
4. Put whipped cream, vanilla and macaroons into it
5. Pour in a wet mold and set to stiffen
6. Instead of macaroons boiled rice may be used
7. Serve unmolded
