## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of water
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 cake of yeast
- 6-7 cupfuls of flour
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm water
2. Add enough flour to make it like batter and let it rise
3. Heat the remaining water, pour it in a bowl
4. Mix in salt and butter, and add flour until the batter is like thin dough
5. Add the yeast mixture and gradually stir in the flour evenly to the dough and work until the dough is smooth and pliable
6. Cover the dough with a cloth and set in a warm place to rise
7. When it has risen well, knead thoroughly
