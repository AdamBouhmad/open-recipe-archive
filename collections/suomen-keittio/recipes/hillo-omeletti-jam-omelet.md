## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- sugar
- 3 tablespoons milk or cream
- jam
- fruit sauce

## Directions

1. Beat separately whites and yolks of 3 eggs.
2. Mix the yolks with some sugar, 3 tablespoons of milk or cream.
3. Add the whites beaten stiff.
4. Bake as usually.
5. Cover with any kind of jam.
6. Fold once.
7. Serve hot with fruit sauce.
