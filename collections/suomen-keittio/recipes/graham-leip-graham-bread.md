## Ingredients

- 1 pint warm milk or water
- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- 4 tablespoons molasses
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
- 3/4 or 1 yeast cake
- 3 1/2 cups graham flour
- 1 cup white flour

## Directions

1. Prepare and bake as given for water bread.
