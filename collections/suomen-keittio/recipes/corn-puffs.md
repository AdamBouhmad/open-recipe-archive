## Ingredients

- 1/2 can of corn
- 4 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder

## Directions

1. Force the corn through a sieve
2. Add salt, flour, baking powder, yolks and finally whites
3. Drop the mixture with spoon into boiling lard and cook until brown
4. When green corn is used, grate the corns fine; then prepare the puffs as of canned corn
