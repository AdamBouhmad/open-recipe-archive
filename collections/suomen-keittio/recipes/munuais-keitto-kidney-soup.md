## Ingredients

- 3 kidneys of veal or 2 kidneys of ox
- 1 quart of water
- 1 quart of meat stock
- 1 onion
- 1 stalk of celery
- 1 carrot
- 1 parsnip
- 1 sprig of parsley
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- salt and pepper
- 1/2 cupful of tomato liquor
- sherry or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the fat from the kidneys and cut them into small cubes.
2. Cut the vegetables in the same way and brown all in butter.
3. Mix with the flour and pour the boiling stock slowly on it.
4. Let boil covered for 2 hours.
5. At last add the tomato liquor, seasonings and wine or lemon juice into the soup.
6. Color with Kitchen Bouquet if necessary.
