## Ingredients

- 4 quarts of bouillon
- 1 lb. of carrots
- 1 turnip
- 1 onion
- 1 root or 4 stalks of celery
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 yolks of eggs
- 1 cupful of cream or milk
- salt and pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean, scrape and cut the vegetables into pieces.
2. Put them into the kettle with butter, a little salt and sugar.
3. Let simmer in butter, stirring constantly, until lightly browned.
4. Add the stock and let it boil slowly until the vegetables are tender.
5. Pass through a sieve.
6. Thicken with butter and flour.
7. At the last add the yolks of eggs and cream into it, whipping well.
