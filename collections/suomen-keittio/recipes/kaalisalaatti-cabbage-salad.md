## Ingredients

- 1 small head of cabbage
- French dressing
- celery
- Spanish onion
- peanuts
- boiled salad dressing
- olives
- apples

## Directions

1. Shred or cut the cabbage into cubes
2. Serve mixed with French dressing
3. Alternatively, take equal parts of cabbage and celery, add a Spanish onion chopped and some peanuts
4. Mix with French dressing or boiled salad dressing
5. Sprinkle the top with chopped olives
6. Instead of celery, apples may be used, omitting the onions
