## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup butter
- 1/2 cup milk or water
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 8 egg whites
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar together until creamy.
2. Add water without stirring.
3. Add spices and the stiffly beaten egg whites.
4. Sift flour with baking powder several times.
5. Add to the mixture and beat until smooth.
6. Bake in an unbuffered tin in a slow oven, increasing the heat after a while.
7. Let cool in the tin, upside down.
