## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 or 3/4 cup flour
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks to a froth, add milk, salt, butter and flour
2. Last of all mix with the stiffly beaten egg whites
3. Fry thin pancakes in plenty of butter
4. Spread with jelly or jam, roll up
5. Serve immediately
