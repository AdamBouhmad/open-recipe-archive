## Ingredients

- 3 cups fish
- 3 tablespoons butter
- ½ cup liquid of scallops
- 3 tablespoons cream
- Salt and pepper
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Bring the fish to the boiling point, strain, grind.
2. Rub the butter until creamy, add the fish, liquid, salt and pepper.
3. Put into a buttered mold, sprinkle with bread crumbs and bake.
4. If used whole, place the scallops in a buttered mold; make Béchamel sauce using liquid of scallops for it.
5. Pour over the fish, sprinkle with bread crumbs and dot with butter.
