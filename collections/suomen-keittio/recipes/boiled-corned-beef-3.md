## Ingredients

- Corned beef
- Seasonings and vegetables to taste
- Cabbage or other vegetables (optional)

## Directions

1. Put the corned beef in deep cold water.
2. Place on the fire and let slowly come to boiling point.
3. Simmer steadily, allowing half an hour to a pound.
4. Use seasonings and vegetables to taste as required.
5. Cabbage and any other kind of vegetable may be served with corned beef.
