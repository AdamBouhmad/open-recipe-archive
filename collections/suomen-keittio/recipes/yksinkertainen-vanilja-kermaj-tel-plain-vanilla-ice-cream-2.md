## Ingredients

- 1 quart of cream
- 3/4 cupful of sugar
- 1 vanilla bean or 1 teaspoonful of vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Scald cream and sugar on the fire.
2. Add flavoring when cool and freeze.
3. If rich cream is used, dilute it with a little milk.
