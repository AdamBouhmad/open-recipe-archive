## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. of apples
- 2.5 lbs. of sugar
- 3 cupfuls of water

## Directions

1. Rinse apples, cut in pieces and put to boil with water until soft.
2. Press through a sieve and put to boil again with sugar, stirring constantly, until marmalade is firm.
3. Pour on plates greased with cooking oil or butter, which when cooled are cut into pieces, rolled in crystal sugar and dried.
