## Ingredients

- currant jelly
- slices of lemon and orange
- mustard
- orange juice
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Dissolve currant jelly.
2. Add cooked slices of lemon and orange.
3. Mix with a dash of mustard and orange juice.
4. Season with cayenne.
