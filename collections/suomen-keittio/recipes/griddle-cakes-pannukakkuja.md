## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of milk
- 1-2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of baking powder
- 2 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Sift flour with salt and baking powder.
2. Beat eggs with sugar, add milk into it.
3. Pour it slowly into flour.
4. Mix with melted butter last.
5. Drop by spoonful into hot buttered pan and bake until brown on both sides.
6. Serve with butter and maple syrup.
