## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 slice onion
- 1/2 cup sifted bread crumbs
- Salt, pepper and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook milk with butter, slice of onion and sifted bread crumbs.
2. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg.
3. Remove the onion, bake the mixture in oven in a pan of water for 30 minutes.
4. Fry bread crumbs in butter, sprinkle on top of the sauce.
5. Serve with boiled chicken having the sauce around the chicken on the platter.
