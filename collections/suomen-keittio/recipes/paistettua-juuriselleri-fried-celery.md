## Ingredients

- Celery roots
- Salted water
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Butter or lard
- Melted butter
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cook roots of celery in salted water until tender
2. When cool, cut in thin slices
3. Dip in egg and bread crumbs
4. Fry in butter or cook in deep lard
5. Pour some melted butter over, sprinkle with parsley
