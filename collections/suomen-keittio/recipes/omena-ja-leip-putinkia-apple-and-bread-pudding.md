## Ingredients

- 3 cups apples
- 2 cups bread
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Cinnamon
- Rind of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the bread and the apples in small cubes, mix with lemon rind, sugar and cinnamon.
2. Put into a buttered mold, pour 0.5 cup water over.
3. Dot with butter.
4. Bake covered, removing the cover only to brown the surface.
5. Serve with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
