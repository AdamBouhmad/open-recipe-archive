## Ingredients

- Brussels sprouts
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- baking soda
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the sprouts
2. Soak for 1/2 hour in water mixed with vinegar
3. Put on the fire in boiling salt water with a teaspoonful of baking soda
4. Cook until tender
5. Remove immediately from the water
6. Mix with butter, salt and pepper
