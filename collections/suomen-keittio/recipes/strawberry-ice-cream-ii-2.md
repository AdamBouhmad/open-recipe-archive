## Ingredients

- 1 quart of cream
- 1 basket of strawberries
- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla
- juice of ½ lemon
- 4 yolks of eggs
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Put ½ pint of cream or milk on the fire.
2. Beat ½ cupful of sugar with yolks.
3. Pour the boiling milk slowly on and whip the mixture in a double-boiler on the fire until it is thick.
4. When cool, add strawberries, washed and mixed with the rest of the sugar, pressed through a sieve.
5. Add vanilla and lemon juice into the mixture.
6. Then freeze.
