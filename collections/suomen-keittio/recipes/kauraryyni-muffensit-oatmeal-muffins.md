## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of flour
- 1 cupful of cooked oatmeal
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- ¾ cupful of milk
- 2 eggs
- 3 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- ½ teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Mix salt, sugar and baking powder with flour
2. Mix milk with the oatmeal
3. Beat eggs until light
4. Add the oatmeal and milk to the eggs
5. Pour this gradually into the flour mixture
6. Mix the batter well
7. Put in buttered molds
8. Bake in a moderately hot oven
