## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 3 cups sugar
- 1 grated pineapple
- Juice of 2 oranges
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar in the water for 15 minutes.
2. When cool add the pineapple and the juices.
3. Freeze.
