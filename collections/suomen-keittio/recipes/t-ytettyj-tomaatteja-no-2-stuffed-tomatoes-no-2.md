## Ingredients

- 6 tomatoes
- 2 ounces bread
- 1/2 cup rich milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- salt
- pepper
- chopped onion
- chopped parsley
- sugar
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cut a slice off the tops of six tomatoes, scoop out the insides.
2. Soak 2 ozs. of bread in 1/2 cup rich milk.
3. Add tomato, 2 yolks of egg, 2 tablespoons melted butter, salt, pepper, chopped onion, chopped parsley and some sugar.
4. Last of all add 2 whites of egg beaten stiff.
5. Sprinkle the cavities with salt, stuff.
6. Bake covered in a buttered mold for 30 or 40 minutes.
