## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup butter

## Directions

1. Mix together all the dry ingredients in a bowl.
2. Work in the butter with finger tips; add milk.
3. Bake in 2 cake tins in oven for 20 minutes.
4. Wash the strawberries, sweeten with sugar.
5. Have a layer of strawberries between and on top of the cakes.
6. Cover with whipped cream.
