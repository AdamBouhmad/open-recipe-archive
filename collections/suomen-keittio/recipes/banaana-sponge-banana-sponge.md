## Ingredients

- 1 cup mashed banana
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon (1/4 package) gelatine
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 or 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Dissolve the gelatine in cold water.
2. Put into a saucepan with mashed banana, lemon juice and sugar.
3. Heat, let cool.
4. When it begins to set, beat to a froth and add the egg whites beaten stiff.
5. Pour into a mold dipped in water to set.
6. Serve garnished with whipped cream and banana.
