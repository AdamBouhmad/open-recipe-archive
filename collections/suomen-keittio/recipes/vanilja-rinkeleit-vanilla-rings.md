## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 3 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/2 pound almonds

## Directions

1. Rub until frothy butter
2. Beat egg with sugar
3. Combine the mixtures
4. Add minced almonds, vanilla and flour
5. Beat well
6. Force through a pastry bag forming into small rings on a baking sheet
