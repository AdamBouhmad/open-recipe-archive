## Ingredients

- fish
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- onions, sliced and fried
- cream

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean, rinse and wipe.
2. Remove the backbone.
3. Run through a meat chopper several times, mixing it while grinding with lumps of butter.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Form into round flat cakes.
6. Dip in egg and bread crumbs.
7. Fry in butter or lard.
8. Place on a platter.
9. Line with a few sliced fried onions.
10. Add a little cream into the drippings and pour on the fish.
