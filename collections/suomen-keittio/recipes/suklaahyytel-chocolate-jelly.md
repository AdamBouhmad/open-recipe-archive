## Ingredients

- 1 pint of rich milk
- 2 ozs. of chocolate
- 8-9 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 8 leaves of gelatine
- 1 cupful of cream
- 1/2 teaspoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil up milk
2. Mix with vanilla, melted chocolate, gelatine leaves and a part of sugar
3. Stir well until all ingredients have dissolved and the mixture has boiled up
4. Set aside to cool
5. Whip cream and the rest of the sugar to a stiff froth
6. Mix with chocolate mixture just when getting firm
7. Turn in a mold and let get quite firm
8. Serve unmolded, garnished with whipped cream
