## Ingredients

- two chickens
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- egg
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Clean and dissect two chickens.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Dip in bread crumbs, in egg and again in bread crumbs.
4. Fry in butter or bake in oven about half an hour.
5. Pour a cream sauce on a serving dish and arrange the chicken upon it.
6. Place thin slices of fried bacon on the chicken and garnish with corn fritters.
