## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1½ lemon juice and yellow rind
- 4 tablespoons water
- ¾ cup sugar
- pastry for one open crust
- meringue made from 3 egg whites, 6 tablespoons sugar, ½ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks with sugar, add lemon juice, rind and water.
2. Stir on the fire in a double boiler until thick; let cool.
3. Add the beaten whites.
4. Bake on an open crust.
5. When done, coat with meringue, if desired; brown the meringue in oven.
