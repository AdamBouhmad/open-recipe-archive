## Ingredients

- 3 pints of thick cream
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 6-7 leaves of gelatine
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla or a few drops of lemon juice
- 1/2 cupful of water
- lady fingers or puff paste

## Directions

1. Dissolve gelatine in 1/2 cupful of water
2. Whip cream to a froth, add sugar, vanilla and gelatine when the latter has cooled down a little
3. Cover the bottom and the sides of the mold with lady fingers, or roll puff paste into a sheet and cut slices in shape of the mold, laying them on the bottom and sides
4. Fill the mold with cream mixture and set to harden
5. Unmold, garnish with wine jelly and whipped cream
6. The mold may be filled also with fruit creams
