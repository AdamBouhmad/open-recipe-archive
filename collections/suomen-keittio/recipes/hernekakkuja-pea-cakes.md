## Ingredients

- 1 cup boiled mashed peas
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup milk or cream
- 5 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Mix the mashed peas with butter, milk, eggs beaten and flour
2. Add salt and pepper to taste
3. Form into cakes
4. Fry in butter
5. Serve with meat or fish
