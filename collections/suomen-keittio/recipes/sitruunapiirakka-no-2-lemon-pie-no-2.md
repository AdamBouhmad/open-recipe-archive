## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- ¼ cup lemon juice and the yellow rind of 1 lemon grated
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 or 4 tablespoons cornstarch or flour
- pastry for one open crust
- meringue

## Directions

1. Put on the fire the water with lemon juice, rind and butter.
2. Mix the sugar with the flour, pour gradually the boiling water into the flour, cook for a few minutes.
3. Add the yolks, turn the mixture on an open pie crust.
4. Bake until firm.
5. Cover with meringue, continue baking until brown.
