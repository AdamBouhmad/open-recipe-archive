## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 lemon
- 1 tablespoon bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Whip the yolks with the sugar, add the yellow rind and the juice of the lemon and the bread crumbs.
2. Beat the egg whites until firm, add to the yolks.
3. Bake in a well-buttered mold for 20-30 minutes, having the mold in a pan of water.
