## Ingredients

- fried calf's or sheep's kidneys
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- 2 egg yolks
- cream
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Grind or chop fried calf's or sheep's kidneys.
2. Put into a saucepan with 2 tablespoons butter, salt, pepper and lemon juice.
3. Let boil for a few minutes.
4. Remove from fire, add 2 yolks of eggs and a little cream.
5. Reheat.
6. Serve hot on toast.
