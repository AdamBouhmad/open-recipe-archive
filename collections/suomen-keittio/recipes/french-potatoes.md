## Ingredients

- potatoes
- lard
- salt

## Directions

1. Pare potatoes, wash and wipe dry
2. Cut in pieces of uniform size to any shape you want
3. Cook in lard until lightly browned
4. Sprinkle with salt
5. Serve on a folded napkin
