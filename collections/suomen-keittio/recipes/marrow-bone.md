## Ingredients

- Marrow bones
- Cold water
- Thick batter
- Meat stock
- Parsley
- Lettuce
- Toast
- Butter

## Directions

1. Saw marrow bone into pieces 2-3 inches long.
2. Scrape the meat off carefully and put the bones into cold water for half an hour.
3. Cover both ends with a thick batter and tie a clean cloth around them.
4. Put in a kettle into boiling meat stock and let simmer 1½ hours.
5. Remove the cloth and the batter.
6. Place the marrowbones on buttered toast.
7. Garnish with parsley and lettuce and serve while very hot.
