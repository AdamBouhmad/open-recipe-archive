## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of white stock
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of boiled and stewed chestnuts
- 1 slice of onion
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- salt and pepper
- 1 cupful of cream

## Directions

1. Soak the chestnuts about 2 minutes in water to remove the shells.
2. Remove the shells while warm.
3. Cook in stock until soft and rub through a sieve.
4. Cook the onions in butter, add flour and chestnuts into it and stock as much as required.
5. Boil for 5 minutes; season, and at last add two tablespoonfuls of madeira to flavor it.
