## Ingredients

- lima beans
- salted water
- butter
- salt
- cream or thin milk sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Put lima beans into boiling salted water
2. Cook until tender
3. Drain
4. Season with salt and add butter
5. Add some cream or thin milk sauce according to taste
