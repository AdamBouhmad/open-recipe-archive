## Ingredients

- apples
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare and core the apples, put on the fire with a little water.
2. Let boil covered, until soft.
3. Force through a strainer and sweeten to taste with sugar.
4. Apples cooked without paring make a better sauce.
5. Serve with roast pork and turkey.
