## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. of raspberries
- 4 lbs. of sugar
- 2 cupfuls of water

## Directions

1. Cook water with sugar to a clear syrup threading when dropped from the tip of spoon.
2. Skim carefully.
3. Put the berries into the syrup, simmer slowly, taking care not to break the berries, shaking the kettle and skimming until the scum ceases to rise.
4. When clarified pour into a dry jar.
