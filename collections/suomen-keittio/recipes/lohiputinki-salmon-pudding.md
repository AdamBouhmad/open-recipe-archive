## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- 1 quart milk
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- ½ onion
- ½ pound salmon
- Salt, pepper and dash of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the rice until half done
2. Strain
3. Mix with boiled milk and seasonings
4. Add the salmon cut in fine pieces (if salmon is salty, it should be soaked first)
5. Bake in a buttered mold
6. Serve with melted butter
