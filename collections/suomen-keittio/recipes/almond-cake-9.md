## Ingredients

- ¾ cupful of butter
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 6 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 4 eggs
- 1 cupful of flour
- ¼ lb. of almonds
- 1 teaspoonful of baking powder

## Directions

1. Peel almonds and grind finely
2. Beat yolks of eggs and sugar to a froth
3. Mix with cream, butter, flour, baking powder and almonds
4. Add whites of eggs, whipped to a froth
5. Pour the mixture in a buttered mold or in small forms and bake in a moderate oven
