## Ingredients

- 3 pounds of granulated sugar
- 2 lemons
- 1 ounce of wine acid
- 1½ ounces of powdered ginger
- 13 quarts of water
- 1 yeast cake

## Directions

1. Grate yellow rind of lemons
2. Slice the lemons and put in a jar with sugar, wine acid, ginger and grated yellow lemon rind
3. Pour 1½ quarts of water over it and let stand covered over night
4. Cook the rest of the water and stir it into the above mixture
5. Mix with yeast when cooled and let stand over night
6. Strain and bottle up carefully leaving the yeast on the bottom of the jar
7. Cork the bottles
8. The drink is ready to be served after 8—10 days
