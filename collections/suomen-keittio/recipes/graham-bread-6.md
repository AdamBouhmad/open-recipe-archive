## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk or water
- 3 tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of molasses
- 2 teaspoonfuls of salt
- 1/2 cake of yeast
- 3 cupfuls of graham flour
- 1 cupful of wheat flour

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough and bake like water bread.
