## Ingredients

- whites of 3 eggs
- 7 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1/3 teaspoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Whip whites of 3 eggs until stiff
2. Add slowly 7 tablespoonfuls of sugar and 1/3 teaspoonful of vanilla
