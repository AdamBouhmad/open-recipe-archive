## Ingredients

- 2 herrings
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 2 cups rich milk
- ½ cup butter
- ½ onion
- 3 eggs
- Some pepper and sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the fish, remove the bones and skin, force through a meat chopper
2. Cook the chopped onion in butter, add bread crumbs, milk and eggs beaten
3. Stir gradually into the fish, season
4. Put in a buttered mold, bake in oven in a pan of water
