## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of flour
- 1 cupful of milk
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 cupful of butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Sift flour with salt and baking powder.
2. Whip eggs with sugar, add milk and melted butter.
3. Pour it slowly into the flour kneading well.
4. Bake in greased muffin tins.
5. Serve with tea or as luncheon bread.
