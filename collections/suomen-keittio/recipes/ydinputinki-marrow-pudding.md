## Ingredients

- 2 cups bread crumbs
- 4 eggs
- 1 pint rich milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 6 ounces marrow
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/4 pound almonds
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup cream

## Directions

1. Mix the eggs into the milk, add the bread crumbs and spices.
2. Chop the marrow fine, mix with flour.
3. Combine with the bread mixture, add raisins, almonds and sugar.
4. Pour into a buttered mold sprinkled with bread crumbs.
5. Bake for 1 hour.
6. Serve with lemon sauce.
