## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salted water
- 1 quart of vinegar
- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- cloves, 1 teaspoonful
- white pepper, 1 teaspoonful
- ginger, a little
- mace

## Directions

1. Cut cauliflower in small pieces
2. Cook in salted water 10-15 minutes
3. Place carefully into jars
4. Cook 1 quart of vinegar with 1/2 cupful of sugar, season with ginger and mace
5. Have the spices tied in a bag
6. Drain the brine on the cauliflower into the jar
7. Cover closely and keep in a cool place
