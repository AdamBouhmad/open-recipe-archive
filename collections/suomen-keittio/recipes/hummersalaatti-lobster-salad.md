## Ingredients

- boiled lobster
- mayonnaise
- lettuce
- lobster claws
- hard boiled eggs
- celery

## Directions

1. Cut boiled lobster in small cubes.
2. Let stand in a cold place until served.
3. Mix with mayonnaise.
4. Place on a platter on a bed of lettuce.
5. Garnish around with lobster claws and hard boiled eggs.
