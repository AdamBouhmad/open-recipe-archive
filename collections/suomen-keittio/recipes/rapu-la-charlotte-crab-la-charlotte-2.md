## Ingredients

- boiled crabs
- ¾ cupful of milk
- 4 tablespoonfuls of gelatine
- 2 tablespoonfuls of mayonnaise sauce
- 4 tablespoonfuls of cream
- truffles
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Shell boiled crabs, and cut the meat into cubes.
2. Dissolve the gelatine into the milk.
3. When cool add gradually the mayonnaise sauce.
4. Add last the whipped cream.
5. Garnish small forms with truffles, lay the mixture on the bottom.
6. When firm lay pieces of crab meat on it, and cover it with layer of the mixture.
7. Let stand until set.
8. Turn on a serving-dish, garnish with lettuce.
