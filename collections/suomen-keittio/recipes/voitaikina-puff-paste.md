## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1 pound flour
- 1 egg
- 1 cup water
- 1 teaspoon gin
- juice of lemon
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Wash the butter in ice water and force the water out by wrapping the butter tightly into a towel.
2. Whip the egg, add water, lemon juice, gin and flour mixed with 1 teaspoon of butter.
3. Knead with fingers to an even paste.
4. Roll, place on top of the paste the butter flattened to a cake.
5. Fold up the sheet of paste so as to enclose the butter.
6. Place on ice in a dish for 1 hour.
7. Roll the paste into an oblong square and fold in three.
8. Turn over, reroll and fold in three again.
9. Repeat this operation three times rolling out twice and folding up three times each time.
10. Place the paste on ice for 15 minutes between each operation.
11. Rerolling must be done in the opposite direction.
12. Bake in a hot oven.
