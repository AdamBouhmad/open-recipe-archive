## Ingredients

- 2 yolks of eggs
- 3/4 cupful of sugar
- 3/4 cupful of rich cream
- 10 cardamom seeds
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 3 cupfuls of flour
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Whip yolks of eggs, sugar and cream to a thick froth.
2. Add cardamom, soda and flour enough to be rolled on board.
3. Roll into a thin sheet, cut into strips.
4. Form them into small flat rings.
5. Bake in oven or cook in fat.
6. Sprinkle with sugar.
