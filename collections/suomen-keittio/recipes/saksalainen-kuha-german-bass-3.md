## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 lbs. of fresh bass
- 1 lb. of butter
- 5 eggs
- 1 cupful of sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean bass and cut in pieces.
2. Sprinkle with salt.
3. Melt butter in a casserole and put the bass into it.
4. Bake covered in oven.
5. When half done add beaten eggs and cream.
6. Continue baking until thoroughly done.
7. Serve in the casserole.
