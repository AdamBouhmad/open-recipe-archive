## Ingredients

- 1 wild duck
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 small onion sliced
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup water
- 3 cups tomato
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the duck in nice pieces, dip in flour and brown with butter and onion.
2. Add the water and the tomato, continue cooking for half an hour covered.
3. Remove the duck from the pan, strain the drippings, season.
4. Arrange on a platter, pour the gravy over.
5. Serve with rice.
