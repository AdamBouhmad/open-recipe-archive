## Ingredients

- cherries
- sugar equal in weight to the cherries
- 2 cupfuls of water per 5 pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse cherries and remove the pits.
2. Weigh the cherries and take an equal weight of sugar and 2 cupfuls of water for every 5 pounds of sugar.
3. Cook sugar and water to a clear syrup that forms threads when dropped from a spoon, carefully skimming off the foam.
4. Add the berries and let them heat slowly.
5. Cook on low heat, shaking the saucepan.
