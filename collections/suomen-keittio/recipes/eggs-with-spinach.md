## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- cooked spinach
- melted butter
- cream
- salt
- pepper
- milksauce

## Directions

1. Cut 4 hard-boiled eggs lengthwise into halves and rub the yolks through a sieve.
2. Mix cooked chopped spinach with a little melted butter and cream, season with salt and pepper.
3. Fill the halved whites with this mixture.
4. Place on a dish, pour over them a rich milksauce.
5. Sprinkle with the yolks and place in oven to brown slightly.
