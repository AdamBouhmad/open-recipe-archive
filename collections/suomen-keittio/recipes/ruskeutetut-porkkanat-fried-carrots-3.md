## Ingredients

- carrots
- salted water
- butter
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel carrots and cut into dice.
2. Cook in salted water until half done.
3. Drain off the water.
4. Brown the carrots with butter in a frying pan.
5. Season with a dash of sugar if desired.
