## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of apples, cut in pieces
- 3 cupfuls of bread, cut in pieces
- 1 cupful of sugar
- a little cinnamon
- 1 tablespoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Cut bread and apples in small cubes and mix with sugar and cinnamon.
2. Dot with bits of butter.
3. Bake in oven, first covered, then uncovered to brown the surface.
4. Serve with whipped cream.
