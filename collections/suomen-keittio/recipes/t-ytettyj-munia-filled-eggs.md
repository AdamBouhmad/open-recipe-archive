## Ingredients

- 3 hard boiled eggs
- boiled mushrooms
- milk sauce
- lemon juice or onion juice
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut 3 hard boiled eggs neatly in halves
2. Separate the yolks and mix with chopped boiled mushrooms, 1 tablespoon of milk sauce, a few drops of lemon or onion juice, salt and pepper
3. Fill the halves with the mixture
4. Place together so as to give the appearance of whole eggs
5. Dip in egg and roll in bread crumbs
6. Bake quickly in a hot oven in a buttered mold
