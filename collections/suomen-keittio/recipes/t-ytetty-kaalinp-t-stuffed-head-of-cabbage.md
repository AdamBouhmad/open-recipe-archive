## Ingredients

- Head of cabbage
- Ground meat
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bouillon
- Mushrooms
- Rice
- Tomato
- Chestnuts or chicken

## Directions

1. Cook a head of cabbage whole for 1/2 an hour and drain
2. When cool, open the outer leaves and remove the inner leaves, chop finely
3. Fry in butter until brown
4. Mix the fried cabbage with meat farce
5. Stuff the cabbage with this mixture and close the cabbage upon itself
6. Tie with cord crosswise
7. Bake in a buttered pan
8. When brown on top, sprinkle with salt and pepper
9. Pour some boiling bouillon into the pan for basting the cabbage with
10. Have enough liquid in the pan
11. Cover and continue baking until tender
12. Alternately, the head of cabbage may be stuffed with rice mixed with mushrooms: have chopped mushrooms, add a few spoonfuls of boiled rice, some tomato, melted butter and salt, or use chestnuts or cold chicken instead of mushrooms
