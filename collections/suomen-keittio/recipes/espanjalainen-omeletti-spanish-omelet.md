## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon chopped onion
- 1 tablespoon red and green pepper skinned and cut in pieces
- 1½ cups tomato
- 2 tablespoons chopped mushrooms
- 1 tablespoon caper
- 1 tablespoon chopped olives
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Cook slowly in butter the onion, olives, pepper and tomato for 15 minutes covered.
2. Add the mushrooms and caper.
3. Prepare a plain or a French omelet.
4. Turn the gravy over it and serve.
