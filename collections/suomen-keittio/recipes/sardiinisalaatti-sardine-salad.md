## Ingredients

- sardines
- 1 pickle
- 2 hard boiled egg whites
- lettuce leaves
- boiled mayonnaise
- hard boiled egg yolks

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from sardines and cut into pieces
2. Add 1 chopped pickle and 2 hard boiled egg whites
3. Place on a bed of lettuce leaves
4. Cover with mayonnaise
5. Garnish the top with hard boiled egg yolks rubbed through a sieve
