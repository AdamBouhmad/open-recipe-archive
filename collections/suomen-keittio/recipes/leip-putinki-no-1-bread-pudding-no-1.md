## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread cut in cubes
- 2 cups milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3 eggs
- 3/4 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

## Directions

1. Soak the bread in milk
2. Lay the pieces of bread in a buttered mold
3. Beat the eggs with the sugar, add milk, vanilla, butter and salt
4. Pour over the bread
5. Bake in oven in a pan of water until firm
6. Optional: add raisins and grated rind of lemon, or when nearly done cover the top with jelly, apple sauce or stewed fruit
7. Beat the egg whites with sugar, spread over the fruit, and brown in oven
