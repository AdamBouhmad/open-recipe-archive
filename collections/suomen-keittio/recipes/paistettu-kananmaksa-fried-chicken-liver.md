## Ingredients

- Chicken liver
- 1 tablespoon butter
- Flour
- Brown sauce
- Lemon juice
- Salt
- Pepper
- Kitchen Bouquet (optional)

## Directions

1. Split the liver and wipe.
2. Melt a tablespoon of butter in a frying pan.
3. Fry the liver in the butter for 5 minutes, turning often so as not to burn it.
4. Sprinkle with flour.
5. Add some brown sauce and lemon juice to the drippings.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Cook gently for 10 minutes.
8. Add some Kitchen Bouquet if the color is not brown enough.
