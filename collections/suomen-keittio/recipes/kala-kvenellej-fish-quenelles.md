## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound of boned fish
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 tablespoonful cream
- 1 egg
- flour

## Directions

1. Grind the boned fish
2. Pound well with the butter
3. Force through a sieve
4. Add cream and egg, working the mixture to a smooth paste
5. Add flour enough to make a firm batter
6. Form into small quenelles
7. Cook in salted water or in fish stock
8. Serve in fish or crab soup
