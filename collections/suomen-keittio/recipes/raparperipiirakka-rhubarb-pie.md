## Ingredients

- 2 cups rhubarb cut in pieces
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- cornstarch or flour
- pastry for two crusts

## Directions

1. Skin the rhubarb, cut in pieces inch long, mix with the sugar, let stand for 1 hour.
2. Add some cornstarch or flour, if needed.
3. Bake between two crusts.
