## Ingredients

- ½ pound almonds
- 8 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and cut in pieces.
2. Beat the egg white until firm.
3. Add the sugar and the almonds.
4. Drop by the spoonful on a baking sheet.
5. Bake in a slow oven until light brown and hard.
6. Chopped dates may be used in almond meringue.
