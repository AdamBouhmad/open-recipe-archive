## Ingredients

- Potatoes of equal size
- Butter or lard
- Salt

## Directions

1. Peel potatoes of equal size, cut in two lengthwise
2. Place in a pan with butter or lard
3. Sprinkle with salt
4. Bake until tender and nice brown
5. Potatoes may be baked in a dripping pan with roast beef or roast mutton
