## Ingredients

- 1 quart of bouillon
- several kinds of half cooked vegetables, cut into pieces
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Put several kinds of half cooked vegetables, cut into pieces, into 1 quart of bouillon.
2. Let simmer until soft but not stewed.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
