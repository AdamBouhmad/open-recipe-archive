## Ingredients

- 1 big spoonful of butter
- whites of 2 eggs
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 3 big spoonfuls of American cheese
- 2 tablespoonfuls of whipped cream
- paprika
- mustard

## Directions

1. Mix the butter and the yolks on the fire until thick
2. Add the cheese and seasonings
3. Lastly fold in the whites beaten stiff
