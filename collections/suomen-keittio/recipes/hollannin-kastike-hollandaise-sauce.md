## Ingredients

- 2 heaping tablespoons butter
- 4 yolks of egg
- juice of 1 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- pepper
- 2 2/3 cups boiling water

## Directions

1. Rub until foamy 2 heaping tablespoons of butter in a saucepan
2. Add 4 yolks one by one, whipping well between each
3. Mix with juice of 1 1/2 lemon, 1/2 teaspoon of salt and some pepper
4. Let stand for a while
5. Add 2 2/3 cups of boiling water and beat the sauce in a pan of boiling water until it becomes thick
6. Remove from the fire and continue to stir for a while
7. Serve with fish, asparagus, cauliflower, etc.
