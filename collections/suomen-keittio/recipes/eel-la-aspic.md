## Ingredients

- eel
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- onion
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the eel, remove the backbone.
2. Cut the larger eels in 3 inch pieces.
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and lemon juice, let stand 10 min.
4. Roll and fasten tightly with string.
5. Put into boiling water, place on the range, add salt, pepper and onion; let simmer until tender.
6. Lift to cool.
7. Make jelly of the liquor as directed in last recipe.
8. Cut the rolls crosswise into slices and lay into an individual mold to get firm, or cover the bottom of a platter with jelly, lay the fish on it, pour jelly over it.
9. Arrange the jellied pieces on a serving dish, lay bits of jelly in center of them, garnish around with tomato, lemon and lettuce leaves.
