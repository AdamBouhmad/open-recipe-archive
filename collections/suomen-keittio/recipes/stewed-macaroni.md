## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound of macaroni
- 1/2 onion
- 1/2 green pepper
- 1 tiny carrot
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 quart of fresh cut tomatoes
- 1/2 cupful of water
- salt
- 4 tablespoonfuls of chopped mushrooms
- 2 tablespoonfuls of grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 pound of macaroni cut in pieces in salted water and drain.
2. Fry 1/2 onion, 1/2 green pepper and 1 tiny carrot with 2 tablespoonfuls of butter until brown.
3. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of flour and stir thoroughly.
4. Add 1 quart of fresh cut tomatoes and 1/2 cupful of water.
5. Season with salt and let simmer 30-40 minutes.
6. Strain and mix 4 tablespoonfuls of chopped mushrooms and let simmer 5 minutes.
7. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of grated cheese.
8. Pour the mixture over the boiled macaroni.
