## Ingredients

- 2 cupfuls of mashed potatoes
- 1 1/2 cupfuls of mashed chestnuts
- 2 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs
- 3 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. If the mixture seems too thick, add cream
3. Form round cakes from the mixture
4. Fry in butter, or dip in egg and bread crumbs and cook in fat
