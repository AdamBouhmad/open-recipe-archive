## Ingredients

- 2 cups butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 4 cups flour
- 10 eggs
- 1/4 pound almonds blanched and chopped
- 2 pounds currants
- 2 pounds raisins cut in pieces
- Cinnamon, mace and nutmeg, 1/2 teaspoon each
- 1/2 cup brandy
- 1 teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Rub until creamy the butter with the sugar
2. Work in the egg yolks
3. Add spices, raisins and currants rolled in flour and soda sifted with flour
4. Add last the egg whites beaten until firm
5. Bake in a buttered tin 2 or 3 hours in a moderate oven
6. When risen reduce the heat and cover the cake with paper
