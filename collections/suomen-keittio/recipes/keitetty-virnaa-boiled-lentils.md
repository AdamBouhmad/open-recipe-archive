## Ingredients

- 1 pound lentils
- cold water
- butter
- sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Rinse 1 pound of lentils
2. Put on the fire in cold water
3. Let simmer with some butter and sugar until tender
4. Make a sauce of 1 tablespoon butter and 1 tablespoon flour mixed with liquor of lentils
5. Bring to boiling
6. Season with salt and lemon juice
7. Alternatively, drain the lentils and add salt, butter and lemon juice
