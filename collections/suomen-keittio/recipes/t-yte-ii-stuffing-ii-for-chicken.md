## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- ½ cupful of chicken stock
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Fry onion in butter
2. Add soaked bread crumbs and parsley
3. Dilute with chicken stock
4. Add 1 egg
5. Mix well
