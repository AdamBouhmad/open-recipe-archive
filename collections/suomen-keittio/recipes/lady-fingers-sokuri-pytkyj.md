## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- 1/2 cupful of flour
- 1/4 teaspoonful of vanilla
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Whip yolks of eggs with sugar
2. Add spices and flour into it
3. At the last mix with whites, beaten to a stiff froth
4. Put the mixture in a pastry bag with tube
5. Lay paper on a baking sheet
6. Force the mixture through the pastry bag on paper into cookies four inches long
7. Sprinkle with powdered sugar
8. Bake in a moderate oven to a light brown
9. If preferred, stick two together with white of egg
