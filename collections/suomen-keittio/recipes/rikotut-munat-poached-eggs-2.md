## Ingredients

- eggs
- water
- salt
- butter
- bread

## Directions

1. Place a frying pan over the fire
2. Half fill with boiling water mixed with a little salt
3. Break the eggs carefully one by one into a cup and slip them gently upon the water
4. Boil until the whites are firm
5. Remove very carefully from the pan
6. Serve on buttered toast
