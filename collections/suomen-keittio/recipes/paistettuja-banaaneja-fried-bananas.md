## Ingredients

- Bananas
- Salt
- Bread crumbs
- Egg
- Butter or lard

## Directions

1. Peel and split the bananas, sprinkle with salt.
2. Turn in bread crumbs, egg and in bread crumbs again.
3. Fry in butter or lard.
4. Serve very hot.
