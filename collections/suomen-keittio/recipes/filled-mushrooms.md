## Ingredients

- big mushrooms of equal size
- boiled chicken
- chopped mushrooms
- cream
- Chaufroid sauce
- bread crumbs
- egg
- bacon
- fat

## Directions

1. Peel big mushrooms of equal size and cut off the stems
2. Prepare a filling of boiled chicken and chopped mushrooms, mixed with a little cream
3. Lay this mixture between the mushrooms
4. Baste with Chaufroid sauce
5. Let cool, dredge with bread crumbs, dip in egg and lastly dredge with bread crumbs
6. Fry in fat or bake in oven, having a little slice of bacon on each
7. Bake covered 15 minutes
