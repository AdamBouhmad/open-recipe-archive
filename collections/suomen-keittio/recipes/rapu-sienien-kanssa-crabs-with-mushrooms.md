## Ingredients

- 2 cups crab meat boiled and cut in pieces
- 1 1/2 cups mushrooms chopped
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- Salt, pepper and lemon juice
- 1 slice of onion
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Cook the onion with butter, add flour, mushrooms, crab meat, spices and milk
2. Boil quickly
3. Remove from fire, add some sherry and cream
4. Serve hot
5. Or fill the crab shells with the mixture (can be harder than the mixture above)
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs, dot with butter
7. Brown in oven
