## Ingredients

- small tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- water
- eggs

## Directions

1. Use small tomatoes of equal size and cover with boiling water.
2. Skin the tomatoes, cut off the tops, and scoop carefully.
3. Place in a saucepan and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Put a little lump of butter on the bottom of the space and break an egg into each tomato.
5. Put a little water with some butter into a baking dish, place the tomatoes in it, and bake 15 minutes.
6. Alternatively, fill the tomatoes with scrambled eggs and bake.
7. Serve on buttered toast.
