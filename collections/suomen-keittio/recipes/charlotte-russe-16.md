## Ingredients

- 3 pints of thick cream
- 1 cupful of sugar
- 6-7 leaves of gelatine
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla or a little lemon

## Directions

1. Dissolve gelatine in 1/2 cupful of water
2. Whip cream to a froth
3. Add sugar and vanilla
4. Add gelatine when it has cooled slightly
5. Line the bottom and sides of a mold with long biscuits (lady fingers) or prepare a wrapper from pastry
