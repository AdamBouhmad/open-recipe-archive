## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk or water
- 3 cupfuls of wheat flour
- rye flour
- 3/4 cupful of butter or lard
- 1 cupful of molasses or brown sugar
- 1 cake of yeast
- 2 teaspoonfuls of salt
- 1 tablespoonful of caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast in a small quantity of lukewarm milk, mix with flour, set to rise.
2. Scald the rest of the milk, stir butter, molasses, salt and caraway seeds into it.
3. Mix with flour to make a very soft batter.
4. Pour the risen sponge into the batter, add rye flour enough to prepare a moderately thick dough.
5. Knead the dough well, set to rise.
6. Toss on board, knead again, form into round cakes or put in buttered baking pans to rise.
7. Bake in a moderate oven until quite done.
