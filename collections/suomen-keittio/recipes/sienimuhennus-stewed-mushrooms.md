## Ingredients

- Mushrooms
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Flour
- Rich milk
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms and cut into pieces
2. Fry with 2 tablespoons butter for 5 minutes
3. Remove the mushrooms from the pan
4. Make a sauce by adding flour and rich milk to the drippings
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Stir the mushrooms into the sauce and heat thoroughly
