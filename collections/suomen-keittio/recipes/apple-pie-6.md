## Ingredients

- pastry for pieplate
- sliced apples
- 1/2 cupful of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice
- a little salt
- cinnamon
- a little butter
- water
- egg

## Directions

1. Cover pieplate with pastry
2. Fill with sliced apples
3. Mix 1/2 cupful of sugar, 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice, a little salt and cinnamon
4. Pour over the apples
5. Dot with a little butter
6. Moisten the edges of the pastry with water
7. Place upper crust on
8. Press the edges together and fold them under
9. Brush over with egg
10. Bake in a moderate oven 1 hour
