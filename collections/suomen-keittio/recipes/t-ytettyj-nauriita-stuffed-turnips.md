## Ingredients

- 12 middle-sized turnips
- 1/2 cupful almonds
- 1/2 cupful bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter salt
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 2 eggs
- nutmeg and pepper

## Directions

1. Wash turnips and cook almost tender in water with pepper and salt.
2. Peel when cooled.
3. Hollow out the turnips and mash the removed flesh.
4. Mix the mashed flesh with sugar, almonds, bread crumbs, butter salt, eggs, nutmeg and pepper.
