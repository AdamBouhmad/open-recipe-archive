## Ingredients

- 2 cups lemon or wine jelly
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Prepare 2 cups of lemon or wine jelly and let cool.
2. Before it is firm, beat to a froth.
3. Beat 3 egg whites until firm.
4. Combine the mixtures and continue to beat until it is stiff enough to hold its shape.
5. Mold or drop with a spoon into a glass dish.
6. Serve with whipped cream, vanilla sauce or custard.
