## Ingredients

- 1 quart chestnuts
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon parsley
- 1 egg
- bouillon

## Directions

1. Have a quart of chestnuts in boiling water so as to remove the shells.
2. Drain and shell.
3. Place the shelled chestnuts in water or bouillon and cook until tender.
4. Rub through a sieve.
5. Add 2 tablespoons of butter, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 teaspoon of parsley, 1 egg, and some bouillon if necessary.
6. For stuffing turkey, add bread crumbs or mashed sweet potatoes to the stuffing.
