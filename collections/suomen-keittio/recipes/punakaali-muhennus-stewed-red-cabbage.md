## Ingredients

- 2 red cabbages
- 1 onion
- 3 apples or 3 tomatoes
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1 tablespoonful of parsley
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1/2 cupful of wine
- a little pepper
- 1 tablespoonful of currant jelly

## Directions

1. Cut cabbage in thin slices
2. Remove the thickest stalks
3. Melt 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
4. Cook onion in it
5. Add the cabbage and apples, cut in pieces
6. Add sugar, salt, parsley and pepper
7. Let boil tightly covered 1-2 hours
8. Add wine, the rest of the butter and 1 tablespoonful of currant jelly
9. Let boil 5 minutes
10. Serve with meat
