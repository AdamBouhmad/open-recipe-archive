## Ingredients

- light colored, fresh, crisp lettuce
- French dressing
- minced parsley

## Directions

1. Let stand in cold water for a while.
2. Remove leaves from the stalk, wash quickly one by one in cold water.
3. Place them on a clean towel, so the water may drip from the leaves.
4. Arrange the lettuce in circle into a salad bowl, having the hearts in middle.
5. Pour French dressing on, sprinkle with minced parsley.
