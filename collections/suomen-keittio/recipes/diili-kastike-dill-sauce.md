## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 cupfuls of beef stock
- 1 tablespoonful of vinegar or lemon juice
- 1-2 tablespoonfuls of minced dill
- a little salt and sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and beef stock
2. Season with sugar, vinegar and dill
3. One egg yolk may be added to it
4. Serve hot with boiled mutton
