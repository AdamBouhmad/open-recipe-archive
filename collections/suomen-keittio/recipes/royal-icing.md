## Ingredients

- white of an egg
- 1 cupful of confectioner's sugar
- lemon juice or other flavoring

## Directions

1. Put white of an egg in bowl, mix slowly with 1 cupful of confectioner's sugar.
2. Stir a few drops of lemon or any other flavoring into it, whipping the mixture until even and smooth.
3. Place the icing in centre of cake, when the latter is yet a little warm, spreading evenly with wet knife.
4. If the icing is done before cake, cover it with wet cloth so that it will not get firm.
5. When using a little water and more sugar the icing will be smoother.
6. The icing may be colored pink with carmine or fruit juice, green with spinach juice, brown with chocolate, yellow with saffron.
