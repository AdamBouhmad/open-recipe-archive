## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon chopped onion
- Fillets of roast or boiled chicken
- Meat stock
- Butter and flour for thickening
- 2 egg yolks
- Cream

## Directions

1. Cook the onion in butter
2. Add diced chicken fillets and meat stock
3. Thicken with butter and flour
4. Add 2 egg yolks diluted with cream
5. Heat but do not boil
