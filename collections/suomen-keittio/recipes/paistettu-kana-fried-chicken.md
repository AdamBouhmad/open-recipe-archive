## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Butter or lard
- Milk
- Cream
- Flour
- Bouillon

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken in pieces.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Roll in beaten egg and bread crumbs.
4. Fry until brown in butter or lard.
5. Place in the oven covered for 15 minutes.
6. Serve with milk sauce made only of milk or mixed with cream, or make gravy of drippings by adding flour, bouillon and cream.
