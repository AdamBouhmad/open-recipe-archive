## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 cupfuls of rich cream
- 10 ozs. of sugar
- 10 ozs. of flour
- 1/2 cupful of flour
- grated yellow lemon rind

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs with sugar to a froth
2. Add beaten cream, flour and lemon rind
3. Turn on a buttered mold and bake 1/2-1 hour
