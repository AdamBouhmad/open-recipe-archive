## Ingredients

- 2 cups oysters
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 cup milk or cream
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare a sauce of butter, flour and milk
2. Add the beaten egg yolk to the sauce
3. Add the oysters to the mixture
4. Heat through over the fire until thick
5. Serve hot
