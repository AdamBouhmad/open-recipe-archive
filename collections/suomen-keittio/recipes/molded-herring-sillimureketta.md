## Ingredients

- 2 herrings
- 1 cupful of crackercrumbs
- 2 cupfuls of rich milk
- 1/2 cupful of butter
- 1/2 onion
- 3 eggs
- a little pepper and sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the herrings, bone and skin, chop with meat chopper.
2. Cook the chopped onion in butter, add cracker crumbs, milk and beaten eggs.
3. Mix gradually with the herrings.
4. Season.
5. Turn in a buttered mold.
6. Bake in a pan of water.
