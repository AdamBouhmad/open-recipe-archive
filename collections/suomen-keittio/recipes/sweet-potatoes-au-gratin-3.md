## Ingredients

- five cold sweet potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- 1 tablespoonful of brown sugar
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut five cold sweet potatoes into dice.
2. Put in a buttered mold, sprinkle with salt and pepper and 1 tablespoonful of brown sugar.
3. Dot with bits of butter, sprinkle with bread crumbs.
4. Bake in oven, covered, 20 minutes.
5. Then uncover and leave in oven until brown.
