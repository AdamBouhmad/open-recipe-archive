## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- parsley butter or mayonnaise or Bearnaise sauce

## Directions

1. Cut tomatoes crosswise in two
2. Do not peel them
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Broil with skin side down on slow fire, turning often until done
5. Place on hot dish
6. Spread with parsley butter, hot mayonnaise, or Bearnaise sauce
