## Ingredients

- pork tenderloin with ribs
- lemon juice
- salt
- ginger
- pepper
- apples
- water
- bread crumbs
- egg

## Directions

1. Crack the ribs
2. Rub the meat with lemon juice, salt, ginger and pepper
3. Place in a pan with the skin side down having a few slices of apple on the ribs
4. Roast in a hot oven adding a little water when needed
5. Turn the roast over, sprinkle with bread crumbs, brown in a hot oven
6. When tender, remove from the pan and remove the meat from the backbone
7. Cut in slices and make a gravy of the drippings
8. Strain the gravy over the meat
9. Alternatively, remove the backbone before roasting, spread meat with egg, roll in bread crumbs and roast
10. Or open one side lengthwise, fill with slices of apples, sew up and roast
