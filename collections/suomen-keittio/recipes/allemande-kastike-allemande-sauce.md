## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1½ cups white beef stock
- ¼ cup cream
- 2 yolks of egg
- Lemon juice
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare as for milk sauce
2. Add some lemon juice
3. Last of all add the yolks of egg and cream
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Serve with fish, meat or vegetables
