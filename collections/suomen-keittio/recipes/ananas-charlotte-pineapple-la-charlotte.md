## Ingredients

- 1 pineapple
- 1/2 package gelatine
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 pint whipped cream

## Directions

1. Peel the pineapple, remove the eyes. Grate.
2. Dissolve 1/2 package of gelatine in pineapple juice over the fire, add 1/2 cup of sugar, pour into the grated pineapple.
3. When cool, add 1 pint of whipped cream.
4. Put into a wet mold to set.
