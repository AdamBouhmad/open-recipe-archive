## Ingredients

- rice
- salt
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash the rice carefully in several waters
2. Put on the fire in plenty of boiling salted water
3. Cook slowly for 20 minutes or until the rice is tender but not mushy
4. Drain and rinse well with cold water
5. Sprinkle with salt and dot with butter
6. Put on the side of the range stirring now and then so as to evaporate the moisture, or place the rice in the strainer into an open oven to evaporate
7. Serve as a substitute for potatoes
