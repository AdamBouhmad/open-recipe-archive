## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound almonds
- 1 1/4 pounds powdered sugar
- 2 egg whites
- jelly

## Directions

1. Blanch and grate the almonds
2. Pound with sugar in a mortar or in a wooden bowl
3. Beat the egg whites to a firm froth
4. Combine with the mixture above
5. Drop by the spoonful on a baking sheet
6. Press lightly with finger in the center of each
7. Put jelly in
8. Bake in a slow oven
9. Let harden
