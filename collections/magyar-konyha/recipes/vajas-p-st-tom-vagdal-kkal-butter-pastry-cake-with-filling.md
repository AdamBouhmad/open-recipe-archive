## Ingredients

- butter pastry dough as described in recipe 372
- egg white

## Directions

1. Prepare butter pastry dough as described in recipe 372
2. Roll out to about knife-blade thickness
3. Cut out a round or rectangular piece
4. Brush the outer edge about one finger-width with egg white
5. Roll out another piece of dough similarly
6. Place it on top
7. If the piece is round, cut out small coin-sized pieces with a small doughnut cutter and arrange them on the pastry edge, always so that the second piece covers half of the first
8. If rectangular, cut two-finger-wide strips with a hot knife from the dough and place them on the edge
