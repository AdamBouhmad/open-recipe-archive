## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- vinegar
- oil
- pickled cucumber
- capers
- tarragon
- green parsley
- caviar
- one to two boiled egg yolks
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare exactly as tartar sauce, except without mustard
2. Add the following ingredients: pickled cucumber, capers, tarragon, green parsley, whole caviar, and one to two boiled egg yolks pressed through a sieve, with a small amount of vinegar
