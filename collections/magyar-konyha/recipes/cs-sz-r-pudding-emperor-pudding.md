## Ingredients

- 9 dekagrams butter
- 18 dekagrams sugar
- little vanilla
- 18 dekagrams soaked almonds
- cold water
- 10 dekagrams citron
- 1 whole egg
- 5 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Whip 9 dekagrams butter until fluffy
2. Pound 18 dekagrams sugar with a little vanilla and the same amount of soaked almonds, moistening with a little cold water so it does not become oily
3. Finely chop 10 dekagrams citron
4. Put everything into the whipped butter
5. Add 1 whole egg and 5 egg yolks one after another
6. Add the stiff whites of 4 eggs
7. Pour into a buttered mold with the bottom decorated neatly with citron and steam for three-quarters of an hour
8. Turn onto a plate and pour wine sauce around it
