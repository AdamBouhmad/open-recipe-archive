## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams soaked almond, cut in slivers
- 28 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- 1 orange, zest
- 1 lemon, zest and juice
- pistachio, finely chopped
- water

## Directions

1. Mix 28 dekagrams of soaked almond cut in slivers with 28 dekagrams of finely ground sugar
2. Finely chop the zest of 1 orange and 1 lemon and add with their juices to the almond and sugar
3. Mix well and let stand for half an hour so it swells well, as it must be moist
4. Place 3 spoonfuls of this mixture in a pan or brass bowl
5. Roast until light brown, stirring constantly so it doesn't burn and gets even color
6. Then place by the spoonful on a board moistened with water
7. Spread with a knife into thin wafer shape
8. Sprinkle with finely chopped pistachio
9. When hardened, remove and prepare the rest of the wafers the same way
