## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 7 deciliters hot milk
- 10 dekagrams butter
- vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Cook grated noodle dough made from 2 eggs in 7 deciliters hot milk
2. When well cooked and thick, place in a pot in which 10 dekagrams butter has been melted
3. Mix together and braise with continuous stirring
4. When serving sprinkle with vanilla sugar
