## Ingredients

- pancakes
- cooked lean smoked goose (sódar)
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 deciliters good sour cream
- butter
- smoked goose

## Directions

1. Cut pancakes into haluska (noodle) shapes
2. Chop cooked lean smoked goose into small pieces
3. Mix the chopped goose with the pancakes in a bowl
4. Beat 3 egg yolks with 2 deciliters good sour cream thoroughly
5. Mix this into the goose and pancake mixture
6. Butter special dumplings molds (kádkák) and coat densely with finely chopped smoked goose
7. Fill with the goose-pancake mixture
8. Braise in a water bath
