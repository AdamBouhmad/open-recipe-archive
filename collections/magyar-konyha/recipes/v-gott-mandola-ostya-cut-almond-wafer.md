## Ingredients

- flour
- sugar
- 1 egg
- milk
- 28 dekagramm swollen and cut almonds
- 28 dekagramm finely ground sugar
- 3 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- lemon juice
- 1 lemon zest finely chopped
- 1 orange zest rubbed with sugar
- 7 dekagramm candied citron cut into small cubes
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a dough from a small amount of flour, sugar, 1 egg, and milk and bake leaves like a wafer
2. Stack these on top of each other and place a plate on top so they stay straight
3. Mix together 28 dekagramm swollen and cut almonds and 28 dekagramm finely ground sugar with 3 stiffly beaten egg whites, a little lemon juice, the finely chopped zest of 1 lemon, the zest of 1 orange rubbed with sugar, 7 dekagramm candied citron cut into small cubes, and a little ground cinnamon
4. Mix all well together
5. Cut the baked wafers into beautiful rounds
6. Coat with the dough
7. Bake on a sheet to a beautiful light yellow and immediately bend onto round wood
