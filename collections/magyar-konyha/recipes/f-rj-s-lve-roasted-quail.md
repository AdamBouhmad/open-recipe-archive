## Ingredients

- quail
- salt
- 7 deciliters wine
- 7 deciliters vinegar
- cloves
- butter for basting

## Directions

1. Clean and pluck the quail well, rinse it thoroughly, salt it, and let it lie in salt for a while
2. Pour 7 deciliters of wine and the same amount of vinegar over it, add a few cloves, and let it marinate in the brine overnight
3. Roast it slowly and carefully
4. A small sauce can be made from the brine if desired
