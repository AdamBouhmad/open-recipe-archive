## Ingredients

- suckling pig (3-4 weeks old)
- finely ground resin
- water
- lard-pieces
- bread roll with bacon pieces
- fine pins and thread
- bay leaf or similar seasonings

## Directions

1. Kill the pig with a thrust through the neck aimed at the heart
2. Dust finely with ground resin and scald with boiling water, then remove the bristles by hand
3. Fold the front legs under the breast and the hind legs under themselves
4. Stuff the belly cavity (after removing the intestines) with a bread roll studded with pieces of bacon and sew closed with fine pins and thread
5. Run the spit lengthwise through the pig so it exits at the snout without making a hole
6. During roasting, frequently baste with peeled bacon on a long fork
7. Keep the fire moderate so that no blisters form and the skin remains tender, but not so weak that the skin becomes soft and pale - it should be a beautiful golden-brown color, crispy and glass-like
8. After removing from the spit, take out the pins and thread
9. Make a deep cut between the head and neck to allow trapped steam to escape and prevent the skin from softening
