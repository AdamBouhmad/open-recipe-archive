## Ingredients

- Beef
- Bacon and ham larded with vegetables
- Bacon slices
- Red onion
- Carrot
- Whole peppercorns, several
- Allspice
- White wine, four deciliters
- Broth, three serving spoons
- Braised cabbage

## Directions

1. Lard the beef with bacon and ham that have been rolled in minced vegetables
2. Line the pot with bacon slices, red onion, and carrot
3. Place the beef on top, add several whole peppercorns and allspice
4. Braise for five to six hours, pouring a small amount of broth over it from time to time to prevent burning and hardening
5. When it begins to become tender, pour four deciliters of white wine and three serving spoons of broth over it
6. Continue braising until tender, strain the sauce through a sieve, and remove the fat
7. Serve with braised cabbage as a crown
