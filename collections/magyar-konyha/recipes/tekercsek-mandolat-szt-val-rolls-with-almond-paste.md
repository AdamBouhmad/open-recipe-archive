## Ingredients

- butter dough
- ten dekagrams of puffed almonds
- ten dekagrams of sugar
- lemon or orange zest
- one and three-quarter dekagrams of fresh butter
- four egg yolks
- egg white

## Directions

1. Prepare butter dough
2. First, grind ten dekagrams of puffed almonds into fine dough
3. Crush the same amount of sugar rubbed on lemon or orange, crush it, and grind again with one and three-quarter dekagrams of fresh butter, four egg yolks, and the almonds until it becomes a paste
4. Roll out the dough to the thickness of a knife blade
5. Cut finger-length rectangular pieces with a warm knife
6. Brush the edge with egg white
7. Then spread almond paste on it with a knife, but not quite to the edge
8. Fold together, press the two ends slightly with the knife back and fry in fat
9. The fat should not be very hot so the rolls fry slowly to a nice golden-brown color
10. Sprinkle with vanilla sugar and neatly arrange on a plate covered with a cloth
