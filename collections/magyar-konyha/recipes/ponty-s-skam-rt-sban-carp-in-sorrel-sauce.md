## Ingredients

- carp
- salt
- melted butter
- sorrel sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the carp and make cuts lengthwise on both sides without cutting all the way through
2. Salt it, coat with melted butter, grill on a gridiron and baste several times with butter
3. When nicely browned and cooked through, place good sorrel sauce on a plate and arrange the fish on top
