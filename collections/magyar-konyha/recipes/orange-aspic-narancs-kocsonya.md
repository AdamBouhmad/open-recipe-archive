## Ingredients

- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 2 orange peels
- 10 oranges
- 2 lemons
- 4 deciliters water
- 4.5 dekagrams isinglass

## Directions

1. Gently rub 42 dekagrams sugar on 2 orange peels; do not rub hard or it will become bitter.
2. Scrape off the rubbed sugar with a knife and place in a soup bowl.
3. Squeeze juice from 10 oranges and 2 lemons and dissolve the sugar in this juice.
4. May add 4 additional deciliters of water if desired.
5. Once sugar is completely dissolved, add 4.5 dekagrams isinglass and mix well with the juice.
6. Strain through a large, double-stretched cloth as with other aspics, pour into molds, and place on ice.
7. If decorating this aspic, pour only 2 centimeters height into mold and freeze on ice; then arrange candied orange segments in star pattern around mold, add another 2 centimeters of mixture and freeze; once firm, pour remaining mixture on top and keep on ice until use.
