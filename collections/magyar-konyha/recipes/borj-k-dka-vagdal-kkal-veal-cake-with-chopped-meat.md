## Ingredients

- veal
- butter
- cartilage
- salt water
- ten to twelve mushrooms
- fresh parsley
- potatoes
- six large boiled floury potatoes
- eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare a light butter sauce
2. Braise the veal and cut into cubes
3. Cut cartilage boiled in salted water into similar pieces
4. Clean and chop ten to twelve mushrooms and braise with chopped fresh parsley and a little butter
5. Add everything to the butter sauce and cook until the sauce is completely reduced
6. Peel and cool six large boiled floury potatoes
7. When cooled, grate them and beat in a bowl with five decagrams of butter
8. Gradually add one whole egg and the yolks of four eggs, beating well
9. Mix in one whole egg white beaten to stiff peaks and salt
10. Fill buttered cake molds, lined with fine breadcrumbs, half full with the beaten potato
11. Place the chopped meat mixture in the center and top with more potato
12. Bake at low heat
