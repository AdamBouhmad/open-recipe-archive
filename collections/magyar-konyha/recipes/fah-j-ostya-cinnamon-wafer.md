## Ingredients

- 28 dekagramm swollen and finely ground almonds
- 4 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 1/2 kilogramm ground sugar
- 1 dekagramm cinnamon
- 1 lemon, zest finely chopped
- 1/2 lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix finely 28 dekagramm swollen and finely ground almonds with 4 stiffly beaten egg whites, 1/2 kilogramm ground sugar, 1 dekagramm cinnamon, the finely chopped zest of 1 lemon, and 1/2 lemon juice
2. Spread the dough knife-thickness by the spoonful onto a butter-coated sheet
3. Bake and while warm bend onto round wooden sticks
