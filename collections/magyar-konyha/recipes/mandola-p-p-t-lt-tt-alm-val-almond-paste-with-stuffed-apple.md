## Ingredients

- 17 dekagramm softened almonds
- 17 dekagramm sugar
- 5 dekagramm raisins
- zest of 1 lemon, finely chopped
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- 8-10 large apples
- preserves
- 2 egg whites for meringue
- apricot for meringue

## Directions

1. Finely grind 17 dekagramm softened almonds and 17 dekagramm sugar.
2. Place in a bowl with 5 dekagramm raisins and finely chopped zest of 1 lemon.
3. Beat with 2 whole eggs and 4 egg yolks for half an hour.
4. Fold in the stiffly beaten whites of 4 eggs.
5. Peel 8-10 large apples, hollow them out, and parboil them briefly in water.
6. Place the apples in a porcelain dish that will also be used for serving.
7. Fill the apples with preserves as desired, sprinkle with sugar.
8. Pour the paste over the apples and place in the oven.
9. Once baked, prepare a meringue from the whites of 2 eggs and apricot.
10. Arrange the meringue around the edge of the dish, heap high in a crown with a wide knife.
11. Make incisions with a demitasse spoon about two fingers wide apart to resemble a plaited crown.
12. Return to the oven and bake for another half hour.
