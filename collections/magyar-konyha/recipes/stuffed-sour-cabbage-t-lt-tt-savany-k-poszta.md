## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram young pork
- a small piece of bacon
- a handful of washed rice
- a little salt
- ground pepper
- 2 whole eggs
- cabbage leaves
- finely chopped cabbage
- thin gravy
- 3-4 tablespoons sour cream
- finely chopped dill
- ham slices

## Directions

1. Place the pork and bacon in a bowl
2. Add a handful of washed rice, a little salt, ground pepper and 2 whole eggs
3. Mix everything well together
4. Fill cabbage leaves with this mixture, roll them up tightly
5. Place in a pot with finely chopped cabbage
6. Put over heat and cook for 1-2 hours
7. Prepare a thin gravy, add the cabbage and cook for at least another hour
8. Add 3-4 tablespoons sour cream and finely chopped dill and braise a little more
9. When serving, surround the cabbage with the filling and add ham slices
