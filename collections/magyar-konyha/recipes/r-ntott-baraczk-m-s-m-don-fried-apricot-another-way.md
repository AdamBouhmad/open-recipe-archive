## Ingredients

- Twelve large ripe apricots
- 14 dekagram finely ground sugar
- Two tablespoons cream

## Directions

1. Peel and halve twelve large ripe apricots
2. Place in a porcelain bowl
3. Sprinkle with 14 dekagram finely ground sugar
4. Pour two tablespoons cream over them
5. Let stand for two hours, shaking several times so the liquid permeates well
6. Dip in batter, coat in fine breadcrumbs
7. Fry in hot fat until beautifully golden
8. Cook the remaining juice into a syrup
9. Arrange the fried apricots nicely on a plate and pour the syrup over them
10. Sprinkle with coarsely ground sugar
11. The same method can be used for orange slices: peel the white skin well from the oranges and divide into six slices
