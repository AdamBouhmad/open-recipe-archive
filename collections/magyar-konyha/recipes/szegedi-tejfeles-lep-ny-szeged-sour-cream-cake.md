## Ingredients

- 500 grams flour
- 28 dekagrams butter
- 3 eggs
- 4 fillérs yeast
- milk
- salt
- 1 liter sour cream
- 12 eggs

## Directions

1. Spread flour on a pastry board and crumble butter into it
2. Add 3 whole eggs, yeast dissolved in milk, salt, and enough milk to make a stiff dough
3. Cover and place in a warm spot to rise
4. Mix 1 liter sour cream with 12 whole eggs, 28 dekagrams flour, and salt
5. Roll out the dough to finger-thickness
6. Place in a greased baking pan
7. Spread the sour cream mixture evenly over it
8. Brush the top with egg mixed with sour cream
9. Press down the edges with your fingers
10. Bake until done, about 45 minutes
11. Remove from oven, cut into square pieces, and serve hot
12. Sugar may be served on the side
