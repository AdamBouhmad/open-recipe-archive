## Ingredients

- cooked beef
- bacon slices
- carrot slices
- red onion slices
- flour
- good broth
- red wine
- carrot
- butter
- green parsley
- sugar
- three large potatoes
- egg yolks
- salt
- whole eggs

## Directions

1. Place the cooked meat in a pot lined with bacon, carrot, and red onion slices
2. Turn frequently so it browns on all sides
3. Remove the meat and sprinkle with flour
4. When the flour is browned, dilute with half good broth and half red wine
5. Simmer until well reduced and strain
6. Using a special tool, cut small balls from carrot and braise them gently with butter, green parsley, and sugar without browning
7. Cook three large potatoes, peel, and grate them when cooled
8. Whip in a piece of butter, mix with finely chopped parsley, potatoes, salt, two whole eggs and two egg yolks
9. Coat the inner part of the meat evenly with this mixture and bake in the oven until the crust browns nicely
10. Serve the meat in the center of the dish, place the brown sauce at the bottom, and arrange the braised carrots around the meat like a wreath
