## Ingredients

- duck
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Fatten the duck for at least one week before roasting
2. Pluck the feathers and scald with water
3. Carefully remove all remaining feathers
4. Always open the duck along the breast: remove the entrails
5. Clean the liver and gizzard and set aside
6. Salt inside and outside
7. Place in a roasting pan, add a little water
8. Put in a hot oven for one hour
9. Turn frequently and roast to a nice pink color
10. Prepare rice cake made with the liver and gizzard mixed in as a side
11. When serving, carve and arrange on a long platter; garnish with the liver-rice cake
