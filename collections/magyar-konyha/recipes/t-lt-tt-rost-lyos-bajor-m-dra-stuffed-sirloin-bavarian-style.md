## Ingredients

- beautiful sirloin
- salt
- several anchovy fillets
- capers
- onion
- green parsley, finely chopped
- butter
- two or three egg yolks
- broth
- sour cream
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Beat and salt the sirloin well
2. Remove anchovy fillets and add finely chopped capers, onion, and green parsley
3. Beat butter until foamy, add two or three egg yolks, then the finely chopped mixture and mix well
4. Fill the sirloin with this mixture and roll it up
5. Place in a pot, add a little broth and braise until crumbly
6. Pour six spoonfuls of sour cream over it and braise a little longer
7. Strain off the fat and sprinkle with lemon zest before serving
