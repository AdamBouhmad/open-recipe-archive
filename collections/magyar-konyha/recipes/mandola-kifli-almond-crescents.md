## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams crushed and sifted sugar
- half an egg white
- a few drops of lemon juice
- 14 dekagrams blanched almonds, finely chopped
- 10 dekagrams coarsely chopped almonds
- 10 dekagrams crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar into a stiff icing with half an egg white and a few drops of lemon juice
2. Mix the blanched and finely chopped almonds into it
3. Previously mix 10 dekagrams coarsely chopped almonds with 10 dekagrams crushed sugar
4. Place a coffee spoonful of the beaten dough into the coarsely chopped sugar-almond mixture, press it wide with your palm, and shape into small crescents, then dust generously with almonds and sugar
5. Bake the crescents on waxed parchment in gentle heat
