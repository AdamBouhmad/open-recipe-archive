## Ingredients

- about four and a half kilograms of top round beef
- salt
- bacon
- pickled tongue
- red onion
- four button mushrooms
- green parsley
- black pepper
- bacon slices
- lean ham slices
- two heads of red onion
- two carrots
- strong broth
- strong twine

## Directions

1. Pound a cleaned piece of top round beef until broad and salt it
2. Cut thin strips of bacon and pickled tongue
3. Finely mince red onion, four button mushrooms, and green parsley, add black pepper and mix well
4. Mix the minced bacon with this mixture
5. Remove the bones from the meat and make small holes in the inner side of the flattened meat without piercing through or damaging the outer side
6. Alternately stuff with the prepared bacon-tongue mixture, leaving a two-finger width gap between rows
7. Roll the meat tightly like a barrel and bind firmly with strong twine
8. Place bacon slices in a long pot, add one or two slices of lean ham, two heads of diced red onion and two carrots
9. Place the rolled meat on top and fill halfway with strong broth
10. Cover and braise for several hours, turning frequently and adding small amounts of broth as the sauce reduces
11. When the meat is completely tender, strain the sauce, remove the twine, and slice with a sharp knife
12. Arrange the slices on a dish, pour sauce over them, and garnish with small potatoes or mixed vegetables
