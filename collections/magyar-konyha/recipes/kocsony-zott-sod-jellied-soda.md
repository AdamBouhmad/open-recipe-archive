## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- 4 deciliters white old wine
- 2 dekagrams dissolved isinglass
- 21 dekagrams sugar rubbed on lemon

## Directions

1. In a pan, beat together the egg yolks, white old wine, dissolved isinglass, and sugar rubbed on lemon until foamy
2. Place on gentle coals and continue beating until it begins to thicken
3. Set the pan in cold water and continue beating until it cools slightly
4. Pour into a mold, freeze in a cold place, turn out onto a plate, and serve
