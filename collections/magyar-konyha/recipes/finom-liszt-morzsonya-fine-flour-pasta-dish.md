## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1.5 liters milk
- fat or butter
- cinnamon sugar
- abundant raisins

## Directions

1. Make a soft dough from 3 eggs that must be pourable
2. Bring 1.5 liters milk to a boil
3. When boiling, pour the dough in from a good height so that it becomes long strips
4. When the milk is cooked, add abundant fat or butter
5. Sprinkle cinnamon sugar among it and add abundant raisins
6. Put it in the oven to brown
7. When serving, sprinkle with cinnamon sugar
