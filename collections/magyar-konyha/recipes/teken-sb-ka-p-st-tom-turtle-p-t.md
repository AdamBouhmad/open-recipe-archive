## Ingredients

- butter pastry
- egg white
- turtle
- salted water
- butter sauce
- boiled asparagus
- shrimp tails
- button mushrooms

## Directions

1. Prepare a butter pastry and roll it out to the width of a knife blade, make a bowl pâté from it, brush with egg white and bake nicely.
2. Clean the turtle as already described, cook in salted water, prepare a light butter sauce, add boiled asparagus, shelled shrimp tails and button mushrooms, as well as the turtle, and braise together.
3. When the pâté is baked, place on a serving dish, carefully cut off the lid and fill in the mixture nicely, put the lid back on, and bring quickly to the table.
