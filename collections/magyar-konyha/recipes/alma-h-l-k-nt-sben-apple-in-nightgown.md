## Ingredients

- Crumbly pastry
- Five or six tart apples
- 9 dekagram sugar
- A little ground vanilla
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Prepare crumbly pastry and roll it out to the thickness of a knife
2. Cut rectangular slices from it
3. Peel the apples, cut them into cubes, put them in a pot
4. Add the sugar and a little ground vanilla and stew them well
5. Brush the edges of the pastry with egg white
6. Put as much filling in the center as the pastry can hold
7. Fold up the four corners of the pastry and glue them together with egg white
8. Brush with egg white, bake until beautifully golden yellow
9. Sprinkle with sugar and serve to the table
