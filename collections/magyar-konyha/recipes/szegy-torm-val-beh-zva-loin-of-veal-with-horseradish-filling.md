## Ingredients

- beautiful piece of loin, about four kilograms
- almond-based horseradish sauce
- four egg yolks
- melted butter
- fine breadcrumbs
- green peas
- carrot

## Directions

1. Cook a beautiful piece of loin, about four kilograms, until tender and remove from broth and strain
2. Prepare an almond-based horseradish sauce, made thicker than for beef
3. Mix four egg yolks into the horseradish sauce
4. Coat the loin with this mixture to the thickness of a finger
5. Drizzle with melted butter and sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs
6. Half an hour before serving, place in the oven and bake until golden brown
7. Serve with green peas and carrots on the side
