## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams sugar rubbed on 2 oranges
- 12 egg yolks
- 4 deciliters good Somlyai wine
- 4 egg whites
- 21 dekagrams powdered sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Finely crush the sugar rubbed on 2 oranges and beat well with 12 egg yolks
2. Stir in 4 deciliters of good Somlyai wine
3. Place over charcoal fire and stir until it begins to thicken
4. Pour into a porcelain dish and cool
5. Place in the freezing container
6. When the ice cream has become a solid mass, bury it in ice for more than an hour
7. Meanwhile, beat 4 egg whites to very stiff peaks so no liquid remains
8. Mix in 21 dekagrams of finely sifted crushed sugar and a little vanilla gently and quickly with the flat side of a cooking spoon without stirring
9. Fill this mixture into a pastry bag and pipe beautiful tall Spanish puffs onto paper
10. Sprinkle finely with sugar using a small tulle bag
11. Bake in a cooled oven without allowing them to brown
12. Leave in the oven until they peel off the paper easily and dry
13. Turn the ice cream onto a platter and arrange the puffs in a spiral shape, using the largest ones for the first coil, then progressively smaller ones, with the smallest at the top so it resembles an artichoke
