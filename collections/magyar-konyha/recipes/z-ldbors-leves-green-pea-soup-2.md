## Ingredients

- butter the size of an egg
- 1 liter green peas
- green parsley, finely chopped
- a piece of sugar
- a little flour
- meat broth
- salt
- 1/4 liter sweet milk

## Directions

1. Put butter the size of an egg in a pot; when hot, add the washed green peas, finely chopped green parsley, and a piece of sugar
2. Stew covered over gentle heat
3. When soft, sprinkle a little flour on top and let it brown slightly
4. Thin with meat broth, season with salt
5. Add 1/4 liter of sweet milk
