## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams coffee
- 7 deciiliters cream
- 3 large cooking spoonfuls flour
- 10 dekagrams butter
- 8 egg yolks
- powdered sugar to taste
- 4 egg whites beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Roast the coffee well and place it hot into 7 deciiliters of hot cream
2. Cover well and let stand for half an hour so the cream absorbs the coffee aroma
3. Place 3 large cooking spoonfuls of flour in a pot, strain the cream through fine sieve onto the flour and mix well
4. Place on coals and stir until it begins to boil
5. Cook for a few more minutes and then cool
6. Beat 10 dekagrams of butter until foamy
7. Mix in the cooled paste and stir well with 8 egg yolks
8. Add powdered sugar to taste
9. Stir for another half hour
10. Gently fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
11. Place the paste in a buttered torte wrapper and bake for half an hour
12. Remove the ring, sprinkle with finely powdered sugar and serve quickly to the table
