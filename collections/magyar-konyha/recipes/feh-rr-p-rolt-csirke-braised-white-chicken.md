## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Salt
- Bacon
- Parsley root
- Yellow carrot
- Whole pepper
- Nutmeg flowers
- Broth
- Flour
- White paper

## Directions

1. After blanching the chicken, extract the breastbone.
2. Salt it and prepare it well.
3. Line a pot with bacon, parsley root, yellow carrot, whole pepper, and nutmeg flowers.
4. Place the chicken on top.
5. Cover with thin bacon slices.
6. Pour two spoonfuls of broth over it.
7. Place white paper on the pot, then put the lid on top.
8. Braise slowly.
9. After braising, remove the two chickens, cover them again with bacon to keep them white.
10. Sprinkle a little flour on the braised roots and pour enough broth over them to make a sauce.
