## Ingredients

- approximately 4 deciliters good black coffee
- 4 deciliters good cream
- 6 egg yolks
- 14 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- 2 dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Strain the coffee as cleanly as possible.
2. Mix the coffee with an equal amount of good cream.
3. In a pan, thoroughly beat together 6 egg yolks and 14 dekagrams finely ground sugar.
4. Add the mixed coffee to the eggs and sugar.
5. Place the pan on charcoal fire and stir constantly until it begins to thicken.
6. Remove immediately from heat to prevent curdling and pour into a clean pan.
7. Mix in 2 dekagrams dissolved isinglass.
8. Stir until cooled, then pour into molds where it remains until use.
9. Unmold as with other aspics.
