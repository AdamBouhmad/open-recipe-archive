## Ingredients

- almond paste (as in recipe 693)
- butter
- whipped cream or ice cream for filling

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as in recipe number 693
2. Obtain special metal cup molds from a tinsmith with a thick center pin to leave a large opening
3. Pour the dough into cups greased with butter
4. Bake at low heat
5. Once cooled, fill the opening with whipped cream or ice cream just before serving
