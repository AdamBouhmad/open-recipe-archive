## Ingredients

- 1.5 litres cherries
- 2 crumbly rolls, grated
- 11 dekagrams swollen, finely ground almonds
- zest of half a lemon
- sugar
- 7 dekagrams butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Wash the cherries, remove stems and pits, and chop coarsely
2. Moisten grated crumbly rolls with the cherry liquid
3. Add the finely ground almonds, minced lemon zest, and sugar to taste
4. Cream 7 dekagrams of butter and beat in 6 well-whisked egg yolks
5. Add the cherry mixture, almonds, and bread
6. Stir for a further quarter hour
7. Gently fold in the stiff foam of 4 egg whites
8. Put the mixture into a buttered mould and bake on a steady heat
