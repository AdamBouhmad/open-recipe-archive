## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters fresh strawberries
- 21 dekagramms sugar
- 3 tablespoons water
- juice of half a lemon
- sugar to taste
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Clean the strawberries and press through a sieve
2. Cook the sugar with 3 tablespoons water until sticky
3. Add the pressed strawberries and cook until thick
4. Place in a bowl and cool
5. Squeeze in the lemon juice and add sugar to taste
6. Stir for half an hour
7. While stirring, fold in the stiff peaks of 6 egg whites gradually
8. Quickly pile high on a butter-greased plate
9. Bake in a completely cooled oven for half an hour
10. Serve quickly to the table as it easily collapses
