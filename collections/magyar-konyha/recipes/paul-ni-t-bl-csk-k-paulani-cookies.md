## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams finely ground and sifted sugar
- 14 dekagrams flour
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 12 ground cloves
- zest of 1 lemon, finely chopped
- zest of 1 orange, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons Marsala or Malaga wine
- candied almonds

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar, flour, cinnamon, cloves, lemon zest, and orange zest on a board
2. Pour the wine over the mixture
3. Work it into a firm dough using a wide knife
4. Roll out to the thickness of a knife blade
5. Cut into strips two finger-widths wide
6. Cut boat shapes from these strips
7. Place a half-puffed almond in the middle of each boat
8. Coat the baking sheet with wax
9. As soon as the cookies go into the oven, brush with sugar water
10. Dry at very gentle heat
