## Ingredients

- small peeled Masansky apples with centers removed
- water
- sugar
- lemon juice
- preserved currants
- soda from recipe 390

## Directions

1. Cook small peeled Masansky apples with centers removed in water, sugar, and a little lemon juice until soft but not falling apart
2. Place in a deep cup
3. Fill with preserved currants
4. Pour soda (as prepared in recipe 390) over the apples
5. Place in a cold location
6. Before serving, arrange decoratively with Spanish pastries
