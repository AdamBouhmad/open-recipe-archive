## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram ripe cornelian cherries
- 40 dekagrams refined sugar
- 1.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Cook the ripe cornelian cherries in the refined sugar for five minutes
2. Set the syrup aside and allow it to cool completely
3. Pass through a fine sieve
4. Mix with the dissolved isinglass
5. Pour into the desired mold
6. Set in crushed ice and freeze
7. The sugar should be cleaned with nine deciliters of water so that after mixing with the isinglass, it will fill a one-liter mold
