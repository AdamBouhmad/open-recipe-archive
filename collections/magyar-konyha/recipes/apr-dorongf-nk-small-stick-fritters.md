## Ingredients

- half kilogram fine flour
- 3 dekagrams fresh butter
- salt
- 28 dekagrams finely ground almonds
- ground cinnamon
- finely chopped zest of 1 lemon
- 14 dekagrams powdered sugar
- 1 whole egg and 4 egg yolks
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Chop 3 dekagrams fresh butter into half kilogram flour, salt lightly and work well with the rolling pin
2. Mix in 28 dekagrams finely ground almonds, ground cinnamon, finely chopped lemon zest and 14 dekagrams powdered sugar thoroughly with the flour
3. Add 1 whole egg and 4 egg yolks, make the dough with a wide knife
4. Stretch to the thickness of a knife edge
5. Cut to the length and width of the baking sheet
6. Prepare fat in a small pot, tie the dough to the sheet with string and fry until golden yellow
