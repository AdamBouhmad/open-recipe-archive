## Ingredients

- butter
- fresh parsley, finely chopped
- freshly shelled peas
- flour
- meat broth
- sugar
- salt
- filled sausages (for garnish)

## Directions

1. Melt butter in a pan and slowly stew the finely chopped fresh parsley and the cleaned shelled peas
2. Stir frequently to prevent browning
3. Sprinkle with a little flour
4. Once the flour is absorbed, pour in broth and add a piece of sugar
5. Garnish with filled sausages
6. To keep the green peas green, cook in an uncovered pot; once cooked, drain quickly and pour cold water over them
7. The sauce should be prepared separately: melt a little butter, add a small spoonful of flour and brown it, add finely chopped fresh parsley and fry for one minute, dilute with good meat broth and cook, add the green peas with two spoons of sugar, shake frequently but do not let it cook long so as not to lose color
