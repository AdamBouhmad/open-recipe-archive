## Ingredients

- firm, tender, not too large kale heads
- salt water
- cooked veal
- duck liver (already breaded and fried)
- roll (soaked in milk)
- fresh parsley
- red onion
- cooked kale
- salt
- pepper
- good broth
- eggs
- twine
- sauce
- fried veal feet

## Directions

1. Clean the kale heads and hollow them out carefully
2. Cook in salted water and drain the liquid completely
3. Place on a sieve to drain thoroughly
4. Prepare the stuffing: chop cooked or braised veal, fresh parsley, and a small piece of red onion finely
5. Melt a small spoonful of goose fat in a pan, fry a spoonful of flour in it
6. Add the chopped parsley and onion, brown lightly
7. Add the chopped veal, mix well
8. Pour a little broth over it and boil well
9. Beat two eggs and add them while stirring continuously until a thick mass forms
10. Cool the mixture
11. Form this into a thick sausage shape
12. Separate the cabbage leaves and place the sausage inside
13. Arrange the leaves neatly so it forms a kale head again
14. Tie with twine and place in the prepared sauce
15. At serving time, cut the heads in half and place neatly on the dish
16. Pour the sauce over them and serve with fried veal feet
