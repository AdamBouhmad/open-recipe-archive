## Ingredients

- young kohlrabi
- fresh parsley
- fat
- chicken
- flour
- nutmeg flower
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Peel young kohlrabi and cut into thin knife-blade-thick slices
2. Cut out round slices with a small cutter
3. Braise with finely chopped fresh parsley and rendered fat, occasionally adding a little broth so they become soft while remaining white
4. Clean and portion one or two chickens
5. Braise the chicken pieces similarly with rendered fat
6. Sprinkle flour over the chicken to achieve the proper thickness
7. Add a little nutmeg flower and cook until the chickens are slightly tender
8. Add one or two spoonfuls of good sour cream to the kohlrabi
9. Once everything is braised tender, arrange the kohlrabi in the center of the plate and the chicken pieces beautifully around the edge
