## Ingredients

- four dekagrams finely crushed red china
- almond oil (nine grams total used)
- twenty-seven grams cleaned and melted beef marrow

## Directions

1. First grind the four dekagrams of finely crushed red china with a small amount of almond oil
2. Add enough almond oil so that nine grams total is used
3. Add this mixture in small portions to twenty-seven grams of cleaned and melted beef marrow
4. Grind together until cooled
5. If desired, a fragrant oil can be mixed in
