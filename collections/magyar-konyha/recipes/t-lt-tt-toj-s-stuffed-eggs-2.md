## Ingredients

- 10 eggs
- bread roll soaked in milk
- egg yolks, grated
- sour cream
- tablespoon powdered sugar
- teaspoon cinnamon
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- 7 deciliter sour cream
- saffron
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Hard boil the eggs and cut them in half, removing the yolks.
2. For ten eggs, chop less than one bread roll that has been soaked in milk.
3. Grate the egg yolks and add them to the bread.
4. Add a little sour cream, a tablespoon of powdered sugar, and a teaspoon of cinnamon, and mix together.
5. Butter a flat dish and sprinkle with breadcrumbs.
6. Place the halved eggs on the dish and fill them with the prepared mixture.
7. Take 7 deciliter of sour cream for ten eggs, mix in saffron, cinnamon, and sugar, and pour over the eggs in the dish.
8. Place in the oven and brown until golden, then serve in the same dish.
