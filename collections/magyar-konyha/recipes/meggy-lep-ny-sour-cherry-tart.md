## Ingredients

- crumbly dough
- ripe sour cherries
- sugar
- egg white

## Directions

1. Prepare crumbly dough and roll out to three knife-widths thickness
2. Place on a baking sheet, make a finger-width border cut along the edge and fold up all around
3. Pit beautiful ripe sour cherries, strain their juice, and arrange on the tart
4. Sprinkle with sugar, brush the folded edges with egg white, and bake
5. When done, cut into equal portions, sprinkle with sugar and arrange neatly on a platter
