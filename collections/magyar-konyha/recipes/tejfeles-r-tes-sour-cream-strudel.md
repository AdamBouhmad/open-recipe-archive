## Ingredients

- strudel dough
- 10 decagrams butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 3 deciliters sour cream
- 4 egg whites
- 14 decagrams cleaned raisins
- 7 decagrams chopped almonds or fine breadcrumbs
- 7 decagrams broken sugar

## Directions

1. Stretch strudel dough finely and let it dry for 15 minutes
2. Beat 10 decagrams butter with 4 egg yolks until fluffy
3. Stir in 3 deciliters sour cream
4. Fold in the stiff peaks of 4 egg whites
5. Brush the stretched dough with this mixture
6. Sprinkle with 14 decagrams raisins, 7 decagrams chopped almonds or breadcrumbs, and 7 decagrams broken sugar, leaving about a hand's breadth empty at one edge
7. Fold gently
8. Place in a buttered baking dish
9. Bake slowly
