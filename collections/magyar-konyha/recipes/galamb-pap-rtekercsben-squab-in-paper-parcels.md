## Ingredients

- 2-3 squabs
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- button mushrooms
- squab liver
- pepper
- anchovies
- bacon
- white paper
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean 2-3 squabs and split each lengthwise into two pieces
2. Season with salt and soften with a knife to make them slightly wider
3. Chop together parsley, onion, button mushrooms, squab liver, pepper, and anchovies for the stuffing
4. Slice bacon thinly
5. Place squab halves on white paper
6. Spread the inside of each squab piece with the stuffing
7. Continue this process until all squab pieces and stuffing are used
8. Cover the meat with additional bacon slices
9. Wrap in paper and fold the ends neatly
10. Make as many parcels as there are squab halves
11. Place on a grill, brush with butter, and roast slowly
12. Serve in the paper on the table
