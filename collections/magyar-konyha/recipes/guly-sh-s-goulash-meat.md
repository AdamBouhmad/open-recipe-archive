## Ingredients

- meat cut into cubes, one kilogram
- water, one and a half litres
- onions, three
- caraway seeds
- salt
- carrot

## Directions

1. Cook the nicely cut meat in cubes with one and a half litres of water, three onions, caraway seeds, salt, and carrot in a pot for four hours. If the liquid cooks down too much, you can add water. Commonly, boiled potatoes or dumplings are also served with this.
