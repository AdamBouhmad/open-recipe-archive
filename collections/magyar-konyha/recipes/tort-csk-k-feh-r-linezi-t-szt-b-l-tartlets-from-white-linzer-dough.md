## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams flour
- 28 dekagrams butter
- 14 dekagrams blanched almonds, soaked
- 14 dekagrams ground sugar
- zest of 1 lemon, finely chopped
- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 1 whole egg
- jam for filling

## Directions

1. Work 28 dekagrams of butter into thin pieces into 28 dekagrams of flour on a board, working well with a rolling pin
2. Add 14 dekagrams of blanched, finely ground almonds, the same amount of finely ground sugar, the finely chopped zest of 1 lemon, and 4 hard-boiled egg yolks
3. Bind the dough together with 1 whole egg using the rolling pin
4. Roll out to knife-blade thickness
5. Fill one half with cooked currants
6. Cut star shapes from the center of the other half with a small cutter
7. Brush the edges of the filled dough with egg white
8. Place the cut-out piece on top
9. Brush with beaten egg and bake until golden
