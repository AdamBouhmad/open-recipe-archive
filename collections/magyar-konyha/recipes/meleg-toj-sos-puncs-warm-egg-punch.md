## Ingredients

- half a bottle of Bakateri or other white wine
- half a bottle of arak
- eighty dekagrams of crushed sugar
- juice of two oranges and two lemons
- finely peeled zest of one orange
- eighteen eggs
- seven deciliters of water

## Directions

1. In a deep vessel, combine six whole eggs and twelve egg yolks with the sugar, stirring well together
2. Slowly add the wine, arak, and water, as well as the zest of one orange
3. Place the vessel in a pan over charcoal fire
4. Beat the mixture with birch twigs tied together until it thickens completely and fills the vessel with fine foam
5. Remove from heat and continue beating for six more minutes
6. Strain through a fine sieve into glasses
