## Ingredients

- one wild duck
- salt
- marinade
- cream
- butter
- flour
- duck liver
- capers

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the duck lightly, then clean thoroughly.
2. Remove the entrails, salt well, and if weather permits, marinate for one day.
3. Lard the duck and roast until golden-brown, basting frequently with the marinade and cream.
4. When roasted, finely chop the duck's liver and mix it into a roux made from melted butter and flour.
5. Add a few spoonfuls of the marinade and juice that dripped from the duck during roasting, along with two spoonfuls of cream.
6. Add capers and pour this sauce over the duck when serving.
