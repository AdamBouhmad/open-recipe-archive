## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams butter
- 1 whole egg
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 dekagrams yeast
- 6 tablespoons lukewarm cream
- 7 dekagrams powdered sugar
- 25 dekagrams flour
- salt
- melted butter
- finely chopped almonds
- coarsely broken sugar
- marmalade

## Directions

1. Beat 14 dekagrams butter until foamy, then slowly add 1 whole egg and 4 egg yolks
2. Dissolve 2 dekagrams yeast in 6 tablespoons lukewarm cream and fold in gradually
3. Mix in 7 dekagrams powdered sugar, 25 dekagrams flour and a little salt
4. Spread the mixture evenly on a long baking sheet brushed with melted butter using a large knife
5. Let rise, then brush with beaten egg, sprinkle with finely chopped almonds and coarsely broken sugar
6. Bake until beautifully golden yellow, then cut into rectangular pieces and fill with marmalade of your choice
