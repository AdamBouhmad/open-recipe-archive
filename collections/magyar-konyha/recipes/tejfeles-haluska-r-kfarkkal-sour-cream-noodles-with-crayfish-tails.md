## Ingredients

- 7 dekagrams butter
- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons sour cream
- salt
- flour
- 7 deciliters milk
- crayfish tails
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cream 7 dekagrams butter
2. Add 4 eggs one after another
3. Add 4 tablespoons sour cream, salt, and enough flour to make it properly firm
4. Bring 7 deciliters milk to a boil
5. Drop noodles into milk with a spoon and cook
6. Remove and place on a buttered plate
7. Place crayfish tails between them and pour sour cream over
8. Decorate the top with nicely fried crayfish tails
