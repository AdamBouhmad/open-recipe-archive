## Ingredients

- four decilitres of milk
- three eggs
- preserved plums for filling
- butter
- sour cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. From four decilitres of milk and three eggs, make thin pancakes
2. Fill them very thinly with preserved plums
3. Fold them together and cut them in half in the middle, as well as trim the uneven two ends
4. Place a little butter and good sour cream on a plate, then arrange the filled pancakes in a star shape on the bottom of the plate
5. Again add sour cream and butter, then pancake, and so on, until everything is arranged
6. Finally add a little more sour cream and small pieces of butter on top
7. Let it come to a boil slightly and serve sprinkled with sugar on the table
