## Ingredients

- young tender pork leg
- salt
- pepper
- carrots, sliced into rings
- celery, sliced into rings
- parsley root, sliced into rings
- considerable amount of caraway

## Directions

1. From a young tender pork leg, remove the bone without cutting the meat in two, then tie the leg into a nice round shape and salt it
2. Slice carrots, celery, and parsley root into rings, and place on a long baking sheet with pepper and a considerable amount of caraway to roast
3. When it begins to brown, pour half a liter of water over it and braise it until quite tender
4. Press the braised vegetables through a sieve and pour the pressed vegetables into the baking sheet under the roast
5. Slice the roast into nice rings, place in the center of the plate and pour the broth underneath, and serve very hot
