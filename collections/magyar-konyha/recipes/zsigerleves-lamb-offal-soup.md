## Ingredients

- lamb liver
- lamb lungs
- vegetables
- parsley
- carrot
- salt
- white roux
- fresh green parsley
- hard-boiled eggs
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Wash the lamb liver and lungs and put on to cook
2. Add vegetables, parsley, carrot, and a little salt
3. When cooked, strain the broth and chop the lungs and liver finely
4. Make a white roux, add finely chopped fresh green parsley, pour in the broth and add the chopped lungs and liver
5. Hard-boil a pair of eggs, chop them together, add to the soup and let it come to a boil
6. Sour cream can be added when serving
