## Ingredients

- chicken
- half a roll
- two hard-boiled eggs
- good meat broth
- marrow, finely chopped
- fried liver
- bread roll

## Directions

1. Cut chicken and half a roll into pieces and bread them
2. Pound two hard-boiled eggs together with the breaded pieces in a mortar
3. Place in a pot and pour good meat broth over it
4. Let cook for one hour and strain through a sieve
5. If the soup is too thick, thin it with a little clear broth
6. When serving, add finely chopped marrow that has been cooked until tender, fried liver, and bread
