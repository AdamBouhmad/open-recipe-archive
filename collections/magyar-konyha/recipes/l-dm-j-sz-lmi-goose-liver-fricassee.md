## Ingredients

- one or two beautiful goose livers
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- thin bacon slices
- onions
- flour
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Wash the goose livers and salt them, mixing pepper into the salt
2. Stud each liver with three or four cloves
3. Place thin bacon slices and two heads of onion cut into slices in a pot
4. Place the livers on top and cover with a few more thin bacon slices
5. Put live coals on the pot lid and braise slowly
6. When nicely golden, remove the livers and sprinkle a small spoonful of flour into the pot, allowing it to brown
7. Thin with a good ladle of beef broth and cook well
8. Remove the cloves from the livers and cut away all edge pieces from the liver
9. Cut thick slices from the center of the liver and arrange on the dish rim, keeping warm
10. When the sauce is well reduced, strain it
11. Press the onion, bacon, and liver remnants through a sieve into a small pot
12. Thin with one or two spoonfuls of sauce and cook well until it becomes a thick paste
13. Spread this paste on the bottom of the dish
14. Arrange the liver slices in a crown around the fricassee
15. Pour a few spoonfuls of sauce over the liver and serve hot
