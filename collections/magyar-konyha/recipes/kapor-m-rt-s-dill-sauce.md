## Ingredients

- fresh dill, cleaned and washed
- rendered fat
- flour
- meat stock
- three to four tablespoons sour cream
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Finely chop the clean dill as you would cabbage
2. Brown one tablespoon of flour in rendered fat
3. Add the dill to this
4. Thin with meat stock
5. When sufficiently cooked, add three to four tablespoons of sour cream
6. Cook a bit more and the sauce is ready
7. If you want to give this sauce a sour taste, cook it with a little lemon juice
