## Ingredients

- walnuts
- butter
- sugar
- egg yolks
- egg white foam
- cornstarch
- preserved fruit
- lemon or orange icing

## Directions

1. Combine walnuts lightly into the mixture.
2. Butter two equal cake layers and their rims, fill each with half the mixture, and bake at gentle heat.
3. Turn out onto a sieve and once cooled, fill one layer with preserved fruit and place the other on top.
4. Coat with lemon or orange icing.
5. Note that the cake layers must not be very thick, otherwise they will easily become fatty and heavy; conversely, if baked in thin layers, the cake will be very light and airy.
6. The walnuts must not be broken but only chopped.
