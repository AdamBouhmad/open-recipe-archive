## Ingredients

- raw liver soaked in cold water
- pork caul fat
- large red onion
- lard
- black pepper
- paprika
- cooked millet porridge
- black pepper
- hot water
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak raw liver in cold water and chop finely with a chopping knife, removing any vessels and knots
2. Remove all fat from the white liver and briefly boil and finely chop a handful of clean caul fat
3. Braise a large red onion in lard with pepper and a small amount of paprika
4. Mix in a moderate amount of cooked millet porridge, plenty of black pepper, a few spoonfuls of hot water to make the filling loose, and proper salt
5. The liver filling should be well-fatted and loose so the sausage will be tender
6. Add plenty of spices
7. Fill the intestines with a sausage filler, secure the ends with small wooden sticks, and boil in hot salted water
