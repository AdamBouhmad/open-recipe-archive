## Ingredients

- Twenty-eight dekagrams rice
- One and one-half liters milk
- Twenty-one dekagrams butter, beaten fluffy
- Four whole eggs
- Four egg yolks
- Ten dekagrams powdered sugar
- Vanilla
- Optional: raisins and currants

## Directions

1. Pick over and wash rice
2. Cook in one and one-half liters milk until not quite soft
3. Beat butter until fluffy
4. Mix in four whole eggs and four egg yolks one by one
5. Add cooled rice, powdered sugar and vanilla
6. Do not stir vigorously so rice grains remain whole
7. Pour into a buttered dish
8. Place in oven and bake until golden brown
9. Break apart with a spatula
10. Sprinkle with sugar and serve
11. Optional raisins and currants may be mixed into the dough
