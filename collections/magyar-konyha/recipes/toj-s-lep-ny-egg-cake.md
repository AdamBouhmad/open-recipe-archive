## Ingredients

- 10 hard-boiled eggs
- 10 dekagrams butter, beaten to foam
- bread soaked in milk
- green parsley, finely chopped
- 2 whole eggs and 2 yolks
- 2 deciliters sour cream
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop the 10 hard-boiled eggs finely
2. Mix into the foamed butter: bread soaked in milk, finely chopped green parsley
3. Beat in 2 whole eggs and 2 yolks
4. Add 2 deciliters sour cream with a little salt
5. Place the mixture in a buttered pan
6. Bake nicely
7. When done, turn out onto a plate and serve at table
