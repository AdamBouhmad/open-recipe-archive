## Ingredients

- lampreys
- salt
- oysters
- pike liver, minced
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- pea broth
- lemon juice and peel
- sour cream

## Directions

1. After opening the lampreys, salt them and lard them so they maintain their shape and cook them well seasoned
2. Take oysters from their shells, cook them through, add minced pike liver and braise both together in butter
3. Brown some breadcrumbs in butter, pour 2 tablespoons pea broth over it, add lemon juice and peel and 2 tablespoons sour cream
4. Arrange lampreys on a plate, pour the sauce over, bring once to a boil and serve
