## Ingredients

- one kilogram fresh mutton tallow
- seventy-five dekagrams vegetable oil
- thirteen dekagrams hair powder
- four and one-half dekagrams amber dye
- three dekagrams orange blossom oil
- two dekagrams cedarwood oil
- two dekagrams musk dye
- seventy drops of cinnamon oil
- four grams Peruvian balsam
- four grams rosewood oil
- two grams clove oil
- four grams thyme oil

## Directions

1. Combine one kilogram of fresh mutton tallow with seventy-five dekagrams of vegetable oil, thirteen dekagrams of hair powder, four and one-half dekagrams of amber dye, three dekagrams of orange blossom oil, two dekagrams of cedarwood oil, two dekagrams of musk dye, seventy drops of cinnamon oil, four grams of Peruvian balsam, four grams of rosewood oil, two grams of clove oil, and four grams of thyme oil
