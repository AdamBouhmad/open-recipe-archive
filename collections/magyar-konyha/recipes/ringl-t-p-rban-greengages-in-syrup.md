## Ingredients

- largest beautiful green ripe greengages
- sugar
- water
- cooked sugar water

## Directions

1. Wipe the largest beautiful green ripe greengages individually and pierce several times with a needle
2. Place them in the necessary jar, fill halfway with cooked and cooled sugar water, bind well and simmer for 3-4 minutes (per instructions 961)
3. All varieties of plums are prepared the same way, removing the pit first, then arrange neatly, fill halfway with cooked sugar water, and treat as with the others
4. For greengages, add 1 small spoonful of fine rum to each jar, which enhances the good flavor
