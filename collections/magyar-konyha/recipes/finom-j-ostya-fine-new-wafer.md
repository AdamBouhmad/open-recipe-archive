## Ingredients

- 28 dekagramm finely ground sugar with a small stick vanilla
- 21 dekagramm fine flour
- 4 eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 7 dekagramm melted butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Mix well in a bowl 28 dekagramm finely ground sugar with a small stick vanilla, 21 dekagramm fine flour, 4 eggs, and 4 egg yolks
2. Add 7 dekagramm melted butter and enough cream so the dough is light and liquid
3. Mix well again
4. Heat the iron, coat with a little butter, wipe with paper
5. Heat again, place a large teaspoon of dough on the iron
6. Bake until nicely golden-red
7. Remove the iron from the fire at once and either roll into thin tubes or shape into a funnel
8. Repeat until all is baked
9. Keep the fire even and the iron at constant heat
