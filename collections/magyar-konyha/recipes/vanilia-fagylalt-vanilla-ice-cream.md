## Ingredients

- 21 decagrams sugar
- 1.5 grams ground vanilla
- 8 egg yolks
- 7 deciliters boiled cream

## Directions

1. In a pan, mix well the sugar, ground vanilla, and egg yolks
2. Pour on 7 deciliters of boiled cream and mix well
3. Place on a charcoal fire and stir continuously until it begins to thicken
4. Quickly remove from fire, pour into another pan, and stir for a while longer to prevent curdling
5. When cooled, press through a fine sieve, pour into the ice cream mold, and proceed as with other ice creams
