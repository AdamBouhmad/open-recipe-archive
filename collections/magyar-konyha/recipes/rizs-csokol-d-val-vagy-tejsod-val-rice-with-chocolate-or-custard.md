## Ingredients

- 21 dekagrams rice, blanched
- 1 liter heavy cream
- 4 tablets finely grated chocolate
- 8 dekagrams softened and finely ground almonds
- 10 dekagrams sugar
- whole preserved cherries
- 4 deciliters heavy cream
- 5 egg yolks
- 10 dekagrams ground sugar
- finely ground vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook the blanched rice in one liter of heavy cream
2. Add the finely grated chocolate, softened almonds, and sugar
3. Cook until the rice is soft but the grains remain whole
4. Pile high in the center of a dish
5. Decorate with whole preserved cherries
6. Bake in a cooled oven
7. For the custard: Whip four deciliters of heavy cream with five egg yolks, ten dekagrams ground sugar, and a little finely ground vanilla
8. Place over low heat and stir constantly until it begins to thicken
9. Remove quickly from heat to prevent curdling
10. Pour the custard over the baked rice
