## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams soaked and finely ground almond
- 14 dekagrams ground sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 lemon, finely chopped zest
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix 14 dekagrams of soaked and finely ground almond with 14 dekagrams of ground sugar
2. Beat 2 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks into it alternately
3. When well and foamy, add the finely chopped zest of 1 lemon
4. Mix well
5. Fill a mold (kádka) buttered with this mixture
6. Bake at gentle heat
