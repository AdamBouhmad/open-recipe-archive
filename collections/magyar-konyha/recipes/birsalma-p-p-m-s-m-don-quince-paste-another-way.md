## Ingredients

- 3 large quinces
- 2 dry, finely grated rolls
- half a coffee cup heavy cream
- 9 dekagrams almonds, finely ground
- butter, whipped until frothy, egg-sized piece
- zest of 1 lemon, finely chopped
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- sugar to taste
- 4 egg whites, whipped stiff
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the quinces and cool them
2. Peel and grate them
3. Moisten the grated rolls with the heavy cream
4. Finely grind the almonds and mix with the rolls and cream
5. Add the mixture to an egg-sized piece of whipped butter
6. Add the finely chopped lemon zest, two whole eggs, and four egg yolks
7. Stir everything for half an hour
8. Add sugar until sufficiently sweet
9. Gently fold in the stiffly whipped egg whites
10. Pour into a buttered mold sprinkled with fine breadcrumbs
11. Bake in the oven
12. This paste requires slightly longer baking than other pastes
