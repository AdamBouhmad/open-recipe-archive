## Ingredients

- turkey and duck wings and giblets
- green parsley
- carrot
- leek
- butter sauce
- 30-40 crayfish tails
- asparagus
- veal
- roll soaked in milk
- marrow
- red onion
- green parsley
- salt
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook turkey and duck wings and giblets with green parsley, carrot, and leek until tender
2. Prepare a light butter sauce
3. Cut the wings and giblets finely and place in the sauce
4. Add 30-40 crayfish tails and finely chopped asparagus
5. Combine veal, half a roll soaked in milk, a little marrow, red onion, green parsley chopped finely, and salt with two egg yolks
6. Form small dumplings from this mixture
7. Fry them until golden brown
8. Add to the wings and giblets in the sauce
9. Bring slightly to a boil together and serve
