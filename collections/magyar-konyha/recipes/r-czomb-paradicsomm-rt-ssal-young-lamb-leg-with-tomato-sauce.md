## Ingredients

- young lamb leg
- bacon
- red onion, sliced
- thyme
- ivy leaf
- water
- vinegar
- 24-30 tomatoes
- flour
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Place the lamb leg in a copper pan lined with bacon, red onion slices, thyme, and ivy leaf
2. Pour water and vinegar over it
3. Braise until tender
4. Once the liquid has reduced, add the tomatoes
5. Cover and cook until very soft
6. Remove the meat
7. Sprinkle flour into the liquid and brown it
8. Pour good meat broth over it
9. Pass through a sieve over the meat
10. Boil together repeatedly
