## Ingredients

- 7 decilitres boiling cream
- 12 dekagrams rice
- 10 dekagrams butter
- 6 egg yolks
- 7 dekagrams finely ground almonds
- vanilla
- 10 dekagrams ground sugar
- 4 egg whites
- breadcrumb powder
- apricot compote

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in boiling cream until soft
2. Cool and crush fine in a mortar
3. Mix the creamed butter with 6 egg yolks, the finely ground almonds, vanilla, and sugar
4. Fold in the stiff foam of 4 egg whites
5. Butter a mould and coat with fine breadcrumb powder
6. Fill half with the pap and stuff with apricot compote or other filling
7. Cover with the remaining pap and bake slowly
