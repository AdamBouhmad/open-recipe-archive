## Ingredients

- Any poultry
- Salt
- One and a half kilos of seedless raisins
- Three liters of cooking liquid
- Thin red roux
- Saffron, a good amount
- Lemon with peel, in quantity sufficient for sourness

## Directions

1. Cook the poultry in plenty of salted broth until soft
2. When the meat is cooked, add the seedless raisins to three liters of broth and cook until soft
3. Thicken with a thin red roux
4. The broth should not be thick
5. Add lemon with peel in sufficient quantity to make it appropriately sour
6. Add good saffron to achieve a beautiful yellow color
