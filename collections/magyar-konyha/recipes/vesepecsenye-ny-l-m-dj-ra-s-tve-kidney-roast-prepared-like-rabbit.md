## Ingredients

- kidney
- brine used for rabbit
- salt
- bacon slices
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Remove the membrane from the kidney and cut off the bones
2. Marinate as one would marinate rabbit
3. Lard in four rows
4. Roast for two hours over steady heat
5. Baste frequently with the brine during roasting
6. Before fully cooked, baste several times with sour cream to give it a nice colour
7. Prepare the same sauce as for rabbit, with which capers may be mixed
