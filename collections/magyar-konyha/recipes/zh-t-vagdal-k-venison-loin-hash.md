## Ingredients

- leftover roasted venison loin or haunch
- bacon slices
- carrot slices
- onion slices
- cloves
- flour
- brown broth
- lemon zest
- good sour cream
- capers
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Remove meat from bones and cut into large cubes
2. Place bacon, carrot, and onion slices and a few cloves in a pot, add the venison bones on top
3. Cover and braise until nicely light brown
4. Sprinkle with two small tablespoons flour and brown
5. Add a ladleful of brown broth, some lemon zest, and a coffee cup of good sour cream
6. Cook well and pass through a sieve
7. Add one tablespoon capers and the minced meat
8. Cook with frequent stirring until the sauce thickens well
9. Pile in the center of the dish and surround with potato rounds
