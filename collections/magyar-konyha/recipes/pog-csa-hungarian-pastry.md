## Ingredients

- four decilitres of flour
- four decilitres of finely chopped lard
- salt
- two egg yolks
- three tablespoons of sour cream
- two tablespoons of wine
- two dekagrams of yeast dissolved in milk

## Directions

1. On a work surface with a rolling pin, work together four decilitres of flour, four decilitres of finely chopped lard, and a little salt.
2. Add two egg yolks, three tablespoons of sour cream, two tablespoons of wine, and two dekagrams of yeast dissolved in milk, and knead by hand until it becomes smooth dough, roll out very thin, fold together, roll out again, and repeat this three times until at the fourth rolling out a strip the width of a finger remains.
3. Cut out with a medium-sized doughnut cutter and arrange nicely on a baking sheet, place in a warm spot to rise.
4. When risen, make light cuts on the surface of the pogácsa with a knife, brush with egg and bake until nicely golden. This very simple dish can be enjoyed cold and warm, and is especially a favorite food of men.
