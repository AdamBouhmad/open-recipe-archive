## Ingredients

- half kilogram beautiful, large ripe raspberries
- half kilogram sugar
- one glass of water

## Directions

1. For half kilogram of beautiful large ripe raspberries, use the same amount of sugar, which cook with one glass of water over embers until it forms a jelly-like consistency when tested, then remove from heat and cool. Add the raspberries and briefly bring to a rapid boil over high heat without stirring or shaking, so the fruit remains whole. If making a sauce or filling, press the raspberries through a sieve and proceed as above.
