## Ingredients

- Sponge cake batter
- 10 dekagrams blanched, dried, and finely chopped almonds
- 10 dekagrams crushed sugar
- Well-ripened, peeled peaches
- Chocolate icing

## Directions

1. Prepare sponge cake batter and spread on buttered baking sheet two knife-widths thick
2. Bake slowly at gentle heat
3. When the sponge cake has stood in the oven for a few minutes and a skin has formed, thickly sprinkle with sugared almonds without removing the sheet completely from the oven
4. Continue baking until golden yellow
5. Remove and cut immediately with sharp thin knife into pieces the size of playing cards
6. Peel from the sheet
7. Cook well-ripened, peeled peaches with equal amount of crushed sugar into marmalade
8. Coat half of the slices with marmalade one knife-width thick
9. Place the other half on top
10. Draw black stripes one finger width wide on four sides with chocolate icing
11. When dry, arrange neatly on serving plate
