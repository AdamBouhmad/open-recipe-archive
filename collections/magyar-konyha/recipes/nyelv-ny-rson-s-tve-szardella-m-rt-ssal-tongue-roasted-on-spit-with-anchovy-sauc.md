## Ingredients

- fresh large tongue
- bacon
- anchovy
- butter
- 2 decilitres sour cream
- bread crumbs
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Blanch fresh large tongue until tender, then remove skin.
2. Do not remove from pot until wanting to put on spit; similarly must serve immediately from spit to table.
3. After removing skin, lard neatly with two parts bacon and one part anchovy.
4. Thread on thin spit.
5. Melt a piece of butter, add 2 decilitres sour cream.
6. During roasting baste the tongue with this mixture three or four times.
7. Sprinkle with bread crumbs, squeeze lemon juice over it, baste once more with the sauce.
8. Roast quickly, place on dish and pour sauce under it.
