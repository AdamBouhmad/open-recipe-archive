## Ingredients

- ripe grapes
- bread crumbs
- butter
- strudel dough
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Seed ripe grapes
2. Fry bread crumbs in butter
3. Make common strudel dough and roll it out
4. Sprinkle with grape seeds, bread crumbs, sugar, and cinnamon
5. Roll up lightly and bake
