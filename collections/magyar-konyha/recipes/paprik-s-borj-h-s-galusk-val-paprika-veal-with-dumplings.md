## Ingredients

- fresh parsley
- red onion
- butter
- salt
- veal, cut into cubes
- flour
- meat broth
- paprika
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Chop parsley and red onion together and braise in butter
2. Season with salt
3. Add the cubed veal and braise
4. Dust with flour
5. Moisten with meat broth
6. Braise until tender
7. Add paprika and a few spoonfuls of sour cream
