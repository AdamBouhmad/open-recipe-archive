## Ingredients

- pancake batter
- four eggs
- fifteen crayfish tails with claws
- half a roll soaked in milk
- butter
- green parsley
- mushrooms
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Prepare pancakes and set aside
2. Make a batter from four eggs
3. Shell and clean fifteen crayfish tails and claws
4. Coarsely chop the crayfish, the bread soaked in milk, and mushrooms that have been braised in butter with green parsley
5. Cream butter with several tablespoons of sour cream
6. Mix in the chopped ingredients
7. Spread the filling on the pancakes and place on a plate
8. Add sour cream and butter on top, place hot coals above, and brown lightly
