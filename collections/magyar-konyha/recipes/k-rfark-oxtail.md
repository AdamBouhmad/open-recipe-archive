## Ingredients

- oxtail
- salt
- red onion
- carrot
- lemon zest
- peppercorns
- bay leaves
- thyme
- clove pepper
- vinegar
- water
- melted fat
- flour
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut the oxtail into thin pieces
2. Wash it well with warm water and salt it
3. Place it in a pot with red onion, carrot, lemon zest, several peppercorns, bay leaf, thyme, and clove pepper
4. Fill the pot with vinegar and water and cook the oxtail until it is half soft, then remove the fat
5. Brown two spoonfuls of flour in melted fat and dilute it with the above sauce, which you have first strained
6. Add the oxtail with three spoonfuls of sour cream and braise until completely tender
7. This can also be served in a puff pastry vol-au-vent
