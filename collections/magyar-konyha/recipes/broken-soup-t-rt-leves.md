## Ingredients

- large pike head
- salt water
- pea water
- pike
- bread soaked in milk
- eggs
- green parsley

## Directions

1. Made exactly as on meat days, except use a large pike head instead of chicken and pea water instead of meat broth
2. Serve with pike dumplings
3. For the pike dumplings: cook a good piece of pike, about one kilogram, in salted water
4. Clean of bones and skin
5. Add two beaten bread slices soaked in milk and squeezed out, three beaten eggs and green parsley
