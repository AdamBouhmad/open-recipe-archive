## Ingredients

- raspberries
- 7 deciliter raspberry juice
- 2 lemons
- 35 dekagramm sugar
- 4.5 dekagramm dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Press the cleaned raspberries through cloth
2. To 7 deciliter of raspberry juice, add juice of 2 lemons and 35 dekagramm sugar dissolved in the juice
3. Mix in 4.5 dekagramm dissolved isinglass
4. Pass through clean cloth
5. Pour into a mold and freeze on ice
