## Ingredients

- pork caul fat lard
- flour
- eggs
- salt water
- filling options: cheese, rice, apple, cabbage, or jam

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as for other filled pastries
2. Brush the stretched dough generously with pork caul fat lard on a stretching board, not on the table
3. Do not cut off the edges but fold them back and stretch 6, 10, or even 12 layers on top of each other
4. When all layers are stretched, fold the entire sheet accordion-style into eight layers and allow to rest in cold winter weather for several days if needed
5. When ready to use, stretch to knife-blade thickness and fill with cheese, rice, apple, cabbage, or jam as desired
6. Gather all edges of the dough together to form a round filled pastry
7. Bake quickly in a hot oven
