## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams shelled hazelnuts, dried well in cloth
- 28 dekagrams sifted sugar
- egg white
- apricot marmalade
- lemon icing

## Directions

1. Mix the hazelnuts well with the sifted sugar
2. On a board with a wide knife, work it well with egg white added slowly, so the dough is not too soft
3. Stretch out on a sugar-dusted board to two knife-blade widths thick
4. Cut with a sharp knife dipped in sugar into finger-length and thumb-wide sticks
5. Place on thinly waxed parchment and dry in a well-cooled oven
6. Remove with a knife and cool
7. Fill with apricot marmalade, place another on top, and dip in lemon icing
