## Ingredients

- 500 grams blanched almonds, finely chopped
- 8 egg whites
- 56 dekagrams sugar, finely crushed and sifted

## Directions

1. Finely chop the almonds, taking care that the pieces are as equal as possible
2. Whip the 8 egg whites into stiff peaks and gently mix in the finely crushed and sifted sugar
3. Place a small spoonful of this mixture on the chopped almonds and gently roll it in the almonds by hand
4. Using a card, place on waxed parchment and form into a crescent shape
5. Continue until all the mixture has been rolled in the almonds
6. Bake the crescents in gentle heat
