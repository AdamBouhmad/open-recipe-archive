## Ingredients

- 28 decagrams calf liver
- three-quarter part of a roll
- two eggs
- fat
- good meat broth
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Fry the calf liver, roll, and two eggs in fat
2. Pound finely in a mortar
3. Place in a pot and pour good meat broth over it
4. Add a little nutmeg
5. Cook for half an hour
6. Strain through a sieve and return to the pot
7. Cook a little longer
8. Serve over fried liver dumplings
