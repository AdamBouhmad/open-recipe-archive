## Ingredients

- half kilogram sugar
- one glass of water
- good soup apricots
- apricot syrup, same amount as sugar

## Directions

1. Cook half a kilogram of sugar with one glass of water, well clarified, until it threads.
2. Then pit good apricots, press them through a sieve, and add them to the clarified and cooled sugar, cooking over charcoal heat for a short while.
3. Test on a plate and if it does not run but quickly becomes gelatinous, it is good.
4. Then when cooled, pour into glasses as with the others.
5. The pitted apricots can be cut into small pieces and mixed directly into the sugar without sieving; for other purposes treat it as above; this latter version is even better for filled pastries.
