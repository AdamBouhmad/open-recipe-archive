## Ingredients

- 14 dekagramms finely ground sugar
- 14 dekagramms almonds
- 14 dekagramms fine chocolate
- 10 dekagramms butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 5 egg whites
- 2 tablespoons apricot jam
- 4 egg whites (for topping)
- sugar

## Directions

1. Grind the sugar finely and moisten the almonds during grinding to keep them from becoming oily
2. Place the chocolate in a cooled oven until softened
3. Quickly mix in the butter, then the sugar and almonds
4. Slowly add 8 egg yolks
5. When beaten foamy, add the stiff peaks of 5 egg whites
6. Fill a deep and tall butter-greased mold
7. Bake at gentle but steady heat
8. Turn out half an hour before serving and cool slightly
9. Mix 2 tablespoons apricot jam with the same amount of sugar
10. Fold in the stiff peaks of 4 egg whites gradually
11. Fill a Spanish pastry bag and pipe strips into any indentations in the mold
12. Pipe a crown shape at the bottom, then successively smaller ones above, resembling a pineapple
13. Return to the oven until the meringue is dry
14. Serve quickly to the table
15. Note: Turn out onto a flat plate and place this on a plate covered with a tablecloth
