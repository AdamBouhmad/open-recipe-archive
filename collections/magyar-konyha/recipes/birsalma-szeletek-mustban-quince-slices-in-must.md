## Ingredients

- good must
- quince

## Directions

1. Semi-cook good must and quince in desired quantities
2. Remove the foam and pour into a new clay pot
3. Bring to a boil a few times over gentle charcoal
4. Add cleaned quince cut into quarters
5. Cook until sufficiently soft, adding finely ground cinnamon and clove buds during cooking
6. When cooled, place in glass jars where it can be kept throughout the winter
