## Ingredients

- orange juice (half of total citrus)
- lemon juice (slightly more than half of total citrus)
- 1 glass lemon juice
- 4 glasses rice water
- 4 glasses crushed sugar
- half glass rum
- egg whites of 8 eggs

## Directions

1. Squeeze equal parts orange and lemon juice with slightly more lemon
2. Measure 1 glass juice, 4 glasses rice water, 4 glasses crushed sugar, and half glass rum
3. Mix well and begin to freeze
4. When half-frozen, gently mix in the stiff snow from 8 egg whites one at a time
5. Work well into the ice cream
6. Continue stirring and working throughout the freezing until the entire mixture becomes a stiff foam
7. Before serving, chill the glass container by burying it in ice, as it melts easily
