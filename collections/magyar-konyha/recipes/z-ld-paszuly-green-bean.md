## Ingredients

- tender green beans
- fat
- parsley
- red onion, small amount
- garlic, small amount
- sour cream
- pork cutlet for garnish

## Directions

1. Clean the tender green beans and cut into slices
2. Blanch with hot water and set aside
3. Heat two full spoonfuls of fat
4. Add good amount of parsley and small amount of chopped red and garlic onion
5. Add the beans if well drained
6. Braise until tender with frequent stirring
7. Dust with a small amount of flour and braise further
8. Pour on a small amount of good broth and sufficient sour cream
9. Garnish with pork cutlet
