## Ingredients

- cabbage
- water
- smoked sausage cut into rings
- smoked meat cut into small slices
- thin roux
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put cabbage in a pot with its own liquid and a little water
2. Add the smoked sausage rings and smoked meat slices
3. When cooked, make a thin roux so there is plenty of broth
4. Season lightly with pepper, keeping the sour taste
5. Serve hot
