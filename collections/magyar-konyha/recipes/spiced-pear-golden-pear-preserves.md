## Ingredients

- 56 dekagrams pears
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- glass of water
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel the pears and trim the stem so that only half remains on the pear
2. Brush each pear with lemon juice so they remain beautifully white
3. Place them in water while you finish peeling all of them
4. Boil the sugar with a glass of water only until the sugar is completely dissolved
5. Place the pears in the boiling syrup and turn them with a spoon so they cook evenly
6. Cook until they can be pierced with a needle, but they should not become very soft
7. Remove from heat and cool completely
8. Arrange them nicely in jars and pour the sugar water over them so the pears are completely covered
9. Tie the jars closed and place them in a water bath, boiling for two minutes
10. These pears are very beautiful for garnishing compotes
