## Ingredients

- lamprey
- salt
- butter
- flour
- onion
- lemon peel and juice
- pea broth with vegetables
- 25 small shellfish
- 6-8 mushrooms
- fresh parsley
- 0.5 cup good wine
- 3-4 tablespoons sour cream
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the lamprey and form it into a ring, wash and salt it
2. Melt butter, stir in 2 teaspoons flour, add half an onion and a piece of lemon peel, dilute with pea broth in which roots have been cooked
3. Add the lamprey and bring to a boil so it remains whole, then strain the sauce and add lemon juice and bring to a boil again
4. Meanwhile, shell 25 small shellfish and cut 6-8 mushrooms into leaves, braise with fresh butter and finely chopped green parsley
5. Add 0.5 cup good wine, 3-4 tablespoons sour cream, the shelled shellfish and braise together for several minutes
6. In a small cup, beat 2 egg yolks well, add the lamprey sauce, place the cup on a bed of coals and strain the sauce with continuous stirring
7. Place the lamprey in the center of the dish, pour a little sauce over it and arrange the shellfish and mushrooms in a wreath around the lamprey
