## Ingredients

- Small meaty young chickens
- Red onions
- Yellow carrots
- Green parsley
- Black pepper
- Cloves
- Water
- Vinegar
- Flour
- Butter
- Sour cream

## Directions

1. Select small meaty young chickens and prepare them, then blanch them.
2. Season lightly with salt and prepare them nicely.
3. Place in a pot with red onions, yellow carrots, green parsley, a little pepper, and a few cloves.
4. Fill with three parts water and one part vinegar.
5. Simmer until half cooked.
6. In the meantime, brown two spoonfuls of flour in hot butter, then strain the sauce over it.
7. Add two or three spoonfuls of sour cream.
8. Return the chickens to the pot and cook until completely tender.
