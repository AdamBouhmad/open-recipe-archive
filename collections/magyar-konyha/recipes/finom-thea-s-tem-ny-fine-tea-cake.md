## Ingredients

- 1 cup crushed sugar
- 1 cup crushed shelled almonds
- 1 cup flour
- 1 whole egg
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 lemon juice and grated zest
- 1 egg white
- powdered sugar
- paper for baking

## Directions

1. Make a good stiff dough from 1 cup crushed sugar, 1 cup crushed shelled almonds, 1 cup flour, 1 whole egg, 2 egg yolks, 1/2 lemon juice and grated zest
2. Roll out to knife-blade thickness
3. Beat 1 egg white with powdered sugar until stiff foam
4. Cover the rolled dough with this foam
5. With a warm knife cut into finger-thick rods
6. Place on buttered paper on a pan and bake at gentle heat until golden
7. Can also be covered with chocolate
