## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams sugar in pieces
- 2 deciiliters water
- 28 dekagrams fresh sieved raspberry
- 5 dekagrams butter beaten to a foam
- 14 dekagrams softened and finely pounded almonds
- 7 dekagrams bread crumb soaked in milk
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Put the sugar pieces in a brass bowl and pour 2 deciiliters of water over them and let stand for a while
2. Place on a bed of coals and cook until it drips thickly
3. Add 28 dekagrams of fresh sieved raspberry and cook to a thick consistency
4. Remove from the fire and cool
5. Mix 5 dekagrams of foamy butter with 14 dekagrams of softened and finely pounded almonds and 7 dekagrams of bread crumb soaked in milk
6. Add 2 whole eggs and 4 egg yolks and stir together for half an hour
7. Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
8. Fill a buttered torte wrapper with the paste and bake nicely
