## Ingredients

- pike
- water
- oysters
- fresh butter
- sour cream
- lemon zest
- lemon juice
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook pike in water and place on a plate.
2. Remove the oysters and simmer them slightly in fresh butter.
3. Add them to the pike along with sour cream, lemon zest, and the juice of one lemon, the oyster liquor and a little fine breadcrumbs.
4. Bring to a boil.
