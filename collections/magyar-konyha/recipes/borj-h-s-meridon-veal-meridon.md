## Ingredients

- half a kilogram veal meat
- salt
- bacon slices
- red onion
- carrot
- meat broth
- green parsley
- five decagrams butter
- two tablespoons flour
- three deciliters milk
- one whole egg
- one egg yolk
- ragout ingredients: veal meat, veal breast, asparagus, shrimp tails, button mushrooms

## Directions

1. Clean and salt half a kilogram of veal meat, braise in a pot with bacon slices, sliced red onion, and sliced carrot, constantly adding a little broth so it does not brown
2. Once the meat is braised, chop finely, add finely chopped onion and green parsley, braise briefly with a little butter, then add the minced mixture and braise a little more
3. Make a béchamel from five decagrams butter, two tablespoons flour, and three deciliters of milk; mix in the minced mixture, season with salt, beat in one whole egg and one egg yolk, and mix well
4. Fill a butter-coated pot 2 centimeters high with the minced mixture and coat the entire perimeter of the pot 1 finger-width thick with the mixture
5. Prepare a ragout from veal meat, veal breast, asparagus, shrimp tails, and button mushrooms, cooking the sauce until thick, then beat in two egg yolks and fill the lined pot with the ragout
