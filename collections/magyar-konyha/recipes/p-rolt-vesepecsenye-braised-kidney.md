## Ingredients

- kidney front part
- salt
- bacon
- red onion
- carrot
- broth
- vinegar
- sour cream
- parsley
- red onion (1 head)
- garlic
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the front part of the kidney and salt it
2. Place sliced bacon, sliced red onion, and sliced carrot in a pot with the kidney on top
3. Braise slowly, pouring a little broth over it occasionally to prevent it from burning or browning too much
4. When nicely brown and tender, add a little vinegar and one or two spoons of sour cream and braise a while longer
5. Remove and slice thinly
6. Finely chop parsley, one head of red onion, and a little garlic
7. Place a few spoons of sour cream and butter in a pot, then alternate layers: a layer of kidney, sprinkled with the finely chopped mixture, then butter, sour cream and a spoon of vinegar, then kidney again, until everything is used
8. Finally, place coals on top and braise slowly
