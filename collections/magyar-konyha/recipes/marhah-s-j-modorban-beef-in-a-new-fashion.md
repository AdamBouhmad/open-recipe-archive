## Ingredients

- three kilograms of beef loin
- bacon
- salt

## Directions

1. For fourteen to sixteen people, take three kilograms of beef loin
2. Remove all membranes from the meat
3. Lard it in three to four rows
4. Salt it and prepare for braising as described previously
5. Take particular care that the bacon remains whole and the beef loin braised to a beautiful brown color
