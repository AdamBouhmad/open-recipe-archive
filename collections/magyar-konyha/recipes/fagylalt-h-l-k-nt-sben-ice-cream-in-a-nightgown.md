## Ingredients

- vanilla ice cream
- raspberry, sour cherry, or currant ice cream

## Directions

1. Prepare vanilla ice cream and freeze it hard
2. In another mold, prepare raspberry, sour cherry, or currant ice cream and freeze it similarly; the vanilla ice cream mold should be only half full
3. Once frozen solid, repeatedly spread the ice cream on the inner sides of the mold, always taking the ice cream from the center so a deep hollow remains that does not reach the bottom, with the whole mold being newly coated in thickness
4. Remove 3 large spoonfuls of vanilla ice cream into an ice-chilled bowl, quickly fill the hollow with the liquid ice cream, spread it strongly and smoothly with a spoon, and cover the top with the removed vanilla ice cream
5. Close the mold, freshen the ice, and turn it rapidly in the ice for a quarter hour, drain the water well, cover the mold with ice, and leave it there until serving
6. Then quickly turn it out, cut into slices, and serve at once
