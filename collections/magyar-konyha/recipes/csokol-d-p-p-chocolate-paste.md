## Ingredients

- 14 dekagramms finely ground almonds
- 10 dekagramms finely ground sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 7 dekagramms finely grated chocolate
- fine ground vanilla
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Place almonds and sugar in a bowl
2. Beat in the egg yolks one by one and stir for a whole hour
3. Grind the chocolate finely with a little vanilla
4. Beat the 3 egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in
5. Fill a butter-greased mold
6. Bake until nicely done
