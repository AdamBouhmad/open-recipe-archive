## Ingredients

- 4 deciliters good clean wine vinegar
- 5 dekagrams dried tarragon herb
- 15 grams raisins
- 1 dekagram ginger
- 10 cloves
- 2 dekagrams fresh garlic

## Directions

1. Pour 4 deciliters of good clean wine vinegar into a somewhat larger bottle.
2. Add 5 dekagrams of dried tarragon herb, 15 grams of raisins, 1 dekagram of ginger, 10 cloves, and 2 dekagrams of fresh garlic.
3. Place the bottle in the sun, and stirring frequently, let it stand for 4 weeks, then filter the vinegar through cloth.
