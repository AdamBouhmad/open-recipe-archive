## Ingredients

- piece of pike cooked in salted water
- 18 asparagus spears
- mushrooms, finely chopped
- green parsley
- crayfish tails
- salt
- melted butter
- sour cream
- 8 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook pike in salted water, remove bones, and chop finely
2. Cook 18 asparagus spears in salted water
3. Stew finely chopped mushrooms with green parsley
4. Cook and peel a few crayfish tails
5. Chop everything into small cubes and sauté in melted butter
6. Add salt, a few tablespoons sour cream, and stew well
7. Carefully pour 8 eggs into boiling salted water so they stay whole
8. Cook only until they begin to set, but do not let them harden
9. Serve the ragout piled high in the middle of the plate
10. Arrange the eggs around it in a wreath shape, lifting them out with a slotted spoon
