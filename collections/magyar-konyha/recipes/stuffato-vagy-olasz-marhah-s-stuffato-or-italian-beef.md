## Ingredients

- three kilograms of beef chuck
- fourteen decagrams of bacon
- salt
- ground cinnamon
- garlic
- four deciliters of white wine
- seven deciliters of beef broth
- eight medium tomatoes
- sugar
- macaroni

## Directions

1. Trim excessive fat from three kilograms of beef chuck, pound well, and salt
2. Cut fourteen decagrams of bacon into long pieces and dust lightly with salt and cinnamon
3. Finely mince garlic, crush it, and mix with bacon
4. Lard the meat as done for beef à la Modenese
5. Place the meat in a pot and add four deciliters of white wine and seven deciliters of beef broth
6. Braise slowly and continuously for four hours, turning frequently
7. After four hours, add eight medium tomatoes that have been braised and strained, along with a piece of sugar
8. Continue braising slowly for another hour
9. Skim off the fat, arrange the meat on a serving dish, garnish with macaroni, and serve the remaining strained sauce in a bowl on the side
