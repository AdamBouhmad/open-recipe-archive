## Ingredients

- one liter millet porridge
- quarter kilogram bacon
- three liters hot water
- salt
- one large sheet of dried pasta, broken into small pieces

## Directions

1. Dice the bacon finely and render it until crispy
2. Put the millet porridge into the hot fat (do not wash it, only rub it well in cloth)
3. Let it fry in the fat for a few minutes
4. Pour three liters of hot water over it and salt it
5. When the porridge is half cooked, add the broken dried pasta
6. When the porridge and pasta have absorbed all the liquid, stir in the pasta and let it simmer gently for another quarter hour, shaking occasionally but not stirring
