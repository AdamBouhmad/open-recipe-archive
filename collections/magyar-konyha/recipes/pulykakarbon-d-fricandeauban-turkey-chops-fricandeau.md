## Ingredients

- young large turkey
- salted water
- bacon
- ham
- veal knuckle
- onion
- brown broth
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Blanch a young large turkey in salted water; remove the thighs as large as possible, remove the wings and breast on both sides, keeping the wing as a chop.
2. Remove the thighbone up to the middle of the joint; the upper part of the thigh should remain nicely exposed, as should the upper part of the wing.
3. Stud densely with finely chopped bacon, cut the bacon short so it looks nicely curled.
4. Place a piece of ham and veal knuckle in a pot with a little onion, pour three to four spoonfuls of brown broth over, cover and braise until the chops are well tender.
5. Remove them, strain the sauce through a sieve, skim the fat and cook until it becomes glaze.
6. Return the chops to it, squeeze the juice of one lemon over them, turn the chops well in the glaze, then arrange on a plate, pour the small amount of sauce over them and decorate with a wreath made from butter pastry.
7. It can also be served with sorrel sauce.
