## Ingredients

- tender kohlrabi
- salt water
- cooked or braised meat
- fresh parsley
- red onion
- goose fat
- flour
- broth
- butter sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the tender kohlrabi in salted water until half-cooked
2. Cut off a small lid from the top and hollow it out well
3. Chop cooked or braised meat, fresh parsley and a small piece of red onion finely
4. Melt a small spoonful of goose fat, fry a spoonful of flour in it
5. Add the chopped parsley and onion, brown lightly
6. Add the chopped veal meat, mix well
7. Pour a little broth over it and cook well
8. Fill the hollowed kohlrabi with this mixture
9. Place in the prepared butter sauce
10. Let simmer with frequent shaking for a quarter hour
11. Arrange neatly on the dish and serve
