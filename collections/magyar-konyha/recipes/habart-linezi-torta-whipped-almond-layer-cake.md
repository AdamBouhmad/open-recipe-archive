## Ingredients

- 28 dekagramm soaked almonds
- 28 dekagramm sugar
- a little vanilla
- zest of one orange, finely chopped
- 28 dekagramm butter, whipped
- juice of half a lemon
- 4 egg yolks
- 28 dekagramm fine flour
- wafer

## Directions

1. Finely grind soaked almonds while moistening with water to prevent them from becoming oily
2. Add same amount of sugar, a little vanilla, and finely chopped orange zest
3. Whip butter until frothy and add lemon juice
4. Mix in egg yolks one by one
5. Mix in sugar, almonds and orange zest
6. Finally mix in fine flour
7. Make either a beautifully braided cake or three equal layers
8. Coat with egg white, sprinkle with sugar and bake
9. When baked, fill each layer with different preserves
10. Stack layers and either coat with orange frosting or decorate with candied fruit
11. For braided cake, use a band around the form since dough will flow
