## Ingredients

- veal chop
- salt
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the chop in a press, weighting it with something flat
2. When cooled, cut into thin slices
3. Salt the slices lightly
4. Dip in egg and coat with breadcrumbs
5. Fry quickly in butter over high heat
6. Arrange on a plate in circles
7. Pour tomato sauce in the center
