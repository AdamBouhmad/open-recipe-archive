## Ingredients

- 1.5 kilograms strong honey
- 25 dekagrams red powdered tartar
- water
- 17 liters good brandy
- 0.5 kilogram rye bread
- 2 liters strong vinegar

## Directions

1. Pour 1.5 kilograms of strong honey and 25 dekagrams of red powdered tartar into a pot filled with water.
2. Keep the liquid at a boil with slow stirring until the tartar dissolves, then filter through cloth.
3. Cool this decoction until you can hold your hand in it, then pour it into a vinegar barrel and add 17 liters of good brandy.
4. Cut 0.5 kilogram of rye bread into small pieces, cover it with 2 liters of strong vinegar, heat it almost to boiling, then pour the mixture into the barrel, shake it well, place the barrel in a warm spot, and after 6-8 weeks carefully draw off the vinegar.
