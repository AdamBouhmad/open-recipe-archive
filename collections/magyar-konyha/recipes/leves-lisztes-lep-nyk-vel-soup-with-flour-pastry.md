## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams butter
- 2 eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- flour
- 4 egg whites
- salt
- crab broth

## Directions

1. Beat 14 dekagrams butter until foamy
2. Beat in 2 whole eggs and 4 egg yolks
3. One after another, beat in enough flour to make a light dough and 4 egg whites beaten stiff
4. Season with salt
5. Butter a baking sheet or wide pot, put in the dough and bake until golden
6. When done, transfer to a board and cut out neatly with a cutter
7. Place in the soup bowl and pour crab broth over it
