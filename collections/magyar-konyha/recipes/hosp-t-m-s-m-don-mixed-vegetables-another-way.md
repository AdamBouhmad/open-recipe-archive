## Ingredients

- tender kohlrabi
- carrots
- green peas
- butter
- flour
- good broth
- sugar
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. Clean tender kohlrabi and carrots
2. With a small chisel, carve small balls from each, equal portions from each vegetable
3. Cook the same amount of green peas for a few minutes in salted water and drain
4. Melt butter and lightly brown a spoonful of flour
5. Thin with good broth and cook
6. Add the kohlrabi and carrot balls
7. Add a piece of sugar and braise until tender with frequent stirring
8. Add the green peas and cook the sauce completely
9. Be careful that the vegetables remain whole and do not become mushy
10. Prepare beautiful cauliflower divided into small florets
11. Cook tender in good meat broth, keeping them whole
12. When serving, heap the vegetables in the center of the plate like a mound
13. Arrange the cauliflower around the edge of the plate so it forms a wreath
14. Breaded chicken or veal cutlet is usually served with it
