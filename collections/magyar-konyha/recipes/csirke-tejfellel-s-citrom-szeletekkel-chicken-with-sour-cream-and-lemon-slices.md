## Ingredients

- Two small chickens
- Sour cream
- Lemon with peel
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Blanch two small chickens and rinse them several times with warm water.
2. Cut into the usual pieces and salt them, then let them rest in the salt for a quarter hour.
3. Pour good sour cream into a pot.
4. Cut a soup lemon thinly with its peel, carefully removing the seeds.
5. Layer half the lemon slices in the sour cream, then half the chicken pieces, then more sour cream.
6. Add the remaining lemon slices, then the rest of the chicken.
7. Top with sour cream and small pieces of butter.
8. Note: do not stir these chickens, only shake the pot gently and cook only until done, as otherwise they lose both flavor and appearance.
9. When serving, arrange the chickens neatly on a platter and pour the sauce underneath.
