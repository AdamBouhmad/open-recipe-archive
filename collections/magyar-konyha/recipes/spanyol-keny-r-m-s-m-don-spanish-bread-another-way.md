## Ingredients

- elongated rolls
- preserved currants
- red wine, sweetened
- flour
- sugar
- cinnamon
- fat for frying
- finely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Cut rolls into slices as before.
2. Fill with preserved currants and soak in sweetened red wine.
3. Make a thin batter from red wine, flour, sugar, and cinnamon.
4. Dip the filled roll slices into the batter and fry in fat.
5. Sprinkle immediately with finely ground sugar and brown with a hot spatula.
6. Note: Do not soak these slices as strongly in wine as the previous recipe, or they will fall apart and absorb fat.
