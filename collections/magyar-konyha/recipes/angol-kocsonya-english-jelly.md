## Ingredients

- 42 dekagramm sugar
- 4 oranges
- 4 lemons
- 14 egg yolks
- 2 deciliter French champagne
- 8 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon rum
- 2 dekagramm dissolved isinglass
- almond oil
- large Spanish pastries

## Directions

1. Rub half of the sugar on an orange and the other half on a lemon
2. Squeeze the juice from 4 oranges and 4 lemons and strain through cloth
3. Crush the sugar and place in a pot and whisk together with the egg yolks, squeezed juice, and 2 deciliter French champagne
4. Place over charcoal fire and stir continuously until it becomes a thick mass
5. Meanwhile beat 8 egg whites into stiff peaks
6. Remove the pot from the coals and when the mixture begins to boil, fold in the beaten egg whites
7. Add 1 tablespoon of rum and 2 dekagramm dissolved isinglass
8. When cooled, pour into a deep mold that has been previously greased with pure almond oil
9. Place in crushed ice and freeze completely
10. Turn out onto a plate lined with a napkin and surround the edge of the jelly with large Spanish pastries arranged in a circle
