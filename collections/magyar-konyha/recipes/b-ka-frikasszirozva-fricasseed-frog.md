## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- half red onion
- pea water
- lemon rind
- frogs
- sour cream
- eggs
- cold water
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. In a pot, melt a piece of butter, add a spoonful of flour and half a red onion and fry for only one minute so it does not brown
2. Dilute with pea water, add a little lemon rind and cook for a while
3. Add the frogs, add a spoonful of sour cream and cook a little longer
4. In a small pot, place two to three egg yolks, a teaspoonful of cold water and the juice of one lemon, strain the sauce over them, stir on the warm stove for a while longer, pour over the frogs and serve immediately
