## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Butter
- Flour
- Tomatoes
- Meat broth
- Sour cream
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens and cut them into the usual pieces.
2. Braise in butter and sprinkle a little flour over them.
3. Dilute with the strained tomatoes, which have been previously well cooked in meat broth.
4. Simmer the chickens until tender.
5. Finally, add a good spoonful of sour cream and a piece of sugar.
6. Arrange nicely on a platter and pour the sauce over them.
