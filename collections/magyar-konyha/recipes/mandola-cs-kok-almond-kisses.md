## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams sugar with a little vanilla, crushed and sifted
- 2 egg whites
- 28 dekagrams blanched almonds, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar into a stiff icing with the stiffly beaten egg whites
2. Mix the blanched and finely chopped almonds into this mixture
3. Place spoonful-sized pieces on wafer paper placed on a baking sheet
4. Bake quickly in a gentle oven
