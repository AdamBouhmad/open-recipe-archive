## Ingredients

- Seven deciliters of sour cherries
- A small amount of wine
- Four deciliters of wine
- Four deciliters of water
- Broken cinnamon
- Sugar
- Lemon peel, a small amount
- Toasted bread points

## Directions

1. Put the sour cherries in a pot with a small amount of wine and braise until reduced to a paste
2. Press through clean cloth
3. In a pot, pour four deciliters of wine and four deciliters of water over the paste
4. Flavor with broken cinnamon, sugar, and a small amount of lemon peel
5. Bring to a boil and serve into toasted bread points
