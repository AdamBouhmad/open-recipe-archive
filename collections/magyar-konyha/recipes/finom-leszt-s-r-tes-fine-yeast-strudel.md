## Ingredients

- 7 dekagramm butter
- six egg yolks
- 2 decziliters sweet sour cream
- one tablespoon yeast dissolved in three and one-half dekagramm sour cream
- two spoonfuls crushed sugar
- salt
- flour
- chopped almond
- citron
- raisins
- currants
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Beat 7 dekagramm butter until fluffy
2. Mix in six egg yolks
3. Add 2 decziliters sweet sour cream, one tablespoon yeast dissolved in three and one-half dekagramm sour cream, two spoonfuls crushed sugar, and salt
4. Mix together
5. Add enough flour to make the dough suitable for pâté
6. Quickly mix it with the rest
7. Let the dough rise in a warm place
8. Stretch it to the thickness of a knife blade
9. Fill it with chopped almond, citron, raisins, and currants
10. Fold it together
11. Place it on a butter-brushed pastry paper
12. Let it rise once more
13. Bake it
