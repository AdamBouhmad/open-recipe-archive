## Ingredients

- 4 deciliters cream
- 6 egg yolks
- sugar
- vanilla
- 4 to 6 pitted cooked cherries or stewed cherries in sugar
- gold leaf paper

## Directions

1. Line beautiful egg-shaped cake molds with shortcrust pastry
2. Fill with gold leaf paper shaped into a ball like the mold shape
3. Bake until golden brown in the oven
4. Meanwhile, prepare cream custard by mixing cream, egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla in a pot, stirring constantly over moderate heat until thickened
5. Set aside
6. When cakes are baked, remove the paper ball
7. Place 4 to 6 cherries in each cake that have been cooked in sugar and drained well
8. Fill the cakes with the cream custard
9. Return to the oven until the custard hardens
10. When serving, turn out onto a plate, sprinkle with vanilla sugar, and arrange on a platter so the cakes resemble bowls filled with cream custard
