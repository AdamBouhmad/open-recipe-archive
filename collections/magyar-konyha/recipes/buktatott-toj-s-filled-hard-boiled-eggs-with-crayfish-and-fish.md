## Ingredients

- 6 hard-boiled eggs
- finely chopped crayfish tails
- bread soaked in milk, grated
- 1 whole egg
- 3 tablespoons sour cream
- salt
- small piece of crayfish butter
- pike, finely shredded
- crayfish tails, cut lengthwise
- mushrooms
- green parsley
- green peas
- butter
- pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut 6 hard-boiled eggs in half lengthwise
2. Place in a mortar: chopped crayfish tails, grated milk-soaked bread, 1 whole egg, 3 tablespoons sour cream, salt, and a small piece of crayfish butter
3. Pound finely
4. Place half of this filling on a crayfish butter-greased dish
5. Prepare a ragout from: shredded pike, lengthwise-cut crayfish tails, and mushrooms
6. Stew this with green parsley and green peas in butter
7. Season with salt and a little pepper
8. Stew together with the crayfish tails
9. Fill the eggs with this ragout
10. Place the filled eggs in the dish
11. Top with the remaining half of the filling
12. Add a few tablespoons sour cream, a little crayfish butter, and fine breadcrumbs
13. Place in the oven and let heat through
