## Ingredients

- dried lavender flowers
- 4 grams vitriol oil
- strong purified alcohol
- various fragrant oils

## Directions

1. Fill three-quarters of a large pot with dried lavender flowers
2. Add 4 grams vitriol oil
3. Shake well
4. Allow to stand for one hour
5. Pour strong purified alcohol over it
6. Bind the bottle with a bladder
7. Allow to stand and digest for fourteen days
8. Various fragrant oils may be added
