## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter cream
- 16 dekagrams finely ground almonds
- little lemon zest
- 2 egg yolks
- little flour
- cold milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Add 16 dekagrams finely ground almonds to 0.5 liter cream with a little lemon zest
2. Place on heat and boil covered for a quarter hour
3. Mix 2 egg yolks with a little flour and cold milk
4. Pour the boiled cream over it
5. Add sugar and bring to a boil
6. Strain and serve as sauce for pudding or cake
