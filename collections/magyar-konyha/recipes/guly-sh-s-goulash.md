## Ingredients

- one kilo of beef chuck
- egg-sized amount of fat
- salt
- one large head of onion
- one teaspoon of paprika
- three liters of hot water

## Directions

1. Cut one kilo of beef chuck into large pieces
2. Place an egg-sized amount of fat in a pot and add the cut meat with salt, one large head of finely chopped onion, and one teaspoon of paprika
3. Braise covered for half an hour
4. Pour three liters of hot water over it and cook for one and a half hours until the meat is tender
