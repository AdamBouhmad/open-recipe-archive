## Ingredients

- one pheasant
- salt
- lard
- butter
- lemon peel
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After scalding the pheasant, remove the entrails and clean thoroughly.
2. Salt it well and lard it with finely chopped lard.
3. Roast slowly, basting frequently with butter.
4. When done, decorate with lemon peel and serve with lemon juice poured over it.
