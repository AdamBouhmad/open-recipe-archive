## Ingredients

- two or three egg yolks
- a few drops of vinegar
- two to three decis of oil
- pepper
- French or English mustard
- good quality vinegar

## Directions

1. Salt the egg yolks
2. Add a few drops of vinegar
3. While continuously stirring with a whisk, add the oil drop by drop (be careful as adding too much oil at once will cause it to separate)
4. When finished, pepper and add French or English mustard
5. Dilute to the proper consistency with good quality vinegar
