## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram fresh mutton tallow
- 75 dekagrams vegetable oil
- 25 dekagrams hair powder
- 7 dekagrams Peruvian balsam
- 10 dekagrams vanilla tincture

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
