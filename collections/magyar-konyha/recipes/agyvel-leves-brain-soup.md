## Ingredients

- one whole calf brain
- beef broth
- butter
- ten to fifteen button mushrooms (champignons), coarsely chopped
- little finely chopped parsley
- half a cooking spoon flour
- crushed nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook one whole calf brain in beef broth
2. When cooked, strain through a sieve
3. Warm a piece of butter and braise ten to fifteen coarsely chopped button mushrooms with a little finely chopped parsley for a while
4. Sprinkle half a cooking spoon of flour over it and braise a little longer
5. Add the brain and pour good beef broth over it
6. A little crushed nutmeg may be added
7. Bring everything to a boil
8. Serve over brain dumplings or over fried, lengthwise-sliced bread
