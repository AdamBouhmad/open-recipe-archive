## Ingredients

- 5 liters purified 70° alcohol
- 2 kilograms fresh jasmine flowers
- 4 grams bergamot oil
- 4.5 dekagrams cinnamon, crushed to powder

## Directions

1. Add 2 kilograms fresh jasmine flowers, 4 grams bergamot oil, and 4.5 dekagrams crushed cinnamon to 5 liters purified 70° alcohol
2. Allow to digest
