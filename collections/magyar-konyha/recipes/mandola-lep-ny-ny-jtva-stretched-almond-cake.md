## Ingredients

- dough as in recipe 465
- 14 dekagrams almonds
- 14 dekagrams raisins
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- butter
- sour cream
- egg white
- powdered sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Prepare and handle the dough exactly as in recipe 465
2. Soak the almonds and chop finely
3. Remove seeds from raisins and chop similarly
4. Finely grind the sugar
5. Add desired flavoring to the filling
6. Mix all filling ingredients together
7. Fill the cake twice as in recipe 465
8. When the final layer is stretched, brush with egg white
9. Sprinkle with sugar
10. Bake in gentle heat
11. When done, sprinkle with powdered sugar and vanilla
