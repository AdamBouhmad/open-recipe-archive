## Ingredients

- currants in bunches
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare the sugar as in the previous recipe; for 500 grams of sugar, take the same amount of bunched currants.
2. Select the most beautiful ones, bring to a boil five or six times, then carefully remove with a slotted spoon onto a sieve, placing the drained juice with the rest.
3. Boil until thick, then remove from the fire, carefully place the currants into it and cool slightly.
4. Before it cools completely, fill into glasses and tie them as you would other preserved glasses.
