## Ingredients

- half kilogram of flour
- quarter kilogram of powdered sugar
- one good spoonful of ground cinnamon
- a few spoonfuls of good sour cream
- wine
- egg yolks
- hot fat
- cinnamon sugar

## Directions

1. Mix half kilogram of flour, quarter kilogram of powdered sugar, one good spoonful of ground cinnamon, a few spoonfuls of sour cream, the same of wine, and enough egg yolks to make a moderately stiff dough
2. Roll out to the thickness of a knife blade
3. Cut into small squares
4. Wrap around a rolling pin with thick string in a spiral shape
5. Fry in hot fat until brown
6. Dust with cinnamon sugar
7. Can also be filled with jam
