## Ingredients

- butter pastry dough
- egg white

## Directions

1. Prepare butter pastry dough — see below
2. After folding 3 times, roll out to the thickness of a knife blade
3. Cut out with medium-sized fritter cutters in the desired quantity
4. Place on a baking sheet
5. Brush the center with egg white
