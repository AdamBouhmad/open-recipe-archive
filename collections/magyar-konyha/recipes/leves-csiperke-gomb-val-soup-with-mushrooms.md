## Ingredients

- button mushrooms
- butter
- green parsley, finely chopped
- flour
- meat broth
- nutmeg
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean the button mushrooms and cut into thin slices
2. Stew gently in a little butter with finely chopped green parsley
3. Sprinkle a little flour on top and when well reduced, add enough meat broth to make it like a thin sauce
4. Add a little nutmeg and a spoonful of sour cream
5. Let cook a little longer and serve over flour pastry or fried bread
