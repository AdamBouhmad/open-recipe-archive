## Ingredients

- four decilitres milk
- crispy sour apple
- sugar
- one spoonful preserved apricot
- seven dekagrams softened almond
- seven dekagrams raisin
- egg
- fine breadcrumbs
- fat
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make fine pancakes from four decilitres milk, then peel crispy sour apple, cut it into thin slices, pair them, add enough sugar to make it sweet enough, also add a spoonful of preserved apricot, remove from heat and cool
2. Chop seven dekagrams softened almond and the same amount of raisin together into fine oblong pieces and mix this with the apple, then fill the pancake with it, namely: place a piece of filling on each pancake and spread with a knife, then fold it together, cut in the middle, as well as cut off both ends, dip in beaten egg, sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs and fry nicely golden in fat and serve sprinkled with sugar
