## Ingredients

- twenty-eight dekagrams of peeled sweet almonds
- two dekagrams of peeled bitter almonds
- water
- twenty-eight dekagrams of crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Finely grate the sweet and bitter almonds with water
2. Mix with two liters of cold water slowly
3. Let stand for a short time
4. Strain through a clean, well-soaked cloth
5. Mix the almonds again with a little water and press
6. Mix well with the crushed sugar
7. Strain again and pour into bottles and set on ice
