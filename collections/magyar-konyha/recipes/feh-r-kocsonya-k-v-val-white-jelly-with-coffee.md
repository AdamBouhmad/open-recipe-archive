## Ingredients

- 7 deciliter good cream
- 7 dekagramm coffee
- 8 egg yolks
- 18 dekagramm crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Heat 7 deciliter of good cream
2. Roast 7 dekagramm of coffee and place in a soup bowl
3. Pour the hot cream over it immediately and cover with a lid
4. Let stand for a quarter hour
5. Strain through a sieve
6. In a pot, whisk together 8 egg yolks with 18 dekagramm crushed sugar
7. Mix this with the cream
8. Pour into a deep porcelain cup and place in a water bath
9. Freeze in a cold place for a quarter hour before serving
