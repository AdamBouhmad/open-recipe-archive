## Ingredients

- Veal leg
- Salt
- Four anchovies, cut lengthwise
- Garlic, cut very thinly

## Directions

1. Prepare and salt the leg
2. Make holes in it with a thin knife
3. Stud with lengthwise-cut anchovies and very thinly sliced garlic
4. Let rest for one hour
5. Roast over steady heat
6. Leave on the spit about half an hour longer than usual
7. Baste diligently with butter
8. Half an hour before serving pour six to eight spoonfuls of sour cream over it
9. Place on serving dish and pour sauce underneath
