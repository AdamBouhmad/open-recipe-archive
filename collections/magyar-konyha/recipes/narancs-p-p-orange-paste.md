## Ingredients

- 21 dekagrams almonds
- fresh water
- 2 oranges
- 21 dekagrams sugar
- 1 whole egg
- 4 egg yolks
- juice of 2 lemons
- 4 egg whites, whipped stiff

## Directions

1. Grind the almonds, sprinkling lightly with fresh water during grinding to prevent them becoming oily
2. Rub the sugar on two oranges, then grind it finely
3. Place the ground almonds and sugar in a bowl
4. Beat in the whole egg and four egg yolks
5. Stir in the lemon juice and beat everything for one hour
6. Finally fold in the stiffly whipped egg whites
7. Spread the paste on a buttered pastry sheet and bake
8. When nearly finished baking, whip two tablespoons of raspberry marmalade with seven dekagrams of ground sugar until very frothy
9. Fold in the stiff egg white meringue of four eggs, a little at a time, ensuring it is not watery
10. Remove the pastry sheet rim
11. Shape the meringue into a crown on top of the paste, piling it high with a knife
12. Make indentations every two finger-widths with a card edge to resemble a braided crown
13. Sprinkle with ground sugar
14. Return to the oven
15. Work very quickly to prevent the paste from collapsing
