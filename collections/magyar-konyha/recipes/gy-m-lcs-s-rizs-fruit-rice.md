## Ingredients

- seven decilitres of cream
- one and one-quarter kilograms of picked and washed rice
- eighteen dekagrams of pounded sugar
- salt
- three tablespoons of apricot or raspberry marmalade
- orange or lemon zest
- sugar
- lemon juice
- six egg whites
- butter
- candied fruits in syrup
- candied peach, orange, melon slices, pineapple, Reine Claude plums, Spanish cherries, or sour cherries

## Directions

1. Cook the picked and well-washed rice in seven decilitres of cream until soft, add eighteen dekagrams of pounded sugar and a little salt, then cool it
2. Meanwhile, mix three tablespoons of apricot or raspberry marmalade with sugar finely pounded and sifted on orange or lemon zest, then mix in the juice of half a lemon, and finally slowly mix in the stiffly beaten snow of six egg whites
3. Place one-third of the rice in a deep porcelain pan buttered with butter and spread it evenly, arrange candied fruits in syrup on it, such as apricot, orange, melon slices, pineapple, Reine Claude, Spanish cherry, or sour cherry
4. Spread the second third of the rice on top, arrange it again with fruit, and cover with the remaining rice
5. Then shape a peak from the apricot or raspberry foam on top of the rice with a silver spoon, and bake the whole in the oven at gentle heat for half an hour, then serve immediately on the table
6. If this dish succeeds, it has a very beautiful appearance and splendid flavor
