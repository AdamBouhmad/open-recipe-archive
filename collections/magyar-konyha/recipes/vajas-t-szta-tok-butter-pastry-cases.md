## Ingredients

- butter pastry
- eight eggs
- crayfish tails (on fast days)
- ham cut into large cubes (on meat days)
- pickled tongue slices
- sausage slices
- frog escalope (on fast days)

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter pastry and roll it out to the thickness of a knife blade
2. Place it smoothly into a four-cornered mold without creases
3. Fill with clean paper and bake quickly
4. Meanwhile, prepare a mixture from eight eggs
5. On fast days, mix in crayfish tails cut lengthwise; on meat days, cut ham into large cubes and add
6. Once the case is baked, remove the paper and turn out from the mold
7. Place on a long dish and fill with the egg mixture
8. Arrange the crayfish tails or ham cubes on top
9. Edge the dish rim with slices of pickled tongue and sausage; on fast days use frog escalope instead
