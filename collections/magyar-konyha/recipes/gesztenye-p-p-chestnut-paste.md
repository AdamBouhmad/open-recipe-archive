## Ingredients

- Half kilogram Italian chestnuts
- Two deciliters good cream
- Eight dekagrams sweet almonds
- Twenty-one dekagrams sifted sugar
- Several spoonfuls cold cream
- Five dekagrams butter
- Five egg yolks
- Three egg whites
- Eight to ten roasted chestnuts
- Ten dekagrams sugar
- Half coffee cup water
- One lemon

## Directions

1. Roast the Italian chestnuts until soft without browning
2. Peel them and while warm grind until resembling dough
3. Put in a pan and mix smooth with two deciliters good cream
4. Boil with constant stirring
5. Clean eight dekagrams sweet almonds and grind as finely as the chestnuts
6. Press the chestnuts through a sieve and put in a pan with the almonds, twenty-one dekagrams sifted sugar, and several spoonfuls cold cream
7. Stew with constant stirring into a thick paste
8. Remove from heat and while warm mix in five dekagrams butter and cool
9. Gently mix in five egg yolks and stiffly beaten whites of three eggs
10. Butter the mold
11. Roast eight to ten chestnuts, peel them, and cut in half
12. Boil ten dekagrams sugar with half a coffee cup water until thick with large bubbles forming
13. Dip the chestnuts in this and place on marble, and when cool cut them in half
14. Line the mold with these so the sugar-coated side touches the mold side
15. Fill with the paste
16. Squeeze a lemon, strain, and boil with fourteen dekagrams sugar until large bubbles form
17. Cool, and when jelly-like, turn out the paste and coat with this glaze
