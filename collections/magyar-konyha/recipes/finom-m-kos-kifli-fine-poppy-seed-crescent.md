## Ingredients

- 1.8 liters dry flour
- 42 dekagrams butter
- 12 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons milk
- 5 dekagrams fresh cheese yeast dissolved in lukewarm milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- lukewarm milk as needed
- salt as needed
- poppy seed filling (as for poppy seed strudel)
- beaten egg for brushing
- For walnut filling: shelled walnuts, good white honey, milk, finely chopped candied lemon zest, ground vanilla

## Directions

1. Place flour on a board and salt it
2. Work in the butter leaf by leaf using a rolling pin
3. Add 12 egg yolks, 6 tablespoons milk, 5 dekagrams fresh cheese yeast dissolved in lukewarm milk, 1 tablespoon sugar, and lukewarm milk
4. Mix into a dough, not too stiff
5. Use a wide knife to work the dough, avoiding touching it with bare hands
6. Once the dough comes together, form small pieces
7. Cover with cloth and let rise well
8. Prepare poppy seed filling as for poppy seed strudel
9. Roll out each piece to the thickness of a knife blade
10. Spread filling to the same thickness
11. Fold in half, place on buttered paper, and let rise slightly
12. Brush with beaten egg and bake
13. For walnut filling: chop shelled walnuts finely and mix with good white honey and enough milk to make it spreadable
14. Add finely chopped candied lemon zest and ground vanilla
15. Simmer briefly, then cool without letting it set completely so it can be spread
