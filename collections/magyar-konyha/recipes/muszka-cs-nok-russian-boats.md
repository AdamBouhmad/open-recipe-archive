## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams crushed sugar
- 14 dekagrams finely chopped almonds
- 1.5 dekagrams finely ground cinnamon
- 1.5 dekagrams cloves
- finely chopped zest of one lemon
- egg white
- fine sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. On a board, make dough from 14 dekagrams of crushed sugar, 14 dekagrams of finely chopped almonds, 1.5 dekagrams of finely ground cinnamon, 1.5 dekagrams of cloves, and the finely chopped zest of one lemon, using the stiffly beaten white of one egg
2. Dust the board with fine sugar and form boats, wreaths, or whatever shapes you like from it
3. Bake on a sheet greased with wax in a gentle oven and store dry
