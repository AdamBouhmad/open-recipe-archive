## Ingredients

- 4 large starchy potatoes
- 7 dekagrams butter
- Green parsley, finely chopped
- 1 egg
- 3 egg yolks
- Salt
- Fine bread crumbs
- Hot fat

## Directions

1. Cook 4 large starchy potatoes, peel them, and grate them.
2. Beat 7 dekagrams of butter until fluffy, add finely chopped green parsley, 1 whole egg, and 3 egg yolks.
3. Mix everything well together, then mix in the potato with a little salt.
4. Form walnut-sized dumplings, roll them in fine bread crumbs, and fry them until a beautiful yellow in hot fat.
5. Place in the soup dish and pour potato broth over them.
