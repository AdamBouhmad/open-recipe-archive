## Ingredients

- pine birds
- salt
- butter
- thin bacon slices
- thin bread slices
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Pluck the birds and cut off the head and wings at the first joint
2. Salt and peck the feet
3. Cross them on the spit at the ribs, placing each bird next to the other
4. Place two thin slices of bacon between each bird and a thin bread slice between the bacon
5. Drizzle butter while roasting
6. Before fully roasted, sprinkle with breadcrumbs and finish roasting
