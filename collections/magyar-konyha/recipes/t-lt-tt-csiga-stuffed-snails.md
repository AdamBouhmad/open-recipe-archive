## Ingredients

- large snails
- salt
- green parsley
- sardelle (anchovy), cleaned and rinsed
- lemon zest
- snails, part of them
- butter, beaten
- lemon juice
- fine breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the snails and remove from their shells
2. Salt them and let sit in salt for a while
3. Rinse the shells thoroughly
4. Clean the snails of slime and rinse the tails
5. Chop finely: green parsley, cleaned and rinsed sardelle, a little lemon zest, and a part of the snails
6. Mix into beaten butter
7. Add a little lemon juice and a small handful of fine breadcrumbs
8. Mix everything well
9. Fill the snail shells with this mixture, alternating with snail halves, until the shell is full
10. Place on a tin plate
11. Put in the oven and the dish is ready
