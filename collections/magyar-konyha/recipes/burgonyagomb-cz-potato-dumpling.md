## Ingredients

- 80 decagrams grated potato
- 21 decagrams fine flour
- 14 decagrams bread cut into small cubes
- 21 decagrams butter
- 2 or 3 eggs
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Mix together 80 decagrams grated potato with 21 decagrams fine flour lightly
2. Fry 14 decagrams bread cut into small cubes in 21 decagrams butter until stiff
3. Sprinkle these on the potato and mix lightly together
4. Beat 2 or 3 eggs and pour over and with flour's help make dumplings from it
5. Cook in salty water and serve as flour dumplings
