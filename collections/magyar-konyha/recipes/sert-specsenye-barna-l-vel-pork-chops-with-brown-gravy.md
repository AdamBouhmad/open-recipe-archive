## Ingredients

- hot fat
- one head red onion
- one parsley root
- carrot
- celery
- paprika
- black pepper
- pork meat (white chop or leg)
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. In hot fat, cut one head of red onion, one parsley root, carrot, and celery into thin slices
2. Quickly braise until soft
3. When softened, brown with a little paprika and ground black pepper
4. Add the cleaned and salted pork meat
5. Pour a little water underneath
6. Braise quickly until tender
7. If the liquid reduces, pour in hot salted water by the spoonful so the vegetables do not burn, but the gravy must be dark brown
8. When the meat is tender, remove it and slice it, placing on a hot platter
9. Pour hot water over the browned vegetables and bring to a boil
10. Strain the gravy through a sieve, mashing the browned vegetables through it over the sliced meat
11. This produces a very pleasant sauce, the main thing being that it should not burn but be dark brown
