## Ingredients

- half a liter dry peas
- three liters clean spring water
- carrot
- parsnip root
- celery
- a few fried bread crumbs
- a little nutmeg flower

## Directions

1. Put the dry peas in a pot and fill with three liters of clean spring water
2. Add carrot, parsnip root, celery, a few fried bread crumbs, and a little nutmeg flower
3. Boil slowly and use as needed
