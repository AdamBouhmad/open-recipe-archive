## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons lard
- 3 tablespoons coarsely crushed sugar
- red onion, finely chopped
- sour cabbage
- sour cabbage juice
- a little flour
- a little broth

## Directions

1. Heat the lard in a pot
2. Add the coarsely crushed sugar and allow it to brown nicely
3. Add the finely chopped red onion and fry lightly
4. Press out the juice from the sour cabbage and add the cabbage to the browned sugar
5. Braise slowly, stirring frequently with a large fork, until it takes on a nice brown color
6. Sprinkle a little flour over it and braise again well
7. Pour a little broth over it, but it should not become soup-like
8. Cook together for some time
