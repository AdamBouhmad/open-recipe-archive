## Ingredients

- Puff pastry dough
- Egg whites
- 90 grams blanched almonds
- 90 grams finely crushed sugar
- Preserved fruit

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry dough and roll it to knife-blade thickness
2. Cut three equal-sized leaves from the dough
3. Cut beautiful arches into them with a warm knife
4. Brush with egg white, but not all the way to the edges so the dough will rise
5. Chop the blanched almonds finely and mix well with an equal amount of finely crushed sugar
6. Sprinkle the leaves with this mixture and bake at sustained heat
7. Once baked, brush each leaf with preserved fruit and stack them on top of each other
8. Top the final leaf with preserved cherries or decorate with icing
