## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram beef chuck (felsár-szélt)
- 1 egg-sized piece of lard
- salt
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 3 liters hot water
- 1 plate of potatoes, peeled and quartered

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into large pieces
2. Place the lard in a pot and add the cut meat with salt, chopped onion, and paprika
3. Braise covered for half an hour
4. Pour 3 liters of hot water over it and cook for an hour and a half
5. When the meat is tender, add the potatoes
6. When the potatoes are cooked, the dish is ready to serve
