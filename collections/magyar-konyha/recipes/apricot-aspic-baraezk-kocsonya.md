## Ingredients

- 30 beautiful ripe apricots
- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 4 deciliters water
- 1 lemon
- 4.5 dekagrams dissolved and strained isinglass

## Directions

1. Peel 30 beautiful ripe apricots, remove pits, and place in a soup bowl.
2. In a brass pan, cook 42 dekagrams sugar with 4 deciliters water only until completely dissolved.
3. Pour the hot syrup over the apricots, cover, and let stand 24 hours.
4. Strain slowly without pressing, allowing liquid to flow drop by drop.
5. Mix in juice of 1 lemon.
6. Finally mix in 4.5 dekagrams dissolved and strained isinglass.
7. Strain through cloth lined with blotting paper, and if the mixture is beautifully clear, pour into molds and freeze.
