## Ingredients

- puff pastry folded five times
- twenty-eight dekagrams cake leaves
- egg white
- preserved currant jam
- preserved peach jam
- preserved rosehip marmalade
- egg white foam
- sugar

## Directions

1. Roll out puff pastry folded five times to two knife-widths thickness
2. From twenty-eight dekagrams of cake leaf, cut four leaves of the size of the cake leaf, coat them with egg white, place each one on the sheet, and bake slowly, but in such a way that they remain beautifully white, except for one leaf
3. Coat one leaf with preserved currant jam, place the other leaf on it, coat it with preserved peach jam, place the third leaf on it, coat it with preserved rosehip marmalade, finally place the last leaf on it, to which you place the following icing: Add to the stiff foam of fresh egg white in a small bowl enough sugar so that it becomes thin dough consistency
