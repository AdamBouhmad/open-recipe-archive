## Ingredients

- forty crayfish tails and claws
- crumbly bread roll
- sour cream
- crayfish butter
- one whole egg and two egg yolks
- four to five spoonfuls

## Directions

1. Extract the tails and claws from forty crayfish
2. Chop them coarsely
3. Grate crumbly bread roll and moisten it with sour cream
4. Beat a piece of crayfish butter until fluffy
5. Mix in one whole egg and two egg yolks
6. Add the moistened bread crumbs and the chopped crayfish pieces
7. Add four to five spoonfuls (of sour cream or broth)
