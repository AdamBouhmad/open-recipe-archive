## Ingredients

- beautiful black, fleshy cherries
- sugar
- water
- 60 dekagrams cherries requires 15 dekagrams sugar and 2 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from beautiful black, fleshy cherries, wash and strain them
2. Calculate 15 dekagrams sugar for 60 dekagrams cherries, cook with 2 deciliters water until completely dissolved
3. Fill clean and uniform-sized jars with cherries up to their necks
4. Bind them with thoroughly washed cow bladder so tightly that no air can enter
5. Arrange and process as per instructions 961
