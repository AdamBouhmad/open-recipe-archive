## Ingredients

- woodcocks, cleaned
- bacon slices
- bread fried in fat
- good broth
- woodcock intestines
- green parsley, chopped
- red onion
- butter
- large bread triangles
- fat

## Directions

1. If the woodcocks are well cleaned, remove their intestines and set aside.
2. Wrap the woodcocks in bacon slices and roast them.
3. Cut out the finest pieces of breast; grind the remaining parts together with fried bread in a mortar until it becomes a paste.
4. For two woodcocks, use less than one bread roll.
5. Pass through a fine sieve, put in a pot, fill halfway with good broth, and simmer until it becomes a paste.
6. Meanwhile, chop the woodcock intestines finely and stew them in butter with finely chopped green parsley and a little red onion.
7. Cut large triangles from bread and fry them in fat.
8. Spread the stewed mixture thickly on these bread pieces.
9. Place the pieces of breast cut from the woodcocks on the bottom of a dish, arrange the forcemeat and the spread bread slices neatly around the edge of the dish.
