## Ingredients

- three liters of fresh water
- juice of three lemons
- twenty-two dekagrams of sugar
- finely peeled zest of lemon

## Directions

1. Pour the lemon juice into the water
2. Add the sugar and finely peeled lemon zest
3. Cover and let stand cold for one hour
4. Strain and serve
