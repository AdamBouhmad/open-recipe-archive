## Ingredients

- 18 dekagram fine flour
- 14 dekagram ground sugar
- 14 dekagram almonds ground with shells
- 9 dekagram butter
- A little ground cinnamon
- Fat

## Directions

1. Work together on a board with a roller: 18 dekagram fine flour, 14 dekagram ground sugar, 14 dekagram almonds ground with shells, 9 dekagram butter, and a little ground cinnamon
2. Roll out to the thickness of a small finger
3. Cut out almonds with the appropriate mold
4. Fry in hot fat until beautifully golden
5. Place on a plate, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon, serve warm to the table
