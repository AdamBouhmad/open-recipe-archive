## Ingredients

- three kilograms pork with skin
- salt
- fat for basting

## Directions

1. Wash the pork with skin and salt it
2. Make deep cuts lengthwise and then crosswise into the skin with a sharp knife
3. Thread onto a spit
4. Roast to golden color, frequently basting with its fat
5. When well roasted, the skin should be crispy like glass but still tender to bite
