## Ingredients

- one tablespoon flour
- two deciliters sour cream
- one part beef stock
- one part cooked beets
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix one tablespoon of flour with two deciliters of sour cream
2. Add one part beef stock, one part cooked beets, vinegar and sugar
3. Cut the cooked beets into cubes or long pieces and add them shortly before serving
4. Bring to a boil once or twice and it is ready
5. This sauce should not cook long as it will otherwise lose its beautiful color
