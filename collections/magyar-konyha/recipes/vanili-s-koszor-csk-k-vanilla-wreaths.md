## Ingredients

- 10 dekagrams sugar
- 18 dekagrams fine flour
- 14 dekagrams butter
- 7 dekagrams blanched almonds, soaked
- 7 dekagrams sugar
- a little vanilla
- egg for glazing
- apricot syrup
- candied cherries for garnish

## Directions

1. Mix 10 dekagrams of sugar with 18 dekagrams of fine flour on a board
2. Work 14 dekagrams of butter into the flour and sugar with a rolling pin until the butter absorbs the flour
3. Work repeatedly with a wide knife until fine dough forms, adding no liquid
4. Roll the dough to knife-blade thickness
5. Cut small wreaths with a doughnut cutter and make a small hole in the center with a small cutter to form a wreath shape
6. Finely chop 7 dekagrams of soaked almonds and finely grind the same amount of sugar with a little vanilla, mix together
7. Brush the wreaths with beaten egg and sprinkle densely with the almond and sugar mixture
8. Brush the side of the wreaths not covered with almonds with apricot syrup
9. Place another wreath on top
10. Pile high on a plate and place candied cherries in the opening of the wreaths
11. Pretzels can also be made from this dough, sprinkled with almonds and sugar
