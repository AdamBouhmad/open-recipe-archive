## Ingredients

- fourteen dekagrams of bacon
- three dried bread rolls
- four deciliters of flour
- two deciliters of milk
- one whole egg
- one egg yolk
- fresh green parsley
- good beef broth

## Directions

1. Chop fourteen dekagrams of bacon into cubes and similarly chop three dried bread rolls
2. Let the bacon melt in a pan, but only until it looks glassy
3. Strain off the fat, add finely chopped fresh green parsley and let it brown slightly
4. Add the bread roll to it and similarly brown it with continuous stirring
5. Remove from heat, pour into a flat bowl and let cool
6. Make a dough from four deciliters of flour, two deciliters of milk, one whole egg and one egg yolk, kneading it well
7. Add the cooled bread roll mixture, knead it together, and form dumplings
8. Cook in good beef broth all at once
