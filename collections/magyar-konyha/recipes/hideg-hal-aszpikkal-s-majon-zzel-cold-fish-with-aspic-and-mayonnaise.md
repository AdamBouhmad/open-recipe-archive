## Ingredients

- a middle piece of pike or pike-perch
- salt
- water
- white vinegar
- whole red onion
- carrot
- peppercorns
- cloves
- aspic

## Directions

1. Clean and salt a middle piece of pike or pike-perch
2. Cook in equal parts water and white vinegar with whole red onion, carrot, peppercorns, and cloves
3. Let cool, then cut into finger-thick pieces
4. Arrange evenly and high in the center of a round plate
5. Coat smoothly with mayonnaise using a wide knife so no fish is visible
6. Pour aspic into a wide pan and when set, turn out the jelly
7. Cut into triangular pieces of pinky-finger length with a slightly warm knife so the wider side of the triangle faces the mayonnaise
8. Place an aspic star on top of the mayonnaise in the center for a neat appearance
