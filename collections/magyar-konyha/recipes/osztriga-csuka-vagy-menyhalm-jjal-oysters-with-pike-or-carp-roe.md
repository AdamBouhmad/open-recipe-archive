## Ingredients

- pike or carp roe
- salt
- fine breadcrumbs
- fat for frying
- oysters
- butter
- ground pepper
- lemon peel
- lemon juice
- bread for coating

## Directions

1. Salt the pike or carp roe and cut into small pieces
2. Roll in fine breadcrumbs and fry in fat
3. Open the oysters and place them in a butter-greased pot in alternating layers of roe and oyster
4. Sprinkle each layer with ground pepper and finely chopped lemon peel, adding pieces of butter
5. Squeeze the juice from two lemons into the pot
6. Cover with hot coals on top and let it steam slowly
7. When serving, arrange triangular fried bread slices around the edge of the plate or use puff pastry rings for garnish
