## Ingredients

- 7 dekagrams citronade, finely and lengthwise chopped
- 7 dekagrams almonds, finely and lengthwise chopped
- 3 dekagrams pistachios
- 14 dekagrams powdered sugar
- 1 whole egg
- 3 egg yolks
- puff pastry

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry
2. Finely and lengthwise chop the citronade, almonds, and pistachios
3. Pound 14 dekagrams sugar finely; place half in a bowl with the finely chopped almonds
4. Mix the other half of sugar with the chopped mixture
5. Beat 1 whole egg and 3 egg yolks into the mixture and whisk well
6. Roll out the puff pastry to knife-edge thickness
7. Fill the molds with it
8. Place some of the chopped mixture in each mold and cover with the beaten mixture instead of a lid
