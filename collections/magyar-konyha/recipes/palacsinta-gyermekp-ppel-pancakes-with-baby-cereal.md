## Ingredients

- twelve to fifteen pancakes
- three tablespoons flour
- milk
- seven dekagrams butter
- one whole egg and four egg yolks
- handful of swollen ground almonds
- seven dekagrams finely ground sugar
- vanilla
- three egg yolks
- two deciliters sour cream
- sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Cook twelve to fifteen pancakes
2. In a saucepan, mix three tablespoons flour smoothly with milk and place over heat, stirring until it begins to thicken, then cook a little longer
3. Pour onto a plate and cool
4. Beat seven dekagrams butter until foamy, add the cereal, then beat in one whole egg and four egg yolks one at a time
5. Add a handful of swollen finely ground almonds, seven dekagrams finely ground sugar and a little vanilla, and stir for another quarter hour
6. Spread the mixture on the pancakes, roll them up and cut into square pieces
7. Arrange the cut pancakes in even rows in a buttered saucepan
8. Mix three egg yolks with two deciliters sour cream, a little sugar and vanilla, pour over and steam for half an hour
