## Ingredients

- 25 decagrams bacon, sliced
- 1 kilogram pork belly, cut lengthwise

## Directions

1. Fry the sliced bacon and pork belly together in a pot
2. Add cabbage and braise
3. Stir frequently and cook until the cabbage is well-browned
