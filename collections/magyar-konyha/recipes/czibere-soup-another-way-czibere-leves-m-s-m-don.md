## Ingredients

- half a handful wheat flour bran
- one liter water
- bread starter
- millet porridge
- sour cream
- eggs

## Directions

1. Scald half a handful of wheat flour bran with one liter of water
2. When cooled slightly, strain and add a little bread starter to the lukewarm liquid
3. Leave in a warm place where it will sour
4. If it becomes too sour, mix with a little water and bring to a boil
5. When boiling, add a tablespoon of millet porridge
6. In a soup bowl, beat one quarter liter of sour cream and an egg until frothy
7. Pour the hot soup over it and mix together
