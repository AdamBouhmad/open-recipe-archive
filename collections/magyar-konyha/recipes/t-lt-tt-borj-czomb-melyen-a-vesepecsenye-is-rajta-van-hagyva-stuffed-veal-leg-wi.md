## Ingredients

- Veal hind quarter, upper part
- Six extracted anchovies
- Chopped meat from the removed upper part
- Red onion, small amount
- Lemon zest
- Marrow
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and spices
- Sour cream
- Oysters, extracted
- Potatoes, as available by season
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut off the upper part of the hind quarter down to the kidney, leaving the lower part on the leg
2. Deeply split the entire leg with a long knife
3. Prepare the filling: chop six extracted anchovies, the chopped meat from the removed part, small amount of red onion, lemon zest, marrow, and green parsley; salt and spice it; chop all finely, pound in mortar and add a little sour cream; extracted oysters and potatoes can be added according to season
4. Fill the roast with a large syringe, pierce the leg if desired, seal the openings with lard, and roast slowly
5. Cook butter and sour cream and baste the roast with it
6. Make a good sauce from the drippings with oysters and potatoes
