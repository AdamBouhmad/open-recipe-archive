## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Oil
- Mixed spices
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the artichokes thoroughly, cut off the tips, and cook until tender
2. Clean out the rough fibres from inside
3. Place on an oiled rack, drizzle with oil, and sprinkle with mixed spices
4. Roast slowly
5. Arrange on a plate, dress with vinegar and oil, and serve cold
