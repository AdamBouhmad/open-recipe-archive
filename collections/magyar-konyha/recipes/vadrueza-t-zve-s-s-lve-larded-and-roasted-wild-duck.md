## Ingredients

- wild duck
- brine
- salt
- bacon slices
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean the wild duck well
2. Place in brine for several days
3. Salt and lard neatly on the breast in six rows
4. During roasting, baste frequently with brine and sour cream
5. Serve with the sauce that dripped from it
