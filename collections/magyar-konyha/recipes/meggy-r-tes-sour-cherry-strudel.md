## Ingredients

- strudel dough
- 7 deciliters ripe sour cherries
- 14 decagrams coarsely crushed almonds
- 14 decagrams sugar
- 1 crumbly white bread, grated
- lemon peel, finely chopped
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough
2. Pit 7 deciliters of ripe sour cherries, stew them slightly and strain the juice
3. Mix 14 decagrams coarsely crushed almonds, 14 decagrams sugar, grated crumbly bread and finely chopped lemon peel
4. Stretch the dough finely and brush with melted butter
5. Spread the cherries evenly on it
6. Sprinkle the rest of the filling on top
7. Fold gently together
8. Place on a buttered parchment paper
9. Brush with melted butter and bake
