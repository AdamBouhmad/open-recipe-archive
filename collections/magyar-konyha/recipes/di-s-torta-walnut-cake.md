## Ingredients

- twenty-eight dekagrams of finely ground sugar
- twelve egg yolks
- twelve dekagrams of walnut kernel, finely broken
- zest of half a lemon
- a little cinnamon
- twelve egg whites
- a handful of white flour or fine breadcrumbs
- butter and breadcrumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Beat together the finely ground sugar and twelve egg yolks for half an hour.
2. Add the finely broken walnut kernel to the mixture.
3. Add the zest of half a lemon and a little cinnamon.
4. Finally add the twelve egg whites beaten into stiff foam, and a handful of white flour or fine breadcrumbs.
5. Beat well together.
6. Pour into a cake mold buttered and dusted with breadcrumbs.
7. Bake until done.
