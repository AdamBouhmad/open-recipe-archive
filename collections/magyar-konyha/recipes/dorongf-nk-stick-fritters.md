## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams flour
- 1 deciliter lukewarm sour cream
- 1.5 dekagrams brewer's yeast dissolved in sour cream
- 7 dekagrams melted butter
- 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
- salt
- beef fat
- finely chopped almonds
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make dough from flour, lukewarm sour cream, yeast, melted butter, eggs and egg yolks, and salt
2. Beat well and let rise
3. When slightly risen, place on a flour-dusted board and stretch to the thickness of a little finger
4. Cut into strips two finger-widths wide
5. Grease a spit with good beef fat, heat it, wrap the strips around it and press gently with your hand so they don't fall off
6. During frying, brush with butter and slowly fry until golden yellow
7. When half done, sprinkle with finely chopped almonds
8. Finish frying, remove from spit, sprinkle with sugar and fry the rest the same way
