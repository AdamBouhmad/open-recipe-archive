## Ingredients

- four eggs
- flour
- preserved plum jam
- salt
- brown butter

## Directions

1. Make a dough with four eggs and flour
2. Roll out the dough
3. Place small mounds of preserved plum jam at intervals
4. Cover with another piece of dough
5. Cut out semicircular shapes with a fluted pastry wheel
6. Cook in salted water
7. Rinse with cold water
8. Serve with hot brown butter
