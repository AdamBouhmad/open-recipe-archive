## Ingredients

- thin bacon slices
- chunks of beef for broth
- liver
- good quality bones, broken into pieces
- two heads of red onion
- several carrots
- kohlrabi
- kale
- parsley
- celery
- several peppercorns
- cloves

## Directions

1. Lay thin bacon slices in a pot
2. Add chunks of beef for broth, liver, and broken bones
3. Add two heads of red onion, several carrots, kohlrabi, kale, parsley, and celery, all sliced
4. Add several peppercorns and cloves
5. Cover the pot and let everything braise until it turns a beautiful brown color
6. To prevent burning, occasionally moisten with a little broth, but never stir so the broth stays clear
7. One hour before serving, pour regular broth over it and let it simmer slowly so the broth becomes strong
8. Strain and add meat dumplings or other garnish
