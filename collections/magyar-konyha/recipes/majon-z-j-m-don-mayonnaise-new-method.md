## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- eight tablespoons finest oil
- eight tablespoons fine vinegar with lemon juice
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat eight egg yolks, eight tablespoons finest oil, and eight tablespoons fine vinegar with lemon juice and a little salt to a frothy consistency in a stone vessel.
2. Place the vessel in another vessel filled with hot water and put it on the fire.
3. When the water around the vessel boils, stir until the mayonnaise begins to thicken.
4. Then place in cold water and stir until it cools completely.
5. This mayonnaise always succeeds and is ready in a quarter hour.
