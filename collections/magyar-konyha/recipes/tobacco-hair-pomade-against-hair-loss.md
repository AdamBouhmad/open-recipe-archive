## Ingredients

- 1.5 grams tobacco or powdered tobacco
- hot water
- 10 dekagrams clarified beef marrow

## Directions

1. Place tobacco in a container
2. Pour hot water over it
3. After ten hours, strain and press the infusion
4. Let the liquid settle
5. Evaporate until 45 centigrams remain
6. Mix with 10 dekagrams clarified beef marrow
7. Scent as desired
