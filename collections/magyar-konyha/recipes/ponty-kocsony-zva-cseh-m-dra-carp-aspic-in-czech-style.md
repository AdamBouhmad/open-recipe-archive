## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg live carp
- 0.5 cup strong vinegar
- 1 large carrot
- 1 onion
- parsley root
- small piece of celery
- 1 liter hot beer
- 1 tablespoon honey
- cloves
- clove pepper
- coarsely ground pepper
- half a lemon peel
- walnuts
- carp blood

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the live carp into large pieces and carefully collect the blood in a bowl with half a cup of strong vinegar
2. Cut the carrot, onion, parsley root, and celery into fine strips and brown them nicely in a clean pan over flame with continuous stirring
3. Transfer to a pot, pour over 1 liter hot beer and 1 tablespoon honey
4. Add cloves, clove pepper, coarsely ground pepper, and half a lemon peel cut lengthwise, add the fish pieces, pour in a small cup of vinegar and cook the fish well
5. Place the fish pieces in a deep dish, but continue cooking the head separately
6. In another pot, brown 2 tablespoons honey or sugar, and when nicely browned, add a handful of coarsely crushed walnuts and the reserved carp blood to the sauce
7. Bring to a boil several times, then pour over the fish and let it set in a cold place
