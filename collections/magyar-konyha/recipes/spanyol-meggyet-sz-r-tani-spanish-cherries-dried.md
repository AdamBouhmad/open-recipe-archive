## Ingredients

- Spanish cherries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from the Spanish cherries and place them on a drying board
2. Once they are dried so that no more juice comes out of them, remove the pit
3. Press two or three together and press them with your fingers to at least the width of a krajcár
4. Place them in the cooled oven for a few hours, sprinkle with sugar while warm, and pack into containers
