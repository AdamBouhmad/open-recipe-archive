## Ingredients

- two spoonfuls of béchamel
- two egg yolks
- half a kilogram of crab butter

## Directions

1. Place two spoonfuls of béchamel in a pot and heat well
2. Beat in two egg yolks
3. Add the crab butter in small portions while continuously beating
4. When ready, serve hot
