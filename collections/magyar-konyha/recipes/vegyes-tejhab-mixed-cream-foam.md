## Ingredients

- 1 liter good heavy cream
- ground sugar
- vanilla
- freshly pressed raspberry juice
- 10 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- a little egg white
- strong, cooled black coffee
- sugar
- 4.5 dekagrams isinglass

## Directions

1. Place 1 liter of good heavy cream in a large brass preserving basin, set on ice or cool in a good cellar
2. Beat with a whip until the entire mass becomes thick foam
3. Divide into three parts
4. Leave the first part white and add enough ground sugar and vanilla to make it sufficiently sweet
5. Mix the second part quickly with freshly pressed raspberry juice, 10 dekagrams finely ground sugar, and a little egg white
6. Mix the third part with strong, previously cooled black coffee and enough sugar to make it sufficiently sweet (2-3 tablespoons of coffee with a third of the foam is enough to give it the proper brown color and good coffee flavor)
7. Dissolve isinglass, strain it, cool it, and divide it among the three parts
8. Pour the coffee foam first into a round mold or basin and set on ice
9. While the first is freezing, place the other two foams in a cool place
10. When the first has frozen, pour the red foam on top, freeze again, and finally add the white foam
11. Before serving, dip the mold briefly in warm water and carefully turn the foam out onto a plate
