## Ingredients

- elongated rolls
- preserved apricots
- beaten eggs
- fine breadcrumbs
- fat for frying
- sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut elongated rolls into knife-blade-thin slices.
2. Spread with preserved apricots and place another slice on top.
3. Soak in well-sweetened red wine until saturated.
4. Dip in beaten eggs and roll in fine breadcrumbs.
5. Fry in fat and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
