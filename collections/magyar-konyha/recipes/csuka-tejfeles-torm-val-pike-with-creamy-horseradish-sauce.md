## Ingredients

- pike
- salt
- a little carrot
- parsley root
- two heads of red onion
- horseradish root
- flour
- butter
- fish broth

## Directions

1. After cleaning and filleting the fish, clean it completely of blood, salt it and let it stand for a while.
2. Cook a little carrot with parsley root and two heads of red onion cut into four pieces in salted water.
3. Place half a grated horseradish root in a pan, add a spoonful of flour, place a piece of butter in it and with a spoon of the broth in which the fish was cooked, bring the horseradish to a slight boil.
4. Place the fish on the serving dish and pour the sauce over it.
