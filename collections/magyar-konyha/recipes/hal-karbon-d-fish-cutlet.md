## Ingredients

- about one kilogram pike from the middle
- salt
- water
- half kilogram carp
- flour
- butter
- half grated breadcrumbs
- milk
- small red onion
- lemon zest
- pepper
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook about one kilogram of pike from the middle in salted water, only until the fish is not raw
2. Take half kilogram of carp, salt and coat in flour well, then fry in butter
3. Remove bones from both; soak half grated breadcrumbs in milk, wring out and chop fine with the fish
4. Similarly add a small red onion, a little lemon zest, and a little ground pepper
5. Mix well with three egg yolks
6. Shape small cutlets from this mixture
