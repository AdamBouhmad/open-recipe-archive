## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- red onion head, divided into four parts
- lemon rind
- pea water
- lemon juice
- cleaned squash
- eggs

## Directions

1. Melt a piece of butter and fry a full spoon of flour in it until golden
2. Add the red onion head divided into four parts and lemon rind
3. Fill with pea water and cook until done
4. Add the juice of one lemon and cook the cleaned squash in this sauce in such a way that it does not fall apart
5. Before serving, beat two egg yolks in a pot, strain the sauce over them, place on coals, stir constantly until it begins to thicken
6. Pour it over the plated squash and serve immediately
