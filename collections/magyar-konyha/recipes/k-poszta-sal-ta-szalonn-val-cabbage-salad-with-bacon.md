## Ingredients

- Tender red head cabbage
- Salt
- Diced bacon
- White vinegar

## Directions

1. Take tender red head cabbage, remove the coarse ribs from the leaves, press the leaves together firmly, and slice them as finely as possible
2. Cut a piece of bacon into small cubes and fry it in a pan until beautifully golden
3. Remove the bacon and pour white vinegar over it, then bring it to a boil
4. Place the sliced cabbage in a basin, salt it, and pour the hot vinegar over it
5. Cover well and let it stand for a while
6. Strain off the vinegar, bring it to a boil again, and pour it over the cabbage as before, repeating this process once or twice more
7. Place on a warm plate, top with the fried bacon, add a little vinegar, and serve
