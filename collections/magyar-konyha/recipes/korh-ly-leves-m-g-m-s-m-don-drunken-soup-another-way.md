## Ingredients

- One winter cabbage head
- Lard, a small amount
- Flour, a small amount
- Meat broth
- Egg yolks, six to eight
- Sour cream, a small amount
- Lard, a small amount
- Bread slices
- Roasted capon and chicken breasts, finely cut
- Veal and mutton

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves from a winter cabbage head and rinse well, then chop finely
2. In a copper pan, melt a small amount of lard, add a small amount of flour and then the cabbage, which should be braised gently
3. Transfer everything to a pot and fill with meat broth
4. Thicken by mixing in six to eight egg yolks, add a small amount of sour cream and lard
5. Cook slowly over moderate heat, being careful not to let the soup become too thick
6. When serving, add bread slices, finely cut pieces from roasted capon and chicken breasts, and veal and mutton
