## Ingredients

- 7 lemons
- 8 deciliters water
- 35 dekagrams ground sugar
- 2 lemon peels
- 4.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Mix juice of 7 lemons with 8 deciliters water.
2. Dissolve 35 dekagrams ground sugar (rubbed on 2 lemon peels) in this liquid.
3. Mix in 4.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass.
4. Strain through stretched cloth until beautifully clear.
