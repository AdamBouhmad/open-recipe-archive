## Ingredients

- butter pastry dough
- 8 eggs
- crayfish tails, halved lengthwise
- ham, cut into large cubes

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter pastry dough as described below
2. Roll it out to the thickness of a knife blade
3. Line a square form smoothly and evenly without looking
4. Fill with clean paper and bake quickly
5. Prepare a mixture from 8 eggs to which on fasting day only crayfish tails halved lengthwise are mixed in, or on meat days ham is cut into large cubes and placed in it
6. When the case is baked, remove the paper and turn out from the form
7. Place on a long dish and pour the mixture into it
