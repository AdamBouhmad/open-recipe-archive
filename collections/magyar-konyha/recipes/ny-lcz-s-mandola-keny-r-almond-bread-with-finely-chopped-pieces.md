## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams blanched almonds
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- a little vanilla
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Finely chop the blanched almonds
2. Crush the sugar with a little vanilla and sift it
3. Whip the 3 egg whites into stiff peaks and combine with the sugar
4. Beat together for half an hour until it becomes a thick, foamy icing, but it should not be thin; it is safer to add only half of the stiffly beaten egg whites at first, and if after some beating it is still very thick, add the remaining egg white
5. Once the icing is foamy, add the almonds and mix with the rest
6. Using a spoon, form small peaked shapes on wafer paper
7. Bake in a gentle oven; when well dried, break off the wafer paper and pile high on a plate
