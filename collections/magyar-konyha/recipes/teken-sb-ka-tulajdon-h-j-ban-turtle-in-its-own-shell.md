## Ingredients

- turtle
- salt
- water
- butter
- bread crumbs
- lemon peel
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook the turtle in salted water until very soft
2. Open the shell and remove the meat into four pieces
3. Set aside the liquid found in the shell
4. Carefully separate the liver from the gallbladder without breaking it
5. Place several pieces of butter in the shell
6. Add the liquid found in it
7. Add the meat cleaned of its skin
8. Add a little bread crumbs, several pieces of butter, chopped lemon peel, and lemon juice
9. Place in a pot
10. Put hot coals on top
11. Bring to a vigorous boil and the dish is ready
