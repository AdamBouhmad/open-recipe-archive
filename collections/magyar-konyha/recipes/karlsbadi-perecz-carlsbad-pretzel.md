## Ingredients

- one and a half kilograms of flour
- five deciliters of cold sour cream
- seven dekagrams of cheese yeast
- one tablespoon of crushed sugar
- salt
- thirty-five dekagrams of butter

## Directions

1. From one and a half kilograms of flour, five deciliters of cold sour cream, seven dekagrams of cheese yeast, and one tablespoon of crushed sugar, well mixed, prepare good firm sweet bread dough
2. Salt the dough
3. Once it has been kneaded until it separates from the hand, knead in thirty-five dekagrams of butter in pieces
4. Once all the butter is in and the dough is well kneaded, form small pretzels
5. Place these in a warm place and let them rise
6. Brush with melted butter
7. Bake in a cooled oven
8. These pretzels are very good with tea and coffee
