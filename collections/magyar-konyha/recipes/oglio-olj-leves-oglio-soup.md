## Ingredients

- large onion
- kohlrabi
- carrot
- four hard cabbage heads
- several peppercorns and clove pepper
- eighty decagrams (1 1/2 pounds) soup meat
- two calf feet
- one rooster
- bacon slices

## Directions

1. Cut onion, kohlrabi, carrot into slices
2. Cut four hard cabbage heads into four equal parts
3. Cut the eighty decagrams of soup meat, two calf feet, and one rooster into pieces
4. Slowly braise all of these in a pot lined with bacon slices until nicely browned on the bottom
5. During cooking frequently pour little meat stock over so it does not burn
6. Cook for one and a half hours and then set aside to settle
7. Strain and serve in small cups at table
8. From the same soup are also made the fat slices, except they must be well cleaned of fat and cooked until thickened, which when cooled can be cut up and stored
