## Ingredients

- About 1/2 kilogram of roasted capon, turkey, or veal
- Bread, cut into large pieces
- Fat
- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- Nutmeg flower, a small amount
- 1.5 liters good beef broth

## Directions

1. From roasted capon, turkey, or veal, take about 1/2 kilogram and remove all brown skin, then chop finely with a kitchen knife.
2. Fry large pieces of milky bread cut into cubes in fat until a light yellow color.
3. Pound the 4 hard-boiled egg yolks finely in a large mortar together with the chopped meat, then place in a pot.
4. Pour 1.5 liters of good broth over it, add a small amount of nutmeg flower, and let it simmer for a good half hour.
5. Pass through a sieve and pour into marrow dumplings.
