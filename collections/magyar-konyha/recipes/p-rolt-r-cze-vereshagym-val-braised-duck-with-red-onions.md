## Ingredients

- duck
- bacon slices
- ham
- red onions
- carrot
- flour
- broth
- white vinegar
- lemon zest
- butter

## Directions

1. After plucking the duck, salt and prepare it.
2. Slice bacon, ham, red onions and carrot into rings and place in a pot.
3. Place the ducks on top and braise slowly until soft and browned on all sides.
4. Remove the ducks, sprinkle two small spoonfuls of flour on them and brown them.
5. Dilute with good broth, add a little white vinegar and cook the sauce well.
6. Strain the sauce through a sieve, return the ducks to it, add a little lemon zest and braise slightly more.
7. Boil 30-40 walnut-sized red onions in water for a minute, having cut off a leaf from top and bottom, then place in cold water and peel the upper half.
8. Heat a piece of butter, place the onions in it, turn them frequently so they brown evenly, add a little brown broth and salt, cook until tender without falling apart.
9. When serving, place the ducks in the center of the dish and arrange the braised onions in a wreath shape around them.
