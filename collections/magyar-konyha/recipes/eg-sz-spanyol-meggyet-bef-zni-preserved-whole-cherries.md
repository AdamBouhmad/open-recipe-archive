## Ingredients

- 500 grams sugar
- 1 glass water
- 500 grams cherries

## Directions

1. Boil 500 grams of sugar with one glass of water, removing the scum during boiling so it is completely clear, and boil until it throws heavy drops.
2. Then add 500 grams of the most beautiful type of cherry, having trimmed half of the stem, do not stir it with a spoon, but shake the pan several times so it mixes well with the sugar, and boil for a quarter hour.
3. Then remove the cherries with a slotted spoon and boil the juice for another quarter hour.
4. When removing from the fire, place the cherries back into it, and handle as you would other preserved items when filling.
