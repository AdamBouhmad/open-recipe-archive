## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- 14 dekagramm finely ground sugar
- 14 dekagramm finely ground almonds
- 7 dekagramm finely chopped citron peel
- wafer strips
- citron peel leaves

## Directions

1. Beat the stiff foam of 2 egg whites with 14 dekagramm finely ground sugar for one hour until it becomes thick frosting
2. Add 14 dekagramm finely ground almonds and 7 dekagramm finely chopped citron peel so it becomes a thick paste
3. Spread this paste onto strips cut from wafer, one finger long and two fingers wide, to a thickness of half a finger
4. Bake slowly in a cool oven
5. When the wafers are coated with the mixture, cut thin leaves from citron peel and arrange them on top in a beautiful pattern
