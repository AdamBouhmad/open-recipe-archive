## Ingredients

- twenty-eight decagrams of curd cheese
- four eggs
- sugar
- sour cream
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix twenty-eight decagrams of curd cheese with four eggs and a little sugar
2. Add a little sour cream
3. Treat this filling the same way as the plum jam filling
4. Cut and cook as for fruit dumplings
