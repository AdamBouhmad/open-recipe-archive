## Ingredients

- small muscat pears
- sugar
- water
- white vinegar
- 500 grams pears requires 25 dekagrams sugar, 1 glass water, and 2 tablespoons strong white vinegar

## Directions

1. For 500 grams of such small pears, take 25 dekagrams sugar
2. Clean the pears with a cloth and cut off half of the stem
3. Cook the sugar with 1 glass water until completely dissolved, add 2 tablespoons strong white vinegar and boil several times
4. Place the pears in, turn with a silver spoon, boil for a few minutes, then cool
5. Arrange the pears neatly in the jar, pour the liquid underneath, bind with white blotting paper and bladder, boil for 3 minutes, then cool completely in water
