## Ingredients

- strudel dough
- a piece of lean ham, finely chopped
- melted butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 deciliters sour cream

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough, work it well, cover with a warm baking dish and let it rest
2. Meanwhile, finely chop a piece of lean ham
3. Stretch the dough finely and brush with melted butter
4. Mix 2 whole eggs with 2 deciliters sour cream
5. Pour this over the dough and spread evenly on all sides
6. Fold gently together
7. Place in a buttered baking dish
8. Sprinkle the strudel with melted butter
9. Bake until golden brown
