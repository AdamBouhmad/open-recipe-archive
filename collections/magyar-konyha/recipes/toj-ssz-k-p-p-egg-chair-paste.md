## Ingredients

- 6 hard-boiled eggs
- 14 dekagrams softened almonds
- 7 dekagrams butter beaten to a foam
- 1 whole egg
- 4 egg yolks
- powdered sugar to taste
- vanilla to taste
- 3 egg whites beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Pound the hard-boiled eggs and softened almonds separately until fine
2. Mix the pounded mixture into the foamy butter
3. Add the whole egg and four egg yolks and mix together
4. Add powdered sugar and vanilla to taste
5. Gently fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
6. Place in a buttered dish, form a ring around the edge, and bake slowly
