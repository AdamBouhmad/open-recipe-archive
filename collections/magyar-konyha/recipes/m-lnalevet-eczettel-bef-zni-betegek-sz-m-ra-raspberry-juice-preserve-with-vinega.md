## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters raspberries
- 4 decilitres good white vinegar
- 56 dekagrams sugar
- 4 decilitres water

## Directions

1. Clean the raspberries, wash them, put them in a clean vessel; pour 4 decilitres of good white vinegar over 1.5 liters of raspberries and let it stand for several hours.
2. Press through cloth and strain; cook 56 dekagrams of clear juice with 70 dekagrams sugar and 4 decilitres water until the sugar threads and throws large bubbles, then add the juice, cook and treat it the same as the previous recipe.
