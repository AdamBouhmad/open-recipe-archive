## Ingredients

- Several potatoes
- Water
- Green parsley, finely chopped
- Marjoram
- Red onion
- Roux
- Paprika
- Salt
- Milk

## Directions

1. Wash and peel several potatoes, cut them into cubes, and put them on to cook in water.
2. When the potato is soft, make a thin roux with finely chopped parsley green, marjoram, and red onion.
3. Brown it with paprika and pour over the potato cooking liquid.
4. Finally add salt and milk and serve.
