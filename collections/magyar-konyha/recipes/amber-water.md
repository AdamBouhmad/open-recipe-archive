## Ingredients

- 5 liters 70° purified alcohol
- 17 deciliters water
- 8 grams amber
- 14 dekagrams lemon peel
- 14 dekagrams cinnamon
- 6 grams vanilla
- 8 grams bergamot oil
- 10 drops rose oil

## Directions

1. Pour alcohol and water over crushed spices
2. Digest
3. Add fragrant oils
