## Ingredients

- kidney
- salt
- vinegar
- water
- red onion
- carrot
- lemon zest
- bay leaves
- peppercorns
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and bones from a nice kidney and salt it
2. Prepare a marinade from one part vinegar and two parts water
3. Add one head of red onion, carrot, lemon zest, two bay leaves, and several peppercorns to the marinade and cook it well
4. Pour the hot marinade over the kidney, cover it, and let it stand in a cool place for 12-24 hours
5. Pierce the kidney in three to four rows
6. Remove the red onion and carrot from the marinade and place them in a pot with the kidney on top
7. Place coals on top and braise slowly for two hours until the color becomes yellowish-brown
8. Pour four deciliters of sour cream over it and braise for another quarter hour until the sauce becomes a nice yellow color
9. Place the kidney on a serving dish and pour the sauce underneath
