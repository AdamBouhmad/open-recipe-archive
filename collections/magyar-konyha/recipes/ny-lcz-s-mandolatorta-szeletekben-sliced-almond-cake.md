## Ingredients

- 18 dekagramm finely ground and sifted sugar
- egg whites
- lemon juice
- almonds, sliced
- chocolate frosting
- 10 dekagramm sugar rubbed with one or two oranges
- egg white for paste
- wafer strips

## Directions

1. Whip sugar, lemon juice and egg white into thick frosting
2. Gently mix in sliced almonds
3. In separate bowl, whip chocolate frosting without lemon juice and mix in sliced almonds
4. Butter a pyramid cake form
5. Cut wafer into finger-wide strips and coat with filling to knife-blade thickness
6. Line form alternately with white and dark strips
7. Dry in cooled oven
8. Make hard paste from sugar rubbed with orange and egg white
9. Roll out to two knife-blade thicknesses and cut leaf shapes with small pastry cutter
10. Cut centers with tiny cutter to form wreaths
11. Place wreaths on buttered sheet and dry in cooled oven
12. Glue wreaths to cake with hardened sugar
13. Place preserved green plums, preserved walnuts, or preserved green almonds alternately in each wreath
