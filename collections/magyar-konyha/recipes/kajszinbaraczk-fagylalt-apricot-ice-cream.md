## Ingredients

- 40-45 large ripe apricots
- 0.5 kilogram sugar
- 7 deciliters water
- juice of 3 lemons

## Directions

1. Peel 40-45 large ripe apricots, split them, remove the pits, and place in a large soup bowl
2. Cook 0.5 kilogram of sugar with 7 deciliters of water until dissolved
3. Pour the hot mixture over the apricots, cover, and let stand for 24 hours
4. Strain through a sieve without pressing
5. Mix in the juice of 3 lemons
6. Treat it like the others
