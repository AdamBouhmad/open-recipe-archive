## Ingredients

- cherries
- 1/2 kilogram sugar per 1/2 kilogram juice

## Directions

1. Crush the cherries, express the juice, strain it, take the same amount of sugar for each 1/2 kilogram of juice, otherwise treat it exactly as described in recipe 936.
