## Ingredients

- 9 decagrams butter, beaten until fluffy
- 6 whole eggs
- 1.5 decagrams soaked yeast
- 2 spoonfuls broken sugar
- a little salt
- 4 deciliters warm sour cream
- about 1 liter flour
- 14 decagrams plumped almonds
- 14 decagrams sugar
- 7 deciliters stewed sour cherries

## Directions

1. Beat 9 decagrams butter until fluffy and add 6 whole eggs one by one
2. Add 1.5 decagrams soaked yeast, 2 spoonfuls broken sugar, a little salt, 4 deciliters warm sour cream and enough flour so the dough is neither too thin nor too stiff
3. Let the dough rise well covered
4. Meanwhile grind 14 decagrams plumped almonds and 14 decagrams sugar
5. Stew 7 deciliters cherries, pit them and strain
6. When the dough has risen well, place on a cloth sprinkled with flour and stretch to knife-edge thickness
7. Only at the moment of use, mix the sugar and almonds and sprinkle on the dough
8. Place the strained cherries on top and sprinkle with broken sugar
9. Fold gently the edge of the dough that remained unfilled
10. Stretch the dough lengthwise so the strudel is not too thick
11. Place on a buttered baking sheet
12. Let rise for another quarter hour
13. Brush with egg white and sugar
14. Bake in gentle but sustained heat
15. When baked, place on a sieve and cool
16. Serve dusted with sugar
17. This can also be made the same way with crushed walnuts, sugar, vanilla and chopped raisins
