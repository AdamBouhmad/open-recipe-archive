## Ingredients

- goose liver
- 7 dekagrams butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 rolls
- milk
- green parsley, finely chopped
- red onion
- salt
- pepper
- fine bread crumbs
- 2 egg whites
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the goose liver and press through a sieve
2. Beat 7 dekagrams butter until foamy
3. Mix in 2 eggs and 2 egg yolks well beaten
4. Soak 2 rolls with crusts removed in milk
5. Add finely chopped green parsley, red onion, salt, and pepper
6. Add the soaked roll and fine bread crumbs
7. Once everything is mixed, fold in the foam from 2 egg whites
8. Pour the mixture into the pot, bake, and cut into slanted rectangles
9. Pour broken liver broth over it
