## Ingredients

- ripe cherries
- flour
- sugar
- wine
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Tie the ripe cherries together in groups of four
2. Cut their stems evenly
3. Prepare a thin batter from flour, sugar, and wine, like breadcrumb dough
4. Dip the cherries well into the batter
5. Place in hot fat and shake the pan so they do not settle too quickly
6. Transfer to a strainer and place absorbent paper underneath to absorb the fat
7. Sprinkle with sugar
8. Arrange on a platter with the stem end up
9. Serve warm
