## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- red onion
- vegetables and roots
- water
- roux
- salt

## Directions

1. Crush the tomatoes and place in a pot with red onion and vegetables
2. Pour in a little water and let cook well
3. Make a roux thick or thin to taste
4. Press the tomatoes through a sieve and add to the roux
5. Boil everything well together and salt to taste
6. If desired, add noodle dumplings, short pasta, or rice to the soup
