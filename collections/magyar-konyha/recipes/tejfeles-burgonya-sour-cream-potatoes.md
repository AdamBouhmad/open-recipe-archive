## Ingredients

- several potatoes
- half kilogram meat
- several eggs
- seven decilitres sour cream
- breadcrumbs
- pepper
- salt
- fat

## Directions

1. Cook several potatoes in their skins
2. While they cook, roast half kilogram meat and cook several eggs hard
3. Peel the boiled potatoes and cut into slices, chop the meat very finely
4. Cut the eggs into slices
5. Prepare a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pot
6. Layer in the pot: one layer of potatoes (salted), then a layer of chopped meat and eggs with sour cream; repeat layers of potatoes, meat, and eggs
7. Add a little pepper and plenty of grated breadcrumbs between each layer
8. Place in the oven for quarter of an hour
9. The potatoes can also be prepared this way with only sour cream, without meat and eggs
