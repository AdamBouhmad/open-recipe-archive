## Ingredients

- 3 pieces grated chocolate
- powdered sugar
- shelled almonds finely chopped
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 lemon juice
- crushed sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. Measure 3 pieces grated chocolate by the cup
2. Take the same amount of powdered sugar and the same amount of finely chopped shelled almonds
3. Work together with 3 egg yolks and 1/2 lemon juice until very stiff
4. Place on crushed sugar so it does not stick to the board
5. Flatten with a sugared knife to finger thickness
6. With a sugared knife cut into finger-thick rods
7. Place on a buttered and floured pan and bake at gentle heat
