## Ingredients

- ½ kilogram sugar
- 10 decagrams violet leaves, cleaned of all green parts
- 1 glass water

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar in a glass of water, skimming the foam while boiling, until it threads
2. Remove from heat and let cool halfway
3. Pound the violet leaves very finely in a mortar and extract the juice
4. Pour the juice into the sugar and stir as quickly as possible with a new wooden spoon
5. Fill the mass into a case made of white double paper
6. When cooled, peel the conserve from the paper and cut into rod- or square-shaped pieces with a knife
7. Note: conserves should be stirred until the boiled sugar reaches the consistency and density of thick baby food; then mix in four spoonfuls of juice from any pressed fruit
