## Ingredients

- seven decziliters fine flour
- one whole egg and one egg yolk
- 7 dekagramm butter
- salt
- milk
- half kilogram almond
- one lemon's juice
- lemon peel
- 14 dekagramm sugar
- 10 dekagramm candied citron
- five dekagramm pistachio
- melted butter
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. From seven decziliters fine flour, one whole egg, one egg yolk, 7 dekagramm butter, salt, and milk, prepare strudel dough
2. Work it well and let it rest for a while
3. Coarsely crush half kilogram of almond
4. Moisten it with one lemon's juice and chop the peel finely
5. Finely crush 14 dekagramm sugar
6. Finely chop 10 dekagramm candied citron and five dekagramm pistachio
7. Mix all ingredients together well
8. Stretch the dough finely
9. Brush with melted butter
10. Spread the filling smoothly on it
11. Fold it together
12. Place the strudel on a butter-brushed pastry paper
13. Sprinkle with sugar and bake
