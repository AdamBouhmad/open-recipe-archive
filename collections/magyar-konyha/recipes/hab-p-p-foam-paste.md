## Ingredients

- 9 dekagrams preserved apricot
- 7 dekagrams finely powdered sugar
- juice of half a lemon
- 6 egg whites beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Place preserved apricot in a bowl
2. Add finely powdered sugar and stir for a quarter hour
3. Press in the juice of half a lemon
4. Gradually fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites by spoonfuls
5. Place the foam in the center of a buttered dish and heap it into a peak shape
6. Using the edge of a card, make five to six deep long cuts in the foam at proper distances from one another
7. Form a head from the foam and place on top
8. Bake in a very cool oven; when done in half an hour, serve immediately to the table as it collapses easily
