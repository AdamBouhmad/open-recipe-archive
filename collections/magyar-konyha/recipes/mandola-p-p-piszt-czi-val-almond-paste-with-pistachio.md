## Ingredients

- 14 dekagramm butter, beaten foamy
- 14 dekagramm finely ground almonds
- 10 egg yolks
- 7 dekagramm peeled pistachios
- 14 dekagramm citronade
- 14 dekagramm finely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 14 dekagramm butter until foamy.
2. Add 14 dekagramm finely ground almonds and beat in 10 egg yolks one after another, stirring for a full hour.
3. Cut 7 dekagramm peeled pistachios and 14 dekagramm citronade into long strips.
4. Add this mixture along with 14 dekagramm finely ground sugar to the beaten paste.
5. Stir until everything is well combined.
6. Pour onto a buttered torta leaf and bake evenly.
