## Ingredients

- 56 dekagrams ripe quince
- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 4 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Peel the quince and cut into slices two centimeters wide
2. Remove the seedy portion from the center and place in cold water
3. Boil the sugar with four deciliters of water until dissolved
4. Place the quince slices in the boiling syrup and stir continuously and very carefully with a silver spoon so the slices cook evenly
5. Cook until they can be pierced with a needle, but they must not become completely soft
6. Remove from heat and cool completely
7. Fill the jars, place them in a water bath (see recipe 961), and leave for at most three minutes
