## Ingredients

- one teaspoon of Liebig meat extract
- finely chopped lemon zest
- several peppercorns
- allspice
- a few bay leaves
- meat broth
- one tablespoon of French mustard
- a little anchovy butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook a teaspoon of Liebig meat extract, finely chopped lemon zest, peppercorns, allspice, and bay leaves well in meat broth
2. Add one tablespoon of French mustard, a little anchovy butter, and lemon juice
3. Bring to a boil again
4. Press through a sieve
5. If very thick, dilute with a little hot meat broth
