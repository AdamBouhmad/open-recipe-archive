## Ingredients

- water
- one litre cornmeal
- fourteen dekagrams bacon slices
- parmesan cheese (same amount as bacon)
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil water in a one-and-a-half litre pot, and when it boils well, pour out one quarter
2. Salt the water remaining in the pot, place a cooking spoon in the center, and around it pack in one litre of sweet cornmeal, and let it cook for half an hour more
3. Meanwhile, fry out fourteen dekagrams of bacon slices and grate the same amount of parmesan cheese onto a plate
4. Into the bottom of a warmed bowl, place one quarter of the melted bacon fat and cheese, then one quarter of the cooked cornmeal, then bacon fat and cheese again, then cornmeal, and so on
5. Finally, place the bacon slices and remaining cheese on top
