## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- hot vinegar
- water
- carrots
- parsley root
- onion, sliced
- celery
- bay leaf
- ginger
- cloves
- peppercorns
- lemon zest
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Wash and skin the rabbit thoroughly
2. Lard it nicely
3. Pour hot vinegar and water over it
4. Add sliced carrots, parsley root, sliced onion, celery, bay leaf, ginger, cloves, peppercorns and lemon zest
5. Let the rabbit sit in this marinade for one or two days, turning it several times during this period
6. When ready to cook, thread the rabbit onto a spit, tie its legs up
7. Roast, basting alternately with butter and sour sauce, then with sour cream at intervals
8. When well roasted, place on a platter and garnish with lemon slices
9. Strain the liquid in the pan and serve separately in a small dish
