## Ingredients

- small cleaned chickens
- butter
- flour
- good stock
- sour cream
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. Prepare and blanch small chickens
2. Melt butter without making it hot
3. Add 2 tablespoons flour and lightly brown
4. Deglaze with good stock
5. Add 2 tablespoons sour cream and cook together well
6. Strain the sauce
7. Place the blanched chickens in the sauce and cook until sufficiently tender
8. Clean cauliflower and cook in salted water
9. Keep small rosettes whole, cut larger ones into pieces
10. Add the cauliflower to the sauce and cook together
11. For serving: place the chickens in the center of the dish and arrange the cauliflower around
12. Enrich the sauce with egg yolks (fricassée style) and pour over the chickens
