## Ingredients

- 180 grams rice
- 70 grams raisins
- 50 grams currants
- 4 tart apples
- 180 grams crushed sugar
- butter
- preserved sour cherries
- preserved fruit with syrup

## Directions

1. Soften the rice in warm water for a quarter hour, then strain through a sieve
2. Clean, wash and sort the raisins and currants
3. Peel the tart apples, slice them into pieces, and sift the crushed sugar
4. Line the bottom of a buttered pot thickly with apple slices
5. Make a layer of heavily sugared rice, then a small amount of raisins and currants
6. Make another layer of apples, sugar, and rice, then a layer of preserved sour cherries
7. Add more apples and the remaining raisins and currants
8. Top with a final layer of sugared rice
9. Fill the pot with well-sugared preserved fruit syrup so that it covers the rice
10. Place in a warm oven and leave until the liquid is completely absorbed
11. While cooking, prepare cone-shaped pastry
12. Turn the rice out onto a plate and decorate beautifully with the pastry
