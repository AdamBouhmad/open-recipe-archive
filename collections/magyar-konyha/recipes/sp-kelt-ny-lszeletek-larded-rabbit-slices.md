## Ingredients

- one rabbit, cleaned
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned rabbit into pieces two fingers thick
2. Lard the pieces
3. Let them sit in a sour brine for several days
4. Braise them
5. Glaze them
6. Serve in peppery or tomato sauce
