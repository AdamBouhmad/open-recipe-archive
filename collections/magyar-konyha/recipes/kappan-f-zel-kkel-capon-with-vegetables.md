## Ingredients

- a Styrian capon
- bacon slices
- strong beef broth
- eight white cauliflower rosettes
- salted water
- butter
- four large tomatoes
- meat hash filling
- eight pieces of good carrots
- capon broth
- three tablespoons butter sauce
- four egg yolks
- fresh butter
- half lemon juice

## Directions

1. After cleanest plucking, lard the capon
2. Place in a suitable pot, cover with bacon slices
3. Pour strong beef broth over it and braise slowly
4. Clean eight white cauliflower rosettes and cook tender in salted water with a little butter, then cover and set aside
5. Cut four large tomatoes in half, fill with meat hash, place in a pot
6. Pour beef broth over and place coals on top, braise until tender
7. Similarly cut eight pieces of good carrots neatly and braise tender with broth and sugar
8. Strain the capon broth and mix with three tablespoons butter sauce
9. Cook down to half
10. Beat well with four egg yolks, a piece of fresh butter, and juice of half a lemon
11. Heat through, strain into a small pot and keep warm
12. At serving time, cut the capon neatly into pieces
13. Arrange the vegetables around it and pour the sauce over
