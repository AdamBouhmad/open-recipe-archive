## Ingredients

- 1/4 litre milk
- 8 dekagrams butter
- 16 dekagrams flour
- 3 whole eggs
- 12 dekagrams grated Parmesan cheese
- salt
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Put 1/4 litre milk and 8 dekagrams butter into a saucepan and let them boil together well
2. Mix in 16 dekagrams flour and stir over the heat until the mixture falls from the spoon
3. Transfer the dough to a ceramic bowl, stir until cool and gradually mix in 3 whole eggs, 12 dekagrams grated Parmesan cheese and a little salt
4. Place finger-width thick, walnut-sized pieces of this dough onto a butter-greased sheet using a pastry bag, coat with egg yolk and bake in a moderate oven
5. When serving, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and serve hot with tea
