## Ingredients

- one kilogram of fish (best from bony parts such as head and tail) from catfish and carp, or possibly several kinds
- three roots
- one carrot
- vinegar
- fat
- flour
- two red onions, finely chopped
- crushed paprika
- bay leaf
- whole pepper
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut three roots and one carrot into thin long slices and place them in a pot suitable for making soup and cook until the vegetables are soft
2. Cut the fish into pieces, place it in the pot and pour on enough vinegar to make it sour and let the fish cook
3. In a pot, heat fat and when hot, fry one spoon of flour until bread-colored
4. Add two finely chopped red onion heads, a small spoon of crushed paprika, a little bay leaf and whole pepper and when wilted, dilute with the fish broth and boil for a short time
5. When the fish is cooked, add a little sour cream, bring to a boil again and then serve
