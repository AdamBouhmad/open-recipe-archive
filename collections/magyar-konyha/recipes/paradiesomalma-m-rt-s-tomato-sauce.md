## Ingredients

- six to seven tomatoes
- butter
- flour
- meat stock
- sugar
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean six to seven tomatoes well and wash them
2. Break each into several pieces
3. Braise tender in a pot
4. Melt a piece of butter and add one tablespoon of flour to it and brown slightly
5. Pass the braised tomatoes through a sieve and thin the roux with it
6. If too thick, add a little more meat stock, then add a piece of sugar and sour cream
