## Ingredients

- 5 tart apples
- sugar
- 2 crumbly bread rolls
- cream
- 90 grams finely ground almonds
- 90 grams finely ground sugar
- 1 lemon zest
- 70 grams butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, chop into small pieces and fry with sugar until it becomes a paste
2. Grate the bread rolls and moisten with cream
3. Take finely ground almonds and finely ground sugar
4. Chop lemon zest into small pieces
5. Whip the butter until foamy
6. Add the whole eggs and egg yolks one by one, beating together
7. Add the apple paste with the other ingredients and beat for half an hour
8. Finally fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
9. Pour the paste into a buttered mold sprinkled with fine breadcrumbs
10. Bake slowly
11. This pudding must bake somewhat longer than ordinary pudding, since the apple contains more moisture than the other mixture
