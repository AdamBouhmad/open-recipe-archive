## Ingredients

- seven decilitres millet porridge (well rinsed)
- one and a half litres hot salted water
- seven dekagrams pork fat
- two decilitres sour cream
- crumbled salty cheese

## Directions

1. Cook the well-rinsed millet porridge in one and a half litres of hot salted water, stirring constantly to prevent burning, until it is so thick that the cooking spoon stands in it
2. Tear this into haluska-sized pieces, place them around the edge of a large bowl to cool so it keeps its shape
3. Once cooled, move them from the bowl's edge to the center with a knife, and always place new ones on the edge until all are torn
4. In another pot, melt seven dekagrams of pork fat, add two decilitres of good sour cream, pour this hot over the haluska, top with crumbled salty cheese, and shake the whole thing well
