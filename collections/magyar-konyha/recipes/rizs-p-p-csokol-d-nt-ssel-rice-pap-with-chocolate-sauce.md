## Ingredients

- rice pap (prepared unfilled)
- cream custard or chocolate sauce
- 4 bars fine chocolate
- 4 decilitres cream
- ground sugar
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare rice pap as before, but without filling
2. Cook the pap in a steamer pot
3. For the chocolate sauce: boil 4 bars of fine chocolate in 4 decilitres of cream until thickened
4. In a separate pan, whisk together a small amount of ground sugar and 3 egg yolks
5. Pour the chocolate into the egg mixture with continuous stirring
6. Place on the fire and whisk until foamy
7. Pour the chocolate sauce around the cooked pap
