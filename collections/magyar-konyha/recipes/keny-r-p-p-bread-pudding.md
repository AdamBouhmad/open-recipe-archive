## Ingredients

- 140 grams softened almonds
- 140 grams sugar
- 1 lemon zest
- ground cinnamon
- candied citron
- 50 grams good unsour breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoons wine (Menes)

## Directions

1. Grind the softened almonds
2. Cook the same amount of sugar
3. Chop the lemon zest finely
4. Add ground cinnamon, finely chopped candied citron, and good unsour breadcrumbs moistened with wine
