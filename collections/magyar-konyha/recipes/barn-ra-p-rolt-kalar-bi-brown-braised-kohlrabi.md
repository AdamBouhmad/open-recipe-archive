## Ingredients

- kohlrabi
- sugar
- butter
- flour
- broth
- fried veal liver

## Directions

1. After cleaning and peeling the kohlrabi, cut into equal cubes
2. Brown a piece of sugar in butter
3. Add the kohlrabi and braise until nicely brown
4. Sprinkle a little flour over it and pour enough broth to make it not too soupy
5. Finish cooking with this
6. When serving, add fried veal liver to it
7. Prepare the fried liver as follows: take as much nice white veal liver as needed, blanch it slightly and cut each piece into two or three parts
8. Season with salt, dip in beaten egg, coat with fine breadcrumbs and fry quickly in lard until golden brown
