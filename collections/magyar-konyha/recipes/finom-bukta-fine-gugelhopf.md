## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 4 deka yeast
- lukewarm sugar milk
- 1 kilogram flour
- salt
- juice of half a lemon
- grated lemon zest
- warm milk as needed
- 4 egg whites
- peeled chopped almonds
- raisins
- vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 8 egg yolks with 4 tablespoons powdered sugar and 4 tablespoons butter until foamy
2. Dissolve 4 deka yeast in lukewarm, sugared milk
3. Add 1 kilogram flour, salt, lemon juice and grated zest, and as much warm milk as it absorbs, and stir into a very soft dough, always in one direction, for a whole hour
4. Beat 4 egg whites into stiff peaks and fold into the dough
5. Heat the vessel in which it will rise and bake, and butter it and coat with breadcrumbs
6. Pour two finger-widths of dough into the vessel, then butter it, and sprinkle with peeled chopped almonds and raisins
7. Alternate layers of dough with almonds and raisins, filling the vessel halfway, and butter the top
8. Cover with a warm lid and let rise in a warm place
9. When the vessel is full, bake at appropriate heat
10. When done, sprinkle generously with vanilla sugar
