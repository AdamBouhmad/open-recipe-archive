## Ingredients

- large capon
- salt
- yellow roux
- breadcrumbs
- anchovies
- parsley
- lemon zest
- red onion
- white wine
- fresh oysters

## Directions

1. Lightly salt a beautiful large capon and prepare it, then roast it brown while keeping it moist.
2. Make a yellow roux and fry a little breadcrumb in it, add several chopped anchovies, green parsley, lemon zest and a little red onion.
3. Pour two deciliters of white wine over and bring to a boil together for a time.
4. Rinse fresh oysters with wine, place them together with the roasted capon, bring to a boil together, dish it up and arrange the oysters with the sauce on top.
