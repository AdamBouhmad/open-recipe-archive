## Ingredients

- Buttered pastry
- Small masánszki apples
- One or two peeled apples
- Sugar
- Preserved currants or cherries
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Prepare buttered pastry
2. Peel small apples and hollow out their cores and stems
3. Stew one or two peeled apples with sugar, press through a sieve
4. Mix with preserved currants or cherries
5. Fill the hollowed apples with this mixture
6. Cut a round leaf from another apple and place on top
7. Roll out the buttered pastry to the thickness of a knife
8. Cut rectangular pieces from it
9. Brush the corners with egg yolk
10. Place the filled apple in the center
11. Fold the four brushed corners over it
12. Brush with egg, bake until beautifully golden yellow like a roll
