## Ingredients

- sixteen to twenty pancakes
- four deciliters sour cream
- nine dekagrams finely ground almonds
- sugar
- vanilla
- four egg yolks
- crumbly dough

## Directions

1. Cook sixteen to twenty pancakes and cut them into strips
2. Bring four deciliters sour cream to a boil, add nine dekagrams finely ground almonds, a piece of sugar, a little vanilla and the cut pancakes and cook through well, but the strips should remain visible and not become a paste
3. Cool, beat four egg yolks over it and mix well, noting that it should be thicker than common cereal
4. Roll out crumbly dough as thin as lasagna dough, place in a saucepan or mold without wrinkles, add the pancake mixture, cover with dough and bake
5. When done, turn out onto a platter
