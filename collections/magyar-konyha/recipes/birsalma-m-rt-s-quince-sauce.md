## Ingredients

- two large ripe, cooked quinces
- two deciliters sour cream
- half teaspoon flour
- one tablespoon stock
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and grate two large ripe, cooked quinces
2. Mix two deciliters of sour cream with half a teaspoon of flour and add one tablespoon of stock
3. Cook for half a quarter hour
4. Add the grated quince and enough sugar to make it sweet enough
