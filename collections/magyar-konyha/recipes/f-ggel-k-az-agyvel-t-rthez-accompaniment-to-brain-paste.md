## Ingredients

- brain paste
- butter
- pastry cutter

## Directions

1. Spread the brain paste prepared in the above manner onto a buttered baking sheet to the thickness of a little finger
2. Bake until nicely golden brown
3. Cut out with a pastry cutter in the size of a coin and use to surround the meat hash
4. Heap the meat hash in the center of the dish and enclose it with a crown made from the cut-out pieces
