## Ingredients

- cold roasted veal
- butter the size of an egg
- one tablespoon flour
- good broth
- one tablespoon sour cream
- a little ground nutmeg
- asparagus
- mushrooms
- butter
- fresh parsley
- artichokes or more asparagus
- salted water

## Directions

1. Cut cold roasted veal into large cubes
2. Melt butter the size of an egg, add one tablespoon flour and brown it slightly
3. Dilute with good broth, add one tablespoon sour cream and a little ground nutmeg
4. When the sauce is ready, add the veal cubes, as well as 25-30 pieces of asparagus and mushrooms that have been braised in butter with fresh parsley
5. Braise everything together until no sauce remains
6. Pile in the shape of a mound in a round dish and decorate with a crown made of puff pastry
7. If preparing in a season when asparagus is not available, use artichokes instead, boiled in salted water and cut into pieces
