## Ingredients

- four tablespoons flour
- three eggs
- milk
- salt
- clear fat

## Directions

1. Beat three eggs into four tablespoons flour in a pot.
2. Mix the two together well.
3. Slowly pour in milk until the batter is as thin as a nice smooth crepe batter.
4. Season lightly with salt.
5. Melt clear fat in a frying pan.
6. Drop the batter in with a foam skimmer.
7. Shake the pan several times so the peas brown evenly on all sides.
8. Add to the soup only at the moment of serving, as they should not become too soft.
