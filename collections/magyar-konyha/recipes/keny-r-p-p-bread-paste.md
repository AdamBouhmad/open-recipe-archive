## Ingredients

- 14 dekagramms softened almonds
- 14 dekagramms sugar
- zest of one lemon, finely chopped
- ground cinnamon
- finely chopped citron
- 5 dekagramms good, not sour bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons Tokay wine
- 10 dekagramms egg yolks
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Crush the softened almonds
2. Cook the sugar
3. Chop the lemon zest finely
4. Moisten the bread crumbs with Tokay wine
5. Stir all ingredients together with the egg yolks for half an hour
6. Beat the 4 egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in
7. Fill a butter-greased mold with the paste
8. Cook in a steam bath for three-quarters of an hour
9. Turn out and serve with wine sauce if desired
