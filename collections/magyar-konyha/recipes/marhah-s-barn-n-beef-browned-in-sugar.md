## Ingredients

- piece of sugar
- wine
- cooked beef
- beef broth
- butter
- flour
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. In a pot, boil sugar and a small amount of wine until it turns chestnut brown
2. Remove the cooked beef from the cooking liquid, dry it well, and place it in the brown sugar
3. Brown it on all sides
4. Pour a small amount of beef broth over it
5. Add a small piece of butter rolled in flour and finely chopped lemon zest
6. Remove the fat and strain the sauce through a sieve over the meat
