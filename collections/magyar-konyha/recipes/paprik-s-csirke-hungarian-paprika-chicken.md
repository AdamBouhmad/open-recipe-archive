## Ingredients

- young chickens
- onion
- butter
- paprika
- flour
- chicken broth
- sour cream
- parsley

## Directions

1. Singe the chickens, cut them up, wash clean, cut into uniform pieces and lightly salt
2. Finely chop an onion and braise it in butter in a copper pan until golden
3. Add the chicken pieces and half a tablespoon of paprika
4. Cover and let simmer, shaking frequently until tender
5. Dust with a little flour, add a little chicken broth and a few spoonfuls of sour cream
6. Let simmer slowly, skim the foam carefully and serve with fresh parsley
