## Ingredients

- zest of 1 lemon, finely chopped
- 12 spoonfuls of finely ground sugar
- 1 egg white, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Mix the finely chopped zest of 1 lemon with 12 spoonfuls of finely ground sugar
2. Make the dough with the stiff egg white, which should not be soft
3. Roll out and cut into tablets
4. Place on a wax-coated tray
5. Bake at gentle heat
