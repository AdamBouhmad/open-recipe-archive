## Ingredients

- 40 dekagrams sugar
- half a cup of water
- 500 grams almonds, cleaned with cloth

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar with half a cup of water until it threads and throws large bubbles during cooking
2. Add the cleaned almonds and constantly stir with a wooden spoon over gentle embers until the almonds have absorbed all the sugar and dried
3. Be careful that they do not become oily; as soon as they begin to pop, remove from heat and cover the pan until they cool
4. If you want the almonds to be red, mix in cochineal juice
