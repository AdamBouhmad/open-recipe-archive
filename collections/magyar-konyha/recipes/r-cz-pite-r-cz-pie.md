## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams soaked almonds
- 28 dekagrams raisins
- powdered cinnamon
- 14 dekagrams dried currants
- 14 dekagrams powdered sugar
- dough as in recipe 412
- egg yolk
- sour cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Finely chop the soaked almonds and the same amount of raisins
2. Add a little powdered cinnamon, 14 dekagrams dried currants, and 14 dekagrams powdered sugar
3. Mix all well together
4. Prepare dough as in recipe 412 and handle the same way
5. When the dough is drawn out, filled, and prepared up to the egg brushing step, cut it with a sharp knife into large, long cubes
6. Bake in gentle heat in the oven
7. As soon as it is baked, remove at once from the baking dish, as it would soften and lose its beautiful appearance
8. Cut the pieces completely through and arrange on a serving dish in a double star shape
9. Sprinkle with sugar
10. A spoonful of sour cream should be mixed into the butter used for spreading these cakes, otherwise they would be too dry
