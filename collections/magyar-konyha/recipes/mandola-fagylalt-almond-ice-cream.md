## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams sweet almonds
- a few bitter almonds
- little cream
- 1 liter cream
- piece of vanilla
- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 12 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Soak the sweet and a few bitter almonds, remove their skin, and finely grind or grate the almonds with a little cream
2. Boil 1 liter of cream with a piece of vanilla and 42 dekagrams sugar together
3. Mix thoroughly with 12 egg yolks and the almonds
4. On the fire beat to a thick foam (crème)
5. Strain through a fine sieve or cheesecloth
6. When cooled, freeze
