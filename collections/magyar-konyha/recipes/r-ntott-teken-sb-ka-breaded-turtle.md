## Ingredients

- turtle
- salt
- water
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- fresh lard
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Cook, clean, and cut the turtle into pieces as described previously
2. Beat several eggs
3. Dip the turtle pieces in the egg
4. Coat with fine bread crumbs
5. Fry in fresh lard until golden
6. Garnish with fried parsley and serve
