## Ingredients

- crumbly pastry
- 8-10 boiled potatoes
- salt
- cooked ham, finely chopped
- grated Parmesan cheese
- sour cream
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare crumbly pastry and stretch it to knife-thickness
2. Line a pot with the pastry
3. Slice 8-10 boiled potatoes and salt them
4. Finely chop some cooked ham
5. Layer potatoes, then ham, then potatoes again in the pâté dish
6. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese
7. Add a little sour cream and butter on top
8. Continue layering until the pot is full
9. Prepare a pastry lid
10. Brush with egg white and bake slowly in the oven
