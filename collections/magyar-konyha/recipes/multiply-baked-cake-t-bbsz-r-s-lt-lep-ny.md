## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams butter
- flour
- fresh sour cherries
- powdered sugar
- 7 decilitres cream
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- vanilla
- 10 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a crumbly dough from 28 dekagrams butter and roll it as thin as a knife blade
2. Place it on a flat prepared pan and prick it with a fork to prevent it from puffing up during baking
3. Bake in the oven until golden
4. Cover with enough fresh sour cherries to cover the bottom and sprinkle well with powdered sugar and bake through
5. When the dough is well baked, prepare 7 decilitres of cream with 14 dekagrams sugar, a little vanilla and ten egg yolks, whisk together in a pan over gentle heat until it begins to thicken
6. Pour half of this mixture carefully over the fruit, quickly place in the oven until set, then pour the remaining half over and place in the oven again
7. When set, remove, cool, cut into large cubes and serve
8. This cake can be made the same way with apricots or raspberries - peel the apricots, cut them in half, cover the dough with them, sprinkle well with sugar and bake; when baked, pour the same sauce over it as before; note that the inner edge of the pan should be well coated with egg white and the dough pressed together firmly at the edges or it will fall off; for the sauce use a coffee spoon of sifted vanilla
