## Ingredients

- twenty-eight decagrams veal
- green parsley
- red onion
- butter
- flour
- meat broth
- two egg yolks
- thin milk cream
- egg
- fine bread crumbs
- fat
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Braise twenty-eight decagrams veal carefully so it remains as white as possible.
2. Finely chop green parsley and walnut-sized red onion.
3. Melt a piece of butter, add one teaspoon flour and brown slightly.
4. Add the chopped mixture and braise slightly.
5. Pour in half a serving spoon of meat broth and reduce until thick.
6. Mix in two egg yolks and remove from heat.
7. Cut thin slices of milk cream into knife-thick rings and spread with the meat filling.
8. Shape into shields and moisten with milk.
9. Dip in beaten egg and fine bread crumbs.
10. Fry golden brown in fat.
11. Serve with good meat broth.
