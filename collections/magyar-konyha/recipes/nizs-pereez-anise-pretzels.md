## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams flour
- 10 dekagrams butter
- 14 dekagrams crushed sugar
- 1 lemon, finely chopped rind
- Few anise seeds
- 2 whole eggs
- Egg white for brushing

## Directions

1. Mix flour and butter on board with roller
2. Add crushed sugar, finely chopped lemon rind, and a few anise seeds
3. Beat 2 whole eggs on top
4. Assemble the dough
5. Form into pretzel shapes
6. Brush with egg white
7. Bake at gentle heat
