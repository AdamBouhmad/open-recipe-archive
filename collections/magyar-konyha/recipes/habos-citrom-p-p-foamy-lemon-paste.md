## Ingredients

- 18 dekagramm sugar lightly rubbed with lemon
- juice of 2 lemons
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 egg whites
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Crush 18 dekagramm sugar lightly rubbed with lemon, place in a pan, and squeeze the juice of 2 lemons over it.
2. Keep on low heat until the lemon dissolves, then set aside.
3. Once cooled, beat in 8 egg yolks one after another.
4. Return to low heat and stir constantly until it begins to steam and becomes slightly thick.
5. Remove from heat and stir until cold.
6. Gently fold in the stiffly beaten whites of 8 eggs.
7. Heap into a porcelain pan to form a peak, smooth with a wide knife, and create spiral formations with a demitasse spoon.
8. Form a mushroom shape on top, sprinkle with fine sugar, and bake at very gentle heat or cook in a bain-marie.
9. Serve quickly at table once light brown, as this paste is delicate and collapses easily.
10. This paste can also be made with orange: for 8 eggs, use one large soup orange, lightly rub the sugar with the zest, squeeze the juice of one lemon and one orange over it, and otherwise prepare as with lemon.
