## Ingredients

- About sixty Italian chestnuts
- 7 dekagram butter
- 37.5 dekagram butter
- 14 dekagram ground sugar
- Two deciliter cream
- Six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Roast and clean about sixty Italian chestnuts, removing all black spots with a fine knife
2. Cut them in half
3. Finely crush one half and mix with 7 dekagram butter
4. Press through a sieve
5. In a pot, mix this paste with 37.5 dekagram butter, 14 dekagram ground sugar, and two deciliter cream
6. Place on gentle heat and stir continuously until the paste becomes slightly firm
7. Gradually add six egg yolks while stirring
8. Continue cooking for a few more minutes with constant stirring
9. Place the paste on a buttered board and cool
10. When completely cooled, form balls the size of the chestnut halves removed
11. Flatten the paste, place a chestnut in the center
12. Fold the other half of the paste over it and continue until all are done
13. Dip in beaten egg, coat in fine breadcrumbs
14. Fry in hot fat until beautifully golden
15. Sprinkle with sugar and vanilla, serve warm to the table
