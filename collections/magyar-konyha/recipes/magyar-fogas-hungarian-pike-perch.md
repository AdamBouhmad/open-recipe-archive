## Ingredients

- pike-perch
- salt
- string
- hot salted water
- dill vinegar
- butter
- nutmeg
- five egg yolks
- lemon
- green parsley

## Directions

1. After the fish has lain on ice for several days, clean it
2. Split it open and rinse well
3. Salt it thoroughly
4. Tie the fins with string and let it rest
5. One hour before serving, tie it in a special fish cloth
6. Place in hot salted water flavored with a few drops of dill vinegar and cook
7. Make a fine butter sauce with the fish broth, add nutmeg, and bring to a boil
8. Beat five egg yolks with one lemon together, combine with the butter sauce like a roux, and pour into a sauce cup
9. Place the fish on a cloth-covered board, then in an oblong dish
10. Remove the string and arrange on the table garnished with green parsley and served with sauce
