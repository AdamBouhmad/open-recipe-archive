## Ingredients

- 6 deciliters fine flour
- 7 dekagrams butter, thinly sliced
- salt
- 4 eggs
- 1 spoonful sour cream
- a little wine
- 7 dekagrams butter, beaten until fluffy
- 10 dekagrams sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Work the flour and butter slices together well on a board with a rolling pin
2. Season with salt
3. Knead with 4 eggs, a spoonful of sour cream, and a little wine
4. Work the not overly firm dough well and prepare dropped noodles from it
5. Cook these in sour cream and cool
6. Beat 7 dekagrams of butter until fluffy, then add 4 whole eggs one after another and beat each in well
7. Add the noodles with 10 dekagrams of sugar and vanilla, mix well
8. Place in a buttered baking dish and cook in a water bath
