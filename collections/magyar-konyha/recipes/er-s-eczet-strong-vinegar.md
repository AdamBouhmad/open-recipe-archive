## Ingredients

- 20 dekagrams tarragon
- 10 dekagrams shallot
- 7 dekagrams pearl onion
- 10 dekagrams bay leaf
- 7 dekagrams angelica root
- 10 dekagrams capers
- 10 dekagrams anchovy
- 7 deciliters strong vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop 20 dekagrams of tarragon, 10 dekagrams of shallot, 7 dekagrams of pearl onion, 10 dekagrams of bay leaf, 7 dekagrams of angelica root, 10 dekagrams of capers, and the same amount of anchovy.
2. Pour into a bottle and cover with 7 deciliters of strong vinegar, stop it well, and place it in the sun or next to a warm oven for four weeks.
3. Then press through cloth and filter the vinegar once more.
