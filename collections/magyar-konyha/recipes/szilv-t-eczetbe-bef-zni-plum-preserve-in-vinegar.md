## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters white vinegar
- 1 kilogram 80 dekagrams sugar
- 6 dekagrams cinnamon
- 2 grams cloves
- 2.5 kilograms very ripe plums

## Directions

1. Cook 1.5 liters white vinegar with 1 kilogram 80 dekagrams sugar, 6 dekagrams cinnamon, and 2 grams cloves (break the last two roughly) into a syrup, strain it, and dry the remaining spices on paper.
2. When the syrup has cooled, place 2.5 kilograms of very ripe beautiful plums, pierced gently with a needle, into a preserve jar, pour the cold syrup over them and leave open for 10 days.
3. On the 11th day, remove the plums and cook the syrup down to thickness again, then finally add the plums and cook very gently with it, then carefully place in a jar, sprinkling the spices dried during the ten days in layers and pouring the syrup over it.
4. Seal the following day and keep in a cool place.
