## Ingredients

- good quality apples, unpeeled, cut into small pieces, with cores removed
- large raisins, cut into small pieces
- seven dekagrams each of apple and raisins
- twenty-eight dekagrams fresh unsalted butter
- seven dekagrams yellow wax

## Directions

1. Cut the apples unpeeled into small pieces, removing only the cores
2. Cut the large raisins into small pieces
3. In a crucible, cook seven dekagrams of apple and seven dekagrams of raisins with twenty-eight dekagrams of fresh unsalted butter and seven dekagrams of yellow wax with continuous stirring over gentle heat until a drop thrown onto glowing coals no longer sizzles
4. Press the pomade into a rose-water bottle and store for use
