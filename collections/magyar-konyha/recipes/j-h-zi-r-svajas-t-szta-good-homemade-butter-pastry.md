## Ingredients

- 250 grams flour
- 250 grams fresh firm butter
- salt
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
- 1 hazelnut-sized piece of butter
- 1 teaspoon rum
- 4 egg yolks
- milk as needed
- egg wash
- blanched chopped almonds with sugar

## Directions

1. Take 250 grams of flour and 250 grams of fresh firm butter
2. Work half of the flour with the butter and a little salt using a rolling pin into a dough
3. When finely worked, roll out to the size of a plate and place on ice
4. Make strudel dough with the other half of flour, a little salt, one tablespoon powdered sugar, one hazelnut-sized piece of butter, one teaspoon rum, four egg yolks, and as much milk as it will take up
5. Work until small bubbles form on it and it separates from the board, which shows it is well worked
6. Shape into a loaf and let rest for one hour
7. Roll the dough quite thin
8. Place the butter dough on it and roll together quite thin
9. Fold as one folds a tablecloth and let rest for half an hour in a cold place
10. Roll out three times at half-hour intervals
11. Cut with a hot knife as desired
12. Brush with egg
13. Sprinkle generously with blanched chopped almonds with sugar
14. Bake quickly at a hot fire; small kipferl or cream-filled pastries can also be made from it
