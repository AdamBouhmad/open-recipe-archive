## Ingredients

- strudel dough
- chopped or grated salted cabbage
- hot water
- pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. This version is only fine if the cabbage for filling is not fried in fat
2. Pour boiling water over the chopped or grated salted cabbage using a stick, and when half-cooled, pour onto a sieve so it drains well
3. Wring out thoroughly so not a drop of water remains
4. Spread on a flat plate
5. Scatter generously over the rolled and well-dried strudel dough with this white cabbage and sprinkle generously with peppered sugar
6. Roll together into strudel, brush the top with egg, bake at high heat to beautiful brown
7. This strudel is light, not fatty, and very fine
