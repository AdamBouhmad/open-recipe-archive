## Ingredients

- kidney
- salt
- bacon
- red onion
- carrot
- small potatoes (walnut-sized, peeled)
- flour (2 spoons)
- broth
- vinegar
- lemon zest
- sour cream (2 full spoons)

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the kidney, salt it, and lard it nicely
2. Place bacon, sliced red onion, and sliced carrot in a pot, with the meat on top
3. Braise slowly, turning frequently so the meat does not brown too much and softens on all sides
4. Add peeled, walnut-sized potatoes
5. When the meat and potatoes are browned and tender, remove them
6. Sprinkle flour (2 spoons) over the braised vegetables, brown it, and fill with broth
7. Add a little vinegar and lemon zest and cook the sauce well
8. Skim off the fat and strain through a sieve
9. Put the meat and potatoes back in, add 2 full spoons of sour cream and cook well
10. When serving, arrange the potatoes like a crown around the edge of the plate and place the kidney in the center
