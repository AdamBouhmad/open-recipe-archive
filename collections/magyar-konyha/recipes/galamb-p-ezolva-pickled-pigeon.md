## Ingredients

- pigeons
- salt
- pickle
- finely chopped bacon
- sour cream
- two deciliters sour cream
- one tablespoon flour
- pickle
- pigeon drippings
- oblong-cut lemon zest

## Directions

1. After cleanly plucking the pigeons, season them well with salt
2. Prepare a good pickle and leave them in it for one day
3. Lard with finely chopped bacon
4. Roast slowly, basting several times with the pickle and sour cream as well
5. Prepare the sauce from two deciliters of sour cream, beating it with one tablespoon flour
6. Pour the pickle over it, as well as what dripped from the pigeon
7. Also add oblong-cut lemon zest
8. Heat slightly and serve the pigeons with the sauce poured over
