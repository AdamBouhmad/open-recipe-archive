## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams butter
- 9 egg yolks
- 10 dekagrams soaked almonds
- 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 2 decilitres cream
- 1.5 dekagrams fresh yeast
- salt
- flour
- sour cherries

## Directions

1. Beat 14 dekagrams butter until fluffy, add 9 egg yolks one by one, stirring well
2. Mix in 10 dekagrams soaked and finely ground almonds, 3 tablespoons powdered sugar with the butter and eggs well
3. Incorporate 2 decilitres of good cream and 1.5 dekagrams of fresh yeast soaked in cream, add a little salt and enough fine flour to make a light dough that should not be runny but should not be very stiff either, beat with flour until the dough pulls away from the spoon
4. Grease the mold or pan with butter, wash the sour cherries (having removed their stems beforehand) and stew with powdered sugar, strain the juice well
5. Line the entire mold with this juice, place the smaller portion of dough on top and let it rise well
6. When the dough has risen in the mold, place in the oven and bake just enough so that the cherries do not sink; now pull the mold out slightly without removing it from the oven, place the remaining cherries on top, carefully cover with the remaining dough, bake until golden, and when baked, turn out and sprinkle with sugar
