## Ingredients

- three tablespoons flour
- one liter good fresh cream
- half a Spanish onion (if for meat broth)
- a piece of ham (if for meat broth)
- meat glaze tablet (if making strong meat béchamel)

## Directions

1. Beat three tablespoons flour with one liter of good fresh cream finely
2. Cook until the desired thickness is reached
3. Pass through a sieve and use for the intended purpose
4. If for meat broth, add half a Spanish onion and a piece of ham during cooking, and if you want to make the meat béchamel strong, add a meat glaze tablet
5. Béchamel can also be made simply by gently swelling flour in fat and cooking fresh milk to a certain thickness
