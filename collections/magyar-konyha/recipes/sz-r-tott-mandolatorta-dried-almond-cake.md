## Ingredients

- 28 dekagramm soaked and ground almonds
- 28 dekagramm sugar
- zest of a beautiful lemon with its juice
- 3.5 dekagramm coarsely chopped citron
- 1 whole egg and 2 egg yolks
- wafer
- egg white for coating
- crushed sugar for sprinkling
- whole cherries with stems for decoration

## Directions

1. Dry soaked ground almonds with sugar in a copper pan over charcoal fire with constant stirring until they stick together
2. Finely grate lemon zest and add with juice
3. Add chopped citron
4. Add whole egg and two egg yolks and mix well
5. Fill three-quarters of the mixture into a pyramid cake form lined with wafer
6. Form small wreaths from remaining mixture
7. Arrange wreaths on the wafer in a beautiful order
8. Coat with egg white and sprinkle with crushed sugar
9. Bake at gentle heat
10. When done, decorate with frosting
11. Place whole cherries with stems in the wreaths
