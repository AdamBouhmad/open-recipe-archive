## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilograms flour
- 3/8 fat or butter
- 2 dekagrams yeast
- 1/2 coffee cup milk
- sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 1/2 coffee cup wine
- salt
- 2 egg yolks
- cream
- butter
- 2 egg whites
- curd cheese

## Directions

1. To 1/2 kilograms flour add 3/8 fat or butter and rub together well
2. Soak 2 dekagrams yeast in 1/2 coffee cup milk with three pieces of sugar to let it rise
3. With 4 egg yolks, 1/2 coffee cup wine, proper salt and the yeast, quickly knead a soft dough - if kneaded too long it becomes tough and hard, not crumbly
4. Let it rest for a few hours; when it has rested and appears to have risen, flatten it in a pan to the thickness of a knife blade, or slightly thicker
5. Force the curd cheese through thick cloth to remove the whey, then place in a bowl
6. With 2 egg yolks, a few spoonfuls of sweet sour cream, a piece of butter the size of an egg, and beaten foam from the 2 egg whites, mix the curd cheese together
