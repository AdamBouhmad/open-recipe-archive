## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams sugar
- coffee cup of water
- 21 dekagrams softened almonds
- zest of one orange
- zest of one lemon
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 3-4 cloves
- 10 dekagrams bread roll crumbs
- puff pastry dough
- egg white

## Directions

1. In a pot, cook 28 dekagrams of sugar with a coffee cup of water over coals until the sugar is completely dissolved
2. Crush 21 dekagrams of softened almonds, remove the zest from one orange, cook it in water, and chop it finely together with the zest of one lemon
3. Mix in 1 teaspoon of crushed cinnamon (which can contain 3-4 cloves) and 10 dekagrams of bread roll crumbs with the other ingredients
4. Add this mixture to the boiling sugar, keeping it on the fire with continuous stirring until the mixture has the proper thickness, then set it aside to cool
5. Prepare the dough in the same manner as in the previous recipe and treat the doughnuts in the same way
6. These doughnuts can be kept for a longer time in a box or covered with glass in a dry place
7. The balls made from the filling should be the size of a walnut
