## Ingredients

- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 2 deciliters water
- 500 grams softened and chopped almonds
- 7 dekagrams chopped pistachios
- 10 dekagrams chopped citron
- 1 lemon, zest and juice
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pour 2 deciliters of water over 42 dekagrams sugar and cook until it threads; then add 500 grams of softened and chopped almonds, 7 dekagrams chopped pistachios, 10 dekagrams chopped citron, 1 lemon with both its finely chopped zest and juice
2. Let it stand a little, remove from the fire and cool it; then mix in 3 egg yolks
