## Ingredients

- good meat broth
- semolina
- fourteen decagrams crayfish butter or other butter
- five eggs
- semolina puree
- salt
- butter
- fine bread crumbs
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Boil good meat broth in a pot.
2. Cook semolina into it until it becomes a thick porridge.
3. Remove from heat and let cool.
4. Beat fourteen decagrams crayfish or other butter until fluffy.
5. Mix in five eggs alternately with one egg and one spoon of puree.
6. Season lightly with salt.
7. Grease the molds with butter and sprinkle with fine bread crumbs.
8. When baked, unmold and place in a soup bowl.
9. Pour beef broth over it.
