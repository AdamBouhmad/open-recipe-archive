## Ingredients

- kale sprouts
- salt water
- butter
- flour
- broth
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean the sprouts, wash them and cook in salted water
2. Drain and place in the prepared butter sauce and finish cooking with it
3. The sauce should cook well, especially if using this to garnish beef
4. Can also be prepared with sour cream
