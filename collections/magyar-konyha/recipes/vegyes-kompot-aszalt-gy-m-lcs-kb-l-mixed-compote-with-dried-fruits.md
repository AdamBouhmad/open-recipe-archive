## Ingredients

- Dried Spanish cherries or small prune plums
- Dried autumn apricots
- Dried and pressed pears
- Water
- Sugar
- A little lemon peel

## Directions

1. Cook the dried Spanish cherries or prune plums, dried autumn apricots, and dried and pressed pears in water with sugar and a little lemon peel
2. When serving, pile the cherries or prune plums high in the middle, then arrange the apricots and pears around them, keeping their stems intact
