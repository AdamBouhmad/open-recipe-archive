## Ingredients

- one kilogram of cod
- salt water
- eight anchovies
- green parsley
- capers
- butter
- sour cream
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook one kilogram of cod in salted water
2. Finely chop eight anchovies, green parsley and a little capers together
3. Place a few pieces of butter on a plate and add one to two spoonfuls of sour cream
4. Tear the cod into small pieces and arrange one layer on the plate
5. Then arrange one layer of the chopped mixture with a little breadcrumbs, several spoonfuls of sour cream and one to two pieces of butter
6. Continue in this manner until the plate is full
7. Sprinkle the whole with breadcrumbs, sour cream and butter
8. Place in the oven and bake until nicely golden
