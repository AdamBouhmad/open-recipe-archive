## Ingredients

- finely grated horseradish
- hot meat broth
- sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the finely grated horseradish on a clean plate and put in the oven for a short time so the excessive strength of the horseradish evaporates
2. Pour hot meat broth over it
3. Add a spoonful of sugar and a few drops of vinegar and bring to a boil
4. Cool before serving
