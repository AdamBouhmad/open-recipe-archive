## Ingredients

- Crumbly puff pastry
- Butter
- Twenty-one dekagrams almonds (some bitter)
- Twenty-one dekagrams fine broken sugar
- One lemon
- Seven whole eggs
- Four egg yolks
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Prepare crumbly puff pastry and tear off twenty small pieces
2. Cover with warm cloth and let rise half an hour
3. Butter the plate needed
4. Stretch the pastry very finely, brushing each layer with melted butter; stretch out six layers
5. For the seventh layer prepare the filling: soften twenty-one dekagrams almonds (some of which should be bitter) and grind finely
6. Put the same amount of fine broken sugar and finely chopped zest of one lemon in the bowl
7. Mix in seven whole eggs and four egg yolks one by one and beat until foamy
8. Spread half this filling on the seventh layer of pastry
9. Then stretch six more layers on top, brush each with melted butter
10. Spread the other half of the filling on top
11. Finally stretch the remaining pastry on top
12. Cut off the pastry hanging over the edge, brush the top with butter, and bake slowly until the pastry is beautiful golden yellow and crispy
