## Ingredients

- one liter of good sweet milk
- vanilla
- ten egg yolks
- ten tablespoons of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Boil one liter of good sweet milk with a piece of vanilla
2. Put ten egg yolks and ten tablespoons of powdered sugar in a narrow tall dish
3. Beat them with a wooden spoon until the egg and sugar become foamy, which takes about half an hour
4. When the milk is hot, slowly pour it into the sugared egg while continuously stirring with a wooden spoon
5. Keep at an equal warm temperature until serving but not boiling and continuously stir with the spoon, because if placed in a very hot place and the egg milk mixture is left unstirred, it will curdle uselessly
6. Therefore pay great attention to preparing the sauce and make it only shortly before serving
7. If by chance there is not enough egg for this sauce, the foamy beaten egg and sugar can be added
