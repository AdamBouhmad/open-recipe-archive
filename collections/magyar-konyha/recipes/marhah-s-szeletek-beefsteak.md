## Ingredients

- 7-8 large yellow potatoes
- 7 decagrams butter
- bagdány pecsenyé (beef)
- 3 medium red onions
- parsley, green and chopped
- black pepper, crushed
- fat, several spoonfuls

## Directions

1. Wash 7-8 large yellow potatoes, peel and slice them
2. Cook until completely soft in salted water
3. Drain and mix with 7 decagrams of butter
4. Press through a fine sieve and keep warm
5. Cut thin, round slices from well-beaten and cleaned beef
6. Salt them and mix in 3 medium red onions, a handful of chopped green parsley and a knife-point of crushed black pepper
7. Place several spoonfuls of fat in a flat pot and place the slices with the mixture in it
8. Braise until the slices are cooked; the red onion and parsley should not break apart but only become light yellow
9. At serving time, place the potato purée at the bottom of the dish and heap it in the middle
10. Coat the slices thickly with the braised red onion and parsley, first straining off the fat and mixing it into the purée
11. Arrange the slices nicely around the edge of the dish so they form a crown
