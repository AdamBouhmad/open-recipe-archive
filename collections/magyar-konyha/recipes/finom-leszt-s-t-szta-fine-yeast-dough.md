## Ingredients

- half kilogram flour
- 28 dekagrams butter
- salt
- 1 tablespoon ground sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons sour cream
- 14 dekagrams butter
- 3 deciliters sour cream with dissolved yeast

## Directions

1. Cut the butter into the flour and work finely with salt
2. Add the ground sugar, 8 egg yolks, sour cream, butter and yeast dissolved in sour cream
3. Prepare the dough with a wide knife and make rolls or other shapes from it
