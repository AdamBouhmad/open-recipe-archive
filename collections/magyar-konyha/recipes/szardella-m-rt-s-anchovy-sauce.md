## Ingredients

- seven dekagrams anchovies, skinned
- green parsley, finely chopped
- red onion, finely chopped
- yellow roux
- good stock
- ground pepper
- two tablespoons sour cream
- lemon juice
- lemon zest (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the skinned anchovies and wash them, remove their spines
2. Chop finely together with green parsley and red onion
3. Prepare a yellow roux
4. Add the chopped mixture to it and brown
5. Thin with one to two tablespoons of good stock
6. Add ground pepper and finally two tablespoons of sour cream
7. Season with lemon juice
8. A little lemon zest can also be added
