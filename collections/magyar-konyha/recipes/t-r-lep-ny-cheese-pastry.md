## Ingredients

- 500 grams flour
- 3/8 lard or butter
- 20 grams yeast
- half coffee cup milk
- 3 pieces sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- half coffee cup wine
- salt to taste
- cottage cheese
- 2 egg yolks
- a few tablespoons sour cream
- one egg-sized piece of clarified butter
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Take 500 grams flour and 3/8 its weight in lard or butter, rub well together
2. Dissolve 20 grams yeast in half a coffee cup milk with 3 pieces of sugar
3. Mix the yeast mixture with 4 egg yolks, half a coffee cup wine, salt, and quickly knead into a soft dough—if kneaded too long it becomes tough and not crumbly
4. Let rest for several hours until visibly risen
5. Flatten to knife-blade thickness on a baking sheet
6. Press cottage cheese through thick cloth to remove whey, then place in a bowl
7. Mix the pressed cheese with 2 egg yolks, a few tablespoons of sour cream, one egg-sized piece of clarified butter, and whipped egg white, adding a pinch of salt
8. Spread the cheese mixture finger-thick over the dough on the baking sheet, sprinkle with flour and butter generously over the flour so a beautiful brown crust forms on top while the cheese stays soft
9. Fold the edges of the dough back onto the sheet about a finger's width and press with your fingers to create a decorative border
10. Brush the folded edge with egg and bake at high heat until beautifully browned
