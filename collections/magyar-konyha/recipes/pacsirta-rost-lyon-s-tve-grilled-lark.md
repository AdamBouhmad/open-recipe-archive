## Ingredients

- lark
- salt
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Pluck the lark and cut off the head
2. Carefully remove the innards without removing the fat
3. Soak in water for a quarter hour
4. Salt and place on a skewer over the grill
5. Roast while frequently basting with butter
6. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs
