## Ingredients

- two herrings, cleaned of skin
- two pieces of marinated eel
- two spools of fish
- seven dekagrams of tuna
- seven dekagrams of marinated salmon
- four to five anchovies
- small sea crabs for decoration
- capers for decoration
- pistachios for decoration
- wine vinegar
- fine oil

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned herrings, marinated eel pieces, spools, tuna, marinated salmon, and anchovies into slices.
2. Arrange these pieces together beautifully on a flat plate so that the whole has an attractive appearance.
3. Decorate the top with small sea crabs, capers, and pistachios according to taste.
4. Pour wine vinegar and fine oil over the entire dish.
