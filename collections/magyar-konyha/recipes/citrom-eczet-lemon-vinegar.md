## Ingredients

- beautiful lemons with thin skin
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Take the yellow skin of beautiful lemons with thin skin and peel it very thinly, then cut it into small cubes.
2. Peel away the white and tough skin, and cut the lemon into slices, removing the seeds.
3. Place the lemon and its yellow skin in a glass bottle, pour wine vinegar over it, and place it in the sun or in a warm oven for a time, stoppered.
4. This vinegar gives a pleasant taste to many dishes.
