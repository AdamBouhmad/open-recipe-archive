## Ingredients

- beef roast slices
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- fat

## Directions

1. Take well-beaten roast slices, remove the bones, dip them in beaten egg
2. Coat thoroughly with breadcrumbs and fry quickly in hot fat
3. When placing the slices on a plate, garnish with fresh green parsley
