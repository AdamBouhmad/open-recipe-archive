## Ingredients

- 10 dekagrams melted butter
- 3 cooking spoonfuls flour
- 4 deciliters hot milk
- 6 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- butter for greasing
- 1 to 1.5 calf brains
- salted water
- finely chopped red onion
- fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Roast the flour in the melted butter
2. Slowly pour in the hot milk while continuously stirring and cook until it becomes a smooth paste
3. Remove from heat and cool
4. Slowly mix in 6 egg yolks, season lightly with salt
5. Mix in the stiff snow from 3 egg whites
6. Grease a large flat baking dish or plate with a two-finger wide rim with butter
7. Place one third of the paste in it and bake
8. Meanwhile, scald the calf brain in salted water, clean it and cut into small slices
9. Braise with finely chopped red onion and fresh parsley, season with a little salt and pepper
10. Grease a baking dish with butter
11. Divide the baked paste into three equal slices
12. Place the first slice in the dish and fill it with the prepared brains
13. Add the second slice and fill again, then add the third slice and fill that as well
14. Finally cover with the remaining unbaked dough
15. Cook in a water bath for a good half hour
16. Turn out onto a platter and serve quickly
17. For a sweet version, place fresh and heavily sugared fruit such as apricots between the baked layers instead of brains, and sprinkle with sugar when baked; do not salt the paste but mix in vanilla sugar instead
