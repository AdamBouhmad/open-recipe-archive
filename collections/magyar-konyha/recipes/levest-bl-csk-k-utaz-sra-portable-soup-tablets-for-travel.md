## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilogram soup tablets
- 2.5 kilograms lean meat from the loin
- 6 liters water
- 1 old hen
- 2 veal feet
- onions, sliced
- veal shank
- veal knuckles
- 1 kilogram lean pork
- 1 to 1.5 kilograms mutton
- 4 deciliters water
- carrots
- parsley
- parsnip root

## Directions

1. Cook 0.5 kilogram soup tablets with 2.5 kilograms lean meat, 6 liters water, an old hen, and two veal feet until completely tender
2. In a large pot, layer sliced onions, veal shank, veal knuckles, lean pork, and mutton
3. Pour 4 deciliters water over it and add carrots, parsley, and parsnip root
4. Braise until the liquid is brown at the bottom
5. Pour the cooked meat broth over it and simmer slowly for 3-4 hours to keep the broth clear
6. When everything is very soft and the meat nearly inedible, strain through a fine sieve, then through cheesecloth, and transfer to another pot
7. Slowly reduce until it resembles a meat glaze (glace), from about 6 liters down to 2 liters
8. Strain through clean cheesecloth and pour onto a flat dish to cool
9. Once completely cooled and set, cut into pieces the size of chocolate tablets and lay on paper in a cool, airy place to dry
10. When completely dry, store in a dry place for later use
11. Note: if dissolving a tablet in hot water while traveling to quickly make broth, do not salt it further as it would become sour
