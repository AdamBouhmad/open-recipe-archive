## Ingredients

- beautiful piece of beef
- bacon slices
- red onion slices
- carrot slices
- flour
- good broth
- seven to ten decagrams of rice
- white broth
- lean ham
- green parsley
- two deciliters of sour cream

## Directions

1. Cook a beautiful piece of beef until tender and place in a pot with a bottom lined with bacon, red onion, and carrot slices
2. Cover and braise slowly, turning frequently so it browns on all sides
3. Remove the meat and sprinkle with a spoonful of flour, browning it
4. Pour good broth over it and reduce the sauce to a thick consistency
5. Meanwhile, cook seven to ten decagrams of rice in white broth until tender
6. Coat the top part of the meat with the rice using a wide knife
7. Finely chop a piece of lean ham and green parsley, mix with two deciliters of sour cream
8. Pour this mixture over the rice crust and place in the oven
9. Before serving, strain the brown sauce into the serving dish, place the meat on top without damaging the crust
