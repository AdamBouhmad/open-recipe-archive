## Ingredients

- 14 dekagramm finely chopped walnuts
- 14 dekagramm soaked and finely ground almonds
- 28 dekagramm sugar
- a little vanilla
- 14 egg yolks
- 10 egg whites

## Directions

1. In bowl, stir finely chopped walnuts, soaked ground almonds, sugar and a little vanilla with fourteen egg yolks for half an hour, stirring constantly
2. Finally gently fold in stiffly beaten egg white foam
3. Butter two equal cake layers and their rings
4. Fill each with half the mixture and bake at gentle heat
5. Turn onto sieve and when cooled, fill one layer with preserves
6. Place second layer on top and coat with lemon or orange frosting
7. Note: cake layers should not be too thick or they will become heavy and fatty; thin layers make the cake light and fluffy
8. Walnuts should not be ground but only chopped together
