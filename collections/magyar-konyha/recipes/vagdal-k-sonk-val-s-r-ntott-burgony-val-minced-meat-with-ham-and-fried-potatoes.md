## Ingredients

- butter
- veal meat
- veal breast
- green parsley
- button mushrooms
- artichokes or asparagus
- potatoes
- seven decagrams butter
- one whole egg
- three egg yolks
- salt
- fine bread crumbs
- ham

## Directions

1. Heat a little butter and add finely diced veal meat and veal breast with green parsley-braised button mushrooms and artichokes or asparagus according to season; braise until only little sauce remains
2. Meanwhile, cook four or five good starchy potatoes, peel them, cool them, and grate them
3. Beat seven decagrams of butter until fluffy, add one whole egg and three egg yolks one by one, add a little salt and grated potato to form flat round croquettes about two knife-widths thick and the size of a one-forint silver coin
4. Dip into beaten egg and fine bread crumbs and fry golden in fat
5. Beat two egg yolks into the minced meat sauce and arrange on a round platter; densely cover with the fried potatoes sprinkled with finely chopped ham and border the platter with ham
