## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams soaked and finely ground almonds
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- lemon zest, finely chopped
- 5 dekagrams roll crumbs
- 1 egg
- 6 egg yolks
- 5 egg whites
- butter
- sour cherries

## Directions

1. Place the finely ground almonds and sugar in a bowl with finely chopped lemon zest
2. Add roll crumbs that are dry enough to grate
3. Beat 1 whole egg and 6 egg yolks over the mixture and stir for half an hour
4. Finally fold in stiffly beaten 5 egg whites
5. Coat a baking dish with butter
6. Line the bottom completely with sour cherries
7. Fill half with the batter
8. Bake in the oven until the dough is firm enough that the cherries do not sink
9. Slightly pull the baking dish out from the oven without removing it
10. Completely line the baked dough with more cherries
11. Top with the remaining batter
12. Bake completely
13. For the same cake with currants or raspberries: clean and wash 10 dekagrams of the finest berries and let them drain on a sieve until dry
