## Ingredients

- twenty-eight dekagramm flour
- one egg
- lukewarm water
- 7 dekagramm butter
- two whole eggs and three egg yolks
- four decziliters sour cream
- three decziliters semolina
- flour for dusting

## Directions

1. From twenty-eight dekagramm flour, one egg, and a little lukewarm water, prepare firm strudel dough
2. While it rests, beat 7 dekagramm butter until fluffy
3. Add two whole eggs and three egg yolks to it and mix well
4. Once finely mixed, add four decziliters sour cream and three decziliters semolina
5. Stretch the dough very thinly
6. Brush the filling halfway across it and fold it together
7. Using a wooden spoon handle dusted with flour, make indentations at equal distances and cut with a knife in two
8. Press the ends together well with your fingers
9. Boil the strudel pieces in hot water, but only until they float through, otherwise they will fall apart
10. Transfer to a plate and sprinkle each piece with finely chopped bread rolls fried in butter
