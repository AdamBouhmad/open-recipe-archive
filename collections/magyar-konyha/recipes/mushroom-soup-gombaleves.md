## Ingredients

- clove mushrooms
- pea broth
- butter
- green parsley
- flour
- black pepper
- nutmeg flower
- sour cream
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Wash the clove mushrooms several times in warm water
2. Pour pea broth over them and cook in it
3. Pour the broth into another pot
4. Braise the mushrooms in butter and green parsley
5. Sprinkle a cooking spoon of flour over them
6. Pour the pea broth over it
7. Add a little ground pepper, nutmeg flower and a good tablespoon of sour cream
8. Serve on fried bread
