## Ingredients

- coffee-cup amount of egg yolks
- same amount of melted butter
- same amount of lukewarm cream
- 14 dekagrams cream with dissolved yeast
- salt
- flour warmed in a bowl beforehand

## Directions

1. Prepare dough slightly firmer than puff pastry from the egg yolks, melted butter, lukewarm cream, yeast dissolved in cream, salt and flour
2. Beat the dough until it becomes fine and smooth
3. Place it in a cloth coated with butter, tie it loosely and place in cold water
4. Leave it in the water until the dough floats to the surface
5. Remove it, roll out to finger-thickness on a board well dusted with flour
6. Make rolls filled with preserves or other shapes, brush with egg white, sprinkle with sugared almonds and bake
