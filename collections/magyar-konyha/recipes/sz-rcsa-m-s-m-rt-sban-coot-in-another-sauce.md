## Ingredients

- coot
- butter
- carrot
- parsley root
- Spanish onion
- lemon peel
- cloves
- bay leaf
- vinegar
- pea broth
- flour
- wine

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the coot and cut into several pieces
2. Rinse well
3. Coat a pot with butter and add carrot, parsley root, Spanish onion, lemon peel, cloves, and bay leaf
4. Place the coot on top and braise
5. Pour vinegar and pea broth over it and bring to a boil
6. Make a yellow roux
7. Add the braised coot with sauce and a little wine to the roux
8. Bring to a boil
9. Plate and pour the sauce over it
