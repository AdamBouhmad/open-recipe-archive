## Ingredients

- Two kilograms roast beef
- Bouillon, two serving spoons
- Green parsley
- Small amount onion
- Eighteen mushrooms
- Butter
- Cracked pepper
- Salt
- White paper

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from the beef, pound well, place in a pot, and pour two serving spoons of bouillon over it
2. Braise until the liquid has completely evaporated
3. Finely chop green parsley, a small amount of onion, and eighteen mushrooms
4. Mix with a piece of butter, cracked pepper, and salt
5. Coat the beef slices with this mixture
6. Wrap each slice in white paper that has been cut with decorative edges and folded
7. Butter another sheet of paper, place it on the roasting pan, arrange the paper-wrapped beef on it
8. Roast over gentle heat, turning frequently on all sides
9. When completely cooked, serve the beef in the paper wrappings
