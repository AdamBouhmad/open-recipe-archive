## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams butter, well mixed
- 2 whole eggs
- 14 dekagrams blanched and ground almonds
- 14 dekagrams ground sugar
- zest of 1 lemon, finely chopped
- 14 dekagrams fine flour

## Directions

1. Beat 2 whole eggs into 14 dekagrams of well-mixed butter and blend well
2. Add 14 dekagrams of blanched and ground almonds, the same amount of ground sugar, and the finely chopped zest of 1 lemon, and mix everything well
3. Finally add 14 dekagrams of fine flour and stir until well combined
4. Pour the mixture into buttered tartlet molds
5. Bake in a gently heated oven until golden
6. Turn out of molds. Can be frosted with icing or made into a cake
