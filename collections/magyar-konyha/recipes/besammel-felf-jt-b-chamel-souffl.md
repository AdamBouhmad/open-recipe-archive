## Ingredients

- 140 grams fresh melted butter
- 120 grams fine flour
- 400 milliliters cream or good milk
- salt
- 10 egg yolks
- 40 grams ground and sifted sugar with vanilla
- 6 egg whites
- butter
- peach or raspberry syrup, wine or milk sauce

## Directions

1. In a pot, brown the fine flour in the melted butter
2. When the flour foams, gradually add cream or milk with constant stirring
3. Stir the whole mixture until it separates from the pot, then season lightly with salt
4. Cool on a plate
5. Beat the paste with ten egg yolks until foamy
6. Mix in sugar ground with vanilla
7. Finally fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
8. Pour the mixture into a well-buttered mold and either steam for three-quarters of an hour or bake in a not too hot oven until a beautiful light yellow
9. Turn out onto a plate and pour peach or raspberry syrup, wine or milk sauce underneath
