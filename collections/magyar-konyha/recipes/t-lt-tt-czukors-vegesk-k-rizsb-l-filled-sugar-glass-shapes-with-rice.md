## Ingredients

- twelve dekagrams of rice
- seven deciliters of sour cream
- seven dekagrams of finely crushed almonds
- sugar
- flour
- preserved apricot
- egg white
- beaten egg
- fine bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook twelve dekagrams of rice soft in seven deciliters of sour cream
2. Add seven dekagrams of finely crushed almonds and enough sugar to make it sufficiently sweet
3. Once the rice is soft, cool it slightly and place it spoonful by spoonful on a board dusted with flour
4. With a knife, flatten into triangular pieces
5. Fill with preserved apricot
6. Brush the edge with egg white and roll it together in the shape of a small sugar-glass
7. Finally, dip into beaten egg and then into fine bread crumbs and fry in fat
