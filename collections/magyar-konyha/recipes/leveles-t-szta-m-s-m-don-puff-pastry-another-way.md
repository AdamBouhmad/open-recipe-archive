## Ingredients

- 7 dekagramms butter
- 4 tablespoons fine flour
- 4 deciliters warm milk
- sugar to taste
- a little vanilla
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- 4 dekagramms softened almonds
- 14 dekagramms finely ground and sifted sugar (for topping)
- preserves (for filling)

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and quickly stir in the flour, then cook with warm milk while continuously stirring until well cooked and a smooth thick paste forms
2. Cool and add sugar to taste and a little vanilla
3. Slowly fold in the egg yolks and the stiff peaks of 4 egg whites
4. Grease round copper pans lined with lead with a two-finger-wide rim
5. Fill with the paste to finger thickness
6. Bake at gentle heat
7. When well baked, turn out onto a clean board
8. Cut out 5-6 leaves with a medium-sized cutter
9. Place the first leaf on a plate, spread with preserves, add the second leaf and cover again with preserves, continuing until all leaves are stacked and filled
10. Mix 14 dekagramms finely ground and sifted sugar with stiff peaks of 3 egg whites and a little vanilla
11. Coat the entire pastry smoothly with this
12. Fill a Spanish pastry bag with the remainder and pipe small Spanish pastries the size of hazelnuts on the pastry in neat rows
13. Prepare 4 dekagramms softened almonds, dry well with a cloth and chop finely
14. Mix with the same amount of finely ground sugar
15. Sprinkle the entire pastry with this
16. Bake in a hot oven or a light golden yellow color
