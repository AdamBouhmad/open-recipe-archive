## Ingredients

- lung soaked in cold water and briefly boiled
- spleen
- small amount of raw liver
- caul fat cleaned in cold water
- large red onion
- black pepper
- quarter kilogram cooked rice
- cloves
- black ground pepper
- marjoram, sifted through a sieve
- salt
- hot water

## Directions

1. Soak the lung in cold water and briefly boil it, then clean it from the bronchi
2. Finely chop the spleen and small amount of raw liver with a chopping knife
3. Briefly boil and finely chop the cleaned caul fat
4. Braise a large red onion in lard with black pepper until soft, then chop very finely
5. Mix the cooked rice, a small amount of cloves, black ground pepper, plenty of marjoram (sifted), proper amount of salt, and a few spoonfuls of hot water with all the ingredients to create a tender mass
6. Fill the clean intestines with a sausage filler to seven-handspan-length pieces
7. Secure both ends of the sausage with thin wooden sticks
8. Boil in hot salted water
