## Ingredients

- fresh, ripe wild strawberries
- sugar, half kilogram of sugar per half kilogram of strawberries
- water, two deciliters

## Directions

1. Select beautiful, ripe wild strawberries, press them through a sieve.
2. For half a kilogram of strawberries, use the same amount of sugar, which you cook in two deciliters of water until it threads.
3. Then add the pressed strawberries and stir constantly until it comes to a boil several times.
4. It is particularly important to note that it must not cook longer, as otherwise it will lose its beautiful color and acquire a bad taste; then when cooled, pour into glasses.
