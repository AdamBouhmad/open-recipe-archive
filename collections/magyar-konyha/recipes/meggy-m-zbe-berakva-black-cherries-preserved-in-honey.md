## Ingredients

- larger black cherries with stems removed
- 3 dekagrams cinnamon per 4.25 liter barrel
- 1 dekagram cloves per 4.25 liter barrel
- 2 liters good white honey
- clean linen cloth
- clean linen for the bung

## Directions

1. Prepare a barrel slightly smaller than one used for cucumbers.
2. Wash and dry large black cherries after removing their stems.
3. For a 4.25 liter barrel, crush 3 dekagrams cinnamon and 1 dekagram cloves.
4. Divide the spices equally among three small bags made from clean linen and sew them closed.
5. Place one spice bag on the bottom of the barrel.
6. Cook 2 liters of good white honey, skim it clean, and let it cool.
7. Pour some of the cooled liquid honey on the barrel bottom and place some cherries on top.
8. Add another spice bag on the cherries, then the remaining cherries, and place the third spice bag and remaining honey on top.
9. Leave two finger-widths of space at the top of the barrel.
10. Place clean linen on the bung, drive it in firmly, and store in a cool place.
