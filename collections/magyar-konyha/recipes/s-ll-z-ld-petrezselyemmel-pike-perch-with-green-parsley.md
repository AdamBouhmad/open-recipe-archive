## Ingredients

- pike-perch
- salt
- salted water
- various root vegetables
- fine breadcrumbs
- 10 decagrams butter
- green parsley
- small potatoes

## Directions

1. Clean the pike-perch and let it sit in salt for an hour
2. Cook in salted water with various root vegetables, taking care it does not fall apart
3. Place on a long platter, sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs
4. Melt 10 decagrams butter, add finely chopped green parsley and pour over the fish
5. The platter rim can be decorated with small potatoes fried in butter
