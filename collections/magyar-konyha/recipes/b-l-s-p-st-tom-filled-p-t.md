## Ingredients

- 500 grams veal from the thigh
- fat from near the kidney
- red onion, finely chopped
- lemon zest, finely chopped
- 1 bread roll, grated crust, soaked in milk and wrung out
- 3 eggs
- butter
- milk
- 2 whole eggs
- cooked veal sweetbreads
- udder
- lemon juice
- crumbly pastry

## Directions

1. Finely chop 500 grams veal from the thigh, fat from near the kidney, red onion, and lemon zest together
2. Soak a grated bread roll crust in milk, wring it out well, and add to the mixture
3. Remove small membranes and fibers from the mixture
4. Prepare a soft batter from 3 eggs, a small piece of butter, and milk
5. Mix everything finely together, remove from heat, stir in 2 whole eggs and let stand
6. Prepare a filling from cooked veal sweetbreads and udder, and fricassee this with 2 egg yolks and lemon juice
7. Prepare crumbly pastry and line a pâté dish and its bottom with it to one small finger's thickness
8. Place half of the filling in the center
9. Layer the fricasseed mixture on top
10. Shape the other half of the filling into a round form on top
11. Roll finger-thickness strips from the remaining dough and arrange them like a grill over the filling so the filling shows through everywhere
12. Brush with beaten egg and bake for three-quarters of an hour
13. Before serving the pâté, alternate asparagus tips and crayfish tails in the spaces of the grill where the filling shows through
