## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram fresh pork skin, cleaned of all fat
- piece of veal knuckle
- 3 liters fresh water
- salt
- vegetables: root vegetables, red onion, carrot, kohlrabi, 2 heads white cabbage
- several peppercorns and cloves
- 3-4 deciliters strong vinegar
- 4 egg whites
- 1 whole egg in shell

## Directions

1. Rinse the pork skin several times in warm water and clean it of all fat
2. Place it in a pot with a piece of veal knuckle, pour 3 liters fresh water over it, add salt and vegetables
3. Clean off the scum frequently and simmer until half-cooked, then refill the pot with warm water
4. Continue boiling until the skins are so soft they can be pressed apart and the aspic is reduced to about 1.5 liters
5. In a separate pan, sauté sliced bacon, 1 kilogram soup meat, red onion, carrot, kohlrabi, and 2 heads white cabbage, all cut into slices, with several peppercorns and cloves
6. Cover and braise, stirring occasionally, until nicely browned without burning
7. Pour the liquid in which the skins were cooked into this, add 3-4 deciliters strong vinegar and any remaining pork skins not yet softened
8. Cook everything until reduced to about 1.5 liters
9. Beat 4 egg whites in a pot until foamy and beat in 1 whole egg with its shell
10. Pour the previously strained, fat-free, warm aspic into the eggs and beat well with a whisk during pouring
11. Let it stand quietly in a warm place for three hours
12. Strain slowly and pass through a stretched cloth
13. If not completely clear, strain once more
14. Cool it in a cold place and use as desired for meat dishes, mayonnaise, or preparation of cold foods
