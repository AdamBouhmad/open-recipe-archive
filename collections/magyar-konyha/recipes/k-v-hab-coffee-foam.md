## Ingredients

- very strong coffee
- 1 liter heavy cream
- 18 dekagrams sifted sugar
- 2 tablespoons strong black coffee
- 4.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass
- Spanish doughnuts for garnish

## Directions

1. Brew very strong coffee and filter it through fine cloth when completely clear
2. Add sugar generously and set on ice
3. Whip 1 liter of excellent heavy cream in a basin on ice until it becomes foam
4. Add sifted sugar to the basin, add 2 tablespoons of strong black coffee, and beat together
5. Gradually add the whipped cream
6. If the foam is too white, add more coffee so it becomes dark yellow with prominent coffee flavor
7. Finally, fold in the dissolved, cooled, and strained isinglass
8. Pour into a mold and set on ice
9. Before serving, turn out onto a plate and arrange neatly with Spanish doughnuts
