## Ingredients

- 500 grams sugar
- 2 deciliters water
- 500 grams ripe serviceberries

## Directions

1. Boil 500 grams of sugar with two deciliters of water until it throws heavy drops, removing the scum during boiling.
2. Wash 500 grams of ripe serviceberries, scald with boiling water, and drain well.
3. Place them in the boiling sugar, do not stir much so they do not separate from the seeds.
4. After boiling for a quarter hour, remove them with a slotted spoon, and boil the juice until it is thick.
5. Then remove from the fire, place the serviceberries back in, and handle the same as the previous one when filling.
