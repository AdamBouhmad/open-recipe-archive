## Ingredients

- a beautiful carp
- vinegar
- beer
- parsley root
- red onion
- celery
- bay leaf
- thyme
- whole peppercorns
- allspice
- cloves
- lemon peel
- sugar
- walnuts
- black bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the carp thoroughly, fillet it, and collect its blood in a pot, pour a little vinegar into the fish so that all blood comes out of it and cook it.
2. Prepare the broth in a pan: put two parts beer and one part vinegar into it, as well as parsley root cut lengthwise, red onion, celery, bay leaf, thyme, a few whole peppercorns, allspice, cloves, lemon peel, a piece of sugar and a few walnut meats; cover and cook for one hour.
3. Roast a handful of black bread crumbs in butter, add it, also add the blood, cut the fish into pieces and add it to the broth; if the sauce is still thin, a little grated gingerbread can be added.
4. When the fish has cooked for a good quarter hour, it is ready; then place it on the serving dish, strain the sauce over it and serve to the table.
