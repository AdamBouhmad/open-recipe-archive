## Ingredients

- parsley root and other roots
- bay leaves
- capers
- black pepper
- red onion
- diced bacon
- flour
- a little flour for browning
- Liebig meat extract (5 grams)
- salt
- sour cream (several spoonfuls)
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Stew parsley root and other roots with bay leaves, capers, black pepper, red onion, and diced bacon until all moisture from the roots and onion has evaporated
2. Sprinkle with a little flour and brown it, stirring constantly
3. Pour in a little marinade and bring to a boil
4. When well cooked, press through a sieve
5. Add 5 grams of Liebig meat extract
6. Season with salt
7. Finally, pour in several spoonfuls of sour cream
8. If too thick, dilute with meat broth and cook for another quarter hour
9. Serve separately in a bowl with roasted meat or game
