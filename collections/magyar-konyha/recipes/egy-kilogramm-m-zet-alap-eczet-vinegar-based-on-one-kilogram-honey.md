## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram honey
- 40 dekagrams powdered tartar
- 1.5 liters good grain brandy
- 12 liters lukewarm rainwater or boiled well water

## Directions

1. Pour 1 kilogram of honey, 40 dekagrams of powdered tartar, and 1.5 liters of good grain brandy into a twelve-liter barrel.
2. Fill it completely with lukewarm rainwater or boiled well water and let it stand in a warm place for three months.
3. After this time, every month you can take out 5 liters of the best vinegar from the barrel, then pour 7 deciliters of brandy and 43 deciliters of water into it.
