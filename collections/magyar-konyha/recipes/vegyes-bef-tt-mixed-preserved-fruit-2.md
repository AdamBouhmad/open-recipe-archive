## Ingredients

- half kilogram sweet, juicy black cherries
- half kilogram sour cherries
- half kilogram sugar
- one glass of water
- half kilogram raspberries

## Directions

1. Pit the black cherries and sour cherries, collecting their juices. Cook the sugar with one glass of water, skimming it until completely clear. Add the cherries and their juices, and cook for one hour. Add the raspberries and cook with the other fruit until a drop tested on a plate no longer runs but stays in place. Remove from heat and pour into dry, pre-warmed glasses so they do not crack. Leave the glass open for twenty-four hours, then seal with paper.
