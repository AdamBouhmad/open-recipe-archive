## Ingredients

- twenty-eight dekagrams softened almonds
- the same amount of sugar
- one lemon, finely chopped zest
- knife-point amount of ground cinnamon
- two to three ground cloves
- ten dekagrams crumbs from well-baked, not sour black bread
- one to two coffee-spoonfuls of Málaga or Madeira wine
- one whole egg
- eight egg yolks
- four egg whites
- butter for coating the pan

## Directions

1. Finely grind the softened almonds, moistening slightly with a little water during grinding
2. Finely grind the same amount of sugar similarly
3. Prepare the finely chopped lemon zest, knife-point amount of ground cinnamon, and two to three ground cloves
4. Grate ten dekagrams of crumbs from well-baked, not sour black bread
5. Moisten the crumbs with one to two coffee-spoonfuls of Málaga or Madeira wine, making them only damp, not wet
6. Place all ingredients in a bowl, adding one whole egg and eight egg yolks one after another
7. Stir for half an hour until foamy
8. Gently mix in the stiff snow of four egg whites
9. Pour the mixture into a buttered pan or mold
10. Bake at not very high heat
