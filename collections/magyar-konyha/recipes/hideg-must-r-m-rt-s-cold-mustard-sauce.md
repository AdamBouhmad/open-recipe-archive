## Ingredients

- four eggs
- three anchovies, cleaned of bones and spines
- red onion
- green parsley
- mustard
- sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil four eggs hard
2. Add three anchovies cleaned of bones and spines
3. Chop together with red onion, green parsley and egg
4. Put in a bowl
5. Prepare with mustard, sugar and vinegar and serve
