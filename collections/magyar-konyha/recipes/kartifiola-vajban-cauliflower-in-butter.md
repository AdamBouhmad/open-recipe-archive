## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the cauliflower as described above
2. When cooked and drained, arrange the florets neatly in rose shape in the center of a warmed plate
3. Pour hot butter evenly over them
4. Serve quickly to the table
5. Can be garnished with golden-braised cabbage, which gives the dish a more beautiful color
6. Can also be served with breadcrumbs fried in butter
