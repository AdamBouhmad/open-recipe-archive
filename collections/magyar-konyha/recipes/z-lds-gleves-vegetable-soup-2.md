## Ingredients

- vegetables
- butter
- flour
- salt
- spices
- eggs
- sour cream
- bread

## Directions

1. For soup for six people, clean the vegetables well, chop finely
2. Melt a piece of butter, braise the vegetables with it, sprinkle in little flour, salt and season well, and let cook thoroughly
3. Mix together two egg yolks, little sour cream and a piece of butter, pour the soup over it, place on the fire and stir until it begins to thicken
4. Serve over fried bread pieces
