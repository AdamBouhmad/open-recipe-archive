## Ingredients

- tablespoon rice
- 1 liter water
- juice of several lemons
- 1 glass lemon juice
- 4 glasses crushed sugar
- half glass rum
- 4 glasses rice water

## Directions

1. Cook a tablespoon of rice in 1 liter water, skim well
2. When rice has burst, remove from fire
3. Squeeze juice from several lemons
4. Measure 1 glass lemon juice, 4 glasses crushed sugar, half glass rum, and 4 glasses rice water
5. Place in a bowl but stir frequently until sugar dissolves and the mixture cools
6. Proceed as with other ice creams
7. Rice water may be replaced with light Holland or corn tea
