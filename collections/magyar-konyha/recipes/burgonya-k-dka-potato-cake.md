## Ingredients

- 18 dekagrams good floury cooked peeled and cooled potato, grated
- 14 dekagrams finely powdered sugar
- 7 dekagrams finely powdered and soaked almonds
- zest of one lemon, finely chopped
- 2 whole eggs
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Place grated potato in a bowl with powdered sugar, blanched almonds, lemon zest, whole eggs, and egg yolks
2. Beat until well foamed
3. Fold in stiffly beaten egg white
4. Fill buttered molds halfway
5. Bake at gentle heat
