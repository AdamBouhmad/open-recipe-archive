## Ingredients

- young white turnips
- butter
- crushed sugar
- brown broth
- glace
- ham

## Directions

1. Peel the young white turnips, wash them and cut each into five or six equal parts shaped like crescents
2. These slices should be the length and thickness of a small finger
3. In melted butter brown two spoonfuls of crushed sugar until golden
4. Add the turnips and when they are well mixed with the sugar, pour half a serving spoon of brown broth over them
5. Shake well and braise until the turnips are soft but do not fall apart
6. Finally add a serving spoon of glace and cook this into a large sauce
7. Turn the turnips on all sides, pour the little sauce into a plate, arrange the turnips on it in triple wreath form with a fork, and garnish the edge of the plate with finely chopped ham
