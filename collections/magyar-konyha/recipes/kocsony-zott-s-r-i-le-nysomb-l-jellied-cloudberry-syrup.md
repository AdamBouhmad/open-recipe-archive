## Ingredients

- cloudberries with stems removed
- equal amount of sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Place the cloudberries in a pot with slightly more water than needed for cooking
2. Boil rapidly over high heat so the color is preserved
3. Press through a sieve
4. Boil the sugar until it threads and throws heavy drops
5. Take equal amounts of fruit and sugar
6. When the fruit is added to the sugar, bring to a boil only a few more times
7. Remove from heat, cool, and pour into glasses
