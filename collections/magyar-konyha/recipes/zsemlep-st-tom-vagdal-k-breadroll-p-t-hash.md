## Ingredients

- round rolls the size of pastries
- eggs
- fine breadcrumbs
- veal sweetbreads
- veal meat
- mushrooms
- butter
- fresh parsley
- artichokes or asparagus
- salted water
- white meat broth
- shrimp tails and legs
- buttered sauce

## Directions

1. Lightly grate the crust of round rolls the size of pastries
2. Cut a small crown-sized lid in the center and hollow out the interior completely
3. Dip the roll in slightly salted egg, roll in fine breadcrumbs and quickly fry until nicely golden
4. Prepare minced veal from veal sweetbreads and veal meat, cutting into pea-sized cubes
5. Cut six cleaned mushrooms into similar cubes and braise with butter and fresh parsley
6. Cook artichokes or asparagus in salted water and set aside the small roses and tips
7. Keep warm in white meat broth
8. Clean 25-30 shrimp tails and legs, set aside the most beautiful tails, chop the rest along with the artichokes and asparagus into cubes
9. Prepare a buttered sauce, set aside half when done
10. Into the other half, add the veal meat, sweetbreads, mushrooms, artichokes, and shrimp
11. Cook the sauce well and fill the fried rolls with this mixture
