## Ingredients

- egg whites
- egg yolks
- sour cherries
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat together one whole egg and six egg yolks, stirring for half an hour
2. Fold in stiff meringue made from five egg whites
3. Grease a baking pan with butter and line the bottom with whole sour cherries
4. Fill pan halfway with the mixture and bake in the oven until the batter has set so firmly that the cherries do not sink into it
5. Pull the pan slightly out of the oven without removing it completely, cover the cooked batter entirely with more sour cherries, add the remaining batter on top, and bake completely through
