## Ingredients

- eighteen dekagrams rice
- warm water
- seven dekagrams raisins
- five dekagrams currants
- four crumbly sour apples
- eighteen dekagrams ground sugar
- butter
- preserved cherries

## Directions

1. Soften eighteen dekagrams rice in warm water for a quarter hour, then sieve
2. Clean, wash and dry seven dekagrams raisins and five dekagrams currants
3. Peel four crumbly sour apples, cut into slices and sift eighteen dekagrams ground sugar
4. Line the bottom of a buttered saucepan thickly with apple slices
5. Add a layer of heavily sugared rice, then some raisins and currants, then another layer of apples, sugar, rice and a layer of preserved cherries, then repeat with apples
