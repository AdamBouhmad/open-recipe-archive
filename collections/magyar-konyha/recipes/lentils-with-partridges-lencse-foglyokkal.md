## Ingredients

- partridges
- salt
- thin wooden skewers
- large lentils
- butter
- red onion
- bacon
- lemon juice
- red wine

## Directions

1. Clean the partridges well and cut off their heads
2. Fold back their wings and salt them
3. Fit them with thin wooden skewers
4. Cook large lentils until soft so the grains remain whole
5. Strain the water from the lentils
6. Put a little butter and finely chopped red onion in a pot and add the lentils to braise
7. Roast or braise the partridges with bacon, lemon juice, red onion, and a little red wine
8. Pour the partridge sauce over the lentils and braise together for a short time
9. When serving, place the partridges in the center of the plate and arrange the lentils in a wreath shape around them
