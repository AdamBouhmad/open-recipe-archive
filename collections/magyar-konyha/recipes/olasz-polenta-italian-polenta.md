## Ingredients

- 2 liter salted water
- corn meal for polenta
- butter
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Boil salted water in a pan
2. Add cornmeal and cook until thick dough forms
3. Work the dough as finely as possible with a spoon
4. Spread on a board and shape into a round ball
5. Wrap in a cloth and let rise for one hour
6. Cut into knife-thickness slices using strong thread
7. Layer the polenta slices in a buttered mold, sprinkling each layer with grated Parmesan cheese
8. Top the last layer with cheese
9. Bake at high heat for one hour
10. Drain the fat and carefully separate with a knife
11. Turn out and serve with a golden-brown and crispy crust
