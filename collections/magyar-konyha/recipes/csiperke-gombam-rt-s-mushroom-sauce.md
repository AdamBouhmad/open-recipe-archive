## Ingredients

- six to seven peeled button mushrooms
- green parsley, finely chopped
- butter
- flour
- good stock
- three to four tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Remove the interior of six to seven peeled button mushrooms
2. Slice the rest and chop finely with the green parsley
3. Braise in butter and sprinkle with flour
4. Once sufficiently evaporated, add one tablespoon of good stock and finally three to four tablespoons of sour cream
5. Bring to a gentle boil
