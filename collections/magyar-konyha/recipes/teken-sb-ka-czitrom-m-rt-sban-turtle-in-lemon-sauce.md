## Ingredients

- turtle
- butter
- flour
- pea broth
- red onion
- lemon peel
- lemon juice
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cook the turtle and cut into four pieces
2. Melt butter and brown a spoonful of flour in it
3. Dilute with pea broth
4. Add a head of red onion (which is removed before serving)
5. Reduce the sauce
6. Strain the sauce
7. Season with lemon peel and juice
8. Add a little sour cream
9. Add the turtle pieces and bring to a boil for several minutes
10. Plate neatly
