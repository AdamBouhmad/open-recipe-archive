## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- eggshell measure of water
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat 4 eggs into a bowl, pour water measured with eggshell into the middle, and knead with as much flour as it absorbs, until the dough becomes shiny from kneading
2. This should be rather stiff than soft, although it is harder to roll out
3. Divide into two portions
