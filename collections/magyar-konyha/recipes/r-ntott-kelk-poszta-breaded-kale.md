## Ingredients

- firm kale heads
- salt water
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- flour
- lard
- butter
- sour cream
- bone marrow
- onion
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the kale heads in salted water until tender and drain
2. Allow to cool, then cut each head into four equal parts
3. Beat two eggs well and dip the kale into them
4. Coat half in breadcrumbs and half in flour, then fry in lard until golden brown
5. Place small pieces of butter on a serving dish, arrange the breaded kale on top
6. Add a good portion of sour cream on top and a little butter
7. Place coals on top and let simmer for half an hour
8. Prepare the bone marrow patties as follows: after the marrow is cooked, clean it from its membrane
9. Chop some onion and fresh parsley finely
10. Melt a little butter in a pan, add the marrow, onion, and parsley
11. Season with salt and pepper, mix well and brown lightly
12. Cut two rolls into slices of finger-thickness, shape them round or square
13. Coat with a knife-thickness layer of marrow and cover with another slice, continuing as needed
14. Quickly dip these filled slices in milk, beat two or three eggs and dip the patties into them
15. Sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs and fry until golden brown
16. Serve the bone marrow patties alongside the breaded kale
