## Ingredients

- 4 large ripe quinces
- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 7 deciliters fresh water
- juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Peel 4 large ripe quinces and cut into thin slices
2. Cook in a preserving pan with 42 dekagrams sugar and 7 deciliters fresh water until the quince is completely soft
3. Cool and press through clean, thin kitchen cloth
4. Mix with the juice of 2 lemons
5. Cool completely
6. Pour into freezing container and proceed as with other ice creams
7. The same mixture is used to make jelly, but for that use one and one-quarter dekagrams of dissolved isinglass, and strain as with all jellies through kitchen cloth and blotting paper
