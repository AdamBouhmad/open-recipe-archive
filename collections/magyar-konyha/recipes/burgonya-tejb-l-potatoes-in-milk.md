## Ingredients

- ten to twelve boiled potatoes
- seven deciliters good milk
- butter
- one small coffee cup cream
- eggs

## Directions

1. Peel the boiled potatoes and cut into thick slices
2. Bring the milk to a boil
3. Add the potatoes and cook until the milk reduces without turning to mush
4. Place several pieces of butter on top and pour a small coffee cup of cream over it
5. Brown quickly until nicely browned
6. Place an egg-based ring around the edge of the plate
7. Serve in the same dish
8. The potatoes should be neither soupy nor dry
