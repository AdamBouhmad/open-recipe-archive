## Ingredients

- cooked veal innards
- parsley
- bread roll
- milk
- seven dekagrams butter
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- mace
- pepper
- one whole egg
- three egg yolks
- flour for dough
- egg white
- lard

## Directions

1. From cooked veal innards remove the larynx, spleen and membrane
2. Chop the rest finely with parsley and milk-soaked bread
3. Melt seven dekagrams of butter
4. Add a handful of breadcrumbs and fry until golden
5. Add the chopped mixture with salt, mace and a little pepper
6. Braise slightly
7. Beat in one whole egg and three egg yolks
8. Quickly stir and remove from heat
9. Continue stirring until eggs are well combined
10. Prepare a crumbly dough for the mushroom stems
11. Roll out to the thickness of a knife back
12. Cut into cubes two finger widths wide
13. Roll these onto the wooden spoon handle
14. Brush slightly with egg white to hold together
15. Shape mushroom caps from the chopped mixture above the stems
16. Roll in beaten egg and then breadcrumbs
17. Fry in lard
18. Place in soup bowl and pour brown broth over them
