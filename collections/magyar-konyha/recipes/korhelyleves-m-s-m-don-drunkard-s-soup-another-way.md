## Ingredients

- cabbage
- fat
- red onion
- milk
- paprika
- sour cream
- broth
- rice
- fish
- frankfurter sausage

## Directions

1. Cook the cabbage
2. When soft, fry fat and chopped red onion in a pan with milk without browning
3. Add enough paprika to make it strong enough
4. Pour in the cabbage and sour cream and make as much soup as needed
5. Boil everything well together
6. Add rice and fish, as well as frankfurter sausage cut into pieces
