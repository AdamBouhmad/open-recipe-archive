## Ingredients

- beef or veal bagdany
- salt
- hard-boiled eggs
- salted pork, finely chopped
- green parsley
- black pepper
- saffron
- salt

## Directions

1. Salt the bagdany
2. Slit the skin around it
3. Split the meat lengthwise in a cross pattern
4. Grate 6-8-10 hard-boiled eggs according to the size of the meat
5. Add a good piece of chopped salted pork chopped very finely, green parsley, abundant black pepper, one knife-tip of good saffron and salt
6. Mix these together
7. Fill the bagdany with this mixture
8. Roast until nicely browned
9. Optionally pound the meat to make it more tender
