## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt water
- butter sauce
- 2 deciliters sour cream
- fine breadcrumbs
- hot butter

## Directions

1. Clean the asparagus
2. Cook in salted water but not until too soft
3. Place in butter sauce or arrange in a platter with tips facing inward
4. Pour 2 deciliters of sour cream over it
5. Sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs and pour hot butter over it
