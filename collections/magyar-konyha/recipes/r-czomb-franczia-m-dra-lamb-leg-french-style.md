## Ingredients

- lamb leg
- salt
- green parsley
- onion
- thyme
- pepper
- bacon
- thread
- brown broth
- onions
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Remove the bone from the leg and trim the fat from the skin, then salt it
2. Chop green parsley, onion, and thyme finely, add pepper, and prepare strips of bacon, coating them in this mixture
3. Make holes in the leg with a sharp knife and lard it with bacon, then wrap it completely with thread so the bacon does not fall out
4. Place parsley, onion, and several peppercorns in a pot, add the leg, fill with brown broth, cover and braise until completely tender
5. Remove the leg, strain the sauce and boil it until only one or two spoonfuls remain
6. Return the leg to the sauce and turn it several times in the small amount of sauce
7. Chop six or seven onions coarsely, melt a piece of butter and place the onions in it with pepper, fry until golden
8. Sprinkle flour over and brown it, add several spoonfuls of broth so it does not burn, add another spoonful of broth and cook for half an hour longer
9. Place the sauce on the serving plate and the leg on top
