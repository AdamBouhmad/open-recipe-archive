## Ingredients

- one kilogram mutton tallow
- seventy-five dekagrams vegetable oil
- thirteen dekagrams hair powder
- ten dekagrams jasmine oil
- three dekagrams bergamot oil
- two dekagrams cedarwood oil
- four grams clove oil
- two dekagrams musk dye

## Directions

1. Mix one kilogram of mutton tallow with seventy-five dekagrams of vegetable oil, thirteen dekagrams of hair powder, ten dekagrams of jasmine oil, three dekagrams of bergamot oil, two dekagrams of cedarwood oil, four grams of clove oil, and two dekagrams of musk dye well together
2. Dye the mixture to a brownish color
