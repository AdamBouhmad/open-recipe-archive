## Ingredients

- 42-56 dekagramm sugar
- 1 stick vanilla
- juice of 4 lemons
- 0.5 bottle Madeira wine
- 0.5 bottle white wine
- 6 dekagramm isinglass

## Directions

1. Once the sugar is dissolved, cook it with the vanilla
2. Let it simmer covered by the fire for half an hour
3. Once cooled, combine with the lukewarm aspic, the strained lemon juice, Madeira and white wine
4. Strain the entire mixture through a cloth
5. After testing the firmness, pour into the mold and freeze
