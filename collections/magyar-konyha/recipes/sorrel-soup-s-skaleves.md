## Ingredients

- butter or lard
- flour
- three handfuls sorrel
- salt
- three egg yolks
- butter
- three tablespoons sour cream
- fried bread slices or pea paste fried in fat

## Directions

1. Make a light, thin roux with butter or lard in a pot
2. Clean and finely chop three handfuls of sorrel and place in the roux
3. Braise slightly and thin or thicken with liquid as desired
4. Before serving, beat three egg yolks, a little butter and three tablespoons of sour cream together
5. Pour the hot soup over it
6. Serve on fried bread slices or pea paste fried in fat
