## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilogram peeled chestnuts
- piece of vanilla
- milk
- 1.5 liters cream
- 12 egg yolks
- 35 dekagrams sugar
- 28 dekagrams raspberry jelly
- 14 dekagrams cleaned raisins
- 14 dekagrams currants
- 7 dekagrams candied lemon peel cut into cubes
- white wine
- sugar
- 4 deciliters whipped cream
- sugar
- 1 glass maraschino

## Directions

1. Cook 0.5 kilogram of peeled chestnuts with a piece of vanilla in milk until soft and press through a sieve
2. Beat 1.5 liters of cream with 12 egg yolks and 35 dekagrams of sugar over fire until foamy, then press through a sieve
3. Add 28 dekagrams of raspberry jelly and combine with the chestnut purée
4. Briefly heat 14 dekagrams of cleaned raisins, 14 dekagrams of currants, and 7 dekagrams of diced candied lemon peel with a little white wine and sugar, then let cool
5. When the chestnut mixture has frozen solid, mix in 4 deciliters of whipped cream, the raisins and candied lemon peel
6. Turn the freezing container a few more times
7. Pour the ice cream into a tall or dome-shaped mold
8. Close the mold with its lid and place between salt and ice for about 2 hours
9. Turn the ice cream onto a platter
10. Serve separately a sauce of whipped cream mixed with sugar and a small glass of maraschino
