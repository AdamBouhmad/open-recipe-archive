## Ingredients

- Beef
- Six to eight slices of bacon
- One whole carrot, sliced
- Red onion
- Good broth
- Young kohlrabi, several
- Large carrots
- Butter
- Sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the beef as usual
2. When it begins to become tender, place six to eight bacon slices, one whole sliced carrot, and red onion in a pot
3. Place the beef on top, cover, and braise slowly
4. From time to time pour a small amount of good broth over it and turn on all sides so it browns beautifully
5. Take several young kohlrabi and large carrots and using a special vegetable scoop (the kind used for kohlrabi), cut them into small balls
6. Melt a piece of butter in the pot with the meat, add a piece of sugar, and when it dissolves, add the carrot and kohlrabi balls
7. Braise until tender, pouring a small amount of broth frequently over them to prevent browning
8. Before serving, remove the fat from the sauce, strain through a sieve, and arrange the braised kohlrabi and carrot around the platter as a border
9. Place the meat in the center, pour the sauce over, and the dish is ready
10. If serving with potatoes, treat the meat the same way but add a few slices of ham, boil small potatoes, peel them, fry them in butter, and use them as a crown around the meat
