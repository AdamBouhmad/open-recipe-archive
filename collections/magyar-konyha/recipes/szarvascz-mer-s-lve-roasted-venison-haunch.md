## Ingredients

- venison haunch
- salt
- brine
- bacon slices
- fat
- paper
- caper sauce

## Directions

1. Wash the venison haunch and remove the skin
2. Beat it well to tenderize
3. Salt generously and place in brine for two to three days
4. Lard with bacon slices
5. Roast and cover with fat-greased paper to prevent the bacon from burning
6. Baste several times with the brine
7. When well roasted, place on a plate and pour caper sauce over it
