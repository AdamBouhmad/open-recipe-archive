## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram fine flour
- 6 whole eggs
- 30 grams yeast
- 2 tablespoons sugar powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- warm milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix together one kilogram fine flour, 6 whole eggs, 30 grams yeast, 2 tablespoons sugar powder, and one teaspoon salt
2. Add enough warm (but not hot) milk so the dough is neither too stiff nor too soft
3. Knead well and let rise slightly
4. When the dough begins to spread, stretch with a rolling pin to about one finger-thickness
5. Brush with good butter
6. Fold in the four corners so that the four corners meet in the middle
7. Let rest again for 10-15 minutes
8. After rest, stretch again and brush with butter
9. Finally, after it rises slightly, stretch one more time to barely a finger-thickness
10. Cut short strips, barely one span long and two fingers wide from the dough
11. Each strip should be brushed with egg and sprinkled with almonds
12. Place curved butter dough on a baking sheet or flat pan and bake immediately at normal heat without rising, for this way it will be nicely layered
