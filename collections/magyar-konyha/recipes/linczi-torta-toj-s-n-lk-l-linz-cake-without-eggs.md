## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams softened almonds
- 28 dekagrams sugar with vanilla
- 1 lemon, zest
- 28 dekagrams butter
- cold water
- 1 lemon, juice
- 28 dekagrams flour

## Directions

1. Pound the softened almonds finely, moistening with water during pounding so that it does not become oily
2. Pound the same amount of sugar with vanilla finely and chop the zest of one lemon into small pieces
3. Beat 28 dekagrams butter until foamy, mixing in a spoonful of cold water during the beating, as well as the juice of one lemon, the almonds, and the sugar
4. Once well beaten into a foamy state, gently mix in 28 dekagrams flour
5. From this, prepare either a rod cake or a soup cake; in the first case, fill it with preserves, cover nicely with the rod, place the ring on top, coat with egg white, dust with coarsely pounded sugar, and then bake at sustained heat
6. This dough can also be used for small pastries, such as small cakes, pretzels, etc.
