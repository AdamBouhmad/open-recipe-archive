## Ingredients

- 10-12 peeled apples
- 3-4 tablespoons peach marmalade
- 100 grams finely ground and thinned sugar
- 140 grams rice
- 100 grams softened almonds
- 700 milliliters cream
- 70 grams butter
- sugar and ground vanilla
- 1 whole egg
- 5 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Cut the peeled apples into slices the thickness of a finger
2. Add peach marmalade and thinned sugar to the apples
3. Cook gently with repeated shaking until soft, but keep them whole and maintain their shape
4. Coarsely crush the rice and similarly the softened almonds
5. Rinse the rice and cook both in cream, stirring constantly until thick
6. Remove from heat and stir in butter while still warm
7. Cool and add enough sugar and vanilla to sweeten adequately
8. Whip the paste with one whole egg and five egg yolks for a quarter hour with continuous stirring
9. Finally fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
10. Pour half the paste into a buttered pot
11. Distribute the apple slices, placing less attractive ones evenly on the filled mixture
12. Pour the remaining paste over them
13. Bake slowly
14. Keep the remaining apple slices warm and cook the syrup until thick
15. Pour this syrup over the baked pudding when turned out onto a plate
16. Form a double ring from the apple slices, sprinkle with sugar and serve quickly to the table
