## Ingredients

- crayfish
- rice
- pepper
- green parsley
- salt
- fat
- crayfish butter

## Directions

1. Calculate at least two larger or three smaller crayfish per person
2. For each crayfish take one spoon of rice, add a pinch of ground pepper, a pinch of finely chopped green parsley leaves, little salt, and per person one hazelnut-sized piece of fat
3. Mix these well together
4. Fill the back of the crayfish halfway; do not fill completely because when it cooks it will swell and if there is no space the rice will remain hard, and the tail will be difficult to push back since it must be used to close the filling so it does not run out during cooking
5. After the stuffed crayfish cooks for about half an hour, scatter the remaining spiced rice that was left over from the filling into the soup so it cooks too until it softens nicely but is not too soft
6. When done, strain the crayfish butter and add to it
