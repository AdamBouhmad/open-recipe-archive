## Ingredients

- frogs
- butter
- flour
- fresh parsley
- pea broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean and soak the frogs thoroughly
2. Cut off the front legs and interlock the back legs together
3. Salt the frogs
4. Prepare a sauce with butter, flour made into a roux, and finely chopped fresh parsley
5. Pour pea broth over the sauce
6. Add the frogs to the sauce
7. Bring to a boil and serve
