## Ingredients

- one quarter front of lamb
- two liters salted water
- one egg-sized amount of lard
- one large tablespoon flour
- one cup cold water
- one teaspoon chopped fresh tarragon leaves
- six tablespoons vinegar
- a few tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Boil the quarter front of lamb in two liters of salted water
2. While it cooks, prepare a nice brown roux from one egg-sized amount of lard and one large tablespoon flour
3. When the roux is hottest, pour in one cup cold water and immediately add it to the hot lamb broth
4. Add one teaspoon chopped fresh tarragon leaves and six tablespoons vinegar
5. Boil for five minutes with these, then remove from high heat until serving
6. Pour in a few tablespoons of sour cream and serve in a deep bowl
