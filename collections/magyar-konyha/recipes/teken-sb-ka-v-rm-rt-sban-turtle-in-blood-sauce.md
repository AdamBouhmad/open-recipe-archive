## Ingredients

- turtle
- salt
- water
- butter
- flour
- red onion
- cloves
- fish broth or cooking water
- lemon peel
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After bleeding the turtle, place in hot salted water and set aside the blood in a cup
2. Cook the turtle until its shell can be opened (test several times)
3. Remove the meat from both shells, peel off the outer skin, and cut into desired pieces
4. Make a light brown sauce with butter and flour
5. Add a head of red onion studded with cloves
6. Dilute with fish broth or the water in which the turtle was cooked
7. Add lemon peel and half a lemon's worth of juice
8. Add the turtle pieces and cook a bit longer
