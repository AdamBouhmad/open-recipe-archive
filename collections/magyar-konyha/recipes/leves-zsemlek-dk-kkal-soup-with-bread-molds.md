## Ingredients

- 2 rolls
- 4 deciliters milk
- 8 dekagrams butter
- 1 egg
- 4 egg yolks
- salt
- 3 egg whites
- filling or ground meat

## Directions

1. Remove crusts from 2 rolls, cut into slices
2. Pour 4 deciliters of milk over them and cook to a fine paste with continuous stirring
3. Remove from heat and let cool completely after mixing in 8 dekagrams butter
4. Beat in 1 whole egg and 4 egg yolks one after another
5. Season slightly with salt
6. Finally gently fold in 3 stiffly beaten egg whites
7. Butter the molds, put a spoonful of paste in each, add a teaspoon of filling or ground meat on top
8. Cover with another spoonful of paste and bake nicely
9. Note: the bread paste must not be thin, otherwise the filling will sink to the bottom; generally the molds must be filled quickly and immediately placed in the water bath
