## Ingredients

- rétes-tészta (strudel dough)
- plum preserve
- fat for frying
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Stretch the ordinarily prepared thin strudel dough and fold it into four layers
2. Grease each layer slightly with fat
3. On the edge of the folded dough lying on the cloth, spread plum preserve very thinly and about half a finger's breadth wide using a knife
4. Fold the plum preserve-lined band onto the empty dough narrowly and cut with a knife so that you create one finger-width wide, plum preserve-filled dough strips from the entire large strudel dough
5. Once finished, cut the long plum preserve-filled dough strips into one span-length pieces
6. Form them into S-shaped, small spiral strudels
7. Put one spoonful of fat into a flat pan and place over high heat
8. When the fat is hot, fill the pan with spiral strudels
9. Watch carefully during baking as this fine dry dough browns very quickly
10. Transfer to a flat plate and serve without sugar; anyone who wishes can sprinkle sugar on their own
