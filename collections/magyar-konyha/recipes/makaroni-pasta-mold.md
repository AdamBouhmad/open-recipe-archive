## Ingredients

- 500 grams pasta
- white broth
- 25 dekagrams butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 3 tablespoons sweet milk
- 4 whole eggs
- ham, finely chopped
- finely grated cheese
- butter for greasing the mold

## Directions

1. Cook the pasta in white broth.
2. Melt 25 dekagrams of butter, add 1 tablespoon flour and let it simmer slightly.
3. Increase with 3 tablespoons of sweet milk and remove from heat.
4. When cooled, beat in 4 whole eggs.
5. Add the cooked pasta and shake well.
6. Grease a mold with butter and pour in half the pasta.
7. Sprinkle a layer of finely chopped ham and finely grated cheese.
8. Add another layer of pasta and repeat three times until the mold is full.
9. Boil the mold in hot water for 1.5 hours, then serve.
10. Optional sauce: Stick cloves into an onion head and fry in a little fat, add 2 tablespoons flour, fill with good meat broth, strain through a sieve, and add sautéed button mushrooms.
