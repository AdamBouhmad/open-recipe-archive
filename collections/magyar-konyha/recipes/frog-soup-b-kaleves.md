## Ingredients

- a piece of butter
- finely chopped green parsley
- frogs
- flour
- pea broth
- salt
- nutmeg flower
- sour cream
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Melt butter and add finely chopped green parsley, braising slightly
2. Clean the frogs thoroughly and braise slightly in butter
3. Sprinkle a tablespoon or two of flour over them and braise slightly
4. Lighten with pea broth and add a little salt and nutmeg flower
5. When the soup is cooked and the frogs are tender, add two tablespoons of sour cream
6. Serve on fried bread
