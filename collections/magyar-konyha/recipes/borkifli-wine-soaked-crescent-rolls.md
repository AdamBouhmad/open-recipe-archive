## Ingredients

- six crumbly crescent rolls
- preserved cherries or raspberries without seeds
- sugar
- four deciliters good red wine

## Directions

1. Grate the crust of six crumbly crescent rolls and cut them in half lengthwise
2. Hollow out the interior and fill with preserved cherries or raspberries without seeds
3. Make a small stopper from the removed crumb
4. Dissolve sugar in four deciliters of good red wine in a deep dish to make it sweet
5. Pour the wine mixture over the crescent rolls and soften them in it
6. Turn them several times so they absorb the liquid evenly on all sides, but do not let them become too soft
7. Roll them in fine breadcrumbs and fry them quickly in fat until golden
8. Sprinkle with sugar and vanilla
