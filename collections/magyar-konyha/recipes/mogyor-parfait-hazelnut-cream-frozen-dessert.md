## Ingredients

- 21 dekagrams hazelnut kernels
- milk
- 1.4 liters hot sweet cream
- 16 egg yolks
- 42 dekagrams powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Finely grind 21 dekagrams hazelnut kernels in milk, then pour into a pot.
2. Pour in 1.4 liters hot sweet cream and stir until boiling, then allow to cool.
3. Thoroughly blend 16 egg yolks with 42 dekagrams powdered sugar.
4. Press the cream through a clean kitchen cloth and pour into the eggs.
5. Stir over gentle charcoal fire with a whisk until nearly boiling, but it must not boil.
6. After straining, continue stirring until cooled, then pour into the freezer.
