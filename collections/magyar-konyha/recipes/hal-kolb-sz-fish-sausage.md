## Ingredients

- one kilogram pike from the middle
- salt
- water
- equal amount of carp
- flour
- milk
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- parsley
- egg yolks
- pepper
- cream

## Directions

1. Cook one kilogram of pike from the middle in salted water
2. Take an equal piece of carp, salt it, coat in flour, and fry
3. Remove bones from both fish and chop fine with breadcrumbs soaked in milk and wrung out
4. Meanwhile, braise finely chopped parsley in butter for a few minutes
5. Beat four egg yolks
6. Pound everything together in a large mortar until fine like dough
7. Add salt and pepper to taste
8. Place in a bowl and mix in three egg yolks and two spoons of cream
9. Fill a syringe and make sausages an ell long
10. Before cooking, place in warm water for several minutes, then fry nicely in butter
11. Serve hot
12. Note: sheep casings should be soaked and rinsed several times in warm water before filling
