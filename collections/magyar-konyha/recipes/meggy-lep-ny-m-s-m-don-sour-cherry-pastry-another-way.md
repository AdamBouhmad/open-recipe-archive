## Ingredients

- 70 grams butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 140 grams finely ground almonds
- 140 grams finely ground sugar
- 4 egg whites
- 145 grams fine buttered breadcrumbs
- 500 grams ripe fresh sour cherries
- butter for greasing
- fine breadcrumbs for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Beat 70 grams butter until fluffy
2. Add 8 egg yolks one by one, mixing well after each addition
3. Fold in 140 grams finely ground almonds and 140 grams finely ground sugar
4. Mix in 4 egg whites beaten stiff
5. Stir the whole mixture together for one hour
6. Add 145 grams fine buttered breadcrumbs
7. Finally fold in 500 grams ripe fresh sour cherries
8. Butter the pastry sheets and sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs
9. Fill with the prepared mixture
10. Bake for half an hour
