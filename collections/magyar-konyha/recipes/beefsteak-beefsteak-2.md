## Ingredients

- kidney (1 kilo) from the thick part
- pepper
- parsley (finely chopped, green)
- fat (1 wooden spoon)
- tarhonyá
- sour cabbage
- rice dish
- potatoes
- eggs
- horseradish
- beet juice
- parsley leaves
- gherkins

## Directions

1. Take one kilo of kidney from the thick part and clean it of membranes and sinews so it gets a neat round form but remains rounded
2. Sprinkle it with pepper and finely chopped green parsley
3. Prepare it in the morning this way and leave it in the cellar covered until evening
4. When needed, place a wooden spoon of fat in a flat iron pan over heat
5. In the smoking, hot fat, sear it quickly so it is pink outside but remains rare inside (or cook longer if preferred)
6. Arrange the seared meat lengthwise on a long dish and surround it alternating spoonfuls of: tarhonyá, sour cabbage, rice dish, potatoes and possibly more similar items
7. Add a talkerli-baked egg to each slice of meat, occasionally forming roses from scraped horseradish slices
8. Place a small pinch of white horseradish ribbon and a small pinch dipped in beet juice
9. Arrange parsley leaves on both sides as stems and place small gherkins neatly between
