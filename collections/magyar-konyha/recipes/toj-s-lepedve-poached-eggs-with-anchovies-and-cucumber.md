## Ingredients

- butter
- 6 tablespoons sour cream
- 12 eggs
- 6 well-soaked sardelles (anchovies), cleaned and deboned
- sour cucumber, cut into long slices

## Directions

1. Butter a dish and add 6 tablespoons sour cream to it
2. Break 12 eggs into the dish, taking care to keep them whole
3. Clean the sardelles (anchovies) and remove their bones
4. Cut them into oblong pieces and place them among the eggs
5. Cut sour cucumber into long slices and arrange them around the eggs so they form a green wreath
6. Place enough coals on top so that the eggs cook through but do not harden
