## Ingredients

- forty-two dekagrams flour
- twenty-eight dekagrams butter
- twenty-eight dekagrams softened almonds
- twenty-eight dekagrams finely ground sugar
- one lemon, finely chopped zest
- cooked cherries or currants for filling
- egg white
- coarse sugar

## Directions

1. Work the flour well with the butter using a rolling pin
2. Mix together the softened almonds, finely ground sugar, and finely chopped lemon zest
3. Prepare the pastry so it holds together
4. Roll to a finger-width thickness
5. Place on a pastry leaf
6. Fill with cooked cherries or currants
7. Make a lattice pattern on top
8. Surround with a band
9. Brush with egg white
10. Dust with coarse sugar
11. Bake at gentle heat
