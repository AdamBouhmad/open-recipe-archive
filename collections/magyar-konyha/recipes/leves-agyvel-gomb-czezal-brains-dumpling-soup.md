## Ingredients

- veal brains
- salt
- water
- parsley
- pepper
- butter
- bread roll
- milk
- five dekagrams butter
- two egg yolks
- breadcrumbs
- beef broth
- brown or white broth

## Directions

1. Wash the veal brains thoroughly and cook in salted water
2. Remove the membrane and chop finely with parsley
3. Add salt and a little crushed pepper
4. Sauté in butter
5. Soak half a bread roll in milk and crush in a mortar
6. Beat five dekagrams of butter until frothy
7. Mix the brains into the butter
8. Add two whole egg yolks
9. Add a little breadcrumb
10. Form small dumplings and cook in good beef broth
11. Place in soup bowl and pour brown or white broth over them
