## Ingredients

- 8 deciiliters cream
- semolina
- 7 dekagrams butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 10 dekagrams powdered sugar
- powdered vanilla
- 6 egg whites beaten stiff
- candied lemon peel
- preserved currants

## Directions

1. Cook semolina in 8 deciiliters of cream until it becomes a thick paste
2. Cool completely
3. Finely mix 7 dekagrams of butter with 8 egg yolks
4. Add the semolina paste and stir for a while longer
5. Mix in 10 dekagrams of powdered sugar with a small amount of powdered vanilla
6. Gently fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
7. Butter a pot well and place finely cut candied lemon peel on the bottom, forming a star shape
8. Put part of the mixture in, then preserved currants, then the rest of the mixture
9. Cook in a steam bath, carefully turn onto a plate and serve
