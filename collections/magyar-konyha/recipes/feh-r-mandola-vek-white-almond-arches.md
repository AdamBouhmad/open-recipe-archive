## Ingredients

- 14 dekagramm sugar
- 2 tablespoons water
- 14 dekagramm ground almonds
- 1 lemon, zest finely chopped and juice
- 1 egg white, stiffly beaten

## Directions

1. Cook 14 dekagramm sugar with 2 tablespoons water until it threads
2. Add 14 dekagramm ground almonds, the finely chopped zest and juice of 1 lemon, and 1 stiffly beaten egg white
3. Dry the dough over charcoal heat until it can be bent
4. Remove from heat and let cool
5. Spread onto wafers of desired size
6. Bend the arches over a round baking sheet and dry them
