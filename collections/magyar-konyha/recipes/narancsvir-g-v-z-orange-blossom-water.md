## Ingredients

- 3 kilograms fresh orange blossoms, without sepals and stems
- 2 liters alcohol
- 4 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Digest 3 kilograms fresh orange blossoms with 2 liters alcohol and 4 deciliters water
