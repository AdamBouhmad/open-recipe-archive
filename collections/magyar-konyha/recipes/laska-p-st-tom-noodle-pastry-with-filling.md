## Ingredients

- laska dough
- salted water
- butter
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- fat
- chopped filling or forcemeat
- green parsley

## Directions

1. Make fine chopped noodles from laska dough.
2. Cook them well in salted water and drain.
3. While still warm, mix in butter the size of a walnut.
4. Press the laska firmly into a large, wide pan spread with butter to a depth of two finger-widths and let it cool.
5. Cut out rounds the size of a forint using a pastry cutter.
6. Dip in beaten, salted egg, roll in breadcrumbs, and fry in fat.
7. Using a small cutter, make a hole in the center down to the bottom to create a crown shape.
8. Fill the opening with forcemeat or filling and serve hot, garnished with green parsley.
