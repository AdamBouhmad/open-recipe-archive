## Ingredients

- one and a half litres fresh cornmeal
- hot water
- a handful of wheat flour
- fat

## Directions

1. Pour one and a half litres of fresh cornmeal into a bowl and scald with hot water, let it become quite stiff, and set it aside for two hours in a warm place, the so-called nest
2. Then mix in a handful of wheat flour and let it cool for a couple of hours
3. Now spread the dough thinly (about pinky-finger thickness) onto a sheet thickly greased with fat, then pour fat generously over the top
4. Bake máié in very hot heat and cut into cubes
