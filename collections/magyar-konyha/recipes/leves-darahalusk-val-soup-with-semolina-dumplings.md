## Ingredients

- 10 dekagrams butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 5 deciliters (a good measure) semolina
- Salt

## Directions

1. Rub 10 dekagrams of butter well, mix 4 egg yolks into it, and add 5 deciliters of semolina and a little salt.
2. When everything is well combined, throw the dumplings into the beef broth with a spoon.
