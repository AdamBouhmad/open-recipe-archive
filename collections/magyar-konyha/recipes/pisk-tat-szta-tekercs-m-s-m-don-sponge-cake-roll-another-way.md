## Ingredients

- twenty-eight dekagrams sugar
- orange zest
- fifteen egg yolks
- eighteen dekagrams flour
- seven egg whites
- fourteen dekagrams sugar
- butter
- fifteen to twenty medium apples
- eighteen dekagrams broken sugar
- three spoonfuls preserved peach syrup
- twenty-one dekagrams walnuts
- half orange peel
- half lemon peel
- cinnamon
- poppy seed

## Directions

1. Rub twenty-eight dekagrams of sugar on orange, break it, sift it, beat fifteen egg yolks into it, and stir for an hour
2. Then lightly mix in eighteen dekagrams of flour, finally add the stiff foam of seven egg whites
3. Since this amount is too much for one roll, prepare it from fourteen dekagrams of sugar as follows: Spread the dough thinly onto a long sheet buttered with butter and bake it at gentle heat until roll-colored
4. Peel fifteen to twenty medium apples beforehand, remove their cores, cut them roughly into pieces, and cook into marmalade with eighteen dekagrams of broken sugar with frequent stirring
5. Then finely mix in three spoonfuls of preserved peach syrup, let it cool, coat the roll with it to a knife-width thickness, place it in the oven again until it can be rolled together, and treat it as before
6. This roll can also be filled with walnuts: cut twenty-one dekagrams of beautiful walnuts finely, as well as the peel of half an orange and half a lemon, stir this with broken cinnamon and twenty-one dekagrams of sugar dissolved in a coffee cup of water, and cook only until the sugar has penetrated everything well and the filling can be spread well
7. Filled with poppy seed it is also very good and beautiful, as it resembles a yellowish-black ribbon; the poppy seed, once broken, is prepared in the same way as the walnuts
