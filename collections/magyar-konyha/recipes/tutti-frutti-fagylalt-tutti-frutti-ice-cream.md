## Ingredients

- 1 orange and 1 lemon, zest rubbed on sugar
- 2 oranges and 2 lemons, juice pressed
- 500 grams sugar
- 4 deciliters water
- preserved walnuts
- green fruit (green almonds, ringlots, quince paste, etc.)
- equal parts of each fruit, cut into small cubes

## Directions

1. Rub the zest of 1 orange and 1 lemon on sugar, not too deeply
2. Press the juice of 2 oranges and 2 lemons
3. Cook 500 grams sugar in 4 deciliters water until the sugar is well dissolved
4. Pour it into the juice, dissolve the rubbed sugar similarly in the juice, and mix well
5. Add preserved walnuts and various types of green fruit, such as green almonds, ringlots, quince paste, etc., using equal parts of each, cut into small cubes
6. Proceed as with other ice creams
7. When it begins to thicken, add the chopped fruit and its juice, and work it well into the ice cream; the more fruit you add, the better
