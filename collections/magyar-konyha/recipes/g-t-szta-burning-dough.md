## Ingredients

- 4 deciliters hot milk
- 4 deciliters fine flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the flour into the hot milk with continuous stirring so it does not become lumpy
2. Add a little salt, smooth it out, and when the dough separates from the spoon, set it aside and fry it
3. Once the dough has cooled, pound it fine in a clean mortar with 6 whole eggs
4. Press it from a pastry bag into hot fat and fry until a beautiful light brown color
