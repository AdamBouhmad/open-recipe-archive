## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters good heavy cream
- 5 tablespoons apricot or raspberry marmalade
- 14 dekagrams finely broken sugar
- A few drops of almond liqueur (if using raspberry)
- 1.5 dekagrams dissolved and cooled isinglass

## Directions

1. Set seven deciliters of good heavy cream on ice in a brass pan and whip it with a whisk until the entire mass becomes stiff foam
2. In a pan, beat five tablespoons of apricot or raspberry marmalade with fourteen dekagrams of finely broken sugar until frothy
3. If using raspberry, add a few drops of almond liqueur to give it a pale pink color
4. Gently and slowly mix the foam with this mixture
5. Add the dissolved and cooled isinglass
6. Bury the mold in ice, pour in the mixture, and freeze on ice
7. For serving, top the foam with Spanish fritters or close with a wreath made from and dried puff pastry
