## Ingredients

- One capon
- Twenty-five snails
- Seven dekagrams anchovies
- One head red onion
- Fresh parsley, chopped finely
- Juice of one lemon
- Fourteen dekagrams butter, beaten until fluffy

## Directions

1. Cook the snails
2. Extract the snail meat from the shells
3. Mix snail meat with anchovies, red onion, parsley, lemon juice and fluffy butter, blending well
4. Stuff the capon with this mixture
5. Sew the capon closed so the stuffing does not fall out
6. Roast beautifully
7. Baste with sour cream and butter while roasting
