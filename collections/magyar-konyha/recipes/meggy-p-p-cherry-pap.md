## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 3 whole eggs
- finely ground vanilla
- 28 dekagrams ground sugar
- 28 dekagrams fine flour
- fresh cherries

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and beat in the egg yolks and whole eggs one by one
2. Add finely ground vanilla and ground sugar
3. Gently fold in the flour
4. Pour half the batter into a well-buttered mould
5. Arrange a row of fresh cherries on top
6. Sprinkle the cherries with sugar
7. Cover with the remaining batter
8. Bake for one hour
