## Ingredients

- two rolls, grated peel
- milk
- ten dekagrams of soaked almonds
- fourteen dekagrams of raisins and small dried grapes
- seven dekagrams of butter
- two whole eggs and four egg yolks
- three egg whites
- citron or orange peel
- sugar
- white paper

## Directions

1. Soak the grated peel of two rolls in milk
2. Crush ten dekagrams of soaked almonds
3. Clean fourteen dekagrams of raisins and small dried grapes
4. Beat seven dekagrams of butter until fluffy
5. Squeeze the rolls well, chop finely and add to the butter
6. Add two whole eggs and four egg yolks one after another and stir for a quarter hour
7. Fold in the stiff whites of three eggs
8. Add the almonds, raisins and small grapes
9. Add a piece of sugar rubbed with citron or orange peel and mix well
10. Line a pot with butter, cut a white paper to fit the bottom and butter it
11. Arrange attractively with raisins and soaked almonds on the bottom
12. Add the pudding mixture and cook in steam for one hour
13. Turn the pudding onto a plate and carefully remove the paper
14. Pour the prepared wine sauce around it
15. For the sauce: crush sugar rubbed with citron, put in a pot with ten egg yolks, mix with twenty tablespoons of good white wine and place over glowing coals
16. Stir the sauce continuously until it thickens and rises, then remove quickly from the fire and pour onto the plate
