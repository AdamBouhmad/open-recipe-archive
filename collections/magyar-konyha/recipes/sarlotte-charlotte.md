## Ingredients

- 12-15 peeled apples
- 4-5 tablespoons apricot syrup
- 10 dekagramm sugar
- coffee cup of water
- butter
- knife-thickness slices of sweet bread

## Directions

1. Cut peeled apples into thin slices
2. Place apricot syrup, sugar, and water in a pot
3. Set over moderate heat and stew the apples frequently shaking, until soft without falling apart and the syrup is well mixed with the apples
4. Line a well-buttered mold with knife-thickness slices of sweet bread
5. Layer one row of apples, then one row of bread slices drizzled with melted butter
6. Continue alternating until the mold is full
7. Cover the top with bread, pour melted butter over it
8. Bake until the top bread is golden and crispy
9. Turn out and sprinkle with sugar
