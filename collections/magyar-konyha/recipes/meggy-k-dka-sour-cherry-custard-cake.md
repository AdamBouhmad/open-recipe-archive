## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams sifted sugar
- 7 dekagrams soaked and finely ground almond
- 4 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 1 lemon, finely chopped zest
- 7 dekagrams breadcrumb (kifli)
- butter
- sour cherries

## Directions

1. Stir together for one hour: 14 dekagrams of sifted sugar, 7 dekagrams of soaked and finely ground almond, 4 whole eggs, 4 egg yolks, and 4 egg whites beaten stiffly
2. Finally add the finely chopped zest of 1 lemon and 7 dekagrams of breadcrumb (kifli)
3. Mix this well with the rest
4. Fill a buttered mold (kádka) with this mixture
5. Place 5 sour cherries in each one
6. Bake at gentle heat
