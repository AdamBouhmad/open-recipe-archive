## Ingredients

- large lamprey loin
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- butter
- 2 deciliters sour cream
- lemon juice and peel
- capers
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Clean the large loin, cut lengthwise but not all the way through so it remains whole
2. Season with salt mixed with ground pepper, coat with melted butter and roast on a gridiron
3. When cooked through, place on a plate, add some butter, pour 2 deciliters sour cream over it
4. Add lemon juice, finely chopped lemon peel, a little capers, a handful of breadcrumbs and some butter, top with coals, bring to a boil and serve
