## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram sugar
- 3 decilitres water
- 1/2 kilogram Spanish cherries
- 1 coffee cup good white vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook 1/2 kilogram sugar with 3 decilitres water, clean it well and cook until it forms heavy drops.
2. Take 1/2 kilogram Spanish cherries for 1/2 kilogram sugar, trim their stems very finely, add them to the sugar and cook for approximately fifteen minutes, being careful that they remain whole.
3. Remove the cherries with a skimmer and continue cooking the syrup, pour in 1 coffee cup of good white vinegar and cook until the syrup becomes thick.
4. When the cherries have cooled, place them in a glass jar, pour the cold syrup over them and leave it open for twenty-four hours, then seal it like other preserves.
