## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- a pinch of salt
- fine flour for light dough

## Directions

1. Place 2 whole eggs, 2 egg yolks, 2 tablespoons sugar, 2 tablespoons sour cream, and a pinch of salt in a bowl and mix well
2. On a board prepare a light dough from fine flour
3. Roll out to a thin sheet very carefully so the dough does not tear
4. Fold in half and cut out with a rose-shaped cutter or a medium-sized fritter cutter if the rose cutter is not available
