## Ingredients

- one liter good white wine
- three deciliters water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- six egg yolks
- three tablespoons sweet sour cream
- fine bread pieces
- fine sugar
- caramelized sugar

## Directions

1. Mix one liter of good white wine with three deciliters of water so it is not too strong
2. Add sugar and cinnamon
3. Cook for half an hour
4. Before serving, remove the spices
5. Beat six egg yolks in a pot and add three full tablespoons of sweet sour cream
6. Beat well and pour the wine in slowly and gradually
7. Place in a soup bowl and add finely cut, generously sprinkled with fine sugar, and caramelized (the melted sugar is like glass) bread pieces with a hot spatula
8. Quickly bring the soup to the table so the bread does not soften
