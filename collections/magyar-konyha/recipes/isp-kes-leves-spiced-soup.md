## Ingredients

- fresh pork
- calves' feet or pork feet
- salt
- red onion
- black pepper
- paprika
- sugar
- vinegar
- saffron

## Directions

1. Cut the pork and feet into pieces and put to cook, salting them
2. When the feet are tender, add red onion cut into cubes, using one large onion per foot
3. Cook together until the onion is soft
4. Make a nice pink thin roux
5. Add black pepper, a little paprika, and a tablespoon of powdered sugar to the roux
6. Thin the roux with the feet liquid and add vinegar
7. Add good saffron so the broth is a nice yellow color
