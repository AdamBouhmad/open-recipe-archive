## Ingredients

- 70 grams butter
- 400 milliliters cream with 4 egg yolks well mixed
- 20 grams fresh cheese yeast
- 40 grams sugar
- a little salt
- flour as needed
- 4 egg whites beaten stiffly

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until fluffy
2. Add the cream mixed with egg yolks, fresh cheese yeast, sugar, and a little salt
3. Add flour until the dough is light, then fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
4. The dough should be approximately twice as thick as morzsóka dough
5. Place in a warm location and let it rise until it doubles in volume
6. Bake in the appropriate pan, top with preserves, sprinkle with sugar, and serve warm
