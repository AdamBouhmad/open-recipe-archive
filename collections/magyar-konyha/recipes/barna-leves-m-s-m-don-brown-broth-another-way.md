## Ingredients

- meat and bones prepared in the regular manner with less vegetables
- four slices of bacon
- fatty bone
- hazelnut-sized piece of sugar
- piece of veal liver or spleen
- 12 dekagrams carrot, sliced into rings
- one parsley root
- celery
- kohlrabi
- one small head of red onion
- peppercorns
- cloves

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat and bones in the regular manner with less vegetables and let cook for one hour
2. In a pot, sauté the fatty bone with four slices of bacon until browned
3. Add a hazelnut-sized piece of sugar and a piece of veal liver or spleen
4. Add 12 dekagrams of carrot sliced into rings, parsley root, celery, kohlrabi, one small head of red onion, peppercorns, and cloves
5. Leave uncovered on the fire and let braise until the bacon turns brown, but be careful not to burn it
6. From time to time pour a spoonful of broth over the bones and vegetables
7. Let simmer until the vegetables are well braised, then brown with frequent stirring
8. When the meat is cooked, pour its broth over the bones and vegetables and bring to a boil together
9. Set aside to cool, then strain and remove the fat
10. Serve with meat dumplings
11. If not salted, the brown broth can be kept for 2-3 days
