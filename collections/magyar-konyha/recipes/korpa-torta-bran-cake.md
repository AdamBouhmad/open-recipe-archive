## Ingredients

- 7 dekagramm softened almonds, sliced
- 21 dekagramm almonds, cleaned and ground with skin
- 28 dekagramm ground sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 egg white, stiffly beaten
- 2 more egg yolks
- 1 more egg white, stiffly beaten
- 2 tablets grated chocolate
- 7 dekagramm citron peel, cut lengthwise
- butter for greasing
- orange icing
- green fruit for decoration

## Directions

1. Slice the softened almonds, clean the almonds with a cloth and grind with skin
2. Put in a bowl with ground sugar
3. Add 2 egg yolks and the stiffly beaten white of 1 egg and stir for a while
4. Then mix in 2 more egg yolks and the stiffly beaten white of 1 more egg, and continue this way until foamy
5. Add 2 tablets grated chocolate, the sliced almonds, and 7 dekagramm of lengthwise-cut citron peel
6. Grease the cake sheet with the hoop together with butter, fill in the mixture and place immediately in the oven so the almonds and citron do not settle
7. Bake for three-quarters of an hour with gentle but sustained heat
8. Cover with orange icing and decorate with green fruit
