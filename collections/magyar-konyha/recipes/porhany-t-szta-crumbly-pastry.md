## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams flour
- 28 dekagrams butter
- 2 egg whites
- 1 full tablespoon wine
- sour cream as needed for moisture
- salt

## Directions

1. Into 28 dekagrams lightly salted flour, slice the same amount of butter
2. Work the butter with the flour using a rolling pin, but do not touch with the hands
3. Add two egg whites, one full tablespoon of wine, and enough sour cream to make it moist
4. Mix well with a large knife so everything is combined
5. The dough should not be stiff
6. Roll out three times as thin as possible
7. Fold in four parts on the same side
8. Use no more flour than absolutely necessary
9. Then use the dough as desired
10. Note that soft butter should be used for crumbly pastry
