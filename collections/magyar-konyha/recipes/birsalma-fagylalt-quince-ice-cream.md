## Ingredients

- 4 large ripe quinces, peeled and sliced thinly
- 42 decagrams sugar
- 7 deciliters fresh water

## Directions

1. Cook the quince slices with sugar and water in a preserving pan until the quince is completely softened
2. Cool it, then press through a clean, non-dense cloth
3. Mix with the juice of 2 lemons and cool completely
4. Pour into the ice cream mold and proceed as with other ice creams
5. For jelly made from the same mixture, use 1.5 decagrams of dissolved isinglass, strain through cloth and blotting paper as with all jellies
