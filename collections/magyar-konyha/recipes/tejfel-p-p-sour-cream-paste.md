## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons flour
- 4 deciliters heavy cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place the flour in a pot and gradually pour in the heavy cream, stirring well to avoid lumps
2. Add a little sugar and bring to a boil with constant stirring
3. When thickened, place a large pot lid inverted as a triangle underneath with a strong charcoal fire below
4. When well heated, butter one side quickly
5. Spoon some of the paste onto it and bake for one minute
6. Scrape with a knife and leave only paper-thick layer on the lid
7. Leave until it browns, then butter again
8. Cut into pieces and peel off with a knife, shaping into small wafers
9. Work quickly to prevent burning
