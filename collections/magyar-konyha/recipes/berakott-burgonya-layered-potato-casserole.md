## Ingredients

- ten to fifteen potatoes of equal size
- ten eggs
- ham, finely chopped
- sour cream, eight decilitres
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes so that they do not burst. Also cook ten eggs hard, then place in cold water and clean them. Cut the potatoes and eggs into slices. Take a casserole of about two liters capacity and layer as follows: a row of sliced potatoes salted, sprinkle finely chopped ham on top, then sliced egg and salt again, then ham again, potatoes, and so on until the dish is full. Pour eight decilitres of sour cream over the top, place in the oven and bake.
