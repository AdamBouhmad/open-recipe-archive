## Ingredients

- one and one-quarter decilitres of fine semolina
- seven decilitres of milk
- five whole eggs
- two decilitres of milk
- vanilla
- sugar
- fourteen dekagrams of butter
- salt
- raisins

## Directions

1. Four hours before use, soak one and one-quarter decilitres of fine semolina in seven decilitres of milk, then set aside
2. Beat five whole eggs with two decilitres of milk and vanilla sifted with sugar, then mix with the soaked semolina
3. Melt fourteen dekagrams of butter in a wide shallow pan, pour the mixture into it with a little salt and braise it to a beautiful golden yellow
4. You can also add fourteen dekagrams of raisins during the braising
5. Serve the porridge immediately as it loses much of its quality from prolonged warming
6. Heap it high on the plate, sprinkling each layer well with sugar
