## Ingredients

- Two goose livers
- Butter
- Salt
- Clove, crushed
- Two egg yolks
- Sour sour cream
- Lemon-sugar
- Flour
- Beaten egg
- Bread crumbs
- Butter
- Broth
- Butter
- Hot sour cream
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Clean two goose livers well and cut into beautiful slices
2. Place half on a buttered baking sheet, salt, and flavor with crushed clove
3. Mix two egg yolks with sour sour cream and lemon-sugar, pour over the liver and bake in the oven
4. Flour the other half of the liver, dip in beaten egg then bread crumbs, fry in butter, and constantly baste with broth
5. Finally, melt butter with hot sour cream and sugar for the sauce, and pour over the baked liver when this sauce is ready
