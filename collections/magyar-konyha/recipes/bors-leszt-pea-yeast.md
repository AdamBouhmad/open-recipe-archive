## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon of peas
- 4 deciliters of boiling water

## Directions

1. Crush a tablespoon of peas
2. Pour into 4 deciliters of boiling water
3. Place the mixture in a warm location for one night
4. The next morning, foam will appear on the surface, which has all the properties of good yeast
