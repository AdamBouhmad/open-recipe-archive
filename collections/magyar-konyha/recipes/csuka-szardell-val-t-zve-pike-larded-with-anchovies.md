## Ingredients

- pike
- salt
- anchovies
- butter
- sour cream
- lemon juice
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Clean the pike in the usual way, fillet it and wash it thoroughly and salt it only very lightly.
2. Clean the washed anchovies of their bones and chop them lengthwise like bacon and lard the pike with them in two rows.
3. Place a piece of butter in a pan, add three spoonfuls of sour cream and the juice of one lemon and baste the fish with this several times during cooking.
4. When cooked, place it on the serving dish, add another spoonful of sour cream and a small piece of butter to it, bring to a boil, pour it over the fish and sprinkle lengthwise chopped lemon peel over it.
