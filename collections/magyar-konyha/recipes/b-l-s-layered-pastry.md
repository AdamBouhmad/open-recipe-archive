## Ingredients

- good puff pastry (made into 24 egg-sized pieces)
- fat
- fourteen dekagrams rice
- milk
- small raisins
- large raisins
- sugar
- cinnamon
- salt

## Directions

1. Divide good puff pastry into pieces the size of 24 eggs
2. Place two buttered baking sheets side by side, which will make the dough larger
3. Roll out the first piece of dough over these two sheets and brush with fat
4. Roll out seven more sheets of dough on top, so the bottom of the dough consists of eight such layers, each brushed separately with fat
5. Prepare the filling as follows: soak fourteen dekagrams of rice in milk, strain through a sieve so all moisture drains off, place this on the dough, adding small and large raisins
6. After sprinkling this with sugar and a little broken cinnamon, place eight more sheets of dough on top, each brushed separately with fat
7. Then comes the filling again prepared in the above manner, and on top, like before, eight more sheets of remaining dough
8. Trim the dough scraps protruding from the edge of the pan with a rolling pin or knife, cut the dough itself into cubes with a pastry wheel, brush the top with fat, bake over gentle heat and serve dusted with sugar
9. Bélés can also be filled with cheese or apples
