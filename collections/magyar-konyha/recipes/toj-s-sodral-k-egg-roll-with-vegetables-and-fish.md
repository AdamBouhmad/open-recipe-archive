## Ingredients

- 10 eggs
- salt
- butter
- mushrooms, finely chopped
- green parsley
- piece of fried pike, deboned
- 15-20 asparagus spears
- 20 crayfish tails and claws
- 3 eggs for the mixture
- sour cream
- crayfish butter

## Directions

1. Beat 10 eggs in a pan and salt them
2. Melt butter in a pancake pan and add a small portion of the beaten eggs
3. Let it spread in the pan and cook like a pancake; repeat until all egg is used
4. Prepare the filling: stew finely chopped mushrooms with butter and green parsley
5. Chop the fried, deboned pike into small pieces
6. Cook asparagus in salted water and cut into oblong pieces
7. Chop 20 crayfish tails and claws into cubes
8. Make a mixture from 3 eggs and beat it finely
9. Add all of this to the stewed mushrooms
10. Add a few tablespoons sour cream and stew slightly
11. When the egg pancakes are cooked, fill them with the mixture and fold them together
12. Cut off the edges and cut in the middle and divide
13. Arrange the egg rolls neatly on a dish greased with crayfish butter, with 2-3 tablespoons sour cream poured in
14. Salt slightly
15. Pour another tablespoon of sour cream on top
16. Decorate with crayfish tails
17. Place in the oven, let it heat through for a few minutes, and serve quickly
