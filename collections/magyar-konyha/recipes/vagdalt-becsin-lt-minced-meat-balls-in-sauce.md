## Ingredients

- veal and pork together, chopped
- smoked bacon, a small amount, cut into very small cubes
- raw rice, two tablespoons
- flour, one tablespoon
- one whole egg
- half a bread roll, soaked and chopped
- ground black pepper, a small amount
- salt
- hot fat for frying
- flour for breading
- hot water
- lemon or vinegar
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Chop the veal and pork together with a knife, and a small amount of smoked bacon into very small cubes. Mix together two tablespoons of raw rice, one tablespoon of flour, one whole egg, half a bread roll soaked and chopped, a small amount of ground black pepper, and the proper amount of salt with the chopped meat. Form into dumplings the size of apples, roll them well in flour, and fry them a nice golden color in hot fat. Once fried, remove them and make a light roux from the fat, dilute it with hot water, and place the cooked dumplings in it. Once they have cooked through, season with lemon or vinegar and generously add sour cream before serving.
