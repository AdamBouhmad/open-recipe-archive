## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams flour
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 full tablespoon sour cream
- juice of half a lemon
- 28 dekagrams butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Salt 28 dekagrams of flour on the kneading board and divide into two parts
2. From one half make a light dough, stiffer than strudel dough
3. Add one egg yolk, one full tablespoon sour cream, and juice of half a lemon
4. Knead until it separates from the hand
5. Shape into a loaf and set aside
6. Into the other half of flour work 28 dekagrams of butter and stretch with the rolling pin until the butter and flour are completely united
7. Roll the dough to knife-blade width
8. Shape the butter into a long piece and place in the middle of the dough
9. Fold the dough over from all four sides
10. Beat wide with the rolling pin and stretch as thin as possible
11. Fold together again
12. Repeat this three times, using as little flour as possible when folding
13. The fourth time stretch thin and use as desired
