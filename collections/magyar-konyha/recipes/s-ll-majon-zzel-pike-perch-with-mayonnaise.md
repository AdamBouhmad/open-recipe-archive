## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg pike-perch
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- 4 cooked egg yolks
- crushed ice
- 1 cup fresh olive oil
- small lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon aspic
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the pike-perch, cut into slices two fingers wide
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, braise in melted butter and place in a cold location
3. Before serving, arrange the fish slices in a wreath shape on a plate
4. For the mayonnaise: press 4 cooked egg yolks through a sieve, mix on crushed ice with 1 cup fresh olive oil, a little lemon juice, 1 tablespoon aspic and a little vinegar until smooth
5. Coat the fish piece with this mayonnaise, and decorate the plate rim with either finely chopped aspic or small pastry shapes
