## Ingredients

- 3 medium-sized potatoes
- 1 liter broth
- 1/2 roll
- Hot fat
- 3 eggs
- Broth

## Directions

1. Cook 3 medium-sized potatoes in 1 liter of broth and after cooling and peeling, grate them.
2. Cut half a roll into larger slices and fry them until crispy in hot fat, but not brown.
3. Cook 3 eggs hard, peel them, remove the yolks, and pound the remainder finely in a mortar.
4. Place in a pot and thin with broth, cooking for one hour.
5. Pass the whole mixture through a sieve and arrange on fried potato dumplings and marrow cubes the size of forints.
