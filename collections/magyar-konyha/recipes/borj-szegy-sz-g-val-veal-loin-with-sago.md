## Ingredients

- veal loin
- red onion sliced
- carrot
- bacon
- flour
- stock
- vinegar
- lemon peel
- sour cream
- sago
- butter pastry

## Directions

1. Remove the ribs from the loin and blanch
2. Place sliced red onion, carrot, and bacon in a pot and place the loin on top
3. Braise slowly until nicely browned
4. Remove and sprinkle two spoonfuls of flour on it and brown
5. Thin with stock and add a few spoonfuls of vinegar
6. Strain through a sieve
7. Return the loin to the pot, add finely chopped long lemon peel, three spoonfuls of good sour cream, and 3-4 dekagrams of sago that has been cleaned and washed
8. Braise until the sago is tender
9. When serving, place in the center of the dish, pour the sauce over it, and prepare a wreath of butter pastry
