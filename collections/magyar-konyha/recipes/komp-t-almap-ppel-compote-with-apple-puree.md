## Ingredients

- Eight to nine good quality apples
- Eighteen dekagrams sugar
- Stewed Spanish cherries with syrup and sufficient sugar
- Stewed apricots
- Ringlot plums

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, remove their cores, and cut them into rough pieces
2. Cook them in a pot with eighteen dekagrams of sugar, stirring frequently, until they form a thick puree, then cool
3. Spread the puree in the middle of a compote cup in the shape of a sugar loaf using a wide knife
4. Surround it with beautifully stewed Spanish cherries whose syrup has been simmered with sufficient sugar
5. Decorate the puree neatly with stewed apricots and ringlot plums, placing the most beautiful ones on top
