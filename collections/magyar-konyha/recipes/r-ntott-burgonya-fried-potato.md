## Ingredients

- leftover veal roast or cooked veal
- butter
- flour
- broth
- sour cream
- nutmeg
- mushrooms
- asparagus or artichoke
- medium potatoes
- eggs
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of leftover veal roast or cooked veal into small cubes.
2. Melt a piece of butter the size of half an egg and add half a spoonful of flour.
3. Brown it slightly, then mix with one cup of good broth and a spoonful of sour cream seasoned with nutmeg.
4. Add the chopped meat, stewed mushrooms, and asparagus or artichoke cut into cubes.
5. Braise everything together until the sauce is reduced.
6. Cook medium-sized potatoes until soft, peel them, let them cool, and cut off the top and bottom so they stand on the plate.
7. Hollow them out slightly, beat two or three eggs depending on the number of potatoes, dip the potatoes in the eggs, then in breadcrumbs mixed with an equal amount of flour.
8. Fry in fat and quickly fill with the prepared filling while still hot.
