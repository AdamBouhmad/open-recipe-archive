## Ingredients

- twenty-eight dekagrams of butter
- forty-two dekagrams of flour
- fourteen dekagrams of crushed sugar
- three egg yolks
- sweet sour cream
- preserved apricots
- cinnamon sugar

## Directions

1. On a board, work together twenty-eight dekagrams of butter, forty-two dekagrams of flour, and fourteen dekagrams of crushed sugar well with a rolling pin
2. Add three egg yolks and enough sweet sour cream so the dough achieves proper firmness
3. Work this finely with a wide knife, not by hand
4. Fold together and roll out three times as with butter dough
5. At the third rolling, roll half the dough slightly thicker than noodle dough and brush with preserved apricot
6. Roll out the other half of the dough in the same way and place it on top
7. Cut into rectangular sheets four fingers wide and tie them to a cinnamon doughnut tube with string
8. Fry in fat and sprinkle thickly with sugar and cinnamon
9. Note that the sheets should not be filled completely to the edges with preserve, so it does not run out during frying; it is better to first cut the sheets and then fill each one separately, brush the outer part with egg white, place the other sheet on top and press slightly along the edge
