## Ingredients

- 7 orange shells
- half the orange aspic from recipe 812
- one-third the almond aspic from recipe 810
- cream aspic

## Directions

1. Carefully hollow out 7 oranges and place in clean cold water.
2. Prepare half the orange aspic and one-third the almond aspic from recipes 812 and 810; these two aspics should hold better with isinglass than before.
3. Remove orange shells from water and dry well with clean cloth.
4. Place on a sieve set over broken ice.
5. Into each shell, pour 1 spoonful of cream aspic; once frozen, pour 1.5 spoonfuls orange aspic over it, then 2 spoonfuls almond aspic, continuing this way to the middle.
6. Then continue layering until completely filled, ensuring layers are equal in height and always well frozen.
7. Before serving, divide each orange into 6 parts with a sharp, thin knife and arrange neatly on a plate.
