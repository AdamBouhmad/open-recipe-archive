## Ingredients

- quail
- salt
- 7 decilitres wine
- 7 decilitres vinegar
- cloves
- brine

## Directions

1. Pluck the quail well and wash thoroughly
2. Salt and let sit in salt for a time
3. Pour 7 decilitres wine and 7 decilitres vinegar over it
4. Add cloves
5. Place in brine overnight
6. Roast slowly
7. A light sauce can be made from the brine
