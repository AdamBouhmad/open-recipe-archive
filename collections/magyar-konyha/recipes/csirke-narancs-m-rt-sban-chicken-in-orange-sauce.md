## Ingredients

- Three small meaty chickens
- Finely chopped bacon
- Butter
- Flour
- Brown broth
- Orange zest
- Lemon zest
- Sugar
- Orange juice
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Blanch and prepare three small meaty chickens.
2. Lard with finely chopped bacon and roast until they turn a beautiful golden color.
3. Prepare a light roux with butter and flour.
4. Dilute with brown broth.
5. Add a little orange and lemon zest, a piece of sugar, and the juice of one orange and half a lemon.
6. Cook well.
7. Remove all fat from the sauce.
8. Place the chickens in it and simmer for a few more minutes.
