## Ingredients

- 12 pippin apples
- 56 decagrams sugar
- 3 lemons, juice
- 6 decagrams isinglass
- 1 bottle white wine
- 1 lemon, zest

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cook them with 14 decagrams sugar, one lemon zest, and a little wine or water, like compote, but so the apples do not fall apart
2. From the remaining sugar, lemon juice, lukewarm isinglass, and white wine, prepare a gelée
3. Layer this with the apples into a mold according to the method described in recipe 901
4. Preserved and well-drained fruit can be placed between layers
