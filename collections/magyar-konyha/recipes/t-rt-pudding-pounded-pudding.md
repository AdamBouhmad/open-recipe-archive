## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams butter
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- 14 dekagrams almonds
- 1 lemon zest
- 1 roll crust
- milk
- 8 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- wine sauce

## Directions

1. Finely pound 14 dekagrams butter, the same amount of sugar and the same amount of almonds together
2. Finely chop the zest of 1 lemon
3. Grate the crust of 1 roll, soak in milk and squeeze out well
4. Pound everything finely in a mortar
5. Put in a bowl and add 8 egg yolks one after another
6. Stir for half an hour
7. Add the stiff whites of 4 eggs
8. Pour into a buttered basin, steam and serve with wine sauce
