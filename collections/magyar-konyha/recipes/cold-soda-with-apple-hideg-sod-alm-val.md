## Ingredients

- small peeled apples with centers removed
- water
- sugar
- lemon juice
- soda (as prepared in recipe 390)
- preserved currants
- Spanish fritters for garnish

## Directions

1. Cook small peeled apples with centers removed in water, sugar, and a little lemon juice until soft but not falling apart.
2. Place in deep bowls and fill with preserved currants.
3. Pour soda (prepared as in recipe 390) over the apples.
4. Place in a cold location.
5. Before serving, garnish decoratively with Spanish fritters.
