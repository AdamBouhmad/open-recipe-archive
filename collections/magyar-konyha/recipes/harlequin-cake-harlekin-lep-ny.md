## Ingredients

- wafer batter
- puff dough
- 21 dekagrams soaked almonds
- sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons candied orange peel
- 2 bars finely grated chocolate
- 1.5 dekagrams green almonds
- quince slices in syrup
- 4 or 5 green preserved walnuts
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Using the waffle iron, bake 10-12 round wafers
2. Prepare puff dough and roll it as fine as noodle dough, line a wide flat baking pan with it smoothly
3. Finely crush 21 dekagrams of soaked almonds, moistening with water during crushing
4. Crush the same amount of sugar finely and sift it, then place both in a bowl, add 8 egg yolks and stir until white and fluffy
5. Divide into three parts: to the first part add 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar rubbed with orange, to the second part add 2 bars of finely grated chocolate, to the third part add 1.5 dekagrams of green almonds and the same amount of quince slices preserved in syrup and 4 or 5 green preserved walnuts, all cut into small cubes, and mix this with the almond mixture
6. Divide the stiff meringue from 6 egg whites into three parts and gently fold into each mixture
7. Place the portion with the orange sugar into the lined pan, cover with a wafer sheet, place the second mixture on top and cover again with a wafer sheet, finally place the fruited mixture in the oven and bake in gentle but sustained heat
8. When baked, turn out, cool slightly and serve dusted with sugar
