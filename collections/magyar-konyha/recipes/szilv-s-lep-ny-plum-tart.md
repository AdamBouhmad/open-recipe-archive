## Ingredients

- fine yeast dough
- ripe blanched plums
- sugar
- chopped almonds
- cinnamon
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Roll out fine yeast dough to small finger width and place on a flat baking sheet
2. Arrange ripe, blanched and halved plums densely side by side
3. Sprinkle with broken sugar, finely chopped almonds, cinnamon and lemon zest
4. Let rise and bake
5. When done, cut into pieces like the previous recipe and arrange on a platter with sugar sprinkled on top
