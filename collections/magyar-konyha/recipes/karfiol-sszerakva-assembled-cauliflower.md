## Ingredients

- cauliflower, cleaned and chopped
- young chicken
- rice
- hard-boiled eggs, sliced
- salted water
- fat or butter
- breadcrumbs
- black pepper, ground
- fresh parsley, chopped
- paprika
- green onion
- sweet sour cream
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil the cauliflower and chicken separately in salted water
2. Cook the rice until tender
3. Grease a baking dish with fat or butter and coat with breadcrumbs
4. Layer the ingredients: cauliflower, rice with black pepper and parsley, chicken pieces, rice, egg slices, paprika and green onion, repeating until the dish is full
5. Sprinkle fat or butter and breadcrumbs between layers
6. Pour sweet sour cream over the top
7. Sprinkle with butter and breadcrumbs
8. Bake until fully cooked in the oven; serve in the baking dish
