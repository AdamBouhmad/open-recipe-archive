## Ingredients

- lamb cutlets
- butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- red onion
- tarragon
- broth
- vinegar
- two tablespoons of sour cream
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut blanched lamb cutlets into pieces
2. Melt butter and fry one tablespoon of flour in it
3. Add red onion cut into four pieces and a bunch of tarragon
4. Dilute with broth
5. Add a little vinegar and two tablespoons of sour cream, as well as the lamb cutlets
6. Cook until sufficiently tender
7. Place the lamb on a plate and strain the sauce
8. Bring the sauce back to a boil
9. Beat two egg yolks well in a pan, add the sauce, and pour over the meat slices on the plate
