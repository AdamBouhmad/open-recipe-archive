## Ingredients

- veal loin
- pork for larding
- flour
- butter
- stock
- burnt wine
- salt
- nutmeg flower
- pepper
- red onion
- bay leaf
- parsley
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Remove the skin
2. Lard the loin with pork
3. Roll in flour
4. Roast in butter
5. When browned, pour stock and burnt wine over it
6. Add salt, nutmeg flower, pepper, red onion, bay leaf and parsley
7. When the veal loin is tender, make a sauce with sour cream
