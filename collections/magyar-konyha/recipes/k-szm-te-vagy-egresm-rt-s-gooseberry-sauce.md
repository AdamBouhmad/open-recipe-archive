## Ingredients

- cleaned and washed gooseberries
- butter or lard
- flour
- meat stock
- sugar
- three to four tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Braise the cleaned and washed gooseberries lightly in butter or lard
2. Sprinkle with as much flour as the quantity of gooseberries requires
3. Once this has evaporated sufficiently, add a little meat stock, sugar, and three to four tablespoons of sour cream
4. Cook until the gooseberries become soft but still remain whole
5. Can also be passed through a sieve
