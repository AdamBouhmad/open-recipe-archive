## Ingredients

- 42 dekagrams walnuts
- 1/2 teaspoon powdered cinnamon
- 1 orange, zest finely chopped
- 1 lemon, zest finely chopped
- 28 dekagrams sugar
- 2 deciliters water
- 25 dekagrams raisins
- crumbly dough
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Shell and finely grind the walnuts
2. Add ground cinnamon and finely chopped orange and lemon zests, mix well
3. Place sugar in a pot with water and boil until the sugar completely dissolves
4. Add the walnut mixture and keep on heat until the walnuts are well swollen and the whole mass becomes thick
5. Meanwhile, prepare crumbly dough
6. Roll out the dough in two pieces to knife-thickness
7. Place on the prepared baking dish
8. Spread the filling to two fingers' thickness
9. Sprinkle 25 dekagrams raisins on top
10. Cover with the other layer of dough, press edges together
11. Brush with egg yolk and bake
12. Remove from the oven while the dough is still crispy but the filling not dried out
13. The same may be made with almonds instead, but these should not be soaked, but ground with the skin on
