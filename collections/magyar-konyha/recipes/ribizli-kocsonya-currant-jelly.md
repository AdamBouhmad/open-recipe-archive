## Ingredients

- large ripe currants
- forty-two dekagrams sugar
- four deciliters water
- seven deciliters currant juice
- juice of two lemons
- four and one-half dekagrams dissolved isinglass
- finely crushed ice

## Directions

1. Remove ripe currants from their stems and extract their juice
2. Cook forty-two dekagrams of sugar in a brass pan with four deciliters of water until it forms heavy drops
3. Add seven deciliters of currant juice, stir well
4. Squeeze the juice of two lemons into it and cool slightly
5. Mix in four and one-half dekagrams of dissolved isinglass and strain the entire mixture through clean cloth
6. If not sufficiently clear and transparent, repeat the straining
7. Pour into an attractive mold, place on finely crushed ice
8. When sufficiently frozen, unmold onto a serving dish and present at table
