## Ingredients

- 7 dekagramm swollen almonds
- 10 dekagramm sugar with vanilla
- a teaspoon of flour
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 deciliters cream

## Directions

1. Pound 7 dekagramm swollen almonds very fine; similarly pound 10 dekagramm sugar with vanilla
2. Place in a pot with a teaspoon of flour and 2 whole eggs
3. Mix the dough well
4. Slowly pour in 2 deciliters cream
5. Once well mixed, bake the wafers over a flame fire on a wafer iron
6. The small roller on which you quickly rotate them should not be thicker than a little finger
