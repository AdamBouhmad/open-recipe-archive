## Ingredients

- bacon or fat
- green parsley
- garlic
- lemon zest
- peppercorns
- roasted beef
- vinegar
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Place a few slices of bacon or fat, green parsley, a small piece of garlic, lemon zest, all chopped finely, and several peppercorns in a pot
2. Wash the roasted beef well, beat it, and salt it
3. Place it in the pot, cover it, and braise slowly
4. Frequently pour a spoonful of liquid over it so it has only very little sauce
5. Strain off the fat, add a little vinegar and one to two spoonfuls of sour cream
6. Braise well; the sauce should be scant and thick
7. For half a kilogram of roasted beef, use two small heads of red onion, a small bunch of parsley, and a small piece of garlic
