## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 firm heads of cabbage
- salt
- 1 tablespoon goose fat
- 1 tablespoon crushed sugar
- 1 teaspoon flour
- 2 tablespoons good white vinegar
- a few tablespoons sour cream
- broth

## Directions

1. Clean the outer leaves of the cabbage head and chop finely
2. Place in a bowl, salt it, and let it rest in the salt for a good hour
3. Heat 1 tablespoon goose fat and add 1 tablespoon crushed sugar
4. Brown the sugar with frequent stirring
5. Press out the cabbage, place in a pot and put in the oven
6. Stir frequently with a fork
7. When nicely browned, sprinkle with a small teaspoon of flour and braise for a short time
8. Pour 2 tablespoons good white vinegar over it and braise for a few more minutes
9. Pour in a few tablespoons of sour cream and the same amount of broth
10. Braise until it becomes a good sauce
11. The cabbage should be soft but not mushy, and should remain whole, so stir with a fork
12. If serving with pheasant: pluck, prepare and salt the pheasant, pierce the breast and roast quickly so the outside is completely brown but not fully cooked through; place in the finished cabbage and braise together for half an hour; when serving, place the pheasant in the center of the platter and arrange the cabbage around it
13. Alternatively, a capon can be used the same way
