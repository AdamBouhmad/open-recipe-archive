## Ingredients

- 10 dekagrams rice
- 7 deciliters milk
- 7 dekagrams ground almonds
- 5 dekagrams fresh butter
- 6 egg yolks
- sugar and vanilla to taste
- 4 egg whites
- 10 large apricots
- 18 dekagrams sugar
- 2 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Grind 10 dekagrams of cleaned rice very finely in a mortar.
2. Bring 7 deciliters of milk to a boil, add the rice and 7 dekagrams of ground almonds, and stir continuously until a thick paste forms.
3. Remove from heat and stir in 5 dekagrams fresh butter, then cool.
4. Add 6 egg yolks one by one and sugar with vanilla to taste, beat until frothy.
5. Gently fold in the stiffly beaten whites of 4 eggs.
6. Pour into a buttered ring mold and steam for three-quarters of an hour.
7. Meanwhile, peel 10 large apricots, cut in half, and remove pits.
8. Cook 18 dekagrams sugar with 2 deciliters water until it forms heavy drops, add apricots and simmer only a moment so they remain whole.
9. Remove with a spoon to a plate, sprinkle with powdered sugar, and set aside in a warm place.
10. Cook the syrup until it becomes a light jelly.
11. Unmold the pudding onto a plate, fill the ring opening with apricots, coat with jelly, and pour the rest over the fruit.
