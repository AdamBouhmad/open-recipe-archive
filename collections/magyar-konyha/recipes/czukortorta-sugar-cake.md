## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams butter
- 28 dekagrams flour
- 28 dekagrams powdered sugar
- a little vanilla
- zest of one lemon, finely chopped
- 5 hard-boiled egg yolks, sieved
- 3 egg yolks
- cream as needed for moistening
- 24 dekagrams sugar
- juice of half a lemon
- a little egg yolk
- jam of choice for filling
- lemon glaze

## Directions

1. Mix together butter, flour, powdered sugar, vanilla, lemon zest, and hard-boiled sieved egg yolks well
2. Place butter in the center and work together with a rolling pin
3. Add 3 egg yolks and cream as needed to moisten the dough
4. Combine the dough with a broad knife; the dough should not be stiff
5. Roll out the dough to the thickness of a little finger
6. Cut three equal layers from it and bake them nicely, then cool
7. Spread jam on each layer, assemble them together, and cover with lemon glaze
8. For the glaze: place 24 dekagrams sugar (rubbed lightly on lemon then finely powdered and sieved) in a bowl
9. Add juice of half a lemon and a little egg yolk and stir continuously
10. If the glaze is not thin enough, add more juice and stir for half an hour
11. When well foamed, cover the cake with it
12. In a cooled oven, slowly dry the glaze
