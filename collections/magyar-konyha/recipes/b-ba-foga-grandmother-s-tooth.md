## Ingredients

- 6 whole eggs
- salt
- flour
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Beat 6 whole eggs in a bowl and salt it and beat well by hand
2. Take so much flour that it is rather soft than stiff, but must be worked very much
3. On the kneading board roll between the hands and cut into cubes, cutting four sides on each
4. The fat in which it is constantly fried should not be very hot
