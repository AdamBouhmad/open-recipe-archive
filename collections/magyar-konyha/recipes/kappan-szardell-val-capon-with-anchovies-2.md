## Ingredients

- Capon
- Salt
- Three anchovies
- Butter
- mixed well together

## Directions

1. Clean the capon and salt it
2. Let it rest in salt for a while
3. Remove the bones from three anchovies and chop finely
4. Mix finely with well-beaten butter
5. Coat the inside of the capon well with this mixture
6. Dry the capon and roast it
7. Baste frequently with butter
8. When beautifully golden, place on serving dish
9. Pour sauce underneath
