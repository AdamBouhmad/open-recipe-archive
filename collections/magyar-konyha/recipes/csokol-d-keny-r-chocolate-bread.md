## Ingredients

- Sponge cake batter
- Preserved peaches or raspberries
- Chocolate coating

## Directions

1. Prepare sponge cake batter and spread smoothly two knife-widths thick on buttered sheet with wide knife
2. Bake at gentle heat
3. Cool and cut round leaves with a five-crown-sized cutter
4. Spread with preserved peaches or raspberries
5. Place another leaf on top
6. Dip entirely in chocolate coating with two forks
7. Place on parchment paper until completely dry
8. Peel gently from paper
