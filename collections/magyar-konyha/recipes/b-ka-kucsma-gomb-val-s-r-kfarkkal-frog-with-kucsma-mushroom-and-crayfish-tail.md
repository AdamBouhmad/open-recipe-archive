## Ingredients

- frogs
- butter
- kucsma mushrooms
- parsley
- crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Clean the frogs and place them in cold water
2. Prepare a light butter sauce
3. Sauté the cleaned kucsma mushrooms in butter with fresh parsley
4. Add the mushrooms to the butter sauce
5. Squeeze the frogs well and add them to the sauce along with the peeled crayfish tails
6. Bring to a simmer
7. Plate and arrange the crayfish tails around the edge of the platter
