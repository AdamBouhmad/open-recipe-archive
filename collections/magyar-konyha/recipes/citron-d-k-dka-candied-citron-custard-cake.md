## Ingredients

- butter pastry
- 7 dekagrams candied citron, finely cut lengthwise
- 7 dekagrams almond, finely cut lengthwise
- 3 dekagrams pistachio
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- 1 whole egg
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare butter pastry
2. Finely cut lengthwise 7 dekagrams of candied citron, the same amount of almond, and 3 dekagrams of pistachio
3. Break up 14 dekagrams of sugar finely; place half in a bowl and add the finely ground almond to it
4. Mix the other half of the sugar with the chopped mixture
5. Beat 1 whole egg and 3 egg yolks onto this until well and foamy
6. Roll out the butter pastry to knife-thickness
7. Fill the molds (kádka) with it
8. Place some of the chopped mixture in each mold
9. Pour the egg mixture over as a covering instead of a lid
10. Bake in a cooled oven
