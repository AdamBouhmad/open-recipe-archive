## Ingredients

- six dry anise-free pretzels or same amount of horns
- hot salted water
- seven dekagrams pork fat or butter
- two decilitres sour cream
- crumbled salty cheese
- salt

## Directions

1. Break six dry anise-free pretzels or the same amount of horns into pieces or small slices
2. Pour hot salted water over them in a pot
3. Then cover and immediately strain off the water, leaving them covered in their own steam for quarter of an hour
4. Meanwhile, melt seven dekagrams of pork fat or butter, pour in two decilitres of sour cream, pour this over the pretzels, sprinkle with crumbled salty cheese, and shake the whole thing well together
