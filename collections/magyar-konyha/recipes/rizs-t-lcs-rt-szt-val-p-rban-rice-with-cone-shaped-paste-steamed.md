## Ingredients

- rice
- good milk
- 10 dekagrams softened and finely ground almonds
- sugar
- 7 dekagrams butter
- 14 dekagrams rice
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Make cones and round sheets the same way as for the noodle dish
2. Cook rice in good milk
3. Add 10 dekagrams softened and finely ground almonds and enough sugar to make it sufficiently sweet
4. When the rice is soft cool it and place in 7 dekagrams creamed butter
5. Mix 2 whole eggs and 4 egg yolks with 14 dekagrams rice and finally beat in 4 egg whites
