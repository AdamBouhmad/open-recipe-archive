## Ingredients

- lamb leg
- anchovy fillets
- bacon
- thread
- green parsley
- onion
- garlic
- peppercorns
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. If the leg has been in water for a while, remove the bone from the meat and pull off the fatty membrane
2. Drill holes through it with a knife and lard alternately with anchovy and bacon
3. Cut the bacon to the thickness and length of half the little finger
4. Wrap the leg tightly with strong thread so the bacon and anchovy do not fall out
5. Place in a pot that should not be larger than the leg
6. Add a bunch of green parsley, onion, a little garlic, and several peppercorns
7. Braise slowly for three to four hours
8. When the leg is tender, remove the thread and unwrap it, strain the sauce and clean the leg of fat
9. Chop green parsley, onion, several anchovies, and hard-boiled egg finely, add to the sauce
10. Pour a little broth over, return the leg to the sauce and braise a little longer
