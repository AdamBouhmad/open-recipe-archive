## Ingredients

- good homemade bread, sliced thinly
- strong meat broth
- butter
- egg per person

## Directions

1. Cook thinly sliced good homemade bread in strong meat broth
2. Strain the mixture through a sieve
3. Heat a piece of butter in a pot and pour the strained mixture into it
4. Bring to a boil
5. Poach one egg per person as soft-boiled eggs, taking care that the eggs remain whole
6. Warm the eggs in hot meat broth for a few minutes
7. Serve together with the bread soup
