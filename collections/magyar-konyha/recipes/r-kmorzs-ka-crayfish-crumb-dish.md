## Ingredients

- 4.5 deciliters fine flour
- 7 deciliters cold milk
- salt
- 10 dekagrams crayfish butter
- 8 whole eggs
- 24 crayfish tails chopped fine
- 12 dekagrams crayfish butter
- sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Whisk flour and cold milk together slowly in a pot
2. Season with salt
3. Add 10 dekagrams melted crayfish butter, 8 whole eggs, and 24 finely chopped crayfish tails and mix well
4. Melt 12 dekagrams crayfish butter in a wide flat pot
5. Pour the batter into it
6. Place in a moderately hot oven watching that it does not burn on the bottom
7. When the batter is golden on top, stir it and braise until it no longer sticks to the roof of the mouth
8. Serve immediately as it will otherwise dry out
9. If desired sweet, sprinkle with sugar when braising and serving
