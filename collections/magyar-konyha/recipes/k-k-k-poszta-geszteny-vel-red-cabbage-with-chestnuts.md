## Ingredients

- red cabbage
- chestnuts (Italian, 500 grams for 8-10 servings)
- butter
- flour
- sausage

## Directions

1. Clean the red cabbage thoroughly and blanch it well with hot water
2. Cook until tender and drain
3. Prepare a brownish roux
4. Chop the cabbage finely with a knife and add it to the roux, then braise it together with the sauce for a while
5. Roast the Italian chestnuts until they shine beautifully
6. Heap the chestnuts in the center of the dish in a dome shape and arrange the cabbage in a wreath around them
7. Add sausage on the side
