## Ingredients

- 5 kilograms raw sugar
- 50 kilograms warm water
- Juice of a lemon and its finely cut peel
- 2 dekagrams cinnamon
- Half a kilogram of rinsed beer yeast
- 10 kilograms of the best wine vinegar, previously boiled
- 2 tablespoons yeast
- 2 kilograms salt and pepper sauce
- 4 grams crystallized wine tartrate per half kilogram
- 2 grams potassium bitartrate per half kilogram

## Directions

1. Dissolve the raw sugar in warm water
2. Add lemon juice and peel, cinnamon, and rinsed beer yeast
3. Place the mixture in a barrel, shake well, and set in a place with a temperature of 10-15° R.
4. After fermentation, strain the liquid
5. Mix the strained liquid with 10 kilograms of the best previously boiled wine vinegar and 2 tablespoons yeast
6. Pour the liquid into earthenware vessels and expose to a temperature of 20-25° R. until sufficiently sour
7. Mix the finished vinegar with 2 kilograms of salt and pepper sauce
8. After two days, pour into small bottles
9. To each half kilogram of vinegar add 4 grams of crystallized wine tartrate and 2 grams of potassium bitartrate
10. Close the bottles tightly after adding these substances and store in a cool place
