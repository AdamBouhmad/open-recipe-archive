## Ingredients

- several masanszki apples
- three to four more apples
- five dekagrams citronade
- seven dekagrams sugar
- a good spoonful of preserved apricot
- water
- egg white
- sugar
- narrow puff pastry strips
- preserved sour cherry with stem

## Directions

1. Peel several masanszki apples and hollow them out as deeply as possible so that the apple is empty but does not pierce through, it must have a thin bottom
2. Peel three to four more apples and cut them into cubes, chop five dekagrams citronade finely
3. In a pan, stew seven dekagrams sugar and a good spoonful of preserved apricot with the apple, citronade and a little water until it becomes marmalade
4. Fill the hollowed apples with this filling, brush with egg white, sprinkle with sugar, and wrap with narrow puff pastry strips like a spiral, brush the strips with egg white and bake nicely
5. Pile high on the plate, sprinkle with sugar and place on each one a preserved sour cherry with stem instead of a stem
