## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters ripe strawberries
- juice of 2 lemons
- 30 dekagrams sugar
- 1 glass water

## Directions

1. Clean and press 1.5 liters of ripe strawberries through a sieve
2. Press in the juice of 2 lemons
3. Cook 30 dekagrams of sugar with 1 glass of water until it forms heavy drops
4. Combine with the strawberry mixture
5. Cool and pour into the freezing container
