## Ingredients

- raspberries, currants, or cherries
- 1/2 kilogram broken sugar per 1/2 kilogram juice

## Directions

1. Press the raspberries, currants, or cherries through a clean cloth and strain the juice through filter paper so it is beautifully clear, doing this in a cold place so the juice does not boil.
2. Measure the juice and take the same amount of broken sugar for each 1/2 kilogram of juice, place the sugar with the juice in a clean white soup bowl and stir with a silver spoon until the sugar is completely dissolved.
3. Pour the juice into a dry glass bottle, filling only to the neck, then quickly stopper it firmly, cork it, and keep in a cool place.
4. If preparing the juice from cherries, crush them in a mortar so the seed flavor is also perceptible, press the juice through cloth, strain it and treat it the same way as above.
