## Ingredients

- kidney
- salt
- wine
- vinegar
- water
- lemon zest
- bay leaf
- cloves
- red onion
- pepper
- capers (3 dekagrams)
- sardelles
- bacon
- parsley
- sour cream (2 deciliters)

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the kidney, wash it thoroughly, and salt it
2. Prepare a marinade with wine, vinegar, water, lemon zest, bay leaf, and a red onion studded with a few cloves
3. Boil the marinade and pour it hot over the kidney with a few peppercorns
4. Leave it overnight in the marinade
5. The next day, braise it until very tender using the method previously described
6. Finely chop capers, lemon, cleaned sardelles, a small piece of bacon, parsley, and one onion head taken from the marinade
7. Fry this mixture in butter and add it to the meat
8. Add 2 deciliters of sour cream
9. Braise gently, skim off the fat
10. Place the kidney on a serving dish and strain the sauce over it
