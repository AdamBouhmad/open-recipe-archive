## Ingredients

- turtle
- salt
- water
- butter
- flour
- pea broth
- parsley
- peas
- mushrooms
- 12 crayfish tails
- butter pastry

## Directions

1. Cook the turtle in salted water until soft
2. Break the shell in two and remove the cooked turtle
3. Remove the black skin from the legs and other parts
4. Carefully remove the liver and organs without breaking the gallbladder
5. Cut the turtle into four pieces
6. Make a light butter sauce diluted with pea broth
7. Melt butter and braise parsley, 2 deciliters of peas, 12 finely chopped mushrooms, and 15-20 crayfish tails
8. When everything is braised, add to the turtle and cook well together
9. There should be only a little sauce when served
10. Can be surrounded with a crown made of butter pastry
