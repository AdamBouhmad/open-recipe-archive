## Ingredients

- 500 dekagramm sugar, finely ground
- 500 dekagramm softened almonds, sliced
- almond oil
- lemon
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Finely grind the sugar and slice the softened almonds and dry in a lukewarm oven
2. Place the ground sugar in a brass preserving pan over strong charcoal fire, stirring continuously with a cooking spoon
3. Break up the small pellets that form, until the sugar completely melts and begins to turn yellowish
4. Quickly add the dried almonds and stir until both are well mixed and have taken on a light breadcrumb color
5. Quickly fill the caramelized mixture, carefully greased with fine almond oil, into a goblet or basket shape
6. Press the entire form as thin as possible with a lemon
7. When completely cooled, carefully turn out and place on a plate nicely covered with a kitchen cloth
8. Fill the entire opening high with whipped cream, which gives it a very beautiful appearance
