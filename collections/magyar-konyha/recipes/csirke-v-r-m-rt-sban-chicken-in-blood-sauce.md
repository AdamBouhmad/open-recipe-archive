## Ingredients

- chicken blood
- vinegar
- bacon
- red onion
- whole peppercorns
- cloves
- flour
- lemon juice
- chicken

## Directions

1. When killing the chickens, collect the blood in a bowl with a small amount of vinegar
2. Mix the blood with the vinegar so it does not coagulate
3. Pluck and clean the chickens and salt them well
4. Place bacon pieces in a pot
5. Cut red onion in half and place in the center
6. Pierce each piece with three cloves
7. Add a few whole peppercorns and cloves
8. Add the chicken pieces and braise but do not allow them to brown
9. Remove the chicken pieces
10. Dust with 2 tablespoons flour and brown
11. Deglaze with liquid and reduce
12. Add a small amount of lemon juice and cook well
13. Strain through a sieve
14. Return the chicken pieces and cook until tender
15. Mix the blood with the sauce and cook together for a few minutes more
