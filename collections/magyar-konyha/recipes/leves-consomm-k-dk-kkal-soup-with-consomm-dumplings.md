## Ingredients

- 2 well-roasted chickens
- A piece of roasted veal
- 1 liter strong chicken broth
- 9 egg yolks
- Butter
- Consommé

## Directions

1. Chop 2 well-roasted chickens and a piece of roasted veal and pound finely in a mortar.
2. Pour 1 liter of strong chicken broth over it, pass through a sieve, mix together with 9 egg yolks, and pass through the sieve again.
3. Grease the dumplings with butter and fill with consommé and braise.
4. When the consommé sets, turn it out onto a soup dish and pour light brown broth over it.
5. To braise: Pour enough water into a deep pot so it reaches halfway up the mold; when the water boils, place the mold in it, cover with an iron lid, and place on the hot plate of the stove with as much heat as required.
6. For larger quantities of such a dish, it often must be cooked longer than an hour in this water, so if the water evaporates, it must be replaced with hot water, but the mentioned level must never be exceeded, as the contents would be in danger of the water overflowing it.
