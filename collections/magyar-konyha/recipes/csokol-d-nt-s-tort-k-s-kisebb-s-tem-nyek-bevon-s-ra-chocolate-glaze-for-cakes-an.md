## Ingredients

- 21 grams fine chocolate
- 18 grams sugar
- 2 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Coarsely crush 21 grams of fine chocolate with 18 grams of sugar and cook in a sugar pan with 2 deciliters of water with continuous stirring until it boils with large bubbles and becomes light child-porridge thickness
2. Remove from heat and stir quickly, continuously and as long as for punch preserve until it passes the test, that is, until a drop dripped on paper does not spread but hardens
3. Place the cake on a sheet of paper, pour the glaze evenly over it, quickly spread what runs down onto the sides of the cake so that it is completely covered and let it stand for a few hours
4. Decorate with whole fruits or in the following way: mix finely 1 spoonful of beautiful yellow peach syrup with 2 teaspoons of crushed sugar, fill this into a small syringe and when the chocolate or lemon coating has hardened, press either arches or star shapes onto it
5. These decorations can be made in two colors, that is with yellow peach and red raspberry syrup
