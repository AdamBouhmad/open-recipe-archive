## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter dried peas
- 3 liters fresh spring water
- carrot
- parsley root
- celery
- a few fried breadcrumbs
- a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put 0.5 liter dried peas in a pot and fill with 3 liters of clean spring water
2. Cook, adding carrot, parsley root, celery, a few fried breadcrumbs, and a little nutmeg
3. Simmer slowly and use as needed for fasting dishes and soups
