## Ingredients

- Several large floury potatoes
- Salt
- Butter
- Red onion, finely chopped
- Sheep cheese (juhtúró)

## Directions

1. Peel and wash the potatoes
2. Cook in salted water until soft
3. Slice the cooked potatoes and place on a plate
4. Pour over butter in which finely chopped red onion has been braised
5. Sprinkle with sheep cheese, toss and serve hot
