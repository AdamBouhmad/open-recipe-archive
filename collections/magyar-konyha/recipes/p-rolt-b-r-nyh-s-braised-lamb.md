## Ingredients

- two lamb legs
- two heads of red onion
- carrot
- thyme
- bay leaf
- three tablespoons of brown broth
- veal bones
- lard
- flour
- vinegar
- lemon zest
- six large cucumbers

## Directions

1. Blanch two lamb legs and thread them
2. Place sliced red onion and carrot in a pot, put the legs on top
3. Add thyme, bay leaf, three tablespoons of brown broth, and chopped veal bones
4. Cook until it becomes glace
5. Heat a tablespoon of lard and place the braised onion and carrot with one tablespoon of flour in it
6. Brown well and dilute with broth
7. Add a little vinegar and lemon zest and cook well
8. Strain through a sieve
9. Cut six large cucumbers into leaves and add to the sauce and bring to a boil
10. Place the legs in the glace, turning them so they are coated on all sides, especially on the side where they were threaded
11. Place on a plate, pour the sauce on the bottom, and arrange the small cucumbers around
