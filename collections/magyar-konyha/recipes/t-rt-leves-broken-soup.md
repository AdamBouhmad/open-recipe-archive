## Ingredients

- pike head
- pea broth
- breadcrumbs
- soaked in milk
- eggs
- green parsley
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the soup the same way as on meat days, but instead of chicken use a large pike head, and instead of meat broth use pea water.
2. Serve the soup with pike dumplings prepared as follows: Cook a nice piece of pike, about one kilogram, in salted water.
3. Clean the pike from bones and skin.
4. Chop two pieces of breadcrumbs soaked in milk and pressed out, three beaten eggs, and chopped green parsley finely together.
5. Beat seven dekagrams of butter until fluffy.
6. Add the chopped mixture to the butter and mix well.
7. Add one whole egg and three egg yolks, mix everything well.
8. Season with a little salt.
9. Do not knead the dough too firmly.
10. Shape small dumplings, roll them in fine breadcrumbs, and fry them.
