## Ingredients

- cleaned pike
- salt
- water
- cloves
- bay leaf
- hard-boiled eggs
- parsley
- brown butter

## Directions

1. Clean and split the pike
2. Cook in salted water with a few cloves and bay leaf
3. Carefully place on a serving dish
4. Sprinkle with finely chopped hard-boiled eggs and parsley
5. Pour hot brown butter over it
