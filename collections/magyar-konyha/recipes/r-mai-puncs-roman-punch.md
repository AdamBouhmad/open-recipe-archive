## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilogram sugar
- 7 deciliters water
- 2 lemons, rind
- 3 lemons, juice
- 4 deciliters fine arrack
- 1 bottle champagne
- 4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Boil 0.5 kilogram sugar in 7 deciliters water
2. Remove the foam
3. Add the rind of 2 lemons rubbed on sugar
4. When the sugar has cooled, squeeze the juice of 3 lemons into it
5. Strain the whole through a sieve
6. Freeze hard
7. During continuous turning of the container, mix in 4 deciliters fine arrack, 1 bottle champagne, and the stiffly beaten snow of 4 egg whites, incorporating the latter most easily with a whisk
8. Pour this punch into champagne glasses and serve regularly alongside roast
