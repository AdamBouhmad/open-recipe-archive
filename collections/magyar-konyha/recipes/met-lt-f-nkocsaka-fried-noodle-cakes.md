## Ingredients

- Four deciliters flour
- One egg
- Two egg yolks
- One liter hot cream
- Ten dekagrams finely ground almonds
- Vanilla
- Butter
- Flour for dusting
- Beaten egg for dipping
- Breadcrumbs
- Fat for frying
- Sugar and vanilla for serving

## Directions

1. Make common noodle dough from flour, egg and egg yolks
2. Roll out and cut wide noodles
3. Cook in one liter hot cream
4. Add finely ground almonds and vanilla, cook until cream is completely reduced
5. Spread on a buttered baking sheet to the thickness of a finger and cool
6. Turn out onto a flour-dusted board
7. Break into small fried cake pieces
8. Dip in beaten egg, coat with flour and fine breadcrumbs
9. Fry until golden yellow in fat
10. Sprinkle with sugar and vanilla when serving
