## Ingredients

- currants
- 7 deciliters currant juice
- half kilogram sugar
- 3 deciliters water
- juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Press currants through cloth
2. Cook half kilogram sugar in 3 deciliters water until half-reduced
3. Pour into the currant juice
4. Mix in the juice of 2 lemons
5. Place in freezing container
