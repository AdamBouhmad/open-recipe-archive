## Ingredients

- sour cabbage
- red onion
- pike
- salt water
- butter
- red onion
- sour cream
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the cabbage in the usual way, add a little roux with red onion and cook the sauce through.
2. Pour salted boiling water over the pike, remove its bones and shred into small pieces.
3. Melt a little butter, add two heads of red onion cut into thin slices and roast until a nice golden color.
4. Then place several spoonfuls of sour cream, a little butter and one portion of the shredded pike on a plate, salt it, and add the roasted onion.
