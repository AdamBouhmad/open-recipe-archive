## Ingredients

- fifty-six dekagrams fine sugar
- three grams vanilla
- five eggs
- zest of one orange, finely chopped
- fifty-six dekagrams finest flour

## Directions

1. Grind the fine sugar with vanilla very finely
2. Add five eggs and the finely chopped orange zest to the mixture
3. Beat everything together for one hour
4. Add the fifty-six dekagrams finest flour to the dough
5. Place the dough on a floured board
6. Roll out to the thickness of two knife-blade widths
7. Press the necessary form onto it and cut through with a knife
8. Place the tablets on a tray greased with wax
9. Bake at gentle heat for ten minutes, so that their color remains white
