## Ingredients

- 50 kilograms of starch
- 350 kilograms of warm water
- 1 kilogram of malt flour
- 10 kilograms of water

## Directions

1. Mix 50 kilograms of starch with 350 kilograms of warm water
2. Cool to 55° R temperature
3. Mix in 1 kilogram of malt flour that has previously been mixed with 10 kilograms of water
4. Keep the mixture at 53° R temperature for 2.5 hours and dilute with water to vinegar
