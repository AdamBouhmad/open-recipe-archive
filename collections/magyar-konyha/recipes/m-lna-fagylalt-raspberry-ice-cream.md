## Ingredients

- raspberries
- 7 deciliters milk
- 28 dekagrams sugar
- juice of 4 lemons

## Directions

1. Press the raspberries through clean cloth
2. Dissolve 28 dekagrams of sugar in 7 deciliters of milk
3. Add the juice of 4 lemons to the mixture
4. Pour the mixture into the freezing container and treat it like the other ice creams
