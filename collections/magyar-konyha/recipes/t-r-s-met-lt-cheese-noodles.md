## Ingredients

- one and a half liters of dried curd cheese
- butter
- three whole eggs
- three egg yolks
- salt
- flour
- hot milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Finely combine the dried curd cheese with butter
2. Mix in three whole eggs, three egg yolks, and a little salt
3. Make a dough with flour
4. On a floured board, form the dough into noodles the length of a finger and thick
5. Fry in a bronze pan with butter until golden brown
6. Pour into hot milk
7. Season well with sugar and let it boil
