## Ingredients

- larger leg of mutton
- salt
- green parsley
- onion
- five to six mushrooms (csiperke)
- crushed pepper
- butter (piece the size of an egg)
- white wine (two deciliters)

## Directions

1. Clean the larger leg of mutton and cut it almost all the way through without separating it completely
2. Salt it and let it sit in the salt for one hour
3. Place it in a long pot
4. Finely chop green parsley, onion and five to six mushrooms together and add them to the pot with crushed pepper and a piece of butter the size of an egg all at once
5. Pour two deciliters of white wine over it
6. Cover the pot, place coals on top and cook through
7. Frequently pour the sauce over it with a spoon
8. When completely cooked, place it on a plate and serve with the small amount of sauce underneath
