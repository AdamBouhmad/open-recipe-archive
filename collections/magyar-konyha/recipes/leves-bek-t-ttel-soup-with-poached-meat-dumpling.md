## Ingredients

- Roasted veal
- Capon and turkey breast
- Roll soaked in milk
- 3 eggs
- Green parsley, finely chopped
- 7 dekagrams butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- Kitchen towel

## Directions

1. Chop roasted veal, capon breast, and turkey breast finely, soak a roll in milk and squeeze it out, beat 3 eggs, and chop green parsley finely.
2. Beat 7 dekagrams of butter until fluffy, throw in the chopped mixture, add 3 eggs one after another, salt, pepper, a little bread crumbs, and 2 tablespoons of good sour cream.
3. Mix everything well together, place the mixture on a kitchen towel greased with butter, tie it together, and boil in hot salted water for half an hour.
4. Carefully remove it, place it in a soup dish, and pour brown broth over it.
