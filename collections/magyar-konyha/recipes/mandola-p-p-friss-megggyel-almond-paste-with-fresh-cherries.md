## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams almonds
- 28 dekagrams sugar
- zest of half a lemon, finely chopped
- 1 whole egg
- 6 egg yolks
- fresh ripe cherries, with stems removed
- sugar for poaching the cherries

## Directions

1. Soften the almonds and crush the sugar, then sift the sugar
2. Chop the lemon zest finely and place everything in a bowl
3. Beat in the whole egg and six egg yolks one after another
4. Stir the mixture for half an hour
5. Wash the ripe cherries, remove the stems, and lightly poach them with sugar so they remain whole
6. Butter a mold and line it with paper, then butter the paper again
7. Arrange the cherries neatly on the bottom of the mold
8. Spread half the almond paste on top and set in a water bath
9. After five to ten minutes, when the paste has firmed and the cherries have not sunk, arrange the remaining cherries on top
10. Pour the remaining almond paste over them
11. Work quickly without removing the mold from the water bath
12. Cover immediately and poach whole for one hour
