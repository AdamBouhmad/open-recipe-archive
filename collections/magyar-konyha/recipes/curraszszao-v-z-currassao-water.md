## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams currassao orange peel
- 21 dekagrams orange blossoms
- 10 dekagrams Florentine iris root
- 14 dekagrams lemon balm
- 18 dekagrams jasmine flowers
- 4.5 dekagrams raisins
- 4.5 dekagrams clove buds
- paradise seeds
- 1 dekagram Peruvian balsam
- 1.5 liters alcohol
- 1 dekagram orange oil
- 4 grams bergamot oil

## Directions

1. Fill 1.5 liters of alcohol with the currassao orange peel, orange blossoms, Florentine iris root, lemon balm, jasmine flowers, raisins, clove buds, paradise seeds, and Peruvian balsam
2. Allow to digest
3. Strain the liquid
4. Add 1 dekagram orange oil and 4 grams bergamot oil to the strained liquid
5. Shake well
