## Ingredients

- Two chickens
- Small green peas
- Butter
- Crayfish tails
- Crayfish butter
- Sour cream
- Breadcrumbs
- Nutmeg flowers
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare two chickens as if you were going to stuff them.
2. For the stuffing: braise small green peas in butter.
3. Shell crayfish tails and cut them into small cubes, then braise in crayfish butter.
4. Add a little sour cream, breadcrumbs, a little nutmeg flower, and salt.
5. Stuff the chickens with this mixture.
6. Skewer on thin skewers and roast nicely.
7. These chickens can also be used for soup.
