## Ingredients

- calf's head
- salted water
- butter
- two tablespoons of flour
- medium-sized red onion
- green parsley
- broth
- grated nutmeg
- three eggs
- butter pastry for garnish

## Directions

1. Cook the calf's head in salted water until tender
2. Remove and place in cold water
3. Melt a piece of butter and fry two tablespoons of flour in it until golden
4. Add red onion cut in half and green parsley
5. Dilute with broth and cook the sauce
6. Cut the head into equal pieces and place in a pot
7. Strain the sauce over it
8. Add a little grated nutmeg and bring to a boil together
9. Before serving, beat three eggs well in a pan, gradually add the sauce, place the pan on a hot stove and stir until it thickens
10. Place the head on a plate, pour the sauce over it, and decorate the edge with a wreath made of butter pastry
