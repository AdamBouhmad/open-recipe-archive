## Ingredients

- peeled and halved apricots
- 1 liter vinegar per 2 kilograms fruit
- 80 dekagrams sugar per 2 kilograms fruit

## Directions

1. Take peeled and halved apricots and cook once with vinegar prepared with sugar, calculating 1 liter vinegar and 80 dekagrams sugar per 2 kilograms of fruit.
2. Then treat them as with melon.
