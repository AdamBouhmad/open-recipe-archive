## Ingredients

- ½ kilogram sugar
- 1.25 liters wild or garden strawberries
- 6 decagrams isinglass
- ½ bottle white wine or champagne
- 2 lemons, juice strained

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar in 3 deciliters of water
2. Add the washed strawberries
3. Cover in a porcelain bowl and let stand for several hours
4. Strain the sugar through a cloth
5. Mix the lukewarm isinglass, wine, and strained lemon juice together
6. Layer the gelée with the fine garden strawberries (which have been washed and marinated with sugar) into a mold according to the method described in recipe 901, alternating layers
7. In winter, preserved strawberry syrup can be used instead of fresh strawberries, but use less sugar since the syrup is already sweetened with sugar
