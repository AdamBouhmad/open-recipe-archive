## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams flour
- 14 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- the finely chopped zest of one lemon
- cinnamon
- a small amount of finely ground cloves
- hot honey
- split almonds

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with finely ground sugar, the finely chopped zest of a lemon, cinnamon, and a small amount of finely ground cloves
2. Make a dough with hot honey to the consistency of butter dough
3. Work the dough for a good half hour
4. Let it rest for three-quarters of an hour
5. Roll out to half a finger thickness
6. Shape into boats or other forms
7. Top with split almonds
8. Bake quickly in a well-heated oven
