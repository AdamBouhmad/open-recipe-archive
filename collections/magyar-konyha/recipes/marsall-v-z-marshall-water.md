## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram purified alcohol
- 7 dekagrams cinnamon essence
- 2 dekagrams sassafras essence
- 14 dekagrams bergamot oil
- 14 dekagrams amber essence
- 2 dekagrams jasmine essence
- 7 dekagrams ambergris essence
- 7 dekagrams musk essence

## Directions

1. Add all ingredients to 1 kilogram purified alcohol
2. Shake well
