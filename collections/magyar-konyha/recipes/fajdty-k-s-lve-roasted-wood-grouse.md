## Ingredients

- wood grouse
- white paper
- butter
- marinade
- sour cream

## Directions

1. If the wood grouse is young, roast like a pheasant
2. Do not pluck the head but wrap it in white paper
3. Prepare it nicely
4. Lard the breast densely and roast
5. If the grouse is older, marinate and lard like haunch of venison
6. During roasting, baste several times with the marinade
7. When almost done, pour a few spoonfuls of sour cream under the marinade and baste several more times
