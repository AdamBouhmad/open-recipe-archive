## Ingredients

- 1 liter fresh cherries
- 0.5 liter water
- 0.25 liter wine
- sugar
- 1 piece cinnamon
- lemon zest
- clove
- 1 tablespoon flour

## Directions

1. Crush 1 liter of fresh cherries together with their pits in a mortar
2. Cook in 0.5 liter water and 0.25 liter wine
3. Strain through a sieve
4. Add sugar, 1 piece cinnamon, lemon zest, clove, and 1 tablespoon flour mixed with a little water
5. Bring to a boil again and serve alongside cake or pudding
