## Ingredients

- strudel dough
- 14 decagrams rice
- 8 deciliters milk
- 7 decagrams butter, beaten until fluffy
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- broken sugar
- a little vanilla

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough
2. Cook 14 decagrams rice in 8 deciliters milk until soft
3. Mix 7 decagrams butter beaten until fluffy with 5 egg yolks and the stiff peaks of 3 egg whites
4. Add broken sugar and a little vanilla
5. Fill the stretched dough with this mixture
6. Fold the strudel together
7. Place in a buttered baking dish
8. Bake until golden brown
9. Sprinkle with sugar when serving
