## Ingredients

- peaches
- water
- sugar, same amount of sugar as peaches, half kilogram

## Directions

1. Place the peaches in boiling water, bring to a boil several times, remove the skins, remove the pit, press through a sieve, and prepare the sugar as you would for other fruits.
2. Add the sieved peaches to the clarified sugar, mix well with it, and cook slowly until a drop tested on a plate remains firm.
