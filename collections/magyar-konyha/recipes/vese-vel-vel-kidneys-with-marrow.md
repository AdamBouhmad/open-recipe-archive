## Ingredients

- kidneys
- marrow, cleaned
- hot fat
- salt
- pepper
- eggs (one per kidney used)

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the kidneys, remove the stones by cutting them out, then grate them and place in hot fat together with the cleaned marrow. Salt them and for each kidney used, beat one egg over them. Finally sprinkle with pepper and serve.
