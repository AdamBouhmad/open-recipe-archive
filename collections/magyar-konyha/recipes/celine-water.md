## Ingredients

- 10 dekagrams lemon peel
- 10 dekagrams orange peel
- 28 dekagrams black bread crust
- 4.5 dekagrams anise
- 4.5 dekagrams cinnamon
- 4.5 dekagrams caraway
- 1 dekagrams nutmeg flower
- 1 dekagrams lavender
- 4 grams vanilla
- 3 liters alcohol
- 2 grams bergamot oil
- 4 grams orange flower oil
- 80 drops jasmine oil

## Directions

1. Chop all solid ingredients finely
2. Pour 3 liters alcohol over them
3. Digest
4. Strain the liquid
5. Add 2 grams bergamot oil and 4 grams orange flower oil to the filtered liquid
6. Add 80 drops jasmine oil
7. Shake well
