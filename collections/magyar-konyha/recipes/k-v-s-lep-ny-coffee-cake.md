## Ingredients

- eighteen dekagrams fine flour
- fourteen dekagrams soft butter
- ten dekagrams finely ground sugar
- three hard-boiled egg yolks
- one egg yolk
- ten dekagrams sugar
- half cup of black coffee
- three egg whites
- seven dekagrams ground sugar
- jam for coating

## Directions

1. On a board, work the flour with the soft butter in layers
2. Mix finely ground sugar with flour
3. Add three hard-boiled egg yolks
4. Work everything well with the roller
5. Gather the mixture together and add one egg yolk, work well with knife
6. Roll out the smooth dough to three knife-widths thickness
7. Place on buttered sheet
8. Bake slowly at low heat
9. When baked and cooled, cut into two-finger wide and span-long strips
10. Coat the strips with jam
11. Cook ten dekagrams sugar with half cup black coffee until it begins to thread
12. When cooled, beat three egg whites to stiff snow
13. With the whisk, mix in seven dekagrams ground sugar
14. Add the cooked coffee
15. Spread the whole mixture smoothly on the strips
16. Dry in cooled oven
17. When dried, pile high on a cloth-covered plate
