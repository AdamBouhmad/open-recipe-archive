## Ingredients

- 5 liters 72° alcohol
- 18 dekagrams bergamot oil
- 2 dekagrams lemon oil
- 1 dekagrams jasmine oil
- orange flower water as needed

## Directions

1. Dissolve bergamot oil, lemon oil, and jasmine oil in alcohol
2. Add orange flower water until alcohol is 5° strength
