## Ingredients

- three medium-sized nice cauliflower heads
- beef stock
- butter
- flour
- nutmeg flower
- sour cream
- eggs
- bread

## Directions

1. Clean three nice medium-sized cauliflower heads, cut into equal pieces and cook in good strong beef stock
2. Heat a piece of butter in a pot, add two full spoons of cooking flour, bring to a slight boil, and pour the stock from the now soft cauliflower over it
3. Add little finely chopped nutmeg flower and two full spoons of sour cream
4. Before serving, place the cauliflower in the soup, thicken the soup with two egg yolks, and pour over fried bread and fried goose liver
