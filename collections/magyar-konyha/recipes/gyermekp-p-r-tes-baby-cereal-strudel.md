## Ingredients

- rétes-tészta (strudel dough)
- thick baby cereal
- 4 dekagramm butter
- two whole eggs and four egg yolks
- one lemon
- 10 dekagramm sugar
- four egg whites
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough and thick baby cereal
2. Beat 4 dekagramm butter until fluffy
3. Mix in the cooled baby cereal
4. Add two whole eggs and four egg yolks and stir for half an hour
5. Rub 10 dekagramm sugar on one lemon to obtain lemon sugar
6. Add this to the mixture
7. Finally gently fold in the stiffly beaten whites of four eggs
8. Stretch the dough finely
9. Spread the baby cereal filling on it to the thickness of a knife blade
10. Fold it together gently
11. Place it in a butter-brushed pan and bake until golden
12. When baked, serve with powdered sugar sprinkled on top
