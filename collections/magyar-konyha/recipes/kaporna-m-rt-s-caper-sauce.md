## Ingredients

- light brown roux
- ten to twelve decagrams capers
- good broth
- fresh cream

## Directions

1. Prepare a light brown roux
2. Coarsely chop the capers with a chopping knife and add to the roux
3. Braise for a few minutes
4. Loosen with a small spoonful of good broth
5. Add two to three spoons of fresh cream and cook well together
