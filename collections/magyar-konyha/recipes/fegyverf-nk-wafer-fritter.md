## Ingredients

- 7 dekagramm butter beaten foamy
- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 7 dekagramm swollen and finely ground almonds
- 9 dekagramm ground sugar with vanilla
- 4-5 tablespoons cream
- 7 dekagramm fine flour

## Directions

1. Beat 7 dekagramm butter foamy and slowly beat in 1 whole egg and 1 egg yolk
2. Mix in well 7 dekagramm swollen and finely ground almonds and 9 dekagramm ground sugar with vanilla
3. Add 4-5 tablespoons cream and finally 7 dekagramm fine flour mixed with the rest
4. Heat the wafer fritter iron on a flame, first coat with butter, wipe with paper, heat again
5. Place a small spoonful of dough on the iron, press the iron together well, turn several times over the fire so the dough is brownish-yellow on both sides
6. Quickly peel off with a knife and place in arch shape
7. Once all are baked, let sit in a warm place as they easily soften
8. Pile high on a plate and sprinkle with sugar
