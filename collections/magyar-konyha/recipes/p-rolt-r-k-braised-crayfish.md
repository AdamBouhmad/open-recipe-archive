## Ingredients

- crayfish
- butter
- fresh parsley
- garlic
- salt
- sour cream
- white wine

## Directions

1. Rinse the crayfish thoroughly
2. Melt butter and add finely chopped parsley and garlic
3. Add the crayfish and salt
4. Braise for a while
5. Add several tablespoons of sour cream and white wine
6. Continue braising until the crayfish turn red
