## Ingredients

- 7 dekagrams rice
- butter
- 4 deciliters cream
- vanilla sugar to taste
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- butter puff pastry cases
- apricot preserve

## Directions

1. Grind 7 dekagrams of cleaned rice very finely.
2. Heat butter in a pan, add the rice and fry, then pour 4 deciliters cream over it and stir until thick.
3. If too thick, add more cream; it should have the consistency of a soufflé.
4. Add vanilla sugar to taste.
5. Remove from heat, beat in 3 egg yolks until frothy, then slowly fold in the stiffly beaten whites of 3 eggs.
6. Line round molds with butter puff pastry, add a good spoonful of mixture, top with a little apricot preserve, then fill completely with the remaining mixture.
7. Place in the oven and bake slowly.
