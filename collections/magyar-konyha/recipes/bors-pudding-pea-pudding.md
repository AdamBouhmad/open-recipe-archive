## Ingredients

- one liter of dried peas, cooked until soft
- melted butter
- flour (one cooking spoonful)
- good meat broth (one serving spoon)
- cooked tongue, finely sliced and pickled
- roasted sausages, browned
- salt

## Directions

1. Press the cooked peas through a sieve
2. Brown one cooking spoonful of flour in melted butter until golden
3. Mix with the pressed peas
4. Add one serving spoon of good meat broth and cook until it becomes a thick paste, then cool
5. Grease a pan well with butter and fill it with the pea mixture to a height of 2 cm
6. Add a layer of cooked tongue sliced finely and pickled
7. Then add another layer of peas and a row of beautifully browned sausages cut into pieces and split through the middle
8. Continue alternating peas and pickled tongue until the pan is full
9. Steam for half an hour
10. Turn out onto a plate and decorate with finely chopped pickled tongue
