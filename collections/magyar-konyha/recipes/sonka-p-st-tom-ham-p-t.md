## Ingredients

- half kilogram of cooked ham
- two decilitres of milk
- two eggs
- sour cream
- butter
- crumbly pastry

## Directions

1. Chop half kilogram of cooked ham finely, removing the fat, prepare a pancake from two decilitres of milk and two eggs, and cut slightly wider than thin slices, place in a bowl, mix the larger part of the chopped ham with a large coffee cup of good sour cream, but so that it remains somewhat liquid.
2. Line a pot greased with butter with crumbly pastry, roll out the pastry to the thickness of a thin knife blade and line the pot smoothly so that it does not wrinkle, sprinkle the bottom of the pastry with the chopped ham and prepare a lid from pastry, brush with egg white and bake.
