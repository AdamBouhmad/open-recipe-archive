## Ingredients

- 1 goose liver
- 9-10 slices of red onion
- carrots, sliced into rings
- cloves
- 2 deciliters of sour cream
- breadcrumbs, a handful
- small pieces of butter
- 14 decagrams (1/4 pound) rice, cleaned
- parsley, finely chopped
- Spanish onion, quartered
- stock
- black pepper
- crayfish butter
- mushrooms, finely chopped
- crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Wash and salt the goose liver and place it in a pot
2. Place 9-10 slices of red onion and sliced carrots under the liver
3. Pierce the liver with cloves
4. Pour 2 deciliters of sour cream over it
5. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and small pieces of butter
6. Roast for one hour
7. Remove the liver, clean it of sauce and fat
8. Pour a few spoonfuls of stock over the remaining vegetables and quickly reduce
9. Place the liver in a dish where the rice ring should already be prepared and pour the small amount of sauce over it
10. For the rice ring: melt a small piece of butter and add finely chopped parsley
11. Add 14 decagrams of cleaned rice to the butter
12. Add the Spanish onion quartered
13. Slowly braise the rice, occasionally pouring small amounts of stock so it does not break
14. When softened, remove the onion and add pepper and crayfish butter
15. In another pot, melt butter and add finely chopped parsley with several finely chopped mushrooms
16. Braise with a little black pepper until the sauce has evaporated
17. Sprinkle a little flour over it
18. Pour in one or two spoonfuls of sour cream and let it evaporate again
19. Form a rice ring in the dish and fill it with the braised mushrooms and crayfish tails
20. Leave two finger-widths of space between them so the whole resembles a ribbon
21. Place the goose liver in the middle
