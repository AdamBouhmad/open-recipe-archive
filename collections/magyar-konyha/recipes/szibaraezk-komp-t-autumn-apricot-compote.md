## Ingredients

- Eight apricots
- Approximately ten dekagrams sugar
- Half a glass of water
- Rum

## Directions

1. Boil the apricots in hot water for one minute, then place them in cold water
2. When cooled, peel them, cut them in half, and remove the pits
3. Cook approximately ten dekagrams of sugar with half a glass of water for eight apricots
4. Once the sugar has dissolved, add the apricots and cook until they absorb the sugar on both sides
5. Arrange them neatly in a cup and continue cooking the syrup
6. Add a coffee spoon of good rum to it
7. When the syrup begins to thicken, pour it over the apricots and place the whole dish on ice or in a cellar
