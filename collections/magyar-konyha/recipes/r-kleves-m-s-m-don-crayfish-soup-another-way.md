## Ingredients

- thirty crayfish
- beef stock
- butter
- flour
- bread

## Directions

1. Cook about thirty crayfish in beef stock for six people
2. Remove the meat from the shells and pound both finely in a mortar
3. Heat a piece of butter and add the pounded crayfish shells, braise them in the butter for a while
4. Strain this crayfish butter through fine cloth, return the remaining shells to the stock in which the crayfish were cooked and let boil well
5. Heat the extracted crayfish butter, add two full spoons of flour and bring to a slight boil without browning
6. Pour over the same stock in which the crayfish shells were cooked, strain through a sieve and serve on fried bread
7. Break up the crayfish tails and claws before putting them in the mortar
