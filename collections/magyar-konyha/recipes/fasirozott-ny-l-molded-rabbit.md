## Ingredients

- piece of cold roasted rabbit
- piece of venison
- red onion
- bread soaked in beef broth
- egg yolks
- sour cream
- goose fat
- fine bread crumbs
- rabbit mold
- thin venison or rabbit loin slices
- brown roux
- stock
- vinegar
- lemon peel
- capers
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Chop finely cold roasted rabbit, venison, a little red onion and bread soaked in beef broth
2. Add two egg yolks and two spoonfuls of sour cream and mix everything well
3. Butter a rabbit mold and coat with fine bread crumbs
4. Place part of the minced meat in it
5. Line the center of the mold with thin venison or rabbit loin slices and place the rest of the minced meat on top
6. Bake nicely
7. Make a small brown roux, thin with stock, add a little vinegar, lemon peel, capers and finally one or two spoonfuls of good sour cream and cook
8. Turn the rabbit out onto a plate and pour the sauce over it
