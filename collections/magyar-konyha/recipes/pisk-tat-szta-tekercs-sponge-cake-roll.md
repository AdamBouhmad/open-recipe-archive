## Ingredients

- fourteen dekagrams sugar
- sponge cake dough
- butter
- preserved peach or raspberry marmalade
- egg white
- powdered sugar
- coarse sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as described in recipe 672, fourteen dekagrams of sugar is sufficient for ten to twelve people
2. Pour this dough onto a long baking sheet buttered with butter and smooth it evenly with a knife to a knife-width thickness
3. Bake at gentle heat until the dough becomes dark yellow
4. Then coat entirely with preserved peach or raspberry marmalade and place it in the oven again for a moment so that the dough softens and can be rolled together
5. Remove the baking sheet and roll the cake along its length into a roll so that the roll is beautifully thick
6. Place it in the already cooled oven for eight to ten minutes
7. When this time has elapsed, remove it, let it cool, and let it harden
8. With a sharp knife, cut the entire roll into knife-width thick slices and arrange them in three or four wreaths on the plate
9. Before cutting the roll, coat it completely with snow using a large feather, roll it in coarsely broken sugar, place it in the cooled oven again, but only until it dries, then when cooled, cut it
