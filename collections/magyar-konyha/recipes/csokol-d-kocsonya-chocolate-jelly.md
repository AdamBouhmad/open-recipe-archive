## Ingredients

- 6 tablets fine chocolate
- 7 deciliter cream
- 1 dekagramm dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Cook 6 tablets of fine chocolate in 7 deciliter of cream with continuous stirring
2. When slightly thickened, add 1 dekagramm dissolved isinglass
3. Pour into a mold and freeze on ice
