## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams good chocolate
- 7 dekagrams sugar
- 18 dekagrams blanched almonds
- egg white

## Directions

1. Finely crush the chocolate and sugar together
2. Blanch and chop the almonds - chop half finely and half into larger pieces
3. Prepare stiff peaks from egg white and beat the sugar and chocolate into a thick icing with it
4. Once well foamy, first beat in the finely chopped almonds, and once well mixed, beat in the coarsely chopped almonds
5. Using a coffee spoon, place onto wafer paper cut into rounds
6. Dry in a gentle oven
