## Ingredients

- yellowish roux
- finely chopped red onion
- meat broth
- lemon zest
- lemon juice
- fresh cream

## Directions

1. Prepare a yellowish roux and add finely chopped red onion
2. When this has browned a little, pour in enough broth for the proper thickness
3. Cut the zest of half a lemon into long thin pieces and add with its juice
4. Cook well together
5. Finally add two spoons of fresh cream and the sauce is ready
