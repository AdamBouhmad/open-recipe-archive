## Ingredients

- lampreys
- salt
- flour
- lemon juice
- sour cream
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- pea broth

## Directions

1. Open the lampreys, salt them and lard them so they keep their nice round shape
2. Let them sit briefly in salt and dry them, dust with flour and bread them nicely
3. Squeeze lemon juice over, add sour cream, butter-fried breadcrumbs, a little butter, finely chopped lemon peel and a little pea broth
4. Braise briefly and serve
