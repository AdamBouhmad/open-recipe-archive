## Ingredients

- middle sections of carp
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- finely chopped red onion
- butter
- paprika
- fish stock
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean the middle sections of carp and salt them
2. Place in a pan and add water and vinegar, but only enough to not completely cover the fish
3. Allow to cook slowly
4. Sauté finely chopped red onion in butter until golden
5. Add half a teaspoon of paprika and let it stew
6. Add a little fish stock and two deciliters of sour cream
7. Bring the sauce to a boil and skim off foam
8. Strain the sauce
9. Place the carp in a serving dish and pour a little sauce over it
10. Serve the remaining sauce separately in a cup
