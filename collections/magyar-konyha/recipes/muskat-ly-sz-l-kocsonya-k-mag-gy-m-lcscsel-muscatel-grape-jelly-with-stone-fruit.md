## Ingredients

- well-ripened flavorful grapes
- forty-two dekagrams sugar
- seven deciliters grape juice
- juice of three lemons
- four and one-half dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Use a ring-shaped mold with a center opening that has a bottom, embedded in ice
2. Remove ripe flavorful grapes from their stems and extract the juice by pressing through cloth
3. For seven deciliters of juice, prepare forty-two dekagrams of sugar
4. Cook the sugar with a cup of ordinary water until it begins to form threads
5. Pour into the juice and mix well together
6. Squeeze in the juice of three lemons
7. Add four and one-half dekagrams of dissolved isinglass
