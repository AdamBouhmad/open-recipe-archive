## Ingredients

- cabbage
- fat
- flour
- red onion
- paprika
- catfish

## Directions

1. Cook the cabbage for one hour
2. Prepare a thin roux and fry the red onion and paprika in it
3. Dilute the cabbage in this mixture
4. Clean and rinse the catfish, cut it into pieces and add it
5. Cook together for a while, but in such a way that it is not very thick and enough broth remains
