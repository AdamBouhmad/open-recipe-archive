## Ingredients

- head lettuce, beautiful yellow
- butter
- sour cream
- breadcrumbs
- veal cutlet for garnish

## Directions

1. Take beautiful yellow head lettuce, remove the green leaves
2. Cut each head into four pieces
3. Wash several times until clean
4. Blanch in salted water, but it should not be too soft
5. Drain the water
6. Coat a plate with butter
7. Add four to five spoonfuls of sour cream
8. Add lettuce and continue layering
9. Top with sour cream and several small pieces of butter
10. Sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs
11. Place in the oven and cook through
12. Garnish with veal cutlet
