## Ingredients

- same as foamed milk
- 28 dekagrams ripe strawberries, pressed through a sieve
- 28 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- 3.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Prepare the same foamed milk as in the previous recipe
2. Mix 28 dekagrams of ripe sieved strawberries with an equal amount of finely ground sugar
3. Fold this together with the foamed milk and 3.5 dekagrams of dissolved isinglass
4. Pour into an attractive mold and freeze on ice
5. Before turning out, dip the mold for a moment in boiling water, dry it, and turn the foam out onto a plate
