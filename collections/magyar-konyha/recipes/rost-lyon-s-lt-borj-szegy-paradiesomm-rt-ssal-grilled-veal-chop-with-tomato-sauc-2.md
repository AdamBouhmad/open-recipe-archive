## Ingredients

- nice marbled beef cut
- fat
- fresh parsley
- red onion
- lemon zest
- whole pepper
- vinegar
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Beat the beef, wash and salt it
2. Place fat, fresh parsley, red onion, lemon zest, and whole pepper in a pot
3. Add the beef and braise slowly with a small amount of liquid
4. When the meat is tender, pour vinegar and sour cream over it
