## Ingredients

- firm head cabbage
- kohlrabi
- carrot
- equal parts of each
- four or five button mushrooms, thinly sliced
- butter or goose fat
- nutmeg
- pepper
- meat broth
- toasted bread, sliced lengthwise

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage, kohlrabi, and carrot into equal parts
2. Add the thinly sliced mushrooms
3. Heat butter or goose fat in a pot and add the cut vegetables
4. Braise for about half an hour
5. Add a little nutmeg and pepper
6. Pour meat broth over the vegetables
7. Cook until the vegetables are tender
8. Serve on toasted, lengthwise-sliced bread
