## Ingredients

- Mutton meat
- Salt
- Paprika
- Water

## Directions

1. Cut the mutton into beautiful pieces
2. In a kettle or pot, add salt and paprika proportional to the amount of meat, then add the meat and water so it does not completely cover it
3. Do not stir with a spoon, but shake the pot and turn over several times while cooking
