## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram fresh mutton tallow
- 75 dekagrams vegetable oil
- 13 dekagrams hair powder
- 4.5 dekagrams amber coloring
- 3 dekagrams orange flower oil
- 2 dekagrams cedarwood oil
- 2 dekagrams musk coloring
- 70 drops cinnamon oil
- 4 grams Peruvian balsam
- 4 grams rosewood oil
- 2 grams clove oil
- 4 grams thyme oil

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients with the fresh mutton tallow base
