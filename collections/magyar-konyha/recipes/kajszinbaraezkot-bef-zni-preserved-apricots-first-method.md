## Ingredients

- well-ripened apricots
- fifty-six dekagrams sugar
- one glass of water
- seventy dekagrams syrup

## Directions

1. Peel well-ripened apricots after removing the pits and press through a sieve. Cook them with fifty-six dekagrams of sugar and one glass of water. During cooking, continuously remove the scum to make it completely clean. When it begins to form heavy drops, add the syrup. For fifty-six dekagrams of sugar, add seventy dekagrams of syrup and cook quickly, always stirring well so it does not burn. When a test drop from the preserve does not run on a plate but stays in place, it is done cooking. Remove from heat and pour into jars.
