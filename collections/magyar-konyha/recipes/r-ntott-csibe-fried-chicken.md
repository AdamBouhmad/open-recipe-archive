## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- flour
- egg thinned with water
- fine breadcrumbs
- hot fat
- fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the chicken and cut into pieces
2. Leave in salt for a time
3. Coat each piece one after another in flour
4. Dip in egg thinned with water
5. Coat with fine breadcrumbs
6. Fry in hot fat
7. Arrange on a platter
8. Sprinkle with fried parsley
9. Serve warm
