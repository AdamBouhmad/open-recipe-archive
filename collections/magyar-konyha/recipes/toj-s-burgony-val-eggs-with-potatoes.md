## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- butter
- sour cream
- eggs
- salt
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Slice the cooked potatoes
2. Add pieces of butter and several tablespoons of sour cream
3. In a baking dish, alternate layers of potatoes and eggs
4. Season each layer with salt, butter, and sour cream
5. Continue layering until the dish is full, ending with a potato layer
6. Cover everything with sour cream
7. Add pieces of butter on top and sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs
8. Place hot coals underneath and on top, and bake until golden brown
