## Ingredients

- three kilograms kidney roast beef
- bacon (to lard)
- salt
- beef broth
- butter
- lemon zest
- green peas
- salt water
- fresh parsley (finely chopped)
- beef broth
- sugar (crushed)
- hot butter
- three kilograms beef from the chuck
- sour cabbage
- macaroni
- salt water
- fresh hot butter
- lean finely chopped ham
- carrots (one and a half centimeter pieces)
- butter (half egg-sized)
- sugar (crushed)

- artichokes

## Directions

1. Clean the kidney roast beef of all connective tissue from the wider part
2. Pierce it in 3-4 rows, season with salt
3. Prepare for braising as previously described, ensuring the bacon remains whole and the beef roasts to a nice brown
4. Before serving, completely coat the beef in a small amount of sauce that should be glace-like consistency, then slice
5. Similarly cook three kilograms of chuck beef
6. Serve the beef surrounded by a crown of garnishes: green peas (boiled in salted water, mixed with finely chopped parsley, a little broth, and crushed sugar in hot butter, then shaken well and braised slightly to maintain their green color)
7. Prepare sour cabbage braised to a nice brown-yellow as described in the book
8. Cook macaroni in salted water, toss in fresh hot butter, sprinkle generously with lean finely chopped ham
9. Prepare carrots by cutting into one and a half centimeter pieces, shaping the bottom point finely and the top proportionally into a head
10. Melt half an egg-sized piece of butter, stir in a spoon of crushed sugar, brown to golden yellow, pour in a spoonful of white broth, add the carrots, season lightly with salt, shake several times until they become completely soft but remain whole
11. Before serving, cut the white-cooked meat and kidney roast beef into uniform pieces, arrange on a long platter in cross pattern so one row is white meat and the next is brown meat
12. Arrange the crown of garnishes around the meat (green peas, braised cabbage, macaroni with ham, carrots) in high, evenly wide strips
