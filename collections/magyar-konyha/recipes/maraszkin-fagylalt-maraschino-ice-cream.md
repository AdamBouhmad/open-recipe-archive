## Ingredients

- 21 dekagrams sugar
- 4 deciliters water
- approximately 4 deciliters maraschino liqueur
- juice of 6 lemons

## Directions

1. Cook 21 dekagrams sugar in 4 deciliters water
2. Pour in approximately 4 deciliters maraschino liqueur
3. Press in the juice of 6 lemons
4. Proceed as with other ice creams
5. Work quickly and salt the ice heavily around the freezing container, as alcoholic ice creams freeze with difficulty
