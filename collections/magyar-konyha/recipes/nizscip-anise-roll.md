## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams butter beaten to foam
- 10 egg yolks
- 14 dekagrams yeast dissolved in warm cream
- 2 deciliters lukewarm cream
- salt
- 2 tablespoons ground sugar
- flour as needed
- egg white
- coarsely broken anise and sugar for sprinkling
- sugar and finely chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to foam and gradually mix in the egg yolks
2. Add the dissolved yeast and lukewarm cream
3. Add salt, ground sugar and enough flour so the dough is as thick as beaten egg noodle dough
4. Stir until it separates from the spoon, place in a warm spot and let it rise
5. Form small rolls on a fat-greased baking sheet
6. Brush with egg white and sprinkle thickly with coarsely broken anise and sugar, or with sugar and finely chopped almonds
7. Bake until golden
