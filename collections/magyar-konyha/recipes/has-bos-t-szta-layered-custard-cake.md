## Ingredients

- crumbly kifli cut into long pieces
- cream
- butter
- finely chopped almond
- raisins
- currants
- powdered sugar
- vanilla
- 2 deciliter good cream
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Dampen the kifli pieces with cream
2. Sprinkle the buttered porcelain mold with chopped almonds, raisins, currants, powdered sugar, and vanilla
3. Place one row of soaked kifli and sprinkle similarly with the mixture above
4. Continue until the mold is full
5. Beat together two deciliter cream, three egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla
6. Pour this mixture over the layered cake
7. Bake until done
