## Ingredients

- 42 decagrams flour
- 5 whole eggs
- 5 egg yolks
- 4 spoonfuls sweet sour cream
- salt
- cream and butter

## Directions

1. Mix well together 42 decagrams flour with 5 whole eggs and 5 egg yolks, 4 spoonfuls sweet sour cream, and a little salt
2. Beat all parts of the dough with the axe handle for a good hour
3. Form small pretzels from it and place them in hot water, 7-8 on a cooking spoon (head down) in the center of the pot
4. When the water brings the pretzels up, lift the spoon and remove them, quickly drain the water, place the pretzels on a board and press down by hand one by one to correct form, continuing until all are cooked
5. Turn them in creamy butter, place on a sheet close together, and bake in a moderately hot oven
6. This amount makes about 80 pretzels, which are good for coffee and tea and have the advantage of keeping for weeks without hardening or losing their flavor
