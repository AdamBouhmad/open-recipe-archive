## Ingredients

- strong coffee
- 7 deciiliters cream
- 9 dekagrams butter
- 4 cooking spoonfuls fine flour
- powdered sugar to taste
- 1 whole egg
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Mix strong coffee with cream in the proportion of 2 cups coffee to 7 deciiliters cream
2. Melt 9 dekagrams of butter in a pot and mix in 4 cooking spoonfuls of fine flour
3. Gradually add the hot coffee cream while stirring constantly, making a smooth paste; add the coffee gradually
4. When well cooked, cool it
5. Mix in powdered sugar to taste
6. Add 1 whole egg and 5 egg yolks one after another
7. Finally fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
8. Butter a mold or pot, pour in the mixture and bake slowly or cook in a steam bath
