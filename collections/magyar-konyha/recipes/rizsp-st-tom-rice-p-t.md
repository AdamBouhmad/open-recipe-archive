## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams rice
- milk
- crayfish butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- veal ear
- asparagus
- crayfish tails
- button mushrooms
- butter
- meat broth
- crumbly pastry

## Directions

1. Cook 14 dekagrams rice in milk until thick and cool it
2. Beat crayfish butter and add 4 egg yolks to it
3. Add the cooled rice and 4 egg whites and mix gently
4. Prepare a diced filling from veal ear, asparagus, crayfish tails, and button mushrooms
5. Melt some crayfish butter and brown the filling in it
6. Beat 2 egg yolks in a small cup, dilute with cold meat broth, pour over the filling and mix well
7. Prepare crumbly pastry and line a pot with it
8. Place half of the rice mixture in the pot
9. Layer the filling on top
10. Spread the remaining rice mixture on top and smooth it nicely
11. Prepare a pastry lid
12. Brush with egg yolk and bake slowly
