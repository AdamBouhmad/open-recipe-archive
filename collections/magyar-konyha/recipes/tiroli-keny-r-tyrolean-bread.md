## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams softened butter
- 28 dekagrams blanched and ground almonds
- 28 dekagrams imperial flour
- 14 dekagrams sifted sugar
- 1 dekagram anise
- juice and finely chopped zest of 1/2 orange
- 5 egg yolks
- a little genuine Tokay wine

## Directions

1. Work 28 dekagrams of softened butter, the same amount of blanched and ground almonds, the same amount of imperial flour, 14 dekagrams of sifted sugar, 1 dekagram of anise, the juice and finely chopped zest of 1/2 orange, 5 egg yolks, and a little genuine Tokay wine into a smooth dough in a mixing bowl
2. Knead lightly by hand on a board for a while longer
3. Roll the dough into thin layers
4. Fold in half three times, then roll out again to knife-blade thickness
5. Cut into equal boat shapes
6. Place on butter-coated sheet metal and bake at gentle heat until light golden
7. When cooled, brush with fine apricot marmalade and place another on top so that the two lower parts of the boats are joined by the apricot marmalade
