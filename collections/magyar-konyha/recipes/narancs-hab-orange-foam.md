## Ingredients

- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 6 large oranges and 8 small oranges, for juice
- 4 lemons, for juice
- fresh egg white
- 4.5 dekagrams dissolved, strained, cooled isinglass

## Directions

1. Rub 42 dekagrams of sugar on 3 oranges, being careful not to rub too deeply lest it become bitter
2. Squeeze the juice from 6 large and 8 small oranges and mix it with the juice of 4 lemons
3. Pour over the sugar to dissolve it
4. If not sweet enough, add a little more sugar
5. Pour this juice into a deep preserving basin, set on ice, add fresh egg white, and beat with a whip until the entire mass becomes foam
6. Cool the juice very well on ice beforehand and keep it on ice during beating
7. If the mixture will not come together, add a little more egg white
8. Finally, fold in 4.5 dekagrams of previously strained and cooled dissolved isinglass
9. Pour into a mold, set on ice until frozen
10. The same method can be used to make foam from any fresh fruit, such as raspberries, currants, or cherries; press the fruit through cloth, dissolve the sugar with it, and proceed in the same manner
