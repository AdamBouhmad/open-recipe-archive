## Ingredients

- 500 grams Spanish cherries with stems half-cut
- 500 grams finely crushed sugar
- 1 coffee cup good white vinegar

## Directions

1. Place a few spoonfuls of sugar in a preserving jar.
2. Layer alternately: cherries, sugar, cherries, sugar, until all is used.
3. Pour 1 coffee cup of good white vinegar over it.
4. Tie the jar and leave in the sun for five to six days during which time the sugar dissolves.
5. Strain the liquid and cook it until it forms heavy drops.
6. Cool it, pour over the cherries, and tie closed only after 24 hours.
