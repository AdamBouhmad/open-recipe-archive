## Ingredients

- lentils
- salt
- fried pike head or carp head or carp head
- fried breadcrumbs
- butter
- flour
- pike liver
- toasted breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Clean the lentils thoroughly, wash them, and cook them.
2. Salt them.
3. Make a brown broth, using fried pike head, carp head, or carp head.
4. Crush this with fried breadcrumb pieces in a mortar.
5. Make a brown roux with butter and flour.
6. Pour the lentils and the crushed mixture together with the broth into it.
7. Cook well.
8. Slightly sour it, strain through a sieve, salt, and serve over fried pike liver and toasted breadcrumb slices.
