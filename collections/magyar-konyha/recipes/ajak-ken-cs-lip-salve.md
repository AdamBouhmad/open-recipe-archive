## Ingredients

- seven dekagrams fresh pork fat
- four and one-half dekagrams white wax
- two dekagrams jasmine oil
- twelve grams beef marrow
- twenty drops of clove oil

## Directions

1. Melt seven dekagrams of fresh pork fat, four and one-half dekagrams of white wax, two dekagrams of jasmine oil, and twelve grams of beef marrow over gentle heat
2. Remove the mixture from the heat once melted
3. Add twenty drops of clove oil
