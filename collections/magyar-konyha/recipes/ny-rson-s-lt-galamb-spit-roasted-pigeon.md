## Ingredients

- Pigeons
- Bacon slices
- Salt

## Directions

1. Clean the pigeons and salt them
2. Wrap them in bacon slices
3. Thread three pigeons onto a spit
4. Roast until beautifully golden brown
5. Remove from spit and take off the bacon wrapping
6. Serve garnished with small green salad
