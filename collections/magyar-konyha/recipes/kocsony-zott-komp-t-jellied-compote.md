## Ingredients

- 700 ml sour cream
- 3 tablespoons canned apricots
- 10 grams fine sugar
- 1.5 grams dissolved isinglass
- quince or Masánszki apple
- 3 oranges
- sugar
- water
- lemon

## Directions

1. Whip 700 ml sour cream into foam and place on a sieve to drain well
2. Mix 3 tablespoons canned apricots with 10 grams fine sugar, then add the foam little by little
3. Stir in 1.5 grams dissolved isinglass
4. Pour into a pan and freeze
5. Cook quince in water and sugar, or Masánszki apple with lemon, water and sugar
6. Prepare orange peel as described in the previous recipe
7. Peel orange clean, separate into segments and dip in concentrated sugar, then chill
8. Layer the compote at serving time
9. Top with jellied fruit cut into decorative shapes, arrange the orange peel neatly on top
