## Ingredients

- Half kilogram stewed veal
- half red onion
- little fresh parsley
- clarified goose fat or butter
- flour
- half serving spoon good broth
- fine pancakes
- egg white
- fine breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Chop the stewed veal finely with half red onion and a little fresh parsley.
2. In clarified goose fat or butter, brown a spoonful of flour, add the chopped veal, pour half a serving spoon of good broth over it, and cook down until thick.
3. Meanwhile, make fine pancakes and cut them into squares.
4. Spread filling on each pancake, roll them up, dip in beaten egg and fine breadcrumbs, then fry in fat.
5. When serving, stack them in a cone shape.
