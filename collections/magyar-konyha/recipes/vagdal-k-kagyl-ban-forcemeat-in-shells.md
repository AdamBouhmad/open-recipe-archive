## Ingredients

- 5 decagrams butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3 deciliters good milk
- ground veal or meat (vagdalék)
- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- butter for greasing
- calf's sweetbread ragout
- veal ragout
- asparagus
- crayfish tails
- button mushrooms (csiperke-gomba)
- marrow carbonate for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare a béchamel sauce from 5 decagrams butter, 2 tablespoons flour, and 3 deciliters good milk
2. Mix the forcemeat (vagdalék) into the béchamel, season with salt
3. Beat in 1 whole egg and 1 egg yolk, mix well
4. Butter a shell dish (kagylókat) generously
5. Prepare a ragout from calf's sweetbread (borjúkedes), asparagus, crayfish tails, and button mushrooms, reduce the sauce until thick
6. Beat 2 egg yolks into the ragout
7. Fill the buttered shells with forcemeat to a thickness of one finger all around
8. Fill the center with the ragout
9. Cover with the remaining forcemeat to finger thickness
10. Braise for one hour
11. Turn out onto a serving dish and garnish with marrow carbonate
