## Ingredients

- Sixteen ripe, beautiful large apricots
- Two emperor's rolls (czipó)
- Milk
- Ten dekagrams softened almonds
- Seven dekagrams butter
- Six egg yolks
- Sugar to taste
- Four egg whites

## Directions

1. Peel the apricots and remove the pits
2. Stew the apricots in a pan with frequent shaking until soft
3. Press through a sieve and cool
4. Gently grate the crust of two emperor's rolls
5. Soak in milk, squeeze well, and chop finely
6. Finely grind ten dekagrams of softened almonds
7. Beat seven dekagrams of butter until fluffy
8. Mix in the six egg yolks one by one
9. First mix in the apricot, then the almonds and bread
10. Add enough sugar to taste sweet
11. When well mixed, gently fold in the stiffly beaten whites of four eggs
12. Pour into a pan lined with butter and bake
