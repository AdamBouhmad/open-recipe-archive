## Ingredients

- 21 dekagrams sugar
- large orange, rind
- 8 egg yolks
- 7 deciliters hot cream

## Directions

1. Finely rub sugar on a large orange
2. Beat well with 8 egg yolks
3. During continuous beating, slowly pour in 7 deciliters hot cream
4. Beat on a gentle fire until it begins to thicken
5. Quickly pour into another pot so it doesn't curdle
6. Beat in that pot until it cools
7. Strain through a sieve
8. Place in the container and treat as with the previous one
