## Ingredients

- 42 decagrams almonds (including a few bitter ones) per 7 deciliters water
- 7 deciliters water
- 28 decagrams crushed sugar
- 14 decagrams quince paste
- 14 decagrams green plums preserved in sugar
- 14 decagrams preserved sour cherries
- 7 decagrams green walnuts

## Directions

1. Prepare strong almond milk by swelling the almonds and leaving them in cold water for half an hour, grinding them finely into a paste, thinning with 7 deciliters water, setting aside for half an hour, pressing through cloth, grinding again, pouring the almond milk over again, pressing through, and dissolving 28 decagrams crushed sugar in it
2. Cut the quince paste, preserved green plums, preserved sour cherries, and green walnuts into cubes
3. Pour the almond milk into a pan and proceed as with other ice creams; when already half-frozen, mix in the preserved fruits and freeze completely
