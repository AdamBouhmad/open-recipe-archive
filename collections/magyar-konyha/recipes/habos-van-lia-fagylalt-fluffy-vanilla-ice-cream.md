## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters cream
- 7 deciliters good cream
- ground sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Prepare ice cream from 7 deciliters of cream as in the previous recipe
2. When cooled, whip 7 deciliters of good cream into stiff peaks
3. Quickly mix with finely crushed sugar and vanilla
4. Combine the whole with the cooled mixture, pour into the mold, and place on ice, where it freezes very quickly even without turning
5. If you want to freeze it quickly and strongly, turn it for a quarter hour
