## Ingredients

- fourteen dekagrams butter, beaten to foam
- twelve dekagrams sugar
- three egg yolks
- fourteen dekagrams flour
- three egg whites
- seven dekagrams currants
- seven dekagrams raisins
- ten dekagrams citron peel, chopped coarsely
- two tablets chocolate, chopped coarsely

## Directions

1. Add sugar and egg yolks to the foamed butter
2. Add flour, the stiff snow of egg whites, currants, raisins, citron peel, and chocolate
3. Spread on buttered sheet at finger thickness
4. Bake slowly
5. While still warm, cut into rods
