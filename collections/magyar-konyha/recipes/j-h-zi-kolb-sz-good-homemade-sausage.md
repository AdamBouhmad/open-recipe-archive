## Ingredients

- 5 kilos mixed meat, finely chopped but not too fine
- half liter cold water
- quarter liter wine
- abundant black pepper, ground
- marjoram
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop the mixed meat well but not too finely
2. Mix together the meat, cold water, wine, abundant black pepper, marjoram, and salt
3. Work everything together thoroughly so that the spices, salt, water and wine penetrate evenly throughout
4. Fill casings with the mixture, packing firmly
5. Optionally add lemon or garlic to the filling
6. Smoke as soon as possible for more flavor
7. Smoke quickly to prevent the sausage from drying out
