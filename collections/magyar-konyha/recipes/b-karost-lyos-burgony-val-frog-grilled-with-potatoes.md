## Ingredients

- large frogs
- butter, melted
- breadcrumbs, fine
- cooked potatoes
- bacon
- ham
- red onion, sliced
- carrots
- flour
- stock, 1 coffee cup
- lemon, juice of 1

## Directions

1. Cut thighs from large frogs and remove the bones, joining two thighs together
2. Re-insert the removed bone on the other side
3. Coat the small grilled pieces with melted butter and coat with fine breadcrumbs
4. Roast over gentle coals, pouring melted butter over several times until they become a beautiful golden brown color
5. Peel cooked potatoes and cut into walnut-sized pieces
6. Shape into rounds and slowly braise in a pot with several pieces of bacon, ham, red onion slices and carrots
7. Pour several spoonfuls of stock over them multiple times so they brown and soften nicely
8. Remove and sprinkle a little flour over them
9. Brown the flour, pour in one coffee cup of stock and cook well together
10. Squeeze the juice of one lemon into it
11. Arrange the potatoes in a ring shape on the dish
12. Strain the sauce through a sieve and place the grilled pieces in two rows in the middle of the dish
13. The sauce should not cover the grilled pieces, so take a small long dish and arrange them so that the bone shows from each one and the sauce is so little that it covers neither the potatoes nor the grilled pieces
