## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- 3 liters water
- 1 liter well water
- 28 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- 4.5 dekagrams isinglass, boiled down to 5 spoonfuls

## Directions

1. Pour 3 liters of water over 14 dekagrams sweet almonds and 6 bitter almonds and let stand 24 hours, after which they can be easily peeled.
2. Alternatively, if time is short, place in hot water to peel, then place in fresh water for a while to harden and make them brittle.
3. Remove, dry with clean cloth, and pound to paste in mortar.
4. Place the ground almonds in a porcelain pan, pour 1 liter of well water over them, and stir finely with silver spoon.
5. Strain through cloth, pour the strained liquid back over the almonds, and strain again, pressing with greatest force until all liquid is extracted.
6. Beat this almond milk with 28 dekagrams finely ground sugar.
7. Mix in 4.5 dekagrams isinglass that has been boiled down to 5 spoonfuls.
8. Strain again through cloth, pour into molds, and place on ice.
