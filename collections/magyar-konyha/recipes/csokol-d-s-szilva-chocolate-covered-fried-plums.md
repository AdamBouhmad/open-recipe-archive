## Ingredients

- seven deciliters dried plums
- hot fat
- three tablets chocolate
- ten decagrams powdered sugar
- sixteen to twenty apple slices
- candied cherries
- common pastry dough

## Directions

1. Wash the dried plums and scald without cooking them soft
2. When cooled and dried well, remove the pit
3. Place in hot fat and shake the pan continuously to fry quickly until crisp
4. Do not leave on heat too long as they burn easily
5. Grate three tablets of chocolate finely and mix with ten decagrams powdered sugar
6. As soon as the plums are removed from the pan and cleaned of fat, place in the chocolate mixture
7. Stir well with a spoon until completely coated
8. Heap high in the center of a platter
9. Cut out the core from sixteen to twenty apple slices with a small cutter
10. Dip in common pastry dough and fry quickly in hot fat
11. Let fat drip well and sprinkle heavily with powdered sugar until completely white
12. Place a candied cherry with stem in the opening in the center of each apple slice
13. Arrange apple slices around the edge of the platter
