## Ingredients

- aspic (prepared as in previous recipe)
- thinly sliced good Verona salami
- thinly sliced cold pheasant or woodcock, or turkey roast if unavailable
- thinly sliced ham

## Directions

1. Prepare aspic as in the previous recipe
2. Once it has set, pour an amount two-finger-widths thick into a basin and set on ice to gel
3. When set, arrange a layer of thinly sliced Verona salami to cover the bottom
4. Pour another two-finger-width layer of aspic over it and allow to gel
5. Arrange a layer of thinly sliced cold pheasant or woodcock, or turkey roast
6. Pour aspic over and let gel, then add a layer of thinly sliced ham
7. Pour aspic over and continue this process until the basin is full
