## Ingredients

- two cleaned chickens
- two rolls
- milk
- butter
- green parsley
- 5-6 eggs
- salt
- spices

## Directions

1. Soak two rolls in milk and squeeze out
2. Braise the squeezed rolls with green parsley in butter
3. When the filling has cooled, beat in 5-6 eggs
4. Season the filling with salt and spices
5. Stuff the chickens through the neck opening
6. Tie them closed
7. Roast on a spit or in a pan until golden brown
