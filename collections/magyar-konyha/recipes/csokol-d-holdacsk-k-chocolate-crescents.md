## Ingredients

- twenty-five dekagrams grated chocolate
- half kilogram finely ground and sifted sugar
- three egg whites

## Directions

1. On a rolling board, mix grated chocolate well with finely ground and sifted sugar
2. Work with a large kitchen knife into a smooth dough using three egg whites
3. If it does not hold together well, add a little more egg white
4. The dough should be rather firm than soft
5. Roll out to knife-width thickness
6. Form crescents with a donut cutter
7. Place on waxed sheet
8. Bake slowly at low heat
