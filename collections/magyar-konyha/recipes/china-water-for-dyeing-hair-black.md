## Ingredients

- 4.5 dekagrams distilled water
- 2 grams silver nitrate
- 50 drops ammonia

## Directions

1. Dissolve silver nitrate in distilled water
2. Add ammonia extract
3. Comb hair well with this mixture
4. Dry in the sun
5. Do not let it touch the skin
