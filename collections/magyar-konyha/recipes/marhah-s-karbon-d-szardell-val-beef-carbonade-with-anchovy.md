## Ingredients

- kidney steak
- salt
- butter
- lemon
- heavy cream
- brains
- parsley
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- eggs

## Directions

1. Trim all fibrous parts from the kidney steak and chop finely
2. Make small carbonades, salt them, coat with melted butter, and place in a pot
3. Squeeze lemon juice over and cover with two deciliters of heavy cream
4. Cover and place coals on top, braising until the sauce reduces
5. Place on a round dish
6. Cut triangular bread slices and fry them
7. Separately, cook brains in salted water, chop them finely, and braise with a little butter, parsley, salt, and pepper
8. Spread this mixture on the fried bread, place another piece of bread on top so the brain does not show
9. Arrange the bread pieces around the dish rim
10. Place the carbonades in the center in a circle, forming a crown
