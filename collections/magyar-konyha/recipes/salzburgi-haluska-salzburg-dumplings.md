## Ingredients

- Four deciliters cream
- Two dekagrams butter
- Flour
- Six egg yolks
- Three egg whites, stiffly beaten
- Salt
- One and one-quarter dekagrams powdered sugar
- Vanilla
- Four additional deciliters cream for cooking

## Directions

1. Put cream and butter in a pot and bring to boil
2. Mix in flour to make a firm, hot dough
3. While still warm, mix in six egg yolks and stiffly beaten egg whites
4. Add salt, powdered sugar and vanilla, mix well
5. Bring four additional deciliters of cream to boil
6. Drop hazelnut-sized dumplings into the boiling cream
7. Remove cooked dumplings and keep warm; repeat until all are cooked
8. Mix six egg yolks with cold cream and blend with the cream the dumplings were cooked in
9. Add sugar and vanilla
10. Place dumplings in a buttered dish, pour sauce over
11. Bake in steam bath for half an hour
12. Unmold and serve
