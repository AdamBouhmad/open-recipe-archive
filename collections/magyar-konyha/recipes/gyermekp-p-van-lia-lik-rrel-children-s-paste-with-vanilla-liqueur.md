## Ingredients

- 6 cooking spoonfuls flour
- 7 deciiliters milk
- 7 dekagrams butter beaten to a foam
- 1 whole egg
- 6 egg yolks
- powdered sugar to taste
- powdered vanilla to taste
- 4 egg whites beaten stiff
- common wafer batter

## Directions

1. Mix 6 cooking spoonfuls of flour finely with 7 deciiliters of cold milk, adding the milk gradually to the flour so it does not become lumpy
2. Place on charcoal fire and stir constantly until thick, then cool
3. Mix the paste into 7 dekagrams of foamy butter
4. Add 1 whole egg and 6 egg yolks and mix together
5. Add powdered sugar and powdered vanilla to taste
6. Stir for half an hour
7. Finally fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
8. Place the paste in a buttered pot and put in a steam bath
9. Make small cones from common wafer batter, treat them like regular waffles but use a rolling pin that is sharpened fine at the bottom instead of a round one, bake the wafer smaller and wrap it around this rolling pin to make small cones
