## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilogram macaroni
- white broth
- 25 dekagrams butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 3 tablespoons sweet milk
- 4 whole eggs
- ham
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Cook 0.5 kilogram macaroni in white broth
2. Melt 25 dekagrams butter and add 1 tablespoon flour, let it simmer slightly
3. Enlarge with 3 tablespoons sweet milk and remove from heat
4. When cooled, mix in 4 whole eggs
5. Add the cooked macaroni and shake well
6. Pour half of the macaroni into a buttered mold
7. Sprinkle a layer of finely chopped ham and finely grated cheese on top
8. Add another layer of macaroni and repeat this three times until the mold is full
9. Cook the pasta in hot water together with the mold for 1.5 hours then turn out
