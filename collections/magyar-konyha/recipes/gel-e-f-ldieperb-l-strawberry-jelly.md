## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilogramm sugar
- 1.25 liters wild or garden strawberries
- 6 dekagramm isinglass
- 0.5 bottle white wine or champagne
- 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar in 3 deciliter water
2. Add the washed strawberries to it
3. Let stand in a porcelain bowl covered for several hours
4. Strain the sugar through a cloth and combine with the lukewarm aspic, wine and the strained juice from 2 lemons
5. Use beautiful garden strawberries that have been washed
