## Ingredients

- tender kohlrabi
- salt water
- butter
- sour cream
- cooked kohlrabi
- good filling (as for stuffed kohlrabi)
- pickled tongue
- egg yolks
- coffee cup of sour cream
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the kohlrabi in salted water after peeling and salting it until it begins to soften slightly
2. Drain and cut into slices of knife-thickness width
3. Melt butter in a pan, quickly mix with a good spoonful of fresh sour cream
4. Add the chopped kohlrabi and braise for a quarter hour with frequent shaking
5. Keep the kohlrabi pale by braising on low heat
6. Prepare a good filling as needed for stuffed kohlrabi
7. Coat the bottom of a buttered pan alternately with a layer of kohlrabi and an equal-sized piece of finely chopped and cooked pickled tongue
8. When the first row is neatly arranged on the bottom, spread the filling smoothly over it
9. Add another row of braised kohlrabi slices, but without pickled tongue this time
10. Then add filling again and continue this way until everything is used
11. Beat three egg yolks with a coffee cup of thin sour cream and a little salt until well mixed
12. Pour this mixture over the kohlrabi and steam for one hour
13. At serving time, turn out onto a dish and pour good butter sauce underneath, which contains finely peeled and chopped kohlrabi cooked in it
14. Border the edges with pickled tongue
