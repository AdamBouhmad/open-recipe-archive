## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- veal, cut into small cubes
- butter sauce
- asparagus, chopped
- crayfish tails
- nutmeg flower, a small amount
- sour cream
- butter
- sausage for garnish

## Directions

1. Peel cooked potatoes and cool them
2. Cut off a piece from each potato so it stands upright on a plate, and cut a lid from the top
3. Carefully hollow out the potatoes
4. Put the diced veal in butter sauce along with chopped asparagus and crayfish tails and a small amount of nutmeg flower, cook everything well so nothing of the sauce can be seen
5. Fill the potatoes with this mixture
6. Replace the lids
7. Place on a plate, add sour cream and butter, and bake until they turn a nice golden color
8. Garnish with sausage if desired
