## Ingredients

- bread cake dough (as per recipe 680)
- lemon frosting
- pistachios

## Directions

1. Pour bread cake dough (as per recipe 680) into small paper cases
2. Bake at gentle heat
3. When baked, coat with lemon frosting and beautifully decorate with finely cut pistachios
4. Place again in the oven that has cooled to the appropriate temperature so the frosting just dries
5. Serve in the cases to the table
