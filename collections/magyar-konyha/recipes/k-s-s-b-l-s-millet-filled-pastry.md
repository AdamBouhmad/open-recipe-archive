## Ingredients

- one and a half kilograms flour
- one tablespoon lard
- two egg yolks
- salt water
- hot lard for brushing
- millet porridge cooked with pepper and plenty of lard, cooled

## Directions

1. Prepare a pastry dough with flour, lard, egg yolks, and salt water (or milk can be used instead of water for a less crusty dough)
2. Knead well until smooth, then stretch it out and let it rest
3. After resting, stretch it out on the table and brush with hot lard, then stretch all the remaining dough layers on top of each other
4. Divide into four parts and fold the parts on top of each other, then cut the resulting thick dough into ten-centimeter square pieces
5. Fill each square with a walnut-sized piece of cooled millet porridge, gather all four edges of the dough together to form small rounded filled pastries, pressing them slightly flat so the porridge spreads inside
6. Bake in a hot oven until beautifully golden—they should bake quickly and rise well
