## Ingredients

- fresh rose petals or violet, carnation, orange blossom, bee balm, jasmine leaves
- good wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Pick fresh rose petals or violet, carnation, orange blossom, bee balm, jasmine or similar flower leaves (depending on which type of vinegar you wish to make)
2. Clean them of any insects that may be on them
3. Place them unwashed in a bottle so that it fills three-quarters of the bottle
4. Pour good wine vinegar into it almost completely full
5. Tie it with perforated paper and leave it in the sun for 10-14 days to infuse
6. Then strain it and place it in a clean, well-stoppered bottle
7. Full violet leaves are worth more than the sucker ones
8. These vinegars are good not only for salads but for all kinds of chopped dishes and sauces
