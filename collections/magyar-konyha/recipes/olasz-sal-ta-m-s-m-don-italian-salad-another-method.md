## Ingredients

- Two herring, cleaned of skin
- Two pieces marinated eel
- Two spools of fish
- Seven dekagrams tuna
- Seven dekagrams marinated salmon
- Four to five sardelles
- Small seafood crustaceans
- Capers
- Pistachios
- Vinegar with oil

## Directions

1. For five or six servings, cut two cleaned herrings, two pieces of marinated eel, two spools of fish, seven dekagrams of tuna, the same amount of marinated salmon, and four to five sardelles into slices
2. Arrange these pieces together neatly on a flat plate so that the whole has an attractive appearance
3. On top, as decoration, place small seafood crustaceans, capers, and pistachios as desired
4. Pour vinegar mixed with fine oil over the entire dish
