## Ingredients

- 2-3 kilograms well-ripened, peeled Bergamot pears with stems removed
- 1.5 liters vinegar
- 80 dekagrams sugar
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. For 2 to 3 kilograms of well-ripened, peeled Bergamot pears, cook 1.5 liters vinegar with 80 dekagrams sugar and soften the pears in it.
2. Cook the vinegar slightly more with cloves and cinnamon and strain through cloth over the pears.
3. Repeat this process for two days or until the vinegar becomes thin again.
4. Then pour hot over the pears, cover with paper or bladder, and tie very firmly.
