## Ingredients

- beautiful ripe quinces
- 1/2 kilogram sugar per 1/2 kilogram clear juice

## Directions

1. Peel beautiful ripe quinces, remove the cores, crush the quinces in a large mortar, press through cloth and let stand for one hour so the thick part settles, then strain the juice and prepare it like other juices.
2. Use the same amount of sugar for each 1/2 kilogram of clear juice.
