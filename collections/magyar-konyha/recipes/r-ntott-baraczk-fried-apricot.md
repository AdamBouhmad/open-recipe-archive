## Ingredients

- Beautiful large apricots
- A few apricots
- A piece of citronage
- Citron peel
- Sugar
- Wine
- Sugar
- Flour
- Fat

## Directions

1. Peel beautiful large apricots and remove their pits
2. Finely chop a few apricots, a piece of citronage, and citron peel together
3. Add a piece of sugar and stew slightly
4. Fill the apricots with this mixture
5. Fasten together with small wooden skewers
6. Dip in thin pastry made from wine, sugar, and flour
7. Quickly fry in fat until beautifully golden
8. Sprinkle with sugar and serve warm to the table
