## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams softened and finely pounded almonds
- 28 dekagrams finely pounded sugar
- 1 lemon, zest, finely chopped
- 2 whole eggs
- 6 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- butter for greasing the mold

## Directions

1. Place the softened and finely pounded almonds, the same amount of finely pounded sugar, and the finely chopped zest of one lemon in a bowl, into which beat 2 whole eggs and 6 egg yolks one after another and whip this for half an hour
2. The duration of whipping cannot be precisely determined; generally it is enough when the batter is already white and begins to rise in the bowl; it must never be whipped so long that it falls together again, otherwise the cake will be very hard
3. Once the batter is good, gently mix in the stiff snow of 3 egg whites, place the mixture in a mold greased with butter, and bake slowly
4. A whipped cake must never be exposed to great heat; once it is baked, which can be easily determined with a testing needle, turn it out and cool it and cover it with such a chocolate icing whose preparation method is found in no. 683
