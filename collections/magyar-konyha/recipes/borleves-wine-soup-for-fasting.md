## Ingredients

- One liter of good white wine
- Sugar, a piece
- Cinnamon, a small amount
- Lemon peel
- Cloves, a few
- Egg yolks, four
- Sugar, for dusting
- Bread slices

## Directions

1. Bring one liter of good white wine to a boil with a piece of sugar, a small amount of cinnamon, lemon peel, and a few cloves
2. Before serving, remove the spices
3. In a bowl, mix together four egg yolks and slowly pour the hot wine into them so they do not curdle
4. Serve with bread slices dusted with sugar
