## Ingredients

- three lamb chops, kept in fresh water
- red onion, sliced
- parsley root, chopped
- celery
- carrots
- butter
- bacon
- fine parsley
- morels, cleaned
- flour
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place the lamb chops in a copper pan lined with bacon and fat, along with red onion, parsley root, celery, carrots, and butter
2. Cover well and braise until tender
3. In a separate pot, melt butter and add finely chopped parsley
4. Add cleaned morels and soften
5. Add three spoonfuls of flour and braise together for a time
6. Pour the lamb stock over the mushrooms
7. Squeeze lemon juice over it
8. Add the lamb chops and simmer until fully cooked
