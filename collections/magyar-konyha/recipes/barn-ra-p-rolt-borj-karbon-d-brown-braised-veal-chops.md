## Ingredients

- veal chops
- bacon slices
- red onion
- carrot
- flour
- broth
- lemon zest
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the veal chops well, remove the membrane from the bone so the bone is completely clean where the meat begins
2. Beat well and shape into chops with a broad knife, season with salt
3. Place a few slices of bacon, sliced red onion, and carrot in a pot
4. Place the chops on top and braise slowly, turning several times so both sides brown
5. Remove and sprinkle flour into the braising liquid and brown well
6. Dilute with broth and cook the sauce for a good half hour
7. Strain through a sieve and remove the fat
8. Place the chops in with lemon zest and the juice of one lemon
9. Arrange neatly in a wreath shape and pour the sauce over
