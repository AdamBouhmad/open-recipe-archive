## Ingredients

- puff pastry folded five times
- egg white
- soaked almond, chopped
- vanilla sugar, finely ground
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry folded five times
2. Roll it out to knife-thickness
3. Cut into strips a span long and two fingers wide
4. Brush with egg white
5. Sprinkle with soaked chopped almond and finely ground vanilla sugar
6. Bake on a wafer form until golden
7. Before cooling, sprinkle with sugar and serve on the table
8. In the same way from the puff pastry cut long, narrow sticks
9. Brush these also with egg white, sprinkle with almond and vanilla sugar
10. Bake on a pan, sprinkle with sugar while hot, and pile high on the plate
