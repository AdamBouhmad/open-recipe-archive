## Ingredients

- 28 dekagramm finely ground sugar
- 12 whole eggs
- 19 dekagramm fine flour
- whipped cream
- sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Put 28 dekagramm finely ground sugar into a brass bowl
2. Beat 12 whole eggs over it and beat well with the whisk
3. Place over gentle charcoal heat and beat continuously until the mixture becomes thick and white, handling the heat carefully so the mixture does not boil
4. When thick and white, remove from heat and beat a while longer
5. With the flat side of a cooking spoon, stir in 19 dekagramm fine flour
6. Fill a piping bag with the mixture and pipe small carnival fritter-sized drops onto white paper
7. Sprinkle with finely ground sugar
8. Place on a baking sheet and bake at gentle heat until beautiful and crispy
9. Once baked, carefully remove from the sheet
10. Fill with whipped cream that has been prepared beforehand with sugar and vanilla
11. Stack them two by two so they form a fritter shape
12. Arrange on a plate lined with a kitchen cloth
13. Serve quickly to the table
14. These fritters can also be filled with ice cream or strawberry foam
