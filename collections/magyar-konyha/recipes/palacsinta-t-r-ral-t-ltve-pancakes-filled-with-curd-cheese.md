## Ingredients

- four decilitres of milk
- three eggs
- fourteen dekagrams of butter beaten until frothy
- two whole eggs
- six egg yolks
- one kilogram of grated curd cheese
- four decilitres of good sour cream
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. From four decilitres of milk and three eggs, make thin pancakes
2. Prepare the filling: beat fourteen dekagrams of butter until frothy, mix in two whole eggs and six egg yolks, then combine with one kilogram of grated curd cheese, four decilitres of good sour cream, a little salt, and enough sugar to sweeten it well
3. Fill the pancakes with this mixture
4. Fold them together, trim the two ends, place them in a butter-greased mold, and bake them in the oven
5. Serve dusted with sugar on the table
