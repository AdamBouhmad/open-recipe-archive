## Ingredients

- Dumplings as described in recipe 526
- Cabbage, finely chopped
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare dumplings as described in recipe 526
2. Braise finely chopped cabbage in butter
3. When lightly browned, season lightly with salt and pepper
4. Mix with the dumplings and serve
