## Ingredients

- large Italian chestnuts
- 21 dekagrams broken sugar
- half a cup water

## Directions

1. Roast the large Italian chestnuts until the shells can be cleanly removed
2. Cook the broken sugar with half a cup of water until it becomes caramel (raw sugar), as one would cook it when making sugar tablets
3. Test if the caramel is ready by dipping a thin stick first in cold water then in the boiling sugar and again in cold water; the sugar should break cleanly and not be sticky, otherwise the chestnut will stick to the teeth
4. Once the sugar is properly cooked, dip the shelled chestnuts one by one into it
5. Place on a stone where they will become beautifully glass-like
6. Arrange immediately in the familiar boxes
