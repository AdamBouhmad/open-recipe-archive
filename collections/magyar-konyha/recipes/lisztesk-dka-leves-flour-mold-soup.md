## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams butter
- 5 egg yolks
- 5 egg whites beaten stiff
- 4-5 tablespoons fine flour
- salt
- crab broth

## Directions

1. Beat 14 dekagrams butter until foamy
2. Mix in 5 egg yolks and 5 stiffly beaten egg whites
3. Add 4-5 tablespoons fine flour and a little salt
4. Beat until foamy
5. Butter the molds, fill halfway with the mixture
6. Bake in the oven
7. Place in the soup bowl and pour crab broth over it
