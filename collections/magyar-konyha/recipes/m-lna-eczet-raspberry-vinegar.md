## Ingredients

- 3 dekagrams fresh good raspberries
- 2 kilograms well-boiled vinegar

## Directions

1. Crush 3 dekagrams of fresh good raspberries slightly, and mix them in a bottle with 2 kilograms of well-boiled vinegar.
2. When the vinegar becomes beautifully red and has a pleasant raspberry smell, carefully pour off the liquid without pressing it, then let it settle.
3. Filter through grey blotting paper, fill small bottles completely with it, bind them tightly with bladder, and place them in the cellar in sand.
