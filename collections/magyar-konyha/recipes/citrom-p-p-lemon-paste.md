## Ingredients

- 14 dekagramm softened and ground almonds
- juice of half a lemon
- 14 dekagramm sugar
- 2 tablespoons water
- 6 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- citronade
- finely chopped lemon zest
- butter

## Directions

1. Place the softened and ground almonds in a bowl and moisten with the juice of half a lemon.
2. Roughly crush 14 dekagramm sugar, pour 2 tablespoons of water over it, and cook until it thickens.
3. Pour the hot syrup over the almonds, mix well, and let cool.
4. Once completely cooled, beat in 6 egg yolks one after another, stirring for three-quarters of an hour.
5. Gently fold in the stiffly beaten whites of 3 eggs.
6. Add citronade and finely chopped lemon zest to the paste.
7. Pour into a buttered porcelain dish and bake slowly in the oven or cook in a bain-marie.
8. Note: this paste must be served at table immediately after baking, as it collapses otherwise.
