## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Fine breadcrumbs
- Hot butter

## Directions

1. Prepare and blanch the chickens as in the previous recipe and braise them in vinegar.
2. Place whole on a serving platter.
3. Sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs and pour hot butter over them.
