## Ingredients

- various fish: pike, carp, sterlet, sturgeon, zander
- salt
- yellow roux
- red onion
- red wine
- water
- fresh parsley
- green onion
- lemon zest
- cloves

## Directions

1. Clean various fish such as pike, carp, sterlet, sturgeon and zander, and let them lie in salt for half an hour.
2. Cut them into pieces.
3. Prepare a little yellow roux, place a red onion cut in half in it.
4. Mix with half red wine and half water.
5. Add fresh parsley, green onion, a little lemon zest and cloves, and cook the sauce well.
6. Add the fish and simmer slowly.
7. Once cooked, place on a plate, strain the sauce, but it must cook well, and place triangular fried bread slices on the edge of the plate.
