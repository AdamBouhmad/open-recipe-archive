## Ingredients

- fourteen dekagrams finely ground sugar
- four egg yolks
- four egg whites
- seven dekagrams fine dry flour
- fine ground sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Beat fourteen dekagrams of finely ground sugar with four egg yolks for fifteen minutes
2. Mix in the stiff snow of four egg whites
3. Finally add seven dekagrams of fine dry flour and mix well
4. Fill paper cases about halfway or slightly higher
5. Sprinkle with finely ground sugar
6. Bake at low heat
