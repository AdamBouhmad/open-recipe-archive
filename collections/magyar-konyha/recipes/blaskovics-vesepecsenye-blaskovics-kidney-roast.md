## Ingredients

- nice rested kidney roast
- salt
- 20 deka butter
- mixed vegetables, sliced into rings
- 3 bay leaves
- 1 whole lemon zest
- 1 ginger root
- a little whole black pepper
- 1/2 liter red wine
- water

## Directions

1. Lard a nice rested kidney roast, salt it, and set aside
2. Melt 20 deka butter in a pan, slice mixed vegetables into rings and place in the butter
3. Place the kidney roast on top, cover and braise until the vegetables turn yellow
4. When this has happened, add among the vegetables three bay leaves, the zest of one whole lemon, one ginger root, and a little whole black pepper, and pour in 1/2 liter red wine mixed with water and braise until the kidney roast becomes tender; if the broth becomes too thick, a little wine can be added
5. When the meat is tender enough, remove it, strain the broth through a sieve and pour it over the meat
