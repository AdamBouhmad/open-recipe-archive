## Ingredients

- an egg-sized piece of crayfish butter beaten fluffy
- 1 whole egg
- 3 egg yolks
- 400 milliliters lukewarm cream
- 37.5 grams fresh cheese yeast
- salt
- flour as needed
- melted crayfish butter for dipping and coating

## Directions

1. Beat the crayfish butter until fluffy
2. Add 1 whole egg and 3 egg yolks one after another
3. Add the lukewarm cream, fresh cheese yeast, salt, and enough flour so the dough can be rolled out well without being too stiff
4. Place in a warm location and let rise
5. Roll out to the thickness of two knife-backs and cut out small noodles with a cutter dipped in melted crayfish butter
6. Arrange the noodles row by row on a small pastry sheet coated with crayfish butter, with its rim also coated, so that the edge of one noodle rests on the next one
7. Once the bottom of the pastry sheet is completely covered, coat with melted crayfish butter and place the second row in the same order on top, coat with crayfish butter, and continue this way until everything is arranged
8. Let it rise in a warm place
9. Before putting it in the oven, coat with crayfish butter and bake at lower heat than the previous recipe so it does not lose its color but still bakes well
10. Turn out onto a platter and serve with warm sugared cream in a bowl
