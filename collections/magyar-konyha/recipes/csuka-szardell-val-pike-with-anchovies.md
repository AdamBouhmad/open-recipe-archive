## Ingredients

- large pike, about two kilograms in the middle
- salt
- eight anchovies
- red onion
- fresh parsley
- lemon zest
- pepper
- sour cream
- lemon juice
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Salt a large middle piece of pike and let it stand for an hour.
2. Dry the fish with a clean cloth.
3. Chop finely together eight anchovies, one red onion, fresh parsley, and one lemon zest, add a little pepper and mix well.
4. Coat the entire piece of fish with this mixture.
5. Place in a baking pan, pour three deciliters of sour cream over it, add the juice of one lemon, several pieces of butter and a handful of breadcrumbs.
6. Place coals on top and cook well.
7. Serve on a plate and pour the sauce over it.
