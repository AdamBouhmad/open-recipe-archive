## Ingredients

- half a kilogram each of: stewed plums, pressed pear, good figs, dates, fresh raisins
- 25 dekagrams citronade
- 25 dekagrams quince paste
- 25 dekagrams Milan chocolate softened in a warm oven
- 25 dekagrams stewed almonds
- 25 dekagrams walnuts
- 25 dekagrams Italian hazelnuts
- a small teaspoon of cinnamon
- brioche dough of a certain quantity
- egg for coating
- butter for coating the paper

## Directions

1. Cut the stewed plums, pressed pear, figs, dates, and fresh raisins into equal cubes, half a kilogram of each
2. Similarly cut the citronade, quince paste, and chocolate into cubes
3. Cut the stewed almonds, walnuts, and Italian hazelnuts into cubes
4. Place all the cut ingredients together in a large bowl with a small teaspoon of cinnamon
5. Knead into a quantity of brioche dough
6. Take about a bread-roll portion of the dough and knead it until all the cut fruit is well incorporated, since the dough merely serves to hold the other sweets together and should barely be noticeable
7. Shape into a long loaf
8. Wrap in rectangular sheets of brioche dough
9. Brush with egg
10. Place on paper coated with butter
11. Bake in a sustained hot oven
