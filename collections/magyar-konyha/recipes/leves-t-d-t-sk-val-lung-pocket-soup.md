## Ingredients

- veal lung
- veal heart
- butter
- parsley
- red onion
- salt
- two eggs
- puff pastry dough
- flour
- white broth
- brown broth

## Directions

1. Cook veal lung and heart until tender
2. Remove the pharynx and chop the rest finely
3. Melt a piece of butter
4. Add finely chopped parsley and a little red onion
5. Sauté slightly
6. Add the chopped lung with a little salt
7. Fry briefly and stir well
8. Add two well-beaten eggs and remove from heat
9. Let cool
10. Prepare common puff pastry dough and roll out finely
11. Fill with the lung mixture leaving one and a half arm-lengths empty
12. Cut this empty part in half down the middle
13. Roll the dough from all sides so the empty dough covers the filled part several times
14. Press with a wooden spoon handle dipped in flour to make small indentations
15. Cut in half with a knife
16. Press the ends together
17. Cook the pockets in white broth
18. Place in bowl and pour brown broth over them
