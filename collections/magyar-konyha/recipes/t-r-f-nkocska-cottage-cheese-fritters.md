## Ingredients

- Puff pastry (prepared as described below)
- Egg white
- Seven dekagrams butter
- Four egg yolks
- Cottage cheese (enough to fill a small coffee cup), finely grated
- Salt
- Three tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry and fold it three times, then roll out to knife-thickness
2. Cut out medium-sized rounds with a donut cutter and place on a baking sheet
3. Brush the centers with egg white
4. Cut smaller rounds with a slightly smaller cutter (small enough to fit inside the first) and place them on top of the first rounds
5. Beat seven dekagrams butter until light, then add egg yolks one at a time
6. Mix in the finely grated cottage cheese, season lightly with salt, and blend in three tablespoons sour cream; the mixture should be thick, not thin
7. Using a knife dipped in sour cream, form the filling into pointed mounds on the centers of the fritters
8. Cut rounds from the second set of pastry pieces using the same smaller cutter, then cut out a small center with an even smaller cutter to create a crown shape
9. Place the crown on top of the cottage cheese mound to partially enclose it
10. Brush the pastry with egg white and bake until a beautiful light golden color
