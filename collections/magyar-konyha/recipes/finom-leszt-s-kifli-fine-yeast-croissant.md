## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters flour
- 280 grams butter
- salt
- 3 tablespoons ground sugar
- little vanilla
- 6 egg yolks
- 36 grams melted fresh cheese yeast
- little sour cream

## Directions

1. Shred 280 grams butter into 7 deciliters flour, then smooth it finely and salt it
2. Add 3 tablespoons ground sugar, little vanilla, 6 egg yolks and 36 grams melted fresh cheese yeast and little sour cream
3. Prepare the dough with a wide knife, it should not be very hard
4. Roll out to twice knife-edge thickness, cut out rectangular leaves from it
5. Fill with preserved apricots or currants, fold together and let rise
6. Brush with beaten egg, sprinkle with ground sugar and finely chopped almonds, and bake at gentle heat
