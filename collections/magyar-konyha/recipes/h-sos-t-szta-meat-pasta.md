## Ingredients

- beautiful capon or young turkey breast
- 1.5 bread rolls, crusts and insides
- 2 deciliters milk
- 9 dekagrams butter
- 3 whole eggs
- salt
- nutmeg flower
- carrots, sliced
- red onion

## Directions

1. Cut the breast from a beautiful capon or young turkey
2. Grate 1.5 bread roll crusts and slice thinly
3. Moisten with 2 deciliters milk
4. Add 9 dekagrams butter
5. Place on a charcoal fire and braise with continuous stirring, beating in 3 whole eggs during cooking, until it becomes a thick paste that separates from the spoon
6. Cool it
7. In a large mortar, finely pound the cut capon breast with a little salt and nutmeg flower and pass through a sieve
8. In a pot, braise the rest of the capon with butter, several slices of carrot, and red onion until light yellow
9. From time to time pour a little broth on it and when the meat is tender, continue as directed
