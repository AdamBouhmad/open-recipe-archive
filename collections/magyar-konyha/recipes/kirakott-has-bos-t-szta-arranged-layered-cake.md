## Ingredients

- brioche sliced thin
- cream
- butter
- currants
- soaked and separated almonds
- raisins
- pistachios
- 3 deciliter cream
- 10 dekagramm sugar
- vanilla
- 5 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pour cream over thin brioche slices and let them soak well
2. Butter a melon mold
3. Layer brioche pieces thickly sprinkled with currants
4. Layer brioche pieces arranged with soaked separated almonds
5. Layer brioche pieces with raisins
6. Layer brioche pieces with pistachios, then currants again, continuing until all the mold is filled with layers
7. Add a layer of soaked brioche sprinkled thickly with soaked finely ground almonds, sugar, and vanilla
8. Continue layering until the mold is full
9. Beat together three deciliter cream, ten dekagramm sugar, vanilla, and five egg yolks
10. Pour this mixture over the top and set in a water bath
11. Steam continuously for half an hour until the liquid evaporates
12. Carefully turn out and serve either plain or with cream sauce
