## Ingredients

- six hard-boiled eggs
- bread soaked in milk
- butter
- green parsley
- salt
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut six hard-boiled eggs in half lengthwise
2. Crush the yolks finely in a mortar
3. Soak half a roll in milk and squeeze it out
4. Cream together egg-yolk-sized pieces of butter
5. Mix with finely chopped green parsley, crushed yolk, half the soaked bread with its soaking milk, and salt
6. Add two tablespoons of sour cream
7. Mix everything well together
8. Fill the egg white halves with this mixture
9. Place small pieces of butter in a dish and set the eggs in them
10. Pour sour cream over the top and add more butter pieces
11. Bake in the oven until nicely browned
