## Ingredients

- one tablespoon lard
- small amount of red onion
- a knife point of paprika
- eggs
- salt
- fresh parsley
- chives
- sour cream
- breadcrumbs
- grated Parmesan cheese
- diced ham

## Directions

1. In one tablespoon of lard, braise a small amount of red onion until golden
2. Add a knife point of paprika
3. Break the eggs carefully into the hot lard, being careful not to break the yolks
4. Salt them individually
5. Sprinkle finely chopped fresh parsley, chives, and paprika over the yolks
6. Pour sour cream over the eggs to cover them
7. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and bake quickly
8. Bake in the same dish in which it will be served to the table
9. Make it even more delicious by adding grated Parmesan cheese and diced ham
