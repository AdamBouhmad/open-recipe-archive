## Ingredients

- 1 well-ripened lemon
- 10 dekagramm sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil one well-ripened lemon until very soft, changing the water five or six times during cooking to remove the bitter taste.
2. Once so soft it can be easily mashed, cut into pieces and remove the seeds.
3. Press through a sieve and place in a bowl with 10 dekagramm sugar.
4. Beat well with 2 egg yolks.
5. Gradually fold in the stiffly beaten whites of 6 eggs.
6. Pour into a buttered porcelain dish and bake slowly in the oven at gentle heat or cook in a bain-marie.
7. Serve immediately at table, as this paste easily collapses.
