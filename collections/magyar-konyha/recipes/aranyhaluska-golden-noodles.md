## Ingredients

- dough as for recipe 578
- melted fat for dipping
- butter for coating the pastry sheet and rim
- 90 grams almonds
- 140 grams raisins
- 90 grams sugar
- a little cinnamon
- egg white for brushing

## Directions

1. Prepare dough as for recipe 578 and roll out to the thickness of two knife-backs
2. Cut out small noodles about the size of a twenty, dipping the cutter in melted fat instead of flour
3. Coat the pastry sheet and its rim with butter
4. Blanch the almonds, clean them, and chop them finely with the skin; also chop 140 grams of raisins finely
5. Crush 90 grams of sugar and a little cinnamon finely and mix all together
6. Place a row of golden noodles on the pastry sheet so that each one overlaps the one before it, continuing until the bottom is completely covered
7. Sprinkle with the almond mixture and place another row of noodles in the same order
8. Continue in this way
9. Place in a warm location and let it rise well, brush with egg white, and bake at a sustained high temperature, as this dough requires more time and greater heat than other doughs
