## Ingredients

- a little goose fat
- 2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- pressed sour cabbage
- 14 dekagrams rice
- a little butter or goose fat
- 1/2 peeled red onion, kept whole
- ham slices, triangular
- finely chopped ham
- butter for greasing the pot

## Directions

1. Melt a little goose fat in a pot
2. Add 2 tablespoons crushed sugar and brown it nicely with frequent stirring
3. Add the pressed sour cabbage and braise it frequently with a long fork until it becomes a nice golden yellow and soft
4. Meanwhile, braise 14 dekagrams of rice gently with a little butter or goose fat and half a peeled red onion that should remain whole, until soft
5. Line the bottom of a butter-greased pot with triangular ham slices arranged nicely side by side
6. Layer with braised rice, sprinkled densely with finely chopped ham
7. Add a layer of brown cabbage, similarly sprinkled with ham
8. Continue alternating layers until the pot is full
9. Cover with a fitting lid and braise for half an hour
10. If using potatoes instead of rice, cook and peel them, cut into thin slices and layer the same way as the rice, but pour fresh sour cream over each potato layer so they do not dry out
11. Turn out onto a platter and top with sausage or pickled tongue
