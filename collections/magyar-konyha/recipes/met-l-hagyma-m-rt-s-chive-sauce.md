## Ingredients

- three eggs
- three fresh anchovies or one herring
- egg yolks
- fine olive oil
- two tablespoons finely chopped chives
- white vinegar
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil three eggs hard
2. Finely chop the whites
3. Clean and wash three fresh anchovies or one herring, cut into cubes
4. Smooth the egg yolks with fine olive oil
5. Add two tablespoons of finely chopped chives and the above ingredients to this
6. Thin with white vinegar and mix well
7. A little sugar can also be added
