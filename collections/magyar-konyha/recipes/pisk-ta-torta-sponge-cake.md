## Ingredients

- forty-two dekagrams of sugar rubbed on an orange
- twenty-two egg yolks
- twenty-one dekagrams of fine flour or the finest ground and sifted cornstarch
- twelve egg whites
- three identical cake leaves and hoops
- butter for coating
- jam for filling
- chocolate icing or fresh raspberry icing for coating
- seventeen to twenty-one dekagrams of powdered sugar
- fresh strawberries for the raspberry icing

## Directions

1. Grind the sugar rubbed on orange and sift it.
2. Beat it in a basin with twenty-two egg yolks for one hour evenly.
3. Gently fold in twenty-one dekagrams of fine flour or the finest ground and sifted cornstarch, as well as the stiff foam of twelve egg whites.
4. Coat three identical cake leaves and hoops with butter.
5. Divide the batter into three equal parts and bake slowly over gentle heat.
6. When well baked, turn the cake leaves out onto a sieve, cool completely.
7. Then coat every layer with different preserves, stack them on top of each other, and cover with either chocolate icing or fresh raspberry icing.
8. For the raspberry or strawberry icing: the strawberries should be small and beautifully red.
9. Sift seventeen to twenty-one dekagrams of powdered sugar finely on a sieve.
10. Press the fruit through clean cloth and stir into the sugar by spoonfuls so the icing is not too thin.
11. When the icing is foamy, coat the cake smoothly and evenly with a knife and dry it completely in a cool oven.
