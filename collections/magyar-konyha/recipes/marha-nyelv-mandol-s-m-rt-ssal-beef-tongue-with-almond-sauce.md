## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- salt
- water
- bacon slices or red onion
- carrot
- cloves
- veal bones
- flour
- red wine
- broth
- ground almonds (5-7 dekagrams)
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue in salted water until tender
2. Clean the tongue of its skin
3. Place it in a pot with bacon slices or sliced red onion, sliced carrot, a few cloves, and veal bones
4. Braise these together until nicely browned
5. Sprinkle a spoonful of flour over it and brown it
6. Deglaze with half red wine and half broth
7. Cook well and strain the sauce through a sieve into another pot
8. Add 5-7 dekagrams of ground almonds to the sauce
9. Place the tongue back in and cook well
10. Before serving, garnish the tongue with almonds
11. Pour the sauce into the serving dish and place the tongue on top
