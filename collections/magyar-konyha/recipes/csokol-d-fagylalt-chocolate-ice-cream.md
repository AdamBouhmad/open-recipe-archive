## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters good cream
- 6 tablets fine chocolate
- 14 dekagrams sugar

## Directions

1. Cook and stir 7 deciliters of good cream with 6 tablets of fine chocolate and 14 dekagrams of sugar until the chocolate is completely dissolved
2. Cool the mixture
3. Pour into the freezing container and treat it like the other ice creams
