## Ingredients

- 15 decagrams rice
- 7 deciliters milk
- butter, egg-sized piece
- 6 egg yolks
- finely chopped parsley
- little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in milk until thick
2. Set aside to cool
3. Mix the butter with the egg yolks, finely chopped parsley, and a little nutmeg until fluffy
4. Add the cooled rice to the mixture
5. Grease a baking pan with butter and pour in the rice
6. Bake until light golden
7. Turn out onto a board and cut into equal square pieces
8. Serve with white or brown broth
