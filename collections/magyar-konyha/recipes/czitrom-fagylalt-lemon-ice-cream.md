## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilogram sugar
- 13 deciliters water
- 1 lemon, thinly peeled rind
- 5-6 lemons, juice

## Directions

1. Boil 0.5 kilogram sugar with 13 deciliters water
2. Remove the foam
3. Add the rind of a thinly peeled lemon
4. When the sugar has cooled, squeeze the juice of 5-6 lemons into it
5. Freeze the whole thing
