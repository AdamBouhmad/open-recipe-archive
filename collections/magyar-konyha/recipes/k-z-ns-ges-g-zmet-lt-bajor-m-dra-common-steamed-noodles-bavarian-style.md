## Ingredients

- 500 grams flour, warmed
- lukewarm milk
- 37.5 grams melted yeast
- a piece of melted butter about the size of an egg
- milk as needed for moistening
- 1 egg
- 2 egg yolks
- a little salt
- cream for steaming
- a little butter for steaming

## Directions

1. Place the warmed flour in a bowl and make a small dough from lukewarm milk and melted yeast, let it rise well
2. Add the melted butter and enough milk to moisten the dough
3. Beat in 1 egg and 2 egg yolks
4. Make the dough, which should be as stiff as bread dough
5. Beat it well, salt it lightly, cover it, and place in a warm location
6. Once it has risen about halfway, place on a floured board
7. Roll out to the thickness of two knife-backs
8. Cut into small pieces with a cutter and cover to let rise again
9. Pour cream to a depth of two finger-widths in a flat shallow pan, add a little butter, and bring to a boil
10. Add the noodles, cover with a lid that fits well, place clean paper on top, and coals above
11. Steam for about 15 minutes
