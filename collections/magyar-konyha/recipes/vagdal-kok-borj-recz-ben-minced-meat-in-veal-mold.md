## Ingredients

- 38 dekagrams veal
- red onion
- bacon
- leftover roasted turkey or capon if available
- bread soaked in milk
- parsley
- salt
- black pepper
- egg yolks
- eggs
- butter
- veal mold
- roux
- brown stock
- lemon peel
- sour cream
- capers
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Braise the veal with red onion and bacon
2. If there is leftover roasted turkey or capon, add it as well
3. Chop the braised meat with bacon, red onion, roast, and bread soaked in milk and parsley finely together
4. Add salt and ground pepper
5. Mix in two egg yolks and two eggs
6. Butter a pot, place the veal mold in it and then the minced meat
7. Cover the top again with veal mold
8. Cover and bake nicely brown in the oven
9. Make a small roux, thin with brown stock, add a little lemon peel and cook well
10. Finally add two spoonfuls of sour cream, small capers and lemon juice and cook a little more
11. Turn the minced meat out onto a plate, pour the sauce over it, and garnish with small cut-out lemon peel
