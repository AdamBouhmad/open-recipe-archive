## Ingredients

- apples or pears
- water
- lemon juice
- sugar
- currant or raspberry syrup (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the apples or pears, cut them in half, remove the seeds
2. Stew them with a glass of water, the juice of one lemon, and a piece of sugar
3. If you have currant or raspberry syrup, add 2 spoonfuls of it
4. Place the apples or pears on a dish and pour the liquid over them
