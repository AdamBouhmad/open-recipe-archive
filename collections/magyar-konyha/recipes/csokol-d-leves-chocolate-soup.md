## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk
- 4 pieces of grated chocolate
- a little sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- toasted bread, cut into small slices

## Directions

1. Cook the milk with grated chocolate and sugar together, stirring well
2. Beat 2 egg yolks in a bowl and pour the soup over them
3. Serve over toasted bread slices
