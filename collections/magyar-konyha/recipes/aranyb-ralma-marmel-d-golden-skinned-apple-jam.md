## Ingredients

- apples
- water
- sugar, for half kilogram of sugar use sixty-five decagrams of apple pulp
- cold water

## Directions

1. Cook the apples in water until they soften but do not fall apart; then place in cold water and when cooled, peel and press through a sieve.
2. Now place in a brass pan over gentle charcoal heat and stir constantly until thick.
3. Prepare the sugar as you would for other fruits and clarify it; add the apple when it begins to form heavy drops, mix well with the sugar and cook until a drop placed on a plate as a test does not run but remains firm.
4. If the sugar has cooked well, half an hour is sufficient; then when cooled, pour into glasses.
