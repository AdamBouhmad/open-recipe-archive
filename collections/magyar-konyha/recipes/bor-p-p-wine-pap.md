## Ingredients

- 4 grated bread rolls
- 5 dekagrams butter
- 4 decilitres white wine
- 7 dekagrams butter
- 1 whole egg
- 5 egg yolks
- zest of 1 lemon
- 14 dekagrams ground sugar
- 4 egg whites
- fine breadcrumb powder

## Directions

1. Toast the grated bread in 5 dekagrams of butter without browning
2. Thin with 4 decilitres of white wine
3. Cook with continuous stirring and cool
4. Cream 7 dekagrams of butter and beat in the whole egg and 5 egg yolks one by one
5. Add the minced lemon zest and 14 dekagrams of ground sugar
6. When foamy, gently fold in the stiff foam of 4 egg whites
7. Fill a buttered mould coated with fine breadcrumb powder with the pap
8. Bake until done
