## Ingredients

- Seven deciliters dried and fresh cherries, washed
- Fourteen dekagrams sugar
- One glass white wine
- Two glasses water
- A little lemon or orange peel
- Juice of one orange
- Orange peel, chopped into long strips

## Directions

1. Cook the washed dried and fresh cherries with fourteen dekagrams sugar, one glass of white wine, two glasses of water, and a little lemon or orange peel until they soften
2. Remove them with a perforated spoon and pile them on a plate
3. Continue cooking the syrup for a while longer
4. Squeeze the juice of one orange into it, strain through cloth, and pour over the cherries
5. Place in a cold location
6. Before serving at table, sprinkle with finely chopped orange peel
7. Dried plums can be prepared the same way, using excellent quality fruit
