## Ingredients

- Young lamb meat
- Broth
- Sour cream
- Flour, a small amount
- Vinegar
- Fresh tarragon leaves
- Spices, to taste

## Directions

1. Cook tender lamb meat in plenty of broth until soft
2. When cooked, thicken with plenty of sour cream and a small amount of flour
3. Sour with vinegar and add fresh tarragon leaves
4. Season generously with spices to taste
