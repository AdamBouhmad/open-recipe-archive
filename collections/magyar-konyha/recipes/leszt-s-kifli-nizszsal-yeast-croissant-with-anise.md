## Ingredients

- 280 grams flour
- 210 grams butter
- salt
- 1 whole egg
- 2 egg yolks
- 36 grams cheese yeast dissolved in milk
- 5 tablespoons sour cream
- small raisins
- raisins
- chopped almond
- sugar
- citron peel
- egg white
- sugar rice
- anise

## Directions

1. Layer in 280 grams flour 210 grams butter leaf by leaf, smooth it well with a roller on a board, and salt it
2. Prepare the dough with 1 whole egg, 2 egg yolks, 36 grams cheese yeast dissolved in milk, and 5 tablespoons sour cream
3. Roll out twice and cut into rectangular leaves
4. Fill with small raisins, raisins, chopped almond, sugar and citron peel
5. Fold together, brush with egg white, place on a pan and let rise slowly
6. Bake in the oven at gentle heat
7. Prepare sugared rice to sprinkle on top, sprinkle with anise, and put back in the oven to dry
