## Ingredients

- young hen
- butter
- flour
- broth
- sour cream
- lemon juice
- veal cutlets

## Directions

1. After cleaning and preparing the hens, blanch and slice them nicely.
2. Prepare the sauce as follows: melt seven dekagrams of butter, blend smoothly with two spoonfuls of flour, two spoonfuls of broth, half a spoonful of sour cream and the juice of one lemon.
3. The hens, which have been nearly cooked in broth beforehand, are only braised in this sauce with continuous stirring.
4. At the same time, shape beautiful cutlets from veal and cook their sauce until reduced.
5. Pile the hens high in the center of the dish and pour their sauce over them; arrange the cutlets around the edge in a wreath, poured with their own broth.
