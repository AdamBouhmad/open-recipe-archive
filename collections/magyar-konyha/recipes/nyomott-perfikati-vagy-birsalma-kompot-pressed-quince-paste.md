## Ingredients

- quince
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare quince marmalade, cooking it well and allowing it to cool
2. Form into balls the size of a walnut
3. Roll flat on paper sprinkled with fine sugar in a dry place in the air
4. Roll thinner each day with a roller, sprinkling with sugar, until wafer-thin
5. Store in rolls for future use
