## Ingredients

- 0.5 kilogram strong alcohol
- 4 grams orange blossom oil
- 1 gram clove oil
- 0.5 kilogram bouquet water
- 14 dekagrams rose water
- 2 grams benzoin dye
- 1 gram ambergris essence
- 1 gram musk essence

## Directions

1. Dissolve 4 grams orange blossom oil and 1 gram clove oil in 0.5 kilogram strong alcohol
2. Add 0.5 kilogram bouquet water, 14 dekagrams rose water, 2 grams benzoin dye, 1 gram ambergris essence, and 1 gram musk essence
3. Mix well
4. Strain
