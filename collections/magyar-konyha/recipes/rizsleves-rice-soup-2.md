## Ingredients

- Cleaned chicken
- Salt
- Green parsley, finely chopped
- Butter
- Beef broth
- 14 dekagrams (1/4 pound) rice
- Nutmeg flower, a small amount
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned chicken into pieces and salt it lightly, allowing it to sit in the salt.
2. Place the chicken with finely chopped green parsley into a piece of melted butter and braise it slowly, pouring a little broth over it several times so it remains beautifully white until tender.
3. Pour another spoonful or two of broth over it and braise briefly.
4. Wash the carefully selected rice in lukewarm water and cook it in good beef broth for half an hour before serving so it becomes tender but remains whole, adding a small amount of nutmeg flower.
5. Beat 2 egg yolks in a small dish with a little broth, stirring well.
6. Place the chicken with the rice in a soup pot, pour this broth over it and serve.
