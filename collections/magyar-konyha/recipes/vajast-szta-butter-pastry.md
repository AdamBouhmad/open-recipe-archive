## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams flour
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- 1 tablespoon white vinegar or 2 tablespoons wine
- water as needed
- salt

## Directions

1. Salt the flour on the kneading board
2. Beat in an egg, two tablespoons of sour cream, and one tablespoon of white vinegar or two tablespoons of wine
3. Add a little water if not wet enough; the dough should be somewhat stiffer than ordinary strudel dough
4. Knead until it separates from the hand, then form into a round loaf
5. Continue kneading while shaping
6. Cover with a cloth and let rest for fifteen minutes
7. Roll out to the thickness of a knife blade
8. Knead 28 dekagrams of butter well and form into a wide sheet
9. Place this in the middle of the dough
10. Fold the dough over it from all four sides
11. Dust the board lightly with flour
12. Beat the dough with a rolling pin and stretch as thin as possible
13. Fold in four parts on the same side and brush off the flour
14. Use minimal flour when dusting to prevent sticking
15. Roll lightly and let rest for fifteen minutes in a cool place (in hot weather place between two plates on ice)
16. Roll out again
17. Repeat this process three times; the fourth time shape as desired
