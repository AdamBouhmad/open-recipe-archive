## Ingredients

- dough as in previous recipe
- flour
- preserved apricots, currants or sour cherries
- chopped nuts
- sugar
- raisins
- ground poppy seed
- lemon or orange zest
- cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the same dough as the previous recipe and let it rise well
2. Place on a flour-dusted board and roll out to a good thick knife-width
3. Cut into hand-width and arm-length pieces with a pastry cutter
4. Fill with preserved apricots, currants or sour cherries
5. Alternatively, mix ground nuts, sugar and raisins, or ground poppy seed, sugar and finely chopped lemon or orange zest, mixing with a few tablespoons of cream to a thick mass
6. Spread this filling on the pieces, roll them up
7. Place upright in a buttered baking dish or cake pan, buttering each roll so they separate when baked
8. Let rise in a warm place and bake at gentle but steady heat
