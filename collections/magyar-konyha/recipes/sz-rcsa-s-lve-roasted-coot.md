## Ingredients

- coot
- water
- brine
- salt
- sour cream
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the coot
2. Soak in water for one hour
3. Place in brine for one day
4. Salt and prepare
5. Roast well, basting frequently with brine during cooking
6. Toward the end, baste with sour cream and butter
7. Place on a plate and pour the sauce over it
