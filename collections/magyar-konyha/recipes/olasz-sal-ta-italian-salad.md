## Ingredients

- Multiple varieties of green salad
- Several cooked potatoes
- A piece of eel
- A large herring, well-soaked and cleaned
- Several spools of fish
- Several hard-boiled eggs
- Red beets, finely chopped
- Aspic

## Directions

1. Cut the fish and eel into slices and arrange them neatly in a salad cup with various green lettuces and boiled potatoes
2. Remove the yolks from several hard-boiled eggs and press the whites through a sieve
3. Cut the whites into strips and use a small cutter to make half-moon shapes from them
4. Add finely chopped red beets and use these to decorate the salad beautifully
5. Create three rings: the first from egg yolk, the second from red beets, and the third from egg white, with aspic placed in the center
