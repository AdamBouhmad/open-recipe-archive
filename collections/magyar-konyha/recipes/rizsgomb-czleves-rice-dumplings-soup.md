## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams rice
- water
- meat broth
- butter
- 7 dekagrams butter
- 1 egg
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- flour
- brown broth

## Directions

1. Wash the picked rice in lukewarm water well
2. Put in a pot with a little meat broth and a piece of butter and stew until soft
3. Beat 7 dekagrams butter until foamy
4. Mix in 1 whole egg and 3 egg yolks as well as the cooled fat
5. Season with salt and add a little flour to hold it together
6. Form small dumplings and cook them in white meat broth
7. Once cooked, remove and place in the soup bowl
8. Pour brown broth over it
