## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams softened almonds
- 18 dekagrams crushed and sifted sugar
- 2 teaspoons canned apricots
- additional canned apricots as needed
- sugar for dusting
- fine powdered sugar
- snow
- lemon juice
- white paper cone

## Directions

1. Crush 14 dekagrams of softened almonds into fine dough without moistening them during crushing
2. On a board with a wide knife, thoroughly rub together the crushed almonds with 18 dekagrams of crushed and sifted sugar and 2 teaspoons of canned apricots
3. If the mixture is not moist enough, add a little more canned apricot preserves, which enhances the beautiful color of the dough, but it must not be too soft
4. Once the mass is well worked, dust the board with sugar and form small pretzels from the mixture
5. Place these on a tray lightly greased with wax
6. Mix fine powdered sugar with a little snow and lemon juice to make thick icing
7. Fill white paper cones with the icing and decorate the pretzels beautifully
8. Place in a gentle oven and dry them
9. These pretzels taste magnificent and have the property that in a dry place, in a box or covered with glass, they will keep for several weeks
