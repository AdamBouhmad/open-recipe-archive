## Ingredients

- four deciliters strong coffee
- sugar
- three dekagrams dissolved isinglass
- twenty-one dekagrams almonds (including several bitter ones)
- four deciliters water
- two dekagrams isinglass

## Directions

1. Prepare four deciliters of strong coffee and sweeten well
2. Mix in three dekagrams of dissolved isinglass
3. When the coffee is clear and well mixed with the isinglass, strain through table linen
4. Pour into a melon-shaped mold in alternating sections: first fill one section, when frozen, fill another section of the mold, place on ice until frozen, and continue alternating
5. Prepare almond milk by making a milk from twenty-one dekagrams of almonds (including a few bitter ones) with four deciliters of water
6. Sweeten the almond milk well and mix in two dekagrams of isinglass
7. Strain once more
8. When the coffee in the mold is well frozen, slowly pour in the almond milk to fill the mold
9. Place on ice and freeze
10. When unmolding, work quickly and carefully so the coffee does not mix with the almond milk, keeping the sections in their own colors, one white and one black
