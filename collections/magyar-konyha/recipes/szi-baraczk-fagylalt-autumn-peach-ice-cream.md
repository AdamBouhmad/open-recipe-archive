## Ingredients

- fully ripe, very juicy autumn peaches
- sugar
- half a coffee cup Tokaji wine
- 56 dekagrams strained peach pulp
- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 1 liter water
- juice of 4 lemons
- elderflower liqueur

## Directions

1. Peel completely ripe autumn peaches and cut into segments
2. Coat well with sugar in a deep bowl
3. Pour half a coffee cup of Tokaji wine over them
4. Let stand for 3-4 hours and press through a sieve
5. Cook 56 dekagrams strained peach pulp with 42 dekagrams sugar and 1 liter water until it forms large drops
6. Pour this into the peach juice
7. Press in the juice of 4 lemons
8. Dissolve a little elderflower liqueur in it for a pale red color
9. Pour into a freezing container
