## Ingredients

- fresh pork meat, cut into pieces
- onion
- paprika
- fat
- salt
- hot water
- cabbage, boiled soft in water
- ground black pepper

## Directions

1. Fry the pork pieces until browned
2. Add paprika and onion in fat
3. Do not add liquid until the meat begins to cook in its own fat
4. Season with salt
5. Add hot water gradually if needed until the meat is tender
6. Let the liquid cook away so the meat has a roasted appearance
7. Add the pre-boiled cabbage and stir in
8. Add ground black pepper
9. Simmer together for fifteen minutes
10. When serving, sprinkle with ground black pepper
