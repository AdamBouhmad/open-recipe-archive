## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters flour
- 25 dekagrams lard
- 7 dekagrams finely chopped blanched almonds
- 28 dekagrams powdered sugar
- a little cinnamon
- a little cloves
- grated zest of one lemon and a little juice
- 1 egg
- 1.5 dekagrams yeast dissolved in milk
- jam for filling
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Mix flour with lard, blanched finely chopped almonds, powdered sugar, cinnamon, cloves, lemon zest and juice, egg, and yeast dissolved in milk
2. Place half the dough in a buttered pan
3. Fill the top with jam
4. Arrange the other half of the dough as small rods over the jam
5. Let it rise slightly in the pan
6. Brush with egg and bake
