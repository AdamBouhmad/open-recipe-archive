## Ingredients

- large, ripe peaches
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and halve large, ripe peaches
2. Place on a drying board in a warm oven
3. When well dried and a nice light brown color, remove them
4. Press one slice flat against the other with your finger for each peach
5. Dip every peach in braided sugar
6. Place back on the drying board and put in the oven for two hours
7. Dip in sugar once more and dry again
