## Ingredients

- cooked turkey
- half a veal breast
- six button mushrooms
- twenty shrimp tails and claws
- fourteen decagrams shrimp butter
- three deciliters green peas
- green parsley
- four deciliters sour cream
- one and a half crumbly rolls
- two eggs
- two egg yolks
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked turkey, veal breast, button mushrooms, and shrimp tails and claws into small cubes
2. Make shrimp butter from the shells
3. Stew the button mushrooms with the shrimp butter, green peas, and finely chopped green parsley
4. Add the minced mixture, stew briefly, and season with salt; let cool
5. Pour the sour cream over the crumbly roll crumbs, add two eggs and two egg yolks, and mix well
6. Coat the pastry molds with shrimp butter and fill them with the minced mixture, coat with the crumbly dough, add a little shrimp butter on top, and bake slowly
