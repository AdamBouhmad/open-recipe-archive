## Ingredients

- Three to four quinces
- Water
- Twenty-four dekagrams sugar
- Two deciliters water

## Directions

1. Bring the quinces to a slight boil in water, then place them in cold water
2. When cooled, peel them and divide into four segments, removing the cores
3. Cook twenty-four dekagrams sugar with two deciliters of water until the sugar forms heavy drops
4. Place the quinces in the sugar syrup, turning them several times so they soften on both sides without falling apart
5. Arrange on a serving dish and pour the light syrup over them
