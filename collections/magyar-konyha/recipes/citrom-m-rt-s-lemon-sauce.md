## Ingredients

- yellow roux
- finely chopped red onion
- meat stock
- half a lemon's zest
- lemon juice
- two tablespoons fresh sour cream

## Directions

1. Prepare a yellow roux and add the finely chopped red onion to it
2. When it has browned slightly, pour in enough stock so that it reaches the desired consistency
3. Cut the zest of half a lemon into long strips and add it along with the lemon juice
4. Cook well
5. Finally add two tablespoons of sour cream and the sauce is ready
