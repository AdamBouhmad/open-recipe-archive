## Ingredients

- crayfish
- crayfish shells and claws
- butter
- fish stock (consommé)
- 2 liters fish stock
- 100-150 grams white bread crumb
- parsley
- eggs

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from the cooked crayfish and set aside the shells
2. Pound the cooked trunk with the meat inside and the claws finely in a mortar
3. Place the pounded mixture in a pot and pour over it the liquid in which the crayfish were cooked
4. Add 2 liters of fish stock (consommé) and 100-150 grams of white bread crumb
5. Bring the whole to a boil and cook for about an hour on a careful fire so it does not reduce too much
6. After an hour, strain through a fine sieve and set the stock aside
7. Prepare crayfish butter: place the cooked crayfish shells and claw shells in a pot, fry with parsley and butter, then pound in a mortar, pour over with hot thin meat stock, set in a cold place, and after cooling the crayfish butter will gather on top of the liquid and should be removed and set aside
8. Stuff the nicer crayfish trunks, 2-3 pieces per person
9. The filling is made from bread soaked in milk, worked with butter, 8 egg yolks, little breadcrumb and crayfish butter, from which small dumplings are also made
10. Cook the dumplings in the prepared stock together with crayfish butter
11. Before serving, add a few spoonfuls of sour cream to the whole soup according to its quantity, and serve the whole soup hot with the picked crayfish meat
