## Ingredients

- veal cutlets
- melted butter
- breadcrumbs
- sour cream
- butter
- lemon zest
- capers
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the membrane completely from the cutlet bones, pound to shape nicely round, salt and coat with melted butter
2. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and broil nicely
3. Place on a plate, pour three deciliters of good sour cream over it
4. Add a few pieces of butter, finely chopped lemon zest, and capers on top
5. Place coals above and below, bring to a boil and finally squeeze out the juice of one lemon over it
