## Ingredients

- one liter good milk
- handful of swollen finely ground almonds
- a little vanilla
- sugar
- three egg yolks
- toasted breadcrumb slices

## Directions

1. Cook one liter of good milk with a handful of swollen finely ground almonds and a little vanilla.
2. Sweeten to taste.
3. Before serving, beat three egg yolks well and pour the hot milk over them.
4. While pouring the milk onto the eggs, stir vigorously so that the whole does not curdle.
5. Serve over toasted breadcrumb slices.
