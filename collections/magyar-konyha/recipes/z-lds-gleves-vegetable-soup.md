## Ingredients

- common soup vegetables
- butter
- pea broth
- salt
- nutmeg
- four egg yolks
- two spoonfuls sour cream
- butter
- toasted breadcrumbs
- fried fish roe or pike liver

## Directions

1. Clean and wash common soup vegetables as on meat days.
2. Chop them as finely as possible.
3. Braise them in butter in a pot.
4. Mix with one liter of pea broth, salt, and nutmeg.
5. Before serving, beat four egg yolks, two spoonfuls of sour cream, and a piece of butter together.
6. Pour the hot broth over it and serve over toasted breadcrumb slices.
7. Top with sliced fried fish roe or pike liver if desired.
