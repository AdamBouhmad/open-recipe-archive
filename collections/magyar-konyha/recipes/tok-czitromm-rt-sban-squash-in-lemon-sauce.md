## Ingredients

- squash
- salt
- butter
- flour
- red onion, divided into four parts
- pea water
- lemon rind
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After cleaning and salting the squash, let it sit in the salt for half an hour
2. In a pot, melt a piece of butter and fry two full spoons of flour in it
3. Add the red onion divided into four parts
4. Dilute with pea water and add the squash, cooking until completely done
5. Add a little lemon rind and just before serving, squeeze the juice of one lemon over it
