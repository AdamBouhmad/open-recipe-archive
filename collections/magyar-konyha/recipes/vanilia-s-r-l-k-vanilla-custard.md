## Ingredients

- seven egg yolks
- seven decilitres of cream
- sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Make a thin batter from seven egg yolks
2. Bring seven decilitres of cream to a boil and add a piece of sugar and a little pounded vanilla
3. When it is boiling, carefully pour in the batter, boil for a few minutes, and remove with a skimming spoon
4. When everything has been cooked this way, pile it up high in a deep plate and sprinkle every layer with sugar, almonds, and finely pounded vanilla
5. Crown the top with small, thinly sliced almonds and brown it nicely in the oven
6. When baked, pour vanilla custard (crème) around the edge of the plate
