## Ingredients

- lemons
- four cups crushed sugar per one cup lemon juice
- one-half cup rum per one cup lemon juice
- four cups boiled rice water or corn tea or Dutch tea (the former is more pleasant due to vanilla flavor)
- seven deciliters of corn kernels
- eighteen deciliters water
- five dekagrams dissolved isinglass
- alkanet for coloring (optional)

## Directions

1. Squeeze the lemons and strain the juice through clean cloth
2. Place all the above items in a soup bowl: one cup lemon juice, four cups crushed sugar, one-half cup rum, and four cups boiled rice water or corn tea
3. Rinse seven deciliters of corn kernels and boil them with eighteen deciliters of water for about an hour or longer
4. Strain and prepare the punch with this liquid
5. Stir the items in the bowl frequently until the sugar is completely dissolved
6. Add five dekagrams of dissolved isinglass mixed with the rest
7. Strain this jelly through table linen
8. Optionally color it red with alkanet, then place in a mold and freeze on ice
