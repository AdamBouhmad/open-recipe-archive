## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams powdered sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 7 dekagrams blanched almonds, chopped
- 14 dekagrams ground almonds
- 7 dekagrams pistachios
- 4.5 dekagrams citron peel, chopped
- zest of 1 orange, chopped
- a little flour
- paper tubes for baking

## Directions

1. Beat the powdered sugar with the whole eggs and egg yolks in a bowl
2. Add the chopped blanched almonds, ground almonds, pistachios, and citron peel
3. Add the orange zest
4. Mix well with a little flour
5. Fill paper tubes with the mixture
6. Bake gently
7. When baked, remove and cut into equal bars like twice-baked bread
8. Dry in the oven
