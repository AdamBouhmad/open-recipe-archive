## Ingredients

- 2 large or 3 small oranges, zest grated
- 6 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 3 egg yolks
- 5 dekagramm butter, beaten to foam
- 28 dekagramm swollen and finely broken almonds
- 28 dekagramm sifted sugar
- 1.5 dekagramm fine flour
- preserved sour cherries
- egg white
- coarsely broken sugar

## Directions

1. Grate the zest of 2 large or 3 small oranges and finely pound it in a mortar with 6 hard-boiled egg yolks.
2. Beat 5 dekagramm of butter to a foam, then gradually blend in 3 egg yolks one after another.
3. Add 28 dekagramm of swollen and finely broken almonds, and stir for half an hour.
4. Add the same amount of sifted sugar and stir for another quarter hour.
5. Add the orange zest mixed with egg yolks, and mix everything well with 1.5 dekagramm of fine flour.
6. Divide the mixture into two parts, place in buttered cake layers, spread evenly with a knife, and fill with preserved sour cherries.
7. Pipe the third part in a ring shape over the top.
8. Brush the cake with egg white and sprinkle with coarsely broken sugar.
9. Bake slowly and decorate with icing.
