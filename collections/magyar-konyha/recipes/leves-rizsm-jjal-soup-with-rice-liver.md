## Ingredients

- goose liver mixture
- flour
- hot broth
- brown broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the mixture as for goose liver pâté recipe number 53a, with the difference that one and a half spoonfuls of flour are added instead of egg white
2. Press this mixture through a larger grater with a wooden spoon into hot broth
3. To keep the rice beautifully whole and uniform do this in a pot
4. Remove gradually with a spoon as it cooks
5. Place in soup bowl and serve brown broth over it
