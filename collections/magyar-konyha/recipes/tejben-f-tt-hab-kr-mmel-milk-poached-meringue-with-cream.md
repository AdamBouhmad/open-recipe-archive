## Ingredients

- ten egg whites
- sugar
- vanilla
- milk
- sixteen egg yolks
- seven deciliters milk
- sugar
- vanilla or grated lemon zest

## Directions

1. Beat ten egg whites until stiff peaks form, adding enough sugar and vanilla for sweetness and flavor
2. Using a spoon, cook dumplings of this meringue in milk and heap them high on a platter
3. Once all dumplings are cooked, strain the milk
4. For the cream: beat sixteen egg yolks well with seven deciliters of the lukewarm milk in which the dumplings were cooked
5. Add enough sugar for sweetness and a little vanilla or grated lemon zest
6. Bring to a boil while constantly stirring until it thickens slightly
7. Transfer from one container to another to cool and prevent curdling
8. Once cooled, pour the cream around the dumplings on the platter
