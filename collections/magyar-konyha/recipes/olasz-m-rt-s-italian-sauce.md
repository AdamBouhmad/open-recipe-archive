## Ingredients

- three hard-boiled egg whites
- three fresh sardines or one herring
- salt
- fine oil
- two spoons finely chopped shallot onion
- white vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop the hard-boiled egg whites finely and the sardines or herring (which must first be cleaned of salt and washed) into cubes
2. Whisk the hard-boiled egg yolks well with fine oil
3. Add the finely chopped shallot onion and the ingredients above
4. Loosen with white vinegar and mix everything well together
