## Ingredients

- 6 lat butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 6 lat pressed cooked potato
- salt
- a little nutmeg
- 10 lat finely chopped cooked herring
- 4 egg whites, well beaten
- Madeira sauce

## Directions

1. Mix together 6 lat butter, 4 egg yolks, 6 lat pressed cooked potato, salt, a little nutmeg, and 10 lat finely chopped cooked herring
2. Add the well beaten whites of the four eggs and place the whole mixture in a greased smooth mold, and bake in the oven until nicely brown
3. Turn onto a plate, crown the top and serve the small cutlets already braised and made from herring farce
4. Serve Madeira sauce separately in a cup
