## Ingredients

- two whole eggs
- two egg yolks
- two tablespoons of crushed sugar
- two tablespoons of sour cream
- a little salt
- fine flour

## Directions

1. Put two whole eggs, two egg yolks, two tablespoons of crushed sugar, two tablespoons of sour cream, and a little salt into a pot and mix well
2. On a board, prepare a light dough from fine flour and knead it
3. Roll this out into a thin sheet, but do this carefully so the dough does not tear
4. Fold in half and cut with a rose-shaped cutter or a medium-sized doughnut cutter if rose-shaped is not available
5. Stack five such sheets on top of each other, which then form the doughnut
6. Brush the middle of each sheet with egg white and press together in the middle with your finger
7. Once the sheets are cut, make five cuts into the edge of each so they look like rose petals
8. Cover with a cloth so they do not dry out
9. Fry in fat until a nice golden color, turning several times during frying to achieve an even color
10. Carefully remove and place on a sieve covered with absorbent paper
11. Once all are fried, place one whole preserved sour cherry or currant in the middle of each doughnut
12. Sprinkle with finely crushed sugar and arrange neatly on a plate
