## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters green peas
- 1 tablespoon finely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Calculate one tablespoon of finely ground sugar per seven deciliters of peas
2. Braise the peas with the sugar in a pan until the peas, which release all their moisture during braising, become completely dry
3. Stir the peas continuously during braising
4. Spread them in a thin layer on strong paper, preferably sugar paper
5. Dry them in the oven after bread baking in an oven that is no longer very hot, without letting the peas brown
6. Use them in winter as fresh peas
