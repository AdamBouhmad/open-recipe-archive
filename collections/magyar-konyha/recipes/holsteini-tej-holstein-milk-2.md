## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams soaked almonds
- 7 deciliters boiled good cream
- 21 dekagrams sugar
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Finely grind the soaked almonds, moistening with a little water during grinding so it doesn't become oily
2. Add this to 7 deciliters of boiled good cream, cook together for a few moments, then strain through cloth
3. Dissolve 21 dekagrams sugar in it
4. Add the stiffly beaten snow of 5 egg whites to it
5. Place on charcoal fire and beat until the entire mixture becomes foam and has a shiny white color, taking care it does not begin to boil
6. When everything is well mixed, shiny and white, pour into shiny white coffee cups or deep glass bowls, place in ice and freeze
7. If you want to turn it out, mix 2 dekagrams dissolved isinglass into it, pour into a mold and freeze
