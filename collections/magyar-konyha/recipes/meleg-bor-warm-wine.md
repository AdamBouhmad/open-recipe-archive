## Ingredients

- good white or red wine
- sugar to taste
- several cloves
- a piece of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Sweeten the wine to taste
2. Add the cloves and cinnamon
3. Bring to a boil
4. Remove the spices
5. Serve hot in glasses or in bottles wrapped with cloth
