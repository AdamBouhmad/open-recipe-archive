## Ingredients

- rétestésztát (strudel dough)
- 10 dekagramm sugar
- six egg yolks
- 10 dekagramm finely ground almond
- two bars of chocolate
- four egg whites
- melted butter
- grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough
2. Mix 10 dekagramm sugar, six egg yolks, and 10 dekagramm finely ground almond together well
3. Grate two bars of chocolate
4. Mix the grated chocolate with the stiffly beaten whites of four eggs
5. Combine this with the egg yolk mixture
6. Stretch the dough finely and dry it
7. Brush with melted butter
8. Spread the filling onto it in the thickness of a knife blade
9. Fold it together gently
10. Place it in a baking dish brushed with butter
11. Sprinkle with grated chocolate and bake until golden
