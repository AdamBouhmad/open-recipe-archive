## Ingredients

- medium-sized veal leg
- salt
- smoked tongue already cooked
- little marrow
- butter
- head of red onion studded with cloves
- half lemon rind and its juice
- 3 decilitres wine
- 2 decilitres broth
- 3-4 decagrams capers
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Wash and salt a medium-sized veal leg.
2. Cut already cooked smoked tongue and a little marrow into long pieces.
3. Make holes with a narrow knife and lard alternately with tongue or marrow.
4. Line a pan with butter, place red onion head studded with cloves, half lemon rind and its juice in it.
5. Place the leg on top, pour 3 decilitres wine and 2 decilitres broth over it.
6. Cover and braise slowly for two hours, turning several times.
7. When completely braised and crumbly, chop 3-4 decagrams capers.
8. Place the leg in another pan, strain the sauce over it and add the capers mixed with a few spoonfuls of sour cream.
9. Braise a little more, place the leg on dish and pour the sauce over it.
