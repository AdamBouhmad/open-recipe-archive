## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters thick heavy cream
- 14 dekagrams finest chocolate
- 2 deciliters water
- 14 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- 2 dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Set the thick heavy cream on ice 2 hours before use
2. Beat in a brass basin with a thick whip until the entire mass becomes foam
3. Cook 14 dekagrams of finest chocolate in 2 deciliters of water with continuous stirring until it reaches the consistency of child's porridge
4. Set aside
5. Mix this chocolate with 14 dekagrams of finely ground sugar and fold together with 2 dekagrams of dissolved isinglass into the foam
6. Pour into a mold, set on ice until frozen
