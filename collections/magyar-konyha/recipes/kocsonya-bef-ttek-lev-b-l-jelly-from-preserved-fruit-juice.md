## Ingredients

- 4 deciliters preserved fruit juice
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- 4 deciliters water
- 3 lemons, for juice
- dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Cook 14 dekagrams of sugar in 4 deciliters of water, and as soon as the sugar is dissolved, pour it into the preserved juice and mix well
2. Squeeze the juice of 3 lemons into it
3. Fold in the dissolved isinglass and proceed as with other jellies
4. If the preserved juice is thick, take more water and pour the juice into the water sweetened with sugar once the sugar has dissolved, and boil for a few moments
