## Ingredients

- fourteen dekagrams barley
- two deciliters water
- one kilogram sugar
- almond oil

## Directions

1. Put fourteen dekagrams barley into two deciliters of water and bring to fire, cooking down to one cup
2. Strain through cloth onto one kilogram sugar
3. Cook the sugar down and pour onto a stone plate coated with almond oil
4. Let the sugar rest for a few minutes, then fold it together
5. Place on a board coated with almond oil
6. Cut into pieces 15-20 centimeters long with scissors
7. Pull lengthwise and place on sheet metal
