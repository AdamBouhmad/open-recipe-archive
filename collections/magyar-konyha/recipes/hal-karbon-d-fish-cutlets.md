## Ingredients

- one kilogram pike from the middle section
- half kilogram carp
- salt
- flour
- butter
- half grated roll soaked in milk
- small onion head
- lemon peel
- ground pepper
- three egg yolks
- breadcrumbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook the pike in salted water until just cooked through
2. Salt the carp and roll in flour, then fry in butter until done, removing all bones
3. Soak half a grated roll in milk and squeeze out the liquid
4. Mince the cooked pike and carp together with the squeezed bread
5. Add finely chopped small onion, lemon peel, and ground pepper
6. Mix everything well with three egg yolks
7. Form the mixture into small cutlets
8. Coat with melted butter, roll in fine breadcrumbs, and fry in fresh butter until golden
9. Before serving, melt a little butter with finely chopped lemon peel and lemon juice
10. Pour this hot sauce over the cutlets when serving
11. Alternatively, the same mixture can be formed into small sausages, dipped in beaten egg, coated with breadcrumbs, and fried, served with lemon sauce
