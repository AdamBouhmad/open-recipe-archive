## Ingredients

- pumpkin
- salt
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the pumpkin and salt it
2. Squeeze out the salty water from the pieces
3. Bring vinegar and water to a boil
4. Fill jars halfway with this mixture and place the cut pumpkin pieces in them
5. Tie the jars well closed and place in a water bath, leaving them there until the pumpkin begins to boil
6. In winter or when using, add to a roux and prepare like in summer with a little sour cream
