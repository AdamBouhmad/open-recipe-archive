## Ingredients

- large piece of catfish
- salt
- finely minced bacon
- veal slices
- ham pieces
- parsley
- scallion
- peppercorns
- 2 deciliters white wine
- 2 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Wash the catfish, salt it and let it sit in salt for an hour
2. Lard it with finely minced bacon in five or six rows
3. Place in a pot with veal slices, ham pieces, a bundle of parsley, scallion, whole peppercorns, 2 deciliters white wine and the same amount of water, cover and cook slowly
4. When the fish is cooked, remove it, strain the sauce through a sieve, remove fat and reduce until 2 deciliters remain
5. Return the fish to the sauce to warm through completely, serve on a plate and pour the sauce over
