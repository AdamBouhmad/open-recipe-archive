## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams soaked almonds
- lemon juice
- 21 dekagrams powdered sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- 1 egg
- flour
- lard
- cinnamon or lemon zest

## Directions

1. Finely grind half of the soaked almonds and pour lemon juice over them
2. Cut the other half into long, fine pieces
3. Place powdered sugar in a bowl, add the finely ground almonds and 6 egg yolks, and stir together for half an hour
4. Beat 3 egg whites into stiff peaks and fold into the mixture
5. Prepare a chopped noodle dough from 1 egg, 1 egg yolk, sugar, and flour
6. Fry the noodles in lard until golden
7. Break the noodles into small pieces and mix with the almond pieces
8. Season with cinnamon or lemon zest and mix well
9. Coat a flat baking dish with butter and line it with puff pastry
10. Fill with the almond mixture
11. Cover with rounds of puff pastry
12. Bake slowly until golden and crispy
13. Turn out onto a serving plate and sprinkle with sugar
