## Ingredients

- eggs
- bread roll (zsemla)
- milk
- sour cream
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Hard boil eggs, cut in half, and remove the yolks
2. For 10 eggs, soak bread roll in milk and chop
3. Grate the hard-boiled egg yolks and add to the bread
4. Add a little sour cream, 1 tablespoon powdered sugar, and 1 teaspoon cinnamon, mix together
5. Butter a shallow dish and sprinkle with bread crumbs
6. Place the halved eggs in the dish
7. Fill the eggs with the prepared mixture
