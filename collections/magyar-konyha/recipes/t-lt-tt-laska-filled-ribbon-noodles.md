## Ingredients

- One liter milk
- Three eggs
- Crayfish butter (rákvaj)
- Crayfish tail
- One bread roll
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Bring one liter milk to boil
2. Cook short ribbon noodles made from three eggs in the milk and cool
3. Butter a dish with crayfish butter
4. Beat crayfish butter the size of half an egg until fluffy
5. Mix with a bread roll soaked in milk and crayfish tail
6. Place half of the noodles on the dish
7. Pour the mixture over
8. Place remaining noodles on top with more crayfish butter
9. Bake until golden
10. Sprinkle with sugar and serve
