## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter dried peas, large and beautiful
- 1.5 liters fresh water
- a piece of butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- finely chopped parsley
- meat broth
- salt
- noodle dough with flour

## Directions

1. Wash the peas and cook until completely tender in 1.5 liters fresh water
2. Heat a piece of butter and add flour, roasting until golden
3. Add finely chopped parsley and let it swell slightly
4. Pour the meat broth with the peas over it and cook well
5. Pass through a sieve and return to the pot
6. Salt to taste and serve over noodle dumplings
