## Ingredients

- 6 large floury potatoes
- 1 whole egg
- 3 egg yolks
- a lump of butter about the size of an egg, beaten fluffy
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Cook and peel the potatoes, then grate them
2. Beat 1 whole egg and 3 egg yolks into the fluffy beaten butter
3. Add the grated potatoes and a little salt
4. Stir for a long time
5. If the dough becomes too stiff, add another egg, as it should not be stiffer than the previous recipe
6. Bake as you would the previous tolka
7. Use to accompany braised beef or as a side dish for other meats
