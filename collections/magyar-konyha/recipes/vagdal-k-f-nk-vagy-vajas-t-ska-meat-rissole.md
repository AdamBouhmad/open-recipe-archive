## Ingredients

- half kilogram braised veal, not browned
- green parsley
- 1 onion
- butter or goose fat
- small teaspoon flour
- half ladle broth
- lard pastry dough
- egg white
- fine breadcrumbs
- hot fat
- fried parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Chop half a kilogram of braised veal finely, along with some green parsley and one onion.
2. Melt a little butter or goose fat, mix in a small teaspoon of flour, lightly brown the chopped parsley and onion, then add the chopped veal.
3. Pour half a ladle of broth over it and simmer until it becomes a thick paste.
4. Roll out lard pastry thinly and cut into pieces the width of the palm and length of a finger.
5. Brush one end with egg white and coat lengthwise with filling.
6. Roll up and press the two ends together firmly so they do not open during frying.
7. Brush the whole thing with egg white, sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs, and fry golden in hot fat.
8. Drain on clean absorbent paper and arrange neatly in a dish.
9. Garnish with fried parsley.
