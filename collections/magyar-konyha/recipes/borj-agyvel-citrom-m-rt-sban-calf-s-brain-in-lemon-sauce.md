## Ingredients

- butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- red onion
- good broth
- lemon zest
- lemon juice
- calf's brain
- salted water

## Directions

1. Fry one tablespoon of flour in melted butter
2. Add red onion cut in half and dilute with good broth
3. When the sauce is cooked, remove the onion and add lemon zest and lemon juice
4. Cook the calf's brain in salted water, remove its membrane cleanly, and cut into equal pieces
5. Place in the sauce, bring to a boil for a few minutes, and serve
