## Ingredients

- one kilogram of roasted meat
- salt
- lard
- red onion (two heads), finely chopped
- potatoes, cooked
- sour cream (two deciliters)
- butter or other fat
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Beat the roasted meat well, salt it, and set aside for half an hour
2. Put a little lard and the finely chopped red onion in a pan, add the roasted meat, and stew until tender, turning frequently
3. Once tender, remove and cut into equal oblong pieces
4. Return to the pan, add two deciliters of sour cream and stew a while longer
5. Slice the cooked potatoes
6. Grease a dish with butter or other fat, add a little sour cream
7. Layer the stewed roasted meat and onion that cooked with it, then a row of potatoes, then roasted meat again, and continue alternating until all ingredients are used
8. Top with sour cream and several pieces of butter, sprinkle with breadcrumbs
9. Place coals above and below so it browns nicely
