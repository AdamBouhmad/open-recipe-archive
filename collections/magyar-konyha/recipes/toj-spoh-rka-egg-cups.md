## Ingredients

- twelve hard-boiled eggs
- bread soaked in milk
- crayfish tails
- crayfish butter
- three whole eggs
- salt
- sour cream
- mushrooms
- green parsley
- prepared fish
- nutmeg
- sour cream for topping
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Hard-boil twelve eggs and peel them
2. Cut off the top and bottom to make lids
3. Hollow out the yolks without breaking the whites
4. Chop the yolks and cut lids finely with bread soaked in milk
5. Crush several crayfish tails, crayfish butter, and three whole eggs with salt in a mortar
6. Mix with six tablespoons of sour cream
7. Fill the egg whites with this mixture
8. For the sauce: dice mushrooms and cut cooked good quality fish into small cubes
9. Combine with green parsley, crayfish tails, and a little nutmeg
10. Braise lightly in crayfish butter
11. Place this mixture on a buttered plate
12. Stand the filled eggs on top
13. Pour two deciliters of sour cream over them
14. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and crayfish butter
15. Bake until golden brown
