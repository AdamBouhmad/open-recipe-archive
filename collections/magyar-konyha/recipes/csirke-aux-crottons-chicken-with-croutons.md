## Ingredients

- 2-3 large chickens
- thin bacon slices
- white paper
- chicken giblets
- chicken liver
- green parsley
- chopped onion
- 20-25 mushrooms
- butter
- soup stock
- salt
- pepper
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Salt 2-3 large chickens and clean well
2. Layer with thin bacon slices
3. Wrap in white paper and roast on a spit, being careful not to brown
4. Cook the giblets and when tender, finely chop with the liver, green parsley, chopped onion, and 20-25 mushrooms
5. Melt a piece of butter
6. Place the chopped mixture in the butter and braise for a while
7. Add 1 serving spoon of soup stock, a small amount of salt and pepper, and a few tablespoons of sour cream
8. Cook well
9. When the chickens are roasted, pour the sauce into the dish
10. Place the chickens on top
11. The chickens should not look roasted in appearance, but should remain beautifully white
