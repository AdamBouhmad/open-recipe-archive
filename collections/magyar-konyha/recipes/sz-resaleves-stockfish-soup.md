## Ingredients

- stockfish
- parsley
- carrot
- leek
- celery
- cabbage
- various vegetables
- salt
- butter
- flour
- potato dumplings

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the stockfish and place it on the fire as if you were making beef broth, treating it in the same manner.
2. Calculate one stockfish as equivalent to half a kilogram of meat and measure the water accordingly.
3. Add parsley, carrot, leek, celery, cabbage, and various other vegetables.
4. Salt and cook as long as beef broth.
5. Strain it.
6. Make a thin roux from butter and flour, mix it with the broth, and cook well.
7. Prepare fried potato dumplings, place them in the tureen, and pour the soup over them.
