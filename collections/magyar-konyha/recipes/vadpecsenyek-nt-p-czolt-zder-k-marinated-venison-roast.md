## Ingredients

- venison haunch
- 2 liters water
- 1 liter vinegar
- 2 tin spoons salt
- 1 spoon peppercorns
- 6 bay leaves
- 1 large onion
- 6 cloves
- thin bacon slices
- 1 tin spoon flour
- 2 decilitres sour cream
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Remove the outer membrane from the venison with a knife and place in a bowl
2. In a pot, combine 2 liters water, 1 liter vinegar, 2 tin spoons salt, 1 spoon peppercorns, 6 bay leaves, 1 large onion, and 6 cloves
3. Bring the mixture to a boil and cook for a quarter hour
4. Pour the hot brine over the venison in the bowl while hot
5. Cover and place in a cool location
6. Turn the meat in the brine twice daily
7. The meat can be cooked after just one day, but keep longer if possible
8. When ready to roast, place on a board and lard with thin bacon slices along the spine in four rows and on the sides
9. Place in a roasting pan and pour 1 liter of strained brine over it
10. Roast in a hot oven for one hour, basting several times
11. In a bowl, combine 1 tin spoon flour with 2 decilitres sour cream and mix together
12. Pour this mixture into the roasting liquid and simmer for a few minutes
13. Cut the venison with a sharp knife across the backbone so the bone is severed in each slice
14. Arrange in a long dish, garnish with lemon slices, and pour the sauce underneath
