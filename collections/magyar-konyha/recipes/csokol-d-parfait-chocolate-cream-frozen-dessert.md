## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams vanilla chocolate
- 0.3 liters hot milk
- 1.1 liters boiled and hot sweet cream
- 28 dekagrams crushed sugar
- 12 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Melt 28 dekagrams vanilla chocolate in 0.3 liters hot milk over charcoal fire, stirring slowly.
2. Pour in 1.1 liters boiled and hot sweet cream and cook everything together slowly for a quarter hour, then cool.
3. In the meantime, put 28 dekagrams crushed sugar in a pot and beat with 12 egg yolks until foamy.
4. Slowly pour the cooled chocolate into the egg mixture.
5. Strain through fine hair or silk sieve into a porcelain bowl.
6. Stir until completely cooled, then pour into the freezing mold and serve either as a whole block or in individual portions on ice cream plates.
