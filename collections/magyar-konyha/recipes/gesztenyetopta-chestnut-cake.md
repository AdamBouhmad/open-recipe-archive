## Ingredients

- one-half kilogram of chestnuts
- fifty-six dekagrams of powdered sugar
- seven egg yolks
- one bar of grated chocolate
- seven egg whites
- butter for the mold
- jam or whipped cream for filling
- rum sugar icing for coating

## Directions

1. Peel the chestnuts and cook them until soft.
2. Strain them and press through a sieve.
3. Mix the pressed chestnut thoroughly with fifty-six dekagrams of powdered sugar, seven egg yolks.
4. Add one bar of grated chocolate and seven egg whites beaten into foam.
5. Bake in a buttered mold.
6. When baked, cut the cake with a sharp knife into six to ten slices.
7. Spread jam or whipped cream between the individual cake layers.
8. Cover the finished cake with rum sugar icing.
