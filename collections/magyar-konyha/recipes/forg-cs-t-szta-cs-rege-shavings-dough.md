## Ingredients

- Fourteen dekagrams flour
- Seven dekagrams sugar powder
- Spoonful of wine
- Fat for frying
- Sugar for finishing

## Directions

1. Mix flour, sugar powder and a spoonful of wine well on a board
2. Roll out
3. Cut into two-finger-thick pieces with a dumpling cutter
4. Fry in fat
5. Finish with sugar and serve
