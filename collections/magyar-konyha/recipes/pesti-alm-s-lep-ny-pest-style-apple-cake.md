## Ingredients

- One liter fine flour
- Ten dekagrams butter
- One whole egg
- Milk
- Fourteen dekagrams softened almonds
- Twenty-one dekagrams raisins
- Fourteen dekagrams sugar
- Little cinnamon
- Sour, crumbly, peeled apples
- Butter for brushing
- Egg white
- Fine vanilla
- Sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry from one liter fine flour, ten dekagrams butter, one whole egg, and milk
2. Work it well and tear off twenty-five small pieces (cipócskák)
3. Cover with a warm cloth and let rise for half an hour
4. Finely chop fourteen dekagrams softened almonds
5. Remove pits from twenty-one dekagrams raisins and chop roughly
6. Finely grind fourteen dekagrams sugar and little cinnamon and mix well together
7. Cut sour, crumbly, peeled apples into very thin slices
8. Butter a flat plate prepared for this purpose
9. Stretch out one piece at a time over the plate
10. Brush each layer over the plate with melted butter and continue until eight layers are stretched out
11. Line with the chopped apples and half of the filling on top
12. Then again eight layers of pastry, each brushed with butter individually, then again a row of apples and filling
13. Finally stretch out the remaining nine pieces
14. Smooth the pastry hanging over the plate edge with a rolling pin so the layers stay together
15. Brush the top layer with egg white and pierce the pastry with vanilla a few times so it does not puff up during baking
16. Put in the oven and bake until nicely light brown
17. When baked, sprinkle with sugar and serve; can also be served cold
