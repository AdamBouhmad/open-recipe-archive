## Ingredients

- 4 deciliters hot milk
- 4 deciliters fine flour
- a piece of butter
- 10 egg yolks
- salt
- 1 spoonful of powdered sugar
- 6 egg whites, whipped stiff

## Directions

1. Cook the hot milk with the fine flour together
2. Let it become a thick, smooth paste with continuous stirring, adding a piece of butter
3. When cooled, stir in the egg yolks one at a time
4. Add salt, 1 spoonful of powdered sugar
5. Finally fold in the whipped egg whites
6. Handle as described in the preceding recipe
