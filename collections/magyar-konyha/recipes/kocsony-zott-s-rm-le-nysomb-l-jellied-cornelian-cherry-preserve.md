## Ingredients

- Cornelian cherries, stems removed
- water, slightly more than needed for cooking
- sugar, equal amount to the fruit

## Directions

1. Place the cornelian cherries, with stems removed, in a pot and pour over slightly more water than is necessary for them to cook.
2. Cook quickly over high heat so they retain their color, then press through a sieve.
3. Cook the sugar until it threads and forms heavy drops.
4. Take equal quantities of sugar and fruit; if you add the fruit to the sugar, it may only be brought to a boil a few times, then remove from the heat and when cooled, pour into glasses.
