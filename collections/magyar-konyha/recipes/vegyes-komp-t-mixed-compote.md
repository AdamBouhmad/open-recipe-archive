## Ingredients

- large white cherries
- sugar
- water
- lemon juice
- large sour cherries
- small pears
- rum
- large peaches

## Directions

1. Stew large white cherries in sugared water and lemon juice
2. Remove the stems from large sour cherries and stew them separately in water and sugar
3. Peel small pears that ripen at the same time and stew them separately in water and sugar without stems
4. When soft, add a spoonful of rum to them, simmer for a few more minutes and cool
5. Boil 9 decagrams of sugar with a cup of water until the sugar begins to thread
6. Clean 5 large peaches, cut them in half, remove the pits, dip them in the sugar, and boil until the sugar coats them completely, being careful they do not fall apart
7. When serving the compote, place the white cherries in the center of the dish, piled high, arrange the sour cherries in a wreath around the cherries, arrange the peaches around this, and finally the small pears, which are left whole, and to keep them beautifully white, brush them with lemon juice after peeling; these form the outer wreath, arranged so their stems point upward
