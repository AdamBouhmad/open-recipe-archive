## Ingredients

- pork, cut into small pieces
- red onion, finely chopped in generous amount
- paprika, small amount
- cabbage
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Place the pork cut into small pieces in a pot, add a generous amount of finely chopped red onion and a small amount of paprika, and braise with cabbage until soft. When sufficiently soft, pour sour cream over it so it becomes soupy, cook briefly, and it is done.
