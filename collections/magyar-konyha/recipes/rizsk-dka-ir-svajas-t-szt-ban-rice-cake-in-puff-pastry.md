## Ingredients

- 14 dekagramm rice
- 7 deciliter milk
- 9 dekagramm ground almond
- butter for frothing
- 10 dekagramm sugar rubbed with orange zest
- 1 whole egg
- 4 egg yolks
- puff pastry

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in milk until well softened
2. Add ground almond to the cooked rice and let cool
3. Place the cooled rice in frothed butter
4. Add the sugar rubbed with orange zest, beaten until frothy
5. During stirring, add one whole egg and four egg yolks
6. Prepare puff pastry and roll it out as if making noodles
7. Line long, flat molds with puff pastry
8. Fill each mold with the rice mixture
9. Cover with pastry and bake until golden brown
