## Ingredients

- 56 dekagrams rice, well washed
- 7 deciliters cream
- piece of vanilla
- 21 dekagrams fine sugar
- 4 deciliters cream

## Directions

1. Cook 56 dekagrams washed rice in 7 deciliters cream with a piece of vanilla cut into small pieces until the rice is soft
2. Press finely
3. Beat 21 dekagrams finely crushed sugar with the rice in a bowl until frothy
4. Add 4 deciliters cream
5. Pour into freezing container
6. Bury in well-salted ice and let freeze
7. During freezing, scrape down around the container twice without stirring
8. When turned out, line the bottom with two rows of Spanish fritters arranged like a crown
