## Ingredients

- seven deciliters of flour
- one whole egg
- water

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with one whole egg and water into a very stiff dough, more dry than wet
2. Knead the dough well
3. Press through a coarse-mesh sieve
4. Then press through progressively finer sieves to create tarhonya of various sizes
5. Dry in the sun
6. Use in soups, with meat, with chicken, with pörkölt, etc.
