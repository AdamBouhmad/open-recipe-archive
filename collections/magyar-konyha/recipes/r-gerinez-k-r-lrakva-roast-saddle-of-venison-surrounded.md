## Ingredients

- one saddle of venison
- twine
- carrots
- lettuce
- beef stock
- salt
- pepper
- sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Tie the venison saddle with twine to maintain its shape and roast it.
2. Arrange it on the plate and surround it with carrots and lettuce.
3. Cut the carrots into equal balls, wash them, and braise them with beef stock, salt, pepper, and sugar.
4. Wash the lettuce, clean away the stems, braise with butter, crush with a knife, and arrange in squares on the plate.
