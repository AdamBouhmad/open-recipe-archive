## Ingredients

- Beautiful white cauliflower
- Salt
- Water
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Use beautiful white cauliflower; pour boiling salted water over the whole rosettes
2. When they are sufficiently soft, strain off the water and rinse with fresh water so they remain beautifully white
3. Season lightly with salt, arrange neatly on a plate, and dress with vinegar and oil
