## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters good milk
- 14 dekagrams rice, well picked and cleanly washed
- 7 deciliters good heavy cream
- 4 egg whites
- 18 dekagrams sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. In a pot lined with butter, bring seven deciliters of good milk to a boil and cook fourteen dekagrams of well-picked and cleanly washed rice until soft
2. In a pot, beat seven deciliters of good heavy cream well with four egg whites
3. Place over gentle heat and stir until it comes to a slight boil
4. Add eighteen dekagrams of sugar and vanilla
5. Place the rice in a deep bowl, pour the mixture over it, mix well
6. Place on ice or in a cellar to freeze
