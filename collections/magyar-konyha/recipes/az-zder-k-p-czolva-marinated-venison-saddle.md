## Ingredients

- one venison saddle
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaves
- red onion
- cloves

## Directions

1. Remove the outer membrane-like skin from the venison saddle with a knife and place it in a dish.
2. To prepare the marinade for one whole venison saddle, put in a pot: two liters of water, one liter of vinegar, two tin spoonfuls of salt, one spoonful of pepper, six bay leaves, one large head of red onion, and six cloves.
3. Mix these together, bring to a boil, and boil for a quarter hour.
4. Pour this hot marinade over the venison in the dish, cover, and keep in a cool place.
5. The meat can be kept this way for one to two days or even longer, turning it twice daily.
