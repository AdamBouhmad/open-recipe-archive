## Ingredients

- 17 dekagrams soaked almonds
- cold water
- 17 dekagrams sugar
- 7 dekagrams black bread
- 1 tablespoon malaga or Menes wine
- lemon zest
- little cinnamon
- 1 whole egg
- 5 egg yolks
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Grind 17 dekagrams soaked almonds, moistening with a little cold water to prevent them from becoming oily
2. Finely grind the same amount of sugar
3. Grate 7 dekagrams black bread and moisten with 1 tablespoon malaga or Menes wine
4. Finely chop lemon zest with a little cinnamon and put in a bowl
5. Add 1 whole egg and 5 egg yolks one after another, stirring until fluffy, which takes about half an hour
6. Add lightly whipped whites of 5 eggs
7. Pour into a buttered basin or mold and steam
8. After three-quarters of an hour turn onto a plate and pour wine sauce over it
