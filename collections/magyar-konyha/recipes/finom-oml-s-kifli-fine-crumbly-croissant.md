## Ingredients

- 750 grams flour
- 500 grams fat
- salt
- knife-tip amount of ground clove
- 1 coffee spoon ground cinnamon
- 6 egg yolks
- 30 grams yeast
- warm sugared water
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- sliced almonds
- cinnamon sugar
- clove sugar

## Directions

1. Take 750 grams flour and 500 grams fat, and with half the flour mix the fat, little salt, knife-tip of ground clove, 1 coffee spoon ground cinnamon together with a pastry roller, and set aside one egg-sized piece of this dough, rolling it out to half a finger thickness and putting it in a cold place
2. With the remaining flour, 6 egg yolks, 30 grams yeast dissolved in warm sugared water, and 3 tablespoons sugar and the egg-sized piece of fatty dough, knead a fine soft dough, working it for a long time until it becomes smooth and bubbly
3. Place in a warm location and let it rise well, then turn it onto a board and stretch it to the proper size, and fold in the cooled fatty dough, letting it rest for half an hour folded
4. Roll out three times at half-hour intervals, finally leaving it knife-edge thin
5. Sprinkle with sliced almonds, cinnamon sugar, and clove sugar
6. Cut into cubes with a hot knife and form croissants from the cubes, coat with egg, sprinkle almonds on top and bake quickly until nicely golden
7. It rises beautifully, fine and crumbly like puff pastry and layered
