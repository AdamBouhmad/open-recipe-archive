## Ingredients

- quails, cleaned
- bacon slices
- piece of veal
- green parsley
- onion
- salt
- half glass white wine
- good broth

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned quails in a pot.
2. Add a few slices of bacon, a piece of veal, some green parsley, an onion, a little salt, half a glass of white wine, and several spoonfuls of good broth.
3. Braise over low heat.
4. Remove the quails and strain the sauce of fat.
5. Place the quails in the center of a dish, pour the sauce over them, and close in a rice border as described in recipe 119.
