## Ingredients

- 21 dekagrams best quality green coffee
- 7 deciliters hot water
- juice of 6 lemons
- 28 dekagrams sugar
- 4.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Pour 7 deciliters of hot water over 21 dekagrams of green coffee in a soup pot
2. Cover well and let stand for 1 hour
3. Strain through clean cloth
4. Add the juice of 6 lemons
5. Dissolve 28 dekagrams of sugar in the liquid
6. Add 4.5 dekagrams of dissolved isinglass
7. Strain through cloth
8. Pour into mold and freeze on ice
