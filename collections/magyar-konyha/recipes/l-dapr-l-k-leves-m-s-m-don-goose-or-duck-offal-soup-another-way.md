## Ingredients

- goose or duck offal
- nutmeg
- rice broth
- flour
- green parsley
- pepper
- cold water
- goose liver
- button mushrooms

## Directions

1. Clean and finely chop goose or duck offal.
2. Cook with a little nutmeg until half tender.
3. Add a little rice broth.
4. When the meat is half soft, prepare a light roux with finely chopped green parsley and pepper added.
5. Thin with a little cold water so it is not lumpy.
6. Add to the offal and continue cooking.
7. Remove the goose liver, cut into nice long pieces.
8. Pour the soup over it and serve.
9. If available, button mushrooms can be added for good flavor, though the soup is good without them.
