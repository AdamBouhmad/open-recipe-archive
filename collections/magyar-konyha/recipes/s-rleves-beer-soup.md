## Ingredients

- 1 liter white beer
- a little cinnamon
- lemon peel
- sugar to taste
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter good hot milk foam
- breadcrumbs, fried and cut into long pieces

## Directions

1. Cook the beer with cinnamon, lemon peel, and sugar until well reduced
2. Beat 6 egg yolks in a bowl and slowly pour in 1 deciliter of good hot milk foam, stirring well
3. Serve over fried breadcrumb pieces
