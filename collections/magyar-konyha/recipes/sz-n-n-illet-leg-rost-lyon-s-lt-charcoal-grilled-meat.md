## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram young beef brisket or kidney loin with skin and flap
- salt
- paprika

## Directions

1. Take a large pile of hot charcoal from the store-stove and place it on the stove top, using hardwood charcoal only, not coal
2. Place a low wire mesh screen on four legs over the charcoal
3. Cut the meat into pieces the size of a plate and salt them
4. Arrange the meat in a single layer on the wire mesh above the charcoal
5. Keep the burning charcoal lively beneath the mesh, as the meat must cook continuously on high heat or it will become spoiled
6. When one side is nicely browned, flip it with a fork; when both sides are browned, remove it to small white boards
7. Serve each person on such a board so they may cut it themselves as they prefer, consuming it hot
8. Pour paprika and salt to the edge of each small board with a teaspoon for each person
9. This can also be made with lamb brisket, which is much more tender, or with beautiful large beef rib steaks; fatty meat is better
