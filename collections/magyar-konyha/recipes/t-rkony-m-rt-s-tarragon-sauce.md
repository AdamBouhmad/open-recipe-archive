## Ingredients

- fresh tarragon, cleaned and finely chopped
- hot meat broth
- one deciliter of sour cream mixed with a little flour
- salt
- lemon juice or vinegar (optional, if not sour enough)

## Directions

1. Pour hot meat broth over the finely chopped, well-washed tarragon
2. Add a deciliter of sour cream mixed with a little flour
3. If not sour enough, add a teaspoonful of lemon juice or vinegar
4. Cook until thickened to half consistency and salt
5. Alternatively, stew the finely chopped fresh tarragon leaves first in butter or lard, then add the meat broth, flour-thickened sour cream, and a little vinegar
