## Ingredients

- 14 dekagramms cooked, floury potatoes
- 7 dekagramms butter
- 8 eggs
- 7 dekagramms finely ground almonds (some bitter)
- 10 dekagramms ground sugar
- zest of one lemon, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Crush the cooked potatoes and butter together in a mortar
2. Place in a bowl
3. Beat in the eggs one by one
4. Add the almonds, sugar and lemon zest
5. Stir until fluffy
6. Fold in the stiff peaks of 4 egg whites
7. Grease a cake pan with butter
8. Fill with the mixture
9. Bake slowly
10. When baked, remove the rim, sprinkle with sugar and serve to the table
