## Ingredients

- one kilogram beef loin slice
- one spoon fat
- salt
- two decilitres vinegar
- one carrot, round-cut
- parsley root
- kohlrabi
- celery
- one onion
- several peppercorns
- four large potatoes
- water

## Directions

1. Place beef loin slice in a pot with one spoon fat, salt, and two decilitres vinegar
2. Add carrot, parsley root, kohlrabi, celery, one onion, and several peppercorns
3. Heat and cook until tender, always replenishing the liquid with a little cold water if it reduces
4. When the roast is soft, add four large potatoes, peeled and cut into slices, with enough water to cover them
5. Cover again and cook without stirring until the potatoes are soft and their fat is released
6. Cut the roast into slices and serve the vegetables and potatoes mixed together; this is a very tasty roast
