## Ingredients

- four deciliters good sour cream
- one small teaspoon flour
- one tablespoon stock
- a handful of soaked almonds
- a piece of sugar
- grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Mix the sour cream well with a small teaspoon of flour
2. Pour in one tablespoon of stock
3. Crush the soaked almonds and add them to the sauce together with a piece of sugar
4. Finally add grated horseradish
