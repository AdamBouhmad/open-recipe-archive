## Ingredients

- 2 oranges, zest
- 28 dekagramm sugar
- 1/2 orange, juice
- pistachios, coarsely ground

## Directions

1. Rub 2 orange zests on 28 dekagramm sugar, crush and sift it
2. In a bowl, beat together with 1/2 orange juice for about 15 minutes
3. Add a little more juice and continue beating until the ice becomes thick and does not flow
4. Spread the ice knife-thickness onto wafers cut into long strips
5. Sprinkle with coarsely ground pistachios
6. Dry in very gentle heat
