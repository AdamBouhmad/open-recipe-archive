## Ingredients

- twenty-eight dekagrams of roasted coffee
- seven deciliters of good light coffee
- sugar to taste
- one and a half liters of good sour cream
- fourteen dekagrams of sugar

## Directions

1. Make good light coffee from the roasted coffee
2. Sweeten well and let cool in an ice cream mold without stirring
3. Mix the sour cream with fourteen dekagrams of sugar
4. Beat on ice until foamy
5. Just before serving, mix into the coffee
6. Serve in glass cups or bowls
