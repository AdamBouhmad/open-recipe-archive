## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- butter
- flour
- good broth
- sour cream
- nutmeg flower, finely ground

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower
2. If the florets are large, cut them into four pieces
3. Cook in salted water
4. Melt butter and brown two spoonfuls of flour in it
5. Thin with good broth, add two to three spoonfuls of sour cream and a small amount of finely ground nutmeg flower
6. Cut the cauliflower stem short and arrange the florets prettily in rose shape in the plate
7. Pour the well-cooked sauce over them and bring to a boil
8. When serving, be careful that the florets look beautiful
9. Can be served with breaded chicken or frog cutlets
