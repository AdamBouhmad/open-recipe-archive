## Ingredients

- carefully selected paczal (tripe)
- hot water
- salt
- vegetables
- half liter good sour cream
- a few tablespoons fine flour
- a few hard-boiled eggs
- fresh parsley leaves
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the carefully selected paczal thoroughly with hot water and broth
2. Slice it thinly on a board
3. Place in hot water with salt and cook well
4. Add vegetables to it
5. Prepare a binding from half liter good sour cream and a few tablespoons fine flour
6. Chop a few hard-boiled eggs into cubes and mix in
7. Carefully add the binding, sprinkle in fresh parsley leaves
8. Bring to a boil and serve
9. Alternatively, instead of the sour cream binding, prepare a fatty breadcrumb roux and use that to bind it, season with a little lemon juice and serve
