## Ingredients

- 42 dekagramm sugar
- 42 dekagramm almonds with skin, finely ground
- zest of 1 lemon, finely chopped
- zest of half an orange, finely chopped
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 2-3 cloves
- egg white
- butter or wax for greasing
- powdered sugar
- candied citrus peel

## Directions

1. Mix together the finely ground sugar and almonds with skin, finely chopped lemon and orange zest, ground cinnamon with cloves
2. On a board, knead the mixture with egg white using a large knife into a firm dough, being careful not to make it too soft or liquid
3. Sprinkle the board and dough with finely ground sugar and roll out to the thickness of a finger
4. Cut three equal sheets from it and place on a baking sheet greased with butter or wax
5. On one of the sheets before baking, press a lattice from Spanish puff paste using a pastry bag, as is customary for Linz torte
6. The dough for this must be quite firm and should be mixed with sugar and vanilla just as for puff cake dough
7. Around the edge of the sheet press small Spanish puff pastries so that the lattice perimeter forms a wreath shape
8. When the sheets have dried and the lattice has hardened, fill each with different preserves and stack them
9. Place the lattice on top, sprinkle with sugar, and arrange with preserved fruit
10. Bake slowly in a cooled oven until dry
