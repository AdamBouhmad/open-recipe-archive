## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams veal, roasted white
- 2 rolls, grated and soaked in milk
- 7 dekagrams butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- Salt
- Fine bread crumbs
- Fresh butter

## Directions

1. Chop 28 dekagrams of white-roasted veal finely, add 2 grated rolls soaked in milk (also chopped finely), and pound both finely in a mortar.
2. Beat 7 dekagrams of butter until fluffy, add 2 eggs and 1 egg yolk, and add the chopped mixture.
3. Season with salt and mix well; if still too soft, add a little fine bread crumbs.
4. Form small dumplings, fry them until a beautiful yellow in fresh butter, pour hot brown broth over them, and serve.
