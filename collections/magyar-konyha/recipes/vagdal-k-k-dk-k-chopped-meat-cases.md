## Ingredients

- roasted turkey
- half calf sweetbread
- six mushrooms
- twenty crayfish tails and claws
- crayfish butter
- green peas
- fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Chop roasted turkey, half calf sweetbread, six mushrooms, and the tails and claws of twenty crayfish into small cubes
2. Make fourteen decagrams of crayfish butter from the shells
3. Braise the mushrooms in the crayfish butter with three deciliters of green peas and finely chopped fresh parsley
4. Add the chopped ingredients and continue cooking
