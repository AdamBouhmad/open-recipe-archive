## Ingredients

- two or three firm tomatoes
- salt
- red onion
- oil
- strong vinegar

## Directions

1. With a sharp knife, slice two or three firm tomatoes into thin rounds
2. Place these in a soup plate
3. Salt heavily and sprinkle with finely chopped red onion
4. Layer more tomato slices on top, salt these
5. Sprinkle with red onion and pepper
6. Finally pour oil and plenty of strong vinegar over everything
7. Place the salad in a cool place where it must stand for at least an hour to be palatable
8. Served with beef it is particularly tasty
