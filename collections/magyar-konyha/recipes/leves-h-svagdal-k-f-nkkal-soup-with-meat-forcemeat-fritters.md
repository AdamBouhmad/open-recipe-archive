## Ingredients

- leftover roasted meat, especially turkey breast
- one or two button mushrooms
- half a bread roll soaked in milk
- green parsley
- salt
- egg yolk
- fine bread crumbs
- fat
- brown sauce

## Directions

1. Finely chop leftover roasted meat, especially turkey breast, one or two button mushrooms, half a bread roll soaked in milk, and green parsley.
2. Add a little salt and egg yolk and mix together.
3. Place the forcemeat on a board sprinkled with fine bread crumbs.
4. Stretch it to the thickness of a finger.
5. Cut out thumb-sized fritters with a cutter.
6. Fry light brown in fat.
7. Place in a soup bowl.
8. Pour hot brown sauce over it and serve at once to the table.
