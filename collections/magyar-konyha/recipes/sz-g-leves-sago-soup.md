## Ingredients

- seven to nine dekagrams sago
- five dekagrams butter
- flour
- cold white broth
- two egg yolks
- fresh sour cream

## Directions

1. Warm five dekagrams butter without making it hot
2. Add the sago and braise with frequent stirring, keeping it beautifully white
3. Pour in a small spoonful of fine flour and stir several times
4. Braise a few more minutes
5. Pour cold white broth over it and let it cook until the grains are large and shiny like glass
6. Beat two egg yolks with a good spoonful of fresh sour cream
7. At the moment of serving pour this mixture into the soup
