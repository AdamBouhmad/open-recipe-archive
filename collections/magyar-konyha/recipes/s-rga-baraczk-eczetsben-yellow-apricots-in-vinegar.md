## Ingredients

- peeled and halved apricots
- vinegar
- sugar
- 2 kilograms fruit requires 1 liter vinegar and 80 dekagrams sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the peeled and halved apricots once with sugar and vinegar (calculating 2 kilograms fruit to 1 liter vinegar and 80 dekagrams sugar)
2. Then treat them as you would melon
