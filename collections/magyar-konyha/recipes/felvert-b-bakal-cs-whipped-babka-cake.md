## Ingredients

- 140 grams butter
- 10 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter sour cream
- 36 grams cheese yeast
- little salt
- sugar
- 1 lemon rind
- 240 grams fine flour
- chopped almonds
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Whip 140 grams butter finely and mix in 10 egg yolks one by one
2. Mix 36 grams cheese yeast into 1 deciliter sour cream and slowly add this to the butter mixture
3. Salt lightly, rub a piece of sugar with a lemon rind, crush it and add with 240 grams fine flour
4. Stir only until the flour is well mixed
5. Fill a mold buttered and sprinkled with chopped almonds and breadcrumbs halfway, let it rise in a warm place and then bake slowly
