## Ingredients

- Veal leg
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Clean the leg thoroughly and salt it
2. Let it rest in salt for two hours
3. Thread it onto a spit so the upper part is rounded and the shanks can be tied to the spit in proper direction
4. Roast over steady heat or in the oven
5. Baste with butter and broth
6. Finally drizzle only pure butter on it so it takes on a beautiful color and can be served glossy on the table
7. Pour the broth underneath if desired
