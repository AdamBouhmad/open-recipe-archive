## Ingredients

- 4 deciliter fresh sour cream
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 tablespoons flour
- sugar
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Pour three tablespoons of fresh sour cream into a pot
2. Gradually stir in six egg yolks and six tablespoons flour
3. Add sugar to taste
4. Pour in the remaining sour cream
5. Stir well and fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
6. Bake in the appropriate mold until done
7. Sprinkle with fine sugar and serve
