## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams sugar
- water
- 14 dekagrams ground almond
- 1 lemon, zest finely chopped and juice
- 1 egg white, stiffly beaten

## Directions

1. Cook 14 dekagrams of sugar with 2 spoonfuls of water until it threads
2. Add 14 dekagrams of ground almond, the finely chopped zest and juice of 1 lemon, and the stiffly beaten white of 1 egg
