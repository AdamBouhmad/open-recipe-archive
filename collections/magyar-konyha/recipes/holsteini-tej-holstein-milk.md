## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams softened almonds
- 7 deciliters good heavy cream, boiled
- water

## Directions

1. Finely grind 14 dekagrams of softened almonds, moistening slightly with water during grinding so it does not become oily
2. Add this to 7 deciliters of boiled good heavy cream and heat for several moments
