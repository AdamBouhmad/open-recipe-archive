## Ingredients

- seven egg whites
- half a liter white strong meat broth
- butter
- one young cabbage
- two kohlrabi
- two carrots
- fresh goose fat
- three-quarter part of a roll

## Directions

1. Beat seven egg whites well in a pot and mix with half a liter of white strong meat broth
2. Pour into a butter-greased pot and poach
3. Meanwhile, clean a young cabbage, two kohlrabi, and two carrots
4. Braise them with a little butter or fresh goose fat, but in such a way that they become brown, for which purpose pour a little meat broth over them several times
5. Finally, cut three-quarter part of a roll lengthwise and fry until golden in fat
6. When the consommé is solidified from braising, turn it out onto a clean board
7. Cut it with a dumpling cutter into crescent-shaped or round slices
8. Place the vegetables, the fried bread, and the consommé in a soup bowl
9. Pour brown broth over it
