## Ingredients

- 1 part white wax
- 4 parts lard
- 1 part rose water

## Directions

1. Melt white wax in a crucible
2. Add 4 parts lard and melt both together
3. When cooled, add 1 part rose water
4. Use for sore breasts and chapped skin
