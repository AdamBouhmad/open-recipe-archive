## Ingredients

- 100 grams butter
- 5 egg yolks
- salt
- 1 tablespoon ground sugar
- 36 grams melted fresh yeast
- 4 deciliters lukewarm sour cream
- flour
- 2 deciliters finely ground poppy
- 1 lemon rind finely chopped
- knife-tip amount of cinnamon
- 120 grams sugar
- half coffee cup sour cream

## Directions

1. Whip 100 grams butter until foamy and mix in 5 egg yolks, little salt, 1 tablespoon ground sugar, 36 grams melted fresh yeast and 4 deciliters lukewarm sour cream
2. When all is well mixed, add so much flour that the dough is rather soft than hard and stir until it pulls away from the spoon
3. Mix together 2 deciliters finely ground poppy with 1 lemon rind chopped finely, knife-tip of cinnamon and 120 grams sugar, and cook this with half a coffee cup sour cream
4. Butter the kádkák molds, put some dough in them, fill with the poppy mixture, cover again with dough, not letting the molds be completely full
5. Let rise in a warm place and bake nicely
6. When baked, turn out onto a plate and sprinkle with sugar
