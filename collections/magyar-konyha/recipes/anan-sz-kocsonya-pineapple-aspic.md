## Ingredients

- two large, ripe pineapples
- eight deciliters water
- thirty-five dekagrams sugar
- fourteen dekagrams sugar
- two deciliters water
- juice of three lemons
- one and one-quarter dekagrams dissolved fish glue

## Directions

1. Peel the two large ripe pineapples and crush the peel in a mortar
2. Pour eight deciliters water over it
3. Dissolve in a brass pan and strain
4. In this liquid cook thirty-five dekagrams sugar until it becomes invisible
5. Cut the pineapple into thin slices
6. Cook fourteen dekagrams sugar in two deciliters water until it begins to thicken
7. Place the slices into this syrup, bring to a boil for a few minutes
8. Set aside and let cool
9. Strain off the juice and mix with the already-cooked sugar
10. Add the juice of three lemons and one and one-quarter dekagrams dissolved fish glue
11. After mixing everything well, strain through a taut kitchen cloth
12. When the entire aspic mass is clear, pour two centimeters height into a mold that has been strongly packed in ice
13. Let it freeze, then arrange half of the pineapple slices on top
14. Pour over half a coffee cup of unfrozen juice, which must then freeze to hold the pieces in place
15. Pour aspic juice over it, and when it has frozen, arrange the second half of the pineapple slices on top
16. When these are also frozen as the first ones were, pour the final part of the aspic over it
17. Cover the whole with a tin plate loaded with ice and keep in the ice until, with care and speed, you turn it out onto the table
