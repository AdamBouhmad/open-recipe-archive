## Ingredients

- one and one half grated rolls
- four and one half decilitres of good cream
- seven dekagrams of soaked finely ground almonds
- sugar
- seven dekagrams of butter
- three egg yolks and two whole eggs
- five dekagrams of citronât cut into large cubes
- preserved green walnuts, green plums, pitted cherries and cornelian cherries
- three egg whites
- rum for filling

## Directions

1. Pour four and one half decilitres of good cream over one and one half grated rolls
2. Add seven dekagrams of soaked finely ground almonds and enough sugar for sweetness
3. Cook to a paste with continuous stirring and cool
4. Mix in seven dekagrams of butter, three egg yolks and two whole eggs one after another
5. Add five dekagrams of citronât cut into large cubes, preserved green walnuts, green plums, pitted cherries and cornelian cherries in equal amounts and as much as so the roll is not overly noticeable
6. Finally fold in a little stiff white of three eggs
7. Put the pudding in a buttered ring mould and steam for one hour
8. Turn out when serving and at that moment fill the hollow in the pudding with good rum, add a few sugar lumps, set alight and serve quickly flaming
9. This paste is especially suitable for suppers
10. Arrange the bottom of the mould attractively with half-moon shaped citronât pieces and soaked almonds
11. Serve with orange juice sweetened with a little sugar or wine sauce
