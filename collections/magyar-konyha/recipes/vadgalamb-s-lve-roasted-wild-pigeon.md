## Ingredients

- wild pigeon
- salt
- vinegar
- wine
- onion
- bay leaf
- lemon peel
- bacon
- sour cream
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and salt the pigeon
2. Make a brine from vinegar, wine, onion, bay leaf and lemon peel
3. Place the pigeons in the brine for two to three days, especially if they are old
4. Lard with finely chopped bacon and roast slowly
5. Baste frequently with brine and sour cream during roasting
6. Prepare the sauce by making a roux with a few spoonfuls of flour in melted butter
7. Pour enough brine over to make the sauce sufficiently thick
8. Add the drippings from the pigeons and a few spoonfuls of sour cream, and bring to a boil
9. Place the roasted pigeons on a plate and pour the sauce underneath
