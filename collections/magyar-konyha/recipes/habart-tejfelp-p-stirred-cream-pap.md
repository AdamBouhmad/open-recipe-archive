## Ingredients

- 4 decilitres milk
- butter
- flour (amount to absorb 5-6 tablespoons of butter)
- sugar
- 8 dekagrams finely ground almonds
- vanilla
- 4 decilitres cream
- 6 egg yolks
- egg white foam

## Directions

1. Prepare wafer cones and many small pasta pieces
2. Make a béchamel from the milk
3. Add the pasta pieces (double the amount of the béchamel), the finely ground almonds, sugar to taste, and a small amount of ground vanilla to a pot
4. Pour 4 decilitres of cream over it and cook to a pap consistency with continuous stirring, thinning with more milk if too thick
5. Cool the mixture
6. Beat 6 egg yolks into the cooled mixture one by one until well foamed
7. Gently fold in the stiff foam of a few egg whites
8. Butter a mould and coat it densely with small pasta pieces
9. Fill with the pap and steam for three-quarters of an hour
10. When serving, turn out and arrange the wafer cones in a neat order, fill the cones with compote, and serve quickly
