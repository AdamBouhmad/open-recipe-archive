## Ingredients

- nine egg yolks
- powdered sugar
- lemon
- quarter kilogram of blanched almonds
- whole lemon zest
- ground cinnamon
- ground cloves
- nine egg whites
- two crispy rolls
- butter
- flour
- one liter of black cherries

## Directions

1. Beat nine egg yolks with nine spoonfuls of powdered sugar and lemon juice until it thickens and becomes foamy
2. Finely chop quarter kilogram of blanched almonds and divide in half
3. Mix half into the egg yolk mixture
4. Add the grated zest of one whole lemon, one teaspoon of ground cinnamon, and a knife-point of ground cloves
5. Whip nine egg whites into stiff peaks
6. Add the sifted crumbs of two crispy rolls and fold in the meringue
7. Pour into a buttered and floured baking pan
8. Bake in moderate heat in the oven
9. Pit one liter of black cherries
10. When the cake has formed a crust on top, sprinkle the cherries on it and return to bake
11. The heavy cherries will sink partway into the soft dough
12. When the cake is done, whip four to six egg whites into stiff peaks
13. Sweeten well with sugar and coat the top of the cake with it, one finger thickness
14. Sprinkle the remaining ground almonds on top
15. When the meringue has turned golden, place on a sieve so the bottom does not soften
