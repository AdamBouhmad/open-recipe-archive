## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons fine flour
- 7 deciliters sour cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 140 grams granulated sugar
- vanilla
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- 1 egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Mix flour finely with sour cream, egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla
2. Cook to a smooth paste with continuous stirring, cool slightly
3. Spread thinly and evenly on buttered parchment paper using a wide knife moistened in water to the thickness of a little finger and cool
4. Warm the parchment slightly and carefully turn the milk jelly onto a flour-dusted board
5. Cut out coin-sized fritters with a cutter
6. Coat densely with fine breadcrumbs, flip with a wide knife and coat the other side with egg and breadcrumbs
7. Note that these fritters must not be touched by hand as they are very delicate and crumbs would be pressed into the milk jelly, causing loss of quality and appearance
8. Fry in hot fat to a beautiful golden color, drain the fat, dust with vanilla sugar and serve
