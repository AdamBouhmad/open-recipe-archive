## Ingredients

- leftover roasted poultry
- one tablespoon hot fat
- a good handful of breadcrumbs or good homemade bread crumbs
- small raisins
- cleaned finely broken almonds
- meat broth
- lemon slices
- optional: strong boiled wine

## Directions

1. Toast a good handful of breadcrumbs in one tablespoon hot fat until beautifully red
2. Add small raisins and cleaned finely broken almonds generously
3. Dilute with meat broth, sour with lemon slices
4. When it comes to a boil, add the roasted poultry pieces
5. Let cook for a little while until the meat is heated through, then remove from heat and keep warm until serving
6. Alternatively, dilute with good strong boiled wine, in which case only lemon zest is added
