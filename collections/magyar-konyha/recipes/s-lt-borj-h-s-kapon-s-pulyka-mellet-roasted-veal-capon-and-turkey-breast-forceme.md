## Ingredients

- roasted veal
- capon breast
- turkey breast
- bread roll
- milk
- three eggs
- green parsley
- seven decagrams butter
- three eggs
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- two tablespoons sour cream
- brown sauce

## Directions

1. Finely chop the roasted veal, capon breast, and turkey breast.
2. Soak bread roll in milk and squeeze dry.
3. Chop three eggs and finely chop green parsley together.
4. Beat seven decagrams butter until fluffy.
5. Add the chopped meat mixture to the beaten butter.
6. Beat in three eggs one at a time.
7. Add salt, pepper, a little bread crumbs, and two tablespoons of sour cream.
8. Mix everything together well.
9. Place the forcemeat mixture on a cloth greased with butter, tie it up.
10. Cook in boiling salted water for half an hour.
11. Remove carefully and place in a soup bowl.
12. Pour brown sauce over it.
