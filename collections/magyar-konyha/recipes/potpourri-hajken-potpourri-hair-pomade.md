## Ingredients

- one kilogram fresh mutton tallow
- seventy-five dekagrams vegetable oil
- sixty-five dekagrams hair powder
- three dekagrams lavender oil
- three dekagrams bergamot oil
- three dekagrams orange blossom oil
- four grams clove oil

## Directions

1. Combine one kilogram of fresh mutton tallow, seventy-five dekagrams of vegetable oil, sixty-five dekagrams of hair powder, three dekagrams of lavender oil, three dekagrams of bergamot oil, three dekagrams of orange blossom oil, and four grams of clove oil
