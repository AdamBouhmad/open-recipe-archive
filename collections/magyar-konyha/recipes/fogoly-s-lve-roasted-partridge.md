## Ingredients

- partridge
- salt
- thin bacon slices the size of a palm
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the partridge, salt it
2. Cut thin bacon slices the size of a palm
3. Fold the partridge's wings and legs neatly and prepare it
4. During roasting, baste it frequently with butter
5. Instead of wrapping it in bacon leaves, the partridge can also be larded with finely chopped bacon
