## Ingredients

- pancakes
- butter
- seven dekagrams swollen and finely ground almonds
- ten dekagrams finely ground sugar
- vanilla
- six egg yolks
- four deciliters sour cream
- two deciliters strained strawberries, raspberries or currants
- ten to fourteen dekagrams sugar

## Directions

1. Cook pancakes and cut them into fine strips
2. Thickly sprinkle a buttered saucepan with the strips, leaving about one finger's width of space at the top
3. In a bowl, combine seven dekagrams swollen and finely ground almonds, ten dekagrams finely ground sugar, a little vanilla and six egg yolks, mix well with four deciliters sour cream
4. Pour over the pancakes and steam
5. When serving, turn out onto a platter
6. Cook two deciliters strained strawberries, raspberries or currants with ten to fourteen dekagrams ground sugar until thick and coat the top of the pancakes smoothly with this
