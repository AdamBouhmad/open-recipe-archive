## Ingredients

- sour cabbage
- red onion
- brown roux
- roe, blanched in salted water and fried in butter

## Directions

1. Once the sour cabbage is cooked soft, add it to a brown roux with red onion
2. Add the roe that has been blanched in salted water and fried in butter
3. Cook together well until fully combined
