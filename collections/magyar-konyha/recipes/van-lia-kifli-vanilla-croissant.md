## Ingredients

- 280 grams butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 70 grams sugar
- finely ground vanilla
- 36 grams melted cheese yeast
- 2 deciliters lukewarm sour cream
- salt
- flour
- apricots or currants
- egg white
- sugar

## Directions

1. Whip 280 grams butter until foamy and mix in 8 egg yolks one by one
2. Add 70 grams sugar, finely ground vanilla, 36 grams melted cheese yeast, 2 deciliters lukewarm sour cream, little salt and flour
3. Beat lightly and let the dough rise
4. Place on a flour-sprinkled board, roll out and cut out triangular leaves from it
5. Fill with apricots or currants and fold together, brush with egg white, sprinkle with sugar and serve at table
