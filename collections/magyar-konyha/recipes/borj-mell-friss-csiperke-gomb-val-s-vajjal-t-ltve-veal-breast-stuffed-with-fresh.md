## Ingredients

- Veal breast, split
- Fresh button mushrooms, small and cleaned
- Butter, 125-150 grams
- Salt
- Hot water

## Directions

1. Stuff the split veal breast with small cleaned fresh button mushrooms and 125-150 grams of fine butter
2. Salt the mushrooms slightly before filling and sewing closed
3. Place in a baking pan, salt, and pour hot water over it
4. Bake, basting frequently
5. When the broth is beautifully brown, sprinkle a spoon of flour over it and pour in a very small amount of sour cream; the broth should be beautifully brown
