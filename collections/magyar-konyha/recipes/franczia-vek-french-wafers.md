## Ingredients

- 3 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 21 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- ½ lemon, juice
- 21 dekagrams soaked almond, finely cut lengthwise
- 2 dekagrams candied orange peel
- ½ lemon, zest
- 2 dekagrams candied citron, finely chopped
- wafer sheets
- butter

## Directions

1. Place the stiffly beaten whites of 3 eggs in a bowl with 21 dekagrams of finely ground sugar
2. Slowly press in the juice of ½ lemon
3. Stir until thick and beautifully white
4. Mix in 21 dekagrams of soaked almond, finely cut lengthwise, 2 dekagrams of candied orange peel, the zest of ½ lemon and 2 dekagrams of candied citron, all similarly finely chopped
5. Spread this mixture evenly on wafer sheets two fingers wide and a span long
6. Place on a wafer baking pan previously buttered
7. Bake at gentle heat
