## Ingredients

- carp middle sections
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- onion
- butter
- paprika
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean the carp sections, salt them, place in a pan, pour water and vinegar over them, just enough so the fish is not completely covered, and simmer slowly
2. Finely chop onion and braise it in butter until golden, add half a teaspoon of paprika which should also braise
3. Add two deciliters of sour cream to a little of the fish broth, bring to a boil, skim, and strain the sauce
4. Place the carp in a dish, pour a little of the sauce over it, and serve the rest of the sauce separately in a bowl
