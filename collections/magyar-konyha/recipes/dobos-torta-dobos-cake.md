## Ingredients

- four egg whites
- seven dekagrams of sugar
- five egg yolks
- seven dekagrams of sugar
- ten dekagrams of flour
- creamy butter for the mold and flour for dusting
- fourteen dekagrams of butter
- twenty-eight dekagrams of sugar
- fourteen dekagrams of chocolate
- four egg yolks
- a little vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat four egg whites into half-stiff foam and mix in seven dekagrams of sugar.
2. Rub together seven dekagrams of sugar with five egg yolks until foamy.
3. Add ten dekagrams of flour to the egg yolk mixture.
4. Fold in the beaten egg whites.
5. Coat the cake mold with creamy butter and dust with flour.
6. Spread a knife's-thickness layer of the batter onto the mold and bake. Six to seven layers can be made from the given quantity.
7. For the filling: rub fourteen dekagrams of butter until foamy.
8. Cook twenty-eight dekagrams of sugar into syrup.
9. Add to the foamy butter fourteen dekagrams of dissolved and cooled chocolate, four egg yolks, and a little vanilla.
10. When the sugar syrup is also cooled, add it to the butter and mix together.
11. When the entire mixture is completely cooled, spread it on the sponge layers, placing a second layer on each coated layer.
12. For the icing: caramelize fourteen dekagrams of broken sugar to golden color and pour it over the top of the finished cake, smoothing it with a blunt knife.
