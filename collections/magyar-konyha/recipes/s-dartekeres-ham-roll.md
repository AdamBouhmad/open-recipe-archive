## Ingredients

- 10 dekagram butter
- 5 egg yolks
- salt
- 7 dekagram fine flour
- 3 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- ham, chopped
- good Parmesan cheese, grated
- 2 spoonfuls grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 spoonfuls sour cream
- sour cream
- ham
- Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Beat 10 dekagram butter until fluffy and gradually add 5 egg yolks one after another.
2. Add a little salt, 7 dekagram fine flour, and 3 egg whites stiffly beaten.
3. Spread the mixture evenly on a buttered pan to the thickness of a knife back and bake over low heat.
4. Chop some ham finely and grate good Parmesan cheese.
5. When the dough is light breadcrumb-yellow, remove the pan and sprinkle with ham and 2 spoonfuls of grated Parmesan cheese, then pour 2 spoonfuls of sour cream over it.
6. Return the pan to the oven for a few moments until it pulls up nicely.
7. Remove and cut the edge of the dough with a knife, then roll it up, not too tightly.
8. Spread with sour cream, sprinkle with ham and a little Parmesan cheese, and return to a not-too-hot oven for a few more moments.
9. Before serving, cut into slices three knife-widths wide with a sharp knife and arrange crosswise on a dish.
10. These rolls can also be used well for garnishing ragout.
