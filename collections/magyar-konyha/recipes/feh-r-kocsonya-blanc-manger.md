## Ingredients

- 28 dekagramm blanched and finely ground almonds
- 1 liter hot cream
- sugar
- 2 dekagramm dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Place 28 dekagramm of blanched and finely ground almonds in a porcelain pot
2. Pour 1 liter of hot cream over them and whisk well
3. Strain through cloth into a tin-lined basin
4. Add enough sugar to make it sufficiently sweet
5. Mix in 2 dekagramm dissolved isinglass
6. Place in a deep porcelain cup and stir several times with a silver spoon to prevent a film from forming on top
7. Place on crushed ice or in a cellar and freeze
