## Ingredients

- 1 calf's head
- salt
- water
- green parsley
- carrots
- 2 heads red onion
- a little lemon zest
- whole pepper
- clove pepper
- 1 calf's foot
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the calf's head in half and cook it in salted water with green parsley, carrots, two heads of red onion, and a little lemon zest until it becomes very tender
2. Remove it and place it in cold water for a while
3. Cook the broth in which it was cooked with a little whole pepper and clove pepper and one calf's foot until it reduces to three deciliters
4. Add the juice of one lemon, strain through a sieve, and set the sauce aside slightly
5. Remove the bones from the calf's head and cut the meat into long pieces
6. Place into a mold, strain the sauce once more through clean cloth, pour it over the meat, and place in a cold location until it becomes jelly
7. Once jellied, turn it out
