## Ingredients

- two grated rolls
- two decilitres of cream
- 90 grams of crab butter
- 40-50 crab tails and legs
- two decilitres of young green peas
- butter
- salt
- five egg yolks
- one whole egg
- three egg whites

## Directions

1. Soak the grated rolls in two decilitres of cream
2. Beat 90 grams of crab butter until fluffy
3. Squeeze out the bread crumbs well and add them to the butter
4. Add the crab tails and legs, cut in half only in the middle
5. Cook two decilitres of very young green peas in butter so that they do not lose their color
6. Slowly add this, a little salt, five egg yolks and one whole egg
7. When beaten until fluffy, gently mix in the stiff whites of three eggs
8. Coat the mould with crab butter
9. Line it with whole crab tails, green peas or asparagus tips
10. Fill with the mixture
11. Steam for one hour
