## Ingredients

- A handful of very good hops
- 1 kilogram of water
- 1 egg white
- 25 dekagrams wheat flour

## Directions

1. Boil a handful of very good hops with 1 kilogram of water until it can be mixed with 1 egg white and 25 dekagrams of wheat flour
