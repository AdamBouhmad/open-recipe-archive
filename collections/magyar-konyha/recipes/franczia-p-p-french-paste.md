## Ingredients

- 4 deciiliters milk
- common pancake batter
- lard for frying
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 deciiliters cream
- small amount of powdered vanilla

## Directions

1. Make ordinary pancake batter from 4 deciiliters of milk
2. Pass the batter through a skimming spoon with large holes into hot lard and fry the peas until golden
3. Remove the fried peas and continue until all the batter is fried
4. In a small pot, mix 14 dekagrams sugar with 8 egg yolks and add 8 deciiliters of cream and a small amount of powdered vanilla
5. Moisten the fried peas with milk and place in a buttered mold
6. Pour the cream and egg mixture over them
7. Cook in a steam bath for half an hour
8. Turn out onto a plate and serve
