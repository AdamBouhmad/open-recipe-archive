## Ingredients

- buttered dough
- apricots
- sugar
- egg

## Directions

1. Prepare buttered dough and roll out to good knife-width thickness
2. Cut circles the size of a coffee cup saucer
3. Arrange blanched apricots, halved in the middle and well sugared, on the circles
4. Make a small finger-width cut along the edge and fold up, creating a crown-shaped border
5. Brush with egg and when done, sprinkle with sugar and serve
