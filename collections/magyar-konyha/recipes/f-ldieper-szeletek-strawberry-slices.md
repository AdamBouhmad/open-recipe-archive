## Ingredients

- sponge cake dough
- butter
- 2 dekiliters ripe and stemmed fine sifted red strawberries
- finely ground and sifted sugar
- alkermes liqueur

## Directions

1. Prepare sponge cake dough, spread on a buttered baking sheet to twice knife-edge thickness, and bake at gentle heat
2. Cool, then cut into long slices or boat shapes
3. Fill with compote and place another slice on top
4. Prepare icing by beating ripe, stemmed, finely sifted red strawberries with finely ground and sifted sugar by the spoonful to prevent it from being too thin
5. Cover the slices or boats evenly with this icing
6. If the color is not attractive, add one or two drops of alkermes liqueur, but it should be at most pale pink
7. Dry in a cooled oven
8. These slices can also be covered with raspberry or strawberry compote, using raspberry or strawberry juice instead of lemon juice or rum
