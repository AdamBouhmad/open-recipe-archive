## Ingredients

- starchy potatoes
- two decagrams butter
- three whole eggs
- green parsley
- salt
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs
- fat
- parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Cook and peel starchy potatoes, grate them cold on a grater
2. Beat two decagrams of butter until fluffy for four or five potatoes, break in three whole eggs, add the grated potatoes with salt and green parsley, and form an elongated roll
3. Dip into beaten egg and bread crumbs and fry light brown in fat
4. Optionally mix a little parmesan cheese with the bread crumbs and finish with fried green parsley
