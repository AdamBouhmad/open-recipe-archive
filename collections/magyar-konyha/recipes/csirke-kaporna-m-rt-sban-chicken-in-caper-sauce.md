## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Butter
- Flour
- Red onion
- Broth
- White vinegar
- Sour cream
- Capers
- Lemon zest

## Directions

1. Prepare the chickens nicely and blanch them.
2. In warm butter, brown two spoonfuls of flour and add a whole red onion.
3. Dilute with broth and cook the sauce.
4. Strain the sauce through a sieve.
5. Place the chickens in it.
6. Add one spoonful of good white vinegar, three spoonfuls of sour cream, a handful of capers, and finely chopped lemon zest.
7. Simmer until the chickens are tender, then serve with the sauce spooned underneath.
