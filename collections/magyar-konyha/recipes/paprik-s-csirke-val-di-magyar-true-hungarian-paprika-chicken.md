## Ingredients

- chickens
- fresh bacon
- rose paprika
- water
- sour cream
- parsley
- dumplings

## Directions

1. Singe the chickens and cut into regular pieces (from one chicken: 2 thighs, 2 second thighs, 1 bishop's cut, 1 beggar's purse, the head and neck, 2 wings with giblets and liver, and 4-5 breast pieces if the chicken is larger; small chicks should be quartered)
2. Place the cut meat in a bowl filled with water
3. Chop fresh bacon into small cubes (2-3 decagrams per chicken) and render it in a pot until breadcrumb-colored
4. For each chicken, add 1 teaspoon of rose paprika to the hot fat where the rendered bacon remains, stir quickly and add the chicken with plenty of water
5. Cover and braise, if the liquid reduces completely it can be increased with fat-free broth
6. Season with salt at mid-cooking, and when the chicken is sufficiently tender, add 1-2 tablespoons of sweet unsoured sour cream per medium chicken
7. Once it comes to a boil, add a good pinch of finely chopped green parsley for each chicken, let it come to a boil again, serve and garnish with fine whipped dumplings
