## Ingredients

- tender veal from the hind part
- fat
- onion
- black pepper
- clove pepper
- paprika
- flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Put fat in a pot and fry abundant onion and a few black and clove peppercorns until golden, add one teaspoon of paprika
2. Beat the veal well but not so it falls apart, salt it and coat in flour, place immediately in the hot fat
3. This roast should cook quickly, three-quarters of an hour is enough
4. To serve, pour a little broth underneath, and when sliced onto a plate, strain the sauce through a sieve onto it while hot
5. The sauce should be a nice red color; the veal remains white from the quick cooking, looks very nice and is tender and fine
