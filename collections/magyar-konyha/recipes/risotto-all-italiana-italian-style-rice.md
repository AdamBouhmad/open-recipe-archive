## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams rice
- 3 carrots
- 3 kohlrabis
- 2 half heads of cabbage
- breast of roasted chicken or turkey
- half a large goose liver, fried in fat
- salt, pepper, nutmeg
- fresh butter or goose fat
- 1 tablespoon good meat broth
- meat broth for serving

## Directions

1. Wash the rice and clean the vegetables, cutting them into thin slices
2. Cut the chicken breast and goose liver similarly into thin slices and season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg
3. Coat a pot with fresh butter or goose fat
4. Layer rice, then vegetables, then meat and liver, and repeat until the pot is full
5. Cover with rice and dot with a few pieces of butter
6. Pour half a tablespoon of good meat broth over it
7. Cover the pot with a lid and braise in the oven until the rice and vegetables are soft, the rice remaining whole and not becoming soupy; add more broth if needed
8. Cover the pot with a folded kitchen towel and serve in a soup tureen with strained meat broth
