## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- parsley
- onion for cutting
- yellow carrot
- salt
- finely chopped bacon
- bacon
- red onion
- yellow carrot
- ham
- veal shank
- brown gravy
- good vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the beef tongue in a pot with a bunch of parsley, cutting onion, yellow carrot and salt, and cook until the skin can be pulled off.
2. Lard it with finely chopped bacon in three or four rows.
3. Place bacon, red onion, yellow carrot, ham and veal shank all sliced into a pan.
4. Place the tongues in it and fill with brown gravy.
5. Cover the pan and braise gently until tender.
6. Remove the tongues and strain the gravy.
7. Pour two spoonfuls of good vinegar into it and cook until it becomes glace.
8. Place the tongue back in it, turn on all sides and the dish is ready.
9. Place braised small red onions around the edge of the plate.
