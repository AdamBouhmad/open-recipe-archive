## Ingredients

- half kilogram pineapple
- half kilogram finely ground and sifted sugar
- beef bladder for sealing
- clean paper

## Directions

1. For half a kilogram of fruit, the same amount of finely ground and sifted sugar is needed.
2. The bottles needed for this must be clean and dry; sprinkle sugar on the bottom of the jar to the thickness of a knife blade, peel the pineapple, and cut into slices of knife-blade thickness.
3. Place a layer of pineapple on the first layer of sugar, cover this again with ground sugar, place pineapple again, and so on until all is arranged.
4. Cover thickly with sugar on top, and seal well with beef bladder, under which you place clean paper, and set in a place well exposed to the sun, leaving it there until the sugar is completely dissolved and the juice becomes clear and transparent.
5. Then place in a cool, dry location.
