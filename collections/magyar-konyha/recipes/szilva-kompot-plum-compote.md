## Ingredients

- Fresh plums
- Water
- Sugar
- Lemon peel

## Directions

1. Peel the fresh plums finely, place them in a pot, and pour a little water over them
2. Add a piece of sugar and bring to a boil several times
3. Transfer to a designated cup
4. Add lemon peel to the sauce and cook until it thickens
5. Strain it, pour over the plums, and serve cold at table
6. To give these last two compotes a more elegant appearance, they can be decorated with a row of white and a row of red orange slices
