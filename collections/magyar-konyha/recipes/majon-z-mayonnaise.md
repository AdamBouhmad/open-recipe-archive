## Ingredients

- two coffee cups melted aspic
- one small coffee cup pure olive oil
- knife-tip of ground pepper
- lemon juice
- one tablespoon white vinegar

## Directions

1. Pour the melted aspic, olive oil, ground pepper, lemon juice, and white vinegar into a brass cooking vessel
2. Place the vessel on ice
3. Beat with a whisk until it becomes a thick, shiny mass
4. Use for cold meat dishes and fish
