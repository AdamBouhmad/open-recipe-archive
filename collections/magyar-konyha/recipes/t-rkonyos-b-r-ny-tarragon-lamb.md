## Ingredients

- one quarter of lamb from the front
- two liters of salted water
- one egg-sized amount of lard
- one large tablespoon of flour

## Directions

1. Place one quarter of lamb from the front in two liters of salted water to cook
2. While it cooks, prepare ingredients with one egg-sized amount of lard and one large tablespoon of flour
