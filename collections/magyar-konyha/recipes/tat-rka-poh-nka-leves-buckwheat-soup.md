## Ingredients

- two deciliters buckwheat
- two egg whites
- good meat broth
- two egg whites
- piece of fresh butter
- two full spoons of fresh sour cream

## Directions

1. Mix two egg whites into the buckwheat
2. Dry the mixed buckwheat well in a warm place
3. Once completely dry, rub with a knife until it returns to its previous form
4. Cook in good meat broth and let simmer for a few minutes
5. In a pot, beat two egg whites, a piece of fresh butter, and two full spoons of fresh sour cream together well
6. Pour the broth into this mixture
7. Serve
