## Ingredients

- 28 dekagramm butter
- apple marmalade
- fine sugar
- preserved peach

## Directions

1. Make a puff pastry turned five times from 28 dekagramm butter
2. Roll it to knife-thickness
3. Place the entire rolled dough on a sheet
4. Coat with apple marmalade to a half-finger thickness
5. Sprinkle with fine sugar
6. Bake in a good hot oven
7. Coat the apple surface with preserved peach
