## Ingredients

- frogs
- morel mushrooms (kucsma-gomba)
- butter
- green parsley
- vajas mártás (butter sauce)

## Directions

1. Blanch the frogs and place them in cold water
2. Prepare a little butter sauce
3. Braise the thoroughly cleaned morel mushrooms in butter with green parsley, place them in the butter sauce
4. Squeeze the frogs well and place them in the sauce as well
