## Ingredients

- barley grits
- water
- lemon juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Soak barley grits in water
2. Wash by hand and rub to remove the flour
3. Pour water over it and cook for two hours
4. Strain off the thin milk
5. Dilute the remainder by adding hot water
6. Add lemon juice and sugar
