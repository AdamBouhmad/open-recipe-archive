## Ingredients

- rice
- white broth
- seven dekagrams butter
- one whole egg
- six egg yolks
- salt
- four egg whites
- butter for greasing
- filling
- white broth

## Directions

1. Clean the rice and grind in a mortar to flour
2. Mix with cold white broth
3. Cook to a thick paste with constant stirring
4. Remove from heat and mix the still-hot paste with seven dekagrams butter
5. Let cool
6. Beat in one whole egg and six egg yolks
7. Add a little salt
8. When sufficiently mixed, fold in the stiffly beaten whites of four eggs
9. Grease dumplings with butter and fill halfway
10. Add filling to the center and cover with the above mixture without filling completely
11. Bake in oven and pour white broth over them
