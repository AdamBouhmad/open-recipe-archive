## Ingredients

- rétestésztát (strudel dough)
- three bars of chocolate
- 14 dekagramm softened almond
- 10 dekagramm sugar
- several spoonfuls melted butter
- five spoonfuls sour cream

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough as described in recipe 480 and stretch it finely
2. Dry it well
3. Grate three bars of chocolate and chop 14 dekagramm softened almond finely
4. Mix 10 dekagramm sugar with the almond
5. Pour several spoonfuls of melted butter over the dough
6. Sprinkle the chocolate and almond mixture over it
7. Pour five spoonfuls of sour cream over it
8. Fold it together
9. Pour cream into a baking dish to a thickness of one finger width
10. Place the strudel into the dish and bake it
