## Ingredients

- throat roast
- lungs
- larynx
- small amount of rendered intestine fat
- cooked millet porridge
- salt
- black pepper, ground
- parboiled red onion
- marjoram
- hot water

## Directions

1. Chop together the throat roast, blood vessel pieces from the lungs, and larynx while warm
2. Add small amount of rendered intestine fat
3. Mix in cooked millet porridge in proportion to the meat and fat
4. Season with salt, abundant black pepper, parboiled red onion and marjoram
5. Add hot water by spoonfuls to make the filling thin and easy to stuff
6. Fill the intestine casings with the mixture using a sausage stuffer
7. Seal both ends of the sausage with small wooden sticks
8. Cook in hot salted water
9. Test for doneness by piercing with a straw; when clear fat rather than red water comes out, the sausage is cooked
