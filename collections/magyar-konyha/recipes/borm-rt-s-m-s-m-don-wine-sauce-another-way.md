## Ingredients

- sixteen egg yolks
- twenty five dekagrams of powdered sugar
- one liter of good wine
- half a lemon peel
- broken cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat sixteen egg yolks in a deep dish with twenty five dekagrams of powdered sugar until foamy
2. Put one liter of good wine in a ceramic or porcelain dish to boil
3. Add half a lemon peel and a knife's point of broken cinnamon to the wine
4. When the spiced wine is boiling, slowly pour it into the sugar and egg mixture while constantly stirring with a wooden spoon
5. Place the mixture at the edge of the fire where it stays continuously warm but not actively boiling
6. Prepare the wine sauce only shortly before serving as it will curdle if left standing
7. Continuously stir during this short time so it becomes thick and foamy
