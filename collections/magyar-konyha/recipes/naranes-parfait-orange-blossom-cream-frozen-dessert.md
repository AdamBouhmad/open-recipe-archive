## Ingredients

- 5 dekagrams roasted orange blossom
- 1.4 liters hot sweet cream
- egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Add 5 dekagrams roasted orange blossom to 1.4 liters hot sweet cream.
2. Follow the procedure already known from previous recipes.
3. Note: when slowly pouring the cream into the egg yolks, stir very carefully so that the mixture does not break.
