## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- milk
- 7 dekagrams butter, beaten fluffy
- 7 dekagrams finely ground almonds
- 7 egg yolks
- 10 dekagrams sugar
- lemon or orange zest
- 4 egg whites
- preserved currants
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Make grated dough from 2 eggs and cook it in milk
2. When thick and well cooked, remove from heat and cool
3. Into 7 dekagrams of fluffy beaten butter add the same amount of finely ground almonds and 7 egg yolks, as well as 10 dekagrams of sugar rubbed with lemon or orange zest
4. Stir for half an hour
5. Add the cooled grated dough and finally the stiff snow from 4 egg whites
6. Pour half the mixture into a buttered baking dish
7. Fill with preserved currants
8. Pour the other half of the mixture over it
9. Bake nicely and serve dusted with sugar
