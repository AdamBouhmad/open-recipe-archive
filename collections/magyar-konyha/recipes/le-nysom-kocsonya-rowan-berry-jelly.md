## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram well-ripened rowan berries
- 40 dekagrams refined sugar
- 3.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Cook 1 kilogram of well-ripened rowan berries with 40 dekagrams of refined sugar for 5 minutes
2. Set the syrup aside
3. When completely cooled, strain through a fine sieve
4. Mix with 3.5 dekagrams of dissolved isinglass
5. Pour into the appropriate mold
6. Place in cracked ice and freeze as with the above recipes
