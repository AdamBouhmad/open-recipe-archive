## Ingredients

- 14 decagrams butter, beaten until fluffy
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 spoon broken sugar
- 2 deciliters sour cream
- 1.5 decagrams fresh cheese yeast dissolved in the sour cream
- a little salt
- 7 deciliters flour
- 4 deciliters poppy seeds, finely ground
- 21 decagrams broken sugar
- a little cinnamon
- peel of half a lemon and half an orange, finely chopped
- 8 spoonfuls good sour cream

## Directions

1. Beat 14 decagrams butter until fluffy and add 8 egg yolks one by one
2. Well mixed, add 1 spoon broken sugar, 2 deciliters sour cream, fresh cheese yeast, a little salt and 7 deciliters flour
3. Mix everything well, knead the dough finely and let it rise slightly
4. When the dough has risen, place on a board well sprinkled with flour and stretch to knife-edge thickness
5. Finely grind 4 deciliters poppy seeds, mix with 21 decagrams broken sugar, a little cinnamon, and finely chopped peel of half a lemon and half an orange
6. Sprinkle the dough thickly with this filling, pour 8 spoonfuls of good sour cream over it and fold together
7. Place in a buttered flat dish and let it rise
8. Brush with butter, bake and sprinkle generously with sugar before serving
