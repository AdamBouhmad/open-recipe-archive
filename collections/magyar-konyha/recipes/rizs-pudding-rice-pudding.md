## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams rice
- 9 dekagrams ground almonds
- milk
- 10 dekagrams butter
- 1 whole egg
- 4 egg yolks
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- little vanilla
- 8 egg yolks
- 7 deciliters cream

## Directions

1. Cook 14 dekagrams rice with 9 dekagrams ground almonds in milk and cool
2. Whip 10 dekagrams butter until fluffy
3. Add the rice, 1 whole egg and 4 egg yolks
4. Mix well and pour into a buttered mold and steam
5. Pour cream sauce over it, made as follows: pound 14 dekagrams sugar with a little vanilla and mix well with 8 egg yolks in a pan
6. Slowly add 7 deciliters of thick boiled cream
7. Place the pan over coals and keep stirring until it begins to thicken
8. Remove quickly from heat and pour over the pudding
