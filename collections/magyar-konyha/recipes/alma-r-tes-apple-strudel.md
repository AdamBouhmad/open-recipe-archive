## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs
- butter
- warm water
- melted butter
- 4 large sour peeled apples
- 100 grams soaked broken almonds
- 100 grams sifted sugar
- 140 grams cleaned raisins
- breadcrumbs
- cinnamon
- candied lemon peel

## Directions

1. Prepare ordinary strudel dough with flour, eggs, a little butter, and warm water
2. Cover with a warm pot and let rise
3. Roll out as thinly as possible and brush with melted butter
4. Finely dice 4 large sour peeled apples
5. Mix 100 grams soaked broken almonds with 100 grams sifted sugar
6. Clean and wash 140 grams raisins and dry with cloth
7. On the rolled and brushed dough, first scatter apples, then almonds, sugar, raisins, and finally a little breadcrumbs
8. Gently fold together and shape into strudel
9. Brush with melted butter and place in a flat buttered pan
10. Bake until done; cinnamon and candied lemon peel may be added to the filling
