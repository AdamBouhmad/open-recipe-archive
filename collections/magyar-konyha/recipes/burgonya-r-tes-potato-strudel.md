## Ingredients

- eighteen dekagramm potato
- 14 dekagramm butter
- six egg yolks
- 7 dekagramm finely ground almond
- 10 dekagramm crushed sugar
- one lemon's peel, finely chopped
- three egg whites
- rétes-tészta (strudel dough)

## Directions

1. Cook and cool the potato, then grate eighteen dekagramm potato
2. Beat 14 dekagramm butter until fluffy
3. Add the potato to it
4. Mix in six egg yolks, 7 dekagramm finely ground almond, 10 dekagramm crushed sugar, finely chopped peel of one lemon, and finally fold in the stiffly beaten whites of three eggs
5. Stir everything together for half an hour
6. Stretch the strudel dough finely and spread the filling on it
7. Spread it to the thickness of a knife blade
8. Fold it together gently
9. Brush with butter and place in the oven to bake until golden
10. Note: this strudel must be served immediately after baking so it does not collapse
