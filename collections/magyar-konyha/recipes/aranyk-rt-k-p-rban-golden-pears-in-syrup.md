## Ingredients

- golden pears
- sugar
- water
- lemon juice
- 560 grams pears requires 14 dekagrams sugar and 1 glass water

## Directions

1. Peel the golden pears which are twice as large as muscat pears, clean the stem so that only half remains on the pear
2. Once peeled, coat each pear with lemon juice so they remain beautifully white, place them in water until all are peeled
3. Calculate 14 dekagrams sugar for 560 grams fruit, cook with 1 glass water only until the sugar dissolves
