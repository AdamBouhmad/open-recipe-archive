## Ingredients

- 1.4 liters boiling sweet cream
- 3.5 dekagrams fragrant tea
- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 16 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place 3.5 dekagrams fragrant tea in 1.4 liters boiling sweet cream, cover well, and cool.
2. Then proceed as above with 42 dekagrams sugar and 16 egg yolks.
