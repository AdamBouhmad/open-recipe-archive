## Ingredients

- red cabbage
- hot water
- salt
- pepper
- sugar
- butter or fat for roux
- flour
- roasted chestnuts
- sausage

## Directions

1. Wash the red cabbage thoroughly and pour hot water over it twice, then cook until soft and drain. Then chop finely and braise in a brownish roux for some time. Pour two large tablespoons of broth over it, add a small amount of pepper and a piece of sugar and braise for another hour. Finally arrange roasted chestnuts on top and add sausage at serving time.
