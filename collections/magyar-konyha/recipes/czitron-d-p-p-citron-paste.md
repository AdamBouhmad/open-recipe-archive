## Ingredients

- 3 stale, crumbly grated kipferl rolls
- 1 coffee cup cream
- 7 dekagramms softened and finely ground almonds
- 7 dekagramms butter, beaten until fluffy
- 1 whole egg and 5 egg yolks
- 10 dekagramms finely ground sugar
- 7 dekagramms citron, cut into laska shapes
- 3 egg whites
- fresh citron slices

## Directions

1. Pour cream over the grated kipferl
2. Mix the soaked crumbs with the almonds into the beaten butter
3. Add the whole egg and egg yolks one by one
4. Add the sugar and citron pieces
5. Beat the 3 egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in
6. Grease a pot with butter and line the bottom with white paper greased with butter
7. Cut thin slices from fresh citron and arrange them in a double star pattern on the bottom of the pot
8. Slowly add the beaten mixture
9. Steam for three-quarters of an hour
10. Turn out onto a plate, carefully remove the paper so the star is not damaged
11. Serve quickly to the table
