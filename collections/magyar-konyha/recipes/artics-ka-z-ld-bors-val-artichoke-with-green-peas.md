## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt
- water
- butter
- fresh parsley
- green peas
- roasted veal
- nutmeg flower
- fried goose liver (for garnish)

## Directions

1. Cook the artichokes in salted water
2. Remove the interior cleanly
3. Stew the green peas with butter and fresh parsley
4. Prepare a little butter sauce
5. Cut roasted veal into cubes
6. Add a little butter sauce to the veal with the green peas and a little nutmeg flower
7. Fill the artichokes with this mixture
8. Place them in the butter sauce and cook well
9. Garnish with fried goose liver
