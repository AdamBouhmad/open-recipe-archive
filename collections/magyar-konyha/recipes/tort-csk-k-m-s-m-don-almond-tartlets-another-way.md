## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams blanched almonds, soaked
- 14 dekagrams ground sugar
- 14 dekagrams fine flour
- zest of 1 lemon, finely chopped
- 14 dekagrams butter
- 2 egg yolks
- cream as needed for moistening the dough

## Directions

1. Finely grind 14 dekagrams of soaked blanched almonds and sift 14 dekagrams of ground sugar, reserving the coarser portions to sprinkle on finished pastries
2. Mix the sugar and almonds with 14 dekagrams of fine flour and the finely chopped zest of 1 lemon, combining well on a board
3. Cut 14 dekagrams of butter into thin pieces and work into the mixture
4. Add 2 egg yolks and enough cream to moisten the dough as needed
5. Work the dough well until smooth and even
6. Form small tartlets and fill with jam of your choice
7. Make a small lattice on top
8. Brush the tartlets with egg white, sprinkle with the reserved coarse sugar
9. Bake in a gentle oven
