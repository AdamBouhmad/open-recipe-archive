## Ingredients

- veal loin cleaned
- bread
- milk
- asparagus
- crayfish
- crayfish butter
- parsley
- green peas
- egg
- egg yolk
- beef broth
- crayfish mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the loin and prepare the stuffing
2. Soak grated bread in milk
3. Blanch the asparagus and crayfish, clean and cut into small cubes
4. Make crayfish butter from the shells
5. Squeeze out the bread, chop finely and braise slightly with parsley
6. Add the asparagus with the crayfish tails and a little green peas and braise
7. Remove from heat, season and salt, cool slightly
8. Beat one egg and one yolk and mix well together
9. Stuff the loin and place in a pot on pebbles
10. Pour beef broth over it and braise until nicely tender
11. Before serving, prepare crayfish mushroom sauce and pour it over the loin
