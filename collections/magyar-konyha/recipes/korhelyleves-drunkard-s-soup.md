## Ingredients

- small amount of cabbage
- water
- small meat slices or smoked sausage cut into rings
- flour for roux
- broth
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place small amount of cabbage in a pot, cover generously with its own liquid and a little water
2. Add small meat slices or smoked sausage cut into rings
3. When cooked, make a thin roux with plenty of broth, keeping the sour taste
4. Season lightly with pepper
5. Serve the soup hot
