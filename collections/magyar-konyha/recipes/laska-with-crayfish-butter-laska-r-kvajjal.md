## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- dough for noodles
- salted water
- melted crayfish butter
- 2 deciliters sour cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 4 spoonfuls crayfish butter
- crayfish tails, cut into cubes

## Directions

1. Prepare noodle dough from 2 eggs
2. Roll out thinly
3. Cut into laska shapes with a pastry wheel
4. Cook in salted water and drain
5. Place the laska in melted crayfish butter, stir well, and place on a buttered serving dish
6. Beat 3 egg yolks into 2 deciliters of sour cream with 4 spoonfuls of crayfish butter and mix well
7. Scatter cubed crayfish tails over the laska
8. Pour the sour cream mixture over it
9. Cover the top entirely with crayfish tails
10. Bake in the oven for a quarter hour
