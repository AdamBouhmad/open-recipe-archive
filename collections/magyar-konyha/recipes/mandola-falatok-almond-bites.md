## Ingredients

- Puff pastry
- 14 dekagrams blanched almonds
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- 1 lemon, finely chopped rind
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Cut rectangular leaves from puff pastry
2. Pound the blanched almonds and sugar together
3. Mix with finely chopped lemon rind and beat with egg yolks in a bowl for half an hour
4. Coat with egg whites
5. Place a teaspoon of almond paste in the center of each pastry leaf
6. Press opposite corners together
7. Place immediately on baking sheet
8. Brush with egg white
9. Bake at moderate heat until golden yellow
10. Serve warm sprinkled with sugar
