## Ingredients

- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 3 lemons
- 7 deciliters water
- 4.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Rub 42 dekagrams sugar very gently on 3 lemons.
2. Mix the sugar with 7 deciliters water.
3. Once the sugar has dissolved, mix in 4.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass.
4. Strain through cloth and freeze in molds like other aspics.
