## Ingredients

- beautiful rose petals
- water
- vitriol spirit

## Directions

1. Spread cloth on an earthenware dish
2. Place good quality rose petals on the cloth
3. Cover with a tortoiseshell lid with edges and good seal
4. Place hot coals on the lid
5. Keep the fire going until the petals are dried
6. The true juice will evaporate from the petals and drip through the cloth into the dish
7. When the petals are dried, remove the cloth, clean it from the dried petals, put the water in a container, seal it well, then continue the procedure with fresh petals
8. Alternatively: Pour water into a dish and add rose petals, then add two to three drops of vitriol spirit, which is sufficient to impart both color and the fragrance of roses to the water
