## Ingredients

- strudel dough
- 35 ripe plums
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- crumbly white bread, grated
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough
2. Cut 35 ripe plums into thin rings
3. Finely grind a little cinnamon
4. Finely chop lemon peel and grate crumbly white bread
5. Stretch the dough finely and brush with melted butter
6. Spread the plums and remaining filling on it
