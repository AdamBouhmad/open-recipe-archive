## Ingredients

- 5 liters strong alcohol
- 4 grams clove oil
- 4 grams clove extract
- 4 grams bergamot oil
- 1 gram thyme oil
- 4.5 dekagrams rose extract
- 4.5 dekagrams orange extract
- 4.5 dekagrams cinnamon extract
- 4.5 dekagrams mignonette extract
- 4.5 dekagrams jasmine extract
- 4.5 dekagrams jonquil extract
- 4.5 dekagrams violet extract
- 4.5 dekagrams tuberose extract
- 1 gram amber essence
- 2 grams musk essence

## Directions

1. Dissolve clove oil, clove extract, bergamot oil, and thyme oil in strong alcohol
2. Add rose, orange, cinnamon, mignonette, jasmine, jonquil, violet, and tuberose extracts
3. Add amber essence and musk essence
4. Shake well
