## Ingredients

- carp
- hot vinegar
- vinegar
- water
- cloves
- whole peppercorns
- lemon peel
- parsley root
- salt
- green parsley

## Directions

1. Fillet the carp, wash it, cut it into pieces and pour hot vinegar over it so that it becomes a beautiful blue colour.
2. Make the broth from vinegar and water so that it has a good sour taste, add a few cloves, whole peppercorns, lemon peel, parsley root and salt, bring to a boil, place the fish in it, cover and cook slowly.
3. When cooked, place blotting paper on it and let it stand for a while, then place it on the serving dish and arrange torn green parsley on top or around the edge of the dish.
4. Mirror carp is best for this because it has few scales. It can also be served cold with vinegar and oil.
