## Ingredients

- half liter flour
- 1 liter crumbled curd cheese
- 4 eggs
- few tablespoons good sour cream
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix flour with crumbled curd cheese
2. Add four eggs, a few tablespoons sour cream, and salt, and knead into soft dough
3. Work quickly so the curd remains in pieces throughout the dough
4. On a well-floured board, roll out to half-finger thickness
5. Cut out with a small perforator (like a forint coin)
6. Boil in salted water until they float
7. Pour hot butter fried to a golden color over them
8. Sprinkle with red butter breadcrumbs and serve
