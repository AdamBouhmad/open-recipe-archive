## Ingredients

- one plate of clean, finely chopped, washed sorrel
- rendered goose fat
- flour
- meat stock
- one coffee cup good sour cream
- one coffee spoon Liebig extract

## Directions

1. Place the clean, finely chopped, washed sorrel in melted goose fat and braise
2. Sprinkle flour over it and braise well
3. Add only a very small amount of stock and one coffee cup of good sour cream, as well as one coffee spoon of Liebig extract
4. Cook a bit more
