## Ingredients

- pike with skin intact
- red onion
- fresh parsley
- anchovies
- grated bread soaked in milk
- butter
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- three eggs
- sour cream
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Clean the pike of scales and cut it in half along the back, removing all its meat along with the bones, but the head and tail must remain on the spine and the entire skin must remain intact.
2. Remove the bones from the meat and chop it finely with red onion, fresh parsley, and several anchovies cleaned of skin and bones.
3. Add grated bread soaked in milk and squeezed, a piece of butter, a little nutmeg, pepper, salt and three eggs, and mix until as smooth as dough.
4. Fill the pike's skin completely with this filling and sew it shut along the back.
5. Place the pike in a well-buttered pan and roast until a beautiful golden color, basting five or six times with fresh sour cream or butter during roasting.
6. Once roasted, place the fish upright on a plate and slice it beautifully and evenly with a sharp knife, pour the sauce over it and sprinkle evenly with thinly sliced lemon zest.
7. Pike can be prepared the same way, and if carp is to be used this way, only the sour cream and lemon zest should be omitted, so the fish should be basted during roasting only with melted butter.
