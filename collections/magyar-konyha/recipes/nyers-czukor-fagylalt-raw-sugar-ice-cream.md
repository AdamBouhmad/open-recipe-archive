## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams sugar
- 0.5 glass water
- 1 liter hot cream

## Directions

1. Cook 28 dekagrams of sugar with 0.5 glass of water until it becomes raw sugar, that is, when placed on stone it can be cut and is slightly yellowish but not bitter
2. Pour 1 liter of hot cream over it and cook the sugar in the cream until completely dissolved
3. Cool and pour into the freezing container
