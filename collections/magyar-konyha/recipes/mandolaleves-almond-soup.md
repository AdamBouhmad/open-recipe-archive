## Ingredients

- seven deciliters sweet milk
- fourteen dekagrams sugar
- a little vanilla
- twelve dekagrams swollen finely ground almonds
- four egg yolks
- toasted breadcrumb slices

## Directions

1. Heat seven deciliters of sweet milk with fourteen dekagrams of sugar and a little vanilla.
2. Add twelve dekagrams of swollen finely ground almonds.
3. Bring to a boil once.
4. Before serving, beat four egg yolks in a pot and mix them with hot milk.
5. Place toasted breadcrumb slices in the soup tureen and pour half the soup over them.
6. Whip the other half of the soup until frothy and pour it over the bread slices in the tureen until it is full of foam.
7. Then pour the rest of the soup into the tureen gradually, which should be placed over a bed of coals.
