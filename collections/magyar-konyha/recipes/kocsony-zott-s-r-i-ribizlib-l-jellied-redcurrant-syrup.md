## Ingredients

- ½ kilogram sugar
- ½ kilogram redcurrants, picked
- 2 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar in 2 deciliters of water until it threads, forming large bubbles while boiling
2. Wash the redcurrants and place on a fine-mesh sieve so the water drains off and they dry
3. When the sugar is ready, add the redcurrants, stir, bring to a boil two or three times
4. Pour onto a clean sieve standing over a soup bowl and let flow slowly without pressing through
5. Pour into glasses where it will gel
6. Prepared the same way from raspberries
