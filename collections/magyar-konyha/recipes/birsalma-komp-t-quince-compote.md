## Ingredients

- 3-4 quinces
- water
- 240 grams sugar

## Directions

1. Boil 3-4 quinces briefly in water
2. Place in cold water and chill
3. Remove skin and divide into 4 segments
4. Remove cores
5. Cook 240 grams sugar with 200 ml water until it forms heavy drops
6. Place quinces in sugar syrup and turn several times so both sides soften without falling apart
7. Arrange on a plate and pour the small amount of sauce underneath
