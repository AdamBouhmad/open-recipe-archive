## Ingredients

- a beautiful two kilogramme pike
- salt
- white vinegar
- water
- various roots
- lemon peel
- red onion
- whole peppercorns
- grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Salt a beautiful two kilogramme pike and leave it in salt for one hour.
2. You can leave the fish whole or cut it into pieces and then place it in a pan, pour one part white vinegar and three parts water over it, add all kinds of roots, lemon peel, red onion, a few whole peppercorns and cook slowly.
3. When cooked, immediately place it on the serving dish from the broth, sprinkle a good portion of grated horseradish over it, then pour a little of the hot broth in which the fish was cooked over it.
4. Cover with another plate, leave it covered for a while and then serve to the table.
