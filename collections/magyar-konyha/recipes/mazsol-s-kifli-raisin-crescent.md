## Ingredients

- 2 liters flour
- 0.5 kilogram butter
- 5 dekagrams melted cheese yeast
- 6 tablespoons sour cream
- 10 egg yolks
- lukewarm milk as needed
- salt as needed
- 1 kilogram raisins
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- 2 tablespoons water
- 14 dekagrams candied citron, finely chopped
- candied lemon zest (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough in the same way as recipe 595
2. For the filling: chop the raisins finely
3. Cook the sugar in 2 tablespoons of water until it threads
4. Add the raisins to the syrup
5. Add the candied citron and mix well
6. Cool the filling
7. Fill the croissants with this mixture
8. Optionally add finely chopped candied lemon zest to the filling
