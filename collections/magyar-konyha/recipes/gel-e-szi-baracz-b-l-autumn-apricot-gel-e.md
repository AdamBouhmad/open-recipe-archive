## Ingredients

- 10 apricots
- 56 decagrams sugar
- 6 decagrams isinglass or clarified gelatin
- ½ bottle white wine
- 3 lemons, juice strained
- 4 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar in 4 deciliters of water
2. Add the peeled and halved apricots along with the pits
3. Let cook, skimming off the foam
4. Cover and let cool
5. Strain through a sieve, filtering the sugar through a cloth
6. Mix the lukewarm isinglass, strained juice of 3 lemons, and wine together
7. Layer the gelée with the apricots into a mold according to the method described above
