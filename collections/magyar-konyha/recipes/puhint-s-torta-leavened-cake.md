## Ingredients

- 1/8 kilogram butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 4 spoonfuls powdered sugar
- 3 decagrams yeast
- warm milk
- 1 kilogram flour
- salt
- abundant lemon zest
- 1/8 kilogram raisins and small grapes
- 8 egg whites
- grated chocolate
- powdered sugar
- vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Beat together 1/8 kilogram butter and 8 egg yolks with 4 spoonfuls powdered sugar
2. Dissolve 3 decagrams yeast in warm milk
3. Take 1 kilogram flour and mix with the yeast mixture, appropriate salt, abundant lemon zest, and as much milk as it absorbs to make a good soft dough
4. Work the dough for a long time, always stirring in one direction to make it fine and soft
5. When worked, mix in 1/8 kilogram raisins and small grapes
6. Butter and flour a large pan and pour the dough into it, then let it rise in a warm place
7. When well risen and the pan is full, put it into the oven to bake
8. When nearly done, beat 8 egg whites until stiff, mix with grated chocolate and powdered sugar, and thickly coat the baked dough
9. Return to the oven until the foam dries on it
10. Slice when half cold, as the knife would crush the fine soft dough when hot, and sprinkle with vanilla sugar
