## Ingredients

- beautiful ripe sour cherries with stems removed
- 7 deciliters sour cherry juice
- juice of 2 lemons
- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 2 deciliters water
- 4.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Wash ripe cherries and crush in a mortar with their pits
2. Extract the juice
3. Use 7 deciliters of cherry juice with the strained juice of 2 lemons
4. Cook 42 dekagrams of sugar with 2 deciliters of water until it forms heavy drops
5. Mix the cooked sugar with the cherry juice
6. Add 4.5 dekagrams of dissolved isinglass
7. Strain through cloth and blotting paper
8. Pour into mold and freeze
