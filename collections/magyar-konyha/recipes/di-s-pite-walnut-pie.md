## Ingredients

- stretched puff pastry
- butter
- walnuts, finely ground
- sugar
- lemon zest, grated
- raisins
- thick syrup

## Directions

1. Layer the thinly drawn and dried puff pastry into a greased baking pan, brushing each layer with butter
2. Between each layer, sprinkle finely ground sugared walnuts generously, grated lemon zest, and abundant raisins
3. Continue until the pan is full
4. Place in the oven to bake
5. When baked, pour thick syrup over it
6. Alternatively use the juice from any sugared preserves, boiled up
7. Return to the oven for about ten more minutes
8. Remove and turn onto a sieve
9. When half-cooled, slice as desired
