## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams flour
- 7 dekagrams butter
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- 28 dekagrams walnuts, finely chopped
- sugar for filling (same amount as walnuts)
- 7 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix flour and butter well
2. Add 3 tablespoons sugar and 1 egg yolk to make dough
3. Roll out and partially dry in the oven
4. Meanwhile prepare the filling: cook the finely chopped walnuts, equal amount of sugar, and 7 egg yolks until well thickened
5. Cool the filling slightly
6. Fill the pastry with the mixture
7. Once the pastry is baked and cooled, cut into portions
