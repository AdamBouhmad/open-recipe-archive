## Ingredients

- 7 dekagrams butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- flour as needed
- salt
- 7 deciliters cream
- sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream together 7 dekagrams butter
2. Add 2 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks one after another
3. Season with salt and add enough flour to bind the dough but keep it soft
4. Bring 7 deciliters cream to a boil with a little sugar
5. Drop noodles evenly into the cream with a spoon
6. When cooked, remove and place in a deep porcelain dish or pot
7. Beat 4 egg yolks in a small pot
8. Pour the cream with crushed sugar and vanilla into the egg yolks and whisk well
9. Pour over the noodles
10. Place in the oven until the noodles brown and the cream becomes thick
11. Sprinkle with sugar and serve
