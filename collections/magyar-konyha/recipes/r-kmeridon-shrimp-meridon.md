## Ingredients

- two grated bread rolls
- two decilitres of sour cream
- nine dekagrams of shrimp butter
- forty to fifty shrimp tails and legs
- two decilitres of young green peas
- butter
- salt
- five egg yolks
- one whole egg
- three egg whites
- shrimp butter for the mold
- whole shrimp tails
- green peas or asparagus heads

## Directions

1. Soak two grated bread rolls in two decilitres of sour cream; then beat nine dekagrams of shrimp butter until frothy, squeeze out the crumbs well and add them, as well as the shelled tails and legs of forty to fifty shrimp, which are cut only in the middle.
2. Braise two decilitres of completely young green peas in butter so that it does not lose its color, add this, a little salt, five egg yolks and one whole egg gradually, and when beaten frothy, gently mix in three egg whites beaten stiff.
3. Grease the mold with shrimp butter, arrange neatly with whole shrimp tails, green peas or asparagus heads, fill the whole mixture in.
