## Ingredients

- 10 dekagrams butter
- 1 whole egg
- 4 egg yolks
- flour
- salt
- hot milk
- sugar
- 4 deciliters milk
- flour
- sugar
- vanilla
- 9 dekagrams ground almonds
- 4 deciliters good cream
- 5 egg yolks
- peach or currant jam

## Directions

1. Cream 10 dekagrams butter
2. Add 1 whole egg and 4 egg yolks one after another and whisk
3. Season lightly with salt and add only enough flour to resemble light noodle dough
4. Fill a syringe with smooth round tube with this dough and pre-wet it with warm milk so the dough does not stick
5. Push the dough into hot milk cutting equal pieces with a knife continuously
6. Add a piece of sugar to the milk in which the extruded noodles are cooking
7. Remove with a skimming spoon when cooked and set aside
8. Make a thick pap from 4 deciliters milk, a few tablespoons flour, and sugar stirring continuously until smooth
9. When well cooked place a pot lid on iron legs with good coals underneath
10. When the tin-plated part of the lid which should be on top is well heated, quickly coat with butter
11. Place a spoonful of pap on it and spread out
12. Leave for a few minutes until it adheres to the bottom
13. Scrape it off with a wide knife leaving as much as a sheet of paper
14. When it begins to brown coat with butter and cut rectangular leaves
15. Roll these on a small wooden piece which is thicker on top and pointed below and about finger-length to form cone shapes
16. Continue until you have enough cones to abundantly fill the dough
17. Make large round sheets from the rest of the dough the size of the pot you plan to use
18. Coat the pot well with butter and on the bottom place a baked dough sheet
19. Add a row of noodles sprinkled with sugar, vanilla, and 9 dekagrams crushed almonds with shell
20. Then another sheet etc. until the pot is full
21. Whisk together 4 deciliters good cream or if the pot is larger somewhat more with sugar, vanilla, and 5 egg yolks and pour over
22. Place in a steam bath and leave for three-quarters of an hour
23. Then unmold and arrange the cones in nice order around it
24. Fill the cones with peach or currant jam
