## Ingredients

- seven dekagrams freshly picked rose petals
- small amount of cochineal
- forty-two dekagrams purified sugar
- four deciliters good cherry water
- juice of three lemons
- five and one-half dekagrams dissolved isinglass
- finely crushed ice

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over freshly picked and separated rose petals with a small amount of cochineal and forty-two dekagrams of purified sugar
2. Cover well and let stand
3. When completely cooled, strain through cloth dampened in warm water into a porcelain cup
4. Mix with four deciliters of good cherry water, juice of three lemons, and five and one-half dekagrams of dissolved isinglass
5. Pour into a mold embedded in finely crushed ice
6. Cover well, place ice on top, and let freeze for two hours
7. Unmold in the usual manner
8. Handle all flower jellies in this manner; for jellies made from carnations, use carnation pepper
