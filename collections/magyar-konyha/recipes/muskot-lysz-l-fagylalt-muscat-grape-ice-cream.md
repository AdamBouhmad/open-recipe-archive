## Ingredients

- muscat grapes
- 7 deciliters grape juice
- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 1 glass water
- juice of 4 lemons

## Directions

1. Remove muscat grapes from stems and press them
2. Cook 42 dekagrams of sugar with 1 glass of water over 7 deciliters of juice until it forms heavy drops
3. Pour this into the juice
4. Add the juice of 4 lemons
5. Pour into the freezing container and freeze
