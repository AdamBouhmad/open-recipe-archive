## Ingredients

- several whole eggs
- salt
- white bread (sliced finger-thick)
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Beat several whole eggs and mix with a little salt
2. Dip finger-thick slices of white bread into this mixture and fry them in hot fat
3. It is tasty if the bread slice is dipped in milk
