## Ingredients

- cleaned pullet
- green parsley
- butter
- meat stock
- rice

## Directions

1. Cut cleaned pullet into pieces, salt lightly and let stand in salt
2. Place the chicken with finely chopped green parsley in a piece of melted butter and braise slowly, but to keep it nicely white, frequently pour little meat stock over it until soft
3. Then pour over one or two more spoonfuls of meat stock and braise slightly more
