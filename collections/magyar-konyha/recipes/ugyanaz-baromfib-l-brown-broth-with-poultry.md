## Ingredients

- one whole chicken or capon
- water
- small amount of clean fat
- one teaspoon ground sugar
- carrot, sliced into rings
- parsley root
- celery
- red onion
- peppercorns
- kale, if available

## Directions

1. Put the whole chicken in a pot and pour in enough water to cover it, then cook
2. In a pot, put a small amount of clean fat and one teaspoon ground sugar
3. Add carrot sliced into rings, parsley root, celery, red onion, and peppercorns, and if available, a little kale
4. Cover and sauté on the fire with frequent stirring
5. When browned, add to the chicken
6. When the chicken is cooked tender, strain the broth and return it to the fire
7. Cook fine noodles in the broth and serve with the shredded chicken meat
