## Ingredients

- 7 dekagramm butter
- half kilogramm sponge cake, roughly crumbled
- 7 egg yolks
- 14 dekagramm sugar, rubbed with lemon
- 5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat 7 dekagramm butter until foamy.
2. Add half kilogramm of roughly crumbled sponge cake.
3. Beat in 7 egg yolks one after another.
4. Add 14 dekagramm sugar that has been rubbed with lemon zest.
5. Once all is beaten until foamy, fold in the stiffly beaten whites of 5 eggs.
6. Butter a mold and cook in a bain-marie for three-quarters of an hour.
