## Ingredients

- eighteen very thin pancakes
- fourteen dekagrams softened almond
- lemon zest
- fourteen dekagrams sugar
- one whole egg
- three egg yolks
- four egg yolks
- crushed sugar
- two decilitres good cream
- butter

## Directions

1. Make or bake eighteen very thin pancakes, crush fourteen dekagrams softened almond finely, chop lemon zest finely, place this and fourteen dekagrams sugar in a bowl, beat one whole egg and three egg yolks on it one after another and beat until foamy
2. Spread the almond paste on the pancakes and fold them, cut into pieces of finger thickness
3. Grease a pan or porcelain plate with butter, stand the pancakes upright around the sides in two or three rows, put four egg yolks, a little crushed sugar and two decilitres good cream in a pot, mix well and pour over
4. Place in the oven and brown on top
5. The same way the pancakes can be filled with raisins and small grapes, or with preserved sour cherry, crushed nuts, cinnamon, sugar
