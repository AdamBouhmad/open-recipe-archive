## Ingredients

- butter
- asparagus
- frogs
- pea broth or pea water
- salt
- spices
- sour cream
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Braise butter, asparagus and frogs in a pot
2. Pour pea broth or pea water over it
3. Bring well to a boil, season with spices and salt
4. Add two tablespoons of sour cream
5. Serve on fried bread
