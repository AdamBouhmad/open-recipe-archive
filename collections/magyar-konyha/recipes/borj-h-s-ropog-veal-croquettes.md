## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram of veal
- 7 decagrams of bacon
- 1 head of red onion
- carrots, sliced into rings
- 2 whole eggs
- white or brown butter sauce
- wafers
- breadcrumbs, fine
- egg, beaten
- veal kidneys
- asparagus or artichoke, depending on availability
- mushrooms
- crayfish tails
- parsley, green and fried

## Directions

1. Clean and salt 1 kilogram of veal and cut into large pieces
2. Place 7 decagrams of bacon, 1 head of red onion, and sliced carrots at the bottom of the pot
3. Braise until the onion and carrots are completely soft and the veal becomes light brown, occasionally pouring stock over so it does not burn
4. When well braised, beat 2 whole eggs into it and remove from heat without stirring
5. Cool until the eggs set without hardening
6. When cooled, place the whole mixture with bacon and onion on a cutting board and chop finely
7. Place back in the pot and pour several spoonfuls of good white or brown butter sauce over it
8. Cook until thick and cool
9. When cooled, quickly dip wafers in fresh water and place a spoonful of chopped meat in the middle
10. Fold together well and roll in beaten egg and fine breadcrumbs
11. Fry in hot fat
12. Place a mixture made from veal kidneys, asparagus or artichoke, mushrooms and crayfish tails at the bottom of the dish
13. Pile the croquettes high and garnish with fried green parsley
