## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters good cream
- 18 dekagrams sugar
- 1.5 grams vanilla
- 9 egg yolks
- 3.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Boil 7 deciliters of good cream and remove from heat
2. Cool completely
3. Finely pound 18 dekagrams of sugar with 1.5 grams of vanilla
4. Mix this in a pot with 9 egg yolks
5. Slowly add the cream, blending well
6. Place on charcoal fire and stir constantly until it begins to thicken
7. Transfer to a clean pot and stir until cooled to prevent curdling
8. Add 3.5 dekagrams of dissolved isinglass
9. Mix well, pour into mold, and freeze on ice
