## Ingredients

- one liter of table wine
- half a lemon peel
- broken cinnamon
- two cloves
- twenty egg yolks
- twenty dekagrams of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Put half a lemon peel, a knife's point of broken cinnamon and two cloves in one liter of table wine
2. Boil together in a porcelain dish
3. While the wine heats, beat twenty egg yolks in a deep pot with twenty dekagrams of powdered sugar until completely foamy
4. When the wine is hot, slowly pour it into the egg and sugar mixture while continuously stirring the wine sauce with a wooden spoon so the entire pot becomes filled with thick foamy sauce
5. Do not cook further as the egg would curdle with the wine
6. Keep in a warm place and continuously stir with the spoon until serving, otherwise it cannot be beaten foamy again
