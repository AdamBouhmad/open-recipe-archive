## Ingredients

- two-thirds the amount of almond aspic
- 4 fresh egg whites
- isinglass (omitted)

## Directions

1. Prepare two-thirds of the almond aspic recipe without isinglass.
2. Whip 4 fresh egg whites in a brass bowl into stiff peaks.
3. Pour the almond aspic into the whites.
4. Whisk vigorously with a whisk and continue whisking over a hot basin of embers until the aspic mixes with the snow, becomes shiny and thick.
5. Immediately remove from heat, pour into small glasses or aspic bowls, and freeze on ice.
6. Once completely frozen, serve in the bowl without unmolding.
