## Ingredients

- a capon
- salt
- butter
- brown roux
- red wine
- meat broth
- six anchovies
- red carrot
- fresh parsley
- lemon zest
- crushed pepper

## Directions

1. Pluck the capon cleanly, season with salt, prepare and roast it as in the previous recipe
2. Prepare a brown roux with butter
3. Dilute half with red wine and half with broth
4. Finely chop six anchovies, red carrot, fresh parsley, and lemon zest
5. Add all to the roux with crushed pepper
6. Cook the sauce well
7. Add the capon and braise until tender
8. Serve
