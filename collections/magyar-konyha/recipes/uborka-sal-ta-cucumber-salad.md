## Ingredients

- Peeled cucumber
- Salt
- Minced garlic
- Fine oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Slice the peeled cucumber very thinly and salt it
2. Let it stand for several hours
3. Strain off the salty water
4. Mix in minced garlic
5. Pour fine oil and vinegar over it and mix well
6. Sprinkle with crushed pepper
