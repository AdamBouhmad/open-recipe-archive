## Ingredients

- buttered dough
- 28 decagrams plumped almonds
- 28 decagrams sugar
- peel of 1 orange and 1 lemon, finely chopped
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 10 decagrams finely chopped candied lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare buttered dough and stretch it long, like dough for noodles
2. Finely grind 28 decagrams plumped almonds and 28 decagrams sugar
3. Finely chop the peel of 1 orange and 1 lemon
4. Place everything in a bowl, add 2 whole eggs and 4 egg yolks
5. Beat for half an hour until well fluffy
6. Pour onto the buttered dough and spread with a knife
7. Sprinkle with 10 decagrams finely chopped candied lemon
8. Fold gently together
9. Place on a buttered parchment paper
10. Brush with egg white
11. Bake in strong heat
