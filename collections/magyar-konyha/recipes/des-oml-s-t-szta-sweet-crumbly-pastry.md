## Ingredients

- fat
- sugar
- flour
- two decagrams of yeast
- four egg yolks
- ground cloves
- ground cinnamon
- milk
- jam

## Directions

1. Use equal amounts of fat and sugar, flour equal to the combined amount
2. Add two decagrams of yeast, four egg yolks, a knife-point of ground cloves, and twice as much ground cinnamon
3. Add milk and mix into a soft dough, working quickly
4. Spread half the dough thinly into a baking pan
5. Coat generously with jam
6. Roll the other half into finger-thick sticks
7. Arrange over the jam-covered dough
8. Bake in moderate heat until golden yellow
9. It is hard the first day but becomes softer the longer it stands, keeping 8-10 days
10. Slice as desired
11. Can also be filled with apple
