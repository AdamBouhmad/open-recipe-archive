## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram fine flour
- salt
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- water
- butter
- sheep's cheese
- fresh lard cracklings

## Directions

1. Place 1 kilogram fine flour in a large bowl
2. Season with salt
3. Beat in 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
4. Pour in a little water and mix everything with a cooking spoon
5. When the dough is well worked, throw noodles from a board with handle into boiling water using a knife
6. When cooked and risen to the surface, remove with a slotted spoon
7. Place in a pot coated with butter
8. Pour butter over and sprinkle with sheep's cheese and fine cracklings from fresh lard
