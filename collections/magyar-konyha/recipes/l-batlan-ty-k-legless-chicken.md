## Ingredients

- pancake batter (made much thicker and stiffer than usual)
- hot fat
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare pancake batter, but much thicker and stiffer than regular pancake batter
2. Take a rounded spoonful and place it in hot fat, and carefully, so it does not fall apart, fry it until golden
3. Serve dusted with sugar
