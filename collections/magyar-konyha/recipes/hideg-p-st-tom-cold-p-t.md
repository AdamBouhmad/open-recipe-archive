## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams bacon
- roasted venison or rabbit meat
- 5 bread rolls, crusts and insides
- good meat broth
- 5 egg yolks
- black pepper, ground
- nutmeg flower
- salt
- 3 goose livers
- butter
- 2 pheasants
- 2 partridges
- 10-12 truffle mushrooms
- green parsley
- 1 kilogram butter
- 750 dekagrams flour
- egg white
- aspic

## Directions

1. Finely chop 28 dekagrams bacon and roasted venison or rabbit meat, removing meat from bones
2. Grate 5 bread roll crusts and cook the insides in good meat broth
3. Mix the finely chopped meat with bacon
4. Add 5 egg yolks and mix well
5. Pass through a sieve
6. Add ground black pepper, nutmeg flower, and salt
7. Braise 3 goose livers in butter
8. Roast 2 pheasants and 2 partridges, cut them up, and remove meat from bones
9. Slice 10-12 truffle mushrooms and braise with butter and green parsley
10. Prepare crumbly pastry from 1 kilogram butter and 750 dekagrams flour (this pastry should be less crumbly than usual to hold the filling, so use more flour than butter)
11. Make a pâté of ordinary form with a much higher crown
12. Layer the filling with alternating rows: filling, truffle mushrooms, braised goose liver pieces, truffle mushrooms again, more filling, pheasant and partridge pieces, and so on until full
13. Close with pastry as usual
14. Decorate the top with pastry dough if desired
15. Brush with egg white and bake at steady heat for two hours
16. After removing from the oven, cool it
17. Cut the lid quite large and fill the interior of the pâté with aspic
