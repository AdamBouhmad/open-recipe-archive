## Ingredients

- one hundred fifty not quite ripe greengages
- cloth
- needle
- two parts fresh water
- one part white vinegar
- one kilogram sugar
- one liter water

## Directions

1. Clean one hundred fifty not quite ripe greengages with a cloth and prick all over with a needle
2. Soften them in two parts fresh water and one part white vinegar for three to four hours
3. Cook one kilogram sugar with one liter water and skim it well
