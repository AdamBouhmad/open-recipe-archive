## Ingredients

- woodcock
- salt
- thin bacon leaves
- butter
- goose fat
- onion
- parsley
- pepper
- bread slices
- fat

## Directions

1. Clean the woodcock and remove the innards, keeping the liver and entrails aside
2. Wash thoroughly and salt
3. Prepare and tie thin bacon leaves on the breast
4. Roast
5. Chop the entrails and liver finely together
6. Melt butter and goose fat, add chopped onion and parsley, heat slightly
7. Add the chopped entrails and liver, season with salt and a pinch of pepper
8. Simmer
9. Cut bread into knife-thickness slices and fry in fat
10. Arrange the woodcocks on a plate, spread the simmered filling on the bread slices, and arrange around the edge of the plate
