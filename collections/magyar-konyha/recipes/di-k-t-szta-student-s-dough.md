## Ingredients

- Eighteen dekagrams flour
- Sixteen dekagrams butter
- Four deciliters hot milk
- Six egg whites, stiffly beaten
- Sugar
- Good concentrated broth

## Directions

1. Mix flour and butter well together
2. Cook in four deciliters hot milk
3. Cool
4. Mix in stiffly beaten egg whites from six eggs
5. Sweeten well
6. Pour into a mold
7. Place in steam bath
8. Put some coals on the lid
9. Serve with good concentrated broth poured over
