## Ingredients

- common egg pancake
- asparagus heads, blanched
- braised mushrooms
- shredded pike
- pike liver
- crayfish butter
- flour
- 1 egg and 1 egg yolk
- salt

## Directions

1. Bake a common egg pancake and when cooled, cut each piece into several parts so the sausages will be finger-length
2. From blanched asparagus heads, braised mushrooms, shredded pike, and pike liver (which has been fried first), prepare a filling
3. Melt a piece of crayfish butter, add a little flour and the chopped filling
4. Braise slightly, then add 1 egg and 1 egg yolk
5. Mix well, remove from heat quickly and cool
6. Spread onto the divided egg pancake pieces to a knife-width thickness
7. Roll up and arrange nicely in a buttered baking dish
8. Half an hour before serving, beat 4 egg yolks and 4 deciliters of sour cream together
9. Pour over and cook in a water bath
