## Ingredients

- 8.5 kilogram rosemary extract
- 1 kilogram mint extract
- 9 kilogram purified concentrated alcohol
- 3.5 kilogram purified concentrated alcohol
- 38 dekagrams lemon oil
- 8 grams rosemary oil
- 3.5 kilogram purified concentrated alcohol
- 8 grams rosemary oil
- 20 grams neroli oil
- 3.5 kilogram purified concentrated alcohol
- 1.5 grams clove oil
- 6 grams orange flower oil
- 6 grams orange peel oil
- 6 grams cedarwood oil
- 6 grams bergamot oil
- 6 grams rosemary oil
- 1.5 kilogram purified alcohol
- 1.5 kilogram mint extract
- 3.5 kilogram purified concentrated alcohol
- 10 dekagrams bergamot oil
- 12 dekagrams bergamot oil

## Directions

1. Mix rosemary extract with mint extract with continuous shaking
2. Add 9 kilogram purified concentrated alcohol
3. Let stand for several hours after stirring well
4. In a bottle, mix 3.5 kilogram purified concentrated alcohol with 38 dekagrams lemon oil and 8 grams rosemary oil
5. After several hours, unite this mixture with the first one by continuous strong mixing
6. Let entire mixture stand for twenty-four hours
7. Add 3.5 kilogram alcohol and 8 grams rosemary oil and drop in 20 grams neroli oil with continuous stirring
8. Shake mixture for about fifteen minutes
9. Let stand for thirty-six hours, shaking for fifteen minutes every twelve hours
10. Before each shaking, add 3.5 kilogram alcohol (total 8.5 kilogram)
11. Add 3.5 kilogram alcohol and 1.5 grams clove oil and digest
12. Alternatively: dissolve 6 grams each of orange flower oil, orange peel oil, cedarwood oil, bergamot oil, and rosemary oil in 1.5 kilogram purified alcohol and digest
13. Or: shake 1.5 kilogram mint extract well with 1.5 kilogram rosemary extract, after six hours add 3.5 kilogram purified concentrated alcohol, 10 dekagrams bergamot oil, and 12 dekagrams bergamot oil
