## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams butter
- 7 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter cream
- 2 dekagrams yeast
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- fine flour
- salt
- brandied sour cherries
- chopped almonds
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 12 dekagrams butter until foamy, then gradually add 7 egg yolks one by one
2. Dissolve 2 dekagrams yeast in cream and mix with 1 tablespoon sugar, and let stand until the yeast begins to work
3. Slowly fold the yeast mixture into the beaten butter
4. Season lightly with salt and add enough fine flour to make a light dough
5. Stir until the dough leaves the spoon and beat it slightly more
6. Butter a mold and coat with chopped almonds, then fill halfway with the dough
7. With a spoon dipped in flour, smooth it out, then place a row of brandied sour cherries on top
8. Cover with the remaining dough, let rise, and bake at gentle heat
9. When done, turn out and sprinkle with sugar and serve
