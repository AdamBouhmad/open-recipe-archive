## Ingredients

- pike
- salt
- vinegar
- water
- carrot
- red onion
- bay leaf
- lemon zest
- whole peppercorns
- dill
- two decagrams of isinglass

## Directions

1. Cut up the pike and salt it
2. In a pot, combine one and a half liters each of vinegar and water
3. Add carrot, red onion, bay leaf, lemon zest, whole peppercorns, dill, and two decagrams of isinglass
4. Bring to a boil
5. Add the fish and cook until done
6. Place in a serving dish
7. Strain the broth through a kitchen towel stretched over the legs of an inverted chair
8. Pour half of the broth into a deep mold and allow to set
9. Arrange the pike on top, decorated with olives
10. Serve the pike aspic at table
