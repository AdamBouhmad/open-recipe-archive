## Ingredients

- trout
- pike
- small red onion
- bread roll soaked in milk
- lemon zest
- fresh parsley
- salt
- pepper
- one or two eggs
- melted butter
- breadcrumbs
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Remove the entrails from the trout, clean it and salt it, then let it stand for a while.
2. Take a piece of pike, carefully remove its bones, and chop it finely with a small red onion, milk-soaked and squeezed-out half bread roll, a little lemon zest and fresh parsley.
3. Add salt and ground pepper to the mixture, beat one or two eggs into it and mix everything well.
4. Dry the trout with a cloth, spread the filling on it, coat with melted butter, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, place in a buttered baking pan.
5. Put in the oven, baste several times with butter so they acquire a nice color.
6. Place on a buttered plate, pour a little sour cream over it and simmer slightly.
