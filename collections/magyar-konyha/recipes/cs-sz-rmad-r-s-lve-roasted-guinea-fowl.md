## Ingredients

- guinea fowl
- salt
- bacon
- butter
- oyster
- potato
- lemon peel
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the guinea fowl, wash and salt it
2. Insert a piece of bacon inside
3. Truss and roast until golden brown
4. Prepare a sauce with butter, oyster, potato, lemon peel and lemon juice
5. Place the roasted guinea fowl on a plate and pour the sauce underneath
