## Ingredients

- 4 deciliters sour cream
- 6 whole eggs
- 30 dekagrams flour
- 1.5 liters milk
- butter
- fine breadcrumbs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Whisk together sour cream and 6 whole eggs finely
2. Add 30 dekagrams flour and salt to make noodle dough
3. Bring 1.5 liters milk to a boil in a pot
4. Drop noodles into boiling milk using a spoon and cook until done
5. Remove with a slotted spoon so they remain whole
6. Place on a plate coated with butter and sprinkled with fine breadcrumbs
7. Sprinkle breadcrumbs on top again
8. Place in the oven and brown nicely
9. Can be served sprinkled with sugar
