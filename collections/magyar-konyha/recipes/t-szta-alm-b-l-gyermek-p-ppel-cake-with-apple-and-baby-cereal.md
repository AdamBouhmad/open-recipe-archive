## Ingredients

- four decilitres milk
- five dekagrams butter
- six egg yolks
- ten dekagrams sugar grated on lemon zest
- four egg whites
- six masanszki apples
- butter
- fine breadcrumbs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare thick baby cereal from four decilitres milk, place the cooled cereal in five dekagrams butter beaten foamy, beat in six egg yolks and mix well, also ten dekagrams sugar grated on lemon zest, and finally mix in the stiff peaks of four egg whites
2. Peel six masanszki apples and cut them into oblong pieces and mix them into the beaten cereal
3. Pour the mixture onto a butter-greased, fine breadcrumb-sprinkled torte sheet, bake in a warm oven, turn out onto a plate and sprinkle with sugar
