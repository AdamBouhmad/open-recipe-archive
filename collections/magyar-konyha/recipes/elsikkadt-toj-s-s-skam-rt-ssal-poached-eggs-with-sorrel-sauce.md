## Ingredients

- butter
- sorrel
- flour
- salt
- sour cream
- white vinegar
- water
- eggs

## Directions

1. Melt butter and add finely chopped sorrel
2. Braise until the liquid has evaporated
3. Sprinkle with a little flour and braise again
4. Season with salt and add sour cream
5. Simmer until fully cooked
6. In a pot, bring one part white vinegar and three parts water to a boil with salt
7. Crack eggs into the boiling liquid, being careful to keep them whole and not let them overcook
8. Place the sorrel sauce in a dish
9. Remove the poached eggs with a slotted spoon and carefully place in the sauce, keeping them whole
