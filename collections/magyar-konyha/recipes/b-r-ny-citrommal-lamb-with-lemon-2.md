## Ingredients

- two liters water
- one quarter lamb from the front
- salt
- parsley root
- carrot
- celery
- small head red onion
- peppercorns
- flour
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Place one quarter lamb in two liters of water, cut into pieces
2. Add salt, a little parsley root, carrot, celery, a small head of red onion and a few peppercorns
3. Cook the lamb with these for three-quarters of an hour
4. While cooking, prepare a mixture from one spoonful of flour and one egg yolk
