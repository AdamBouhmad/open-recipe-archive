## Ingredients

- 42 dekagramm sugar
- 1 bottle champagne
- juice of 4 lemons
- 6 dekagramm isinglass or equivalent amount of aspic

## Directions

1. Combine the cooled sugar with the lukewarm aspic, strained lemon juice and champagne
2. Strain through a cloth
3. After testing the firmness of the jelly, place one quarter of the mixture in a porcelain vessel and set it on ice
4. Beat the remaining jelly with a whisk until it becomes white, foamy and begins to thicken
5. Fill the mold one quarter full with this beaten mixture
6. Once the beaten mass has almost completely frozen, pour the remaining strained jelly over it and completely freeze the whole on ice
7. To imitate champagne bubbles, combine the sugar with lemon juice, aspic and one quarter of the champagne and stir on ice until it begins to thicken, then slowly pour in the remaining champagne by spoonfuls, creating foam bubbles in the jelly, then pour into the mold and freeze on ice
