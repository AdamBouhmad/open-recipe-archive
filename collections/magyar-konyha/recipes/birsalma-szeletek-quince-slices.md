## Ingredients

- 500 grams quince
- 14 dekagrams softened and finely ground almonds
- 500 grams finely ground sugar
- 21 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- 21 dekagrams softened and finely ground almonds
- 7 dekagrams bread crumbs moistened with 1 tablespoon honey wine
- 10 dekagrams finely ground chocolate dissolved in a pan with 1 spoonful water with repeated stirring

## Directions

1. Cook the quince and strain it
2. Let it stand covered for a while
3. Peel and press the flesh through a sieve
4. In a pan with continuous stirring, prepare a firm mixture from 500 grams quince, 14 dekagrams softened and finely ground almonds, and 500 grams finely ground sugar
5. Place this mixture on a clean board heavily sprinkled with sugar
6. Cool completely, sprinkle well with sugar, roll to twice knife-edge thickness
7. Cut into slices two fingers wide and a span long with a sharp knife
8. Place on paper sprinkled with sugar and dry in the drying oven
9. Meanwhile, prepare a mixture from 21 dekagrams finely ground sugar, 21 dekagrams softened and finely ground almonds, 7 dekagrams bread crumbs moistened with 1 tablespoon honey wine, and 10 dekagrams finely ground chocolate dissolved in a pan with 1 spoonful water with repeated stirring
