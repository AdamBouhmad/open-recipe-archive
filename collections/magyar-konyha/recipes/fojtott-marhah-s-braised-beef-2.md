## Ingredients

- Beef chunks
- Smoked tongue, cut lengthwise
- Bacon
- Salt
- Fresh bacon
- Red onion
- Carrot, sliced
- Lemon zest, small amount
- Whole peppercorns, several
- Marjoram
- Seven deciliters wine
- Small amount vinegar
- Water

## Directions

1. Lard the beef chunks with pieces of smoked tongue and bacon, then salt them
2. Place fresh bacon, red onion, sliced carrot, small amount of lemon zest, several whole peppercorns, and marjoram in a pot
3. Add the beef chunks
4. Pour seven deciliters of wine, a small amount of vinegar, and water over the meat
5. Cover tightly and braise slowly
6. When the meat is tender enough, remove it and skim the fat from the surface
7. Braise the vegetables well
8. Before serving, return the meat to the pot and braise briefly
9. Place on a serving platter, strain part of the sauce through a sieve over the meat, and serve the remaining sauce in a bowl
