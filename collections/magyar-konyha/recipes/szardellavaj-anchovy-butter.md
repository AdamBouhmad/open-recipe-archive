## Ingredients

- finely chopped anchovy
- butter (two to three times the amount of anchovy)
- vinegar (if serving plain)

## Directions

1. Grind finely chopped anchovy with two to three times as much butter
2. Press through a sieve
3. Use for various dishes, especially sauces and fillings
4. If enjoying plain, add a little butter and vinegar during preparation
