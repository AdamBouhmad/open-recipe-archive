## Ingredients

- good homemade bread, sliced very thinly
- soup fat or butter
- finely chopped onion
- good meat broth
- crushed pepper
- nutmeg
- egg per person
- sausage, chopped into small pieces
- finely chopped roots
- finely minced onion

## Directions

1. Cut good homemade bread into very thin slices
2. Heat soup fat or butter in a pot and warm it
3. Add finely chopped onion and bring to a boil
4. Throw in the sliced bread and lightly toast it similarly
5. Pour good meat broth over it
6. Add crushed pepper and nutmeg
7. Cook the soup well, being careful that the bread does not fall apart
8. Before serving, place one egg per person in the hot broth, which should not become hard
9. Add chopped sausage and finely cut roots
10. Sprinkle with finely minced onion
