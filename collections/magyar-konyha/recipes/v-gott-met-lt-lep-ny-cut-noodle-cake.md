## Ingredients

- Twenty-eight dekagrams softened almonds
- Lemon juice
- Twenty-one dekagrams broken sugar
- Six egg yolks
- Three egg whites
- One egg
- One egg yolk
- Sugar
- Flour
- Fat for frying

## Directions

1. Grind half of twenty-eight dekagrams softened almonds finely and pour lemon juice over it
2. Cut the other half into long, fine pieces
3. Put twenty-one dekagrams broken sugar in a bowl, add the finely ground almonds and yolks of six eggs
4. Stir together for half an hour
5. Mix in the stiffly beaten whites of three eggs
6. Prepare cut noodles from one egg, one egg yolk, sugar, and flour
7. Fry in fat until beautiful golden
