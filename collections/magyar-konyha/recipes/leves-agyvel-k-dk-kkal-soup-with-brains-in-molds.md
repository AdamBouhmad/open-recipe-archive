## Ingredients

- two bread rolls
- milk
- four deciliters milk
- seven decagrams butter
- salt
- one egg
- three egg whites
- fine bread crumbs
- butter
- calf brain
- salt
- water
- green parsley
- brown sauce

## Directions

1. Rasp off the crust of two bread rolls, soften them in milk, and squeeze dry.
2. Place in a pot, pour four deciliters milk over them, bring to a boil over coals with continuous stirring until reduced to a puree.
3. Remove from heat and let cool.
4. Beat seven decagrams butter until fluffy.
5. Add a little salt, one whole egg, and the foam of three egg whites.
6. Place a little of the forcemeat in molds greased with butter and sprinkled with fine bread crumbs.
7. Fill the center with prepared brains and cover with the remaining forcemeat without completely filling.
8. The brains must first be cooked in salted water, then blanched, and braised with butter, finely chopped green parsley.
9. Cook the molds in steam for half an hour.
10. Unmold, place in a bowl, and pour brown sauce over it.
