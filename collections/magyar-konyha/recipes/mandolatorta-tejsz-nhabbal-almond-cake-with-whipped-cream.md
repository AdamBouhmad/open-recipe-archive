## Ingredients

- 28 dekagramm soaked and ground almonds
- 28 dekagramm sugar rubbed on lemon
- 1 whole egg and 7 egg yolks
- 3.5 dekagramm sifted bread crumbs
- 4 egg whites
- 7 deciliter good cream
- sugar for sweetness
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Whip soaked ground almonds with lemon sugar, whole egg and seven egg yolks until it becomes good and frothy, about half an hour
2. Add sifted bread crumbs
3. Finally fold in stiffly beaten egg white foam
4. Butter two equal cake layers and their rings
5. Fill mixture and bake slowly
6. Before serving, whip seven deciliters of good cream and add enough sugar and vanilla for sweetness
7. When almond layers are completely cooled, place on plate
8. Add half the whipped cream
9. Place second layer on top and remaining cream on top
10. Decorate edges with large Spanish pastry shell wreaths and serve quickly
