## Ingredients

- 4 deciliters good sweet sour cream, warmed
- 10 egg yolks
- 13.75 grams fresh baker's yeast dissolved in sour cream
- 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
- 1 liter fine dry flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Add egg yolks, yeast dissolved in sour cream, and sugar to the warmed sour cream and mix well together
2. Place 1 liter fine flour in a bowl, salt it, strain the above mixture into it
3. Beat the dough, place on a flour-dusted board
4. Handle it separately in the same manner as recipe 555
