## Ingredients

- 8-12 oranges
- 0.5 kilogramm sugar
- 2 lemons
- 0.75 bottle white wine
- 6 dekagramm isinglass

## Directions

1. Place the finely chopped sugar in a bowl, add the finely peeled zest from one orange, and let stand covered until the sugar is dissolved
2. Meanwhile, squeeze the juice from the oranges and lemons and strain together as described above
3. Combine the strained liquid with the lukewarm aspic, wine and sugar
4. Color the jelly red with cochineal or other coloring
5. Strain through a cloth, test for firmness, pour into the mold and freeze
6. To garnish with orange shells: hollow out the orange skins and scrape off the inner white side well, beat some of the orange jelly until white and foamy, fill the shells with this, once frozen cut into quarters and use to surround the jelly
7. Alternatively, segment the oranges like a compote, peel and remove the seeds, then marinate with sugar, pour a little orange jelly into the mold, freeze it, arrange a layer of orange segments on top, then jelly, continuing in layers until the mold is full
