## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter cream
- 16 dekagrams sugar
- little vanilla
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon flour

## Directions

1. Put 0.5 liter cream on heat with 16 dekagrams sugar and a little vanilla and bring to a boil
2. Mix 2 egg yolks with 1 tablespoon flour
3. Pour the hot cream over it while continuously stirring
4. Return to heat and bring to a boil while continuously stirring
