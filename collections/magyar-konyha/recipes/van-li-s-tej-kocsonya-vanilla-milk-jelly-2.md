## Ingredients

- 18 dekagrams sugar
- ground vanilla pod
- 14 dekagrams egg yolks
- 2 deciliters heavy cream, boiled

## Directions

1. Beat together the sugar, ground vanilla, and egg yolks
2. Slowly pour in the boiled heavy cream and beat well until it begins to thicken
3. Remove the pan from heat, pour the milk jelly into cups, and let it cool
4. Sprinkle generously with finely ground sugar and brown it with a hot spatula
