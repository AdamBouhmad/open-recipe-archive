## Ingredients

- pineapple
- 42 dekagrams pineapple syrup
- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 4 deciliters water
- 3 lemons, juice

## Directions

1. Peel the pineapple and cut the finest flesh into pieces
2. Cook with a little water until it becomes completely syrup, then strain through a sieve
3. Cook 42 dekagrams of strained syrup with the same amount of sugar and 4 deciliters of water until the sugar is well dissolved
4. Pour into the syrup
5. Squeeze the juice of 3 lemons into it
6. Mix well and treat it as instructed in the previous section
