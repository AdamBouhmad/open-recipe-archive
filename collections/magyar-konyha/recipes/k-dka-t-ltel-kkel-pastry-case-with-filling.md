## Ingredients

- veal or other leftover roast pieces
- bread roll, crust scraped off and soaked in milk
- green parsley, chopped
- lobster tail
- salt
- butter
- 1 whole egg and 2 egg yolks
- lard pastry dough

## Directions

1. Chop veal or other leftover roast pieces finely.
2. Scrape the crust from half a bread roll, soak in milk, and squeeze dry.
3. Add finely chopped green parsley, lobster tail, and salt.
4. Beat butter until fluffy and mix in the above ingredients with 1 whole egg and 2 egg yolks, stirring until it becomes a paste.
5. Prepare a lard pastry (see pastry section below) and roll it out as finely as lasagna dough.
6. Form flat pastry cases, fill with this stuffing, cover with the same dough, and bake in the oven until beautifully browned.
7. Turn the pastry cases out onto a dish and serve.
