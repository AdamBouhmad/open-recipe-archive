## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams flour
- salt
- 1 small spoon ground sugar
- 14 dekagrams butter
- 2 egg yolks
- sweet cream as needed for moistening
- sugar for sprinkling
- 7 dekagrams finely chopped almonds
- 4 deciliters cream
- 5 egg yolks
- sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with salt and ground sugar
2. Cut butter into small pieces and work into the flour with a rolling pin until well combined
3. Add two egg yolks and as much sweet cream as needed to moisten the dough
4. Combine the dough with a wide knife, roll out and fold in three times like puff pastry
5. Divide into smaller portions and roll out into round leaves
6. Cut with a pastry cutter and fry like ordinary snow pastry
7. Sprinkle with sugar and serve on the table
8. Alternatively, for use as a dessert, heap high on a dish and sprinkle with finely chopped almonds
9. Beat 4 deciliters cream with 5 egg yolks, sugar and vanilla until well combined
10. Pour over the pastry, place in the oven and bake for half an hour
