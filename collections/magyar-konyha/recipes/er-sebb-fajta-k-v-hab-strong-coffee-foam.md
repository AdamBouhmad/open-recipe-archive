## Ingredients

- 7 tablespoons very strong, clear, cold coffee
- 10 dekagrams ground sugar
- 3.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass
- 7 deciliters excellent heavy cream
- crushed ice

## Directions

1. Place 7 tablespoons of very strong, clear, cold coffee in a basin with 10 dekagrams ground sugar
2. When dissolved, add 3.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass and set on ice
3. Beat 7 deciliters of excellent heavy cream with crushed ice in a brass basin using a whip until it becomes stiff foam
4. Quickly fold in the prepared coffee
5. If the flavor is very weak and color light, add more coffee
6. Pour into mold and freeze on ice
