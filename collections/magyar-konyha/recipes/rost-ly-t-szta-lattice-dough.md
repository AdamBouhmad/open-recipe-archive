## Ingredients

- seven deciliters of fine flour
- salt
- twenty-eight dekagrams of lard
- twenty-one dekagrams of sifted sugar
- zest of one lemon, finely chopped
- a little cinnamon
- crushed clove and pepper
- one large egg

## Directions

1. Mix seven deciliters of fine flour with salt and twenty-eight dekagrams of lard
2. Add twenty-one dekagrams of sifted sugar, finely chopped zest of one lemon, a little cinnamon, and crushed clove and pepper
3. Work all this well together with a rolling pin
4. Make a hole in the middle of the whole, crack a large egg into it and make fine dough with a wide knife
5. Roll out to the thickness of the little finger and place on four sheet pans
6. Create a lattice on top with thin dough sticks
7. Brush with beaten egg
8. Bake to a nice light brown
9. Once removed from the oven, cut into equal rectangular pieces and arrange neatly on a plate
