## Ingredients

- 1 vanilla pod, finely chopped
- 1.6 liters good sweet cream
- 18 egg yolks
- 35 dekagrams finely crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Chop 1 vanilla pod finely and cook with 1.6 liters good sweet cream for eight minutes, then remove to cool.
2. In the meantime, beat 18 egg yolks with 35 dekagrams finely crushed sugar for a quarter hour.
3. Slowly pour the vanilla cream into the egg mixture.
4. Beat over charcoal fire with a whisk until the eggs thicken or the mixture begins to boil.
5. Remove from fire, strain through silk sieve, and continue stirring until completely cooled.
6. Fill the mold with the mixture and freeze until it reaches the firmness of frozen butter. For smaller tables, the above quantities should be used proportionally.
