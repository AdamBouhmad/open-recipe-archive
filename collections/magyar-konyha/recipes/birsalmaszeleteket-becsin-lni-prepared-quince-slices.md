## Ingredients

- one kilogram well-ripened quince
- three deciliters water
- three deciliters vinegar
- half kilogram sugar
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Peel one kilogram of well-ripened quince and divide into four or six pieces
2. Remove the seeds and hard center portions
3. Place three deciliters water, the same amount of vinegar, and half kilogram sugar in a brass pan over fire
4. Add the quince pieces to keep them white
5. Before cooking, pierce each slice with a few pieces of cinnamon stick and two clove buds
6. Cook until soft, then remove and place in jars
7. Continue cooking the liquid until enough remains to cover the slices
8. When cooled, pour over the slices and store as done with others
