## Ingredients

- best quality well-ripened apricots
- half kilogram apricots
- half kilogram finely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the best quality well-ripened apricots and press through a sieve. For half kilogram of apricots, take the same amount of finely ground sugar and cook rapidly for half an hour in a brass preserving basin, then remove from heat and pour as hot as possible into jars. This preserve is particularly good for pastries that are filled after baking, as well as for carnival doughnuts. It has a beautiful appearance and perfectly clear flavor.
