## Ingredients

- Cleaned asparagus
- Yellow leafy salad
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Tie the cleaned asparagus in a bundle and cook it
2. Drain the water from it and arrange it on a plate so the asparagus heads point inward, then cool completely
3. Clean a beautiful yellow leafy salad thoroughly
4. Arrange it on the plate in a star shape around the asparagus
5. Salt it, pour oil mixed with good vinegar over it, and serve after a quarter hour
