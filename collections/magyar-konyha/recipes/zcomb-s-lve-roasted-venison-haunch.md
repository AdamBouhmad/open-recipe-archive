## Ingredients

- venison haunch
- salt
- bacon for larding
- vinegar
- water
- cloves
- red onion
- carrot slices
- lemon peel
- sour cream
- flour

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the venison haunch well, remove the membrane, salt it
2. Lard it neatly and cover with brine made of 1 part boiled vinegar and 3 parts boiled water with cloves, red onion, carrot slices, lemon peel, and salt
3. Thread the roast onto a spit and baste it frequently with the brine during roasting, which is placed in a pan beneath the roast so the bacon fat drips into it
4. When it is slowly roasted through, make the sauce: whip 4 tablespoons sour cream with a spoonful of flour, then mix it with the brine until it is thick
5. Baste the roast several more times with this sauce, put the small amount of remaining sauce in the dish, and place the roast on top
