## Ingredients

- potatoes
- veal offal
- parsley
- red onion, small head
- five anchovies, washed and cleaned of bones
- butter
- salt
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes until tender, peel and slice them
2. Cook veal offal until tender and chop it coarsely
3. Chop parsley, red onion, and anchovies finely
4. Melt butter and add all the chopped ingredients, add salt and braise everything together well
5. Place a few spoonfuls of sour cream and a few pieces of butter on a plate
6. Add a layer of potatoes, salt them
7. Add more sour cream and another layer of potatoes, continue until everything is arranged on the plate
8. Top with butter and sour cream
9. Garnish with anchovies cut lengthwise and bake until golden
