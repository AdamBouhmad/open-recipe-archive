## Ingredients

- bones
- vegetables and seasonings as for brown beef broth

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same manner as brown beef broth, using bones instead of meat
2. This is particularly useful when serving roasted meat, braised beef, game roasts, etc., as the bones and other scraps from these can be made into a very good broth by this method
3. Lung meat is best for this broth
