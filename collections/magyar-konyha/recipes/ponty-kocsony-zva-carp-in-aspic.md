## Ingredients

- a beautiful piece of carp
- salt
- white wine vinegar
- water
- bay leaf
- carrot
- lemon peel
- whole peppercorns
- red onion
- cloves
- fish bladder
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cut a beautiful piece of carp into pieces and salt it.
2. Make the broth as follows: put two decilitres white wine vinegar, one and a half litres water, bay leaf, carrot, lemon peel, whole peppercorns, red onion cut into small pieces, three to four cloves and two decagrams of fish bladder into a pan and cook it all slowly.
3. Place the fish in it and cook it, place it on the serving dish, strain the broth and cool it.
4. Then beat two whole eggs into the broth together with their shells and place it on embers again and bring to a slow boil.
5. Stretch cloth over the legs of an overturned chair, carefully pour the broth over it and strain it slowly so that it becomes clear; then pour half of it into a pan or mould and let it set, arrange it with sea crabs, place the fish on top and then pour the other half of the broth over it.
