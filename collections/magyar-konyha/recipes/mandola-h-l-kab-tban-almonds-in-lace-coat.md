## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams finely crushed sugar
- 1 egg white
- wafer
- blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar into a stiff icing with the stiffly beaten egg white over half an hour
2. Cut round leaves from wafer, place a blanched almond on each and cover with a coffee spoonful of icing
3. Once all are prepared this way, bake or rather dry in a gentle oven and then arrange on a plate
