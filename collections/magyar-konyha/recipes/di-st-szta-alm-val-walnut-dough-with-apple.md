## Ingredients

- Butter the size of an egg
- Two tablespoons flour
- Four deciliters hot milk
- Four apples
- Twenty walnuts
- Sugar
- Nine egg yolks
- Five egg whites, stiffly beaten

## Directions

1. Make a béchamel: melt butter, add flour and mix until smooth
2. Gradually add hot milk while constantly stirring
3. Cook well and cool
4. Put in a basin when cooled
5. Peel apples and cut into cubes
6. Crush twenty walnuts coarsely and add sugar to taste
7. Beat nine egg yolks in one by one and stir until sufficiently fluffy
8. Finally mix in stiffly beaten egg whites
9. Pour into a buttered mold
10. Braise for one hour
