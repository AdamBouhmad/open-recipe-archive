## Ingredients

- three liters of water
- one kilo of fresh pork from the shoulder or leg
- salt
- three to four cloves of garlic
- four tablespoons of flour
- six tablespoons of good sour cream
- one pair of whole eggs
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put three liters of water in a four-liter pot and add one kilo of fresh pork chopped into portions, either from the shoulder or leg
2. Add salt and three to four peeled cloves of garlic to the water
3. When the meat is very tender, strain the broth through a sieve to remove small bones that fell from the meat
4. Return the meat to the clean broth and prepare a liaison
5. In a bowl mix four tablespoons of flour, six tablespoons of good sour cream, and a pair of whole eggs, stirring well with the flour
6. When the liaison is smooth, slowly pour the hot meat broth into the bowl with the liaison
7. Return the broth to the pot and add enough vinegar to make it nicely sour
8. If desired, add finely chopped garlic after preparation
9. Keep in a warm place until serving, but do not boil for long as it may curdle
