## Ingredients

- parchment pears
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Peel the parchment pears, remove the core leaving the stem intact
2. Cook the pears in sugared water until soft
3. Remove them one by one and drain
4. Place on paper-covered sheet metal and dry in a well-cooled oven
5. Press lengthwise between two wooden boards until wide and flat
6. Dry again thoroughly and press even thinner
7. Repeat this process three to four times until completely flat and knife-blade thin and well dried
8. Layer in rolls, placing finely ground sugar between each layer
9. Fill the rolls completely and store in a dry place until needed
