## Ingredients

- firm lettuce heads
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut nice firm lettuce heads into two or four parts
2. Wash thoroughly and place on a cloth
3. Put pepper and salt in the salad bowl
4. Pour two parts oil and one part vinegar
5. Turn the lettuce well in this mixture and serve
