## Ingredients

- two bread rolls
- milk
- four deciliters milk
- calf brain
- ten decagrams butter
- eight egg yolks
- three egg whites
- butter
- fine bread crumbs
- salt
- water
- green parsley
- pepper

## Directions

1. Rasp off the crust of two bread rolls, cut into thin slices, place in a pot.
2. Pour four deciliters milk over them and let soften for an hour.
3. Place on coals and cook with continuous stirring until it becomes a puree.
4. Remove from heat and let cool.
5. Mix the cooled puree with prepared brains and ten decagrams butter beaten until fluffy.
6. Mix in eight egg yolks well beaten.
7. Gently fold in the foam of three egg whites.
8. Fill the forcemeat into a mold greased with butter and sprinkled with fine crumbs.
9. Cook by steaming for three-quarters of an hour.
10. Carefully unmold onto a clean board and cut into finger-thick slices.
11. Place in a soup bowl and pour brown sauce over it.
12. The brains must be cooked in salted water, placed in cold water, the membrane removed, finely chopped, and braised with butter, green parsley, a little pepper, and salt as needed before use.
