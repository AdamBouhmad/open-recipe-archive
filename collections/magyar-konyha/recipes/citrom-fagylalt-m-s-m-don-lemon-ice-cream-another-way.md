## Ingredients

- 2 lemons
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- juice of 12 lemons
- 28 dekagrams sugar
- 4 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Gently rub 14 dekagrams sugar on 2 lemons
2. Place the yellow sugar in a bowl
3. Press the juice of 12 lemons onto it
4. Cook 28 dekagrams sugar with 4 deciliters water until it forms heavy drops
5. Cool the syrup
6. Mix the juice with the cooled syrup
7. Place in a freezing container
