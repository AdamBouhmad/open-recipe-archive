## Ingredients

- 18 dekagrams sugar
- Broken piece of vanilla
- 14 dekagrams egg yolks
- 2 deciliters boiled heavy cream

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar with broken piece of vanilla and fourteen dekagrams of egg yolks
2. Slowly pour two deciliters of boiled heavy cream onto it and beat well until it begins to thicken
3. Remove the pan from heat
4. Pour the milk jelly into a cup and cool it
5. Sprinkle well with finely broken sugar and brown with a hot spatula
