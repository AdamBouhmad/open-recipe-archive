## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams blanched almonds
- 18 dekagrams crushed and sifted sugar
- 2 coffee spoonfuls of preserved apricots

## Directions

1. Crush the blanched almonds into a fine dough without wetting the almonds during crushing
2. Crush the sugar and thoroughly rub it on a board with a wide knife together with the almonds and the preserved apricots
3. If the mixture is not moist enough, add a little more preserved apricot, which improves the beautiful color of the dough, but it should not be too soft
4. Once the mass is well worked, dust the board with sugar and form small pretzels from the mass
