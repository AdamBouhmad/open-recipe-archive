## Ingredients

- pigeons
- salt
- bacon slices
- small salad

## Directions

1. Clean the pigeons and salt
2. Wrap in bacon slices
3. Thread three on a skewer
4. Roast to a nice pink color
5. When removed from the spit, remove the bacon and serve
6. Can be decorated with small green salad
