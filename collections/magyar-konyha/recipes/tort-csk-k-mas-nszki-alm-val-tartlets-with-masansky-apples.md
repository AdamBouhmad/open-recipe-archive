## Ingredients

- crumb dough (morzsatészta)
- dried peas for blind baking
- fine sugar
- small masansky apples
- water
- sugar
- lemon juice
- cochineal or carmine for coloring
- gelatin for setting

## Directions

1. Roll crumb dough to knife-blade thickness
2. Line flat molds with the dough and place a thin dough ring on the edge
3. Fill the molds with dried peas, place in oven and bake golden
4. When well baked, remove the peas from the molds and take the molds out of the forms, sprinkle with fine ground sugar
5. Peel small masansky apples after removing the core, keeping them whole
6. Put water, sugar, and lemon juice in a pot and stew the apples until soft
7. Remove the apples and boil down the liquid to a thick jelly, color with cochineal
8. Remove from heat and cool
9. Fill the molds with apples and cover with the jelly
