## Ingredients

- three liters of water
- seven deciliters of small red beans
- two bay leaves
- two cloves of garlic
- thin roux
- one and a half liters of water
- salt and vinegar
- butter
- bread rolls

## Directions

1. Cook the beans, bay leaves, and garlic in three liters of water until soft
2. Press through a coarse sieve
3. Make a thin roux and cook again with one and a half liters of water
4. Add a little salt and vinegar
5. Pour over bread roll slices fried in butter and cut lengthwise
