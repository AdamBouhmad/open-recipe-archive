## Ingredients

- fresh goose liver
- salt
- spices
- 1 white onion
- parsley greens
- several fresh mushrooms
- butter
- veal cutlets
- pork caul
- fat
- Croutons
- Sherry sauce
- caviar
- pastry rolls

## Directions

1. Press fresh goose liver through a sieve, add a little salt and spices, and mix well
2. Finely chop one white onion, a little parsley greens, and several fresh mushrooms, and braise in a little butter; when cooled, add to the goose liver purée and mix together
3. Now prepare the veal cutlets
4. Salt them and spread a little purée on one side and wrap in pork caul
5. Place in a pan and pour over a little fat, and bake in a hot oven for 10-15 minutes
6. Place under each cutlet a round Crouton, good Sherry sauce, and on each one a pastry roll filled with caviar
