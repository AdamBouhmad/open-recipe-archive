## Ingredients

- 3 egg whites
- 28 dekagrams sugar
- 28 dekagrams shelled and crushed almonds
- finely chopped zest of one lemon
- juice of half a lemon
- wafer star shapes
- Spanish cherry or other preserved fruit

## Directions

1. Beat 28 dekagrams of sugar with the foam of 3 egg whites into white, stiff icing
2. Mix for another quarter hour with 28 dekagrams of shelled and crushed almonds, the finely chopped zest of one lemon, and the juice of half a lemon
3. Coat the wafer star shapes with the mixture to the thickness of a knife blade, leaving one point in the center of the star free
4. Place a Spanish cherry or other preserved fruit on this point
5. Place the stars on a buttered sheet and bake in a gentle oven until light yellow
