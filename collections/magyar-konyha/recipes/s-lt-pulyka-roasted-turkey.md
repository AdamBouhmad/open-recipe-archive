## Ingredients

- turkey
- salt
- bacon
- bread
- milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the turkey
2. Remove the breast and rinse several times
3. Cook well
4. Lard the breast neatly with bacon
5. Soak bread in milk and place in the crop
6. Roast at an even temperature
7. If the turkey is large, almost two hours are needed for roasting
8. Baste with butter during roasting
9. Serve the drippings that fall from it at the table
