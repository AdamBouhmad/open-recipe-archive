## Ingredients

- small plate of spring violet leaves
- 7 deciliters hot water
- 42 dekagrams sugar
- 4.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Tear violet leaves without leaving any green on them
2. Pour 7 deciliters of hot water over a small plate of violet leaves in a soup bowl
3. Cover well and let stand for 1 hour
4. Strain the leaves through a sieve
5. Dissolve 42 dekagrams of sugar in the liquid
6. When completely dissolved, add 4.5 dekagrams of dissolved isinglass
7. Strain the mixture through cloth and blotting paper
8. Pour into mold and freeze
