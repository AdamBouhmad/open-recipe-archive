## Ingredients

- crumbly rolls
- veal meat or roast leftovers
- green parsley
- red onion
- flour
- meat broth
- sour cream
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- fat

## Directions

1. Grate the crust of crumbly rolls, cut them in half in the middle, and hollow them out
2. Coarsely chop veal meat or roast leftovers, green parsley, and red onion; add half a tablespoon of flour and fry briefly, then add the chopped mixture, pour a tablespoon of broth over it, and cook until there is no liquid remaining
3. Fill the rolls with this stuffing, cover with the hollowed-out top, pour a little sour cream over them, and let soften, turning frequently so they absorb the liquid evenly without falling apart
4. Beat a few eggs, dip the rolls in the beaten egg, coat with bread crumbs, and fry golden brown in fat
5. Garnish with fried green parsley when serving
