## Ingredients

- 7 decagrams butter
- 6 egg yolks
- lemon zest
- 4 deciliters cheese
- sugar
- sour cream
- 6 egg whites
- strudel dough

## Directions

1. Break 7 decagrams butter over coals
2. Add 6 egg yolks, lemon zest, 4 deciliters cheese, sugar, and sour cream
3. Finally add the foam from 6 egg whites
4. Pour the strudel dough with butter
5. Spread the filling on it, roll up the strudel and bake in the usual manner
