## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram veal
- bacon slices
- carrots
- cloves
- onion
- good brown stock
- half a bread roll (zsemle)
- 5 egg yolks
- glace (lész)
- lemon juice
- ring mold (abroncsforma)
- butter
- cooked veal sweetbread (borjúkedes)
- roasted veal
- crayfish tails
- asparagus or artichokes
- button mushrooms (csiperke-gomba)
- parsley

## Directions

1. Braise 1 kilogram veal with bacon slices, carrots, cloves, and onion, occasionally adding good stock, until nicely browned
2. Remove the meat and chop finely
3. Soak half a bread roll in brown stock, squeeze out, and add to meat
4. Pound the mixture with 5 egg yolks in a mortar until it becomes a paste
5. Place in a pot, add a little brown stock and lemon juice and a little glace, cook well until it resembles a purée
6. Pass through a sieve
7. Butter a ring mold and fill it with this mixture
8. Braise in a water bath for three-quarters of an hour
9. Meanwhile, cut cooked veal sweetbread and roasted veal into cubes, include crayfish tails, asparagus or artichokes in small pieces, some sliced button mushrooms, and finely chopped parsley
10. Braise this garnish mixture in butter and cook well
11. Turn out the brown ring mold onto a serving dish
12. Fill the center opening with the garnish mixture
13. Alternate crayfish tails, asparagus tips, and artichoke rosettes around the ring for service
