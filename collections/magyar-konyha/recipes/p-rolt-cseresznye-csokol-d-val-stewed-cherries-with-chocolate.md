## Ingredients

- Good broth
- Black cherries
- One and a half liter cherries
- Two deciliter sweet cream
- Finely grated fine chocolate

## Directions

1. Remove stems from black cherries and wash them
2. Put in a pot and stew until soft
3. Pour two deciliter sweet cream over the one and a half liter of cherries
4. Bring to a boil with the cherries
5. Add finely grated fine chocolate to a bowl
6. Cook the mixture until the chocolate is dissolved
7. Pour onto a plate
8. Arrange sponge cake pieces neatly around it
