## Ingredients

- fresh sweet woodruff (Asperula odorata) picked before flowering
- weak white wine
- sugar to taste
- one orange sliced into rings

## Directions

1. Let the fresh woodruff wilt in the shade for 5-6 hours
2. Clean it of thick stems
3. Put in a bowl and cover with weak white wine (seven deciliters per handful of herb)
4. Sweeten to taste
5. Add the orange sliced into rings
6. After standing for one hour, pour into glasses and serve with the orange slices
