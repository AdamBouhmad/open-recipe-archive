## Ingredients

- 6 hard-boiled eggs
- 1/2 bread roll, grated and soaked in milk
- butter the size of half an egg
- green parsley, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the hard-boiled eggs in half lengthwise
2. Finely crush the yolks in a mortar
3. Mix the grated, milk-soaked bread, butter, chopped parsley, crushed yolks, salt, and sour cream together well
4. Fill the egg whites with this mixture
5. Place small pieces of butter in a dish
6. Stand the eggs in the dish
7. Pour sour cream over the top and add a few pieces of butter
8. Bake in the oven until nicely browned
