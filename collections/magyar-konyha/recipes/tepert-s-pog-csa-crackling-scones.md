## Ingredients

- half kilogram fresh, finely chopped cracklings
- one kilogram flour
- two deciliters sour cream
- a few tablespoons milk
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Rub the finely chopped cracklings together with the flour well between your hands
2. Knead together with sour cream and a few tablespoons of milk so that the dough is rather firm than soft
3. Roll out the dough to one and a half finger thickness
4. Cut out with a scone cutter and place on a baking sheet
5. Brush with egg and bake until slightly browned
