## Ingredients

- fourteen dekagrams of well-washed rice
- seven decilitres of milk or good broth
- ten dekagrams of butter
- one whole egg
- five egg yolks
- thirty shrimp tails and claws
- two or three soft-cooked goose or turkey forcemeat
- one piece of cooked calf's head
- salt
- three egg whites
- white paper
- asparagus heads

## Directions

1. Cook fourteen dekagrams of well-washed rice in seven decilitres of milk or good broth; when cooked tender, let cool and place in ten dekagrams of butter beaten until frothy, then gradually mix in one whole egg and five egg yolks one after another.
2. Add the shelled tails and claws of thirty shrimp, as well as two or three portions of soft-cooked goose or turkey forcemeat and one piece of cooked calf's head cut into strips, a little salt, and finally gradually three egg whites beaten stiff.
3. Grease a pot with butter, place white paper on the bottom of the pot, cut to fit, brush with a little butter, arrange neatly with whole shrimp tails and asparagus heads cooked in salted water, place the rice in it and cook in a water bath for one hour, carefully turn out onto a serving dish, remove the paper, and bring immediately to the table.
