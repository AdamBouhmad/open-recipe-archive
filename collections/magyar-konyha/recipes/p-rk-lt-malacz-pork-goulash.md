## Ingredients

- suckling pig, cut into pieces
- smoked bacon, twenty-eight dekagrams, cut into cubes
- red onion, finely chopped
- paprika
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the suckling pig into nice pieces and wash it, then place in a dry pot so that the water drains from it. In the meantime, fry the diced bacon in a pot until crispy. Brown the finely chopped red onion in the rendered fat, and briefly do the same with the paprika so it takes on a nice color. Then add the meat, salt it, and stir frequently during cooking so it does not stick. Do not cover it, otherwise it will produce too much liquid and will not cook down completely to the fat sauce. Prepare pörkölt chicken, duck, turkey, etc. in the same manner.
