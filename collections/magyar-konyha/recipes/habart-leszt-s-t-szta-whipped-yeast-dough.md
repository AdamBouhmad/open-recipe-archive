## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams butter
- 2 deciliters fresh sour cream
- 5 egg yolks
- 14 dekagrams sour cream with dissolved yeast
- salt
- ground sugar
- half kilogram flour
- preserved fruit or berries for filling

## Directions

1. Beat the butter until foamy
2. Combine the sour cream, egg yolks, and yeast dissolved in sour cream and mix well
3. Strain through a sieve and add slowly to the beaten butter, one spoonful at a time
4. Never add more than one spoonful at a time and only after the previous one is well beaten in
5. Add a little salt and ground sugar
6. When all the mixture is combined, add half kilogram flour, beat well and prepare a stick dough
7. Spread the dough rolled to the thickness of a new hand on a butter-coated pastry leaf
8. Arrange with preserved fruit or berries and decorate with strips made from the remaining dough
9. Make a border around the edge of the leaf and bake at sustained heat
10. Before placing in the oven, it must rise like all yeast dough, then brush with egg white and sprinkle thickly with coarsely broken sugar and almonds
