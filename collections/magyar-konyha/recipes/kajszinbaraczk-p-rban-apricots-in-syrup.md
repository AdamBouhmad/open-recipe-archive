## Ingredients

- beautiful yellow apricots
- sugar
- water
- 500 grams fruit requires 25 dekagrams sugar and 1 glass water

## Directions

1. Peel beautiful golden apricots, split them, and remove the pits
2. Calculate 25 dekagrams sugar for 500 grams fruit, cook with 1 glass water and clarify until it forms heavy drops, then cool
3. Arrange the apricots neatly in the jar, fill almost to the top with sugar, bind well and simmer for 2-3 minutes (per instructions 961)
