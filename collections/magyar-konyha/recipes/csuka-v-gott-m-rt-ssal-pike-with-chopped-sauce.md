## Ingredients

- pike
- salted water
- capers
- two anchovies
- butter
- ground black pepper
- pea broth
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook a piece of pike in salted water, remove its bones and chop it finely together with capers and two anchovies.
2. Melt a piece of butter, add the chopped mixture together with a little ground black pepper and braise it well.
3. When braised, fill it up with clear pea broth and a little vinegar and bring it to a boil.
4. Clean and salt a whole pike, melt a piece of butter in a pan, place the fish in it and braise it until it is braised on all sides.
5. Pour the sauce with the chopped mixture over it and bring it to a slight boil.
