## Ingredients

- 42 dekagrams finest mocha coffee
- 1.4 liters very hot sweet cream
- 42 dekagrams finely crushed sugar
- 16 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Roast 42 dekagrams finest mocha coffee to a light brown.
2. Immediately pour it into 1.4 liters very hot sweet cream straight from the roaster, cover well, and allow to cool.
3. In the meantime, thoroughly blend 42 dekagrams finely crushed sugar with 16 egg yolks.
4. Slowly pour the strained coffee cream into the eggs.
5. Strain and beat again with a whisk until cooled.
6. Freeze in the mold to a butter-soft consistency.
