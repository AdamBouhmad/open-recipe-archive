## Ingredients

- ½ kilogram sugar
- ½ kilogram apples
- water as needed

## Directions

1. Peel and cut the apples into pieces and cook, adding only as much water as is needed for cooking
2. When cooked, press through a thin cloth
3. Boil the sugar until it threads and forms large bubbles while boiling
4. Add the strained apple juice to the sugar and bring to a boil for a few minutes
5. Cool and pour into glasses
