## Ingredients

- Melted butter, a good cooking spoon's worth
- Flour, a good cooking spoon's worth
- Red onion, finely chopped
- Seven deciliters of sour cream
- Seven deciliters of pea broth
- Salt
- Nutmeg, finely chopped
- Clove, finely chopped
- Toasted bread points

## Directions

1. In melted butter, lightly fry a good cooking spoon's worth of flour until yellowish
2. Add finely chopped red onion
3. Mix in seven deciliters of sour cream and seven deciliters of pea broth together and blend well
4. Boil for a short time, then season with salt, finely chopped nutmeg, and clove
5. Serve into toasted bread points, as with the previous soup
