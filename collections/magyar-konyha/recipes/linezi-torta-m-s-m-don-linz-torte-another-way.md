## Ingredients

- 24 dekagrams butter
- 26 dekagrams finely powdered sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- 14 dekagrams blanched finely chopped almonds
- 26 dekagrams fine flour
- a little chopped lemon zest
- jam for filling

## Directions

1. Beat butter until foamy
2. Add 26 dekagrams finely powdered sugar and stir for a quarter hour
3. Beat in 3 whole eggs, blanched finely chopped almonds, fine flour, and a little chopped lemon zest and mix well
4. Place half of the dough on a buttered sheet
5. From the other half, make small rods and arrange them nicely in a lattice pattern on top
6. Brush with egg wash and bake until nicely golden
