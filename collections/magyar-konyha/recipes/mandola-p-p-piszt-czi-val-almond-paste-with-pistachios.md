## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams butter, whipped until frothy
- 14 dekagrams finely ground almonds
- 10 egg yolks
- 7 dekagrams blanched pistachios
- 7 dekagrams citron, cut lengthwise
- 14 dekagrams finely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Add the finely ground almonds to the whipped butter
2. Mix in the egg yolks one after another, stirring for a whole hour
3. Cut the blanched pistachios and citron lengthwise into the same size
4. Add the pistachios and citron together with the finely ground sugar to the whipped paste
5. Stir until everything is well combined
6. Spread on a buttered pastry sheet
7. Bake in even heat until golden
