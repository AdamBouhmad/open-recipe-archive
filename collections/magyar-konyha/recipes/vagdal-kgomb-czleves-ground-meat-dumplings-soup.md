## Ingredients

- a pair of veal sweetbreads and udder
- a few button mushrooms
- butter
- 3 egg yolks
- milk
- nutmeg
- salt
- egg white
- bread crumbs
- white broken broth

## Directions

1. Blanch the veal sweetbreads and udder slightly, chop finely with a few button mushrooms
2. Melt a piece of butter and put in the chopped mixture
3. Beat 3 egg yolks into a little milk and whisk well
4. Pour over the mixture, add nutmeg and salt, mix well
5. Cut square wafers, brush with egg white, place half a spoon of the mixture on each
6. Form into round dumplings, roll in egg yolk then bread crumbs
7. Fry in hot fat
8. Place in the soup bowl and pour white broken broth over it
