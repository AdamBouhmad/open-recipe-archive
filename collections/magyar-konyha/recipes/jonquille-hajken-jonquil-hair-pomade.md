## Ingredients

- one kilogram mutton tallow
- seventy-five dekagrams vegetable oil
- thirteen dekagrams hair powder
- two dekagrams musk dye
- two dekagrams bergamot oil
- four grams thyme
- four grams orange blossom oil
- twelve grams amber dye

## Directions

1. Take one kilogram of mutton tallow with seventy-five dekagrams of vegetable oil and thirteen dekagrams of hair powder, mix them together and dye to a pale yellow color
2. Then add two dekagrams of musk dye, the same amount of bergamot oil, four grams of thyme, the same amount of orange blossom oil, and twelve grams of amber dye
