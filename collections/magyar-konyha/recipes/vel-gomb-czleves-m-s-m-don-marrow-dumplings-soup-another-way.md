## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 rolls
- milk
- 10 dekagrams bacon, finely chopped
- green parsley, finely chopped
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- fine bread crumbs
- bone marrow

## Directions

1. Soak 1 1/2 rolls in milk
2. Finely chop bacon and crush together with finely chopped green parsley
3. Mix in 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks
4. Season with salt
5. Squeeze out the bread well, chop finely, and mix with the other ingredients
6. Finally add enough fine bread crumbs so the mixture holds together
7. Clean the bone marrow designated for this well, rinse in water, and chop into hazelnut-sized pieces
8. Form small dumplings and place a piece of marrow in the center of each
9. Cook in hot broth until the marrow is cooked through
