## Ingredients

- a large capon
- salt
- yellow roux
- breadcrumbs
- anchovies
- fresh parsley
- lemon zest
- red onion
- two deciliters white wine
- fresh small clams

## Directions

1. Season the capon lightly with salt and prepare it
2. Roast until golden brown, but keeping it in a sauce
3. Prepare a yellow roux and toast breadcrumbs in it
4. Add finely chopped anchovies, fresh parsley, lemon zest, and red onion
5. Pour two deciliters of white wine over it
6. Cook together for a while
7. Rinse fresh small clams in wine
8. Add the roasted capon together with the clams
9. Cook together, transfer to a serving dish
10. Arrange the clams on top with the sauce
