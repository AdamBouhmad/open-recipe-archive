## Ingredients

- Twenty-eight decagrams of fresh lard, cut into small cubes
- One small head of red onion, finely chopped
- Paprika
- Salt
- One quarter of a lamb, cut into beautiful pieces and rinsed

## Directions

1. Melt the lard cubes in a pot until the cracklings are beautifully yellow
2. Sauté the finely chopped red onion and paprika
3. Add salt and the lamb pieces cut into beautiful pieces and rinsed, to the lard mixture
4. Cover and let cook; there is no need to add broth because the meat will create its own liquid under the cover
