## Ingredients

- small bunch of asparagus
- twenty to thirty crayfish
- salted water
- half a bread roll
- milk
- piece of veal roast
- four dekagrams of butter
- one whole egg
- three egg yolks
- salt
- stiffly beaten egg whites from three eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the small bunch of asparagus and twenty to thirty crayfish in salted water
2. Soak half a bread roll in milk and squeeze it out
3. Chop this together with a piece of veal roast into small pieces
4. Whip four dekagrams of butter and mix in one whole egg and three egg yolks, stirring for fifteen minutes
5. Add a little salt and fold in stiffly beaten egg whites from three eggs
6. Fill buttered dumplings with this mixture
7. Place in steam for half an hour
8. Turn out into a soup bowl and pour good brown broth over it
