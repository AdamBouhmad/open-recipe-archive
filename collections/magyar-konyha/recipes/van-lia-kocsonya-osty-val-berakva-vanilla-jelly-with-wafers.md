## Ingredients

- vanilla jelly (from previous recipe)
- small wafers
- 6-7 wafer sheet slices

## Directions

1. Pour 2 centimeters height of vanilla jelly into a small round mold
2. Place in cracked ice and freeze
3. Meanwhile, bake small wafers and 6-7 slices from wafer sheet batter
4. Place the pot with remaining jelly on ice and semi-freeze
5. When the jelly in the mold has frozen, place a round wafer sheet on it
6. Spread a spoonful of semi-frozen jelly on it
7. Continue this way until all mixture is filled in
8. Freeze firmly, turn out onto a plate, and arrange in 3 rows with small wafers
