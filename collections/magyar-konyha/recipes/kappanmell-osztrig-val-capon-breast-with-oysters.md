## Ingredients

- capon or young turkey breast
- oysters
- butter
- lemon zest
- lemon juice
- fine breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the capon or young turkey breast into small slices
2. Extract the oysters and place meat and oysters alternately in a buttered pot
3. Add finely chopped lemon zest and the juice of one or two lemons
4. Sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs and add a little butter
5. Place coals on the lid and braise slowly until very little sauce remains
