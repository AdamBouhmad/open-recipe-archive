## Ingredients

- beautiful, large, ripe quinces
- water
- sugar, same amount of quince pulp as sugar, half kilogram
- cold water

## Directions

1. Bake or cook beautiful large ripe quinces in water until soft, then press through a fine sieve, and cook the sugar, which you have dipped in cold water, until it threads.
2. For half a kilogram of sugar use the same amount of quince pulp; cook this with one glass of water until it threads, then add the pressed quince, cook for half an hour, then cool and pour into glasses.
