## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters hot sour cream
- 10 dekagrams hot roasted coffee
- 6 spoonfuls fine flour
- 7 egg yolks
- sugar
- butter for greasing
- beaten egg
- breadcrumbs
- hot lard for frying

## Directions

1. Pour the hot roasted coffee into the hot sour cream, cover and let stand for half an hour, then strain and cool
2. Mix finely with 6 spoonfuls of fine flour and 7 egg yolks
3. While continuously stirring cook it to a paste and add enough sugar to sweeten it well
4. When well cooked, remove from heat and cool
5. Spread the paste onto two large torte leaves greased with butter to a thickness of two knife-widths and cool completely
6. Place the torte leaves on the cooktop for a few minutes so the butter melts
7. Turn the paste out onto a floured board and cut out with medium-sized pastry cutters
8. Dip in beaten egg, coat with breadcrumbs and fry in hot lard until golden
9. Coat thickly with sugar and press with a hot spatula
10. Can be prepared the same way with chocolate, cooking 5 bars of chocolate in 7 deciliters of sour cream and treating it the same as the coffee pastry
