## Ingredients

- young kohlrabi
- salted water
- roasted or braised meat
- fresh parsley
- red onion
- goose fat
- flour
- veal
- butter sauce
- breaded chicken or goose liver

## Directions

1. Cook the young kohlrabi in salted water until half-softened
2. Cut off a small lid from the top and hollow it out well
3. Finely chop roasted or braised meat, fresh parsley, and a piece of red onion
4. Melt a small spoonful of goose fat, brown a tablespoon of flour in it
5. Add the chopped parsley and red onion, brown them slightly
6. Add the chopped veal meat and mix well
7. Pour a little broth over it and cook well
8. Fill the hollowed-out kohlrabi with this filling
9. Place it in the prepared butter sauce
10. Shake it frequently and let it cook for a quarter hour
11. Arrange beautifully on a plate and serve with breaded chicken or goose liver
