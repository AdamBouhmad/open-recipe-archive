## Ingredients

- 16 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- 10 dekagrams fine flour
- 7 egg whites, beaten stiff
- butter for coating the sheet pan
- coarsely broken sugar for sprinkling
- any milk-based glaze

## Directions

1. Mix the finely ground sugar and fine flour well together
2. Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
3. Spread the mixture onto a buttered sheet pan with a wide knife to paper-thin thickness
4. Place in a moderately warm oven for a few moments until a thin layer forms on top
5. Sprinkle evenly with coarsely broken sugar
6. Return to the oven until it becomes a light golden yellow
7. Cut into strips as long as a hand-span and as wide as a finger with a sharp knife
8. Roll quickly onto a cylindrical form of finger thickness to resemble wafer shavings
9. Heap high on a serving plate and serve with any milk-based glaze
