## Ingredients

- sour cabbage, three handfuls
- fat, one good spoonful
- rice groats, twenty-eight dekagrams
- water
- salt
- spices
- beef or pork, half kilogram
- sour cream, seven decilitres

## Directions

1. Place the sour cabbage in fat and cover it so it steams, but stir it frequently so it does not stick. In another pot, place the rice groats in fat and dilute with a little water, braise until it bursts. By the time the cabbage is soft and the rice is cooked, half a kilogram of either beef or pork should be cooked. Strain its broth (which can be used for other purposes) and chop the meat finely, keeping its salt and spices to make it tasty. Add salt and spices to the rice as well. Now prepare all three components together—the rice, cabbage, and meat—along with seven decilitres of good sour cream.
