## Ingredients

- kidney
- salt
- bacon
- red onion
- carrot
- clove pepper
- flour
- vinegar
- sour cream
- anchovies
- capers
- lemon zest
- hazelnuts

## Directions

1. Clean the meaty part of the kidney well from skin and fat
2. Wash it and cut it into small pieces
3. Salt it and shape round slices with a knife
4. Place bacon, red onion, and carrot cut into slices in a pot and line the bottom of the pot with them
5. Place the slices in the pot with several clove peppers and braise covered until they become a nice light brown color
6. Remove them and sprinkle a spoonful of flour on top
7. Add two spoonfuls of vinegar and the same amount of sour cream
8. Cook well and strain through a sieve
9. Soak two anchovies in water and remove their bones
10. Chop two decagrams of capers, a small head of red onion, and a hazelnut-sized piece of red onion finely together
11. Add this to the sauce, place the slices back in, and cook well
