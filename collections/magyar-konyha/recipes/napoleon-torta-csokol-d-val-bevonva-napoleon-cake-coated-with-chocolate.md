## Ingredients

- 28 dekagramm chocolate, softened in a warm oven
- butter
- 28 dekagramm almonds, shelled, broken, and sifted
- 28 dekagramm sugar, broken with vanilla and sifted
- 16 egg yolks
- egg white foam
- 14 dekagramm cornstarch
- apricot marmalade
- chocolate glaze
- various preserved fruits

## Directions

1. Soften 28 dekagramm chocolate in a warm oven and blend finely in a bowl with a piece of butter.
2. Add another piece of butter and blend smoothly, then continue alternating butter additions until 28 dekagramm of butter is fully incorporated.
3. Slowly add 28 dekagramm of shelled, broken, and sifted almonds, the same amount of sugar broken with vanilla and sifted, and 16 egg yolks.
4. Stir this mixture foamily for half an hour.
5. Finally fold in the stiffly beaten egg white foam and 14 dekagramm of cornstarch.
6. Prepare four equal, low-rimmed cake layers, butter them, dust with flour, fill with the mixture, and bake in a gently heated oven.
7. Once fully baked, turn out onto a sieve and cool.
8. Place on parchment paper and spread apricot marmalade to the thickness of a knife blade.
9. Stack the layers on top of each other and trim neatly.
10. Coat the entire cake evenly with chocolate glaze and set aside to dry.
11. Once completely dry, decorate with various preserved fruits.
