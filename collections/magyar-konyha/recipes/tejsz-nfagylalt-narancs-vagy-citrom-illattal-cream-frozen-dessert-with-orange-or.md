## Ingredients

- 1 orange or 1 large lemon, peel and pith
- 1.4 liters strong boiling sweet cream
- eggs and sugar as in previous recipes

## Directions

1. Peel 1 orange or 1 large lemon and carefully remove all the white inner pith from the colored peel so that no white pith remains.
2. Place the orange or lemon peel in 1.4 liters of strong boiling sweet cream, cover the pot, and allow to cool.
3. Follow the same preparation method as the previous cream frozen desserts.
