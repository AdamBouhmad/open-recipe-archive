## Ingredients

- partridge
- salt
- thin bacon slices
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the partridge
2. Salt
3. Cut palm-sized thin bacon slices
4. Fold the wings and feet of the partridges neatly and prepare
5. Baste frequently with butter while roasting
