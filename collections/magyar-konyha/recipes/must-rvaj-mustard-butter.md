## Ingredients

- several boiled, salted and peppered egg yolks
- butter in appropriate quantity
- several spoonfuls of good quality mustard

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with butter until foamy
2. Finally mix in several spoonfuls of good quality mustard
