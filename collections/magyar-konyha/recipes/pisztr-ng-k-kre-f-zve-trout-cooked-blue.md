## Ingredients

- trout
- salt
- red onion
- bay leaves
- lemon zest
- whole pepper
- white wine vinegar
- water
- oil

## Directions

1. Do not remove the trout from the water until you want to use it
2. Strike it on the head, open it and remove the insides, quickly rinse it so the blood comes out and sprinkle it well with good white wine vinegar
3. Then in a pot of equal parts water and white wine vinegar add salt, red onion, bay leaves, lemon zest and several whole pepper corns and bring to a boil
4. Place the trout in it and bring to a boil; then remove from heat and pour fresh vinegar over it again, cover the trout and serve cold or hot as desired
5. If serving cold, cool it in its broth and serve with vinegar and oil
