## Ingredients

- two heads red onion
- one coffee spoon crushed sugar
- rendered fat
- strong good vinegar
- stock

## Directions

1. Coarsely chop two heads of red onion and put with one coffee spoon of crushed sugar into a small amount of melted fat
2. Brown it nicely
3. Thin with stock
4. Add one tablespoon of strong good vinegar
5. Cook well and pass through a sieve
