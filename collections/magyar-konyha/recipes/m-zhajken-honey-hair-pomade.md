## Ingredients

- twenty-eight dekagrams honey
- seven dekagrams pork fat
- fourteen dekagrams Peruvian balsam
- seventy-five centigrams cedarwood oil
- seventy-five centigrams nutmeg oil
- four and one-half dekagrams musk

## Directions

1. Melt together twenty-eight dekagrams of honey, seven dekagrams of pork fat, and fourteen dekagrams of Peruvian balsam
2. During cooling, mix in seventy-five centigrams of cedarwood oil, the same amount of nutmeg oil, and four and one-half dekagrams of musk
