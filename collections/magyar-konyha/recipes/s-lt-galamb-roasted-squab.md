## Ingredients

- young fat pigeons
- butter
- breadcrumb crusts
- milk
- fresh parsley
- salt
- eggs

## Directions

1. Kill young fat pigeons, pluck with feathers, scald and clean
2. Salt and let rest for half an hour
3. For two pigeons, prepare stuffing as follows: beat butter until foamy
4. Grate one and a half breadcrumb crusts and soak in milk, squeeze well
5. Add finely chopped fresh parsley and a little salt
6. Mix well together
7. Beat two eggs and one egg yolk and add
8. Stir for a while longer
9. Fill the pigeons with the stuffing using a funnel, tie and roast for half an hour
10. Baste with butter during roasting
