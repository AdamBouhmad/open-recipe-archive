## Ingredients

- 24 dekagramm cooked floury potatoes, grated
- 14 dekagramm finely ground sugar
- a little vanilla
- 10 dekagramm softened and finely ground almonds
- zest of 1 orange, finely chopped
- 4 whole eggs
- 8 egg yolks
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Cook and cool the best kind of floury potatoes and grate 24 dekagramm of them
2. Mix in a bowl with finely ground sugar, a little vanilla, softened and finely ground almonds, and finely chopped orange zest
3. Beat in 4 whole eggs and 8 egg yolks one after another, stirring continuously for half an hour until the mixture grows and becomes foamy
4. Meanwhile grease three equal cake sheets with the hoops together with butter
5. Fill the mixture in three equal parts and bake to a nice light brown
6. Remove from the sheet, place on a sieve and cool
7. Fill two sheets with preserves of your choice, stack them evenly and decorate the top sheet as desired
8. Note: this cake must be prepared on the day it will be used, as it dries quickly
