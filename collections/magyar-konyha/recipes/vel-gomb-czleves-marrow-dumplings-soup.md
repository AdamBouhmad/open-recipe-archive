## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams bone marrow
- 2 rolls
- milk
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- chicken broth

## Directions

1. Clean the bone marrow and cut one-third into cubes and the rest into slices
2. Place in a bowl to melt and beat slightly until foamy
3. Add 2 grated rolls soaked in milk, 2 whole eggs and 2 egg yolks one after another
4. Add the marrow cubes as well
5. Mix everything together well
6. Form small dumplings and cook them in chicken broth
7. Once cooked through, serve
