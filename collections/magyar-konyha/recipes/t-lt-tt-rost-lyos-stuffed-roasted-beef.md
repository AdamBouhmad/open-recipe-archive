## Ingredients

- roasted beef
- salt
- green parsley
- red onion
- lemon zest
- sour cream
- bacon
- thyme
- bay leaves
- peppercorns
- clove pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Remove the fat and bones from the roasted beef, beat it well with the flat of a knife, and salt it
2. Chop one slice of roasted beef finely and mix it with green parsley, red onion, and lemon zest, and salt it
3. Mix this filling with sour cream so that it can be spread well
4. Spread this filling on a piece of roasted beef to the thickness of a knife spine
5. Place another piece of roasted beef on top, fasten it in four places, and do the same with the rest
6. Place a pot with a few slices of bacon, red onion, thyme, and bay leaves and line the bottom
7. Place the roasted beef pieces in the pot with several peppercorns and clove pepper, braise until tender, and serve
8. Cook the sauce until golden, sprinkle a spoonful of flour on top, brown it, add a spoonful of liquid and several spoonfuls of sour cream
9. Strain it over the roasted beef and cook once more
