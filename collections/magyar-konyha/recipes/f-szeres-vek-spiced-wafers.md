## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams almond with skin, finely ground
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- good amount of cinnamon
- cloves
- lemon zest, finely chopped
- egg white
- wafer sheets
- butter
- icing

## Directions

1. Mix in a bowl: 14 dekagrams of almond with skin finely ground, the same amount of sugar, good amount of cinnamon and cloves, finely chopped lemon zest, beaten foamy with egg white (the batter should not be as thin as common beaten batter)
2. Spread on wafer sheets two fingers wide and a span long, to knife-thickness
3. Place on a buttered pan
4. Bake at gentle heat, or rather dry it
5. When completely hardened, spread part of stiffly beaten icing through a cone with a pinhead opening or paper cone in a nice pattern on the wafers
6. Mix the other part with a little food coloring and decorate the wafers nicely with it
7. Return to the oven where it dries completely
