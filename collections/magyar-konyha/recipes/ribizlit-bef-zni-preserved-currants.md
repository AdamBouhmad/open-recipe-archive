## Ingredients

- currants
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Remove beautiful ripe currants from the stem.
2. For 500 grams of sugar, boil with one glass of water until it throws large bubbles.
3. Take the same amount of stemmed currants and treat them as you would raspberries.
