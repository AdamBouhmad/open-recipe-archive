## Ingredients

- two or three tart apples
- butter
- flour
- meat broth
- lemon zest
- lemon juice
- sugar
- fresh cream
- Liebig meat extract (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the tart apples and cut them into thin slices
2. Braise until soft in a little butter
3. Sprinkle a spoon of flour into the apples and brown it
4. Pour a spoonful of broth over it
5. Add the zest of half a lemon cut lengthwise, a little lemon juice, and enough sugar to make it sweet enough
6. Finally add two spoons of fresh cream, bring to a light boil and the sauce is ready
7. A teaspoon of Liebig meat extract gives an excellent flavor to this and other meat sauces
