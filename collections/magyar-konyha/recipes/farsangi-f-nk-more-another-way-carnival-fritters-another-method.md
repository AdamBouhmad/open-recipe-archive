## Ingredients

- 90 grams butter, creamed
- 6 egg yolks
- a pinch of salt
- 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
- 2 deciliters warm sour cream
- 13.5 grams fresh baker's yeast dissolved in the sour cream
- flour as needed for a light dough

## Directions

1. Mix the creamed butter with egg yolks one by one, then add salt, sugar, and the warm sour cream in which the yeast has been dissolved
2. Add flour until a light dough forms
3. Stir until the dough separates from the spoon, then beat slightly
4. Let rise in a warm place
5. Once risen, place on a flour-dusted board and roll out to the thickness of a little finger
6. Cut out fritters and once they rise again, fry in hot fat
