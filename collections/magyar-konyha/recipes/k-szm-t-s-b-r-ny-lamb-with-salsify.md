## Ingredients

- one quarter lamb from the front
- various vegetables
- salt
- water
- flour
- sour cream
- salsify
- sugar
- parsley

## Directions

1. Put one quarter lamb with various vegetables in two liters of salted water to cook
2. Use vegetables such as those used in regular soup
3. While the lamb is cooking, prepare a mixture of one spoonful of flour and two deciliters of sour cream in a deep bowl
4. When the lamb is tender, slowly pour its broth into the mixture while continuously stirring
5. Place the meat in the sour cream broth and add one liter of nicely cleaned salsify, two spoonfuls of sugar and one teaspoonful of finely chopped parsley
6. Bring to a boil again
7. When the salsify is tender, remove from heat until serving
8. Serve in deep bowls
