## Ingredients

- 12 dekagrams rice
- 7 deciliters cream
- 7 dekagrams finely ground almonds
- sugar to taste
- flour for dusting
- canned apricots for filling
- egg white
- beaten egg
- fine breadcrumbs
- fat for frying
- grated chocolate or sugar for coating

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in cream until soft
2. Add the finely ground almonds and sugar to taste
3. When the rice is soft, let it cool slightly
4. Place it on a board dusted with flour and flatten into triangular pieces with a knife
5. Fill each piece with canned apricot
6. Brush the edges with egg white and roll into a small sugar-glass shape
7. Dip in beaten egg, then in fine breadcrumbs
8. Fry in hot fat
9. When removed from fat, dip in grated chocolate or sugar
10. Heap high on the serving dish
