## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- 12 egg whites
- 56 dekagrams sugar
- 14 dekagrams blanched almonds, finely chopped
- 1 lemon, zest finely chopped
- 56 dekagrams flour

## Directions

1. Place egg yolks in a bowl, whip the egg whites into stiff peaks
2. Finely crush the sugar and beat it together with the peaks and egg yolks using a whisk for a good hour until thick
3. In a pan, toast the blanched and finely chopped almonds until golden
4. Once cooled, mix into the beaten mixture
5. Add the finely chopped lemon zest and flour and mix everything well
6. Fill the dough into a buttered twice-baked bread form and bake slowly
7. Once baked and cooled, either slice like twice-baked bread or cut into small pieces
