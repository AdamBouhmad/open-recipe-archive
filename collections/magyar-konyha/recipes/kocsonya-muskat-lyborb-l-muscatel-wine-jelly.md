## Ingredients

- twenty-eight dekagrams sugar
- two deciliters water
- one-half liter muscatel wine
- juice of two lemons
- four and one-half dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Cook twenty-eight dekagrams of sugar with two deciliters of water until it forms large drops
2. Pour this into one-half liter of muscatel wine
3. Squeeze in the juice of two lemons
4. Add four and one-half dekagrams of dissolved isinglass
5. Strain through clean cloth as with other jellies
6. Pour into a mold, place on ice, and freeze
7. Prepare similarly from tokay or malaga wine, as well as from various liqueurs, especially good pineapple liqueur
