## Ingredients

- Two eggs
- Fine noodle dough
- One liter hot milk
- Five dekagrams butter
- Four deciliters cream
- Four egg yolks
- Sugar
- Vanilla

## Directions

1. Make fine noodle dough from two eggs and roll it out
2. Cut wide noodles from it and cook in one liter hot milk
3. When sufficiently cooked, remove from heat and beat in five dekagrams butter
4. Place in a baking dish lined with butter
5. Pour four deciliters cream mixed with four egg yolks over it
6. Place in oven and bake until light golden yellow
7. Sprinkle well with sugar and vanilla before serving
