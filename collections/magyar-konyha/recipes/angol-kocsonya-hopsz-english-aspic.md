## Ingredients

- forty-two dekagrams sugar
- four oranges
- four lemons
- fourteen egg yolks
- two deciliters French champagne
- eight egg whites

## Directions

1. Rub one half of forty-two dekagrams sugar on an orange and the other half on a lemon
2. Squeeze out the juice from four oranges and four lemons
3. Strain the juice through cloth
4. Crush the sugar and place in a pot
5. Beat together with fourteen egg yolks, the squeezed juice, and two deciliters French champagne
6. Place on charcoal fire and stir continuously until it becomes a thick mass
7. In the meantime, beat eight egg whites into stiff snow
8. Remove the pot from the coals
