## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- butter sauce
- 4 deciliters green peas
- butter
- parsley
- crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Blanch 2 chickens and cut into four pieces
2. Prepare a small amount of butter sauce
3. Place the chicken pieces in the sauce
4. Braise 4 deciliters green peas with butter and parsley
5. When cooked (text cuts off)
