## Ingredients

- 3/4 kilogram flour
- 1/2 kilogram fat
- salt
- water
- 3/4 kilogram cow cheese, crumbled
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Mix 3/4 kilogram flour with 1/2 kilogram fat by rubbing together
2. Mix with salty water to make a soft dough
3. Suddenly knead in the same amount of crumbled cow cheese as there was flour, so that the cheese remains in pieces
4. Form into the size of an egg and make long scones from it
5. Cut, brush with egg, bake until nicely golden, and serve hot
