## Ingredients

- 4 veal ears
- 100 grams beef marrow fat
- same amount flour
- 3 liters water
- red onion
- spices
- 20 grams salt
- 10 grams pepper
- white bread crumb
- hot fat
- veal tongue
- venison mushroom
- goose liver
- rooster comb
- other mushrooms
- 2/10 liter venison mushroom broth
- same amount mushroom broth
- poultry essence
- 1 liter brown sauce
- stuffed crayfish
- piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Blanch 4 veal ears in rapidly boiling water for 20 minutes.
2. Plunge in cold water, drain and clean carefully.
3. Prepare the broth: melt 100 grams beef marrow fat, mix in the same amount flour, pour 3 liters water over it.
4. Add pieces of red onion, few spices, 20 grams salt and 10 grams pepper.
5. Bring to boil once, stir with spoon, place cleaned veal ears in it and cook gently in covered pan until tender.
6. Remove and drain the moisture, dry carefully.
7. Cut a rectangular cone from white bread crumb 8 centimetres high with base 5 square centimetres and top 3 square centimetres.
8. Fry golden brown in hot fat and place in the center of the plate.
9. Arrange the veal ears on the four sides of it.
10. Prepare ragout from veal tongue, venison mushroom, goose liver, rooster comb and other mushroom.
11. Mix with the following sauce: in a pan put 2/10 liter venison mushroom broth, the same amount mushroom broth and poultry essence, add 1 liter brown sauce and reduce to half thickness.
12. Mix the ragout with this sauce and decorate the arranged veal ears with it and stuffed crayfish, surrounding with piquant sauce.
