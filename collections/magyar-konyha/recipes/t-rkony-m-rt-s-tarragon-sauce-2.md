## Ingredients

- tarragon, leafed off and finely chopped, well washed
- hot meat stock
- one deciliter sour cream
- flour
- lemon juice or wine vinegar (optional)
- salt

## Directions

1. Pour hot meat stock over the leafed off, finely chopped, well washed tarragon
2. Add one deciliter of sour cream mixed with a little flour
3. If not sour enough, supplement with one coffee spoon of lemon juice or wine vinegar
4. Cook to half-thick consistency, season with salt and serve
