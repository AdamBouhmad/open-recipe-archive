## Ingredients

- pea shoots
- young chicken or squab
- thin red roux
- fresh parsley
- ground black pepper
- paprika
- salted water
- sweet cream
- saffron
- nutmeg blossom
- fried bread cubes

## Directions

1. Roughly chop the pea shoots
2. Prepare a thin red roux
3. Cook the chicken or squab in salted water until done
4. Braise the chopped pea shoots in the roux for a few minutes
5. Dilute the roux with the broth from the chicken
6. Add the cooked meat and simmer for fifteen minutes
7. Before serving, pour in a glass of sweet cream
8. Pour into a serving bowl and sprinkle with saffron, nutmeg blossom, and fried bread cubes
