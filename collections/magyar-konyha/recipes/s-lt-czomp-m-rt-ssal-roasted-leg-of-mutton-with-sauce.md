## Ingredients

- large leg of mutton
- salt
- crushed pepper
- melted butter
- sour cream (two deciliters)
- lemon juice
- lemon rind, finely chopped
- capers, finely chopped
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the large leg of mutton and cut it lengthwise, but not all the way through, so it remains whole
2. Season it with salt mixed with crushed pepper
3. Coat it with melted butter and roast it on a grate until fully cooked
4. When roasted through, place it on a plate, add a few pieces of butter on top
5. Pour two deciliters of sour cream over it and add the juice of one lemon
6. Add finely chopped lemon rind, a little capers, a few handfuls of breadcrumbs and a few pieces of butter
7. Place coals on top, bring it to a boil and serve
