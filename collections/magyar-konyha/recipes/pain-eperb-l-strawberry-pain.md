## Ingredients

- 56 decagrams fine garden or wild strawberries, sifted
- 42 decagrams sugar
- 2 deciliters water
- 6 decagrams isinglass or equivalent gelatin
- 1-2 lemons, juice

## Directions

1. Chill a smooth cylindrical mold on ice
2. Add a few spoonfuls of well-strained and slightly thickened wine gelée and rotate the mold on all sides until covered with a layer of gelée
3. Place the mold back on ice and decorate with raw or preserved fruit as desired
4. In a porcelain bowl, combine the strained strawberry purée with the sugar syrup (made by cooking sugar in 2 deciliters of water to a not very thick syrup), the isinglass or gelatin, and lemon juice
5. Stir this mixture on ice in the porcelain bowl until it becomes thick
6. Fill the prepared mold with this mass and freeze
7. A test is necessary here as well; if the gelée becomes too stiff, add a little water
