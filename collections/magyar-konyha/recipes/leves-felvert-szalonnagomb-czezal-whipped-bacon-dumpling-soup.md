## Ingredients

- four dry bread rolls
- four egg yolks
- two deciliters of condensed milk
- twenty-one dekagrams of fresh bacon
- two whole eggs
- two egg yolks
- small handful of flour
- white meat broth

## Directions

1. Scrape the crust from four dry bread rolls and cut into small cubes
2. Mix four egg yolks with two deciliters of condensed milk and pour over the bread
3. Finely chop twenty-one dekagrams of fresh bacon with a kitchen knife and place in a bowl
4. Beat it with two whole eggs and two egg yolks until well foamed
5. Slowly fold in the soaked bread cubes
6. Add a small handful of flour, only as much as so they do not fall apart in the soup
7. Form dumplings from the mixture and place in good white meat broth
