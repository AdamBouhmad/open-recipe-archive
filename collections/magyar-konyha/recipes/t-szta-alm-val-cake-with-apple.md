## Ingredients

- fifteen masanszki apples or eight larger apples of a different kind
- sugar
- seven dekagrams softened almond
- fourteen dekagrams sugar
- seven dekagrams citronade
- half a roll
- four egg yolks
- four egg whites
- butter
- fine breadcrumbs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel fifteen masanszki or eight larger apples of a non-sweet kind, chop finely and stew gently in sugar so they soften a little
2. Crush seven dekagrams softened almond and fourteen dekagrams sugar, chop seven dekagrams citronade finely and grate half a roll, mix all this well with four egg yolks and mix in the stiff peaks of four egg whites
3. Pour the mixture into a butter-greased, fine breadcrumb-sprinkled pan, place in the oven and bake for three-quarters of an hour
4. Turn out onto a plate and serve sprinkled with sugar
