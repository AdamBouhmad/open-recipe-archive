## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams sugar for sponge cake dough
- butter for greasing
- sugar for dusting
- 7 deciliters good cream

## Directions

1. Prepare sponge cake dough from 14 dekagrams of sugar, spread it smoothly on a well-buttered sheet with a wide knife to the thickness of a knife blade
2. Dust with sugar and bake at gentle heat until light bread-yellow
3. Quickly cut long strips with a sharp knife, each as wide as the palm and as long as a span
4. Place the sheet back in the oven for a few moments, then pull it out without removing it completely, so the dough does not harden and become brittle; therefore the sheet must always be kept warm
5. Peel off the cut strips one by one with the knife and quickly bend them over a small wooden roller such as those found in toy shops, so that the two ends of the dough come together
6. Hold it together in your hand until the dough has hardened, then carefully remove it so it does not break
7. Place it on a sieve in a warm place so it does not soften, and continue until all are bent
8. Good cream will be prepared separately
