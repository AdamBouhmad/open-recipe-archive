## Ingredients

- beef roast (rib cut)
- fat
- fresh parsley
- onion
- lemon zest
- whole black pepper
- vinegar
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Beat a nice rib cut of beef, wash and salt it
2. Put fat, green parsley, red onion, lemon zest and whole pepper in a pot, add the roast and braise slowly, pouring in a little broth
3. When the meat has softened, pour vinegar and sour cream over it
