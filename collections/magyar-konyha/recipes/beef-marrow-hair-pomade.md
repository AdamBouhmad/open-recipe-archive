## Ingredients

- 4 dekagrams beef marrow
- fresh water
- equal amount unsalted lard
- 4 dekagrams walnut oil
- pair of ox feet
- wine
- 14 dekagrams mutton tallow
- 28 dekagrams clarified lard
- fragrant oils
- 28 dekagrams beef marrow
- 4.5 dekagrams white wax
- 4.5 dekagrams walnut oil
- 10 dekagrams freshly pressed apple juice
- 4 grams nutmeg oil

## Directions

1. Boil 4 dekagrams beef marrow daily in fresh water
2. Mix with equal amount unsalted lard and melt together
3. Beat with foaming rod until mixture cools almost completely
4. Mix in 4 dekagrams walnut oil
5. Alternative method: chop a pair of ox feet, boil in water for four hours, cool, add small amount of wine, 14 dekagrams mutton tallow, and 28 dekagrams clarified lard, melt over gentle heat, add fragrant oils
6. Or: melt 28 dekagrams beef marrow at moderate heat with 4.5 dekagrams white wax and 4.5 dekagrams walnut oil, mix in by stirring 10 dekagrams freshly pressed apple juice and 4 grams nutmeg oil
