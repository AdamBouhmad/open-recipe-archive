## Ingredients

- one cleaned turkey
- one egg-sized amount of lard
- one head of red onion, finely chopped
- one tablespoon of paprika
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned turkey into small pieces.
2. In a pot, melt the lard; when hot, add the finely chopped red onion and paprika.
3. Add the turkey meat to the paprika-lard mixture and salt it.
4. Cover and braise, stirring often, for one to two hours (depending on whether the turkey is young or old), until it browns.
5. Add small amounts of water from time to time to prevent the meat from drying out and browning too much.
6. Serve in a long dish and border with rice mixed with the turkey's finely chopped liver and giblets.
