## Ingredients

- finely peeled zest of two oranges
- half a kilogram of sugar cut into pieces
- two bottles of good but light wine
- juice of five oranges
- one bottle of champagne chilled on ice

## Directions

1. Put the orange zest in a bowl
2. Add the cut sugar
3. Pour the wine over it
4. Squeeze in the orange juice
5. Let stand for a few hours
6. Strain and add the chilled champagne
7. Fill glasses and serve very cold
