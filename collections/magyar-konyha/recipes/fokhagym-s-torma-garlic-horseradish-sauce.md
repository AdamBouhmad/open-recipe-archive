## Ingredients

- bread roll, sliced
- garlic cloves
- fatty meat stock
- grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Cook bread sliced into pieces with several cloves of garlic in fatty meat stock until it becomes fine enough to smooth
2. Then mix with grated horseradish and serve
