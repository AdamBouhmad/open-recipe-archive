## Ingredients

- strudel dough
- thirty-five ripe beautiful plum stones
- a little cinnamon
- lemon zest
- crumbly kifli
- melted butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare strudel dough, then cut thirty-five ripe beautiful plum stones into thin rings and crush a little cinnamon, chop lemon zest finely and grate crumbly kifli
2. Stretch the dough finely, brush with melted butter, sprinkle the plums and the other ingredients on it, fold it gently together and, having sprinkled with butter, place in a butter-greased pan
3. When baked, serve to the table sprinkled with sugar
