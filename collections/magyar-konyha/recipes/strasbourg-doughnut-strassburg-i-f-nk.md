## Ingredients

- twenty-eight dekagrams of good firm butter
- eight egg yolks
- five dekagrams of cheese yeast soaked and mixed in sour cream
- a coffee cup of sour cream
- seven dekagrams of sugar crushed on lemon zest
- salt

## Directions

1. Place twenty-eight dekagrams of good firm butter, eight egg yolks, five dekagrams of cheese yeast soaked and mixed in sour cream, a coffee cup of sour cream, seven dekagrams of sugar crushed on lemon zest, and salt into a bowl
2. Beat this with a cooking spoon until the mixture is well combined and resembles a paste
3. Note that this dough should not be stirred, only beaten with a spoon until well combined
4. Once done, add enough flour so the dough resembles sweet bread dough
5. The flour should be beaten in and not stirred in
6. Once the dough is finely worked, let it rise slightly
7. Finally, place on a floured board, cut into egg-sized pieces, form into round rolls, place on buttered paper
8. Sprinkle with puffed, coarsely chopped almonds, sugar, and vanilla
9. Bake at a gentle heat
