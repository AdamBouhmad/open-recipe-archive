## Ingredients

- Fresh pork and pig's feet, as much as needed, cut into pieces
- Salt
- Red onion, one large onion per pig's foot piece, cut into cubes
- Black pepper
- Paprika, a small amount
- One tablespoon powdered sugar
- Vinegar
- Saffron, a good amount

## Directions

1. Cook the salted pig's feet pieces in water until soft
2. When the pig's feet are cooked soft, add the diced red onion and cook together
3. When the onion is also soft, prepare a beautiful red thin roux
4. Add black pepper, a small amount of paprika, and one tablespoon powdered sugar to the roux
5. Dilute the roux with the pig's feet cooking liquid
6. Pour in vinegar and add good saffron so that the broth becomes beautifully yellow
