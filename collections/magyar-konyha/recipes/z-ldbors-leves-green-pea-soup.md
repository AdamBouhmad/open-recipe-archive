## Ingredients

- tender green peas in their pods
- butter
- fried bread slices
- 2 or 3 fried pike heads
- salt
- clear pea broth

## Directions

1. Braise tender green peas in their pods in butter until tender
2. Crush them together with fried bread slices and fried pike heads in a mortar
3. Place in a pot and pour good clear pea broth over it
4. Salt and cook well
5. Pass through a sieve and return to the pot
6. Bring to a boil again and serve over fried bread or fried bread cubes
