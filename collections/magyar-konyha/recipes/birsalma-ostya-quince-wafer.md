## Ingredients

- ripe quinces
- 32 dekagramm quince pulp
- 28 dekagramm sugar rubbed with lemon zest
- 1 lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Scald beautiful ripe quinces with skin in water until softened
2. Peel and scrape the flesh with a knife without including the hard core
3. Mix finely 32 dekagramm of the scraped pulp strained through a sieve with 28 dekagramm sugar rubbed on lemon zest, adding the juice of 1 lemon
4. Spread very thinly on small porcelain plates coated with butter
5. Place the plates in a warm oven until the mixture dries enough to be pulled off
6. Roll onto round wooden sticks prepared for this
7. Brush the end with egg white so they stay together
8. Dry in a warm oven
9. Note: when rolling, the part of the wafer that lay on the buttered plate forms the outer side
