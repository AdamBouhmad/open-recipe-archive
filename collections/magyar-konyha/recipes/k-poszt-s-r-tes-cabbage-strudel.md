## Ingredients

- 1 firm head of cabbage
- salt
- broken sugar
- lard
- chopped red onion
- pepper

## Directions

1. Slice and salt a firm head of cabbage, chop finely
2. Fry broken sugar in lard until browned
3. Add a little chopped red onion and fry
4. Add the cabbage from which you have first squeezed out the liquid, cook until red, season with pepper, and set aside to cool
5. Scatter thinly on half the dough, gently roll together, brush the top with fat, and bake until crispy
