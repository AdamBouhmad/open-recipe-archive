## Ingredients

- seven to nine white-skinned red onions
- goose fat
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- broth
- five pairs sausages
- fried breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Clean the onions and cut them into large pieces.
2. Heat a few spoonfuls of goose fat and add the onions with salt and pepper.
3. Braise them slowly over gentle coals until they become golden yellow.
4. Sprinkle flour over them and braise a little longer with the flour.
5. Pour a cup of broth over them and cook until the sauce is completely reduced.
6. Fry five pairs of sausages so that they brown nicely but do not overcook.
7. Add the sausages to the braised red onions.
8. Braise together for fifteen minutes and place in a tureen.
9. Border the edge with fried breadcrumbs cut into large triangles.
