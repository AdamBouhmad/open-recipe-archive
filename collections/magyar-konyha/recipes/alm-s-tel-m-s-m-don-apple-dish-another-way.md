## Ingredients

- white house bread
- good wine
- crushed sugar
- butter
- finely chopped apple slices
- sugar
- chopped raisins
- small grapes
- almond
- lemon zest
- crushed cinnamon

## Directions

1. Moisten fine crumbs grated from white house bread with good wine, sprinkle in a few spoonfuls of crushed sugar
2. Place a finger-thickness layer of moistened crumbs in a butter-greased pan, dot with small pieces of butter, then place finely chopped and well-sugared apple slices on it
3. Finally place finely chopped raisins, small grapes, almond, lemon zest and crushed cinnamon, then repeat all of this and so on until the pan is full
4. Place in the oven and bake for three-quarters of an hour, turn out onto a plate and sprinkle with sugar
