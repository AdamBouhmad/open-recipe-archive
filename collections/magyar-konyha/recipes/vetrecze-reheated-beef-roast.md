## Ingredients

- leftover beef roast
- hot fat
- cold water
- vinegar
- pepper
- onions

## Directions

1. Place thinly sliced leftover beef roast into hot fat
2. Season with salt and add a little cold water and several spoonfuls of vinegar
3. When the liquid has reduced and the fat begins to fry, add a little pepper and several onions sliced thinly
4. When the onions begin to brown, it is ready to serve
5. Serve with potato puree
