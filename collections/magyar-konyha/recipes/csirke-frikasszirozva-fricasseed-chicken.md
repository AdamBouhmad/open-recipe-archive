## Ingredients

- Small chickens
- Butter
- Flour
- Red onion
- Green parsley
- Broth
- Egg yolks
- Salt

## Directions

1. Open and blanch small chickens.
2. Heat a piece of butter, add two spoonfuls of flour, a whole red onion, and a bunch of green parsley.
3. Dilute with broth and cook the sauce, then strain it.
4. Place the chickens in the strained sauce and simmer until cooked.
5. When ready to serve, place two egg yolks in a bowl, break them up, add the sauce and stir constantly over gentle heat to thicken.
6. Serve the chickens with the sauce poured over them.
7. It can also be prepared like fricasseed veal head with sour garnish.
