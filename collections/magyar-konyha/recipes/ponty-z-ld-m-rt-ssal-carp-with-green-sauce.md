## Ingredients

- carp
- salt
- vinegar
- water
- peppercorns
- allspice
- thyme
- green parsley
- sorrel
- five anchovies
- capers
- three hard-boiled eggs
- table oil

## Directions

1. After the carp has lain in salt for one hour, scald it with a broth made of half vinegar and half water, into which peppercorns, allspice and thyme have been cooked, and then cool it.
2. Cook a handful of green parsley and the same amount of sorrel until soft and squeeze out the water well.
3. Remove the bones from five water-soaked anchovies and pound them finely in a mortar with a similar amount of capers and three hard-boiled eggs and press through a sieve.
4. Mix well with a few spoonfuls of fine table oil and vinegar and pour it over the dish.
