## Ingredients

- 420 grams flour, warmed
- a little salt
- 1 spoon broken sugar
- 200 milliliters lukewarm cream
- 5 egg yolks
- 70 grams melted butter
- 37.5 grams cheese yeast
- an egg-sized piece of butter
- an equal amount of sugar
- 200 milliliters milk
- 5 egg yolks
- 100 grams broken sugar
- a little vanilla
- 400 milliliters boiled cream

## Directions

1. Warm the flour in a bowl and salt it, add 1 spoon of broken sugar
2. Mix together the lukewarm cream, 5 egg yolks, melted butter, and cheese yeast
3. Take as much of the flour as needed for making the dough, then pour the entire mixture through a sieve onto the flour and stir until the dough separates from the spoon
4. Stir a little more, but it should not be too stiff even though it is difficult to work
5. Place on a well-floured board and roll out to the thickness of two knife-backs
6. Cut out the noodles with a medium-sized pastry cutter and let rise
7. Place an egg-sized piece of butter, an equal amount of sugar, and milk to a depth of two finger-widths in a flat, wide pan, bring to a boil, and add the noodles when well risen
8. Cover with clean paper and then with a well-fitting iron lid, steam for 15-30 minutes
9. While this is cooking, prepare the sauce: mix 5 egg yolks, 100 grams broken sugar, a little vanilla, and 400 milliliters boiled cream in a pot, place over coals and constantly stir so it does not curdle, until it begins to thicken
10. Pour into a bowl, place the noodles on a platter, sprinkle with sugar, and serve with the sauce
