## Ingredients

- Two young chickens
- Butter
- Sauce
- Four deciliters green peas
- Parsley
- Crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Blanch two young chickens and cut them into four pieces.
2. Prepare a light buttered sauce and place the chicken pieces in it.
3. Braise four deciliters of green peas with butter and parsley until softened.
4. Add the chicken to the peas along with shelled crayfish tails.
5. Simmer together for another quarter hour.
6. When serving, place the chickens in the center of the plate and arrange the peas around the edge like a wreath, garnished with crayfish tails, leaving about two finger-widths of the peas uncovered.
