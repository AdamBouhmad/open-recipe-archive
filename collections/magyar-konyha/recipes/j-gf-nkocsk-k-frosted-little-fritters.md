## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites
- 28 dekagramm sifted vanilla sugar
- 4.5 dekagramm hair powder
- 21 dekagramm cleaned and finely chopped sweet almonds
- 4.5 dekagramm candied orange peel
- 4.5 dekagramm citron peel
- 4.5 dekagramm pistachios
- wafer leaves

## Directions

1. Beat the stiff foam of 6 egg whites with 28 dekagramm sifted vanilla sugar for one hour until it becomes white frosting
2. Add 4.5 dekagramm hair powder, 21 dekagramm cleaned and finely chopped sweet almonds, 4.5 dekagramm candied orange peel, 4.5 dekagramm citron peel, and 4.5 dekagramm pistachios
3. Chop everything equally with the almond
4. Beat the whole mixture well
5. Spread this mixture onto leaves cut from wafers to the thickness of a small finger
6. Bake slowly at gentle heat
