## Ingredients

- a nice piece of pike
- salt
- white vinegar
- water
- red onion
- parsley
- carrot
- bay leaf
- herring
- breadcrumbs
- cream

## Directions

1. Leave a nice piece of pike in salt for one hour
2. Cook in equal parts white vinegar and water with red onion, parsley, carrot, and bay leaf
3. When cooked, cut into thin slices
4. Place alternating layers of pike and herring in a pan (having removed herring skin and bones and soaked well, then cut into long pieces)
5. Pour a little breadcrumbs and one spoon of good cream over them
6. Continue alternating layers of pike and herring, pouring with lemon juice and cream until the pan is full
7. Cover the pan and steam for one hour
8. Carefully turn out onto a plate so it does not fall apart
9. Border the plate with puff pastry ribbons rolled to knife-edge thickness and pricked with a medium-sized doughnut cutter
10. Brush edges with egg white and overlap the leaves so one covers half of the other
11. Arrange the entire border around the food in the center of the plate
