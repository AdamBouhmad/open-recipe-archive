## Ingredients

- tongue
- salt
- water
- bacon slices or red onion
- yellow carrot
- allspice
- veal bones
- flour
- red wine
- broth
- 5-7 decagrams ground almond

## Directions

1. Cook tongue in salted water until tender.
2. When cleaned of skin, place in pan.
3. Add bacon slices or red onion, yellow carrot sliced, few allspice and veal bones.
4. Braise all until nicely browned.
5. Sprinkle with spoonful of flour and brown it.
6. Dilute with half red wine and half broth.
7. Cook well, strain sauce through sieve into another pan.
8. Add 5-7 decagrams ground almond to it.
9. Place tongue in it and cook well.
10. Before serving, lard the tongue with the almond, pour sauce into dish and place tongue on top.
