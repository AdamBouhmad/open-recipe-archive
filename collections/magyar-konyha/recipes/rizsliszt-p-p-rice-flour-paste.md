## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams rice, washed and dried
- 7 deciliters cold milk
- 7 dekagrams softened and finely ground almonds
- 10 dekagrams butter
- sugar rubbed on an orange
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites, whipped stiff
- whole preserved Spanish cherries
- quince paste

## Directions

1. Grind the washed and dried rice into fine flour
2. Place in a pot and gradually stir in the cold milk until smooth
3. Add the softened almonds and place over low heat
4. Cook with constant stirring into a thick, smooth paste
5. Remove from heat and while warm, stir in the butter and sugar rubbed on the orange
6. Cool
7. Stir in the egg yolks one after another
8. Fold in the stiffly whipped egg whites
9. Arrange whole preserved cherries and quince paste in a double star pattern on the bottom of a buttered mold or pot
10. Carefully fill with the paste
11. Set in a water bath for three-quarters of an hour and turn out
12. Surround the edge of the paste with cherry or raspberry syrup made with boiled sugar
