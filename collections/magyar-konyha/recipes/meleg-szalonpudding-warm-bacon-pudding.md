## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- four and one half decilitres of good sour cream
- vanilla
- powdered sugar
- ten dekagrams of candied fruit of various types
- six egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat eight egg yolks with four and one half decilitres of good sour cream
2. Add finely ground vanilla and powdered sugar to taste
3. Place the mixture over charcoal fire and stir until it begins to thicken
4. Remove quickly and stir for a few minutes so it does not curdle
5. Set aside until completely cooled
6. Cut ten dekagrams of various candied fruits into small cubes and mix into the cream
7. Gradually fold in the stiff whites of six eggs
8. Line a buttered mould with seven dekagrams of fine ladyfinger biscuits, placing them with their shiny sides facing outward and standing upright
9. Place the mixture in the mould
10. Steam for three quarters of an hour
11. Turn out and serve immediately
