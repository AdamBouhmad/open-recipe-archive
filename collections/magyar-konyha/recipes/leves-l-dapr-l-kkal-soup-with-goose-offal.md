## Ingredients

- butter
- green parsley
- six button mushrooms
- flour
- goose offal
- meat broth
- nutmeg
- toasted bread pieces
- goose liver

## Directions

1. Place butter, finely chopped green parsley, and six sliced button mushrooms in a frying pan.
2. Braise for a quarter hour.
3. Sprinkle two tablespoons flour over it.
4. Cook the goose offal in broth until soft enough.
5. Cut the meat into appropriate pieces, adding finely chopped nutmeg and cooking a bit longer.
6. Serve on long toasted bread pieces.
7. Cook the liver separately and chop it into pieces.
8. Add it to the soup.
9. This soup can also be made without button mushrooms.
