## Ingredients

- 4 deciliters fruit syrup
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- 4 deciliters water
- juice of 3 lemons

## Directions

1. Cook 14 dekagrams of sugar with 4 deciliters of water until the sugar is dissolved
2. Pour this hot mixture into 4 deciliters of fruit syrup and mix well until completely dissolved
3. Add the juice of 3 lemons
4. Treat it like other ice creams
