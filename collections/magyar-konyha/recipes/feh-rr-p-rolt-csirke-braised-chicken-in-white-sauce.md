## Ingredients

- chicken
- bacon
- parsley root
- carrot
- whole pepper
- mace
- broth
- flour
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. After plucking the chicken, remove the breastbone, salt it well and prepare it.
2. Line a pot with bacon, parsley root, carrot, whole pepper and mace.
3. Place the chicken on top, cover with thin bacon slices, pour two spoonfuls of broth over it.
4. Place white paper on top, cover the pot and braise slowly.
5. Remove the two chickens, cover them again with bacon so they remain white in color.
6. Sprinkle flour over the braised vegetables and pour on as much broth as needed.
7. When well cooked, strain the sauce, add mushrooms, remove the bacon from the chickens, place it in the pot and bring to a boil once more.
