## Ingredients

- red onion
- paprika
- fat
- water
- fish
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry the red onion and paprika in fat
2. Pour four deciliters of water over it and bring to a boil
3. Add the salted fish and cook for half an hour
4. Do not stir with a spoon, but shake it occasionally so it does not burn
