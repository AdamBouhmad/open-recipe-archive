## Ingredients

- bread roll grated and sliced thin
- four deciliters milk
- egg-sized piece of crayfish butter
- one whole egg
- three egg yolks
- salt
- twenty-five crayfish tails
- three egg whites
- bread rings
- crayfish butter
- broth

## Directions

1. Place thinly sliced grated bread in a pot
2. Pour four deciliters of milk over it
3. Cook with constant stirring to a paste and let cool
4. Beat an egg-sized piece of crayfish butter until fluffy
5. Add the cooked and softened bread
6. Beat in one whole egg and three egg yolks
7. Stir for half an hour
8. Add salt and twenty-five roughly chopped crayfish tails
9. Gently mix in the beaten whites of three eggs
10. Spread bread rings with crayfish butter and join them together
11. After half an hour turn into soup bowl and pour broth over them
