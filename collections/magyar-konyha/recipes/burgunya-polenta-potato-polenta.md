## Ingredients

- potato pudding made with breadcrumbs
- butter
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. First prepare a potato pudding with breadcrumbs
2. Once cooled, cut into slices in the manner described for polenta
3. Layer in a buttered mold with grated cheese
4. Bake in the same manner as polenta
