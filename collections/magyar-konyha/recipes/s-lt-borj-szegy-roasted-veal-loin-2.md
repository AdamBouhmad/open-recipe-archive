## Ingredients

- veal loin with skin
- bacon for larding
- flour
- butter
- meat stock
- burnt wine
- salt
- nutmeg flower
- pepper
- red onion
- bay leaf
- parsley
- sour cream for sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the veal loin
2. Lard the loin with bacon
3. Roll in flour
4. Roast in butter
5. When browned, pour stock and burnt wine over it
6. Add salt, nutmeg flower, pepper, red onion, bay leaf, and parsley
7. When the veal loin is tender, prepare a sauce with sour cream
