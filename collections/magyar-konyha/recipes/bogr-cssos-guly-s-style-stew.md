## Ingredients

- Salt
- Paprika, preferably fresh in the pod
- Lamb meat, finely chopped
- Water

## Directions

1. Put salt and paprika in a kettle, then add finely chopped lamb meat
2. Pour in water to cover everything
3. Cook over gentle heat for one hour, then turn and continue cooking until it reduces to fat
