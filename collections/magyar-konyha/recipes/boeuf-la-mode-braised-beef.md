## Ingredients

- Beef without bones
- Ham
- Bacon, cut into finger-length and thickness pieces
- Green parsley, minced
- Small onion, minced
- Several mushrooms, minced
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bacon slices
- Red onion, sliced in rings
- Carrot, sliced
- Whole peppercorns, several
- Four deciliters white wine
- Good broth

## Directions

1. Lard the beef with pieces of ham and bacon
2. Prepare a mixture of minced green parsley, minced onion, several minced mushrooms, salt, and pepper
3. Roll the bacon pieces in this mixture and insert into the beef
4. Place the beef in a pot that is not much larger than necessary
5. Line the bottom of the pot with bacon slices, sliced red onion, and sliced carrot
6. Place the beef on top, cover tightly, and braise slowly for four hours, turning frequently to prevent burning
7. Pour four deciliters of white wine and the same amount of good broth over the meat
8. Continue braising until the meat is tender enough, strain the sauce through a sieve, and pour over the meat on the platter
9. This dish can also be served cold
