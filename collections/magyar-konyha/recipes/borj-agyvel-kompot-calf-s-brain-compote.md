## Ingredients

- calf's brain
- salted water
- butter sauce
- 10-12 mushrooms
- butter
- green parsley
- twenty crayfish
- twenty asparagus tips
- butter pastry wreath or braised mushrooms

## Directions

1. Rinse the calf's brain in water, blanch it in salted water, remove its skin, and cut into large pieces
2. Prepare a butter sauce
3. Slice 10-12 mushrooms and braise with butter and green parsley
4. Clean the tails and claws from twenty crayfish
5. Cut the heads from twenty asparagus tips boiled in salted water
6. Place all of this together with the brain in the sauce
7. Bring to a boil several times and serve neatly
8. Place the asparagus, crayfish tails, and brain in the center of the plate, and arrange around it a butter pastry wreath or braised mushrooms
