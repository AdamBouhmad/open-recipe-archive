## Ingredients

- 7 dekagrams butter, beaten fluffy
- 6 egg yolks
- 8 spoonfuls sour cream
- 7 dekagrams sugar, rubbed with lemon and ground
- salt
- 6 spoonfuls fine flour
- 6 egg whites
- butter for greasing
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Into 7 dekagrams of fluffy beaten butter, gradually mix 6 egg yolks and then 8 spoonfuls of sour cream, one spoonful at a time
2. Add 7 dekagrams of sugar rubbed with lemon, a little salt, and 6 spoonfuls of fine flour
3. Finally, when well mixed, add the stiff snow from 6 egg whites
4. Pour the batter into a buttered baking dish
5. Bake in the oven until golden yellow
6. Break up with two knives
7. Heap high on a plate and serve dusted with sugar
8. Alternatively, for Emperor's Boats: place the batter in a buttered baking dish, bake until light yellow, cut into diagonal cubes with a sharp knife and make a cross-cut on top, fry until golden in hot lard, fill the cut as desired with preserved fruit and serve dusted with sugar
