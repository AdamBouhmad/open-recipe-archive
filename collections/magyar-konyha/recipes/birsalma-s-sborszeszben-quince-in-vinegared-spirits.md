## Ingredients

- quince or quince-pear
- 56 dekagrams sugar per 56 dekagrams quince
- 42 dekagrams sugar per 56 dekagrams quince
- strong vinegared spirits, same amount as the sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Peel the quince or quince-pear and cut into eight or more pieces depending on size.
2. Remove the seeds and place the quince in cold water.
3. Soften well in hot water and drain thoroughly.
4. Take 56 dekagrams quince to 42 dekagrams sugar and cook not too thickly.
5. Prepare the quince over three days as with other fruit, cooking the sugar quite thickly the last time.
6. Let cool and mix with an equal amount of strong vinegared spirits.
7. Pour over the quince already packed in glass jars.
