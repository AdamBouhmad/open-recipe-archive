## Ingredients

- 5 dekagrams good vanilla pods
- 7 dekagrams Peruvian balsam
- 3.5 dekagrams cinnamon
- 4 grams bergamot oil
- 1.5 liters 72° alcohol
- 4 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Pour 1.5 liters 72° alcohol and 4 deciliters water over the vanilla pods, Peruvian balsam, cinnamon, and bergamot oil
2. Allow to digest
