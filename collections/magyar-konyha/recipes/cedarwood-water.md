## Ingredients

- 5 liters 72° alcohol
- 4.5 dekagrams bergamot oil
- 7 dekagrams lemon oil
- 1 dekagrams lavender oil
- 1.5 dekagrams Peruvian balsam
- 8 grams cinnamon oil
- orange flower water as needed

## Directions

1. Mix bergamot oil, lemon oil, lavender oil, Peruvian balsam, and cinnamon oil in alcohol
2. Add orange flower water to dilute alcohol to 28°
3. Filter
