## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- vinegar
- water
- red onion
- lemon peel
- roots
- whole black pepper
- salt
- lard
- cream
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. After the rabbit is beheaded, washed, and carefully cleaned of its bluish membranes, salt it well.
2. Boil vinegar, water, red onion, lemon peel, some roots, and whole black pepper together for half an hour to make a marinade.
3. Pour this hot marinade over the rabbit and let it sit for one or two days to become properly tender.
4. Before threading onto the spit, neatly lard the back and thighs.
5. Roast slowly on the spit, basting frequently with the marinade during cooking.
6. When the rabbit is halfway cooked, pour a few spoonfuls of cream over it.
7. When perfectly roasted and arranged neatly on the plate, cover it with thinly sliced lemon without seeds, arranged in overlapping fashion lengthwise.
8. Strain the sauce and pour it under the roasted rabbit.
