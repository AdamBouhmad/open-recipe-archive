## Ingredients

- veal legs cleaned
- veal head cleaned
- 1.5 liters water
- salt
- pepper
- 2 decilitres good sour cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 good spoonful flour
- hot meat broth
- 2 whole lemons

## Directions

1. Put the nicely cleaned veal legs and head to cook in 1.5 liters water for one hour.
2. Remove from broth and remove all bones from the meat.
3. Place back in the broth and season with salt and pepper, bring to boil again.
4. Meanwhile prepare a liaison in a deep bowl for one head and four legs: take 2 decilitres good sour cream and 4 egg yolks, 1 good spoonful flour.
5. Mix well so it is not lumpy.
6. Slowly pour the hot meat broth into the liaison in the bowl with continuous stirring.
7. Once incorporated, pour back into the warm pot, remove from strong heat as the egg will curdle.
8. At serving time squeeze the juice of 2 whole lemons into it, but not before as it would become bitter.
9. Serve in deep bowl.
