## Ingredients

- 10 hard-boiled eggs
- 1/2 bread roll soaked in milk
- 10 deca fresh bacon, chopped into very small cubes
- 1 tablespoon sour cream
- 1 whole raw egg
- green parsley
- celery leaves
- onion, finely chopped
- 3 types of greens, 1 tablespoon total
- black pepper and clove pepper, a knife point of each
- salt
- paprika red fat for drizzling
- breadcrumbs
- 1/2 liter salted sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut the hard-boiled eggs in half lengthwise and grate the yolks
2. Soak the bread roll well in milk and chop it finely
3. Chop the fresh bacon very finely into small cubes
4. Chop the green parsley, celery leaves, and onion finely; the total of these three greens should be 1 tablespoon
5. Mix together: the grated egg yolks, soaked bread, bacon, sour cream, raw egg, greens, black pepper, clove pepper, and salt
6. Form small balls the size of the egg yolk
7. Roll in breadcrumbs
8. Place the hard-boiled egg halves in a greased and breadcrumbed dish
9. Fill with the small balls
10. Pour 1/2 liter of salted sour cream over
11. Drizzle with paprika red fat
12. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs
13. Bake in the oven for 1/2 hour
14. Serve in the same dish it was cooked in
