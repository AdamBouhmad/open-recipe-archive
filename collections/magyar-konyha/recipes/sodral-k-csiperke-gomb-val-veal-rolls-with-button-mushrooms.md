## Ingredients

- veal thigh
- roast leftovers
- bacon
- cooked ham
- green parsley
- onion
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- two egg yolks
- two tablespoons sour cream
- butter
- white wine
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices from veal thigh, soften them well, and set aside
2. Finely chop roast leftovers, a small piece of bacon, cooked ham, green parsley, onion, and mushrooms; add salt, pepper, two egg yolks, and two tablespoons sour cream, and mix well
3. Spread this mixture on the veal slices and roll them up
4. Line a pot with a few bacon slices, place the rolls on top, cover with bacon slices, place embers on the lid, and braise for two hours
5. Strain the fat, cut the rolls lengthwise in half, and arrange on a platter so the filling shows
6. Clean small button mushrooms, melt a piece of butter, add green parsley and mushrooms, braise until the sauce evaporates, dust with a little flour, braise again, then add half a coffee cup of white wine, the same amount of broth, and two full tablespoons of sour cream; cook until only little sauce remains
7. Skim the fat from the mushroom sauce, arrange the mushrooms in a wreath around the edge of the platter, and place the rolls in the center
