## Ingredients

- 42-56 dekagramm sugar
- 1 bottle white wine
- 4 lemons
- 6 dekagramm isinglass or equivalent amount of aspic made from pork skin, gelatin, deer antlers, or calf's feet

## Directions

1. Pour 4 deciliter water over the sugar cut into small pieces
2. Once dissolved, bring to a boil
3. Squeeze the juice from the lemons into it and let it simmer briefly over the fire, skimming off the foam as cleanly as possible
4. Once cooled, combine in a porcelain or earthenware vessel with the aspic, which should be only lukewarm
5. Add the finely peeled zest from one lemon and the white wine, adding the latter very carefully
6. Let stand covered for half an hour, then strain through a cloth that has been rinsed in warm water
7. Test the firmness by the method described above, then pour into the mold and freeze on ice or in cold water
8. At serving time, hold the mold briefly in warm water, dry it, and turn the jelly out onto a dish
