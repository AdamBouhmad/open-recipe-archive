## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- green parsley
- butter
- flour
- pea broth
- salt
- sour cream
- toasted breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Wash the mushrooms several times until completely clean.
2. Stew them in butter with finely chopped green parsley.
3. Add a spoonful of flour and stew again.
4. Pour in as much pea broth as needed for soup.
5. Salt it and cook well.
6. Before serving, add a spoonful of sour cream and pour over toasted breadcrumb slices.
