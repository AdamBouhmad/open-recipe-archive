## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters ripe strawberries, strained through a sieve
- 21 dekagrams broken sugar
- 1 liter good heavy cream
- 1.5 dekagrams dissolved and cooled isinglass

## Directions

1. Mix the strained strawberries with the broken sugar
2. Whip one liter of good heavy cream into foam
3. Mix the foam with the strained strawberries
4. Mix in the dissolved and cooled isinglass
5. Pour into a mold and freeze on ice
6. Before serving, turn out and decorate with a wreath of spun sugar
7. This foam can also be used for filling Spanish wind cakes
