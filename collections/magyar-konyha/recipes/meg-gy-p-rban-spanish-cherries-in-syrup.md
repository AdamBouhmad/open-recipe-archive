## Ingredients

- Spanish cherries
- sugar
- water
- 1 kilogram fruit requires 2 deciliters water for every 500 grams sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the stems from beautiful Spanish cherries, wash and strain them
2. Place them in jars, fill more than halfway with cooked and cooled sugar water (calculating 2 deciliters water for every 500 grams sugar)
3. Treat them as you would regular cherries
