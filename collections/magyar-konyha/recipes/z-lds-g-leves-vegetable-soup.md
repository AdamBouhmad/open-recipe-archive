## Ingredients

- One beautiful yellow cabbage
- One yellow carrot
- Lard, a small amount
- Meat broth
- One egg yolk
- Two tablespoons milk

## Directions

1. Cut the yellow cabbage and carrot into thick noodle-like pieces
2. Put a small amount of lard in a pot and when hot, add the carrot and let it braise slowly
3. When the carrot is halfway soft, add the cabbage and continue braising
4. When both are very soft, thin with meat broth and bring to a boil
5. Mix one egg yolk with two tablespoons of milk and pour the soup over it
