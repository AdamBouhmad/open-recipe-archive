## Ingredients

- veal loin
- cold water
- butter
- flour
- good stock
- lemon peel
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Blanch the veal loin
2. Place in cold water for a few minutes and rinse off the foam cleanly
3. Cut into thin slices
4. Melt butter in a pot, toast two spoonfuls of flour in it without browning it
5. Thin it with good stock and place the veal meat in it
6. Add lemon peel and the juice of one lemon
7. Cook until sufficiently tender
