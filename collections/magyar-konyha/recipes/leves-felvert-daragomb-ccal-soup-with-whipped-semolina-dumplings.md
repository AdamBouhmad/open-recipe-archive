## Ingredients

- ten decagrams butter
- four eggs
- four deciliters semolina
- meat broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat ten decagrams butter until fluffy.
2. Slowly add four whole eggs.
3. Mix in four deciliters semolina.
4. Test with one dumpling first, as they can become either too soft or too hard.
5. If the test dumpling succeeds, make small dumplings.
6. Cook them in good meat broth.
7. Let them rest for a quarter hour.
8. Place in a soup bowl and pour the strained meat broth over them.
