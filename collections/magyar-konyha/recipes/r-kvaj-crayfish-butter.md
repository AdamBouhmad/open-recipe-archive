## Ingredients

- crayfish scraps (shell, legs, body)
- whole small crayfish (with intestines and stomach removed, scissors and tail cut off for other uses)
- cooked crayfish shells
- water
- butter (half the amount of crayfish)

## Directions

1. Crush the crayfish scraps, shell, legs and small whole crayfish (after removing intestines and stomach) finely
2. Cook with a little water
3. Mix with butter equal to half the weight of the crayfish, while hot
4. Strain through cloth and let cool
