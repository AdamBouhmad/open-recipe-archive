## Ingredients

- bread slices
- Malaga or Menezes wine
- butter
- ripe apricots
- sugar
- fresh fruit or compote

## Directions

1. Dampen the bread slices with Malaga or Menezes wine
2. Line a buttered pot with bread slices so no gaps remain
3. Make a layer of peeled and sliced ripe apricots, sprinkled with sugar
4. Add another layer of bread slices
5. Add a layer of fresh fruit or compote (but not marmalade)
6. Continue layering until the pot is full
7. Cover with bread slices and bake until nicely browned
8. Carefully turn out onto a plate, sprinkle with sugar, and serve
