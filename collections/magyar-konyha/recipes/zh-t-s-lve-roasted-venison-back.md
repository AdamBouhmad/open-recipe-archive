## Ingredients

- venison back
- vinegar
- red onion
- bacon for larding
- paper
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Prepare it in exactly the same way as the haunch
2. Marinate it with vinegar and red onion, and let it lie in the brine for one or two days
3. Remove the skin, lard it with bacon, wrap it in paper, and roast it slowly
4. Baste it several times with the brine and sour cream
5. Serve it on the dish in the same way as the venison haunch
