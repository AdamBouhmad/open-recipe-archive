## Ingredients

- twenty-eight dekagrams veal liver
- bread roll
- milk
- parsley
- red onion
- seven dekagrams butter
- two whole eggs
- one egg yolk
- breadcrumbs
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Chop veal liver finely and press through a sieve
2. Soak bread roll in milk, squeeze out and chop finely with parsley and a pea-sized piece of red onion
3. Beat seven dekagrams of butter until frothy
4. Mix in two whole eggs and one egg yolk
5. Combine this with the liver and bread mixture
6. Add enough breadcrumbs to form nice small dumplings
7. Cook in good beef broth
