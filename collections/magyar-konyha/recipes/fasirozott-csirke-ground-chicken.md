## Ingredients

- Beautiful meaty chickens
- Bacon
- Grated roll soaked in milk
- Eggs
- Nutmeg flowers
- Salt
- Beef broth
- Mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. After blanching beautiful meaty chickens, let them soak for a while in warm water until they turn white nicely.
2. Split them in half along the back and remove all the bones up to the wings and legs.
3. Cut the meat, membrane, and all stringy parts together with a little bacon into small pieces.
4. Grate and milk-soak half a roll, wring it out well, beat two eggs into it, and stir over the fire until it becomes thick.
5. Crush the meat together with the roll in a mortar until fine.
6. Add a little nutmeg flower and salt.
7. Dry the chickens with a cloth, pour one or two spoonfuls of beef broth over them.
8. Braise slowly for half an hour and prepare a little mushroom sauce for them.
9. When serving, remove the bacon from the chickens, place on a platter, and pour the sauce over them.
