## Ingredients

- young fat squabs
- salt
- butter
- milk
- rolls
- green parsley
- saffron
- two whole eggs
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Inflate young fat squabs by blowing through the feather sockets to plump them
2. Pluck them
3. Let them sit in salt for half an hour
4. For two squabs, melt a piece of butter over coals
5. Soak one and a half rolls in milk and chop together with green parsley, saffron and salt
6. Mix everything together
7. Beat in two whole eggs and two egg yolks
8. Stuff the squabs with the filling
9. Thread them onto a spit
10. Roast slowly until golden brown
