## Ingredients

- 500 grams sugar
- 2 deciliters water
- 28 dekagrams blanched and finely chopped almonds
- 7 dekagrams citronade
- 7 dekagrams quince marmalade
- 4 preserved walnuts
- zest of two lemons
- wafers
- powdered sugar
- preserved fruit for decoration

## Directions

1. Cook sugar and water together in a copper pan until clarified
2. When the sugar has cooked enough to begin to thread, add the blanched and finely chopped almonds, citronade, quince marmalade, preserved walnuts, and lemon zest
3. Stir until it begins to harden
4. Place the whisked mixture on a wafer-lined cake base
5. Cool, sprinkle with powdered sugar, and decorate with preserved fruit
