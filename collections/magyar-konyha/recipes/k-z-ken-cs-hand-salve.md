## Ingredients

- equal amounts of beef marrow and almond oil
- a small amount of lavender oil

## Directions

1. Mix equal amounts of beef marrow and almond oil over heat
2. Add a small amount of lavender oil for good fragrance
3. This salve is for rubbing into the hands to obtain fine, soft skin
4. In the evening before bed, rub the hands thoroughly with this salve, then put on gloves
5. In the morning, wash the pomade off the hands cleanly with almond meal and soapy water
