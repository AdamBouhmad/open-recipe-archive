## Ingredients

- 21 dekagrams benzoin resin
- 18 dekagrams storax
- 3 dekagrams laudanum resin
- 2 dekagrams turpentine
- rose oil
- clove oil
- lavender oil

## Directions

1. Powder 21 dekagrams benzoin resin, 18 dekagrams storax, and 3 dekagrams laudanum resin
2. Mix with 2 dekagrams turpentine
3. Place in a container, then place this container in another container filled with hot water
4. Allow to remain until the materials have melted
5. When the mixture is completely liquid, add rose oil, clove oil, and lavender oil
