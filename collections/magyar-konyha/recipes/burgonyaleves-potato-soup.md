## Ingredients

- parsley
- parsnip
- carrot
- celery
- water
- salt
- potatoes
- butter
- flour
- parsley, finely chopped
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put parsley, parsnip, carrot, and celery in a pot, fill with water, salt, and cook for two hours
2. Peel the potatoes, cut into cubes, and strain the broth over them
3. Cook until tender
4. Make a thin yellow roux with butter and flour
5. Add finely chopped parsley to it, let it swell with the broth, add nutmeg, and cook everything well
6. When the potatoes are tender, serve the soup over fried bread
