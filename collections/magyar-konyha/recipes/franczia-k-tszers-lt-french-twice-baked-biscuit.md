## Ingredients

- 10 whole eggs with shells
- 10 spoonfuls of sifted sugar
- flour equal in weight to 7 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Weigh 10 whole eggs with shells and measure the same weight of sifted sugar
2. Measure flour equal in weight to 7 whole eggs
3. Separate the whites from the yolks of the 10 eggs
4. Beat the egg whites into stiff foam
5. Place in a bowl and beat in the egg yolks, then the sugar
6. Stir for half an hour
7. Quickly mix in the measured dry flour, stirring only until well combined
8. Coat the sheet mold with a little butter
9. Pour in the dough
10. Sprinkle the top with sifted sugar
11. Bake in a gentle oven for 10-12 minutes
12. Cut into slices of knife-blade thickness
13. Return to the oven and toast lightly
14. French sponge cake can also be made from this dough
