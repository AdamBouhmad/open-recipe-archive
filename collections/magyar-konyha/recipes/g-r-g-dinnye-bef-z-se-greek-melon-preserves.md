## Ingredients

- Greek melon with thick rind and very red flesh
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Select a Greek melon with thick rind and very red flesh
2. Cut off both ends of the melon to a width of two fingers, leaving the flesh intact on the rind
3. Prick the rind
4. Follow the same preservation method as described for yellow melon: leave in cold water overnight, spread on a cloth the next day to allow water to drain and the melon to dry, pass through a sieve and treat with sulfur to keep it light in color
5. Prepare a good thick syrup, pour into a bowl and let cool
6. Cook the melon slices in thin syrup for a short time, adding orange peel and thin sugar
7. For several days, boil down the liquid until the slices become transparent
8. Pour into glass jars, add a small amount of salicylic acid, tie down well and finally sterilize
