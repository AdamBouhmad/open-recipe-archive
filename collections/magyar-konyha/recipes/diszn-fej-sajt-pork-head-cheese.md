## Ingredients

- fresh pork head
- 4-5 pieces of fresh pork skin, palm-sized
- salt
- one red onion, parboiled
- one whole lemon
- paprika
- black pepper
- clove pepper
- a few spoonfuls thin red blood
- cleaned stomach casing

## Directions

1. Boil the fresh pork head and fresh pork skin until tender
2. Let cool until half-cold, then remove meat from bones
3. Cut meat into long, thin slices and the pork skin into thin strips
4. Salt appropriately and gently mix the meat with skin so they do not break
5. Finely chop the parboiled red onion and squeeze lemon juice into it, grate in the zest
6. Add a little paprika, abundant black and clove pepper
7. Add a few spoonfuls of thin red blood to achieve a nice dark color
8. Gently fold everything together, stirring slowly so as not to break the meat and skin, until evenly colored and flavored throughout
9. Fill the cleaned stomach casing well with the mixture
10. Boil in hot salted water for half an hour
11. Pierce with a thin needle; if clear fat rather than red liquid comes out, it is done
12. Remove and press flat while warm
13. Either smoke it or use it fresh; dry in air for twenty-four hours for additional flavor
