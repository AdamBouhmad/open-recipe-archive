## Ingredients

- best quality black, juicy, ripe cherries
- half kilogram sugar
- three deciliters of water
- crushed vanilla

## Directions

1. Pit the best quality black, juicy, ripe cherries so their juice is not lost. Cook half kilogram of sugar with three deciliters of water, skim it, and when completely clear, add one kilogram of pitted cherries with their juice. Cook until test drops stay in place on a plate, then cool and pour into jars as with other preserves. A little crushed vanilla may also be added.
