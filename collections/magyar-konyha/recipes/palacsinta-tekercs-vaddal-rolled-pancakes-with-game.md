## Ingredients

- fine pancakes
- leftover roasted venison or rabbit
- little lemon zest
- bread roll
- milk
- half lemon juice
- one or two egg yolks
- egg white
- breadcrumbs
- fat
- green parsley

## Directions

1. Make fine uniform pancakes.
2. Chop leftover roasted venison or rabbit finely, along with a little lemon zest.
3. Remove the crust from a bread roll, soak half of it in milk, squeeze it well, and chop it finely with the game meat.
4. Place this mixture in a bowl, add half lemon juice and one or two egg yolks depending on the amount of filling.
5. The filling should be spreadable but not thin.
6. Spread the filling smoothly on each pancake with a knife, but not quite to the edges.
7. Brush the ends with egg yolk, roll up, and trim the ends.
8. Dip in beaten egg, roll in breadcrumbs, and fry in fat.
9. When cooked, place on a plate and top with fried green parsley.
