## Ingredients

- ripe muscatel grapes
- juice of four lemons
- thirty-five dekagrams sugar
- four deciliters water
- four and one-half dekagrams dissolved isinglass
- finely crushed ice

## Directions

1. Remove the stem from ripe muscatel grapes
2. Extract juice from the berries by pressing through cloth until you have one liter of juice
3. Press the zest of four lemons through blotting paper several times
4. Cook thirty-five dekagrams of sugar with four deciliters of water until it forms heavy drops
5. Mix together with four and one-half dekagrams of dissolved isinglass and the other ingredients
6. Strain through table linen
7. Pour into a grape cluster-shaped mold that has been embedded in finely crushed ice
8. Allow to freeze for two hours, then carefully unmold onto a serving dish
