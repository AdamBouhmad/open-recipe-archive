## Ingredients

- 20 eggshells
- 1/4 kilo stewed rice
- 1/4 kilo veal lung stewed and finely chopped
- 1/8 kilo salted bacon cut into tiny cubes
- ground black pepper and clove pepper abundantly
- one head onion finely chopped
- green parsley
- one whole egg raw
- little good saffron
- nutmeg flower
- thin red roux
- sweet sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean the eggshells nicely
2. Prepare the filling: for 20 eggshells mix 1/4 kilo stewed rice, 1/4 kilo veal lung stewed and finely chopped, 1/8 kilo salted bacon cut into tiny cubes, ground black and clove pepper abundantly, one head onion finely chopped, green parsley, one whole raw egg, little saffron and nutmeg flower
3. Mix these together well
4. Fill the eggshells very firmly with the filling
5. Place in a pan with the openings facing upward
6. Pour hot water over them and cook until soft
7. Make a thin red roux
8. Pour abundant sweet sour cream over the soup
9. The fine egg crayfish soup is ready
