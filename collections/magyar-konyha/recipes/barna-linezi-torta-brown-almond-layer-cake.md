## Ingredients

- 28 dekagramm almonds with shells
- zest of one lemon, finely chopped
- 28 dekagramm finely ground sugar
- 1 dekagramm cinnamon
- a few cloves
- 28 dekagramm fine flour
- 28 dekagramm butter
- 3 egg yolks
- cream for sweetness

## Directions

1. Finely grind almonds with shells
2. Finely chop lemon zest
3. Finely grind sugar, cinnamon and cloves
4. Work flour and butter well together on board with rolling pin
5. Mix in prepared ingredients
6. Add three egg yolks and enough cream for sweetness
7. Assemble dough with wide knife
8. Can make braided cake with preserves or layers baked and filled which is best
9. Dough should not be stiffer than necessary for easy preparation
