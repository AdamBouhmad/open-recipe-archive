## Ingredients

- cleaned rabbit
- bacon for larding
- sour pickle brine
- butter for braising
- burnt wine
- salt
- nutmeg flower
- pepper
- red onion
- bay leaf
- parsley
- sour cream for sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned rabbit into slices two fingers thick
2. Lard the pieces with bacon
3. Leave in sour pickle brine for several days
4. Braise the rabbit
5. Glaze it
6. Serve in pepper or tomato sauce
7. Alternatively, prepare a sauce with sour cream when the rabbit is tender
