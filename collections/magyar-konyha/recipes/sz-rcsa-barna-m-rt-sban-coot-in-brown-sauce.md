## Ingredients

- coot
- vinegar
- water
- thyme
- lemon peel
- red onion
- salt
- butter
- flour
- pea broth
- wine

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the coot and cut into several pieces
2. Rinse several times
3. Prepare a marinade of half vinegar and half water with thyme, lemon peel, red onion, and salt
4. Bring the marinade to a boil and add the coot pieces, braise until tender
5. Prepare a brown sauce: make a light roux with butter and flour
6. Dilute with half the marinade in which the coot was cooked and half pea broth
7. Bring to a boil, thin with wine
8. Add the coot pieces and braise a bit longer
9. Strain the sauce through a sieve
10. Plate the coot and pour the brown sauce over it
