## Ingredients

- roasted beef
- salt
- anchovies
- capers
- red onion
- green parsley
- lemon zest
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Remove the fat and bones from the roasted beef
2. Beat it well with the flat side of a knife and salt it
3. For one and a half kilograms of roasted beef, take six anchovies, wash them, and remove the bones
4. Add two decagrams of capers, two small heads of red onion, some green parsley, and lemon zest
5. Chop all of this finely
6. Melt a piece of butter, place the chopped mixture in it, and brown it slightly
7. Fill the roasted beef with this filling
8. Fold it together, place it in a pot, and braise until it browns
9. Pour three deciliters of sour cream over it and braise for another half hour
10. Strain off the fat and serve neatly
