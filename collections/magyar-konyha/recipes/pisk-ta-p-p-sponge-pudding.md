## Ingredients

- 1 lemon or orange
- 140 grams sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 70 grams fine flour or sifted potato starch
- 5 egg whites
- butter
- sour cherry or currant syrup cooked in sugar
- chocolate made from cream

## Directions

1. Lightly rub the sugar on a lemon or orange, grind finely and sift
2. Put with two egg yolks in a bowl and beat for three-quarters of an hour
3. Gently fold in seven dekagrams fine flour or sifted potato starch with the stiffly beaten whites of five eggs
4. Pour the whipped mixture into a buttered mold and steam for three-quarters of an hour
5. Do not give too much heat on top so it does not brown, but only steams out
6. When serving, turn out onto a plate and pour sour cherry or currant syrup cooked in sugar around it
7. Or prepare a chocolate cream from chocolate cooked in cream, beat it into a mousse, and heap it high in a wide ring so the beautiful yellow pudding rises only in the center
