## Ingredients

- carrot
- kohlrabi
- butter
- fresh parsley
- flour
- good broth
- sugar
- calf brains
- bacon fat
- green parsley
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- broth
- wafer cookies
- fresh water
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Clean the carrot and kohlrabi and cut into slices
2. With a cutter make small crescents from each variety in equal quantities
3. Melt a piece of butter the size of an egg and add the above with finely chopped parsley and braise until soft without browning
4. Sprinkle a spoonful of flour over it, braise a bit longer and thin with good broth
5. Add a piece of sugar and cook well
6. Serve with breaded brain sausages prepared as follows: after cooking the brains in salted water, place in cold water, clean from the thin membrane and chop finely
7. Melt a piece of butter, add finely chopped green parsley and the brains, a little salt, pepper, a few handfuls of breadcrumbs and a little broth, and cook everything well
8. Cut wafer cookies in half, dip in fresh water, place a small piece of the brain mixture on it, fold together, dip in beaten egg, flour and breadcrumbs and fry beautifully in fat
