## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams hazelnut mixture
- 14 dekagrams sugar for the frosting
- Egg white
- Few drops lemon juice
- White frosting

## Directions

1. Prepare dough as specified in recipe 766
2. Spread two knife-widths thick on sugar-sprinkled board in rectangular leaf shape
3. Cover smoothly with white frosting
4. Coat baking sheet with wax beforehand
5. Cut frosted hazelnut dough into sticks two centimeters wide and finger length
6. Place on sheet with knife
7. Dry in completely cool oven
8. Peel from sheet with knife and arrange neatly on plate
9. Finely pound and sift 14 dekagrams sugar per 28 dekagrams hazelnut mixture
10. Beat frosting with egg white and a few drops lemon juice in small bowl until stiff
