## Ingredients

- Spanish wind paste as made for cake number 705
- 8 dekagrams almonds
- 8 dekagrams sugar
- fine vanilla
- almonds and sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Prepare Spanish wind paste as made for cake number 705
2. Soak 8 dekagrams of almonds, rinse them several times, dry them and chop finely
3. Grind 8 dekagrams of sugar with fine vanilla and mix with the almonds
4. Draw small finger-sized stags on paper
5. Fill the pastry tube with Spanish wind paste in portions and pipe out following the drawn lines so that each subsequent line overlaps the previous one
6. Sprinkle densely with almonds and sugar
7. Place on a sheet pan
8. Bake in a gentle oven; the oven must not be hot so that they remain beautifully white
9. Leave in the oven until they separate easily from the paper
10. Heap high on a serving plate and present at table alongside ice cream
11. Can also be served as a pastry, but make them slightly larger and before serving, coat the side that was on the paper with some marmalade
