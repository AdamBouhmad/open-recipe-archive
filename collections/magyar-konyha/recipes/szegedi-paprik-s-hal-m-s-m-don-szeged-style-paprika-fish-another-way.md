## Ingredients

- small piece of sterlet
- small catfish
- carp
- red onion
- paprika (Szeged, bright red)
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well with a knife on the outside
2. If the fish has scaled skin, remove the scales; if it has smooth skin, scrape off the slimy part with a knife
3. Cut each fish open at the belly, remove the intestines and cut the whole fish into pieces two fingers thick
4. Place three small heads of red onion cut into thin slices in a pot for three fish
5. No fat is needed for this fish
6. Salt the fish, place it on the onion, sprinkle with one tin spoon of beautiful bright red Szeged paprika and pour on enough water to cover the fish
7. Place on high heat, cover and do not stir until serving, as the fish has weak flesh that boils very quickly and would fall apart if stirred
8. It needs to cook for twenty minutes and then it is done
9. When serving, use a deep plate and quickly pour the entire fish into it at once so the flesh does not fall apart
10. Serve a spoon alongside this paprika fish
