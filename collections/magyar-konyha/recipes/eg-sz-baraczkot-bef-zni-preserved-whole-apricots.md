## Ingredients

- apricots that are firm and yellowish, not fully ripe
- half kilogram sugar
- apricots equal in quantity to the sugar
- one glass of water

## Directions

1. The apricots should not be fully ripe, but rather firm and yellowish. For half kilogram of sugar, take the same amount of apricots. Cook the sugar with one glass of water, clean it, and cook until it forms heavy drops but does not thread. Wash the apricots, dry them with cloth, halve them, place in the sugar, and bring to boil three times on all sides so they do not become soft and their skin remains beautiful and whole.
