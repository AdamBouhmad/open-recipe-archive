## Ingredients

- 7 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 large herring, well-soaked and skinned
- carp roe
- fat
- butter
- 5 tablespoons sour cream
- green parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Cut 7 hard-boiled eggs in half lengthwise
2. Chop the herring into small pieces
3. Remove and keep the roe from a carp
4. Fry both the herring and roe in fat
5. Spread 5 tablespoons sour cream on a buttered dish
6. Arrange the eggs neatly on the dish
7. Place a piece of herring and fried carp roe next to each egg half until the bottom of the dish is covered
8. Add a few tablespoons sour cream on top of the eggs
9. Sprinkle with finely chopped green parsley
10. Place coals on top so the sour cream boils
