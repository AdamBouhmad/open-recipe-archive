## Ingredients

- 1 liter sugar peas
- 10 deka butter
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 1/2 liters meat broth
- butter the size of an egg
- a little glaze

## Directions

1. Wash the sugar peas thoroughly and stew them slowly with 10 deka butter, a little salt, and white pepper until soft
2. Press through a sieve
3. Continue cooking slowly with 1 1/2 liters of meat broth until completely thickened so that it flows off the spoon
4. During cooking, skim the fat off the soup well
5. Before serving, bring to a boil for one minute with butter the size of an egg and a little glaze
6. Pour over bread slices fried in butter
7. The soup should have a nice light color
