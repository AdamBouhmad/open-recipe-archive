## Ingredients

- three or four terrapins
- butter
- green finely chopped parsley
- flour
- pea broth
- black pepper
- nutmeg flower
- sour cream
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Cut off the heads, feet and tails of three or four terrapins
2. Scald, remove the meat, cut finely and cook in salted water
3. Place butter and finely chopped green parsley in a pot
4. Braise the terrapin well in this
5. Sprinkle with a little flour and pour as much pea broth as needed over it
6. Add a little black pepper, nutmeg flower and two tablespoons of sour cream
7. Cook slightly more and serve on fried bread
