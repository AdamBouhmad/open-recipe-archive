## Ingredients

- noble variety perfectly ripe fresh yellow melon
- sugar
- water
- 500 grams fruit requires 17 dekagrams sugar and 3 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Peel a most noble variety, perfectly ripe fresh yellow melon and cut into small slices
2. Calculate 17 dekagrams sugar for 500 grams fruit, cook well with 3 deciliters water, then cool
3. Pour over the arranged melon slices, bind the jars well and treat according to instructions 961
