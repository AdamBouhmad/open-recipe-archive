## Ingredients

- one and a half liters water
- vinegar
- fish pieces, cleaned and cut
- fish roe
- two bay leaves
- thyme
- lemon peel
- one onion cut into four parts
- salt
- nutmeg flower
- black pepper
- pale yellow butter roux
- sour cream
- fried bread
- pike liver fried in butter

## Directions

1. Pour water and a little vinegar into a pot
2. Add the cleaned and cut fish pieces and fish roe
3. Add two bay leaves, thyme, lemon peel, one onion cut into four parts, salt, nutmeg flower and pepper
4. Cook for two hours
5. Prepare a thin pale yellow butter roux and lighten the soup with it
6. Cook for another half hour
7. Add a few tablespoons of sour cream
8. Before serving the soup on fried bread, remove the onion, thyme and bay leaves
9. Pike liver fried in butter can also be added
