## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter boiled cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 10 dekagrams grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Bring 0.5 liter cream to a boil with 3 egg yolks and 10 dekagrams grated chocolate while continuously stirring
