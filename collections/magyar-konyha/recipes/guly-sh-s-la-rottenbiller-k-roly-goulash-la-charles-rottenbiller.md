## Ingredients

- beef
- red onion in generous amount
- salt
- one whole tomato
- potatoes, peeled and chopped
- dumplings (optional, as alternative to potatoes)

## Directions

1. Chop the beef together as is customary, add a generous amount of red onion, and then place over heat as beef is usually prepared. Once you have removed the scum, salt sufficiently and add one whole tomato. When the tomato has become soft, remove it, press it through a sieve, and return it to the pot. When the meat begins to soften, add peeled and chopped potatoes and when these are cooked, serve the goulash. Instead of potatoes, separately made dumplings can also be served alongside the goulash.
