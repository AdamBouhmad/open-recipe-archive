## Ingredients

- one kilogram lean loin
- salt
- one spoon cold fat
- paprika
- onions
- peppercorns
- three decilitres wine
- two decilitres vinegar
- cold water
- macaroni

## Directions

1. Cut slices from lean loin, beat them well with a mallet, and salt them
2. Place one spoon cold fat in a pot with a layer of meat
3. Sprinkle with a little paprika and thinly sliced onions
4. Add several peppercorns, then another layer of meat without paprika
5. Pour three decilitres wine, two decilitres vinegar, and several spoonfuls of cold water over the layered meat
6. Cover and cook until the meat is tender, occasionally adding a little wine and water if needed
7. Then roast until nicely browned, stirring constantly in the pot
8. Serve with its own fat and macaroni on the side
