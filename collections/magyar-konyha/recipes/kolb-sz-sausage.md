## Ingredients

- pork
- salt
- ground pepper
- marjoram
- lemon zest
- water
- beef or pork casing
- pork fat

## Directions

1. Finely chop pork meat, salt it, season with ground pepper, marjoram and lemon zest
2. Dilute this thick mixture with water and place in a sausage stuffer, attach beef or pork casing to the end
3. Quickly fill the sausage in this manner and fry it in pork fat
