## Ingredients

- 5 quinces
- 21 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- 9 egg whites, whipped stiff

## Directions

1. Cook the quinces until soft
2. Peel them and pass through a sieve
3. Place in a bowl and cool
4. When cooled, add the finely ground sugar and stir for a quarter hour
5. Slowly fold in the stiffly whipped egg whites
6. Pour the mixture into a buttered porcelain mold
7. Mound it into a cone shape with a wide knife
8. Make six indentations
9. Set in a water bath and cook for half an hour
10. Note: Do not place a metal cover on top, as the paste would burn
