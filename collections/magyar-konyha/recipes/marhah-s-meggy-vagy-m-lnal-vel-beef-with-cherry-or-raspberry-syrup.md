## Ingredients

- piece of lean top round beef
- butter
- sugar
- glacé or brown broth
- raspberry or cherry syrup

## Directions

1. Cook a piece of lean top round beef until tender
2. Melt butter in a pot, add a few pieces of sugar, and brown it beautifully
3. Add a small amount of glacé or brown broth and let it simmer
4. Just before serving, add one or two spoonfuls of raspberry or cherry syrup
5. Simmer well with frequent turning, ensuring both sides become beautifully glossy
