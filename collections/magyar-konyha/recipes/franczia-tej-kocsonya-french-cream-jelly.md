## Ingredients

- 21 dekagramm crushed sugar
- vanilla
- 10 egg yolks
- 7 deciliter boiled and cooled heavy cream
- 7 deciliter best quality cream
- 2 tablespoons rum
- 2 dekagramm dissolved isinglass
- almond oil

## Directions

1. Whisk together 21 dekagramm crushed sugar with vanilla, 10 egg yolks, and 7 deciliter of boiled and cooled heavy cream in a glazed pot
2. Place over charcoal fire and cook with continuous stirring until it reaches the consistency of thick pancake batter
3. Pour into another vessel and continue stirring to prevent it from breaking
4. Cool completely
5. Whip 7 deciliter of best quality cream and fold in vanilla sugar
6. Mix in 2 tablespoons rum and 2 dekagramm dissolved isinglass
7. Combine with the cooled cream mixture
8. Pour into a mold greased with fine almond oil
9. Freeze on crushed ice
