## Ingredients

- four and a half decilitres fine flour
- seven decilitres milk
- two whole eggs
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat four and a half decilitres of fine flour, seven decilitres of milk, and two whole eggs together well and salt lightly
2. Pour this mixture onto a buttered sheet, brush the top with butter, and let it bake over gentle heat
3. When baked, brush the top with butter
