## Ingredients

- four to five medium-sized raw peeled potatoes
- goose fat or other fat
- flour
- green parsley, finely chopped
- meat stock
- two tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Cut the raw peeled potatoes into cubes
2. Brown a small amount of flour in goose fat or other fat
3. Add the potatoes and finely chopped green parsley to this
4. Mix well together
5. Thin with meat stock
6. Cook with two tablespoons of sour cream and serve
