## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams English white lead
- 6 deciliters rose water
- 14 dekagrams alcohol
- 2 dekagrams camphor

## Directions

1. Rub 14 dekagrams English white lead finely with 6 deciliters rose water
2. Dissolve 2 dekagrams camphor in 14 dekagrams alcohol
3. Mix the two liquids together
