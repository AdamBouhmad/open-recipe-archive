## Ingredients

- Fourteen dekagrams rice
- Seven deciliters milk
- Nine dekagrams ground almonds

## Directions

1. Cook fourteen dekagrams rice in seven deciliters milk
2. Add nine dekagrams ground almonds when rice is well cooked
