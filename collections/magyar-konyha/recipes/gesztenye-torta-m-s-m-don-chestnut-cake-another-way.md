## Ingredients

- 15 large beautiful roasted chestnuts
- 21 dekagramm butter, beaten to foam
- 21 dekagramm ground sugar
- 14 dekagramm finely ground almonds
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Peel 15 large beautiful roasted chestnuts and chop finely
2. Add to the foamed butter with ground sugar, chestnuts, and finely ground almonds
3. Stir together and fill onto a cake sheet greased with butter
4. Bake slowly
