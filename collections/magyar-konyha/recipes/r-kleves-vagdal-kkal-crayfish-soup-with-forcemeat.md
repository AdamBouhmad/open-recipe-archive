## Ingredients

- thirty crayfish
- pike head
- toasted breadcrumbs
- good pea broth
- asparagus head
- stewed mushrooms
- pike liver
- pike meat
- crayfish butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Scald thirty crayfish.
2. From fifteen crayfish that have been crushed, remove the small pieces.
3. Flesh the other fifteen crayfish, setting aside their tails and claws.
4. In a mortar, crush two fried pike heads and several slices of toasted breadcrumbs.
5. Pour good pea broth over it and bring to a boil.
6. Strain through a sieve.
7. Prepare the blanched asparagus head, stewed mushrooms, pike liver, and finely torn pike meat, as well as the crayfish tails.
8. Melt a piece of crayfish butter in a pot.
9. Sprinkle a little flour over it.
10. Pour the prepared ingredients into it, add the strained crayfish broth, bring to a boil once more, and serve on toasted breadcrumb pieces.
