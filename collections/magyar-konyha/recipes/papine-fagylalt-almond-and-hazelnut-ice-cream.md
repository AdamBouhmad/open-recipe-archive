## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams soaked almonds
- 14 dekagrams large hazelnuts
- fresh walnuts (optional, in season)
- 1 liter well-boiled cream
- 42 dekagrams sugar

## Directions

1. Crush 14 dekagrams soaked almonds and 14 dekagrams hazelnuts to a fine paste
2. If walnuts are in season, peel them and add a good handful to the mixture
3. When everything is finely crushed, pour 1 liter of well-boiled cream, not hot but warm, over it
4. Mix the nuts well with the cream
5. Press through cloth
6. Dissolve 42 dekagrams sugar in it
7. Cool and proceed as with other ice creams
