## Ingredients

- five-times beaten puff pastry
- peach marmalade
- egg white
- finely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Roll out the bottom layer of five-times beaten puff pastry to knife-edge thickness
2. Spread with peach marmalade to knife-edge thickness
3. Lay another layer on top
4. Glaze the same way as described for apple slices
5. Cut an elegant shape in the middle of each slice with a fine knife, going through the top layer only, which looks very nice when baked
6. Brush with a little egg white and sprinkle with finely ground sugar
