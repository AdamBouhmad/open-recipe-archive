## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- milk
- grated Parmesan cheese
- butter (20-25 dgr more)
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Make a roux from butter and flour
2. Dilute with milk to obtain a fairly thick béchamel
3. Simmer the béchamel while stirring
4. Add one weak tablespoon of grated Parmesan cheese per three tablespoons of béchamel
5. Slowly mix in 20-25 dgr of butter while continuously stirring with a whisk to obtain a smooth sauce
6. After adding the cheese, do not stir over heat, only keep it warm
7. Season with pepper and salt
8. Use with pre-cooked fish, meat, or eggs by pouring the thick sauce over them, sprinkling with Parmesan cheese and baking in a hot oven for one to two minutes
