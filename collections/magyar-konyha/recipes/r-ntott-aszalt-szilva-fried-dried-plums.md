## Ingredients

- dried plums
- almond kernels
- flour
- sugar
- wine
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Wash the plums and cook until softened but still whole
2. Remove the pit and replace it with a blanched, pitted almond that has been soaked
3. Prepare a light batter from flour, sugar, and wine
4. Dip the plums in the batter
5. Fry in hot fat until golden
6. Sprinkle with sugar and serve
