## Ingredients

- two or three pigeons
- salt
- bacon slices
- red onion
- carrots
- flour
- good broth
- good vinegar
- lemon zest
- whole peppercorns
- capers
- two tablespoons sour cream
- butter pastry

## Directions

1. Season two or three pigeons with salt and prepare them nicely
2. Place several slices of bacon, red onion, and carrots in a pot, place the pigeons on top and braise until nicely browned
3. Remove them, sprinkle a little flour over them, brown, dilute with good broth and a little good vinegar
4. Add a little lemon zest and several whole peppercorns, cook the sauce well and strain through a sieve
5. Add fine capers and two tablespoons of sour cream
6. Place the pigeons back in and braise a little more
7. Decorate with a garland of butter pastry
