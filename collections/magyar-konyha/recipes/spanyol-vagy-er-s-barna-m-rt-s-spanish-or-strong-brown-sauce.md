## Ingredients

- all kinds of roots (parsley, carrot, celery)
- red onion, sliced into rings
- meat scraps (best is veal)
- diced fresh bacon
- ground black pepper
- bay leaves
- flour
- Liebig meat extract
- meat broth
- white wine (one good tablespoon)
- rum (one teaspoon)
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Brown all kinds of roots, red onion sliced into rings, meat scraps, and diced fresh bacon with a little pepper and bay leaves
2. Sprinkle with a little flour, as much as the fat from the mixture will absorb
3. Once the flour is browned, pour in meat broth strengthened with Liebig meat extract and cook well
4. Before serving, add one good tablespoon of white wine, one teaspoon of rum, and lemon juice
5. Press through a sieve
6. Bring to a boil once more and serve
