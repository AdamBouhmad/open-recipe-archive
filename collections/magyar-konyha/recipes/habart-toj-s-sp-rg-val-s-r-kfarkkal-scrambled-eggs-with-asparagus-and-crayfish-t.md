## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- salt
- asparagus bundle boiled in salted water
- twenty crayfish tails
- ground pepper
- crayfish butter

## Directions

1. Beat twelve eggs with salt in a pot until well mixed
2. Cut the boiled asparagus into long pieces
3. Shell twenty crayfish tails and cut each one in the middle
4. Add the asparagus and crayfish tails to the eggs with a little ground pepper and mix well
5. Put the mixture into melted crayfish butter
6. Stir frequently and break into small pieces
7. Serve in a small round bowl, piling it high in the center and garnishing around it with mushrooms
