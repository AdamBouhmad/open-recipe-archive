## Ingredients

- fourteen dekagrams of rice
- seven decilitres of milk
- ten dekagrams of butter
- one whole egg
- five egg yolks
- salt
- three egg whites
- butter sauce
- three cooked calf's tongues
- fifteen to twenty shelled shrimp tails
- six button mushrooms braised in butter
- asparagus or artichoke

## Directions

1. Cook fourteen dekagrams of rice in seven decilitres of milk, when cooled, mix with ten dekagrams of butter, one whole egg and five egg yolks, season lightly with salt, and finally gently mix in three egg whites beaten stiff.
2. Meanwhile, prepare a butter sauce, cut three cooked calf's tongues, fifteen to twenty shelled shrimp tails, six button mushrooms braised in butter, and a few asparagus or artichoke cooked in salted water into large cubes, place in the butter sauce and cook well.
3. When the sauce is completely cooked, mix two egg yolks with two tablespoons of cold broth and stir into the mixture, bring to a boil once more and let cool.
4. Arrange the bottom of a well-greased pot neatly with shrimp tails and asparagus heads, place half of the beaten rice into it, then the ragout in the middle and the other half of the rice on top, then place in a water bath where it should remain for three-quarters of an hour.
