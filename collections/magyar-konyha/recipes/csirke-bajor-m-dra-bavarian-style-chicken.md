## Ingredients

- 1 large meaty young chicken
- thinly sliced bacon
- sour cream
- white wine
- whole peppercorns
- nutmeg flowers
- fine breadcrumbs
- butter
- red onion

## Directions

1. Take a large meaty young chicken suitable for cooking with a cream sauce
2. Pluck and clean the chicken
3. Lard with thinly sliced bacon
4. Carefully cut into two pieces
5. Line a pot with thinly sliced bacon
6. Add a small amount of sliced red onion
7. Place the chicken pieces with the larded side up
8. Add a small amount of sour cream, the same amount of white wine, a few whole peppercorns, and nutmeg flowers
9. Sprinkle with fine breadcrumbs and dot with butter pieces
10. Place in the oven and braise until tender and slightly browned
