## Ingredients

- meat for pörkölt
- white wine, two decilitres
- pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare exactly as pörkölt with lamb meat. Just before serving, pour two decilitres of white wine over it, add a small amount of pepper, and when it boils, serve.
