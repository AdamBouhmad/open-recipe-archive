## Ingredients

- thin child's porridge
- sugar
- vanilla
- butter
- good cream
- more sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Prepare a thin child's porridge and sweeten well with sugar
2. Add finely crushed vanilla
3. Place a flat sheet on embers and heat it
4. Butter the sheet
5. Spread a small amount of the porridge on it and spread with a knife
6. Leave for a moment until it sticks
7. Scrape with the knife very thin, leaving only paper thickness
8. When it begins to color, quickly cut it and roll as small wafers
9. Fold what can be folded; break the rest into pieces and continue cooking until a good-sized wafer piece is ready
10. Prepare a thin porridge from good cream and sweeten strongly
11. Add a good amount of finely crushed vanilla
12. Cool and place in freezing container
13. Freeze with continuous stirring and working
14. When beginning to freeze, place the wafer pieces in the container and work well into the ice cream
15. When completely hard, keep in ice for one more hour
16. Turn out and decorate with the small wafers
