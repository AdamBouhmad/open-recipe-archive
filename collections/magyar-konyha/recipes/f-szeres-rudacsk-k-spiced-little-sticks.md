## Ingredients

- dough (as per recipe 685)
- white frosting
- pistachios

## Directions

1. Prepare dough as in recipe 685
2. Roll it out to the thickness of a knife blade
3. Cover with white frosting
4. Cut into sticks one finger long and wide
5. Decorate with pistachios
6. Place on a baking sheet coated with wax
7. Dry at gentle heat so the frosting does not lose its color but remains beautifully white
