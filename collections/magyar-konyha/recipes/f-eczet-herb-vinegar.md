## Ingredients

- tarragon vinegar
- basil vinegar
- bee balm vinegar
- rosemary vinegar
- lavender flowers
- several pieces of dried lemon and orange peel
- cinnamon
- nutmeg flower
- long pepper
- strong wine vinegar

## Directions

1. For this, take tarragon, basil, bee balm, and rosemary vinegars and lavender flowers, chop them coarsely together with several pieces of dried lemon and orange peel, and place all of this with a little cinnamon, nutmeg flower, and long pepper in a bottle, pour strong wine vinegar over it.
2. Bind the bottle with simple paper, perforate it, and leave the vinegar to absorb in the sun for 14 days.
3. Then filter through blotting paper and put it into small, well-stoppered bottles.
4. This vinegar is very good for minced meat dishes and sour sauces.
