## Ingredients

- 21 dekagrams ground sugar
- water
- 28 dekagrams soaked and lengthwise-chopped almond
- 1 lemon, zest
- 1½ lemons, juice
- wafer sheets
- sugar
- icing

## Directions

1. Cook 21 dekagrams of ground sugar in a brass pan with 4 spoonfuls of water
2. Add 28 dekagrams of soaked and lengthwise-chopped almond and roast it slightly
3. Rub the zest of 1 lemon on sugar and add it with the juice of 1½ lemons
4. Mix well
5. Spread the roasted mixture on wafers of desired size in half-finger width
6. Place on a wax-covered pan
7. Sprinkle with ground sugar
8. Bake at gentle heat
9. Quickly bend over a form or rolling pin into wafer shape and coat with icing
