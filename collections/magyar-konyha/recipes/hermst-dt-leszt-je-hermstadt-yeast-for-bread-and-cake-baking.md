## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram wheat malt
- 4 kilograms barley malt
- Water
- 21-34 liters of spiced liquor
- 14 liters with 10 dekagrams of good hops
- 7 kilograms coarse wheat flour
- Cold running water

## Directions

1. From coarsely ground wheat and barley malt, prepare the required amount of spiced liquor
2. Boil in a pot until only 12 liters remain
3. Before the liquid has reduced to this amount, add approximately 14 liters containing 10 dekagrams of good hops, then boil down to the stated amount
4. Strain the liquid and press out the remaining hops
5. Mix 7 kilograms of coarse wheat flour with enough cold running water to form a thick paste
6. Pour this into the prepared liquid and stir well together
7. Let this mixture stand in a place with a temperature of 14° R. for 30 hours, after which the yeast is ready for use
8. In summer it keeps for 3 weeks in a cool cellar; in winter for two months
9. To prepare fresh yeast, this yeast can be used to start fermentation
