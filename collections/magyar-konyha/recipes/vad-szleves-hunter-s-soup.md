## Ingredients

- Strong beef broth, completely defatted
- Partridge or woodcock, cleaned of feathers and innards
- Beef broth
- 4 egg yolks
- Nutmeg flower, a small amount

## Directions

1. Prepare a very strong beef broth completely free of fat, which should be strained through a kitchen towel and kept warm.
2. Pound a cleaned partridge or woodcock (with innards included) raw in a mortar very well.
3. Pass through a sieve together with a tablespoon of beef broth, 4 egg yolks, and a small amount of nutmeg flower.
4. Mix into the prepared broth and arrange into small game meat dumplings to serve.
