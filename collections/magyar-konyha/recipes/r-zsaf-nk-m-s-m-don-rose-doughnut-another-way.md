## Ingredients

- seven deciliters of flour
- seven dekagrams of butter
- six tablespoons of crushed sugar
- twelve egg yolks
- three tablespoons of sour cream
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Cut seven dekagrams of butter into seven deciliters of flour
2. Work well with a rolling pin
3. Add six tablespoons of crushed sugar, twelve egg yolks, three tablespoons of sour cream, and a little salt
4. Prepare the dough with a wide knife and work it well
5. Otherwise, handle it as with the previous doughnut
