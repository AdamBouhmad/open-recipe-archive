## Ingredients

- four eggs
- water (eggshell measure)
- flour (as much as the dough will absorb)

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs into a bowl, add water measured with an eggshell, and knead with flour until a stiff dough forms and becomes shiny from kneading
2. Divide the dough into two pieces
3. Roll out the dough slightly thicker than a knife's edge and cut the entire sheet into finger-wide strips
4. Tear these strips into small pieces with your fingers and cook in already boiling water
5. Cook only until they rise well to the surface of the water and boil for 10 minutes
6. Remove with a slotted spoon, drain the water well, rinse with cold water, place in a bowl, salt it, pour hot fat over it, top with cheese, a little sour cream, and nicely fried bacon cracklings, and serve hot
