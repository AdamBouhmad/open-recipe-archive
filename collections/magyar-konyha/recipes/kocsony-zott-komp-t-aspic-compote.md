## Ingredients

- sour cream
- preserved peaches
- sugar
- isinglass

## Directions

1. Whip 7 deciliters of sour cream into foam and place on a sieve to drain well
2. Finely mash 3 spoonfuls of preserved peaches with 10 decagrams of finely pounded sugar, add the foam spoonful by spoonful
3. Mix in 1.5 decagrams dissolved isinglass
