## Ingredients

- 42 grams crushed sugar
- Lemon juice
- Orange juice
- 28 grams peeled and sliced Masansky apples
- Rind of 1 orange
- Half a lemon rind
- 7 grams pistachios
- 7 grams blanched and swelled almonds
- 7 grams candied citronpeel
- 14 grams candied walnuts
- Wafer sheets
- Water

## Directions

1. Cook 42 grams of crushed sugar with lemon and orange juice instead of water in a brass pan until it threads
2. Add 28 grams of peeled and sliced Masansky apples to the sugar, constantly stir it while boiling so it becomes a paste without burning
3. Remove from heat and add the rind of 1 orange and half a lemon cut into large cubes, 7 grams of pistachios, the same amount of blanched and swelled almonds, the same amount of candied citronpeel, and 14 grams of candied walnuts, all coarsely chopped, and mix well
4. Line the cake leaf with wafers, make a ring around it and coat this as well with wafers, and fill with the jelly, which should be at least three fingers high
