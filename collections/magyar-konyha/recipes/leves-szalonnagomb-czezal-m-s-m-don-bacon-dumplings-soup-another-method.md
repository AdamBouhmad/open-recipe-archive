## Ingredients

- fourteen dekagrams of bacon
- bread roll
- egg-sized amount of butter or fat
- fresh green parsley
- salt
- two whole eggs
- one egg yolk
- fresh water
- bread roll
- flour

## Directions

1. Chop fourteen dekagrams of bacon and a bread roll into small cubes together
2. Melt the bacon slightly and brown the chopped bread with it until it turns yellowish, then set aside to cool
3. Whip an egg-sized amount of butter or fat, fold in a little finely chopped fresh green parsley and a little salt
4. Mix in two whole eggs and one egg yolk
5. Add the cooled bread and bacon mixture
6. Finally add a bread roll soaked in fresh water, well squeezed out and finely chopped, and as much flour as is needed to hold the mixture together
7. Test with one dumpling before making them all
