## Ingredients

- chicken
- beef broth
- anchovies
- fresh olive oil
- lemon juice
- strong vinegar
- lemon zest
- capers

## Directions

1. After nicely plucking and preparing two beautiful white chickens, let them stew until tender in good beef broth.
2. Finely chop two large anchovies and mix them with a little fresh olive oil, adding the juice of half a lemon and a little strong vinegar.
3. After the mixture is made, cut the cooled chickens neatly into pieces.
4. Dip each piece into the well-blended sauce and arrange neatly on a plate.
5. Sprinkle with finely shredded lemon zest and capers.
