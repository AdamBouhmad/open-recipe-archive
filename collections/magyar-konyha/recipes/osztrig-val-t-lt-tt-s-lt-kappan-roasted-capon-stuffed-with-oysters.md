## Ingredients

- four decagrams butter
- handful of breadcrumbs
- twenty shucked oysters
- salt
- mace
- capon
- flour
- good broth
- capon drippings
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Melt four decagrams of butter and mix with a handful of breadcrumbs, twenty shucked oysters, salt, and mace
2. Stuff the capon with this mixture
3. Roast the capon nicely
4. Prepare a golden roux from a little butter and flour
5. Add a spoonful of good broth, the capon drippings, twenty oysters, and lemon juice
6. Simmer well
7. Pour the sauce underneath when serving
