## Ingredients

- butter
- parsley
- puffball mushrooms
- 28 decagrams veal meat
- salt
- half a roll
- milk
- sour cream
- two egg yolks
- bread crumbs
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. In melted butter, braise finely chopped parsley, a handful of carefully washed puffball mushrooms, and 28 decagrams of veal meat
2. Once done, chop finely, salt, add half a roll soaked in milk and squeezed out, pound everything finely in a mortar, then add one or two tablespoons of sour cream and two egg yolks
3. Salt large puffball mushrooms and let them lie in salt for a while after washing them very thoroughly, dry them, remove the stem, fill with the minced mixture, attach the stem back, and place inside
4. In a pot, add finely chopped butter and a little green parsley, place the mushrooms on top, cover with a few pieces of fresh butter, sprinkle with bread crumbs, add a few more small pieces of butter, and bake slowly in the oven
