## Ingredients

- six masanszki apples
- sugar
- three spoonfuls fine flour
- six egg yolks
- ten dekagrams crushed sugar
- a little vanilla
- four decilitres heavy cream
- five egg whites
- three spoonfuls apricot syrup
- ten dekagrams finely crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Cut six masanszki apples into thin slices, sprinkle well with sugar, and place in a porcelain or silver pan
2. In another pan, mix three spoonfuls fine flour smoothly with six egg yolks, add ten dekagrams crushed sugar and a little vanilla, thin it with four decilitres heavy cream, which is gradually mixed smoothly with the flour and egg and cook with constant stirring until it becomes a paste
3. Pour it over the apples and bake in the oven for a quarter hour
4. During this time, beat five egg whites stiffly with three spoonfuls apricot syrup and ten dekagrams finely crushed sugar, beat foamily
5. Make a crown with finger width and same height on the concentrated juice so that it is only visible in the middle, smooth the crown nicely with a knife, make deep cuts with a coffee spoon handle so it looks nicely braided, sprinkle with fine sugar, and place in a cooled oven
6. Serve to the table after half an hour
