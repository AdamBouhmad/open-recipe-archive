## Ingredients

- celery
- cress (Kresse), endive, or rampion
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Cut celery into slices
2. Cook it and place in a bowl
3. Wash the cress (Kresse), endive, or rampion and place on a sieve on top
4. Prepare with vinegar and oil
