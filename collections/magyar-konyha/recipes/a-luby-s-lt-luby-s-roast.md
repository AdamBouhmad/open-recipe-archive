## Ingredients

- beef sirloin (500 grams), lean
- pork meat (500 grams), somewhat fatty, preferably from the shoulder
- veal sirloin (500 grams)
- salt
- fat (egg-sized piece)
- red onion (6 heads), sliced
- weak vinegar (1 glass)
- sugar (1 lump)
- small potatoes, whole
- bacon slices

## Directions

1. Take half kilo of lean beef sirloin and beat it as thin as possible with a mallet, being careful not to shred it but keeping it whole and nicely thin
2. Take half kilo of pork meat that is somewhat fatty, preferably cut from the shoulder, and carefully beat it flat without tearing it
3. Take half kilo of veal sirloin and beat it flat in one piece
4. When all three pieces of meat are prepared this way, salt them and place on a board with the beef slice on the bottom, the pork slice on the beef, and the veal on top
5. When stacked nicely, roll them together tightly so the meat takes on a cylinder shape
6. Tie it tightly around with clean, flat string to keep it from falling apart during cooking
7. Lard the smooth side of the cylinder with bacon slices in two rows
8. Place an egg-sized piece of fat in a pot and when melted, place the meat lengthwise in it
9. Add six sliced red onion heads and a glass of weak vinegar
10. Cover the pot and place it over even, strong heat
11. When the broth reduces but the meat is not yet browning, remove from heat and strain the onion broth through a sieve
12. Put it back in the pot with a lump of sugar and return the meat to it
13. Cover again and roast over high heat until the meat begins to brown slightly, being careful that the fat does not burn as this would ruin the pleasant flavor of the roast
14. When nicely browned, remove from the pot and remove the string
15. Cut into nice round slices, arrange on a long dish, and surround completely with browned small whole potatoes
