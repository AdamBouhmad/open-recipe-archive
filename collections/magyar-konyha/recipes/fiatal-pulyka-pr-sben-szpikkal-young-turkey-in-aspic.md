## Ingredients

- 1 young turkey, capon-sized
- carrot
- red onion
- fresh parsley
- cloves
- salt
- whole pepper
- 2 parts white meat broth
- 1 part white wine
- aspic (prepared separately)

## Directions

1. Clean the young turkey properly and place it in a large pot where it fits comfortably
2. Add carrot, red onion, fresh parsley, a few cloves, salt, and whole pepper
3. Pour in 2 parts white meat broth and 1 part white wine
4. Cook slowly over gentle heat until fully cooked but not falling apart, removing scum during cooking to keep it clean
5. Remove and cool it completely, cleaning it well
6. Prepare aspic as described in recipe 315
7. Pour two-finger-widths of aspic into a basin or deep mold that will hold the turkey
8. Set on ice and let gel completely
9. Place the turkey on the aspic, breast-side down
10. Pour aspic over it to about halfway and let gel
11. Once gelled, fill completely so the turkey is entirely covered and let gel well
12. Before serving, briefly dip the basin in warm water, dry quickly with a cloth, and unmold onto a plate
