## Ingredients

- Crumbly pastry
- Apples
- Broken sugar
- Little vanilla
- Fourteen dekagrams softened almonds
- Fine broken sugar
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Prepare crumbly pastry and roll out to knife-back thickness
2. Place on a large flat plate
3. Peel apples and cut into thin slices
4. Densely line the cake bottom with apple slices
5. Sprinkle well with broken sugar and little vanilla
6. Sprinkle fourteen dekagrams softened and finely chopped almonds on the apples
7. Again sprinkle fine broken sugar
8. Place another sheet of pastry on top, no thicker than the first
9. Press together well so the two layers do not separate
10. Brush the top with egg white
11. Bake in steady heat
12. Alternative method: cut apples into small cubes, mix almonds with skin, sugar and vanilla together, and add twenty-eight dekagrams raisins
