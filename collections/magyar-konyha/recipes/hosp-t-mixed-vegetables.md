## Ingredients

- kohlrabi
- carrots
- turnips
- flour
- butter
- broth
- goose fat
- thin bacon slices
- cooked pickled tongue
- braised savoy cabbage
- ham

## Directions

1. Chop kohlrabi, carrots and turnips neatly
2. Dust each with a small amount of flour separately
3. Braise in butter, pour a small amount of broth over
4. Stir carefully so pieces do not break apart
5. Line the bottom of a mold lined with goose fat with thin bacon slices
6. Add a layer of kohlrabi and a layer of cooked pickled tongue cut into thin slices
7. Add a layer of braised savoy cabbage and again tongue
8. Finally add the turnips
9. Place the mold in a steam bath for fifteen minutes
10. Turn out onto a plate
11. Line the edge neatly with cut ham
12. Serve quickly to the table after removing the bacon from the top
