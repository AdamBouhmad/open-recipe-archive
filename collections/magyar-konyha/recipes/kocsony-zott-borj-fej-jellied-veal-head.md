## Ingredients

- veal head
- salt
- water
- fresh parsley
- carrot
- 2 heads red onion
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cut the veal head in half down the middle
2. Cook it in salted water with fresh parsley, carrot, 2 heads red onion, and a little lemon zest until very tender
3. Remove it and place in cold water for a while
