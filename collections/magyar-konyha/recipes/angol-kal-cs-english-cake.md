## Ingredients

- 21 dekagrams butter, whipped fluffy
- 4 egg yolks
- 21 dekagrams sugar, grated on lemon and crushed and sifted
- 21 dekagrams fine flour
- 4 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 3.5 dekagrams currants
- 3.5 dekagrams raisins
- 5 dekagrams quince paste, cut into small cubes

## Directions

1. Gradually mix egg yolks into whipped butter
2. Add crushed and sifted sugar and flour
3. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites
4. Quickly add currants, raisins, and small cubes of quince paste to the batter
5. Fill buttered molds used for twice-baked bread
6. Bake at gentle heat
7. Turn out onto a sieve and cool
8. Cut into knife-width thick slices without cutting completely through
9. Sprinkle heavily with sifted sugar
10. Glaze with hot spatula
11. When sugar has hardened, cut slices completely through
12. Arrange on cloth-lined plate in a wreath or triangular shape of six to eight layers
