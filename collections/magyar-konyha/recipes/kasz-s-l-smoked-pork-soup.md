## Ingredients

- Smoked pork shoulder
- Sour sour cream, a good amount
- Egg yolks, a few
- Wine vinegar
- Bay leaf

## Directions

1. Cut the smoked pork shoulder into pieces and cook in plenty of broth
2. When cooked, thicken with good sour sour cream, adding a few egg yolks
3. Sour with wine vinegar and add bay leaf
