## Ingredients

- sour cucumbers
- light brown roux
- meat broth
- fresh cream
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Cut the sour cucumbers into thin slices as much as needed
2. Prepare a thin light brown roux and loosen it with good broth
3. Add the cucumber with two to three spoons of fresh cream
4. Add a little lemon zest and bring to a boil
5. The cucumber can also be grated
