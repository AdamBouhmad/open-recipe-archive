## Ingredients

- 100 grams butter
- 6 egg yolks
- salt
- 1 tablespoon ground sugar
- 36 grams yeast soaked in milk
- 2 deciliters lukewarm sour cream
- flour
- 90 grams almonds
- 90 grams ground sugar
- little cinnamon
- 100 grams raisins

## Directions

1. Mix 6 egg yolks one by one into 100 grams whipped butter, add little salt, 1 tablespoon ground sugar, 36 grams yeast soaked in milk, 2 deciliters lukewarm sour cream into a pot and mix well
2. Set aside while the butter and egg are whipped, and wait until the yeast begins to work, then strain it and slowly mix into the butter
3. Then mix in so much flour that it becomes a light dough but does not flow, being rather like funnel-cake dough
4. When you have stirred until it pulls from the spoon, cover and let rise in a warm place
5. Meanwhile finely grind 90 grams almonds cleaned with cloth with the skin, and well mix with the same amount of ground sugar and a little cinnamon, and chop 100 grams raisins together
6. When the dough has risen, butter the mold, fill it with half the dough and smooth it with a spoon dipped in flour, sprinkle evenly with the almond and sugar mixture, sprinkle the raisins on and cover with the rest of the dough, set in a warm place
7. When well risen, bake at gentle but lasting heat, turn onto a sieve and finely sprinkle with ground sugar
