## Ingredients

- chicken
- hard-boiled eggs
- soaked roll
- bacon
- black pepper
- saffron
- parsley
- chives

## Directions

1. For one chicken, grate three hard-boiled eggs, add one-quarter of a soaked roll, one-eighth kilo of bacon cut into small cubes, a good amount of black pepper, and a good pinch of saffron, along with plenty of green parsley and chives
2. Mix this mixture together and stuff the nicely fattened chicken with it, whose skin has been well lifted
3. The stuffing is not runny and must be pushed into the chicken by hand
4. When cooked, it is very fine, tender and extraordinarily delicious
