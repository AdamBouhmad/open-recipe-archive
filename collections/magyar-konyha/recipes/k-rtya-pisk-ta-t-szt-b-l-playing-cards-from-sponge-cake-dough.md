## Ingredients

- sponge cake dough (as per recipe 673)
- butter
- common dough
- raspberry or apricot marmalade
- lemon or orange preserve

## Directions

1. Make sponge cake dough as described under recipe 673, which makes particularly good and beautiful cards
2. Spread the dough onto a baking sheet coated with butter to a knife-blade thickness
3. Make a border around the edge of the sheet from common dough so the sponge cake does not flow out during baking
4. Bake at gentle heat
5. When the dough has cooled, cut into small playing card shapes with a sharp knife
6. Fill half of the cards with raspberry or apricot marmalade
7. Place the remaining leaves on top nicely
8. Dip the cards quickly into lemon or orange preserve using two forks, place on white paper, and as quickly as possible before the preserve hardens, arrange them with pre-made suit and heart shapes
9. This way make one eye, two eyes, three eyes up to ten eyes
