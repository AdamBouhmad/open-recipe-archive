## Ingredients

- 10 dekagrams lavender flowers
- 10 dekagrams rose petals
- 10 dekagrams jasmine flowers
- 10 dekagrams orange blossoms
- 10 dekagrams lemon peel
- 10 dekagrams orange peel
- 3.5-5.5 dekagrams cinnamon
- 3.5-5.5 dekagrams paradise seeds
- 3.5-5.5 dekagrams nutmeg flowers
- 3.5-5.5 dekagrams clove buds
- 2 dekagrams Peruvian balsam
- 9 dekagrams Florentine iris root
- 2 dekagrams benzoin resin
- 8 grams bergamot oil
- 7 centigrams musk
- 2 liters 65° alcohol
- 4 deciliters rose water
- 4 grams vinegar-spirit
- 20 drops ambergris dye

## Directions

1. Chop all ingredients finely
2. Pour 2 liters 65° alcohol and 4 deciliters rose water over the mixture
3. Allow to digest
4. Strain
5. Add 4 grams vinegar-spirit and 20 drops ambergris dye
