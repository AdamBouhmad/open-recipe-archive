## Ingredients

- beautiful lamb leg
- salt
- anchovy fillets
- thyme
- marjoram
- half kilogram rice
- salted water
- 42 dekagrams bacon
- thread

## Directions

1. Clean the leg thoroughly of its membrane, peel the meat from the bone, trim it slightly, salt it, make holes with a narrow knife and lard with anchovy that has been well soaked and cleaned of bones
2. Take equal quantities of thyme and marjoram, rub the leg well with these, place in a wooden vessel, cover with a fitted lid, place a stone on top and leave in a cool place throughout the whole day
3. The next day cook half kilogram rice in salted water until the grains burst
4. Cut 42 dekagrams bacon into fine slices and line the pot with it, place half the rice on it and cover the rice with bacon slices
5. Put the lid on the pot and seal it well with dough, braise for three hours at even heat and place a little coals on the lid
6. After three hours open the pot, remove the bacon, strain its fat and turn the whole thing out onto a large plate
