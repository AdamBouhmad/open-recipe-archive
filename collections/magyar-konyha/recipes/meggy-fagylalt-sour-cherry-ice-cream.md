## Ingredients

- sour cherries
- 42 decagrams sugar
- 2 deciliters water
- 3 lemons
- 4 deciliters cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the sour cherries clean, crush them in a mortar, and press through cloth
2. Cook 42 decagrams sugar in 2 deciliters water until the sugar is completely dissolved
3. Press the juice of 3 lemons into the cherry juice, mix well, and proceed as with other ice creams
4. If you want to mix it with cream, use 4 deciliters of good cream per 7 deciliters of juice, dissolve 14 decagrams sugar in it, and mix it with the cherry juice; otherwise proceed as with other ice creams, except that if cream is added, the lemon juice is omitted
