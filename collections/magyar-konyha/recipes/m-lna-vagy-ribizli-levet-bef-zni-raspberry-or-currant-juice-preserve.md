## Ingredients

- 1/2 kilogram clear juice from raspberries or currants
- 1/2 kilogram sugar
- 4 decilitres water

## Directions

1. Press the juice of these fruits through cloth, then strain through filter paper.
2. Cook 1/2 kilogram clear juice with 1/2 kilogram sugar and 4 decilitres water, clean it well and cook until it threads and throws large bubbles.
3. Add the juice, cook on a gentle charcoal fire for a good quarter hour and carefully remove the foam.
4. Test by dropping a few drops on a plate, and if it no longer spreads, the juice is sufficiently cooked.
5. This juice must not cook any longer as otherwise it would lose flavor and color; then cool it, pour into jars, and put in a cool place.
