## Ingredients

- cabbage
- a little red onion
- pike
- salted water
- a little butter
- two heads of red onion
- sour cream
- breadcrumb
- milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the cabbage in the usual way, thicken it with a little red onion and cook it down with the sauce.
2. Scald the pike with salted water, remove its bones and tear it into small pieces.
3. Melt a little butter, add two heads of red onion cut into thin slices and brown it nicely to a golden colour.
4. On a plate, arrange several spoonfuls of sour cream, a little butter and one portion of the shredded pike, salt it, then add some of the browned onion, then again three or four spoonfuls of sour cream, one spoonful of breadcrumb, again some of the fish, and so on until everything is used up.
5. Pour the last layer with milk and braised onion, place several pieces of butter on the breadcrumb, put it in the oven and cook for a quarter hour, then serve to the table together with the cabbage.
