## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters grape juice
- 42 dekagrams sugar
- common water
- juice of 3 lemons
- 4.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass
- 2 ripe, peeled apricots cut into 4 pieces
- 4-5 mirabelle plums
- ripe blue grapes

## Directions

1. Embed a ring-shaped mold with a center opening in ice
2. Remove ripe flavorful grapes from their stems and press the juice through cloth
3. For 7 deciliters of juice, use 42 dekagrams of sugar
4. Cook the sugar with a cup of common water until the sugar begins to thread
5. Pour the juice into the sugar mixture and blend well
6. Press the juice of 3 lemons and mix with 4.5 dekagrams of dissolved isinglass
7. Strain through stretched cloth lined with blotting paper
8. Fill the outer form's empty space to the edges
9. Leave on ice until firm
10. In the empty center, carefully arrange 2 beautiful ripe peeled apricots cut into 4 pieces, 4-5 mirabelle plums, and ripe blue grapes dusted with sugar
11. Fill with remaining jelly and freeze
12. Turn out onto a plate similar to other jellies
