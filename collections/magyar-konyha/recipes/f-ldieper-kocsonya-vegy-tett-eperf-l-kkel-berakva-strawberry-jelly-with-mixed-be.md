## Ingredients

- 42 dekagramm sugar
- 7 deciliter water
- 1.5 liter ripe cleaned strawberries
- 2 lemons
- alkormös for coloring if needed
- 4.5 dekagramm dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Cook 42 dekagramm sugar with 7 deciliter water until the sugar begins to form heavy drops
2. Add 1.5 liter of well-ripened cleaned strawberries and boil together with the sugar
3. Pass through a sieve and let drip
4. When cooled, add the juice of 2 lemons
5. If the color is not a beautiful pink, color with alkormös
6. Note that this jelly should be pale pink
7. Dissolve 4.5 dekagramm isinglass and mix in
8. Pass through stretched cloth and filter until completely clear, repeating if necessary
9. While this happens, bury a flat round mold in ice with a small round mold in the center, held down with a stone
10. Pour the jelly into the ring formed around the small mold and freeze for about one hour
11. When the jelly has set, pour hot water carefully into the small mold to remove the jelly
12. Once removed, fill the empty space with about 30 pineapple strawberries, the same number of beautiful wild strawberries, raspberries, and white and red currants that have been cleaned and sugared
13. Pour the reserved jelly over the fruit and return to ice to freeze
14. Unmold using the usual method
