## Ingredients

- parsley
- carrot
- celery
- onion
- pea broth
- butter
- 2 small tablespoons flour
- parsley, finely chopped
- egg dumplings or fried grated noodles

## Directions

1. Put parsley, carrot, celery, and onion in a pot and fill with pea broth, cooking for two hours
2. Melt a piece of butter, add 2 small tablespoons of flour and roast until golden
3. Add finely chopped parsley and brown it slightly
4. Dilute with the vegetable broth and cook for another half hour
5. Strain through a sieve
6. Make beaten egg dumplings and cook them in the soup, or prepare fried grated noodles by making common grated dough, melting butter with a little chopped parsley, frying the noodles until golden with frequent stirring, and cooking in the soup
