## Ingredients

- one and a half kilogram pike
- salt
- four anchovies
- fresh parsley
- half head red onion
- butter
- sour cream

## Directions

1. Clean and salt a pike weighing one and a half kilograms and let it stand for an hour.
2. Chop finely together four anchovies, a little parsley and half a red onion head.
3. Beat a piece of butter and add the chopped mixture, mix well.
4. Coat the inside of the fish with half of this mixture and tie it shut.
5. Roast it nicely.
6. During roasting, pour sour cream and the remaining mixture over it.
7. Once roasted, place on a plate with the sauce underneath.
