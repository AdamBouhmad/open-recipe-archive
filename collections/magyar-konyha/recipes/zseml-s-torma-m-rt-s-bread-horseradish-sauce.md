## Ingredients

- one bread roll, crust
- good stock
- fresh butter
- grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Grate the crust of one bread roll
2. Soften it in good stock and cook well
3. Smooth it so that no lumps remain
4. Melt a small amount of fresh butter and add the finely cooked bread roll together with grated horseradish to it
5. Cook for several more minutes
