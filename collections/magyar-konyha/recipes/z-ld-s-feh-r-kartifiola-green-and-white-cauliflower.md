## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- meat broth, well salted
- butter sauce
- ham roll
- thin bacon slice

## Directions

1. After cleaning the cauliflower and cutting the stem short, cook it in well-salted meat broth so that the florets remain whole and beautiful
2. Prepare a small amount of butter sauce
3. Cook the cut cauliflower stems in meat broth until so tender they can be pressed through a fine sieve
4. Mix the pressed stems with the butter sauce
5. Bring to a boil for a few minutes so it looks like light baby puree
6. Place this mixture on the bottom of the plate
7. Arrange the green cauliflower florets in the center
8. Place the white florets closely beside them so they form a cone shape about a finger-long
9. Insert the end of the ham roll with a knife under the floret
10. The thin bacon slice comes up to form a ribbon shape
