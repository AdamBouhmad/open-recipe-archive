## Ingredients

- 70 dekagrams Italian chestnuts
- 21 dekagrams sugar
- 2 deciliters water
- 3 tablespoons good rum
- 4 deciliters good cream
- finely crushed and well-salted ice

## Directions

1. Cook 70 dekagrams of Italian chestnuts until soft, peel them, and press through a sieve
2. Cook 21 dekagrams of sugar with 2 deciliters of water until it forms large bubbles while boiling and becomes thick
3. Finely beat the pressed chestnuts in a bowl with the cooked sugar
4. Add 3 tablespoons of good rum and mix well, making sure the mixture is very cold
5. Finally, gently fold in the stiffly beaten whipped cream of 4 deciliters of good cream
6. Immediately pour into the freezing container and place in finely crushed, well-salted ice
7. Do not turn, but let it freeze
8. During freezing, occasionally insert a stick around the edges of the container to mix the frozen mixture with the still-liquid part until the entire mass is frozen
9. Turn out, wrap with spun sugar columns, and place dipped chestnuts in the center
