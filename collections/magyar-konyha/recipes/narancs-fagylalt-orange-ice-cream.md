## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams sugar
- 2 oranges
- juice of 10 oranges
- juice of 3 lemons
- 4 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Gently rub 14 dekagrams sugar on 2 oranges so it does not become bitter
2. Squeeze the juice from 10 oranges and 3 lemons
3. Cook the 14 dekagrams sugar with 4 deciliters water
4. When dissolved, pour into the orange juice
5. Mix well
6. Cool completely and pour into freezing container
