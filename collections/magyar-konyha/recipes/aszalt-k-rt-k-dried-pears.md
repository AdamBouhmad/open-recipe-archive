## Ingredients

- ripe, large pears without defects
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Cook the ripe, large pears in a clean pot until they appear soft
2. Drain and peel them
3. Set aside the peels on a plate
4. Layer the pears in a press and press them
5. Place the press over a clean basin to catch the liquid
6. Let the pears press for a quarter hour, then press more strongly and let stand another quarter hour
7. Remove and place on a drying board, put in the oven for several hours until they turn a nice reddish-brown color
8. Cook the pressed pear liquid and peel with pieces of sugar until it becomes a thick syrup
9. Dip the pears well into this syrup and place in the oven to dry
10. Repeat the dipping in syrup two to three times, then dry thoroughly
11. Once cooled, layer in rolls and store in a dry place
