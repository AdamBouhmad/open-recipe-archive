## Ingredients

- pork fat layer and fat bed
- pork skin
- cooked millet porridge
- salt
- black ground pepper
- cooked red onion
- marjoram
- hot water

## Directions

1. Use the pork fat layer and fat bed, briefly boil them, and chop finely along with plenty of boiled skin
2. Mix an equal amount of cooked millet porridge with the meat and fat
3. Add proper salt, plenty of ground black pepper, cooked red onion as in liver sausage, and plenty of marjoram
4. Add a few spoonfuls of hot water to make the filling tender and easy to fill with a sausage filler
5. Secure both ends of the sausage with small wooden sticks and boil in hot salted water
6. To test if the sausage is cooked through, pierce it with a straw—if clear fat (not red liquid) comes out, the sausage is done and can be removed from the hot water
