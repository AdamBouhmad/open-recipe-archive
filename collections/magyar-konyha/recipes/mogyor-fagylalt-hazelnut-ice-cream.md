## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams large Italian hazelnuts
- 7 deciliters good cream
- 21 dekagrams ground sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the large Italian hazelnuts similarly to almonds
2. Clean in cold water
3. Grind to a fine paste
4. Boil 7 deciliters of good cream
5. Place the ground hazelnuts in a soup pot
6. Pour the cream warm over it
7. Let stand for one hour, stirring frequently
8. Add 21 dekagrams ground sugar
9. Stir frequently
10. When the sugar has melted and the mixture has cooled, strain through cloth
11. Pour into the container and treat as with the other ice creams
