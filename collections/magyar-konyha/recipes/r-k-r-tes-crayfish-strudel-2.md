## Ingredients

- 40 crayfish tails and claws
- crumbly white bread, grated
- sour cream
- crayfish butter, beaten until fluffy
- 1 whole egg
- 2 egg yolks
- 4-5 spoons fresh sour cream
- strudel dough
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Shell and coarsely chop the crayfish tails and claws
2. Mix grated crumbly bread with sour cream
3. Beat crayfish butter until fluffy and mix in 1 whole egg and 2 egg yolks
4. Add the moistened breadcrumbs and chopped crayfish tails with 4-5 spoonfuls of fresh sour cream to the butter mixture and stir well
5. Prepare strudel dough, stretch it finely and dry it
6. Brush with melted butter
7. Spread the filling on the dough and fold together
8. Brush the top with melted crayfish butter
9. Bake at gentle heat until golden
