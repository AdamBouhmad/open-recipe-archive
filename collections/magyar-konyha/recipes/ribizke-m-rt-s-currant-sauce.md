## Ingredients

- 7-72 liters of currants
- cold water
- a little butter or lard
- a piece of sugar
- meat broth
- sour cream
- a little flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the seeds from the currants and wash them in cold water
2. Stew them slowly in a little butter or lard, well covered, with a piece of sugar added
3. Pour meat broth over them
4. Mix sour cream with a little flour and combine the two mixtures
5. Salt lightly and bring to a boil
