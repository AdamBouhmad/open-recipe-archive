## Ingredients

- 2 deciliters beautiful white melted honey
- 1.5 bread rolls, finely grated crumbs
- 10 dekagrams almonds with shell, crushed
- good portion of cinnamon
- cloves
- finely chopped zest of one lemon and one orange
- crumbly, slightly firm puff pastry dough
- egg white

## Directions

1. Into 2 deciliters of beautiful white melted honey, place the finely grated crumbs of 1.5 bread rolls, 10 dekagrams of almonds crushed with shell, a good portion of cinnamon and cloves, and the finely chopped zest of one lemon and one orange
2. Simmer slightly, then cool
3. Form hazelnut-sized balls from the mixture
4. Prepare crumbly, slightly firm puff pastry dough, roll it out like noodle dough, and place the balls inside
5. Brush the dough around the filling with egg white, dip the pastry tool into flour, cut out the doughnuts with it, and bake at gentle heat
