## Ingredients

- 7 dekagrams butter
- 4 egg yolks
- Egg white foam from 4 eggs
- Salt
- 8 tablespoons fine flour

## Directions

1. Beat 7 dekagrams of butter until fluffy, mix 4 egg yolks into it, add the foam from 4 egg whites, a little salt, and 8 tablespoons of fine flour.
2. As a precaution, you can test one dumpling cooked in hot beef broth; if it falls apart, add a little more flour.
3. Throw the dumplings into the hot broth with a spoon, cover, and let simmer for eight minutes.
4. Place individually in the soup dish and pour the broth over them.
