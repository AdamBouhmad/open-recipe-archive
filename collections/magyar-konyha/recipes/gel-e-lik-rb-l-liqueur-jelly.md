## Ingredients

- as described in recipe 897 with modifications: either half a bottle of wine with the same amount of liqueur replacing the missing wine, or an equivalent amount of water with half a bottle of liqueur instead of wine
- suitable liqueurs including maraschino, cherry water, crème de noyaux, parfait d'amour, vanilla or curaçao

## Directions

1. If using the first three liqueurs, the jelly remains white
2. If using the last three liqueurs, color the jelly with cochineal or other red coloring
