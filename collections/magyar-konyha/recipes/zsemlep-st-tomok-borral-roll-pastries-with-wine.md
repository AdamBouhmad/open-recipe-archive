## Ingredients

- 12 small round rolls
- 10 dekagrams almonds
- 10 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- 1 lemon, finely chopped peel
- 1 whole egg
- 3 egg yolks
- 7 deciliters red wine, well sweetened
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Grate the crust of small round rolls, cut a small lid, and hollow out the center completely.
2. Fry the rolls in fat.
3. Mix 10 dekagrams almonds and 10 dekagrams finely ground sugar with the finely chopped peel of 1 lemon.
4. Beat in 1 whole egg and 3 egg yolks until frothy.
5. Fill the rolls with this mixture and replace the lids.
6. Place the rolls in a pan with 7 deciliters of well-sweetened red wine.
7. Place embers on top and braise until the rolls have absorbed all the wine but are not dried out.
