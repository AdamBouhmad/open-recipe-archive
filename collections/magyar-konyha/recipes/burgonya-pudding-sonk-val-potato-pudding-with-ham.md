## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams cooked starchy potatoes
- 10 dekagrams butter
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 28 dekagrams finely chopped ham
- salt
- grated Parmesan cheese
- hot butter

## Directions

1. Grate 28 dekagrams cooked starchy potatoes
2. Whip 10 dekagrams butter until fluffy
3. Add 3 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks one after another
4. Add the grated potatoes and 28 dekagrams finely chopped ham
5. Season with salt and stir well
6. Pour into a buttered basin and steam
7. After 1 hour remove the pudding, turn onto a plate, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and pour hot butter over it
