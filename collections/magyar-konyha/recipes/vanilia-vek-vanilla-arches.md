## Ingredients

- 14 dekagramm finely ground sugar
- 14 dekagramm ground and swollen almonds
- a small amount finely chopped lemon zest
- 1 teaspoon ground vanilla
- 2 dekagramm fine flour
- 2 egg whites, stiffly beaten

## Directions

1. Mix well 14 dekagramm finely ground sugar, 14 dekagramm ground and swollen almonds, a small amount finely chopped lemon zest, 1 teaspoon ground vanilla, 2 dekagramm fine flour, and 2 stiffly beaten egg whites
2. Spread the mixture onto a wax-coated baking sheet
3. Place in a not very hot oven
4. When nicely golden, cut into square pieces
5. Quickly bend over a small wooden roller
6. Repeat until all are bent, then place in a warm location so they do not soften
