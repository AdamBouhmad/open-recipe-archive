## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams soaked almond, cut in slivers
- 14 dekagrams finely ground and sifted chocolate
- 7 dekagrams finely ground and sifted sugar
- 3 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- wafer sheets
- butter
- icing

## Directions

1. Cut 14 dekagrams of soaked almond in slivers
2. Add 14 dekagrams of finely ground and sifted chocolate and 7 dekagrams of finely ground and sifted sugar
3. Beat stiff whites from 3 eggs
4. Place the almond, chocolate and sugar in a bowl
5. Stir in the beaten whites gradually, so the batter isn't too thin, as it must not spread
6. Cut wafer sheets two fingers wide and a span long
7. Spread the batter on with a knife
8. Dry at gentle heat on a butter-covered pan
9. When hardened, can be decorated with white or red icing
