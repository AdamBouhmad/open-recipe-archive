## Ingredients

- tender green peas with pods
- butter
- a few fried bread slices
- two or three fried pike heads
- good clean pea broth
- salt
- fried bread croutons or fried bread cubes

## Directions

1. Braise tender green peas with pods until soft in butter
2. Pound the peas together with a few fried bread slices and two or three fried pike heads in a mortar
3. Place in a pot and pour good clean pea broth over it
4. Salt well and cook thoroughly
5. Strain through a sieve
6. Place in a pot and bring to a boil once more
7. Serve on fried bread croutons or fried bread cubes
