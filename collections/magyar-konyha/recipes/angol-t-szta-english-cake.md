## Ingredients

- twenty-one dekagrams of fine small tea rusks
- preserved apricots or raspberries for filling
- butter
- seven decilitres of good cream
- egg yolks
- fourteen dekagrams of sugar
- vanilla
- cream sauce or chocolate sauce

## Directions

1. Fill twenty-one dekagrams of fine small tea rusks with preserved apricots or raspberries; coat one half, place the other piece on top, and when all are filled this way, arrange them neatly in a butter-greased mold
2. Beat seven decilitres of good cream well with egg yolks, fourteen dekagrams of sugar, and a little vanilla, pour it on top, and set aside for an hour so that the rusks absorb it well
3. Then braise for three-quarters of an hour
4. At serving time, turn it out and add cream sauce or chocolate sauce to the edge
