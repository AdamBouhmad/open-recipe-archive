## Ingredients

- seven dekagrams fresh mutton tallow
- one and one-half kilograms vegetable oil
- two kilograms hair powder
- seven dekagrams jasmine oil

## Directions

1. Mix seven dekagrams of fresh mutton tallow, one and one-half kilograms of vegetable oil, and two kilograms of hair powder together by gentle melting
2. During continuous stirring, mix in seven dekagrams of jasmine oil
