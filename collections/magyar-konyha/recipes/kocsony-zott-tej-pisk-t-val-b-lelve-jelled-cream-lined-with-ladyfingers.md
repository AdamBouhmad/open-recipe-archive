## Ingredients

- Round, flat mold
- Ladyfinger biscuits
- 1 liter good heavy cream
- 18 dekagrams finely broken sugar
- 3 grams finely broken vanilla
- 1.25 dekagrams dissolved isinglass

## Directions

1. Line a round flat mold all around and on the bottom with beautiful ladyfingers, with the shiny side of the biscuit facing the mold wall
2. Line the openings with a double row so the cream cannot break through anywhere
3. Whip one liter of good heavy cream into stiff foam
4. When the entire mass has been whipped into foam, mix in the finely broken sugar and finely broken vanilla
5. Mix in the dissolved isinglass once it has cooled and been strained
6. Fill the lined mold with this mixture
7. Set on finely broken ice
8. When it has frozen solid, dip the mold for a few moments in hot water, dry with a cloth, carefully unmold with a knife, and turn out onto a plate
