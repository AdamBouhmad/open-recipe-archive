## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams not too hard butter
- 42 dekagrams flour
- salt
- 18 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks, sieved
- 2 more egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons cream
- 10 dekagrams soaked and well-dried almonds, finely chopped
- 10 dekagrams ground sugar
- egg white
- vanilla sugar
- sweet whipped cream

## Directions

1. Work the butter with the flour and salt on a board
2. Add the finely ground sugar, the 4 sieved hard egg yolks, and combine well
3. Add 2 more egg yolks and 2 tablespoons cream
4. Work everything into dough with a wide knife
5. Roll out to knife-blade thickness
6. Cut into strips two centimeters wide with a fluted cutter
7. Coat an ell-long metal form the width of half a bottle neck with butter
8. Wrap the strip around it so the second wrap is always on top of the first and nothing of the form shows
9. When all are rolled, mix the chopped almonds with ground sugar
10. Brush the puffs with egg white
11. Coat densely with almonds and sugar except on the side where the form lies
12. Coat the form with butter
13. Place the puffs on it
14. Bake to a nice roll color
15. Carefully remove from the form
16. Coat with vanilla sugar
17. Fill the opening with sweet whipped cream so the cream shows on both sides
18. Arrange neatly on a plate
