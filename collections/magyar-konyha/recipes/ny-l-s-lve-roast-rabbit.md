## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- salt
- vinegar
- water
- red onion
- lemon juice
- peppercorns
- lard
- cream
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. After removing the entrails and cleaning the rabbit thoroughly, remove its blue skin and salt it well.
2. Prepare a marinade from vinegar, water, red onion, lemon juice, and a few peppercorns; boil this together for half an hour.
3. Place the rabbit in this hot marinade and leave it for one to two days.
4. Before roasting, lard the back and thighs with lard, then roast slowly, basting frequently with the marinade.
5. When the rabbit is halfway cooked, pour a few spoonfuls of cream over it.
6. When fully roasted and placed on the plate, surround it with the thinnest possible lemon slices from which the seeds have been removed.
7. Strain the sauce and pour it under the rabbit.
