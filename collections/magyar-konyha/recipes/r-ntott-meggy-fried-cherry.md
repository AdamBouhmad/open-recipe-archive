## Ingredients

- Beautiful ripe cherries
- Flour
- Sugar
- Wine
- Fat

## Directions

1. Tie beautiful ripe cherries together in groups of four
2. Cut their stems evenly
3. Prepare a thin batter from flour, sugar, and wine as thin as breadcrumb pastry
4. Dip the cherries well into this
5. Put in hot fat and shake the pan so they don't settle too quickly
6. Place on a strainer and put absorbent paper underneath
