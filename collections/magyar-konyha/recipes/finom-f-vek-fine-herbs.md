## Ingredients

- several button mushrooms, finely chopped
- green parsley, finely chopped
- red onion, finely chopped
- butter in small amount

## Directions

1. Mix together the finely chopped mushrooms, parsley, and red onion
2. Combine with a small amount of butter
3. Gently simmer
4. Use with various dishes
