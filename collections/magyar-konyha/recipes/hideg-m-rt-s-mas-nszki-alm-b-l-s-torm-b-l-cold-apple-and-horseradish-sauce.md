## Ingredients

- four to six Massendie apples
- a small piece of horseradish
- a spoonful of crushed sugar
- white vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and grate four to six Massendie apples
2. Grate a small piece of horseradish and place it in a bowl
3. Mix well with the grated apple and a spoonful of crushed sugar
4. Thin with white vinegar
5. Use twice as many apples as horseradish
