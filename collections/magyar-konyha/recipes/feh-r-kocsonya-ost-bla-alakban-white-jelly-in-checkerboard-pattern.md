## Ingredients

- white jelly
- 2 tablets grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Prepare white jelly and pour into a flat rectangular dish
2. Place on ice and freeze completely
3. When well frozen, mark in advance and cut lengthwise six times and widthwise six times to form equal square cubes
4. Carefully remove every other cube with a spoon handle, leaving alternating cubes in place
5. Cook the removed cubes with 2 tablets of grated chocolate
6. When cooled, fill the empty spaces with the chocolate mixture
7. Return to ice and freeze again to form a checkerboard pattern
