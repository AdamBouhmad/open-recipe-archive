## Ingredients

- one fine pheasant
- lard
- ham
- lemon peel
- butter
- ham
- mushrooms, finely chopped
- flour
- brown stock
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Bone the pheasant while keeping it whole, stuff it, and restore it to its original shape.
2. Wrap it in paper with lard, ham, and lemon peel, and place on a buttered pot lid.
3. Put it in the oven.
4. To prepare the sauce: warm a small amount of butter in a pot, add ham and finely chopped mushrooms, sprinkle in a small amount of flour, and pour brown stock over it.
5. Simmer briefly, skim off the fat, and add lemon juice to make it tart.
6. Pour this sauce under the pheasant when serving.
