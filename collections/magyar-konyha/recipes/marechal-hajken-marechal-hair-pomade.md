## Ingredients

- one kilogram mutton tallow
- sixty-five dekagrams vegetable oil
- five hundred grams hair powder
- thirteen dekagrams jasmine oil
- two dekagrams cedarwood oil
- three dekagrams bergamot oil
- one dekagram orange blossom oil
- two dekagrams clove oil
- sixteen drops of cinnamon oil

## Directions

1. Combine one kilogram of mutton tallow with sixty-five dekagrams of vegetable oil, five hundred grams of hair powder, thirteen dekagrams of jasmine oil, two dekagrams of cedarwood oil, three dekagrams of bergamot oil, one dekagram of orange blossom oil, two dekagrams of clove oil, and sixteen drops of cinnamon oil
