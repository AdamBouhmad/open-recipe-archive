## Ingredients

- 10 dekagrams of the finest currants or raspberries
- fruit juice
- 14 dekagrams sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean and wash 10 dekagrams of the finest currants or raspberries and dry them on a sieve
2. At the moment of placing the cake in the oven, fold the fruit into the batter, pour into the butter-greased pan, and immediately place in the oven to prevent the fruit from sinking to the bottom
3. For lighter fruits like raspberries this method works well, but for heavy cherries it is difficult
4. Press out 7 decilitres of juice from the fruit and cook it with 14 dekagrams sugar until thick, cool it, turn the cake out onto a plate, and coat it with the syrup, which should have the appearance of light jelly
5. This cake can be made either baked in a pan or in a baker's oven
