## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters boiled thick cream
- 21 dekagrams powdered sugar
- 3 grams powdered vanilla
- 10 egg yolks
- 4 deciliters good cream
- 4.5 dekagrams dissolved isinglass
- candied cherries
- green almonds
- candied pear slices (half yellow, half red)
- large candied rowan berries

## Directions

1. When 7 deciliters of boiled thick cream has cooled, mix in 21 dekagrams powdered sugar, 3 grams powdered vanilla, and 10 egg yolks in a pot
2. Place on charcoal fire and stir constantly until it begins to thicken
3. Remove quickly from heat, pour into another pot, and continue stirring to prevent curdling
4. Let cool completely
5. Whip 4 deciliters of good cream on cracked ice with a whisk until stiff
6. Cool 4.5 dekagrams of dissolved isinglass completely
7. When the egg mixture is completely cold and thickened, fold in the whipped cream and isinglass so the entire mixture becomes foamy
8. Add more powdered sugar if needed
9. Coat the mold with fine fresh almond oil
10. Prepare candied cherries (half yellow, half red), cut pear slices into thin leaves and cut out circles and crescents with small cutters, also cut thin strips for stems, and prepare large candied rowan berries
11. Arrange a beautiful wreath of pear, rowan, and cherry around the mold's circumference
12. Cut green almonds into leaves and arrange a beautiful rose or star in the center with almond and red pear
13. Fill the mold halfway with foamy mixture
14. Place in cracked ice, cover the mold, and place ice on top so it freezes quickly
15. If too hard and fruit sinks, place remaining fruit (except rowan) in the center
16. Bury the mold well in ice and let freeze through
17. After 2-3 hours, dip the mold briefly in warm water and turn out
