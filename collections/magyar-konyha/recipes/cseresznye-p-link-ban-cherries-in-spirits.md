## Ingredients

- one quarter liter fresh squeezed raspberry juice
- seven deciliters spirits
- eighty-four dekagrams sugar
- large black sour cherries with stems removed

## Directions

1. Mix the fresh squeezed raspberry juice well with the spirits
2. Completely dissolve the sugar in this mixture
3. Pour into a glass jar
4. Add as many large black sour cherries as needed so that the spirits still rises two finger-widths above the cherries
5. Tie the jar closed and store in a cool place
