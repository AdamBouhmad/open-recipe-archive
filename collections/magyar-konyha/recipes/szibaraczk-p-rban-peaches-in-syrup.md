## Ingredients

- beautiful ripe but not soft garden peaches
- sugar
- water
- 9 dekagrams sugar per jar

## Directions

1. Peel beautiful ripe but not soft garden peaches thinly and cut them in half
2. Arrange them neatly in jars so that each peach half falls on top of the already placed one, until the jars are filled to the neck
3. Calculate 9 dekagrams sugar per jar and cook with enough fresh water to fill the prepared jars, then cool and pour over
4. Arrange and bind as described in instructions 961, let boil for 2 minutes only
5. Once the peaches appear soft, do not cool them in the basin but quickly wrap the jars in warmed cloths and set aside
6. Once completely cooled, clean and store as with the others
