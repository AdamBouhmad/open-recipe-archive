## Ingredients

- crayfish
- salt
- parsley
- caraway seeds
- rice
- butter
- 30 crayfish tails
- egg yolk
- sour cream
- white wine

## Directions

1. Cook the crayfish with salt, parsley, and caraway seeds
2. Clean and shell the crayfish
3. Braise rice in butter with finely chopped parsley
4. Chop 30 crayfish tails coarsely and add with salt
5. Cool the mixture
6. Mix with egg yolk and three tablespoons of sour cream
7. Stuff the crayfish shells with this mixture
8. Replace the lower shell so the crayfish retains its whole shape
9. Press the shell firmly together
10. Place on a platter
11. Add six tablespoons of sour cream, several tablespoons of white wine, and caraway seeds
12. Place hot coals above and below the crayfish
13. Bring to a boil and the dish is ready
