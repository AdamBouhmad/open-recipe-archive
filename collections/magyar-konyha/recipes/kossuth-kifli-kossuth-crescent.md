## Ingredients

- 25 dekagrams butter
- 25 dekagrams powdered sugar
- egg yolks
- 25 dekagrams light flour
- 3 egg whites
- grated lemon peel
- 1 egg white for brushing
- chopped almonds
- vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Beat 25 dekagrams butter and 25 dekagrams powdered sugar with egg yolks until frothy.
2. Slowly add 25 dekagrams light flour.
3. Add the stiffly beaten whites of 3 eggs.
4. Add grated lemon peel.
5. Pour into a 25 cm square pan, spread with a knife, brush with 1 egg white, sprinkle with chopped almonds, and bake carefully.
6. Keep it pale, do not brown.
7. When done, cut into crescent shapes with a pastry cutter.
8. Place on a sieve and when cooled, arrange on a plate and sprinkle with vanilla sugar.
