## Ingredients

- 1 lemon, juice
- 14 dekagrams finely crushed sugar
- 14 dekagrams finely chopped almonds
- Wafers

## Directions

1. Extract juice from lemon
2. Mix with finely crushed sugar
3. Let stand for 12 hours
4. Mix in finely chopped almonds
5. Dry the mixture on wafers
6. When cooled, cut into slices
