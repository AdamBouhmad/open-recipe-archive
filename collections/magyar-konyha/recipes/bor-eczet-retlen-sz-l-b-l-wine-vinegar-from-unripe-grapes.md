## Ingredients

- Unripe grapes, picked before ripening
- Water

## Directions

1. Press the unripe grapes that have been picked early
2. Place the juice in a pot and set it over heat, boiling for half an hour while carefully skimming off the foam
3. Pour into bottles and place in the sun for several days until fermentation begins
4. Pour into clean bottles, seal, and place in the cellar
5. This vinegar is especially good for chopped dishes
