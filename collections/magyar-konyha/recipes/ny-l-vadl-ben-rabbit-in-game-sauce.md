## Ingredients

- Rabbit, cleaned
- Parsley, a few sprigs
- Carrot, sliced
- Red onion, sliced
- Water, half
- Wine vinegar, half
- Hungarian pepper
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Lemon peel, a small amount of each

## Directions

1. Clean the rabbit and marinade it in the following marinade: chop into slices a few sprigs of parsley, carrot, and red onion
2. In a container, prepare half water and half wine vinegar
3. Add Hungarian pepper, thyme, bay leaf, and lemon peel, a small amount of each
4. Bring to a good boil and when sufficiently boiled, pour over the already cleaned rabbit and cover well
5. When cooled, bring to a boil again and pour off twice more
