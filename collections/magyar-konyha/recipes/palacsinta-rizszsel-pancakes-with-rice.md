## Ingredients

- ten dekagrams rice
- seven deciliters milk
- ten dekagrams finely ground almonds
- seven dekagrams butter
- two whole eggs and four egg yolks
- sugar
- zest of lemon-sugar rubbed orange
- two tablets fine chocolate
- three tablespoons apricot syrup
- four egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook ten dekagrams washed rice in seven deciliters milk until soft enough, adding ten dekagrams finely ground almonds during cooking
2. Thicken and cool
3. Beat seven dekagrams butter until foamy, add the rice, then beat in two whole eggs and four egg yolks one at a time and stir for half an hour
4. Add enough sugar for sweetness
5. Divide the mixture into three parts: the first remains as is but add zest of lemon-sugar rubbed orange; to the second add two tablets of fine chocolate; to the third add three tablespoons of apricot syrup mixed smooth and stir well for color
6. Fold stiffly beaten egg whites from four egg whites into each part in three portions
7. Butter a round mold, cook the pancakes as thin as possible and line the entire mold with them
8. Layer the orange-flavored portion, cover with pancakes, add the chocolate portion, cover with pancakes, and finish with the final portion of cereal, cover with pancakes and bake in the oven until nicely browned
9. When done, turn out onto a platter and serve at once
