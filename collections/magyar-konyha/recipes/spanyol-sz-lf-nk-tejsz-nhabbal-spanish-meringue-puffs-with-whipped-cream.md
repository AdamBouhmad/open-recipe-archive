## Ingredients

- 4 egg whites
- 24 dekagrams finely ground sugar
- a little vanilla
- pistachio nuts, soaked and finely chopped
- 4 deciliters cream
- vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks
2. Quickly combine with the finely ground sugar and vanilla
3. Fill a pastry bag with the mixture
4. Pipe half-egg-sized meringue puffs onto baking paper
5. Place on a baking sheet
6. Bake in a gentle oven until white and firm
7. Leave in the oven until hardened and easily peeling from the paper
8. Chop soaked pistachio nuts finely
9. Brush the lower edge of each meringue with egg white
10. Densely coat with chopped pistachios
11. Return to the oven to dry
12. Whip the cream to stiff peaks
13. Add vanilla sugar to taste and mix well
14. Carefully hollow out the meringue puffs without piercing through
15. Fill with the whipped cream
16. Stack two together with cream holding them
17. The green pistachio strip goes in the middle of the puff for a neat appearance
18. Arrange on a cloth-lined plate and pile high and neatly
