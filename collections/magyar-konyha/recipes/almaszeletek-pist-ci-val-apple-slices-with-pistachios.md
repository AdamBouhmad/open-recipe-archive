## Ingredients

- 28 dekagrams butter
- flour for five-times beaten puff pastry
- apple marmalade
- fine sugar
- preserved peaches
- finely chopped pistachios

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry by beating butter five times with flour, rolled to knife-edge thickness
2. Place the entire rolled-out pastry on a baking sheet
3. Spread apple marmalade on it to half-finger thickness
4. Sprinkle with fine sugar and bake in a good hot oven
5. After baking, spread preserved peach on the apple surface
6. Sprinkle with finely chopped pistachios
7. Return to the oven for two minutes
8. Once completely cooled, cut into pieces three fingers wide and four fingers long
9. Sprinkle with fine sugar and serve on the table in neat order
