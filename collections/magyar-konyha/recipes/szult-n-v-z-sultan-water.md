## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram purified alcohol
- 3.5 dekagrams Peruvian balsam essence
- 7 dekagrams clove dye
- 7 dekagrams tolu balsam essence
- 3.5 dekagrams jonquille essence
- 5 dekagrams hyacinth essence
- 5 dekagrams reseda essence
- 2 dekagrams ambergris essence
- 1 dekagram musk essence
- 14 dekagrams rose essence
- 14 dekagrams orange essence

## Directions

1. Add all ingredients to 1 kilogram purified alcohol
2. Shake well
