## Ingredients

- 500 grams apricots
- 500 grams sugar
- 1 glass water

## Directions

1. The apricots must not be completely ripe, but hard and yellowish.
2. Wash the apricots and dry them with cloth.
3. Cut them in half in the middle and place them in sugar.
4. Bring to a boil three times on all sides, so they do not soften and their skin remains intact.
5. Remove them with a silver spoon, cool them, and pour the syrup over them; the glass remains open.
6. After twenty-four hours, strain the syrup, bring it to a boil again, and pour it slightly warmer over the fruit.
7. Repeat this for three days; on the fourth day, boil the syrup again until it thickens, cool it, and pour it over.
8. On the fifth day, tie the glass closed.
