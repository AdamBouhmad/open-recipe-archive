## Ingredients

- fat or butter
- red onion
- paprika
- fish
- salt

## Directions

1. Heat a spoonful of fat or butter in a pot
2. Sauté three heads of red onion cut lengthwise until softened
3. Add a knife-point of red paprika for color
4. Add the chopped and salted fish
5. Cover and allow to cook
