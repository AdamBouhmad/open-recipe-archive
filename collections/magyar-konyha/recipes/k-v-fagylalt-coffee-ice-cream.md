## Ingredients

- 14 dekagrams roasted coffee
- 7 deciliters hot cream
- 21 dekagrams sugar
- 8 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place 14 dekagrams of roasted coffee in a soup pot and pour 7 deciliters of hot cream over it as soon as the coffee is added
2. Cover and let stand for half an hour
3. Strain into a pot into which you have already beaten 21 dekagrams of sugar well with 8 egg yolks
4. Place over charcoal fire and stir continuously until it thickens
5. Pour into another pot and stir for a while longer until cooled
6. Treat it like other ice creams
