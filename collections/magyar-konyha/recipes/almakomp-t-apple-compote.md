## Ingredients

- large uniform apples
- peach syrup
- water
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel large uniform apples, leaving the stem on
2. Dilute 2-3 tablespoons of peach syrup with water, diluting it enough for stewing the apples
3. Add a piece of sugar and squeeze in the juice of one lemon
4. Add the apples, bring to a boil without letting them fall apart
5. Place on a dish and add the sauce beneath
