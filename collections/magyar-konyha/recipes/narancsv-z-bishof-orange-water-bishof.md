## Ingredients

- one bitter orange
- half a sweet orange
- seven deciliters of red wine
- half a kilogram of sugar
- juice of one orange

## Directions

1. Finely peel the bitter orange and half the sweet orange
2. Add the peels to the red wine
3. Cover and let stand for eight days
4. Boil the sugar, dripping the juice of one orange over it
5. When cooled, mix in the strained wine
6. Pour this extract into bottles and add to warm red wine
