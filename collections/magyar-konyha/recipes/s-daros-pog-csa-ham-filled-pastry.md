## Ingredients

- sixty dekagrams of flour
- four dekagrams of good butter
- two egg yolks
- two tablespoons of sour cream
- salt
- white old wine
- seventy-five dekagrams of finely chopped lean ham
- six dekagrams of grated Parmesan cheese
- sweet sour cream

## Directions

1. Work together with a knife sixty dekagrams of flour, four dekagrams of good butter, two egg yolks, two tablespoons of sour cream, a little salt, and as much white old wine as needed for crumbly pastry, then let rest in a cool room or cellar for two to three hours, then roll out to the thickness of a knife blade.
2. For the filling, take seventy-five dekagrams of finely chopped lean ham, six dekagrams of grated Parmesan cheese, and enough sweet sour cream to hold the mixture together.
3. A teaspoon of this filling goes into each pogácsa, which are first cut out with a larger and then, when the filling is already in, with a smaller pogácsa cutter.
4. Brush the tops of the pogácsa with egg and bake until nicely golden-red.
