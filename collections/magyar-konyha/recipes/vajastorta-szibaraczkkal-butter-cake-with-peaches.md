## Ingredients

- Puff pastry dough
- Butter
- Egg white
- 24 peaches
- Finely crushed sugar
- Sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Prepare puff pastry dough and work it as described in the previous recipe, rolling it to little finger thickness
2. Cut out a leaf with a warm knife, then cut a smaller leaf from it such that the first has a border two fingers wide
3. Stretch the smaller piece so it matches the crown in size
4. Place on a butter-coated pastry leaf and brush the edge with egg white as far as the crown requires, then place the crown on top, doing this with great care to maintain the round shape
5. Peel 24 beautiful peaches and cut them in half
6. Coat one half with finely crushed sugar and line the bottom of the cake all the way to the crown with these pieces
7. Bake in a moderately cooled oven for half an hour
8. If the ring has risen high, sprinkle it with fine sugar on top and all around and glaze it to a red-golden color with good flame fire which is kindled in one corner of the oven for this purpose
9. Cook the other half of the peaches in a syrup made from 140 grams of sugar until it is jelly-thick
10. Arrange the peaches neatly in the cake and when serving, pour the syrup over it
