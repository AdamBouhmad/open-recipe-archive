## Ingredients

- 28 dekagramm sugar
- coffee cup of water
- 28 dekagramm softened almonds
- zest of half a lemon, finely chopped
- juice of half a lemon
- 1 egg white, stiffly beaten
- butter for greasing
- wafer
- candied fruit

## Directions

1. Cook sugar with coffee cup of water in a pan until it threads
2. Grind the softened almonds, finely chop the lemon zest
3. Add the almonds, lemon zest, lemon juice, and stiffly beaten egg white to the sugar and mix well
4. Cook until it becomes a solid mass
5. Spread half of the mass on a pyramid-shaped mold greased with butter and lined with wafer and bake with gentle heat
6. Form the other half into wreaths, bake similarly
7. When both are removed from the oven, glaze the wreaths in a nice order onto the cake with fondu sugar
8. Place candied plum in each wreath and decorate the cake with icing
