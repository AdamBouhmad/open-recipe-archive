## Ingredients

- one tablespoon cooking fat
- one teaspoon sugar
- red onion, sliced into rings
- veal liver, sliced
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Put one tablespoon cooking fat and one teaspoon sugar in a pot
2. Add sliced red onion rings and veal liver slices
3. Sauté until the onion turns red
4. Pour in meat broth and let it boil well
5. Once it has come to a boil, let it cool
6. Strain and remove the fat
7. Serve with cooked peas
