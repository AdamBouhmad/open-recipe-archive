## Ingredients

- 28 dekagram butter for buttered pastry
- Large autumn apricots
- Finely ground sugar
- Egg white

## Directions

1. Prepare buttered pastry with 28 dekagram butter, fold it three times
2. Roll out to the thickness of a knife blade
3. Cut into equal rectangles with a warm knife
4. Peel large autumn apricots, halve them, remove the pit
5. Dip the apricot halves in finely ground sugar
6. Place in the center of the cut pastry
7. Brush the four corners with egg white
8. Fold up, press together and bake nicely
9. While still warm, sprinkle with sugar
10. For preserved apricots: remove from the jar a quarter hour before, strain the juice well
11. When placing in the pastry center, dip in ground sugar
12. Cook the preserved juice with sugar until thick
13. When the pastry is baked, pour through the small holes that form during baking using a coffee spoon
14. Sprinkle with sugar and serve quickly and hot to the table
