## Ingredients

- cleaned chicken
- salt
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Press down and remove the breastbone of the cleaned chicken
2. Salt it
3. Bend the legs in
4. Roast until nicely golden brown, basting frequently with butter
5. If desired, sprinkle with breadcrumbs during roasting
6. Roast a bit longer
7. Serve quickly to the table
8. Note: Chicken is more tender and flavorful if killed one day before use, bled, placed in cold water and left for twenty-four hours
