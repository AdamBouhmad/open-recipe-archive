## Ingredients

- 42 grams almonds
- 42 grams sugar
- Vanilla
- 5 grams crumbly crumbs
- 28 grams finely crushed chocolate
- Bowl of water
- 17 grams butter beaten until foamy
- 1 whole egg
- 15 egg yolks
- 7 egg whites
- Preserved peaches or raspberries
- Chocolate glaze

## Directions

1. Clean the almonds with cloth and crush them finely with their skins, pass through a fine sieve; crush what remains again, pass through the sieve and repeat until all is sifted
2. Crush 42 grams of sugar with a little vanilla and sift it together with 5 grams of crumbly crumbs, then mix everything together
3. Cook 28 grams of finely crushed chocolate with a cup of water in a brass pan with continuous stirring until it becomes completely smooth and child-porridge thick, then set aside
4. Put the almonds, sugar and crumbs into 17 grams of butter beaten until foamy, add 1 whole egg and 15 egg yolks, stir for half an hour
5. Now add the cooled chocolate and stir it in, then gently fold in the stiff foam of 7 egg whites
6. Butter two equal pans and fill each with half the mixture
7. Bake at gentle but sustained heat for three-quarters of an hour
8. Test with a knitting needle inserted into the bottom; if it comes out dry, the cake is baked; then cool the cake for a few minutes
9. Place one part of the cake on a appropriately sized cake leaf, fill with preserved peaches or raspberries, place the other cake on top and cover with chocolate glaze as found in the following recipe
