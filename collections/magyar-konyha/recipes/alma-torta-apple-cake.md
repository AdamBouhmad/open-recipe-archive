## Ingredients

- twenty-one dekagrams of fine flour
- fourteen dekagrams of finely ground sugar
- finely chopped lemon zest
- twenty-one dekagrams of butter
- one whole egg and one egg yolk
- sour cream as needed for moistening the dough
- five to six good large apples
- half a coffee cup of water
- a piece of sugar
- a little ground vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix together finely the flour, finely ground sugar, and finely chopped lemon zest.
2. Work in twenty-one dekagrams of butter.
3. Add one whole egg and one egg yolk.
4. Add enough sour cream to moisten the dough, which should not be very hard.
5. Work it together with a wide knife.
6. Fold it three times as you would puff pastry.
7. Peel five to six good large apples and cook them with half a coffee cup of water, a piece of sugar, and a little ground vanilla until they become a good marmalade.
8. Roll out the dough to knife-thickness and place on a cake sheet.
9. Spread the cooled apples on it in finger-thickness.
10. Make two finger-wide strips of dough around the edge to prevent the apples from running out.
11. Weave the entire cake with small dough rods.
12. Coat with egg white, sprinkle with sugar, and bake for three-quarters of an hour over lasting heat.
