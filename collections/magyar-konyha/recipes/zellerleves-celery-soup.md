## Ingredients

- one celery
- fat or butter
- nutmeg flower
- sugar
- meat stock
- egg yolks
- sweet sour cream
- bread

## Directions

1. Cut one celery into small thin cubes
2. Put a small piece of fat or butter in a pot and when hot add the celery and braise it slowly until soft
3. When soft, pass through a sieve, bring to a boil with little nutmeg flower, one piece of sugar, and little meat stock
4. In the soup bowl mix 3 egg yolks with 3/4 liter of sweet sour cream, pour the celery with hot meat stock over it on fried bread cubes or pea pasta
