## Ingredients

- plums
- 2 kilograms plums to 6 deciliters vinegar
- 60 dekagrams sugar
- whole cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Peel the plums either with a knife or by holding them in a strainer of hot water until the skin can be pulled off.
2. Once all are peeled, place in clean earthenware or glass jars.
3. For 2 kilograms plums, cook 6 deciliters vinegar with 60 dekagrams sugar, whole cinnamon, and cloves.
4. Skim it clean and pour cold over the plums.
5. Place the jars or crocks filled with plums into a vessel filled with cold water.
6. Set on fire and let stand for a quarter hour after the water boils, then let cool in the water.
7. After 8 to 12 days, cook the vinegar again and pour cold over the plums, just covering them completely.
8. Cover the jars with paper or bladder and tie very firmly.
