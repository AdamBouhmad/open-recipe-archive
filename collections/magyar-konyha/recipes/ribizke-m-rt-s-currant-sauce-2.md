## Ingredients

- one-half to three-quarter liter currants
- butter or lard
- sugar
- meat stock
- sour cream
- flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the seeds from the currants and wash them in cold water
2. Braise gently in butter or lard (well covered) and add a piece of sugar to it
3. Pour meat stock over it
4. Mix sour cream with a small amount of flour
5. Combine both mixtures, season lightly with salt and bring to a boil
