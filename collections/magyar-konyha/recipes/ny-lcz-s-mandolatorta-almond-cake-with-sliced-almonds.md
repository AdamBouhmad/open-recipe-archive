## Ingredients

- 42 dekagramm sugar
- 2 deciliter water
- 500 dekagramm soaked almonds, sliced
- 7 dekagramm chopped pistachios
- 10 dekagramm chopped citron
- 1 lemon with finely chopped peel and juice
- 3 egg yolks
- wafer for cake base

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water until it forms a thread
2. Add the sliced soaked almonds, chopped pistachios, chopped citron, and lemon peel and juice
3. Remove from heat and let cool slightly
4. Mix in three egg yolks
5. Line cake form with wafer
6. Shape the cake and bake at gentle heat
7. Decorate with frosting and candied fruit
