## Ingredients

- melon, peeled and sliced
- 1.5 liters vinegar per 500 grams melon
- 80 dekagrams sugar per 500 grams melon
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel the melon and cut into slices, place in a basin.
2. For each 500 grams of melon, cook 1.5 liters vinegar with 80 dekagrams sugar and pour over the melon.
3. Repeat this process.
4. On the third day, cook the vinegar with the melon together, then remove it and pack in a jar with cloves and cinnamon.
5. Cook the vinegar carefully with frequent skimming until slightly reduced and pour hot over the melon slices.
6. If the vinegar becomes thin again after a few days, cook it once more.
