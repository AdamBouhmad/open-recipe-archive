## Ingredients

- fine small sugar peas
- salt
- 28 dekagrams sugar
- 8 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Wash the peas and salt them
2. After half an hour, dry them with a clean cloth
3. Fill the jars, but not completely full
4. Boil the sugar with eight deciliters of water for about six jars of peas until dissolved
5. When the peas are filled in the jars and the sugar water has cooled, pour enough of it over the peas to just cover them
6. Tie the jars closed and place them in a water bath for two minutes
