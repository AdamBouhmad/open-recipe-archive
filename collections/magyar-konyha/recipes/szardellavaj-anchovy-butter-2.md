## Ingredients

- finely chopped anchovies
- two to three times as much butter

## Directions

1. Grind the finely chopped anchovies together with the butter
2. Press through a sieve
3. Used for various dishes, especially sauces and fillings. If to be enjoyed on its own, add a small amount of butter and vinegar during preparation
