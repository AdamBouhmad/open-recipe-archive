## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- butter
- green parsley
- anchovies
- sour cream
- breadcrumbs
- flour
- salt
- fat for frying
- lemon juice
- onion

## Directions

1. Peel hard-boiled eggs and cut a small portion from the bottom so they stand upright
2. Cut a lid from the top and hollow out the yolk
3. Crush the yolk finely in a mortar
4. Cream butter and mix with finely chopped green parsley, minced anchovies, crushed yolk, sour cream, breadcrumbs, and salt
5. Fill the eggs with this mixture
6. Dip the filled eggs in beaten egg
7. Coat half with breadcrumbs and half with flour
8. Fry in fat
9. Make a sauce from lemon juice, anchovies, green parsley, and chopped onion mixed into thin roux and thinned with pea broth, adding sour cream
10. Boil everything together until the sauce is thick
11. Place the eggs in a dish and pour the sauce over them
