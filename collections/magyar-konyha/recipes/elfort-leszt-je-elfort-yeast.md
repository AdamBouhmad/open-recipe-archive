## Ingredients

- 1 part finely ground barley malt
- 3 parts boiling water
- Good beer yeast (one sixteenth part)

## Directions

1. Pour one part of finely ground barley malt into a barrel
2. Add three parts of boiling water and stir for a while, then close the vessel with a lid
3. After standing for two hours, pour off the liquid from the malt
4. Boil for three hours
5. Pour back into the barrel and when cooled, add one sixteenth part of good beer yeast
6. After several days, fermentation is complete and the yeast has settled
7. If a small amount of surface yeast appears, remove it and later mix with the bottom yeast
8. Pour the prepared yeast into bottles; after 24 hours, pour off the liquid, cover the yeast with cold water, plug the bottles, and keep them in a vessel filled with cold water
