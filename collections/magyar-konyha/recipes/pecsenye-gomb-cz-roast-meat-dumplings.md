## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram beef roast, finely chopped
- 10 deka rice, cooked in salted and fatty water
- 1 teaspoon ground pepper
- zest of half a lemon, grated
- salt
- 15 deka white bacon, chopped
- 2 whole raw eggs
- hot fat for frying

## Directions

1. Chop the beef roast very finely
2. Cook the rice in salted and fatty water and cool it
3. Add the rice to the chopped meat, season with ground pepper, grated lemon zest, and salt
4. Chop the bacon and add it
5. Mix well with two whole raw eggs and form into dumplings the size of eggs
6. Flatten them slightly on a board so they cook through more easily
7. Fry them in not too much hot fat until cooked through
8. Can be served with a sauce of sour cream and lemon juice, or without sour cream; surround with macaroni or tarhunya
