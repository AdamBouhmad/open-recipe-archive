## Ingredients

- 4 beautiful ripe oranges
- lemon aspic (prepared as in recipe 808)

## Directions

1. Carefully peel 4 beautiful ripe oranges.
2. Divide each into 8 segments.
3. Pour lemon aspic into molds as with strawberry aspic, and carefully arrange orange segments in the center.
4. Fill completely with aspic and freeze.
5. Ensure fruit pits are removed and the aspic remains clean and transparent.
