## Ingredients

- lamb loin
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- garlic
- onion
- butter
- white wine
- water
- one egg yolk
- one dozen okras

## Directions

1. Take the loin, season it with salt, parsley, pepper, garlic and onion, all pounded together to form a paste
2. Wrap it in paper greased with butter and roast it on a spit over coals, removing the paper a short time before serving so it takes on color
3. Serve with a sauce made from the broth that dripped in the vessel placed under the spit, to which are added one glass of white wine, another of water, one egg yolk and one dozen okras
4. Bring to the fire and after taking two boils, serve with the roast
