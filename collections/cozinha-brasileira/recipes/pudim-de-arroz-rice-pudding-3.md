## Ingredients

- half a plate of rice
- one bottle of milk
- one pound of sugar beaten with five egg yolks
- a little salt
- clove
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil half a plate of rice with one bottle of milk until dry
2. Add one pound of sugar beaten with five egg yolks
3. Mix well, add a little salt, clove, and nutmeg
4. Pour this dough into a pudding mold, bake in the oven and serve with proper sauce
