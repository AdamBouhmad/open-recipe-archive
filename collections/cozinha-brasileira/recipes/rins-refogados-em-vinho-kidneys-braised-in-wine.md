## Ingredients

- Pig's kidneys
- Fat for frying
- Wheat flour
- Two glasses of white wine
- One spoonful of vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- One onion, chopped

## Directions

1. Cut the kidneys into small pieces
2. Fry them in fat for some time
3. Mix in a spoonful of wheat flour and stir
4. Add two glasses of white wine, a spoonful of vinegar, salt, pepper, and a chopped onion
5. Allow to boil over coals for half an hour and serve
