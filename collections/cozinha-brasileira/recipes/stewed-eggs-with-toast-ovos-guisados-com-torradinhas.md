## Ingredients

- quarter pound of butter
- twelve eggs
- cow butter
- bread crumb
- one glass of white wine
- two egg yolks
- nutmeg
- one spoon of lemon juice
- cornstarch
- sugar

## Directions

1. Melt a quarter pound of butter in a pan that can withstand the heat of the fire; when melted, break a dozen eggs into it and place the pan over coals
2. In another pan, fry in cow butter a portion of bread crumbs cut into pieces; when browned, place them over the eggs
3. Make a sauce of one glass of boiled white wine, two egg yolks, a little nutmeg, one spoon of lemon juice and a pinch of cornstarch; pour over the bread, place a little sugar on top and cover with a lid full of coals; after a quarter of an hour, serve
