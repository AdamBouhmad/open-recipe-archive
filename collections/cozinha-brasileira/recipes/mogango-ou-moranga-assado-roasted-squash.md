## Ingredients

- squash
- water
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Take a squash, cut it into eight or more pieces
2. Remove the seeds and cook in water
3. When the pieces are cooked, place them on a plate, cover them with breadcrumbs and place the plate over the oven
4. Let them roast and serve
