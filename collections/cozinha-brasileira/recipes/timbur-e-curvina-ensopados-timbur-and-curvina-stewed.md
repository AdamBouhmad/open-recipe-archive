## Ingredients

- water
- parsley
- onion
- root of celery
- salt
- pepper
- fish
- manioc flour
- beaten eggs
- melted fat
- tablespoon of fat
- chopped onion
- tomatoes
- mustard flour
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of water with parsley, onion, a root of celery, salt and pepper
2. Once hot, place the fish in this broth
3. Give it a few boils, then remove the skin of the fish with the scales, sprinkle with manioc flour, pass in beaten eggs, toast and sprinkle
4. Pour over the fish a portion of melted fat, and roast the fish over a grill on both sides
5. Fry on the other side one tablespoon of fat, a chopped onion, a portion of tomatoes, salt, pepper and a little mustard flour
6. Mix and add one chicara of water and one chalice of boiled vinegar
