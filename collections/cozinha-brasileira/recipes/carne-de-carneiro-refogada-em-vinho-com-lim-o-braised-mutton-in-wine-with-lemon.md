## Ingredients

- mutton meat, cut into pieces
- water
- butter
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- one glass of white wine
- bay leaves
- onions
- lemon juice
- sugar
- cooked raisins

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces and scald them in hot water
2. Braise them in a little butter and one spoonful of wheat flour until they are browned
3. Add one glass of white wine, some bay leaves, onions, lemon juice, sugar and cooked raisins
