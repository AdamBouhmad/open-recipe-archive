## Ingredients

- chicken
- quarter pound of chopped bacon
- cará
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- pepper
- three egg yolks
- polvilho
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take a chicken cut into pieces and place on the fire in a pan with a quarter pound of chopped bacon and a cará cut into pieces with salt, parsley, onion leaves and pepper
2. Let them boil until cooked
3. Thicken the sauce with three egg yolks and a pinch of polvilho dissolved in two spoonfuls of lemon juice and serve the chicken
