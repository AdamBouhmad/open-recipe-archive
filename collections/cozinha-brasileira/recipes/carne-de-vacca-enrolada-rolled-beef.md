## Ingredients

- beef
- salt
- saltpeter
- clove
- fresh bacon
- twine
- water

## Directions

1. Take the beef, cut it into thin and long slices and sprinkle over them salt, a little saltpeter and ground clove.
2. Cover them with equal slices of fresh bacon with another layer of the same seasonings.
3. Roll these two slices then, the beef should be on the outside.
4. Tie these rolls with twine and after two hours boil them in water.
5. To put this meat on the table, after it cools cut it into transverse slices and serve with a cold sauce also.
