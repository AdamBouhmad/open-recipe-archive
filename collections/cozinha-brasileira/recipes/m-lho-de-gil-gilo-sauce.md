## Ingredients

- three or four gilos cut into small pieces
- eight cups of beef substance
- a little salt
- four peppercorns
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Cut three or four gilos into small pieces in a saucepan
2. Add eight cups of beef substance, a little salt, four peppercorns, and the juice of one lemon
3. Boil and serve with roasted beef or pork
