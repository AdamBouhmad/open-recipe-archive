## Ingredients

- leftover lamb
- fern shoots
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- celery
- half bowl milk
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one tablespoon cow butter

## Directions

1. Cut leftover lamb into small pieces
2. Cook with fern shoots, salt, parsley, onion leaves, celery, and half bowl milk
3. Finally add one tablespoon wheat flour fried in one tablespoon cow butter until lightly browned
4. Serve
