## Ingredients

- one chicken, cut into four parts
- butter
- one bottle of milk
- half spoon of cornstarch
- salt
- nutmeg
- cinnamon
- four egg yolks
- one spoon of sugar
- one cup of wine

## Directions

1. Fry the chicken pieces with a little butter
2. Boil one bottle of milk with half spoon of cornstarch, salt, nutmeg, and cinnamon
3. Beat four egg yolks with one spoon of sugar
4. Slowly add the hot milk to the egg mixture
5. Return to the fire, add the chicken pieces and one cup of wine
6. When cooked, serve
