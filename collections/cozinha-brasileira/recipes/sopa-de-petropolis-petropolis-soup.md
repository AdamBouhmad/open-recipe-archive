## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 4 or 5 tablespoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt
- 1 bottle water
- 1 half bottle rich milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- cinnamon
- fennel

## Directions

1. Knead the yolk of two eggs with four or five tablespoons of sugar, a tablespoon of butter, and salt
2. Add a bottle of water, stirring constantly
3. Let everything boil a little
4. Finally, add half a bottle of rich milk, two tablespoons of sugar, a little cinnamon and fennel
5. Serve
