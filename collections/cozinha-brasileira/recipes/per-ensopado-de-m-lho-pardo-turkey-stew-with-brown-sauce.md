## Ingredients

- turkey blood
- one cup of vinegar
- turkey wings, giblets, legs and neck
- one pound of veal cut into pieces
- two tablespoons of fat
- one bowl of meat broth or water
- a handful of raisins
- some peeled and cut apples
- salt
- sugar
- nutmeg
- clove
- one cut onion
- one tablespoon of wheat flour

## Directions

1. When killing the turkey, collect its blood in a bowl containing one cup of vinegar
2. After cleaning the turkey, take the wings, giblets, legs, neck, one pound of cut veal and fry everything with two tablespoons of fat
3. In another pan over heat place one containing the blood and one bowl of meat broth or water, a handful of raisins, some peeled and cut apples, salt, sugar, nutmeg, clove, one cut onion and one tablespoon of wheat flour
4. While boiling, add to this sauce the turkey and veal meat already fried
5. Leave to boil for two hours
6. Serve with cooked macaroni or wheat flour balls mixed with eggs
