## Ingredients

- one guinea fowl
- thin strips of bacon
- salt
- liver of the bird
- half a quarter of finely chopped bacon
- a portion of bread crumb moistened in milk
- one spoonful of sugar
- nutmeg
- banana leaves

## Directions

1. Lard the breast of one of these birds with thin strips of bacon; sprinkle with salt, and stuff it with its liver chopped and mixed with half a quarter of finely chopped bacon, a portion of bread crumb moistened in milk, one spoonful of sugar, a little salt and grated nutmeg
2. Wrap the bird in banana leaves and roast it over coals and serve
