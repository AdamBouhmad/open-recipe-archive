## Ingredients

- bread sliced into slices
- milk
- egg yolks from beaten eggs
- sugar syrup at mirror point
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak bread sliced into slices in milk, drain them
2. Pass these slices through beaten egg yolks and place them in a pan with sugar syrup at mirror point
3. Remove them, place on plates, cover with sugar and cinnamon, and serve
4. Alternatively, soak the bread in white wine instead of milk
