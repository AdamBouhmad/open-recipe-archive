## Ingredients

- meat
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces and place in a barrel between layers of salt
2. After two days, remove and hang in the air and let dry
3. Place again between layers of salt
4. To use this meat, give it one boiling with water
5. Pour out this water and place the meat in cold water for twelve to eighteen hours
6. In this state it can be used
