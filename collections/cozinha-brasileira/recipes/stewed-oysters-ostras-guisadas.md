## Ingredients

- two spoons of butter
- one spoon of wheat flour
- little salt
- pepper
- half a bottle of white wine
- carapicús (pickled), chopped and cooked
- two dozen large oysters

## Directions

1. Melt two spoons of butter
2. Mix in one spoon of wheat flour, little salt, and pepper
3. Add half a bottle of white wine
4. Once boiling, add a portion of chopped cooked carapicús and two dozen large oysters
5. Heat through
6. Serve, placing the oysters on top of the stew
