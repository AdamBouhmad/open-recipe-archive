## Ingredients

- couves (cabbage)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- gordura (fat)
- farinha de trigo (wheat flour)
- agua (water)

## Directions

1. Chop a portion of cabbage and blanch it with a little vinegar
2. Separately, melt two spoons of fat with a spoon of wheat flour, and when hot, add the cabbage and a cup of water
3. Having boiled for a quarter of an hour over not very moderate heat, serve it with beef or pork
