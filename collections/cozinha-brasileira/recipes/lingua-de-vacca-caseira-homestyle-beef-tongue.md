## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- bacon strips
- broken Indian pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- Indian cloves
- chopped chives
- veal
- onions
- thyme
- bay leaf
- meat broth
- butter
- wheat flour
- pickled cucumbers
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a beef tongue, blanch it, and after leaving it for some time in cold water, remove the excess fats and superfluous flesh
2. Fill it with bacon strips seasoned with broken Indian pepper, salt, nutmeg, Indian cloves and finely chopped chives
3. Cook it with bacon and a piece of veal
4. Then add onions, thyme, bay leaf and Indian cloves
5. Moisten with meat broth and let it cook over a very gentle fire for five hours
6. When serving, remove the skin, cut the tongue in two parts, and place on the serving dish
7. Toast some minced onions in butter, mix with wheat flour dissolved in the tongue broth, add a little vinegar and broken Indian pepper
8. When this sauce is reduced to half, pour it over the tongue on which pickled cucumbers have been previously placed
