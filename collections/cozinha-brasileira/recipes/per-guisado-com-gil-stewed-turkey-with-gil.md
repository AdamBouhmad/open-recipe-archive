## Ingredients

- turkey pieces
- water
- salt
- cornstarch
- white wine
- cloves
- nutmeg
- giló

## Directions

1. Boil the turkey pieces in water and salt until cooked
2. Remove the pieces and separate the meat from the bones, cutting it into smaller pieces
3. Thicken the broth, and when little remains, add to it one spoon of cornstarch, dissolved in one cup of white wine, two cloves, a little grated nutmeg
4. Let it boil a little and place on a plate, garnishing it around with gilos boiled in water and salt, and serve
