## Ingredients

- twelve egg yolks
- one pound of ground crackers
- nutmeg
- one bottle of milk
- salt
- cinnamon
- a portion of raisins

## Directions

1. Beat twelve egg yolks with one pound of ground crackers, nutmeg, one bottle of milk, salt, cinnamon and a portion of raisins
2. Boil everything
3. When cooked and thick, place in a buttered mold
4. Bake in the oven
5. Serve with an appropriate sauce
