## Ingredients

- a portion of asparagus
- toasted bread slices
- a little sugar
- a portion of meat broth

## Directions

1. Blanch a portion of asparagus
2. Let cool and drain, then dry with a towel
3. Place in a tureen with toasted bread slices and a little sugar
4. Pour a portion of meat broth over it, enough to make soup
5. Serve hot
