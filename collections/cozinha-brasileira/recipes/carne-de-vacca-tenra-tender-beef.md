## Ingredients

- beef cut
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- Indian cloves
- garlic
- bay leaf
- bacon strips
- peppercorns
- Indian cloves pounded
- bacon strips
- cups of water
- paper
- sauce for meat

## Directions

1. Soak the beef cut for three days in vinegar to which salt, pepper, Indian cloves, garlic, and bay leaf have been added
2. Make holes the thickness of a finger, filling them with bacon strips covered with pepper and pounded Indian cloves
3. Place the prepared meat in a pan lined with bacon and a few cups of water
4. Cover and secure the lid with glued paper strips
5. Braise in the oven
6. Serve with some meat sauce
