## Ingredients

- pigeons
- giblets
- pork liver
- quarter pound of bacon
- salt
- peppers
- sugar
- cassava flour
- cup of wine
- eggs
- butter
- cheese
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Open the birds along the back and remove all bones
2. Chop the giblets with a pork liver, quarter pound of bacon, salt, peppers, sugar, cassava flour, a cup of wine, and six whole eggs
3. Stuff the pigeons with this mixture and place in a well-buttered baking dish or casserole with the remaining stuffing
4. Cover with a layer of cassava flour, grated cheese, and a bacon slice
5. Roast in the oven
6. When cooked, remove the bacon, turn the casserole over onto a plate so the pie comes out whole
7. Cover with sugar and ground cinnamon and serve
