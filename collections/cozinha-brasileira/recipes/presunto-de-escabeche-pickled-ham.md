## Ingredients

- one ham leg
- salt
- four ounces of saltpeter
- serpol
- bay leaf
- Indian cloves
- two bottles of white wine

## Directions

1. Remove the hip bone, half of the thigh bone, and place the ham leg in a suitable vessel
2. Season it with salt, four ounces of saltpeter, add serpol, bay leaf, Indian cloves, and two bottles of white wine
3. Turn it on all sides, but only once a day, keeping it there for seven to eight days before cooking it
4. Remove the skin, then wrap it in three layers of paper anointed with sweet oil or butter
5. Cook it either on the spit or in the oven, requiring three and a half hours to cook
6. Serve it with a Robert sauce or Spanish sauce with Madeira wine
