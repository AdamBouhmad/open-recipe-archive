## Ingredients

- lamb brains
- hot water
- four cooked eggs
- half a pound of ham
- two onions
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- two spoonfuls of fine cornmeal
- butter
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Scald the brains in hot water; after cooling, chop them with four cooked eggs, half a pound of ham, two onions, a little parsley, salt, pepper and two spoonfuls of fine cornmeal
2. Fry a little butter in a casserole; when hot, add the chopped mixture and when fried, turn it to fry on the other side
3. Cover with grated cheese and serve
