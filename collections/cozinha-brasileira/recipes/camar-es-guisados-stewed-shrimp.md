## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- portion of washed shrimp
- butter
- parsley
- scallion leaves
- wheat flour
- one cup of water
- sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Place water and salt in a pan and when boiling add a portion of washed shrimp
2. When cooked, shell them and braise in butter with parsley and scallion leaves, sprinkling with wheat flour
3. Immediately add one cup of water and the juice of a sour orange
4. Let boil a little more and serve
