## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of bacon, chopped into small pieces
- one tablespoon butter
- four pigeons
- handful of carapicús cut into pieces
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one cup of meat broth
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry a quarter pound of chopped bacon in one tablespoon of butter
2. When half fried, add four pigeons to the same fat, turning them until of good color on all sides
3. Add a handful of carapicús cut into pieces, salt, pepper, parsley, onion leaves, and one cup of meat broth
4. Let everything boil well over moderate fire
5. When the pigeons are cooked, place them in a serving dish
6. Arrange the innards with the carapicús around them and on top the sauce after adding one tablespoon of wheat flour mixed in vinegar
