## Ingredients

- two pounds of veal
- a dozen Nantes sardines
- half a pound of bacon
- a handful of carapicús
- a dozen olives
- some peppers
- two onions
- two cloves
- a handful of dried rusks ground
- four tablespoons of butter
- four egg yolks
- nutmeg
- cinnamon
- one tablespoon of sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Mince everything very finely and fill the cavity of the turkey's belly with this mixture
2. Knead a handful of dried rusks ground with four tablespoons of butter, four egg yolks, nutmeg, cinnamon, one tablespoon of sugar and a little salt
3. Fill the turkey's crop with this mixture
4. Wrap in thin slices of bacon
5. Place in a pan to roast in the oven
6. Serve with its sauce
