## Ingredients

- roots of two tayobas
- water
- salt
- butter
- grated cheese
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take the roots of two tayobas; boil them in water and salt
2. When cooked, peel them
3. Cut them into slices
4. Fry them in butter
5. Place them on a plate, sprinkled with grated cheese and plenty of sugar
6. Serve
