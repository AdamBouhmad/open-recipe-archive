## Ingredients

- turkey pieces
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- beets
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- bread
- onion

## Directions

1. Take the turkey pieces, coat them in breadcrumbs, and fry them in plenty of butter
2. On the other hand, take one or more beet roots, which are boiled in water and salt
3. Then they are peeled and grated, sautéing this mass with a little butter and a spoon of vinegar
4. Pour this onto the plate with the turkey pieces on top, covering everything with small pieces of bread and chopped onion, both fried in butter
