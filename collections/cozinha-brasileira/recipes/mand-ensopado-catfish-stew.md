## Ingredients

- one salted catfish, cut into steaks
- water
- two tablespoons of lard
- one tablespoon of corn flour
- peppercorns
- parsley
- two cups of milk

## Directions

1. Boil one salted catfish cut into steaks
2. When cooked, melt two tablespoons of lard and mix with one tablespoon of corn flour, peppercorns and parsley
3. Add two cups of milk
4. Place the fish in this sauce and add the strained and reduced cooking broth
5. Let boil until the broth is well reduced and serve
