## Ingredients

- chicken parts
- melted fat
- one onion, split
- parsley
- four cups of water
- one spoonful of cornmeal
- one spoonful of vinegar
- mangaritas
- water
- salt
- caruru

## Directions

1. Sauté the chicken parts in melted fat with one split onion and parsley
2. Add four cups of water, one spoonful of cornmeal and one spoonful of vinegar
3. Let boil over coals until the chicken is cooked and serve with mangaritas cooked in water and salt, placing in the bottom of the tray some caruru sautéed in fat
