## Ingredients

- one flounder
- three eggs
- one spoon wheat flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the fish perfectly
2. Mix three beaten eggs with one spoon of wheat flour to make a thick paste
3. Place a portion of butter in a frying pan and heat well
4. Coat the fish in the egg and flour paste and fry over high heat
5. Serve
