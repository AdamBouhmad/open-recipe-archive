## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- salt
- garlic
- peppers
- tomatoes
- three spoonfuls of fat
- prickly pears
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- water or meat broth

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbit into quarters and soak these in salt, garlic, peppers, and crushed tomatoes for twenty-four hours
2. Sauté with the same seasonings in three spoonfuls of fat
3. Add a portion of peeled prickly pears, mix and sprinkle with one spoonful of wheat flour
4. Add a bowlful of water or meat broth and serve
