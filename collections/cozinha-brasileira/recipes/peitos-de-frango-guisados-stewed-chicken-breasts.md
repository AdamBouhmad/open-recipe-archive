## Ingredients

- chicken breasts
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- wine
- meat broth
- egg yolks
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Remove breasts from chickens and cut into strips
2. Braise with chopped bacon, salt, pepper, chopped parsley, and onion leaves
3. When cooked, add a glass of wine and a little meat broth
4. Let boil a little more, remove the meat and strain the broth
5. Remove the fat, thicken the broth with two egg yolks
6. Put the meat back with peeled lemon slices, warm, and serve
