## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- water
- salt
- onion
- fat
- hardtack or biscuits

## Directions

1. Boil tomatoes in water and salt until they fall apart
2. Pour this broth into a pan in which an onion has been fried
3. Chop some hardtack or biscuits and add to the pan
