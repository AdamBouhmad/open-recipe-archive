## Ingredients

- a portion of tapir meat
- salt
- banana or caeté leaves
- peppers comaris
- lemon juice
- the juice that runs from the meat

## Directions

1. Take a portion of tapir meat and rub it with salt
2. Wrap it in banana or caeté leaves
3. Introduce the meat thus prepared into a hole made in the earth in which enough wood has been burned to heat it
4. Cover with earth, lighting another portion of wood on top
5. After two to three hours, remove the meat which will have acquired an excellent taste
6. Serve with a sauce made with comaris peppers, lemon juice, and the juice that runs from the meat when it is cut
