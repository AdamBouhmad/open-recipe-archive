## Ingredients

- meat
- water
- roots
- herbs
- salt
- 1 cup barley per jar of broth

## Directions

1. Bring the meat to a boil in as much water as is needed
2. Add roots, herbs, and salt
3. Remove the scum from time to time
4. When cooked, strain it
5. Add one cup of barley for each jar of broth
6. Let it boil for one more hour and serve at table
