## Ingredients

- any of these vegetables
- water
- salt
- milk
- two egg yolks
- grated cheese
- ground pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and boil any of these vegetables in salted water
2. Remove with a skimmer and dry with a towel
3. Boil afterwards in milk, thickening at the end with two egg yolks
4. Pour onto a plate, covering it with layers of grated cheese and ground pepper
5. Place in the oven until the cheese is browned and serve
