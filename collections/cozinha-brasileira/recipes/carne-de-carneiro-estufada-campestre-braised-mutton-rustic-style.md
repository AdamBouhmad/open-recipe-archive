## Ingredients

- one portion mutton
- garlic cloves
- salt
- pepper
- fat, one spoon
- nutmeg, a little
- two bay leaves
- one cup wine
- one cup water

## Directions

1. Take a portion of mutton, lard it by inserting some garlic cloves; season with salt and pepper, and place in a pot with one spoon of fat, a little nutmeg, two bay leaves, one cup wine and one cup water
2. Cover the pot tightly and let boil with low fire for two to three hours
3. Serve with some sauce or salad
