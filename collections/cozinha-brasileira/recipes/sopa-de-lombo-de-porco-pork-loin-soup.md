## Ingredients

- fresh pork loin
- salt
- garlic
- red wine
- fat
- bread slices
- Minas cheese
- sugar
- 4 or 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Place a fresh pork loin in a ceramic vessel seasoned with salt, garlic, and a little red wine
2. Leave it in this state for 12 hours
3. Then roast it in the oven or on a grill
4. After this, cut it into thin slices and place them in layers in a fat-greased pan, beginning with a layer of meat slices at the bottom, then a layer of bread slices above it, and above this last one a layer of grated Minas cheese, and so on until the vessel is filled
5. The last layer should be of grated bread crusts, over which sugar is sprinkled and 4 or 6 beaten eggs are poured over everything
6. Place the pan in the oven to cook the soup
