## Ingredients

- butter
- aromatic herbs
- wheat flour
- beef broth
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- cooked beef
- beef fat
- wheat flour for coating
- pork fat or sweet oil or melted butter for frying

## Directions

1. Sauté a portion of aromatic herbs in two spoons of butter
2. Add a spoon of wheat flour moistened with a cup of beef broth
3. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg
4. Leave to boil until the sauce thickens
5. Chop the cooked beef to which beef fat has been added
6. Pour the sauce over it and mix well
7. Form meatballs from the chopped beef
8. Roll in wheat flour and fry in pork fat, sweet oil, or melted butter
