## Ingredients

- one tapir loin
- lardoon slices of bacon
- one cup of wine
- one glass of vinegar
- two tablespoons of sugar
- two onions, cut
- one clove of garlic
- salt
- peppers
- two cloves

## Directions

1. Place the tapir loin in a pan lined with lardoon slices of bacon, with one cup of wine, one glass of vinegar, two tablespoons of sugar, two cut onions, one clove of garlic, salt, peppers, and two cloves
2. Leave the meat in these seasonings for six hours, turning from time to time
3. Seal the lid, place the pan over the fire, and boil for two to three hours
4. Serve with the strained sauce without fat
