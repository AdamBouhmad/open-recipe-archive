## Ingredients

- two spoons of fat
- parsley
- onions
- one lemon, peeled and sliced into rounds
- peppers
- cooked dried cod

## Directions

1. Melt two spoons of fat in a skillet
2. Mix with some parsley, onions, lemon sliced into rounds and some peppers
3. Add the cooked cod to this sauce
4. Place the skillet over coals or in the oven and roast for half an hour
5. Serve
