## Ingredients

- Lettuce
- Salt
- Sugar
- Vinegar
- Bacon
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Chop the leaves of four heads of lettuce after washing them
2. Place them in a terrine with salt two hours before serving
3. Squeeze them, then place them on a plate with sugar, six spoonfuls of vinegar and a quarter of bacon cut into small pieces and fried
4. Mix everything and garnish with two hard-boiled eggs cut into slices, placing them on top of the salad
5. Serve
