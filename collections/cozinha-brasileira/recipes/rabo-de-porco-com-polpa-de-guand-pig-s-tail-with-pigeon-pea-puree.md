## Ingredients

- Pig's tails
- Salt for brine
- Pigeon peas
- Carrots
- Onions
- Herbs for flavor
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Place the pig's tails in brine for eight days
2. Place them in a vessel with pigeon peas, carrots, onions, a bundle of aromatic herbs, water and salt
3. If the tails have not been brined, make a puree of the pigeon peas, reducing it on the fire so it is not too thin
4. Place the tails over the puree and serve
5. As an alternative to pigeon pea puree, one may use lentils, chickpeas, mangalós or beans
