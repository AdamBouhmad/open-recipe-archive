## Ingredients

- peas
- fat
- onion leaves
- parsley
- garlic clove
- water
- cornstarch
- vinegar
- caruru, finely chopped
- ora-pro-nobis leaves or okra, sliced
- roasted veal meat, cut into pieces
- cornmeal porridge or cassava flour thickener

## Directions

1. Sauté a little peas in fat with onion leaves, parsley, and a garlic clove
2. Add a few cups of water, a spoonful of cornstarch and another of vinegar
3. Add a handful of finely chopped caruru and some ora-pro-nobis leaves or sliced okra, and pieces of roasted meat
4. Let boil for half an hour and serve with cornmeal porridge or cassava flour thickener
