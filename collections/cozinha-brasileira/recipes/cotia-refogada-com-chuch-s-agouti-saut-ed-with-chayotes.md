## Ingredients

- one agouti
- three spoonfuls of fat
- chayotes
- one cup of water
- one goblet of brandy
- one spoonful of lime juice
- one small spoonful of cassava starch
- salt
- parsley
- peppers
- ginger

## Directions

1. Take an agouti, clean it and cut it into pieces, and fry them in three spoonfuls of fat
2. When fried, remove them and sauté in the same fat a portion of peeled chayotes
3. Add one cup of water, one goblet of brandy, one spoonful of lime juice, one small spoonful of cassava starch, salt, parsley, peppers, and a little grated ginger
4. Let it boil over embers until the meat is cooked and serve
