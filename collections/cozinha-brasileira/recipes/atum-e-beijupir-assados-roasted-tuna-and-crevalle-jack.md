## Ingredients

- postas (steaks) of tuna or crevalle jack, two fingers thick
- sweet oil
- garlic clove, crushed
- two onions, sliced
- bay leaves
- salt
- peppers
- cloves
- parsley
- two spoons of butter
- one spoon of wheat flour
- two cups of white wine
- lemon slices, peeled

## Directions

1. Soak the fish steaks in sweet oil with crushed garlic, sliced onions, bay leaves, salt, peppers, cloves and parsley for three to four hours, turning the fish occasionally
2. Remove and drain the fish
3. Roast on a grill over moderate fire
4. Melt two spoons of butter and mix with one spoon of wheat flour, salt and peppers
5. Add two cups of white wine and lemon slices
6. Bring to a boil and serve hot with the fish
