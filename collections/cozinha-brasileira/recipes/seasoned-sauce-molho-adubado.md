## Ingredients

- bacon skins
- beef hand, cut into small pieces
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- bay leaf
- clove
- onions
- carrots
- turkey, goose or duck
- one cup of white wine
- equal amount of water
- meat sauce substance
- half cup of brandy

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a casserole with bacon skins and a hand of beef cut into small pieces, salt, pepper, parsley, chives, bay leaf, clove, onions, carrots, and place on them the turkey, goose or duck that you wish to cook; add one cup of white wine, another equal amount of water and of substance for sauce, as well as half a cup of brandy; let it boil over a moderate fire for three to four hours, sealing the lid of the casserole with strips of greased paper so that the vapors do not escape, and serve.
