## Ingredients

- young chicken
- salt
- wheat flour
- beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Cut a young chicken into four pieces and sprinkle with salt
2. Wrap first in wheat flour, then in beaten eggs and again in wheat flour
3. Fry on both sides in butter over high heat and serve the chicken with salads or marmalade
