## Ingredients

- mangarito
- salt
- butter
- wheat flour
- egg yolks
- milk

## Directions

1. Peel a portion of mangarito and place sprinkled with salt in a pan that goes in the oven until roasted
2. When roasted, melt two spoons of cow butter with one of wheat flour
3. Add two beaten egg yolks and two cups of rich milk
4. Let boil for some moments, pour the mangarito in this sauce, give it a boil, and serve
