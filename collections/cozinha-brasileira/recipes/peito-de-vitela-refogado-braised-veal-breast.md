## Ingredients

- veal breast
- bacon
- flour
- water
- capers
- carapicú
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces the size of a walnut
2. Fry a portion of chopped bacon
3. Add a little flour and let it brown
4. Add the veal meat, stir, and add a little water
5. Add some capers, some scalded and washed carapicú, and some peeled lemon slices without seeds
6. Let it boil
7. Serve when cooked
