## Ingredients

- small leaves of Brussels sprouts
- meat broth
- thyme

## Directions

1. Take the small leaves of Brussels sprouts
2. Cook them in meat broth for half an hour
3. Pour this meat broth over toasted bread into which a sprig of thyme has been previously placed
