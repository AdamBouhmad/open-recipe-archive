## Ingredients

- four spoonfuls of fine cornmeal
- one spoonful of arrowroot
- two well-beaten eggs
- a little salt
- lemon zest
- two cups of milk

## Directions

1. Make a batter with four spoonfuls of fine cornmeal, one spoonful of arrowroot, two well-beaten eggs, a little salt, lemon zest, and two cups of milk
2. Proceed as already stated
