## Ingredients

- pigeons
- salt
- pepper juice
- water
- fat

## Directions

1. Take clean pigeons and rub them with salt and pepper juice, leaving them in these seasonings for three hours to be impregnated
2. Boil them for a quarter hour in water
3. Remove, let cool, and dry them
4. Place them in a barrel with plenty of fat and let them boil until nearly cooked
5. Remove them and pour the melted fat over them, ensuring the pigeons are covered by it
6. Seal the barrel and keep it in this state until needed
