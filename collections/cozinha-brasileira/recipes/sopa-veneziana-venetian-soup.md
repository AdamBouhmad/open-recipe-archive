## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- salt
- beef broth
- grated cheese
- sugar
- cinnamon
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of rice with little water and little salt on moderate heat
2. When cooked, add a portion of beef broth and let it boil until dry
3. When in this state, place on a serving dish and cover with a layer of grated cheese, sugar and cinnamon, pouring melted butter over everything
