## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- butter or melted fat
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- breadcrumbs
- onion finely chopped and fried in butter

## Directions

1. Cut, beat and trim the chops.
2. Dip in melted butter or fat.
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and ground nutmeg.
4. Cover with breadcrumbs and finely chopped onion fried briefly in butter.
5. Fry on both sides.
6. Serve with sauce or vegetables.
