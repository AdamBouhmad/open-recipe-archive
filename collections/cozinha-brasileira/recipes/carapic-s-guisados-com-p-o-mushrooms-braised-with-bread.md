## Ingredients

- manteiga (butter)
- pão ralado (bread crumbs)
- carapicús (mushrooms)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- pimenta (pepper)

## Directions

1. Melt two spoons of butter
2. Mix them with a portion of bread crumbs and add a portion of carapicús cooked in water and salt, pepper, and a cup of vinegar
3. Mix well, let it boil still over moderate heat, and serve
