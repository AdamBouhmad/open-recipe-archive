## Ingredients

- liver
- wheat flour
- fat
- water
- onion
- salt
- peppers
- eggplants

## Directions

1. Take the liver and cut it into slices the thickness of a finger.
2. Coat these in wheat flour and fry them on both sides in fat.
3. Once entirely fried, add two large cups of water, a cut onion, salt, and peppers.
4. Let them boil over low heat.
5. Half an hour before serving, add some eggplants cut into pieces.
