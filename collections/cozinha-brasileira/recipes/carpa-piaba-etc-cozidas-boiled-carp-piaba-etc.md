## Ingredients

- one fish
- one bowl of water
- one cup of white wine
- salt
- peppers
- onions
- parsley
- one cup of vinegar
- one garlic clove
- two cloves
- vinegar
- sweet oil
- one egg yolk

## Directions

1. Clean and wash one fish without removing the scales
2. In another pan, boil one bowl of water, one cup of white wine, salt, peppers, onions, parsley
3. When boiling, add the fish, which should be completely covered
4. In another pan, boil one cup of vinegar, one garlic clove, and two cloves, and pour hot over the fish
5. When the fish is cooked, remove the skin with the scales and serve cold with its own strained and reduced broth, mixing vinegar, sweet oil and one cooked egg yolk broken up in the cold sauce
