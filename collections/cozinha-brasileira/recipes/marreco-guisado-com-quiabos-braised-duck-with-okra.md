## Ingredients

- 1 duck, cut into pieces
- water
- salt
- onion
- parsley
- celery root
- two tablespoons butter
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- strained broth
- okra cut into rounds
- fried duck pieces
- juice of one lemon
- slices of another lemon, peeled

## Directions

1. Cut the duck into pieces and boil them in water with salt, onion, parsley, and celery root
2. When almost cooked, remove them and fry them in two tablespoons of butter, turning them until of good color
3. Remove them again and add one tablespoon of wheat flour to the same fat
4. Stir the strained broth in which the duck was cooked until reduced to four cups
5. Add a portion of okra cut into rounds, the fried duck pieces, the juice of one lemon, and slices of another peeled lemon
6. Serve at the table
