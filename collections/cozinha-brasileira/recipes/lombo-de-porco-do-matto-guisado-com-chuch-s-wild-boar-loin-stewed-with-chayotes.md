## Ingredients

- one wild boar loin
- water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one chalice of white wine
- one handful of bread crumb
- two tablespoons of cow butter
- chayotes
- one egg yolk
- one cup of milk cream

## Directions

1. Cut the loin into pieces and boil in water with salt, pepper, parsley, onion leaves, white wine, and bread crumb
2. When cooked, remove and fry in two tablespoons of cow butter
3. Add the strained broth through a sieve and peeled and cut chayotes
4. Let cook until the broth is reduced
5. Incorporate one egg yolk beaten in one cup of milk cream
6. Serve
