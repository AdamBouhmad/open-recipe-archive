## Ingredients

- sorrel
- one spoonful of butter
- half spoonful of wheat flour
- two egg yolks
- salt
- eight to ten poached eggs

## Directions

1. Pound a portion of sorrel.
2. Squeeze out the juice and strain through cloth.
3. Add one spoonful of butter, half a spoonful of wheat flour, two egg yolks, and a little salt.
4. Simmer for a while, stirring.
5. Add eight to ten poached eggs in salted water.
6. Simmer once more and serve.
