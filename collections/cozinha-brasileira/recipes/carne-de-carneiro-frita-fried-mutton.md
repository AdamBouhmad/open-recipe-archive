## Ingredients

- mutton slices
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chopped onion
- half spoonful of butter
- mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Cut and beat the slices, season with salt, pepper, parsley and chopped onion
2. Place a pot over high heat with half a spoonful of butter
3. When the butter is very hot, put the mutton slices in it
4. Fry both sides and serve with spicy mustard sauce
5. They can also be grilled with a little butter, cooking both sides
