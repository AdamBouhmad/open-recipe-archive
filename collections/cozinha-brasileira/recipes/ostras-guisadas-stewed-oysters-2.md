## Ingredients

- butter
- wheat flour
- salt
- pepper
- white wine
- carapicús

## Directions

1. Melt two tablespoons of butter, mix in one of wheat flour, little salt and pepper
2. Add half a bottle of white wine
3. When boiled, add a portion of chopped cooked carapicús
