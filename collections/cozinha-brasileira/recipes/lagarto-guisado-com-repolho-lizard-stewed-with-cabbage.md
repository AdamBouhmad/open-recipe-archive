## Ingredients

- one lizard
- water
- salt
- peppers
- tomatoes
- parsley
- onion
- carapicús (a vegetable), sliced
- fat
- one glass of vinegar
- cabbage

## Directions

1. Boil a lizard in water with salt, peppers, tomatoes, parsley, onion, and a portion of sliced carapicús
2. When cooked, remove and braise with one spoonful of fat, one glass of vinegar, and the strained broth
3. Let it boil until the broth is reduced
4. Serve with cabbage, adding a little vinegar
