## Ingredients

- mutton, cut into small pieces
- water
- salt
- cucumbers, peeled, deseeded and cut lengthwise
- vinegar
- sugar, half a spoon
- wheat flour, fried

## Directions

1. Cut the mutton into small pieces and cook in water with salt
2. Finally add peeled, deseeded and lengthwise-cut cucumbers
3. When everything is cooked, add a little vinegar, half a spoon of sugar and a little fried wheat flour
