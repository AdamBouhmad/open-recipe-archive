## Ingredients

- a portion of cangica
- ground peanuts
- sugar
- water
- one bottle of milk

## Directions

1. Take a portion of cangica
2. Put to cook with ground peanuts, sugar and plenty of water
3. When cooked, add one bottle of milk
4. Boil a little more and serve
