## Ingredients

- juice of twelve oranges
- half pound sugar
- egg white
- one bottle of jelly

## Directions

1. Boil the juice of twelve oranges with the syrup of half pound of sugar at thread stage
2. Clarify the sugar with egg white, and after straining, add one bottle of jelly
3. Boil once more and pour into a vessel
4. Let it cool, and when hard, place the vessel in a pan with hot water so the jelly slips off
5. Turn over onto a plate and serve
