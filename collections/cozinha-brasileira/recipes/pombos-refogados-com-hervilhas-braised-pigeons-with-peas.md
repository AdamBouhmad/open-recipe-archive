## Ingredients

- four young pigeons
- two tablespoons butter
- portion of fresh peas
- two cups of water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- nutmeg
- two egg yolks
- one glass of white wine
- pinch of sugar

## Directions

1. Take four young pigeons and braise them in two tablespoons of butter
2. When they have taken color, remove them and cut them in half
3. Place a portion of fresh peas in the same fat and then two cups of water
4. Add the pieces of pigeons, salt, pepper, parsley, and nutmeg
5. Let them cook over live coals
6. When serving, add two egg yolks mixed in one glass of white wine and a pinch of sugar
