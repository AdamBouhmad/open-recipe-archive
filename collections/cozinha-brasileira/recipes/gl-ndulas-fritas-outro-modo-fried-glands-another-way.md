## Ingredients

- veal glands
- hot water
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- lemon juice
- onion
- butter
- endive, chicory, or chopped cabbage for serving

## Directions

1. Scald the glands in hot water
2. Place in a mixture of salt, pepper, garlic, lemon juice, and chopped onion for one hour
3. Fry in butter and serve with endive, chicory, or chopped cabbage
