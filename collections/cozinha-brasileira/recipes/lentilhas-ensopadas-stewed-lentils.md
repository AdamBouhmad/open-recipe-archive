## Ingredients

- a portion of lentils
- water and salt
- two spoons of butter
- a little salt
- one chopped onion
- one cup of vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of lentils in water and salt
2. When cooked, fry two spoons of butter, a little salt, one chopped onion
3. Add the lentils with broth and one cup of vinegar
4. Let them boil a little more and serve
