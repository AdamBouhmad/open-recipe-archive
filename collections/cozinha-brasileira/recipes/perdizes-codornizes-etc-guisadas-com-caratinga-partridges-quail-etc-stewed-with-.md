## Ingredients

- partridge, quail, or similar bird
- caratinga
- water
- salt
- onion
- tomatoes
- fat
- water
- lemon juice
- parsley
- half a garlic clove
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook a bird with a caratinga in water and salt
2. When the bird is cooked, remove it and let the caratinga continue cooking until tender, which you can tell by piercing it easily with a feather, then remove it
3. Brown chopped onion and a dozen tomatoes in two spoons of fat
4. Add a cup of water, a glass of lemon juice, parsley, half a garlic clove, and half a spoon of sugar
5. Let boil once more and add the bird and caratinga cut into pieces
6. Boil a little more and serve
