## Ingredients

- half a dozen large cucumbers
- wheat flour
- fat
- one glass of vinegar
- one cup of water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel half a dozen large cucumbers
2. Cut into eight parts
3. Remove the seeds
4. Coat the pieces in wheat flour
5. Sauté in fat
6. Add one glass of vinegar, one cup of water, salt and pepper
7. Let boil for half an hour over moderate heat
8. Serve
