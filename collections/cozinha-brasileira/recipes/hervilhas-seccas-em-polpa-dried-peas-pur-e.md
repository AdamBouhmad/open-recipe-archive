## Ingredients

- a portion of dried peas
- water and salt
- a piece of chopped bacon
- a chopped onion
- bread, cut in small pieces and fried in lard

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of dried peas in water and salt
2. Drain the water and pass through a sieve
3. Fry a piece of chopped bacon, add the purée and stir, putting the mass on a plate
4. Put on top bread cut in small pieces fried in lard until well toasted and a chopped onion, and serve
