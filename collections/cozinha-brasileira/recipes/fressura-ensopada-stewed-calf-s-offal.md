## Ingredients

- butter
- two onions
- one calf offal
- one glass of wine
- one glass of water
- salt
- onion
- clove
- nutmeg
- lemon juice
- one tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Fry a little butter and two cut onions with one washed calf offal
2. Scald the offal and cut it
3. After fifteen minutes, add one glass of wine, one of water, salt, onion, clove, nutmeg, lemon juice and one tablespoon of sugar
