## Ingredients

- one capon
- fruits of the prickly pear cactus
- half pound of chopped bacon
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- three cups of white wine
- one cup of water

## Directions

1. Fill the capon with the fruits of the prickly pear cactus, which have been previously passed through ashes or coals to clean them of their thorns
2. Peel the fruits and cut them up, and put them in a pan with half pound of chopped bacon, salt, pepper, nutmeg, three cups of white wine and one cup of water
3. Cover the pan with a seal and simmer over moderate fire until the fruits are cooked
4. Remove the fat from the sauce and serve with it
