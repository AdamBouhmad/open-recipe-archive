## Ingredients

- some carrots
- fresh butter
- one spoon of wheat flour
- salt
- onion leaves
- one cup of water
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel some carrots by scraping and cut them into slices
2. Braise them in fresh butter with one spoon of wheat flour, a pinch of salt, chopped onion leaves
3. Add one cup of water, salt and pepper
4. Let it boil a little and serve with roasted poultry
