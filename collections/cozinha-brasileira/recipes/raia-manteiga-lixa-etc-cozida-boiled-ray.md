## Ingredients

- Ray fish
- Salted water
- Parsley
- One onion
- Pepper
- One glass of vinegar
- Melted butter
- Wheat flour
- One teacup white wine

## Directions

1. Boil the fish in salted water with parsley, one onion, pepper and one glass of vinegar
2. When cooked, remove the skin
3. Place on a plate
4. Serve with a sauce made of melted butter, wheat flour and one teacup of white wine
