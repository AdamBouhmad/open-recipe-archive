## Ingredients

- small fish
- fat
- water
- salt
- parsley leaves
- onions
- Indian pepper
- egg yolks
- cassava flour or cornmeal

## Directions

1. Fry small fish first in a little fat
2. Make them boil afterwards in water seasoned with salt, parsley leaves, onions and Indian pepper
3. After boiling, thicken the broth by adding two beaten egg yolks that have been first mixed in a little of the broth
4. Let boil for another half hour, then pour the broth over cassava flour or cornmeal
