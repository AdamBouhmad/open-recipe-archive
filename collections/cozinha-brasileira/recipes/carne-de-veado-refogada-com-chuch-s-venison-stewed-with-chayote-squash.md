## Ingredients

- venison meat
- vinegar
- salt
- onion
- celery
- ginger
- cloves from India
- parsley
- marjoram
- fat
- wheat flour
- one bottle of white wine
- chayote squash

## Directions

1. Soak the meat for two days in vinegar, salt, onion, celery, ginger, cloves from India, parsley and marjoram
2. Remove and braise in fat, turning the pieces to brown on all sides
3. Sprinkle with wheat flour
4. Add one bottle of white wine and a portion of peeled and chopped chayote squash
5. Let simmer on coals until cooked and serve
