## Ingredients

- medium-sized or small fish
- salt
- ground pepper
- manioc flour
- fat

## Directions

1. Only medium-sized or small fish should be fried
2. Clean them, remove the scales, split them down the middle so that the two halves remain attached at the back
3. Sprinkle with salt, ground pepper and wrap them in manioc flour
4. Fry them in fat until cooked and of good color, and serve
