## Ingredients

- one dozen sardines in barrel
- six to eight chalices of broth
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley
- bay leaf
- a little salt
- vinegar
- lemon juice
- sour orange juice

## Directions

1. After well washing one dozen sardines in barrel and removing the bones, put them in a pot with six to eight chalices of broth, pepper, nutmeg, parsley, bay leaf, and a little salt
2. Leave to boil for one hour over moderate fire
3. Serve with vinegar, lemon juice, or sour orange juice, for game meats
