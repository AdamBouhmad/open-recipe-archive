## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- water
- butter
- onion
- salt
- garlic

## Directions

1. Choose and wash a portion of chickpeas and put to cook over the fire for six hours or more, supplying with water from time to time.
2. Once cooked, melt a spoonful of butter with a chopped onion, salt and a crushed clove of garlic.
3. Add the chickpeas with their broth, boil until the water reduces and serve.
