## Ingredients

- bird offal
- half a quarter of bacon
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- one onion
- two boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Chop the bird offal with half a quarter of bacon, salt, parsley, pepper, one onion and two boiled eggs
2. fill the bird with this mixture
3. sew the opening shut
4. braise the bird in fat, turning it on all sides
5. then place it on a spit and wrap it in paper greased with fat
6. roast over regular heat and serve with some spicy sauce or salad
