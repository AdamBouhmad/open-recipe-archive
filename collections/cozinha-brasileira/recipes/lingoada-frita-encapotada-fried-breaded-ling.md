## Ingredients

- fish meat
- salt
- pepper
- two eggs
- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- one tablespoon of melted butter
- butter for frying
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the fish meat into strips two fingers in width, dry them with a towel, and sprinkle with a little salt and pepper
2. Beat two eggs with one tablespoon of wheat flour and one tablespoon of melted butter
3. Pass the fish in this batter, and then fry it in butter
4. Place on a plate, squeeze lemon juice over it and serve
