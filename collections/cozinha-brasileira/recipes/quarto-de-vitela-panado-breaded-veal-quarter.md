## Ingredients

- veal quarter
- dried biscuits, ground
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the quarter for five minutes, remove it and let it cool to cover it with ground dried biscuits
2. Spit it and roast it, occasionally spooning melted butter over it so it does not dry
3. Serve with Robert sauce or tomato sauce, or also with salads, potatoes, sardines, or lettuces
