## Ingredients

- one duck
- one chopped onion
- salt
- two spoons of fat
- two pounds of wheat flour
- four eggs
- one spoon of butter
- a portion of peeled raisins
- milk as needed

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from a duck
2. Fry the meat with one chopped onion and salt in two spoons of fat
3. Make a dough with two pounds of wheat flour, four eggs, one spoon of butter, a portion of peeled raisins and enough milk to form a thick mixture
4. Mix well
5. Spread on a board
6. Place the duck meat on this dough
7. Fold the edges together
8. Place in a napkin
9. Put in a pot with plenty of water
10. When cooked, serve with an appropriate sauce
