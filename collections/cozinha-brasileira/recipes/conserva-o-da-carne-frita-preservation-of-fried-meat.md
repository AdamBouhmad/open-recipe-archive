## Ingredients

- meat
- water
- salt
- fat

## Directions

1. Boil the meat cut into pieces in little water and salt
2. Remove and dry with a cloth
3. Fry a portion of fat
4. Add the meat and let it cook until browned
5. Remove and place in pans
6. Pour hot fat on top
7. To use it, simply heat it
