## Ingredients

- one bird
- fat for browning
- one onion, chopped
- one spoon of wheat flour
- a portion of fern shoots (boiled in water and salt)
- one bottle of water
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- onion leaves

## Directions

1. Take the bird and after cleaning, braise it in fat, turning it on all sides
2. Cut it into pieces and place them back in the same fat after having browned a chopped onion and one spoon of wheat flour in it
3. Add a portion of fern shoots (boiled in water and salt), one bottle of water, salt, peppers, parsley and onion leaves
4. Let it simmer until the broth reduces
5. Serve the pieces of bird over the fern shoots arranged on a plate
