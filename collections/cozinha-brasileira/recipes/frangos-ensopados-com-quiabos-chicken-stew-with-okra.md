## Ingredients

- chicken
- fat
- water
- okra
- polvilho
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- juice of one terra orange
- cornmeal mush

## Directions

1. Sauté the pieces of a chicken in two spoonfuls of fat until golden on both sides
2. Add half a bottle of water, a portion of cut okra, half a spoonful of polvilho, salt, parsley, onion and the juice of a terra orange
3. Let them boil until cooked and serve with cornmeal mush
