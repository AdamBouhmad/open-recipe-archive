## Ingredients

- calf cutlets
- butter
- wheat flour
- onion
- broth
- salt
- nutmeg
- cloves
- sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry the cutlets in a little butter until they are browned on both sides and remove them
2. In the same butter, add a spoonful of wheat flour and a chopped onion which is fried until toasted
3. Add several cups of broth, salt, nutmeg, cloves, a little sugar and vinegar
4. Simmer the cutlets in this sauce until they are cooked
