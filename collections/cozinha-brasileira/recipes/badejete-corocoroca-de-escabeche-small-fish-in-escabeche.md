## Ingredients

- small fish
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaves
- nutmeg
- hot vinegar
- sweet olive oil

## Directions

1. Wash a portion of fish without removing the scales.
2. Salt them well.
3. Place them in a pot with pepper, bay leaves and nutmeg.
4. Pour hot vinegar on top, enough to cover them, mixed with a quarter part sweet olive oil.
5. After fifteen days, they can be eaten simply or made into a salad.
