## Ingredients

- venison meat
- two spoonfuls of fat
- one bowl of water
- salt
- parsley
- one onion, cut
- peppercorns
- half a dozen tomatoes
- one small spoonful of cassava starch dissolved in half a chalice of water
- coconut milk from Bahia
- lemon juice
- okra, cut in rounds

## Directions

1. Take the venison meat and braise it in two spoonfuls of fat
2. Add one bowl of water, salt, parsley, one cut onion, peppercorns, half a dozen tomatoes
3. Add one small spoonful of cassava starch dissolved in half a chalice of coconut milk from Bahia
4. Add lemon juice and a portion of okra cut in rounds
5. Let simmer until the broth is reduced to three cups and serve
