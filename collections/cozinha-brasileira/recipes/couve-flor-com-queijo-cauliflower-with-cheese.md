## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- water
- salt
- butter
- Flemish cheese
- white sauce
- breadcrumbs
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Peel cauliflower and place in a casserole with boiling water, salt and a little butter
2. Fifteen minutes are enough to cook them
3. Remove with a skimmer and let drain
4. Cover with finely grated Flemish cheese and place on a plate that can go on the fire
5. Pour over a thick white sauce, sprinkle with cheese and breadcrumbs
6. Pour melted butter on top and in this state place over low heat; twenty minutes are enough to prepare them
