## Ingredients

- lentils
- sugar
- vinegar
- onion

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils
2. Pass half of them through a sieve
3. Mix the sieved lentils with the other half
4. Season with sugar, vinegar, and a sliced onion
5. Give it one more boil
