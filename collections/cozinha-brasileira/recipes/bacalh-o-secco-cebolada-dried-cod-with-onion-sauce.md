## Ingredients

- one dozen onions, sliced into rounds
- two spoons of fat
- peppers
- one cup of vinegar
- cooked dried cod

## Directions

1. Fry the sliced onions in two spoons of fat
2. Sprinkle with peppers and when browned add one cup of vinegar
3. Boil briefly and pour this sauce over the cooked cod on the plate
4. Serve hot
