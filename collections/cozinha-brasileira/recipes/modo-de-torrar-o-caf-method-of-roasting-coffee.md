## Ingredients

- coffee
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the coffee well and dry it in the sun; the coffee should always be washed because many dye coffee with lead and other harmful substances; place in an iron box with a small door, secured by a handle, and place this over the fire until the coffee has taken on a suitable color, and keep it after grinding; in many places a spoon of sugar is added to each pound of coffee after it is ready.
