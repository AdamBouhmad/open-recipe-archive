## Ingredients

- half a dozen fresh or preserved peppercorns
- juice of one gallego lemon
- six to eight spoons of beef broth

## Directions

1. Mash half a dozen fresh or preserved peppercorns
2. Squeeze the juice of one gallego lemon over them
3. Add six to eight spoons of beef broth
4. Serve with boiled meats, cabbage, etc.
