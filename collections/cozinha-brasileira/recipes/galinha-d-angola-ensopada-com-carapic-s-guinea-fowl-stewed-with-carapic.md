## Ingredients

- one guinea fowl
- three spoonfuls of fat
- carapicú
- half a dozen tomatoes
- salt
- one onion
- peppers
- parsley
- one glass of vinegar
- one cup of water

## Directions

1. Braise one bird cut into pieces with three spoonfuls of fat, add a portion of chopped carapicú, half a dozen tomatoes, salt, one chopped onion, peppers, parsley, and finally after stirring, one glass of vinegar and one cup of water
2. Let it boil and when cooked, serve
