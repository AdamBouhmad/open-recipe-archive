## Ingredients

- one or two fish
- milk
- salt
- two tablespoons fat
- two tablespoons cassava flour
- one cut onion
- peppers

## Directions

1. Take one or two fish, remove the scales, clean and wash them, cut into pieces and boil in milk and salt
2. When cooked, melt two tablespoons of fat in a vessel over coals
3. Mix two tablespoons of cassava flour, one cut onion and peppers
4. Add the milk and finally the fish
5. Let it boil for another half hour and serve
