## Ingredients

- slices of bread
- butter
- six hard-boiled eggs
- one onion
- parsley leaves
- salt
- pepper
- six beaten eggs
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Fry slices of bread in butter.
2. Place the fried bread on a plate that can withstand direct heat from the fire.
3. Shell and chop six hard-boiled eggs.
4. Mix the chopped eggs with one onion, parsley leaves, salt, and pepper.
5. Add the mixture to the plate with the fried bread.
6. Pour six beaten eggs mixed with grated cheese over the top.
7. Place the plate over coals and cover with a lid filled with coals.
8. When cooked, serve.
