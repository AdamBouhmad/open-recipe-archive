## Ingredients

- two pounds fresh cheese
- two cups cream
- six egg yolks
- one pound sugar
- juice of sour orange

## Directions

1. Mash two pounds of fresh cheese in two cups of cream
2. Add six egg yolks, one pound of sugar, and a little juice of sour orange
3. Fill puff pastry forms with this mass
4. Bake in the oven and serve
