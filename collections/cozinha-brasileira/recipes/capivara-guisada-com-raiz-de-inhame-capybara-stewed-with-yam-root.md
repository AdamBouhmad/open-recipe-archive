## Ingredients

- capybara meat
- yam root
- water
- salt
- gordura
- onion
- lemon or orange juice
- egg yolks
- comari peppers
- parsley

## Directions

1. Put a piece of capybara meat and one or two yam roots in a pan with plenty of water and salt
2. Leave it to boil
3. When everything is cooked, remove and cut into pieces, both the meat and the yam
4. Then sauté them in a little fat and one chopped onion
5. Add a glass of lemon or orange juice and two egg yolks dissolved in a cup of water, comari peppers and parsley
6. After boiling for half an hour, serve
