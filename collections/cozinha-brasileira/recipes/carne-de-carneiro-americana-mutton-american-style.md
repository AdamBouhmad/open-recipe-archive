## Ingredients

- piece of mutton
- vinegar, a little
- fat, one spoon
- pepper powder
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a piece of mutton and roast on a spit; when roasted, remove to be chopped
2. Mix the juice that dripped with a little vinegar, one spoon fat, pepper powder and salt, and place over fire
3. Add the chopped meat to this
4. After cooking for half an hour, serve
