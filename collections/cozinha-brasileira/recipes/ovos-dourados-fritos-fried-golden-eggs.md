## Ingredients

- slices of bread
- milk
- four beaten eggs
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- butter or beef fat
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak slices of bread in milk.
2. Remove and let them drain.
3. Pass the bread slices through four beaten eggs mixed with one spoonful of wheat flour.
4. Fry in a saucepan with butter or beef fat.
5. When fried on one side, cover with sugar and cinnamon.
6. Serve.
