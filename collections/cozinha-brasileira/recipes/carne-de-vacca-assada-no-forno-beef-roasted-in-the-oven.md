## Ingredients

- good cut of beef
- lard with strips of bacon dusted in Indian pepper
- strips of bacon
- half a bottle of red wine
- pepper
- salt
- garlic
- onions
- bay leaf
- butter
- paper

## Directions

1. Lard the beef with strips of bacon dusted in Indian pepper
2. Place in a pan lined with strips of bacon, with more strips prepared to cover it
3. Add half a bottle of red wine, pepper, salt, garlic, onions, and bay leaf
4. Cover the pan, securing it with strips of paper greased with butter and glued
5. Bake in the oven for six hours and serve
