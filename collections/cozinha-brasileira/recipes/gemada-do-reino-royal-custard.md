## Ingredients

- half a dozen egg yolks
- four spoonfuls of sugar
- a little grated nutmeg
- ground cinnamon
- grated vanilla
- one bottle of white wine

## Directions

1. Beat half a dozen egg yolks with four spoonfuls of sugar, a little grated nutmeg, ground cinnamon and grated vanilla
2. Add little by little one bottle of white wine
3. Then place on the fire
4. Let boil several times
5. Serve plain, to serve as a sauce for puddings
