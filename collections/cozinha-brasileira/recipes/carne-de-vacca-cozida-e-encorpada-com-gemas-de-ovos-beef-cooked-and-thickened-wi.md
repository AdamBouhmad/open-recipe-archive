## Ingredients

- parsley
- chives
- butter
- flour
- beef broth
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- cooked beef
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Chop parsley and chives, and place them with butter in a casserole
2. After they have heated, add a spoon of flour
3. Stir and pour over a cup of beef broth
4. Stir until boiling; season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg
5. Boil this sauce for a few minutes and place over the cooked beef cut into slices
6. At the moment of serving, thicken with egg yolks
