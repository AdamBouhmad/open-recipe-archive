## Ingredients

- venison meat
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion
- fat

## Directions

1. Cut the venison meat into slices the thickness of a finger
2. Beat them well and wrap them in salt, pepper, finely chopped parsley and onion
3. Fry some fat in a saucepan in another place and put the steaks in this hot fat
4. Fry them on both sides to take on a beautiful color
5. Serve with side dishes or fried carapicús
