## Ingredients

- pork liver
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- minced garlic
- chives
- sage
- wheat flour
- white wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut liver into equal slices
2. Melt a little butter in a frying pan and place the liver slices in it
3. Season with pepper and salt, adding minced garlic, chives, and sage
4. When cooked on both sides, drain the butter
5. Add one spoonful of wheat flour diluted in half a cup of white wine, being careful not to let it boil
6. When the sauce is thick, add the juice of one lemon and serve
