## Ingredients

- fresh fish
- salt
- vinegar
- pepper
- clove
- onion
- parsley

## Directions

1. As soon as the fish is caught, scale and clean it well without breaking it; apply plenty of salt, leaving it in this brine for three to four hours; cut into portions, placing them in a pan that is boiling with vinegar, pepper, clove, onion and parsley; after three to four boils, remove with a skimmer, place in a barrel, and pour cold strained vinegar over the top; cover and keep.
