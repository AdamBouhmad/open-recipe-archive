## Ingredients

- four spoonfuls of cornstarch or arrowroot
- one bottle of milk
- six egg yolks
- three spoonfuls of sugar
- sugar
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Dissolve four spoonfuls of cornstarch or arrowroot in one bottle of milk
2. Mix in six beaten egg yolks with three spoonfuls of sugar
3. Place everything on the fire and boil, stirring constantly until it thickens
4. When cold, place on a plate
5. Cover with sugar and cinnamon powder
6. Send to the table
