## Ingredients

- one yam
- water
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook one yam in water and salt
2. When cooked, peel and cut into slices the thickness of a finger
3. Put these slices on a plate and cover them with layers of sugar
4. Put in the oven and when it has taken color, serve
