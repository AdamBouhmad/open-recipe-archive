## Ingredients

- breasts from one dozen small birds
- butter
- wheat flour
- rest of the small birds
- one glass of white wine
- two cups of water
- half dozen tomatoes

## Directions

1. Fry the breasts from one dozen small birds in butter
2. In another pan, melt a little butter and mix one spoon of wheat flour, the rest of the small birds, one glass of white wine, two cups of water and half dozen tomatoes
3. Let it simmer for half an hour
4. Add the fried breasts to this sauce
5. Simmer a little more
6. Serve
