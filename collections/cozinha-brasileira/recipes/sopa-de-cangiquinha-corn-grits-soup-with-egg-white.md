## Ingredients

- a portion of corn grits
- four egg yolks
- half a bottle of white wine
- sugar
- cinnamon
- egg whites
- water

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of corn grits
2. Beat four egg yolks with half a bottle of white wine
3. Add to the corn grits with sugar and cinnamon
4. Beat egg whites separately until they form a compact mass
5. Drop spoonfuls of beaten egg white into boiling water in small pearls
6. Remove the cooked egg white snow and set aside
7. Place the cooked egg white on top of the corn grits soup as garnish
8. Sprinkle with ground cinnamon
