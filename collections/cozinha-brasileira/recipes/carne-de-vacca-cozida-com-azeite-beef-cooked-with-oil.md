## Ingredients

- cooked beef
- parsley
- onion
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked beef into slices and place on a plate with whole scalded parsley and chopped onion
2. At the same time as the beef, serve in a sauce boat a cold sauce of oil or a restorative sauce
