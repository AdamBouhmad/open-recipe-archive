## Ingredients

- beef
- salt
- ground pepper
- wheat flour
- butter
- meat broth
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into very fine and small slices and sprinkle with salt and ground pepper and wrap in wheat flour, then fry on both sides in butter which should already be hot on the fire.
2. Remove ten minutes later and fry in the rest of the butter one spoon of wheat flour until it acquires golden color.
3. Add some cups of broth, some mushrooms, salt, seasonings as desired, and finally put in the beefsteaks.
4. Let them boil for half an hour and serve.
