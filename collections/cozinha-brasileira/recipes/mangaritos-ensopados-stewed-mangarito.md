## Ingredients

- mangarito
- fat
- wheat flour
- water
- lemon juice
- parsley
- salt
- onion leaves

## Directions

1. Peel a portion of mangarito and cut into pieces
2. Fry in two spoons of fat and one spoon of wheat flour
3. Add a bottle of water, a glass of lemon juice, parsley, salt, and onion leaves
4. Leave to boil over moderate heat and serve when cooked
