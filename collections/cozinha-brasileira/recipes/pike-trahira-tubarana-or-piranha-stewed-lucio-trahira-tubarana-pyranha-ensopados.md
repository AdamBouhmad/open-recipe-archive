## Ingredients

- Fish scaled and cleaned
- Red wine
- Cloves
- Bay leaves
- Onions
- Parsley
- Serpol
- Salt
- Pepper
- One spoon of butter
- Vinegar
- Sweet olive oil

## Directions

1. Boil the scaled and cleaned fish in enough red wine with cloves, bay leaves, onions, parsley, serpol, salt and pepper
2. When colored, add one spoon of butter
3. Let boil a little more and serve with the sauce
4. When wanting to eat it cold, make a sauce with vinegar, sweet olive oil, salt and pepper, and omit the fish sauce
