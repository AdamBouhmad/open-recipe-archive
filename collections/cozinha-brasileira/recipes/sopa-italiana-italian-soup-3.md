## Ingredients

- 2 pounds mutton
- 1/2 pound bacon
- 6 jars water
- 1/2 jar white wine
- 1 glass vinegar
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper from India
- 1 spoon butter
- sugar
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook 2 pounds of mutton and half a pound of bacon in 6 jars of water
2. Add half a jar of white wine, a glass of vinegar, cloves, and a little cinnamon
3. When everything is cooked, strain it
4. Add a little pepper from India, a spoon of butter, a little sugar, and half a dozen beaten egg yolks
