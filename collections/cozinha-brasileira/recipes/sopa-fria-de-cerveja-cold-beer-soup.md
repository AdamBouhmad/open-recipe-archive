## Ingredients

- grated bread crust
- sugar
- lemon peel
- slices of Galician lemon
- raisins
- half beer
- half water
- white wine

## Directions

1. Make as the cold soup, adding more raisins, and replacing water with half beer and half water, and replacing red wine with white wine
