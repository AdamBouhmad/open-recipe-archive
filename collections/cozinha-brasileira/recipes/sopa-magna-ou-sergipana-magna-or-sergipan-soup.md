## Ingredients

- a handful of vegetables (greens or cabbage)
- carrots
- parsley root
- celery root
- potatoes
- salt
- bread crust
- turtle egg fat for frying

## Directions

1. Put a handful of vegetables (greens or cabbage), carrots, parsley root, celery root, potatoes and salt on the fire to cook
2. Boil for a long time
3. Strain the broth over bread crust that has been fried in turtle egg fat
