## Ingredients

- one pound of wheat flour
- four egg yolks
- half a quarter of butter or fat
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Knead one pound of wheat flour with four egg yolks, half a quarter of butter or fat, and a little salt
2. After kneading well, extend over a board, spreading it with wheat flour so the dough does not stick to the board
3. Cut this dough with the rim of a glass to make round pieces and fill according to the taste of the pastries desired
4. If desired, brown them by coating with beaten egg yolk using a feather, and bake in the oven
