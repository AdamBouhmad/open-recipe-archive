## Ingredients

- one pork liver
- six onions
- fatty meat broth
- eight ounces of caul fat, cut into pieces
- one quarter of a bottle of fatty milk
- one half bottle of blood, prepared as previously stated
- salt
- pimenta comari
- pimenta da India
- parsley
- chives
- thyme
- marjoram
- cilantro

## Directions

1. Chop the liver well.
2. Cook six onions in fatty meat broth, chop them, and mix with the already chopped liver.
3. Add eight ounces of caul fat cut into pieces, one quarter bottle of fatty milk, and one half bottle of blood that has been prepared as previously stated.
4. Season the whole mixture with ground salt, pimenta comari, pimenta da India, parsley, chives, thyme, marjoram, and cilantro.
5. Place over low heat only to warm through, stirring so that the blood does not stick to the bottom of the vessel.
6. Fill the casings with this mixture in the same manner as previously explained.
7. Once the sausages are ready, cook them in meat broth.
8. After draining, store them.
9. When you want to eat them, roast them on the grill wrapped in paper greased with butter.
