## Ingredients

- half a plate of chosen and washed rice
- two spoonfuls of fat
- one bottle of milk
- salt
- onion leaves
- a piece of cheese cut into thin slices

## Directions

1. Choose and wash half a plate of rice
2. Sauté in two spoonfuls of fat, stir
3. Add one bottle of milk, salt, onion leaves, and a piece of cheese cut into thin slices
4. Cover and cook over moderate heat until the rice is well dried
5. Pour into the plate and serve
