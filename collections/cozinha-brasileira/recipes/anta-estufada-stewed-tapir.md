## Ingredients

- tapir loin
- pork fat slices
- one cup of wine
- one glass of vinegar
- two tablespoons of sugar
- two cut onions
- one clove of garlic
- salt
- pepper
- two cloves

## Directions

1. Put a tapir loin in a pan lined with slices of pork fat, with one cup of wine, one glass of vinegar, two tablespoons of sugar, two cut onions, one clove of garlic, salt, pepper and two cloves
2. Leave the meat in these seasonings for six hours, turning from time to time
3. Seal the lid, put the pan on the fire and boil for two to three hours; serve with the strained sauce without fat
