## Ingredients

- young fava beans
- butter
- wheat flour
- cream or whole milk
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. After scalding and draining, put them in a casserole with a little butter.
2. Sprinkle a little wheat flour on top and sauté.
3. Moisten with cream or whole milk, let cook slowly.
4. Thicken with egg yolks and serve.
