## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- carapicú filling
- bacon strips
- salt
- clove
- onion
- twine

## Directions

1. Open the shoulder lengthwise
2. Remove the bone
3. Fill inside with carapicú
4. Lard the outside with thin bacon strips
5. Add salt, clove, and onion
6. Tie with twine, giving it a round shape
7. Roast in a pan over coals or in the oven
8. Serve with its own sauce
