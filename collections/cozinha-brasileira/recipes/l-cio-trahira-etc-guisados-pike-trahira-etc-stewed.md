## Ingredients

- one large fish, cut into steaks
- water
- salt
- peppercorns
- parsley
- onion leaves
- sour orange juice
- dozen tomatoes
- two tablespoons of lard
- okra
- corn flour porridge

## Directions

1. Boil one large fish cut into steaks, adding water, salt, peppercorns, parsley, onion leaves and sour orange juice
2. When cooked, fry a dozen tomatoes in two tablespoons of lard
3. Add the broth and fish, along with okra cut into rounds
4. Let boil for a quarter hour and serve with corn flour porridge
