## Ingredients

- pork meat
- squash
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- onions
- cream

## Directions

1. Boil a piece of pork meat and a peeled squash divided into pieces and with the seeds removed, with salt, peppers, parsley and onions.
2. When cooked, remove the meat and the pieces of squash, which are well pounded and passed through a sieve to form a pulp, to which half a cup of cream is added.
3. Cut the meat into small pieces, which are fried in little fat and a chopped onion; when the meat is well browned, pour it with the fat and onion over the squash pulp.
