## Ingredients

- three pigeons
- fat
- parsley
- chopped onion
- salt
- peppers
- puff pastry
- quarter pound bacon
- four chopped eggs
- one cup wine

## Directions

1. Fry three pigeons in fat with parsley, chopped onion, salt and peppers
2. Place the pigeons in a puff pastry casing over a quarter pound of bacon and four chopped and sautéed eggs with one cup of wine
3. Cover the pigeons with the same chopped mixture
4. Cover, bake in the oven and serve
