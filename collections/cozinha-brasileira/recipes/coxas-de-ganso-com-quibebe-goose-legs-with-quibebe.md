## Ingredients

- six goose legs
- bacon, finely chopped
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- broth
- onion

## Directions

1. Take six goose legs, open them, remove the bones, and fill the cavity with finely chopped bacon, salt, parsley and pepper
2. Then tie the legs and wrap each one in a slice of bacon, roast them in a pan with one cup of broth, one onion, salt and parsley, placing it either in the oven or over coals, also putting coals on top of the lid
3. When cooked, serve with quibebe
