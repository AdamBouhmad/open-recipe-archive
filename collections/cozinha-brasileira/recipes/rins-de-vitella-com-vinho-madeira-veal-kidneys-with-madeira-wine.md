## Ingredients

- veal kidneys
- water
- butter
- wheat flour
- Madeira wine
- salt
- nutmeg
- sugar
- brazas

## Directions

1. Clean and blanch the kidneys, leaving them in water until they cool
2. Cut them into small slices and fry them in butter, then add a spoonful of wheat flour
3. Mix everything very well, adding a cup of Madeira wine, little salt, nutmeg and a little sugar
4. Let cook covered and boil over hot coals, then serve
