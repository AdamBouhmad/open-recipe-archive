## Ingredients

- peas
- water
- salt
- fat
- onion
- nutmeg
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of peas in little water and salt.
2. Then melt a spoonful of fat, mix with a chopped onion, a little nutmeg and a spoonful of grated bread.
3. Add the cooked peas with the broth, let cook a little more and serve.
