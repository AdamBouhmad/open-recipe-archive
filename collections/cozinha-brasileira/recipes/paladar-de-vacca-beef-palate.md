## Ingredients

- beef palate
- water
- salt
- veal udder, finely cooked
- stuffing
- bacon slices
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. After removing the palate, soak it in water to release the blood
2. Blanch it and remove the skin with a knife
3. Wash in cold water and scrape a second time, cleaning it well and removing the black meat
4. Boil for at least five hours in salted water
5. When tender to the touch, remove from heat
6. Cut into wide strips and arrange on these strips a cooked stuffing, covering it with finely cooked veal udder
7. Add more stuffing and roll these strips, arranging them around a platter
8. Fill the center with stuffing, leaving a hollow in the middle
9. Cover all with bacon slices and place on a vessel with heat below and above or in the oven
10. After cooking about a quarter hour, drain the fat and pour Italian sauce into the hollow left between the stuffing
