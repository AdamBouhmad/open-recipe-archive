## Ingredients

- fava beans
- salt
- water
- lard
- onion
- parsley
- vinegar or sour orange juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Take a portion of fava beans and soak them in cold water for several hours.
2. Remove them and put them to cook in water and salt.
3. Once boiled, remove the pan from the fire and drain the water.
4. In another part, fry a quarter of chopped lard with a chopped onion, parsley and salt.
5. Add the fava beans and stir, then add the water in which the fava beans cooked.
6. Let it boil until it dries, and serve.
7. If desired, add a spoonful of vinegar or sour orange juice at this point.
