## Ingredients

- clean scaled fish
- water
- parsley
- celery
- onions
- peppers
- bay leaves
- hot fat
- palmetto
- fish broth
- one spoon of tapioca starch
- one cup of lime juice

## Directions

1. Boil clean scaled fish in enough water to cover them; add parsley, celery, onions, peppers, and bay leaves; when cooked, remove and pass through hot fat, removing them quickly
2. In the same fat, sauté finely chopped and washed palmetto, add the strained fish broth and one spoon of tapioca starch dissolved in one cup of lime juice; let boil until the palmetto is cooked, place the fish inside, heat and serve
