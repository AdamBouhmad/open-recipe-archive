## Ingredients

- one goose
- goose liver
- two egg yolks
- a handful of bread crumb
- salt
- pepper
- half a hundred pine nuts or peeled chestnuts

## Directions

1. Take the goose liver, two egg yolks, a handful of bread crumb, salt and pepper, all finely chopped.
2. Mix them with half a hundred peeled pine nuts or chestnuts.
3. Fill the goose with this stuffing.
4. Roast it on a spit; when cooked and of good color, serve it. This stuffing is the best for goose, as it absorbs the excess fat.
