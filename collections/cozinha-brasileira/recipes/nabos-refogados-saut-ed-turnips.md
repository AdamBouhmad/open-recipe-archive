## Ingredients

- turnips
- water
- butter
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- arrowroot
- mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Peel a dozen turnips, cut into slices, and boil in water with a spoon of butter, salt, parsley, and an onion
2. When cooked, remove with a skimmer and drain
3. Strain the broth, reduce it, and add a spoon of arrowroot and another of mustard sauce
4. After boiling, add the turnips, let cook a little more, and serve
