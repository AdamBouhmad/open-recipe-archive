## Ingredients

- beef short ribs
- salt
- ground pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef short ribs, remove the tendons, and cut into slices the thickness of a finger
2. Beat them for a while, rubbing the slices with salt and ground pepper
3. Place on the grill over a lively fire until the ribs are roasted
4. Serve with a chef's sauce, fried potatoes, or a spicy sauce
