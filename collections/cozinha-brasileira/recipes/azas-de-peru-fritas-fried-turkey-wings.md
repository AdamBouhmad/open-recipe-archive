## Ingredients

- turkey wings
- turkey neck
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- six eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Take the turkey wings and neck and cook in water with salt, parsley, and onion leaves
2. When cooked, remove the bones and cut the meat into small pieces
3. Beat with six eggs
4. Fry in lard
5. When fried, serve
