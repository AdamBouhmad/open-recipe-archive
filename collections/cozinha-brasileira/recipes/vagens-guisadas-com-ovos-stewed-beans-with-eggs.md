## Ingredients

- a portion of beans
- water
- salt
- pepper
- eggs

## Directions

1. Take a portion of beans, remove the ends and chop them
2. Put to cook in water, salt and pepper
3. Let them boil well, and when boiling, break eggs inside so they remain whole
4. Let boil a little more and serve
