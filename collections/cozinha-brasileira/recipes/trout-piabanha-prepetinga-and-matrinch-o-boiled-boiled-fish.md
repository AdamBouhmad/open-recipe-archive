## Ingredients

- clean fish
- water
- salt
- white wine
- one onion
- salt
- peppers
- bay leaves
- two spoons of butter
- two spoons of wheat flour
- fish broth

## Directions

1. After cleaning the fish, place in water and salt, then boil in white wine with a cut onion, salt, peppers, and bay leaves; when cooked, remove the fish and place them in a place to keep them warm
2. Melt two spoons of butter, mixing two spoons of wheat flour; add the fish broth, and having boiled, pour over the fish and serve
