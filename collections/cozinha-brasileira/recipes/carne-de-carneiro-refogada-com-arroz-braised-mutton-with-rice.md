## Ingredients

- half pound of bacon
- mutton meat, cut into pieces
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- rice, chosen and washed
- one egg yolk
- water

## Directions

1. Fry the bacon and remove the cracklings
2. Add the mutton meat cut into pieces and braise them, adding salt, pepper, parsley and onion leaves until they take on a good color
3. Remove them and put the chosen, washed and drained rice in the hot fat
4. Stir so it does not burn, and when it has taken on enough heat, add a bowl of water in which one egg yolk has been mixed
5. Add the meat, letting everything cook near the fire until it dries out, then serve
