## Ingredients

- butter
- small peeled onions
- wheat flour
- beef broth
- red wine
- mushrooms or carapicús
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaves
- thyme
- cooked beef slices

## Directions

1. Place a little butter in a frying pan with small peeled onions and let them brown over low heat
2. Add a spoon of wheat flour and sauté the onions
3. Then add a cup of beef broth and another of red wine
4. Add some mushrooms or carapicús
5. Season with salt, pepper, bay leaves, and thyme
6. Leave to cook
7. Pour this sailor-style sauce over slices of cooked beef prepared beforehand
8. Leave to heat near the fire for half an hour before serving
