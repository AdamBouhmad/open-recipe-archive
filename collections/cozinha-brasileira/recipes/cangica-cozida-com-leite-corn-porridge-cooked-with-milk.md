## Ingredients

- a portion of cangica
- milk
- three spoonfuls of cow's butter
- four spoonfuls of grated cheese

## Directions

1. Take a portion of cangica and boil in milk
2. When cooked, add three spoonfuls of cow's butter and four spoonfuls of grated cheese
3. Boil a little more and serve
