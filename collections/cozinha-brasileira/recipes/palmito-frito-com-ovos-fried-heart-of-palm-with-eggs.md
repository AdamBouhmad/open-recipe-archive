## Ingredients

- one heart of palm
- water
- salt
- fat
- salt
- pepper
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cut a heart of palm into pieces and boil in salted water
2. Remove and place in a pot with fat to fry
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Break eggs on top and continue to boil until eggs are cooked and serve
