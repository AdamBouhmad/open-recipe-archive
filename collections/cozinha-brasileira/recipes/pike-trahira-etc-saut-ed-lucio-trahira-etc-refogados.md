## Ingredients

- Fish cleaned
- Butter
- One glass of white wine
- Same amount of water
- Salt
- Parsley
- Onion
- Pepper
- Manioc flour for pirão
- Fish broth

## Directions

1. Take the cleaned fish and pass in butter, turning on all sides
2. Add one glass of white wine and the same amount of water, salt, parsley, onion, and pepper
3. Let boil until cooked
4. Serve dry and hot with pirão made of manioc flour and the fish broth
