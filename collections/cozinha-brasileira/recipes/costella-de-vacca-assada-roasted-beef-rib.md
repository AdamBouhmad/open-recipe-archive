## Ingredients

- piece of rib below the shoulder
- sweet oil
- salt
- broken Indian pepper
- lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Take a piece of rib below the shoulder, remove the bones except the right rib bone
2. Crush well and soak the short ribs in sweet oil, salt, and broken Indian pepper
3. Then place on the grill over a gentle fire, turning occasionally to avoid burning
4. After half an hour, remove from fire and serve at table with lemon juice and a little butter
