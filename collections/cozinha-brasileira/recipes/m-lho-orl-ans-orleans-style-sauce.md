## Ingredients

- four spoons of vinegar
- pepper
- chopped onions
- butter
- wheat flour
- beef broth or water
- four pickled cucumbers
- one cooked carrot
- three hard boiled egg whites
- loins of three anchovies
- one spoon of capers

## Directions

1. Put four spoons of vinegar, pepper, chopped onions, and butter in a saucepan
2. Boil and add wheat flour fried in butter prepared beforehand until it takes a dark color
3. Dilute with beef broth or water
4. When serving, add four chopped pickles, one cooked carrot, three hard boiled egg whites cut into pieces, the loins of three finely chopped anchovies, and one spoon of capers
5. Heat slightly and serve with ducks, beef, or veal
