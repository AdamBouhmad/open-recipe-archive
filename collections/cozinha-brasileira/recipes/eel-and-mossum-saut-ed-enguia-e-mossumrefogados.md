## Ingredients

- Two or three small eels
- Fat
- One onion
- One spoon of wheat flour
- Salt and pepper
- One bottle of water
- One chalice of sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut the eels into pieces after removing the skin
2. Sauté these pieces in fat until cooked
3. Remove and place on a plate
4. In the same butter, fry the onion, wheat flour, a little salt and pepper
5. Add one bottle of water and one chalice of sour orange juice
6. Let it boil and pour hot over the eels
7. Serve
