## Ingredients

- eight beef ribs
- potatoes
- butter

## Directions

1. Scrape the intermediate bones so that no meat remains on them for the width of one inch.
2. Put on the spit and roast for three and a half hours, being careful not to let it dry out.
3. Remove from the spit and place on a platter.
4. Surround with potatoes toasted in butter.
