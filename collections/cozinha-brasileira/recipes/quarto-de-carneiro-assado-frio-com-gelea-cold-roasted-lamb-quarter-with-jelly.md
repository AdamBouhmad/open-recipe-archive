## Ingredients

- Lamb quarter
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Onion
- Bay leaves
- Cloves
- Veal feet
- Butter
- Jelly

## Directions

1. Beat the lamb quarter well
2. Soak for one day in vinegar with salt, parsley, pepper, onion, bay leaves, and cloves
3. Cook with four veal feet
4. When nearly completely cooked, remove and fry in a pan with butter
5. After browning well on both sides, let cool
6. Thicken the broth until it reaches jelly point
7. Strain and when cold, garnish the lamb quarter with slices of jelly
