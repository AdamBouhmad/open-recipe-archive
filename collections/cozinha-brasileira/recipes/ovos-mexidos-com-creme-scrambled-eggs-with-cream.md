## Ingredients

- six eggs
- sweetened milk
- orange blossom water
- six egg yolks
- a little wheat flour
- a quarter pound of refined sugar

## Directions

1. Poach six eggs in milk sweetened and flavored with orange blossom water, and let them drain and cool.
2. Add to the milk that was used to poach them six egg yolks, a little wheat flour, and a quarter pound of refined sugar.
3. Mix, pass through a fine sieve, and put the cream to thicken over a gentle heat.
4. Pour sugar over the eggs, pour the cream on top, and serve.
