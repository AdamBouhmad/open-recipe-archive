## Ingredients

- veal liver
- half pound butter
- half pound fresh bacon, chopped finely
- onions, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- six beaten eggs
- four ounces scalded raisins
- two spoons wheat flour
- four spoons sugar
- one glass white wine
- bacon slices

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into small pieces, pass them through a coarse sieve
2. Mix with half pound butter, half pound fresh chopped bacon, chopped onions, salt, pepper, six beaten eggs, four ounces scalded raisins, two spoons of wheat flour, four spoons of sugar, one glass of white wine
3. Pour this mixture into a pan lined with bacon slices
4. Cook its contents in the oven or over coals, also covering the lid with coals
5. Serve with some sauce for pudding
