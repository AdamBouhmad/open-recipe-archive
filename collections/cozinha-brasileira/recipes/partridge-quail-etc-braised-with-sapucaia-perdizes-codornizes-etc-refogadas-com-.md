## Ingredients

- birds roasted on a spit
- two cups of wine
- two egg yolks
- a pinch of polvilho
- portion of peeled and cut sapucaia fruits
- one tablespoon of sugar
- juice of one lemon
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut the roasted birds on a spit into pieces
2. on the other hand make a sauce with two cups of wine, two beaten egg yolks, a pinch of polvilho, a portion of peeled and cut sapucaia fruits, one tablespoon of sugar, the juice of one lemon, little salt and nutmeg
3. put the meat in it
4. let it boil for another quarter of an hour and serve
