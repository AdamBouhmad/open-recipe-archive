## Ingredients

- one loin
- salt
- green peppers
- lemon juice
- garlic
- chopped parsley
- onions
- bacon slices
- peeled pine nuts

## Directions

1. Take one loin, season it with salt, green peppers, lemon juice, garlic, finely chopped parsley and onions.
2. Wrap it in slices of bacon and place it in a pan with a portion of peeled pine nuts.
3. Put it in the oven or over coals, also covering the lid with coals.
4. When roasted, serve with the pine nuts in the same plate.
