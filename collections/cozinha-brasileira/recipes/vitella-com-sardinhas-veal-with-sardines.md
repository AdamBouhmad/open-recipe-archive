## Ingredients

- veal meat
- water
- salt
- butter
- broth
- nutmeg
- sardines
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut veal meat into finger-sized slices
2. Cook in salted water
3. Place in a casserole with a little butter, a spoonful of broth, a little nutmeg and let braise
4. Before serving, add whole washed sardines, heat and serve, arranging the meat and sardines symmetrically on the plate, sprinkling breadcrumbs on top and pouring the broth from the casserole over it
