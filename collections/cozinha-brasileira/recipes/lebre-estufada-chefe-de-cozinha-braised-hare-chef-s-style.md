## Ingredients

- loins from two hares
- salt
- garlic
- chopped parsley
- thyme (serpol)
- pepper
- bay leaves
- onions
- basil
- cumin
- lemon juice
- bacon lard
- bacon strips
- half a bottle of brandy (aguardente)
- equal amount of white wine
- two pounds of chopped veal

## Directions

1. Soak the hare loins in salt, garlic, chopped parsley, thyme, pepper, bay leaves, onions, basil, cumin, and lemon juice for twenty-four hours.
2. Remove the loins, dry them, and lard them with bacon strips.
3. Place them in a pan lined with bacon strips with half a bottle of brandy and an equal amount of white wine.
4. Let everything boil for three to four hours over a brisk fire with two pounds of chopped veal.
5. Strain and reduce the sauce, pour it over the hare, and serve cold.
