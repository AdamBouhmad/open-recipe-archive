## Ingredients

- 1 duck, cut into pieces
- salt
- pepper
- wheat flour
- egg yolks
- fat for frying
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one glass of vinegar
- pigeon peas cooked in water
- salt
- onion leaves
- parsley leaves

## Directions

1. Cut the duck into pieces and rub with salt and pepper
2. Roll them in wheat flour and then pass through egg yolks
3. Fry in little fat over a lively fire
4. Remove the pieces and add one tablespoon of wheat flour to the same fat
5. Stir and add one glass of vinegar and a portion of pigeon peas cooked in water, salt, onion leaves, and parsley leaves
6. Let them boil over low fire until the water dries, and serve the pigeon peas with the fried duck on top
