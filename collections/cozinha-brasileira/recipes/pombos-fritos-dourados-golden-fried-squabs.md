## Ingredients

- squabs
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- chopped parsley
- pepper
- butter
- six to eight eggs

## Directions

1. Cut squabs in half and flatten with a palmatoria
2. Wrap them in a coating of breadcrumbs with salt, chopped parsley and pepper
3. Fry on both sides in butter
4. When fried and of good color, place on a plate and pour over them six to eight beaten eggs
5. Place in the oven and allow to take good color, then serve
