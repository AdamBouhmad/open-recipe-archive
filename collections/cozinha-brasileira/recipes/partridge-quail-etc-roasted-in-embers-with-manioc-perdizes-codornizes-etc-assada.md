## Ingredients

- birds
- salt
- juice of pepper
- strips of bacon
- manioc
- milk
- one tablespoon of sugar
- little cinnamon
- two egg yolks
- one chalice of wine

## Directions

1. Rub the birds with salt and juice of pepper
2. wrap them in fine strips of bacon and put them in the embers with some manioc
3. when cooked remove them
4. unwrap the bacon and serve with the manioc peeled and with a sauce made of boiled milk with one tablespoon of sugar, a little ground cinnamon and incorporated with two egg yolks dissolved in one chalice of wine
