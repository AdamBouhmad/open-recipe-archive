## Ingredients

- lamb lungs
- lamb liver
- lamb heart
- lamb kidneys
- lamb feet
- two onions
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- two cloves
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- eggs

## Directions

1. Clean and cook the lungs, liver, heart, kidneys and feet, removing the bones
2. Chop everything together with two onions, some parsley, salt, pepper and two cloves
3. Fry with two spoonfuls of butter
4. Place on a plate, cover with breadcrumbs and beaten eggs
5. Heat in the oven and serve in this arrangement
