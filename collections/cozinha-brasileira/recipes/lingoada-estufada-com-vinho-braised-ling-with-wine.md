## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of butter
- one or two cleaned fish sprinkled with salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- one cup of white wine

## Directions

1. Put two tablespoons of butter in a dish that can resist the action of fire and one or two cleaned fish sprinkled with salt, pepper and grated nutmeg
2. Add one cup of white wine, and after covering, boil it between two fires and serve it very hot
