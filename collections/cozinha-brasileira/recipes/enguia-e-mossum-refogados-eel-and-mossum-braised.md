## Ingredients

- two or three small eels
- fat
- one onion
- one spoon of wheat flour
- salt
- pepper
- one bottle of water
- one chalice of sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut two or three small eels into pieces after removing the skin
2. Braise these pieces in fat until cooked
3. Remove them and place them on a plate
4. In the same butter, fry one onion, one spoon of wheat flour, a little salt and pepper
5. Add one bottle of water and one chalice of sour orange juice, let it boil, pour it hot over the eels and serve
