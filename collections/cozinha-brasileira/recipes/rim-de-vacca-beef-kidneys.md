## Ingredients

- beef kidney
- water
- salt
- butter
- wheat flour
- white wine
- chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Split the kidney and remove the hard nerve cord, then cut into small pieces
2. Place for three minutes in hot salted water, away from the fire
3. After draining, dry it
4. Melt a spoonful of butter in a casserole, add the kidney and let it fry for five minutes
5. Remove from fire and add a spoonful of wheat flour
6. Stir and add more a glass of white wine, chopped parsley, salt and pepper
7. Let it boil once more and serve
