## Ingredients

- six to eight eggs
- salt
- chopped chive leaves
- ground pepper
- one spoon of butter

## Directions

1. Break six to eight eggs into a bowl
2. Beat them with a little ground salt, chopped chive leaves and a little ground pepper
3. Melt one spoon of cow butter in a frying pan
4. When melted and still not very hot, pour in the eggs, continuing to stir so they don't stick to the bottom
5. When fried, serve
