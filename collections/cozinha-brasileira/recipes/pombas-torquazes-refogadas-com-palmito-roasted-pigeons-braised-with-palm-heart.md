## Ingredients

- two pigeons
- two spoonfuls of fat
- salt
- bread crusts, grated
- parsley leaves
- one glass of white wine
- one palm heart
- water

## Directions

1. Braise two pigeons in two spoonfuls of fat, adding a little salt
2. Split them in half and add some grated bread crusts, parsley leaves, and one glass of white wine, letting them boil for a quarter hour over coals
3. When cooked, add one chopped palm heart that has been boiled in water and salt, and one cup of water
4. Let it boil a bit more and serve
