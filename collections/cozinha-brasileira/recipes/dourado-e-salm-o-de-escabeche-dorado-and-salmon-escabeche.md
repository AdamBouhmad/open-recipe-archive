## Ingredients

- fish cut into steaks
- sour orange or vinegar juice
- salt
- chopped onion
- garlic
- pepper
- clove
- a piece of lard
- one or two spoons of cornmeal
- carapicus or chopped mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into steaks and place them in a soak in sour orange or vinegar juice, salt, chopped onion, garlic, pepper and clove
2. Then fry a piece of lard and add one or two spoons of cornmeal (fine cornmeal)
3. Stir, add the fish with all its sauce and a portion of carapicus or chopped mushrooms
4. Let it boil over moderate heat and serve
