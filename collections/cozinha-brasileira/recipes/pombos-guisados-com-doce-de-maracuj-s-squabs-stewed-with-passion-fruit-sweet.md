## Ingredients

- young squabs
- butter
- a portion of passion fruit pulp
- two tablespoons sugar
- some cloves
- cinnamon
- one glass white wine

## Directions

1. Take young squabs and fry them in butter
2. Place them in a pot with a portion of passion fruit pulp, two tablespoons sugar, some cloves, cinnamon and one glass of white wine
3. Allow to boil over very low fire
4. When cooked, serve the passion fruit sweet with the squabs on top
