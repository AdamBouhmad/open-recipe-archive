## Ingredients

- venison loin
- four spoonfuls of fat
- two spoonfuls of wheat flour
- one cup of vinegar
- one bottle of water
- salt
- peppercorns
- parsley
- one onion, cut
- nutmeg, grated
- white beans or pigeon peas

## Directions

1. Braise the loin in four spoonfuls of fat
2. After it is cooked, sprinkle it with two spoonfuls of wheat flour
3. Add one cup of vinegar, one bottle of water, salt, some peppercorns, parsley, one cut onion, grated nutmeg, and a portion of white beans or pigeon peas
