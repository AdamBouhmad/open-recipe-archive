## Ingredients

- six eggs
- salt
- parsley
- borage leaves
- one spoon of fat
- one chopped onion
- ground pepper

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs with a little salt, parsley and chopped borage leaves
2. In another pan, melt one spoon of fat with one chopped onion; when very hot, pour in the eggs, stir and when fried, serve, covering with a little ground pepper
