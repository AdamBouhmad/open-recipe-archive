## Ingredients

- lamb or kid loin or quarter
- water
- salt
- pieces of bacon
- parsley
- onion
- garlic clove
- two tablespoons wheat flour
- two tablespoons butter
- one cup white wine
- juice of one lemon
- sugar
- raisins
- lemon slices, peeled and pitted

## Directions

1. Place lamb or kid loin or quarter in a pan with water, salt, pieces of bacon, parsley, onion, and garlic clove
2. Boil until cooked
3. Fry two tablespoons wheat flour with two tablespoons butter until dark brass-colored
4. Add the strained broth, one cup white wine, lemon juice, sugar, raisins, and lemon slices
5. Add the meat and boil over moderate heat until the broth is reduced to few cups
6. Serve with this sauce
