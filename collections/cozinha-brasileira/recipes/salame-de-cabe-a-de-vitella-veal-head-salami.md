## Ingredients

- veal head
- veal feet
- veal heart
- water
- salt
- eggs
- almonds, chopped
- lemon zest
- clove
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the head, feet and heart of veal in salted water, removing the scum
2. When cooked, remove bones from the head and feet and cut all the meat into small pieces
3. Cut the heart into slices the length of a finger and the tongue after removing the skin
4. Add some boiled eggs, chopped almonds, lemon zest, clove, pepper and salt, then place this mixture in a cloth and tie it tightly
5. Thicken the broth to serve with it
