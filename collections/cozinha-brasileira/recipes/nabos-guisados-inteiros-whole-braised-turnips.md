## Ingredients

- turnips
- water
- butter
- wheat flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and turn the turnips and boil in water for five minutes
2. Separately, melt two spoons of butter with two of wheat flour
3. Let the flour brown a little, stirring, and add a cup of broth or water, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and a spoon of sugar
4. Boil the turnips in this sauce and serve
