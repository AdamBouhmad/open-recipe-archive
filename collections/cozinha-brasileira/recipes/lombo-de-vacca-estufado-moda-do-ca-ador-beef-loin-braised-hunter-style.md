## Ingredients

- beef loin
- ham slices
- rabbit meat strips
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- lard slices
- beef broth, one spoonful
- dry Madeira wine, two glasses
- lard, one pound chopped finely
- onions, two
- parsley sprigs
- thyme
- bay leaves
- valeriana (blessed herb)
- cloves
- roasted ham slices
- rabbit thighs
- butter

## Directions

1. Take a good piece of loin and prepare it by filling it with ham slices and rabbit meat strips seasoned with salt, pepper and nutmeg
2. Cover the loin entirely with lard slices
3. After tying it, place it in a covered pot over heat
4. Add a spoonful of meat broth, two glasses of dry Madeira wine, a pound of finely chopped lard, two onions, a sprig of parsley, thyme, bay leaves, valeriana, cloves, some salt, pepper, some roasted ham slices and rabbit thighs
5. Cover with a sheet of paper oiled with butter
6. Two hours before serving, bring to a boil; then let the loin drain
7. Remove the strings and pass the broth through a silk sieve, removing the fat, leaving it reduced to half the meat essence
8. Add two spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and half a glass of Madeira wine to the broth and remove from heat
9. At this point add a little fresh butter and, divided into three parts, two-thirds are placed in the serving dish where the loin is placed, basted with meat essence, and the rest of the sauce is poured into a sauce boat garnished with rabbit loins
