## Ingredients

- a portion of barley
- water
- salt
- two spoonfuls of butter
- two cups of milk cream

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of barley in water and salt
2. When cooked, add two spoonfuls of butter and two cups of milk cream
3. Serve
