## Ingredients

- lobster
- butter
- white wine
- salt
- peppers

## Directions

1. Tie the lobster firmly on the spit
2. Roast over a live fire, basting it with melted butter, white wine, salt and peppers
3. When cooked (known when the hard shell becomes tender), serve with its sauce
