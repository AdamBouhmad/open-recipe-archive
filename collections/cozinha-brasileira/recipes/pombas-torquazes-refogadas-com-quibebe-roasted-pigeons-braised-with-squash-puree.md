## Ingredients

- pigeons
- salt
- fat
- wheat flour
- one cup of milk
- one squash
- water
- one cup of cream
- two spoonfuls of sugar
- eight spoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Split the pigeons in half, salt them, and fry them in fat on both sides
2. Dust them with wheat flour, add one cup of milk, and let them boil over coals
3. On the other side, cook a peeled, halved, and seeded squash in water and salt
4. Remove the pieces and mash them to make a pulp
5. Mix in one cup of cream, two spoonfuls of sugar and eight spoonfuls of butter
6. Place on a plate, put the pigeons with the sauce on top, and serve
