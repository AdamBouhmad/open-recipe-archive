## Ingredients

- six eggs
- two chopped chicken livers
- salt
- Comarí peppers
- parsley
- chopped onion leaves
- pepper
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs and add two chopped chicken livers, season with salt, Comarí peppers, parsley and chopped onion leaves
2. Fry in very hot fat, place on a plate, and serve, having sprinkled with pepper and grated nutmeg
