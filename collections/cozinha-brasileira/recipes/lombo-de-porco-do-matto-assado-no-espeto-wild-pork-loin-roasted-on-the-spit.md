## Ingredients

- one loin
- salt
- sour orange juice
- tutú de feijão
- salads

## Directions

1. Take one loin, rub it with salt and sour orange juice.
2. Thread it on the spit and roast it.
3. In the first fifteen minutes it should be over a flame, and afterwards over a bed of coals, without moistening it.
4. When roasted, serve with tutú de feijão and salads.
