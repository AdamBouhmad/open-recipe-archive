## Ingredients

- beef
- salt
- garlic
- vinegar
- banana leaves or caeté leaves

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of beef the thickness of three fingers into slices.
2. Season the slices with salt and garlic, adding a little vinegar.
3. Wrap them in banana or caeté leaves so the meat does not get ashes on it.
4. Place them in hot embers, covering them properly.
5. Leave them for three to four hours, renewing the embers from time to time.
6. The meat prepared this way is very delicious and retains all its juice.
