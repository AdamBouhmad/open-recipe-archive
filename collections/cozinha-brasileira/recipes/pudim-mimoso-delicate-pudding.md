## Ingredients

- eight ounces of sugar
- eight ounces of butter
- eight ounces of polvilho
- eight egg yolks
- a little salt
- vanilla
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix eight ounces of sugar, eight ounces of butter, eight ounces of polvilho and eight egg yolks
2. Add a little salt, vanilla and nutmeg
3. Mix well for one hour
4. Place in a buttered mold
5. Bake in the oven for half an hour
