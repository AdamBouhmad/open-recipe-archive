## Ingredients

- half a pound of chocolate
- half a pound of sugar
- two egg yolks
- two bottles of milk
- ground cinnamon
- half a spoonful of flower water

## Directions

1. Grate half a pound of chocolate
2. Place it in a vessel with half a pound of sugar and two beaten egg yolks
3. Add two bottles of milk
4. Beat with a proper chocolate whisk
5. Place over fire to boil, continuing to beat
6. After it has boiled, add a little ground cinnamon and half a spoonful of flower water
7. Remove from fire and serve hot
