## Ingredients

- turkey pieces
- a portion of peeled small potatoes
- butter
- chopped parsley
- peeled lemon slices

## Directions

1. Take the turkey pieces and a portion of peeled small potatoes which fry in butter over moderate heat
2. When cooked, remove with a skimmer
3. Place on the plate covering them with a portion of chopped parsley and some peeled lemon slices
