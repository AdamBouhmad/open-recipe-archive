## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Boil milk with sugar, cinnamon, and egg yolks
2. Pour everything into a dish and cover with beaten egg whites boiled in water
3. Sprinkle with plenty of sugar
4. Heat a saber in the fire and burn the sugar lengthwise, making different figures
