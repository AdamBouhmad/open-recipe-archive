## Ingredients

- repolho (cabbage)
- sal (salt)
- vinagre (vinegar)

## Directions

1. Chop and season a cabbage with salt, and place it in a pan with a cup of vinegar
2. Compress it with a weight and leave it for twenty-four hours
3. Then braise it as already explained for fermented cabbage
