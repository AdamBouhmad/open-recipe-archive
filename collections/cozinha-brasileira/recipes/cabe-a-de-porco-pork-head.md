## Ingredients

- pork head
- fresh pork meat
- herbs
- nutmeg
- clove
- pepper
- salt
- onions
- chives
- salt pork
- carrots
- bay leaves
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Stuff a pork head with pieces of fresh pork meat after carefully removing all the bones
2. Season with aromatic herbs, nutmeg, clove, pepper, salt, onions, and chopped chives
3. Place everything in a vessel and leave for eight to ten days
4. Remove the head from the vessel and let it drain
5. Sew up the openings where the bones were removed and tie with string so it maintains its shape during cooking
6. Wrap it in white cloth tied at both ends
7. Place it in a frying pan with the head bones, salt pork, carrots, onions, six to eight bay leaves, sprigs of chives, clove, a little salt, and some pieces of pork or other meat
8. Moisten everything with meat broth
9. Cook over low heat for ten hours
10. Remove the head and squeeze it gently to release the liquid it contains, being careful not to lose its shape
11. Trim any protruding points and serve cold
