## Ingredients

- one capon
- bacon, thin slices
- butter
- paper

## Directions

1. After the capon is killed and plucked in hot water, pass it over a smokeless flame to remove the remaining feathers
2. Open and clean the capon
3. Thread it on a spit
4. Cover the breast with a thin slice of bacon, and the entire body with paper anointed in butter
5. When roasted, remove the paper and bacon, and serve
