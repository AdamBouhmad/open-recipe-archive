## Ingredients

- three spoonfuls of wheat flour
- three spoonfuls of butter
- rabbit pieces
- one cup of vinegar
- one bowlful of water
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- pepper
- chickpeas
- two egg yolks
- half cup of water
- three spoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Toast three spoonfuls of wheat flour in three spoonfuls of butter until browned
2. Add the pieces of a rabbit and immediately add one cup of vinegar, one bowlful of water, salt, parsley, onion, pepper, and a portion of chickpeas
3. Let it cook and when cooked and the broth reduced, incorporate the broth with two egg yolks beaten in half cup of water and three spoonfuls of sugar
4. Serve
