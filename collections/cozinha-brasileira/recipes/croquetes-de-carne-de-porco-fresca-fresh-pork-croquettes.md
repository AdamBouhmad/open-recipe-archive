## Ingredients

- leftover cooked or roasted pork
- salt
- garlic
- parsley
- pimenta da India
- bay leaf
- thyme
- vinegar
- two raw eggs
- dried bread crumbs or toasted bread crumb
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Take the leftover cooked or roasted pork and chop it finely with a cleaver.
2. Season it with salt, garlic, parsley, pimenta da India, bay leaf, and thyme, all finely chopped.
3. Add two tablespoons of vinegar, knead well with two raw eggs, adding a little dried bread crumbs or toasted bread crumb.
4. If you do not have bread crumbs or dried bread, substitute with two tablespoons of wheat flour.
5. From this mixture, form little balls and fry them in butter.
6. Serve with sprigs of parsley or a Robert sauce.
7. These can be prepared in the same manner with beef, veal, mutton, poultry, or game.
