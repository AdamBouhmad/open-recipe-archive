## Ingredients

- Lamb tails
- Hot water
- Beaten eggs
- Bread crumbs
- Melted butter
- Chuchús

## Directions

1. Scald the tails in hot water
2. Wrap them in beaten eggs and bread crumbs
3. Roast on the grill
4. Drizzle melted butter over them
5. Serve with stewed chuchús
