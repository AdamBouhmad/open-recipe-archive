## Ingredients

- a portion of barley
- milk
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of barley in milk
2. When cooked, let the milk coagulate
3. Cut afterwards into slices
4. Fry in butter
5. Serve covered with breadcrumbs
