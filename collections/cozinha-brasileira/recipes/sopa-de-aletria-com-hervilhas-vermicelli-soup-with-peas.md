## Ingredients

- carrots
- turnips
- onions
- celery
- garlic
- clove from India
- beef broth
- vermicelli
- peas

## Directions

1. Put in beef broth a portion of carrots, turnips, onions, celery, garlic and some clove heads from India
2. Let it cook for two hours
3. Strain through a cloth and add a portion of vermicelli scalded in hot water and a portion of peas cooked only in water
