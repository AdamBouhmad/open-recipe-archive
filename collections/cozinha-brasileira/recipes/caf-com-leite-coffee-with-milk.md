## Ingredients

- milk
- ground coffee

## Directions

1. Heat a portion of milk until boiling
2. While boiling, add the ground coffee
3. Stir and strain through a cloth
4. Serve
5. Alternatively, serve strained coffee in one pot and milk in another, allowing each guest to mix according to their taste
