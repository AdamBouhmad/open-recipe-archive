## Ingredients

- three tablespoons of butter
- two tablespoons of wheat flour
- venison loin
- one bottle of water
- salt
- few pimento peppers
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one root of taro or yam

## Directions

1. Melt three tablespoons of butter and brown two tablespoons of wheat flour in it
2. Then add the loin cut into slices
3. Add one bottle of water, salt, few pimento peppers, parsley, onion leaves and one root of taro or yam cooked in water and salt and cut into slices
4. Let boil for one hour over moderate heat and serve
