## Ingredients

- veal shoulder
- salt
- garlic
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Lift the skin covering the shoulder
2. Insert a small tube under it to lift it
3. Put it on the spit
4. Season with salt, garlic and vinegar
5. Roast over a brisk fire for the first minutes to harden it, then finish roasting over coals
6. Frequently moisten it with its own sauce
7. Serve with salad
