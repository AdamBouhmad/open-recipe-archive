## Ingredients

- dozen mushrooms or champignons, cut
- one carrot
- one celery root
- one onion, cut in slices
- chives
- parsley
- beef broth
- white wine
- bread
- milk
- one garlic clove

## Directions

1. Fry over a moderate fire a dozen cut mushrooms (or champignons), one carrot, one celery root, one onion cut in slices, chives, parsley; then add little by little while stirring one cup of beef broth, and add finally an equal amount of white wine and parsley to taste; let it boil one hour and pass through a silk or hair sieve.
2. On the other side, boil bread in a cup of milk until dry, and pass through a sieve; add this pulp to the broth and serve; some people like to boil with the bread one garlic clove.
