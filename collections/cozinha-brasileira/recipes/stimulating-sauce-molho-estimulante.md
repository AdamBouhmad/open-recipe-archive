## Ingredients

- one cup of vinegar
- serpol
- bay leaves
- one garlic clove
- onion
- pepper
- beef broth
- substance of roasted meat
- wheat flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Place in a casserole one cup of vinegar, serpol, bay leaves, one garlic clove, onion, pepper; let everything boil until it reduces to two-thirds; add beef broth, a little substance of roasted meat, a little wheat flour fried with butter, and finally whatever is available, strain.
