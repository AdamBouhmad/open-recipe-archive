## Ingredients

- one chicken
- salt
- pepper
- one sprig of parsley
- onion leaves
- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- four to six cups of meat broth
- three egg yolks, beaten
- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one glass of white wine
- a little nutmeg
- lemon slices, peeled and without seeds

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken pieces with salt, pepper, a sprig of parsley, onion leaves, and one tablespoon of wheat flour
2. Separately, make a sauce with four to six cups of meat broth and three beaten egg yolks, one tablespoon of wheat flour, one tablespoon of butter, one tablespoon of sugar, one glass of white wine, and a little nutmeg
3. Place the cooked chicken pieces in this sauce
4. Simmer a little longer
5. Serve with peeled lemon slices without seeds on top
