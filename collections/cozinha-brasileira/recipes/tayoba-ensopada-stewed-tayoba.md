## Ingredients

- tayoba
- wheat flour
- grated cheese
- fat
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the root of a tayoba cooked into small slices
2. Separately, sauté a spoon of flour and two of grated cheese in two spoons of fat, salt, and pepper
