## Ingredients

- roasted lamb
- tayoba fern shoot tips, peeled and scalded
- one tablespoon fat
- three cups white wine
- salt
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- one tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Cut roasted lamb into pieces and peel and scald tayoba shoot tips
2. Braise in a pan with one tablespoon fat and three cups white wine, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and one tablespoon sugar
3. Boil near the fire for one hour
4. Serve with cornmeal angú
