## Ingredients

- one monkey
- water
- salt
- a dozen land bananas with skin
- two tablespoons of wheat flour
- the same amount of butter
- one cup of white wine
- two tablespoons of sugar
- one tablespoon of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the head from the monkey and boil it in water and salt with a dozen land bananas with skin
2. When the meat is cooked, brown two tablespoons of wheat flour in the same amount of butter, and before it colors, add one cup of white wine with two tablespoons of sugar and one of lemon juice
3. After boiling a little, add the meat without bones and cut into pieces
4. Let it boil once more, and pour everything over the bananas peeled and placed whole on a plate, and serve
