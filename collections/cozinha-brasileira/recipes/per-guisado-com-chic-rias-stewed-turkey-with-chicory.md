## Ingredients

- turkey
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- cumin
- breadcrumbs
- eggs
- butter
- white wine

## Directions

1. Boil the turkey cut into pieces in water, salt, parsley, onion leaves and a pinch of cumin
2. When cooked, remove the meat, coat it in breadcrumbs and pass it through eggs, then fry it in butter
3. Reduce the broth to one cup, strain it and mix it with another cup of white wine
4. Pour it over the meat, placed over sautéed chicory
