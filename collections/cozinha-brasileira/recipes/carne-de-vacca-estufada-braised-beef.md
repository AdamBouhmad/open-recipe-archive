## Ingredients

- beef, larded with pieces of fresh bacon
- pieces of fresh bacon
- onions, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- wheat flour
- one cup of broth
- tubaras (a type of vegetable)
- mushrooms
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place the larded beef (filled with pieces of bacon) in a pot, garnished with pieces of bacon, chopped onions, salt, and pepper, and let it braise over moderate heat, keeping it well covered.
2. When cooked, remove the meat and fry a little wheat flour in fat; then add one cup of broth, tubaras, mushrooms, lemon juice and a little salt.
3. Let it boil and serve the meat with this sauce.
