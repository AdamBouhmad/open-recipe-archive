## Ingredients

- parsley
- onion leaves
- half an onion
- bread crumbs
- milk
- three egg yolks
- butter
- six to eight eggs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Chop parsley, onion leaves, and half an onion.
2. Mix with bread crumbs softened in milk and mixed with three egg yolks.
3. Knead until it becomes a soft paste.
4. Spread this paste on a plate greased with butter.
5. Place over coals until the paste is almost cooked.
6. Pour six to eight eggs over the top.
7. Sprinkle with salt and ground pepper.
8. Cover with a lid filled with coals.
9. Cook until done and serve.
