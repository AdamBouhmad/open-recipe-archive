## Ingredients

- one ham
- wine with garlic
- salt
- pepper
- sweet oil
- sage branch
- half bottle of white wine

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the ham, leaving all the fat
2. Place it in garlic wine for three days with salt, pepper, sweet oil, a sage branch, and half a bottle of white wine
3. Put it on the spit and roast it for two hours, basting it with the garlic wine sauce
4. After cooking, serve it with a sauce made from the juice that dripped, adding chopped scallions and half of the garlic wine where the ham was
