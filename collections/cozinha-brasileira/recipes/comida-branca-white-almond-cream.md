## Ingredients

- one pound of sweet almonds
- eight bitter almonds
- milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take one pound of sweet almonds and eight bitter almonds; crush them well after preparing.
2. Bring milk and sugar to a boil, and dissolve the almonds in the boiling milk, then pass through a fine sieve.
3. Return to the fire and let cook, stirring, and reduce.
4. When thick, place on a dish and serve.
