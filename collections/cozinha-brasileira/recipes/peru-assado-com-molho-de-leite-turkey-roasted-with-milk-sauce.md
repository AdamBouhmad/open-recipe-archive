## Ingredients

- turkey
- turkey liver
- calf liver
- bread crumbs
- red wine
- two tablespoons of sugar
- nutmeg
- cardamom
- salt

## Directions

1. Take the turkey and fill the breast with its liver and a piece of calf liver passed through a sieve and mixed with a handful of bread crumbs moistened with red wine
2. Add two tablespoons of sugar, grated nutmeg, cardamom, and a little salt
3. Spit the turkey and roast
