## Ingredients

- veal shoulder
- butter
- onion leaves
- salt
- pepper
- meat broth
- lemon

## Directions

1. Place the veal shoulder in a pan with a little butter, salt, onion leaves, pepper and two cups of meat broth
2. Roast over coals or in the oven
3. Serve with the proper sauce, adding some peeled lemon slices without seeds before serving
