## Ingredients

- one spoon of wheat flour or corn meal
- one spoon of fat or cow's butter
- one chopped onion
- one spoon of mustard powder
- a few slices of lemon
- salt
- beef broth
- a little vinegar
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry one spoon of wheat flour or corn meal in one spoon of fat or cow's butter until browned
2. Add one chopped onion and one spoon of mustard powder
3. Add a few slices of lemon and salt
4. Add as much beef broth as needed
5. Add a little vinegar, sugar, and lemon juice
6. Serve
