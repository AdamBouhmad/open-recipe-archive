## Ingredients

- turkey legs
- potatoes, peeled
- butter
- parsley, chopped
- lemon, peeled and sliced

## Directions

1. Take the turkey legs and a portion of peeled potatoes and fry them in butter over moderate heat
2. When cooked, remove them with a skimmer
3. Place on a plate, covering them with a portion of chopped parsley and some slices of peeled lemon
