## Ingredients

- squabs
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- pepper
- one half cup meat broth
- three tablespoons vinegar
- two pinches chopped onion
- the same amount of parsley
- one teaspoon mustard powder

## Directions

1. Clean the squabs and cut them in half along the spine
2. Flatten them by beating with a piece of wood
3. Roast them in hot butter
4. Cover both sides with a good layer of breadcrumbs, seasoning with salt and pepper
5. Grill on both sides
6. Serve with the following sauce: boil one half cup meat broth with three tablespoons vinegar, two pinches chopped onion and the same amount of parsley, salt, pepper and one teaspoon mustard powder
7. For this preparation, the youngest squabs should be sought
