## Ingredients

- veal chops
- salt
- pepper
- cabbage leaves
- onion

## Directions

1. Cut the chops and braise them in boiling water
2. Add salt, pepper, some cabbage leaves, and onion
3. Let them cook
4. Remove the chops and strain the broth, letting it reduce
5. Remove the fat from the broth
6. Serve the chops with the broth poured over them
