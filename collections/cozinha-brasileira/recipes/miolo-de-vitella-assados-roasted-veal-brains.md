## Ingredients

- veal brains
- water
- salt
- grated cheese
- sugar
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Boil the veal brains for half an hour in water and salt
2. After cleaning, let cool and slice onto a plate with grated cheese on the bottom
3. Add another layer of cheese on top, then sugar, cinnamon, and ground cloves
4. Cover the plate and place over coals with fire on top
5. Let roast until they take on a good color and serve
