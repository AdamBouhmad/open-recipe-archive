## Ingredients

- oysters or shellfish
- white sauce
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Boil oysters or shellfish, adding the water contained in their shells
2. They cook with low heat
3. After cooked, let them drain
4. Pour white sauce on top with the juice of 1 lemon
5. This sauce serves to put over roasted and cooked fish
