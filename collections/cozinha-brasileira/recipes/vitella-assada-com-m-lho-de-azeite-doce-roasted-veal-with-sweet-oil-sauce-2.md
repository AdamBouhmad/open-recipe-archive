## Ingredients

- veal piece
- sweet oil
- lemon juice
- anchovies
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Thread the veal onto a spit
2. While roasting, baste with a sauce made of sweet oil, lemon juice, anchovies, onion, salt, pepper, and chopped parsley
3. At the end, sprinkle grated breadcrumbs over the roast
4. Remove from the spit and serve with the drippings that fell onto the plate below the spit
