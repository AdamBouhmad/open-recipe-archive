## Ingredients

- half a plate of chosen and washed rice
- one bottle of milk
- little salt
- parsley
- cow's butter

## Directions

1. Cook half a plate of chosen and washed rice in one bottle of milk with little salt and parsley
2. When cooked and dried, pour into the plate
3. Serve with a little cow's butter on top
