## Ingredients

- water squash, peeled and chopped
- milk
- salt
- wheat flour
- butter
- parsley
- onion

## Directions

1. Boil the peeled and chopped water squash in milk and salt
2. When cooked and the milk is nearly dried up, add a spoonful of wheat flour fried in two spoonfuls of butter, parsley, and onion
3. Boil several times and serve
