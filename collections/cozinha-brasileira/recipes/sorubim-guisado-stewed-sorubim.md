## Ingredients

- loins of sorubim
- wheat flour or cangica cornmeal
- two tablespoons of fat
- carapicus
- half a dozen artichoke hearts
- parsley
- onion leaves
- thyme
- one chicara of meat broth
- salt
- pepper
- lemon or sour land orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut the loins of sorubim into pieces
2. Wrap these in wheat flour or cangica cornmeal
3. Sauté in two tablespoons of fat until they take on a copper color
4. Place in a casserole a portion of carapicus and half a dozen artichoke hearts, parsley, onion leaves and thyme
5. Add one chicara of meat broth, salt, pepper and the fish with the sauce
6. Let boil for half an hour over moderate heat
7. Once cooked, pour onto a plate with lemon or sour land orange juice and serve
