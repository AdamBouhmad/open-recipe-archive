## Ingredients

- sirloin slices
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take some slices of sirloin the thickness of a finger.
2. Beat this meat so it becomes flatter; then trim the skins around it.
3. Dip it for some minutes in hot butter with salt and pepper.
4. Take it from there to a skillet, which should be over fire, turning the slices after a few minutes.
5. Serve these steaks with the same butter and chopped parsley.
