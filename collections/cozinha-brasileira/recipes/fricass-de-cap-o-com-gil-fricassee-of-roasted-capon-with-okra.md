## Ingredients

- one capon roasted on a spit
- two pounds of roasted mutton meat
- two tablespoons of wheat flour toasted in butter
- one chicara of broth
- one chicara of red wine
- nutmeg
- one dozen okras
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Take one capon roasted on a spit and two pounds of roasted mutton meat
2. Cut into pieces
3. Make a sauce from two tablespoons of wheat flour toasted in an equal amount of butter
4. Add one chicara of broth and one chicara of red wine to the flour mixture
5. Add the minced meat with a little nutmeg
6. When cooked, arrange around the plate one dozen okras that have been boiled in water and salt
