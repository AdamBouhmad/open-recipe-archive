## Ingredients

- young fat chickens
- thin strips of bacon
- butter
- green peas

## Directions

1. Lard the young fat chickens with thin bacon strips
2. Cover with paper greased with butter
3. Roast on a spit over a lively fire
4. Serve with its own drippings and green peas
