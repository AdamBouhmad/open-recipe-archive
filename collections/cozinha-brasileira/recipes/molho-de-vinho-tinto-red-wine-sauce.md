## Ingredients

- Red wine
- Water
- Sugar
- Lemon zest
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix one bottle of red wine, half of water, two spoonfuls of sugar, lemon zest and five cold egg yolks
2. Place over coals, let it boil for fifteen minutes
3. Serve with rice pudding
