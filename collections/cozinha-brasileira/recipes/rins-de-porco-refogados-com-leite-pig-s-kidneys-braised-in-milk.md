## Ingredients

- Pig's kidneys
- Salt
- Onion leaves
- Two cups of milk
- Two egg yolks
- One spoonful of sugar
- One glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Cut the pig's kidneys into thin slices
2. Add salt and onion leaves with two cups of milk
3. Once cooked, add two beaten egg yolks mixed with a spoonful of sugar and a glass of white wine
4. Bring to a boil once more and serve
