## Ingredients

- some beets
- two spoons of butter
- one spoon of wheat flour
- one cup of wine
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil some beets and after peeling, grate them
2. On the other hand, melt two spoons of butter with one of wheat flour, one cup of wine, salt, pepper, nutmeg
3. When boiled, add the pulp of the beets
4. Let it dry over moderate heat, stirring, and serve
