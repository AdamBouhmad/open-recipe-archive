## Ingredients

- veal meat
- sweet oil
- onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut veal meat into slices and marinate for three to four hours in sweet oil, sliced onion, parsley, salt, pepper, and a little vinegar
2. Roast the slices on both sides on the grill and serve with chef's sauce
