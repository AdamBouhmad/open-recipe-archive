## Ingredients

- two chickens
- four spoonfuls of fat
- four cups of water
- salt
- parsley
- onions
- peppers
- one palm heart
- chicken livers and gizzards
- eight hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Brown two chickens with four spoonfuls of fat
2. Cut them afterward into pieces and add four cups of water, salt, parsley, onions, peppers, and a palm heart cut up
3. Let it boil over coals until the broth dries out
4. Remove the bones and add the chicken livers and gizzards, and eight hard-boiled eggs
5. Fill the mold with this hash, bake in the oven, and serve hot
