## Ingredients

- Three pork loins
- Wine with garlic
- Meat substance
- Pepper sauce

## Directions

1. After preparing and larding three loins, place them in wine with garlic for twenty-four hours
2. After this time, drain them and stuff them with meat substance
3. Serve with a pepper sauce
