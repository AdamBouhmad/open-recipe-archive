## Ingredients

- Fresh and fatty pork meat
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Take a piece of fresh, fatty pork meat and cut it into small pieces
2. Place all pieces in a vessel with a small quantity of water and salt
3. Activate the fire
4. As the water evaporates, continue pressing the pieces with a skimmer so they release all water
5. When the rendered pieces are well cooked and have achieved a nice color, remove from the vessel and drain
6. Serve
