## Ingredients

- one pound of wheat flour
- six egg yolks
- a little salt
- milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Knead one pound of wheat flour with six egg yolks, a little salt and milk
2. Knead well and extend over a board, coat the surface with butter
3. Cut the crusts with a glass and proceed as in the previous article
