## Ingredients

- frog legs
- water
- salt
- fat
- wheat flour
- one onion, minced
- one bottle of water
- one glass of lemon juice
- two egg yolks
- one spoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. Take the frog legs and boil for half an hour in water and salt
2. Separate the meat from the bones
3. Braise the meat in one spoonful of fat, half a spoonful of wheat flour, and a minced onion
4. Add one bottle of water, one glass of lemon juice, two egg yolks, and one spoonful of sugar
5. Let boil briefly and serve
