## Ingredients

- one ham leg
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- breadcrumbs
- cinnamon
- sugar
- raisins
- ham cooking broth

## Directions

1. Salt the ham leg and leave it for some days
2. After this, boil it with a little pepper and onions until cooked
3. Remove the skin and cover it with a paste made of grated breadcrumbs, cinnamon, sugar, and raisins moistened with the broth in which the ham was cooked
4. Place it thus prepared in a pan and leave it to roast in the oven
5. Serve it
