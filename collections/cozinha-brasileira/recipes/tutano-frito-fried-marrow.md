## Ingredients

- marrow from backbone
- wheat flour
- butter
- onion slices
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take the marrow that is in the backbone, roll it in wheat flour
2. Fry it in a little butter with some onion slices, salt, nutmeg, and serve
