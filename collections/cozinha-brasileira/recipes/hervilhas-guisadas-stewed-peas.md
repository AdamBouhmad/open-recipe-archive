## Ingredients

- a piece of bacon
- chopped parsley
- a portion of boiled peas in water and salt
- one egg yolk, beaten
- one cup of wine
- one spoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry a piece of bacon with chopped parsley
2. Add a portion of peas boiled in water and salt
3. Mix and add one egg yolk beaten with one cup of wine and one spoon of vinegar
4. Boil a little more and serve
