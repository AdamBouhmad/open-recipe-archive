## Ingredients

- asparagus sprouts
- water
- salt
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Tie the sprouts in small bundles
2. Place them to boil in salted water
3. Once boiled, remove and serve with melted butter fried with breadcrumbs
