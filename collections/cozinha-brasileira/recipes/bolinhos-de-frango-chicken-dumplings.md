## Ingredients

- meat of one chicken roasted on a spit
- half a pound of ham
- half a pound of veal meat
- a quarter of bacon
- one onion
- one spoon of wheat flour
- four egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- ground clove
- one cup of wine
- three egg yolks
- one cup of milk
- one spoon of sugar
- a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take the meat of one chicken roasted on a spit, half a pound of ham, half a pound of veal meat, a quarter of bacon, and one onion.
2. Mince everything well, mix with one spoon of wheat flour, four egg yolks, salt, pepper and ground clove.
3. Make small dumplings and fry them in butter.
4. When almost cooked, add one cup of wine, three egg yolks dissolved in one cup of milk, one spoon of sugar and a little nutmeg.
5. Let it come to a boil and serve.
