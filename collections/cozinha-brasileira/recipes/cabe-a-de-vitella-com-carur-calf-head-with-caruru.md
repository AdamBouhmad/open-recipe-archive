## Ingredients

- calf head
- water
- salt
- caruru
- cornstarch
- butter
- parsley
- pepper
- onion leaves
- beef broth
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cook a calf head in water and salt
2. Remove the bones and cut the meat into small pieces
3. Take a handful of finely chopped caruru and fry in a saucepan where a sauce has been made with cornstarch fried in butter, parsley, salt, pepper, onion leaves, beef broth, and lemon juice
4. When cooked, serve with cornmeal porridge (angú de fubá de moinho)
