## Ingredients

- one chicken, cut into pieces
- wine with garlic
- hot butter
- one cup of water
- one spoon of wheat flour
- juice of one lemon
- lemon slices without seeds
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Soak the chicken pieces in garlic wine for some time
2. Braise in hot butter until browned
3. Add one cup of water and one spoon of wheat flour
4. Add lemon juice and lemon slices
5. Let simmer until cooked
6. Thicken the sauce with two egg yolks and serve
