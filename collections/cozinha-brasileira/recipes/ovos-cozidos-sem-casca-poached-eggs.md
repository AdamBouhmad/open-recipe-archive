## Ingredients

- eggs
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Heat salted water in a wide vessel.
2. Break the eggs into the water carefully so they remain whole and keep their shape, staying separate from each other so they do not stick together.
3. Cook while counting to three hundred.
4. Remove with a skimmer.
5. Serve with meat sauce or vegetables.
