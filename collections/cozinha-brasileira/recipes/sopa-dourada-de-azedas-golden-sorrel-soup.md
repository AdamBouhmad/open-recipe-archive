## Ingredients

- sorrel
- lettuce plant
- chervil
- fat
- meat broth
- eight egg yolks
- bread

## Directions

1. Make it the same way as sorrel soup
2. But before pouring the soup over the bread, thicken the broth with eight beaten egg yolks in a portion of cold broth
3. Give it another boil and pour over toasted bread
