## Ingredients

- mutton shoulder
- fine strips of bacon
- strips of ham
- salt
- parsley
- bay leaves
- marjoram
- meat broth
- lemon juice
- peas or squash shoots

## Directions

1. Scald the mutton shoulder
2. Lard it with fine strips of bacon and ham
3. Put it in a pan with salt, parsley, bay leaves, marjoram, one cup of broth, and one spoon of lemon juice
4. Let it boil over coals for two hours
5. Strain the broth, remove the fat, and reduce it to caramel point
6. Pour this over the shoulder
7. Serve with fresh peas or squash shoots
