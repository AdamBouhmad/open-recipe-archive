## Ingredients

- one or two beet roots
- salt
- sweet oil
- vinegar
- ground pepper
- one onion
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel one or two beet roots
2. Grate them and season with salt, sweet oil, vinegar, ground pepper, one finely chopped onion and a portion of grated nutmeg
3. Serve
