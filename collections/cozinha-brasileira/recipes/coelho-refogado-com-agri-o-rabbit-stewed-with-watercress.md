## Ingredients

- rabbit
- pork fat
- onion
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- white wine
- watercress

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbit into pieces and sauté them in a little pork fat until well browned
2. Add one chopped onion, salt, peppers, parsley, and a cup of white wine
3. When the pieces are cooked, sauté a portion of chopped and scalded watercress in the same sauce
4. Place the watercress at the bottom of the plate and the pieces on top and serve
