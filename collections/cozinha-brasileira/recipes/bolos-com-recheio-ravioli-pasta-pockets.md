## Ingredients

- eight ounces of wheat flour
- three eggs
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- ground cinnamon
- beef or chicken meat
- fat
- pepper
- bay leaf
- tomatoes
- grated cheese
- onion
- butter

## Directions

1. Knead eight ounces of wheat flour with three eggs, little salt, grated nutmeg and ground cinnamon
2. When of good consistency, unroll the dough on a cloth and stretch it until it is as thin as a thread
3. Cut into three-inch square pieces and place one small spoonful of filling in the middle of each square
4. For the filling: finely mince a portion of beef or chicken meat and sauté in hot fat; add salt, pepper, bay leaf and tomatoes
5. Fold the edges and seal so the minced meat stays inside
6. Cut the edges with a pastry wheel and finally boil in a sufficiently large pot for two to three hours
7. When cooked, remove with a skimmer, let drain well, and serve covered with grated cheese and an onion minced and fried in butter
