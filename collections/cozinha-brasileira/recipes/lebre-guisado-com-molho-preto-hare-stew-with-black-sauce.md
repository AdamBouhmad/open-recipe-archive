## Ingredients

- hare
- bacon lard strips
- one cup water
- one cup wine
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- cumin
- onion leaves
- hare liver
- two hen livers
- hen blood
- orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut the hare into pieces and boil it with bacon lard strips, one cup of water, one cup of wine, salt, pepper, parsley, bay leaf, marjoram, cumin, and onion leaves
2. When cooked, remove it and pass the broth through a sieve
3. Add the hare liver, the blood, and the liver of two hens mixed together in orange juice
4. Heat, stirring continuously
5. Place the hare in the sauce
6. When everything is hot, serve
