## Ingredients

- three to four dozen fresh whole okras
- water
- salt
- fat
- vinegar
- plenty of comaré peppers

## Directions

1. Take three to four dozen fresh whole okras
2. Boil in water and salt
3. After boiling three to four times, remove them
4. Place on a plate
5. Serve with a sauce of fat, vinegar and plenty of comaré peppers on top
