## Ingredients

- 1 cleaned suckling pig, cut into pieces
- lard
- salt
- garlic
- Comarí pepper
- chopped parsley
- hot water
- spiced sauce on the side

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned pig into pieces and place in a vessel already containing hot lard.
2. Add salt, garlic, Comarí pepper, and chopped parsley and reduce the heat.
3. After cooking for half an hour, add some hot water and continue cooking.
4. When fully cooked and little liquid remains, remove from heat.
5. Serve with a spiced sauce on the side.
