## Ingredients

- one bird (jacú, mutúm, etc.)
- water
- salt
- parsley
- one glass of brandy
- two spoonfuls of fat
- one chopped onion
- a little wheat flour
- one lemon

## Directions

1. Boil the bird for one hour in water with salt, parsley, and one glass of brandy.
2. Cut it into pieces and sauté in two spoonfuls of fat with one chopped onion and a little wheat flour.
3. Add the reduced cooking broth and let boil for one quarter of an hour.
4. Before serving, squeeze the juice of one lemon over the meat and serve with the sauce well reduced.
