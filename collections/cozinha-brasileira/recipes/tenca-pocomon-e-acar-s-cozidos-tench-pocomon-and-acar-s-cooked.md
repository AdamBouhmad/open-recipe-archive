## Ingredients

- tench, pocomon and acarás fish
- water
- salt
- onion
- carapicus
- fat
- pepper
- parsley
- white wine
- three egg yolks

## Directions

1. To clean the scales of these fish easily, place them in hot water for a few minutes, then scale them, cut into pieces if large, and leave whole if small
2. Place in cold water, changing the water two more times
3. Remove and dry well with a towel and place in a casserole with a split onion, a portion of carapicus, and pour melted fat over it
4. Cover with salt, pepper, chopped parsley and place on the fire, let fry over a brisk heat for a few minutes
5. Moisten with a bottle of white wine, remove the casserole, place on the edge of the fire and let boil until the fish are cooked
6. Place the fish on a plate with onions and carapicus, and having broken three egg yolks in the sauce, pour over it, and serve
