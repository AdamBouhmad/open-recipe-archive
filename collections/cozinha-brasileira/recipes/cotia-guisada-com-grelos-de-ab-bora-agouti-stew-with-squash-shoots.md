## Ingredients

- agouti
- water
- salt
- peppers
- ginger
- squash shoots, destalked and bundled
- dozen tomatoes
- two spoons butter
- one cup broth
- one spoon dried breadcrumb powder
- salt
- peppers
- one small chopped onion

## Directions

1. Boil the agouti cut into pieces in water and salt
2. Add peppers, ginger, and a portion of squash shoots destalked and bundled in bundles
3. When cooked, remove the meat and the shoots
4. In another pan, brown a dozen tomatoes in two spoons of butter with one cup of broth, one spoon of dried breadcrumb powder, salt, peppers, and one small chopped onion
5. Let it boil, add the meat and shoots to this sauce
6. Serve after it has boiled a little
