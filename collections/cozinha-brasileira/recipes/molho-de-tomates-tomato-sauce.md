## Ingredients

- ten or twenty tomatoes
- pepper
- one clove of garlic
- bay leaves
- thyme
- parsley
- onions
- butter
- one spoon of wheat flour

## Directions

1. Boil ten or twenty tomatoes with pepper, one clove of garlic, bay leaves, thyme, parsley and onions
2. After cooking, pass the pulp through a fine sieve
3. Place butter and one spoon of wheat flour in a pot
4. Mix in the tomato pulp
5. Let boil a little over coals and serve
