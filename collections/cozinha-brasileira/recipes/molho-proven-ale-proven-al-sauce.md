## Ingredients

- a little parsley
- one clove of garlic
- spoons of sweet olive oil
- a pinch of wheat flour
- beef broth
- white wine
- a bouquet of aromatic herbs

## Directions

1. Heat a little parsley and one clove of garlic in spoons of sweet olive oil
2. Add afterwards a pinch of wheat flour, and then beef broth, white wine, and a bouquet of aromatic herbs
3. Cook for half an hour, remove the fat, the garlic, and the bouquet of aromatic herbs
4. Serve
