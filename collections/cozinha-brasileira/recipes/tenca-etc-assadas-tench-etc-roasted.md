## Ingredients

- fish
- manioc flour
- fresh butter
- parsley
- tomato sauce or piquant sauce

## Directions

1. After cleaning and scaling the fish, wrap them in manioc flour, filling them with fresh butter and chopped parsley, and roast them over a grill
2. Serve with a tomato sauce or piquant sauce
