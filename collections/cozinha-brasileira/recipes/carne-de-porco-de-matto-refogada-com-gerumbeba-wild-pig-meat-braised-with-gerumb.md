## Ingredients

- pork meat from wild pig
- water
- salt
- pepper
- sprig of mint
- garlic
- parsley
- lemon juice
- two tablespoons pork fat
- breadcrumbs
- one cup water
- portion of gerumbeba fruits peeled
- half dozen tomatoes

## Directions

1. Soak the meat for twenty-four hours in water with salt, pepper, a sprig of mint, garlic, parsley, and lemon juice.
2. Fry it in two tablespoons of pork fat, turning it on all sides.
3. Remove and coat in breadcrumbs.
4. Return to the fat and add one cup of water, a portion of peeled gerumbeba fruits, and half a dozen tomatoes.
5. Let it boil for one hour and serve.
