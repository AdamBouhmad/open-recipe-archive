## Ingredients

- fruit
- cornmeal flour

## Directions

1. Pick the fruits before they are fully ripe and on a dry day
2. Place on straw in a well-aired and dry room
3. If the days are cold, place a brazier in the room
4. Then spread a layer of sifted cornmeal flour in a barrel
5. Place a layer of fruits on top so they do not touch each other
6. Fill the barrel in this manner, cover well and keep in a warm and dry place
