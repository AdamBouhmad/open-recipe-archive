## Ingredients

- wheat flour
- five eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix wheat flour with five eggs and a little salt
2. Beat well until air bubbles form when cut
3. Unroll the dough onto a cloth and, when well stretched and thin, roll it up
4. Cut the roll into very fine strips, beginning from one of the ends
5. Once cut into thin threads, keep them for use, preparing as one would do with store-bought vermicelli
