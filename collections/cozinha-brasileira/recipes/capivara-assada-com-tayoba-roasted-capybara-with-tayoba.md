## Ingredients

- capybara meat
- milk serum
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- thyme or serpol
- cloves
- peppers
- tayoba leaves

## Directions

1. After disinfecting the capybara meat, place it to boil for one hour with milk serum, salt, parsley, onion leaves, thyme or serpol, cloves, and peppers
2. Remove it, dry it, and thread it onto a spit
3. Roast over an active fire at first, then moderate
4. Serve with tayoba leaves boiled in water and salt, placed on a plate and covered with the roasted meat
