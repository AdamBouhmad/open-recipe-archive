## Ingredients

- one old chicken, cleaned
- slices of bacon
- one pound of ham, sliced
- one beef foot
- one onion
- clove
- little salt
- pepper
- parsley
- marjoram
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned chicken on some slices of bacon with one pound of ham in slices, covered with more slices of bacon.
2. Place around it a piece of beef foot, one onion, clove, little salt, pepper, parsley and marjoram.
3. Cover everything with meat broth and boil near the fire for four hours.
4. Then carefully remove the chicken so it does not fall apart and place it on a plate with the ham around it.
5. Strain the broth through a sieve and reduce it to a few spoons.
6. Pour this sauce on top and serve cold.
