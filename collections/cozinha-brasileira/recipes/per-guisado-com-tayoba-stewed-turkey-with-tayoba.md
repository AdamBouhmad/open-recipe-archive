## Ingredients

- turkey
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- wheat flour
- butter
- tayoba
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take the turkey, cut it into pieces, and boil these in water, salt, parsley and onion leaves
2. When almost cooked, remove them and place in the same water to cook a tayoba root, cut into pieces
3. Coat the meat in wheat flour, frying it in butter on both sides
4. When the tayoba is cooked, pour it around the meat, pouring over it the reduced and thickened broth with two egg yolks, dissolved in one spoon of vinegar and one spoon of sugar, and serve thus
