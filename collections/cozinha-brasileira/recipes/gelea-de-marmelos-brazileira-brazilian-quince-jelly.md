## Ingredients

- four dozen quinces, split
- one pound sugar syrup
- cloves
- cinnamon
- lemon rinds

## Directions

1. Boil four dozen split quinces
2. When cooked, strain them
3. Add one pound of sugar syrup, some cloves, cinnamon, and lemon rinds
4. Let it boil until reduced to two bottles worth
5. Let it cool and serve
