## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- white cloth strips
- thyme
- bay leaf
- carrots
- onions
- cloves
- parsley
- chives
- some brine
- white wine
- dried bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wrap the feet in strips of white cloth so they do not fall apart and place in a pot with thyme, bay leaf, carrots, onions, cloves, parsley, chives, some brine and white wine.
2. After cooking for twenty-four hours, let them cool in their sauce.
3. When cold, cover with powder of dried bread crumbs, placing them alternately and repeated several times in powder of dried bread crumbs with salt and pepper.
4. Roast on low heat and serve when they have taken on color.
