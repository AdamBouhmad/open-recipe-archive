## Ingredients

- squabs
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- wheat flour
- butter
- grated cheese
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Flatten the squabs, cut in half, with a palmatoria
2. Rub with salt, pepper and lemon juice
3. Coat in wheat flour and fry in butter
4. Grill them
5. When grilled, place in a platter and cover with grated cheese, sugar and cinnamon
6. Place in the oven and allow to roast until of good color, then serve
