## Ingredients

- four tablespoons wheat flour
- two beaten eggs
- two cups milk
- a little salt
- nutmeg
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a mixture of four tablespoons of wheat flour, two beaten eggs, two cups of milk, a little salt and nutmeg
2. Fry in a heated frying pan
3. When well cooked on both sides, cover with sugar and cinnamon powder and serve hot
