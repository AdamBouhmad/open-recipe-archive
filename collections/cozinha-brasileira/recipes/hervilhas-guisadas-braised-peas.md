## Ingredients

- lard
- parsley
- peas
- water
- salt
- egg yolk
- wine
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry a piece of lard with chopped parsley.
2. Add a portion of peas boiled in water and salt, stir and add a beaten egg yolk mixed with a cup of wine and a spoonful of vinegar.
3. Boil a little more and serve.
