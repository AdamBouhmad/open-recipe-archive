## Ingredients

- beef loin, sliced
- butter, melted
- salt
- broken Indian pepper
- tubaras (a type of vegetable)

## Directions

1. Cut a beef loin into slices; place these in a casserole with melted butter, salt, broken Indian pepper, and take them to the fire, whose heat should be stronger at the time they go to table, turning from time to time.
2. As soon as they are equally browned on both sides, remove them, placing them then on a platter, and pouring over them sauce of tubaras or any other.
