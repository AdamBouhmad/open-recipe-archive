## Ingredients

- four pig's feet
- water
- salt
- one onion
- chicken breast
- four hard-boiled eggs
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- puff pastry dough
- white wine

## Directions

1. Boil four pig's feet in water and salt until cooked
2. Remove the bones and cut the meat into small pieces, mix with one onion, chicken breast, and four hard-boiled eggs, all well chopped
3. Season with salt, peppers, and grated nutmeg, and fill the pie mold made of puff pastry with this dough
4. Bake in the oven and when cooked, remove it, lift the lid a little and moisten the meat with a little white wine
5. Finish baking and serve
