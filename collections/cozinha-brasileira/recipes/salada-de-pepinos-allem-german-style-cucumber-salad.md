## Ingredients

- four cucumbers
- salt
- vinegar
- ground pepper
- one chopped onion
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Peel four cucumbers and cut into fine slices
2. Season with salt
3. Let sit for three to four hours
4. Drain and season again with salt, vinegar, ground pepper, one chopped onion and hot fat
5. Serve
