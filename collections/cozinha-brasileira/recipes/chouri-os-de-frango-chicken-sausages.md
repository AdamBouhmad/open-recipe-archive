## Ingredients

- skin of four to five chickens, plucked and cleaned
- breast meat
- thighs
- half a pound of bacon, all finely chopped
- one old bread, grated
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- parsley
- marjoram
- Madeira wine
- water and salt
- bacon slices
- one glass of white wine
- cooked fava beans

## Directions

1. Take the skin of four to five chickens, after plucking and cleaning.
2. Fill them with a mixture made of breast meat, thighs, half a pound of bacon, all finely chopped, one old bread grated, salt, pepper, clove, parsley and marjoram.
3. Moisten with Madeira wine and boil them a little in water and salt.
4. Then place them in a pan with slices of bacon and one glass of white wine.
5. Boil them over coals until cooked, and serve with sautéed fava beans.
