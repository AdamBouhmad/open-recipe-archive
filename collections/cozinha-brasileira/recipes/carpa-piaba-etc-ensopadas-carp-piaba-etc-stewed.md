## Ingredients

- four ounces of bacon
- half pound of beef
- one or two fish in portions
- one bowl of water
- one cup of wine

## Directions

1. Cut four ounces of bacon and half a pound of beef into small pieces, and place with the portions of one or two fish
2. Place to cook with one bowl of water and one cup of wine
