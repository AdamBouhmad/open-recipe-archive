## Ingredients

- cooked beef
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- beef broth
- bread crumb
- butter
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After the beef has been cooked and cooled, cut it into slices
2. Arrange the slices neatly on a metal plate
3. Dust with salt, pepper, parsley, and chopped chives
4. Pour over a cup of beef broth
5. Sprinkle on top breadcrumb fried in butter and a little vinegar
6. Place near the fire for twenty minutes
7. Then remove to serve at the table
