## Ingredients

- three garafas of milk
- half pound of butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- nutmeg
- onion
- veal quarter
- lemon slices
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Boil three garafas of milk with half pound of butter, salt, pepper, parsley, nutmeg, and a halved onion
2. Once it has boiled a little, add the moderately larded veal quarter
3. Bring to a boil again and let cool
4. Remove the quarter and place on a spit, cover with lemon slices and onions, and finally with buttered paper
5. Roast and serve with toasted potatoes, vegetables, and also with salad
