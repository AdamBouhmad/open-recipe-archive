## Ingredients

- leftover roasted lamb loins
- one tablespoon cornstarch
- one tablespoon butter
- two cups white wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut leftover roasted loins into pieces
2. Fry one tablespoon cornstarch with one tablespoon butter until lightly browned
3. Add two cups white wine and a little nutmeg
4. Stir and add the meat
5. Give it a boil and serve
