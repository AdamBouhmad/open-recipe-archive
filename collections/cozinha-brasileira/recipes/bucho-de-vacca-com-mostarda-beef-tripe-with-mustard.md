## Ingredients

- beef tripe, cleaned
- water
- salt
- fat
- dozen small onions
- pepper
- broth
- mustard powder

## Directions

1. With the tripe well cleaned, cut and cooked for two hours in salted water
2. Place on the fire to fry in two spoonfuls of fat together with a dozen small onions, salt, and pepper
3. Add two or three cups of broth
4. Let boil over coals and at serving time, add one spoonful of mustard powder
