## Ingredients

- mutton neck
- cold water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- breadcrumbs
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut the neck meat into slices and place on the fire with cold water, salt, parsley and onion leaves
2. After boiling for half an hour, remove and wrap with breadcrumbs
3. Cover with a little melted butter, grill, and serve with a spicy sauce
