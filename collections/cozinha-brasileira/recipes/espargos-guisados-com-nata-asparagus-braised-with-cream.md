## Ingredients

- asparagus
- water
- salt
- butter
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- one cup of good milk or cream
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil asparagus in salted water and let drain
2. Separate 24 tips and sauté in melted butter
3. At the same time, boil one spoonful of wheat flour with one cup of good milk or cream
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Pour over the asparagus and serve
