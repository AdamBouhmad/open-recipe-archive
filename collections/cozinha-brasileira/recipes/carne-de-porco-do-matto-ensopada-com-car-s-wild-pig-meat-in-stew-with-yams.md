## Ingredients

- pork meat from wild pig cut in pieces
- chopped bacon
- salt
- green peppers
- chopped parsley
- one clove garlic crushed
- nutmeg grated
- one yam peeled and cut in small pieces
- two egg yolks
- one tablespoon flour
- one glass lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the pork meat in pieces and put in a pot over fire with chopped bacon, salt, green peppers, chopped parsley, one crushed garlic clove, a little grated nutmeg, and one yam peeled and cut in small pieces.
2. Let it boil and when cooked and the broth reduced, incorporate two egg yolks beaten with one tablespoon of flour and diluted in one glass of lemon juice.
3. Give it another boil and serve.
