## Ingredients

- leftover roasted lamb
- fat
- two tablespoons cornmeal
- three cups meat broth or water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- a handful of chopped caruru, scalded and boiled

## Directions

1. Cut roasted lamb into pieces
2. Fry briefly in fat with two tablespoons cornmeal
3. Add three cups meat broth or water, salt, parsley, onion leaves, and a handful of scalded and boiled caruru
4. Boil over coals for half an hour
5. Serve
