## Ingredients

- one or two jicama roots
- pimentos comaris
- lemon juice
- salt
- parsley
- chopped onions
- two tablespoons melted fat

## Directions

1. Take one or two jicama roots, cut into slices or grate them
2. Mix with pimentos comaris, lemon juice, salt, parsley and chopped onions
3. Add two tablespoons of hot melted fat
4. Serve with roasts
