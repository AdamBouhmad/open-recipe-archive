## Ingredients

- one pig's head
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- cloves
- cilantro
- half nutmeg
- chives
- two heads of garlic
- ham slices
- vinegar
- white wine
- roots
- bundle of aromatic herbs

## Directions

1. Scald a pig's head in water with salt, then remove all bones
2. Cut the meat, ears, fat, and tongue into very thin slices, being careful not to damage the skin
3. Season with salt, pepper, bay leaf, marjoram, cloves, cilantro, half nutmeg, finely chopped chives, and two heads of finely chopped garlic
4. Place the cleaned head skin in a round casserole and arrange the meat and fat slices on it
5. Add some ham slices, vinegar, and salt
6. Place another equal layer on top and continue alternating, filling the head skin
7. Once filled, sew the skin closed with strong thread and wrap it in white cloth, tied with twine
8. Place in a pot with white wine, roots, a bundle of aromatic herbs, pepper, and salt
9. Let it cook for seven hours
10. When cooked, remove the cloth and place in a vessel that can contain it perfectly
11. Place a board on top that fits inside the vessel, and put a large weight on the board so it takes the shape of the vessel
12. Eat cold
