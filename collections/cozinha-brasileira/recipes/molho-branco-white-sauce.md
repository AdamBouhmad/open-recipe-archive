## Ingredients

- a piece of fat
- veal meat
- a piece of sausage
- 2 cups of meat broth
- 1 glass of wine
- 1 clove of garlic
- salt
- cloves
- pepper
- the crumb of a loaf of bread
- 3 cooked egg yolks

## Directions

1. Fry the veal meat and a piece of sausage in a piece of fat for half an hour
2. Add 2 cups of meat broth, 1 glass of wine, 1 clove of garlic, salt, cloves, and pepper
3. Add the crumb of a loaf of bread and 3 cooked and mashed egg yolks
4. Let it boil for two more hours, strain it and serve
