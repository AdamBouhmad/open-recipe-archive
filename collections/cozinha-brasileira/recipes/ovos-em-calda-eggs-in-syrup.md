## Ingredients

- two dozen eggs
- a little butter
- two pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Beat two dozen eggs well and pour into a casserole with a little butter
2. Fry until the eggs are hard
3. Remove the casserole from the fire and cut the eggs into slices
4. Make a syrup of two pounds of sugar at soft-ball stage
5. Put the slices in the syrup
6. Boil a little more, place on a plate
7. Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar and serve
