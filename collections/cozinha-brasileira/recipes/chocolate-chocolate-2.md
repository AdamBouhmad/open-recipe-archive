## Ingredients

- half a pound of chocolate
- twelve cups of water

## Directions

1. Grate half a pound of chocolate
2. Place it in a vessel
3. Add twelve cups of water
4. Place over fire and let it boil until it thickens
5. Stir with a proper chocolate whisk
6. Serve
