## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- cold water
- hot water
- bacon strips
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- parsley
- chives
- wheat flour
- butter
- onions
- mushrooms
- sugar
- vinegar
- capers

## Directions

1. Place the tongue in cold water for twenty-four hours, changing the water several times
2. Scald it in hot water, scraping it to remove the hard skin
3. Cook it for five hours with small pieces of bacon, pepper, salt, nutmeg, parsley and chopped chives
4. Remove from the fire and split it without dividing into pieces
5. Place on a serving dish
6. Strain the broth and thicken it with wheat flour, fry it in butter
7. Add onions, mushrooms, pepper and after boiling for five minutes, serve with the tongue
8. Or you may add to this sauce a little sugar and vinegar, or serve the tongue with a capers sauce or another thickened sauce
