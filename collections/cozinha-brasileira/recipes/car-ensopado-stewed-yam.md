## Ingredients

- one onion
- parsley
- fat
- one yam peeled and cut into pieces
- one bottle of water
- salt
- one small spoon of fine cornmeal

## Directions

1. Fry one onion and a little chopped parsley in fat
2. Add one peeled and cut yam in pieces, one bottle of water, salt, one small spoon of fine cornmeal
3. Let it boil until cooked and the broth reduced, and serve
