## Ingredients

- One eel
- Water
- White wine
- Parsley
- Onions
- Bay leaves
- Salt and pepper
- Fat or melted butter
- Manioc flour
- Beaten egg yolk
- One chalice of lemon juice
- One egg yolk

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the eel and cut into pieces
2. Boil for half an hour with equal parts water and white wine, parsley, onions, bay leaves, salt and pepper
3. Let cool and remove the fish
4. Pass through fat or melted butter
5. Coat in manioc flour
6. Pass through beaten eggs
7. Cover again with manioc flour
8. Roast on the grill, turning the pieces so they cook on all sides
9. Serve with the reduced broth mixed with one egg yolk dissolved in one chalice of lemon juice
