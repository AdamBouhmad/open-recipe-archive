## Ingredients

- green squashes
- salt
- garlic
- vinegar
- chopped parsley
- plenty of Indian pepper
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Take green squashes, peel them and remove the seeds
2. Chop as finely as possible
3. Season with salt, garlic, vinegar, chopped parsley and plenty of Indian pepper
4. Sauté in a pan with very hot fat
5. Cover the pan and let it cook a little
6. Serve
