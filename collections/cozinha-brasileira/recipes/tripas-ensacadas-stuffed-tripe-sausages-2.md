## Ingredients

- tripe
- beef sweetbread
- bacon slices
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- seasonings
- water
- milk
- carrots
- onions
- cloves
- aromatic herbs

## Directions

1. Having the tripe prepared as already explained, choose the thickest ones in which to stuff the others, which should be cut to the length of two fingers, as well as the sweetbread
2. Add to them bacon slices cut lengthwise, salt, pepper, pounded parsley and the other seasonings mentioned above; leave them in this seasoning for six hours
3. Thread through one end a piece of tripe, one of sweetbread and one of bacon, and so on until the tripe is filled, but without pressing, and tie at both ends
4. When ready, put them to cook for four hours over moderate heat in a vessel in which you have put half water and half milk with carrots, onions, cloves, salt and a bunch of aromatic herbs
5. Let them cool in the same vessel, then remove them and place them to drain; give them a few pricks and put them to roast over moderate heat
