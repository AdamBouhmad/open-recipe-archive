## Ingredients

- hake loins
- cow butter
- dozen tubaras (small fish)
- white wine

## Directions

1. Take the loins of fish and cut them into pieces
2. Sauté them in cow butter and remove them
3. Sauté a dozen tubaras in the same fat, add one cup of white wine and let it boil
4. When cooked, add the hake loins
