## Ingredients

- the game, cut in quarters
- ground salt
- parsley
- chopped onion
- juice of comarí peppers
- egg yolks
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- mangarito, peeled and cut into thin slices

## Directions

1. Take the game and cut it in quarters; season it with ground salt, parsley, chopped onion and juice of comarí peppers
2. Pass them through egg yolks and coat them in breadcrumbs; sauté in butter with a portion of peeled and thinly sliced mangarito, and when fried, serve
