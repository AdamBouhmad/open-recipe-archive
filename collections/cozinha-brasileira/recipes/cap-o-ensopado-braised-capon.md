## Ingredients

- one capon
- salt
- lemon juice
- fat
- four or five cups of meat broth
- pepper
- garlic
- onion
- chive leaves
- the giblets of the capon
- pieces of roasted beef or mutton
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take the whole capon and rub it inside and out with salt and lemon juice
2. Place it in a pan with some fat, four or five cups of broth, salt, pepper, garlic, onion, chive leaves, the capon's giblets, and some pieces of roasted beef or mutton
3. When cooked, strain the sauce (if desired, thicken it with egg yolks) and serve with the sauce
