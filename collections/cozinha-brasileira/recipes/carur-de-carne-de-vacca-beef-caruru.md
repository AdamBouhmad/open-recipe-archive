## Ingredients

- beef
- fat
- salt
- water
- mimoso farina
- one dozen okra
- pepper
- parsley
- green onion

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into small pieces and fry for a quarter hour in fat and salt.
2. Then add to it one cup of water, one spoon of mimoso farina, one dozen okra cut into slices, salt, two or three bird peppers, parsley, green onion.
3. Let it boil for one hour.
4. Serve with farina angu or manioc flour broth.
