## Ingredients

- some slices of well-toasted bread
- some roots and parsley leaves
- hot water
- a little butter
- some egg yolks
- one cup of cold water

## Directions

1. Place some slices of well-toasted bread, some roots and parsley leaves in a pot
2. Pour hot water and a little butter over them and let boil until the bread dissolves
3. Strain this broth and add some egg yolks beaten in one cup of cold water
