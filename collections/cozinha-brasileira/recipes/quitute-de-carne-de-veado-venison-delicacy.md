## Ingredients

- roasted venison meat
- two spoonfuls of wheat flour
- one spoonful of sugar
- three spoonfuls of butter
- white wine
- peeled lemon slices
- scalded raisins
- toasted and chopped peanuts

## Directions

1. Take the roasted venison meat, separate it from the bones and cut it into pieces
2. Make a sauce with two spoonfuls of wheat flour and one spoonful of sugar, which are fried in three spoonfuls of butter until they take on a good colour
3. Add a bottle of white wine, the slices of a peeled lemon, a portion of scalded raisins and an equal amount of toasted and chopped peanuts
4. Place the meat in a casserole, boil it for one hour and serve
