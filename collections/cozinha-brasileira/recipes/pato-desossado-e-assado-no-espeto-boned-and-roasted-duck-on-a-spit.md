## Ingredients

- duck
- one half pound veal meat
- handful carapicús
- four to six grated potatoes (if carapicús unavailable)
- two egg yolks
- parsley, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- two tablespoons butter
- thin bacon slices

## Directions

1. Open the duck along the back and remove the bones with some meat
2. Chop this meat with one half pound of veal meat
3. Mix in a handful of carapicús, or four to six grated potatoes if unavailable, two egg yolks, chopped parsley, salt, pepper, and two tablespoons of butter
4. Fill the duck with this mixture and sew it closed
5. Roast on a spit, wrapped in thin bacon slices
6. Serve with its own sauce, after removing the fat
