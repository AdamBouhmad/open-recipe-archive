## Ingredients

- fresh hake
- salt
- ground peppers
- cassava flour or breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. This fish should only be fried if very fresh
2. Clean it, remove the skin, sprinkle with salt and ground peppers and wrap in cassava flour or breadcrumbs
3. Put it in the frying pan with hot butter; when cooked serve it
