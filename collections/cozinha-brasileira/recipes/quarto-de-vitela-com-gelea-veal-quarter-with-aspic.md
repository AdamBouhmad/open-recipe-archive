## Ingredients

- veal quarter
- white wine
- water
- salt
- four veal feet, cut
- pepper
- three cloves
- nutmeg
- wine
- lemon
- bundle of herbs

## Directions

1. Beat the veal quarter well, soak it in white wine for one day
2. The next day, boil it in water and salt with four cut veal feet, pepper, three cloves of India, nutmeg, a little wine, a split lemon and a bundle of herbs
3. Once cooked, remove it, placing some drops of the broth on a plate and observe if these, once cold, have the point for aspic; otherwise let them boil until this point
4. Then strain the broth, remove the fat, put the quarter back for a few minutes
5. Then remove it and let everything cool
6. The next day, cut the aspic into thin slices and cover the quarter with these
