## Ingredients

- six egg whites
- half pound sugar
- half quarter grated coconut from Bahia
- one spoon of liquor

## Directions

1. Beat the six egg whites with half pound of sugar, half quarter of grated coconut from Bahia, and one spoon of liquor
2. Once a hard mass is formed, drop it in small pieces onto a sheet of thick paper
3. Bake in a very gentle oven
