## Ingredients

- breast of one chicken
- liver of one chicken
- one onion
- a little parsley
- salt
- one tablespoon butter
- half cup white wine
- puff pastry
- two or three oysters
- a little butter
- one egg yolk

## Directions

1. Chop the chicken breast and liver with an onion and a little parsley and salt
2. Simmer a little in one tablespoon of butter and half cup white wine
3. Make some small boxes of puff pastry
4. Line the bottom with some of this chopped mixture
5. Place two or three oysters on top with a little butter
6. Cover and brush the outside with beaten egg yolk
7. Put in the oven and serve when baked
