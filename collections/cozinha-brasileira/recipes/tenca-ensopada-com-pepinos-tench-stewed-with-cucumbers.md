## Ingredients

- fish
- two tablespoons of fat
- chopped onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- garlic clove
- cucumbers
- water
- cornstarch
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean and scale a portion of fish
2. Melt two tablespoons of fat, add a chopped onion, parsley, salt, pepper and a garlic clove
3. Place the fish in this hot fat, add a portion of cucumbers split without the seeds, and enough water
4. Once almost cooked, add one tablespoon of cornstarch dissolved in one chicara of vinegar, boil a little more, and serve
