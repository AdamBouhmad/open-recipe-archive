## Ingredients

- Liver, sliced into equal pieces
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic, chopped
- Chives
- Parsley
- Wheat flour
- White wine
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Melt a little butter in a frying pan and place the liver slices in it
2. Season with pepper and salt, adding chopped garlic, chives and parsley
3. Cook until done on both sides
4. Once cooked, drain the butter
5. Add a spoonful of wheat flour diluted in half a glass of white wine, taking care not to let it boil
6. When the sauce is thick, add the juice of a lemon and serve
