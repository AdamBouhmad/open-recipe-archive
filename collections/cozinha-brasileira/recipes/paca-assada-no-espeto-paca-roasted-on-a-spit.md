## Ingredients

- one very young and fat paca
- water
- salt
- pepper
- sour orange juice
- manjerona
- garlic
- mint leaves

## Directions

1. When the paca is very young and fat, instead of removing the hide, scald it in very hot water and pluck it like a house pig
2. Soak it for six hours in a brine made with sour orange juice, salt, pepper, manjerona, garlic, and some mint leaves
3. Remove it and put it on a spit, roast it like a pig, and serve
