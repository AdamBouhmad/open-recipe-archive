## Ingredients

- green beans
- lard
- about half a bottle of water
- salt
- a little vinegar
- ground Indian pepper

## Directions

1. Chop and remove the ends from green beans
2. Scald and sauté in lard
3. Add about half a bottle of water and salt, and boil over coals, putting on top a little vinegar and ground Indian pepper
