## Ingredients

- a little wheat flour
- butter
- some onions
- chives
- bay leaf
- thyme
- parsley
- pepper
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Fry a little wheat flour with butter until it takes on a reddish-brown color
2. Remove from fire and add finely chopped onions, chives, bay leaf, thyme, parsley and pepper
3. Add several cups of beef broth
4. Let boil for fifteen minutes
5. Strain through a fine sieve and serve
