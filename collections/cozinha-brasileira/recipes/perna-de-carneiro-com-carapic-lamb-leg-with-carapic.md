## Ingredients

- lamb leg
- carapicú
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- white wine

## Directions

1. Split the leg down to the bone
2. Remove about one pound of meat and chop it well with a handful of chosen carapicú that has been scalded
3. Place this filling inside the leg in place of the bone and sew up the opening
4. Lard with bacon seasoned with salt, pepper and nutmeg
5. Brown in fat and when it takes color add one bowl of white wine
6. Finish cooking over moderate fire
7. Serve with its sauce
