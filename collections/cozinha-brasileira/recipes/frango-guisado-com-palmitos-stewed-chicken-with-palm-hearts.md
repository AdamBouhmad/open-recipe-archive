## Ingredients

- one chicken
- one palm heart
- water
- half pound of bacon
- salt
- parsley
- one onion
- a little basil
- two spoons of wheat flour
- one spoon of vinegar
- one spoon of sugar
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook chicken and palm heart cut into pieces and tied in a bundle in water with half pound of bacon, salt, parsley, one onion, and a little basil
2. When cooked, remove the chicken pieces and palm heart, strain the broth and reduce it
3. Mix in two spoons of wheat flour, one spoon of vinegar, one spoon of sugar, two egg yolks
4. When everything is cooked, add back the chicken pieces and palm heart and serve after they have warmed through
