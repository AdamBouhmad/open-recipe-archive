## Ingredients

- one young goose
- bananas or apples, peeled and cut into pieces
- half a plate of chosen and washed rice
- one bowl of water
- parsley
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take a young goose and fill it with peeled bananas or apples cut into pieces.
2. Braise it, roasting it in the pan with little fire in its own fat until well roasted and of uniform color, turning it from time to time.
3. Remove it and pour into this same fat, which should be very hot, half a plate of chosen and washed rice.
4. Stir until everything heats well, then add one bowl of water, the goose cut into pieces, some pieces of bananas, parsley, salt, nutmeg.
5. Allow to boil with little fire until the water dries up.
6. Put the rice on the plate and serve with the meat placed on top.
