## Ingredients

- veal ears
- meat broth
- herb bouquet
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter
- sugar
- vinegar or lemon juice
- raisins

## Directions

1. Clean the ears well
2. Boil them in a pan with meat broth, herb bouquet, salt and pepper
3. Remove the ears after cooking
4. Thicken the strained broth with flour toasted in butter
5. Add a little sugar, vinegar or lemon juice, and some scalded raisins
6. Let it boil a little more with the ears
7. Serve
