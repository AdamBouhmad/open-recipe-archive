## Ingredients

- one bottle of milk
- one pound of refined sugar
- twelve ounces of rice
- a little butter
- zest of one lemon
- four whole eggs
- five egg yolks
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Bring a bottle of milk to a boil with one pound of refined sugar and twelve ounces of rice.
2. When the rice is well cooked, add a little butter and let cool.
3. Mince the lemon zest finely, which is mixed with the milk; add four whole eggs and five egg yolks and mix well.
4. Butter a mold and sprinkle with grated breadcrumbs; shake the mold to remove excess breadcrumbs, and pour the mixture in, placing the mold near the fire and covering it with a lid full of hot coals.
5. At the time of serving, turn the mold onto a dish and serve hot or cold.
