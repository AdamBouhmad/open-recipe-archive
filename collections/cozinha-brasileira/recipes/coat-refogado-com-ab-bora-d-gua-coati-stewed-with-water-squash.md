## Ingredients

- half a dozen tomatoes
- two spoons of fat
- one chopped onion
- parsley
- a portion of water squash chopped into small pieces
- one coati cut into pieces
- one cup of water
- the same amount of sour orange juice
- one small spoon of cassava starch
- one small spoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Strain the tomatoes in two spoons of fat, with a chopped onion and parsley
2. Add a portion of water squash chopped into small pieces and a coati cut into pieces
3. Add one cup of water, another cup of sour orange juice, one small spoon of cassava starch and one of sugar
4. Leave to boil on low heat until cooked and serve
