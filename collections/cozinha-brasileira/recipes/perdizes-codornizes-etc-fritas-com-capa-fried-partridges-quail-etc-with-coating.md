## Ingredients

- partridge, quail, or similar bird
- salt
- pepper juice
- garlic
- wheat flour
- egg yolks
- cinnamon
- sugar
- fat
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the birds into pieces and rub with salt, pepper juice, and garlic
2. Roll in wheat flour and pass through egg yolks
3. Dust with cinnamon and a little sugar
4. Fry in fat on both sides
5. Serve by placing a slice of peeled and squeezed lemon on each piece and pour lemon juice over all
