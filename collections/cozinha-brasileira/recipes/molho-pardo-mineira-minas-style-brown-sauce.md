## Ingredients

- chicken blood
- two spoons of vinegar
- two spoons of fat
- one bottle of water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- Indian cloves
- one spoon of corn meal or half spoon of polvilho
- a little sugar
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. When killing the chicken, catch the blood in a dish containing two spoons of vinegar
2. Fry the chicken cut into pieces in two spoons of fat
3. After fifteen minutes, add one bottle of water, salt, parsley, and onion leaves
4. When cooked, remove the chicken pieces from the broth
5. Add the blood and some Indian cloves, one spoon of corn meal or half spoon of polvilho, and a little sugar
6. Allow to boil for half an hour
7. If desired, thicken with two egg yolks
8. Add the chicken pieces again, bring to a boil once more, and serve
