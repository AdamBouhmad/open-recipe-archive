## Ingredients

- handful of bread crumb softened with milk
- two small spoonfuls of butter
- two dozen peanuts crushed
- two spoonfuls of sugar
- four egg yolks
- nutmeg
- saffron
- salt
- parsley
- clove

## Directions

1. Knead a handful of bread crumb softened with milk and two small spoonfuls of butter
2. Mix with two dozen crushed peanuts and two spoonfuls of sugar, four egg yolks, a little nutmeg and saffron
3. Fill the chicken with this stuffing
4. Put it in a pan, wrapped in slices of bacon, after seasoning with salt, parsley and clove
5. Roast in the oven and after removing the bacon, serve with its sauce and some salad
