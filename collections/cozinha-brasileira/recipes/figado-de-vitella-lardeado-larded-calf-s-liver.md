## Ingredients

- calf liver
- hot water
- white wine
- carrots
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- bundle of cooking herbs

## Directions

1. Lard the liver after scalding it in hot water
2. Let it cool, then place on the fire with white wine, carrots, onion, salt, pepper and a bundle of cooking herbs
3. Let it cook over moderate heat and serve
