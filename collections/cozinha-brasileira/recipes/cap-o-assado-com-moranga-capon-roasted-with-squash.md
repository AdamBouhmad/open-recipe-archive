## Ingredients

- one capon
- a handful of carapicús
- one onion
- parsley
- breadcrumbs
- milk
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- four egg yolks
- bacon strips
- squash with skin
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Fill the capon with a handful of carapicús, one onion, and parsley all finely chopped, mixed with a handful of breadcrumbs softened in milk and mashed with two spoons of butter, salt, pepper, and four egg yolks
2. Place the prepared capon in a pan on bacon strips with squash pieces with skin around it
3. Add two cups of meat broth and cover with other bacon strips
4. Let roast until cooked and serve with squash slices around it
