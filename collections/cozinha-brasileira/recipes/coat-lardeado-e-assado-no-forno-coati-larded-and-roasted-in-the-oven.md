## Ingredients

- two chicken breasts, chopped
- two onions
- four boiled eggs
- one peeled cassava root
- two ounces of bacon
- parsley
- peppers
- a dozen tomatoes
- salt
- vinegar
- bacon strips for larding
- bacon slices for lining the pan

## Directions

1. Chop two chicken breasts, two onions, four boiled eggs, one peeled cassava root, two ounces of bacon, parsley, peppers, a dozen tomatoes and salt
2. Fill the coati with this stuffing
3. After cleaning, rub with salt and vinegar
4. Lard the loin with fine strips of bacon
5. Place in a pan lined with bacon slices and roast over moderate heat
6. When roasted, serve with purslane salad
