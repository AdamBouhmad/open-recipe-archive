## Ingredients

- one paca, cooked in water and salt
- half a pound of bacon
- one onion
- a portion of carapicús (mushrooms)
- a portion of bread crumb softened in milk
- four egg yolks
- salt
- ground pepper
- one clove of garlic
- grated nutmeg
- wheat flour
- egg yolks
- breadcrumbs
- fat

## Directions

1. Cook one paca in water and salt, separating the meat from the bones
2. Chop it with half a pound of bacon, one onion, and a portion of carapicús (mushrooms), a portion of bread crumb softened in milk, four egg yolks, salt, ground pepper, one clove of garlic, and grated nutmeg
3. Mix everything well and form into balls
4. Wrap them in wheat flour and pass them through egg yolks to cover them with breadcrumbs
5. Fry them in plenty of fat
6. Serve with this same fat, or better with some spicy sauce and salads
