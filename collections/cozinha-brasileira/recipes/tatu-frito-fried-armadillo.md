## Ingredients

- one armadillo, shell removed and cleaned
- lemon juice
- tomatoes
- parsley
- onion, sliced
- salt
- peppers
- fat

## Directions

1. After removing the shell and cleaning the armadillo, cut it into pieces and soak in lemon juice, tomatoes, parsley, sliced onion, salt, and peppers for six hours
2. Fry in plenty of fat and serve
