## Ingredients

- Lamb quarter
- Salt
- Garlic
- Cetola
- Butter
- Meat drippings

## Directions

1. Beat the lamb quarter well
2. Marinate for one day in a sauce made of salt, garlic, and chopped cetola
3. Remove the thin skin
4. Lard lightly
5. Place on spit
6. Let roast slowly, moistening at first with melted butter, and afterwards with the juice that collected on the plate
