## Ingredients

- sardines
- sea water or well-salted water
- cloves
- pepper
- nutmeg
- bay leaves
- good sweet oil

## Directions

1. Clean the fish by removing the scales
2. Scald them with sea water or well-salted water
3. Let them cool in the same water
4. Place them in a pan with cloves, pepper, nutmeg and bay leaves
5. Pour good sweet oil over them so the fish are completely covered
6. Store for later use
