## Ingredients

- goose meat
- one spoon of butter
- one bottle of meat broth
- one spoon of wheat flour
- two beaten egg yolks
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- a portion of fresh whole okra

## Directions

1. Cut the goose meat into pieces; after removing the bones, braise them in one spoon of butter.
2. When well fried, add one bottle of meat broth, one spoon of wheat flour, two beaten egg yolks, salt, parsley, onion leaves and a portion of fresh whole okra.
3. Allow everything to boil until the meat is well cooked, and serve.
