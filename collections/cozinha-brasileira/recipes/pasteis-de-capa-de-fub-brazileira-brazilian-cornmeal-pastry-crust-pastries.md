## Ingredients

- four spoonfuls of fine cornmeal
- two eggs
- one small spoonful of arrowroot boiled in one cup of water or milk
- a little salt
- filling as in the recipe above

## Directions

1. Proceed exactly as in the recipe above, with the crust made with four spoonfuls of fine cornmeal, two eggs and one small spoonful of arrowroot boiled in one cup of water or milk and a little salt
2. This crust should be more substantial than one made with wheat flour
