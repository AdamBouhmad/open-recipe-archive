## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- chef-style sauce
- sweet olive oil

## Directions

1. Take a suckling pig after it has been prepared and cleaned, singe it in the flame, and garnish with a chef-style sauce, sew its belly to contain the sauce.
2. Thread it lengthwise on a spit and tie at the ends of the spit.
3. One hour and a half before serving, place it on the fire, and from time to time brush it with a brush dipped in sweet olive oil.
4. Moderate the heat so that the suckling pig gets a nice browning on all sides.
5. At the time of serving, remove it from the spit and make a cut in the skin of the neck so that the skin cracks between the teeth, which is what gives charm to the suckling pig.
6. Take it to the table, placing a chef-style sauce in one tureen and a diluted meat substance sauce in another.
