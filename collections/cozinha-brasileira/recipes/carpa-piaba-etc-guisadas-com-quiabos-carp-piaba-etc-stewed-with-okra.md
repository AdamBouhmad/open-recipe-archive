## Ingredients

- three or four fish
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- peppers
- cloves
- sour orange juice
- two spoons of fat
- two spoons of manioc flour
- okra

## Directions

1. Scale, clean and cut three or four fish into pieces and boil them in water, salt, parsley, onion leaves, peppers, cloves and sour orange juice
2. In another part, fry two spoons of fat and add two spoons of manioc flour
3. Then add the strained and reduced fish broth, a portion of okra cut into rounds and the fish
4. Let them boil for about an hour over very gentle heat and serve
