## Ingredients

- beef tripe
- bread slices
- butter
- Flemish cheese
- bread marrow

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the beef tripe in the same manner as tongues, but without onions
2. When the tripe is well cooked and well reduced, place in a deep platter with a layer of bread slices fried in butter, a layer of beef tripe, a layer of Flemish cheese, and so on
3. Put a little bread marrow on the last layer
4. Let all cook moderately
