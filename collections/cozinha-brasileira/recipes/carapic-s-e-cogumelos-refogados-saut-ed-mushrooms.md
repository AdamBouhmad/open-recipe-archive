## Ingredients

- mushrooms (carapicús)
- fat
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- onion leaves
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take a portion of mushrooms (carapicús), soak them, open them to clean them well of insects, and wash them well
2. Place them in two spoonfuls of very hot fat
3. Add half a bottle of water, one spoonful of vinegar, salt, pepper, onion leaves, and parsley
4. Boil for half an hour over moderate fire
5. Serve
