## Ingredients

- mutton chops with bone
- water
- salt
- pepper
- marjoram
- parsley
- onion leaves

## Directions

1. Cut the chops leaving one bone on each.
2. Beat well and shape into a rounded form with a knife.
3. Cook in water with salt, pepper, marjoram, parsley, and onion leaves.
4. When cooked, grill them.
5. Serve with an appropriate sauce for meat.
