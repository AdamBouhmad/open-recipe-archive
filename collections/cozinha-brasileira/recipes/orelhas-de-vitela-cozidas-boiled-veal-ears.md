## Ingredients

- veal ears
- meat broth
- white wine
- cabbage leaves
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- marjoram
- onion
- clove
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Scald the ears well, clean and blanch them
2. Place in a pan with meat broth, a glass of white wine, cabbage leaves, salt, pepper, parsley, marjoram, onion and clove
3. After cooking, remove them
4. Pass the sauce through a sieve
5. Thicken with egg yolks
6. Serve with the ears
