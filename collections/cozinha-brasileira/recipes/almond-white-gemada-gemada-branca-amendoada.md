## Ingredients

- four ounces of peeled almonds
- four ounces of sugar
- one bottle of milk
- one spoon of polvilho
- nutmeg, grated

## Directions

1. Pound four ounces of peeled almonds with four ounces of sugar
2. Once a homogeneous paste is formed, add one bottle of milk boiled until reduced to half
3. Add one spoon of polvilho and a little grated nutmeg
4. Beat everything
5. Boil, pour onto plates
6. Cover with sugar and cinnamon powder
7. Serve
