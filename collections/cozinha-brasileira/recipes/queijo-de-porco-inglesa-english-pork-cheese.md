## Ingredients

- one pig's head
- salt
- saltpeter powder
- pepper
- cilantro
- nutmeg
- serpol
- bay leaf
- sage
- parsley
- chives
- lemon zest and juice
- white wine
- onions
- carrots
- four cloves
- bundle of aromatic herbs
- chicken breast strips

## Directions

1. Remove bones from a pig's head and cut thin strips from the head and ears, removing the skin in large strips
2. Roll and tie the strips together
3. Place them in escabeche for at least three days in a terrine with salt, a little saltpeter powder, pepper, cilantro, nutmeg, a little serpol, bay leaf, ground sage, parsley, chopped chives, lemon zest and juice
4. Remove from the vinegar, gather all pieces in cloth and tie
5. Cook in water, white wine, onions, carrots, four cloves, a bundle of aromatic herbs, salt, and the head bones, ensuring enough water for the cloth to float
6. Heat a mold in a casserole with the skins that have soaked, and arrange the meats and ears, tongue in alternating layers with a filling made from meat scraps and some chicken breast strips
7. Cover with a tight-fitting lid and place a weight of one arroba on top while hot to make the cheese compact
8. When cold, remove from the mold by placing it briefly in boiling water
9. Eat cold
10. Use the broth to make an excellent soup
