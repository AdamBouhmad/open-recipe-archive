## Ingredients

- one spoon wheat flour
- one onion, chopped
- one spoon brown sugar
- agouti
- one bottle water
- half cup orange juice
- one peeled and chopped taro
- half dozen tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- garlic clove

## Directions

1. Brown one spoon of wheat flour, one chopped onion, and one spoon of brown sugar until they take on a dark amber color
2. Add the agouti cut into pieces and turn them to fry on both sides
3. Add one bottle of water with half a cup of orange juice, one peeled and chopped taro, half a dozen tomatoes, salt, pepper, parsley, and one garlic clove
4. Let it boil for one hour
5. When cooked, serve
