## Ingredients

- a good piece of butter
- wheat flour or cornmeal
- a little nutmeg
- water from cooking the hearts of palm
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Fry a good piece of butter with wheat flour or cornmeal
2. Add a little nutmeg and as much water as needed from cooking the whole hearts of palm
3. Let boil for fifteen minutes
4. Thicken with two egg yolks
5. Pour over the whole hearts of palm and serve
