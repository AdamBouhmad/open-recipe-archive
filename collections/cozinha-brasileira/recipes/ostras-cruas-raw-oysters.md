## Ingredients

- oysters
- pepper
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take a portion of oysters and clean the outside as much as possible
2. Open them by placing on an iron plate with live coals beneath; as soon as the plate heats, they open by themselves
3. With a strong knife, break the joint and scrape the meat attached to move it to the other shell so all meat remains in one shell
4. Dust with a little ground pepper and add a few drops of lemon to each and serve
