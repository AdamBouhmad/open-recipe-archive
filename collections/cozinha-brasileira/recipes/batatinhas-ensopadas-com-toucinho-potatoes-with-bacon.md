## Ingredients

- a quarter of fresh bacon cut into small pieces
- one spoon of wheat flour
- salt
- nutmeg
- one bottle of water
- some peeled raw potatoes cut into pieces

## Directions

1. Fry a quarter of fresh bacon cut into small pieces
2. When fried, add one spoon of wheat flour, salt, nutmeg, one bottle of water and some peeled raw potatoes cut into pieces
3. Let it boil and serve
