## Ingredients

- frog legs with skin removed
- fat
- one glass of wine
- water
- salt
- peppers
- nutmeg
- one spoonful of arrowroot
- one spoonful of sugar
- one lemon, sliced

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the frog legs and cut the toes
2. Braise with one spoonful of fat
3. Add one glass of wine, water, salt, peppers, nutmeg, one spoonful of arrowroot, one spoonful of sugar, and a lemon cut into slices
4. Let boil for half an hour and serve
