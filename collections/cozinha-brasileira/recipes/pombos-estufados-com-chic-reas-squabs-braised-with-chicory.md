## Ingredients

- mature squabs
- bacon
- one glass water
- salt
- nutmeg
- parsley
- chopped onion
- a little chopped mushrooms
- braised chicory

## Directions

1. Take mature squabs and place them in a pot lined with bacon
2. Add one glass of water, salt, nutmeg, parsley, chopped onion and a little chopped mushrooms
3. When cooked, serve over braised chicory
