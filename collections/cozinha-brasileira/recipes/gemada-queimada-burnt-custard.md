## Ingredients

- five spoonfuls of wheat flour
- one spoonful of butter
- one bottle of milk
- eight egg yolks
- three spoonfuls of sugar
- a little grated nutmeg
- sugar
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Fry five spoonfuls of wheat flour in one spoonful of butter
2. Having browned, gradually add one bottle of milk and eight beaten egg yolks with three spoonfuls of sugar and a little grated nutmeg
3. Boil until thickened
4. Place on a plate when cold, with sugar and cinnamon powder
5. Pass a hot iron over the top to caramelize the sugar
