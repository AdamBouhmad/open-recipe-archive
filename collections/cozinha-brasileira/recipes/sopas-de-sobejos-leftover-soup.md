## Ingredients

- roasted and fried meats from the previous day
- hard bread, grated
- egg yolks
- broth from the cooking of the meats
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Cook the roasted and fried meats that remain from the day before
2. When well cooked, remove from the pan, pound and mix with hard grated bread and some egg yolks
3. Form balls from this mixture and place them in a tureen
4. Pour over them the broth remaining from cooking the meats, after adding the necessary seasonings
