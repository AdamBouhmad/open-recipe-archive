## Ingredients

- one or more loaves of bread, sliced
- milk
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- ten to twelve eggs

## Directions

1. Soak bread slices in a little milk
2. Butter a pan and place a layer of bread slices at the bottom
3. Cover with a layer of sugar and ground cinnamon
4. Add another layer of bread slices
5. Continue alternating layers, with the last layer being sugar and butter
6. Pour ten to twelve beaten eggs over everything
7. Cover the pan and place in a hot oven
8. Bake for half an hour
