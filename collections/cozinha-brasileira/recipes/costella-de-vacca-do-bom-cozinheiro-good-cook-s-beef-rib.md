## Ingredients

- beef ribs
- butter
- hot coals

## Directions

1. Fry the ribs greased with butter on both sides
2. Cover the casserole with the lid and place hot coals on top
3. Leave it to cook, without adding anything else, neither water nor sauce, over moderate fire for two hours
4. Serve the ribs with no other sauce beyond their own juice contained in the casserole
