## Ingredients

- two spoons of butter
- one spoon of cassava flour
- parsley
- onion, chopped
- one good cup of milk
- cooked dried cod, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Melt two spoons of butter and mix with one spoon of cassava flour, parsley, chopped onion and one good cup of milk
2. Boil briefly
3. Add the cooked dried cod that has been finely chopped
4. Simmer a bit more and serve
