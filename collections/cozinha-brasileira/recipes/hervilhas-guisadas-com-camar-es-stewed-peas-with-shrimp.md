## Ingredients

- a portion of peas
- water and salt
- two spoons of lard
- onion
- chopped parsley
- one clove of garlic, crushed
- half a dozen tomatoes
- pepper and salt
- a portion of raw shelled shrimp

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of peas in little water and salt
2. In the same time, fry two spoons of lard with onion, chopped parsley, one crushed clove of garlic, half a dozen tomatoes, pepper and salt
3. Add the peas with broth and a portion of raw shelled shrimp
4. Let it boil for half an hour and serve
