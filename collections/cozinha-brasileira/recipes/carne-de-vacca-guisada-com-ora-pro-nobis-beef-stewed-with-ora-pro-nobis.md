## Ingredients

- beef cut into small thin slices
- melted fat
- one glass of water
- one spoon of brandy
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one spoon of cornstarch
- ora-pro-nobis leaves
- one spoon of vinegar or juice of terra orange

## Directions

1. Fry the meat slices in a little melted fat
2. Add one glass of water and one spoon of brandy, salt, pepper, parsley, onion leaves, and one spoon of cornstarch
3. Leave to boil for one hour
4. Add the ora-pro-nobis leaves and one spoon of vinegar or orange juice
5. Give it another boil
6. Serve with cornmeal porridge
