## Ingredients

- fresh beef
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- meat broth
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the fresh beef into thin slices.
2. Layer these in a pan one on top of the other with salt, pepper, and finely chopped parsley, one cup of meat broth.
3. Let them boil for half an hour over coals.
4. Then add to them one spoon of vinegar.
