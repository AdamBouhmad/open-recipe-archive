## Ingredients

- venison shoulder
- water
- salt
- onion
- ginger
- pimento peppers
- parsley
- portion of asparagus sprouts
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the venison shoulder in water, salt, onion, ginger, pimento peppers, parsley and a portion of asparagus sprouts tied in a bundle
2. When the shoulder is cooked, separate the meat from the bones and wrap it in breadcrumbs
3. Fry it in butter
4. Place the asparagus sprouts on a plate with the fried meat on top
5. Pour over the whole the strained and reduced broth and serve
