## Ingredients

- fish
- salted water
- parsley
- celery
- onion
- bay leaves
- pepper
- clove
- thyme
- melted butter
- wheat flour
- white wine
- capers

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, cut it into steaks, and boil in salted water with parsley, celery, onion, bay leaves, pepper, clove and thyme
2. When cooked, remove the steaks, drain them
3. Make a sauce of melted butter with wheat flour and white wine, to which are added a chopped onion and one tablespoon of capers
4. Put the steaks in the same sauce and having boiled a little more, serve
