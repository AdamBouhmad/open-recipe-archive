## Ingredients

- one goose, preferably young
- one dozen onions of medium size
- butter
- goose liver
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Braise in butter one dozen onions of medium size.
2. Mix them with the goose liver, finely chopped, adding salt, pepper, chopped parsley and marjoram.
3. After the goose is seasoned with salt, fill it with this stuffing.
4. Roast it on a spit and serve.
