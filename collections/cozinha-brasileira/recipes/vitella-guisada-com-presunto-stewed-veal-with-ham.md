## Ingredients

- veal sirloin
- lean ham
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- carapicú
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- white wine
- paper

## Directions

1. Lard a veal sirloin with a piece of lean ham cut into strips
2. On the other hand, fry in a little butter some parsley, an onion, half a clove of garlic, a handful of carapicú, salt, pepper and nutmeg
3. Add a glass of white wine and let the sirloin boil for half an hour
4. When cold, wrap in four sheets of paper and let cook for two hours in a casserole with low heat below and above
5. Serve with the same paper
