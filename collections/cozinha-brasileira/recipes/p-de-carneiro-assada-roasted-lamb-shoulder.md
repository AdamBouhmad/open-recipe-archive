## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- water
- salt
- onion
- garlic clove
- parsley
- pepper
- nutmeg
- butter
- dried breadcrumbs
- picante sauce

## Directions

1. Boil a shoulder in water and salt until cooked
2. Cover it with one onion, one garlic clove, finely chopped parsley, pepper and ground nutmeg
3. Pour some spoonfuls of melted butter on top
4. Wrap with dried breadcrumbs pressed and placed in a pan
5. Take to the oven where it is left to roast until it takes on a good color
6. Serve with a picante sauce
