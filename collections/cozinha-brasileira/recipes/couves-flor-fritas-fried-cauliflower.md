## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- water
- salt
- butter
- thin batter

## Directions

1. Proceed as in the previous article, taking care only that the cauliflower is not entirely cooked
2. Pass them two or three times through hot butter
3. Let cool well
4. Soak in thin batter and then fry, taking care that they achieve a good color
5. Remove, let drain and serve
