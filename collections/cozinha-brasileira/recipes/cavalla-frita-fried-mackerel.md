## Ingredients

- one fish
- butter
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- one spoon wheat flour
- two cups water
- lemon juice
- some capers

## Directions

1. Clean a fish and place it in a casserole with butter, garlic, salt and pepper
2. Fry the fish, turning it two or three times, and remove it
3. Add one spoon of wheat flour to the butter, stir
4. Add two cups of water, some lemon juice, and some capers
5. Let it boil and serve with the fish
