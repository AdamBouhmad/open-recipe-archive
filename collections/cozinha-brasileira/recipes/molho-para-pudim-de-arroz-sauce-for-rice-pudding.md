## Ingredients

- Milk
- Egg yolks
- Cinnamon
- Cornstarch
- Sugar
- Rose water or orange flower water

## Directions

1. Boil, stirring constantly, half a bottle of milk, four egg yolks, a piece of cinnamon, a small spoonful of cornstarch, a little sugar, a little rose water or orange flower water
2. Serve
