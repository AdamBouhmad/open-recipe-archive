## Ingredients

- two hard-boiled eggs
- chicken giblets
- parsley
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- thin slice of bacon

## Directions

1. Chop finely two hard-boiled eggs, the chicken giblets and a little parsley and mix with a spoonful of breadcrumbs and another of butter, salt and pepper
2. Fill the chicken with this stuffing
3. Roast on the spit, covering it with a very thin slice of bacon
4. Serve with salad after roasting it properly
