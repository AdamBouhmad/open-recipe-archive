## Ingredients

- chicken pieces
- melted fat
- half a plate of selected and washed rice
- one bottle of hot water
- salt
- perennial onion leaves
- parsley
- half a dozen Indian peppers

## Directions

1. Brown the chicken pieces in melted fat
2. After eight minutes, remove the pieces from the pan
3. In the same fat, when well hot, add half a plate of selected and washed rice
4. Stir until evenly coated with fat
5. Add one bottle of hot water, the chicken pieces, salt, perennial onion leaves, parsley, and half a dozen Indian peppers
6. Let simmer briefly
7. Move the pan near the fire to cook slowly until the rice is dry
8. Serve with the rice
