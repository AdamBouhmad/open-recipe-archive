## Ingredients

- two dozen catfish
- salt
- lemon juice
- bitter peppers
- fat for frying
- juice of one sour orange

## Directions

1. Clean two dozen catfish, rub them with salt, lemon juice, and bitter peppers
2. Fry in fat
3. When fried and golden, serve, squeezing sour orange juice over the top
