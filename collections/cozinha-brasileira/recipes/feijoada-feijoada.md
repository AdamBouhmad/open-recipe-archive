## Ingredients

- beans
- water
- salt
- lard
- sausages
- pork
- dried meat
- colonial meat
- onions
- garlic

## Directions

1. Put the chosen and washed beans in a pan with water, salt, a piece of lard, some sausages, pork, dried meat, colonial meat, two split onions and a clove of garlic.
2. Let boil four to five times, and once cooked and the water reduced, serve.
