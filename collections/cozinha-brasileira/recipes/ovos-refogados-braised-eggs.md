## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- ground pepper
- hot fat or oil
- wheat flour
- butter
- lemon juice
- broth
- bread

## Directions

1. Place a broken egg in a cup, add a little salt and ground pepper, and invert the cup in a frying pan containing hot fat.
2. When the egg is cooked on one side, turn it inside the cup to fry on the other side.
3. Fry a dozen or more eggs in the same way.
4. Serve on a plate, placing on top of each egg a slice of fried bread the same size as the egg, pouring over them a sauce made with a spoonful of wheat flour and another of melted butter, the juice of a lemon, and a cup of broth.
5. After it has boiled, serve.
