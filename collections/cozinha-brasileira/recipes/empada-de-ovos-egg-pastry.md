## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- four tablespoons grated cheese
- two tablespoons fresh butter
- salt
- ground pepper

## Directions

1. Beat a dozen eggs with four tablespoons of grated cheese and two tablespoons of fresh butter
2. Pour everything into a frying pan and fry, stirring until it hardens
3. Season with salt and ground pepper
4. Fill the pastry form with this mixture
5. Leave to bake in the oven and serve
