## Ingredients

- agouti
- handful of carapicus
- four ounces bacon
- half dozen tomatoes
- parsley
- comarí peppers
- two hard-boiled eggs
- garlic clove
- one onion
- grated ginger
- one spoon sugar
- lemon juice
- bacon lard strips
- one cup water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Flay and clean the agouti
2. Prepare a filling with a handful of carapicus, four ounces of bacon, half a dozen tomatoes, parsley, comarí peppers, two hard-boiled eggs, one garlic clove, one onion, grated ginger, and one spoon of sugar, moistened with a little lemon juice
3. Fill the agouti with this mixture
4. Place it in a pan lined with bacon lard strips, one cup of water, a portion of carapicus, salt, and pepper
5. Cover well and cook over coals for two to three hours
6. Serve
