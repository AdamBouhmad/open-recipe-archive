## Ingredients

- one piece of pork meat
- water
- salt
- ginger
- parsley
- onion leaves
- celery
- pepper
- pork fat
- serralha, chopped
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Boil in water with salt, ginger, parsley, onion leaves, celery and pepper, one piece of meat.
2. When cooked, remove it and fry it in pork fat, and add to the broth a portion of chopped serralha.
3. Boil a little and place it on a plate covered with breadcrumbs.
4. Put the meat in the middle and serve.
