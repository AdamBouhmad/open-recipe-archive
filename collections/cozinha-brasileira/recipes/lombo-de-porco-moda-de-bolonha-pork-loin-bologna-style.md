## Ingredients

- Pork loin with loin attached
- Sage leaves
- Vinegar
- Onions
- Carrots
- Lemon zest
- Cloves
- Water or meat broth
- Herbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Redcurrant jelly

## Directions

1. Take a pork loin with the loin still attached
2. Cover it with sage leaves and tie both ends with twine to give it a round shape
3. Place it in a terrine, cover with vinegar, and add some onions and carrots cut into slices, lemon zest, and some cloves
4. Let it marinate for two to three days
5. After this time, place the loin in a casserole, adding half of the marinade liquid, two cups of water or meat broth, two onions, two carrots, and a bundle of aromatic herbs, salt and pepper
6. Cover the casserole, place it over a strong fire which is then reduced, letting it cook for two to three hours
7. Remove the twine and sage leaves, place it on a plate, and serve with a portion of redcurrant jelly in a separate terrine
8. Alternatively, the sauce in which the loin was cooked may be poured over it
