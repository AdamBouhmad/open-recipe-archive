## Ingredients

- venison shoulder
- salt
- lemon juice
- portion of red beets
- one bottle of red wine
- nutmeg
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Anoint the shoulder with salt and lemon juice and roast it a little on the spit over flame to become toasted externally
2. Then remove it and put it in a pot with a portion of red beets, peeled and cut into small pieces, one bottle of red wine, grated nutmeg and the juice of one lemon
3. Let boil over moderate heat for two hours and serve with the beets
