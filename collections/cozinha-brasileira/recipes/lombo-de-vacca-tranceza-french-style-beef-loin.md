## Ingredients

- good piece of beef loin
- pieces of bacon
- garlic wine
- butter
- paper
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Take a good piece of beef loin, from which the membrane is removed; beat it with a mallet, piercing it with pieces of bacon; let it soak in garlic wine for twenty-four hours.
2. After it has drained and been dried with a cloth, place it on a spit, cover it with a sheet of paper greased with butter, let it roast for an hour and a quarter, moistening it every ten minutes with the juice that drips from it.
3. One hour later, remove the paper and let it take color, placing it on the plate which should contain a restorative sauce, pouring over it the substance from the meat itself or a sauce of tubaras.
