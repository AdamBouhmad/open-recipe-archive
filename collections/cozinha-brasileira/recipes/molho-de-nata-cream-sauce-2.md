## Ingredients

- 2 spoons of fat
- 1 spoon of cornmeal
- a bunch of parsley
- chopped chives
- salt
- pepper from India
- a little nutmeg
- 1 cup of milk or cream

## Directions

1. Pour 2 spoons of fat in a pan
2. Add 1 spoon of cornmeal, a bunch of parsley, chopped chives, salt, pepper from India and a little nutmeg
3. Add 1 cup of milk or cream
4. Let it boil for fifteen minutes and serve for potatoes or cod
