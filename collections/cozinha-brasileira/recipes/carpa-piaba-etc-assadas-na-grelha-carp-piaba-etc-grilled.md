## Ingredients

- one fish
- salt
- pepper
- two tablespoons butter
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- salt
- pepper
- one cup of water
- one cup of sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Clean and scale one fish; open it along the back in strips, dust with salt and pepper, and grill
2. On the other hand, melt two tablespoons of butter over coals
3. Mix one tablespoon of wheat flour, salt, pepper, and one cup of water
4. Let it boil a little, add one cup of sour orange juice, place on a plate, put the fish on top, and serve
