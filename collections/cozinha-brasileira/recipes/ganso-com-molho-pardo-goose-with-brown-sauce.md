## Ingredients

- goose legs and feet
- goose giblets, cleaned
- goose head, neck, wings and offal
- blood from the goose, collected in a bowl with vinegar
- six or eight quinces, cut and peeled
- eight bananas
- a portion of sweet potatoes, cut
- salt
- pepper
- marjoram
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take the legs and feet of the goose, which are wrapped with the giblets of the same bird after being well cleaned.
2. Add the head, neck, wings, and offal, and place all in a pan with the blood of the bird, collected in a bowl with vinegar, and dissolved in a bowl of water.
3. Add six or eight quinces, cut and peeled, eight bananas, a portion of sweet potatoes cut, salt, pepper, marjoram and some sugar.
4. If the seasoning is too sour, add sugar; if not, allow to boil, and when cooked, with the broth reduced and thick, serve.
