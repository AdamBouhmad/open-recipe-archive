## Ingredients

- a quarter of almonds
- a quarter of sugar moistened with a few drops of milk
- a cup of milk
- the breast of a hen, finely chopped
- salt
- lemon zest
- eight eggs
- two spoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Crush a quarter of almonds and a quarter of sugar moistened with a few drops of milk.
2. Then add a cup of milk and the breast of a hen finely chopped, salt, lemon zest.
3. Heat, and add eight well-beaten eggs.
4. Mix everything, and melt two spoonfuls of butter in a casserole.
5. Pour this mixture into it, let it fry without stirring, and serve.
