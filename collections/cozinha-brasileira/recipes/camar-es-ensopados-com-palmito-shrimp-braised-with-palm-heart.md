## Ingredients

- palmito
- water
- salt
- portion of shrimp
- two spoons of fat
- one spoon of wheat flour
- chopped parsley
- one cut onion
- pepper
- one cup of white wine
- beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Chop a palmito and boil a little in water and salt
2. Remove the pieces with a skimmer and boil a portion of shrimp in the same water
3. Shell the shrimp after cooling
4. In a plate that can withstand heat, melt two spoons of fat, add one spoon of wheat flour, chopped parsley, a cut onion, pepper and salt
5. Add one cup of white wine and the shrimp with the palmito
6. Stir and pour beaten eggs over everything
7. Put in the oven, let it take good color and serve
