## Ingredients

- five to six armored catfish
- one bottle of milk
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- onion leaves
- one tablespoon of lard
- one tablespoon of cassava flour
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from five to six catfish
2. Boil one bottle of milk with salt, parsley, pepper and onion leaves
3. Add the fish and cook for a quarter hour
4. Remove the fish, strain the broth, melt one tablespoon of lard and mix with one tablespoon of cassava flour
5. Add the broth incorporated with two egg yolks, boil and serve over the fish
