## Ingredients

- cooked beef
- onion
- parsley
- salt
- garlic
- tomatoes
- lard
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After the meat is cooked, cut it into slices.
2. Chop one onion, parsley, add to it salt, garlic and tomatoes, and mix all of this.
3. Having previously on the fire a pan with hot lard, place in it a layer of mixed seasonings, another of meat slices, and so on, until filling it, with the last layer being seasonings.
4. In this state, cover the pan and as soon as the seasonings are cooked, add to them half a glass of vinegar, shake the pan for the sauce to penetrate, and serve.
