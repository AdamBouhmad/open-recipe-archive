## Ingredients

- mutton loin
- thin slices of ham
- slices of bacon
- two onions
- two cloves
- marjoram
- one bay leaf
- salt
- pepper
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Take the mutton loin, cut it into equal slices the thickness of a finger and beat them with the back of a knife
2. Place these pieces in a pot, over thin slices of ham, and covered with slices of bacon
3. Add two onions, two cloves, a little marjoram, a bay leaf, salt, pepper and meat broth
4. Simmer with heat below and above until cooked
5. Remove the slices, reduce the sauce after removing the fat, strain it and serve with the prepared meat
