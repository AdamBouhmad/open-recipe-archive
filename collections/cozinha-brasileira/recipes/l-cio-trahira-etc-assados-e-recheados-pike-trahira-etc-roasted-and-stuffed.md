## Ingredients

- one large fish
- half a dozen small fish, cleaned with bones removed and chopped
- half a dozen cooked shrimp
- one onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- carapicú
- bread crumb soaked in hot milk
- butter
- one cup of hot wine
- one glass of vinegar
- one tablespoon of mustard sauce
- yolk of one cooked egg

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and fill it with the following mixture: half a dozen cleaned and chopped small fish, half a dozen cooked shrimp, one onion, parsley, salt, pepper, carapicú, and bread crumb soaked in hot milk, all well mashed
2. Dust the fish with salt and pepper
3. Wrap it in a buttered paper leaf and tie
4. Thread the fish onto a spit and roast over moderate fire
5. When roasted, make a sauce of one cup of hot wine, one glass of vinegar, one tablespoon of mustard sauce, salt, pepper and the yolk of one cooked egg
6. Boil the sauce briefly and serve it
