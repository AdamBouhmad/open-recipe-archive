## Ingredients

- one wild boar loin
- water
- salt
- pepper
- two cloves
- bay leaves
- one sprig of ground ivy
- parsley
- onion leaves
- half a quart of finely chopped bacon
- fresh broad beans
- three egg yolks
- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- half a cup of sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut the loin into pieces and boil in water with salt, pepper, cloves, bay leaves, ground ivy, parsley, and onion leaves
2. When cooked, strain the broth and fry the meat with half a quart of finely chopped bacon
3. Add fresh broad beans and immediately the broth
4. Let boil until the beans are cooked and the broth is reduced
5. Incorporate three egg yolks beaten with one tablespoon of wheat flour and half a cup of sour orange juice
6. Serve
