## Ingredients

- beef loin
- turnips, about half a dozen
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the loin into pieces the size of a square inch
2. Place them in a casserole dish lined with butter
3. Cut the turnips in the same manner and add them to the meat
4. Cover with melted butter
5. Once everything is cooked, drain the butter
6. Pour the meat and turnips into a serving dish
7. Pour Spanish sauce over it, to which a glass of Madeira wine has been previously added
