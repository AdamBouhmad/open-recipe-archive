## Ingredients

- one coati, skinned and cleaned
- salt
- pepper juice
- tomato juice
- chicken blood

## Directions

1. Rub the coati with salt, pepper juice and tomato juice
2. Leave with these seasonings for three hours to absorb the flavors
3. Anoint with chicken blood
4. Place on a spit and roast over high heat at first, then moderate to take on good color and serve
