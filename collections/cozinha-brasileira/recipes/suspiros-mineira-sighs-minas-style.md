## Ingredients

- four fresh egg whites
- one pound of refined and strained sugar
- lemon zest or ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat four fresh egg whites with one pound of refined and strained sugar
2. Beat until firm
3. Add lemon zest or ground cinnamon
4. Drop spoonfuls of this mixture on paper
5. Place in a very gentle oven
