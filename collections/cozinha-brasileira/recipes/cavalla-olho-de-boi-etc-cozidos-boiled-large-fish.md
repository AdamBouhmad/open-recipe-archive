## Ingredients

- large fish
- water
- salt
- melted butter
- wheat flour
- parsley, chopped
- fish broth
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and open a fish along the back for its entire length.
2. Boil a portion of water with plenty of salt.
3. When boiling, place the fish in it.
4. After half an hour, remove the fish.
5. Make a sauce of melted butter mixed with wheat flour and chopped parsley.
6. Add two cups of fish broth.
7. Let it boil a little more and serve with lemon juice on top.
