## Ingredients

- lamb leg
- cloth
- water
- salt
- bay leaf
- English preserves or mustard

## Directions

1. Wrap the leg in a well-sealed cloth
2. Place it to cook in water that should already be boiling on the fire with salt and a bay leaf
3. Serve with English preserves or mustard
