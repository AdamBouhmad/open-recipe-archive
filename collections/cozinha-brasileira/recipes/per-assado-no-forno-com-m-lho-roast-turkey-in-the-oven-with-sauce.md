## Ingredients

- turkey
- bacon strips
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- garlic
- onion
- cloves
- cup of white wine
- four cups water

## Directions

1. Lard the breast of the turkey with strips of bacon seasoned with salt, pepper, chopped parsley and garlic.
2. Place it in a pan over bacon slices with a cut onion, salt, cloves, a cup of white wine and four cups of water.
3. Turn it from time to time after placing it in the oven.
4. When cooked, serve with its own strained sauce, or with beans, peas, spinach etc.
