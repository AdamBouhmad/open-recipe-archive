## Ingredients

- wild boar meat
- water
- salt
- toasted wheat flour
- onions
- juniper berries
- cloves
- vinegar
- sugar
- rosemary (if fresh juniper berries are unavailable)

## Directions

1. Cut meat into pieces and soak for twelve hours
2. Scald and place over the fire with water and salt
3. Remove the scum
4. When cooked, add plenty of toasted wheat flour, plenty of onions, juniper berries, cloves, salt, vinegar, and sugar
5. Let boil with the meat
6. When serving, remove the meat and strain the sauce
7. Serve
8. If fresh juniper berries are unavailable, add some rosemary leaves
