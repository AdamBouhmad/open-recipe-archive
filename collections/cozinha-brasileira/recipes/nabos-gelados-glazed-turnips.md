## Ingredients

- turnips
- water
- meat broth
- white wine

## Directions

1. Peel and cut turnips into pieces
2. Boil in water for a few minutes, then cook in meat broth until cooked and the broth reduced to a jelly
3. When the jelly begins to stick to the bottom of the pan, remove the turnips
4. Pour a glass of white wine into the pan, stir with a spoon to dissolve the reduced broth, pour it over the turnips, and serve
