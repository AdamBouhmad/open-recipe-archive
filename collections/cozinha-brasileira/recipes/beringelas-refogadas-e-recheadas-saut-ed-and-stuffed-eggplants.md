## Ingredients

- eggplants
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- egg yolk
- bread
- meat broth
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the eggplants
2. Cut them in two parts and remove the pulp
3. Mash this pulp with butter, breadcrumbs, an egg yolk and fill the eggplants with this mixture
4. At the same time, place some slices of bread in a casserole moistened with a cup of meat broth and two beaten eggs
5. Place them over the eggplants
6. Boil for about half an hour and serve
