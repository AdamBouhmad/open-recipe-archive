## Ingredients

- one spoonful of cow butter
- one chalice of white wine
- mushrooms
- one dozen eggs
- half a chalice of wine
- fine salt
- ground pepper
- two spoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Melt one spoonful of cow butter, mix with one chalice of white wine, and boil in this sauce a portion of finely chopped mushrooms
2. Beat one dozen eggs with half a chalice of wine, fine salt, and ground pepper
3. Melt two spoonfuls of butter in a frying pan
4. Pour half the eggs, let fry, and when it begins to stick to the bottom, lift and add one more spoonful of butter
5. When melted, flip the omelette, add the sautéed mushrooms, and pour the rest of the eggs
6. Cover the frying pan with a lid filled with coals
7. When cooked, serve
