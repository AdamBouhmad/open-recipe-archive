## Ingredients

- fish
- sea water or well-salted water
- bread crumbs
- butter
- parsley, chopped
- mustard powder
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. After cleaning the fish, place a pot with sea water or well-salted water on the fire.
2. When boiling, place the fish in it.
3. Remove them immediately, let them drain and serve with fried breadcrumbs in butter with chopped parsley.
4. A little mustard powder can be added, along with some potatoes boiled in the same water.
