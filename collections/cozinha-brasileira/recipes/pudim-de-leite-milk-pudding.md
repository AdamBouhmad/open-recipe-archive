## Ingredients

- one bottle of milk
- three spoons of polvilho
- half a pound of sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon zest
- nutmeg
- one dozen egg yolks
- four egg whites

## Directions

1. Boil one bottle of milk with three spoons of polvilho, half a pound of sugar, cinnamon, lemon zest and nutmeg
2. When it has boiled a little, add one dozen egg yolks and four well-beaten egg whites until very firm
3. Mix everything well
4. Place the mixture in a mold
5. Bake in the oven and serve
