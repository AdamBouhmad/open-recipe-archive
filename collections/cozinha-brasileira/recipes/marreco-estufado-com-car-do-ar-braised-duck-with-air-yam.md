## Ingredients

- 1 duck
- salt
- garlic juice
- onion juice
- ground pepper
- chopped parsley
- vinegar
- air yam, grated
- three tablespoons butter
- three tablespoons grated cheese
- little salt
- ground cinnamon
- bacon slices
- six to eight onions
- one cup of broth
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Soak the duck with salt, garlic juice, onion juice, ground pepper, chopped parsley, and vinegar
2. After six to eight hours, remove it and fill it with grated air yam mixed with three tablespoons of butter, three of grated cheese, little salt, and ground cinnamon
3. Place the filled duck on a slice of bacon and cover with another slice
4. Add six to eight onions, one cup of broth, salt, pepper, and chopped parsley
5. Seal the lid and let it cook for an hour and a half over a regular fire
6. When cooked, remove the duck, strain the sauce, remove the fat, and pour it over the duck on a plate, arranging the onions around it
