## Ingredients

- watercress leaves
- water
- salt
- pepper
- fat

## Directions

1. Pick the watercress leaves and after cleaning and chopping them, boil them in water for some minutes
2. Remove, drain them and sauté them in fat, with a little salt and pepper
3. Boil them afterwards over coals
4. Serve
