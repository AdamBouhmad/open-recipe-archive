## Ingredients

- bread the size of two closed hands
- hot milk
- three tablespoons of wheat flour
- four egg yolks
- two egg whites
- salt
- half tablespoon of sugar
- one tablespoon of butter

## Directions

1. Soak bread the size of two closed hands in hot milk
2. When softened, remove it and knead with three tablespoons of wheat flour, four egg yolks and two egg whites, little salt, half a tablespoon of sugar and one tablespoon of butter
3. Form small balls with this mixture, boil them in water that is already boiling
4. Serve with meat broth or egg sauce
