## Ingredients

- bananas
- cornmeal
- lard
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take a quantity of bananas and thrust them into hot coals
2. Remove and peel them
3. Wrap them in cornmeal
4. Fry them in lard and serve with sugar sprinkled on top
