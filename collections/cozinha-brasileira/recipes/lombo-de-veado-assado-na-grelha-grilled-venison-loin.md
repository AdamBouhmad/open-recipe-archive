## Ingredients

- venison loin
- thin strips of bacon
- salt
- pimento peppers
- garlic
- onions
- bitter orange juice
- mustard
- white wine

## Directions

1. Lard the loin with thin strips of bacon
2. Sprinkle it with salt and anoint it with the juice of pimento peppers, garlic, chopped onions, and bitter orange juice
3. Leave it in these seasonings for six hours
4. Grill it over live fire on both sides to take on good color
5. Serve with its own sauce to which a little ground mustard or preferably English mustard is added; if the sauce is scanty, add a glass of white wine
