## Ingredients

- carrots
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Take a portion of carrots, scrape and cut them into very fine slices
2. After boiling once, drain them
3. Place them in a casserole with a little butter, salt, broken pepper, and a little nutmeg
4. Sauté over the fire, adding a good spoon of reduced Spanish sauce (in this case do not let them boil)
5. Add chopped chives and parsley and serve
