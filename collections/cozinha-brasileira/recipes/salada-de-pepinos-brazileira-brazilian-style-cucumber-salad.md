## Ingredients

- four cucumbers
- four onions
- salt
- pimentos comaris
- lemon juice
- melted fat

## Directions

1. Peel four cucumbers and cut into fine slices
2. Cut four onions equally into slices
3. Mix with salt, pimentos comaris, lemon juice and melted fat
4. Serve
