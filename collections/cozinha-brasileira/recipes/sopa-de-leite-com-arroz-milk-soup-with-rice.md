## Ingredients

- 4 full tablespoons rice
- 1 bottle milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Wash four tablespoons of rice and put in a bottle of milk
2. Let boil for one hour
3. Then add two tablespoons of sugar, two beaten egg yolks, and a little cinnamon
4. Remove from heat
