## Ingredients

- clean fish
- fresh butter
- parsley
- chopped onion
- salt
- one cup of white wine

## Directions

1. Clean some fish and fry them in fresh butter with a little parsley, chopped onion and salt; when fried, remove from heat, and add one cup of white wine, leaving them in this sauce for one hour; roast over grill and serve with the sauce
