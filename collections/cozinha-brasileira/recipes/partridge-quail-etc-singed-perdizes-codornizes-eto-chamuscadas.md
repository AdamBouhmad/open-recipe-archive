## Ingredients

- birds
- bacon
- white wine
- salt
- chopped parsley
- juice of peppers
- juice of garlic

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the birds and place them on a spit
2. roast a little over a flame
3. then lard with bacon and finish roasting over moderate heat
4. baste with white wine to which have been added a little salt, chopped parsley, juice of peppers and of garlic
5. when cooked, serve with some spicy sauce
