## Ingredients

- wheat flour
- butter
- water
- white wine
- salt
- parsley
- coriander
- young carrots
- green peas

## Directions

1. Sauté half a spoon of wheat flour in two spoons of butter
2. Add two cups of water, a glass of white wine, salt, parsley, and coriander
3. After boiling for an instant, add a portion of young carrots and green peas
4. Leave to cook and serve
