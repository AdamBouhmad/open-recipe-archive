## Ingredients

- two pounds mutton
- quarter pound bacon
- cabbage, chopped, washed and squeezed
- water, one cup
- salt
- onion leaves

## Directions

1. Take two pounds mutton and a quarter pound bacon; fry everything together and add the chopped, washed and squeezed cabbage
2. Let sauté, stirring, then add one cup water, salt and some onion leaves; cover well
3. Let boil for one hour over coals and serve
