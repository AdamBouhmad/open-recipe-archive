## Ingredients

- a cut of fatty pork
- water
- salt
- tomatoes
- onions
- chili peppers
- bay leaf
- ginger
- parsley
- garlic
- ground peanuts
- lemon juice
- fubá de cangica (corn flour)

## Directions

1. Take a cut of fatty pork, cut it into pieces the size of hazelnuts, and put it to boil in a little water with salt, tomatoes, cut onions, chili peppers, a bay leaf, ginger, parsley, garlic
2. When the meat is cooked, remove it and strain the broth
3. Add to it a spoonful of ground peanuts, the juice of a lemon and a little corn flour (fubá mimoso)
4. Boil the broth still a little with the meat, and serve
