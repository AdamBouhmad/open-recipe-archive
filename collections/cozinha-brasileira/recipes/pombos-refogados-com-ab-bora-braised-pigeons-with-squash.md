## Ingredients

- small pigeons
- small squashes with still soft skin
- hot water
- bacon piece, chopped
- one tablespoon butter
- salt
- pepper
- half a dozen tomatoes

## Directions

1. Take small pigeons and small squashes with still soft skin and scald them in hot water, leaving them until the water cools
2. Melt a chopped piece of bacon
3. When well heated, add the halved small pigeons and whole squashes, always turning them
4. Add one cup of water, salt, pepper, half a dozen tomatoes
5. Let them dry near the fire
6. After half an hour, serve them
