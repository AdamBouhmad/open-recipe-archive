## Ingredients

- one plate of well-chosen and washed rice
- water
- four bottles of milk
- one tablespoon of butter
- one dozen egg yolks
- ground cinnamon
- flower water

## Directions

1. Take a plate of well-chosen rice washed in cold water and put it to cook in water, letting it cook for a quarter hour
2. Then pour it into a coarse sieve to drain
3. Then put it in a pot with four bottles of milk and let it boil
4. When the milk begins to dry out, add one tablespoon of butter and one dozen egg yolks, passing the yolks through a sieve before adding so they do not curdle
5. Place on dishes, dust with ground cinnamon, and serve; one can also add a little flower water
