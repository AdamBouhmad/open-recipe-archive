## Ingredients

- tea leaves
- hot water

## Directions

1. Place two teaspoons of tea in a pot
2. Pour one cup of hot water over it
3. Bring the pot to the table with another pot of boiling water
4. Pour the boiling water over the tea in the quantity needed
5. Cover the pot and let it steep for two minutes
6. Serve
