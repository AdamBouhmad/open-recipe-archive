## Ingredients

- beef loin, tender
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaves
- cloves
- cebolinhos (small onions)
- Indian pepper or comari

## Directions

1. The beef loin, being tender, is always roasted on a spit. Remove the fat and skin, then take it to a sauce of vinegar, salt, pepper, bay leaves, and cloves, and after twelve hours, place it on a spit and let it roast for an hour and a half or two hours, depending on the thickness of the piece, always turning it.
2. Serve with a sauce made from the juice that dripped from the meat mixed with vinegar, cebolinhos, Indian pepper or comari. One should never lard this loin, as it loses its juice through the cuts made for this purpose, and consequently remains without flavor.
