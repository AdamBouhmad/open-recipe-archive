## Ingredients

- one capon, cut into pieces
- four spoons of hot fat
- one bottle of water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- year-round onion leaves
- one good spoon of fine cornmeal
- one large cará, peeled and cut into pieces

## Directions

1. Brown the capon pieces in four spoons of hot fat
2. When almost fried, add one bottle of water, salt, pepper, parsley, year-round onion leaves, one good spoon of fine cornmeal, and one large cará peeled and cut into pieces
3. Let them boil until cooked, and serve in this state
