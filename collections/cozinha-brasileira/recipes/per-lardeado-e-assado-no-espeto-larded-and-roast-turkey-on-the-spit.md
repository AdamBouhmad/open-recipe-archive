## Ingredients

- young turkey
- fine bacon
- vinegar
- cloves
- bay leaves
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- cup of full-fat milk

## Directions

1. Take a young turkey and lard the breast with fine bacon.
2. Soak it in vinegar with cloves, bay leaves, onion, pepper and salt.
3. After four hours, spit it and roast it, basting it with a cup of full-fat milk and then with the sauce that dripped down.
4. Serve with salads.
