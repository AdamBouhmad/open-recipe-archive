## Ingredients

- beef tongue, cooked or roasted
- broth with vinegar
- pickled peppers
- parsley
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumb
- white wine

## Directions

1. Take a tongue already cooked or roasted and cut into very thin slices
2. Place at the bottom of the serving dish a little broth with vinegar, minced pickled peppers, parsley, onion (all finely chopped), salt, pepper and bread crumb
3. Place a layer of tongue slices on top, over this another layer of bread crumb, and so on until finished
4. Place the dish in the oven, not very hot, and let it cook until the bottom layer begins to roast
5. Serve after moistening with a few spoonfuls of broth and white wine
