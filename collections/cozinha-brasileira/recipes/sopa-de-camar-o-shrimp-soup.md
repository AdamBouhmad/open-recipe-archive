## Ingredients

- shrimp
- water
- salt
- eggs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Boil the shrimp in water and salt
2. After an hour, when large bubbles appear on the surface, slowly drip the contents of two or four eggs without stirring, so that as these drops fall into the broth they coagulate and form small pieces
3. Add a parsley leaf and serve
