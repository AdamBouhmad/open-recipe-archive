## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- broth
- bacon strips
- aromatic herbs
- onions
- Indian cloves
- bacon
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the tongue with six spoonfuls of broth, bacon strips, an herb sauce, one or two onions and Indian cloves
2. When the broth is reduced to a quarter, let it cool
3. Fill the tongue with bacon strips, thread it on the spit and let it roast for an hour
4. Serve with a spicy sauce
