## Ingredients

- some onions
- parsley
- a little water or beef broth
- a little corn meal
- one spoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop some onions and parsley finely
2. Put in a saucepan with a little water or beef broth, a little corn meal, and one spoon of vinegar
3. Allow to boil until the onion is cooked
