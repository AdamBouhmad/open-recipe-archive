## Ingredients

- fish
- water
- white wine
- salt
- peppercorns
- bay leaves
- clove
- parsley
- onions
- two tablespoons of butter
- vinegar
- sweet oil
- cassava flour

## Directions

1. Boil the fish in equal parts water and white wine, ensuring the fish is covered with liquid
2. Add salt, peppercorns, bay leaves, clove, parsley, and onions, and two tablespoons of butter
3. When cooked, remove the fish and serve with vinegar, sweet oil, salt, pepper and a broth made from the fish cooking liquid and cassava flour
