## Ingredients

- Sea water or well salted water
- Half bottle of milk
- Fish

## Directions

1. Put a large amount of sea water or well salted water and half bottle of milk in a large pan
2. Put the cleaned fish in this water and boil over moderate heat
3. When cooked, remove and dry
4. Serve hot with melted butter, or cold with sweet oil and vinegar
