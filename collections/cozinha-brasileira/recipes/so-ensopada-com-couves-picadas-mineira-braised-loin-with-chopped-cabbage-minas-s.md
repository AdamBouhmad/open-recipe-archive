## Ingredients

- Pork loin
- Salt
- Garlic
- Cabbage
- Comarim peppers
- Watercress leaves

## Directions

1. Cut the loin into pieces
2. Season with salt and garlic
3. Fry the pieces
4. Add finely chopped cabbage and comarim peppers
5. Serve
6. Alternatively, finely chopped watercress leaves may be used instead of cabbage
