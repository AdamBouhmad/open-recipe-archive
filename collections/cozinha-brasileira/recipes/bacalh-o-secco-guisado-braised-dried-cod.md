## Ingredients

- dried cod, cooked
- two tablespoons fat
- tomatoes
- one tablespoon cassava flour
- pepper
- parsley
- one cup water
- bitter orange juice

## Directions

1. Cook a piece of dried cod and fry it in two tablespoons of fat with a portion of tomatoes, one tablespoon of cassava flour, pepper and parsley.
2. Add the cod at this time, then one cup of water and the juice of one bitter orange.
3. Let it boil a little and serve.
