## Ingredients

- sorrel leaves
- water
- salt
- butter or fat
- one clove of garlic
- hard-cooked eggs

## Directions

1. After removing the stems from the leaves, wash them in cold water
2. When a portion of water with salt, placed in a casserole, is boiling, throw the leaves inside and allow them to boil for five minutes
3. Remove the leaves with a skimming spoon, and place them on a board, where they are chopped with a knife, and in this state, sauté them in a casserole in which a small portion of butter or fat is melted, adding one well-pounded clove of garlic
4. Place on a serving dish, placing over top some hard-cooked eggs divided in half
