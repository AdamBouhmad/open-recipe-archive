## Ingredients

- two tablespoons cow butter
- two large onions
- one fish cut in pieces
- one bottle of red wine
- handful of breadcrumbs
- salt

## Directions

1. Melt two tablespoons of cow butter, add two large onions cut in half and one fish cut in pieces
2. Add one bottle of red wine
3. Let it boil over moderate fire
4. When cooked, add a handful of breadcrumbs, little salt, boil a bit more and serve
