## Ingredients

- two spoons of vinegar
- two spoons of sweet oil
- a little pepper
- salt
- sugar
- lemon zest
- vinegar as needed

## Directions

1. Beat two spoons of vinegar, two spoons of sweet oil, a little pepper, the same amount of salt and sugar until well mixed
2. Add a little lemon zest and gradually as much vinegar as needed for the sauce
3. Serve cold with cold meats, whether pork or beef, and with fried fish
