## Ingredients

- one fat duck
- one cup meat broth
- salt
- pepper
- onion leaves
- one tayoba root, boiled in water and salt
- one egg yolk
- one small spoon sugar
- one tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the duck into pieces and pass through hot fat
2. When almost cooked, add one cup of meat broth, salt, pepper, onion leaves, and tayoba root that has been boiled in water and salt and cut into slices
3. Let simmer near the fire for one hour
4. Thicken the broth with an egg yolk beaten with one small spoon of sugar and one tablespoon of vinegar
5. Serve with its broth
