## Ingredients

- pork head cut to mid-shoulder
- pork meat
- pork tongue
- salt
- saltpeter
- thyme
- bay leaf
- garlic
- clove
- sage
- Indian pepper
- juniper berries
- coriander
- pork ears
- ham slices
- pickled cucumbers
- pork sausage meat
- lard

## Directions

1. Cut the pork head to mid-shoulder and pass it through the flame to remove any remaining bristles
2. Wash it well and scrape with the back of a knife to clean it, removing the bones carefully without damaging the skin
3. Fill the head with meat cut into strips and the tongue, having first spread them with salt and saltpeter
4. Place it in a large earthenware or stone vessel with thyme, bay leaf, garlic, clove, sage, broken Indian pepper, juniper berries, and coriander
5. Cover the terrine with white cloth and place another vessel on top to seal it as well as possible
6. Leave it to cure in salt for eight days, then allow it to drain
7. Garnish it with pork ears, tongues, thin slices of ham, pickled cucumbers, all cut into thin strips that will have remained soaking with the head
8. Alternatively, garnish the pork head with sausage meat, slices of ham, and shavings of hard lard
