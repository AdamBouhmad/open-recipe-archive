## Ingredients

- a little cow butter
- 2 spoons of starch
- 1 chopped onion
- 2 bell peppers
- salt
- some cups of meat broth

## Directions

1. Heat a little cow butter in a pan
2. Add 2 spoons of starch, 1 chopped onion, and 2 bell peppers with salt
3. Stir and add some cups of meat broth
4. Let it boil for half an hour more and serve with fish and cooked meat
