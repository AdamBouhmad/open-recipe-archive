## Ingredients

- one whole bird
- three spoons of fat
- wheat flour
- one cup of water
- one cup of red wine
- salt
- parsley
- marjoram
- peppers
- tomatoes
- four large cucumbers, peeled and cored

## Directions

1. Braise one whole bird in three spoons of fat
2. Dust it with wheat flour and add one cup of water and one cup of red wine
3. Add salt, parsley, marjoram, peppers, tomatoes and four large cucumbers, peeled, cut into four pieces and cored
4. Cover the pan well
5. Let it cook over coals for two hours
6. Serve. Alternatively, the cucumbers can be chopped and mixed with the tomatoes to stuff the bird before cooking in the same manner
