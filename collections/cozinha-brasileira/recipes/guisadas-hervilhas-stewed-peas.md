## Ingredients

- a spoon of lard
- one onion, chopped
- a little nutmeg
- one spoon of breadcrumbs
- cooked peas with broth

## Directions

1. Fry a spoon of lard with a chopped onion, a little nutmeg and a spoon of breadcrumbs
2. Add the cooked peas with broth
3. Let it cook a little more and serve
