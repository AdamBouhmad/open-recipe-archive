## Ingredients

- a portion of sea water
- some fennel leaves
- one clean fish
- two spoons fresh butter
- one spoon wheat flour
- one cup milk
- one egg yolk
- one cup white wine
- some grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of sea water with some fennel leaves
2. When boiling, add the cleaned fish
3. Cook for half an hour and remove it
4. Place the fish near the fire to keep it warm
5. Melt two spoons of fresh butter and mix with one spoon of wheat flour
6. Add one cup of milk and one egg yolk beaten with one cup of white wine
7. Add some grated nutmeg and serve
