## Ingredients

- veal meat
- bacon
- ham or salted pork meat
- seasonings
- egg yolks
- bacon slices
- wine
- potatoes
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Take veal meat, bacon, a piece of ham or salted pork meat, seasonings, some egg yolks
2. Place this mixture in a casserole lined with bacon slices, also putting some on top
3. Moisten with a glass of wine and roast in the oven or over coals
4. Serve with potatoes cooked in butter before serving; a little lemon juice or vinegar can be added
