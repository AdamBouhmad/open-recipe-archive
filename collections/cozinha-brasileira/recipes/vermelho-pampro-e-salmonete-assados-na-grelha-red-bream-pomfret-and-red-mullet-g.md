## Ingredients

- red bream, pomfret or red mullet
- sweet oil
- salt
- pepper
- two spoons of butter
- fish liver
- capers

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and cut into transverse strips
2. Soak for one hour in sweet oil sprinkled with salt and pepper
3. Grill the fish
4. Melt two spoons of butter, add the fish liver and a portion of capers
5. When boiled, serve the fish with this sauce
