## Ingredients

- hake
- two egg yolks
- one tablespoon of melted butter
- salt
- breadcrumbs
- butter for humidifying
- parsley
- chervil
- vinegar
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Wash and clean perfectly a good fish, open the belly, and remove the skin starting from the tail to the head
2. Beat two egg yolks with one tablespoon of melted butter, and coat the fish sprinkled with salt with this mixture
3. Wrap it then in breadcrumbs, humidifying with butter, and covering again with a new layer of breadcrumbs
4. Grill it
5. Chop a little parsley and chervil and add one cup of vinegar, let it soak for a quarter of an hour; then strain, squeeze and mix two tablespoons of melted butter and one tablespoon of wheat flour
6. Heat it a little over the fire and serve with the fish
