## Ingredients

- pork meat
- salt
- parsley
- garlic
- Indian pepper
- onions
- tomatoes
- vinegar
- pork lard
- mangarito (a type of vegetable)

## Directions

1. Cut the pork meat into thin slices, beat them slightly, and trim the edges
2. Place them in a vessel with salt, parsley, garlic, Indian pepper, cut onions, tomatoes, and a little vinegar
3. Sauté them in a large frying pan in which a little pork lard has been melted beforehand
4. When placing the slices in the pan, arrange them in layers and cover
5. Once cooked, add some cooked and peeled mangaritos
6. After a few minutes, place on a serving dish and serve
