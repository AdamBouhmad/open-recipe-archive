## Ingredients

- one pork liver
- six onions
- rich meat broth
- eight ounces of caul fat, cut into pieces
- one quarter of a bottle of rich milk
- half bottle of blood
- salt, ground
- piri-piri pepper
- pepper from India
- parsley
- chives
- thyme
- winter savory
- coriander

## Directions

1. Take one liver, chop it well
2. Cook six onions in rich meat broth, chop them and mix with the already chopped liver
3. Add eight ounces of caul fat cut into pieces and one quarter of a bottle of rich milk and half a bottle of blood prepared as already described
4. Season everything with ground salt, piri-piri pepper, pepper from India, parsley, chives, thyme, winter savory and coriander
5. Place over a gentle fire only to warm through, stirring so that the blood does not stick to the bottom of the vessel
6. Fill intestines with this mixture in the manner already explained
7. After the sausages are ready, cook them
