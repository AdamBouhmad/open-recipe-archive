## Ingredients

- veal meat
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- dry rusks or crackers

## Directions

1. Cut the veal meat into slices
2. Season with salt, ground pepper, and a beaten egg
3. Sprinkle with ground dry rusks or crackers
4. Fry in butter on both sides and serve
