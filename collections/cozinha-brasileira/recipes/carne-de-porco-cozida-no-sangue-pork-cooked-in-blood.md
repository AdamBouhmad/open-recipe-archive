## Ingredients

- portions of pork meat
- salt
- bay leaves
- pepper
- pork blood
- strong vinegar
- clove
- cornmeal mush or cassava flour mush

## Directions

1. Cook portions of pork meat with salt, bay leaves, and pepper
2. Remove the meat and add the pork blood to the broth, having first dissolved it in strong vinegar and passed it through a sieve
3. Add some cloves and bring to a boil
4. Each time the blood appears to curdle, add a few drops of good vinegar
5. Finally return the meat to the vessel and after a few boils, serve with cornmeal mush or cassava flour mush
