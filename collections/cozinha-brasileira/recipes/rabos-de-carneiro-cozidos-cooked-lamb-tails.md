## Ingredients

- Lamb tails
- Hot water
- Bacon skin
- Onion
- Parsley
- Salt
- Fubá mimoso
- Okra
- Minas cornmeal mush

## Directions

1. Scald the tails in hot water
2. Boil again in hot water with a piece of bacon skin, onion, parsley, and salt
3. When cooked and broth well reduced, add a spoonful of fubá mimoso and a dozen okras cut into slices
4. Let everything boil for half an hour
5. Serve with Minas cornmeal mush
