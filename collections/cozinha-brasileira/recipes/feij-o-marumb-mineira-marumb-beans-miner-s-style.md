## Ingredients

- marumbé beans
- water
- salt
- fat
- parsley
- onion leaves

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of marumbé beans in water and salt.
2. Once cooked, drain the water.
3. Heat a little fat with parsley and onion leaves and add the beans.
4. Stir and add the broth, let boil and serve.
