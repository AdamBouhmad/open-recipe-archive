## Ingredients

- green squash
- salt
- garlic
- vinegar
- parsley
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take some green squash, peel them and remove the seeds
2. Then chop them as finely as possible
3. Season with salt, garlic, vinegar, chopped parsley and plenty of pepper
