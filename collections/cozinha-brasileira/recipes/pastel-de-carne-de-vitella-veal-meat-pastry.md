## Ingredients

- one pound of veal
- one quarter of bacon
- two onions
- parsley
- salt
- peppers
- one cup of water
- one cup of wine

## Directions

1. Mince one pound of veal with one quarter of bacon, two onions, parsley, salt and peppers
2. Place everything in a pan with one cup of water and one cup of wine, and let simmer a little
3. Fill small pastries with puff pastry crust with this mixture, put them in the oven, bake them; when baked, serve
