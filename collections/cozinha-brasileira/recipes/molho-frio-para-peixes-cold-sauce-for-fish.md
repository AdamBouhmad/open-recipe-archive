## Ingredients

- a portion of parsley
- chervil
- chives
- marjoram
- two hard-boiled egg yolks
- four spoons of sweet olive oil
- two spoons of vinegar
- two spoons of powdered mustard

## Directions

1. Blanch a portion of parsley, chervil, chives, and marjoram
2. Chop everything finely and pass through a sieve
3. Add two hard-boiled egg yolks and mix well
4. Add little by little four spoons of sweet olive oil, two spoons of vinegar and two spoons of powdered mustard
5. Put in a terrine and serve
