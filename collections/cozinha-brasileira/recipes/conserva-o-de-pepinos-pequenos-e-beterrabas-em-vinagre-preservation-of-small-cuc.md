## Ingredients

- small cucumbers or beets
- strong vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- chives
- spirits of wine

## Directions

1. Take a portion of small cucumbers or beets, wash them and place in a pan with strong vinegar and salt; bring to a boil once; remove the pan, let cool, and after three days, strain the vinegar which is then boiled with pepper, mustard, chives, and poured hot over the cucumbers or beets; add a glass of spirits of wine and place in a wide-mouth glass jar which is sealed and kept.
