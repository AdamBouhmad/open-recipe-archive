## Ingredients

- one bird
- water
- salt
- four spoons of fat
- one white cabbage, chopped
- half bottle of water
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one glass of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil the bird with as much water and salt as needed
2. When almost cooked, remove it and cut it into pieces
3. Braise the pieces in four spoons of fat
4. When browned, remove them and braise one chopped white cabbage in the same fat
5. Stir and add half bottle of water, salt, parsley, onion leaves and one glass of lemon juice
6. Add the bird pieces
7. Let it simmer until the broth is reduced
8. Serve
