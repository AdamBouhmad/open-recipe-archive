## Ingredients

- partridges
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- clove of India
- lemon juice
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Flatten the partridges with a pomatoria
2. soak them with salt, pepper, onion, clove of India and lemon juice
3. after four hours, pass them in melted butter
4. roast on the grill on both sides
5. serve with salad or spicy sauce
