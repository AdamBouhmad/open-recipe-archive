## Ingredients

- beef ribs
- beef broth
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- Indian cloves
- macaroni
- beef broth

## Directions

1. Boil the rib meat for three hours in beef broth and white wine over gentle fire with sufficient seasoning of salt, pepper, nutmeg, and Indian cloves
2. Remove the casserole from fire and strain the broth, then thicken it
3. On the other hand, cook macaroni in beef broth, and when cooked, remove it and mix with a good portion of thin broth
4. Place in the bottom of the casserole, and pour the sauce in which the rib meat was cooked over everything
