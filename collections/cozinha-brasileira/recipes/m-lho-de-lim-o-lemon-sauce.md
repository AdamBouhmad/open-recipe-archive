## Ingredients

- a small spoon of wheat flour
- a little cow's butter
- four egg yolks
- rind of one lemon
- one glass of white wine
- one spoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Mash a small spoon of wheat flour with a little cow's butter, four egg yolks, and the rind of one lemon
2. Boil, stirring until cooked
3. Add one glass of white wine and one spoon of vinegar
4. Serve with beef tongue, chickens, ducks, etc.
