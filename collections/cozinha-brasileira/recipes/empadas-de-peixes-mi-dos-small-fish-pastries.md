## Ingredients

- meat from one roasted chicken
- four cooked eggs
- one onion
- salt
- one garlic clove
- peppers
- small fried fish
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop the meat from a roasted chicken with four cooked eggs, one onion, salt and one garlic clove
2. Add salt and peppers
3. Place part of this chopped mixture at the bottom of a pastry box
4. Place a layer of small fish fried in butter on top, covering with a layer of the chopped mixture
5. Continue placing the fish alternately until the pastry is filled
6. When full, cover with its lid, bake in the oven and serve
