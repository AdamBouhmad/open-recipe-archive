## Ingredients

- fermented cabbage or finely chopped cabbage
- vinegar
- pork meat
- fat
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- fried or cooked potatoes

## Directions

1. Take a portion of fermented cabbage, or in its absence, finely chopped cabbage, scalded with vinegar.
2. After having fried some pieces of pork meat in two or three spoonfuls of fat, remove them and put the cabbage in the same fat, stir until it is well saturated with fat.
3. At this point, add the meat, two cups of white wine, salt, pepper, a cut onion.
4. Leave by the fire until they are well cooked, and serve with fried or cooked potatoes.
