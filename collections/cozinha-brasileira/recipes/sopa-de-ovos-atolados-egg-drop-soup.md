## Ingredients

- one cup of strained meat broth
- three eggs
- dried rusks, ground
- salt
- carrots
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take one cup of meat broth strained, and beat three eggs in it
2. Pour this cup of broth into the larger portion of broth intended for the soup
3. Add a portion of ground dried rusks, necessary salt, carrots and parsley
4. Let it boil for half an hour more, then serve at table
