## Ingredients

- butter
- a slice of ham
- three chives
- one clove of garlic
- summer savory
- marjoram
- bay leaf
- half a bottle of milk
- a handful of bread crumb or dried rusks
- a portion of fresh pork meat
- lard
- lean bacon
- beef sweetbread
- bread crumb
- six raw egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- seasonings
- milk
- meat broth
- aromatic herbs

## Directions

1. Put a little butter in a casserole with a slice of ham, three chives, one clove of garlic, summer savory, marjoram and bay leaf; place the casserole over a low heat and let it take heat for a quarter of an hour
2. Add to it half a bottle of milk and boil it all until reduced to half; pass it through a silk sieve, put it back in the casserole
3. Add to it a handful of bread crumb or dried rusks, let it boil until the crumb is well soaked
4. Cut into slices a portion of fresh pork meat, lard, lean bacon and beef sweetbread; mix all this with bread crumb, six raw egg yolks, salt, pepper and more seasonings
5. With this mixture fill the tripe, previously well washed and drained, and tie the other end
6. When you wish to eat them, cook them in an equal portion of milk and meat broth, add to them a bunch of aromatic herbs; proceed as already explained
