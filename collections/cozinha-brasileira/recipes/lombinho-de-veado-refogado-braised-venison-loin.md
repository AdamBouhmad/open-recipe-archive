## Ingredients

- two venison loins
- salt pork
- vinegar
- salt
- onion
- parsley leaves
- bay leaves
- twelve tomatoes
- venison meat pieces
- half a cup of water
- half a bottle of white wine
- two spoonfuls of butter
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- carapicús

## Directions

1. Lard the two venison loins with salt pork and soak them for forty-eight hours in vinegar, salt, onion, parsley and bay leaves
2. Put them in a pan between lard pieces with twelve tomatoes and some venison meat pieces, half a cup of water and half a bottle of white wine
3. Let simmer for two to three hours and remove the meat
4. In another place, melt two spoonfuls of butter with one spoonful of wheat flour, adding the strained meat broth and some boiled carapicús
5. Let simmer until the broth is reduced to three or four cups
6. Pour this gravy over the loins and serve
