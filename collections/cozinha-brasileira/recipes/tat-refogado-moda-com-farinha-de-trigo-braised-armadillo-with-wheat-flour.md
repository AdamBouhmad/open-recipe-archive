## Ingredients

- one armadillo
- soap and sour orange or sour lemon for washing
- fat
- one whole onion
- one head of garlic
- peppers from India
- half a bottle of white wine
- a quarter pound of wheat flour
- one glass of white wine

## Directions

1. After removing the shell and odor from the armadillo, wash it with soap and sour orange or sour lemon
2. Wash again in cold water and cut into pieces
3. Braise in fat
4. Add one whole onion, one head of garlic, peppers from India, and half a bottle of white wine
5. Cover as soon as it begins to boil
6. When cooked, dissolve a quarter pound of wheat flour in one glass of white wine and pour over the armadillo, stirring a little to bind, and serve with its broth
