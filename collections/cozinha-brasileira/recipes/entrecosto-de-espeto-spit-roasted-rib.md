## Ingredients

- piece of well-meaty rib
- salt
- ground pepper
- dried bread crumbs, ground

## Directions

1. Take a well-meaty piece of rib, spread it with salt and ground pepper, and roast it on a spit with its own sauce.
2. Once roasted, cover the meaty part with ground dried bread crumbs, let it cook a bit more, and serve.
