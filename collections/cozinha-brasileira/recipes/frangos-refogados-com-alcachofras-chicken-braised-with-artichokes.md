## Ingredients

- two chickens, cut into pieces
- lard or fat for frying
- one spoon of wheat flour
- one bottle of water
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- one onion
- artichoke bottoms
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Fry the chicken pieces in fat; when browned on both sides, remove them
2. Add one spoon of wheat flour to the fat; stir and add one bottle of water
3. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg
4. Add the sliced onion and artichoke bottoms along with the fried chicken pieces
5. Simmer near the fire until everything is cooked
6. Thicken the sauce with two egg yolks and serve
