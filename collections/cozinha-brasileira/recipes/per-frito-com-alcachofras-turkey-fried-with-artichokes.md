## Ingredients

- one turkey
- salt
- chopped parsley
- onion
- garlic
- comari pepper
- three tablespoons of fat

## Directions

1. Cut the turkey into pieces and soak with salt, chopped parsley, onion, garlic and comari pepper for four to six hours
2. Place three tablespoons of fat on the heat and when well hot add the turkey pieces
3. Fry on both sides and place on a plate, garnishing them around the bottom with artichokes, scattering chopped parsley over all and pouring hot fat on top
