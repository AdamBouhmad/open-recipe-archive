## Ingredients

- venison loin
- four tablespoons of fat
- two tablespoons of wheat flour
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- one bottle of water
- salt
- some pimento peppers
- parsley
- one onion
- nutmeg
- white beans or guandús

## Directions

1. Braise the loin in four tablespoons of fat
2. After cooking, dust it with two tablespoons of wheat flour
3. Add one tablespoon of vinegar, one bottle of water, salt, some pimento peppers, parsley, one onion cut up, grated nutmeg, and a portion of white beans or guandús that have been soaked in cold water for two hours
4. Let everything boil for two to three hours over moderate heat and serve
