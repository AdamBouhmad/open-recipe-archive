## Ingredients

- veal quarter
- vinegar
- salt
- saltpetre
- garlic
- pepper
- onion
- bundle of aromatic herbs

## Directions

1. Take the veal quarter, soak it in vinegar with salt, a little saltpetre, garlic, pepper, onion, a bundle of aromatic herbs
2. Remove the film, lard it and roast on the spit
