## Ingredients

- lamb leg meat
- one pound of ham
- half pound of bacon
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- onion
- parsley
- garlic
- breadcrumbs
- four egg yolks
- cloth
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Remove all the meat from the leg and chop it with one pound of ham, half pound of bacon, salt, pepper, clove, onion, parsley and garlic (all finely chopped), a handful of breadcrumbs and four egg yolks
2. Place this mixture around the bone on a cloth that is sewn around it, giving it the shape of a leg
3. Put it to cook in a pan in already boiling water
4. When cooked, remove the cloth and fry the meat in a pan with a little butter
5. Keep the pan on coals until the meat takes color, being careful when it sticks not to break its shape
6. Serve on toasted breadcrumbs fried in butter, placing on top its broth after being reduced and strained
