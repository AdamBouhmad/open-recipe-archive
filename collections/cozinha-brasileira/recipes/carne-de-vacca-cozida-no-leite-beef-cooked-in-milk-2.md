## Ingredients

- washed beef
- milk
- salt
- sugar
- nutmeg
- bay leaves
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Take the washed beef and place it on the fire with milk already boiled with salt.
2. When cooled, add to it a little sugar, nutmeg, little salt, and bay leaves.
3. Let it boil over moderate fire for three to four hours and serve with its own sauce, thickened with wheat flour.
