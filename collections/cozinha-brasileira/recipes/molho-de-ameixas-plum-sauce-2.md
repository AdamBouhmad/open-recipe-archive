## Ingredients

- a portion of plums
- one spoon of cornstarch
- cinnamon
- lemon rind

## Directions

1. Pass a portion of plums through a sieve and remove the pits
2. Add one spoon of cornstarch, cinnamon and lemon rind
3. Leave to boil one hour with little water
