## Ingredients

- butter
- fine salt
- saltpeter
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Take a portion of butter and wash several times in cold water, kneading with a wooden spoon until the water comes out clear
2. Squeeze the butter so it has no moisture
3. Mix four parts fine salt, one of ground saltpeter and one of white sugar
4. Take one ounce of the mixture for each pound of butter, with which it is well incorporated and placed in pieces in a pot, pressing well so it contains no air
5. By this process it keeps fresh and good-tasting for a long time
