## Ingredients

- gordura
- small onions
- rabbit
- red wine
- parsley
- salt
- peppers
- bay leaf
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Fry in three spoonfuls of fat a dozen small whole onions
2. When browned, add a rabbit cut into pieces
3. Stir, adding one bottle of red wine, parsley, salt, peppers, bay leaf, and nutmeg
4. Leave to cook near the fire and serve
