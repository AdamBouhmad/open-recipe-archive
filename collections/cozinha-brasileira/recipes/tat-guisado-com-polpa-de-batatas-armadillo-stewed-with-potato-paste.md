## Ingredients

- one armadillo, cut into pieces
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- peppers with cumin
- cloves
- wheat flour
- three to four spoonfuls of fat
- potatoes, grated
- two egg yolks
- sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut an armadillo into pieces and boil in water with salt, parsley, onion, peppers with cumin, and cloves
2. When cooked, remove the meat, pass it through wheat flour, and braise in three to four spoonfuls of fat
3. When fried, remove it and braise a portion of grated potatoes in the same fat
4. Add the broth reduced to half, boil, stirring until nearly dry
5. Add two egg yolks dissolved in one glass of sour orange juice, place the meat in this paste, heat, and serve together
