## Ingredients

- fat
- beef, cut into small pieces
- onion
- carrot
- clove
- wheat flour or corn meal
- water
- parsley

## Directions

1. Place in a casserole a little fat with beef cut into small pieces, onion, carrot, clove, and fry over coals for half an hour; then add a little wheat flour or corn meal, add water, a little parsley, and let it boil for four hours; then strain through a fine sieve and serve with meat.
