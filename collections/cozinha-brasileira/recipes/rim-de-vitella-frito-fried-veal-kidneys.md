## Ingredients

- veal kidneys
- water
- butter
- onion, chopped
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and blanch the kidneys, leaving them in water until they cool
2. Cut them into rounds and fry in butter with a chopped onion, a little salt and pepper
