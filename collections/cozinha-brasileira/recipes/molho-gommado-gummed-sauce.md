## Ingredients

- two spoons of cornstarch
- four spoons of water or beef broth
- a little nutmeg
- some parsley leaves
- onion
- one spoon of fat
- one clove of garlic
- a little sugar
- a little vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put two spoons of cornstarch in a pot, dissolved in four spoons of water or beef broth
2. Add a little nutmeg, some parsley leaves, onion, one spoon of fat, and one clove of garlic
3. Leave to boil, stirring until it is cooked
4. Add a little sugar, a little vinegar or lemon juice
5. Serve
