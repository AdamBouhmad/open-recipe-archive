## Ingredients

- 1 spoon of starch
- 1 spoon of fat
- salt
- pepper
- half a bottle of milk, already cooked

## Directions

1. Fry the starch in a spoon of fat
2. Add salt, pepper, and little by little half a bottle of already cooked milk
3. Let it boil for a little while and serve
