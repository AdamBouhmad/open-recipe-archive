## Ingredients

- one lizard, cut into pieces
- two spoons of fat
- one chopped onion
- wheat flour
- peas

## Directions

1. Cut the lizard into pieces and stew in two spoons of fat with a chopped onion
2. Sprinkle with wheat flour and add peas
