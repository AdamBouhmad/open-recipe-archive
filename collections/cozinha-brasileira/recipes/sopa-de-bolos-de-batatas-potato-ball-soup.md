## Ingredients

- potatoes
- 3 or 4 egg yolks
- a little butter
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Cook, peel, and grate some small potatoes
2. Add to the potato mixture 3 or 4 egg yolks and a little butter
3. Form small balls
4. Cook the balls in meat broth a short time before serving at table
