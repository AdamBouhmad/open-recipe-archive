## Ingredients

- chickens
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- palm heart
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the chickens into four pieces and dust with salt and pepper
2. Sauté in butter, coat the pieces in breadcrumbs and finish frying in butter
3. In another part, boil a palm heart cut into pieces in water and salt
4. Arrange on a plate covered with a layer of breadcrumbs
5. Place the fried chicken pieces on top
6. Pour over all the sauce in which the chickens were fried and serve
7. Can be served in the same manner with asparagus or squash shoots
