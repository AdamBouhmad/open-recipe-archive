## Ingredients

- fish
- water
- salt
- cassava flour
- hot fat
- wine
- onions
- garlic
- parsley
- bay leaves
- clove
- pepper

## Directions

1. After removing the skin, boil the fish in water and salt
2. Wrap it in cassava flour and pass it through hot fat
3. Add one or two bottles of wine according to the size of the fish, the fish should remain covered
4. Add one or two chopped onions, one garlic clove, parsley, bay leaves, clove, and plenty of pepper
5. Let it boil for half an hour, remove the fish and serve it with its sauce strained and reduced
