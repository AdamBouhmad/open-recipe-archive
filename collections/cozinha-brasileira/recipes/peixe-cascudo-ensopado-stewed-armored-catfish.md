## Ingredients

- four to five armored catfish
- one bottle of water
- one glass of brandy
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- parsley
- celery root
- dozen tomatoes
- one tablespoon of wheat flour

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from four to five catfish
2. Boil one bottle of water with one glass of brandy, salt, pepper, clove, parsley and celery root
3. Add the fish and let boil for a quarter hour
4. Remove the fish and fry a dozen tomatoes with one tablespoon of wheat flour
5. Add the strained fish broth, stir and reduce the broth
6. Pour the sauce over the fish, keep warm near the fire and serve
