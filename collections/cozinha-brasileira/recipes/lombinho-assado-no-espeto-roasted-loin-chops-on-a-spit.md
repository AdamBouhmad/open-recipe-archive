## Ingredients

- loin chops
- sweet oil
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- bay leaf
- onions
- butter
- meat juice
- vinegar
- chives
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Trim and cut the loin chops, but not too thin so they resist the fire
2. Place these slices in a marinade of sweet oil, pepper, salt, parsley, bay leaf and onions for twelve hours
3. Thread them onto a spit, covering the skin part with paper oiled with butter
4. Roast over a brisk fire so the heat penetrates them
5. Remove the paper several moments before serving
6. Remove them from the spit while still rare
7. Serve with a sauce made of meat juice, vinegar, chives, salt and pepper, or a tomato sauce
