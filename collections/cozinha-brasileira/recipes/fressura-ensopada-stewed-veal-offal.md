## Ingredients

- butter
- two onions
- veal offal, cleaned
- one glass wine
- one glass water
- salt
- onion
- cloves
- nutmeg
- lemon juice
- one tablespoon sugar
- ora-pro-nobis leaves

## Directions

1. Fry a little butter with two sliced onions and cleaned veal offal
2. Scald the offal and cut it
3. After fifteen minutes, add one glass wine, one glass water, salt, onion, cloves, nutmeg, lemon juice, one tablespoon sugar, and ora-pro-nobis leaves
4. Let boil over coals for two hours
5. Serve
