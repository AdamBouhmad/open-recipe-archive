## Ingredients

- Pork loin
- Fat
- Water
- Salt
- Garlic
- Cayenne pepper
- Comarim peppers
- Parsley
- Onions
- Tomatoes
- Cornmeal mush

## Directions

1. Cut the loin into pieces
2. Braise these in a little fat
3. Add a little water, salt, garlic, cayenne pepper, comarim peppers, parsley, onions and tomatoes, all finely chopped
4. Allow to cook until reduced to little broth
5. Serve with cornmeal mush
