## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- cloves
- vinegar
- brandy

## Directions

1. Chop a portion of fine cabbage
2. Scald in hot water and squeeze well
3. Place in a barrel or pan with plenty of salt and some cloves
4. Fill the pan with hot vinegar to which a quarter part of brandy is added
5. Cover the vessel and keep stored
