## Ingredients

- veal breast
- bacon
- veal meat
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- nutmeg
- wheat flour
- two egg yolks
- bacon strips
- water
- white wine
- carrots
- onion
- garlic clove
- lemon juice
- peas or green beans

## Directions

1. Take the veal breast and split it in half
2. Fill it with a stuffing made of bacon, veal meat, salt, parsley, pepper, nutmeg, a little wheat flour and two egg yolks
3. Close the opening with stitches
4. Place it in the oven, after wrapping it in bacon strips
5. Add a glass of water mixed with another of white wine, carrots, salt, parsley, pepper, onion, and a garlic clove
6. Once cooked, serve with its sauce, after adding lemon juice; it can also be served with fresh peas or green beans
