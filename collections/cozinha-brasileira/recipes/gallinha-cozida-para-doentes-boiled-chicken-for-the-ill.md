## Ingredients

- one chicken, not more than eight months old and not yet brooded
- water
- salt
- celery root
- parsley root
- two lettuce leaves
- one chicory leaf

## Directions

1. Take a chicken not more than eight months old and that has not yet brooded
2. Kill it with a blow to the neck
3. After ten minutes, before it cools, place it in a pan with boiling water to scald it
4. Pluck the feathers and singe it over a straw fire
5. Open it and wash it thoroughly with hot water
6. Cut it into pieces, separating the legs, wings, neck, breast, etc.
7. Cook it with cold water, little salt, a piece of celery root, a parsley root, two lettuce leaves and one chicory leaf
8. When cooked, serve the broth after removing the fat, according to the doctor's recommendation, either plain, or over toasted bread, or with rice cooked in water and salt, without fat, etc.
