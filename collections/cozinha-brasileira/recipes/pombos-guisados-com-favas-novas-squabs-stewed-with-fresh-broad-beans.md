## Ingredients

- three squabs
- a portion of fresh broad beans
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves

## Directions

1. Place three squabs to boil with a portion of fresh broad beans in water, salt, parsley and onion leaves
2. When cooked, remove the squabs, cut them in half and fry them
