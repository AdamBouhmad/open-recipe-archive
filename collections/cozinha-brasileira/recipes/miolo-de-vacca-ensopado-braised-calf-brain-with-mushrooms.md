## Ingredients

- calf brain
- water
- salt
- mushrooms
- butter
- small onions
- wheat flour
- mushroom broth

## Directions

1. Boil the brain in water and salt.
2. Cook the mushrooms separately, cut into pieces in little water and one spoon of butter.
3. Then fry in butter a dozen small onions until they are golden.
4. Sprinkle over them one spoon of wheat flour, moistening them with one glass of the mushroom broth.
5. Add the brain cut into slices, leave it to boil for half an hour over coals, and serve.
