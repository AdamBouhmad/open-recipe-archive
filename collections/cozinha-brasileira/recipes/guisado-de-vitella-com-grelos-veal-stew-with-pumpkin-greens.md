## Ingredients

- roasted rump or loin, cut into small pieces
- wheat flour
- butter
- sugar
- vinegar
- meat broth
- nutmeg
- onion leaves
- pumpkin greens, blanched in salted water
- brazas

## Directions

1. Fry two spoonfuls of wheat flour in a spoonful of butter until it begins to brown
2. Add a spoonful of sugar and another of vinegar, then three cups of meat broth, nutmeg, and onion leaves
3. Let boil a little, then add two or three handfuls of pumpkin greens blanched in salted water and the meat
4. Let everything boil over hot coals for half an hour and serve
