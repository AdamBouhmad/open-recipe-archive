## Ingredients

- eggs
- parsley
- onion
- salt
- Indian pepper
- garlic
- cornmeal
- borage leaf
- fat
- onion sauce
- Comarí peppers

## Directions

1. Beat a portion of whole eggs well, adding parsley, well-chopped onion, salt, well-ground Indian pepper and garlic; after beating well, add cornmeal until forming a half-hard paste
2. Take one spoon of this paste and place in a frying pan, using spoons to finish the paste and removing the eggs from the pan as they fry; place these eggs on a plate and serve with a sauce made of onions, chopped parsley and Comarí peppers
3. If desired, add a blanched borage leaf to the paste, and thus prepared, place it to fry in very hot fat, continuing until the paste is finished, and serve with the above sauce
4. In the same manner, cooked pieces of chicken or hen can be prepared, already cooked, cleaned and with bones removed, artichoke leaves, heart of palm, fern, asparagus sprouts, etc.
