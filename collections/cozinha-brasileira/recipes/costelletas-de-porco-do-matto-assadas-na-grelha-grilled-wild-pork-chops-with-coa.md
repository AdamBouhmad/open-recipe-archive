## Ingredients

- pork chops with loin attached, one finger thick
- salt
- pepper
- mustard powder
- breadcrumbs
- melted butter
- melted butter mixed with one glass white wine and mustard powder

## Directions

1. Separate each chop with its piece of loin, one finger thick.
2. Beat it and trim it.
3. Marinate in salt, pepper and mustard powder.
4. Wrap in breadcrumbs and pass in melted butter.
5. Grill on both sides and serve with melted butter mixed with one glass of white wine and mustard powder.
