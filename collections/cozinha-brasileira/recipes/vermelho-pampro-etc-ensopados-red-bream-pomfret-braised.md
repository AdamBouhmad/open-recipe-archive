## Ingredients

- red bream, pomfret or similar fish
- water
- salt
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- tomatoes
- onion
- pepper
- one spoon of manioc flour
- one glass of wine
- fish liver

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well and boil in water and salt
2. Remove the skin and coat in breadcrumbs
3. Fry in butter on both sides
4. Place on a plate near the fire to keep warm
5. In the butter, add a portion of tomatoes, a cut onion, salt, pepper and one spoon of manioc flour
6. Stir and add one glass of wine in which the fish liver has been dissolved
7. Boil a little and serve the sauce and fish separately
