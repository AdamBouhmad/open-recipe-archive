## Ingredients

- two tablespoons butter
- two tablespoons cornmeal
- pinch of cornstarch
- vinegar
- one bowl broth
- parsley
- onion leaves
- salt
- nutmeg
- roasted lamb or kid

## Directions

1. Melt two tablespoons butter
2. Add two tablespoons cornmeal, a pinch of cornstarch, vinegar, one bowl broth, parsley, onion leaves, salt, and nutmeg
3. Boil a little and strain through a sieve
4. Add the roasted lamb or kid, cut into slices
5. When hot, serve
