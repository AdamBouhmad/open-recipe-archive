## Ingredients

- oysters
- eggs
- manioc starch
- butter
- fat
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. After boiling the oysters in their own water, dry them one by one with a towel
2. Beat two eggs with half a tablespoon of manioc starch and one tablespoon of melted butter
3. Pass the oysters in this batter, fry in fat and serve sprinkled with pepper and chopped parsley
