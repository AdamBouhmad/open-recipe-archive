## Ingredients

- three pigeons
- a portion of new broad beans
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- two tablespoons of fat
- breadcrumbs
- two tablespoons of wheat flour
- one chopped onion

## Directions

1. Put three pigeons to boil with a portion of new broad beans in water, salt, parsley and onion leaves
2. When cooked, remove the pigeons and cut them in half
3. Fry them on both sides in two tablespoons of fat until they take on good color
4. Coat them in breadcrumbs and fry them again
5. In the same butter, fry two tablespoons of wheat flour and one chopped onion until the flour is toasted
6. Mix in the broad beans and let everything boil until the broth from the beans is reduced
7. Serve together with the pigeons
