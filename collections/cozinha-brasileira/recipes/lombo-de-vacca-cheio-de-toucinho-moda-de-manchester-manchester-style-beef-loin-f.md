## Ingredients

- beef loin
- slices of bacon
- sweet oil
- onion
- parsley sprigs
- bay leaves
- broken Indian pepper
- paper
- small potatoes
- olives or tubaras

## Directions

1. Take a beef loin which is pierced with slices of bacon, placing it on a platter with a glass of sweet oil, an onion cut into rings, some sprigs of parsley, some bay leaves and broken Indian pepper. Turn it many times.
2. Two hours later, place it to drain and after being covered with a sheet of paper greased with sweet oil, place it on the spit. Go to the fire, always coating it with that sauce in which it was soaked.
3. Three quarters of an hour later, remove the paper, and five minutes before going to table, pour over it the substance from the same beef, placing it then on a platter garnished with small potatoes, olives or tubaras.
