## Ingredients

- one capon
- water
- salt
- pepper
- onion leaves
- marjoram
- fern fronds
- butter
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the capon into pieces and cook in water with salt, pepper, onion leaves, and marjoram
2. When cooked, remove and boil the fern fronds tied in bundles in this broth
3. Fry the capon pieces coated in breadcrumbs in butter
4. Remove the fern fronds, untie them, and place on a plate covered with breadcrumbs
5. Put the fried capon pieces on top and pour over all the butter in which the capon pieces were fried and serve
