## Ingredients

- mutton tongues, cooked and peeled
- melted fat
- two spoons of fubá mimoso
- water squash cut in cubes
- bowl of water or meat broth
- salt
- parsley
- celery root
- one onion

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked, peeled tongues into cubes and fry in melted fat with two spoons of fubá mimoso until the fubá begins to brown.
2. Add a portion of water squash also cut in cubes.
3. Stir and when taking on heat, add a bowl of water or meat broth, salt, parsley, celery root, and one sliced onion.
4. Let boil for one hour over moderate heat.
5. Serve.
