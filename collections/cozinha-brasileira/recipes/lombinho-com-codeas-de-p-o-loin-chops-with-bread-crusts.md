## Ingredients

- leftover roasted loin chop
- bread crusts
- butter
- parsley
- capers
- pickles

## Directions

1. Cut into slices the leftovers of roasted loin chop that remained
2. Heat within the juice without boiling
3. Make bread crusts of the same size
4. Fry them in butter until they take color
5. Place on a plate layers of crusts and loin alternately
6. Pour over them the loin juice or melted butter with parsley and some capers or pickles
