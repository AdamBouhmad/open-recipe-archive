## Ingredients

- mushrooms (carapicús)
- wine
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- fat

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of selected and cleaned mushrooms (carapicús)
2. Give them some boils in a little wine and vinegar, adding salt and pepper
3. When cooked, remove and drain them, placing them in a pan containing hot fat
