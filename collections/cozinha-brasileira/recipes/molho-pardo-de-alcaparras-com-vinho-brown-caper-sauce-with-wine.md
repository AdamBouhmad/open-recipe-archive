## Ingredients

- corn meal or wheat flour
- red wine diluted with an equal amount of water
- chopped onions
- bay leaves
- a sprig of parsley
- marjoram
- serpol
- capers
- raisins
- lemon rinds
- Indian cloves
- salt
- sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Make as brown caper sauce, but boil the ingredients in red wine diluted with an equal amount of water instead of beef broth
