## Ingredients

- one pork loin
- parsley
- onions
- bay leaves
- one pound chopped bacon
- one bottle red wine
- orange wine
- one dozen eggplants

## Directions

1. Let one pork loin marinate for six hours in parsley, onions, bay leaves.
2. Place it in a pan with one pound of chopped bacon, one bottle of red wine, or if not good quality, orange wine, and one dozen eggplants.
3. Cover the pan and boil over moderate heat.
