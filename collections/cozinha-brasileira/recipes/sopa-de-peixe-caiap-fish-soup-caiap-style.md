## Ingredients

- beef
- roots
- parsley
- carrots
- celery
- small potatoes
- apples
- fish portions
- wheat flour
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil beef with plenty of roots, parsley, carrots, celery, small potatoes and some apples cut in pieces
2. When almost cooked, add fish portions and let them cook more
3. Strain through a sieve and thicken the broth with wheat flour boiled in vinegar
