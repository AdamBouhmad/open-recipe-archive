## Ingredients

- radishes
- fine salt
- pimento da India in fine powder
- five to six tablespoons strong vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut a portion of radishes into fine slices without peeling them
2. Place in a salad bowl
3. Season with fine salt, pimento da India in fine powder and five to six tablespoons of strong vinegar
4. Serve
