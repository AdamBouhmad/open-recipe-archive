## Ingredients

- turkey
- fine bacon
- salt
- parsley juice
- onion
- paper
- butter
- wine

## Directions

1. Take the turkey, lard it with fine bacon, rub it with salt, parsley juice and onion.
2. Wrap it in paper and roast it on the spit, basting it from time to time with a sauce made of two tablespoons of butter and a cup of wine.
3. When almost cooked, remove the paper, allow it to brown, and serve.
