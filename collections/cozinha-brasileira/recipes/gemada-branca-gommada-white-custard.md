## Ingredients

- one bottle of milk
- a little salt
- four spoonfuls of sugar
- some pieces of cinnamon
- cloves of India
- four ounces of crumb of bread
- one spoonful of cornstarch
- six egg whites

## Directions

1. Boil one bottle of milk with a little salt, four spoonfuls of sugar, some pieces of cinnamon, and cloves of India
2. Reduce to half
3. Add four ounces of crumb of bread and boil until the bread dissolves
4. Strain through a sieve
5. Add one spoonful of cornstarch beaten with six egg whites, mixing the white into the milk little by little
6. Boil again two to three times
7. Place in a bowl and let cool
8. Turn this mass onto a plate so that it comes out whole
9. Cover lightly with sugar and cinnamon powder
10. Serve
