## Ingredients

- three pounds of pork liver
- two pounds of lard
- half pound of lard
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- nutmeg
- cilantro
- serpol
- bay leaf
- chives
- sage
- caul or thin lard slices
- gelatin

## Directions

1. Mince three pounds of pork liver, two pounds of lard, and half pound of lard together
2. Season with salt, pepper, cloves, nutmeg, cilantro, serpol, bay leaf, finely chopped chives, and sage
3. Line a casserole with caul or very thin lard slices so the liver does not stick
4. Place a layer of filling at the bottom, then seasoned lard slices, then another layer of filling, alternating until the casserole is full
5. The last layer should be lard slices
6. Cook in the oven for two hours
7. Let the cheese cool, then remove from the casserole by placing it briefly in boiling water
8. Eat cold, though hot is also very pleasant
