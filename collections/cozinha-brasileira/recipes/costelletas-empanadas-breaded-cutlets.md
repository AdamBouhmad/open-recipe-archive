## Ingredients

- cutlets
- grated bread crusts
- chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- melted fat or butter

## Directions

1. Roll the cutlets in a good layer of grated bread crumbs mixed with chopped parsley, salt and pepper
2. Moisten with melted fat or butter
3. Roast on a grill, adjusting the heat so they do not burn
4. Serve with spicy sauce
