## Ingredients

- Lettuce
- Salt
- Eggs
- Sweet oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Take the leaves of four heads of lettuce, wash them and place them on a plate
2. Sprinkle with salt and place on top two hard-boiled egg yolks, dissolved in two spoonfuls of sweet oil and six spoonfuls of good vinegar
3. Serve
