## Ingredients

- one guinea fowl
- Brazil nuts or small cashew nuts
- three spoonfuls of fat
- one cup of white wine
- one garlic clove
- salt
- parsley
- one onion
- pear
- marjoram
- one orange

## Directions

1. Stuff one bird with Brazil nuts or small cashew nuts with skin removed; sew the opening and braise it in three spoonfuls of fat
2. Add one cup of white wine, one garlic clove, salt, parsley, one onion cut into pieces, pear and a sprig of marjoram
3. Boil over a very lively bed of coals for two hours, and when cooked serve with its strained sauce mixed with one spoonful of orange juice
