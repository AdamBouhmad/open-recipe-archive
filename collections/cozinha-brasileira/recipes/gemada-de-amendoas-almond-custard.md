## Ingredients

- four ounces of peeled almonds
- four spoonfuls of sugar
- eight egg yolks
- a little grated nutmeg
- one bottle of milk

## Directions

1. Pound four ounces of peeled almonds with four spoonfuls of sugar
2. When the paste is homogeneous, mix in eight egg yolks, a little grated nutmeg, and finally one bottle of milk
3. Boil over coals until it thickens
4. Cover with sugar and cinnamon powder
