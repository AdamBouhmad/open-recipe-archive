## Ingredients

- one goose
- water
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- one onion, chopped
- a portion of pigeon peas
- butter
- bread

## Directions

1. Boil the goose, divided into pieces, with water and salt.
2. When half cooked, remove the broth and fry these pieces in their own fat with a little grated nutmeg and one chopped onion.
3. On the other hand, boil a portion of pigeon peas in water and salt, and when cooked, remove them and pass them through a sieve.
4. Braise this pulp in butter, place it on the plate, covering it with bread cut into small pieces and fried in butter.
5. Place the goose on top and pour melted butter over it.
