## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Onions
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Sweet oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of small potatoes, peel them and cut them into thin slices
2. Mix with two onions cut into slices, salt, ground pepper, sweet oil and vinegar
3. Mix everything well
4. Serve with roasts
