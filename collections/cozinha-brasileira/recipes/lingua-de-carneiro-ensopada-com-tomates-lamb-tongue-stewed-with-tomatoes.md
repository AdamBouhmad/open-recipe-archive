## Ingredients

- lamb tongues
- water
- slices of bacon
- salt
- marjoram
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one dozen tomatoes
- cooked rice

## Directions

1. With the tongues peeled, cut them into slices and let them boil with water and some slices of bacon, salt, marjoram, parsley and onion leaves
2. When cooked, remove them from the broth and let this reduce; leaving only two cupfuls, add one dozen tomatoes
3. Give it a boil, mash the tomatoes and put the tongues back in the vessel
4. After taking one boil, serve with cooked rice
