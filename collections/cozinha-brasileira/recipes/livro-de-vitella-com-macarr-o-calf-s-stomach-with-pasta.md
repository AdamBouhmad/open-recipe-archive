## Ingredients

- calf stomach
- water
- salt
- portion of pasta
- grated cheese
- melted butter
- sugar
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Clean the stomach well and cut it into pieces one palm in length
2. Place it to cook in water and salt with a portion of pasta
3. When the stomach and pasta are cooked, drain them
4. Put them in a serving dish and cover with a layer of grated cheese
5. Pour melted butter over them
6. Sprinkle with sugar and ground cinnamon
7. Place in the oven to roast for one hour
8. Serve the calf's stomach this way
