## Ingredients

- calf liver
- wheat flour
- butter
- meat broth
- one glass of wine
- one bay leaf
- slices of lemon
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into slices two fingers thick
2. Lard them, sprinkle with wheat flour, and fry in butter in a casserole
3. Add a little meat broth, a glass of wine, a bay leaf, slices of lemon and salt
4. Let them boil over coals for half an hour and serve
