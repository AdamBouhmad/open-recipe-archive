## Ingredients

- one dorado
- water
- salt
- parsley
- lemon juice
- one spoon of fat
- fish broth
- half a spoon of arrowroot

## Directions

1. Take one dorado, remove the scales and entrails, boil it with water, salt, parsley, a little lemon juice
2. When cooked, melt one spoon of fat, add the fish broth mixed with half a spoon of arrowroot
3. Boil a little, place the fish inside, and after another boil, serve
