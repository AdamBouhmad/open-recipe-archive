## Ingredients

- loins from two hares
- salt
- garlic
- chopped parsley
- thyme (serpol)
- pepper
- bay leaves
- onion leaves
- marjoram
- cumin
- lemon juice
- bacon lard strips
- half bottle brandy
- half bottle white wine
- two pounds chopped veal meat

## Directions

1. Remove the loins from two hares
2. Soak them in salt, garlic, chopped parsley, thyme, pepper, bay leaves, onion leaves, marjoram, cumin, and lemon juice
3. After twenty-four hours, remove the loins, dry them, and lard them with bacon
4. Place them in a pan lined with bacon lard strips with half a bottle of brandy and the same amount of white wine
5. Let everything boil for three to four hours over brisk fire with two pounds of chopped veal meat
6. Strain and reduce the sauce, pour it over the loins
7. Serve cold
