## Ingredients

- agouti
- salt
- peppers
- tomato juice
- bacon lard
- onions, chopped
- parsley
- garlic clove
- half cup water
- one spoon orange juice

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the agouti and clean it thoroughly
2. Rub it with salt, peppers, and tomato juice, leaving it in this seasoning for six hours so the meat becomes well impregnated
3. Place it in a pan over bacon lard with chopped onions, parsley, and one garlic clove
4. Moisten it with half a cup of water and one spoon of orange juice
5. Roast over moderate fire
6. Serve with the defatted gravy
