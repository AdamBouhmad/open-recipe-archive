## Ingredients

- one turkey
- wheat flour
- half a pound of chopped bacon
- salt
- nutmeg
- one onion studded with cloves
- a portion of peeled and scalded squash sprouts
- four cups of meat broth or water
- one egg yolk
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Take the turkey, cut it into pieces, coat them in wheat flour and place in a pan with half a pound of chopped bacon, salt, nutmeg, one onion studded with cloves, a portion of peeled and scalded squash sprouts and four cups of meat broth or water
2. Seal the lid carefully
3. Leave to boil for two hours over coals or near the fire
4. When cooked, place the sprouts on the plate covering them with a thin layer of bread crumbs
5. Place the turkey pieces on top and pour over all the strained and thickened sauce with one egg yolk
6. Serve
