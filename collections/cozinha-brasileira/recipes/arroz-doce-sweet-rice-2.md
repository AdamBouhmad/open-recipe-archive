## Ingredients

- one plate of chosen and washed rice
- four bottles of milk
- half pound of sugar
- salt
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Boil a plate of chosen and washed rice in four bottles of milk with half a pound of sugar, salt, and lemon peel
2. When the rice is cooked and almost dry, add a little flower water
3. Pour into dishes and dust with ground cinnamon
4. Serve as a dessert
