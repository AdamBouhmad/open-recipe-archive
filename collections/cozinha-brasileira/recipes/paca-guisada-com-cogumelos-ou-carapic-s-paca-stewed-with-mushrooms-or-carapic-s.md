## Ingredients

- one paca cut in pieces
- water
- salt
- ginger
- two tablespoons pork fat
- two cups meat broth
- comarí peppers
- one chopped onion
- one clove
- portion of chopped mushrooms
- one teaspoon arrowroot dissolved in two tablespoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil the paca cut in pieces in water with salt and a little ginger.
2. When cooked, remove it and fry in two tablespoons of pork fat.
3. Remove again and add to the fat two cups of meat broth, comarí peppers, one chopped onion, and one clove.
4. Add a portion of chopped mushrooms and one teaspoon of arrowroot dissolved in two tablespoons of lemon juice.
5. Boil everything for a quarter of an hour.
6. Place on a plate, put the paca pieces on top, and serve.
