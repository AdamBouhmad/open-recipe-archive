## Ingredients

- large fish
- butter
- peeled onions
- salt
- pepper
- wheat flour
- water
- lemon juice
- capers

## Directions

1. Clean a fish and place it in a casserole with butter, peeled onions, salt and pepper.
2. Fry the fish, turning it two or three times and remove it.
3. Add one tablespoon of wheat flour to the butter and stir.
4. Add two cups of water, some lemon juice and several capers.
5. Let it boil and serve.
