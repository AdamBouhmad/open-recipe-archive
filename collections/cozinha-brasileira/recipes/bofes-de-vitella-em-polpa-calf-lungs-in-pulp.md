## Ingredients

- calf lungs
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- bread crusts, grated
- sugar
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Wash and drain the lungs
2. Cut them very finely
3. Boil in water, salt and a little vinegar
4. When cooked, add grated bread crusts, a spoonful of sugar, and some lemon slices
5. Serve with cornmeal porridge (angú de fubá de moinho)
