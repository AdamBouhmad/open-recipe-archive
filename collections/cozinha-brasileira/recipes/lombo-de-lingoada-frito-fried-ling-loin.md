## Ingredients

- ling loins
- parsley
- onions
- two tablespoons of melted butter

## Directions

1. Remove the head and tail, remove the dark skin, and take the meat from one side, doing the same from the opposite side
2. If the fish is large, cut the meat into two or three strips
3. After cleaning and drying with a towel, place them in a casserole with chopped parsley and onions
4. Cover completely the fish pieces with this mixture
5. Add two tablespoons of melted butter and having fried a little more, serve hot with this sauce
