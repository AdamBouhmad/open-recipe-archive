## Ingredients

- two large squabs
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- bacon strips
- one cup water
- one onion
- chopped parsley
- one garlic clove
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one cup water
- two dozen okra

## Directions

1. Rub two large squabs with salt and pepper
2. Wrap them in breadcrumbs and then in strips of bacon
3. Place in a pot with one cup of water, sliced onion, chopped parsley and one garlic clove
4. Allow to cook
5. Remove the squabs, strain their broth and remove the fat
6. Add one tablespoon wheat flour and one cup of water
7. Add two dozen okra cut into rounds
8. Allow to cook and reduce
9. Finally pour over the squabs
