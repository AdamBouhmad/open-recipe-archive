## Ingredients

- white beans
- fat
- reduced broth
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of white beans.
2. Once cooked, fry a spoonful of fat in another part.
3. Add the drained beans and stir.
4. Add the reduced broth, salt, parsley and onion leaves.
5. Let it boil a little and serve.
