## Ingredients

- a little butter
- half a spoon of flour
- six egg yolks
- a little nutmeg
- salt
- one spoon of vinegar
- one spoon of water

## Directions

1. Mix a little butter with half a spoon of flour and six egg yolks, a little nutmeg and salt
2. Add one spoon of vinegar and another spoon of water, or as much as is necessary
3. Leave to boil and serve with fish, artichokes, and little potatoes
