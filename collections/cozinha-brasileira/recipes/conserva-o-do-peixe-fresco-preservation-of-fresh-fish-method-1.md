## Ingredients

- fresh fish
- bread crumb
- spirits of wine

## Directions

1. As soon as the fish is caught, soak a portion of bread crumb in spirits of wine and fill the throat and mouth of the fish with it; in this manner the fish is preserved alive in a torpor for fifteen to twenty days; removing the bread from its mouth and placing it in water, it will revive in a few minutes.
