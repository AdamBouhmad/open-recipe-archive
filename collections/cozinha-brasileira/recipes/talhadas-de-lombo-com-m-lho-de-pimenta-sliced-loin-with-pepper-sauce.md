## Ingredients

- Roasted pork loin
- Pepper sauce, reduced
- Bread
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the pork loin into even slices
2. Place them in a casserole in which pepper sauce, well reduced, has been previously prepared
3. Allow to warm in a water bath
4. Serve, placing buttered bread slices around them
