## Ingredients

- beef palate, cooked and cooled
- sweet oil
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- parsley, chopped
- tomato sauce or Robert sauce

## Directions

1. When cooked and cold, immerse in a sauce of sweet oil, salt, pepper, garlic, and a pinch of chopped parsley
2. After two hours, remove and turn several times to ensure it is well soaked
3. Grill and serve with tomato sauce or Robert sauce
