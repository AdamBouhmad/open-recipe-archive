## Ingredients

- one turkey
- one glass of white wine
- two tablespoons of butter

## Directions

1. Take the cleaned turkey and roast it on the spit, moistening it with a glass of white wine mixed with two tablespoons of butter
2. When half cooked, remove it from the spit and place it in a pan with the sauce that dripped
3. Roast it, turning it from time to time
4. When cooked, serve with salads
