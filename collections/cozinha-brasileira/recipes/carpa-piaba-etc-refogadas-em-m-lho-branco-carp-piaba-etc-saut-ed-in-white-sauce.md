## Ingredients

- some onions
- two tablespoons cow butter
- one bottle of white wine
- fish cut in portions
- portion of carapicús
- one spoonful of vinegar
- two tablespoons butter
- two tablespoons wheat flour
- three egg yolks
- one tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Sauté some onions cut in half in two tablespoons of cow butter
2. Add one bottle of white wine, the fish cut in portions, a portion of carapicús, and one spoonful of vinegar
3. Let it boil, and when the fish is cooked, remove it
4. To the broth add two tablespoons of butter and two of wheat flour, three egg yolks beaten in one tablespoon of sugar
5. Let it boil a little more, add the fish and serve
