## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- cloves

## Directions

1. Clean a portion of mushrooms, place them in a terrine over salt, and leave until the next day; after this, squeeze them well, pouring over the mass that remains in the sieve some water to extract more substance.
2. Boil this juice with a little pepper and cloves, skimming from time to time; strain and keep in small glass jars with two cloves and a bit of pepper, so that when desired, one can aromatize some sauce.
