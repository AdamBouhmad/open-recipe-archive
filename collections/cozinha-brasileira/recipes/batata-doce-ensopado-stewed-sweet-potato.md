## Ingredients

- sweet potatoes
- butter
- wheat flour
- white wine
- meat broth
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel a portion of sweet potatoes and cut into pieces
2. Boil briefly in salted water
3. Drain and let drip dry
4. Melt a spoonful of butter with a spoonful of wheat flour, a cup of white wine and another of meat broth, a little salt, and nutmeg
5. Place the potatoes in this sauce and let cook over moderate heat
6. When cooked and the broth reduced, serve
