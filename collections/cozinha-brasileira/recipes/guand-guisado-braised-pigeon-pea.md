## Ingredients

- pigeon peas
- water
- salt
- cornmeal
- fat
- garlic
- parsley
- onion leaves
- sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of pigeon peas in water and salt, drain the water when cooked.
2. Fry a spoonful of cornmeal with two spoonfuls of fat, little salt, a clove of garlic, parsley, onion leaves.
3. Add half a bottle of water, and when the cornmeal is browned, add a glass of sour orange juice.
4. Boil a little and add the pigeon pea in this broth, leaving to boil over coals, serve.
