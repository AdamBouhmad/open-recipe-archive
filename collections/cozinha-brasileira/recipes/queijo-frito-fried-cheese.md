## Ingredients

- cheese, sliced
- egg yolk
- wheat flour
- lard or fat
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut the cheese into slices
2. Dip in egg yolk
3. Wrap in wheat flour
4. Fry in lard on both sides
5. Serve sprinkled with sugar and cinnamon
