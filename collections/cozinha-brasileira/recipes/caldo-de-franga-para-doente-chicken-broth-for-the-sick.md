## Ingredients

- quarter of a chicken (not very fat)
- water
- salt
- a parsley leaf
- a slice of bread

## Directions

1. Cook the quarter of a chicken in a bottle of water with little salt and a parsley leaf
2. After one hour, add a slice of bread
3. Remove from the fire and serve to the sick person
