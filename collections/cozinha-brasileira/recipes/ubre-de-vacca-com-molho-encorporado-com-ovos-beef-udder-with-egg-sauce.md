## Ingredients

- beef udder
- white sauce
- mushrooms
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Cook the udder as explained for the preparation of braised beef heart
2. Let it drain and then cut into pieces
3. Place in a casserole with white sauce, increasing the fire well
4. When adding the sauce, add mushrooms placed in an egg yolk
