## Ingredients

- rabbit
- gordura
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- garlic
- water
- wine
- artichoke bottoms
- starch
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut a rabbit into pieces and pass them in hot fat with one chopped onion, salt, pepper, parsley and a clove of garlic
2. Add a cup of water and another of wine
3. Leave to boil until cooked
4. When cooked, add some artichoke bottoms cooked in water and salt and cut into slices, and a small spoonful of starch dissolved in water and lemon juice
5. Leave to boil and serve
