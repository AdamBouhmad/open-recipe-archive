## Ingredients

- one chicken
- water
- salt
- all necessary seasonings
- half a plate of rice
- a quarter of ham
- a quarter of bacon, chopped
- clove
- pepper
- onion, cut
- a little water
- melted butter
- salad

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken whole in water, salt, and all necessary seasonings.
2. On another side, boil half a plate of rice with a quarter of ham and another of chopped bacon, clove, pepper, salt and cut onion, with a little water, the rice should remain whole after cooking.
3. Fill the chicken with it, and placed on a spit, roast it, moistening it frequently with melted butter, and serve with salad.
