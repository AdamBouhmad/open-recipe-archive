## Ingredients

- quinces
- wheat flour
- two tablespoons
- one egg yolk
- one tablespoon sugar
- one tablespoon butter
- hot water or milk

## Directions

1. Peel some quinces and cut them into slices
2. Remove the center that contains the seeds
3. Make a porridge-like mixture of two tablespoons of wheat flour, one egg yolk, one tablespoon of sugar, one of butter, and the necessary amount of hot water or milk
4. Pass the quince slices in this porridge and then fry them in fat
5. Dust with sugar and cinnamon and serve as a dessert
