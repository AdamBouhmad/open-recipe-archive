## Ingredients

- veal leg
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chervil
- marjoram
- garlic clove
- vinegar
- wheat flour
- bacon slices
- green beans or small roasted potatoes

## Directions

1. Remove the cuts from the leg, lard them, place them in a mixture of salt, pepper, parsley, chervil, marjoram, a crushed garlic clove, a spoon of vinegar, for two hours
2. Then dust with wheat flour and place to roast in the oven, wrapped in very thin bacon slices
3. Once cooked, serve with its own sauce, after removing the fat, garnishing it with green beans or small roasted potatoes
