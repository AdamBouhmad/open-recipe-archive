## Ingredients

- two tablespoons wheat flour
- two tablespoons butter
- one chopped onion
- two cups red wine
- one bottle water
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- one tablespoon terra orange juice

## Directions

1. Fry two tablespoons of wheat flour with two tablespoons of butter until they take color.
2. Add one chopped onion, two cups of red wine, one bottle of water, salt, peppers, parsley, and one tablespoon of terra orange juice.
3. Boil a little.
