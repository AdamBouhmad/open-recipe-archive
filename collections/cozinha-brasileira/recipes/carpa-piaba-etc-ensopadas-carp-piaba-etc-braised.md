## Ingredients

- four ounces of lard
- half a pound of beef
- one or two fish steaks
- one bowl of water
- one cup of red wine
- one onion
- one chalice of vinegar
- one small spoon of arrowroot
- one spoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut four ounces of lard and half a pound of beef into small pieces and place them with the steaks of one or two fish
2. Put them to cook with one bowl of water, one cup of red wine, one chopped onion, one chalice of vinegar
3. Let it boil, and when the fish is cooked, remove it
4. Add to the broth one small spoon of arrowroot and one spoon of sugar
5. When the broth is reduced, pour it over the fish and serve
