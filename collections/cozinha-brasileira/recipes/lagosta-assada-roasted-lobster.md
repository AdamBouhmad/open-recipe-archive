## Ingredients

- portion of lobsters
- water
- salt
- one onion
- parsley
- handful of breadcrumbs
- four egg yolks
- one spoon of butter

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of lobsters in water and salt and let cool
2. When cold, open them, remove the vein from the tail and chop with an onion and parsley
3. Mix with a handful of breadcrumbs, four egg yolks and one spoon of butter
4. Make small cakes with this mixture, fry in butter and serve
