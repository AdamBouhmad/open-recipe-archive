## Ingredients

- beef liver
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into more or less thin slices
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Roast on the grill on both sides
4. Serve by placing one slice on top of another, with a little butter and chopped parsley between the two slices
