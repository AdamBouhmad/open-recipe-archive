## Ingredients

- pork liver
- salt
- pimenta da India
- fennel seeds
- caul fat
- butter
- bread

## Directions

1. Take a pork liver and cut it into three-inch square pieces.
2. Sprinkle them well with salt and pimenta da India, roll each piece in fennel seeds so that each one is entirely covered with this seed.
3. Thread these pieces on a spit after wrapping each one in pieces of caul fat.
4. Twenty minutes are sufficient for them to be roasted.
5. At this point, remove them from the spit, place them on a platter and serve, putting on another plate some slices of bread toasted with butter.
