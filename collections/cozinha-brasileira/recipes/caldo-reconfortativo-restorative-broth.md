## Ingredients

- six large onions
- three carrot roots
- a handful of parsley
- fresh butter
- six ounces of candy sugar
- twenty grains of amber crushed with a piece of bread
- three bottles of water
- one old rooster
- two pounds of beef
- salt
- pepper from India

## Directions

1. Chop the onions, carrot roots, and parsley
2. Sauté them in a pot with a piece of fresh butter
3. When the mixture is ready, add six ounces of candy sugar, twenty grains of crushed amber with a piece of bread, and three bottles of water
4. Boil for an hour, adding water as needed to maintain three bottles of water
5. Separately, kill, pluck, and clean an old rooster, then pound it with its meat and bones in an iron mortar
6. Chop two pounds of beef in the same manner
7. Mix the two meats together, adding salt and pepper from India
8. Place in a pot over active heat, occasionally adding fresh butter so they brown well and do not stick
9. When the meats are browned, gradually add the strained broth from the first preparation, stirring well
10. Let it boil for an hour while adding hot water
11. When complete, strain this broth and take one cup every two hours on the first day, then one good cup morning and evening on following days
12. Usually within eight to ten days, complete recovery is achieved
