## Ingredients

- beef lungs
- salt
- herbs
- wheat flour
- egg yolks
- nutmeg (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the beef lungs and cut them into pieces
2. Cook the pieces in water with salt and herbs
3. Strain the broth
4. Add wheat flour, egg yolks, and if desired, a little nutmeg
