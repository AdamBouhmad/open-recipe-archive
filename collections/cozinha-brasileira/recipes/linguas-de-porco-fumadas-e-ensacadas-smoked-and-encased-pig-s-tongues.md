## Ingredients

- several pig's tongues
- salt
- saltpetre
- marjoram
- bay leaf
- thyme
- juniper berries
- beef intestine casings
- weight for pressing
- water
- wine
- parsley
- onions
- bay leaf
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Scald several pig's tongues to remove their skins.
2. Layer them on top of each other, rubbing each with salt, saltpetre, marjoram, bay leaf, thyme, and juniper berries.
3. Place a weight on top to press them and cover the vessel to exclude air.
4. Leave in a cool place for eight days.
5. Remove the tongues from the brine and allow them to drain.
6. Introduce them into casings, tying both ends securely.
7. Place in smoke.
8. When ready to serve, cook in water, wine, parsley, onions, bay leaf, and marjoram.
9. Allow to cool and serve.
