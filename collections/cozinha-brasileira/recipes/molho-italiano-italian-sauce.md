## Ingredients

- white wine
- a little parsley
- chives
- capers
- salt
- pepper
- one spoon of sweet olive oil
- one clove of garlic
- cloves
- one spoon of butter

## Directions

1. Boil with white wine a little parsley, chives, capers, all very finely minced
2. Add salt, pepper, one spoon of sweet olive oil, and if suitable, one clove of garlic and cloves
3. Leave to boil once more, remove from the fire and mix in one spoon of butter stirring until it melts
4. Serve
