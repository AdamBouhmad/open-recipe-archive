## Ingredients

- two venison shoulders
- some meat
- bacon
- onions
- parsley
- salt
- pimento peppers
- two whole eggs
- portion of bread crumbs
- broth
- butter
- two onions
- parsley
- marjoram
- ginger
- one bottle of wine

## Directions

1. Take the two venison shoulders, remove the bones and some meat that is chopped with an equal amount of bacon, some onions, parsley, salt and pimento peppers, and knead with two whole eggs and a portion of bread crumbs moistened with broth
2. Spread this mixture over the inside of the beaten shoulder and roll it up with the mixture inside
3. Tie the rolls with a string to keep them in this form
4. Braise in hot butter
5. Add two onions, parsley, marjoram, ginger and one bottle of wine, letting everything boil for two hours until well cooked, being careful to keep the pot well covered
6. Place on a plate and cover with the strained and reduced sauce and serve
