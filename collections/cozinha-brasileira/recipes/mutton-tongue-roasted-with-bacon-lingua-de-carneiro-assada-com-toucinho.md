## Ingredients

- one mutton tongue, skin removed
- salt
- ground pepper
- bacon cut in very fine slices

## Directions

1. Rub the tongue with salt and ground pepper.
2. Cut into fine, long slices.
3. Cover these slices on both sides with very fine slices of bacon, tying with thread.
4. Grill and serve.
5. Remove the thread and serve with roasted cassava or cará root.
