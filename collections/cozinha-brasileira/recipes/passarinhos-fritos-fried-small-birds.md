## Ingredients

- small birds
- salt
- peppers
- egg yolks
- wheat flour
- fat
- compote

## Directions

1. Clean the small birds, season with salt and peppers, flatten them with a spatula, coat them in egg yolks, and wrap them in wheat flour
2. Fry them in plenty of fat
3. When cooked, serve with some compote
