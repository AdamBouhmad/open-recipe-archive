## Ingredients

- onion
- butter
- mutton feet, cleaned and scalded
- salt
- pepper
- marjoram
- mustard sauce
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut an onion into slices and fry in butter until beginning to brown
2. Add the cleaned and scalded mutton feet with salt, pepper and marjoram
3. Stir until cooked, and serve with mustard sauce diluted in lemon juice
