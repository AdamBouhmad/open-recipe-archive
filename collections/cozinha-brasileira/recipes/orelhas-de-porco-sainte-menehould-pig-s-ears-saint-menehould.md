## Ingredients

- pig's ears
- meat broth
- red wine
- carrots
- onions
- cloves
- bundle of aromatic herbs
- pepper
- salt
- warm butter
- bread crumb
- egg yolk
- piquant sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the pig's ears in meat broth with red wine, carrots, onions with cloves inserted into one of them, add a bundle of aromatic herbs, broken pepper and salt.
2. Once cooked and cold, brush with a little warm butter, place bread crumb on top.
3. Cover with a beaten egg yolk, put another layer of bread crumb on top, and let it brown in the oven.
4. Serve with a piquant sauce.
