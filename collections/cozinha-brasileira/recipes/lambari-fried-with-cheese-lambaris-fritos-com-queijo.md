## Ingredients

- Lambaris
- Salt
- Pepper
- Grated cheese
- Fat

## Directions

1. Fry a portion of lambaris
2. Place on a plate
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Cover with a layer of grated cheese
5. Pour fat on top
6. Place the plate in the oven until it takes color
7. Serve
