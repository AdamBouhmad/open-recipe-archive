## Ingredients

- Lamb squares
- Cold water
- Salt
- Parsley
- Onion leaves
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Minas cornmeal mush

## Directions

1. Remove excess fat from the lamb squares
2. Roast them a little on the spit, turning several times
3. Remove and cook with cold water, salt, parsley, and onion leaves
4. When nearly cooked, add a spoonful of vinegar
5. If the sauce becomes too acidic, add a spoonful of sugar
6. After boiling a little more, serve with Minas cornmeal mush
