## Ingredients

- corn bagasse
- sugar
- salt
- banana leaves

## Directions

1. Take the bagasse that remained after straining the corn milk
2. Mix each pound with a quarter pound of sugar and a little salt
3. Wrap a piece of the mixture in banana leaf
4. Bake in the oven and serve
