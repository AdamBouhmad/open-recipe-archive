## Ingredients

- one fat bird (arara, papagaio, etc.)
- butter
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- one well-chopped onion
- one cup of white wine
- one spoonful of vinegar
- one spoonful of sugar
- salt
- nutmeg
- cloves
- cinnamon
- two egg yolks
- one glass of cognac

## Directions

1. Take one fat bird and sauté it in butter.
2. Cut it into pieces.
3. In the same butter, brown one spoonful of wheat flour and one well-chopped onion.
4. Add the pieces and one cup of white wine, one spoonful of vinegar, one spoonful of sugar, salt, nutmeg, cloves, and cinnamon.
5. Let cook over low heat.
6. Near the end, mix two egg yolks with one glass of cognac and add to the pan.
7. Serve.
