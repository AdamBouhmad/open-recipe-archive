## Ingredients

- peas
- cauliflower
- carrots
- scallions
- young cucumbers
- chuchús
- palmetto
- corn ear tips
- guandú
- mangalos
- asparagus tips
- samambaia
- beans
- mustard seeds
- peppers of all kinds
- salt
- vinegar
- cloves
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Place in a pan a portion of peas, cauliflower, sliced carrots, scallions, young cucumbers, chuchús, chopped palmetto, tips of green corn ears, guandú, mangalos, asparagus tips, samambaia, beans, mustard seeds, peppers of all kinds, salt, vinegar, cloves, nutmeg
2. Boil for a quarter hour, remove from heat and let cool
3. The next day, heat again and while still hot, place in wide-mouthed jars, the vegetables should be covered with vinegar and covered, keep stored
