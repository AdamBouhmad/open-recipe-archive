## Ingredients

- fish, cut into steaks
- salt
- peppers
- fat or sweet oil
- bay leaves
- cloves
- garlic clove
- sweet oil
- cebola picada (chopped onion)
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into steaks and sprinkle with salt and peppers
2. Coat with fat or sweet oil and roast on a grill
3. Before fully cooked, place the pieces in a pan with bay leaves, salt, peppers, cloves and a garlic clove
4. Pour sweet oil over until the steaks are covered
5. Save for later use
6. To make the salad: remove some steaks, cut into small pieces, mix with sweet oil, chopped onion and vinegar
7. Serve
8. Alternatively, remove steaks from the pan, let drain, sprinkle with parsley, chopped onion and cassava flour, fry in butter and serve with orange juice on top
