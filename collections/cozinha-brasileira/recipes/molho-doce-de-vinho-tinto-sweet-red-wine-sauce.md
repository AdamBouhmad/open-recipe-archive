## Ingredients

- Red or white wine
- Bay leaves
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Sugar
- Cornstarch

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of red or white wine with some bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon and sugar
2. While boiling, add a spoonful of cornstarch dissolved in a cup of wine
3. Let it boil once more
4. Serve with pastas and puddings, or when cold with salami
