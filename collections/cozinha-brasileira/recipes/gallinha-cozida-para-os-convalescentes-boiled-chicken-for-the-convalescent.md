## Ingredients

- half a chicken
- fat
- water
- salt
- one bay leaf
- parsley root
- celery root
- one onion leaf
- half a pound of beef

## Directions

1. Take half a chicken and cut it into pieces
2. Sauté these pieces in a little fat
3. Let them fry for ten minutes
4. Add water, salt, one bay leaf, parsley root, celery root, one onion leaf and half a pound of beef
5. Simmer until everything is well cooked
6. Strain the broth and pour it over slices of bread toasted on the grill, or serve with pasta or vermicelli well cooked, or with rice, mixing the chicken pieces into it
