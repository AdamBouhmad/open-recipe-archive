## Ingredients

- one wild boar leg
- two cups of meat broth
- fat
- salt
- pepper
- slices of peeled lemon
- pine nuts
- roasted peanuts
- white wine
- cow butter

## Directions

1. Cut the leg into slices and brown them a little on the grill on both sides
2. Place in a casserole with two cups of meat broth, a little fat, salt, pepper, and slices of peeled lemon
3. Let boil until cooked
4. Meanwhile, boil pine nuts and when cooked, peel and crush them to form a mass
5. Add roasted and crushed peanuts to this mass, salt, white wine, and cow butter as needed to form a polpa not too thick
6. Place the polpa over the plate with the leg slices
7. Pour the meat sauce over it and serve
