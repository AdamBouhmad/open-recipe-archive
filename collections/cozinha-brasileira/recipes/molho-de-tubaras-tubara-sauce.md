## Ingredients

- a little sweet olive oil
- some tubaras
- mushrooms
- half a clove of garlic
- parsley
- a portion of beef broth
- a chalice of white wine
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Heat a little sweet olive oil over coals
2. Add some tubaras, mushrooms, half a clove of garlic, and parsley
3. Add a portion of beef broth and a chalice of white wine, salt, and pepper
4. Bring to cook, taking care to remove the fat that floats on the sauce
5. Serve
