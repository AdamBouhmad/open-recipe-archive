## Ingredients

- radish root
- one spoon of tapioca starch
- one spoon of vinegar
- a little salt
- sugar
- six cups of water

## Directions

1. Grate the radish root
2. Add one spoon of tapioca starch, one spoon of vinegar, a little salt, sugar, and six cups of water
3. Cook over coals until it begins to boil
4. Serve with cooked meats
