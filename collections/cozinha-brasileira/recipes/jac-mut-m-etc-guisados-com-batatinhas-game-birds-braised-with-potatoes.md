## Ingredients

- one bird (jacú, mutúm, etc.)
- a portion of peeled potatoes
- water
- salt
- hot peppers
- parsley leaves
- onion
- ginger
- marjoram
- two spoonfuls of butter
- four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the bird with a portion of peeled potatoes in water with salt, hot peppers, parsley leaves, onion, ginger, and marjoram.
2. When cooked, remove the bird and cut it into pieces.
3. Sauté the pieces in two spoonfuls of butter.
4. Add the strained and well-reduced cooking broth and let boil for one quarter of an hour more.
5. Mash the potatoes into a homogeneous paste, mix with four egg yolks, and fry in a casserole in two spoonfuls of butter.
6. Serve the meat over the potato preparation.
