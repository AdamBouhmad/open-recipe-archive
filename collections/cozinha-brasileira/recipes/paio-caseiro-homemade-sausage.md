## Ingredients

- pork meat, neither too fat nor too lean
- English potatoes
- salt
- Indian pepper, whole
- nutmeg
- cow tripe, well-washed

## Directions

1. After the meat is boiled, chop it finely.
2. Cook the potatoes, selecting the most floury ones, peel and pound in a mortar.
3. Add the chopped meat to the mortar and pound everything together until well mixed.
4. Add salt, whole Indian pepper and nutmeg.
5. Fill some well-washed cow tripe with the mixture.
6. Tie these tripe at intervals with string, hang them in smoke or in the open air.
7. Cook these sausages in a pot or in well-seasoned meat broth, or eat them roasted on a grill, pricking them with a pin so they do not burst.
