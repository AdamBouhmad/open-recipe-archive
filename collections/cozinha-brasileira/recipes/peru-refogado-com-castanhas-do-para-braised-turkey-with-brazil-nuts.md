## Ingredients

- turkey
- melted lard
- hot water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- Brazil nuts

## Directions

1. Take the turkey cut into pieces and braise in melted lard, turning the pieces until they take on good color evenly
2. Add a glass of hot water, salt, minced parsley, onion leaves, and a quantity of Brazil nuts
3. Simmer over moderate fire for two to three hours
4. Serve with the Brazil nuts
