## Ingredients

- barb and crumatá fish
- red wine
- one onion
- bay leaves
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- two tablespoons butter
- two tablespoons wheat flour

## Directions

1. Scale and wash the fish
2. Boil in red wine with one onion, bay leaves, salt, pepper and parsley
3. When cooked, place the fish on a plate
4. Add to the broth two tablespoons of butter mixed with two tablespoons of wheat flour
5. Let it boil a little longer, pour the sauce over the fish, and serve hot
