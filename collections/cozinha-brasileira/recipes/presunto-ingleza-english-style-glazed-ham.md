## Ingredients

- one ham
- wheat flour
- Madeira wine
- ham stock
- decorative paper

## Directions

1. After removing the salt as previously explained, leave it to cook until the water reduces to three-quarters
2. After cooling in the same vessel, remove the skin and wrap it with a layer of wheat flour paste prepared as for pastries
3. Place it in a frying pan and put it in the oven until almost cooked
4. Remove it from the oven and introduce a cup of Madeira wine through a hole made in the paste and return it to the oven for half an hour
5. Decorate the shank with cut paper and serve with a sauce made from the ham stock diluted in a little Madeira wine
