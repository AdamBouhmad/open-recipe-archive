## Ingredients

- two cups of milk
- one beaten egg white
- a little salt
- half a spoonful of sugar
- two spoonfuls of cornstarch
- plenty of fat

## Directions

1. Mix two cups of milk with one beaten egg white, a little salt, half a spoonful of sugar, and two spoonfuls of cornstarch
2. Heat fat in a cassole on the fire
3. Dip a mold into the dough, holding it by the handle; the mold becomes coated with the dough which is left in the fat
4. When cooked, which is known when the dough entirely releases from the mold, remove it and repeat this operation until the dough is finished
