## Ingredients

- one piece of pork meat
- water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- butter
- one tablespoon cornmeal dissolved in half a cup of vinegar
- whole peeled beans

## Directions

1. Scald one piece of meat and let it cool.
2. Put it on the fire with water, salt, pepper, parsley, onion leaves.
3. When cooked, remove it and strain the broth.
4. Fry the meat in a little butter.
5. When fried, add the broth, thickening it with one tablespoon of cornmeal dissolved in half a cup of vinegar.
6. Add a portion of whole peeled beans and let it boil until everything is well cooked and the broth is almost dry and serve.
