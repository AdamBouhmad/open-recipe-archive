## Ingredients

- twelve onions, cut into small pieces
- caul fat, rendered
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- roasted bird meat
- bread crumb
- caul fat cut into small pieces
- salt
- pepper from India, crushed
- garlic
- chives
- parsley
- six raw egg yolks
- half bottle of milk

## Directions

1. Cut twelve onions into small pieces and cook them in rendered caul fat
2. Add bread crumb boiled in milk and the meat of a roasted bird
3. Chop everything well and pound with bread crumb, using equal parts of bread crumb, bird meat, and caul fat cut into small pieces
4. Add salt, crushed pepper from India, garlic, chives, parsley, six raw egg yolks and half bottle of milk
5. Mix everything in a pan where the cooked onions are
6. Fill intestines with this mixture, leaving them an empty space so they do not burst
7. Tie well and put to cook in milk mixed with half water, not allowing it to boil
8. Remove from heat when cooked and let cool
9. When you wish to eat them, place them over coals or on the grill after wrapping them in paper coated with butter
10. Instead of bird meat, any other meat may be substituted
