## Ingredients

- beef brain
- hot water
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- bay leaves
- garlic
- parsley
- celery root
- carrots

## Directions

1. First bring the brain to hot water and after cleaning of blood and the membrane that covers it, place for one or two hours in cold water
2. Cook in this same water with a small cup of vinegar, salt, pepper, cloves, bay leaves, garlic, parsley, celery root and carrots
3. Three-quarters of an hour are sufficient for it to be cooked
4. Serve with any sauce indicated for meats
