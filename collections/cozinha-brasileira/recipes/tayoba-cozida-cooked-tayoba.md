## Ingredients

- tayoba
- water
- salt
- butter
- grated cheese
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take the roots of two tayobas
2. Boil in water and salt
3. When cooked, peel and cut into slices
4. Fry in butter and place on a plate sprinkled with grated cheese and plenty of sugar, and serve
