## Ingredients

- veal brains
- manioc flour
- butter
- wheat flour
- sugar
- one glass Madeira wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Scald the veal brains and cut into slices
2. Coat in manioc flour and fry in butter
3. In a separate pan, fry a little wheat flour in one tablespoon butter and another of sugar until the flour becomes golden
4. Add one glass Madeira wine
5. After boiling briefly, add the brains to this sauce after ten to twelve minutes
6. Serve with lemon juice and one tablespoon sugar
