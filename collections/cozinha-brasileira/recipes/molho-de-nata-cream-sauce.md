## Ingredients

- Butter
- Wheat flour or cornmeal
- Milk
- Parsley
- Nutmeg
- Sugar
- Salt
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Fry a little butter with wheat flour or cornmeal and four spoonfuls of rich milk
2. Add a little chopped parsley, nutmeg, a little sugar and salt
3. Boil and thicken with two egg yolks
4. Serve with puddings and pastries
