## Ingredients

- turkey wings
- turkey neck
- turkey legs
- a piece of minced salt pork
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- okras

## Directions

1. Take the turkey wings, neck, and legs and place in a pan with minced salt pork
2. Fry a little
3. Add enough water to cover the meat, along with salt, parsley, onion leaves, and a quantity of whole okras
4. Allow to finish cooking
5. Thicken the broth with three egg yolks beaten in half a chicara of lemon juice
6. Serve
