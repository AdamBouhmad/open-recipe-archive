## Ingredients

- half pound sweet almonds or watermelon seeds
- milk for soaking
- six cups milk
- sugar
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Take half pound of sweet almonds or watermelon seeds
2. Pound them, moistening them with a little milk
3. Take six cups of milk, place within them the almonds or watermelon seeds
4. Add a little sugar and orange flower water, and pass everything through a fine sieve
5. Bring to boil over a gentle fire and reduce
6. Pass once more through a sieve, pour onto a plate, and let it cool to serve
