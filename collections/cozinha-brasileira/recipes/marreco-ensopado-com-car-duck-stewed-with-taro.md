## Ingredients

- pieces of duck
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- one bowl of meat broth
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- parsley
- one or two taro roots
- two egg yolks
- one spoonful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Wrap the pieces of duck in breadcrumbs and fry them in butter
2. Add a bowl of meat broth, little salt, pepper, onion, parsley, one or two taro roots if small
3. Let it boil until everything is cooked; remove the duck pieces and the taro cut in slices
4. Having reduced the broth to little liquid, thicken it with two egg yolks and one spoonful of vinegar, and return the meat and taro slices
5. Let it boil a bit more and serve
