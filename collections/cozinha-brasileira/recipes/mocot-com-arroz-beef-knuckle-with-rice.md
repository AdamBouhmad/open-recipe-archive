## Ingredients

- beef hand or foot, cleaned
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- pepper
- rice

## Directions

1. Clean the beef hand or foot well and cut into pieces
2. Cook with salt, parsley, onion leaves, and a little pepper
3. When almost cooked, remove and strain the broth
4. Cook the rice in this broth
5. When the rice is almost dry, add the mocotó and serve
