## Ingredients

- one cleaned chicken
- its offal
- two cooked eggs
- half a quarter pound of bacon
- one onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- two tablespoons of breadcrumbs
- broth or wine
- melted butter
- salad

## Directions

1. Clean the chicken
2. Chop the offal, cooked eggs, bacon, onion, and parsley together with salt and pepper
3. Mix with two tablespoons of breadcrumbs and moisten with a little broth or wine
4. Stuff the chicken's breast and belly cavity with this mixture
5. Tie the chicken closed by folding the neck over the breast and tucking the beak under, crossing the feet over the belly and tucking them under the neck
6. Put on a spit and roast over low heat
7. Baste frequently with melted butter
8. Serve with salad
