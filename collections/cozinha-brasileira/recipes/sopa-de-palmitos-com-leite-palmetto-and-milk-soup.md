## Ingredients

- palmetto
- salt
- fat
- water
- milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Chop, blanch, and sauté the palmetto with a little salt and fat
2. Cook in a little water until done
3. When the water is almost dry, add a bottle of milk cooked with two tablespoons of sugar and two egg yolks
