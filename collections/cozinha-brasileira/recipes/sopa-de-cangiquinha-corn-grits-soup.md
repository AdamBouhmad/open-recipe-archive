## Ingredients

- a portion of corn grits
- ground peanuts
- water
- sugar
- grated cheese
- a little anise herb

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of corn grits as needed
2. Take ground peanuts and mix with water
3. Strain and squeeze the mixture
4. Repeat the straining in fresh water until the water no longer comes out milky
5. Add this peanut milk to the corn grits
6. Mix in sugar, grated cheese, and a little anise herb
