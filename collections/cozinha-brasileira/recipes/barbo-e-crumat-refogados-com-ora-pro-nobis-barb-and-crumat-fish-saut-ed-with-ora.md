## Ingredients

- one tablespoon butter or fat
- one chopped onion
- one tablespoon cassava flour
- some tomatoes
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- one cup of red wine
- one bowl of water
- cleaned and scaled fish
- portion of ora-pro-nobis

## Directions

1. Melt one tablespoon of butter or fat
2. Add one chopped onion, one tablespoon of cassava flour, some tomatoes, parsley, salt, and pepper
3. Add one cup of red wine and one bowl of water
4. When boiling, add the cleaned and scaled fish
5. Once cooked, remove the fish and add a portion of ora-pro-nobis and serve
