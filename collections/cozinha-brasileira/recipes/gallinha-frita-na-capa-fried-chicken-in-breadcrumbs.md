## Ingredients

- legs and wings of two or three chickens
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- salads

## Directions

1. Take the legs and wings of two or three chickens, seasoned with salt, pepper and chopped parsley.
2. Cover them with breadcrumbs and moisten with butter.
3. Roast them on the grill or fry them in a little fat, and serve with salads.
