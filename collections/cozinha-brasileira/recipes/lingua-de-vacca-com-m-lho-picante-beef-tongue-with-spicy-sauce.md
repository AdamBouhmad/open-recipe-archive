## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- carrots
- onions
- celery
- salt
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the tongue for four to five hours with some carrots, onions, celery and salt
2. Remove the skin and split it down the middle
3. Serve placed in one of the indicated spicy sauces in which it should be heated
