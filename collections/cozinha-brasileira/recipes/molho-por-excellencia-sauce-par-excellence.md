## Ingredients

- some sardines
- 1 onion
- a piece of washed butter
- some spoons of wheat flour or starch
- a little bell pepper
- nutmeg
- meat broth
- juice of 2 lemons
- slices of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Wash some sardines and remove the bones; cut them well along with 1 onion
2. Heat a piece of washed butter in a saucepan
3. Once melted, add some spoons of wheat flour or starch, a little bell pepper, and nutmeg
4. Add the chopped sardines, meat broth, juice of 2 lemons, and slices of 1 lemon
5. Let it boil a little and serve
