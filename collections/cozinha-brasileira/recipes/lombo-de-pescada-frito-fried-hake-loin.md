## Ingredients

- Hake fish
- Salt
- Pepper
- Chopped parsley
- Butter

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the fish
2. Take out the loins
3. Season with salt, pepper and chopped parsley
4. Fry in butter
5. Serve when cooked
