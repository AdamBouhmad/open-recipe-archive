## Ingredients

- one paca, squirrel or opossum
- brandy
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- cloves
- marjoram
- paper
- butter

## Directions

1. Take one of these animals and soak it in brandy with salt, pepper, nutmeg, cloves and marjoram
2. After six hours, thread it on a spit, wrapped in paper greased with butter, and roast it over a gentle fire
3. When cooked, remove the paper and serve with some salad
