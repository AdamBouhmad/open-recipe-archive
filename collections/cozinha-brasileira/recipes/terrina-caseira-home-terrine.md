## Ingredients

- lean beef
- lean lard
- parsley
- chives
- cinnamon
- clove
- nutmeg
- thyme
- bay leaves
- brandy
- water

## Directions

1. Cut very lean beef into small slices along with lean lard, parsley, chopped chives, cinnamon, clove, nutmeg, thyme, and a few bay leaves
2. Take a terrine and place at its bottom a layer of beef, another of lard, and a little of the prepared seasonings
3. On top, pour a spoon of brandy and two of water
4. Cover the terrine well and place over hot coals or embers to cook slowly
5. When cooked, drain the fat and serve in the same terrine
