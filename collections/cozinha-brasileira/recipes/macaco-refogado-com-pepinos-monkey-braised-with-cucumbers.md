## Ingredients

- monkey, cleaned and without head
- three spoonfuls of fat
- half a dozen cucumbers
- six onions
- one cup of water
- one glass of sour orange juice
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- chopped parsley
- two egg yolks
- one glass of wine

## Directions

1. Braise the cleaned monkey without head in three spoonfuls of fat.
2. Remove it and in the same fat put half a dozen cucumbers and six onions cut into pieces.
3. Add one cup of water, one glass of sour orange juice, salt, pepper, cloves, chopped parsley, and the pieces of braised monkey.
4. Let boil for one hour.
5. Incorporate the sauce with two beaten and dissolved egg yolks in one glass of wine, and serve.
