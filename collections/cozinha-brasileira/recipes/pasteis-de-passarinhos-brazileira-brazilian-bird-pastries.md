## Ingredients

- a portion of small birds
- vinegar
- ground pepper
- dough

## Directions

1. Fry a portion of small birds until well toasted
2. Sprinkle with vinegar and ground pepper
3. Roll each bird in a round of dough
4. Join the edges and fry in fat
