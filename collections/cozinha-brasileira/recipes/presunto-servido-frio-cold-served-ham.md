## Ingredients

- one ham
- cloth
- serpol
- bay leaf
- garlic
- twelve onions
- six Indian cloves
- carrots
- parsley
- celery
- one ounce of saltpeter
- water or one bottle of white wine

## Directions

1. Soak the ham for twenty-four hours, tied in a cloth
2. Place it in a vessel with serpol, bay leaf, garlic, twelve onions, six Indian cloves, carrots, parsley, celery, and one ounce of saltpeter
3. Moisten it with water or with a bottle of white wine, in such a way that it is well covered
4. To know if the ham is well cooked, test it with a stick; if it enters easily, it is cooked
5. Remove the ham from the heat and let it cool in the same pan until lukewarm
6. Remove it at this point and lift it up
