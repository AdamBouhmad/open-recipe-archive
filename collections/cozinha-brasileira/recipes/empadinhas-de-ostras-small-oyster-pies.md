## Ingredients

- chicken breast and liver
- one onion
- a little parsley
- salt
- one spoonful of butter
- half a cup of white wine
- puff pastry dough
- oysters
- butter
-  egg yolk

## Directions

1. Chop the breast and liver of a chicken with one onion, a little parsley, and salt
2. Boil briefly in one spoonful of butter and half a cup of white wine
3. Make some small boxes of puff pastry, line the bottom with a little of this hash
4. Place on top two or three oysters with a little butter, cover it and brush the top of the crust with egg yolk
