## Ingredients

- two pork loins
- butter
- salt
- ground Indian pepper
- meat extract
- Spanish sauce
- meat broth
- pepper sauce
- bread
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the loins in the manner of small steaks and round them
2. Place them in a frying pan with melted butter, salt, and ground Indian pepper
3. Shortly before serving, sauté the steaks, turn them, and place on a plate
4. In the same frying pan, add a little meat extract, two spoonfuls of Spanish sauce, two of meat broth, and the same amount of pepper sauce
5. Reduce everything together
6. Finally place slices of bread spread with butter and add lemon juice
7. Sauté again with the sauce and serve
