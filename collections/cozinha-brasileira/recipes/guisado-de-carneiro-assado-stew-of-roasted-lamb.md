## Ingredients

- Leftovers of roasted loins
- Polvilho
- Butter
- White wine
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut the leftovers of roasted loins into pieces
2. Fry a spoonful of polvilho with another of butter until lightly browned
3. Add two cups of white wine and a little nutmeg
4. Stir and add the meat immediately after
5. Serve after giving it a boil
