## Ingredients

- black beans
- fat
- salt
- garlic
- onion
- chili pepper
- bean broth
- cornmeal or cassava flour
- sausages
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of beans, drain and season with plenty of fat, salt, garlic, onion, chili pepper.
2. Add the bean broth and a portion of cornmeal or cassava flour.
3. Stir well over the fire until the flour is cooked and serve, pouring a slice of fried or roasted sausages and some rounds of potatoes on top.
