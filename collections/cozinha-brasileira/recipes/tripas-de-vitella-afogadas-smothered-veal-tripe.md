## Ingredients

- veal tripe
- veal sweetbread
- water
- salt
- wheat flour
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- wine or water
- orange juice

## Directions

1. Clean the tripe and sweetbread well, boil them in salted water, remove and let cool
2. Cut everything into small pieces
3. Make a sauce of wheat flour toasted in butter, parsley, an onion and a little wine or water
4. Place the tripe and sweetbread in the sauce, let boil a little longer and add a little orange juice
