## Ingredients

- one snake
- water
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- onion
- marjoram
- cloves
- nutmeg
- ginger
- one spoon of wheat flour
- two spoons of fat
- one bottle of boiled milk
- two egg yolks
- one spoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil a snake in water with salt, peppers, parsley, onion, marjoram, cloves, nutmeg and ginger
2. When cooked, remove and separate the meat from the bones
3. Stew the meat with one spoon of wheat flour and two spoons of fat, not allowing it to fry too much
4. Add one bottle of boiled milk with two egg yolks and one spoon of sugar
5. Leave to boil for a quarter hour and serve
