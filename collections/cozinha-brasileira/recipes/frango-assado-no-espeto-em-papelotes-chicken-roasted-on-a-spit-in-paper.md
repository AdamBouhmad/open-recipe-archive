## Ingredients

- one chicken
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the chicken and place on a spit covered with paper greased with butter
2. Roast over coals
3. When roasted, remove the paper
4. Coat with bread crumbs
5. Pour melted butter on top
6. Turn over the coals until golden, then serve
