## Ingredients

- chocolate
- amber

## Directions

1. Prepare chocolate in the manner already indicated
2. Add one scruple of amber for each pound of chocolate
3. This chocolate is excellent for restoring strength lost through sexual excess or sleep loss, for those suffering in humid weather, for those with dulled thoughts, and for those with a fixed idea that troubles and prevents their peace
