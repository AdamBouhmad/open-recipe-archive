## Ingredients

- monkey
- sour orange juice (laranja da terra azeda)
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- marjoram
- parsley
- bacon strips
- one glass of wine

## Directions

1. Soak the monkey in sour orange juice with salt, pepper, garlic, marjoram, and parsley for twenty-four hours, turning it two or three times.
2. Lard with fine strips of bacon, place in a pan, moisten with one glass of wine.
3. Roast in the oven or over coals; when roasted, serve.
