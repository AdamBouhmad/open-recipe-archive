## Ingredients

- Roasted pork loin
- Fifteen large onions
- Spanish sauce, reduced

## Directions

1. Cut the roasted pork loin into small slices, being careful to remove the skin and nerves
2. Cut the large onions in half and remove their centers
3. Cook them in two spoonfuls of reduced Spanish sauce
4. Allow the loin slices to warm in a water bath until ready to serve
5. At table service, arrange buttered bread slices around them
