## Ingredients

- rabbit
- water
- wine
- salt
- parsley
- marjoram
- nutmeg
- onions
- gordura or azeite doce
- pepper
- vinegar
- broth

## Directions

1. Cut a rabbit into pieces and boil them in water, wine, salt, parsley, marjoram and nutmeg
2. On the other hand, cut a portion of onions into slices and fry them in a casserole with two spoonfuls of fat or sweet oil, salt, and pepper
3. When beginning to boil, add one spoonful of vinegar and another of broth, not stirring, so that the onions remain whole
4. Add the cooked rabbit, heat and serve
