## Ingredients

- Hake loins
- Cow butter
- A dozen tubaras (small fish)
- One teacup white wine

## Directions

1. Take the hake loins and cut them into pieces
2. Sauté them in cow butter and remove
3. Sauté a dozen tubaras in the same fat
4. Add one teacup of white wine and let it boil
5. When cooked, add the pieces of fish
6. Serve after boiling once more
