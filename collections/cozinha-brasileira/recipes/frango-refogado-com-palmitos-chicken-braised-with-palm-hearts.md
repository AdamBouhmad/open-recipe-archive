## Ingredients

- one chicken, cut into pieces
- hot fat
- one finely chopped and washed palm heart
- two cups of water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- ora-pro-nobis leaves
- one small spoon of cornstarch dissolved in cold water
- one spoon of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Braise the chicken pieces in hot fat until half fried; remove them
2. In the same fat, braise the finely chopped palm heart; stir
3. Add two cups of water and the chicken pieces
4. Season with salt, pepper, and parsley
5. Add ora-pro-nobis leaves and the cornstarch dissolved in cold water
6. Add lemon juice
7. Let simmer near the fire until dried; serve with cornmeal porridge
