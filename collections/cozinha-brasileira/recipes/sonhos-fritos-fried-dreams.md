## Ingredients

- half a plate of fine cornmeal
- three cups of boiled milk
- a little salt
- one spoon of sugar
- ten or more eggs
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Scald half a plate of fine cornmeal with three cups of boiled milk, a little salt and one spoon of sugar
2. Mix well
3. Add ten or more eggs to form a soft mixture
4. With a spoon, take small portions and fry in a pan with hot fat
5. Continue until the mixture is finished
6. Cover the fried dreams with sugar and cinnamon
7. Serve for coffee
