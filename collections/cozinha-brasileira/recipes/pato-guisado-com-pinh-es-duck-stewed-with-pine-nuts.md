## Ingredients

- one whole duck
- half a pound of bacon
- one pound of salted pork loin
- peppers
- one onion
- parsley
- celery root
- a portion of pine nuts, cooked and shelled
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Boil one whole duck with half a pound of bacon, one pound of salted pork loin, peppers, one onion, parsley and celery root
2. When cooked, reduce the broth to three cups of liquid
3. Place the duck in a casserole with this sauce, a portion of cooked and shelled pine nuts and the juice of one lemon
4. Let it boil for half an hour between coals and serve
