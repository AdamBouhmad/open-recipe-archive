## Ingredients

- lamb quarter
- sardines
- cloth
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- ginger
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- garlic
- bacon slices
- meat broth
- wine
- water squash
- fat

## Directions

1. Take the lamb quarter, remove the bone, lard the meat, fill the space with sardines
2. Wrap the quarter in cloth and soak it for six hours in vinegar, bay leaves, ginger, salt, pepper, onion, and garlic
3. Put it in a pan lined with bacon slices, two bowls of broth, and one of wine
4. Let it cook in the oven
5. Once cooked, separate it and strain the broth
6. Remove the fat and reduce it to two cups
7. Take a fresh water squash, peel it, remove the seeds, and cut it into cubes
8. Fry the cubes in a little fat and salt
9. Add a little water and let it cook over coals until dry
10. Serve with the quarter, pouring the sauce on it at the table
