## Ingredients

- a slice of bacon for the bottom of the pan
- one paca, squirrel or opossum, cleaned and without head
- three onion heads, whole
- chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- another slice of bacon on top
- white wine, two glasses
- one egg yolk
- one spoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a pan with a slice of bacon, and place either the paca, squirrel or opossum, cleaned and without head, with three whole onion heads, chopped parsley, salt and pepper
2. Cover it with another slice of bacon and roast in the oven or over coals
3. When cooked, remove it and mix into the sauce two glasses of white wine, one egg yolk and one spoon of sugar, bring to a boil, strain the sauce and serve with the roast
