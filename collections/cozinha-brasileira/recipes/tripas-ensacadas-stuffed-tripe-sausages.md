## Ingredients

- tripe from a pig
- water
- salt
- pepper
- summer savory
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- sage
- garlic
- parsley
- chives
- cloves
- nutmeg
- meat broth
- aromatic herbs

## Directions

1. Having cleaned and washed the tripe well, soak it in cold water for twelve hours
2. After it has drained, dry it well, placing it in a vessel where it is seasoned with salt, pepper, summer savory, bay leaf, marjoram, sage, garlic, parsley, chives, cloves, nutmeg, all well pounded, and leave it in this seasoning for two hours
3. Choose the larger and thicker tripe, and in them introduce the smaller whole tripe, tying the ends of the thick tripe
4. Cook the tripe thus stuffed in meat broth and in a sauce of aromatic herbs; after it cools, roast it on the grill
