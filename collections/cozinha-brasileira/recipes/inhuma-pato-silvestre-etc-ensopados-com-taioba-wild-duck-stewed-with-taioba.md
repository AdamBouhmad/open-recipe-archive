## Ingredients

- two spoons of butter
- one spoon of fine cornmeal
- one bowl of water
- one bird, cut into pieces
- one taioba root, cut into pieces
- salt
- parsley
- peppers
- half dozen tomatoes
- a little ginger, grated
- one glass of juice of orange of the land
- two egg yolks
- one spoon of brown sugar
- one glass of red wine

## Directions

1. Melt two spoons of butter with one spoon of fine cornmeal
2. Add one bowl of water, one bird cut into pieces, one taioba root cut into pieces, salt, parsley, peppers, half dozen tomatoes, a little grated ginger and one glass of orange juice
3. Let it simmer until cooked
4. Before serving, add two beaten egg yolks mixed with one spoon of brown sugar and one glass of red wine
