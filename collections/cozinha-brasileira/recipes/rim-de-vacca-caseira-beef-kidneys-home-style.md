## Ingredients

- beef kidneys, sliced thin
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- small onions
- garlic clove
- beef or chicken broth
- egg yolks
- sugar
- milk
- vinegar
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut the kidneys into fine slices and pass through the fire in a little butter
2. Add salt, pepper, parsley, small onions and a garlic clove, all finely chopped
3. When cooked, add a portion of beef or cooked chicken broth and a sauce made with egg yolks, sugar and milk, thickened on the fire without boiling
4. Finally add a little vinegar and do not let boil further so they do not become tough
5. Or some mushrooms can be added to the kidneys at the time they are placed on the fire
