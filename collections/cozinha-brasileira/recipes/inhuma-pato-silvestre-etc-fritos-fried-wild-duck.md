## Ingredients

- one bird, cut into pieces
- salt
- peppers
- tomatoes
- one garlic clove
- one onion, pounded
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the bird into pieces and soak them with salt, peppers, tomatoes, one garlic clove and one pounded onion
2. After six hours, when the meat has taken on the flavor of the seasonings, dry it and roll it in breadcrumbs
3. Fry on both sides in butter
4. When cooked, serve with the same butter
