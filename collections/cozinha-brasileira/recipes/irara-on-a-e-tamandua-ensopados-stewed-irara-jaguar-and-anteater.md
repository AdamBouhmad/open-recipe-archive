## Ingredients

- meat from irara, jaguar, or anteater
- strong vinegar
- salt
- parsley
- comarí peppers
- ginger
- nutmeg
- mustard seeds, ground
- fat
- one spoon wheat flour
- two onions
- two cups water
- one glass vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak the meat in strong vinegar, salt, parsley, comarí peppers, ginger, nutmeg, and a portion of ground mustard seeds
2. After twenty-four hours, remove the meat and fry it in fat, turning to toast on all sides
3. Cut the meat into small pieces
4. Brown one spoon of wheat flour, two onions in the same fat
5. Add two cups of water and one glass of vinegar
6. Let it boil a little and add the meat to this sauce
7. Let it boil near the fire until cooked
8. Serve
