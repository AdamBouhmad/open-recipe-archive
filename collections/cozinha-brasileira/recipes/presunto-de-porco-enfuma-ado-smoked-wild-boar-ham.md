## Ingredients

- one wild boar leg
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- cloves
- red wine

## Directions

1. Trim a wild boar leg and place in a brine with plenty of salt, peppers, onions, cloves, and red wine
2. Leave in this brine for ten to fifteen days with a board placed over the quarter and a large weight on top
3. Remove, dry well, and expose to the sun for one day
4. Wrap in cloth and hang in smoke for fifteen to twenty days
5. After this time, it can be eaten cold and raw, or cooked or roasted
