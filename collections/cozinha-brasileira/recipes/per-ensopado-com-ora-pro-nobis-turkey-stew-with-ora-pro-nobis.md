## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of butter
- turkey pieces
- one cup of water
- two cups of boiled milk
- one garlic clove
- salt
- nutmeg
- two egg yolks
- one tablespoon of sugar
- a handful of ora-pro-nobis leaves

## Directions

1. Melt two tablespoons of butter, and when well hot, add the turkey pieces, turning them to fry on both sides
2. Remove from heat and let cool to then add one cup of water with two cups of boiled milk, one garlic clove, salt, nutmeg and two egg yolks
3. Return everything to heat and after boiling for half an hour over moderate heat, add one tablespoon of sugar and a handful of ora-pro-nobis leaves
4. Boil a little more and serve
