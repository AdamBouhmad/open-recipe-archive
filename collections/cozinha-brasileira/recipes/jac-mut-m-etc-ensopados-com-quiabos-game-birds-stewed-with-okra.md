## Ingredients

- one bird (jacú, mutúm, etc.)
- two spoonfuls of fat
- one bowl of water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- some hot peppers (pimentas comaris)
- one glass of juice from local oranges
- two egg yolks
- one small spoonful of tapioca starch
- okra, cut into pieces

## Directions

1. Take the bird and fry it briefly in two spoonfuls of fat, turning it on all sides.
2. Cut it into pieces and return them to the pan.
3. Add one bowl of water, salt, parsley, onion leaves, and some hot peppers.
4. Let it boil for three quarters of an hour.
5. Mix one glass of local orange juice with two egg yolks, one small spoonful of tapioca starch, and add along with the cut okra.
6. Let it finish cooking and serve.
