## Ingredients

- half pound of butter
- two pounds of ham
- one pound of beef
- chicken thighs
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- bay leaves
- two or three onions
- a dozen cooked egg yolks
- one carafe of milk

## Directions

1. Place over a low fire half pound of butter, two pounds of ham, one pound of beef, and chicken thighs, all cut into small pieces, with salt, pepper, cloves, bay leaves, and two or three onions
2. Fry everything well
3. Add a dozen cooked egg yolks, mashed
4. Add one carafe of milk little by little
5. Let cook for another hour, stirring continuously
6. Pass through a fine sieve and serve
