## Ingredients

- dried cod, cooked
- two tablespoons butter
- wheat flour
- pepper
- onion leaves
- bitter orange slices
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Skin and bone the cooked cod and cut it into slices.
2. Melt two tablespoons of butter, add some wheat flour, pepper and onion leaves.
3. Place the cod in this sauce.
4. Put the pan on coals or in the oven and let it roast for half an hour, then serve.
