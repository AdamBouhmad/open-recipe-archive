## Ingredients

- four cucumbers
- salt
- vinegar
- milk cream
- one tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and cut four cucumbers into thin slices
2. Soak in salt for four hours
3. Drain the moisture
4. Season again with salt, vinegar, milk cream and one tablespoon of sugar
5. Serve
