## Ingredients

- Pork loins
- Pork fat strips
- Veal meat slices
- Carrots
- Onions
- Cloves
- Parsley
- Onion leaves
- Bay leaf
- Butter
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. Remove the loins in their full length and prepare them, larding them with fat
2. They may be left long or given a round shape
3. Place strips of pork fat in a casserole with some slices of veal meat, two carrots, onions, two cloves, parsley, onion leaves, and bay leaf
4. Place the loins on top and cover with a sheet of paper spread with butter
5. Add half a bottle of meat broth
6. Place the casserole over the fire and set fire on top
7. When ready to plate, drain them and place meat substance on top
8. Serve with chicory, sliced cucumbers, cassava fries, or with a tomato sauce or hot sauce
