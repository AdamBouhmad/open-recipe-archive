## Ingredients

- partridges, quail, or similar birds
- salt
- pepper juice
- thin slices of bacon
- cassava
- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- egg yolks
- wine

## Directions

1. Rub the birds with salt and pepper juice
2. Wrap them in thin slices of bacon
3. Place them in the coals with some cassava
4. When cooked, remove them and unwrap the bacon
5. Serve with peeled cassava and a sauce made from milk boiled with one spoon of sugar, a little ground cinnamon, and two egg yolks dissolved in a glass of wine
