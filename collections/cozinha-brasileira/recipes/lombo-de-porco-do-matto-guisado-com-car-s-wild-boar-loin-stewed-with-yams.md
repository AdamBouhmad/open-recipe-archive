## Ingredients

- one wild boar loin
- one yam
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- pepper
- lemon juice from two lemons
- half a quart of butter or fat
- corn flour
- carapicú (a type of fish) chopped

## Directions

1. Boil the loin with the yam cut into pieces, salt, parsley, onion leaves, pepper, and lemon juice from two lemons
2. When cooked, remove and cut into pieces, as well as the yam
3. Fry afterwards in half a quart of butter or fat
4. After frying, dust with corn flour and pour the strained broth over it
5. Add chopped carapicú to the broth
6. Let everything boil until the broth is well reduced
7. Serve
