## Ingredients

- portion of shrimp
- water
- salt
- sweet oil
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of shrimp in water and salt
2. Shell them and season with sweet oil, salt, peppers and vinegar
3. Serve cold
