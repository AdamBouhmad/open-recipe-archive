## Ingredients

- portion of lobsters
- water
- salt
- two spoons of butter
- parsley
- one onion
- handful of chopped carapace
- pepper
- clove
- one cup of wine
- one cup of vinegar
- one cup of water

## Directions

1. Take a portion of lobsters and cook in water and salt
2. When cooked, let cool and shell them
3. Braise in two spoons of butter with parsley, one onion and a handful of chopped carapace
4. Add salt, pepper, clove, one cup of wine, one cup of vinegar and one cup of water
5. Let everything boil over moderate heat and serve
