## Ingredients

- pigeons
- vinegar
- melted butter
- salt
- pepper
- pigeon livers
- sugar
- nutmeg
- bitter orange juice

## Directions

1. Clean the birds, tuck their feet inside, and spit them
2. Roast, basting with a sauce made of vinegar, melted butter, salt, and pepper
3. When roasted, take the sauce and add the pounded livers of the birds, a little sugar, nutmeg, and one spoon of bitter orange juice
4. Boil a little and serve with the pigeons
