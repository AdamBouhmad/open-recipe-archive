## Ingredients

- pound of cooked ham
- pound of veal
- half pound bacon
- four cooked eggs
- parsley
- two onions
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- handful of bread crumb
- white wine
- two tablespoons butter
- dozen ground peanuts
- quarter measure of raisins
- two tablespoons sugar
- nutmeg
- cardamom
- paper

## Directions

1. Chop a pound of cooked ham, a pound of veal, half a pound of bacon, four cooked eggs, parsley, two onions, salt, pepper and cloves, and fill the cavity of the turkey with this mixture.
2. On the other hand, knead a handful of bread crumb softened in white wine, two tablespoons of butter, a dozen ground peanuts, quarter measure of raisins scalded in hot water, two tablespoons of sugar, a little salt, nutmeg and cardamom.
3. Fill the crop of the turkey with this mixture.
4. Lard the breast with very thin strips of bacon, place the turkey on the spit and roast it wrapped in buttered paper, basting it with a cup of wine mixed with four tablespoons of butter.
5. When roasted, serve.
