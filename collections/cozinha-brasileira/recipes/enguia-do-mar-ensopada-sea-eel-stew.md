## Ingredients

- one sea eel
- one spoon fat
- one bowl water
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- two spoons cornmeal
- a handful of carapicús
- one cup lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the skin of the fish and remove the loins and cut into pieces
2. Sauté in one spoon of fat, turning and avoiding browning
3. Add one bowl of water, salt, peppers and parsley
4. Add two spoons of cornmeal and a handful of chopped carapicús
5. Let everything boil
6. Add one cup of lemon juice and serve
