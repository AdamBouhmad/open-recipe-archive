## Ingredients

- a handful of small fish (carapicús)
- water
- parsley
- one onion
- two spoons butter
- a handful of bread crumb soaked in milk
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- two clean fish
- paper leaves
- oil

## Directions

1. Boil a handful of carapicús in water, drain and chop finely
2. Mix with some parsley, one onion, two spoons of butter and a handful of bread crumb soaked in milk
3. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg
4. Fill two cleaned fish with this mixture
5. Wrap them in oiled paper leaves
6. Roast on the grill for a quarter of an hour over moderate fire, turning many times
7. When well cooked, remove the paper and serve
