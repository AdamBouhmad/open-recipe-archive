## Ingredients

- six egg whites
- half a pound of sugar
- one quarter of grated coconut from Bahia
- one spoon of liqueur

## Directions

1. Beat six egg whites with half a pound of sugar, one quarter of grated coconut from Bahia and one spoon of liqueur
2. When a firm mixture forms, drop spoonfuls onto thick paper
3. Bake in a very gentle oven
