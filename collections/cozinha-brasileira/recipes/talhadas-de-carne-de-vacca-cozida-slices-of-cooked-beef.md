## Ingredients

- butter or fat
- seven or eight large onions
- wheat flour
- beef broth
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- cooked beef slices

## Directions

1. Place in a casserole a little butter or fat and seven or eight large onions cut into rings
2. Place this casserole on high heat and stir
3. Over the onions, when golden, pour a spoon of wheat flour and a cup of beef broth, stirring until it boils
4. Add salt, pepper, and a little vinegar
5. After the sauce has boiled for twenty minutes, pour it over the plate on which the slices of cooked beef have been placed
6. Put the plate back on the fire and let it heat for half an hour and serve
