## Ingredients

- a little sweet oil
- some sharks
- mushrooms
- half a clove of garlic
- parsley
- meat broth
- 1 glass of white wine
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Heat a little sweet oil on coals
2. Add some sharks, mushrooms, half a clove of garlic, and parsley
3. Add meat broth, 1 glass of white wine, salt and pepper
4. Let them cook, taking care to remove the fat that floats on the sauce, and serve
