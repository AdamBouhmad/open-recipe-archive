## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- fat for wrapping
- one pound of sugar syrup
- half pound of roasted and crushed peanuts
- cloves
- cinnamon
- one handful of breadcrumbs
- eight egg yolks
- four spoonfuls of grated cheese
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Wrap a rabbit in paper greased with fat and roast it on the spit
2. Remove the bones and chop the meat very finely
3. Mix the meat in one pound of sugar syrup, half pound of roasted and crushed peanuts, cloves and cinnamon
4. When ready, add one handful of breadcrumbs, eight egg yolks, four spoonfuls of grated cheese
5. Mix and place in a compote dish, covering with ground cinnamon
6. Serve as a dessert
