## Ingredients

- one pound of cooked and peeled sweet potatoes
- five ounces of butter
- ten egg yolks
- three ounces of sugar
- a little salt
- lemon zest
- ground cinnamon
- ten egg whites

## Directions

1. Grate one pound of cooked and peeled sweet potatoes
2. Add five ounces of butter, ten egg yolks, three ounces of sugar, a little salt, lemon zest and ground cinnamon
3. Mix well
4. Add ten beaten egg whites
5. Mix everything together
6. Place in a buttered mold
7. Bake in the oven and serve
