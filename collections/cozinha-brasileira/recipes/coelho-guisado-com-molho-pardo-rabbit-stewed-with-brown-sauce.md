## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- bacon sheets
- one bottle of water
- one bottle of red wine
- salt
- parsley
- two onions
- one clove of garlic
- bay leaves
- peppers
- coriander
- rabbit liver
- two chicken livers

## Directions

1. Wrap a rabbit in sheets of bacon and roast it in the oven
2. Cut it into pieces and place them in the following sauce: boil one bottle of water, another of red wine, with salt, parsley, two sliced onions, one clove of garlic, bay leaves, peppers, coriander, the rabbit's liver and two chicken livers
3. When the broth is reduced to half, strain it, having placed the rabbit pieces in this sauce until they heat through, and serve
