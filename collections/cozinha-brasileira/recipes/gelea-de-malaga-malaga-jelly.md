## Ingredients

- one pound sugar
- juice of one lemon
- one pound jelly
- one glass Malaga wine

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with one pound of sugar
2. When at thread stage, add the juice of one lemon, one pound of jelly and one glass of Malaga wine
3. Let it boil for an instant, pour the liquid into a form, and proceed as already stated above
