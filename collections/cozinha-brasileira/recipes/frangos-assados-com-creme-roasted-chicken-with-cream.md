## Ingredients

- one fat chicken
- butter
- bread crumbs
- rich milk
- two egg yolks
- one spoon of sugar
- one spoon of wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Wrap the chicken in paper greased with butter and roast on a spit
2. When almost done, remove the paper
3. Wrap the chicken in bread crumbs and continue roasting
4. Moisten with rich milk
5. When cooked and golden, thicken the drippings with two egg yolks
6. Add one spoon of sugar, one spoon of wine, and nutmeg
7. Let simmer briefly and serve the chicken with this sauce
