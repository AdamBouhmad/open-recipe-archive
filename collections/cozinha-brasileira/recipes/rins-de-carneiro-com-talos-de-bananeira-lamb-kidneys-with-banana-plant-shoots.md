## Ingredients

- lamb kidneys
- one tablespoon cornmeal
- salt
- chopped parsley
- fat
- banana plant shoot tips, peeled and scalded
- two cups meat broth or water
- okra, optional

## Directions

1. Dice and braise kidneys with one tablespoon cornmeal, salt, chopped parsley, and fat
2. Add two banana plant shoot tips that have been peeled and scalded twice in hot water, removing the coarse outer leaves
3. Add two cups meat broth or water
4. Cook over coals until done
5. Serve with Minas cornmeal angú
6. Optionally add half a dozen okras cut into slices for added flavor
