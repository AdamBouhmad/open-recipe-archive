## Ingredients

- one sea eel
- water
- salt
- parsley
- bread crusts
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove the skin of the fish and cut into pieces
2. Boil in well-salted water with parsley
3. When cooked, remove and place on a plate
4. Cover with a portion of bread crusts grated and fried in butter
5. Serve
