## Ingredients

- four pounds mutton, chopped
- handful roasted and crushed peanuts
- salt
- cloves
- nutmeg
- butter, two spoons
- wheat flour, one spoon
- wine, one cup
- raisins, scalded, handful
- sugar, one spoon
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Chop four pounds mutton and mix with a handful of roasted and crushed peanuts, salt, cloves and nutmeg
2. Fry two spoons butter and one spoon wheat flour until dark amber color; add the meat at this point and let it heat, stirring always
3. Add one cup wine, a handful of scalded raisins, one spoon sugar and lemon juice
4. Let the stew boil over coals for half an hour and serve
