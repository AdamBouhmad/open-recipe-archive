## Ingredients

- beef broth
- watercress
- chicory
- lettuce
- sugar
- bitter almonds
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cook a hand of watercress, chicory and lettuce in beef broth
2. After half an hour, strain the broth
3. Add a little sugar and half a dozen crushed bitter almonds
4. Give it one more boil
5. Pour over toasted bread
