## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- bay leaves
- pepper
- clove
- melted fat or sweet oil

## Directions

1. Take some fish, salt them after removing the scales and entrails; leave them in this brine for four hours; remove them, and if the fish are large, cut into portions, wash them and place in the sun until dry; place afterwards in a barrel with bay leaves, peppers, clove, and pour melted fat almost cold or sweet oil over the top until covered; seal the barrel and keep.
