## Ingredients

- melted butter
- scalded raisins
- dried biscuits, ground
- sugar
- salt
- one dozen pounded almonds
- eggs

## Directions

1. Make a stuffing with melted butter, scalded raisins, a portion of dried biscuits ground, a little sugar, little salt, one dozen pounded almonds, and some eggs
2. Lift the skin covering the breast
3. Fill the space with this stuffing, securing the skin with a few stitches
4. Once the breast is thus prepared, spit it and roast it over coals, moistening the meat with melted butter
