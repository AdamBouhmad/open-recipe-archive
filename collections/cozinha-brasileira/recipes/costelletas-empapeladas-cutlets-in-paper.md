## Ingredients

- cutlets
- sweet oil or melted lard
- onions
- carapicú
- salt
- bacon
- bread crumbs
- butter
- eggs
- butter-greased paper

## Directions

1. Moisten the cutlets with sweet oil or melted lard
2. Cover on both sides with two onions, a handful of carapicú, a little salt, bacon, all very finely chopped, bread crumbs and a spoonful of butter
3. Pass through a liquid made of four beaten eggs
4. Wrap in grated bread crumbs
5. Finally wrap in well-buttered paper and go to roast in a large shallow pan placed over coals
6. Serve thus with the paper
