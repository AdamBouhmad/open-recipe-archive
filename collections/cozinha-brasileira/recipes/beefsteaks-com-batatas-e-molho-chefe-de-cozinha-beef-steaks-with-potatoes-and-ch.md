## Ingredients

- beef loin
- twelve equal pieces
- butter
- salt
- potatoes
- chef sauce

## Directions

1. Cut a beef loin into twelve equal pieces.
2. Beat these pieces with bread, trimming afterwards.
3. Put a quarter pound of butter to melt with a little salt; coat in it the beefsteaks and place them to roast over moderate fire.
4. When they are quite firm to the touch, remove from fire, place potatoes around the platter and pour over them a chef sauce.
