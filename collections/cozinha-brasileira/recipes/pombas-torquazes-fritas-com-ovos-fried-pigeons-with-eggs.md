## Ingredients

- pigeons
- salt
- wheat flour
- fat
- one dozen beaten eggs
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Split the pigeons in half, season with salt, coat in wheat flour
2. Fry in a little fat on both sides
3. When cooked, pour one dozen beaten eggs on top and leave on the fire until the eggs are also cooked
4. Place on a plate, inverting the pan so the pigeons are on top
5. Cover with sugar and cinnamon, and serve
