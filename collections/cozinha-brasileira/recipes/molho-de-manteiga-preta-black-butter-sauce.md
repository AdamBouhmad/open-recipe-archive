## Ingredients

- butter
- parsley
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place butter in a skillet and heat until it turns black, without however burning
2. At this point, add whole parsley to fry and pour over the fish
3. Pour vinegar in the skillet, which will finish cooking, and as soon as it is hot, pour it over the fish and serve at once
