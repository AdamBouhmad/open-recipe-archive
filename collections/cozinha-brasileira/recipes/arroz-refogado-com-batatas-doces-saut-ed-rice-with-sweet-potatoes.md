## Ingredients

- water
- fat
- salt
- parsley
- a little Indian pepper
- some peeled sweet potatoes
- one plate of chosen and washed rice

## Directions

1. Put in a pan water, fat, salt, parsley, a little Indian pepper, some peeled sweet potatoes, and one plate of chosen and washed rice
2. Let boil until cooked
3. After drying, serve
