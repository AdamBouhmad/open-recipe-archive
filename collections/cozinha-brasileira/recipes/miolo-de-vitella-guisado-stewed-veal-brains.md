## Ingredients

- veal brains
- water
- butter
- one onion
- salt
- parsley
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one glass wine
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Wash and scald the brains, then cut into slices
2. Fry in butter with one onion, salt, parsley, and one tablespoon wheat flour
3. Add one glass wine and peeled lemon slices without seeds
4. After boiling briefly, serve
