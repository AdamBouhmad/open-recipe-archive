## Ingredients

- one cup vinegar
- serpol
- bay leaves
- one garlic clove
- onion
- pepper
- beef broth
- a little beef substance from roasted meat
- a little wheat flour fried with butter

## Directions

1. Put the vinegar, serpol, bay leaves, garlic clove, onion, and pepper in a saucepan
2. Allow everything to boil until reduced to two thirds
3. Add beef broth and a little beef substance from roasted meat
4. Add a little wheat flour fried with butter
5. Pass through a sieve
6. If desired, melt butter in the saucepan and thicken gradually with wheat flour without allowing it to color
7. Add the sauce mentioned above
8. Add seasonings cut into small pieces
9. Bring everything to boil
10. Pour over cutlets, reheated meats, etc.
