## Ingredients

- three pounds of beef
- four partridges, quails, or capons
- a pound and a half of calf's feet
- one guan
- three carrots
- two onions
- one celery root
- two cloves
- a little fennel
- salt and water
- three quails or capons
- one cup of bread crumb soaked in meat broth

## Directions

1. Put three pounds of beef, four partridges or capons, a pound and a half of calf's feet, one guan, three carrots, two onions, one celery root, two cloves, and a little fennel in a pot with salt and water to make a broth for the soup
2. On the other hand, roast three quails or capons and pound them with the bones in a mortar
3. Mix them with one cup of bread crumb that has been soaked in meat broth
4. Pass this mixture through a sieve, adding a little broth so it becomes thin, and after straining, place over low heat, taking care not to let it boil
5. Soak some bread slices in the broth that is already ready
6. Place these slices at the bottom of a tureen, cover them with the quail pulp, and finally pour the broth
