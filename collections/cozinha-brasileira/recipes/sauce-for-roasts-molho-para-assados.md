## Ingredients

- one chicken breast
- two hard-boiled eggs
- one onion
- four spoonfuls of beef broth
- eight Comarí peppers
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Cut one chicken breast, two hard-boiled eggs, and one onion into very small pieces; add four spoonfuls of beef broth, eight Comarí peppers, crushed with the juice of one lemon, and heat a little to serve with grilled meat.
