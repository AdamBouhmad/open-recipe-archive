## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- bacon strips
- butter
- meat extract or sauce

## Directions

1. Lard the chops by piercing them through on both sides with bacon strips.
2. Pass in butter on both sides and fry over high heat.
3. Trim the bacon ends level with the meat.
4. Let boil, covering with meat extract or sauce.
5. Keep near the fire for a quarter of an hour.
6. Serve.
