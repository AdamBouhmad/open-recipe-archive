## Ingredients

- veal piece
- broth
- white wine
- bay leaf
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice
- breadcrumbs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take a piece of veal and give it two boils
2. Remove the bones and skin
3. Place in a casserole with half broth and half white wine, a bay leaf, pepper and salt
4. When cooked, add plenty of lemon juice, breadcrumbs and a little sugar
5. Serve
