## Ingredients

- onion
- gordura
- rabbit
- potatoes
- water
- salt
- peppers

## Directions

1. Fry one onion in fat
2. When the onion is fried, sauté the pieces of a rabbit in this fat with a portion of raw peeled potatoes cut into slices
3. Add one cup of water, salt and peppers
4. Leave to cook over low fire and serve
