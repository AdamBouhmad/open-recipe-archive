## Ingredients

- lettuce
- water
- salt
- salt
- one onion
- fat or butter
- one cup of water
- salt
- salt
- one beaten egg

## Directions

1. Chop a portion of lettuce, boil it in water for five minutes
2. Remove it immediately, and place it in cold water
3. In another pan, fry a chopped onion in fat or butter
4. Add the drained lettuce, one cup of water, salt, salt, and one beaten egg
5. When it has boiled a little, serve
