## Ingredients

- one barb and crumatá fish
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- equal parts water and wine in sufficient quantity to cover the fish
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- cloves
- parsley
- thyme

## Directions

1. Clean and wash one fish, but without removing the scales
2. Rub with salt, pepper and vinegar
3. Put in a pan equal parts of water and wine in sufficient quantity to cover the fish
4. Add a little salt, pepper, onions, cloves, parsley and thyme
5. When this broth is boiling, add the fish cut in strips
6. Once cooked, scale it, removing the skin with the scales
7. Serve with pirão made from the broth
