## Ingredients

- one chicken, cut in pieces
- half a pound of bacon
- half a pound of ham
- one onion
- two cloves
- one bay leaf
- a little nutmeg
- salt
- peppers
- one cup of water
- one glass of wine

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken pieces in half a pound of bacon, half a pound of ham, one onion, two cloves, one bay leaf, a little nutmeg, salt, peppers, one cup of water, and one glass of wine
