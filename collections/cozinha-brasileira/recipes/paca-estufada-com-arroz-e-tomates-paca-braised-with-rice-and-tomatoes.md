## Ingredients

- one paca
- boiling water
- cold water
- chicken meat
- mushrooms
- cassava flour
- one onion

## Directions

1. Put a paca in a pan and pour boiling water over it and having placed the pan on the fire, let it boil once, then remove the paca, which is then placed in cold water
2. Separately, chop the meat of two chickens, a portion of mushrooms, another of cassava flour and one onion
