## Ingredients

- calf offal
- water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- one onion
- two tablespoons wheat flour
- same amount of butter
- one cup broth

## Directions

1. Boil the offal after washing it in water and salt
2. Add pepper, parsley and one onion
3. When almost cooked, add two tablespoons of wheat flour mixed with the same amount of butter, dissolved in one cup of broth
4. Let it boil for half an hour and serve with cornmeal mush or porridge
