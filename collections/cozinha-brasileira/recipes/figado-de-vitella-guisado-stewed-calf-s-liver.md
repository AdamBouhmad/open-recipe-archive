## Ingredients

- calf liver
- melted hot butter
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one onion
- one tablespoon white wine
- salt
- pepper
- cooking herbs

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into slices and place in a casserole with melted hot butter
2. Let it fry on both sides
3. Then add one tablespoon of wheat flour, a cut onion, stir everything
4. Add one tablespoon of white wine, salt, pepper, cooking herbs and let it boil over moderate heat
