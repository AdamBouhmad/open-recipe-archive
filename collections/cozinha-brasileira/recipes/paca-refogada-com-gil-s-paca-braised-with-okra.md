## Ingredients

- pieces of paca meat
- fat
- two tablespoons of wheat flour
- a dozen tomatoes
- water
- salt
- peppers
- grated ginger
- one chopped onion
- okra

## Directions

1. Braise some pieces of paca meat in fat
2. When fried, remove them and add to the same fat two tablespoons of wheat flour and a dozen tomatoes
3. Stir until they brown, add one bottle of water, salt, peppers, grated ginger, one chopped onion and the paca meat
4. Let it boil until cooked and the broth is reduced to a few tablespoons, and serve with okra blanched in water and salt
