## Ingredients

- lamb leg
- cloth
- twine
- water
- onions
- bay leaves
- parsley
- marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- saltpeter
- vegetables
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Wrap a lamb leg in a well-sealed cloth, tying it firmly with twine
2. Place in a pan that should be on the fire with boiling water
3. Add onions, bay leaves, parsley, marjoram, salt, pepper and a pinch of saltpeter
4. Let it cook for two hours
5. Serve after removing the cloth, with vegetables, potatoes or one of the sauces indicated for meat
