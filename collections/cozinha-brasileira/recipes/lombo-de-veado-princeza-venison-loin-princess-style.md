## Ingredients

- venison loin
- salt pork
- white wine
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- nutmeg
- ginger
- cloves from India
- butter
- bread crumbs
- white wine

## Directions

1. Take the venison loin, lard it with thin strips of salt pork, and soak it in white wine with salt, parsley, pepper, nutmeg, ginger and cloves from India
2. After three days, remove it, having wrapped it in paper greased with butter
3. Roast it on a spit and when roasted, remove the paper and wrap it in bread crumbs
4. Pass it through melted butter and let it roast until it takes on good color
5. Serve with the gravy that dripped, to which one chalice of white wine is added; give it a boil and strain before serving
