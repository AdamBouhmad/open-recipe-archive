## Ingredients

- a piece of tapir meat
- salt
- banana or caeté leaves
- lemon juice
- comaris peppers

## Directions

1. Take a piece of tapir meat and cut it into fine slices
2. Season with salt
3. Wrap each slice in banana or caeté leaves
4. Place in the embers until cooked
5. Serve with a sauce of lemon juice and comaris peppers
