## Ingredients

- two chickens, cut into four pieces
- butter
- two spoons of wheat flour
- one cup of wine
- one cup of water
- two spoons of sugar
- salt
- cinnamon
- one yam root, sliced and pre-cooked for two to three hours in water and salt

## Directions

1. Brown the chicken pieces in butter; remove them
2. In the same butter, braise two spoons of wheat flour until it becomes a light golden color
3. Add one cup of wine and one cup of water
4. Add two spoons of sugar, salt, and cinnamon
5. Return the chicken pieces to the sauce along with the pre-cooked yam root slices
6. Let everything simmer for half an hour and serve
