## Ingredients

- Beets
- Salt
- Sweet oil
- Vinegar
- Ground pepper
- Onion
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel one or two beet roots, grate them and season with salt, sweet oil, vinegar, ground pepper, a finely chopped onion and a portion of grated nutmeg
2. Serve
