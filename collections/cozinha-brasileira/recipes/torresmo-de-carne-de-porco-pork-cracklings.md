## Ingredients

- a piece of fresh and fatty pork
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a piece of fresh and fatty pork and cut it into small pieces
2. Put all these pieces in a vessel with a small quantity of water with salt, and increase the heat
3. When the water evaporates, continue to press the pieces with a skimming ladle so that they release all the water
4. When the cracklings are well cooked and have achieved a nice colour, remove them from the vessel and place them to drain before serving
