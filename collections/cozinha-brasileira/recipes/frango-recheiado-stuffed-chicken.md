## Ingredients

- one chicken
- chicken giblets
- a handful of carapicús
- half dozen sardines
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- one onion
- two cloves of India
- water
- garlic
- sweet oil
- vinegar
- bacon strips

## Directions

1. Open the chicken along the back and remove the bones
2. Fill it with giblets, chopped carapicús, half dozen sardines, little salt, pepper, parsley, one onion, and two cloves of India
3. Soak it in water with garlic, salt, pepper, sweet oil, and vinegar
4. After four to five hours have passed, wrap it in bacon strips and roast on the grill, serving it with its own sauce
