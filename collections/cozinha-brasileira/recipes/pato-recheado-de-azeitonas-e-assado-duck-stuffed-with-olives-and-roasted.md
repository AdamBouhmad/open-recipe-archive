## Ingredients

- duck
- washed olives
- palm hearts, chopped to same size as olives
- bacon slices
- peppers
- onion leaves
- parsley
- manjerona (marjoram)

## Directions

1. Fill the duck with washed olives and palm hearts chopped to the same size
2. Place in a casserole on bacon slices
3. Add peppers, onion leaves, parsley, and manjerona
4. Put in the oven and roast until cooked
5. Serve with its sauce and salads
