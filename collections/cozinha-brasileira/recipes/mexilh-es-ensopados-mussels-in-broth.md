## Ingredients

- mussels
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- wheat flour
- pepper
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the mussels with a hard brush
2. Open them and remove the water by passing through a strainer
3. Reunite the meat in one shell and place in a pan with a little butter, parsley and chopped onion
4. When the butter is melted, add the water from the mussels, dust with half a tablespoon of wheat flour and a little pepper
5. Heat without letting the water boil
6. When serving, add three egg yolks diluted in one glass of lemon juice and send to table
