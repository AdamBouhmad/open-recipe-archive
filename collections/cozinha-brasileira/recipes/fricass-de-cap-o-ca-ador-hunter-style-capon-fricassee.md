## Ingredients

- meat from one capon roasted on a spit
- two pounds of roasted wild pork meat
- one tablespoon of butter
- nutmeg
- pepper
- onion
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- two chicaras of red wine
- one glass of cognac
- one tablespoon of honey
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from one capon roasted on a spit and cut into pieces the size of a walnut
2. Do the same with two pounds of roasted wild pork meat
3. Melt one tablespoon of butter with grated nutmeg, a pinch of ground pepper, and chopped onion
4. Add the meat and stir
5. Add one tablespoon of vinegar, two chicaras of red wine, one glass of cognac, one tablespoon of honey, and two beaten egg yolks
6. Simmer for a quarter hour and serve
