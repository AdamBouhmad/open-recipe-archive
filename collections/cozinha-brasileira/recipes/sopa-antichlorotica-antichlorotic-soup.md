## Ingredients

- pork
- beef
- manioc
- hominy
- salsify root
- celery
- turnips
- carrots
- egg
- turmeric
- sugar
- Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Put a piece of pork and a piece of beef on the fire to cook
2. Cook manioc, hominy, salsify root, celery, turnips and carrots in another pot with water and salt
3. Remove these ingredients once cooked and add them to the meat broth
4. The meat broth should be skimmed several times and should be finely minced
5. Thicken with a beaten egg mixed with a pinch of Indian turmeric
6. Add sugar and a glass of Madeira wine
