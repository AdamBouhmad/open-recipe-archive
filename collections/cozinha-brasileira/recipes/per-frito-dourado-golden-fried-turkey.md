## Ingredients

- turkey pieces
- wheat flour
- butter
- egg yolks
- white wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Coat the turkey pieces in wheat flour and fry them on both sides in a little butter
2. When cooked, pour over them by the spoon a mixture of four egg yolks, one spoon of wheat flour, one glass of wine with grated nutmeg
3. After a little time, serve
