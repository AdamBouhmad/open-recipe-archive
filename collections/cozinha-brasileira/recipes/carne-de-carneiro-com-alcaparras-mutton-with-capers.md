## Ingredients

- mutton meat, cut into pieces
- water
- butter
- bay leaves
- pepper from India
- small onions
- salt
- sardines, chopped
- capers
- lemon slices
- breadcrumb

## Directions

1. Cut the mutton meat into pieces and simmer in little water, butter, bay leaves, pepper from India, small onions and salt
2. A short time before it is cooked, add some chopped sardines, capers, slices of lemon and grated breadcrumb, and serve
