## Ingredients

- one or two eels
- two onions
- one potato or carrots
- parsley
- thyme
- salt
- peppers
- two bowls of water
- one bottle of white wine
- manioc flour or breadcrumbs
- butter
- paper

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from one or two eels and place them in a pot with two onions, one potato or carrots, parsley, thyme, salt, peppers, two bowls of water and one bottle of white wine
2. Let it boil for half an hour, remove the pot from the fire and let it cool
3. Remove the fish and cover them with a layer of manioc flour or breadcrumbs
4. Tie them to a spit wrapping them in paper greased with butter
5. Roast them over regular heat for half an hour, remove the paper and serve with the sauce well reduced
