## Ingredients

- veal breast
- water
- salt
- wine
- lemon juice
- breadcrumbs
- nutmeg
- butter
- shellfish

## Directions

1. Boil the breast in water and salt
2. Remove the skin
3. In a casserole, place a cup of wine, lemon juice, breadcrumbs, a little nutmeg and butter
4. Let it boil a little
5. Add the breast meat and the shellfish
6. Serve when cooked
