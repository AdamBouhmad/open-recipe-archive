## Ingredients

- a piece of bacon
- some carrots
- two onions
- some cups of meat sauce stock
- pepper
- nutmeg
- peppercorn from India
- bay leaves
- parsley
- salt
- 3 spoons of wheat flour
- 1 spoon of fat
- 1 cup of cream

## Directions

1. Fry some carrots and two onions with a piece of bacon
2. Add some cups of meat sauce stock, pepper, nutmeg, peppercorn from India, bay leaves, parsley and salt
3. Let it boil for an hour, strain it
4. Fry 3 spoons of wheat flour in a spoon of fat and add to the sauce
5. Add 1 cup of cream, let it boil a little more, and serve
