## Ingredients

- goose pieces
- lard
- water squash, peeled
- water
- wine
- parsley
- salt
- onion leaves

## Directions

1. Take the goose pieces, sauté them in three spoons of lard, turning them until evenly roasted
2. Remove them and put in the same lard a portion of peeled water squash, with the core removed and cut into small pieces
3. Stir them until well coated with lard, and add to them one cup of water, another of wine and the goose pieces, parsley, salt and onion leaves
4. Place near the fire to finish cooking and serve
