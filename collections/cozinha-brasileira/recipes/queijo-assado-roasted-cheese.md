## Ingredients

- slices of half-cured cheese
- lard or fat
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Thread some slices of half-cured cheese on a spit of bread
2. Coat with a little lard
3. Roast over coals on both sides
4. Serve hot, sprinkling with sugar and cinnamon
5. It can also be roasted on a grill
