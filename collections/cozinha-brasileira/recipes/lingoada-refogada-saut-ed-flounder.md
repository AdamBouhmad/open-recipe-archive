## Ingredients

- one flounder
- two spoons butter
- parsley
- onions
- salt
- peppers
- nutmeg
- two cups white wine

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and place in a casserole with two spoons of butter
2. Add chopped parsley, cut onions, salt, peppers and nutmeg
3. Turn the fish and add two cups of white wine
4. Let it boil over coals and serve when cooked
