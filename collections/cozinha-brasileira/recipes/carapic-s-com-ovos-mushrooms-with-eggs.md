## Ingredients

- carapicús (mushrooms)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- ovos (eggs)
- gordura (fat)
- vinho branco (white wine)
- pimenta (pepper)
- cebola (onion)

## Directions

1. Take a portion of carapicús boiled in water and salt
2. Drain them, mixing them with four eggs
3. Place them in a pan with fat to fry
4. Mix them and when the mixture begins to coagulate, add a cup of white wine, salt, pepper, and a diced onion
5. Let it boil for half an hour and serve
