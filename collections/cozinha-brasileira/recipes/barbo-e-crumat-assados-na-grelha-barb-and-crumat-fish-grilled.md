## Ingredients

- barb and crumatá fish
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- cow butter
- breadcrumbs
- one onion
- cow butter for frying

## Directions

1. Open the fish along the entire length, keeping it secure on one side with the other by the skin of the back
2. Brush with salt, pepper, and chopped parsley
3. Cover with a layer of cow butter and grill over a very moderate fire
4. Place on a plate and serve with breadcrumbs and one onion chopped and fried in cow butter
