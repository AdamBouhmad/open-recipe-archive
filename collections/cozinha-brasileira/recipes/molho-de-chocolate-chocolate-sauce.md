## Ingredients

- Butter
- Egg yolks
- Chocolate powder
- White wine
- Sugar
- Lemon zest
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Let a little butter melt
2. After it is almost melted, add three egg yolks, two spoonfuls of chocolate powder, and as much white wine as is necessary for the required quantity of sauce
3. Add a little sugar, lemon zest and ground cinnamon
4. Boil everything, stirring constantly, over coals
5. Serve for puddings and pastas
