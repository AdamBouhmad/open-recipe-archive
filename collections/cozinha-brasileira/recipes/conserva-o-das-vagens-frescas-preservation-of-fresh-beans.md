## Ingredients

- fresh beans
- water

## Directions

1. Pick the beans on a sunny day; select the most ripened and perfect ones, place in a wide-mouth glass jar; when the jar is full, place it to boil in a pot with water, letting the water reach to the mouth of the jar; let it boil for half an hour and seal the jar well; the beans can thus keep fresh for a year and more.
