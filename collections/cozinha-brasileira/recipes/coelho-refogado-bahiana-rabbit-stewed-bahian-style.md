## Ingredients

- rabbit
- gordura
- wheat flour
- broth
- orange juice
- comari peppers
- pimentos
- ginger
- salt
- parsley
- carapicús

## Directions

1. After cleaning and cutting the rabbit, place it in a casserole with four spoonfuls of hot fat
2. When browned, remove it, lightly coat it in wheat flour, and put it back in the casserole with one cup of broth, another of orange juice, some comari peppers, two pimentos, a little grated ginger, salt, parsley, a portion of selected carapicús, and the fat in which the rabbit was fried
3. Boil a little and when well cooked, serve it
