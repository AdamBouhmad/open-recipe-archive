## Ingredients

- puff pastry dough
- twenty egg whites
- two pounds sugar
- lemon rinds
- vanilla
- one chalice of orange flower water
- one chalice of cognac
- fruits for decoration
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a form of puff pastry for pastries, three to four fingers high
2. Beat twenty egg whites with two pounds of sugar, lemon rinds, vanilla, one chalice of orange flower water and another of cognac
3. Continue beating until a very hard mass is formed
4. Place the form in a very gentle oven and bake
5. Decorate with fruits and melted sugar beaten with two egg yolks
6. Pour the sugar into a vessel with a hole below to form flowers or letters
7. Then cover the tart with a lid with hot coals to harden the sugar
