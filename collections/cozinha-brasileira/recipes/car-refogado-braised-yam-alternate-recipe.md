## Ingredients

- some yams
- bitter orange or lemon
- cold water
- hot fat
- onions
- salt
- garlic
- Indian peppers

## Directions

1. Peel some yams
2. Wash them with bitter orange or lemon and then in cold water
3. Break them into small pieces and put them in a pan with hot fat, onions, salt, garlic and Indian peppers
4. Add a little water, enough to cook the yam
5. Stir from time to time to thicken the broth and serve
