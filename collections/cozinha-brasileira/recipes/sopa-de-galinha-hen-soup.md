## Ingredients

- 1 fat hen
- 3 spoons of fat
- 8 jars of water
- parsley
- onion leaves
- 1 clove of garlic
- peppers from India
- corn flour or cassava flour

## Directions

1. Clean the hen and cut it into pieces
2. Fry it in 3 spoons of fat
3. Add 8 jars of water
4. Add parsley, onion leaves, a clove of garlic, and peppers from India
5. Pour the broth over corn flour or cassava flour placed in a dish and mix everything well
6. Serve the hen meat in a separate plate
