## Ingredients

- four squabs
- lemon juice
- salt
- two tablespoons butter
- one glass wine
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- one cup cream
- four tablespoons sugar
- one sweet orange
- one glass white wine

## Directions

1. Take four squabs and rub with lemon juice and a little salt
2. Cook with two tablespoons butter, one glass of wine, cinnamon and nutmeg
3. When cooked, remove them and mix with one cup of cream, four tablespoons sugar, the juice of one sweet orange and one glass of white wine
4. Allow the mixture to boil once and serve with the squabs
