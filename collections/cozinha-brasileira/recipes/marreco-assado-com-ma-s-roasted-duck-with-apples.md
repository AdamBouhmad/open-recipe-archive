## Ingredients

- one clean duck
- two slices of bacon
- one onion
- two cloves
- one bay leaf
- salt
- pepper
- one cup of broth
- pulp of ten to twelve apples
- two dozen raisins
- two spoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Take a clean duck and boil it for half an hour, wrapped in two slices of bacon, with one onion, two cloves, one bay leaf, salt, pepper and one cup of broth
2. Remove the duck and strain the broth, mixing it with the pulp of ten to twelve apples, two dozen raisins and two spoonfuls of sugar
3. Serve this pulp on a plate with the duck on top
