## Ingredients

- heart of palm
- cow's butter
- wheat flour
- salt
- cloves
- one cup of milk

## Directions

1. Chop the heart of palm into small pieces and fry in cow's butter
2. Add wheat flour, salt, cloves and one cup of milk
3. Let boil over moderate heat and serve
