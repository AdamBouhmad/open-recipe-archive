## Ingredients

- turkey
- salt
- garlic
- onion
- lemon juice
- cooked and peeled pine nuts
- cooked eggs
- half dozen bananas
- lemon slices
- bacon

## Directions

1. Soak the turkey, brushed with salt, garlic, onion and lemon juice, for four hours.
2. Then fill it with cooked and peeled pine nuts, cut cooked eggs, half a dozen sliced bananas and peeled lemon slices without seeds.
3. Place it in a pan lined with bacon and roast it.
4. When cooked, serve it with its own sauce after removing the fat.
