## Ingredients

- one guinea fowl
- milk
- salt
- butter
- wheat flour
- two egg yolks
- one spoonful of sugar
- nutmeg
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil one of these birds in milk and salt
2. When cooked, remove and cut it into pieces that are braised in butter, and dusted with wheat flour
3. When the milk is reduced, incorporate it with two egg yolks, one spoonful of sugar, nutmeg and ground cinnamon; place the bird once more in this sauce
4. Let it boil a little more and serve
