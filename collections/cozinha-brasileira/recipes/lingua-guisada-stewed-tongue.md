## Ingredients

- beef tongue, already boiled and cleaned
- one cup of broth
- half bottle of wine
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, chopped
- two egg yolks
- broth
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take an already boiled and cleaned tongue and cut it into thin slices
2. Boil for one hour over moderate heat in one cup of broth and half a bottle of wine with little salt and pepper
3. Place the pieces on a serving platter
4. Add to the sauce a little chopped parsley and two egg yolks diluted in a little broth and vinegar
5. Let it boil once and pour the sauce over the slices before serving
