## Ingredients

- fifty chestnuts or pine nuts
- water
- beef or hen broth
- butter
- sugar
- bread
- onion

## Directions

1. After peeling fifty chestnuts, put them to boil in a little water to facilitate removing the skin
2. Then boil in beef or hen broth, adding a little butter and sugar
3. After two hours, pour the broth with the chestnuts into a tureen already containing toasted bread
4. Previously add to the broth a fried onion and butter
5. The same way is made the pine nut soup
