## Ingredients

- one flounder
- sea water
- parsley
- butter
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and boil in sea water
2. When cooked, remove and dry
3. Place on a plate and sprinkle with chopped parsley
4. Pour melted butter on top
5. Serve with potatoes boiled in the same water
