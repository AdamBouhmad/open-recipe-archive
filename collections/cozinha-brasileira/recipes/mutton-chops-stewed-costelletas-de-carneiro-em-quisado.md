## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- wheat flour
- butter
- water
- one onion
- one handful of carapicús
- quarter of scalded raisins
- two spoons of sugar
- one spoon of wheat flour fried in butter
- one dozen sapucaia fruits, peeled and cut in long pieces

## Directions

1. Beat the chops and season with salt, pepper, and chopped parsley.
2. Coat in wheat flour and fry on both sides in butter.
3. Add a bowl of water and let it boil over coals with an onion and a handful of carapicús.
4. When cooked, add the scalded raisins, two spoons of sugar, one spoon of flour fried in butter, and the sapucaia fruits cut in long pieces.
5. Serve when ready.
