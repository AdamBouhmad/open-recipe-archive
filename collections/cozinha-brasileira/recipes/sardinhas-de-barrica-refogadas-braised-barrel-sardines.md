## Ingredients

- Sardines
- Fat
- Tapioca flour
- Peppers
- One teacup of vinegar
- Water

## Directions

1. Scald a portion of sardines
2. Put them in cold water and leave to soak for four to six hours
3. Wash and scrape them to clean well
4. Sauté in fat
5. When almost fried, dust with tapioca flour and a little pepper
6. Add one teacup of vinegar and the same amount of water
7. Let boil a little more and serve
