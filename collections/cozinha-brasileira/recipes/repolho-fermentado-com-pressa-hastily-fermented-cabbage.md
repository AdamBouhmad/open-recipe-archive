## Ingredients

- one head of cabbage
- salt
- one cup of vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop and season a cabbage with salt
2. Place it in a pot with one cup of vinegar
3. Compress with a weight and leave for twenty-four hours
4. Afterwards, sauté it as already explained for fermented cabbage
