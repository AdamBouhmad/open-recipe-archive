## Ingredients

- fish
- nutmeg
- half a bottle of white wine
- four to five tablespoons of sweet oil
- juice of one lemon
- two tablespoons of capers
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- one tablespoon of butter

## Directions

1. Thread the fish lengthwise onto a spit, tie the head and tail to keep them secure
2. Roast over a regular fire, moistening from time to time with a sauce made of half a bottle of white wine, four to five tablespoons of sweet oil, and the juice of one lemon
3. When the fish is roasted, remove it from the spit and place it on a plate
4. Serve alongside in a tureen the sauce after adding two tablespoons of capers, salt, pepper, one tablespoon of wheat flour beaten with one tablespoon of fried butter
