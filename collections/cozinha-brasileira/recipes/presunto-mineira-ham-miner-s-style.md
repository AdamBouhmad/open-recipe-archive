## Ingredients

- one ham leg
- water
- salt
- scraped rapadura or muscovado sugar
- equal parts saltpeter
- wine and vinegar in equal parts

## Directions

1. Take a ham leg, and after cutting and trimming it well, place it in a deep vessel covered with fresh water, keeping it there for three to four days
2. At the end of this time, remove it from the water, drain it, and cover it with a sufficient quantity of salt, where it stays for another three days
3. Remove it from there again, place it on a board, rub it with scraped rapadura or muscovado sugar mixed with equal parts of saltpeter, taking care to prick it with the tip of a knife so that the rapadura and saltpeter can be introduced
4. After finishing this process, preserve it in a vessel for another four days
5. From there, place it on a board, putting another board on top with a weight to give it a flattened shape and to make any remaining liquid drain out
6. The next day, place it inside a sack and hang it in smoke for three weeks
7. After this time, rub it with wine and vinegar in equal parts and store it in a dry place
