## Ingredients

- fish
- milk
- wheat flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, scald them with milk, roll them in wheat flour and fry them in butter on both sides until a good color.
2. Alternatively, clean them and pass them one by one through two beaten eggs mixed with one tablespoon of butter, then roll them in grated bread before frying.
