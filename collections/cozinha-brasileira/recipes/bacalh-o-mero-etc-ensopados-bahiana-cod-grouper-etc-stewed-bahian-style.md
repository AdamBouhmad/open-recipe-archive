## Ingredients

- fish, cut into large steaks
- water
- salt
- pepper
- one onion
- ginger
- two spoons of fat
- one dozen tomatoes
- pimentas (peppers), chopped

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into large steaks and boil for half an hour with water, salt, pepper, one onion and ginger
2. Remove the fish
3. Melt two spoons of fat with a dozen tomatoes and chopped peppers
4. Add the strained and reduced broth
5. Serve the fish with this sauce
