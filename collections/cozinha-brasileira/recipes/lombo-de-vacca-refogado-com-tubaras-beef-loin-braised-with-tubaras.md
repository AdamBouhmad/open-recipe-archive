## Ingredients

- beef loin
- tubaras (a type of vegetable)
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the loin into pieces the size of a square inch and place them in a casserole greased with butter. Cut in the same manner half a dozen tubaras which are added to the meat and covered with melted butter.
2. After everything is cooked, drain the butter; pouring onto a platter the meat and tubaras, pour over it a sauce.
