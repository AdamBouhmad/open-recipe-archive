## Ingredients

- medium-thick beefsteak
- butter
- wheat flour
- broth
- herbs
- onion
- carrots
- salt
- pepper
- rice flour
- paprika powder

## Directions

1. Fry well on both sides a medium-thick beefsteak.
2. Remove it from the skillet and put in the butter one spoon of wheat flour.
3. Stir until it becomes a beautiful golden color and pour on it a little broth, enough to completely cover the steak, placed again in the skillet.
4. When this sauce begins to boil, add to it a bunch of aromatic herbs, one onion and chopped carrots.
5. Season with salt and pepper and let the meat boil gently for two to three hours.
6. Half an hour before serving, add one spoon of rice flour, a little paprika powder and two small heads of onion chopped and fried separately in butter.
