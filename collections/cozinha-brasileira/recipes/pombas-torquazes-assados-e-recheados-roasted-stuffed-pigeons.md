## Ingredients

- pigeons
- pigeon livers
- bacon
- chicken livers from two chickens
- olives without pits
- peppers
- half a garlic clove
- banana leaves

## Directions

1. Stuff the pigeons with their livers chopped and mixed with half a quarter of bacon, the livers of two chickens, some pitted olives, peppers and half a garlic clove, all chopped
2. Lard the breast with thin strips of bacon
3. Wrap in banana leaves and roast over moderate fire
4. When cooked, serve with some spicy sauce
