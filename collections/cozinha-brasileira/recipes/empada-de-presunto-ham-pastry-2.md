## Ingredients

- ham
- water
- a little lemon juice
- pastry casing

## Directions

1. Take the ham and after removing the skin, soak it in water with a little lemon juice
2. After twenty-four hours have passed, remove it, dry it and wrap it in a pastry casing for empadas
3. Go to the oven and bake for two to three hours depending on the size of the ham
4. Serve with the same casing
