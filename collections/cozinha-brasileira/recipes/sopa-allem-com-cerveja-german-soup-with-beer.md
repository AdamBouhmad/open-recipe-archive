## Ingredients

- hard bread crust
- sugar
- butter
- beer
- two egg yolks
- caraway

## Directions

1. Grate the crust of hard bread and put it with sugar and a little butter in a soup tureen
2. Pour boiled beer over it thickened with two egg yolks and a little caraway
