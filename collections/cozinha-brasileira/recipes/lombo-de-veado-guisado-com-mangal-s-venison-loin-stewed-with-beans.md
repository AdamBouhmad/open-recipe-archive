## Ingredients

- venison loin
- thin strips of bacon
- water
- salt
- parsley
- pimento pepper
- onion leaves
- ginger
- portion of beans
- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- one cup of sour orange juice
- one tablespoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Lard the loin with thin strips of bacon
2. Boil it in water, salt, parsley, pimento pepper, onion leaves and ginger
3. Put it on the spit and roast over strong fire until almost roasted
4. Cook in the broth a portion of beans, adding to them one tablespoon of wheat flour dissolved in one cup of sour orange juice, one tablespoon of sugar, until the broth is reduced and the beans become almost dry
5. Then add the sauce that dripped from the roasted loin and serve, pouring the beans on top of the loin
