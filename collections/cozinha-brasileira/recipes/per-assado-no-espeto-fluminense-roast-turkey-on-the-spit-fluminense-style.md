## Ingredients

- turkey
- salt
- ground pepper
- onion
- parsley
- lemon juice
- giblets from four chickens
- carapicús
- half pound bacon
- three egg yolks
- nutmeg
- bacon slices
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the turkey with salt, ground pepper, onion and finely chopped parsley.
2. Wrap it in cloth and moisten it with lemon juice.
3. After twenty-four hours, remove the cloth and fill it with the giblets from four chickens, a handful of carapicús, one onion, half a pound of bacon, all finely chopped.
4. Add three egg yolks, salt and nutmeg, and fill the turkey with the mixture.
5. Spit it and roast it, wrapped in very fine bacon slices.
6. When cooked, remove the rest of the bacon, wrap the turkey in grated bread and continue roasting it until it takes on sufficient color.
7. Serve with its sauce after removing the fat.
