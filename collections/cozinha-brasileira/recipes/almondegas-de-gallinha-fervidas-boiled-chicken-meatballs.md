## Ingredients

- meat of one chicken
- one pound of veal
- quarter pound of lard
- salt
- clove
- nutmeg
- four spoonfuls of wheat flour
- six egg yolks
- one spoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. Chop the meat of one chicken, one pound of veal, a quarter pound of lard
2. Add to them salt, clove, nutmeg, four spoonfuls of wheat flour, six egg yolks and one spoonful of sugar
3. Mix well and form balls
4. Cook them in boiling water
5. Remove them and serve with a cream sauce, the one already taught for the golden chicken meatballs
