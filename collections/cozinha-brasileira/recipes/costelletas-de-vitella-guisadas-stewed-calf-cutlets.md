## Ingredients

- calf cutlets
- fine bacon for larding
- water
- salt
- pepper
- cabbage leaves
- onion

## Directions

1. With the cutlets cut, lard them with fine bacon and place in some water that is already boiling
2. Add salt, pepper, some cabbage leaves, onion and let cook
3. Remove the cutlets and strain the broth which is left to reduce and from which the fat is removed and then served over them
