## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- water
- salt
- half an ounce of cumin
- breadcrumbs
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Take a lamb shoulder and cut the meat into pieces
2. Scald these pieces in hot water and place them on the fire with cold water
3. Remove the foam and add some salt, half an ounce of cumin tied in a little bag and breadcrumbs
4. When cooked, serve with vegetables
