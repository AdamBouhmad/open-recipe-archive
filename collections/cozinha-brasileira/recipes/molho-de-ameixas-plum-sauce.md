## Ingredients

- Plums
- Cornstarch
- Cinnamon
- Lemon zest
- Water
- White or red wine
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Pass a portion of plums through a sieve, removing the pits
2. Add a spoonful of cornstarch, cinnamon and lemon zest
3. Let it boil for one hour with little water
4. Strain, squeezing it
5. Add to the juice an equal amount of white or red wine and a little sugar
6. Let it boil for a little while
7. Serve with all puddings and pastas of all kinds
