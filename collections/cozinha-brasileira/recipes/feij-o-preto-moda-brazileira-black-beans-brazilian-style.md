## Ingredients

- black beans
- water
- fat
- onion leaves
- garlic
- salt

## Directions

1. Choose and wash a portion of black beans, drain and put to boil in water for six to eight hours, adding hot water from time to time as needed.
2. Once cooked, drain the water.
3. In another part, melt two spoonfuls of fat, add some onion leaves, a clove of garlic and salt.
4. Then add the beans, stir with a wooden spoon, mash the beans well and add the broth that drains off.
5. Boil until the water almost dries and serve.
