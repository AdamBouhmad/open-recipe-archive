## Ingredients

- roasted veal meat
- meat broth
- raisins
- lemon slices
- dried breadcrumbs or crackers
- sugar
- one glass wine

## Directions

1. Cut the roasted meat into small pieces
2. Add the substance that comes with the meat and meat broth
3. Place over fire with raisins, lemon slices, ground dried breadcrumbs or crackers, a little sugar, and one glass wine
4. Give it another boil and serve
