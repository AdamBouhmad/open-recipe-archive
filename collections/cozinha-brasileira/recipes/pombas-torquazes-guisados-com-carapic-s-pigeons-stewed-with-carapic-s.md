## Ingredients

- four pigeons
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- coriander
- peppers
- three spoonfuls of fat
- carapicús
- six tomatoes
- one glass of vinegar
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil four pigeons in water, salt, parsley, onion leaves, coriander and some peppers
2. When cooked, cut into four pieces and fry in three spoonfuls of fat
3. Add some chopped carapicús and half a dozen tomatoes
4. After stirring, add one glass of vinegar and the broth reduced to three cups and mixed with two egg yolks
5. Let it boil for another quarter hour over coals and serve
