## Ingredients

- squash or pumpkin
- water
- salt
- lard
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut into pieces a squash or pumpkin
2. Remove the seeds and cook the pieces in water and salt
3. When cooked, scrape the pulp and sauté it in melted lard with salt, pepper and a glass of vinegar
4. Boil a little more and serve
