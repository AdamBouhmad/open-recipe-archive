## Ingredients

- half pound dried cod, finely chopped
- six eggs
- one tablespoon cassava flour
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- fat

## Directions

1. Chop half a pound of dried cod very finely and beat it with six eggs, one tablespoon of cassava flour, salt, pepper, parsley and chopped onion leaves.
2. Melt some fat in a frying pan.
3. Pour in the beaten mixture.
4. Fry on both sides until golden and serve.
