## Ingredients

- four veal feet
- water
- salt
- ham, chopped
- onions
- parsley
- pepper
- egg yolks
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- white wine
- cloves
- nutmeg
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the four veal feet in water and salt
2. Remove the bones without tearing the skin
3. Fill the space with ham chopped and mixed with onions, parsley, salt, pepper, egg yolks, bread crumb softened in milk
4. Thus prepared, place them to cook over moderate heat in white wine and some cloves of India, nutmeg, and a spoon of sugar
