## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- thick bacon
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- garlic juice
- beef broth
- small turnip, peeled
- onion
- garlic clove
- bay leaves
- two cloves
- brandy
- ham or beef tongue

## Directions

1. Remove the bone from the leg
2. Lard the cut sides with thick bacon seasoned with salt, pepper, chopped parsley and garlic juice
3. Cook the meat and lard the outside with strips of ham or beef tongue
4. Place in a pan lined with thin bacon slices, with a bowl full of beef broth, one small peeled turnip, one onion, one garlic clove, bay leaves, two cloves, salt, pepper, and a glass of brandy
5. Let cook on moderate heat from below and above
6. When cooked, serve with vegetables, pouring over it the strained and reduced broth
