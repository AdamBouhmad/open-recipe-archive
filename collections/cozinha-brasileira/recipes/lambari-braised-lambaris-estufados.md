## Ingredients

- Two spoons of butter
- Parsley chopped
- Lambaris
- Salt
- Pepper
- Carapicús
- Potatoes
- One glass of wine

## Directions

1. Put two spoons of butter with chopped parsley in a frying pan
2. Place the fish seasoned with salt and pepper, with chopped carapicús or sliced and peeled potatoes, and one glass of wine
3. Place over coals and let boil until the fish are cooked and the broth is reduced
4. Serve
