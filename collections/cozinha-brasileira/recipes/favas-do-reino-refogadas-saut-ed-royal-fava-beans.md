## Ingredients

- royal fava beans
- fat
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a portion of royal fava beans and boil them in water so that you can remove the skin that covers them.
2. By this method, squeezing between the fingers, it comes off easily.
3. In another part, fry a little fat, add half a bottle of water, salt, parsley, onion leaves and the cooked and peeled fava beans.
4. Boil for a quarter of an hour, add a glass of vinegar and serve.
