## Ingredients

- bread
- milk
- four eggs
- four spoonfuls of sugar
- butter
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut bread into slices
2. Soak them in milk for five to ten minutes
3. Remove and pass each slice through a mixture of four beaten eggs and four spoonfuls of sugar
4. Fry the coated slices on both sides in butter
5. Serve covered with sugar and ground cinnamon
