## Ingredients

- one rib
- salt
- vinegar
- juniper berries
- melted butter mixed with red wine
- breadcrumbs
- parsley
- chopped onion

## Directions

1. Take one rib and marinate it in salt, vinegar and juniper berries for three hours.
2. Thread it on the spit and roast it, basting with melted butter mixed with red wine.
3. When almost roasted, cover it with breadcrumbs, parsley and chopped onion.
4. When cooked and of good color, serve it with the sauce that dripped into the dripping pan.
