## Ingredients

- one sea eel
- sea water
- one sour orange
- two spoons butter
- one or two crabs
- one onion
- bay leaves
- cloves

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and cut the fish into pieces
2. Cook in sea water with one peeled and halved sour orange
3. When cooked, remove and sauté with two spoons of butter
4. Add one or two crabs crushed with shell, one onion, bay leaves and cloves
5. Strain this butter and pour over the fish
6. Serve
