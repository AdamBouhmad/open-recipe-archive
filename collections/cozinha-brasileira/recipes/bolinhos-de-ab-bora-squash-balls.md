## Ingredients

- squash
- water
- salt
- four eggs per pound of pulp
- four tablespoons of sugar per pound
- three tablespoons of wheat flour per pound
- fennel
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel and cut a squash into pieces and cook in water with salt
2. When cooked, pass through a sieve and mash each pound of this pulp with four eggs, four tablespoons of sugar and three tablespoons of wheat flour, fennel and cinnamon to taste
3. Form small balls with this mixture, fry them in fat and dust with sugar and cinnamon
4. Serve as a dessert
