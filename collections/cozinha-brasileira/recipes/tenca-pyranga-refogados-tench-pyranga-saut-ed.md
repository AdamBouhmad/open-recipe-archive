## Ingredients

- one head of water
- two chicaras of red wine
- salt
- pepper
- onion leaves
- parsley
- fish
- mustard
- sweet oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil one head of water, two chicaras of red wine, salt, pepper, onion leaves, parsley
2. Once boiling, add the cleaned and scaled fish
3. Once these are almost cooked, add one chicara of hot wine and serve cold with a mustard sauce, sweet oil, vinegar, finely chopped parsley
