## Ingredients

- four veal feet
- water
- salt
- wheat flour
- butter
- raisins
- almonds, peeled and cut lengthwise
- lemon slices, peeled and without seeds

## Directions

1. Clean the four veal feet well, blanch them, soaking them for some time
2. Then boil them in water and salt
3. Remove all the bones, strain the broth
4. Brown a little wheat flour in butter until it colors
5. Add the broth to it, some raisins, almonds peeled and cut lengthwise, some lemon slices peeled and without seeds
6. Let the veal feet meat finish cooking in this sauce and serve
