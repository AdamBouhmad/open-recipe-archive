## Ingredients

- one chicken
- chicken giblets
- ham
- bacon
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- half dozen peeled pine nuts
- bacon strips
- pine nuts for cooking

## Directions

1. Fill the chicken with a mixture of giblets, ham, bacon, parsley, salt, pepper, and peeled pine nuts, all finely chopped
2. Wrap it in bacon strips and cover with paper
3. Place with pine nuts to roast in the embers, putting coals on top
4. When roasted, peel the pine nuts and place on a plate
5. Place the chicken in the center
6. Remove the paper and bacon, and serve
