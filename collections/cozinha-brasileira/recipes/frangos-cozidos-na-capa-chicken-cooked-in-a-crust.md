## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- pepper
- chicken giblets
- onion
- hard-boiled eggs
- parsley
- wheat flour
- slices of bacon

## Directions

1. Take a chicken and rub it with salt and pepper
2. Fill it with the giblets, an onion, some hard-boiled eggs, salt and chopped pepper
3. Wrap it in slices of bacon
4. Cover with a good layer of wheat flour
5. Boil in salted water
6. When cooked, remove the bacon crust and serve with a spicy sauce
