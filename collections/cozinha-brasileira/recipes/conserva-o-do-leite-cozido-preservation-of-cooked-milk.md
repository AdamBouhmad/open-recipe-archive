## Ingredients

- milk
- crystallized soda

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of milk
2. Add to each bottle of milk one eighth of crystallized soda
3. Pour hot into bottles and cover well
4. Place these afterward in a pot with water and boil for half an hour
5. Remove the bottles and seal immediately
6. By this method the milk keeps fresh for one to two months
