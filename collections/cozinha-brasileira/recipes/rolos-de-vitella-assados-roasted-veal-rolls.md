## Ingredients

- veal meat, cut into thin slices
- salt
- pepper
- onion, chopped
- garlic
- parsley
- lemon juice
- paper
- butter
- mushroom sauce
- tubara sauce

## Directions

1. Cut veal meat into thin slices like for steaks
2. Beat them lightly and wrap in cloth, then marinate in salt, pepper, chopped onion, garlic, parsley and a little lemon juice for two hours
3. Then roll them and cover with strips of paper buttered, and thus prepared, place on a spit to roast and serve with mushroom and tubara sauce
