## Ingredients

- potatoes
- one tablespoon polished cornmeal
- one tablespoon sugar
- two beaten eggs
- one tablespoon butter
- salt
- half cup milk

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of potatoes, peel and mash well
2. Mix with one tablespoon of polished cornmeal, one tablespoon of sugar, two beaten eggs, one tablespoon of butter, salt and half cup of milk
3. Proceed as already described
