## Ingredients

- half a bushel of chosen rice
- one bottle of water
- pieces of lard
- salt
- parsley
- some peppers
- two onions
- one dorado head
- parsnips
- nutmeg
- a dozen tomatoes
- milk (optional)

## Directions

1. Put half a bushel of chosen rice in a pot of sufficient capacity with one bottle of water, some pieces of lard, salt, parsley, some peppers and two onions
2. Place a dorado head filled with chopped parsnips, seasoned with salt and nutmeg and mixed with a dozen tomatoes, on this rice
3. Cook for one hour over moderate heat until the rice is cooked and dried
4. Place the rice on a plate and put the head on top, pouring milk over it (if desired), and serve
