## Ingredients

- capon, cut into pieces
- two spoons of fat
- one bowl of water
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one spoon of polvilho (cassava starch) kneaded with two egg yolks
- two cups of milk
- one handful of ora-pro-nobis or two dozen okras
- cornmeal angú

## Directions

1. Brown the capon pieces in two spoons of fat
2. Once half-cooked, add one bowl of water, salt, pepper, cloves, parsley, onion leaves and one spoon of polvilho kneaded with two egg yolks dissolved in two cups of milk
3. Let cook, and at the end add one handful of ora-pro-nobis or two dozen okras cut up
4. Serve with cornmeal angú
