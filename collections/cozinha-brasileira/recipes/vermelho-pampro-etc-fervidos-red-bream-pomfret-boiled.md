## Ingredients

- one bottle of water
- one bottle of wine
- one onion
- bay leaves
- parsley
- salt
- peppers
- red bream, pomfret or similar fish
- sweet oil
- vinegar
- fish liver
- butter
- one egg yolk
- broth

## Directions

1. Place in a casserole a bottle of water, another of wine, a cut onion, bay leaves, parsley, salt and peppers
2. When boiling, add the well-cleaned fish
3. Let cook for a quarter hour and remove to serve
4. Serve with sweet oil and vinegar, or serve hot with a sauce made from the fish liver dissolved in vinegar and boiled with butter, one egg yolk and broth
