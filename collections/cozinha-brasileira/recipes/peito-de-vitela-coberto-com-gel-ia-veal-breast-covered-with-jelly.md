## Ingredients

- veal breast
- veal knuckle
- carrots
- salt
- pepper
- herb bouquet
- lemon

## Directions

1. Open the veal breast and fill with the stuffing indicated for braised breast
2. Cook with veal knuckle cut into pieces, carrots, salt, pepper and herb bouquet
3. When cooked, remove and pass the broth through a sieve
4. Remove the fat and let it thicken
5. Return the breast to the vessel and let it boil a little more
6. Place the breast on a platter, add some peeled lemon slices without seeds
7. Pour the broth over it and let it cool
8. Serve
