## Ingredients

- chicken pieces
- two tablespoons of fat
- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- water squash, chopped into small pieces
- one bottle of water
- salt
- parsley
- marjoram
- perennial onion leaves
- a little juice of sour orange

## Directions

1. Braise the chicken pieces in two tablespoons of fat until they acquire good color
2. Stir in one tablespoon of wheat flour
3. Add the water squash chopped into small pieces
4. Heat, stirring so it does not stick to the bottom of the pan
5. Add one bottle of water, salt, parsley, marjoram, perennial onion leaves, and a little juice of sour orange
6. Simmer over moderate heat until cooked
7. Serve
