## Ingredients

- suckling pig shoulder
- salt
- broken pepper
- sweet oil
- lemon juice
- tartar sauce

## Directions

1. Soak the shoulder in salt, broken pepper, sweet oil and lemon juice.
2. After two hours, roast it over a lively fire.
3. At the moment of serving, place tartar sauce on top.
