## Ingredients

- potatoes
- milk
- salt
- onion

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of peeled potatoes until well cooked
2. Drain the water and mash well until forming a homogeneous paste
3. Mix with a few spoonfuls of milk and salt
4. Place on a plate and pour a chopped onion on top
5. Serve
