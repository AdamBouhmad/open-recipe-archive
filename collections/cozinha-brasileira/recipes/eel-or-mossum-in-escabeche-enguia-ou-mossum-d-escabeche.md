## Ingredients

- Two to three small eels
- One bottle of water
- One bottle of white wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bay leaves
- Parsley
- Cow butter
- Olive oil
- Vinegar
- Parsley pounded

## Directions

1. Remove the skins from two to three small eels and cut into pieces
2. In another part, boil one bottle of water, one bottle of white wine, salt, pepper, bay leaves, parsley, and cow butter
3. Scald the fish with this broth and let soak for three hours
4. Then let boil for half an hour
5. Remove the fish and serve cold with a cold sauce of olive oil, vinegar and chopped parsley
