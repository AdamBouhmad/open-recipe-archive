## Ingredients

- lettuce leaves
- water
- salt
- hot fat
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take a portion of lettuce leaves, wash and scald them
2. Allow to drain and place them in a casserole with hot fat
3. Add salt, parsley, and half a bottle of water
4. When cooked, serve
