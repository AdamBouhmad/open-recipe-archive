## Ingredients

- one bird (arara, papagaio, etc.)
- the bird's giblets
- four cooked and chopped eggs
- salt
- pepper
- one chopped onion
- three spoonfuls of fat

## Directions

1. Stuff the bird with its giblets, four cooked and chopped eggs, salt, pepper, and one chopped onion.
2. Sew the opening and sauté the bird in three spoonfuls of fat, turning it to brown on all sides.
3. Cover the pan and let it finish roasting either in the oven or over coals.
4. When cooked, serve with some spicy sauce or salad.
