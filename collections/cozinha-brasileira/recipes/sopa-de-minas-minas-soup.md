## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- eggs
- cinnamon
- raisins
- manioc flour

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of milk with sugar
2. Add beaten eggs (white and yolk), cinnamon, and raisins
3. Pour this mixture over a little manioc flour
