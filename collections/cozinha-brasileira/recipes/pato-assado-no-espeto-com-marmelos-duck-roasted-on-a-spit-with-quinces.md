## Ingredients

- duck
- quinces, peeled and halved, seeds removed
- squash pieces, if quinces are unavailable
- vinegar
- parsley leaf
- cloves
- lemon juice
- salt
- garlic
- oiled paper

## Directions

1. Fill the duck with peeled quinces, halved and seeded, or squash pieces briefly stewed in vinegar with a parsley leaf and cloves if quinces are unavailable
2. Rub the duck with lemon juice, salt, and garlic
3. Place on a spit and roast, wrapped in oiled paper
4. Finally, remove the paper and let it take color
5. Serve with its sauce
