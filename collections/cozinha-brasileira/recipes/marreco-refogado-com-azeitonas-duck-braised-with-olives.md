## Ingredients

- one duck
- one quarter of bacon, cut in small pieces
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one cup of water or broth
- a portion of pitted and blanched olives

## Directions

1. Fry one duck with a quarter of bacon cut in small pieces, turning it until it takes a good color evenly
2. Add salt, pepper, parsley, onion leaves, one cup of water or broth, and finally a portion of pitted and blanched olives
3. Let it boil over low fire until the meat is cooked and serve
