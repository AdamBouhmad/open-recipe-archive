## Ingredients

- lamb breast
- bacon strips
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- celery root
- water
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- egg yolks
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the lamb breast in a pan on strips of bacon with pepper, salt, parsley and a celery root, so that it is covered with water
2. Let it boil until cooked
3. Remove the bones and cover with grated breadcrumbs moistened in butter
4. Roast on the grill and serve with a sauce made from the broth
5. Strain the broth, remove the fat and thicken with two egg yolks
6. Add one spoonful of vinegar
