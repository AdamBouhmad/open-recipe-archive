## Ingredients

- duck
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- garlic
- old wine
- butter
- one cup water
- one glass brandy
- one half tablespoon wheat flour
- carrots

## Directions

1. Cut the duck into pieces and soak in salt, pepper, lemon juice, and garlic
2. After three hours in this old wine, fry on both sides in a little butter
3. Add one cup of water, one glass of brandy, and one half tablespoon of wheat flour
4. After one boil, add sliced carrots
5. Let simmer near the fire until everything is cooked and serve
