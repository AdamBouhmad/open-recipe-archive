## Ingredients

- 1 half pound rice
- water
- salt
- saffron
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook half a pound of rice in water and salt
2. After the water has dried, pour into a bottle of boiling milk
3. Add a little saffron, salt, and sugar
4. Let boil for half an hour and serve
