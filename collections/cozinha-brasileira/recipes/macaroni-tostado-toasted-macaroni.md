## Ingredients

- macaroni
- good broth
- one onion
- cloves
- salt
- broken pepper
- slices of bread
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- Dutch or Minas cheese, finely grated

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of macaroni in good broth with an onion, cloves, a little salt and broken pepper.
2. When the macaroni is nearly cooked, remove from fire and place in a deep dish surrounded by slices of bread spread with butter.
3. Sprinkle breadcrumbs on top and a good layer of finely grated Dutch or Minas cheese.
4. Pour a little butter on top and brown with a hot iron.
