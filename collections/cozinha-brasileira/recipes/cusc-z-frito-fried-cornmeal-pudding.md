## Ingredients

- cooked cuscúz
- a little fat
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut the cuscúz into slices
2. Fry in a little fat
3. Dust both sides with sugar and cinnamon
4. Serve with roasted meats or tea
