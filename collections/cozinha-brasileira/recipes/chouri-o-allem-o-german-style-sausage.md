## Ingredients

- pork blood
- vinegar
- pork ears
- snout
- tongue
- heart
- lungs
- parts of belly skin with little fat
- water
- salt
- liver
- onions, chopped
- marjoram
- basil
- thyme
- pepper
- cloves
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Prepare the pork blood by placing a little vinegar in the vessel so it does not clot
2. Cut the ears, snout, tongue, heart, lungs and parts of belly skin that have little fat
3. Put everything to boil in a pan with water and salt
4. When everything is cooked, cut it into small pieces
5. Mix it with the blood and mashed liver, salt, chopped onions, marjoram, basil, thyme, pepper, cloves and nutmeg
6. Fill well-washed intestines with this mixture
7. After boiling, store them
