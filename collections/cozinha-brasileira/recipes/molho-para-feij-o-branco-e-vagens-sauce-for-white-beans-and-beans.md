## Ingredients

- six egg yolks
- one spoon of wheat flour
- one cup of vinegar
- three cups of water
- a little butter
- salt
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Mix six egg yolks with one spoon of wheat flour
2. Add one cup of vinegar and three cups of water
3. Add a little butter, salt and a little sugar
4. Boil and heat over coals, stirring until it boils
5. Pour over the cooked beans and serve
