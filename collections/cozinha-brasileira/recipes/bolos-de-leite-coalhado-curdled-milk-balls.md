## Ingredients

- ten tablespoons of fine corn meal
- two tablespoons of tapioca starch
- three eggs
- curdled milk

## Directions

1. Knead ten tablespoons of fine corn meal, two tablespoons of tapioca starch, three eggs, and curdled milk as much as needed to form a dough of good consistency
2. Form balls the size of an egg, cook in boiling water
3. Serve with sesame seeds or a similar topping
