## Ingredients

- some shrimp
- 1 spoon of fat or butter
- some spoons of meat sauce stock
- nutmeg
- pepper from India
- a little starch
- a little vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook some shrimp
2. After cooked, cut them and mix with 1 spoon of fat or butter
3. Fry a little
4. Add some spoons of meat sauce stock, nutmeg and pepper from India
5. Let it boil a little and strain
6. If desired, thicken the sauce with a little starch and a little vinegar, and serve
