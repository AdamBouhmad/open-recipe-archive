## Ingredients

- one loin
- salt
- lemon juice
- bacon for larding wrapped in salt and pepper
- one cup water
- one cup white wine
- carapicús (mushrooms)
- six tomatoes
- one chopped onion
- marjoram
- parsley

## Directions

1. Rub one loin with salt and lemon juice, and lard it with bacon wrapped in salt and pepper.
2. Place it in a pan on a slice of bacon with one cup of water, one cup of white wine, a portion of carapicús, six tomatoes, one chopped onion, marjoram and parsley.
3. Cover the pan with the lid and cook in the oven or over moderate heat.
4. In two to three hours it will be cooked, and then serve it over the carapicús with its sauce.
