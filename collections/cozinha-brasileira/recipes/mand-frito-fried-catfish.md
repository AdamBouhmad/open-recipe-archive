## Ingredients

- fish, cut one finger thick
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- wheat flour or corn flour
- chopped parsley
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into one-finger-thick steaks and sprinkle with salt and pepper
2. Fry in lard on both sides
3. Roll in wheat flour or corn flour mixed with chopped parsley and fry again
4. When golden, serve with lemon juice on top
