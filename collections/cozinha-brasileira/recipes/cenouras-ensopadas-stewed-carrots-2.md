## Ingredients

- some carrots
- water
- one spoon of butter
- one spoon of wheat flour
- two cups of white wine
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil some carrots in water and cut them into slices
2. On the other hand, melt one spoon of butter and another of wheat flour
3. Put the carrots in this mixture, add two cups of white wine, salt and pepper
4. Let it boil
