## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- bacon
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- water
- bacon pieces
- onions
- cabbage leaf
- parsley
- bay leaf
- chayote

## Directions

1. Remove the bone from the shoulder
2. Lard the meat with bacon seasoned with pepper, salt, and nutmeg
3. Roll the shoulder to give it an elongated shape
4. Cook it in water with small pieces of bacon, onions, a cabbage leaf, parsley, and bay leaf
5. Once cooked, remove the meat and strain the broth
6. Remove the fat and reduce it until only a few spoonfuls remain
7. Fry in it a portion of peeled chayote, cut into cubes and scalded in hot water
8. Serve with the shoulder
