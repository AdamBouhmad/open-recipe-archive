## Ingredients

- Lamb neck meat
- Melted fat
- Bowl of meat broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Onion leaves
- Potatoes
- Cará root
- Peas
- Beans or mangaritos

## Directions

1. Braise the neck meat in a little melted fat
2. Add a bowl of meat broth, salt, pepper, parsley, and onion leaves
3. Cook until done
4. Serve with stewed English potatoes, cará root, braised peas, roasted beans or mangaritos
