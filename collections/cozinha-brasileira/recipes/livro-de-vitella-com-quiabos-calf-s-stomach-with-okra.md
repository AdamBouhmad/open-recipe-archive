## Ingredients

- calf stomach
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- one dozen okras
- two egg yolks
- one tablespoon starch
- one tablespoon vinegar
- one cup cold water

## Directions

1. Clean the stomach well and cut it into small pieces
2. Boil in water, salt, parsley and onion
3. When almost cooked, add one dozen okras cut in slices
4. Let it finish cooking, and before removing from the fire add two egg yolks mixed with one tablespoon of starch, one of vinegar and one cup of cold water
5. Serve with cornmeal mush
