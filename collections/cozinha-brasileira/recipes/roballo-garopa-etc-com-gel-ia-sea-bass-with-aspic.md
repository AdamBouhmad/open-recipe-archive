## Ingredients

- Two or three pounds of beef
- Half pound of bacon
- Water
- Salt
- Parsley
- Two onions
- Cloves
- Peppers
- Two spoons of wheat flour
- One bottle of Champagne or good white wine
- Capers

## Directions

1. Boil two or three pounds of beef and half pound of bacon in water with salt, parsley, two onions, cloves and peppers
2. When well boiled, strain
3. Add two spoons of wheat flour, one bottle of Champagne or good white wine and a portion of capers
4. Place the fish over low heat for half an hour
5. Serve with its own broth reduced appropriately
