## Ingredients

- one bird
- liver of the bird
- half pound of pork meat
- half quarter of bacon
- salt
- parsley
- onion, chopped
- one dozen tomatoes
- two cloves
- peppers
- thin slice of bacon
- banana leaves
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Chop the liver of the bird with half pound of pork meat and half quarter of bacon
2. Season with salt, parsley, chopped onion, one dozen tomatoes, two cloves and peppers
3. Stuff the bird with this mixture
4. Wrap it in a thin slice of bacon
5. Wrap it then in banana leaves
6. Place it in hot coals and let it roast for two to three hours
7. Remove, unwrap from the leaf and bacon
8. Serve with a spicy sauce
