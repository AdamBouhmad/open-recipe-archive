## Ingredients

- snails
- butter
- mushrooms
- manioc flour
- parsley
- garlic
- salt
- peppers
- white wine
- water

## Directions

1. Melt two tablespoons of butter
2. Mix in a handful of chopped mushrooms, one tablespoon of manioc flour, chopped parsley, one garlic clove, salt and peppers
3. Add one cup of white wine and another of water
4. Boil, add the snails and serve hot
