## Ingredients

- six to eight eggs
- salt
- chive leaves
- pepper
- one spoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Break six to eight eggs into a bowl.
2. Beat with a little ground salt, chopped chive leaves, and a little ground pepper.
3. Melt one spoonful of butter in a frying pan.
4. When melted and still not very hot, pour in the eggs.
5. Continue stirring so they do not stick to the bottom.
6. When fried, serve.
