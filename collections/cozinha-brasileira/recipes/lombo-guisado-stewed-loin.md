## Ingredients

- beef loin
- butter
- Spanish sauce, two spoonfuls
- mushrooms or carapicús
- turnip sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the loin into thin slices
2. Place these in a casserole, covering them with a good piece of butter
3. Cook over fairly strong heat so they cook without boiling
4. When they are firm under the finger, drain the butter
5. Add two spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and let them cook slowly for several minutes
6. Place in a serving dish garnished with mushrooms or carapicús and a turnip sauce or any other
