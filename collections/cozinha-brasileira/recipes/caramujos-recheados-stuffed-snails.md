## Ingredients

- snails
- white wine
- cinnamon
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg
- fish meat
- bread crumbs
- milk
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- onion
- parsley
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove snails from shells and boil for a moment in white wine with cinnamon, pepper, clove and grated nutmeg
2. Chop with an equal volume of cooked fish meat and knead with the same amount of bread crumbs softened in hot milk, two hard-boiled egg yolks for each dozen snails, one onion, a little parsley, all finely chopped and salt
3. Mix with one or two tablespoons of butter and fill the snail shells with this mixture
4. Place on a plate, heat in the oven and serve
