## Ingredients

- bread crumb or bread interior
- salt
- black pepper
- water
- two egg yolks
- two spoonfuls of cream

## Directions

1. Put bread in a pot with a little salt, black pepper, and enough water to cover it
2. Let boil for half an hour over coals
3. Add two beaten egg yolks mixed with two spoonfuls of cream
4. Heat a little more and serve
