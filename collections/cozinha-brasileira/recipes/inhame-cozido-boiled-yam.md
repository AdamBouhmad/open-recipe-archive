## Ingredients

- yam
- water
- salt
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the root of a yam in water and salt until well cooked, because if not it will burn the throat
2. After cooking, peel and serve with powdered sugar
3. Alternatively, cut into slices, place on a plate for the oven, cover with sugar, and let roast until it takes color
