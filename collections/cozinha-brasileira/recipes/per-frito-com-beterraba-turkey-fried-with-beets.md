## Ingredients

- turkey pieces
- bread crumbs
- butter
- one or more beet roots
- water
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take the turkey pieces, coat them in bread crumbs and fry in plenty of butter
2. On the other hand take one or more beet roots which boil in hot salted water
3. Then peel and grate them, refrying this mixture with a little butter and one tablespoon of vinegar
4. Pour this over the plate with the turkey pieces
