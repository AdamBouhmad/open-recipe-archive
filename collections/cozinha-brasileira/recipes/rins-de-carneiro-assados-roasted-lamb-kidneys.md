## Ingredients

- lamb kidneys
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove fat from kidneys and scald in hot water
2. Split in two halves
3. Dust with salt and pepper
4. Place on grill and roast, dripping a little butter on them
5. Turn to roast the other side
6. Serve with salad
