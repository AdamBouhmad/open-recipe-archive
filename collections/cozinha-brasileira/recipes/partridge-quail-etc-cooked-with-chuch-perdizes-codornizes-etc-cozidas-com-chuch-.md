## Ingredients

- bird
- half a quarter of bacon
- salt
- parsley
- peppers
- marjoram
- onion leaves
- ginger
- half a clove of garlic
- little cilantro

## Directions

1. Boil the bird with half a quarter of bacon, salt, parsley, peppers, marjoram, onion leaves, ginger, half a clove of garlic, and a little cilantro
2. when cooked, remove the bird and reduce
