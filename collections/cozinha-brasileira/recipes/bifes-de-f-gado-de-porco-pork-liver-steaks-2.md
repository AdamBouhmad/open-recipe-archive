## Ingredients

- pork liver
- salt
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- Indian pepper
- half a glass of wine
- lard

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into slices the thickness of a finger and place them in a vessel with salt, garlic, onion cut into pieces, parsley, Indian pepper, and half a glass of wine
2. Leave them in this marinade for two hours
3. Place the slices in a hot pan with a little lard, stacking them one upon another
4. Cover the pan hermetically, putting a little cornmeal mush around the edges
5. Allow them to cook for half an hour over low heat
6. Serve the steaks with the sauce on a bed of cooked fern fronds or prepared carapicú
