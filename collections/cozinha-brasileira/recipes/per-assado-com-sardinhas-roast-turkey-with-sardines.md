## Ingredients

- two chicken breasts
- dozen sardines
- turkey meat
- pine nuts
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- two onions
- marjoram
- bacon
- paper

## Directions

1. Cut two chicken breasts into long strips, as well as a dozen sardines split into four strips each.
2. Mix with chopped turkey meat, a portion of split pine nuts, salt, pepper, parsley, two chopped onions and a little marjoram.
3. Take the turkey, open it along the back, remove the bones and some meat to chop, and fill it with the mixture.
4. Cook it and roast it on the spit, covered with bacon slices and buttered paper.
5. When cooked, serve it with its own sauce.
