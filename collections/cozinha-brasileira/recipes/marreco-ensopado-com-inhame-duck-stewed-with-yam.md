## Ingredients

- one duck, cut in pieces
- fat
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- one bowl of water
- salt
- pepper
- onion leaves
- marjoram
- one yam root
- one spoonful of lemon juice
- one lemon, cut and without seeds

## Directions

1. Cut the duck in pieces and pass them through fat until roasted on all sides
2. Add one spoonful of wheat flour and stir, then add one bowl of water, salt, pepper, onion leaves, marjoram and one yam root cooked beforehand in water and salt and cut in slices
3. Let it boil until everything is cooked, and before serving, add one spoonful of lemon juice and one lemon cut and without seeds
