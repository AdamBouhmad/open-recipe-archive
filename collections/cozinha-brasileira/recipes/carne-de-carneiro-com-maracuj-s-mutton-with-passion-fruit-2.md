## Ingredients

- mutton
- water
- salt
- six to eight passion fruits, pulp
- butter
- cream
- bread crusts, grated
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the mutton in water and salt until cooked
2. Cook the pulp of six to eight passion fruits in a little butter with cream, grated bread crusts and sugar
3. Serve together with the meat
