## Ingredients

- butter
- chives
- serpol
- parsley
- celery
- beef broth
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place in a casserole a little butter, a little chives, serpol, parsley, celery; let it heat, add some cups of beef broth and some spoonfuls of vinegar; let it boil for half an hour and serve with meat cooked or roasted on the grill.
