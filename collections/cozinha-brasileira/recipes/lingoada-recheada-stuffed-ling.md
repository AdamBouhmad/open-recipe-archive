## Ingredients

- fish
- milk
- wheat flour
- butter
- chopped parsley
- lemon juice
- tomatoes

## Directions

1. Remove the skin of the fish only from the dark side; after cutting the head, insert a knife to loosen the spine
2. Leave the fish prepared soaked in milk; remove and let drain
3. Dust with wheat flour and fry in butter
4. Through the hole where the head was missing, remove the dorsal spine that is now loosened, and fill the cavity with butter kneaded with chopped parsley, wheat flour and lemon juice
5. Put everything in a dish, bake in the oven, and serve with a tomato sauce
