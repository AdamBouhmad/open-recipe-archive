## Ingredients

- bird offal
- one onion
- parsley
- half a quarter of bacon
- salt
- peppers
- two boiled eggs
- half a dozen tomatoes
- strips of bacon
- some tomatoes
- one chalice of wine
- slices of peeled lemon

## Directions

1. Chop the bird offal with one onion, parsley, half a quarter of bacon, salt, peppers, two boiled eggs and half a dozen tomatoes
2. fill the bird with this chopped mixture
3. sew the opening shut
4. put it in a pan lined with strips of bacon, with more some tomatoes, one chalice of wine and slices of peeled lemon
5. cover the pan well and let it boil between coals or in the oven
6. when cooked, serve with its own strained and degreased sauce
