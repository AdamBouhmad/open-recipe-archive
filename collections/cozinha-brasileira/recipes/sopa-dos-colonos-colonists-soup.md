## Ingredients

- cabbage, chopped
- salt
- salt pork
- cornmeal, fried
- potatoes, peeled and sliced
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil chopped cabbage with salt and salt pork
2. Strain the broth
3. Add fried cornmeal until cinnamon-colored
4. Add peeled potatoes cut into slices
5. Add lemon juice
