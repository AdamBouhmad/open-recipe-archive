## Ingredients

- one chicken, cut into four pieces
- butter
- one cup of white wine
- three egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Braise the chicken pieces over coals in one spoon of butter with one cup of white wine
2. Season with the yolk of three eggs, salt, and nutmeg
3. When cooked, place on a plate
4. Cover everything with a layer of grated cheese
5. Return to the oven until browned and serve
