## Ingredients

- tapir loin
- pork fat
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- parsley
- onions
- pepper
- vinegar
- paper
- butter or fat
- one egg yolk

## Directions

1. Lard the tapir loin with pork fat seasoned in salt, pepper, garlic
2. Put it to marinate in salt, parsley, onions, pepper comazí, and vinegar, enough to moisten, for twelve hours in this seasoning
3. Thread it on the spit wrapping it in paper greased with butter or fat, and roast over a regular fire
4. When cooked, remove it and coat it with a beaten egg yolk, and give it a few more turns on the spit so the meat takes on good color; serve with salads
