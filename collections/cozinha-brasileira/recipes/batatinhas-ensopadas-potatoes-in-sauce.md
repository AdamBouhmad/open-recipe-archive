## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- onion

## Directions

1. Peel a portion of potatoes and cook in salted water
2. Cut into slices and arrange on a plate with salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg, and chopped onion
3. Let boil a little more and serve
