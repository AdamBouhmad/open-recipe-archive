## Ingredients

- turkey wings
- turkey neck
- turkey legs
- butter
- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- half a bottle of water
- one chicara of wine
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- carapicu fish
- cornmeal mush

## Directions

1. Simmer the turkey wings, neck, and legs briefly
2. Remove the bones and fry in butter
3. Add one tablespoon of wheat flour
4. Add half a bottle of water, one chicara of wine, salt, pepper, chopped onion, and a quantity of carapicu fish
5. Simmer near the fire
6. Serve with cornmeal mush
