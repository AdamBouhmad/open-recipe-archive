## Ingredients

- one tapir loin
- salt
- pepper
- a portion of cooked caras, peeled and cut into fine slices
- one onion
- minced parsley
- little butter

## Directions

1. Take a tapir loin and cut it into slices the thickness of two fingers
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Beat them and layer them one on top of the other in a casserole with little butter, a portion of cooked caras peeled and cut into fine slices, one onion, and minced parsley
4. Let it fry and serve
