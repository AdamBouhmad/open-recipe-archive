## Ingredients

- birds
- salt
- chopped peppers
- chopped parsley
- half a clove of garlic
- bacon strips
- hot milk

## Directions

1. Soak the birds with salt, chopped peppers, chopped parsley and half a clove of garlic
2. after they are well impregnated, lard the birds with thin strips of bacon
3. place them on a spit and roast over moderate heat
4. baste with hot milk
5. when cooked, serve with salads, some compote or with vegetables
