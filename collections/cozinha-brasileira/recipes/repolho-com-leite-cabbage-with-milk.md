## Ingredients

- one head of cabbage
- water
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- wheat flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Take a head of cabbage, remove leaves and wash well
2. Give it a few boils in salted water and when almost cooked, let cool and drain
3. Place in a casserole butter, salt and Indian pepper
4. Heat the fire, stir well
5. Add two pinches of wheat flour and gradually add a little rich milk
6. Reduce and serve
