## Ingredients

- chicken legs, breast, wings and all fleshy parts
- salt and other seasonings
- butter
- grated cheese
- melted butter
- four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the legs, breast, wings and all fleshy parts of the chicken with salt and other seasonings.
2. When almost cooked, remove the bones, trying to keep the parts in shape as much as possible.
3. Fry them in fat until they are browned.
4. Place them on a buttered plate over a layer of grated cheese, and cover with another layer.
5. Pour melted butter and four beaten egg yolks on top.
6. Place the plate in the oven until the eggs are cooked.
