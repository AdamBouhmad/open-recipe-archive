## Ingredients

- white or red wine
- water
- sugar
- butter
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- dried rusks ground
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil equal parts of white or red wine and water
2. Add a little sugar, butter, cinnamon, nutmeg and ground dried rusks
3. Finally add beaten egg yolks
