## Ingredients

- bitter palmito from coconut tree
- one paca cut in pieces
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- celery root
- three tablespoons pork fat
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one chopped onion
- one cup white wine

## Directions

1. Boil the bitter coconut palmito, remove it, and cut it into small pieces.
2. Place it with the paca cut in pieces in water with salt, parsley, onion leaves, and celery root.
3. When cooked, remove the palmito and meat.
4. Fry the meat on both sides in three tablespoons of pork fat.
5. Sprinkle it with one tablespoon of wheat flour.
6. Add one chopped onion and one cup of white wine.
7. Place the palmito on the plate and put the paca pieces on top.
8. Pour the sauce over and serve.
