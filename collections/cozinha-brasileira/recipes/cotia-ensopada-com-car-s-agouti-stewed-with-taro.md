## Ingredients

- one spoonful of wheat flour
- one sliced onion
- one spoonful of brown sugar
- agouti
- taro

## Directions

1. Sauté one spoonful of wheat flour, one sliced onion and one spoonful of brown sugar until they take on a dark amber color
2. Add the agouti cut into pieces and turn to fry
