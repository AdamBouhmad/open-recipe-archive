## Ingredients

- fish, cut three fingers thick
- four ounces of lard, chopped
- half a pound of beef
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- onion leaves
- one glass of white wine
- water
- one cup of white wine
- one tablespoon of sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into three-finger-thick pieces
2. Chop four ounces of lard and half a pound of beef
3. Add salt, parsley, pepper, onion leaves, one glass of white wine and equal amount of water
4. Place in a pan and boil until the fish is cooked
5. Remove the fish, strain the broth and reduce it until well incorporated
6. Pass the fish pieces through this broth and place them on a plate over the remaining sauce
7. Add one cup of white wine and one tablespoon of sour orange juice to dissolve the remaining broth
8. Serve with the same fish
