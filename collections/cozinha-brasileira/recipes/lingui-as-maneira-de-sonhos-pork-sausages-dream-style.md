## Ingredients

- Pork meat
- Fresh pork fat without skin
- Pepper
- Salt
- Thyme
- Parsley
- Bay leaf
- Cloves
- Pork caul

## Directions

1. Take equal amounts of pork meat and fresh pork fat without skin
2. Chop everything as finely as possible
3. Season with pepper, salt, thyme, parsley, bay leaf, and cloves
4. Take small pieces of this mixture and cover them with pieces of pork caul, giving them an elongated and flat shape
5. Roast them over a low fire on the grill and when cooked, serve with cooked cabbage, pigeon peas, and mangalós
6. Alternatively, they can be fried and served on a plate, pouring over them a sauce made with the fat in which they were fried with a little white wine added, letting it boil until reduced to half
