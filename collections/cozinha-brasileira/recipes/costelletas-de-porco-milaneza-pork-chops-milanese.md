## Ingredients

- six fresh pork chops, trimmed
- butter
- breadcrumb powder
- grated cheese
- pimenta da India
- garlic clove
- chives
- meat substance
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Take six fresh trimmed pork chops and spread them with butter.
2. Coat them with breadcrumb powder and grated cheese.
3. Place them in a skillet with melted butter, salt, pimenta da India, a garlic clove, and chives.
4. Sauté the chops only until they take on heat from both sides.
5. Place them in a vessel with fire below and above, and let them cook for a quarter hour.
6. Remove from heat, drain, and remove the fat from the sauce left in the vessel.
7. Melt a little meat substance and a little tomato sauce, reduce this sauce, and place the chops inside to cook slowly.
8. Serve with pasta cooked in water and salt, or with a tomato sauce.
