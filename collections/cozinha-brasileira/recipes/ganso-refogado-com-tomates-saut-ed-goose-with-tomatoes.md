## Ingredients

- goose
- lard
- salt
- pepper
- onion leaves
- tomatoes
- meat broth or white wine
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Take a goose, cut it into pieces which are placed in a pan; if well fatty, heat them slowly at first so they release their fat, and if not fatty, add to them two spoons of lard with the meat, salt, pepper and onion leaves
2. After they are fried a little, add to them a portion of tomatoes, one cup of broth, or better white wine, one spoon of wheat flour
3. Let them boil over low heat until the meat is cooked and serve
