## Ingredients

- 1 box of Nantes sardines
- white wine
- half a bottle of meat broth
- some slices of lemon
- some cloves
- a little cow butter
- 1 chopped onion
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 cup of wine

## Directions

1. Take 1 box of Nantes sardines, remove the bones, clean them, and break them up in a cup of white wine
2. Add half a bottle of meat broth, some slices of lemon, and some cloves
3. Let it boil for half an hour
4. Add a little cow butter and 1 chopped onion
5. Let it boil a little more and strain through a sieve
6. Add 3 egg yolks dissolved in 1 cup of wine
7. Let it boil a little more and serve with fish or meat
