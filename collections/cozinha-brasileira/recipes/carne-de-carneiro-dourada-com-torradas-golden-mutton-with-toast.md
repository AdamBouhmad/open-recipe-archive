## Ingredients

- two pounds mutton
- half pound bacon interspersed with meat
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- onion
- parsley
- six egg yolks, beaten

## Directions

1. Take two pounds mutton and half pound bacon interspersed with meat, salt, pepper, nutmeg, onion and parsley; chop everything very fine and sauté in a little fat with one cup of wine
2. When cooked, pour this onto slices of bread placed on a plate
3. Cover everything with six beaten egg yolks
4. Place in the oven to finish cooking
