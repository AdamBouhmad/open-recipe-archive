## Ingredients

- Fresh eels
- White wine
- One onion
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Boil fresh eels for a few minutes and rub to clean them without removing the skin
2. Cook in white wine with one halved onion, salt and pepper
3. When the strained broth is reduced, serve hot
4. Or, if desired, after placing the fish on a plate, let cool, cover with chopped parsley, pour a little melted butter on top, and serve
