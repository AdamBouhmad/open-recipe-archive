## Ingredients

- one loin
- strips of bacon and pieces of garlic for larding
- salt
- pounded camarís peppers
- sour orange juice
- tomatoes
- potatoes, peeled

## Directions

1. Take one loin, lard it with strips of bacon and pieces of garlic.
2. Marinate in salt, pounded camarís peppers, sour orange juice and tomatoes for six hours.
3. Place it in a pan with some peeled potatoes and let it roast in the oven.
4. When roasted, serve with the potatoes arranged around the loin.
