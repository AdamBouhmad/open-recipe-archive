## Ingredients

- one heart of palm
- fat
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- water

## Directions

1. Take a heart of palm, chop finely and wash in cold water
2. Place in hot fat
3. Add salt, pepper and parsley
4. Stir, add one cup of water
5. Once boiled, serve
