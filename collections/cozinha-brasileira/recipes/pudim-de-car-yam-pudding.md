## Ingredients

- one yam
- two pounds of yam pulp
- one pound of rice flour
- one and a half pounds of sugar
- half a pound of butter
- one dozen egg yolks
- salt
- nutmeg
- cinnamon
- a little yeast

## Directions

1. Boil a yam until cooked
2. Peel and grate it
3. Mix two pounds of this pulp with one pound of rice flour, one and a half pounds of sugar, half a pound of butter, one dozen egg yolks, salt, nutmeg and cinnamon
4. Mix with a little yeast
5. Place in a warm location for four hours
6. Place in a buttered mold
7. Bake in the oven and serve
