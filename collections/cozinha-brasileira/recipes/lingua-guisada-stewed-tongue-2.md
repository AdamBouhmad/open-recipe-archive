## Ingredients

- beef tongue, boiled
- broth
- wine
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take a tongue already boiled, cleaned as above and cut into thin slices
2. Boil it for an hour over moderate fire in a cup of broth and half a bottle of wine, little salt and pepper
