## Ingredients

- capon, cut into pieces
- sweet olive oil
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- garlic
- onion
- juice of bitter orange
- butter
- spicy sauce or salad

## Directions

1. Cut the capon into pieces and soak them for four to six hours in sweet olive oil, salt, parsley, pepper, garlic, onion and juice of bitter orange
2. Melt one spoon of butter, and when well heated, add the capon pieces, frying them on both sides
3. Serve with some spicy sauce or salad
