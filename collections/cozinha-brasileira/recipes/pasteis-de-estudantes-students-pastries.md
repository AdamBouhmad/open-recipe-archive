## Ingredients

- half a pound of brown sugar
- water
- cinnamon
- ground pepper
- manioc flour
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil half a pound of brown sugar with water, cinnamon, and ground pepper until it reaches mirror point
2. Remove from fire and stir in manioc flour until it becomes a medium-firm dough
3. Fill pastries with this dough, fry in fat and cover with sugar and cinnamon, serve
