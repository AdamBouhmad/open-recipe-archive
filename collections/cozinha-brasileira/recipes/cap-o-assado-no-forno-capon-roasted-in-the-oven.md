## Ingredients

- one capon
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- carapicús
- mangaritos
- breadcrumbs
- bacon
- wine

## Directions

1. Fry the whole capon in butter, salt, pepper, parsley and onion leaves, turning it from time to time so it does not burn
2. Fill it with cooked and chopped carapicús and mangaritos, some grated bread, a spoon of butter, salt and pepper
3. Place in a pan, wrapped in bacon, in the oven with a glass of wine
4. Let it cook, then serve
