## Ingredients

- one paca
- salt
- pepper juice
- lemon juice
- tomato juice
- water
- one clove of garlic
- two onions
- parsley
- fat

## Directions

1. Remove the skin of the paca and rub it with salt, pepper juice, lemon juice and tomato juice
2. Roast it on a spit over moderate heat, basting it with a sauce made with two cups of water, one crushed clove of garlic, two cut onions, a little parsley and two tablespoons of fat, which are boiled for a moment in a pan
3. When roasted, serve with its sauce
