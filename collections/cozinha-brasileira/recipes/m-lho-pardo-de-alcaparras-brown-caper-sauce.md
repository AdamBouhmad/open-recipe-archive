## Ingredients

- a few spoons of corn meal or wheat flour
- beef broth
- chopped onions
- bay leaves
- a sprig of parsley
- marjoram
- serpol
- a few spoons of capers
- a few raisins
- lemon rinds
- Indian cloves
- salt
- a little sugar
- one spoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Toast a few spoons of corn meal or wheat flour until they become dark copper color
2. Boil in a small quantity of beef broth with chopped onions, bay leaves, a sprig of parsley, marjoram, serpol, a few spoons of capers, a few raisins, lemon rinds, Indian cloves, salt, a little sugar, and one spoon of vinegar
3. Serve with roasted ducks, beef tongue, game, etc.
