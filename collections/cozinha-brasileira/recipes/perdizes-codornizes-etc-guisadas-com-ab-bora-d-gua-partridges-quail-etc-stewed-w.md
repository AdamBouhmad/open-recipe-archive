## Ingredients

- partridge, quail, or similar bird
- water
- salt
- fat
- wheat flour
- water squash
- parsley
- onion leaves
- coriander
- arrowroot
- bitter orange juice

## Directions

1. Boil the birds in water and salt
2. When almost cooked, remove them and cut into pieces, then fry in two spoons of fat
3. Brown with wheat flour
4. Add a portion of water squash peeled and cut into small pieces
5. Pour the broth over and add parsley, onion leaves, a little coriander, a small spoon of arrowroot, and the juice of a bitter orange
6. Let boil for half an hour, or preferably until the broth thickens, and serve
