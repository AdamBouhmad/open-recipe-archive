## Ingredients

- ten spoonfuls of wheat flour
- ten spoonfuls of grated cheese
- half a dozen eggs
- a little salt
- a little milk if necessary for the dough to have good consistency

## Directions

1. Knead ten spoonfuls of wheat flour, the same amount of grated cheese, half a dozen eggs, a little salt, and if necessary a little milk for the dough to have good consistency
2. Roll out to form pastes from which pieces of one inch in length are cut
3. Place them in a large pot that has boiling water and a little salt
4. When they float to the surface, remove them and serve with fried foods or some rich sauce
