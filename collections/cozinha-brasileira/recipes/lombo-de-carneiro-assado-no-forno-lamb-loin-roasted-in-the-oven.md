## Ingredients

- lamb loin
- bacon strips
- salt
- ground Indian pepper
- garlic juice
- lemon juice
- one onion
- parsley
- breadcrumbs
- green beans or roasted potatoes

## Directions

1. Lard the loin with bacon in thin strips, sprinkle a little salt, ground Indian pepper over it, a little garlic juice and lemon juice, one chopped onion and parsley
2. Leave for two hours with these seasonings to take on the flavor
3. Then wrap it in grated bread and place it in a casserole which is placed in an oven or over coals and let it roast for two hours
4. Serve with green beans or roasted potatoes
