## Ingredients

- bacon
- beef
- water
- potatoes
- cabbage
- squash pieces
- cassava roots
- ears of green corn
- parsley leaves
- salt

## Directions

1. Place in a pot bacon, beef, and sufficient water
2. Remove the scum from time to time
3. When these are cooked, add some potatoes, cabbage, squash pieces, cassava roots, ears of green corn, parsley leaves, and salt to taste
4. Let everything cook for half an hour more
5. Pour the broth over bread slices or cooked rice and serve the cooked meat in a separate dish
