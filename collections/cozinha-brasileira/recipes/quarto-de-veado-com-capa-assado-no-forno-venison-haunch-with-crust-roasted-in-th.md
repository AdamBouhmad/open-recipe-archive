## Ingredients

- venison haunch
- salt
- ground pepper
- eight eggs
- one spoonful of salt
- one pound of wheat flour
- nutmeg
- fat

## Directions

1. Take the venison haunch, beat it on both sides
2. Sprinkle it on all sides with salt and ground pepper
3. Leave it thus for twenty-four hours
4. Make a dough with eight eggs, one spoonful of salt, one pound of wheat flour and a little nutmeg
5. When the dough is ready, wrap the haunch with this dough and grease it with fat
6. Place the haunch in a hot oven to roast
7. In two to three hours it will be cooked and browned, then serve it
