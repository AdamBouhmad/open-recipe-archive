## Ingredients

- green oranges
- salt
- ground Indian pepper

## Directions

1. Squeeze a quantity of green oranges
2. Strain the juice
3. Mix with a little salt and ground Indian pepper
4. Serve cold with cold roasted meat and roasted suckling pig
