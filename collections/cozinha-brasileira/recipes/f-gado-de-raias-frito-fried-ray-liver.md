## Ingredients

- Slices of bread
- Butter
- Ray fish livers
- Parsley
- Onions
- Breadcrumbs
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry slices of bread in butter and place on a plate
2. Melt two spoons of fresh butter
3. Fry the fish livers in it with plenty of chopped parsley and onions
4. Place this fried mixture on the bread slices
5. Dust with breadcrumbs
6. Put in the oven to brown
7. Serve squeezing lemon juice on top
