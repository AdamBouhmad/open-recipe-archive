## Ingredients

- duck pieces
- three tablespoons fat
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one bottle water
- one half cup vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- onion leaves
- parsley
- carurú, washed
- angú de fubá de moinho or pirão de farinha de mandioca

## Directions

1. Fry the duck pieces in three tablespoons of fat in which one tablespoon of wheat flour is already frying
2. Turn the duck pieces to brown on all sides
3. Add one bottle of water, one half cup of vinegar, salt, pepper, onion leaves, parsley, and a portion of washed carurú
4. Let simmer for half an hour uncovered over the fire
5. Serve with angú de fubá de moinho or pirão de farinha de mandioca
