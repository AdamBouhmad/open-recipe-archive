## Ingredients

- butter
- wheat flour
- beef kidney, sliced thin
- seasonings
- bouquet of aromatic herbs
- wine

## Directions

1. Place a portion of butter in a casserole and after melting, add wheat flour, increasing the fire until it becomes the color of brass
2. Add the kidney cut into fine slices with seasonings and a bouquet of aromatic herbs
3. Add wine and let it boil once, thickening it with a little wheat flour
4. Let it cook slowly until its broth is reduced by half, then serve
