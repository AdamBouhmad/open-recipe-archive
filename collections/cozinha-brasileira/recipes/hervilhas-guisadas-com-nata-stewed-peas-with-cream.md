## Ingredients

- one spoon of wheat flour
- one spoon of cow butter
- one chopped onion
- green peas
- a little salt
- two cups of cream or fatty milk

## Directions

1. Brown one spoon of wheat flour with another of cow butter and one chopped onion
2. Add the green peas, a little salt, and two cups of cream or fatty milk
3. Let it boil for half an hour and serve
