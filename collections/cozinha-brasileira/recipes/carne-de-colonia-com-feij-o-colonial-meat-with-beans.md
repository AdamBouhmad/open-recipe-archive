## Ingredients

- piece of meat
- hot water
- parsley
- onion leaves
- cloves
- black beans
- pork belly

## Directions

1. Take a piece of meat, and after cleaning it, place it on the fire with already hot water.
2. Join with parsley, onion leaves, cloves, letting it boil for a quarter of an hour.
3. Remove it, and place it on the fire to cook with black beans and pork belly, in cold water.
4. After everything is cooked, serve it.
