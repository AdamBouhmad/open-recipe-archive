## Ingredients

- water
- eggs
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Put water to boil in a casserole, and when it is boiling, gently break a portion of eggs so they do not burst.
2. When well poached, remove them with a skimming spoon, and proceed this way until all the eggs you wish are finished.
3. After finished, place the eggs in a serving dish, pouring tomato sauce on top, and send to the table.
