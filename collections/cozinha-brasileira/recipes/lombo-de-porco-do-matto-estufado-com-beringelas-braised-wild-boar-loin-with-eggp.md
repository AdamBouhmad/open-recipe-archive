## Ingredients

- one wild boar loin
- parsley
- onions
- bay leaves
- one pound of chopped bacon
- one bottle of red wine
- orange wine (if good red wine is unavailable)
- one dozen eggplants

## Directions

1. Soak the loin in parsley, onions, and bay leaves for six hours
2. Place in a pot with the chopped bacon and red wine (or orange wine if red wine is unavailable)
3. Add the eggplants
4. Cover the pot with the lid
5. Braise over a moderate fire until everything is cooked
6. Serve with the sauce degreased and incorporated with two egg yolks diluted in a little lemon juice
