## Ingredients

- beef
- salt
- banana leaves or caeté leaves

## Directions

1. Dig a hole in the earth deep enough to contain the piece of meat to be cooked.
2. Once ready, place in it the piece of meat seasoned with salt and well wrapped in banana leaves or caeté leaves, so that it does not have contact with the earth.
3. Cover with earth and on top of this light a strong fire.
4. The heated earth communicates its heat to the meat, which in this manner cooks slowly and becomes very succulent. Six to eight hours are necessary to cook the meat, depending on the size of the piece.
