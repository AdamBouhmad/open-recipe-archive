## Ingredients

- pigeons
- giblets
- cassava flour
- butter
- bitter orange juice
- sugar
- salt
- cinnamon
- thin bacon slices
- paper

## Directions

1. Open the birds along the back and remove the bones
2. Make a forcemeat with the giblets, cassava flour, butter, bitter orange juice, one spoon of sugar, salt, and ground cinnamon
3. Stuff the birds and sew the opening
4. Wrap each bird in a thin bacon slice and then in paper
5. Roast in the oven
6. When cooked, remove the paper and bacon and serve with some spicy sauce
