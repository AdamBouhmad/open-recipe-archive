## Ingredients

- piece of beef
- parsley
- pepper
- vinegar
- melted fat
- paper
- tomatoes
- onions
- comari pepper
- salt
- garlic

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef that is beaten strongly with a mallet. Make a sauce of parsley, pepper, vinegar, and a little melted fat, and coat the piece of meat with this sauce.
2. Let it remain there for two hours; then cover it with paper greased with fat, placing it on a sheet metal tray two fingers in height, which is placed over a grill with coals underneath; from time to time, turn it from one side to the other.
3. When you see that nothing more drips from the meat, remove the paper, letting it brown appropriately, and being careful to turn from time to time to avoid burning.
4. Place it on a platter pouring over it a sauce made of tomatoes, onions, a little vinegar, comari pepper, salt and garlic.
