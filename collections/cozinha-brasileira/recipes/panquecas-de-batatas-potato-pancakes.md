## Ingredients

- a portion of small potatoes
- one spoonful of fine cornmeal
- one spoonful of sugar
- two beaten eggs
- one spoonful of butter
- salt
- half a cup of milk

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of small potatoes, peel and mash them well
2. Mix with one spoonful of fine cornmeal, one of sugar, two beaten eggs, one spoonful of butter, salt, and half a cup of milk
3. Proceed as already stated
