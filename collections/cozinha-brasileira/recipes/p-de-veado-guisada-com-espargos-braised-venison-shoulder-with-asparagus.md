## Ingredients

- venison shoulder
- water
- salt
- onion
- ginger
- peppers
- parsley
- asparagus shoots
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the venison shoulder in water with salt, onion, ginger, peppers, parsley and a portion of asparagus shoots tied in a bundle
2. When the shoulder is cooked, separate the meat from the bones and coat it in breadcrumbs
3. Fry it in butter
4. Place the asparagus shoots on a plate with the fried meat on top
5. Pour the strained and reduced broth over all and serve
