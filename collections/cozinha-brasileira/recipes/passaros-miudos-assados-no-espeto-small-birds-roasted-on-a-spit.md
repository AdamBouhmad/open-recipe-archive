## Ingredients

- small birds
- salt
- bacon slices
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the small birds
2. Rub them with salt
3. Wrap each one in a bacon slice
4. Place them on a spit, alternating with bread slices
5. Roast over moderate fire
6. When cooked, serve each bird on one bread slice
