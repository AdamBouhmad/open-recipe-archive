## Ingredients

- powdered coffee
- water
- cold water

## Directions

1. Many use to prepare coffee in the Turkish style, which consists of boiling powdered coffee with water, and after it has boiled, adding a few drops of cold water, which precipitates the powder, and the liquid becomes as if it had been strained; this method is preferred in some countries.
