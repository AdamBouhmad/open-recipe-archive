## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Cream
- White or red wine
- Water
- Butter
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Valeriana

## Directions

1. Beat four egg yolks in a cup of cream
2. Add a glass of white or red wine and two glasses of water
3. Heat in a pot over coals until it begins to boil
4. Then add a spoonful of butter, three spoonfuls of sugar, a little cinnamon and valeriana
5. Serve with puddings and pastas
