## Ingredients

- half a bottle of milk
- a little salt
- one spoon of butter
- two spoons of sugar
- four spoons of wheat flour
- one quarter of fresh cheese
- eight or more eggs

## Directions

1. Boil half a bottle of milk with a little salt, one spoon of butter and two spoons of sugar
2. Add four spoons of wheat flour and one quarter of fresh cheese
3. Mix well
4. Add eight or more eggs to form a soft mixture
5. Proceed as previously described
