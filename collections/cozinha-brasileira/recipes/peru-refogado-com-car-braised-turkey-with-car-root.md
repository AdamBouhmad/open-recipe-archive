## Ingredients

- turkey meat
- lard
- cará root
- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- one bowl of meat broth
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- marjoram
- two egg yolks
- two tablespoons of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the turkey meat into pieces after separating the bones
2. Fry briefly in hot lard and remove
3. Add some cará root cut into slices to the same lard and stir
4. Add one tablespoon of wheat flour
5. Add one bowl of meat broth, salt, parsley, onion leaves, and a little marjoram
6. After one boil, add the meat and cook near the fire until everything is well cooked
7. Add the sauce with two egg yolks beaten in two tablespoons of lemon juice
8. Serve
