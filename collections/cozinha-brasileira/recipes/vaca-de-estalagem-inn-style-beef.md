## Ingredients

- sliced beef
- one calyx of brandy
- two of broth
- butter
- minced parsley
- one spoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Place sliced beef on the fire moistened with one calyx of brandy and two of broth
2. When heated, remove them and put butter on the fire to heat
3. When the butter is melted and hot, add the meat with minced parsley and one spoon of vinegar
4. Leave to boil a little more and serve
