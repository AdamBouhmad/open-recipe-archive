## Ingredients

- capybara meat
- water
- salt
- peppers
- cloves
- thyme
- marjoram
- gordura
- carapicús
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of capybara meat cut into pieces in water, salt, peppers, cloves, thyme and marjoram
2. When cooked, remove and sauté with one spoonful of fat, a portion of carapicús, salt, peppers, a glass of vinegar and a cup of water
3. Leave to cook over low fire for one hour and serve
