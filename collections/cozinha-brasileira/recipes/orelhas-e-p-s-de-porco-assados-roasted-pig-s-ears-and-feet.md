## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- pig's ears
- dried bread crumbs or dried herbs
- onions
- toasted onions
- mustard sauce

## Directions

1. After boiling, cook the feet for four to five hours.
2. Then split them in two, cover with bread crumb powder, and roast on a grill.
3. Cut the ears into thin strips and sauté them with a portion of toasted onions.
4. At the moment of serving, add a little mustard sauce and place the roasted feet on top.
