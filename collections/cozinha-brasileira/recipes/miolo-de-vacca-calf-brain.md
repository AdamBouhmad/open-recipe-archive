## Ingredients

- calf brain
- hot water
- cold water
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- bay leaves
- garlic
- parsley
- celery root
- carrots

## Directions

1. Bring the calf brain to hot water, then clean it of blood and the membrane that covers it.
2. Soak it in cold water for one or two hours.
3. Cook it in this same water with one cup of vinegar, salt, pepper, cloves, bay leaves, garlic, parsley, celery root and carrots.
4. Three quarters of an hour is sufficient for it to be cooked.
5. Serve with any sauce indicated for meats.
