## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- half pound of pork meat
- carapicús
- one onion
- four hard-cooked egg yolks
- salt
- peppers
- nutmeg
- thread
- one bottle of water
- one bottle of wine
- parsley
- marjoram
- coriander
- garlic
- cloves
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take a rabbit, remove its bones, leaving only the head
2. Chop half pound of fatty pork meat, carapicús, one onion, four hard-cooked egg yolks, salt, peppers, and nutmeg
3. Fill the rabbit with this mixture, sew the opening with thread, and cook it with one bottle of water, another of wine, salt, parsley, marjoram, coriander, garlic, and cloves
4. When cooked, remove and wrap it in breadcrumb crusts passed in butter
5. Pour the strained and reduced broth on top, and some slices of peeled lemon, and serve
