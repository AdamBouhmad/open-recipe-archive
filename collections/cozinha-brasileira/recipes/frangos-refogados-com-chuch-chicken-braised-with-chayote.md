## Ingredients

- chicken pieces
- two spoons of fat
- chayotes, peeled and chopped
- one bowl of water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- two egg yolks
- two spoons of vinegar

## Directions

1. Braise the chicken pieces in two spoons of fat, turning them to brown on both sides; remove them
2. In the same fat, braise a portion of peeled and chopped chayotes
3. Add one bowl of water and the chicken pieces
4. Season with salt, parsley, and onion leaves
5. Cook until done
6. Before serving, thicken with two egg yolks beaten with two spoons of vinegar and serve
