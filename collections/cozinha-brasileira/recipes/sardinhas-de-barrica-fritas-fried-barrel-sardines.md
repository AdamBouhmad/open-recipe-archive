## Ingredients

- Sardines
- Brandy
- Fat
- Peppers
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take a portion of sardines
2. Put them on a plate with brandy
3. Place over coals
4. When hot, set the brandy on fire
5. When the brandy has finished burning, put some fat in a frying pan and heat well
6. Put the sardines in it
7. Dust with peppers
8. At serving time, squeeze some lemon juice on top
