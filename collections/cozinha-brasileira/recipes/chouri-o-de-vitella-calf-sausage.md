## Ingredients

- tripe
- calf blood
- fresh bacon
- calf tongue
- calf heart
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- marjoram
- onion
- parsley
- thyme
- water

## Directions

1. Clean and turn the tripe inside out
2. Fill with a mass made of calf blood passed through a sieve, a piece of fresh bacon cut, the tongue and heart of the calf cut in long strips, salt, pepper, cloves, marjoram, onion, parsley and thyme, all finely chopped
3. Cook the sausages in water and salt
4. Before serving, fry in a little fat
