## Ingredients

- fish
- water
- salt
- onion leaves
- new peas (optional)
- cassava flour or cornmeal

## Directions

1. Cook the fish in water and salt with onion leaves, and if desired, some new peas, for one hour
2. Pour the broth into a tureen already containing cassava flour or cornmeal
