## Ingredients

- bananas
- lard
- cornmeal
- egg yolk
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. Open a quantity of bananas lengthwise
2. Fry them in a small amount of lard
3. Add cornmeal, a beaten egg yolk, and a cup of rich milk or cream
4. Let it boil a little and serve
