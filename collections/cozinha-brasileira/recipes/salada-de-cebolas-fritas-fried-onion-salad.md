## Ingredients

- onions
- cow butter
- salt
- pimentos comaris
- mustard sauce
- sugar

## Directions

1. Sauté a portion of chopped onions in cow butter
2. Add salt, pimentos comaris, mustard sauce and a little sugar
3. Serve before the onions begin to brown
