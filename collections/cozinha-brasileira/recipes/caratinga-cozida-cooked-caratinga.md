## Ingredients

- one caratinga
- water
- salt
- one spoon of bread
- milk
- peppers

## Directions

1. Cook a caratinga in water and salt until well cooked
2. Peel and mash it with one spoon of bread, mixing with milk and a little peppers and serve. Also it can be roasted in the coals.
