## Ingredients

- two pounds of pork
- two spoonfuls of fat
- one heart of palm
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- tomatoes
- one cup of water

## Directions

1. Cut two pounds of pork into small pieces
2. Fry in two spoonfuls of fat
3. Add one chopped and washed heart of palm, salt, pepper, parsley, tomatoes and one cup of water
4. Let boil for half an hour and serve
