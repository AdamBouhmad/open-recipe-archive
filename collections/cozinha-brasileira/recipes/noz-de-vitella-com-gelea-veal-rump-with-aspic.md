## Ingredients

- veal rump
- carrots
- onions
- parsley
- chervil
- pepper
- nutmeg
- salt
- roasted beef
- veal hand
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Beat and lard the rump, then place to cook with carrots, onions, parsley, chervil, pepper, nutmeg, salt, pieces of roasted beef, a veal hand, and meat broth
2. When the rump is cooked, remove it, strain the sauce, and let it thicken
3. Pour this over the rump and let cool to serve
