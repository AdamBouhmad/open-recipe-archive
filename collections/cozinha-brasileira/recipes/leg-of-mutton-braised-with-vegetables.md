## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- fresh peas, beans, or squash greens
- salt
- parsley
- clove
- onion leaves
- lean bacon or ham

## Directions

1. Beat the leg well after removing fat and trimming the bone
2. Scald it in hot water, letting it boil for five minutes in this water
3. Remove it and place in cold water, leaving it there until cool
4. Put it back to cook with a portion of fresh peas, beans or squash greens, salt, parsley, one clove, onion leaves of the season, and a piece of lean bacon or ham
5. When all is cooked, separate the broth and evaporate it until reduced to two cups of liquid
6. Return the leg to it, leaving it to cook over coals, turning it until it begins to stick to the bottom of the pan and is well browned
7. Serve with the vegetables
