## Ingredients

- one veal knuckle weighing at least two pounds
- four onions
- a handful of watercress
- three bottles of water
- salt
- pepper from India

## Directions

1. Split the veal knuckle lengthwise through bones and meat into four pieces
2. Sauté with four onions cut into rounds and a handful of chopped watercress
3. When ready, add three bottles of water and boil for two hours, replacing water as it evaporates
4. Season with salt and pepper from India
5. Separately, pound three old pigeons and twenty-five live crabs
6. Toast the mixture in a pot with a little butter
7. When the mixture sticks to the bottom of the pot, gradually add the strained veal knuckle broth until it is used up
8. Boil for an hour
9. Strain everything and take one cup in the morning and another in the afternoon
