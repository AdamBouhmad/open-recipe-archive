## Ingredients

- one heart of palm
- water
- salt
- fat
- onion
- tomatoes
- vinegar
- mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Cut one heart of palm into pieces two inches long
2. Boil in salted water
3. Serve with a sauce of fat, chopped onion, tomatoes, vinegar and mustard sauce
