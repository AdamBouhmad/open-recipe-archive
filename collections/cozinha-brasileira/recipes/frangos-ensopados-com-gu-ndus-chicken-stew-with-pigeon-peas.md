## Ingredients

- two chickens
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- water
- half plate of pigeon peas
- butter
- wheat flour
- vinegar
- chicken broth

## Directions

1. Cook two chickens cut into four pieces with salt, parsley, onion and little water
2. On the other side, boil half a plate of pigeon peas with water and salt
3. Melt two spoonfuls of butter and mix two spoonfuls of wheat flour, two of vinegar and the broth from the chickens
4. After boiling a little, add the chicken pieces and the cooked pigeon peas without their water and let them boil for half an hour more before serving
