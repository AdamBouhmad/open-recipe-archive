## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- small potatoes
- corn meal or wheat flour
- butter
- olives
- capers
- lemon slices
- sardines, chopped
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook mushrooms and small potatoes and peel them; then fry a little corn meal or wheat flour in butter, add the mushrooms and potatoes, olives, capers, lemon slices, some chopped sardines, and a little sugar, and let everything boil.
2. Serve with roasted pork or beef.
