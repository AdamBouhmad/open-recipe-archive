## Ingredients

- mutton, cut into slices
- salt
- pepper
- four spoons wheat flour
- three eggs, beaten
- one cup white wine
- four spoons sugar
- sugar and cinnamon for coating

## Directions

1. Cut the mutton into slices, season with salt and pepper, and grill on both sides
2. Make a thin batter of four spoons wheat flour, three beaten eggs, one cup white wine and four spoons sugar
3. Pour a third of this batter over the meat, and when it is grilled, add the second third of the batter, and at the end the final third (this is done so the batter cooks in layers)
4. Cover with sugar and cinnamon and serve
