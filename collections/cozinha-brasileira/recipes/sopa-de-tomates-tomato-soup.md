## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- water
- salt
- one onion
- biscuits

## Directions

1. Boil the tomatoes in water and salt until they fall apart
2. Fry an onion in a pan
3. Pour the tomato broth over the fried onion
4. Chop some biscuits and add them to the pan
