## Ingredients

- sago
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- slices of Galician lemon
- red wine
- dried plums

## Directions

1. Boil sago with lemon peel, cinnamon and slices of Galician lemon
2. When everything is cooked, add some cups of red wine and some dried plums
3. Let this mixture boil a little longer, then serve
