## Ingredients

- two pounds of fresh fish
- water
- salt
- four dozen oysters
- bread toasts

## Directions

1. Cook two pounds of fresh fish in four bottles of water and salt
2. When well cooked, strain without pressing
3. Add four dozen pounded oysters
4. Let boil for another half hour and pour over bread toasts
