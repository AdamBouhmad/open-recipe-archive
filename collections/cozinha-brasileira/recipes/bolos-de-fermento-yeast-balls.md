## Ingredients

- one pound of wheat flour
- bread yeast
- milk

## Directions

1. Knead the evening before one pound of wheat flour with a little bread yeast and the necessary milk to form a regular dough
2. Place in a warm location and the next day, remove from it some balls the size of a hen's egg and place them in a very large pan that is on the fire with boiling water
3. Let them cook until they float
4. Remove with a skimmer and after draining, serve dusted with grated cheese and melted butter
