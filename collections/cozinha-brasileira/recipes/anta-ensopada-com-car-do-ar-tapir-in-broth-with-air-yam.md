## Ingredients

- a piece of tapir meat
- fat
- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- one chopped onion
- salt
- two pepper corns
- half a dozen tomatoes
- one bowl of water
- one glass of sour orange juice
- air yams peeled and cut into slices

## Directions

1. Take a piece of tapir meat and braise it in fat with one tablespoon of wheat flour, one chopped onion, salt and two pepper corns and half a dozen tomatoes
2. When the meat is equally fried, for which it is turned several times, add one bowl of water, one glass of sour orange juice, and air yams peeled and cut into slices
3. Let it boil until the meat is cooked and the broth is reduced
