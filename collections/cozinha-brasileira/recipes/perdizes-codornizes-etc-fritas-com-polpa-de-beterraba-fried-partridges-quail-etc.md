## Ingredients

- partridge, quail, or similar bird
- salt
- ground Indian pepper
- fat
- onion
- breadcrumbs
- red beets
- water
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Season the bird cut into pieces with salt and ground Indian pepper and fry in plenty of fat with chopped onion and a handful of breadcrumbs
2. Place the meat on a plate with the pulp of red beets cooked in water and salt, grated and mixed with a glass of vinegar
3. If too sour, add one spoon of sugar
4. Pour off the fat with the breadcrumbs and serve
