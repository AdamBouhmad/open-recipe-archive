## Ingredients

- veal meat
- shellfish
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- white wine
- water
- breadcrumbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into finger-thick slices
2. Lard and let braise over moderate heat in butter, salt, a little pepper, nutmeg and two glasses of white wine
3. When cooked, add a dozen shellfish with water
4. Stir, and after adding breadcrumbs and lemon juice, serve with the broth
