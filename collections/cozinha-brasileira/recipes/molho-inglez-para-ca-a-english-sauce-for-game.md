## Ingredients

- half a bottle of milk
- the crumb of a loaf of bread
- twenty or thirty grains of pepper
- salt
- one spoon of beef butter

## Directions

1. Boil the crumb of a loaf of bread in half a bottle of milk
2. Pass through a sieve
3. Add twenty or thirty grains of pepper and salt
4. At the moment of serving, add one spoon of beef butter
5. Serve with feathered game
