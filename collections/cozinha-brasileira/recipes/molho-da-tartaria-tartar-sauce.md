## Ingredients

- marjoram
- chives
- thyme
- salt
- French mustard
- pepper
- vinegar
- sweet oil

## Directions

1. Finely chop marjoram, chives and thyme
2. Add salt, French mustard, pepper and vinegar
3. Gradually add sweet oil, stirring constantly
4. If the sauce becomes thick, add a little more vinegar
5. If it is too salty, add more oil and mustard
6. Serve cold with cold roasted meats or cooked fish
