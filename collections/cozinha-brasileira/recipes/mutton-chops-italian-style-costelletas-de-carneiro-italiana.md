## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- sweet olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the chops and beat them well.
2. Soak them in a sauce of sweet olive oil with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
3. After marinating, grill them.
