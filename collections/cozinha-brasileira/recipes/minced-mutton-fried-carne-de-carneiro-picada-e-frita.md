## Ingredients

- two pounds of mutton, sinews and skin removed
- one pound of pork with some fat
- two onions
- pepper
- salt
- cloves
- parsley
- one handful of powder of good polvilho biscuits
- one spoon of polvilho
- two eggs (yolk and white)
- one glass of wine
- some fat for frying

## Directions

1. Mince the mutton with the pork.
2. Melt a little fat in a pan and when well heated, add the minced meat.
3. Fry on one side, then turn to fry on the other side.
4. Serve with a spicy sauce.
