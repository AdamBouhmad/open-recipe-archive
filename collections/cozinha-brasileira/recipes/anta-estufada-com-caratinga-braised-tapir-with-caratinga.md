## Ingredients

- one tapir loin
- milk
- salt
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- one pan lined with bacon
- one cup of milk cream
- one tablespoon of sugar
- one clove of garlic
- a little grated ginger
- one or two caratinga roots, peeled and cut into slices

## Directions

1. Boil a tapir loin for five to ten minutes in milk and salt
2. Remove it and let it cool
3. Lard it with bacon seasoned with salt and pepper
4. Place it in a pan lined with bacon and one cup of milk cream, salt, one tablespoon of sugar, one clove of garlic, a little grated ginger, and one or two peeled caratinga roots cut into slices
5. Cover the pan, seal it, and leave it to boil over an active fire for two to three hours
6. Serve with its sauce
