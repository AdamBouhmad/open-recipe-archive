## Ingredients

- veal breast
- lettuce leaves
- salt
- parsley
- palmito
- fat
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Cut the breast meat into equal pieces
2. Scald and cook with lettuce leaves, salt and parsley
3. When cooked, sauté chopped palm heart with a little fat
4. Add meat broth
5. Just before serving, add the meat to the casserole, give it another boil
6. Serve
