## Ingredients

- venison loin
- pieces of bacon seasoned with salt
- pimento peppers from India
- garlic
- bacon strips
- two cups of water
- one onion
- cloves
- cinnamon
- new peas
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Lard the venison loin with some thick pieces of bacon seasoned with salt, pimento peppers from India and pieces of a garlic clove
2. Place it in a pot over strips of bacon with two cups of water, one onion studded with cloves and cinnamon
3. Cover the pot well and let boil over coals until cooked
4. On the other hand, boil a portion of new peas in water and salt
5. After draining, braise them in the loin's broth and serve with the loin
