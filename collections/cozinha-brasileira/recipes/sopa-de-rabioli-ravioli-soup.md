## Ingredients

- two pounds of fatty pork
- water
- fat
- salt
- pepper from India
- clove
- nutmeg
- one pound of wheat flour
- four eggs
- grated cheese
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of fatty pork, chop it very fine, and sauté in a pan containing little water, fat, salt, pepper from India, clove and nutmeg
2. On the other hand, make a dough with one pound of wheat flour and four eggs, which should be stretched on a cloth so as to be very thin
3. Cut round pieces from it, in which wrap a portion of the sautéed meat, thus forming small pastries
4. Put them in water that should already be boiling
5. After ten minutes, remove them and put others until all are cooked
6. Then place them on a serving dish, sprinkle grated cheese on top, and cover with melted butter
