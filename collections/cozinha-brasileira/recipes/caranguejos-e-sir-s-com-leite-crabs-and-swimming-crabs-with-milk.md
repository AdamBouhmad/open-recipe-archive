## Ingredients

- crabs
- water
- salt
- two spoons of butter
- one spoon of wheat flour
- one cup of milk
- three egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the legs of the crabs, leaving only the two claws
2. Cut also the tip of the tail and the part anterior to the head and cook in water and salt
3. Melt two spoons of butter and mix with one spoon of wheat flour
4. Immediately add one cup of milk and a little salt
5. When about to remove from fire, add three egg yolks
6. Place the crabs on a plate and pour the sauce over them and serve
