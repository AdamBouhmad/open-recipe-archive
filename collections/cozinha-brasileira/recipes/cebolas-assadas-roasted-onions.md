## Ingredients

- a quarter of mutton meat
- a little chicken breast
- one raw egg
- salt
- pepper
- some boiled onions with centers removed
- grated bread
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Chop a quarter of mutton meat with a little chicken breast
2. Mix with one raw egg, salt and pepper
3. Fill some boiled onions with centers removed with this mixture
4. Arrange on a plate and cover with grated bread
5. Pour some melted butter on top, put in the oven until it takes good color, and serve
