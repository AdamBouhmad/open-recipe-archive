## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- wheat flour
- egg yolks
- cow butter
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Season the chops with salt and pepper.
2. Dip in melted butter and wheat flour, then in egg yolks, and again in wheat flour.
3. Fry in cow butter.
4. Sprinkle with grated cheese and serve.
