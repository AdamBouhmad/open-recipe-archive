## Ingredients

- cassava
- salt
- sugar
- butter
- fat or milk cream

## Directions

1. Roast one or two cassavas in the embers
2. When roasted, peel and arrange on a plate
3. Add a little salt and sugar, pour a little butter, fat or milk cream on top, and place in the oven or over coals, covering with a lid with coals on top
4. When browned, serve hot
