## Ingredients

- veal glands
- water
- butter
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- four cups meat broth
- one cará root, cut in slices
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Give the glands a boil, then place in cold water
2. In a separate preparation, melt a little butter, add one tablespoon wheat flour, salt, parsley, and onion leaves without browning
3. Add four cups meat broth, sliced cará, and the glands cut into pieces
4. Let boil until cooked
5. Before serving, add a little lemon juice
