## Ingredients

- ears of fresh corn
- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- cloves
- fennel

## Directions

1. Take ears of fresh corn that are still milky and sweet, shell and grate them
2. Pass the obtained juice through a sieve
3. Add a little milk, sugar, cinnamon, cloves, and fennel
4. Boil over moderate heat until reduced to half
5. Pour into a tureen to cool
6. Cover with sugar and cinnamon
7. For better appearance, place in the oven to toast slightly
8. Serve cold
