## Ingredients

- half a plate of rice
- one bottle of milk
- half a pound of grated cheese
- half a pound of sugar
- a little salt
- nutmeg
- eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil half a plate of rice with one bottle of milk
2. When cooked and cool, add half a pound of grated cheese, half a pound of sugar, a little salt, nutmeg and eight egg yolks
3. Mix everything well, place the dough in a mold greased with butter, bake in hot ashes covering with a lid full of live coals
4. When cooked, serve with proper sauce
