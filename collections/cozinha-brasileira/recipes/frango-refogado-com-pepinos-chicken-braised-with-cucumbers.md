## Ingredients

- two chickens, cut into pieces
- cow butter
- salt
- pepper
- one spoon of wheat flour
- three cups of water
- one cup of vinegar
- whole mustard
- one onion
- one dozen peeled cucumbers without seeds
- toasted bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Fry the salted and peppered chicken pieces in cow butter until almost cooked; remove them
2. Roll the pieces in toasted bread and fry again until well browned on both sides; remove them
3. In the same butter, add one spoon of wheat flour, then three cups of water and one cup of vinegar
4. Season with salt, pepper, and whole mustard
5. Add the onion and let simmer
6. Add one dozen peeled, seeded cucumbers, cut lengthwise into four pieces
7. Let everything simmer a little more
8. Arrange the cucumbers on a plate covered with bread crumbs
9. Place the chicken pieces on top, pour the sauce over all, and serve
