## Ingredients

- turkey
- turkey giblets
- livers from three or four chickens
- half a pound of salt pork
- grated cheese
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaves
- white wine
- butter
- paper
- onion leaves
- one glass of wine
- two tablespoons of butter
- one chicara of meat broth
- four egg yolks
- garlic

## Directions

1. Stuff the turkey with minced giblets, livers from three or four chickens, and half a pound of salt pork mixed with grated cheese, little salt, pepper, and bay leaves
2. Moisten everything well with white wine
3. Roast on a spit covered with salt pork slices and paper buttered with butter
4. Meanwhile, take onion leaves, salt them, and scald with hot water
5. Mince them very finely and place in a pan with one glass of wine, two tablespoons of butter, one chicara of meat broth, four egg yolks, salt, pepper, and a clove of garlic
6. Cook for a while
7. When the turkey is roasted, remove the salt pork and paper
8. Serve with this sauce
