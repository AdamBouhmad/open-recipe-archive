## Ingredients

- Eight pig's tails
- Egg yolks
- Breadcrumbs or bread powder
- Oil or fat for frying
- Fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the pig's tails in a frying pan
2. Allow them to cool after cooking
3. Cover them with beaten egg yolks and breadcrumbs or bread powder
4. Fry them until golden
5. Place on a plate with a sprig of fried parsley
