## Ingredients

- goose
- butter
- twelve onions of medium size
- goose liver, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, chopped
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Sauté in butter a dozen onions of medium size, mix them with the chopped goose liver, adding salt, pepper, chopped parsley and marjoram
2. After the goose is seasoned with salt, fill it with this stuffing
3. Then roast it on a spit and serve
