## Ingredients

- Two bottles of water
- Salt
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Onion leaves
- Cloves
- One eel
- Manioc flour or bread crumbs
- Melted butter
- Sweet olive oil
- Vinegar
- Parsley pounded

## Directions

1. Put two bottles of water in a pan with salt, parsley, pepper, onion leaves, and cloves
2. When boiling, add pieces of eel after removing the skin
3. Let boil for a quarter of an hour
4. Remove and drain
5. Sprinkle with manioc flour or bread crumbs
6. Pour melted butter on top
7. Serve cold with a sauce of sweet olive oil, vinegar and pounded parsley
