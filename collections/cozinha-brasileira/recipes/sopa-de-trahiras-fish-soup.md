## Ingredients

- small fish
- butter
- wheat flour
- meat broth
- white wine
- valerian
- cardamom
- ginger
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Fry small fish in butter, covered with a little wheat flour
2. Pound them and cook in meat broth
3. After one hour, add white wine in a quantity equal to the broth
4. Add a little valerian, cardamom, and ginger
5. Bring to another boil and pour over bread slices
