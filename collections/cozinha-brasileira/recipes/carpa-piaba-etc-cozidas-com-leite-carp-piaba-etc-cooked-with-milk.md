## Ingredients

- one or two fish
- milk
- salt
- two spoons of fat
- two spoons of manioc flour
- one onion
- peppers

## Directions

1. Take one or two fish, remove the scales, clean and wash them, cutting them into pieces and boiling them in milk and salt
2. When cooked, melt two spoons of fat in a vessel over coals
3. Mix two spoons of manioc flour, one chopped onion and peppers
4. Add the milk and finally the fish
5. Let it boil for another half hour and serve
