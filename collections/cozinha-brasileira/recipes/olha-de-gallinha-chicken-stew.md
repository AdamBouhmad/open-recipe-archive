## Ingredients

- one fat chicken, cut up
- two pigeons
- some mutton ribs
- a piece of pork loin
- onions
- one cabbage, cut up
- one or two lettuce heads
- cassava roots
- sweet potatoes or some pieces of squash
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- water
- sauce for meats

## Directions

1. Take one fat chicken cut up, two pigeons, some mutton ribs, a piece of pork loin, onions, one cabbage cut up, one or two lettuce heads, cassava roots, sweet potatoes or some pieces of squash, salt, parsley and pepper.
2. Let everything boil with water until cooked, and serve with any sauce for meats: a middle dish.
