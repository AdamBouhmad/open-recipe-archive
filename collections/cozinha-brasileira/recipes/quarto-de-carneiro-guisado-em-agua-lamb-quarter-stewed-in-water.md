## Ingredients

- Whole lamb quarter
- Onion
- Garlic clove
- Carrots
- Cabbage leaves
- Bay leaf
- Cloves
- Parsley
- Chervil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Carapicús
- Pickled cucumbers

## Directions

1. Cut a piece from the side of the foot with bone so it fits in the pan
2. After beating and larding, place to boil in water that completely covers it
3. Add onion, a clove of garlic, carrots, cabbage leaves, a bay leaf, two cloves, parsley, chervil, salt, pepper, and a handful of carapicús
4. Always remove the scum and let boil
5. When cooked and water nearly dry, strain the broth and let continue to cook
6. Remove the broth, leaving only what is necessary for the sauce
7. Remove the fat and add some small pickled cucumbers
8. Serve with the meat
