## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- water
- salt
- milk
- sugar
- manioc flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel, clean, and cut cucumbers into pieces
2. Let boil in water and salt
3. When cooked, remove from water and mash, passing through a sieve
4. Add boiled milk with sugar or salt
5. Pour this broth into manioc flour fried in butter, stirring constantly
