## Ingredients

- three or four heads of escarole
- fine salt
- ground pimento da India
- two tablespoons sweet oil or fat
- three tablespoons strong vinegar

## Directions

1. Take three or four heads of escarole and remove the leaves, keeping only the stalks
2. Wash well and cut lengthwise
3. Place in a salad bowl with fine salt, ground pimento da India, two tablespoons of sweet oil or fat and three tablespoons of strong vinegar
