## Ingredients

- meat
- herbs
- roots
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 spoon cornstarch
- 2 spoons corn flour

## Directions

1. Cook the meat with the herbs and roots desired
2. Strain it and return the broth to the fire
3. When it is already boiling, gradually add a mixture made of 3 eggs, half a spoon of cornstarch, and 2 spoons of corn flour
4. Let it boil until the mixture rises to the surface of the broth and serve at table
