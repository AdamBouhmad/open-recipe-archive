## Ingredients

- dried cod
- two tablespoons butter
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- a handful of carapicú or chopped mushrooms
- one onion
- parsley, chopped
- one cup milk
- two handfuls of bread crumb soaked in hot milk
- butter
- one egg, beaten
- breadcrumbs, grated

## Directions

1. Cook a dried cod and remove all the meat, leaving the backbone and tail intact.
2. Put two tablespoons of butter on the fire with one tablespoon of wheat flour, a handful of chopped carapicú or mushrooms, one onion and some chopped parsley.
3. Moisten everything with one cup of milk.
4. Let it boil a little so the carapicú is cooked and mix in the cod meat.
5. Soak two handfuls of bread crumbs in hot milk and knead to form a paste.
6. On a buttered plate, lay a layer of the kneaded bread, forming a fish shape.
7. Place the backbone on top, cover it with the meat and carapicú, and cover this with the remaining kneaded bread.
8. Brush with beaten egg using a feather and sprinkle generously with grated breadcrumbs.
9. Place in the oven and let it brown to serve.
