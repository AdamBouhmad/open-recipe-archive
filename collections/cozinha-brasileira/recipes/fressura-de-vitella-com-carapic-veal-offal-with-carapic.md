## Ingredients

- veal offal
- water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- one onion
- two tablespoons wheat flour
- same amount of butter
- one cup broth
- cornmeal mush or fish paste for serving

## Directions

1. Boil the cleaned offal in water and salt
2. Add pepper, parsley, and one onion
3. When nearly cooked, add two tablespoons wheat flour mixed with an equal amount of butter, dissolved in one cup of broth
4. Let boil for half an hour
5. Serve with cornmeal mush or fish paste
