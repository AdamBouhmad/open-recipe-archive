## Ingredients

- one bird
- salt
- water
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one quarter of bacon
- one pound of veal meat
- one small spoon of cassava starch
- a portion of beans, chopped
- marjoram
- one onion, chopped
- one glass of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil the bird with salt, water, parsley, onion leaves, one quarter of bacon and one pound of veal
2. When cooked, remove it and cut it into pieces
3. Reduce the broth and thicken it with one small spoon of cassava starch
4. Place the meat back in the pan with a portion of chopped beans, marjoram, one chopped onion and one glass of lemon juice
5. Let it simmer until everything is cooked
6. Serve
