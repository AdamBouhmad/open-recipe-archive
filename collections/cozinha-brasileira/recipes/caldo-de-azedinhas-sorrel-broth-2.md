## Ingredients

- one spoonful of fat
- four spoonfuls of wheat flour
- a portion of sorrel leaves, washed
- beef stock
- a little sugar
- nutmeg
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Fry the fat until it stops crackling
2. Add four spoonfuls of wheat flour
3. Add a portion of washed sorrel leaves
4. Stir and mix with beef stock
5. Add a little sugar and nutmeg
6. Pour over toasted bread in a tureen
