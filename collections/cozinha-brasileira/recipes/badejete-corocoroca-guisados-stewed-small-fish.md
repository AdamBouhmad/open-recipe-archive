## Ingredients

- two tablespoons butter
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one cup milk
- bay leaves
- pepper
- small fish
- melted butter
- breadcrumbs, grated

## Directions

1. Melt two tablespoons of butter.
2. Mix in one tablespoon of wheat flour, one cup of milk, bay leaves and little pepper.
3. Place the fish in this sauce and boil them.
4. When cooked, remove them, let them cool and pass them through melted butter.
5. Cover them with a good layer of grated bread.
6. Place on a plate between coals, grill them and serve.
