## Ingredients

- one wild pig head
- water
- salt
- bell peppers
- bay leaves
- juniper berries
- marjoram
- thyme
- ginger
- meat from the same pig
- one pound bacon
- pepper
- parsley
- onions
- cloves
- bread crusts soaked in milk
- bacon for lining
- one cup wine
- one tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil the wild pig head in water with salt, bell peppers, bay leaves, juniper berries, marjoram, thyme, and ginger.
2. When well cooked, remove all the bones.
3. Make a forcemeat with meat from the same pig, one pound of bacon, pepper, parsley, salt, onions, cloves, and bread crusts moistened with milk.
4. Stuff the head with this filling.
5. Place it in a baking dish lined with bacon and add one cup of wine and one tablespoon of lemon juice.
6. Roast in the oven until it has a good color and serve.
