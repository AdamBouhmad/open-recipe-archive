## Ingredients

- partridge, quail, or similar bird
- fat
- water
- salt
- parsley
- peppers
- cumin
- onion leaves
- squash
- lemon juice
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Fry the bird in fat, then cut it into pieces
2. Add water, salt, parsley, peppers, cumin, onion leaves, and a squash peeled, with the seeds removed and cut into pieces
3. Boil until the bird is cooked and the broth is reduced
4. Serve after adding lemon juice beaten with an egg yolk
