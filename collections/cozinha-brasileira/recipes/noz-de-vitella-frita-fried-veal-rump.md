## Ingredients

- veal rump
- cloth
- water
- pork fat
- broth
- ham
- parsley
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- carrots

## Directions

1. Flatten the veal rump wrapped in strong cloth by beating
2. Boil for five minutes to firm it, then lard it
3. Place in a pot with pork fat, broth, a little ham, parsley, onion, salt, pepper, and carrots
4. When cooked, strain the sauce and let it thicken
5. Return the rump to the pot and let it cook over coals
