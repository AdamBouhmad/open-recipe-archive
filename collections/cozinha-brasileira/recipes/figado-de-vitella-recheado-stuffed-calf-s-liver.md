## Ingredients

- calf liver
- salt pork
- water
- salt
- grated bread crumbs
- sugar
- one tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Take the liver and make some holes in it, which are filled with salt pork
2. Let it cook in water and salt
3. Remove the foam from time to time
4. Finally add grated bread crumbs, a little sugar and one tablespoon of vinegar
