## Ingredients

- a portion of chopped beans
- water and salt
- one egg yolk, beaten
- one glass of wine
- one chopped onion
- one cup of water

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of chopped beans in water and salt
2. Add one beaten egg yolk mixed in one glass of wine
3. In the same time, fry one chopped onion, add one cup of water, and when boiled, add the beans with broth
4. Let them finish cooking over coals and serve
