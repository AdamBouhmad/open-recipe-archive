## Ingredients

- eggs
- parsley
- onions
- chives
- salt
- Indian pepper
- garlic

## Directions

1. Beat a portion of whole eggs; after beating well, add parsley, well-chopped onions and chives, salt, Indian pepper, and one clove of garlic; mix everything well
