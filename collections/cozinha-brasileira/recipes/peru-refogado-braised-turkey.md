## Ingredients

- turkey
- four tablespoons of lard
- one bottle of water
- two tablespoons of wheat flour
- salt
- bay leaves
- pepper
- onion
- four egg yolks
- one glass of wine
- lemon juice
- mangarito root

## Directions

1. Cut the turkey into pieces and braise in four tablespoons of lard
2. Add one bottle of water and two tablespoons of wheat flour
3. Add salt, bay leaves, pepper, and chopped onion
4. Finish cooking the turkey
5. Remove the meat and strain the broth
6. Beat four egg yolks with one glass of wine and a little lemon juice into the broth
7. Pour this sauce over the turkey placed in the center of a quantity of mangarito root well fried in butter
8. Serve
