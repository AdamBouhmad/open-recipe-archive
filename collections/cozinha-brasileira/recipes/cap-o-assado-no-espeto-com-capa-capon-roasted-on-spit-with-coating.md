## Ingredients

- one capon
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare as capon roasted on spit
2. When roasted, remove the paper and bacon
3. Cover with breadcrumb crust and let roast a little more, moistening with melted butter until it is a good color, and serve with salads or some fruit preserve without pastry
