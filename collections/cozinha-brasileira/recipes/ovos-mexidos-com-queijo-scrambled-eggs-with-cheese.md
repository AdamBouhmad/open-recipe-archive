## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- four ounces of Flemish cheese or well-cured cheese
- two ounces of butter
- salt
- pepper from India
- a glass of wine

## Directions

1. Break the eggs and beat them well in a casserole
2. Add the butter and grated cheese
3. Place the casserole over a brisk fire and stir continuously until the mixture is sufficiently cooked and still soft
4. Add salt according to the saltiness of the cheese and a portion of pepper from India
5. Serve on a plate previously heated
6. Pour a glass of wine over the top
