## Ingredients

- 1 suckling pig, partially boiled
- ground meat for stuffing, seasoned
- thin slices of ham
- thin slices of bacon
- fish (tubaras or carapicús)
- hard-boiled eggs
- bacon strips for wrapping
- white cloth
- string
- half meat broth and half white wine
- salt
- broken pepper
- roots
- onions
- parsley
- chives
- garlic
- clove
- thyme
- bay leaf
- marjoram

## Directions

1. Boil the suckling pig briefly, then remove all bones and spread it on cloth.
2. Place a layer of seasoned ground meat on top.
3. Layer thin slices of ham over the meat, then a layer of thin bacon slices.
4. Add a layer of fish (tubaras or carapicús), then a layer of hard-boiled eggs.
5. Top with another layer of seasoned ground meat.
6. Wrap the pig (being careful not to disturb the layers) with bacon strips and cover with white cloth tied with string.
7. Cook in equal parts meat broth and white wine for three hours.
8. Add salt, broken pepper, roots, onions, parsley, chives, garlic, clove, thyme, bay leaf, and marjoram.
9. Allow to cool in the cooking vessel and serve cold.
