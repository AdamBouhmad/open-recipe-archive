## Ingredients

- 1 spoon of butter
- 3 spoons of wheat flour
- some pieces of onion
- chopped carrots
- parsley
- cooked milk
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Fry the flour in a spoon of butter
2. Add pieces of onion, chopped carrots, and parsley
3. Add cooked milk, salt, pepper, and nutmeg little by little
4. Let it boil for an hour over low heat and serve with poultry
