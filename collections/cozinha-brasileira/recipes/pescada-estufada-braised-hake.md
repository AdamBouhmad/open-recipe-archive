## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of butter
- parsley
- chopped onion leaves
- two or three fish
- wheat flour
- salt
- peppers
- coals
- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- two egg yolks
- one glass of wine

## Directions

1. Spread two tablespoons of butter on a dish that can withstand the heat of the fire
2. Sprinkle with chopped parsley and onion leaves
3. Place two or three fish wrapped in wheat flour, salt and peppers on top, and cook them over coals
4. When almost cooked, remove them, strain the butter into a casserole, mix it with one tablespoon of wheat flour, two egg yolks and one glass of wine
5. Boil a little more, pour over the fish and serve
