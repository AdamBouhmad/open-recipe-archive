## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- salt
- garlic

## Directions

1. The suckling pig is cleaned and washed as was already explained above.
2. Split it into two halves, with one of them having the head, that is, cut lengthwise, rub it with salt and garlic, leave it in a marinade for twelve hours.
3. Thread each of these halves on a spit, passing the spit between the ribs and the skin.
4. As for the half where the head adheres, the point of the spit comes out through the mouth.
5. Tie the foot to the spit with a cord, and make a small hole in the skin into which the hand is inserted.
6. In this state, it goes to the oven, resting the point of the spit on a support which was proportioned.
