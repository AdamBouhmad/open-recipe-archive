## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- salt
- parsley
- bay leaves
- onions
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Cut, beat and trim the chops.
2. Bring them to boil, seasoned with salt, parsley, bay leaves and onions, until cooked.
3. Strain the broth and reduce it until thick.
4. Place the chops in a buttered pan.
5. Pour the sauce over them, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, and cover with four beaten eggs.
6. Bake in the oven and when the eggs are cooked, serve.
