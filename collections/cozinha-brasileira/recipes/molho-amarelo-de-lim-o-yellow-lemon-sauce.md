## Ingredients

- Butter
- Wheat flour
- Egg yolks
- Lemon zest
- Sugar
- Ground cinnamon
- Nutmeg
- White wine

## Directions

1. Mix butter and wheat flour in equal proportions with egg yolks and lemon zest as in lemon sauce
2. Add a little sugar, ground cinnamon and nutmeg, and as much white wine as necessary for the sauce
3. Place over fire and let it boil a little, stirring constantly
4. Serve with the same dishes as lemon sauce, and also for puddings and pastas
