## Ingredients

- beef lungs
- water
- salt
- parsley leaves
- vinegar
- sugar
- nutmeg
- wheat flour
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash and cut the lungs into small pieces
2. Put to cook in water, salt, parsley leaves, vinegar, sugar and nutmeg
3. Half an hour before serving, add a little wheat flour fried in butter and the juice of one lemon
4. The sauce should be abundant
