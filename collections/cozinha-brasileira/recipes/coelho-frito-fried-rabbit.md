## Ingredients

- rabbit pieces
- water
- salt
- parsley
- breadcrumbs
- egg yolks
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the pieces of a rabbit in water, salt and parsley
2. When cooked, remove and let cool
3. Roll the pieces in breadcrumbs, cover with egg yolks, fry in butter, and serve
