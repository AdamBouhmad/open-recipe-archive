## Ingredients

- a piece of tapir meat
- water
- salt
- fat
- one cup of water
- one cup of white wine
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one tablespoon of lemon juice
- one palm heart cut into pieces
- two egg yolks
- one glass of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil a piece of tapir meat for a few minutes in water and salt
2. Remove it and braise in a little fat, turning on all sides
3. Add one cup of water, one cup of white wine, salt, parsley, onion leaves, one tablespoon of lemon juice and one palm heart cut into pieces
4. Let it boil with moderate heat
5. Place the palm heart on the plate, pouring the meat as strained sauce over it, having thickened it with two beaten egg yolks and one glass of lemon juice; serve
