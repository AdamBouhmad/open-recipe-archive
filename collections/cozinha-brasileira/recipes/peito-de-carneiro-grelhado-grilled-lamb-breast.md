## Ingredients

- lamb breast
- water
- salt
- breadcrumbs
- pepper
- parsley
- picante sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the lamb breast in water and salt until cooked
2. Remove and drain it
3. Wrap in grated breadcrumbs mixed with pepper, salt and chopped parsley
4. Cover with paper and roast on a grill over coals
5. Serve with a picante sauce
