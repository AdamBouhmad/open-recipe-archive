## Ingredients

- a little butter
- one spoon of polvilho
- two egg yolks
- one spoon of English mustard powder
- one cup of beef broth, wine, or vinegared water
- a little salt
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Mix a little butter with one spoon of polvilho, two egg yolks, one spoon of English mustard powder, one cup of beef broth, wine or vinegared water as desired, a little salt, and a little sugar
2. Put in a saucepan over coals and allow to boil a little
3. Serve
