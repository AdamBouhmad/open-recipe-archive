## Ingredients

- 1 pound turtle meat from the inner shell
- 2 pounds beef
- 2 pounds mutton
- 2 or 3 onions
- pepper
- 6 or 8 cloves
- salt
- sliced cooked meat

## Directions

1. Cut the turtle meat into walnut-sized pieces and wash them
2. Boil the turtle meat for four hours over regular heat
3. In another pot, cook the beef and mutton for six hours in water with onions, pepper, cloves, and salt
4. Strain and skim the broth
5. Pour the strained broth into a tureen over slices of cooked meat
6. Add the turtle meat soup to the tureen and serve
