## Ingredients

- one chicken
- half a pound of lard
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one garlic clove
- one spoonful of vinegar
- one bottle of water
- two handfuls of bread crumb
- twelve egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil one chicken with half a pound of lard, salt, peppers, parsley, onion leaves, one garlic clove, one spoonful of vinegar and one bottle of water
2. When cooked, remove the bones and chop the meat together with the lard
3. Mix in two handfuls of bread crumb and twelve egg yolks, mixing everything well
4. Place this mixture by spoonfuls to fry in hot fat and serve
