## Ingredients

- calf stomach
- calf hands and feet
- orange
- water
- salt
- onion
- pepper
- two tablespoons wheat flour
- same amount of butter
- one tablespoon vinegar
- lemon juice
- yolks of three eggs

## Directions

1. Take the stomach, hands and feet of the calf
2. After washing well, scald in hot water, rub with orange to make them white, and cook in water with salt, onion and pepper
3. When cooked, remove the bones from the hands and feet, cut them into pieces, as well as the tripe
4. Pour two tablespoons of wheat flour, the same amount of butter, one tablespoon of vinegar, lemon juice and the yolks of three beaten eggs into the broth, boiling for a little while
5. Return the feet and tripe and after boiling for some more time, serve
