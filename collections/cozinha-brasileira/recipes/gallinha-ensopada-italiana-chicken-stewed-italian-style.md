## Ingredients

- one chicken, cut into pieces
- half a pound of bacon
- half a pound of ham
- one onion
- two cloves
- one bay leaf
- a little nutmeg
- salt
- some peppers
- one cup of water
- one cup of wine
- half a plate of rice
- little water and salt for the rice
- butter
- grated cheese
- six egg yolks
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken pieces in bacon, ham, onion, cloves, bay leaf, nutmeg, salt, peppers, water and wine.
2. On another side, cook half a plate of rice with little water and salt.
3. When the rice is cooked and dry, place it on a buttered serving dish, half of the rice covered with grated cheese.
4. Place the chicken pieces on top, cover with the rest of the rice and another layer of cheese.
5. Pour six egg yolks beaten with a little sugar on top of everything.
6. Place the dish in the oven until the eggs are cooked, and serve.
