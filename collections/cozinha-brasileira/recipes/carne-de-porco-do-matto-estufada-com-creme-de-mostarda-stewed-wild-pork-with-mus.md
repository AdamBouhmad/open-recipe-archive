## Ingredients

- one piece of pork meat
- salt
- pounded camarís peppers
- bacon pieces for larding
- breadcrumbs
- bacon slices
- one cup white wine
- two egg yolks, beaten
- one large cup milk
- one tablespoon mustard powder
- grated nutmeg
- one small teaspoon sugar

## Directions

1. Rub one piece of meat with salt and pounded camarís peppers.
2. Lard it with pieces of bacon and wrap it in breadcrumbs.
3. Put it in a pan with slices of bacon and one cup of white wine.
4. Cover the pan with the lid and let it cook in the oven.
5. When cooked, serve it with the sauce made of two beaten egg yolks boiled with one large cup of milk, one tablespoon of mustard powder, a little grated nutmeg and one small teaspoon of sugar.
