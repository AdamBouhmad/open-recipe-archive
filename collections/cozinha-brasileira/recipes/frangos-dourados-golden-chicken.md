## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- pepper
- paper
- butter
- egg yolks
- young peas

## Directions

1. Roast a chicken on the spit after rubbing with salt and pepper and wrapping in paper greased with butter
2. When roasted, remove it and cut into four pieces
3. Wrap the pieces in beaten egg yolks and fry in butter
4. Sprinkle the rest of the eggs on top and when fried on both sides, serve with fresh young peas
