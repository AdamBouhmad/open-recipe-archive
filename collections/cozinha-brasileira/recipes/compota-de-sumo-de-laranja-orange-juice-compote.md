## Ingredients

- oranges
- cinnamon
- clove
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel a portion of oranges and remove the seeds
2. Put them in a pan to boil, stirring with a breadcrumb spoon, a little cinnamon and clove
3. Pass through a sieve and thicken the juice until it forms a thin porridge
4. Serve; if the oranges are sour, add half a quarter pound of sugar for each orange juice strained through a sieve
