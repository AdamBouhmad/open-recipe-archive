## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- water
- salt
- butter
- garlic
- Indian pepper
- parsley
- onion
- marjoram
- twelve eggs
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Clean the feet and cook them with water and salt until the meat separates from the bones.
2. Drain them in a colander and remove all the bones.
3. Place the meat in a pot with a little butter, salt, garlic, Indian pepper all well ground, parsley, onion and marjoram, and let it sauté.
4. Beat twelve eggs with the whites, add two tablespoons of wheat flour, mix this meat with eggs.
5. Place a tablespoon of butter in a pot and put it over low heat with a lid that has coals on top.
6. Cook slowly and after cooking, serve.
