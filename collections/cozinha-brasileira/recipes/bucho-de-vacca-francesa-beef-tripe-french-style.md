## Ingredients

- half quarter of butter
- dozen small onions
- wheat flour
- half bottle of white wine
- beef tripe, cooked
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Fry in half a quarter of butter a dozen small onions until golden
2. Dust with wheat flour
3. Pour half a bottle of white wine and the cooked tripe cut with necessary seasonings
4. Let boil for half an hour over moderate heat and serve
