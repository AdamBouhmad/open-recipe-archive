## Ingredients

- large fish
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, chopped
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and fill it with butter mixed with salt, pepper and chopped parsley.
2. Wrap it in a sheet of thick paper.
3. Roast it on a grill over moderate fire.
4. When cooked, serve it moistened with lemon juice.
