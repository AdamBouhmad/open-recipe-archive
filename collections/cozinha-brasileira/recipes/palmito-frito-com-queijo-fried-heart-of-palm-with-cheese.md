## Ingredients

- one heart of palm
- water
- salt
- half pound of grated cheese
- a dozen tomatoes
- two spoonfuls of fat
- a little cilantro

## Directions

1. Cut a heart of palm, boil in salted water and when cooked, dry with a towel
2. On the other hand, fry half pound of grated cheese with a dozen tomatoes in two spoonfuls of fat
3. Add the heart of palm and a little cilantro
4. Boil a bit more and serve
