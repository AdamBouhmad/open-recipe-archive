## Ingredients

- wheat flour
- butter
- water
- vinegar
- sugar
- raisins
- almonds
- lemon slices
- beef tongue, cooked

## Directions

1. Knead two spoonfuls of wheat flour with a spoonful of beef butter, dissolved in water and vinegar
2. Add a little sugar, scalded raisins, peeled almonds and some slices of lemon and the slices of cooked tongue
3. Let all boil for half an hour and serve
