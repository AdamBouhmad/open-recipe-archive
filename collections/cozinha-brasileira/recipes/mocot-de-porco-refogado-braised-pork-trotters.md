## Ingredients

- Pork trotters
- Pork fat
- Wheat flour
- Parsley leaves
- Onions
- Pork trotter broth
- Salt

## Directions

1. Boil the trotters until almost cooked, let them cool and remove the bones
2. In another pan, fry a little pork fat with wheat flour, parsley leaves, onions, pork trotter broth and a little salt
3. After it has boiled once, add the pork trotter meat to this sauce, letting it boil a bit more, and serve
