## Ingredients

- chicken pieces
- half a pound of lard, chopped
- one cup of water
- salt
- pepper
- one palm heart, chopped and washed
- cornmeal mush

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken pieces with half a pound of chopped lard and one cup of water, salt and pepper
2. When cooked, remove them and pour into the broth one chopped palm heart washed in cold water
3. Let it cook, stirring, and serve with the chicken and cornmeal mush
