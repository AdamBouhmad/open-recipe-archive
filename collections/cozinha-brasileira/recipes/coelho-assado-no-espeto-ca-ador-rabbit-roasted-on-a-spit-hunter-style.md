## Ingredients

- rabbit
- salt
- bacon lard
- pan drippings
- onion
- parsley
- cloves
- pepper
- ginger
- saffron

## Directions

1. Rub a rabbit with salt, wrap it in fine slices of bacon lard, and roast it on a spit
2. When roasted, remove it and pour the drippings that fell in the dripping pan into a sauce with one chopped onion, parsley, cloves, pepper, ginger and a little saffron
3. Leave to cook over a low fire
4. Place the rabbit on a plate, pouring the prepared sauce over it
5. Make several cuts in different places with the tip of a knife so that the sauce penetrates the rabbit and serve it
