## Ingredients

- one capon
- one handful of carapicús
- half pound of ham
- one quarter of bacon
- the giblets of the capon
- one onion
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- four cooked eggs
- one glass of wine

## Directions

1. Make a stuffing of one handful of carapicús, half pound of ham, one quarter of bacon, the capon's giblets, one onion, salt, parsley, pepper and four cooked eggs
2. Cook everything together with one glass of wine, stirring so it does not stick
3. When cooked, fill the capon's cavity and crop, lifting the skin between the neck and breast for this purpose
4. Place it in the oven in a pan over bacon slices, and bring to the table
