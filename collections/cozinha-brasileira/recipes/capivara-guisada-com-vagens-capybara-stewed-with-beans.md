## Ingredients

- capybara meat
- water
- salt
- cornmeal
- gordura
- beans
- marjoram
- parsley
- onion leaves

## Directions

1. Boil a piece of capybara meat in water and salt
2. When cooked, cut it into pieces
3. In another pan, put one spoonful of cornmeal and another of fat, add the meat and fry, stirring
4. Add two cups of water, a portion of cut beans, salt, marjoram, parsley, and onion leaves
5. Boil until everything is cooked and serve
