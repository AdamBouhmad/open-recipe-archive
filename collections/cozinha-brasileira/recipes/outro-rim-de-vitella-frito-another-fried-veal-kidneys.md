## Ingredients

- veal kidneys
- fat
- white wine
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- bread, grated
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut veal kidneys into thin lengthwise pieces and place them in a pan with melted fat
2. Immediately add a glass of white wine so they do not become fried, a little salt, parsley, and pepper
3. Before serving, add some grated bread and lemon juice
