## Ingredients

- half a pound of polvilho
- one pound of butter
- eight egg yolks
- lemon zest
- salt
- nutmeg
- a portion of raisins

## Directions

1. Mix half a pound of polvilho with one pound of butter, eight egg yolks, lemon zest, salt, nutmeg and a portion of raisins
2. Mix very well
3. Place in a buttered mold
4. Place the mold in a pot to boil
5. Boil for two hours
6. Serve with an appropriate sauce
