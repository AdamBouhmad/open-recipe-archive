## Ingredients

- one spoon of butter
- four spoons of wheat flour
- one bottle of good beef broth
- half a quarter of peeled almonds reduced to fine powder
- half a quarter of sugar
- a little nutmeg
- one cup of milk cream

## Directions

1. Melt the butter
2. Add four spoons of wheat flour and one bottle of good beef broth
3. At the end add half a quarter of peeled almonds reduced to fine powder, half a quarter of sugar, a little nutmeg and one cup of milk cream
4. Serve hot
