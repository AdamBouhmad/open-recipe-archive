## Ingredients

- mutton feet, cleaned
- water
- salt
- cornmeal
- fat
- white wine
- three egg yolks
- bay leaf
- parsley
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned feet in clean water with only salt
2. Cut into small pieces, removing the bones
3. Fry one spoonful of cornmeal in one spoonful of fat until brass-colored
4. Add one glass of white wine with three beaten egg yolks, one bay leaf, a little parsley and lemon juice
5. Let boil a little, then add the feet
6. After boiling for one hour over coals, serve with potato or cassava flour porridge
