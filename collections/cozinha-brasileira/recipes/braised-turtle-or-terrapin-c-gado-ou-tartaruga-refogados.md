## Ingredients

- turtle or terrapin
- fubá mimoso or wheat flour
- butter
- one onion, chopped
- parsley
- one cup of water or wine
- one cup of vinegar
- salt
- peppers

## Directions

1. Cut off the head and feet of the turtle
2. Boil for five minutes
3. Remove the shells and cut the meat into strips
4. Roll them in fubá mimoso or wheat flour
5. Braise in a little butter with chopped onion and parsley
6. Add one cup of water or wine and one cup of vinegar, salt, and peppers
7. Let boil for a quarter of an hour
8. Serve
