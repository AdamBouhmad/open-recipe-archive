## Ingredients

- calf head
- cold water
- wheat flour
- butter
- salt
- onion leaves
- parsley
- beef broth or water
- lemon

## Directions

1. Clean and depilate the head thoroughly with hot water
2. Place in cold water and let soak for one hour
3. Place on a board and opening from the underside, remove all bones without damaging the outer skin, until reaching the eye sockets and the lower part of the skull
4. Remove likewise the bones of the jaws, nose, and those forming the throat
5. Work to give the muscles their original shape
6. Rub with lemon, wrap in cloth, tie with string
7. Cook in a sauce made with a spoonful of wheat flour, another of butter, salt, onion leaves, parsley, eight cups of beef broth or water, and slices of two lemons
8. After cooking for three hours, remove the cloth and string, and serve thus
