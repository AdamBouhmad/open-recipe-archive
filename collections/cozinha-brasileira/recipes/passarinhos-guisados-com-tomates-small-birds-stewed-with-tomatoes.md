## Ingredients

- two dozen tomatoes
- one cup of water
- one glass of white wine
- salt
- peppers
- two dozen small birds, cleaned

## Directions

1. Boil two dozen tomatoes with one cup of water, one glass of white wine, salt and peppers
2. Add two dozen cleaned small birds
3. Let them simmer for half an hour
4. Serve
