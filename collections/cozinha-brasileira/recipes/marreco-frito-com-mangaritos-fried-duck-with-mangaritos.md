## Ingredients

- 1 young duck, cut into pieces
- orange juice
- salt
- parsley
- chopped onion
- some pimiento peppers
- fat for frying
- small mangaritos, peeled
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut a young and new duck into pieces and soak with orange juice, salt, parsley, chopped onion, and some pimiento peppers
2. After three hours, fry in plenty of fat along with a portion of small peeled mangaritos
3. Fry until of good color, then increase the fire and serve, sprinkling on top with some finely chopped parsley
