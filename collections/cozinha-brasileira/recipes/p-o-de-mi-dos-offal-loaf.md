## Ingredients

- half of a pig's head
- two pig's feet
- heart
- some pieces of lard
- pig's tongue
- water
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- sweet oil or vinegar

## Directions

1. Let half the head, two feet, the heart, pieces of lard and the tongue of the pig boil in water and salt until cooked.
2. Remove the skin from the head and feet.
3. Line a cloth with these and the other skins, placing in it the rest of the meat chopped, the heart, the tongue cut into strips, with salt, pepper and cloves.
4. Tie the cloth well and let it cook for half an hour.
5. Let it cool, keeping everything for some time between two boards with a weight on top.
6. Remove the cloth and serve with a cold sauce of sweet oil or vinegar.
