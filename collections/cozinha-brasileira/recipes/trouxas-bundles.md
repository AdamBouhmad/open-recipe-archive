## Ingredients

- one dozen eggs
- one spoon sugar
- salt
- one spoon wheat flour
- nutmeg
- one spoon milk
- lard
- marmalade or other sweets or chopped chicken liver
- sugar and cinnamon for dusting

## Directions

1. Beat one dozen eggs, and when well beaten, add one spoon of sugar, little salt, one spoon of wheat flour, a little nutmeg and one spoon of milk
2. On another part, have over the fire a frying pan greased with lard
3. Pour in gradually two to three spoons of this thin batter, which after frying on both sides is removed and placed on a plate
4. Proceed thus until the mass is finished; these fried ones, which should not be very browned, serve as crepes to roll in them pieces of marmalade, or any other kind of sweet, or chopped chicken liver
5. Dust these bundles with sugar and cinnamon, and serve
