## Ingredients

- peeled apples
- half pound sugar syrup
- cinnamon
- fennel

## Directions

1. Boil peeled apples in little water until they begin to break apart
2. At this point add half a pound of sugar syrup, a little cinnamon and fennel
3. Boil until it thickens a little
4. Place on a plate and serve cold with roasts
