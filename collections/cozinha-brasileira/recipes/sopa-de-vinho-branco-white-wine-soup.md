## Ingredients

- white wine
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- clove
- sugar
- cornstarch

## Directions

1. Bring white wine to boil with lemon peel, cinnamon, clove and sugar
2. When it comes to a boil, add cornstarch dissolved in cold water to thicken it
