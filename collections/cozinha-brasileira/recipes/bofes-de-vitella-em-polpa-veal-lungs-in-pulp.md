## Ingredients

- veal lungs
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- grated bread
- sugar
- lemon

## Directions

1. Wash and let the lungs drain, chop them finely, boil them in water, salt and a little vinegar.
2. When cooked, add to them grated bread crumbs, one spoon of sugar, some slices of lemon, and serve with corn porridge.
