## Ingredients

- venison haunch
- breadcrumbs
- bacon
- water
- white wine
- salt
- peppers
- grated ginger
- onion
- taro
- egg yolks
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Brown the venison haunch, coat it in breadcrumbs and cover it with a slice of bacon
2. Place it in a pot with a bottle of water, another of white wine, salt, peppers, grated ginger, a cut onion and taro cooked, peeled and cut into slices
3. Cover the pot and leave it to boil near the fire until the meat is cooked
4. Remove it and place it on a plate with the taro around it
5. Thicken the broth with two beaten egg yolks mixed in a cup of vinegar and serve
