## Ingredients

- calf stomach
- water
- salt
- some maniocs
- one onion
- fat

## Directions

1. Cook the stomach in water and salt with some maniocs
2. Remove from the pot and drain it
3. Chop it into small pieces and cut the maniocs into slices
4. Add them with a cut onion to a frying pan containing hot fat
5. Stir and serve
