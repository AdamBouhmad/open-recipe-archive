## Ingredients

- Ray fish
- Milk
- One spoon butter
- Half spoon wheat flour
- Parsley
- One onion
- Cloves
- Salt
- Peppers
- Butter
- Tapioca flour or breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the fish
2. Cook in milk with one spoon of butter, half spoon of wheat flour, parsley, one onion, cloves, salt and peppers
3. When cooked, remove and drain, then let cool
4. Coat the fish with butter and wrap in tapioca flour or breadcrumbs
5. Fry on both sides
6. Reduce the strained broth and serve with the fish
