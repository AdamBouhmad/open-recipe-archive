## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- one quarter pound of powdered sugar
- a little lemon zest
- nutmeg
- three spoonfuls of milk cream
- egg whites
- two spoonfuls of butter
- powdered sugar
- one glass of cognac

## Directions

1. Beat eight egg yolks with one quarter pound of powdered sugar, a little lemon zest, nutmeg and three spoonfuls of milk cream
2. Add the whites and beat all together
3. Melt two spoonfuls of butter in a saucepan, and when hot, pour the beaten eggs
4. When fried, place on a plate
5. Cover with a light layer of powdered sugar
6. At serving time, pour one glass of cognac over the omelette
7. Set the omelette on fire
8. When burning, serve
