## Ingredients

- blackberries
- white wine
- sugar
- cinnamon
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Choose and wash a portion of blackberries and boil with white wine, sugar, and cinnamon until they fall apart
2. Pour into a tureen over toasted bread slices and serve
