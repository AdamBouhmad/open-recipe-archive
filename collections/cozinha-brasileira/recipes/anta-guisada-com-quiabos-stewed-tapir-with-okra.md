## Ingredients

- a portion of tapir meat, cut into pieces
- water
- salt
- two tablespoons of fat
- one cup of water
- one tablespoon of cornstarch
- tomatoes
- a portion of okra, cut into slices
- peppers
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one lemon, peeled and cut into slices

## Directions

1. Cut a portion of tapir meat into pieces
2. Boil it in water and salt until cooked
3. Remove it and sauté with two tablespoons of fat, one cup of water, one tablespoon of cornstarch, tomatoes, a portion of okra cut into slices, peppers, parsley, onion leaves, and one peeled lemon cut into slices
4. Let it boil for another half hour
5. Serve with corn porridge made from mill cornmeal
