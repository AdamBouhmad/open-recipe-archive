## Ingredients

- a little fat
- pieces of roasted beef
- a quantity of grated bread crusts
- onion leaves
- parsley
- salt
- one pimiento
- a few cups of beef broth or beef substance for sauces
- vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry in a little fat pieces of roasted beef and a quantity of grated bread crusts
2. After half an hour, add onion leaves, parsley, salt, one pimiento, and a few cups of beef broth or beef substance
3. Allow to boil for another half hour
4. Strain and, if desired, add a little vinegar or lemon juice before serving
