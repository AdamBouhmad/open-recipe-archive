## Ingredients

- couves (cabbage)
- agua (water)
- gordura (fat)
- sal (salt)

## Directions

1. Chop a portion of selected cabbage and wash it with cold water
2. Melt a spoon of fat and salt, add the cabbage, add a little water, let it boil and serve
