## Ingredients

- six to eight eggs
- two spoonfuls of milk
- a little fine salt
- ground pepper
- one spoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Beat six to eight eggs, yolks and whites, with two spoonfuls of milk
2. Add a little fine salt and ground pepper
3. Melt one spoonful of butter in a frying pan
4. When hot, pour the eggs
5. When sticking to the frying pan, lift with the point of a knife and add one more spoonful of butter
6. When melted, flip the eggs, fry on the other side, and serve
7. The taste of this omelette can be varied with seasonings such as nutmeg, onion, ginger, cinnamon and sugar, as well as other additions such as grated cheese, potatoes, breadcrumbs, chopped herbs, chopped meat, etc.
