## Ingredients

- one monkey, cut into pieces
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- wheat flour
- lard
- potatoes
- vinegar
- parsley
- onions
- fat

## Directions

1. Cut the monkey into pieces, except the head
2. Soak them in lemon juice with salt and pepper for six hours
3. Remove them, wrap them in wheat flour, and roast them on a grill, dripping a little lard on them from time to time
4. At this time, take some potatoes which are peeled and cut into slices
5. Add half a cup of good vinegar, a little salt, peppers, chopped parsley and onions
6. Add one tablespoon of fat, stir and serve cold with the fried monkey
