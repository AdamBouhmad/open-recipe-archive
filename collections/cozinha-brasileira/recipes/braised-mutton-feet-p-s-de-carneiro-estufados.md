## Ingredients

- mutton feet
- water
- salt
- pepper
- garlic clove
- onion
- bacon
- carapicu
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil the feet in water, salt, pepper, one garlic clove and one onion cut in pieces until almost cooked
2. Remove the bones and strain the broth through a sieve
3. Return the feet to this broth, whole, with a good piece of bacon, a handful of carapicu and a little vinegar
4. Let boil until the broth is almost dry
5. Place two spoonfuls of the same broth on a platter, cover with dried crackers in powder, placing the mutton feet on top
6. Bake in the oven for half an hour, and serve with pea or lentil puree
