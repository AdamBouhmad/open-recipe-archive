## Ingredients

- two pounds beef
- half pound fresh bacon
- one onion
- one clove garlic
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- four to six spoons wheat flour
- three eggs
- rendered fat

## Directions

1. Chop together two pounds of beef with half pound of fresh bacon, one onion, one clove of garlic, salt, parsley and fine pepper.
2. Add more four to six spoons of wheat flour and three eggs.
3. Knead everything together.
4. Form then balls the size of ordinary English potatoes, which are fried in rendered fat, this should be very hot at the moment the balls are put into it.
5. Serve with any of the sauces indicated for beef.
