## Ingredients

- whole fish
- water
- salt
- butter
- parsley, chopped
- ground pepper
- grated nutmeg
- one bottle of wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak the whole fish in salt water for two hours
2. Remove and place in a pan lined with butter and chopped parsley
3. Sprinkle with ground pepper and grated nutmeg
4. Pour a bottle of wine over the top
5. Cover the pan and place in the oven or over coals with fire below and above
6. Let boil for half an hour
7. Moisten with lemon juice before serving
