## Ingredients

- veal portion
- sweet oil
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Place a veal portion in a pan with six spoonfuls of sweet oil, pepper, salt, parsley, onion, garlic and nutmeg
2. Fry over coals, turning occasionally until cooked, and serve with some spicy sauce
