## Ingredients

- veal meat
- sweet oil
- lemon juice
- anchovies
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, chopped
- bread, grated

## Directions

1. Place the veal on a spit, baste while roasting with a sauce made of sweet oil, lemon juice, anchovies, onion, salt, pepper and chopped parsley
2. Finally put grated bread over the roast
3. Remove from spit and serve with the sauce that dripped on the plate below
