## Ingredients

- artichokes
- water
- butter
- wheat flour
- white wine
- olive oil
- vinegar
- peppers

## Directions

1. Remove the lower leaves of the artichokes which are not eaten
2. Cut the tips of the leaves with scissors
3. Place them in a steamer and set this in a pot with water
4. Cook them by steam
5. When cooked, remove the interior and the choke it contains
6. Serve hot with a sauce of fried butter, wheat flour diluted with white wine, or cold with a sauce of olive oil, vinegar and peppers
