## Ingredients

- one spoon of butter
- one spoon of wheat flour
- two cups of milk
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Melt one spoon of butter
2. Mix one spoon of wheat flour
3. Add two cups of milk, salt and pepper
4. Put the small potatoes in this sauce
5. Let it boil a little and serve
