## Ingredients

- two tablespoons fresh butter
- parsley
- onion leaves
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- half tablespoon wheat flour
- one bowl of water
- dried cod, washed
- one glass of bitter orange juice

## Directions

1. Melt two tablespoons of fresh butter.
2. Add some parsley, onion leaves, pepper, grated nutmeg, half tablespoon of wheat flour and one bowl of water.
3. Place the washed cod in this sauce.
4. Let it cook for half an hour.
5. Serve with this same sauce, after adding one glass of bitter orange juice to it.
