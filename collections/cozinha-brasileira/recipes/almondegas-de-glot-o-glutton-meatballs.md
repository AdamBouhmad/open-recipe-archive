## Ingredients

- two pounds finely chopped beef
- one dozen sardines
- one dozen potatoes
- wheat flour
- one egg
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. To two pounds of finely chopped meat add one dozen sardines and the same amount of potatoes, reducing everything to a paste.
2. To this paste also add two spoons of wheat flour, one egg, salt, pepper and chopped parsley.
3. Form balls which are fried in butter.
