## Ingredients

- washed and dry coffee
- brown sugar or refined sugar
- bread

## Directions

1. Place the washed and dry coffee in a small pan proper for roasting coffee; roast over a lively fire, stirring with a bread spoon until it is of suitable color; remove then from the fire and place in a coarse sieve to let the smoke out; put in the same pan one pound of brown sugar or refined sugar for four pounds of coffee; add the roasted coffee, stir well and let cool, then crush or grind the coffee and save it for daily use.
