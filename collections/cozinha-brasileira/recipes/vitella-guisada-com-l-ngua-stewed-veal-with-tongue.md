## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- veal portion
- butter
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- sweet oil
- white wine
- meat broth
- carapicú

## Directions

1. Take a cooked beef tongue, remove the skin, cut into strips and lard a veal portion with this tongue
2. Fry the portion in butter, turning occasionally
3. On the other hand, fry half a dozen onions with salt, pepper and parsley in three or four spoonfuls of fresh sweet oil, pour this mixture over the veal
4. Add two glasses of white wine and the same amount of meat broth and let finish cooking over moderate heat
5. Finally, add a handful of selected carapicú and serve
