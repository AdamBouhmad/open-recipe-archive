## Ingredients

- game meat, cut in small pieces
- lemon juice
- salt
- parsley
- peppers
- garlic clove
- onions
- marjoram
- fat for frying
- egg yolks
- wheat flour
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut the game meat into small pieces, soaking them in lemon juice, salt, parsley, peppers, a clove of garlic, onions and marjoram; after six hours, fry them in fat, and when fried, brush them with egg yolks, coating them afterwards in wheat flour, roasting them thus prepared on the grill, and dripping melted butter; when fried, serve
