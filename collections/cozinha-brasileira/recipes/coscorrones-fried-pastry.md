## Ingredients

- two cups of milk
- one egg white beaten
- salt
- half tablespoon of sugar
- two tablespoons of tapioca starch

## Directions

1. Mix two cups of milk with one beaten egg white, a little salt, half a tablespoon of sugar, and two tablespoons of tapioca starch
