## Ingredients

- pork loins
- six cloves of garlic
- two tablespoons of Indian pepper powder
- salt
- one bottle of wine
- some chili peppers
- pork tripe
- cornmeal
- cold water

## Directions

1. Cut the loins into small pieces and place in a vessel with six crushed garlic cloves, two tablespoons of Indian pepper powder, plenty of salt, one bottle of wine and some chili peppers.
2. Leave everything mixed for twenty-four hours, stirring occasionally with a wooden spoon.
3. Use pork tripe, and when they are thin, cook two together.
4. Fill the tripe, tie them well at intervals, pass them through boiling water as they are being filled, and place in the smokehouse, away from the fire for twenty days.
5. The tripe should be washed with cornmeal and placed in cold water, which should be changed until the sausages are full, which should be kept in a bag to go to the smokehouse.
6. In the absence of wine to season the meat, replace it with well-salted water.
7. Cold weather is preferable for making sausages.
