## Ingredients

- ten eggs
- a little butter
- sweets of choice

## Directions

1. Beat ten eggs well.
2. Put a little butter in a frying pan, and when it is melted, pour the eggs into it.
3. When cooked, pour them onto a serving dish so that half the eggs remain in the pan.
4. In this state, add the sweets you wish, and cover them with the other part that remains in the pan, and serve hot.
