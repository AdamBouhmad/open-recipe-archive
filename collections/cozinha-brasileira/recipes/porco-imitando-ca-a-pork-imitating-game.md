## Ingredients

- pork leg, ribs or loin
- vinegar
- thyme
- marjoram
- bay leaf
- sage
- coriander
- salt
- juniper berries
- clove
- pepper
- onion
- parsley
- mint sprigs
- walnut hulls

## Directions

1. Take either the leg, ribs or loin of pork, place them to soak in escabeche for twenty-four hours, in vinegar, thyme, marjoram, bay leaf, sage, coriander, salt, juniper berries, clove, pepper, onion, parsley, some sprigs of mint, and the hulls of walnuts.
2. Leave all in escabeche for eight days; the pork meat thus prepared will have the taste and appearance of wild boar.
3. For this it is necessary that the pork be young and lean.
