## Ingredients

- one snake
- salt
- pepper juice
- garlic
- sour orange juice
- egg yolks
- bacon slices
- onion
- parsley
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take a snake and rub with salt, pepper juice, garlic, and sour orange juice
2. Pass through egg yolks and place in a casserole on bacon slices, covering with others
3. Place chopped onion, well-chopped parsley and grated nutmeg on top
4. Roast over coals, covering, and placing a lid on top as well
5. It will be roasted in about one hour and serve with some compote
