## Ingredients

- twelve hard-boiled eggs
- one spoon of butter
- one spoon of wheat flour
- one chopped onion
- one egg yolk
- one cup of milk
- salt
- one spoon of sugar
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Cut a dozen hard-boiled eggs into slices, place on a plate
2. Make a sauce of one spoon of butter, one spoon of wheat flour, and one chopped onion, and fry a little; add one egg yolk beaten in one cup of milk, a little salt, one spoon of sugar and cinnamon powder
3. Pour over the eggs and serve
