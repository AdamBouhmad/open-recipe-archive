## Ingredients

- salted fish
- cachaca
- pork fat
- one onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- manioc flour

## Directions

1. This recipe is mainly for fish that have been preserved salted
2. Soak them for four hours, then dry them well, and place them in a casserole
3. Pour cachaca over them, heating the casserole and adding fire to burn the cachaca and let it burn completely
4. In another part, fry a little pork fat with one split onion, chopped parsley, salt, pepper and a little manioc flour
5. Pouring this over the fish placed on the plate, serve
