## Ingredients

- one pound rice powder
- two bottles milk
- a little salt
- one pound sugar
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil one pound of rice powder with two bottles of milk, a little salt and one pound of sugar, stirring constantly
2. When cooked, remove with a spoon in spoonfuls and arrange on a plate
3. Put in the oven to bake and brown
4. Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar and serve
