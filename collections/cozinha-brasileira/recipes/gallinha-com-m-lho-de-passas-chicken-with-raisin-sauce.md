## Ingredients

- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- one tablespoon of butter
- one bowl of water
- one glass of red wine
- salt
- nutmeg
- three cardamom seeds
- chicken pieces
- one handful of raisins
- one dozen blanched and chopped almonds
- two tablespoons of sugar
- lemon slices, peeled and without seeds

## Directions

1. Fry one tablespoon of wheat flour in one tablespoon of butter until well toasted
2. Add one bowl of water, one glass of red wine, salt, nutmeg, and three cardamom seeds
3. Place the chicken pieces in this sauce
4. Let them simmer until cooked
5. Add one handful of raisins, one dozen blanched and chopped almonds, two tablespoons of sugar, and peeled lemon slices without seeds
6. Bring to a boil once more
7. Serve in this state
