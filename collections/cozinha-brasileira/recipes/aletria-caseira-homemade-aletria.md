## Ingredients

- wheat flour
- five eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Make a dough with wheat flour, five eggs and a little salt
2. Beat well until it shows air bubbles when cut
3. Unroll the dough on a cloth and when well extended and thin, roll it up on itself
4. Cut the roll into very fine strips
