## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- half a dozen onions
- quarter pound of fat
- salt
- pepper
- one cup of water
- one small spoon of cornstarch
- one glass of vinegar
- one spoon of English mustard

## Directions

1. Boil a dozen eggs until hard, shell and cut into four pieces
2. In another pan, fry half a dozen onions in a quarter pound of fat; add salt, pepper and one cup of water with one small spoon of cornstarch dissolved in one glass of vinegar
3. Let boil, and when of good consistency, add one spoon of English mustard; place the eggs in the sauce and serve, heating a little
