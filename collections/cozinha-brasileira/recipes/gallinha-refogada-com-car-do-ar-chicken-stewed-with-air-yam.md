## Ingredients

- one clean chicken
- water
- salt
- onion leaves
- parsley
- marjoram
- large peppers
- air yams, split

## Directions

1. Take the clean chicken and cut it into pieces
2. Place these pieces in a pan with already boiling water, salt, onion leaves, parsley, marjoram, large peppers and split air yams
3. Let them boil and when cooked, serve
