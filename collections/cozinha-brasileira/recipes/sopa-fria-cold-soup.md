## Ingredients

- grated bread crust
- half a pound of sugar
- lemon peel
- two or three slices of Galician lemon
- a little nutmeg grated
- one bottle of red wine
- two bottles of water

## Directions

1. Put in a tureen grated bread crust, half a pound of sugar, lemon peel, two or three slices of Galician lemon, a little grated nutmeg and one bottle of red wine with two bottles of water
2. Serve as is
