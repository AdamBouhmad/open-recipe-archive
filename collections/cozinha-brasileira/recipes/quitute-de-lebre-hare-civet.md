## Ingredients

- hare
- four ounces of bacon cut into pieces
- four ounces of butter
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- one glass of red wine
- one glass of meat broth or water
- salt
- pepper
- seasoning herbs
- some juniper berries
- twelve small onions
- sugar
- hare liver
- hare blood

## Directions

1. Ordinarily the legs are roasted on a spit, and the civet is made from the rest.
2. Cut the hare into pieces, splitting the head as well; set aside the blood and liver.
3. Fry four ounces of bacon cut into pieces with four ounces of butter in a casserole until everything is well browned.
4. Add the pieces of hare to take on color, sprinkling them with one spoonful of wheat flour, and after mixing, add one glass of red wine, another glass of meat broth or water, little salt, pepper, seasonings, a bunch of aromatic herbs, and some juniper berries.
5. When the hare is half-cooked, add twelve small onions, which will be browned in butter with a little sugar.
6. Let them cook; five minutes before serving, add the liver, which is then removed to make a sauce for roasted meat.
7. Add the blood to thicken the sauce, and after it takes a little more heat, serve.
