## Ingredients

- two roe of dourado or piaba fish
- a piece of fresh fish the size of an egg
- six heads of green onions, finely chopped
- a little butter
- parsley
- green onion leaves
- lemon juice
- twelve eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the roe in boiling and slightly salted water.
2. Take a piece of fresh fish the size of an egg, to which six finely chopped heads of green onions are added.
3. Chop together the roe and the fish so as to mix everything very well, and put everything in a casserole with a little butter to sauté until the butter is melted, which is where the delicacy of this dish lies.
4. Take a second portion of butter at will, which is kneaded with parsley and green onion leaves and place this mixture on a long serving dish, on which the omelet is to be placed.
5. Pour a little lemon juice over the mixture and place the dish over coals.
6. Beat twelve eggs and the sautéed roe and fish so that everything is well mixed.
7. Make the omelet in the manner already indicated for other omelets, and place it on the prepared dish.
8. Be careful that neither the eggs nor the fish boil.
