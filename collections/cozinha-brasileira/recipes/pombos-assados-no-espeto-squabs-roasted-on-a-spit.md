## Ingredients

- squabs
- bacon strips

## Directions

1. Take the squabs and wrap each one in a strip of bacon
2. Roast on a spit over moderate fire for about half an hour
3. Serve with its sauce
4. Do not allow the squabs to dry while roasting, otherwise they will become insipid
5. Only roast squabs that can already fly
