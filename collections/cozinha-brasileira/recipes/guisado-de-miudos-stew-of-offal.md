## Ingredients

- pork offal (lungs, heart, liver, kidneys, stomach, tongue)
- water
- salt
- fat
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- pimenta da India
- cumin
- bay leaves
- tomatoes
- wheat flour
- cassava flour or corn flour

## Directions

1. The offal includes the lungs, heart, liver, kidneys, stomach, and tongue.
2. Take the pork offal and blanch in water and salt.
3. After blanching, cut it into small pieces and sauté in very hot fat, having brought all the seasonings such as salt, garlic, chopped onion, parsley, pimenta da India, cumin, bay leaves, and some tomatoes.
4. Add a little water after everything is well seasoned.
5. Take two tablespoons of wheat flour, dissolve in a little water, and thicken the offal, which is eaten with cassava flour or cornmeal mush.
6. If desired, add more water to the broth to make a cassava flour porridge, which is made in a deep platter by pouring the hot broth over it little by little and stirring with a fork.
7. Garnish the platter with sprigs of parsley and some pieces of the offal.
