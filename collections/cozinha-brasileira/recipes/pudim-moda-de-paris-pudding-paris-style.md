## Ingredients

- one pound of beef marrow
- one quarter of bacon
- half a pound of rice powder
- seven egg yolks
- two whole eggs
- half a cup of whole milk
- one glass of vanilla liqueur
- salt
- nutmeg
- half a dozen peeled and minced apples
- one dozen peeled and minced almonds
- two pounds of wheat flour
- one eighth of salt
- two spoonfuls of water
- four eggs
- one quarter of beef kidney fat
- four egg yolks
- one spoonful of arrowroot
- salt
- two spoonfuls of sugar
- one cup of white wine

## Directions

1. Knead one pound of beef marrow with one quarter of bacon, half a pound of rice powder, seven egg yolks, two whole eggs, half a cup of whole milk, one glass of vanilla liqueur, salt, nutmeg, half a dozen peeled and minced apples, and one dozen peeled and minced almonds
2. On the other hand, knead two pounds of wheat flour, one eighth of salt, two spoonfuls of water, four eggs, and one quarter of beef kidney fat
3. Extend this dough over a cloth
4. Combine the above mixture, join the ends of the cloth, tie them, and place the dough to boil in a pot with plenty of water
5. Boil for one hour and remove the cloth carefully so as not to break the dough, serve with a sauce made of four beaten egg yolks with one spoonful of arrowroot, salt, two spoonfuls of sugar, one cup of white wine, bringing everything to a boil a little, and serve
