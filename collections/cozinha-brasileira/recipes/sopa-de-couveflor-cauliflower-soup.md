## Ingredients

- one head of cauliflower, finely chopped
- meat broth
- a little salt
- parsley
- two heads of onions or cauliflower
- fried bread slices

## Directions

1. Blanch one finely chopped head of cauliflower
2. After cooling and draining, cook meat broth with a little salt and parsley
3. When ready to serve, pour this broth into a tureen that already contains two blanched heads of onions or cauliflower, kept whole and separate, over fried bread slices
4. Serve while hot
