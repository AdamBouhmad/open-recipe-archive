## Ingredients

- one bird (arara, papagaio, etc.)
- three spoonfuls of fat
- one dish of selected and washed rice
- one bottle of water
- salt
- parsley
- one chopped onion
- two hot peppers (pimentas comaris)
- four to five tomatoes

## Directions

1. Sauté the bird cut into pieces in three spoonfuls of fat.
2. Remove and, when the fat is hot again, sauté one dish of selected and washed rice.
3. Stir and when well heated, add one bottle of water, salt, parsley, one chopped onion, two hot peppers, four to five tomatoes, and the bird's meat.
4. Let boil for one quarter of an hour.
5. Remove the pan from active heat and let the rice dry, placing the pan near the fire.
6. Serve.
