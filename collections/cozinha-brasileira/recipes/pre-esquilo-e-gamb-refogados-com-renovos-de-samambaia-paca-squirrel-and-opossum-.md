## Ingredients

- one paca, squirrel or opossum
- fat for sautéing
- breadcrumbs
- parsley
- onion, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- one cup of water
- one spoon of vinegar
- fern fronds

## Directions

1. Take a paca, squirrel or opossum, remove the odor, clean it and wash it with hot water
2. Cut off the head and sauté it in fat
3. Turn it to fry equally on all sides, and dust it with breadcrumbs, adding parsley, chopped onion, salt and pepper
4. Add one cup of water and one spoon of vinegar
5. Let it boil for half an hour, and pour it over fern fronds cooked in salted water, arranged on a plate, and serve with its sauce
