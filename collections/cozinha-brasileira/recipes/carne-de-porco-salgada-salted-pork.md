## Ingredients

- pork meat
- salt
- saltpeter

## Directions

1. Use mostly the loin for this purpose
2. Cut the loin into pieces and rub them with crushed salt and saltpeter
3. Arrange them in a vessel and cover, taking care to press the meat pieces well so they do not get air
4. Place a good quantity of salt on top so the meat is completely covered
5. Place a white cloth over the vessel
6. After eight days the meat is sufficiently salted
7. This can be kept for three to four months, though the fresher the salt pork, the more flavorful it is
8. It frequently appears in the composition of all kinds of dishes with cabbage, lentils, or turnip pulp
