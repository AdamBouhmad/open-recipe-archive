## Ingredients

- three spoonfuls of fat
- one onion
- parsley
- rabbit pieces
- salt
- cumin
- lemon slices
- orange juice
- bread slices
- butter
- grated cheese
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Fry an onion and a little chopped parsley in three spoonfuls of fat
2. Add the pieces of the rabbit cut up, with salt, cumin, slices of lemon and orange juice, and let them boil
3. When cooked, place on slices of bread fried in butter, which will have been placed at the bottom of a plate, and on which a layer of grated cheese will have been placed
4. Place on top of this a portion of melted butter
