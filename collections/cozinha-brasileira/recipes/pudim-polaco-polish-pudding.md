## Ingredients

- eight spoons of flour
- eight eggs
- one spoon of marrow
- salt
- a portion of raisins
- milk as needed

## Directions

1. Mix eight spoons of flour with eight eggs, one spoon of marrow, salt and a portion of raisins
2. Add enough milk to form a mixture of good consistency
3. Place this mixture on a cloth
4. Tie it
5. Put in a pot with water to cook over high heat
6. Serve with a sauce for puddings
