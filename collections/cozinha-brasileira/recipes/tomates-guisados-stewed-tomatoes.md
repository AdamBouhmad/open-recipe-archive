## Ingredients

- two dozen ripe tomatoes
- cow's butter
- wheat flour
- pepper
- one glass of wine

## Directions

1. Cut two dozen ripe tomatoes
2. Scald them and remove with a skimmer
3. Place in a casserole with cow's butter
4. Sprinkle with wheat flour and pepper
5. Fry a little
6. Add one glass of wine
7. Let boil a little and serve
