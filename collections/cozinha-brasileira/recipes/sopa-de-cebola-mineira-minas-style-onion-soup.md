## Ingredients

- one or two onions, finely chopped
- butter
- one spoonful of wheat flour or cornmeal
- hot water
- cornmeal or bread slices

## Directions

1. Fry finely chopped onions in a spoonful of butter until they begin to color
2. Add one spoonful of wheat flour or cornmeal and continue frying until cinnamon-colored
3. Add hot water as needed
4. Boil briefly
5. Pour this broth over cornmeal or bread slices
