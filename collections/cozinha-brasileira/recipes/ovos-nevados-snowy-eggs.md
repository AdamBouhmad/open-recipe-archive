## Ingredients

- six egg yolks
- one bottle of boiled milk
- a little salt
- a little sugar
- some pieces of cinnamon
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat six egg yolks and mix in one bottle of boiled milk with a little salt
2. Add a little sugar and some pieces of cinnamon
3. Place on the fire and let boil a little
4. Place in a tureen
5. Separately, beat the egg whites until stiff
6. Having placed a large saucepan on the fire with one or two bottles of water or milk, drop a full spoonful of the beaten white, repeating until the white is finished
7. Remove with a skimmer, letting it drain well
8. Pour over the milk and cover with sugar and cinnamon powder
9. Serve
