## Ingredients

- one paca
- boiling water
- liver from two chickens
- carapicús
- manioc flour
- one onion
- salt
- comarí peppers
- chopped parsley
- two tablespoons pork fat
- water
- one cup orange wine
- bacon slices
- rice
- tomatoes

## Directions

1. Put a paca in a pot and pour boiling water over it.
2. Place the pot over fire, let it boil once, then remove the paca and place it in cold water.
3. Chop together the liver from two chickens, a portion of carapicús, manioc flour, and one onion.
4. Mix with salt, comarí peppers, and chopped parsley.
5. Fry this mixture in two tablespoons of pork fat, adding a little water, and boil for ten minutes.
6. Stuff the paca with this filling.
7. Place it in a pot over bacon slices with one cup of orange wine.
8. Seal the pot lid and boil over moderate fire for three to four hours.
9. Serve with rice fried with tomatoes.
