## Ingredients

- 1 pound of meat
- 1 onion
- 1 parsley root
- a little bacon
- 1/2 cup water
- salt
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Cut 1 pound of meat into small pieces
2. Place them in a pan with an onion, a parsley root, a little bacon, and half a cup of water
3. Let it boil over coals for a quarter hour with the vessel well covered
4. Add 2 jars of water and a little salt
5. Let it boil for one hour
6. Strain the broth over bread slices to serve at table
