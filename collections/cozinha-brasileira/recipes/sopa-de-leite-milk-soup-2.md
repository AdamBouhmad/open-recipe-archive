## Ingredients

- milk
- salt
- sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Boil milk with a little salt and sugar
2. When serving, add four beaten egg yolks and keep on the fire, stirring constantly until nearly boiling
3. Pour the soup into a tureen over bread slices and serve
