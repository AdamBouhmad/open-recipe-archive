## Ingredients

- agouti
- salt
- pepper
- cornmeal from cangica (cornmeal flour)
- egg yolks
- fat, two spoons
- cooked manioc, cut in long strips

## Directions

1. Cut the agouti into pieces and sprinkle with salt and pepper
2. Wrap them in cornmeal from cangica
3. Pass them in egg yolks
4. Cover again with cornmeal from cangica
5. Fry in two spoons of fat with some cooked manioc cut in long strips
6. Turn them so they fry on both sides and take on good color
7. Serve
