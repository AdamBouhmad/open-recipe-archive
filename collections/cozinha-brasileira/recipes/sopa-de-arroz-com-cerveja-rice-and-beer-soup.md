## Ingredients

- one full cup of rice
- one bottle of water
- one bottle of beer
- two spoons of sugar
- raisins
- one egg yolk
- one cup of white wine

## Directions

1. Cook one full cup of rice with one bottle of water, one bottle of beer and two spoons of sugar
2. Finally add some raisins, one well-beaten egg yolk and one cup of white wine, then serve
