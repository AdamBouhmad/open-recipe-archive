## Ingredients

- loins of sorubim
- wheat flour or cangica cornmeal
- two tablespoons of fat
- carapicus
- two chicaras of milk

## Directions

1. Cut the loins of sorubim into strips
2. Wrap them in wheat flour or cangica cornmeal, and sauté in two tablespoons of fat
3. Boil a portion of carapicus in two chicaras of milk, add the fried fish, and let everything boil together for half an hour over moderate heat, and serve
