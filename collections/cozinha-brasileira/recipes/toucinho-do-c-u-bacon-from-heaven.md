## Ingredients

- one quarter pound shelled almonds
- half cup milk
- six egg yolks
- two egg whites
- one pound wheat flour

## Directions

1. Pound one quarter pound of shelled almonds until they form a homogeneous paste
2. Add little by little half cup of milk, six egg yolks, two egg whites, and one pound of wheat flour
3. Knead everything well, place in a form buttered with butter
4. Bake in the oven and serve, having dusted with sugar and ground cinnamon
