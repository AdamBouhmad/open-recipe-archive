## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- tubaras (a type of tuber)
- sausage meat
- caul fat

## Directions

1. After the pig's feet have been prepared and cooked as previously explained, remove all the bones.
2. On the other hand, chop the skin of the tubaras and pound it with a little sausage meat.
3. Stuff the pig's feet with this filling, adding to them some slices of tubaras, and wrapping everything in pieces of caul fat.
4. Place on a moderate fire to roast.
