## Ingredients

- pork meat
- hot fat
- milk
- salt
- parsley leaf
- onion leaf
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the pork meat into thin slices.
2. Pass these in hot fat, put them in a pan with two cups of milk, salt, parsley leaf, onion leaf, cinnamon and nutmeg.
3. Let boil for half an hour over a moderate fire, and when the broth has thickened with two egg yolks, serve.
