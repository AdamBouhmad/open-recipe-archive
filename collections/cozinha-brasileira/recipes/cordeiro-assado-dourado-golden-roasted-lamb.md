## Ingredients

- leftover roasted lamb
- onions
- parsley
- bread slices
- butter
- toasted breadcrumbs or grated cheese
- four egg yolks
- one tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Mince leftover roasted lamb with onions and parsley
2. Place on bread slices fried in butter
3. Cover with toasted breadcrumbs, grated cheese, or both
4. Pour four beaten egg yolks mixed with one tablespoon sugar over the top
5. Place the plate in the oven or over coals
6. Serve
