## Ingredients

- two pork legs
- ten pounds of salt
- half pound of ground pepper from India
- four ounces of saltpeter

## Directions

1. The following recipe is for preparing two hams.
2. After cutting two legs, round them off, cutting away the scraps around them; prick the skin a little with the point of a knife so that the seasoning can penetrate them.
3. Place on a table ten pounds of salt, half a pound of ground pepper from India, and four ounces of saltpeter.
4. After rubbing them with this mixture, place them in a small tub, putting the rest of the salt on top, and leave them there for seven days.
5. After this time, remove them and bring the brine to a boil.
