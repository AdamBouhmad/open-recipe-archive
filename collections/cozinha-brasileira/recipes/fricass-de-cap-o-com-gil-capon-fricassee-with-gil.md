## Ingredients

- one capon roasted on a spit
- two pounds of roasted mutton
- two spoons of wheat flour, toasted
- two spoons of butter
- one cup of broth
- one cup of red wine
- nutmeg
- one dozen giló fruits

## Directions

1. Take one roasted capon and two pounds of roasted mutton; cut into pieces
2. Make a sauce with two spoons of wheat flour toasted in two spoons of butter, and add one cup of broth and one cup of red wine
3. Add the chopped meat with a little nutmeg
4. When cooked, place around the plate one dozen giló fruits, boiled in water and salt
