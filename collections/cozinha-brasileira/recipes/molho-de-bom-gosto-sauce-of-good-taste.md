## Ingredients

- Butter
- Eggs with whites and yolks
- Bread of three ounces
- Milk
- Vanilla
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Beat a spoonful of butter with two eggs with whites and two more yolks
2. Mix with three ounces of bread, soaked in milk
3. Add necessary milk, a little vanilla and sugar
4. Let it boil for a little while
5. Pass through a sieve
6. Serve with any pudding
