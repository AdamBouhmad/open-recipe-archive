## Ingredients

- lettuce
- water
- salt
- wheat flour
- butter
- milk

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of chopped lettuce in water and salt
2. After five minutes, remove and place in cold water
3. In another pan, mix half a spoonful of wheat flour with one spoonful of butter and two spoonfuls of full cream milk
4. Add the lettuce and when it has boiled a little over moderate fire, serve
