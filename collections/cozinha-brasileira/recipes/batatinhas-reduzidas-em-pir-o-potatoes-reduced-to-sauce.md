## Ingredients

- some peeled potatoes
- water
- salt
- cow butter
- pepper
- nutmeg
- one cup of fatty milk

## Directions

1. Cut some peeled potatoes and boil them in water and salt
2. When cooked, drain them, mash them, and put the pulp in a pan with cow butter, salt, pepper, nutmeg and one cup of fatty milk
3. Boil over moderate heat until dry
4. Add one cup of milk to the mass again, boil until dry, repeating this operation five or six times
5. Put on a plate, forming a pyramid, put in the oven to take color, and serve
