## Ingredients

- veal rump
- cloth
- salt
- pepper
- fine pork fat
- butter
- paper
- salad or spicy sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Wrap the veal rump in cloth after rubbing with salt, ground pepper, and fine pork fat
2. Place on the spit, covered with paper greased with butter, and roast
3. When roasted, remove the paper and continue roasting to create color
4. Serve with some salad or spicy sauce
