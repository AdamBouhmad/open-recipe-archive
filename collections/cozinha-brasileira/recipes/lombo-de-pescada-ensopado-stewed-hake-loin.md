## Ingredients

- hake loins
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- breadcrumbs
- beaten eggs
- white wine
- broth

## Directions

1. Remove the loins from a good fish, sprinkle them on all sides with salt and a little pepper
2. Pass them in melted butter, and cover with a layer of breadcrumbs
3. Pass them in beaten eggs and wrap again in another layer of breadcrumbs
4. Roast them on the grill over a very low fire, and when well browned, put them on a plate
5. On the other hand, melt two tablespoons of butter, mix with half a cup of white wine and one cup of broth
6. Boil, pour very hot over the fish, and serve
