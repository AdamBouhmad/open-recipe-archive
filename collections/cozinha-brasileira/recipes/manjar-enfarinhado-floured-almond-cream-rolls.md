## Ingredients

- almond cream mixture
- one pound butter
- four egg yolks
- two tablespoons sugar
- pastry casing
- melted butter
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. After making the almond cream mixture with eggs as already described, knead one pound of butter, four egg yolks and two tablespoons of sugar
2. Remove the mixture with a spoon and place inside a piece of pastry casing
3. Roll and press the edges cutting them with a pastry wheel
4. Fry these rolls in melted butter and serve with sugar and cinnamon
