## Ingredients

- leftover roasted goose
- one quince, peeled and cut
- one onion, cut
- mangaritos or potatoes, fried in butter and sliced
- one cup water
- one cup red wine
- one egg yolk
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one half cup vinegar
- one cup red wine

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover roasted goose into pieces
2. Place on the fire with the peeled and cut quince, cut onion, fried potatoes or mangaritos sliced, one cup of water and one cup of red wine
3. Let simmer a little
4. At the end, add an egg yolk mixed with one tablespoon of wheat flour, dissolved in one half cup of vinegar and one cup of red wine
5. Give it another boil and serve
