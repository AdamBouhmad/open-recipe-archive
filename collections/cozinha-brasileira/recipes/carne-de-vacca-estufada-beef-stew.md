## Ingredients

- cooked beef cut into small pieces
- good piece of butter
- two spoons of wheat flour
- beef broth
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley
- onion leaves
- English potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked beef into small pieces
2. Put these in a pan with a good piece of butter
3. When the butter is melted, add two spoons of wheat flour
4. Stir and add beef broth, salt, pepper, nutmeg, parsley, and onion leaves
5. Leave everything to boil for about one hour
6. Serve with cooked English potatoes
