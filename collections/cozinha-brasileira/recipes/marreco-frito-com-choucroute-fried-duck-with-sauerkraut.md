## Ingredients

- 1 duck, cut into pieces
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- fat for frying
- fermented cabbage or fermented cabbage
- little water
- salt
- one cup of vinegar

## Directions

1. After soaking the duck pieces in salt and pepper, roll them in breadcrumbs
2. Fry in plenty of fat
3. When fried and of good color, remove them
4. In the same fat, place a portion of fermented cabbage or blanched cabbage, adding little water, salt, and one cup of vinegar
5. Let the cabbage braise and finish cooking near the fire, stirring occasionally to avoid burning
6. Serve with the fried duck placed on top
