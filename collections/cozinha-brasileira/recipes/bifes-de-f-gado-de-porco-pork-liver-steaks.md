## Ingredients

- pork liver
- salt
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- pepper from India
- wine
- fat

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into slices the thickness of a finger
2. Place these in a vessel with salt, garlic, onion cut into pieces, parsley, pepper from India, and half a glass of wine
3. After soaking in this seasoning for two hours, place them in a pan with some very hot fat
4. Layer the slices one on top of the other
5. Cover the pan hermetically, putting a little cornmeal mush on the edges
6. Let them cook for half an hour over gentle heat
