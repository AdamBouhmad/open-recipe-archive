## Ingredients

- one chicken
- two bottles milk
- half pound rice powder
- one pound sugar
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook a chicken in two bottles of milk
2. When cooked, remove the chicken, bone it and chop it very fine
3. Pound in a mortar until reduced to a paste
4. Return the paste to the milk with half pound of rice powder, one pound of sugar and chopped lemon peel
5. When the mixture is cooked and incorporated, pour on a plate
6. Cover with sugar and cinnamon, put in the oven to bake and toast and serve
