## Ingredients

- one pound rice powder
- two bottles milk
- eight egg yolks
- one pound sugar
- cloves
- egg whites
- half pound sugar

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of rice powder in two bottles of milk with eight egg yolks, one pound of sugar and cloves
2. When everything is cooked and thick, arrange on a plate
3. Cover with beaten egg whites mixed with half pound of sugar so that it forms a meringue
4. Put in the oven and when browned, serve
