## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- six spoonfuls of broth
- bacon slices
- bouquet of aromatic herbs
- one or two onions
- cloves

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the tongue with six spoonfuls of broth, bacon slices, a bouquet of aromatic herbs, one or two onions, and cloves
2. When the broth is reduced to a quarter of its original amount, let it cool
3. Fill the tongue with bacon strips
4. Thread it onto a spit and roast for one hour
5. Serve with a spicy sauce
