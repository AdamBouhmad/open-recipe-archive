## Ingredients

- venison meat
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- two onions, chopped
- potatoes
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. Cut the venison meat into thin slices and sprinkle with salt and pepper
2. Fry in butter with two chopped onions
3. In another place, cook a portion of potatoes, peel and mash them to form a homogeneous mass
4. Put this mass to simmer with very rich milk or preferably cream
5. Let simmer, stirring well to avoid burning on the bottom
6. When it reaches the necessary consistency, put it on a plate
7. Place the meat with its butter on top and serve
