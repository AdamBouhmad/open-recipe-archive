## Ingredients

- calf liver
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chive leaves
- one garlic clove
- sweet oil or butter
- wheat flour
- grated cheese
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into very fine slices and season with salt, pepper, parsley, chive leaves and one garlic clove
2. Fry in a casserole with sweet oil or butter
3. When cooked, place on the plate
4. Sprinkle with wheat flour and grated cheese
5. Pour a little melted butter over it
6. Place over coals, let it heat well and serve
