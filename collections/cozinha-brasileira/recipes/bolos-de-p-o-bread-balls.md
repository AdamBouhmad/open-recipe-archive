## Ingredients

- bread the size of two closed hands
- milk
- four tablespoons of wheat flour
- four eggs
- half tablespoon of sugar
- salt
- nutmeg
- fat

## Directions

1. Soften in milk a piece of bread the size of two closed hands and knead with four tablespoons of wheat flour, four eggs, half a tablespoon of sugar, a little salt and nutmeg
2. Remove small portions with a spoon and place in boiling water
3. When they float, remove with a skimmer, cut into slices, fry in fat, and serve
