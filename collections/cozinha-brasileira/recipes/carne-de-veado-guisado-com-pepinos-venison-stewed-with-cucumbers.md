## Ingredients

- venison meat
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, minced
- ginger, grated
- water
- bread crumbs
- butter
- sugar
- one chalice of white wine
- two chalices of water
- lemon juice
- lemon, sliced
- two egg yolks
- cucumbers, peeled and cut in fillets

## Directions

1. Cut the venison meat into slices the thickness of a finger, beat them a little and season with salt, pepper, minced parsley and grated ginger
2. Put to boil for five minutes in water that is already hot on the fire
3. Remove and coat in bread crumbs and fry in butter
4. After frying on both sides, remove from the fat, in which pour a portion of bread crumb pieces with one spoonful of sugar; stir
5. Add one chalice of white wine and two of water, the juice of two lemons, one lemon cut in slices, two egg yolks, and a portion of peeled cucumbers cut in fillets
6. Let simmer a little, add the meat slices, and after simmering a little, serve
