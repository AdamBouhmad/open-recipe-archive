## Ingredients

- half a pound of sweet almonds or watermelon seeds
- six glasses of milk
- a little sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Take half a pound of sweet almonds or watermelon seeds; crush them, moistening them with a little milk.
2. Take six glasses of milk, put them with the almonds or watermelon seeds, add a little sugar and orange blossom water, and pass everything through a fine sieve.
3. Bring to a boil over a gentle fire and reduce.
4. Pass through a sieve again, pour into a dish, and let cool before serving.
