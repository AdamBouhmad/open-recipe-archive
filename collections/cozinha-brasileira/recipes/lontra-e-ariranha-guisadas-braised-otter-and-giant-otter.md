## Ingredients

- half or one quarter of an otter or giant otter
- water
- salt
- pepper
- cumin
- parsley leaves
- onions
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- one spoonful of sugar
- two spoonfuls of fat
- one chopped onion
- one cup of water
- half a dozen tomatoes

## Directions

1. Boil half or one quarter of an otter or giant otter in water with salt, pepper, cumin, parsley leaves, and onions.
2. When the meat is cooked, remove the bones and cut into pieces.
3. Fry one spoonful of wheat flour and one spoonful of sugar in two spoonfuls of fat with one chopped onion until browned.
4. Add the meat, stir, and add one cup of water and half a dozen tomatoes.
5. Let everything boil for another half hour and serve.
