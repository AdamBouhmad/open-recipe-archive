## Ingredients

- pie dough
- salt cod cooked in white wine
- hard-boiled eggs
- bacon
- onions
- catfish
- fat
- artichoke hearts
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Take a box of pie dough and place on the bottom a hash made of salt cod cooked in white wine, hard-boiled eggs, bacon, and onions
2. Place on top of it a portion of catfish fried in fat and mixed with artichoke hearts that have been cooked
3. Cover with the rest of the hash, place the lid, and bake in the oven, serve with a spicy sauce
