## Ingredients

- cabeça de repolho (head of cabbage)
- cebola (onion)
- pimenta (pepper)
- noz moscada (nutmeg)
- carne de porco gorda (fatty pork)
- toucinho (bacon)
- salsa (parsley)
- vinagre or sumo de limão (vinegar or lemon juice)
- caldo de carne (meat broth)

## Directions

1. Blanch a head of cabbage, open it a little, remove the core without breaking it, and fill this space with an onion, pepper, nutmeg and a piece of fatty pork
2. Then place the cabbage in a pot lined with bacon strips, two onions, the chopped cabbage core, parsley, a cup of vinegar or lemon juice and enough meat broth
3. Let it boil over moderate heat, and serve it with its own sauce
