## Ingredients

- Lamb square
- Hot water
- Cold water
- Salt
- Parsley
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Half pound of plums
- Quince pieces
- Four spoonfuls of sugar
- Chalice of white wine
- Lemon juice
- Four egg yolks

## Directions

1. Beat the lamb square lightly and scald it in hot water
2. Place in cold water with salt, parsley, bay leaves, and thyme in a vessel over fire
3. Let boil until nearly cooked and broth nearly dry
4. Add half pound of plums, or in their absence, peeled quince pieces without seeds, four spoonfuls of sugar, a chalice of white wine, and a little lemon juice
5. Boil for half an hour
6. Thicken the broth with four egg yolks
7. Serve
