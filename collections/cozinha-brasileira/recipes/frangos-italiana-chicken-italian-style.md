## Ingredients

- two young chickens, cut into pieces
- wheat flour
- lemon juice
- sweet olive oil
- salt
- bay leaves
- pepper
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cut the chickens into pieces and coat with wheat flour
2. Soak in a mixture of lemon juice, sweet olive oil, salt, bay leaves, and pepper
3. When flavored, remove and dry the pieces
4. Pass through grated cheese
5. Fry in butter and serve
