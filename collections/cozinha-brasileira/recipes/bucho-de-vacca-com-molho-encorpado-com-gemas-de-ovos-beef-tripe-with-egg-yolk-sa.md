## Ingredients

- beef tripe, prepared as in recipe 94
- butter
- mushrooms, sliced
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- three egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the beef tripe as described in recipe 94
2. When cooked, cut into round slices the size of a forty-reis coin
3. Place these pieces on the fire with butter, sliced mushrooms, salt, pepper and nutmeg
4. After they have boiled for a little while, add three egg yolks and a little lemon juice
5. Serve the tripe on a platter with slices of bread
