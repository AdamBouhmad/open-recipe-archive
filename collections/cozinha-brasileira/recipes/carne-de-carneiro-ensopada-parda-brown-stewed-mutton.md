## Ingredients

- mutton, cut into pieces
- fat, one spoon
- vinegar, one cup
- salt
- parsley
- peppercorns
- garlic, one clove
- onion leaves
- wheat flour
- sugar, burned
- two cups red wine
- sugar, one spoon
- dozen almonds, chopped
- raisins, scalded

## Directions

1. Cook the mutton cut into pieces with one spoon of fat and one cup of vinegar, salt, parsley, peppercorns, one clove of garlic and onion leaves
2. When almost cooked, add a little wheat flour until well toasted
3. Add burned sugar cooked in a spoon, two cups red wine, one spoon sugar, a dozen chopped almonds and scalded raisins
4. Boil the meat a little more in this sauce and serve
