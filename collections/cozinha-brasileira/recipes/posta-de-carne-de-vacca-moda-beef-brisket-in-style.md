## Ingredients

- beef brisket
- bacon
- salt
- seasonings
- calf meat
- carrots
- onions
- green herbs
- white wine
- cognac
- beef broth
- pepper
- roasted onion

## Directions

1. Fill the brisket with thick pieces of bacon previously salted.
2. Add the appropriate seasonings and tie it.
3. Place it in a casserole.
4. Add a piece of calf meat, two carrots, four onions, a bunch of green herbs, a glass of white wine, a goblet of cognac, two glasses of beef broth, salt, pepper, and a bit of roasted onion.
5. Bring to a boil over high heat.
6. Baste the meat with the sauce in the casserole and cook for five hours over low heat, always basting.
7. Drain and untie the meat, placing it on a platter surrounded by the calf meat cut into pieces.
8. Add the carrots and onions around it.
9. Pass the broth from the casserole through a silk sieve and return it to the fire until it reduces to half.
10. Use this to baste the meat, and serve the rest in a tureen.
