## Ingredients

- beef hand or foot
- hot water
- bacon skins
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- bay leaf
- mustard
- vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Scald a beef hand or foot in hot water
2. After scraping and removing the hooves, cut into pieces at the joints
3. Place on the fire with some bacon skins, salt, pepper, parsley, and bay leaf
4. Boil vigorously for six hours
5. Remove the mocotó pieces and bacon skins, letting the broth thicken
6. When almost ready, add one spoonful of mustard and a little vinegar or lemon juice
7. Serve
