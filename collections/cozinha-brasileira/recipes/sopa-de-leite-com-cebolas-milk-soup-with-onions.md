## Ingredients

- onions
- butter
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- polvilho biscuits
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Fry onions cut into rounds in butter and pour into milk
2. When they begin to brown, add salt and a little pepper
3. Bring to a boil and pour over polvilho biscuits that have been chopped and fried in butter
