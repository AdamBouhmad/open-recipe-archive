## Ingredients

- fresh cod, grouper or cobia fish, cut into steaks
- salt
- water
- cooked potatoes
- melted butter
- parsley, chopped
- or cream sauce with butter
- parsley
- onion, finely chopped
- whole milk

## Directions

1. Soak the fish steaks in salt water for twelve hours
2. Bring a pan of salted water to a boil and add the fish steaks
3. Boil for ten minutes
4. Remove and serve with cooked potatoes and melted butter mixed with chopped parsley and a little of the cooking water
5. Alternatively, make a cream sauce by melting two spoons of fresh butter, adding a pinch of parsley, a finely chopped onion and a cup of whole milk, boil for some time and serve hot with the fish
