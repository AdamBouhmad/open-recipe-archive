## Ingredients

- two spoons butter
- parsley
- onion
- carapicús
- one or two roasted fish
- more parsley
- carapicús
- butter

## Directions

1. In a heat-resistant plate, place two spoons of butter and a thick layer of chopped parsley and onion mixed with chopped carapicús
2. Place one or two roasted fish on top
3. Cover with another portion of herbs and carapicús and another piece of butter
4. Cover the plate with another plate filled with coals
5. Place over fire, cook and serve in the same plate while hot
