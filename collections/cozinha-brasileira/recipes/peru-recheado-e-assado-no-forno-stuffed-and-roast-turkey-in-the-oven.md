## Ingredients

- two pounds veal
- dozen Nantes sardines
- half pound bacon
- handful of carapicús
- dozen olives
- peppers
- two onions
- two cloves
- handful of dried rusks
- four tablespoons butter
- four egg yolks
- nutmeg
- cinnamon
- tablespoon sugar
- salt
- bacon slices

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of veal, a dozen Nantes sardines, half a pound of bacon, a handful of carapicús, a dozen olives, some peppers, two onions and two cloves, chop it all very finely and fill the cavity of the turkey's belly with this mixture.
2. On the other hand, knead a handful of dried rusks ground with four tablespoons of butter, four egg yolks, nutmeg, cinnamon, a tablespoon of sugar and a little salt.
3. With this mixture, fill the crop of the turkey.
4. Wrapped in thin bacon slices, place it in a pan to roast in the oven and serve with its sauce.
