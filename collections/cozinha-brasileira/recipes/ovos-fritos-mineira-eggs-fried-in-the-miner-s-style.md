## Ingredients

- six eggs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Break six eggs into a bowl.
2. Add a little ground salt and pepper.
3. Beat until frothy.
4. Fry a piece of lard in a saucepan until the fat is hot.
5. Pour in the eggs and fry without stirring.
6. When fried (not too hard), place on a plate and serve.
7. Some cover them with a little sugar and powdered cinnamon.
