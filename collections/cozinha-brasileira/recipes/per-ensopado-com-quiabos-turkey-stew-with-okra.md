## Ingredients

- half a pound of bacon
- two tablespoons of wheat flour
- turkey wings, legs and neck
- one bowl of water
- one glass of white wine
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- clove
- some chopped carrots
- a portion of okra
- half a dozen tomatoes

## Directions

1. Fry half a pound of bacon and after melting remove the cracklings
2. Add two tablespoons of wheat flour to the hot fat, stir and then add the turkey wings, legs and neck
3. Let them fry a little, turning the pieces and add one bowl of water, one glass of white wine, salt, parsley, pepper, clove, some chopped carrots, a portion of okra and half a dozen tomatoes
4. Leave to boil for one hour over very moderate heat until the broth is almost dry
5. Serve
