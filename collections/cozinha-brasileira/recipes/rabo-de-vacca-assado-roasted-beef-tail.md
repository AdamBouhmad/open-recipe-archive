## Ingredients

- beef tail
- cold water
- bacon pieces
- veal meat slices
- beef meat slices
- thin bacon strips
- three carrots
- three onions
- cloves
- thyme
- bay leaf
- nutmeg
- meat broth
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Take a beef tail and cut it at each joint
2. Place in cold water for two hours
3. Give it a boil for half an hour
4. Cool in cold water, drain, and dry with a cloth
5. In a casserole, melt bacon pieces and add slices of veal and beef meat, covering with very thin bacon strips
6. Add three carrots, three onions with two cloves inserted in each, thyme, bay leaf, and a little nutmeg
7. Moisten with meat broth and add the tail pieces
8. Boil, gradually reducing the heat for three or four hours
9. Remove and arrange in the center of a platter, surrounding with carrots covered with the meat substance and lettuce leaves
