## Ingredients

- venison meat
- water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- ginger, grated
- fava beans, fresh
- wheat flour
- one spoonful of butter
- one chalice of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces and let it boil with water, salt, some pepper, parsley, onion leaves, grated ginger, and a portion of fresh fava beans
2. When the meat is cooked, fry a little wheat flour in one spoonful of butter until it takes color
3. At this point add one chalice of vinegar and the broth in which the meat and beans were cooked
4. Let the broth reduce, return the meat and beans to it
5. Once everything is heated together, serve
