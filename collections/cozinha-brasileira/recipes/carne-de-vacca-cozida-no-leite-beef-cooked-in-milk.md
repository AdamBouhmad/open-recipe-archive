## Ingredients

- beef
- milk
- salt
- sugar
- nutmeg
- bay leaves

## Directions

1. Take the washed beef and place it on the fire with milk that has already been boiled with salt
2. After it cools, add a little sugar, nutmeg, little salt, and bay leaves
3. Leave to boil
