## Ingredients

- two pounds mutton, chopped (after removing nerves and skin)
- one pound pork with some fat
- two onions
- pepper
- salt
- cloves
- parsley
- handful crushed good biscuit powder
- one spoon cornstarch
- two eggs (yolk and white)
- one cup wine
- fat for frying

## Directions

1. Chop two pounds mutton (after removing nerves and skin) with one pound pork with fat, two onions, pepper, salt, cloves, parsley, a handful of crushed good biscuit powder, one spoon cornstarch, two eggs and one cup wine
2. Melt a little fat in a pot and when very hot, add the chopped mixture
3. When fried on one side, turn to fry the other side and serve with a spicy sauce
