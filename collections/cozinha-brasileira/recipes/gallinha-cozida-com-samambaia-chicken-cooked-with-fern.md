## Ingredients

- one chicken, cut in pieces
- half a pound of bacon, chopped
- water
- salt
- onion leaves
- fern shoots

## Directions

1. Place the chicken pieces with half a pound of chopped bacon in a pan containing water, salt, and onion leaves
2. Let it simmer
3. When the chicken is cooked, remove it
4. In the same broth, simmer the fern shoots
5. When cooked, arrange them in order on the plate and cover with breadcrumbs
6. Pour melted butter over them
7. Serve with the chicken in the middle
