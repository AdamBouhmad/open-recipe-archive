## Ingredients

- celery stalks
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- reduced Spanish sauce
- meat broth
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Take a portion of celery stalks, peel and wash them, using the tender leaves and give them one boil in plenty of water
2. Remove, allow to cool, squeeze to drain the water, and chop them as one chops herbs
3. Place in a casserole a little butter, and with it the chopped celery, with salt, pepper, and a little nutmeg
4. Pour over top a little reduced Spanish sauce and the same amount of meat broth and allow to reduce
5. Serve with slices of bread placed around the plate
