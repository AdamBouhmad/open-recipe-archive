## Ingredients

- meat
- milk
- bacon
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- spicy sauce or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Soak the meat for two days in curdled milk.
2. Lard it and put it to roast on the spit.
3. Brush melted butter on it frequently.
4. When removing from the fire, sprinkle ground breadcrumbs on top.
5. Serve with spicy sauce or tomato sauce.
