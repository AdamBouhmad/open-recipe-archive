## Ingredients

- Butter
- Cornstarch
- Vinegar
- Water
- Marmalade
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Fry a little butter until it takes on color
2. Add a little cornstarch, a little vinegar, water, a piece of marmalade dissolved in hot water, and some slices of lemon
3. Let it boil for one hour
