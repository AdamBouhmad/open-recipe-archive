## Ingredients

- Fish
- Fine strips of bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Half bottle of white wine
- Four to five spoons of sweet olive oil
- Lemon juice
- Two spoons of capers
- One spoon of wheat flour
- One spoon of fried butter

## Directions

1. Remove scales and clean the fish
2. Lard the loins with fine strips of bacon well seasoned with salt, pepper, and nutmeg
3. Insert on the spit lengthwise
4. Tie the head and tail securely
5. Roast over regular fire, occasionally dampening with a sauce made of half a bottle of white wine, four to five spoons of sweet olive oil, and lemon juice
6. When roasted, remove from spit and place on a plate
7. Serve with the sauce in a tureen, after adding two spoons of capers, salt, pepper, one spoon of wheat flour beaten with one spoon of fried butter
