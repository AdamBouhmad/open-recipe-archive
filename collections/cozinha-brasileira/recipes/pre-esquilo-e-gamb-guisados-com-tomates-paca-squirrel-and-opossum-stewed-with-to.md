## Ingredients

- pieces of the game
- water
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- marjoram
- onion leaves
- ginger
- two dozen tomatoes
- two spoons of fat
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the pieces of game in water, salt, peppers, parsley, marjoram, onion leaves and ginger
2. When cooked, remove them
3. On the other hand, sauté two dozen tomatoes with two spoons of fat; add the pieces of game cut in quarters, and add the strained broth reduced by half and incorporated with two egg yolks
4. Let everything boil for a quarter of an hour and serve
