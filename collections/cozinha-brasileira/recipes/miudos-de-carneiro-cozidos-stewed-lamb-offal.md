## Ingredients

- lamb lungs
- lamb heart
- lamb liver
- lamb tongue
- lamb feet
- hot water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- peppers
- butter
- wheat flour
- white wine
- cornmeal (fubá) or cassava flour (farinha de mandioca) for serving

## Directions

1. After cleaning the lungs, heart, liver, tongue and feet of the lamb, scald them in hot water
2. Cook them then in water with salt, parsley, onion leaves and some peppers
3. When cooked, remove the bones from the feet and drain the broth
4. Thicken the broth (reducing it first if necessary) with one spoonful of butter kneaded with another of wheat flour and one glass of white wine
5. Return the offal to this sauce and let it boil a little
6. Serve with cornmeal porridge (angú de fubá de moinho)
