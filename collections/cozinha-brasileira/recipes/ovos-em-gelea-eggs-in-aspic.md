## Ingredients

- four whole eggs
- two cups of meat broth
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Beat four whole eggs with two cups of meat broth, salt, and pepper in a bowl of sufficient capacity.
2. Place the bowl in a pot of hot water, taking care that no water enters the bowl.
3. Leave until the contents of the bowl become hard.
4. Let cool a little.
5. Turn the bowl over onto a plate.
6. Serve.
7. Alternatively, divide the mixture into cups and proceed as described, serving with a spicy sauce or cream.
