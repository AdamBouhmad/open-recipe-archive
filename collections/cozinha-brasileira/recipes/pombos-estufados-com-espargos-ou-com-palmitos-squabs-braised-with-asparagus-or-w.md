## Ingredients

- squabs
- salt
- chopped parsley
- pepper
- onion
- cloves
- sweet oil
- four tablespoons butter kneaded with two tablespoons wheat flour
- one cup meat broth
- a portion of asparagus or palm hearts
- breadcrumbs
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Soak the squabs for twenty-four hours in salt, chopped parsley, pepper, onion, cloves and sweet oil
2. Place them in a pot with four tablespoons butter kneaded with two tablespoons wheat flour and one cup of meat broth
3. Keep them well covered and allow to boil for one hour over moderate fire
4. Meanwhile, boil in water and salt a portion of asparagus or palm hearts, tied in bundles
5. When cooked, place them untied on the plate, covered with breadcrumbs fried in butter
6. Place the squabs on top and pour over everything the sauce from the squabs
