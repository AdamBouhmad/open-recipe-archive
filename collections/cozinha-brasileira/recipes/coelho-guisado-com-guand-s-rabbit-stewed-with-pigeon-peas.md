## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- water
- salt
- parsley
- ginger
- peppers
- pigeon peas
- two spoonfuls of butter kneaded with one spoonful of wheat flour
- two egg yolks
- one cup of vinegar
- two spoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil a rabbit in water, salt, parsley, ginger, peppers, and a portion of pigeon peas
2. Remove the rabbit and reduce the broth
3. At the end add two spoonfuls of butter kneaded with one spoonful of wheat flour, and two egg yolks beaten in one cup of vinegar mixed with two spoonfuls of sugar
4. After it has boiled a little, add the rabbit, continue to boil a little and serve
