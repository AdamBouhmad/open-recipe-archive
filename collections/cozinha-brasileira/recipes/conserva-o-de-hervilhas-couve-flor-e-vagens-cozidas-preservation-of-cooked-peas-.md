## Ingredients

- peas or cauliflower or beans
- water
- salt
- fat
- onion
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Take a portion of any of these vegetables and place in a pan with little water and salt and boil for a quarter hour
2. While still hot, pour into wide-mouthed jars, which should be almost full
3. Place these jars in a pot with water, the water reaching to the neck of the jars, and bring to a boil for fifteen to twenty minutes
4. Cover the jars well and keep stored
5. To use them, melt a little fat in a pan and fry with a little chopped onion and a little wheat flour
6. Add the vegetables and after cooking for half an hour, serve
