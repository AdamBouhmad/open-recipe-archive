## Ingredients

- dried cod
- water
- parsley
- celery root
- peppers
- onion leaves
- fat
- cassava flour
- heart of palm, chopped
- water

## Directions

1. Soak the cod for twelve hours in cold water
2. Wash in hot water
3. Place on fire with cold water, parsley, celery root, peppers and onion leaves
4. When cooked, remove the bones
5. Braise in two spoons of fat, sprinkle with cassava flour
6. Add chopped heart of palm and half a bottle of water
7. Simmer for another half hour and serve
