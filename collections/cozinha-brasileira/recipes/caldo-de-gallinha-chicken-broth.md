## Ingredients

- one chicken
- salt and seasonings
- one spoonful of arrowroot
- two cups of cold water
- two egg yolks
- four spoonfuls of vinegar or juice of lime

## Directions

1. Cook the meat of a chicken as when making soup
2. After adding seasonings to thicken the broth, add one spoonful of arrowroot dissolved in two cups of cold water
3. Add two egg yolks dissolved in four spoonfuls of vinegar or lime juice
