## Ingredients

- one handful of bread crumbs, softened in milk
- one quarter of cow butter
- one quarter of raisins and carapicús, blanched and finely chopped
- one spoon of sugar
- nutmeg
- the giblets of the capon, blanched and finely chopped
- four egg yolks
- bacon, sliced
- grated breadcrumbs
- sweet preserve

## Directions

1. Knead together a handful of bread crumbs softened in milk, one quarter cow butter, one quarter raisins and blanched carapicús finely chopped, one spoon of sugar, nutmeg, the capon's giblets blanched and finely chopped, and four egg yolks
2. Fill the capon with this mixture and roast it in the oven in a pan lined with bacon slices
3. When cooked, roll it in grated breadcrumbs and return it to the oven to brown
4. Serve with some sweet preserve
