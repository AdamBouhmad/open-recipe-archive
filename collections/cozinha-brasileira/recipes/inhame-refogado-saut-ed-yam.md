## Ingredients

- yams
- water
- salt
- fat
- garlic
- onion
- Indian pepper

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of well-washed yams without peeling them in water and salt
2. After cooking, remove the skins and cut into small pieces
3. Sauté in fat with salt, garlic, onion, and Indian peppers
4. Add a little water, boil a little, and serve
