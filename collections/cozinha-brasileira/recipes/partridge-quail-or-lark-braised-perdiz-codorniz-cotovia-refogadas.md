## Ingredients

- one bird
- salt
- pepper
- one pound of fat beef
- parsley
- cilantro
- onion leaves
- one cup water
- one chalice of juice from land orange

## Directions

1. Take the bird, rub it with salt and pepper, and wrap it in strips of bacon
2. place it in a pan with one pound of fat beef, salt, pepper, parsley, cilantro, onion leaves, one cup water, and one chalice of juice from land orange
3. cover the pan and let it boil for one hour over moderate heat
4. then braise one tablespoon of wheat flour in two tablespoons of butter
5. fry the bird in this mixture and immediately add the broth in which the bird was cooked
6. let it cook for another half hour and serve
