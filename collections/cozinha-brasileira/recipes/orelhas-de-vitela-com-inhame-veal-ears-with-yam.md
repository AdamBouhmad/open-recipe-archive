## Ingredients

- veal ears
- water
- salt
- ham
- bread crust
- clove
- nutmeg
- sugar
- butter
- egg yolk
- white wine
- yam roots

## Directions

1. Clean and boil the ears with water and salt
2. Fill them with a mixture of chopped ham, bread crust, clove, nutmeg, sugar, all kneaded together with butter and an egg yolk
3. Fry them in a little butter
4. Add a glass of white wine and yam roots boiled in water and salt
