## Ingredients

- rabbit
- salt
- peppers
- tomatoes
- bacon lard
- breadcrumbs
- milk cream
- cloves

## Directions

1. Remove the head of a rabbit and rub the body with salt, peppers and tomatoes, leaving it in these seasonings for five to six hours
2. Then lard it with slices of bacon, trimming their ends flush with the meat
3. Then wrap the rabbit in breadcrumbs, thread it onto a spit, roast it, basting it with milk cream to which some cloves have been added
