## Ingredients

- pigeons
- bread crumbs
- fat
- salt
- pepper
- squash shoots
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the pigeons into four pieces, coat them in bread crumbs, and fry in plenty of fat
2. Sprinkle with salt and ground pepper
3. When cooked, remove them and braise some squash shoots that have been peeled and boiled in water and salt in the same fat
4. Place the shoots on a plate with the fat and put the pigeons on top, then serve
