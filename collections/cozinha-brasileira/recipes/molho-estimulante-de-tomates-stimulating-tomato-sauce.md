## Ingredients

- two onions, chopped
- twenty or thirty tomatoes
- salt
- thyme
- bay leaves
- a little saffron
- some pimentos
- one or two spoons of beef broth
- one spoon of butter

## Directions

1. Fry two chopped onions, twenty or thirty tomatoes, salt, thyme, bay leaves, a little saffron, and some pimentos with one or two spoons of beef broth and one spoon of butter
2. After everything is cooked, pass through a sieve, pressing well to pass
3. Serve with meats and fish
4. Note: As this sauce is very stimulating, people with weak stomachs should abstain from it
