## Ingredients

- one dozen eggs
- two spoonfuls of milk
- one pound of sugar in syrup after cooling
- puff pastry dough
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat one dozen eggs with two spoonfuls of milk
2. Add one pound of sugar in cooled syrup
3. Return to fire, boil a little, stirring, and fill the puff pastry crusts with this mixture
4. Place the pastries in the oven, bake, and serve with a little sugar on top
