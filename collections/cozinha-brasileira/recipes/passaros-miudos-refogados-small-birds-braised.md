## Ingredients

- small birds
- two spoons of butter
- one peeled and chopped apple
- one onion
- one handful of breadcrumbs
- one glass of white wine
- one glass of water

## Directions

1. After cleaning the small birds, braise them in two spoons of butter
2. When fried, remove them and braise one peeled and chopped apple, one onion and one handful of breadcrumbs in the same butter
3. Moisten everything with one glass of white wine and one glass of water
4. Place the small birds in this sauce
5. Let them simmer for one quarter of an hour
6. Serve each bird placed on one slice of bread fried in butter and pour the sauce over it
