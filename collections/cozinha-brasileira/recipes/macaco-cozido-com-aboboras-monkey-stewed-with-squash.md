## Ingredients

- monkey cut into pieces
- water
- salt
- pepper
- one garlic clove
- parsley
- half a pound of bacon
- one pound of veal

## Directions

1. Boil the monkey cut into pieces in water with salt, pepper, one garlic clove, parsley, half a pound of bacon, and one pound of veal.
