## Ingredients

- four ounces of tubaras, clean
- four ounces of butter
- two cups of meat extract

## Directions

1. Fry four ounces of clean tubaras in four ounces of butter
2. Add two cups of meat extract
3. Let boil over moderate heat for one hour and serve
