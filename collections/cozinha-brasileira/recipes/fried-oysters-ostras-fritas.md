## Ingredients

- oysters
- two eggs
- half a spoon of polvilho
- one spoon of melted butter
- fat for frying
- pepper
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. After having parboiled the oysters in their own water, dry them one by one with a towel
2. Beat two eggs with half a spoon of polvilho and one spoon of melted butter
3. Pass the oysters through this mixture
4. Fry them in fat
5. Serve sprinkled with pepper and chopped parsley
