## Ingredients

- two tablespoons of butter
- a thick layer of chopped parsley and onion mixed with chopped carapicús (small fish)
- one or two roasted fish
- more herbs and carapicús
- another piece of butter

## Directions

1. In a dish that can withstand the action of fire, put two tablespoons of butter and a thick layer of parsley and onion chopped and mixed with chopped carapicús
2. Place one or two roasted fish on top and cover with another portion of herbs and carapicús, and another piece of butter
3. Cover the dish with another filled with hot coals, and placed over the fire, cook it, and serve it in the same dish still hot
