## Ingredients

- Lamb quarter
- Bacon lard with seasonings
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Bay leaves
- Bacon slices
- Carapicú
- Wine
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the beaten lamb quarter into large slices three fingers thick
2. Lard these with bacon seasoned with salt, pepper, garlic, parsley, and bay leaves
3. Place in a well-sealed and sealed casserole over bacon slices with a handful of carapicú
4. Put in oven or over coals with fire on top for one hour
5. Add a glass of wine and a spoonful of lemon juice
6. Let boil for another hour
7. Remove the fat and serve with its own sauce
