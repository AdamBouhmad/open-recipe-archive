## Ingredients

- one guan
- four hard-boiled eggs
- chicken breast
- half a palm heart
- salt
- ground Indian pepper
- white wine
- pie dough

## Directions

1. Open the bird along the back, remove the bones, chop the meat and the liver of the bird
2. Chop four hard-boiled eggs, salt, chicken breast, and half a palm heart
3. Season everything with salt, ground Indian pepper, and boil in white wine
4. When almost cooked, wrap it in a thick layer of pie dough, bake in the oven and serve
