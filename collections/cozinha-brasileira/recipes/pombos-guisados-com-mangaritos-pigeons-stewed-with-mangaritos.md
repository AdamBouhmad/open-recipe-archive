## Ingredients

- four large pigeons
- water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion
- a portion of mangaritos
- butter
- one chopped onion
- one tablespoon of wheat flour

## Directions

1. Boil four large pigeons with water, salt, pepper, parsley, onion and a portion of mangaritos
2. When cooked, remove them and divide into two parts
3. Fry them on both sides in butter and remove
4. In the same butter, fry mangaritos cut into slices; after they are well toasted, remove them from the heat
5. In this same butter, fry one chopped onion and one tablespoon of wheat flour, adding to it one large cup of the broth from the pigeons
6. Let it boil for a little while and add the pieces of pigeons and the mangaritos
7. Give it one more boil and serve
