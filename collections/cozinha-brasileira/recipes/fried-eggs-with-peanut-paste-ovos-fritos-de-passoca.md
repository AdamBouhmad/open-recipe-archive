## Ingredients

- six eggs
- two spoons of crushed peanuts
- salt
- one spoon of sugar
- nutmeg
- two spoons of wheat flour
- two spoons of cow butter

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs and add two spoons of crushed peanuts, a little salt, one spoon of sugar and nutmeg
2. Fry two spoons of wheat flour in two spoons of cow butter, stirring, and when very hot, pour in the eggs, continuing to stir until cooked
3. Serve, covering with sugar and cinnamon
