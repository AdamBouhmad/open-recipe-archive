## Ingredients

- postas of sorubim the thickness of a finger
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- fat
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take some steaks of sorubim the thickness of a finger
2. Sprinkle on both sides with salt, pepper and mustard
3. Fry in fat, and once fried, place on a plate, pouring over each steak a slice of peeled lemon, and squeezing a little lemon juice
4. Put a little hot fat and serve
