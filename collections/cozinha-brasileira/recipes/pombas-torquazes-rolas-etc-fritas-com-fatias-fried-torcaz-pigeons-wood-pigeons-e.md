## Ingredients

- pigeon meat
- quarter pound of bacon
- carapicú (a type of plant)
- cassava flour
- onion
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- white wine
- bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove all meat from the birds and chop with quarter pound of bacon, a handful of carapicú, another of cassava flour, onion, salt, parsley, and pepper
2. Mix everything and boil in a cup of white wine
3. When cooked, serve on slices of bread fried in butter
