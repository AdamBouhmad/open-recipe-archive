## Ingredients

- pork meat
- fat
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- hot water
- air yams
- six tomatoes
- six camari peppers
- two eggs
- two spoonfuls of vinegar

## Directions

1. Take some pieces of pork meat, or also the loin cut into pieces, braise with a little fat, salt, parsley, and onion leaves, stirring.
2. Add a large bowl of water, some peeled air yams cut into pieces, half a dozen tomatoes, six camari peppers.
3. Leave on the fire until everything is cooked.
4. Add two beaten eggs with two spoonfuls of vinegar, and when it has boiled, serve.
