## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- pepper
- saltpeter
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a tongue and after cleaning it, roast it superficially over a very lively fire so that the skin can be removed
2. Rub it with a little pepper, saltpeter and salt ground
3. Place it in a pan covered with salt under a board with a weight on top
4. After fifteen days it is removed and hung in the air
5. To cook it, place it in hot water for two hours, then put it in a pan full of water with some onions, Indian cloves and bay leaf, but without adding salt or pepper
6. Let it cook for six to seven hours over moderate fire and then remove it
7. Let it drain well to go to the table after it cools
