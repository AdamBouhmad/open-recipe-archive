## Ingredients

- beef tripe
- salt
- orange juice
- cold water
- hot water
- Indian pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- eggs
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Wash the tripe well in many waters
2. Place in a vessel with salt and orange juice and let sit until the next day
3. Remove and after washing once more in cold water, scald with hot water, leaving in this water until cooled
4. Place on the fire with cold water, salt, Indian pepper, parsley, and onion leaves
5. Let boil four to five hours
6. Add two or three eggs beaten in cold water
7. Let boil for another half hour
8. After adding slices of lemon, serve
