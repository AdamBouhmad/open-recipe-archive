## Ingredients

- one goose
- one heart of palm, cut
- salt
- pepper
- one onion, chopped
- one sprig of marjoram
- a portion of cloves
- some pieces of cinnamon
- two glasses of white wine
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Fill a goose with one heart of palm, cut and mixed with salt, pepper, one chopped onion, one sprig of marjoram and a portion of cloves studded in the breast, as well as some pieces of cinnamon.
2. Then place it in a pan with two glasses of white wine.
3. Cover the pan tightly and allow to boil over moderate fire for two to three hours.
4. Serve with its own sauce, after removing the fat, and placing around it a portion of potatoes fried in the same sauce.
