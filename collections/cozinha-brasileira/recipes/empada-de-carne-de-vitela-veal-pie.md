## Ingredients

- a piece of veal
- a little butter
- meat stock
- Madeira wine
- seasonings
- one egg

## Directions

1. Take a piece of veal and brown it with a little butter
2. Cut it afterward into small pieces that are placed in a cassole with meat stock, the same amount of Madeira wine, and seasonings
3. Let it boil for half an hour and fill the pie box with this dough
4. Cover it and place in the oven
5. When cooked, beat an egg and brush the dough with it to take on good color, and serve
