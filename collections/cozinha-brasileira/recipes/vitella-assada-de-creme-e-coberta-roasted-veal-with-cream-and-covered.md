## Ingredients

- veal quarter
- dry rusks
- butter
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- nutmeg
- onion

## Directions

1. Prepare as vitella assada de creme, but instead of larding the quarter, cover it with dry rusks before roasting on the spit
