## Ingredients

- one lizard, cleaned
- vinegar, hot
- fat
- one cup of water
- half a dozen tomatoes
- salt
- parsley
- peppers
- one onion, sliced
- one garlic clove
- two egg yolks
- one glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Take a cleaned lizard and scald it in a little hot vinegar
2. Cut it into pieces and braise in fat, immediately adding one cup of water, half a dozen tomatoes, salt, parsley, peppers, a sliced onion, and one garlic clove
3. Let it boil over regular heat for one hour
4. Incorporate the broth with two egg yolks dissolved in one glass of white wine and serve
