## Ingredients

- fish steaks
- water
- salt
- parsley
- marjoram
- pepper
- three egg yolks
- one cup of lemon juice
- two tablespoons of cassava flour
- one tablespoon of lard

## Directions

1. Take fish steaks, wash and boil in water with salt, parsley, marjoram and pepper
2. When cooked, remove the broth and mix three beaten egg yolks with one cup of lemon juice, two tablespoons of cassava flour and one tablespoon of lard
3. Boil until reduced to five or six cups of incorporated sauce, add the fish, heat and serve
