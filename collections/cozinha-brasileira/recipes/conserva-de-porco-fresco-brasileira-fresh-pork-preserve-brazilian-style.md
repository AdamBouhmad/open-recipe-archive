## Ingredients

- pork meat
- garlic wine
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the pork meat into portions and place in garlic wine for twenty-four hours
2. Then place in a vessel to cook in little water, being careful to turn the pieces so they cook evenly
3. Allow the water to reduce, at which point the fat from the portions melts and the meat takes on a suitable color
4. Remove the portions and place them in an earthenware or glazed vessel
5. Pour over them the melted fat in which the portions were fried, ensuring they remain well covered with fat
6. When wishing to use them, remove the desired portions with a fork, being careful to level the fat so it covers the remaining pieces
7. When wishing to serve them, fry them in fat and serve with one of the sauces indicated for meat
