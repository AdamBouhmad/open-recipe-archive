## Ingredients

- fat
- cornmeal
- onion
- white beans
- water
- salt
- eggs
- sour orange juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Fry two spoonfuls of fat with a spoonful of cornmeal and a sliced onion.
2. Add the white beans cooked in water and salt and drained.
3. Stir and add the bean broth.
4. While boiling, break some eggs into it so that they remain whole.
5. If desired, add a glass of sour orange juice and serve.
