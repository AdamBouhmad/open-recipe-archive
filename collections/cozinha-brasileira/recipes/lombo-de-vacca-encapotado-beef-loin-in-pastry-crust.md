## Ingredients

- beef loin
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- celery
- wheat flour
- eggs
- beef butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. After the loin has been seasoned with salt, pepper, bay leaf and celery for twenty-four hours, roast it on a spit
2. Once roasted, make a dough of wheat flour, eggs, beef butter, a little sugar and salt
3. Cover the loin with a thin layer of this dough, wrapping it in paper oiled with butter
4. Roast it for another hour
5. Finally remove the paper and serve with the sauce of your choice
