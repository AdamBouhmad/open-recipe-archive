## Ingredients

- forty tomatoes
- one pound of salted pork meat
- four onions
- parsley
- one clove
- a little pepper
- a quarter pound of good butter
- a portion of meat
- the crumb of one loaf of bread
- bread slices fried in butter

## Directions

1. Press forty tomatoes and place their pulp in a pan with one pound of salted pork meat, four onions, parsley, one clove, a little pepper, and a quarter pound of good butter
2. Sauté everything
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Add a portion of meat and the crumb of one loaf of bread
5. Let boil for one hour
6. Remove the fat and when ready to serve, pour the broth over bread slices fried in butter
