## Ingredients

- seven apples
- marmalade or other preserves
- gelée
- rice prepared for rice pudding
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Peel seven apples and remove the core with a spoon.
2. Cook them in a syrup with half the juice of a lemon.
3. When the apples are cooked, drain them.
4. Spread a layer of marmalade or other preserves on a dish.
5. Place the apples on top, fill the cavity of each with gelée and cover with rice prepared as for rice pudding and serve.
