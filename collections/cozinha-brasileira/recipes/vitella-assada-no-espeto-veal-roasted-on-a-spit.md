## Ingredients

- veal meat
- salt
- garlic
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- buttered paper
- butter
- vinegar
- broth

## Directions

1. Lard the veal by rubbing it with salt, garlic, pepper, parsley, and onion leaves
2. Thread onto a spit, covering with strips of buttered paper
3. When almost cooked, remove the meat and place in a pan with butter, vinegar, and a little broth
4. Cook over high heat to finish cooking and brown well
