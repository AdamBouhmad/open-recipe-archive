## Ingredients

- 4 or 6 sharks
- 1 spoon of sweet oil
- 1 onion
- a little chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- cloves from India
- bay leaves
- some slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Fry 4 or 6 sharks in 1 spoon of sweet oil with 1 onion and a little chopped parsley, salt, pepper, cloves from India, bay leaves and some slices of lemon
