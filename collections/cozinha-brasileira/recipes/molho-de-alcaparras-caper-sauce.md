## Ingredients

- 1 spoon of fat or cow butter
- 1 spoon of cornmeal
- 1 cup of broth or water
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 spoon of vinegar
- nutmeg
- some capers

## Directions

1. Heat a spoon of fat or cow butter in a saucepan
2. When melted, add 1 spoon of cornmeal and stir
3. Little by little add 1 cup of broth or water
4. After boiling, remove from heat and add the egg yolk dissolved in 1 spoon of vinegar
5. Add nutmeg and some capers
6. Let it boil once more and serve with meat or fish
