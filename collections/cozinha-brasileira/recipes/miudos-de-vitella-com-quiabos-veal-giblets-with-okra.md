## Ingredients

- veal tongue
- veal heart
- veal lungs
- veal liver
- water
- salt
- onions
- parsley
- chili pepper
- okra
- one glass wine
- lemon slices
- cornmeal mush for serving

## Directions

1. Cut the tongue and heart into slices
2. Chop the lungs into small pieces and pass the liver through a sieve
3. Place all in a pot with water, salt, sliced onions, parsley, and chili pepper
4. When everything is cooked and the water has almost dried up, add a dozen okra, one glass wine, and lemon slices
5. Give it another boil and serve with cornmeal mush
