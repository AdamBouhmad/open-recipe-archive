## Ingredients

- apples
- one pound of apple pulp
- one quarter pound sugar syrup
- one quarter pound washed raisins
- one quarter pound peeled and chopped almonds
- fennel
- one cup orange flower water

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of apples cut in pieces until they break apart and the water evaporates
2. Pass through a sieve
3. Mix one pound of this pulp with one quarter pound of sugar syrup, one quarter pound of washed raisins, one quarter pound of peeled and chopped almonds, a little fennel and one cup of orange flower water
4. Give it another boil
5. Serve
