## Ingredients

- dried beef from Rio Grande
- hot water
- salt cod
- palm fiber
- eggs
- onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- pork lard

## Directions

1. Soak the Rio Grande dried meat in hot water for twelve hours.
2. Remove and dry it well and chop it very finely.
3. Add to it an equal amount of salt cod.
4. For two pounds of this mixture, add one bowl of palm fiber, six eggs, one onion, parsley, all finely chopped, little salt and pepper.
5. Make a dough, and from it form balls, which are fried in pork lard, and served with corn porridge or bean paste.
