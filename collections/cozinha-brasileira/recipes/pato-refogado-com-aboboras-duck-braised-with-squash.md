## Ingredients

- one young duck
- one cup water
- one tablespoon vinegar
- salt
- one onion, cut
- four to six squash, cut into four to eight pieces

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from the duck bones
2. Pass the meat in fat until it is roasted
3. Add one cup of water, one tablespoon of vinegar, salt, and the cut onion
4. Add four to six squash cut into four to eight pieces, without peeling and without removing the center (the skin should still be as tender as the flesh itself)
5. Cover the pan and finish cooking near the fire
6. Serve
