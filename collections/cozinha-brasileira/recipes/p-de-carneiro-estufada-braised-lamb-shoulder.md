## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- bacon strips
- ham
- carapicú
- onions
- parsley
- salt
- water or meat broth
- cornstarch (polvilho)
- vinegar
- chicory or cabbage

## Directions

1. Place the shoulder in a pan on strips of bacon with ham cut into small pieces, a handful of carapicú, sliced onions, parsley and salt
2. Add a bowl of water or meat broth and let it boil
3. When cooked, remove the fat from the broth as well as the parsley and bacon
4. Thicken with half a spoonful of cornstarch
5. Add one spoonful of vinegar and serve with chicory or sautéed cabbage
