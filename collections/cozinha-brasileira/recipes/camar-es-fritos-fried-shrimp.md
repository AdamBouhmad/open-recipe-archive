## Ingredients

- portion of shrimp
- fat
- onion or parsley
- salt
- pepper
- lemon or sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Shell a portion of shrimp and fry in fat with chopped onion or parsley, salt and plenty of pepper
2. Let them toast well and serve squeezing lemon or sour orange juice over them
