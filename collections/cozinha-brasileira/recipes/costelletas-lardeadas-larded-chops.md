## Ingredients

- chops
- strips of beef tongue
- pieces of palmito (heart of palm)
- pieces of salt pork
- remaining palmito
- one onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- one glass of water
- one glass of orange wine
- 4 to 8 gilos

## Directions

1. Lard the chops alternately with strips of beef tongue and pieces of palmito
2. Place them to cook in a casserole with some pieces of salt pork, the rest of the palmito, a cut onion, parsley, salt, pepper, a glass of water and another of orange wine
3. Just before serving, add 4 to 8 gilos
