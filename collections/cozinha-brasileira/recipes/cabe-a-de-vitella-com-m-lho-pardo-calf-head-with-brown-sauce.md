## Ingredients

- calf head
- calf feet
- calf heart
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- herbs for seasoning
- toasted flour
- raisins
- bread crusts
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place the head, feet and heart to cook in water with salt, parsley, an onion, and a bundle of herbs
2. When all is cooked, remove the bones from the feet and head, leaving the meat and the nasal bone so the head retains its shape
3. Take the tongue, remove its skin, cut into rounds
4. Do the same with the heart
5. Place the prepared head on the plate, with the feet and tongue rounds, and heart rounds around it
6. Serve with sauce made from the broth, with toasted flour, some raisins, bread crusts, vinegar and sugar
