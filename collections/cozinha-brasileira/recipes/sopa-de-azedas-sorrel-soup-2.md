## Ingredients

- a portion of sorrel
- one head of lettuce
- a little chervil, finely chopped
- a little fat
- a portion of meat broth

## Directions

1. Sauté all of this in a little fat
2. Pass this mixture through a sieve so that no stalks or fibers remain
3. Pour a portion of any available meat broth over this sieved mixture
4. Bring to a boil for half an hour
5. Remove the fat
6. Pour over toasted bread slices and serve
