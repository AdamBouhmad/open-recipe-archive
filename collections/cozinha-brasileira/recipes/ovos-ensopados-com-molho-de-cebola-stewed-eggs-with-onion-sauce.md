## Ingredients

- eggs
- one onion
- one spoonful of butter
- wheat flour
- water
- one spoonful of vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil eggs in their shells until hard.
2. Shell and slice them into rounds.
3. Fry a chopped onion in one spoonful of butter.
4. Cover with wheat flour.
5. Add one glass of water, one spoonful of vinegar, salt, and pepper.
6. Simmer for a while.
7. Pour over the egg slices and serve.
