## Ingredients

- ten tablespoons of wheat flour
- ten tablespoons of grated cheese
- half dozen eggs
- salt
- milk if necessary

## Directions

1. Knead ten tablespoons of wheat flour, the same amount of grated cheese, half a dozen eggs, a little salt, and if necessary a little milk for the dough to be of good consistency
2. Roll to form pastries from which cut pieces one inch long
3. Place in a large pan that has boiling water and a little salt
4. When they float, remove and serve with sesame seeds or some fatty sauce
