## Ingredients

- veal meat, preferably the glands
- hot water
- meat broth
- pieces of salt pork
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onions

## Directions

1. Cut the veal meat, preferably the glands, into small pieces
2. Scald in hot water and let it cool
3. Place in a casserole with meat broth, some pieces of salt pork, salt, pepper, parsley and onions
4. Place the well-covered pot over coals and also put coals on top
5. Let it cook and serve with well-cooked sorrel or with some of the sauces for meat
