## Ingredients

- cutlets
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Take the cutlets and season with salt, pepper, a little garlic and lemon juice
2. Let them soak in these substances and beat a little
3. Coat with a little butter and roast on a grill, being careful to turn them
4. Serve with a spicy sauce à Robert or any other
