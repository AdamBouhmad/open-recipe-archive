## Ingredients

- one guinea fowl
- tomatoes
- one onion
- liver of the bird
- half a quarter of bacon
- salt
- peppers
- one spoonful of vinegar
- one cup of water

## Directions

1. Stuff one bird with tomatoes, one cut onion, the chopped liver, half a quarter of bacon, salt, and peppers, and place it in a pan over slices of bacon with one spoonful of vinegar, one cup of water, and more tomatoes
2. Cover the pan well and boil over regular fire; serve with its own sauce passed through a sieve
