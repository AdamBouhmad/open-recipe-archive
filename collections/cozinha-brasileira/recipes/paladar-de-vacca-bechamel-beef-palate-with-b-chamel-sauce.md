## Ingredients

- beef palate
- Béchamel sauce
- ground Indian pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch the palate as explained in recipe 84
2. Cut the palate into small squares and place in a serving platter
3. Cover with Béchamel sauce sprinkled with ground Indian pepper
