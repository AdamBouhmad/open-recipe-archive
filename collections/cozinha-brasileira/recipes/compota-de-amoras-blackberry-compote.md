## Ingredients

- two pounds blackberries
- one pound sugar syrup at thread stage
- cinnamon
- fennel
- clove

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of blackberries and boil them in little water until they break apart
2. Add one pound of sugar syrup at thread stage, cinnamon, fennel and clove
3. Give it another boil
4. Serve with roasts
