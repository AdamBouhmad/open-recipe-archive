## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- salt
- white wine
- cardamom
- lemon peel
- sugar
- plums

## Directions

1. Cook rice in little water and salt
2. When cooked, add as much white wine as needed to give the soup proper consistency
3. Add cardamom, lemon peel, sugar and plums that have been boiled a little beforehand with the rice
4. Bring to a boil again, then serve
