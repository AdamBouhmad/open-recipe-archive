## Ingredients

- Wheat flour
- Onions
- Vinegar
- Meat broth
- Salt
- Indian pepper
- Pork ears
- Bread toasts

## Directions

1. Place some wheat flour and onions in a casserole; when cooked and having taken on a golden color, add half a cup of vinegar and a cup of meat broth, salt, and broken Indian pepper
2. Let them boil a few times
3. Cut the ears of a cooked and seasoned pig into long strips
4. Place them in a casserole, pouring the prepared sauce over them
5. Braise everything over very low heat so it never boils
6. Serve on a platter with bread toasts placed on top and around
