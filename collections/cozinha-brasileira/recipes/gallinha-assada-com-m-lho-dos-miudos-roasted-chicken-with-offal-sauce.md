## Ingredients

- one chicken
- the liver
- mustard powder
- one glass of white wine
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- one tablespoon of sugar
- a little grated nutmeg
- two tablespoons of fat
- one chopped onion

## Directions

1. Roast the chicken without stuffing it
2. Pound the liver well and mix with mustard powder, one glass of white wine, one tablespoon of vinegar, one tablespoon of sugar, and a little grated nutmeg
3. Melt two tablespoons of fat and fry a chopped onion in it
4. Add the mustard mixture with the liver and another glass of wine
5. Let it cook for a while
6. Serve with the roasted chicken
