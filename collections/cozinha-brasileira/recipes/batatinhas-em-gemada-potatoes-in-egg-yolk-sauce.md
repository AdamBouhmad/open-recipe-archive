## Ingredients

- some small potatoes
- one spoon of butter
- one spoon of wheat flour
- salt
- pepper
- one cup of milk
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Peel some small potatoes and boil them in water and salt
2. Cut them into slices
3. On the other hand, melt one spoon of butter with one spoon of wheat flour, salt and pepper
4. Add one cup of milk beaten with two egg yolks to the sauce
5. When it has boiled, serve
