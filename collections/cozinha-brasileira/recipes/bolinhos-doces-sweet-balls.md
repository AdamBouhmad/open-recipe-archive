## Ingredients

- one dozen egg yolks
- one pound of sugar
- six tablespoons of raw grated potatoes
- six tablespoons of grated bread
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- fennel
- sugar and cinnamon for dusting

## Directions

1. Beat one dozen egg yolks with one pound of sugar
2. Add six tablespoons of raw grated potatoes and six tablespoons of grated bread, little salt, grated nutmeg, and fennel
3. Knead everything well and form small balls
4. Bake in the oven on banana leaves and dust with sugar and cinnamon
5. Serve as a dessert
