## Ingredients

- one hen
- its own blood
- two egg yolks
- four tablespoons of vinegar
- two tablespoons of sugar
- one tablespoon of cornstarch
- two cups of cold water

## Directions

1. Cook a hen as for soup
2. After seasoning, add the hen's own blood and two egg yolks dissolved in four tablespoons of vinegar, two tablespoons of sugar and one tablespoon of cornstarch also dissolved in two cups of cold water
3. Serve with corn porridge
