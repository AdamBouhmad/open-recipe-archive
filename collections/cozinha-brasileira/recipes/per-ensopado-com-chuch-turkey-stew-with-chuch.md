## Ingredients

- leftovers of roasted turkey
- two tablespoons of fat
- a portion of peeled and cut chuchús
- salt
- some peppers
- parsley
- onion
- one bowl of meat broth
- one tablespoon of cornmeal

## Directions

1. Take the leftovers of roasted turkey, cut them into pieces and add them with two tablespoons of fat, a portion of peeled and cut chuchús, salt, some peppers, parsley, onion, a bowl of meat broth and one tablespoon of cornmeal
2. Leave to boil for one hour over moderate heat
3. Serve with cornmeal porridge
