## Ingredients

- one or two beets
- water
- one spoon of fat
- two cups of wine
- two egg yolks
- one spoon of sugar
- salt
- garlic

## Directions

1. Boil one or two beets in water
2. Peel and cut into pieces
3. On the other hand, melt one spoon of fat and add the beets
4. Add two cups of wine in which two egg yolks and one spoon of sugar have been dissolved
5. Season with salt and garlic, and having boiled for half an hour, serve
