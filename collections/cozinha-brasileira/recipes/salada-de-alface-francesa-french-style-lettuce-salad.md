## Ingredients

- Lettuce
- Salt
- Sweet oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Take four heads of lettuce, remove the outer leaves, and wash the others two or three times
2. Let them drain in a sieve, pressing a little but without bruising them, and place them on a plate at the time of serving
3. Add a little fine salt, a spoonful of sweet oil and four to five spoonfuls of good vinegar
4. Mix everything
5. Serve
6. The same way is made watercress salad
