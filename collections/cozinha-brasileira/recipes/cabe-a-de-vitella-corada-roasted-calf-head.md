## Ingredients

- calf head
- bacon slices
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the head as explained for boneless calf head
2. Remove from heat, let drain and place in a deep pan over some bacon slices
3. Go to the oven where it is left to roast for one hour
4. Serve with a spicy sauce
