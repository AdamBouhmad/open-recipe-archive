## Ingredients

- some onions
- parsley
- a little water or beef broth
- a little cornmeal
- one spoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop onions and parsley finely
2. Place in a pot with a little water or beef broth, a little cornmeal, and one spoon of vinegar
3. Let boil until the onion is cooked
4. Use this sauce to warm up cold meats
