## Ingredients

- one wild boar leg
- fat
- wheat flour
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- parsley
- celery
- one sprig of mint
- ginger
- one cup of water
- one cup of white wine
- carapicús

## Directions

1. Cut the leg into pieces
2. Fry in fat and when fried, dust with wheat flour
3. Add salt, pepper, lemon juice, parsley, celery, one sprig of mint, ginger, one cup of water, one cup of white wine, and cleaned and peeled carapicús
4. Let boil for one hour over a moderate fire
5. Serve
