## Ingredients

- wheat flour
- sugar
- broth or water
- roasted veal meat, cut into small pieces
- raisins, blanched
- carapicu, cleaned
- salt
- clove
- bay leaves
- vinegar
- brazas

## Directions

1. Toast two spoonfuls of wheat flour, then toast a spoonful of sugar, and dissolve everything in four cups of broth or water
2. Add pieces of roasted meat cut into small pieces, a handful of blanched raisins, an equal amount of cleaned carapicu, salt, clove, bay leaves, a glass of vinegar and the necessary sugar
3. Let boil over hot coals for one hour
