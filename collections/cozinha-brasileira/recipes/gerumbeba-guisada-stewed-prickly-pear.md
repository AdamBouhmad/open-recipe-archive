## Ingredients

- prickly pears
- wheat flour
- lard
- water
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- egg yolk
- sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Place a quantity of prickly pears over hot coals to burn off the spines that cover them
2. Then peel them
3. Coat them in wheat flour
4. Sauté them in lard
5. Add a cup of water, salt, pepper and parsley
6. Boil for a quarter of an hour
7. Incorporate a beaten egg yolk mixed with sour orange juice and serve
