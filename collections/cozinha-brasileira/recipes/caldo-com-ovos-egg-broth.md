## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- ten spoonfuls of meat broth
- a little nutmeg
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix eight eggs in ten spoonfuls of meat broth, a little nutmeg and salt
2. Place this mixture in an earthenware soup tureen inside a pot with boiling water until it hardens
3. Remove small portions of this mass with a small spoon
4. Place them in hot broth when serving at table
