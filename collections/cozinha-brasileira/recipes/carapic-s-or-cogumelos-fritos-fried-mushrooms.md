## Ingredients

- carapicús (mushrooms)
- vinho (wine)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- sal (salt)
- pimenta (pepper)
- gordura (fat)

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of selected and cleaned carapicús (mushrooms)
2. Give them a few boilings in a little wine and vinegar, adding salt and pepper
3. Once cooked, remove and drain them
4. Place them in a pan containing hot fat
5. Mix them a little, and adding the reduced broth, serve them
