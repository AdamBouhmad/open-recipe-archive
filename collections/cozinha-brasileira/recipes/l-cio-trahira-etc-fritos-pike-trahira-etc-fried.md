## Ingredients

- small fish
- salt
- ground pepper
- cassava flour
- lard
- chopped onion

## Directions

1. Choose small fish and dust them with salt and ground pepper
2. Roll them in cassava flour and fry in abundant lard with chopped onion
3. When browned, serve hot
