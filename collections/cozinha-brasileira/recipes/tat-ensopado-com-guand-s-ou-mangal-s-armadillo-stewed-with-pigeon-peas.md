## Ingredients

- one armadillo, cleaned
- fat
- one bowl of water
- salt
- parsley leaves
- onion leaves
- peppers with cumin
- a portion of beans or mangalós or guandús (pigeon peas)
- one spoonful of cornmeal mixed with one glass of lemon juice
- two spoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. With the armadillo cleaned and shell removed, fry it whole in fat, turning equally to fry both sides
2. Add one bowl of water, salt, parsley leaves, onion leaves, peppers with cumin, and a portion of beans or mangalós or guandús
3. Let boil and when cooked, add one spoonful of cornmeal mixed with one glass of lemon juice and two spoonfuls of sugar
4. Boil further until the broth is reduced and serve
