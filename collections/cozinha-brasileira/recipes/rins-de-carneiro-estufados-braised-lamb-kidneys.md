## Ingredients

- lamb kidneys
- fine breadcrumbs
- clove
- nutmeg
- one cup Champagne wine or other white wine

## Directions

1. Remove fat from kidneys and scald in hot water
2. Slice thinly
3. Fry the slices with breadcrumbs, clove, and nutmeg
4. After stirring a little, add one cup Champagne wine or other white wine
5. Boil over coals for half an hour
6. Serve
