## Ingredients

- veal offal
- water
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- cloves
- one cup vinegar
- mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned and scalded offal in water and salt
2. Add pepper, onions, cloves, and one cup vinegar
3. Let boil for three to four hours
4. Serve with a spicy and sour mustard sauce
