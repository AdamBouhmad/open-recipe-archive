## Ingredients

- chicken
- water
- salt
- onion leaves
- young peas
- fat
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cook a chicken cut into pieces in water, salt and onion leaves
2. When cooked, boil fresh peas in the same water
3. On the other hand, fry the chicken pieces in two spoonfuls of fat after rolling them in breadcrumbs
