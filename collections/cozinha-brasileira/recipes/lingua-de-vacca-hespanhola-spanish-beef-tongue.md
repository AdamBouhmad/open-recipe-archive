## Ingredients

- beef tongue, cooked
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- sugar
- wine
- broth

## Directions

1. The tongue, after being well cooked, is opened down the middle without dividing entirely and placed in a pan containing lemon peel, cinnamon, sugar, half a bottle of wine and the same amount of broth
2. Let it boil for half an hour over coals and serve
