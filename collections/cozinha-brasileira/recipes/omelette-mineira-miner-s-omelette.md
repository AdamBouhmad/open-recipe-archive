## Ingredients

- six eggs
- one spoonful of corn flour strained
- borage leaves
- two spoonfuls of fat

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs with one spoonful of strained corn flour
2. Put some finely chopped borage leaves in this batter
3. Fry two spoonfuls of fat
4. Take one spoonful of this mixture and pour it in the fat
5. Repeat this operation until the beaten eggs are finished
6. Serve these cakes with some sauce
