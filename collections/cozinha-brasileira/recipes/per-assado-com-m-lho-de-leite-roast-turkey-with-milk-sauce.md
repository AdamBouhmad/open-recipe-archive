## Ingredients

- turkey
- turkey liver
- veal liver
- handful of grated bread
- red wine
- two tablespoons sugar
- nutmeg
- cardamom
- salt
- paper
- butter
- two cups milk
- two egg yolks
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Take the turkey and fill the breast with its liver and a piece of veal liver passed through a sieve, mixed with a handful of grated bread moistened with red wine, adding two tablespoons of sugar, grated nutmeg, cardamom and a little salt.
2. Spit it and roast it, covered with paper brushed with butter.
3. When cooked, remove the paper.
4. Make a sauce with two cups of milk, two egg yolks, a little butter, salt and a tablespoon of wheat flour.
5. Pour this sauce by spoonfuls over the turkey, continuing to roast it until it takes on a good color.
6. Serve with its sauce.
