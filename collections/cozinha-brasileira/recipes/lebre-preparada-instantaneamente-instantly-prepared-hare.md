## Ingredients

- one still-warm hare
- four ounces of bacon cut into pieces
- a bunch of seasoning herbs
- onions
- salt
- abundant pepper
- one and a half bottles of strong red wine

## Directions

1. Cut the still-warm hare into pieces.
2. Combine the blood with the hare, four ounces of bacon cut into pieces, a bunch of seasoning herbs, onions, salt, and abundant pepper in a large vessel, along with one and a half bottles of strong red wine.
3. Place the vessel over a brisk fire so that the fire surrounds it, and with the first boil the wine will catch fire; one can add a little brandy to make this effect quicker.
4. After the wine finishes burning, wrap the pieces in six spoonfuls of butter, then in wheat flour.
5. Place them back in the vessel and let the sauce reduce.
6. In half an hour the hare will be ready.
