## Ingredients

- a cut of tapir meat
- vinegar
- four to five tablespoons of brown sugar
- one tablespoon of salt
- pepper
- onion leaves
- cloves
- marjoram
- pork fat
- pieces of garlic
- six tablespoons of wheat flour
- two whole eggs
- one tablespoon of beef butter
- milk

## Directions

1. Put a cut of tapir meat to marinate in vinegar with four to five tablespoons of brown sugar, one tablespoon of salt, pepper, onion leaves, cloves and marjoram
2. After twenty-four hours, remove it and lard it with thick pieces of pork fat and small pieces of garlic
3. Wrap it in a coating made with six tablespoons of wheat flour, two whole eggs, one tablespoon of beef butter, and enough milk to form a dough, which is spread on a cloth and in which the cut of meat is wrapped
4. Put the cut of meat thus prepared in a pan, having placed at the bottom of it a slice of pork fat; roast it in the oven or over moderate heat, for which three hours are needed; serve with the coating
