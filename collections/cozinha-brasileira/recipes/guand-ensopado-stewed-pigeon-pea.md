## Ingredients

- pigeon peas
- water
- salt
- fat
- onion
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take a portion of still tender pigeon peas and boil them with little water and salt.
2. Melt a spoonful of fat in a casserole with an onion.
3. Add the pigeon pea with its broth, a little parsley, boil over moderate fire and serve.
