## Ingredients

- meat with bad smell
- live coals without smoke

## Directions

1. Place the meat with bad smell in a pan; when it boils, remove the scum and place in the pan a portion of live coals without smoke; let it boil for five minutes and then remove the meat with a skimmer, after which it can be prepared as desired.
