## Ingredients

- one spoon of fat
- one heaping spoon of wheat flour or corn meal
- two cups hot water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion
- cooked beef, chicken, or veal

## Directions

1. Put the fat in a saucepan and heat until very hot
2. Add, stirring, one heaping spoon of wheat flour or corn meal
3. Add two cups of hot water, salt, pepper, parsley, and onion
4. Add the cooked beef, chicken, or veal
5. Allow everything to cook for another hour over moderate heat
6. If desired, mushrooms, onions, artichokes, or peeled potatoes may be added to cook together
