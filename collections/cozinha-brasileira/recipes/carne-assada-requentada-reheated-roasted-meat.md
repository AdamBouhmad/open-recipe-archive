## Ingredients

- roasted meat
- butter
- paper
- herbs

## Directions

1. Wrap the roasted meat in buttered paper.
2. Put it on the spit and reheat it.
3. The meat reheated this way is as tender as on the first day.
4. When the pieces are very small, warm them on a grill to serve at the table covered with chopped herbs.
