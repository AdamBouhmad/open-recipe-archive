## Ingredients

- one bird (arara, papagaio, etc.)
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- marjoram
- one or two quinces, cut into quarters
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- two spoonfuls of butter
- one chopped onion
- a portion of gerumbeba fruits, peeled and cut into pieces

## Directions

1. Boil the bird with salt, pepper, parsley, onion leaves, marjoram, and one or two quinces cut into quarters.
2. When cooked, remove the bird and cut it into pieces.
3. In two spoonfuls of butter, brown one spoonful of wheat flour and one chopped onion, letting it boil while stirring until it becomes a dark amber color.
4. Add the meat and the cooking broth reduced to two cups of liquid, along with a portion of peeled and cut gerumbeba fruits.
5. Let boil for half an hour over coals and serve.
