## Ingredients

- one plate of rice
- two spoonfuls of fat
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one dozen tomatoes
- a pinch of Indian pepper

## Directions

1. Choose and wash one plate of rice
2. Put a pan over high heat with two spoonfuls of fat and fry a little
3. Add the rice inside and stir for five to eight minutes
4. Add one bottle of water, salt, parsley, onion leaves, one dozen tomatoes, and a pinch of Indian pepper
5. When dried, serve
