## Ingredients

- beef brisket
- salt
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take a good piece of beef brisket and rub it with salt.
2. Place it in a covered pan with only the lid.
3. Roast it in the oven without any other seasoning.
4. Use the juice that has dripped out to make a sauce, adding melted butter and powdered pepper.
