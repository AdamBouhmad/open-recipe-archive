## Ingredients

- four young squabs
- two tablespoons butter
- a portion of fresh peas
- two cups water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- nutmeg
- two egg yolks
- one glass white wine
- a pinch of sugar

## Directions

1. Brown the squabs in butter
2. Once they have taken color, remove and cut them in half
3. In the same fat, add a portion of fresh peas
4. Add two cups of water, the pieces of squab, salt, pepper, parsley, and nutmeg
5. Allow to cook over live coals
6. When serving, add two egg yolks dissolved in a glass of white wine and a pinch of sugar
