## Ingredients

- one spoon of butter
- one spoon of wheat flour
- two chalices of meat extract
- as much red wine
- one whole onion
- a bouquet of aromatic herbs
- a little salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put on the fire in a pot one spoon of butter and another of wheat flour, let it heat, and before browning add two chalices of meat extract and as much red wine, one whole onion and a bouquet of aromatic herbs (the onion and herbs are removed when serving), a little salt
2. Leave to cook half an hour, add the legs, wings and stomach of game or the legs, wings and giblets of fowl (already cooked) and let them heat in this sauce without cooking, adding lemon juice
3. Garnish the bottom of the plate with pieces of bread, on which place the game or fowl, pouring the sauce over and serve
