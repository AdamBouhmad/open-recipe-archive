## Ingredients

- Lettuce
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Melted fat
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Take the leaves of four heads of lettuce, chop them fine, wash them and let them drain on a sieve
2. Place them on a plate with salt, vinegar, hot melted fat and pepper
3. Mix everything well
4. Serve
