## Ingredients

- pieces of duck
- three spoonfuls of fat
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- one bottle of water
- half a cup of vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- onion leaves
- parsley
- a portion of okra, washed

## Directions

1. Take the pieces of duck and fry them in three spoonfuls of fat in which wheat flour is already frying
2. Turn the pieces of duck to brown on all sides
3. Add one bottle of water, half a cup of vinegar, salt, pepper, onion leaves, parsley and a portion of washed okra
4. Let it boil for half an hour over uncovered fire
5. Serve with cornmeal mush or cassava flour pirão
