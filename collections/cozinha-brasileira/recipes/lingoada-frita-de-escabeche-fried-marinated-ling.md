## Ingredients

- fish
- sweet oil
- salt
- peppers
- grated nutmeg
- melted butter
- breadcrumbs
- beaten eggs
- tomatoes
- wine

## Directions

1. Remove the skin of the fish, split it down the middle, also remove the bones, and soak it in sweet oil with a little salt, peppers and grated nutmeg for four hours, turning it from time to time
2. Remove and dry it with absorbent paper, and coat it with melted butter wrapping it then in breadcrumbs, beaten eggs and breadcrumbs again
3. Fry it in butter or roast it on the grill, and serve with a sauce made of tomatoes sautéed in butter with salt and peppers
4. Add one or two cups of wine, boil it a little and serve
