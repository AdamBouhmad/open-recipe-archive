## Ingredients

- Tender and fatty pork meat
- Parsley
- Chives, chopped
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- Summer savory
- Cloves
- Nutmeg
- Whole cayenne pepper
- Beef intestines
- Salt
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. Chop the pork meat well and mix with parsley, chopped chives, thyme, marjoram, summer savory, cloves, nutmeg and whole cayenne pepper
2. Fill beef intestines with this mixture, tying at both ends
3. Place in smoke for two days
4. Cook in meat broth without salt for three hours
5. For onion sausages, take a quantity of onions, chop them and cook in melted fat
6. When nearly cooked, mix with the chopped meat and fill the intestines as described
