## Ingredients

- Pork loin
- Sweet oil
- Salt
- Meat substance
- Robert sauce

## Directions

1. Trim the pork loin as one would trim a veal shoulder for roasting
2. Two hours before serving, cover it with paper coated in sweet oil and roast on a grill over gentle heat
3. Half an hour before serving, remove the paper and sprinkle salt on top and bottom
4. Allow it to brown, place on a serving platter with Robert sauce prepared beforehand
5. Place meat substance over the loin two or three times
