## Ingredients

- milk
- coffee powder

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of milk, and when boiling, add the coffee powder to it, stir, strain through a cloth bag and serve; it is also used to place the strained coffee in one pot and the milk in another, so that each person can use it according to their taste.
