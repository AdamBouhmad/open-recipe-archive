## Ingredients

- shrimp
- fat
- water
- onion leaves
- parsley
- rice or bread toasts

## Directions

1. Fry some shrimp in a little fat
2. Add water afterwards and let them boil for about an hour
3. Add onion leaves and parsley
4. Pour into a tureen containing cooked rice or bread toasts
