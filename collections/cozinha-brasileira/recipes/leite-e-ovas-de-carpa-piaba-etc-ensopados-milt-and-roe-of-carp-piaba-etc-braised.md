## Ingredients

- fish milt and roe
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- wheat flour
- butter
- one cup of white wine
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Place the milt and roe of fish in cold water for twenty-four hours
2. In another vessel, boil a bottle of water with salt and a little vinegar
3. When boiling, place the pieces of milt and roe inside
4. Let them boil for a quarter of an hour, remove them and wrap them in wheat flour
5. Fry them in butter, turning them, and when the flour is browned, add one cup of white wine, salt, pepper, nutmeg
6. Let it boil over coals for a moment and serve
