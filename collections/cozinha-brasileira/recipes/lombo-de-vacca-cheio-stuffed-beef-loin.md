## Ingredients

- beef loin
- lard strips
- sweet oil
- onion
- parsley sprigs
- bay leaves
- broken India pepper

## Directions

1. Take a beef loin and pierce it with lard strips
2. Place it in a serving dish with a glass of sweet oil, an onion cut into rounds, some parsley sprigs, some bay leaves and broken India pepper
3. Turn it frequently
4. After two hours, having drained it, thread it onto a spit
5. Cover with a sheet of paper oiled with sweet oil
6. Place near the fire, basting with the sauce it was in
7. After three-quarters of an hour, remove the paper so it takes color
8. Five minutes before serving, pour the beef essence over it
9. Place in a serving dish with potato pulp prepared with white sauce
