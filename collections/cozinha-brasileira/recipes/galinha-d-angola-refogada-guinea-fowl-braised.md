## Ingredients

- one guinea fowl
- fat
- one onion
- half a dozen tomatoes
- one cup of water
- salt
- chopped parsley
- peppers
- one spoonful of cassava flour
- eight egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from the bird, cut it into pieces and braise it in fat with one chopped onion and half a dozen tomatoes
2. Then add one cup of water, salt, chopped parsley, peppers and one spoonful of cassava flour
3. Let it boil for half an hour and place it on a plate, pouring eight beaten egg yolks on top
4. Place the plate in the oven, let it roast until browned, and serve dusted with sugar
