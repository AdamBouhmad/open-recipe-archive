## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- tubara skins
- sausage meat
- tubara slices
- caul fat

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the feet as previously explained, then remove all the bones.
2. Chop the skin of the tubaras and pound with a little sausage meat.
3. Garnish the feet with this filling, adding some slices of tubaras, and wrap everything in pieces of caul fat.
4. Roast over moderate heat.
