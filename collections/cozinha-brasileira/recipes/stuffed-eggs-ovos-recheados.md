## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- cow butter
- bread crumb
- milk
- salt
- ground pepper
- half an onion
- parsley
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- hot sauce
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Boil a dozen eggs, shell and cut in half lengthwise; remove the yolks and mash them with an equal volume of cow butter and bread crumb moistened in milk
2. Mix salt, ground pepper, half an onion and a little chopped parsley, binding the mixture with two egg yolks; fill the whites with this mixture, spreading the rest on a plate that can withstand the heat of the fire
3. Place the stuffed whites on this mixture, brush with one egg yolk by means of a feather; place over coals, covering with a lid full of coals; when cooked, serve with a vinegar sauce, hot sauce, or with vegetables
