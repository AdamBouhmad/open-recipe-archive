## Ingredients

- wheat flour
- water
- vinegar
- sugar
- raisins
- almonds
- beef tongue, cooked

## Directions

1. Toast a spoonful of wheat flour until well browned
2. Dissolve it in a cup of water and a spoonful of vinegar
3. Add a little sugar, already scalded raisins and peeled almonds
4. Let it boil for half an hour with the tongue, already cooked and cut into slices
5. Serve thus
