## Ingredients

- beef tripe
- fresh bacon, finely chopped
- parsley
- pepper
- onion
- salt
- nutmeg
- water or broth
- cloves
- aromatic herbs

## Directions

1. Cut the tripe into strips one palm in length
2. Cover with finely chopped fresh bacon, parsley, pepper, onion, salt and nutmeg
3. Roll these strips, each roll tied with string
4. Leave them tied and seasoned to cook for three or four hours in water or broth with salt, pepper, cloves and a bouquet of aromatic herbs
5. Serve these rolls after removing the string, with some sauce or vegetables
