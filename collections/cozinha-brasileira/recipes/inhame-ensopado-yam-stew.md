## Ingredients

- yam
- water
- salt
- cheese
- fat
- wheat flour
- pepper
- milk

## Directions

1. Cook the root of a yam and peel and cut it into pieces
2. Cut some pieces of cheese and sauté them together in a casserole with two spoons of fat
3. Add a little wheat flour, a little salt, pepper, and a cup of rich milk
4. Leave to boil for half an hour and serve
5. Instead of milk, one can use a cup of water with a spoon of vinegar, and thicken with an egg yolk
