## Ingredients

- eight whole eggs
- four egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- three spoons of grated cheese
- two spoons of fat
- breadcrumbs
- sugar
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Beat eight whole eggs, four yolks, with salt, pepper and three spoons of grated cheese
2. In another pan, melt two spoons of fat
3. When hot, pour in the eggs; fry, stirring, until fried
4. Place on a plate, cover with breadcrumbs, sugar and cinnamon powder, and serve
