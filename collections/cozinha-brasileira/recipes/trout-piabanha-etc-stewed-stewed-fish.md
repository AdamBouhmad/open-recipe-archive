## Ingredients

- one spoon of fat
- parsley
- chopped onion leaves
- one cup of white wine
- fish
- salt
- nutmeg
- bay leaves
- one onion
- two spoons of fat
- two spoons of corn meal

## Directions

1. Fry one spoon of fat with a little parsley and chopped onion leaves, one cup of white wine and the fish; let over strong heat for half an hour, adding salt, nutmeg, bay leaves, one whole onion, two spoons of fat and two spoons of fine corn meal; boil several more times and serve
