## Ingredients

- salted fish
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- marjoram
- peppercorns
- lemon juice
- flour

## Directions

1. Soak the salted fish in cold water for six hours
2. Boil in water with salt, parsley, onion leaves, marjoram, peppercorns and lemon juice
3. When cooked, strain the broth and thicken it with flour
4. Pour the broth onto a plate, place the fish on top and serve
