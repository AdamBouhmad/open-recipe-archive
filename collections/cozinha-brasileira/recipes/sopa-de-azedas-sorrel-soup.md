## Ingredients

- sorrel
- lettuce plant
- chervil
- fat
- meat broth
- bread

## Directions

1. Take a portion of sorrel, a lettuce plant, and a little finely chopped chervil
2. Sauté all this in a little fat
3. Then pass this mixture through a sieve so that no stalks or fibers remain
4. Pour over this sieved mixture a portion of meat broth of whatever meat is available
5. Let it boil for half an hour
6. After removing the fat, pour it over toasted bread and serve
