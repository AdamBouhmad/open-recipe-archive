## Ingredients

- two or three fish
- water
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- a dozen tomatoes
- two spoons of fat
- three to four egg yolks
- one spoon of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil two or three scaled and cleaned fish in water, salt, parsley and pepper
2. When cooked, fry a dozen tomatoes in two spoons of fat
3. Add the fish broth mixed with three to four egg yolks beaten with one spoon of lemon juice
4. Boil a little and pour the fish into this sauce
5. Let it cook for another half hour over coals and serve
