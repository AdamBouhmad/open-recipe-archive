## Ingredients

- veal quarter
- aspic from the broth

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the veal quarter as already explained, but do not let the quarter finish cooking
2. Remove it before, spit it and finish roasting it over flames
3. Remove it and let it cool, garnishing it the next day with the aspic from the sauce
