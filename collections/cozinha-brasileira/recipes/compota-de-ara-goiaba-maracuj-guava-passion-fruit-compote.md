## Ingredients

- fruit pulp with seeds
- one pound sugar syrup at thread stage
- cinnamon
- clove
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take the pulp with seeds from these fruits
2. Put one pound of pulp in half the sugar syrup at thread stage with cinnamon, clove and nutmeg
3. Boil a little
4. Serve with roasts
