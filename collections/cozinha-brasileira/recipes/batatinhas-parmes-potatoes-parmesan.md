## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- cheese, grated
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of potatoes, peel and cut into pieces
2. Butter a casserole well and place the potatoes cut into slices in alternating layers with layers of grated cheese
3. Let bake in the oven
4. Place on a plate, carefully removing with a knife so they come out whole
5. If desired, sprinkle with sugar and serve
