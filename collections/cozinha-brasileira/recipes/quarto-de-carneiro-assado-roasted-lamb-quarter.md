## Ingredients

- lamb quarter
- hot water
- salt
- ground pepper
- chopped parsley
- strips of bacon
- wheat flour
- butter
- egg yolk
- paper
- fresh vegetables

## Directions

1. After scalding the lamb quarter in hot water, lard it
2. Season with salt, ground pepper, and chopped parsley on the outside with strips of bacon
3. Cover both sides with a paste made of wheat flour with butter and an egg yolk
4. Wrap the entire quarter with paper untied in butter
5. Spit roast it
6. Serve with fresh vegetables
