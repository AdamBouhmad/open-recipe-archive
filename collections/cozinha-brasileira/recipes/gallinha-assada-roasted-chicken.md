## Ingredients

- one clean chicken
- its giblets
- two cooked eggs
- quarter pound of lard
- one onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- two spoonfuls of bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Take the clean chicken and fill the cavity of the breast and belly with its giblets, two cooked eggs, a quarter pound of lard, one onion and parsley, all chopped, salt, pepper and two spoonfuls of bread crumbs
