## Ingredients

- beef short ribs
- butter
- wheat flour
- hot water
- brandy
- mushrooms
- pitted olives

## Directions

1. Free the meat from excess fat and cut into slices
2. Place these in a pan with a little butter
3. Once they are fried on both sides, remove them
4. Put a spoonful of wheat flour in the pan
5. When it takes color and is moistened in hot water and a glass of brandy, add the short rib slices again
6. Let them cook for three or four hours
7. Add some mushrooms and five minutes before serving, some pitted olives
