## Ingredients

- calf liver
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- parsley
- marjoram
- pieces of salt pork
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak the liver in white wine with salt, pepper, clove, parsley and marjoram for twenty-four hours
2. Place on the fire with the seasonings, adding some pieces of salt pork
3. Let it boil
4. When cooked, remove it and strain the broth
5. Thicken with egg yolks, add a little lemon juice and serve
