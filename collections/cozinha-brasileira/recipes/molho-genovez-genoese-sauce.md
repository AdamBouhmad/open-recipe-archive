## Ingredients

- two spoons of cornstarch butter
- sufficient water
- parsley
- mushrooms
- chives
- one chalice of white wine

## Directions

1. Fry two spoons of cornstarch butter until they turn an amber color
2. Add sufficient water, finely minced parsley, mushrooms, and chives, and one chalice of white wine
3. Serve
