## Ingredients

- one coati, somewhat old
- one bottle of aguardente
- one cup of vinegar
- parsley
- salt
- peppers
- cloves
- garlic
- bay leaves
- ginger
- cow's butter
- onions
- one spoon of wheat flour
- one cup of wine
- one egg yolk

## Directions

1. Wrap the coati in cloth and place it in a bottle of aguardente with one cup of vinegar, parsley, salt, peppers, cloves, garlic, bay leaves, and ginger
2. Leave in this marinade for twenty-four hours, turning it two or three times
3. Cut the coati into pieces and fry with some onions in cow's butter
4. Sprinkle with one spoon of wheat flour and add one cup of wine in which an egg yolk has been dissolved
5. Leave to boil near the fire until the meat is very cooked and serve
