## Ingredients

- six quinces
- a dozen chuchús peeled and cut into small pieces
- a dozen plums
- a handful of scalded raisins
- a handful of bread crumb softened in white wine
- two tablespoons of sugar
- cinnamon
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take six quinces, a dozen peeled and cut chuchús, a dozen plums, a handful of scalded raisins and a handful of bread crumb softened in as much white wine as needed, two tablespoons of sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg
2. Mix everything and fill the turkey with the mixture
3. Place it to roast in the oven in a pan over slices of bacon
4. When cooked, serve
