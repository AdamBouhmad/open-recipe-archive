## Ingredients

- turkey
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- bacon
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Brush the turkey with salt and pepper and wrap it in a cloth moistened with vinegar.
2. The next day, place it on the spit, wrapped in fine bacon slices, and roast it.
3. When cooked, remove the bacon, wrap the turkey in grated bread, moistening it with the sauce that dripped down.
4. Allow it to take color and serve.
