## Ingredients

- one duck, cut in pieces
- three spoonfuls of butter
- two spoonfuls of wheat flour
- one bottle of water
- salt
- pepper
- onion leaves
- cane
- a portion of peeled and cut chayotes
- one glass of vinegar
- two spoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the duck in pieces and fry them with three spoonfuls of butter until almost roasted
2. Remove the pieces and in the same butter add two spoonfuls of wheat flour and stir until golden
3. Add a bottle of water, salt, pepper and onion leaves
4. Boil everything a little; add the cane and a portion of peeled and cut chayotes, one glass of vinegar and two spoonfuls of sugar
5. Boil for half an hour more and serve
