## Ingredients

- bunches of sorrel
- butter
- beef broth
- salt
- nutmeg
- sugar
- egg yolks
- polvilho (starch)

## Directions

1. Take some bunches of sorrel, chop them into small pieces, and place them over coals in a covered casserole, letting them boil a little in their own water; then add a little butter, a little beef broth, salt, nutmeg and sugar; let it boil for half an hour, and thicken with some egg yolks and a little polvilho; serve with cooked, roasted fish and boiled beef.
