## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- wheat flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a dough with an egg and wheat flour
2. Cut this dough into small pieces
3. Fry the pieces in butter
4. Serve these pieces by cooking them with meat broth a short time before serving at table
