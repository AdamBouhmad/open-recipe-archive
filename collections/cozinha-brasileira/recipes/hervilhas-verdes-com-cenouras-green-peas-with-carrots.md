## Ingredients

- a portion of green peas
- new carrots, chopped
- a little butter
- about half a bottle of water
- salt
- a little white wine
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Take a portion of green peas and new carrots, chopped and brown in a little butter
2. Add about half a bottle of water and salt
3. When boiled a little, add a little white wine and a little sugar
4. Continue to boil and serve
