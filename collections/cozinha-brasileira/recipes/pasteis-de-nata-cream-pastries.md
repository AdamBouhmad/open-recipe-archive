## Ingredients

- four cups of milk cream
- ten egg yolks
- one spoonful of sugar
- lemon zest
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat four cups of milk cream with ten egg yolks, one spoonful of sugar, and lemon zest
2. Bring to a boil, stir and fill the crusts with this mixture
3. Place in the oven, and when baked, serve covered with sugar and powdered cinnamon
