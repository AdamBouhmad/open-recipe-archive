## Ingredients

- potatoes
- lard or fat
- onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- wheat flour
- water

## Directions

1. Take a portion of potatoes, peel and cut into pieces
2. Fry in lard with a chopped onion, a little parsley, salt, pepper, and a spoonful of wheat flour
3. Stir and add a bottle of water
4. Let boil and reduce
5. Serve
