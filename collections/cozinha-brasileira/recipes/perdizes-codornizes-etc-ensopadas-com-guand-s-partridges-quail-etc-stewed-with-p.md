## Ingredients

- cornmeal flour
- fat
- partridge, quail, or similar bird
- cooked pigeon peas
- water
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Brown one spoon of cornmeal flour with two spoons of fat
2. Add the bird cut into pieces and turn on all sides
3. Add a portion of pigeon peas cooked in water and salt
4. Stir and boil for another half hour
5. Add a glass of vinegar at the end and serve
