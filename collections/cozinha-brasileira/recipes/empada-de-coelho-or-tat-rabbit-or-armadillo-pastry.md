## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- red wine
- cloves
- nutmeg
- sage
- onion
- salt
- peppers
- carapicús (a type of game bird)
- six egg yolks
- one chopped onion
- lemon juice
- pastry casing

## Directions

1. After cleaning the rabbit, cook it in red wine with cloves, nutmeg, sage, onion, salt and peppers
2. Remove the meat from the bones, cut into pieces and mix with a portion of carapicús, six egg yolks and one chopped onion
3. Moisten with lemon juice
4. Bake in a pastry casing and when well baked, serve
