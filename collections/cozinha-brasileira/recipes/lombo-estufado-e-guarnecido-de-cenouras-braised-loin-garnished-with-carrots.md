## Ingredients

- beef loin
- carrots

## Directions

1. Prepare and braise a good piece of loin as usual, after basting it with its own essence
2. Place it in a serving dish garnished with small carrots, also basted with the same meat essence
3. Pour the rest of the sauce strained through cloth into a sauce boat, removing the fat so it remains reduced to half the meat essence
