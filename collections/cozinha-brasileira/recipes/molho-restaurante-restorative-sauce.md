## Ingredients

- watercress
- chervil
- marjoram
- onion leaves
- beef broth
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar
- butter mixed with wheat flour

## Directions

1. Chop equal parts watercress, chervil, marjoram, and onion leaves
2. Place in a pot with beef broth, pepper, salt and vinegar
3. Let boil for fifteen minutes with these herbs
4. Remove from fire and add a little butter mixed with wheat flour
5. Stir until completely melted and serve
