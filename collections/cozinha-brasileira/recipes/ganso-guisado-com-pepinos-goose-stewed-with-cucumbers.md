## Ingredients

- one goose
- water
- salt
- parsley
- one onion studded with cloves
- one dozen cucumbers
- cow butter
- wheat flour
- lemon

## Directions

1. Boil a goose in water, salt, parsley, one onion studded with cloves, until half cooked.
2. Remove the meat from the bones and cut it into long strips the thickness of a finger.
3. Fry these strips in cow butter after rolling them in wheat flour.
4. At this time, peel one dozen cucumbers, remove their cores, and split them into four or eight strips according to their size.
5. Put these strips in the broth in which the goose was cooked; after two boils, place them on the plate with slices of the fried goose on top, pouring over them the butter in which the meat was fried.
6. Having decorated with some slices of lemon without peel, send to the table.
