## Ingredients

- one cabbage, finely chopped
- water
- salt
- half a pound of bacon, chopped
- one duck
- sweet potatoes, peeled

## Directions

1. Boil finely chopped cabbage for a few minutes in water and salt
2. Remove from water and squeeze it to have as little water as possible
3. Place it in the pan with half a pound of chopped bacon and the duck filled with peeled sweet potatoes
4. When cooked, serve the cabbage on a platter and the duck on top
