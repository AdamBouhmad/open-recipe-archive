## Ingredients

- birds
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- handful of breadcrumbs
- same amount of carapicús
- one onion
- one chalice of wine
- slices of peeled lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the birds in half
2. rub with salt and pepper and fry in butter
3. remove after frying on both sides
4. in the same butter, put a handful of breadcrumbs, the same amount of carapicús, and one chopped onion
5. fry a little while stirring and add one chalice of wine and some slices of peeled lemon
6. give it another boil and serve over the birds
