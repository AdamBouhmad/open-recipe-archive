## Ingredients

- butter
- half a pound of bacon slices
- two pounds of beef, cut into small pieces
- beef broth
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- bouquet of herbs
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Line a casserole of considerable capacity with butter; cover the bottom with half a pound of slices of bacon and two pounds of beef, cut into small pieces, placed over a lively fire, and left to fry for a while with the casserole well covered, avoiding however that the bacon does not burn; add from time to time one spoonful of beef broth, then adding two bottles of beef broth, salt, pepper, cloves, a bouquet of herbs and some mushrooms; let everything cook over a moderate fire for three to four hours; remove the fat and strain through a cloth.
