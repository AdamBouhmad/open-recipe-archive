## Ingredients

- okra
- water
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a quantity of okra and remove only the skin that covers them
2. Boil them for five minutes in water and salt
3. Place them on a plate
4. Mix with a little pepper and a spoonful of vinegar and serve
