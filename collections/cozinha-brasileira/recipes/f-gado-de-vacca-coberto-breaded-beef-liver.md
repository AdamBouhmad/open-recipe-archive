## Ingredients

- beef liver
- salt
- ground pepper
- eggs, beaten
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into slices the thickness of a finger
2. Sprinkle with salt and ground pepper
3. Dip in beaten eggs and roll finally in wheat flour
4. In a shallow frying pan, heat butter over high fire
5. When the butter is well heated, fry the slices on both sides
6. Serve plain or with a spicy sauce
