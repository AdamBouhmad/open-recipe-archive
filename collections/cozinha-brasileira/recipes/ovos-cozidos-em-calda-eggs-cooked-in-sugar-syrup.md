## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- twenty egg yolks
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a syrup from one pound of sugar and cook until it reaches the mirror stage.
2. Remove from heat and let it cool.
3. Beat twenty egg yolks, taking care to separate the whites completely as even the smallest particle of white will prevent the yolks from binding with the syrup.
4. Add the beaten yolks to the cooled syrup.
5. Return the vessel to the fire, heat over coals, and stir until it reaches a good consistency.
6. Pour the mixture into cups.
7. Cover with cinnamon.
8. Serve cold.
