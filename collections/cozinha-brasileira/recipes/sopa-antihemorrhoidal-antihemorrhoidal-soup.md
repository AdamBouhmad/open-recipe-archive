## Ingredients

- fat hen
- lettuce
- chicory
- parsley
- sago
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Make broth from a fat hen
2. Once cooked, remove the meat and fat
3. Add a handful of lettuce, chicory, parsley, a little sago and a spoonful of vinegar
4. Boil a little longer
5. Serve at table
