## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- salted pork meat
- carapicú
- onion
- parsley
- pepper
- clove
- cloth
- egg yolks
- terra orange juice

## Directions

1. Remove the bone from the shoulder by cutting the meat lengthwise
2. Cover it with a mixture made of salted pork meat, carapicú, onion and parsley (all finely chopped), pepper and one clove
3. Wrap in cloth securing it with ties
4. When cooked, incorporate the rest of the broth with two egg yolks
5. Add a little juice of terra orange and serve with the meat after removing the cloth
