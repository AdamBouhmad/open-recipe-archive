## Ingredients

- portion of tomatoes
- parsley
- carrot
- peppers
- salt
- two spoons of fat
- portion of shrimp cooked in water and salt
- two cups of fish broth
- one egg yolk
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Fry a portion of tomatoes with parsley, chopped carrot, peppers and salt in two spoons of fat
2. Add a portion of shrimp cooked in water and salt and deshelled
3. Add two cups of fish broth
4. Add one egg yolk dissolved in a glass of lemon juice and serve
