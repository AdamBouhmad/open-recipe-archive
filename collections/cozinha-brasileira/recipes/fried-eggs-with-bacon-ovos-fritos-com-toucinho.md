## Ingredients

- one slice of lean bacon with some meat
- pepper
- six beaten eggs
- one spoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry one slice of lean bacon with some meat, add a little pepper, and when well browned, pour in six beaten eggs mixed with one spoon of vinegar
2. Let finish frying, place on a plate, and serve
