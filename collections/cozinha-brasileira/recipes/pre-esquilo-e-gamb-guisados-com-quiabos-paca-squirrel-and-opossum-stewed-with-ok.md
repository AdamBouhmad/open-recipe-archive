## Ingredients

- two chopped onions
- three spoons of fat
- one bottle of water
- salt
- chopped parsley
- peppers
- one egg yolk
- one glass of sour orange juice
- two dozen okra, cut into slices
- the game, cut in quarters

## Directions

1. Sauté two chopped onions with three spoons of fat, add one bottle of water, salt, chopped parsley, peppers, one egg yolk, one glass of sour orange juice and two dozen okra cut into slices
2. Let it boil for a moment and pour in this sauce the game cut in quarters
3. Cook over moderate heat for an hour and serve
