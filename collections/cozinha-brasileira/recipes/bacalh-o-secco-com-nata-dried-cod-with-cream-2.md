## Ingredients

- dried cod, soaked and cooked
- two spoons of butter
- one spoon of wheat flour
- one small spoon of mustard flour
- peppers
- one cup of milk
- bread crumbs
- grated cheese
- melted butter

## Directions

1. After soaking and cooking the cod, remove the bones and skin
2. Melt two spoons of butter with one spoon of wheat flour, one small spoon of mustard flour and some peppers
3. Add one cup of milk
4. Add the cooked cod to this sauce and simmer briefly
5. Pour into a dish, cover with a layer of bread crumbs and another of grated cheese
6. Pour melted butter on top
7. Place in the oven and let it brown
