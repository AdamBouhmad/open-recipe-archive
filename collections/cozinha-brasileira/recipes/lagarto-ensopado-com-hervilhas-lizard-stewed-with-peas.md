## Ingredients

- one lizard, cut into pieces
- two spoonfuls of fat
- one onion, sliced
- wheat flour
- one glass of white wine
- one bottle of water
- salt
- parsley
- marjoram
- a portion of green peas

## Directions

1. Cut the lizard into pieces and braise in two spoonfuls of fat with a sliced onion
2. Dust with wheat flour
3. Add one glass of white wine and one bottle of water, salt, parsley, marjoram, and a portion of green peas
4. Let everything boil over moderate heat for one hour and serve
