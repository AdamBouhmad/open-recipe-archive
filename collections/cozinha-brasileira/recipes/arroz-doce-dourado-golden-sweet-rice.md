## Ingredients

- half plate of rice
- two bottles of milk
- half pound of sugar
- salt
- lemon peel
- grated nutmeg
- six egg yolks
- one glass of white wine
- two tablespoons of butter
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil half a plate of rice with two bottles of milk, half a pound of sugar, salt, lemon peel, and grated nutmeg
2. When the rice is cooked and almost dry, add six beaten egg yolks mixed with one glass of white wine and two tablespoons of butter
3. Let it boil a little more
4. Place on dishes, dust with ground cinnamon and serve hot
