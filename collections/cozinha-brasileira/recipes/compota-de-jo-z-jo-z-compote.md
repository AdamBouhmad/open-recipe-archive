## Ingredients

- two pounds joáz
- cinnamon
- clove
- one pound sugar

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of joáz and boil them in little water with cinnamon and clove
2. When cooked, strain through a sieve and add the syrup of one pound of sugar
3. Boil until it forms a thin porridge
4. Serve
