## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- carapicús
- thin strips of bacon
- salt
- clove
- onion
- twine

## Directions

1. Take the lamb shoulder, open it lengthwise, remove the bone, and fill it inside with carapicús
2. Lard it on the outside with thin strips of bacon
3. Add salt, clove, and onion
4. Tie it with twine, giving it a round shape
5. Roast it in a pan over coals or in the oven
6. Serve with its own sauce
