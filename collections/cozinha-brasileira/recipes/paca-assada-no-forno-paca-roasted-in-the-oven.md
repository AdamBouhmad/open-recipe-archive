## Ingredients

- one paca
- palm hearts
- chopped bacon
- salt
- pepper
- cassava flour
- meat broth
- bacon slices
- salad

## Directions

1. Fill the paca with a stuffing made of palm hearts, chopped bacon, salt, little pepper, and cassava flour moistened with meat broth
2. Place it in a pan over bacon slices, and let it cook in the oven for two to three hours, and serve with salad
