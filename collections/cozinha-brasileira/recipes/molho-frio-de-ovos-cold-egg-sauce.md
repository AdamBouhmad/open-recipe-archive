## Ingredients

- 4 or 6 egg yolks
- 1 cup of vinegar
- 2 spoons of sweet oil
- juice of lemon
- broth of peppercorn
- a little sugar
- ground salt

## Directions

1. Mash 4 or 6 egg yolks
2. Add 1 cup of vinegar, 2 spoons of sweet oil, juice of lemon, broth of peppercorn, a little sugar and ground salt
3. Serve with cold fish and meats
