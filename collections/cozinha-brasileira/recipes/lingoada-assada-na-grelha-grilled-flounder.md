## Ingredients

- one flounder
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- butter
- wheat flour
- white wine
- capers

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and dry to remove moisture
2. Anoint with butter, salt, pepper and nutmeg
3. Roast on the grill over moderate fire
4. Serve with a white sauce made of melted butter, flour, white wine and capers
