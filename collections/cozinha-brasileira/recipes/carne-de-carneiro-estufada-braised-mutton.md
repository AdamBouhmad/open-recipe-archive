## Ingredients

- four pounds mutton, chopped
- half pound bacon, chopped
- salt
- parsley
- marjoram
- pepper
- celery
- fat, melted
- two cups white wine
- lemon, sliced, peeled and without seeds
- carapicú

## Directions

1. Chop four pounds mutton with half pound bacon, salt, parsley, marjoram, pepper and celery; sauté in a little melted fat and when heated, add two cups white wine, peeled lemon slices without seeds and some carapicú
2. Let this finish cooking over coals and serve
