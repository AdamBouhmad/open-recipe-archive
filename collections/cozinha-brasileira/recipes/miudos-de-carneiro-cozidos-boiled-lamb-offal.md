## Ingredients

- lamb lungs
- heart
- liver
- tongue
- feet
- hot water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- peppers
- one spoonful of butter
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- one glass of white wine

## Directions

1. After cleaning the lungs, heart, liver, tongue and feet of the lamb, scald them in hot water and then cook them in water with salt, parsley, onion leaves and some peppers
2. When they are cooked, remove the bones from the feet, drain the broth which is thickened (reducing it first if necessary) with one spoonful of butter kneaded with another of wheat flour and one glass of white wine
3. Return the offal to this sauce
