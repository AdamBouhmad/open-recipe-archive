## Ingredients

- one tayoba root
- water
- salt
- wheat flour
- salt and ground pepper
- beaten eggs
- fat

## Directions

1. Cut a tayoba root, cooked in water and salt
2. Wrap it in wheat flour; salt it with salt and ground pepper
3. Pass it through beaten eggs
4. Fry it in fat
5. When fried, pour the remaining eggs over top
6. Serve
