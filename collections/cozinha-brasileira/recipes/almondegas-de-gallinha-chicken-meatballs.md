## Ingredients

- one chicken
- quarter pound of lard
- little salt
- grated nutmeg
- three cardamom seeds, crushed
- one handful of bread crumb
- four egg yolks
- one spoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. Take the chicken and remove its meat
2. Chop the meat very finely with a quarter pound of lard, little salt, grated nutmeg, three crushed cardamom seeds, a handful of bread crumb, four egg yolks and one spoonful of sugar
3. Mix everything well and form small balls
4. Fry them in a little fat
5. Serve with fresh vegetables, squash shoots or cooked palm hearts
