## Ingredients

- 1 quarter of butter
- 1 spoon of powdered bell pepper (Kari)
- a little saffron
- nutmeg
- 2 spoons of wheat flour or cornmeal
- a little meat broth

## Directions

1. Heat 1 quarter of butter in a saucepan
2. Add 1 spoon of powdered bell pepper (Kari), a little saffron, nutmeg, 2 spoons of wheat flour or cornmeal, and a little meat broth
3. Let it boil for fifteen minutes over coals, strain through a fine sieve, add 1 more spoon of butter and serve
