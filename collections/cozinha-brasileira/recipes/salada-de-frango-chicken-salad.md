## Ingredients

- one chicken
- lard
- one dozen preserved sardines
- half plate of cooked and peeled potatoes
- two onions
- a little parsley
- ground pepper
- salt
- half cup of vinegar
- same amount of sweet oil
- beet slices

## Directions

1. Fry a chicken in lard and when cooked, remove the bones and cut the meat into small pieces
2. Do the same with one dozen preserved sardines, half plate of cooked and peeled potatoes, two onions, a little parsley, ground pepper, salt, half cup of vinegar, and the same amount of sweet oil
3. Mix everything and arrange on a plate, garnish with cut beetroot slices, and serve with veal or lamb meat and salads
