## Ingredients

- venison haunch
- lard
- strong vinegar
- onions
- garlic cloves
- parsley
- bay leaves
- cloves
- green peppers
- salt
- ginger
- butter
- white wine

## Directions

1. Trim the venison haunch and lard the exterior with bacon
2. Soak it in strong vinegar with onions, two cloves of garlic, parsley, bay leaves, cloves, some green peppers, salt and ginger
3. Leave it for four to six days
4. Thread it on a spit and roast it, basting with the drippings from the haunch to which two spoonfuls of butter and a glass of white wine have been added
