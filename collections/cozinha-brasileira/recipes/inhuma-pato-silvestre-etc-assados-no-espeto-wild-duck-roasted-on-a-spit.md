## Ingredients

- one bird
- salt
- pepper juice
- garlic
- onion
- orange of the land
- boiled rice
- pieces of bacon
- sugar
- white wine
- thin strips of bacon for larding
- banana leaves

## Directions

1. Rub the bird with salt, pepper juice, garlic, onion and orange of the land, leaving it with this seasoning for three to four hours
2. Fill it with boiled rice, pieces of bacon, salt and sugar melted in white wine
3. Lard the breast with thin strips of bacon
4. Wrap it in banana leaves
5. Place it on a spit and roast for two hours
6. When cooked, serve with some salad
