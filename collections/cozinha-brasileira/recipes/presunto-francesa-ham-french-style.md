## Ingredients

- pork leg
- salt
- thyme
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- marjoram
- segorelha
- juniper seeds
- water
- Lisbon wine

## Directions

1. Place a pork leg in a brine composed of salt, thyme, bay leaf, marjoram, marjoram, segorelha, juniper seeds, all moistened with half water and half Lisbon wine.
2. After leaving the ham to soak for fifteen days, drain it and expose it to smoke.
3. Hams prepared in this manner can last a long time, but this result will be achieved with more certainty if rubbed with Lisbon wine, sprinkling them with ash.
4. One must be careful that the leg is entirely covered by the brine.
