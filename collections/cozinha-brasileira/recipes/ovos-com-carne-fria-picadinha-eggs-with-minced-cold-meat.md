## Ingredients

- eggs
- parsley
- onions
- chives
- salt
- pepper from India
- garlic clove
- cold meat, minced
- lard or fat
- tomato and onion sauce

## Directions

1. Beat whole eggs well
2. Add finely chopped parsley, onions, and chives, salt, pepper from India, and one clove of garlic; mix well
3. Add a portion of finely chopped cold meat
4. Place in a casserole with melted but not very hot lard
5. Cook over low heat with a lid of coals on top
6. Serve in the same casserole with a sauce of tomatoes and onions on top, or carefully remove and place on a plate
