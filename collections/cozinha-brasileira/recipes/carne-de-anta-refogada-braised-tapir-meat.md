## Ingredients

- a piece of fresh tapir meat
- water
- fat
- one chopped onion
- two tablespoons of wheat flour
- two cups of water
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- six tomatoes
- juice of one lemon
- slices of another peeled lemon
- one tablespoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Take a piece of fresh tapir meat, scald it and let it cool in the same water
2. Remove it and cut it into small pieces
3. Braise it with a little fat, one chopped onion, and dust with two tablespoons of wheat flour
4. Add two cups of water, salt, pepper, chopped parsley, six tomatoes, the juice of one lemon, slices of another peeled lemon, and one tablespoon of sugar
5. Let it boil for one hour over moderate heat and serve
