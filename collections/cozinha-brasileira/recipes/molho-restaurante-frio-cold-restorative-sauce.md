## Ingredients

- watercress
- chervil
- marjoram
- chives
- garlic
- parsley
- celery leaves
- basil
- capers
- anchovies
- egg yolk
- sweet oil
- vinegar
- mustard powder

## Directions

1. Chop finely: watercress, chervil, marjoram, chives, garlic, parsley, celery leaves, basil, capers, and anchovies
2. Pound all in a stone mortar
3. Add one egg yolk beaten with a little sweet oil
4. Add vinegar from time to time to prevent curdling
5. Continue pounding until the consistency of a sauce is reached
6. For a more restorative sauce, add a little mustard powder
