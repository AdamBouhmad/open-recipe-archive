## Ingredients

- mutton feet, cleaned and scalded
- water
- salt
- bacon skins
- dried cracker powder
- carapicu
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean and scald the feet well, then boil in water, salt and bacon skins until well cooked
2. Remove the bones and cut the bacon skin and feet into small pieces
3. Reduce the broth to a few cups and thicken with two spoonfuls of dried cracker powder
4. Add a handful of carapicu, one spoonful of vinegar and another of sugar
5. Return the feet to this sauce and after boiling a little, serve
