## Ingredients

- one lizard
- vinegar
- melted fat
- salt
- parsley
- cloves
- peppers
- butter
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Soak a lizard in vinegar with a little melted fat, salt, parsley, cloves, and peppers for twelve hours
2. Wrap it in paper greased with butter and place on a spit
3. Roast over moderate heat
4. Remove the paper and dust with wheat flour, dripping fat on top
5. Let it brown and serve with salad or compote
