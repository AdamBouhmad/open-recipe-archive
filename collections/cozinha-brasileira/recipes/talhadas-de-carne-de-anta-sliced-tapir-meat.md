## Ingredients

- tapir meat
- salt
- comaris pepper juice
- fat
- cangica

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into fine slices
2. Beat them with the flat of a knife
3. Trim them and sprinkle with a little salt and a sauce made with plenty of comaris pepper juice
4. Pass them in fat and roast them on a grill on both sides
5. Serve with cangica
