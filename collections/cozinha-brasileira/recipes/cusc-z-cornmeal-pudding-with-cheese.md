## Ingredients

- half a plate of fine cornmeal
- one cup of milk
- a quarter of a cheese cut into pieces
- one spoonful of sugar
- one spoonful of butter
- two egg yolks
- a little fennel
- some raisins
- a little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Moisten half a plate of fine cornmeal with one cup of milk
2. Mix in a quarter of cheese cut into pieces, one spoonful of sugar, one spoonful of butter, two egg yolks, a little fennel, some raisins, and a little grated nutmeg
3. Place this dough in the cuscuzeiro and proceed as in the Cuscúz de Seminário
