## Ingredients

- one bird (jacú, mutúm, etc.)
- water
- salt
- parsley
- hot pepper
- some cabbage leaves
- cloves
- one glass of local orange juice
- breadcrumbs
- fat
- one dozen tomatoes
- one cup of wine
- a handful of carapicús, chopped
- one spoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the bird into pieces and let boil in water with salt, parsley, hot pepper, some cabbage leaves, cloves, and one glass of local orange juice.
2. When cooked, remove the pieces, coat them in breadcrumbs, and sauté in fat, turning to fry on both sides.
3. When fried, add one dozen tomatoes, one cup of wine, the reduced and strained cooking broth, a handful of chopped carapicús, and one spoonful of sugar.
4. Let boil for another half hour over coals and serve.
