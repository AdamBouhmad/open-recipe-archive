## Ingredients

- one monkey, cut into pieces
- water
- salt
- peppers
- one clove of garlic
- parsley
- half a pound of bacon
- nine pounds of veal

## Directions

1. Boil the monkey cut into pieces in water with salt, peppers, one clove of garlic, parsley, half a pound of bacon and nine pounds of veal
2. When cooked, remove the meat and strain the broth, reducing it to three or four cups
3. Add one squash with still-soft skin, peeled and chopped
4. Give it one boil and add the monkey meat and the veal cut into pieces with some peeled lemon slices, and two egg yolks beaten in a glass of sour orange juice
5. After boiling for one more hour, serve
