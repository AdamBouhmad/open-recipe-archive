## Ingredients

- repolho (cabbage)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- manteiga (butter)
- farinha de trigo (wheat flour)
- leite (milk)

## Directions

1. Boil a cabbage in water and salt for a quarter of an hour, remove it and place it in cold water, and then chop it very finely
2. Separately, mix a spoon of butter with a spoon of wheat flour, add the cabbage, a cup of milk, salt, cook close to the fire and serve
