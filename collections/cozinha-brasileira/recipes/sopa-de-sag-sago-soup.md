## Ingredients

- one cup of sago
- four bottles of water
- half a bottle of red wine
- four spoonfuls of sugar
- a little cinnamon
- cloves
- nutmeg
- lemon peel
- or half a pound of quince paste dissolved in water instead of sugar
- a quarter of raisins, washed in hot water

## Directions

1. Boil one cup of sago with four bottles of water until reduced to two
2. Add half a bottle of red wine
3. Add four spoonfuls of sugar, a little cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, and lemon peel (or half a pound of quince paste dissolved in water instead of sugar)
4. Add a quarter portion of raisins washed in hot water
5. Let it cool slightly from the heat and serve
