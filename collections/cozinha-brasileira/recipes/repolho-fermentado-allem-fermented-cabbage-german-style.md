## Ingredients

- gordura (fat)
- farinha de trigo (wheat flour)
- cebola (onion)
- caldo de carne (meat broth)
- repolho fermentado (fermented cabbage)

## Directions

1. Melt four spoons of fat, add a spoon of wheat flour, a diced onion, half a bottle of meat broth, and a portion of washed fermented cabbage
2. Let it boil well and serve
