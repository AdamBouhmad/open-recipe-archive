## Ingredients

- one bottle of water
- one bottle of vinegar
- two tablespoons of fat
- salt
- parsley
- bay leaves
- onion
- pepper
- fish
- manioc flour
- butter or fat

## Directions

1. Beat one bottle of water, another of vinegar, two tablespoons of fat, salt, parsley, bay leaves, onion and pepper
2. Place the fish in this sauce, and after two hours, give it a boil
3. After cooling, remove the fish, remove the skin with the scales, and split down the middle, cover with manioc flour, and fry in butter or fat and serve with manioc flour pirão made with the fish broth
