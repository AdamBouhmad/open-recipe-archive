## Ingredients

- goose breasts from young fattened geese
- one ounce salt per breast
- one eighth ounce niter per breast
- one eighth ounce muscovado sugar per breast

## Directions

1. Soak the goose breasts in brine made with one ounce of salt, one-eighth ounce of niter, and one-eighth ounce of muscovado sugar per breast
2. Leave in this brine for eight days
3. Hang alternately in the sun and in smoke
4. Eat cooked or raw, like ham, to which they are very similar, though more tender and much more delicate
