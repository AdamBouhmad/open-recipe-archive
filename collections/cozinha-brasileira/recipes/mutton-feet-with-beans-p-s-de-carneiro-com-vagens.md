## Ingredients

- mutton feet, cleaned
- water
- salt
- marjoram
- parsley
- onion leaves
- beans

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned feet to cook in water, salt, marjoram, parsley and onion leaves in equal parts
2. When almost cooked, fry the chopped beans in a little fat
3. Add the mutton feet with all the broth to the beans
4. Let them dry on moderate heat, then bring to table
