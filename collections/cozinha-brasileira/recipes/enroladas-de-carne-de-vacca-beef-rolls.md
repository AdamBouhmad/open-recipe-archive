## Ingredients

- beef, sliced thin
- sardines, chopped
- onions, chopped
- ground pepper
- nutmeg
- salt
- piece of bacon the size of a finger
- butter
- wheat flour
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into thin slices; beat these slices with the back of a knife, and add to them chopped sardines and onions, ground pepper, nutmeg, salt and a piece of bacon the size of a finger.
2. Roll them in such a way that the bacon remains in the center, tie them with thread, and place the prepared rolls in butter and let them braise over coals.
3. When cooked, remove them, pouring over the butter one spoon of wheat flour; let it brown, then add to it two cups of water and salt, putting the rolls in this sauce where they finish cooking.
4. Before serving, remove the thread.
