## Ingredients

- one bowl
- four egg yolks
- salt
- pepper powder
- one chalice of sweet olive oil

## Directions

1. Place a bowl inside a pot filled with boiling water
2. Put four egg yolks with salt and pepper powder in the bowl
3. Add little by little one chalice of sweet olive oil, stirring strongly
4. Remove the bowl and serve with all kinds of fish
5. Take great care not to apply too much heat, because the egg yolks will curdle and the sauce will be ruined
