## Ingredients

- coffee
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. The best method of roasting coffee is to roast it without sugar, and after it is roasted and ready, and while still hot, sprinkle it with powdered sugar, which prevents the evaporation of the essential oil that constitutes the aroma of the coffee.
