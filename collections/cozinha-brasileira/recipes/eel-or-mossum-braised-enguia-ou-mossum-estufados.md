## Ingredients

- One or two fresh eels
- Bacon slices
- Beef
- Pork or chopped ham
- Carapicús or mushrooms
- One garlic clove
- One bay leaf
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sliced lemon without skin
- One bottle of white wine

## Directions

1. Scald one or two fresh eels
2. Place in a casserole lined with bacon slices, a piece of beef, a piece of pork or chopped ham, a handful of carapicús or mushrooms, one garlic clove, one bay leaf, salt, pepper, slices of peeled lemon, and one bottle of white wine
3. Let boil over coals
4. When cooked, serve with the strained and reduced sauce
