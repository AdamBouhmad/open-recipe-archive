## Ingredients

- the legs, wings and breast of game
- the rest of the game
- one spoon of sauce substance
- half a spoon of beef butter
- as much flour
- half a cup of red wine
- one small onion
- a bouquet of herbs
- two spoons of sweet olive oil
- the juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Remove the legs, wings and breast of the game, and pound the rest in a mortar
2. Add from time to time one spoon of sauce substance, passing the pulp through a sieve
3. Mix half a spoon of beef butter kneaded with as much flour, half a cup of red wine, one small onion and a bouquet of herbs which are removed afterwards
4. Leave everything to boil for half an hour, add two spoons of sweet olive oil and the juice of one lemon
5. Heat in this sauce, without boiling, the legs and breast of the game
6. Garnish the bottom of the plate with slices of bread fried in butter
7. Place the legs of the game on top, pour the sauce over and serve
