## Ingredients

- parsley root
- onion
- butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- beef broth
- cooked beef
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut into small pieces a parsley root and one onion, and let them fry in half a quarter of butter over low heat, without browning
2. Add a good spoon of wheat flour
3. Season as desired with salt, pepper, and nutmeg
4. Mix in a cup of beef broth, then leave to boil for ten minutes
5. Cut the cooked beef into small pieces and let them boil a little in the sauce
6. Thicken with two beaten egg yolks
