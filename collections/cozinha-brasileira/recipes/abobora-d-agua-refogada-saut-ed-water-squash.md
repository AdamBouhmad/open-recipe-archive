## Ingredients

- one water squash
- fat
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one spoonful of vinegar
- one spoonful of water

## Directions

1. Peel a water squash, remove seeds and chop very fine
2. Sauté in two spoonfuls of melted fat
3. Add salt, little pepper, parsley, onion leaves, one spoonful of vinegar and one spoonful of water
4. Let boil over moderate heat and serve
