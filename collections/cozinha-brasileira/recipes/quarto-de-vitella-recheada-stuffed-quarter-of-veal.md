## Ingredients

- one leg of veal
- veal meat
- pigeons
- wheat flour
- eggs
- parsley
- onion
- two cloves

## Directions

1. Take a leg of veal and remove the bones without opening the meat, so that it forms a sack
2. Fill this cavity with a mixture made of chopped veal and pigeon meat, wheat flour, eggs, parsley, onion and two cloves
3. Place the prepared leg in a pan over lardons of salt pork
4. Roast it over coals in the oven, turning it from time to time
5. Serve with fresh peas, beans or salads
