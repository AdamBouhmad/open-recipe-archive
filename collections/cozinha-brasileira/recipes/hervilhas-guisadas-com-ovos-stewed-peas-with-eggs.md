## Ingredients

- one spoon of lard
- a little salt
- a portion of green peas
- a little water
- sugar
- eggs

## Directions

1. Melt one spoon of lard and a little salt; when hot, brown a portion of green peas
2. Add a little water and sugar
3. Boil, and when boiling, break eggs so they remain whole
4. Let them cook a little more and serve
