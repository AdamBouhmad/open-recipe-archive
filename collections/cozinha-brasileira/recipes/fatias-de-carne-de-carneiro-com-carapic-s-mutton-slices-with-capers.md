## Ingredients

- mutton slices
- white wine
- water
- strips of bacon
- onions
- cloves
- salt
- pepper
- one bay leaf
- one spoonful of butter
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- one spoonful of vinegar
- carapicús (capers), scalded, chosen, chopped and cooked in water and salt

## Directions

1. Cut and beat the slices, cook them partly in white wine and water in a pot lined with strips of bacon
2. Add onions, cloves, salt, pepper and a bay leaf
3. When cooked, remove the slices and the fat from the broth
4. In another pan, melt one spoonful of butter, mix it with one spoonful of wheat flour, one spoonful of vinegar and a portion of scalded, chosen, chopped and cooked capers
5. Add this to the meat broth, and when the broth is well reduced, serve this pulp with the slices
