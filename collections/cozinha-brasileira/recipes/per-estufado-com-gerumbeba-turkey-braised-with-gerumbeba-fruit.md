## Ingredients

- turkey legs, wings and neck
- one quarter pound of chopped bacon
- a dozen fruits of figueira do inferno singed and peeled
- salt
- clove
- onion
- four glasses of white wine

## Directions

1. Cut the turkey legs, wings and neck and place in a pan with one quarter pound of chopped bacon, a dozen fruits of figueira do inferno singed and peeled, salt, clove, onion and four glasses of white wine
2. Seal the lid
3. Leave to boil over moderate heat or in the oven for two to three hours
4. When cooked, place the meat on a plate, the fruits around it with some peeled lemon slices on top, and serve
