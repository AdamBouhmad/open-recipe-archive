## Ingredients

- one ham
- water
- butter
- hot sauce or pea, bean, or fava bean pulp

## Directions

1. Boil a ham in water until well cooked
2. After removing the skin, fry it in cow butter until it has a good color
3. Serve it with a spicy sauce or pea, bean, or fava bean pulp
