## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- thin strips of bacon
- white wine
- two spoonfuls of butter
- salt
- parsley
- garlic
- onions
- peppers

## Directions

1. Lard the entire rabbit with thin strips of bacon
2. After removing the head, thread it on the spit and roast it, moistening it with white wine to which two spoonfuls of butter, salt, chopped parsley, garlic, sliced onions and peppers have been added
3. When roasted, serve it
