## Ingredients

- turkey
- whey
- salt
- sugar
- cloves
- garlic
- bacon
- grated bread
- cream

## Directions

1. Soak the turkey for twenty-four hours in whey with salt, a little sugar, cloves and a clove of garlic.
2. Wrap it in bacon slices and spit it to roast.
3. When almost cooked, remove the bacon, wrap it in grated bread and finish roasting it until it takes on a good color, basting it with cream.
4. Serve at the table.
