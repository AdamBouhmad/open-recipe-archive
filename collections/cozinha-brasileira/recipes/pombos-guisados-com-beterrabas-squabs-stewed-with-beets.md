## Ingredients

- squabs
- water
- salt
- fresh beets
- wheat flour
- butter
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil the squabs in water and salt with some fresh beets
2. When cooked, remove the squabs
3. Coat them in wheat flour and fry in butter
4. Chop the beets and allow to cool
5. Mix with salt, pepper and vinegar
6. Place in the platter and put the fried squabs on top
7. Pour fried butter over everything
8. Serve cold
