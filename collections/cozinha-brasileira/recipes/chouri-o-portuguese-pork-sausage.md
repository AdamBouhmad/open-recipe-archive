## Ingredients

- some onions, finely chopped
- rendered lard
- pork fat cut into small pieces
- pork blood
- milk
- salt
- seasonings

## Directions

1. Chop some onions very finely and cook them in rendered lard
2. When cooked, add a portion of lard cut into small pieces and mix with the pork blood, milk, salt and seasonings
3. Mix everything well
4. Fill cleaned intestines with this mixture, tying at one end
5. Prick with a pin from time to time if there is too much air inside
6. When the intestine is full, tie the other end
7. Cook these sausages in water for only a few minutes until done
8. To know if they are cooked, remove one from the vessel and prick it with a pin; if blood no longer comes out and fat comes out instead, it is cooked
9. Remove from the vessel and let cool
10. When you wish to eat them, place them on the grill, being careful to prick them so they do not burst
11. They may also be fried if desired
