## Ingredients

- mutton feet
- salt
- half a pound of pork
- bacon
- onion
- garlic clove
- three cloves
- pepper
- nitro
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the mutton feet in water and salt
2. When cooked, remove the bones
3. Stuff with the following filling: mince half a pound of pork with a little bacon, one onion, one garlic clove, three cloves, salt, pepper, a little nitro, bread crumbs softened in milk and two egg yolks
4. Fry the feet in fat until well browned, then bring to table
