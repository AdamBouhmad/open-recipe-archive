## Ingredients

- turtle eggs
- one spoon of wheat flour
- two spoons of butter
- one chopped onion
- one glass of vinegar
- one glass of water

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of turtle eggs; when hard, shell and cut in half
2. In another pan, brown one spoon of wheat flour with two spoons of butter, one chopped onion, one glass of vinegar and another of water; let boil a little, place the eggs in the sauce and serve hot
