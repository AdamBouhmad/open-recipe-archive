## Ingredients

- mutton, cut into pieces
- little water
- butter
- bay leaves
- peppercorns
- chives
- salt
- sardines, chopped
- capers
- lemon, sliced
- bread crusts, grated

## Directions

1. Cut the mutton into pieces and boil in little water with butter, bay leaves, peppercorns, chives and salt
2. A short time before the meat is cooked, add some chopped sardines, capers, sliced lemon and grated bread crusts
3. Serve
