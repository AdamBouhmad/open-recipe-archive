## Ingredients

- one goose breast, well fatty
- salt
- pepper
- dried peas or white beans
- water
- goose fat
- one dozen small onions

## Directions

1. Place the goose breast in a casserole with salt and pepper over moderate heat to render the fat first
2. Then increase the heat until the meat is cooked
3. Cook a portion of dried peas or white beans with salt and water separately
4. When cooked, drain and pass through a sieve
5. Sauté this pea purée in goose fat
6. Place the goose breast on a serving dish on top of the purée
7. Cook a dozen small onions in the same goose fat
8. Serve with the onions around the breast
