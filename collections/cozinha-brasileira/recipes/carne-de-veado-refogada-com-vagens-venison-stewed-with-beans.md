## Ingredients

- venison meat
- fat
- two spoonfuls of wheat flour
- beans, chopped
- one bottle of water
- salt
- pepper
- marjoram
- parsley
- onion leaves
- lemon, peeled and sliced

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into slices and fry in fat on both sides until they take on good color
2. Remove them and pour two spoonfuls of wheat flour into the fat; stir
3. Add a portion of chopped beans
4. Add one bottle of water, salt, pepper, marjoram, parsley, onion leaves and slices of peeled lemon
5. Let simmer on low fire and serve
