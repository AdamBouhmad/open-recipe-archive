## Ingredients

- beef loin
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut a beef loin into four portions; take these to the press, roll them, and place them thus rolled in a casserole, seasoned with salt and pepper.
2. Melt a little butter, which is then poured over the meat to go to the fire.
3. At the time of serving this stew, submit it again to more intense heat, turning it. Then place it in a casserole, pouring over it the substance that remains in the vessel.
