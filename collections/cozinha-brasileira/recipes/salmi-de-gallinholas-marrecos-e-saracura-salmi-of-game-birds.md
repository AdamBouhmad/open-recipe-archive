## Ingredients

- three woodcocks
- parsley leaves
- six small onions
- peppers
- butter
- one glass of white wine
- six spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
- three spoonfuls of beef broth
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Roast three woodcocks on a spit and let them cool
2. Carve them as best as possible and place the pieces in a casserole
3. Crush the bones and some remaining bird meat in a mortar with parsley leaves, six small onions and whole peppers
4. In another casserole, put a piece of butter over the fire and add the crushed bones and meat, letting them boil for ten minutes
5. Pour in one glass of white wine, six spoonfuls of Spanish sauce and three spoonfuls of beef broth
6. Reduce everything by half
7. Pass the sauce through a silk sieve and pour it over the woodcock pieces kept hot but not boiling
8. When serving, arrange the pieces on a platter interspersed with slices of toasted bread
