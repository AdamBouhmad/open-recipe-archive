## Ingredients

- four fresh egg whites
- one pound refined and strained sugar
- juice of lemon rinds or a pinch of ground anise
- a small amount of spirits

## Directions

1. Beat four fresh egg whites with one pound of refined and strained sugar
2. Beat until stiff, adding the juice of lemon rinds or a pinch of ground anise
3. Drop two hundred small portions of this mass onto a sheet of paper
4. Place in a very gentle oven to bake
