## Ingredients

- mushrooms (carapicús)
- milk
- one onion
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- wheat flour
- one cup of cream
- one egg

## Directions

1. Blanch a portion of mushrooms (carapicús), allow them to cool and drain
2. Then place them to boil in milk, one chopped onion, pepper, and salt
3. When cooked, add one spoonful of butter mixed with one spoonful of wheat flour, and one cup of cream with one egg dissolved inside
4. Boil a little and serve
