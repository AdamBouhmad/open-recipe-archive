## Ingredients

- tayoba root
- water
- salt
- one spoonful of flour
- two spoonfuls of grated cheese
- two spoonfuls of fat
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- one onion
- two cups of water
- a little vinegar
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked tayoba root into small slices
2. In another pan, sauté one spoonful of flour and two spoonfuls of grated cheese in two spoonfuls of fat, salt, pepper, parsley, and one chopped onion
3. Add two cups of water and a little vinegar
4. Boil for some time and add the tayoba slices
5. Boil for half an hour
6. Thicken the broth with two egg yolks
7. Serve
