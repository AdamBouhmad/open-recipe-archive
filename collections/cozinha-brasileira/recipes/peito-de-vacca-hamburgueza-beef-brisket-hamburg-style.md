## Ingredients

- beef brisket
- ham slices
- seasonings
- bacon slices
- onions
- carrots
- white wine
- cheese

## Directions

1. Cut the brisket into thick slices.
2. Layer thin slices of ham and seasonings on each slice.
3. Place these slices in a pan lined with bacon slices.
4. Put sliced onions, carrots, and more bacon slices on top.
5. Roast in the oven for two hours.
6. Pour half a bottle of white wine over them and finish cooking over moderate heat.
7. Place on a platter and cover with a layer of sliced cheese before serving.
