## Ingredients

- carapicú
- vinegar
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Select and wash the carapicú
2. Give them one boiling in vinegar and leave to drain
3. When dry, keep between layers of salt
4. To use them, soak for some hours in cold water and wash well
