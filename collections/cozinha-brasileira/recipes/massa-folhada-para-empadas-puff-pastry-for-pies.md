## Ingredients

- pie dough
- butter

## Directions

1. Take the dough above, after being well kneaded, extend it on a board
2. Spread butter on top, roll it up and extend it again, buttering it once more
3. Repeat this operation eight to twelve times and make the crust for the pie in the manner already explained
