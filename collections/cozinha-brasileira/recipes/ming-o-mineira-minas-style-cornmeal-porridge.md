## Ingredients

- 4 or 6 tablespoons cornmeal
- 10 or 12 cups water
- salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 tablespoons rich milk

## Directions

1. Put cornmeal in a pot over the fire with water and a little salt
2. Let boil until the contents are cooked, stirring constantly before removing from heat
3. Add a tablespoon of sugar and two tablespoons of rich milk
4. Serve
