## Ingredients

- Brazil nuts, pine nuts, or sapucaia nuts
- lard
- salt
- nutmeg
- milk

## Directions

1. Boil these fruits
2. When cooked, peel them and crush them in a mortar with lard, salt and nutmeg
3. Dilute the mixture with milk
4. Boil this pulp for half an hour and serve
5. Alternative: these fruits can also be boiled or roasted in hot coals and served peeled
