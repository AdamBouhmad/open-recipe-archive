## Ingredients

- butter
- four to six spoonfuls of fine cornmeal
- two bottles of hot water
- salt
- a little parsley

## Directions

1. Fry four to six spoonfuls of fine cornmeal in butter until light coffee-colored
2. Add two bottles of hot water
3. Add necessary salt and a little parsley
4. Let boil
