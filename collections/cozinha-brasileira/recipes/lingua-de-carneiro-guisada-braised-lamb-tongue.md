## Ingredients

- lamb tongue
- thin strips of bacon
- meat broth
- salt
- pepper
- year-round onion leaves
- parsley
- tapioca starch
- one spoonful of vinegar
- one handful of ora-pro-nobis
- cornmeal mush from Minas

## Directions

1. Lard the tongue after boiling and removing the skin with thin strips of bacon and cook it in meat broth with salt, pepper, year-round onion leaves and parsley
2. When cooked, strain the broth through a sieve; thicken it with a little tapioca starch, add one spoonful of vinegar and one handful of ora-pro-nobis
3. Put the tongue back into this sauce and let it boil another half hour over coals and serve with cornmeal mush from Minas
