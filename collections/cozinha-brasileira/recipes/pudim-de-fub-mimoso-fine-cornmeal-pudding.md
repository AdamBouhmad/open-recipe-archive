## Ingredients

- one quarter of butter
- one pound of fine cornmeal
- one spoon of polvilho
- four eggs
- one quarter of sugar
- a portion of scalded raisins
- lemon zest
- salt
- nutmeg
- hot milk as needed

## Directions

1. Mix one quarter of butter with one pound of fine cornmeal, one spoon of polvilho, four eggs, one quarter of sugar
2. Add a portion of scalded raisins, lemon zest, salt and nutmeg
3. Add hot milk to form a thick mixture
4. Tie in a napkin
5. Place in a pot with boiling water
6. Boil until cooked
7. Serve with an appropriate sauce
