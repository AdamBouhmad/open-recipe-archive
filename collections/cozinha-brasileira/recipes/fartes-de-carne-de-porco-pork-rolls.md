## Ingredients

- portion of leftover pork
- pork belly rind or ham rind
- salt
- pepper
- spices

## Directions

1. Take a portion of leftover pork with pork belly rind or ham rind, and cut everything into small pieces and season as one seasons sausages.
2. Take a portion of this meat wrapped in pork belly rind or in strips of ham.
3. Sew at both ends and make as many as the meat yields.
4. Place them in brine for eight days.
5. After that time, take them to the smokehouse and let them dry.
6. When you want to eat them, soak them in water for twenty-four hours.
7. After that, place them to cook in plenty of water and serve.
