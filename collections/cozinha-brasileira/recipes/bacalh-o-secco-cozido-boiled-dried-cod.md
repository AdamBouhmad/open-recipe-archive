## Ingredients

- dried cod
- milk
- parsley
- one tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Soak the dried cod in cold water for twenty-four hours.
2. Remove it and place it in a pot with sufficient milk to cover it.
3. Add parsley and one tablespoon of butter.
4. Let it cook for another half hour.
5. Serve with its reduced sauce, if it is still thin.
