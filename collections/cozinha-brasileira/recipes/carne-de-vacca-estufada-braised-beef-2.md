## Ingredients

- larded beef
- bacon
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- wheat flour
- fat

## Directions

1. Put the larded beef (filled with pieces of bacon) in a pan lined with pieces of bacon, chopped onions, salt and pepper and let it braise over moderate fire, keeping it well covered.
2. When cooked, remove the beef and fry in fat a little wheat flour.
