## Ingredients

- four tablespoons polished cornmeal
- one tablespoon tapioca pearls
- two beaten eggs
- a little salt
- lemon peel
- two cups milk

## Directions

1. Make a mixture of four tablespoons of polished cornmeal with one tablespoon of tapioca pearls, two beaten eggs, a little salt, lemon peel and two cups of milk
2. Proceed as already described
