## Ingredients

- veal brains
- water
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one tablespoon butter
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Clean the veal brains as with beef brains
2. Cut into slices and cook for half an hour with one tablespoon wheat flour, one tablespoon butter, salt, nutmeg, and a little water
3. Make a sauce by melting two tablespoons butter and adding two tablespoons wheat flour, a little salt, parsley, pepper, and lemon juice
4. Serve with this sauce on top
