## Ingredients

- pieces of bacon cut into strips
- pieces of sausages or linguiça
- cooked beef cut into slices
- two spoons of broth
- one spoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry pieces of bacon cut into strips with pieces of sausages or linguiça
2. When they acquire color, add the cooked beef cut into slices with two spoons of broth and one spoon of vinegar
