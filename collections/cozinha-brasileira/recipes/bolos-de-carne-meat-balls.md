## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of roasted meat
- one onion
- four to five eggs
- wheat flour
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mince a quarter pound of roasted meat with one onion
2. Knead with wheat flour, four to five eggs and little nutmeg
3. Take small portions of this mixture and boil them gradually in a pan that is on the fire with hot water
4. When the balls are cooked, serve with sauce or melted butter
