## Ingredients

- one flounder
- butter
- wheat flour
- a handful of carapicús
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and spine of the fish and cut into strips
2. Sauté in butter and remove and drain well
3. Add one spoon of wheat flour to the butter and stir
4. Add a handful of carapicús cooked in white wine
5. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg
6. Let it boil, add the fish and boil for another quarter of an hour
7. Serve
