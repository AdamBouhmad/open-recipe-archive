## Ingredients

- four spoonfuls of wheat flour
- one spoonful of butter
- hot water
- two eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Knead four spoonfuls of wheat flour with one spoonful of butter, hot water, two eggs, and salt
2. Knead everything until it has regular consistency
3. Extend half of it on a plate, with the edges of the dough being half an inch larger than the plate
4. With the other half extended, cover the filling and the dough of which the pie was made
5. Fold the edges of the lower paste over the upper layer and bake in the oven
