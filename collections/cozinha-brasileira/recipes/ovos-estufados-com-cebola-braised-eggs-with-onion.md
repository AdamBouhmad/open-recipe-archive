## Ingredients

- six onions
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- one spoonful of milk
- salt
- pepper
- one dozen eggs

## Directions

1. Chop six onions and fry in one spoonful of wheat flour.
2. Add one spoonful of milk, salt, and pepper.
3. Simmer for a while.
4. Mix in one dozen very well beaten eggs.
5. Place over coals, covering with a lid filled with coals.
6. When cooked, turn the saucepan over onto a plate and serve.
