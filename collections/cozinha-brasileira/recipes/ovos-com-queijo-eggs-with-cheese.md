## Ingredients

- equal parts of breadcrumb and grated cheese
- salt
- pepper
- three or four egg yolks
- one or two spoonfuls of milk
- eight eggs
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Mix on a heat-resistant plate equal parts of breadcrumb and grated cheese.
2. Season with salt and pepper, and mix three or four egg yolks and one or two spoonfuls of milk.
3. Place over coals until hot.
4. Break eight eggs over this mixture, and add a portion of grated cheese.
5. Cover with a lid full of coals until the whites are cooked and the cheese is melted, and serve.
