## Ingredients

- capybara meat
- whey
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- thyme or serpol
- cloves
- peppers
- tayoba leaves
- breadcrumbs
- melted butter
- lemon and comari pepper sauce

## Directions

1. After disinfecting the capybara meat, bring it to a boil for one hour with whey, salt, parsley, onion leaves, thyme or serpol, cloves, and peppers
2. Remove and dry it, then thread it onto a spit
3. Roast over active fire at first, then moderate fire toward the end
4. Serve with tayoba leaves boiled in water and salt, placed on a plate, covered with breadcrumbs and melted butter, and serve with a sauce of lemon and comari peppers
