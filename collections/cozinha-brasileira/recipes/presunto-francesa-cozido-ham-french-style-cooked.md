## Ingredients

- ham
- cold water
- cloth
- water
- white wine
- onion
- roots
- seasoning herbs

## Directions

1. When you wish to cook them, place them in cold water for three to four days to draw out the salt, and after wrapping them in a cloth, place them in a vessel with equal parts water and white wine, onion, some roots and a bundle of seasoning herbs.
2. Must leave them to cook over a moderate fire for five to six hours and let them cool in the same vessel.
3. Remove the skin carefully, leaving the fat.
4. Cover with finely chopped parsley, bread crumb fried in butter and pass a hot iron over them.
