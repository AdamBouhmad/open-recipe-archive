## Ingredients

- one chicken
- half a pound of ham
- water
- little salt
- one onion
- parsley
- squash shoots
- breadcrumbs
- cheese
- sauce

## Directions

1. Take the chicken, remove the meat from the bones, and cut it into pieces.
2. Cook it with half a pound of ham in water, little salt, one onion and parsley.
3. When cooked, remove and mince the meat very finely, as well as the ham.
4. Boil the squash shoots in the broth.
5. When cooked, remove them and place on a plate, putting the minced meat on top.
6. Cover with a layer of breadcrumbs and another of cheese, and pour the strained and reduced sauce on top.
7. Place in a well-heated oven and serve.
