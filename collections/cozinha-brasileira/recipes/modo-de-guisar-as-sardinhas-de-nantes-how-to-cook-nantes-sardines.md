## Ingredients

- preserved sardines or fish in sweet oil
- vinegar
- mustard powder

## Directions

1. The fish may be eaten cold by adding a little vinegar and mustard powder
2. Alternatively, heat them with the oil, and when hot add a little vinegar and serve
