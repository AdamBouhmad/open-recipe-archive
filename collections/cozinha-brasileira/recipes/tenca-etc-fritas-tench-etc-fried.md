## Ingredients

- one bottle of water
- one bottle of vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chopped onions
- two tablespoons of butter
- fish
- manioc flour or breadcrumbs
- beaten eggs
- cow butter

## Directions

1. Place in a casserole one bottle of water, another of vinegar, salt, pepper, parsley, chopped onions and two tablespoons of butter
2. Heat until the butter melts, pour this liquid over a portion of cleaned fish, leaving them in the sauce for three to four hours
3. Remove them, wrap them in manioc flour or breadcrumbs, pass them in beaten eggs, wrapping them again
4. Fry in cow butter and serve
