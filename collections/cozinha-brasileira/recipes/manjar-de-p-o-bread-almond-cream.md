## Ingredients

- two cooked chicken breasts
- quarter pound blanched almonds
- two and a half pounds sugar
- breadcrumbs
- one bottle milk
- four egg yolks
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take two cooked chicken breasts and pound them with a quarter pound of blanched almonds, two and a half pounds of sugar and a portion of breadcrumbs
2. Put in a casserole with one bottle of milk
3. Let boil twice and when it has cooled a little, add four beaten egg yolks
4. Boil again, pour on a plate
5. Cover with sugar and ground cinnamon and serve
