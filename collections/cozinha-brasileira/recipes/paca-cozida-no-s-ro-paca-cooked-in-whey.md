## Ingredients

- one paca, cut into pieces
- whey
- salt
- peppers
- one bottle of hot white wine
- two egg yolks
- one onion
- mushrooms or carapicús
- cornstarch
- okra

## Directions

1. Cut the paca into pieces and boil it with whey, salt and peppers
2. When almost cooked, remove it and finish cooking it in a sauce made of one bottle of hot white wine with two beaten egg yolks, one onion and a portion of chopped mushrooms or carapicús, one tablespoon of cornstarch and some okra cut into slices, and serve
