## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- bacon slices
- ham slices
- beans or peas
- parsley
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- orange juice

## Directions

1. Beat the leg after removing fat and trimming the bone
2. Lard it with slices of bacon and ham
3. Scald in hot water and boil for five minutes, then place in cold water
4. Put on the fire with beans or peas, parsley, one onion, salt and pepper
5. Boil until cooked
6. Separate the broth and reduce to two cups of liquid
7. Add the juice of one orange and return the meat to it
8. Let it boil over coals until well browned and begins to stick to the bottom of the vessel
9. Serve with the vegetables
