## Ingredients

- one bird roasted on a spit
- dozen Nantes sardines
- two onions
- two apples
- dozen small potatoes
- vinegar
- sweet oil
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Take the bird roasted on a spit
2. remove all the meat and cut into small pieces
3. do the same with a dozen Nantes sardines, two onions, two apples and a dozen small potatoes
4. mix everything without crushing
5. add vinegar, sweet oil, salt, pepper, chopped parsley and serve
