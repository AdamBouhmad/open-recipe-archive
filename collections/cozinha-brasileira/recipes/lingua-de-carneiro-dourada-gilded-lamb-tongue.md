## Ingredients

- one lamb tongue
- salt
- cardamom powder
- two cups of milk
- four egg yolks
- two spoonfuls of wheat flour
- half a spoonful of sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel and cook one tongue, then cut it into slices
2. Season these slices with a little salt and cardamom powder and wrap them in a thin batter made of two cups of milk, four egg yolks, two spoonfuls of wheat flour and half a spoonful of sugar
3. Thus prepared, fry the slices on both sides in butter, place them in a casserole and pour the rest of the egg batter on top, letting it fry
