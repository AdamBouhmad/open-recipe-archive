## Ingredients

- Pork loin
- Heart of palm
- Okra
- Ora-pro-nobis leaves
- Comarim peppers
- Salt
- Garlic
- Onion leaves
- Cornmeal mush

## Directions

1. Braise the loin
2. Chop the heart of palm finely and add with the loin
3. Allow to cook together
4. Add okra or, if lacking, ora-pro-nobis leaves, comarim peppers, salt, garlic and chopped onion leaves
5. Allow to cook a few more minutes and serve with cornmeal mush
