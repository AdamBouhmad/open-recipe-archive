## Ingredients

- one plucked chicken
- one pound of veal, chopped
- half a pound of bacon
- chicken offal
- one onion
- salt
- parsley
- two green hot peppers
- six cooked eggs
- two tablespoons of butter
- one cup of water
- chicory

## Directions

1. Pluck the chicken and open it along the back, removing the ribs and breast bones
2. Chop one pound of veal with half a pound of bacon, the chicken offal, one onion, salt, parsley, two green hot peppers, and six cooked eggs
3. Stuff the chicken with this filling
4. Secure the opening with stitches
5. Place in a pan with two tablespoons of butter and one cup of water
6. Simmer over moderate heat until cooked
7. Serve with braised chicory
