## Ingredients

- one or more round loaves of bread
- some spoonfuls of sugar
- hot water
- butter (optional)

## Directions

1. Break one or more round loaves of bread into pieces and place them with some spoonfuls of sugar in a bowl
2. Pour hot water over them
3. Cover with a plate and serve after a quarter hour has passed
4. When the patient can use butter, also add one spoonful to the bowl before pouring the water
