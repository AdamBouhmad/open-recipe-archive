## Ingredients

- venison loin
- white wine
- salt
- pimento peppers
- onions
- garlic
- ginger
- marjoram
- basil
- bacon strips
- cauliflower or cabbage

## Directions

1. Soak the loin in white wine, salt, pimento peppers, onions, garlic, ginger, marjoram and basil for twenty-four hours
2. Remove and wrap it in strips of bacon
3. Place it in a pot with a portion of cauliflower, or lacking that, very finely chopped cabbage, holding the lid down well
4. Boil over regular heat until cooked and the broth reduced, and serve
