## Ingredients

- one roasted chicken
- one pound of ham
- meat broth
- red wine
- salt
- one onion
- lemon juice
- three egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the roasted chicken into pieces with one pound of ham
2. Place in a casserole over the fire with one cup of meat broth, another of red wine, little salt, one cut onion, a little lemon juice, and three egg yolks
3. Let it boil for half an hour over moderate heat and serve
