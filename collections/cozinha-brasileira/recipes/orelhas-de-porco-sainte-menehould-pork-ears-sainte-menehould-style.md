## Ingredients

- Pork ears
- Meat broth
- Red wine
- Carrots
- Onions
- Cloves
- Aromatic herbs
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the pork ears in meat broth with red wine, carrots, and onions, inserting cloves into one of the onions
2. Add a bundle of aromatic herbs, broken pepper and salt
