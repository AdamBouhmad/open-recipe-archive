## Ingredients

- beef udder
- water
- salt
- bouquet of aromatic herbs
- onions
- garlic
- carrots
- glass of aguardente

## Directions

1. Scrape and wash the udder
2. Keep soaked for two hours in cold water
3. After scalding and cooling, cook for five to eight hours with salt, a bouquet of aromatic herbs, onions, garlic, carrots and a glass of aguardente
4. Serve with a spicy sauce
