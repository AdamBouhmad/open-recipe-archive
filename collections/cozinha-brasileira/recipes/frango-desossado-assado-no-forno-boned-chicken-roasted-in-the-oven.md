## Ingredients

- chicken
- two pounds of veal
- half pound of bacon
- two onions
- chicken giblets
- two hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley
- marjoram
- aguardente do reino
- slices of bacon
- two glasses of white wine
- two cups of water
- onion leaves
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Open the chicken through the back and remove all the bones, leaving only the neck, wings, legs and hip bone
2. Chop two pounds of veal with half a pound of bacon, two onions, the chicken giblets, two hard-boiled eggs, salt, pepper, nutmeg, parsley and marjoram
3. Moisten with a little aguardente do reino and fill the chicken with this stuffing
4. Wrap it in slices of bacon and place in a pan with two glasses of white wine, two cups of water, salt, onion leaves and the juice of a lemon
5. Cook until done, remove the chicken, strain the sauce, remove the fat and pour it over the chicken to serve
