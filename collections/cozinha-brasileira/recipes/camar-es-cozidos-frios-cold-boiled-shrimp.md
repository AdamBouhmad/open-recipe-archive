## Ingredients

- portion of shrimp
- salt
- peppers
- chopped parsley
- two onions
- grated nutmeg
- vinegar
- sweet oil
- English mustard flour
- one hard-boiled egg yolk
- one glass of wine

## Directions

1. Cook and shell a portion of shrimp
2. Add salt, peppers, chopped parsley, two onions cut in slices, grated nutmeg, vinegar and sweet oil
3. Mix everything and sprinkle with English mustard flour
4. Place on top a hard-boiled egg yolk dissolved in a glass of wine and serve
