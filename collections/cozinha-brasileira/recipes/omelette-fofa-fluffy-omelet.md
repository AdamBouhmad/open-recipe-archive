## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- six spoonfuls of clean sugar
- some finely chopped lemon zest
- a piece of butter
- refined sugar

## Directions

1. Break eight eggs and separate the whites from the yolks.
2. Mix six spoonfuls of clean sugar with the yolks and some finely chopped lemon zest, as fine as possible, and mix everything together.
3. At the time of serving, beat the whites and mix them with the yolks.
4. Take a piece of butter, which is melted in a frying pan, and after it is melted, pour in the eggs and turn the omelet.
5. When the butter is absorbed, pour it into a deep serving dish buttered, and place this dish over coals.
6. Sprinkle refined sugar on top and place a cover with coals over the dish, being careful that the omelet does not burn, and serve hot.
