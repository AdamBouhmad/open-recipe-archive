## Ingredients

- Lamb square
- Lean bacon lard
- Butter-greased paper
- Giló
- Water
- Salt
- Sauce from roasting

## Directions

1. Beat the lamb square and lard it with lean bacon
2. Spit it and cover with butter-greased paper
3. Roast it
4. When nearly roasted, remove the paper to take on colour
5. Place on a plate
6. Arrange a dozen gilós around it, cooked in water and salt
7. Top with the roasting sauce
