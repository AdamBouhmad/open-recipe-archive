## Ingredients

- veal meat
- butter or fat
- breadcrumbs or dry rusks
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chives
- paper

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into slices
2. Cover both sides with a mixture made of butter or fat, breadcrumb powder or dry rusks, salt, pepper, finely chopped parsley and chives
3. Wrap the prepared slices in buttered paper
4. Roast over coals on the grill and serve with salad
