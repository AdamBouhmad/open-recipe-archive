## Ingredients

- Grumixamas
- Cinnamon
- Lemon zest
- Sugar
- Cornstarch

## Directions

1. Pound and squeeze a portion of grumixamas
2. Boil the juice for half an hour with cinnamon, lemon zest, sugar and a little cornstarch
3. Serve for puddings and pancakes
