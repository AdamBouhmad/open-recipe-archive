## Ingredients

- one or two dozen onions
- two to three spoons of fresh butter
- half a bottle of water
- salt
- pepper
- one spoon of good vinegar
- one cup of wine

## Directions

1. Remove the skins from one or two dozen onions and put them in a pan with two or three spoons of fresh butter
2. Add half a bottle of water, salt and pepper
3. Let it boil for five minutes over a lively fire, then put the pan over coals until the broth is reduced
4. Remove the onions, put them on a plate and add to the sauce one spoon of good vinegar and one cup of wine
5. After boiling a little, pour this sauce over the onions and serve
