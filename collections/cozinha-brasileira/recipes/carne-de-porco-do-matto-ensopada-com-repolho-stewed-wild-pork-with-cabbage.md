## Ingredients

- wild pork, cut into large pieces
- half pound chopped bacon
- salt
- parsley
- one bunch mint
- onion leaves
- marjoram
- one tablespoon polvilho dissolved in a glass of vinegar
- one chopped cabbage, sautéed in a little fat

## Directions

1. Boil the pork cut into large pieces with half pound of chopped bacon, salt, parsley, one bunch of mint, onion leaves and marjoram.
2. When cooked, remove the meat and strain the broth.
3. Add to it one tablespoon of polvilho dissolved in a glass of vinegar and one chopped cabbage sautéed in a little fat.
4. Put the meat back in and let it boil over moderate heat until the broth dries out and serve.
