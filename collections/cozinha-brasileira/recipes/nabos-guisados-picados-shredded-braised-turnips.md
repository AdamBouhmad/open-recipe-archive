## Ingredients

- turnips
- water
- salt
- butter
- nutmeg
- milk
- arrowroot
- egg yolk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the turnips, cut into thin strips, and boil for five minutes in water and salt
2. Remove with a skimmer and drain
3. Sauté in two spoons of butter with salt, nutmeg, three cups of milk, a spoon of arrowroot, an egg yolk, and a spoon of sugar
4. Finish cooking the turnips in this sauce and serve
