## Ingredients

- two squabs
- one quarter butter
- one half of chopped bacon
- the same amount of carapicús
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one cup meat broth
- salt
- parsley
- one lemon

## Directions

1. Cut two squabs into four pieces each
2. Fry one quarter butter with one half chopped bacon and the same amount of carapicús
3. Immediately add the pieces of squab
4. Turn them to brown on all sides
5. Allow to fry for ten to fifteen minutes
6. Remove the squabs and add one tablespoon wheat flour to the butter, stirring
7. Add one cup of meat broth, salt and parsley
8. Return the squabs to the sauce for a moment
9. Serve after squeezing lemon juice over them
