## Ingredients

- one spoonful of butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Heat one spoonful of butter with salt in a frying pan.
2. When hot, carefully break eggs into it so they do not break.
3. Fry for a little while.
4. Cover with a little pepper and nutmeg.
5. Serve.
6. These eggs may be prepared in a deep plate that can withstand the heat of the fire.
