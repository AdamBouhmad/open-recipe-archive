## Ingredients

- beef palate
- water

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape the palate
2. Remove the hard black skin after scalding with hot water
3. Wash several times in hot water and finally in cold water
4. Cut into pieces the thickness of three fingers
5. Cook these slowly for six to seven hours
6. Place on a plate and after draining, serve with any sauce
