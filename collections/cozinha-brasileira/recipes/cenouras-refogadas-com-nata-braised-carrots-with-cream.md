## Ingredients

- some carrots
- butter
- water
- salt
- parsley
- one cup of cream
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Braise in very little butter some carrots, adding water, salt, chopped parsley
2. When cooked, add one cup of cream, letting it finish cooking near the fire
3. Before serving, add two egg yolks dissolved with one spoon of cream
