## Ingredients

- two tablespoons wheat flour
- two tablespoons butter
- one onion, chopped
- two cups red wine
- one bottle water
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- one tablespoon juice of orange
- wild pork, cut into pieces
- ora-pro-nobis leaves, peeled and cleaned

## Directions

1. Fry two tablespoons of wheat flour with two tablespoons of butter until they take color.
2. Add one chopped onion, two cups of red wine, one bottle of water, salt, peppers, parsley, and one tablespoon of juice of orange.
3. Boil a little, and then add the pork meat cut into pieces to this sauce.
4. Let it boil until cooked.
5. At this time, add a portion of ora-pro-nobis leaves, peeled and cleaned.
6. Boil once more and serve.
