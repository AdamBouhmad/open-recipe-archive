## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- one spoonful of fat
- four spoonfuls of fine cornmeal
- a little cold water
- one parsley leaf

## Directions

1. Put a pot with water, salt, and one spoonful of fat on the fire
2. When boiling, gradually add four spoonfuls of fine cornmeal dissolved in a little cold water and one parsley leaf, stirring constantly
3. Let cook for a little more time and remove the pot from the fire
