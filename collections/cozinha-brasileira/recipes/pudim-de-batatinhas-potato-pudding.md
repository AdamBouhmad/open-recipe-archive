## Ingredients

- half a pound of small potatoes
- half a pound of fine cornmeal
- eight egg yolks
- half a pound of butter
- one pound of sugar
- salt
- chopped lemon zest

## Directions

1. Peel and grate half a pound of small potatoes, mix with half a pound of fine cornmeal, eight egg yolks, half a pound of butter, one pound of sugar, salt and chopped lemon zest
2. Place the dough in a beef bladder and boil in a pot with water
