## Ingredients

- one duck
- wheat flour
- four spoonfuls of fat
- half a bottle of water
- same amount of white wine
- salt
- nutmeg
- parsley
- marjoram
- a portion of turnips
- two spoonfuls of butter
- one spoonful of wheat flour

## Directions

1. Take the duck, wrap it in wheat flour and fry it in four spoonfuls of fat, turning it on all sides to fry evenly
2. Add half a bottle of water and the same amount of white wine, salt, nutmeg, parsley and marjoram
3. When almost cooked, add a portion of turnips sautéed in two spoonfuls of butter with one spoonful of wheat flour
4. Let it cook over very low fire and serve
