## Ingredients

- otter or giant otter meat
- hot fat
- one bottle of water
- white beans
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- wheat flour
- vinegar
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into slices and beat slightly.
2. Pass through hot fat.
3. Add one bottle of water, a portion of white beans, salt, parsley, and onion.
4. Let boil until the meat and beans are cooked.
5. Add one spoonful of toasted wheat flour mixed with a little vinegar and an egg yolk.
6. Give it another boil and serve.
