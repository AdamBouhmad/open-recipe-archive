## Ingredients

- one bottle of milk
- little salt
- four spoons of sugar
- pieces of cinnamon
- cloves
- four ounces of bread crumb
- one spoon of polvilho
- six egg whites

## Directions

1. Boil one bottle of milk with little salt, four spoons of sugar, pieces of cinnamon, and cloves, reducing it to half
2. Add four ounces of bread crumb
3. Boil until the bread dissolves
4. Strain through a sieve
5. Add one spoon of polvilho beaten with six egg whites, mixing the white gradually into the milk
6. Boil again two or three times
7. Place in a bowl and let cool
8. Turn this mass out onto a plate so it comes out whole
9. Cover lightly
