## Ingredients

- monkey, cleaned and without head
- one veal or pork liver
- two ounces of bacon
- one onion
- parsley
- two egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- one spoonful of sugar
- bacon strips
- prickly pears (gerumbelas), peeled
- one cup of water
- one dozen tomatoes

## Directions

1. Take a cleaned monkey without head and fill it with a stuffing made with one veal or pork liver, two ounces of bacon, one onion, a little parsley, all finely chopped and mashed with two egg yolks, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and one spoonful of sugar.
2. Place it in a pan lined with bacon strips with a portion of peeled prickly pears, one cup of water, and one dozen tomatoes.
3. Cover the pan and let boil for three hours over a moderate fire.
4. When cooked, serve with the strained and degreased sauce.
