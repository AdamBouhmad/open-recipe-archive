## Ingredients

- postas (steaks) of tuna or crevalle jack
- red wine
- vinegar
- garlic clove
- bay leaves
- cloves
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- sweet oil

## Directions

1. Soak the fish steaks in red wine, vinegar, a garlic clove, bay leaves, cloves, nutmeg, pepper and salt for three to four hours
2. Place the steaks in a pan with some of this marinade, a spoon of sweet oil
3. Set the pan over coals and cook for one hour
4. Serve with the hot sauce
