## Ingredients

- one duck
- Canadian plums, peeled and pitted, or preserved plums mixed with raisins and pieces of peeled quince
- half a pound of bacon, cut in pieces
- two glasses of wine
- salt
- nutmeg
- cardamom
- clove of India
- two dozen peeled plums without pits

## Directions

1. Take the duck and fill it with peeled and pitted Canadian plums, or in their absence with preserved plums mixed with raisins and pieces of peeled quince
2. Place the duck in the pan with half a pound of bacon cut in pieces, two glasses of wine, little salt, nutmeg, cardamom, clove of India, two dozen peeled plums without pits
3. Seal the lid and boil it over regularly hot fire
