## Ingredients

- lobsters
- salt
- water
- bread crumbs
- milk
- butter
- egg yolks
- sugar
- nutmeg
- wheat flour
- white wine
- cream

## Directions

1. Boil lobsters in salted water
2. Remove the tail and shell
3. Fill the shell with a mixture made from bread crumbs softened in milk, kneaded with butter, egg yolks, sugar, grated nutmeg and wheat flour as needed to form a paste
4. Peel the tails
5. Melt two tablespoons of butter, add one tablespoon of wheat flour, two egg yolks dissolved in two tablespoons of white wine, one cup of cream, a little sugar and grated nutmeg
6. Boil everything, add the tail and stuffed shell with the mixture
7. Boil for another quarter hour and serve
