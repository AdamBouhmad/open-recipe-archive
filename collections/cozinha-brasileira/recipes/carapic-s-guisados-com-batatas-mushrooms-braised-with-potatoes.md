## Ingredients

- carapicús (mushrooms)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- gordura (fat)
- sumo de limão (lemon juice)
- batatas or mangaritos (potatoes or taro)
- farinha de trigo (wheat flour)
- pimenta (pepper)
- salsa (parsley)

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of carapicús in water and salt
2. Remove them with a skimming spoon and place them in a casserole with fat and a cup of lemon juice
3. Mix and add a portion of peeled and sliced potatoes or taro
4. Fry them in fat to which a little wheat flour has been added and two cups of water
5. Place over the fire, adding salt, pepper and chopped parsley
6. Having boiled over moderate heat, serve them
