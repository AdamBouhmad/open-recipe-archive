## Ingredients

- beef tripe
- carrots
- onions
- small amount of bacon
- bouquet of aromatic herbs
- four cloves
- four cloves of garlic
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- small amount of ham
- white wine
- water

## Directions

1. Scrape the opened tripe and wash in many waters
2. Scald in hot water, then soak for twenty-four hours, changing the water many times
3. Place carrots, onions, a little bacon, a bouquet of aromatic herbs, cloves, four garlic cloves, and pepper in a casserole
4. Add the drained and dried tripe, sprinkled with salt and nutmeg, with a little ham, white wine and a small amount of water
5. Seal the casserole well, applying strips of glued paper, and let it boil for six or seven hours in the oven
6. Serve hot with vegetables
