## Ingredients

- one capon, cut into pieces
- three spoons of cow butter
- one bowl of water
- one spoon of wheat flour
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- one clove
- turnips, peeled and sliced thin
- two egg yolks
- one cup of white wine

## Directions

1. Cut the capon into pieces and fry them in three spoons of cow butter
2. Add one bowl of water, one spoon of wheat flour, salt, parsley, onion, one clove, and a portion of peeled turnips sliced thinly
3. Let cook, and finally add two egg yolks dissolved in one cup of white wine
4. Serve when cooked
