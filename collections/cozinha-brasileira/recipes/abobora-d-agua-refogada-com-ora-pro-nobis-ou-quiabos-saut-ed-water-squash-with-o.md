## Ingredients

- a piece of pork
- fat
- one water squash
- tomatoes
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- one cup of meat broth or water
- a portion of ora-pro-nobis

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of pork into small pieces and fry in fat
2. Add one chopped water squash, tomatoes, salt, parsley, pepper and one cup of meat broth or water
3. Let boil for half an hour over moderate heat
4. Add a portion of ora-pro-nobis and once it has boiled again, serve
