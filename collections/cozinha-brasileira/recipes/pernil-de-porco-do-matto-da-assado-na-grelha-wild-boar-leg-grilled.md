## Ingredients

- one wild boar leg
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onions
- bay leaves
- pepper
- cassava flour
- fat
- lemon juice
- small potatoes

## Directions

1. Boil in water with salt, parsley, onions, bay leaves, and pepper one wild boar leg
2. When it has boiled two or three times, cut into slices the thickness of two fingers
3. Coat in cassava flour and grill, drizzling with a little fat and lemon juice
4. Serve with small potatoes
