## Ingredients

- good piece of beef from the chuck covered with fat
- water
- salt
- cloves
- roots
- small onions
- cabbage
- meat broth substance

## Directions

1. Take a good piece of beef from the chuck covered with fat
2. Remove the bone and tie it, then place it in a large pot
3. Break the bone and place it inside
4. Fill three-quarters of the pot with cold water and let it cook moderately
5. Skim slowly and add cold water from time to time
6. When well skimmed, add a little salt and two cloves, taking care that everything boils very slowly for five hours
7. When adding salt, also add the roots to be cooked
8. Test with a knife: if it enters easily and exits the same way, the beef is cooked
9. Remove, let it drain, and brush two or three times with meat broth substance
10. Place on a platter with small onions and cabbage around it, all small and covered with meat broth substance
