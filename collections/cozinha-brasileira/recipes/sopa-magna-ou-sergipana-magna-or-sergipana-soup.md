## Ingredients

- vegetables
- cabbage or kale
- carrots
- parsley root
- celery root
- potatoes
- salt
- bread crust
- fat
- turtle egg fat

## Directions

1. Put a handful of vegetables (cabbage or kale, carrots, parsley root, celery root, potatoes) and salt on the fire
2. Boil for a long time
3. Strain the broth over bread crust fried in turtle egg fat
