## Ingredients

- artichokes
- water
- salt
- chopped filling
- wheat flour
- butter
- wine

## Directions

1. Boil a quantity of artichokes in water and salt
2. Remove the choke from the center
3. Fill this space with chopped filling
4. Pour over them a sauce made of toasted wheat flour, butter, and two cups of wine
5. Let it boil a little and serve
