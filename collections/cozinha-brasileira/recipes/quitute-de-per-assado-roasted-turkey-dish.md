## Ingredients

- butter
- wheat flour
- carapicú
- parsley
- onion, chopped
- white wine
- meat broth
- roasted turkey pieces

## Directions

1. Melt two spoons of butter, mix in one spoon of wheat flour, a handful of carapicú, parsley and a chopped onion
2. Add to them one cup of white wine and two of meat broth
3. Let it boil and pour into this sauce the pieces of roasted turkey and, having boiled a little, serve
