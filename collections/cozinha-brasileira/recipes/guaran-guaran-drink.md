## Ingredients

- guaraná
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Scrape a portion of guaraná with a coarse grater
2. Place it in a glass with sugar
3. Pour warm or cold water over it according to taste or necessity
4. Drink with a spoon
5. This drink is very refreshing and serves well in great heat to prevent fever and to stop diarrhea
