## Ingredients

- folhas de repolho (cabbage leaves)
- sal (salt)
- pimenta (pepper)
- sumo de limão (lemon juice)
- gordura (fat)

## Directions

1. Take a portion of cabbage leaves and chop them very finely
2. Blanch them, and with a pan of hot fat on the fire, add the drained leaves
3. Add salt, pepper and a spoon of lemon juice
4. Boil for half an hour and serve
