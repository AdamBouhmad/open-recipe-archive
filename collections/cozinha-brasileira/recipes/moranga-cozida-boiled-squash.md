## Ingredients

- squash or pumpkin
- water
- salt
- lard
- vinegar
- onion

## Directions

1. Take a squash or pumpkin
2. Cut it into pieces
3. Remove the seeds and peel them
4. Boil the pieces in water and salt
5. When cooked, place them on a plate and serve with a sauce of melted lard, vinegar and chopped onion poured over top
