## Ingredients

- one spoon of wheat flour
- one spoon of butter
- three or four onions, chopped
- one spoon of vinegar
- French mustard

## Directions

1. Place in a pot one spoon of wheat flour and one spoon of butter
2. Heat until it takes on a russet color
3. Add three or four chopped onions
4. Continue heating until the flour becomes a reddish-brown color
5. Add one spoon of vinegar and French mustard
6. Mix everything together
7. Serve with cold pork loin or turkey
