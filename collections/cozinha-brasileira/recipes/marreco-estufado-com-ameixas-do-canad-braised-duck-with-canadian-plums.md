## Ingredients

- 1 duck
- Canadian plums, peeled and pitted, or preserved plums mixed with raisins and pieces of peeled quince
- half a pound of bacon cut into pieces
- two glasses of wine
- salt
- nutmeg
- cardamom
- cloves
- two dozen plums, peeled and pitted

## Directions

1. Take the duck and fill it with Canadian plums, peeled and pitted, or in their absence, with preserved plums mixed with raisins and pieces of peeled quince
2. Place the duck in a pan with half a pound of bacon cut into pieces, two glasses of wine, little salt, nutmeg, cardamom, cloves, and two dozen plums peeled and without pits
3. Seal the lid and cook over a regularly hot fire
4. After one hour, remove the duck and place it on a plate with the plums arranged around it
5. Strain the sauce, remove the fat, and serve it over the roast
