## Ingredients

- Armadillo
- soap
- sour orange or lemon
- one whole onion
- one head of garlic
- peppercorns from India
- half a bottle of white wine
- one quarter pound of wheat flour
- one chalice of white wine

## Directions

1. Remove the shell and odor from the armadillo, wash it with soap and sour orange or lemon
2. Wash it again in cold water and cut into pieces
3. Brown the pieces in fat
4. Add one whole onion, one head of garlic, peppercorns from India, and half a bottle of white wine
5. Braise once it begins to boil
6. When cooked, dissolve one quarter pound of wheat flour in one chalice of white wine and pour it over the armadillo, stirring a little to thicken
7. Serve with its gravy
