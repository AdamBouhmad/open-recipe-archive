## Ingredients

- mushrooms (carapicús)
- butter
- wheat flour
- juice of one lemon
- half a bottle of red wine
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Select and wash a portion of mushrooms (carapicús)
2. Melt two spoonfuls of butter, adding to them one spoonful of wheat flour, one glass of lemon juice, half a bottle of red wine, salt, and pepper
3. Add the mushrooms afterward, and when they have boiled a little, serve
