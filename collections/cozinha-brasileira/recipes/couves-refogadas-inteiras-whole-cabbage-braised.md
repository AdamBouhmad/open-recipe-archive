## Ingredients

- folhas de couves (cabbage leaves)
- gordura (fat)
- sal (salt)
- pimentas comarís (peppers)

## Directions

1. Select a portion of cabbage leaves, break them with your nails, wash them, and braise them in a spoon of fat
2. Add a little salt and peppers, mix, and add half a bottle of water
3. Let it boil a little longer and serve
