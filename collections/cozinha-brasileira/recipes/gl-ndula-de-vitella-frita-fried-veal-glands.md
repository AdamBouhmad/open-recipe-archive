## Ingredients

- veal glands
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- garlic
- wheat flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the gland for fifteen to twenty minutes in water
2. Let cool, then soak for two hours in a mixture of vinegar, salt, parsley, pepper, and garlic
3. Coat in wheat flour and fry in butter
