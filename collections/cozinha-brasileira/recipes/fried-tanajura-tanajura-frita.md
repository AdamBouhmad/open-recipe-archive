## Ingredients

- tanajuras (a type of ant)
- hot water
- fat for frying
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take a portion of tanajuras and scald them with hot water
2. Remove the abdomens
3. Fry them in fat
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
5. When well toasted, serve as a surprise dish
