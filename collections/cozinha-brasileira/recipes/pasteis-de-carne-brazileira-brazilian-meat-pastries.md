## Ingredients

- one pound of beef or mutton
- half a pound of bacon
- one onion
- salt
- plenty of cumin pepper
- parsley
- one cup of water
- one glass of brandy
- one glass of lemon juice
- olives
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mince one pound of beef or mutton with half a pound of bacon, one onion, salt, plenty of cumin pepper, and parsley
2. Sauté everything with one cup of water, one glass of brandy and one glass of lemon juice
3. When cooked, extend the dough for empada crust, and cutting into circles with a glass, place in the middle of each one a spoonful of the meat and some olives
4. Fold the edges and place the pastries in a pan with hot fat, leaving to fry until golden
5. Remove, cover with sugar and cinnamon and serve
