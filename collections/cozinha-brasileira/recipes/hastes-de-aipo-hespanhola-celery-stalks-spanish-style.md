## Ingredients

- celery stalks
- water
- salt
- two ounces of butter
- India pepper
- Spanish sauce
- one bottle of meat broth

## Directions

1. Cut the celery stalks, all the same size, leaving aside the root
2. Boil for twenty-five minutes in water and salt
3. Remove and place in cold water
4. Then allow to drain
5. Place in a casserole with two ounces of butter, India pepper, a portion of Spanish sauce, and one bottle of meat broth
6. Keep near the fire for twenty-five minutes
7. Place on a serving dish, pour the sauce over top
8. Serve
