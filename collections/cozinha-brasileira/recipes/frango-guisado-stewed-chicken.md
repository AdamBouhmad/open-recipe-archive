## Ingredients

- one chicken
- water
- one onion
- half pound of bacon
- salt
- parsley
- cloves
- pepper
- a little saffron
- vinegar
- a handful of bread crumb
- eight egg yolks
- two glasses of wine
- one spoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil water with cut onion, chopped bacon, salt, and parsley
2. When boiling, add the chicken, cloves, pepper, saffron, and vinegar
3. When the chicken is cooked, remove and cut it, leaving the bones aside
4. Strain the broth and reduce it
5. Mix with a handful of bread crumb, eight egg yolks, two glasses of wine, and one spoon of sugar
6. Return the chicken pieces to this sauce
7. Let it boil a little more and serve at the table
