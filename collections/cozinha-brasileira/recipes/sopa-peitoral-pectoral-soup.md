## Ingredients

- cow's hand
- water
- raisins
- honey
- fennel herb
- milk
- eggs

## Directions

1. Boil a cow's hand with plenty of water until cooked
2. Strain the broth
3. Add half a pound of raisins, six spoonfuls of honey and a little fennel herb to the broth
4. Let it boil until reduced to two bottles
5. Mix in a bottle of milk and three beaten eggs
6. Give it one more boil
7. Serve
