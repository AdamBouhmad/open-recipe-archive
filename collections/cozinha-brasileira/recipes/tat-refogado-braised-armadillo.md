## Ingredients

- one armadillo
- fat
- tomatoes
- salt
- peppers
- marjoram
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one cup of water
- one spoonful of vinegar
- one small spoonful of arrowroot

## Directions

1. Take an armadillo, singe it, and then place over coals to remove the shell easily
2. Remove the shell and clean
3. Cut off the head and feet and cut the rest into pieces
4. Braise in fat with tomatoes, salt, peppers, marjoram, parsley, and onion leaves
5. Add one cup of water, one spoonful of vinegar, and one small spoonful of arrowroot
6. Boil for half an hour and serve
