## Ingredients

- turkey pieces
- salt
- ground pepper
- bread crumbs
- egg yolks
- butter
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Take the turkey pieces, season with salt and ground pepper, coat in bread crumbs, then pass through egg yolks and fry in a little butter
2. When cooked, place on a plate and cover with a layer of grated cheese
3. Place in the oven and let take on good color and serve
