## Ingredients

- meat
- salt
- brown sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces
2. Scald and dry it and rub with salt
3. Hang it in the air and then place in layers between salt and brown sugar inside a barrel
4. Cover and keep stored
5. Use one quarter of sugar for six pounds of salt
