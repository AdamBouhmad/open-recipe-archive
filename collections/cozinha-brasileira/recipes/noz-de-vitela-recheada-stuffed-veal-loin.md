## Ingredients

- veal loin
- turkey or chicken meat
- bacon slices
- carrots
- white wine

## Directions

1. Beat the loin wrapped in cloth
2. Open it on one side and fill with chopped turkey or chicken meat
3. Sew the hole with thread
4. Place the prepared loin in a pan lined with bacon slices and carrots
5. Moisten with a glass of white wine
6. Cook over moderate heat
7. Serve with fresh vegetables
