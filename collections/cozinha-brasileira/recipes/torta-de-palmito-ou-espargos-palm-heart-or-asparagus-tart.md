## Ingredients

- portion of palm hearts or asparagus
- water and salt
- eight egg yolks
- half pound sugar
- lemon rinds
- juice of one lemon
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of palm hearts or asparagus in water and salt
2. When cooked, chop them very fine and mix with eight egg yolks beaten with half pound of sugar, lemon rinds and the juice of one lemon
3. Fill a portion of forms with this mass or fill just one form, with all being buttered
4. Take to the oven, bake well, place on a serving dish, cover with powdered sugar, and serve
