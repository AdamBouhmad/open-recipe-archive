## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- two ounces of fresh butter
- meat essence

## Directions

1. Take twelve eggs and two ounces of fresh butter
2. Beat them together
3. Add meat essence gradually
4. Add salt and pepper from India
5. Place in a casserole, stirring constantly with a spatula until of good consistency
6. Serve on a plate previously heated over coals
