## Ingredients

- onion
- chives
- chervil
- one clove of garlic
- pepper
- vinegar
- sweet olive oil

## Directions

1. Put in a plate onion, chives, chervil, one clove of garlic, all finely minced and pepper
2. Dilute with vinegar and sweet olive oil
3. Mix a little and stir to combine
