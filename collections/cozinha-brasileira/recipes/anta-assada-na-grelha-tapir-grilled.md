## Ingredients

- tapir meat
- salt
- pepper
- chopped onion
- parsley
- butter
- salad or burrage leaves
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into slices the thickness of a finger, sprinkle with salt, pepper, chopped onion and parsley
2. Beat it well with bread, pass it in butter, and roast it on the grill; serve it with salad or with finely chopped burrage leaves with salt and vinegar
