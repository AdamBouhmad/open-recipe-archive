## Ingredients

- leftover roasted kid
- carapicús
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- pitted olives
- butter
- bread

## Directions

1. Take the leftover roasted kid and chop it finely with carapicús, garlic, onion, parsley, and pitted olives
2. Fry them in a little butter
3. Place on slices of bread fried in butter
4. Put the plate in the oven
5. Serve hot
