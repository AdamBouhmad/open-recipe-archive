## Ingredients

- black beans
- water
- salt
- lard
- parsley
- onion
- chili peppers
- garlic
- hot water
- cassava flour
- onion leaves

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of beans in water and salt.
2. Once cooked, drain the broth.
3. Fry a piece of chopped lard with parsley, onion, chili peppers, salt and a clove of garlic.
4. Add the beans and stir, mashing.
5. Add a portion of hot water and cassava flour, stirring until it forms a half-firm paste.
6. Put on the plate, covering with a branch of parsley and chopped onion leaves, and pouring a half portion of fat on top.
7. Serve with pork loins, roasted suckling pig or fried sausages.
