## Ingredients

- young meat from irara, jaguar, or anteater
- lemon juice
- tomatoes, crushed
- comarí peppers
- garlic clove
- onion with cloves

## Directions

1. The meat of these three animals is prepared in the same way
2. The meat should be very young
3. After cleaning, soak it in lemon juice with crushed tomatoes, comarí peppers, one garlic clove, and one onion studded with cloves
4. Leave the meat for twenty-four hours to absorb the seasoning flavors and become more tender
5. Pass it in hot fat and place it on the spit
6. Roast over moderate fire
7. Serve
