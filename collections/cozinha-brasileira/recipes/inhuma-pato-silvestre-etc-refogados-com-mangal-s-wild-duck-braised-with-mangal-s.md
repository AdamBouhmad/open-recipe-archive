## Ingredients

- two spoons of wheat flour
- four spoons of butter
- one onion, chopped
- one bird, cut into pieces
- a portion of mangalós, cooked in water and salt
- one glass of vinegar
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one sprig of marjoram

## Directions

1. Braise two spoons of wheat flour with four spoons of butter until the flour is golden
2. Add one chopped onion and the bird cut into pieces
3. Stir and add a portion of cooked mangalós, one glass of vinegar, parsley, onion leaves and one sprig of marjoram
4. Let it simmer for one hour over moderate fire
5. Serve
