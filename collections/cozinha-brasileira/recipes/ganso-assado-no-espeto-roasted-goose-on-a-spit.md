## Ingredients

- one goose
- salt
- onion juice

## Directions

1. After plucking and cleaning the goose, rub it with salt and onion juice.
2. Roast it initially over a very lively fire to drain the excess fat.
3. Then roast for one hour over moderate fire to cook it.
4. Finally roast for a quarter hour over a very active fire to take on good color.
5. Serve with its sauce after removing the fat.
