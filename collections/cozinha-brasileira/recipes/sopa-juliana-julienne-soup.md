## Ingredients

- carrots
- turnips
- onions
- cabbage
- sorrel
- butter
- peas
- green beans
- parsley leaves
- salt
- water
- fine cornmeal or wheat flour
- beef butter

## Directions

1. Cut carrots, turnips, onions, cabbage, and sorrel into fine long strips
2. Place on the fire with a little butter and sauté
3. Add peas, green beans, parsley leaves, necessary salt, and a little water
4. Let boil until everything is well cooked
5. Add fine cornmeal or wheat flour fried in beef butter, diluted in sufficient water to make soup
6. Give it another boil and send to the table
7. Note: it can also be made without wheat flour or cornmeal, which is actually better
