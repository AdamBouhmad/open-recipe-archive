## Ingredients

- young turkey
- juniper berries
- cumin seeds
- strong vinegar
- cloves
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaves
- peeled apples
- parsley
- marjoram
- bacon

## Directions

1. Take a young turkey and fill it with juniper berries and cumin seeds.
2. Soak it in strong vinegar with cloves, salt, pepper and bay leaves.
3. After twelve hours, remove the juniper berries and cumin seeds from the soaking liquid and replace them with peeled apples without seeds and split, with a few sprigs of parsley and marjoram.
4. Lard the breast with fine strips of bacon and roast it in the oven in a pan lined with bacon slices.
5. When roasted, serve it with its sauce.
