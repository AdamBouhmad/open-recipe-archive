## Ingredients

- one quarter of cheese
- one glass of white wine
- one spoonful of butter
- salt
- sorrel
- a pinch of coriander seeds
- six beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Grate one quarter of cheese.
2. Place in a saucepan with one glass of white wine, one spoonful of butter, salt, sorrel, and a pinch of coriander seeds.
3. Place over fire and stir until the cheese dissolves completely.
4. Remove from heat and let cool.
5. Add six beaten eggs.
6. Return to fire and simmer this mixture, stirring until cooked.
7. Place on a plate and serve.
