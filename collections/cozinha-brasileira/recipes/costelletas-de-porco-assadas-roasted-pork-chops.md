## Ingredients

- pork chops
- sweet olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- bay leaf
- clove
- pickled cucumbers

## Directions

1. Marinate the pork chops in a mixture of sweet olive oil, salt, pepper, parsley, bay leaf, and clove for three days.
2. Fry them in a skillet or roast them on a grill.
3. Top with a Roberto sauce, with preserved cucumbers cut into rounds.
4. Alternatively, use a spicy sauce, a tomato sauce, or a sorrel pulp sauce.
