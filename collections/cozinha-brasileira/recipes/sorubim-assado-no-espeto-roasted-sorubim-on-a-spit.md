## Ingredients

- one fish
- thin strips of lard
- salt
- cassava flour
- lard
- lemon juice
- one glass of vinegar
- one egg yolk
- mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the entrails and wash the fish
2. Lard it with thin strips of lard and rub with salt
3. Thread onto a spit and roast over moderate fire, moistening with lard and lemon juice
4. When cooked, dust with cassava flour, moisten with broth and turn on the spit a few more times
5. Serve after adding one glass of vinegar, one egg yolk and a little mustard sauce
