## Ingredients

- onions
- one root of cooked celery
- vinegar
- sweet oil or melted fat
- salt
- ground pepper
- mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Cut a portion of onions into thin slices
2. Do the same with one cooked and peeled celery root
3. Place in a plate and pour over vinegar, sweet oil or melted fat, salt, ground pepper and mustard sauce
4. Serve
