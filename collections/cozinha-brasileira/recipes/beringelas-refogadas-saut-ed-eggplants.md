## Ingredients

- eggplants
- water
- salt
- wheat flour
- butter
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the eggplants in two parts
2. Boil them in water and salt
3. Drain them and coat them in wheat flour
4. Place them in a casserole with butter and vinegar
5. Put them in the oven and when browned, serve
