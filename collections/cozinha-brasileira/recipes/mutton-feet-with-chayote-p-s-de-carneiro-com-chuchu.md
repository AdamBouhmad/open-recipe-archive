## Ingredients

- mutton feet
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- bacon pieces
- chayotes, peeled and cut
- egg yolks
- orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut the mutton feet into pieces and cook with salt, parsley, onion leaves and pieces of bacon
2. When almost cooked, add peeled and cut chayotes
3. Let boil a little until the broth reduces
4. Thicken the broth with two beaten egg yolks, adding a little orange juice
