## Ingredients

- one pork roast from wild pig
- bacon tempered with salt and pepper for larding
- one bottle wine or water
- parsley
- onion
- nutmeg
- one dozen terra bananas peeled

## Directions

1. Lard the pork roast with bacon tempered with salt and pepper.
2. Roast it for twenty minutes.
3. Remove and place in a pot with one bottle of wine or water, parsley, onion, nutmeg, and one dozen peeled terra bananas.
4. Boil until cooked.
5. Remove and serve with the strained sauce.
