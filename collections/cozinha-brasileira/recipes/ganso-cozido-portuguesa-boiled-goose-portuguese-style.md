## Ingredients

- one goose
- one bottle of white wine
- one cup of vinegar
- one bowl of meat broth
- salt
- bay leaves
- parsley
- one onion
- two cloves

## Directions

1. Cut the goose into pieces and blanch them in hot water.
2. When cooled, remove and place to cook in one bottle of white wine, one cup of vinegar, one bowl of meat broth, salt, bay leaves, parsley, one onion, two cloves.
3. When cooked, remove the meat and reduce the broth.
4. Serve with this sauce.
