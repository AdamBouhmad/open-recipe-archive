## Ingredients

- half a plate of fine cornmeal
- water to moisten it
- a little salt
- one spoonful of sugar
- a little fennel

## Directions

1. Moisten half a plate of fine cornmeal with as much water as needed to moisten it without binding it
2. Mix in a little salt, one spoonful of sugar, and a little fennel
3. Place everything in a cuscuzeiro, pressing the dough a little, and with this cover a deep pot, with the cuscuzeiro entering the pot without reaching the water that is in the pot
4. Seal all the gaps that remain between the pot and the cuscuzeiro with a little cornmeal paste
5. Let it boil and when cooked, turn the cuscuzeiro upside down over a plate so the dough can come out whole, and serve
