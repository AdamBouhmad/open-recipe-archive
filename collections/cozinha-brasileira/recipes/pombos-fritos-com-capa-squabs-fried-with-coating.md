## Ingredients

- squab halves
- lemon juice
- salt
- garlic juice
- wheat flour
- egg yolks
- sauce or salad

## Directions

1. Take the halves of squabs and flatten them with a palmatoria
2. Soak for three to four hours in lemon juice, salt and garlic juice
3. Wrap in wheat flour
4. Pass through egg yolks
5. Cover again with wheat flour
6. Fry on both sides in fat
7. Serve with some sauce or salad
