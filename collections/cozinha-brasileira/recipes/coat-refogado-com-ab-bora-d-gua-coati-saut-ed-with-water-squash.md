## Ingredients

- half a dozen tomatoes
- two spoons of fat
- one chopped onion
- parsley
- a portion of water squash, chopped in small pieces
- one coatí, cut in pieces
- one cup of water
- the same amount of sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Pass half a dozen tomatoes through two spoons of fat, one chopped onion and parsley; then add a portion of water squash chopped into small pieces and one coatí cut in pieces
2. Add afterwards one cup of water and the same amount of sour orange juice
