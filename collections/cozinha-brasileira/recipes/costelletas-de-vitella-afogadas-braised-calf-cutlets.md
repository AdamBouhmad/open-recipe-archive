## Ingredients

- calf cutlets
- butter
- broth or water
- onion
- parsley
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the cutlets so each piece has a bone not too long
2. Fry in a little butter on both sides until they take color
3. Add some broth or water, onion, parsley, lemon juice, salt and pepper
4. Let cook
5. When cooked, remove them and strain the broth which is thickened with egg yolks to serve with the cutlets
