## Ingredients

- legs of four chickens
- half a pound of ham, chopped
- a little veal
- onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- two cups of meat broth
- two egg yolks
- lemon slices
- one glass of Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Cut the legs of four chickens, clean them and remove the bones
2. Stuff them with chopped half a pound of ham, a little veal, onion, parsley, salt and pepper
3. Cook them to take their primitive form
4. When browned, add two cups of meat broth thickened with two egg yolks, some lemon slices and one glass of Madeira wine
5. Simmer near the fire and serve afterwards
