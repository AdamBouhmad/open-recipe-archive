## Ingredients

- monkey
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- bacon strips
- butter or fat
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Pluck the monkey and clean it well.
2. Rub with salt, pepper, and lemon juice, leaving it in this seasoning for two to three hours.
3. Lard with fine strips of bacon and roast on a spit, wrapped in paper spread with butter or fat.
4. When cooked, remove the paper and roll in bread crumbs.
5. Continue roasting, moistening with butter, until it takes on good color, and serve.
