## Ingredients

- cleaned fish
- paper
- butter
- parsley
- chopped onion
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and wrap them in well-buttered paper
2. Cook them on the grill
3. When roasted, remove the paper and fill the belly with a little butter mixed with chopped parsley, onion, salt and pepper
4. Place the fish on a plate and keep them in a warm place until serving
