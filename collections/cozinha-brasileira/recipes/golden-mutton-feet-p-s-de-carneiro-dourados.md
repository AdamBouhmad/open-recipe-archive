## Ingredients

- mutton feet
- water
- salt
- cooked and chopped ham
- onion
- parsley
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the feet in water and salt
2. Remove the bones and stuff with cooked and chopped ham mixed with one chopped onion and parsley
3. Butter the dish in which they are to be served, placing at the bottom a layer of leftover ham
4. Place the stuffed feet on this layer, covered with the rest of the chopped ham
5. Sprinkle with a layer of breadcrumbs and pour beaten egg yolks over all
6. Bake in the oven until well browned, and serve with salad
