## Ingredients

- partridge, quail, or similar bird
- bitter orange juice
- salt
- peppers
- half a garlic clove
- parsley
- onion leaves
- butter
- onion
- cornmeal or cassava flour

## Directions

1. Cut the birds into pieces and soak in bitter orange juice with salt, peppers, half a garlic clove, parsley, and onion leaves
2. After six hours, dry the pieces, fry them in butter with chopped onion
3. Dust with cornmeal or cassava flour and serve
