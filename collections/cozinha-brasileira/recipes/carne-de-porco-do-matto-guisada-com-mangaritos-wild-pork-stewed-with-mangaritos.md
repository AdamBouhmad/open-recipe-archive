## Ingredients

- one piece of wild pork
- water
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- garlic
- mangaritos, peeled
- four tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Take one piece of wild pork and boil it in water with salt, peppers, parsley, garlic and a portion of peeled mangaritos.
2. When the meat is cooked, remove it and cut it into small pieces, and the mangaritos into slices.
3. Fry both the meat and the mangaritos in four tablespoons of butter and serve.
