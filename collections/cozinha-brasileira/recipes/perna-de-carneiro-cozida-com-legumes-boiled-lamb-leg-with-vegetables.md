## Ingredients

- lamb leg
- water
- peas
- green beans
- squash shoots
- salt
- parsley
- clove
- onion leaves
- bacon or ham

## Directions

1. Beat the leg well after removing the fat and trimming the bone
2. Scald it in hot water, letting it boil five minutes in this water
3. Remove and place in cold water, leaving it until it cools
4. Place again to cook with a portion of fresh peas, beans or squash shoots, salt, parsley, one clove, onion leaves year-round and a piece of lean bacon or ham
5. When all is cooked, separate the broth and evaporate until reduced to two cups of liquid
6. Then return the leg to it, letting it cook over coals and turning it until it begins to stick to the bottom of the pan and is a good color
7. Serve with the vegetables
