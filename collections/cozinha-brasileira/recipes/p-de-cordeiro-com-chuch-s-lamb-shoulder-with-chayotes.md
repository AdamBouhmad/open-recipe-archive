## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- bacon
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- water

## Directions

1. Remove the bone from the shoulder
2. Lard the meat with bacon seasoned with pepper, salt, and nutmeg
3. Roll the shoulder to form an elongated shape
4. Cook in water
