## Ingredients

- Lamb tails
- Hot water
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Eggs
- Chopped onions
- Fat

## Directions

1. Scald the tails in hot water
2. Cut into pieces
3. Season with salt and ground pepper
4. Wrap in beaten eggs and chopped onions
5. Fry in fat
6. Drizzle beaten eggs on top
7. Let them fry as well
8. Serve
