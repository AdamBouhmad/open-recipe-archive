## Ingredients

- one chicken, cut into pieces
- half a pound of chopped bacon
- one bottle of water
- a portion of carapicús, chosen and boiled beforehand
- salt
- parsley
- comari pepper
- nutmeg
- a little crumb of bread
- juice of one lemon
- cornmeal mush or cassava flour paste

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into pieces and place them in the pan with half a pound of chopped bacon.
2. Fry for a while, stirring constantly until the chicken is browned.
3. Then add a bottle of water, a portion of carapicús that have been chosen and boiled beforehand, salt, parsley, comari pepper, nutmeg and a little crumb of bread.
4. Let everything boil until cooked.
5. Before serving, add the juice of one lemon, and serve with cornmeal mush or cassava flour paste.
