## Ingredients

- lentils
- meat broth
- potatoes
- salsify root

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of lentils after washing and selecting them well
2. Once cooked, pass half of them through a sieve, crumbling them
3. Add the unsieved lentils to the sieved ones
4. Add meat broth as needed
5. Add potatoes and salsify root
6. Cook until done
