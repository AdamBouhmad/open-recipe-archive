## Ingredients

- butter
- wheat flour
- egg yolks
- lemon rind
- a little sugar
- ground cinnamon
- nutmeg
- white wine

## Directions

1. Knead in the same proportion butter, wheat flour, egg yolks, and lemon rind, as in lemon sauce
2. Add a little sugar, ground cinnamon and nutmeg, and as much white wine as is necessary for the sauce
3. Put on the fire and leave to boil a little, stirring always
4. Serve for the same dishes as lemon sauce, and also for puddings and pasta
