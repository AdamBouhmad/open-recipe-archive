## Ingredients

- posta (steak) of tuna or crevalle jack
- thin strips of sea eels (conger or moray)
- melted butter
- vinegar
- sliced onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- bay leaves

## Directions

1. Lard a fish steak with thin strips of sea eels
2. Thread onto a spit and roast over moderate fire
3. Continuously baste with melted butter mixed with vinegar, sliced onion, parsley, salt, pepper, cloves and bay leaves
4. Serve with the sauce when the fish is cooked
