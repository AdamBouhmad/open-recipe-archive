## Ingredients

- veal kidneys
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- herbs
- butter
- grated cheese
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut and blanch the kidneys, then cook them in a little white wine with salt, pepper, a bundle of herbs and a little butter until the liquid is almost reduced
2. Place on a plate covered with a layer of grated cheese, also sprinkling some sugar mixed with cinnamon on top, and finally add a little melted butter
