## Ingredients

- Lamb quarter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter

## Directions

1. Remove the kidney from the quarter
2. Beat well
3. Wash in many cold waters
4. Rub finally with salt and pepper
5. Spit it to be roasted, moistening it with butter
