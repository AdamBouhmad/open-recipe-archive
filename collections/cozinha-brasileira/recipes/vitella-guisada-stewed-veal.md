## Ingredients

- veal piece
- seasoning mixture
- carrots
- potatoes
- veal foot
- water
- wine

## Directions

1. Take a larded veal piece and place in a casserole with a seasoning mixture, some carrots, potatoes and veal foot cut into pieces
2. Cook over coals with equal parts water and wine, keeping the pan well covered
3. Remove the veal piece, strain the substance from the sauce that remains at the bottom of the pan and serve the meat with this sauce on top
