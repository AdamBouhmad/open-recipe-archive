## Ingredients

- fish (timbré and curvina)
- water
- parsley
- onion
- celery root
- salt
- pepper
- cassava flour
- eggs
- melted fat
- one spoon of fat
- one chopped onion
- tomatoes
- mustard flour
- one cup of water
- one glass of boiled vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of water with parsley, onion, a celery root, salt and pepper; when hot, place the fish in this broth; allow to boil several times, then remove and skin the fish with the scales
2. Dust with cassava flour, pass through beaten eggs, toast with the flour and pour over the fish a portion of melted fat, and roast the fish over a grill on both sides
3. On the other hand, fry one spoon of fat, one chopped onion, a portion of tomatoes, salt, pepper and a little mustard flour; stir and add one cup of water and one glass of boiled vinegar over the fish, and serve
