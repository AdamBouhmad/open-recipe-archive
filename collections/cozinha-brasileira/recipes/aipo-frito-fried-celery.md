## Ingredients

- celery stalks
- water
- salt
- butter
- wheat flour
- beef meat broth
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the celery stalks as in the preceding article; give them one boil and then allow them to cool and drain
2. In another pan, place in a casserole a piece of butter with a little wheat flour; place over the fire, stirring constantly without allowing to brown
3. Pour a portion of beef meat broth, place the celery stalks, and allow them to cook
4. Remove the stalks and allow to drain
5. Pass them one by one through a liquid batter of wheat flour, and thus moistened they are fried in butter
6. Place on a serving dish, sprinkling a little sugar over top
7. Serve
