## Ingredients

- pieces of duck
- wheat flour
- egg yolks
- butter
- squash or pumpkin
- water
- salt
- fat

## Directions

1. Take the pieces of duck, wrap them in wheat flour, pass them through egg yolks, and wrap them again in wheat flour
2. Fry them in butter
3. Separately, boil a squash or pumpkin with the center removed in water and salt
4. When cooked, remove from water, scrape off the skin, pass the flesh through a sieve, and sauté it with two spoonfuls of fat
5. Place this purée on the plate, put the fried duck pieces on top and serve, pouring over it the butter in which the duck was fried
