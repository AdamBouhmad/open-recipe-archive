## Ingredients

- one dozen small birds
- white wine
- salt
- sage
- onion
- garlic
- cloves
- pepper
- half pound fresh bacon
- four cooked eggs
- bird offal
- peppers

## Directions

1. Clean a dozen small birds, roast them a little on the spit
2. Then stew them in white wine with salt, sage, onion, garlic, cloves and pepper
3. When cooked, place them in a pastry box, interlayering with a chopped mixture made of half pound fresh bacon, four cooked eggs, the birds' offal and peppers
4. Moisten everything with the wine in which the birds were cooked, strain and reduce
5. Bake in the oven and serve hot
