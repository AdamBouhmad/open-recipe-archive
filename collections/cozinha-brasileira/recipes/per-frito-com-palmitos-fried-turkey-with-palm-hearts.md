## Ingredients

- turkey
- salt
- butter
- onion, chopped
- palm heart
- water
- breadcrumbs
- ground pepper

## Directions

1. Take the turkey, cut it into pieces, salt them, and fry them in butter with a chopped onion
2. Place on a plate over a palm heart cut into long pieces, cooked in water and salt, and covered with a layer of breadcrumbs
3. Sprinkle everything with ground pepper and pour over it all the butter in which the turkey was fried, and serve
