## Ingredients

- half a pound of bread crumb
- hot milk
- one spoon of bread
- one dozen egg yolks
- three spoons of butter
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- cloves
- a little salt
- half a pound of sugar
- four egg whites
- one glass of Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Soak half a pound of bread crumb in hot milk
2. When well soaked, remove and mash with a spoon
3. Mix with one dozen egg yolks, three spoons of butter, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, a little salt and half a pound of sugar
4. Add four egg whites beaten with one glass of Madeira wine
5. Place this mixture in a napkin
6. Put in a pot with water to boil
7. Serve with an appropriate sauce
8. Alternatively, it can be placed in a buttered mold and baked in the oven for one hour
