## Ingredients

- veal rump
- butter
- beef broth
- parsley
- celery root
- salt
- pepper
- rice
- fresh peas

## Directions

1. Fry a veal rump in cow butter until it is of good color.
2. Then add to it a bottle of beef broth, a little parsley, a celery root, salt and pepper.
3. After it has boiled for one hour, remove the meat, add to it half a pound of rice, and when this is cooked, add to the rump a bowl of fresh peas, and let it finish cooking.
