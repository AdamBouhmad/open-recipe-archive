## Ingredients

- a portion of fava beans
- water
- salt
- one quarter pound of chopped bacon
- one chopped onion
- parsley
- vinegar or sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Take a portion of fava beans
2. Soak in cold water for some hours
3. Remove and put to cook in water and salt
4. After boiling, remove the pan from the heat and strain the water
5. Fry one quarter pound of chopped bacon with one chopped onion, parsley and salt
6. Add the fava beans
7. Stir and add the water in which the fava beans were cooked
8. Let boil until dried and serve
9. If desired, add one spoonful of vinegar or sour orange juice at this time
