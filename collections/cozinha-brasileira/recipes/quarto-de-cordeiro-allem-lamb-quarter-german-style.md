## Ingredients

- lamb quarter
- hot water
- fine salt
- ground pepper
- chopped parsley
- fine strips of bacon
- wheat flour
- egg yolks
- cow butter

## Directions

1. Scald the lamb quarter in hot water and sprinkle with fine salt, ground pepper, and chopped parsley
2. Cover with fine strips of bacon
3. Roll it in wheat flour and cover with beaten egg yolks
4. Put it in a large casserole with cow butter
5. Let it cook over gentle heat for half an hour
6. Half an hour before serving, add a layer of bread crumbs
7. Let it roast for another half hour over a lively fire
