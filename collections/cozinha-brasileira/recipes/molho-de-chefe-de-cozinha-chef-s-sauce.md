## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- finely chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- chopped chives
- juice of one lemon
- juice of green grapes
- juice of tomatoes
- or a little vinegar

## Directions

1. Place a little fresh butter on a plate with finely chopped parsley, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
2. Add chopped chives if desired
3. Add the juice of one lemon, juice of green grapes or tomatoes, or a little vinegar
