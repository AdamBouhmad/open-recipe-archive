## Ingredients

- pig's ears
- lentils
- guandú
- mangalós
- chickpeas
- water
- onions
- carrots

## Directions

1. Pass the ears through a flame and clean them.
2. Cook them with lentils, guandú, mangalós, and chickpeas in water with onions and carrots.
3. Make a pulp from some of these grains and place it on top.
