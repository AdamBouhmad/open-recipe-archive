## Ingredients

- one fat bird (arara, papagaio, periquito, etc.)
- salt
- hot pepper (pimenta comarí)
- milk cream
- two egg yolks
- one spoonful of fine cornmeal
- cinnamon
- one cup of milk

## Directions

1. Take one fat bird and rub it inside and outside with salt and hot pepper.
2. Spit it and roast it over moderate heat, basting it with milk cream.
3. When roasted, pour over it two egg yolks mashed with one spoonful of fine cornmeal, a little salt, and cinnamon, diluted in one cup of milk.
4. When the bird is cooked, serve with some salad or compote.
