## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- one quarter of powdered sugar
- some lemon zest
- three spoonfuls of milk cream
- egg whites
- a little fine salt
- a little melted butter
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Take eight egg yolks and beat them strongly with one quarter of powdered sugar, some lemon zest and three spoonfuls of milk cream
2. Then add the egg whites with a little fine salt
3. Beat well
4. Fry the mixture in a saucepan with a little melted butter
5. When fried, place on a plate
6. Cover with powdered sugar and having passed a hot iron over it, serve
