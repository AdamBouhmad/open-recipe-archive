## Ingredients

- one or two dozen catfish
- salt
- wheat flour
- fat for frying
- one cup of white wine
- a little chopped parsley
- one onion
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take one or two dozen catfish, open them, remove the entrails, wash well and rub with salt
2. Roll them in wheat flour and fry in fat
3. Once fried on both sides, add one cup of white wine, a little chopped parsley, and one cut onion
4. Let them boil for a quarter of an hour
5. Mix the broth with two egg yolks and serve
