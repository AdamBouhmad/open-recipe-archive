## Ingredients

- Ray fish
- Water
- Salt
- Parsley
- Onions
- Cloves
- Peppers
- Fat
- Tapioca flour
- Sour orange juice
- Broth

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the fish
2. Boil with water, salt, parsley, onions, cloves and peppers
3. When cooked, remove and sauté in fat
4. Dust with tapioca flour and moisten with sour orange juice
5. Add the strained and reduced broth
6. Let boil a little more and serve
