## Ingredients

- venison loin
- salt

## Directions

1. Those who appreciate game do not like venison meat with many side dishes and seasonings
2. Only rub the loin with salt and roast it on a spit over a moderate fire to cook slowly and take on good color
3. Eat it, squeezing lemon juice over it
