## Ingredients

- fish (timbré)
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- bay leaves
- water squash
- fat
- fish broth

## Directions

1. Clean and scale a fish; boil in water, salt, parsley, onion leaves and bay leaves; when cooked, remove the fish
2. On the other hand, sauté a portion of peeled water squash (removing the seeds) finely chopped in two spoons of fat with necessary salt; stir, and when hot add the strained fish broth, let it boil until the broth reduces and serve
