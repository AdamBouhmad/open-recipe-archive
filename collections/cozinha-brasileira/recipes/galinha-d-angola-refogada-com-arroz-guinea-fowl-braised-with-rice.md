## Ingredients

- one guinea fowl
- three spoonfuls of fat
- half a plate of chosen and washed rice
- half a bottle of water
- salt
- parsley leaves
- onion leaves
- half a dozen tomatoes

## Directions

1. Braise the bird in three spoonfuls of fat
2. Remove it, and pour into the same very hot fat half a plate of chosen and washed rice; stir and add half a bottle of water, salt, parsley and onion leaves, half a dozen tomatoes and the bird cut into pieces
3. Let it boil near the fire until the water dries up, and serve
