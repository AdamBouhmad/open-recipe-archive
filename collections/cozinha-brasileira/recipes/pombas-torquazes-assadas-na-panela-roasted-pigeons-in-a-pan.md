## Ingredients

- pigeons
- fat
- salt
- parsley
- bread crumbs
- borage leaves
- vinegar
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Braise the pigeons in fat and turn them to fry on all sides
2. Sprinkle with salt, parsley, a handful of bread crumbs, and cover the pan
3. Let it finish boiling over coals and serve with a sauce made of chopped borage leaves, salt, vinegar and olive oil
