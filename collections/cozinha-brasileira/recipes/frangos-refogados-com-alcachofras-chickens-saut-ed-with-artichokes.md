## Ingredients

- pieces of two chickens
- fat for frying
- one spoon of wheat flour
- one bottle of water
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- one onion, cut
- artichoke bottoms

## Directions

1. Fry the pieces of two chickens in fat.
2. When browned on both sides, remove them and add one spoon of wheat flour to the fat.
3. Stir and immediately add one bottle of water, salt, pepper, nutmeg, one cut onion and some artichoke bottoms and the fried chickens.
