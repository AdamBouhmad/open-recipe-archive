## Ingredients

- two veal kidneys
- half a pound of ground crackers
- one quarter of sugar
- six beaten eggs
- a little salt
- one cup of milk

## Directions

1. Roast two veal kidneys
2. When cold, chop them very finely
3. Mix with half a pound of ground crackers, one quarter of sugar, six beaten eggs, a little salt and one cup of milk
4. Boil everything well in a saucepan
5. Place in a buttered mold
6. Bake in the oven and serve hot
