## Ingredients

- Fresh pork loin
- Sweet olive oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Onions
- Cloves
- Bay leaf
- Hot pepper sauce
- Butter
- Onions for stew
- Wheat flour
- Meat broth
- White wine

## Directions

1. Marinate a fresh pork loin in sweet olive oil for three days with salt, pepper, parsley, onions, cloves, and bay leaf
2. Skewer it and when ready, serve with a hot pepper sauce
3. Alternatively, roast it in a casserole with a little butter, adding an onion
4. Serve with an onion stew made as follows: cut onions into slices, pass them over the fire with a piece of butter until almost cooked, add a pinch of wheat flour and stir until they take on a good color, moisten with meat broth, white wine, salt and Indian pepper, letting it boil until the onions are cooked and no sauce remains
