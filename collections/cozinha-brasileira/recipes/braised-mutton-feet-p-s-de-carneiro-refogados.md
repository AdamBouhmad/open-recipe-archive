## Ingredients

- eight mutton feet, cleaned and scalded
- salt
- one spoonful of vinegar
- small piece of bacon
- cabbage

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned and scalded feet in a pan with salt, one spoonful of vinegar and a small piece of bacon
2. Let cook on moderate heat with cabbage cut in four pieces
3. When cooked, separate the broth and reduce to two cups
4. Thicken with two spoonfuls of fine cornmeal and two egg yolks
5. Place the cabbage on a platter around the feet, pour the sauce over them, and serve
