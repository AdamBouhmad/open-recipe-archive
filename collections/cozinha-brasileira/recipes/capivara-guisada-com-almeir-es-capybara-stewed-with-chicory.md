## Ingredients

- capybara meat
- water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- ginger
- cloves
- gordura
- onion
- lemon juice
- chicory

## Directions

1. Take a piece of capybara meat and boil it in water with salt, pepper, parsley, onion leaves, ginger, and cloves
2. When cooked, sauté it in two spoonfuls of fat with one chopped onion
3. Add a glass of lemon juice and a cup of water
4. Leave it to boil for another half hour
5. Serve over a plate of chicory that has been scalded, chopped, and sautéed in one spoonful of fat with a cup of water and salt; leave to boil a little longer and serve
