## Ingredients

- one pound of mutton, chopped
- half a pound of bacon
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- one onion
- three cooked eggs
- one chicken
- fat
- two cups of water
- onion leaves
- one glass of wine
- mangoes or taro root

## Directions

1. Chop one pound of mutton with half a pound of bacon, parsley, salt, pepper, one onion, and three cooked eggs
2. Stuff and truss the chicken with this mixture
3. Braise it in a little fat, turning it until browned
4. Add two cups of water with salt, pepper, onion leaves, and one glass of wine
5. Let it simmer over moderate heat for about one hour
6. Serve with fried mangoes or stewed taro root
