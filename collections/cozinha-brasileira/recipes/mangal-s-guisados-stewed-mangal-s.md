## Ingredients

- mangalós
- water and salt
- a piece of chopped bacon
- a little water
- a little sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Boil the mangalós in water and salt
2. When cooked, drain the water; fry a piece of chopped bacon and add the mangalós
3. Add a little water and a little sour orange juice and serve
