## Ingredients

- pea purée
- cups of milk cream
- two spoons of sugar
- breadcrumbs
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Take the pea purée, fry in cups of milk cream with two spoons of sugar
2. Boil over coals, stirring until it hardens
3. Put on a plate, and having covered with breadcrumbs and melted butter, serve
