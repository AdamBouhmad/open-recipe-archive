## Ingredients

- one or two turtle eggs
- salt
- ground peppers
- fat
- one spoon of cassava flour

## Directions

1. Break one or two turtle eggs, beat well with a fork; add salt and ground peppers, pour into a pan with fat, fry, mixing in one spoon of cassava flour, and serve
