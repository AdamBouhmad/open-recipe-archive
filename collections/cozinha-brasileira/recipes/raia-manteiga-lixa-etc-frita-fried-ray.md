## Ingredients

- Very young rays
- Wheat flour
- Butter

## Directions

1. Take very young rays and clean them
2. Cut lengthwise
3. Remove the tail and head
4. Dust with wheat flour
5. Fry in butter
6. Serve with the butter poured on top
