## Ingredients

- carapicús (mushrooms)
- leite (milk)
- cebola (onion)
- pimenta (pepper)
- sal (salt)
- manteiga (butter)
- farinha de trigo (wheat flour)
- nata (cream)
- ovo (egg)

## Directions

1. Blanch a portion of carapicús (mushrooms), let them cool and drain them
2. In a pot, bring milk to a simmer with a diced onion, pepper, and salt
3. Once cooked, add a spoon of butter mixed with a spoon of wheat flour, and a cup of cream with a beaten egg mixed in
4. Let them simmer a little longer and serve
