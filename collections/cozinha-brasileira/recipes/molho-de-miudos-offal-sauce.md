## Ingredients

- onion slices
- pieces of beef knuckle
- pork rind pieces
- chopped carrots
- garlic
- cloves
- water

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of a pot with onion slices, pieces of beef knuckle, pork rind pieces, chopped carrots, garlic and cloves
2. Add a little water
3. Keep near the fire, well covered and over high heat until well cooked
4. Add more water
5. Remove the fat, skim and strain through a cloth
