## Ingredients

- Lamb neck meat
- Cold water
- Salt
- Parsley
- Onion leaves
- Bread crumbs
- Melted butter
- Spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the neck meat into slices
2. Place over fire with cold water, salt, parsley, and onion leaves
3. Boil for half an hour
4. Remove and wrap with bread crumbs
5. Cover with a little melted butter
6. Roast on the grill
7. Serve with spicy sauce
