## Ingredients

- wheat flour
- 1 spoon of fat
- meat broth
- finely chopped parsley leaves
- a little vinegar
- 1 spoon of spirits in which preserved peppercorn is kept

## Directions

1. Heat wheat flour and 1 spoon of fat in a saucepan until the flour takes on color
2. Add meat broth and finely chopped parsley leaves
3. Add a little vinegar and 1 spoon of spirits in which preserved peppercorn is kept
4. This sauce serves both for meat and for fish
