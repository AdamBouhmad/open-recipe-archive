## Ingredients

- one clean lizard
- hot vinegar
- fat
- one cup of water
- half a dozen tomatoes
- salt
- parsley
- peppers
- one chopped onion
- one clove of garlic
- two egg yolks
- one cup of white wine

## Directions

1. Take a clean lizard and scald it in a little hot vinegar
2. Cut it into pieces and stew in fat, adding one cup of water, half a dozen tomatoes, salt, parsley, peppers, a chopped onion, and a clove of garlic
3. Leave to boil over regular heat for one hour
4. Incorporate the broth with two egg yolks dissolved in a cup of white wine and serve
