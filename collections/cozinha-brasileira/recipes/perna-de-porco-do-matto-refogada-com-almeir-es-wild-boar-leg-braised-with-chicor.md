## Ingredients

- one wild boar leg
- fat
- one cup of white wine
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- parsley
- chicory

## Directions

1. Braise the leg in a little fat
2. Add one cup of white wine, salt, pepper, cut onions, and parsley
3. Let braise well covered for two hours
4. Separately, boil chopped chicory in water and salt
5. Remove and braise the chicory with a little fat
6. Add the meat broth to the chicory
7. Let boil a little and serve with the wild boar leg
