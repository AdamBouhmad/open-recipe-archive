## Ingredients

- piece of meat from irara, jaguar, or anteater
- two bowls water
- one cup vinegar
- one cup brandy
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- onion leaves
- ginger
- garlic clove
- two cloves
- dozen tomatoes
- three spoons fat
- three egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take a piece of meat and boil it in two bowls of water, one cup of vinegar, one cup of brandy, salt, peppers, parsley, onion leaves, ginger, one garlic clove, two cloves, and a dozen tomatoes
2. When cooked, remove the meat and brown it in three spoons of fat
3. Add the broth reduced to four cups and thickened with three egg yolks
4. Serve
