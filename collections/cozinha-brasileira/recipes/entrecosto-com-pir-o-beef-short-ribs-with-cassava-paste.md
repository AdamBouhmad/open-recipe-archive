## Ingredients

- beef short ribs
- brandy
- onions
- parsley
- bay leaf
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- butter
- cassava flour or potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the short rib meat into slices and place them for twenty-four hours in an infusion of brandy in a dish with slices of onions, parsley, bay leaf, salt, pepper and nutmeg
2. Half an hour before serving, melt a little butter over a lively fire
3. When well hot, add the meat slices
4. Turn them and serve with cassava flour paste or potatoes
