## Ingredients

- one full spoonful of butter
- salt
- pepper
- one spoonful of wheat flour or polvilho
- two egg yolks
- one or two spoonfuls of vinegar
- substance of braised beef

## Directions

1. Put in a casserole one full spoonful of butter, salt and pepper; when melted, add one spoonful of wheat flour or polvilho.
2. When well mixed, remove from the fire and add two beaten egg yolks with one or two spoonfuls of vinegar; add a little substance of braised beef.
3. It is a true golden sauce, very delicate, easy to make, and that can be served with almost all roasted and fried meats, pies, etc; it is used in all cases in which white sauce is used.
