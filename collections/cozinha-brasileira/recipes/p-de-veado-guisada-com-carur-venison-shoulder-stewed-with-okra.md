## Ingredients

- venison shoulder
- water
- salt
- pimento peppers
- parsley
- onion leaves
- bay leaf
- ginger
- two tablespoons of butter
- one onion
- meat broth
- one tablespoon of arrowroot
- twelve tomatoes
- portion of okra
- cornmeal porridge

## Directions

1. Boil the venison shoulder in water, salt, pimento peppers, parsley, onion leaves, bay leaf and ginger
2. When cooked, separate the meat from the bones and cut it into pieces
3. On the other hand, melt two tablespoons of butter, add one chopped onion, the meat broth, one tablespoon of arrowroot, twelve tomatoes and a portion of okra
4. Let reduce and when cooked, add the meat and serve with cornmeal porridge
