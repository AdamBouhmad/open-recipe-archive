## Ingredients

- Lambaris
- Wheat flour
- Cow butter or fat
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Coat lambaris in wheat flour
2. Fry in cow butter or fat until well browned
3. Place on a plate
4. Top with parsley, ground pepper, and serve
5. Or if desired, pour the butter or fat in which they were fried on top
