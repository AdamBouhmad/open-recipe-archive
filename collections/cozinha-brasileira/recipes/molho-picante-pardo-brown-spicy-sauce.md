## Ingredients

- one spoon of wheat flour or cornmeal
- one spoon of fat or butter
- one onion, chopped
- one spoon of mustard powder
- some slices of lemon
- salt
- beef broth as needed
- a little vinegar
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry one spoon of wheat flour or cornmeal in one spoon of fat or butter until browned
2. Add one chopped onion, one spoon of mustard powder, some slices of lemon, salt and beef broth as needed
3. Add a little vinegar, sugar and lemon juice
4. Serve
