## Ingredients

- leftover roasted loin
- bread
- butter
- loin juice or melted butter
- parsley
- capers or pickled cucumbers in vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover roasted loin into slices and heat them in the juice without boiling.
2. Cut bread into slices of the same size and fry them in butter until they brown.
3. Layer the bread slices and loin slices on a plate, one on top of the other.
4. Pour over them the loin juice or melted butter with parsley and some capers, or pickled cucumbers in vinegar.
