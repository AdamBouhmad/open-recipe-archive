## Ingredients

- a dozen large potatoes
- water
- salt
- roasted peanuts equal in weight to the potatoes
- grated cheese
- one spoon of sugar

## Directions

1. Peel a dozen large potatoes and boil them in water and salt
2. When cooked, mash them and reduce to a homogeneous pulp
3. Mix this with equal weight of roasted peanuts, grated cheese and one spoon of sugar
4. Put everything on a plate greased with butter and place over coals, covering at the same time with a lid full of coals, and serve
