## Ingredients

- bread
- white wine
- three egg yolks
- two tablespoons sugar
- butter
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut bread into slices
2. Soak in white wine, remove and let drain
3. Pass the slices through three beaten egg yolks mixed with two tablespoons of sugar
4. Fry in butter
5. Cover with cinnamon and sugar
6. Serve hot
