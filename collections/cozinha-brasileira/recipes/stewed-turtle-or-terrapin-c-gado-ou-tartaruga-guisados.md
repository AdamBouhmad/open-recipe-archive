## Ingredients

- turtle or terrapin
- fat
- one cup of water
- salt
- onion, chopped
- peppers

## Directions

1. Scald the turtle
2. Remove the meat from the shells
3. Braise in hot fat
4. Add one cup of water, salt, chopped onion, and peppers
5. Let boil for a quarter of an hour
6. Serve
