## Ingredients

- one piece of pork meat
- half pound bacon
- salt
- Indian peppers
- parsley
- two onions, cut
- yam roots, cut into small pieces
- butter
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cook one piece of meat with half pound of bacon, salt, Indian peppers, parsley, two cut onions and yam roots cut into small pieces.
2. When the meat is cooked, cut it into thick slices and fry them in butter.
3. Place the yams on the plate, sprinkle a little grated cheese on them, and pour over the butter in which the meat was fried and serve.
