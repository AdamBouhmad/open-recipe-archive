## Ingredients

- half a bottle of white wine
- half a bottle of white beer
- one bottle of water
- lemon peel
- sugar
- one pound of dried rusks ground
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Bring to boil half a bottle of white wine, half a bottle of white beer and one bottle of water with a little lemon peel, sugar and one pound of dried rusks ground
2. Add two beaten egg yolks and let them boil for a little while, then serve
