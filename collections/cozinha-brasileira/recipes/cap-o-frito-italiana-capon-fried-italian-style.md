## Ingredients

- capon, cut into pieces
- vinegar
- sweet olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- parsley, chopped
- half clove of garlic
- clove
- grated cheese
- butter
- cold sauce

## Directions

1. Soak the capon pieces in vinegar, sweet olive oil, salt, pepper, onion, chopped parsley, half clove of garlic and clove
2. Roll them in grated cheese
3. Fry them in butter, serving with a cold sauce
