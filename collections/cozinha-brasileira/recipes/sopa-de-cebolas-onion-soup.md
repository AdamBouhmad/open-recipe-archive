## Ingredients

- butter
- two or three onions, finely chopped
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- water
- salt
- bread, toasted

## Directions

1. Fry two or three finely chopped onions in butter until they begin to brown
2. Add one spoonful of wheat flour and continue frying until the onions are browned
3. Gradually add the necessary water and salt
4. Pour the soup into a tureen with toasted bread
