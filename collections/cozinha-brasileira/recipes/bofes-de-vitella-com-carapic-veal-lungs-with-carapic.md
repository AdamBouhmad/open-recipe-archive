## Ingredients

- veal lungs
- butter
- tapioca flour
- beef broth
- red wine
- cloves
- salt
- pepper
- carapicú
- hot water
- lemon or sour orange juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. After the lungs are well washed, chop them finely and put them in the pot with melted butter.
2. Fry them a little, taking care to stir them.
3. Add to them one spoon of tapioca flour, one cup of broth and another of red wine, cloves, little salt and pepper, and let them cook over coals.
4. Add to them more a portion of carapicú well washed, chosen and scalded in hot water.
5. Let everything finish cooking, and serve, adding to it at this time the juice of a lemon or sour orange and half a spoon of sugar.
6. Eaten with corn porridge.
