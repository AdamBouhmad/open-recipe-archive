## Ingredients

- chicken pieces
- half a pound of bacon, chopped
- one cup of water
- salt
- pepper
- one heart of palm, chopped and washed in cold water

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken pieces with the chopped bacon and one cup of water, salt and pepper
2. When cooked, remove the chicken pieces
3. Add the chopped and washed heart of palm to the broth
4. Let it cook while stirring
5. Serve with the chicken and cornmeal mush
