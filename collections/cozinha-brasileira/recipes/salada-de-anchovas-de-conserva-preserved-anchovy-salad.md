## Ingredients

- anchovies
- four hard-boiled eggs
- parsley
- chives
- garlic
- pimento da India
- one tablespoon vinegar
- one tablespoon butter
- three to four tablespoons milk

## Directions

1. Take a portion of anchovies, wash them and drain well
2. Chop four hard-boiled eggs with parsley, chives and crushed garlic
3. Season with pimento da India and one tablespoon of vinegar
4. Melt one tablespoon of butter in a pan and add this mixture
5. Let it boil and add three to four tablespoons of milk if the sauce is too thick
6. Pour over the anchovies and serve
