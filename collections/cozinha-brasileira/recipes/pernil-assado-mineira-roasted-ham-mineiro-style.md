## Ingredients

- ham
- vinegar
- salt
- garlic
- Indian pepper
- thyme
- bay leaf
- parsley
- chives
- water
- olives
- mangalhos

## Directions

1. Soak the ham for twenty-four hours in vinegar, salt, garlic, Indian pepper, thyme, bay leaf, parsley and chives, all finely chopped.
2. After this time, remove the ham, making holes in it into which the seasoning leaves are introduced, placing one or two selected olives in each hole.
3. Place it in a skillet with enough water to cover it completely and let it boil until the water is completely evaporated, making small holes with a stick from time to time to allow blood to escape and water to penetrate.
4. Place it in an oven with the same skillet, well heated, until it takes on a nice color.
5. Serve with boiled and toasted mangalhos in fat or butter.
