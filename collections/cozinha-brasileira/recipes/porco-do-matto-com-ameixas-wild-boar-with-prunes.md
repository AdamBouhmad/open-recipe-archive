## Ingredients

- wild boar meat
- water
- salt
- butter
- wheat flour
- rapadura (brown sugar)
- two dozen prunes
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- wine
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Cook the meat and scald in salted water
2. Fry afterwards in butter with a little wheat flour
3. Add a little rapadura, two dozen prunes, cinnamon, lemon peel, wine, and a little meat broth
4. Let boil and strain through a sieve
5. Place the meat in this sauce
6. Give it one more boil and serve
