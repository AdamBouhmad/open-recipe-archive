## Ingredients

- dried cod, soaked and cooked
- two tablespoons butter
- one tablespoon wheat flour
- one small teaspoon mustard flour
- pepper
- one cup milk
- breadcrumbs, grated
- grated cheese
- butter, melted

## Directions

1. Soak and cook the dried cod, then remove the bones and skin.
2. Melt two tablespoons of butter with one tablespoon of wheat flour, one small teaspoon of mustard flour and some pepper.
3. Add one cup of milk and place the cod in this sauce.
4. Let it boil a little; then pour everything into a dish.
5. Cover with a layer of grated breadcrumbs and another of grated cheese.
6. Drizzle some melted butter on top.
7. Place in the oven and let it brown to serve at the table.
