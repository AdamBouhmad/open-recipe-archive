## Ingredients

- one lizard, cut into pieces
- hot water
- salt
- lemon juice
- fat
- one onion, minced
- wheat flour
- quibebe (pumpkin stew)

## Directions

1. Cut a lizard into pieces and scald with very hot water, salt, and a little lemon juice
2. Let cool in this water
3. Fry in as much fat as needed, with a minced onion and dusted with wheat flour
4. Place on a plate with quibebe and serve
