## Ingredients

- sirloin slices
- butter
- salt
- potatoes
- onion

## Directions

1. Cut a beef sirloin into slices the thickness of two fingers.
2. Beat these slices on a block with a heat-shield until they are quite tender.
3. Then melt a little butter in a skillet over coals.
4. When melted, put into it the beef slices dusted with a little salt and immediately place the skillet over lively fire, turning the slices from time to time when they are fried on one side.
5. At the same time, fry separately in a separate skillet peeled and sliced potatoes with a chopped onion in very little butter until well toasted, which are placed around the beef on the same plate.
