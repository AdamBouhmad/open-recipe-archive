## Ingredients

- veal meat, roasted and cut into pieces
- butter
- wheat flour
- onion, chopped
- water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- chervil
- lemon juice
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Cut any portion of roasted veal meat into pieces and place them in a casserole with butter, wheat flour, and a chopped onion
2. Let them fry a little, then add a sufficient quantity of water with salt, pepper, parsley, chervil and a little lemon juice
3. Let everything boil over moderate heat for one hour, then serve with vegetables
