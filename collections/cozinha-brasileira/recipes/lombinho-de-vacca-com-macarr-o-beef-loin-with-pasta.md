## Ingredients

- beef loin
- butter
- beef broth
- pasta
- ham
- mushrooms
- beef broth
- white wine

## Directions

1. Cut the loin into slices the thickness of a finger.
2. Fry them in butter, removing them before they are fully cooked.
3. Finish cooking them in a few spoonfuls of beef broth.
4. When sufficiently cooked, remove and place on a platter, leaving a space in the middle for the pasta.
5. Cook the pasta in broth for the necessary time.
6. Soak mushrooms in a mixture of one cup of beef broth and another cup of white wine for a quarter of an hour.
7. Mix the cooked pasta with half a quarter of ham and the same amount of mushrooms cut into small pieces, along with their soaking liquid.
8. Fill the space on the platter with the pasta mixture.
