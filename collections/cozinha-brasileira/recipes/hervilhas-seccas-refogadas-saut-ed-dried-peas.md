## Ingredients

- a portion of dried peas
- water and salt
- a piece of bacon
- one chopped onion
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of dried peas in water and salt
2. When cooked, fry a piece of bacon and one chopped onion
3. Add a little sugar and the cooked dried peas
4. Let it boil a little and serve
