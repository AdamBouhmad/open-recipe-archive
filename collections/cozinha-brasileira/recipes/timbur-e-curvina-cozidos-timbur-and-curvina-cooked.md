## Ingredients

- two bottles of water
- two roots of parsley
- one root of celery
- parsley
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- fish
- manioc flour
- butter or fat

## Directions

1. Boil in two bottles of water two roots of parsley, one of celery, parsley, onion, salt and pepper
2. Once boiled a few times, remove these herbs with a skimmer, place the cleaned and scaled fish in this water, and continue to boil
3. Once cooked, serve with manioc flour pirão made with the fish broth, to which one tablespoon of butter or fat is added
