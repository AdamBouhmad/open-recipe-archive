## Ingredients

- six to eight egg yolks
- four spoonfuls of sugar
- a little ground cinnamon
- egg whites
- fine salt
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat six to eight egg yolks with four spoonfuls of sugar and a little ground cinnamon
2. Separately, beat the whites with a little fine salt until stiff
3. Add the yolks to the whites
4. Pour everything onto a plate placed over the fire
5. Cover it with a lid full of coals
6. The fire should be medium so that the eggs rise
7. When cooked, cover with sugar and cinnamon
8. Serve hot
