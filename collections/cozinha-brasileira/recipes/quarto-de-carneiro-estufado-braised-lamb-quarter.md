## Ingredients

- Lamb quarter
- Butter
- Hot water
- Salt
- Burnt sugar
- Toasted wheat flour
- Vinegar
- Lemon slices
- Capers
- Chopped sardines
- Onion
- Bay leaves
- Pepper
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Fry the lamb quarter in butter until browned
2. Add hot water and salt
3. Remove the quarter and remove the fat from the sauce
4. Add a little burnt sugar, a spoonful of toasted wheat flour, vinegar, lemon slices, capers, chopped sardines, a sliced onion, bay leaves, pepper, and sugar
5. When all is boiling, return the quarter and finish cooking over moderate fire
6. The vessel should be well covered
