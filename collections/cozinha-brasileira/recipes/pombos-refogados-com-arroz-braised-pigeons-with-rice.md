## Ingredients

- four pigeons, plucked and cleaned
- four tablespoons fat
- chosen and washed rice
- one bottle of meat broth
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- one clove
- one cut onion

## Directions

1. Fry the four pigeons in four tablespoons of fat until roasted, turning them until they take on good color
2. Remove them and heat the fat well
3. Add a portion of chosen and washed rice and stir
4. When the rice is hot and evenly greased, add one bottle of meat broth, salt, grated nutmeg, one clove, one cut onion, and the pigeons
5. Let them dry near the fire, and when cooked, serve them
