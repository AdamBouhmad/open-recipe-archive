## Ingredients

- rye bread
- meat broth
- bread crumbs
- onion

## Directions

1. Cut rye bread into slices, place them in a casserole over coals, adding one tablespoon of meat broth
2. When about to boil, add more tablespoons of broth until the bread absorbs no more
3. Then add bread crumbs and an onion chopped into very fine pieces
4. Finally, when serving, add the desired quantity of meat broth to make the soup
