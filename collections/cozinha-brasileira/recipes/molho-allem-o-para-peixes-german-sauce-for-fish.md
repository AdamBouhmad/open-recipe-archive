## Ingredients

- 8 cups of meat sauce stock
- 4 egg yolks
- a little nutmeg
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take 8 cups of meat sauce stock
2. Mix with 4 egg yolks and a little nutmeg
3. Let it boil for fifteen minutes
4. If desired, add a little vinegar and serve, mainly for fish
