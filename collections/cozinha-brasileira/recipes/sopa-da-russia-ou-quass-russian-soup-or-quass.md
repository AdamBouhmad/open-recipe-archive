## Ingredients

- wheat flour
- sugar
- hot water
- warm water
- whey from milk
- salt
- potatoes
- pieces of scalded cod
- butter

## Directions

1. Knead a little wheat flour with sugar and hot water
2. Put this dough in a warm place for three to four days until it ferments
3. At night add more flour and warm water, leaving it in the same warm place
4. The next day combine this dough with whey from milk and necessary salt, some potatoes, pieces of scalded cod, one spoon of butter and let boil until the potatoes are well cooked
5. Finally add a little sugar
6. If the soup is not sour enough, add a little vinegar
