## Ingredients

- turkey
- milk
- salt
- onion leaves
- parsley
- butter
- bread
- cheese, grated
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut the turkey into pieces and boil them in milk, salt, onion leaves and parsley
2. When cooked, remove them and fry them in butter, arranging them on slices of fried bread and then moistened with milk
3. Cover everything with a layer of grated cheese, sugar and cinnamon and take the plate to the oven, where you let it cook until it takes on a good color, and serve
