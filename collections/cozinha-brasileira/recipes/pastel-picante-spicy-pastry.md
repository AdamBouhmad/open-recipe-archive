## Ingredients

- half a pound of ham
- half a pound of veal
- one quarter of fresh bacon
- two onions
- salt
- parsley
- peppers
- nutmeg
- puff pastry dough
- white wine

## Directions

1. Mince half a pound of ham, half a pound of veal, one quarter of fresh bacon, two onions, salt, parsley, peppers and nutmeg
2. Make some small forms of puff pastry and bake in the oven
3. Place a portion of this minced mixture inside the forms, cover the forms with a crust
4. Simmer a little in white wine, place in the oven, and serve
