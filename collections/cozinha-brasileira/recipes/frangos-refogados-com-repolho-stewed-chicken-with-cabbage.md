## Ingredients

- two handfuls of bread crumb moistened with milk
- three dozen sapucaia nuts crushed
- spoonful of sugar
- one pound of lamb chopped
- salt
- onion
- two chickens
- fat
- hard cabbage
- water
- onion leaves
- eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Knead two handfuls of bread crumb moistened with milk, three dozen crushed sapucaia nuts, a spoonful of sugar, one pound of chopped lamb, salt and an onion
2. Fill two chickens with this mixture
3. Brown them in abundant fat, turning them until they are golden
4. Add a hard cabbage cut into four pieces, water, salt and as many onion leaves as necessary
5. Let them boil
6. When cooked, place the chickens on a plate with the cabbage around and pour eight egg yolks on top
7. Put in the oven and serve
