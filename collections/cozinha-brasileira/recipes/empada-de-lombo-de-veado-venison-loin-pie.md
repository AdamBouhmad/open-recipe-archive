## Ingredients

- venison loin
- water
- salt
- white wine
- ginger
- parsley
- marjoram
- four pounds of wheat flour
- half a pound of butter
- four eggs
- milk

## Directions

1. Boil a venison loin in water and salt for half an hour, and following that, in white wine, ginger, parsley, and marjoram, until cooked
2. Afterward, knead four pounds of wheat flour with half a pound of butter, four eggs, and as much milk as is needed to make a dough
3. Wrap it in this dough and bake in the oven
