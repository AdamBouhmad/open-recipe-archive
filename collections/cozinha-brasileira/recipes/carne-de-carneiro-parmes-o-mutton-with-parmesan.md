## Ingredients

- half pound of bacon
- two pounds of mutton meat, cut into small pieces
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- onion
- bread slices
- grated cheese
- butter
- one glass of wine

## Directions

1. Fry half a pound of bacon and when melted, add two pounds of mutton meat cut into small pieces
2. Add salt, pepper, chopped parsley and onion
3. When browned, place them on slices of bread covered with grated cheese, put in a buttered dish
4. Moisten with one glass of wine and finish cooking in the oven
