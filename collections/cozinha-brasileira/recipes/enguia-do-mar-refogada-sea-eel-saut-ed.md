## Ingredients

- one sea eel
- sea water
- one celery root
- parsley
- butter
- wheat flour
- one cup white wine

## Directions

1. Remove the skin of the fish and cut into pieces
2. Boil in sea water with one celery root and parsley
3. When cooked, remove and place in a pan with butter
4. Dust with wheat flour and fry a little
5. Add one cup of white wine
6. Let it boil a little more and serve with its sauce
