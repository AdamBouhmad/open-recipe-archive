## Ingredients

- turkey breasts from two turkeys
- salt pork slices
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- bay leaves
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Cut the breasts from two turkeys, leaving the bones in
2. Remove the skin and lard with thin salt pork strips
3. Simmer briefly in water
4. Cook in salt pork slices, white wine, salt, pepper, onion, and bay leaves
5. When cooked, remove and strain the sauce
6. Remove the fat and reduce the sauce to one tablespoon
7. Pour the sauce over the breasts and serve with fresh vegetables
