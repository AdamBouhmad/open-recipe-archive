## Ingredients

- half pound sugar
- four cups milk, added little by little
- one dozen egg yolks
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Pound half pound of sugar
2. Add little by little four cups of milk, and finally one dozen egg yolks and ground cinnamon
3. Boil a little, stirring well
4. Make a form of puff pastry for pastries, fill it with the mass
5. Having placed it in the oven, bake until a good color and serve
