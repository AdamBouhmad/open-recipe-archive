## Ingredients

- veal meat
- bacon slices
- carrots
- carapicú
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- seasoning sauce
- white wine
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Cut veal meat into slices and place in a pan over bacon slices with some carrots, selected carapicú, sliced onion, salt, pepper, and a seasoning sauce
2. Let roast for an hour, then add a cup of white wine and lemon slices
3. Continue cooking over coals until done
