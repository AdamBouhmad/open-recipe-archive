## Ingredients

- one spoon of corn meal or roasted wheat flour
- two spoons of vinegar
- two cups of wine and four of water
- Indian cloves
- sugar
- raisins
- blanched and chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Boil one spoon of corn meal or roasted wheat flour until it becomes brown
2. Add two spoons of vinegar, two cups of wine and four cups of water
3. Add Indian cloves, sugar, raisins, and blanched almonds cut into small pieces
4. Allow everything to boil for half an hour
5. Serve
