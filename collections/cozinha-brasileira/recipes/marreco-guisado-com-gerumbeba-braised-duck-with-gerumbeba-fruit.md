## Ingredients

- gerumbeba fruits (fruits of the infernal fig tree)
- 1 duck
- rest of the fruits
- bacon slice
- one cup of meat broth
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- one chopped onion

## Directions

1. Take a portion of gerumbeba fruits (fruits of the infernal fig tree), place them on a coal bed, roast and peel them
2. Fill the duck with these fruits and place it in a pan with the remaining fruits on a slice of bacon
3. Add one cup of meat broth, salt, pepper, parsley, and one chopped onion
4. Let it boil over low fire until the meat is cooked
5. Serve with its own sauce, strained through a sieve or coarse cloth
