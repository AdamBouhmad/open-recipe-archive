## Ingredients

- wheat flour
- onion
- parsley
- fat
- carrots
- sugar
- salt
- water
- lemon juice
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Sauté a spoon of wheat flour, a chopped onion, and a little parsley in two spoons of fat
2. Add a portion of carrots cut into slices, a spoon of sugar, salt, two cups of water, and a glass of lemon juice
3. Leave to boil over moderate heat
4. When cooked, incorporate with an egg yolk and serve
