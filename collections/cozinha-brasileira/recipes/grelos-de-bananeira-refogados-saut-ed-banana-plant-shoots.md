## Ingredients

- banana plant shoots
- water
- salt
- fat
- cornmeal
- tomatoes
- half cup of milk

## Directions

1. Take banana plant shoots found at the ends of the bunches
2. Chop them very fine and boil with water and salt
3. Drain well and squeeze
4. Sauté with a little fat
5. Add cornmeal, tomatoes, and half cup of milk
6. Boil at the edge of the fire until cooked and serve
