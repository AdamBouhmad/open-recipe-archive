## Ingredients

- any of these vegetables
- water
- salt
- fat
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Take any of these vegetables, peel and boil for a few minutes in salted water
2. Remove with a skimmer, let drain
3. Place in a casserole with a little fat to fry
4. Add breadcrumbs and salt, stirring to fry evenly
