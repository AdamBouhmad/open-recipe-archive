## Ingredients

- lamb leg
- salt
- aromatic herbs
- garlic
- onion
- clove
- palm hearts
- water
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Lard the leg and cook it with salt, a bunch of aromatic herbs, garlic, onion and clove
2. When cooked, pass the sauce through a sieve and remove the fat
3. Let it reduce to two or three cups of liquid
4. Place the leg on a platter garnished with palm hearts cooked in water and salt
5. Cover with grated cheese and pour sauce on top
6. Put in the oven to brown and serve thus
