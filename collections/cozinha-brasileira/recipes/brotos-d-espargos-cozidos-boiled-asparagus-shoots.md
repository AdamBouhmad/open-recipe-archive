## Ingredients

- brotos de espargos (asparagus shoots)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- manteiga (butter)
- pão ralado (bread crumbs)

## Directions

1. Tie the shoots in small bundles and boil them in water and salt
2. Having boiled, remove them and serve them with melted butter fried with bread crumbs
