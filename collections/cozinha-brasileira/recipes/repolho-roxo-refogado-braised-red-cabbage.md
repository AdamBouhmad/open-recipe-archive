## Ingredients

- repolho roxo (red cabbage)
- cebola (onion)
- salsa (parsley)
- manteiga (butter)
- sal (salt)
- pimenta (pepper)
- assucar (sugar)
- vinho tinto (red wine)

## Directions

1. Chop a red cabbage finely
2. Blanch it and let it cool
3. Place two finely diced onions, parsley, two spoons of butter, salt, pepper and a spoon of sugar in a casserole
4. Let it boil and serve, placing on top a cup of red wine
