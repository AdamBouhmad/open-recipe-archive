## Ingredients

- portion of crabs
- water
- salt
- one spoon of butter
- one spoon of manioc flour
- one cup of white wine
- chopped parsley
- pepper
- crab meat

## Directions

1. Place a portion of crabs in a pan with water and salt
2. When cooked, shell them
3. Melt one spoon of butter and add one spoon of manioc flour, one cup of white wine, salt, chopped parsley and pepper
4. Put everything to boil, add the crab meat and serve after boiling a little more
