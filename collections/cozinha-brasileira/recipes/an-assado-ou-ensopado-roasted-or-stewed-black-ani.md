## Ingredients

- one black ani bird
- salt
- angú de fubá de milho

## Directions

1. After plucking the bird, wash it well to remove half of its smell
2. Rub it only with salt, without adding any other seasoning
3. In this state, roast it on a spit, or cook it in water with salt and fat, and nothing more
4. The broth should be drunk two to three times a day
5. The meat is eaten with angú de fubá de milho
