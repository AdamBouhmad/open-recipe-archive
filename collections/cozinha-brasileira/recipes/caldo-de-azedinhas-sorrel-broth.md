## Ingredients

- one spoonful of fat
- four spoonfuls of wheat flour
- a portion of sorrel leaves
- meat broth
- a little sugar
- nutmeg
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Fry one spoonful of fat until it stops crackling
2. Add four spoonfuls of wheat flour and a portion of washed sorrel leaves
3. Mix and stir with beef broth
4. After adding a little sugar and nutmeg, pour over toasted bread in a tureen
