## Ingredients

- small fish
- salt
- pepper
- two tablespoons butter
- half tablespoon wheat flour
- parsley, chopped
- onion, chopped
- two cups red wine

## Directions

1. Clean, scale and open the fish lengthwise.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Put two tablespoons of butter in a casserole with half tablespoon of wheat flour, chopped parsley and chopped onion.
4. Place the fish in this casserole with two cups of red wine.
5. Boil everything for a quarter hour over a lively fire.
6. Place the fish on a plate, strain the broth and serve.
