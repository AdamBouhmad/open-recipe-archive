## Ingredients

- two spoonfuls of fat or cow's butter
- half a plate of chosen and washed rice
- half a pound of roasted and ground peanuts
- four spoonfuls of sugar
- one bottle of water
- little salt
- lemon peelings

## Directions

1. Fry two spoonfuls of fat or cow's butter until very hot
2. Add half a plate of chosen and washed rice
3. Stir and add half a pound of roasted and ground peanuts with four spoonfuls of sugar
4. Add one bottle of water, little salt, and lemon peelings
5. Boil until well dried
6. Serve
