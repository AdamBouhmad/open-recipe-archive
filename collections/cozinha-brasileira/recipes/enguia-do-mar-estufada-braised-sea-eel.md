## Ingredients

- one sea eel
- salt
- peppers
- one onion
- one cup cream
- one spoon mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Skin the fish and remove the loin and cut into pieces
2. Place in a casserole with salt, peppers and one chopped onion
3. Roast over coals, turning from time to time until perfectly cooked
4. Add one cup of cream and one spoon of mustard sauce
5. Let it boil once more and serve with the fish
