## Ingredients

- two cooked eggs, finely chopped
- chicken giblets
- parsley
- bread crumbs
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- thin bacon strip
- salad

## Directions

1. Mix two finely chopped cooked eggs, chicken giblets, and parsley with one spoon of bread crumbs and one spoon of butter
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Stuff the chicken with this filling
4. Roast on a spit covered with a very thin bacon strip
5. When properly roasted, serve with salad
