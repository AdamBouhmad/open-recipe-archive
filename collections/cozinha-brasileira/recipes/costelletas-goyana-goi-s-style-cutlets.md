## Ingredients

- cutlets
- cornmeal flour
- melted fat
- egg yolks
- grated cheese
- squash sprouts or samambaia sprouts

## Directions

1. Cut the cutlets
2. Coat in cornmeal flour, passing them in melted fat first, then in beaten egg yolks and again in cornmeal flour mixed with grated cheese
3. Fry in fat and serve with squash or samambaia sprouts
