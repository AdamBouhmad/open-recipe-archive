## Ingredients

- tapir loin
- milk
- salt
- pork fat
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil a tapir loin for five to ten minutes in milk and salt
2. Remove it and let it cool
3. Lard it with pork fat seasoned with salt and pepper
