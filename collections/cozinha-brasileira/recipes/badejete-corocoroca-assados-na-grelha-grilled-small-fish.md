## Ingredients

- small fish
- salt
- ground pepper
- melted butter
- grated breadcrumbs
- parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Take a portion of fish, scale them, clean them and wash them, then dry them with a towel.
2. Sprinkle with salt and ground pepper.
3. Brush with melted butter.
4. Roll them in grated breadcrumbs.
5. Grill them and serve with chopped parsley and melted butter poured on top.
