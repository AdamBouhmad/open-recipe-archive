## Ingredients

- lamb tongues
- water
- salt
- water squash
- lard
- two spoonfuls of fine cornmeal
- one bowlful of water or meat broth
- parsley
- celery root
- one onion

## Directions

1. Cook the tongues in water and salt and peel them
2. Cut them into cubes and fry them in melted lard with two spoonfuls of fine cornmeal until the cornmeal takes on a copper color
3. Add a portion of water squash cut equally into cubes
4. Mix and as it takes on the heat of the fire, add one bowlful of water or meat broth, salt, parsley, celery root, and one cut onion
5. Let it boil for one hour over moderate fire and serve
