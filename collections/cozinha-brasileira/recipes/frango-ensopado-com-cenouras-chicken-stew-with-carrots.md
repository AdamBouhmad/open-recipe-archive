## Ingredients

- two chickens
- carrots
- parsley
- wheat flour
- butter
- vinegar
- water
- sugar
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Cut two chickens into pieces and boil them
2. When almost cooked, add a portion of cut carrots with parsley and a spoonful of wheat flour kneaded with two spoonfuls of butter and a spoonful of vinegar dissolved in four cups of water
3. Let them cook near the fire until everything is done
4. Add half a spoonful of sugar and an egg yolk and serve at table
