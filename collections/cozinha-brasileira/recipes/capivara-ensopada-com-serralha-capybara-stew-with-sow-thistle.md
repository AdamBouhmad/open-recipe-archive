## Ingredients

- capybara meat
- gordura
- water
- aguardente
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- cloves
- pepper
- sow thistle

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of capybara meat, scald it, leaving it to cool in the same water
2. Remove and fry it in two spoonfuls of fat with a bowl of water, a glass of aguardente, salt, parsley, onion, cloves, and pepper
3. Leave it to boil until cooked
4. Serve with sow thistle finely chopped, scalded, and sautéed in a little fat after adding some water and salt
