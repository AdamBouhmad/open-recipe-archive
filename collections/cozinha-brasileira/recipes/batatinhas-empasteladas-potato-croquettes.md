## Ingredients

- potatoes
- milk
- butter
- egg yolks
- sugar
- wheat flour
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Take a portion of potatoes, boil, peel, and mash
2. Mix with each pound of mass two spoonfuls of milk, one spoonful of butter, four egg yolks, one spoonful of sugar, and two of wheat flour
3. Knead everything well and form into balls
4. Fry in cow butter and serve
