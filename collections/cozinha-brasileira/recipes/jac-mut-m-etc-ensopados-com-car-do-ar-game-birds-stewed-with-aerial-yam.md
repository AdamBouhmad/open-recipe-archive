## Ingredients

- one bird (jacú, mutúm, etc.)
- one chopped onion
- one spoonful of sugar
- two spoonfuls of fat
- one bowl of water
- one spoonful of fine cornmeal
- one aerial yam, peeled and cut into pieces
- salt
- hot peppers
- parsley
- cumin
- one cup of local orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut the bird into pieces and sauté with one chopped onion and one spoonful of sugar in two spoonfuls of fat, turning the pieces to brown on both sides.
2. Add one bowl of water, one spoonful of fine cornmeal, the peeled and cut aerial yam, salt, hot peppers, parsley, and cumin.
3. Add one cup of local orange juice.
4. Let it boil for one hour and serve when cooked.
