## Ingredients

- carrots
- fat
- salt
- pepper
- sugar
- broth
- white wine

## Directions

1. Scrape a portion of carrots and cut them into slices
2. Fry them in fat with a little salt, pepper, and a spoon of sugar, turning them from time to time so they do not burn
3. When well fried, remove from fire and add a cup of broth and another of white wine
4. Finish cooking and serve with roasts of game
