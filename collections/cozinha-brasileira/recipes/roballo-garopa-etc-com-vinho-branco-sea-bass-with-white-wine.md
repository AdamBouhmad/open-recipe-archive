## Ingredients

- A dozen onions
- Two spoons of butter
- Salt
- Peppers
- Nutmeg
- Small sea bass
- Mustard powder
- Lemon juice
- Melted butter
- One glass of white wine
- Coals

## Directions

1. Put a dozen sliced onions in a frying pan with two spoons of butter
2. Add salt, peppers and nutmeg
3. Place a small cleaned sea bass on top, dusted with salt and mustard powder and moistened with lemon juice
4. Add some melted butter and one glass of white wine
5. Cover the pan with a lid full of coals
6. Roast over coals and serve when cooked
