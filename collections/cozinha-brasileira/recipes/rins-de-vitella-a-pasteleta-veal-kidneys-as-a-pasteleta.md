## Ingredients

- veal kidney
- veal meat
- water
- salt
- eggs
- dried breadcrumbs, ground
- pepper, ground
- parsley
- bread slices
- milk
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon, ground

## Directions

1. Boil the kidney and meat in salted water and let cool
2. Chop finely and add eggs, ground dried breadcrumbs, ground pepper, salt and parsley
3. Spread this filling on bread slices moistened in milk
4. Fry these slices on both sides in a casserole with butter and serve covered with sugar and ground cinnamon
