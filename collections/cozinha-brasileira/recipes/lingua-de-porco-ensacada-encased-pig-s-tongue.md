## Ingredients

- 1 pig's tongue
- salt
- wheat flour
- aromatic herbs
- 1 beef intestine casing

## Directions

1. Boil the pig's tongue over high heat, then remove the skin covering it.
2. Place the tongue in a vessel with abundant salt, wheat flour, and aromatic herbs so it is well covered with this mixture.
3. After leaving it in this pickle for three to four days, place the tongue in a beef intestine casing, ensuring there are no air pockets, and tie both ends of the casing securely.
4. Hang in smoke and leave for one month or more.
5. Cook in meat broth and serve cold.
