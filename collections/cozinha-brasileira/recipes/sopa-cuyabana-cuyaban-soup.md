## Ingredients

- guaraná
- hot water
- one quarter pound of sugar
- one bottle of beer
- one cup of white wine
- slices of lemon
- dried rusks

## Directions

1. Grate some guaraná and mix with one bottle of hot water and one quarter pound of sugar
2. After cooling, add one bottle of beer, one cup of white wine, slices of lemon and dried rusks that have been pounded and toasted
3. Serve cold as a refreshment
