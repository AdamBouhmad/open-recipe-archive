## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- meat broth or hot water
- onion
- pepper
- vinegar
- biscuits

## Directions

1. Peel some cucumbers and remove the inner flesh
2. Cut them into thin slices
3. Cook them in coals with a little salt for nine minutes
4. Remove and dry them
5. Fry them in lard
6. Add meat broth or hot water, onion, pepper, a little vinegar and biscuits
7. Let them boil a little longer
8. Remove from heat
