## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- salt
- peppers
- tomatoes
- lardoon slices of bacon
- breadcrumbs
- cream of milk
- cloves
- cinnamon
- sugar
- lemon

## Directions

1. Remove the head of a rabbit and rub the body with salt, peppers and tomatoes, leaving it in these seasonings for five to six hours
2. Lard it with thin slices of bacon, trimming their ends flush with the meat
3. Wrap the rabbit in breadcrumbs, thread it on the spit
4. Roast it, basting it with cream of milk to which some cloves, cinnamon and a little sugar have been added
5. When roasted, serve it with slices of lemon placed on top, and its sauce served separately
