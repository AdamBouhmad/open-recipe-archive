## Ingredients

- carapicús (mushrooms)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- gordura (fat)
- pimenta moida (ground pepper)
- cebola (onion)
- salsa (parsley)
- queijo ralado (grated cheese)
- aguardente or cognac (brandy or cognac)
- assucar (sugar)
- noz moscada (nutmeg)

## Directions

1. Braise a portion of carapicús boiled in water, salt and vinegar, and let them drain in two spoons of fat with ground pepper, onion and chopped parsley
2. Add a portion of grated cheese, mix everything with a spoon, pouring in a cup of brandy or better yet cognac
3. Let it boil still over coals and pour onto a plate
4. Sprinkle with sugar and nutmeg, and serve
