## Ingredients

- fish steaks
- red wine
- vinegar
- garlic clove
- bay leaves
- clove
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- sweet olive oil

## Directions

1. Soak some steaks for three to four hours in red wine, vinegar, one clove of garlic, bay leaves, clove, nutmeg, pepper and little salt; place them afterwards in a pan with a little of this sauce, one spoon of sweet olive oil, and placing the pan over coals, cook for one hour, and serve with the hot sauce
