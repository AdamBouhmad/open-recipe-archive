## Ingredients

- carrots
- water
- butter
- wheat flour
- white wine
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of carrots in water and cut them into slices
2. Melt butter and wheat flour separately
3. Add the carrots to this mixture
4. Add two cups of white wine, salt, and pepper
5. Leave to boil
