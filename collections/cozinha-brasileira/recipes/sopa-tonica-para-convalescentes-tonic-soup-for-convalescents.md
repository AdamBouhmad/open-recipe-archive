## Ingredients

- beef leg with bones
- mutton
- hen
- water
- small potatoes
- chicory root
- carrots
- nutmeg
- salt
- toasted bread
- rice
- pasta

## Directions

1. Boil for four hours two pounds of beef leg with bones, one pound of mutton and one hen in eight bottles of water, always removing the foam
2. Add small potatoes, chicory root, carrots, a little nutmeg and salt
3. Once everything is cooked and cold, remove the fat and strain
4. Add the broth
5. Pour over toasted bread, rice cooked in water and salt, or pasta
