## Ingredients

- four pounds of mutton meat, chopped
- salt
- parsley
- celery
- onion
- pepper
- butter
- meat broth or water
- palm hearts cooked in water and salt
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Take four pounds of mutton meat, chop it well, removing nerves and skins that appear
2. Season with salt, parsley, celery, onion and pepper
3. Braise in a little melted butter and add a bowl of meat broth or water
4. Let it boil over coals until the broth almost dries out
5. Add palm hearts cooked in water and salt over which place the braised mutton with the broth
6. Cover everything with grated cheese and put in the oven until it takes on a golden color, then serve
