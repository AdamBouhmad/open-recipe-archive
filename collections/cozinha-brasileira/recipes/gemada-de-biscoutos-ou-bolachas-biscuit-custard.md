## Ingredients

- four egg yolks
- two spoonfuls of sugar
- a portion of grated biscuits
- one bottle of milk
- some lemon zest
- cinnamon
- cloves of India

## Directions

1. Beat four egg yolks with two spoonfuls of sugar and a portion of grated biscuits
2. Add little by little one bottle of milk, some lemon zest, cinnamon and cloves of India
3. Let boil until thick
4. Pour over cut biscuits
5. Let cool
6. Cover with a little sugar and cinnamon
7. Serve
