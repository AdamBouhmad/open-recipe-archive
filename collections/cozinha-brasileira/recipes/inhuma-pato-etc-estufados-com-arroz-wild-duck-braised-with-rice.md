## Ingredients

- one quarter of bacon, chopped fine
- one plate of rice, picked and washed
- one bottle of water
- one bird, cut into pieces
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one dozen tomatoes
- one glass of wine

## Directions

1. Fry one quarter of finely chopped bacon
2. When the fat is melted and hot, add one plate of picked and washed rice
3. Stir and add one bottle of water, one bird cut into pieces, salt, parsley, onion leaves, one dozen tomatoes and one glass of wine
4. Cover the pan well
5. Let it simmer for two hours over moderate fire
6. When cooked, serve
