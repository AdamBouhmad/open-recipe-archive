## Ingredients

- mulberries
- water
- salt
- beef broth or meat sauce substance
- spinach, chopped

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of mulberries with water and salt; remove them and let them drain; then place them in a portion of beef broth, or better the substance for sauce, and add a portion of chopped spinach, and serve with cooked fowl, etc.
