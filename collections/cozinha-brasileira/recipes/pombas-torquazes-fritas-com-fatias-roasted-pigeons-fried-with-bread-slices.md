## Ingredients

- meat from pigeons, doves, etc.
- half a quarter of bacon
- a handful of carapicús
- a handful of cassava flour
- one onion
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- one cup of white wine
- bread slices
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Remove all meat from the birds and chop it with half a quarter of bacon, a handful of carapicús, another of cassava flour, one onion, salt, parsley and pepper
2. Mix everything and boil in one cup of white wine
3. When cooked, serve over slices of bread fried in butter
