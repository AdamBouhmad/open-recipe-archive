## Ingredients

- asparagus tips
- eggs
- a little butter

## Directions

1. After boiling a portion of asparagus tips, cut them into small pieces and drain them.
2. Add them to a portion of eggs and beat well.
3. In this state, place this mixture in a casserole with a little butter, and bring to a gentle heat, stirring always with a spatula until they reach good consistency.
