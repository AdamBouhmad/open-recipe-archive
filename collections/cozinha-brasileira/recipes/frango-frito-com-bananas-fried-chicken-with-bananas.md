## Ingredients

- chicken
- butter
- wheat flour
- egg yolks
- dozen bananas
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Roast a chicken on the spit, greased with butter and when almost cooked, remove it and cut into pieces
2. Coat the pieces in wheat flour, then pass through beaten egg yolks, then again in wheat flour and fry in butter
3. On the other hand, take a dozen bananas split in half and coat in wheat flour, fry them equally in butter
4. Serve the fried bananas with the chicken pieces on top and covered with sugar and cinnamon
