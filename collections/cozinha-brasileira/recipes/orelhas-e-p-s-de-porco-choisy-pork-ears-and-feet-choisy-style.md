## Ingredients

- Four pork ears
- Four pork feet
- Water
- Seasonings
- Butter
- Onions
- Spanish sauce
- Mustard sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the ears and feet well; after passing them through a flame, scrape them, wash them many times, and boil in a vessel with plenty of water
2. Cook them with some seasonings and a little butter
3. When cooked, let them cool in their own liquid, drain them and cut into even strips
4. Cut some large onions into strips, place them in a casserole with a little butter, and cook them
5. When the onions are cooked, add three spoonfuls of Spanish sauce, letting it cook slowly by the fire
6. At the moment of serving, place the ears cut into strips in the pan, add a mustard sauce, and serve
