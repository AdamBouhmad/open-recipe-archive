## Ingredients

- turkey breasts from two turkeys
- salt pork strips
- salt
- nutmeg
- parsley
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the breasts from two turkeys and remove the meat in slices, along with the skin
2. Wrap each slice in salt pork strips and season with salt, nutmeg, and parsley
3. Roast all in a pan placed in the oven
4. When cooked, coat in bread crumbs and fry in butter until golden
5. Serve
