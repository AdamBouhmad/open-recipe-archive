## Ingredients

- two dozen sardines in escabeche
- one dozen cooked and peeled potatoes
- two onions
- salt
- two hard-boiled egg yolks
- vinegar
- ground pepper
- beet slices
- carrot slices

## Directions

1. Take two dozen sardines in escabeche and remove the backbone
2. Cut the meat into small pieces
3. Cut a dozen cooked and peeled potatoes into pieces, and two onions into pieces
4. Season with salt, two hard-boiled egg yolks crushed in vinegar and ground pepper
5. Place on a plate and decorate with slices of red beets and carrots
6. Serve with roasts of veal and pork
