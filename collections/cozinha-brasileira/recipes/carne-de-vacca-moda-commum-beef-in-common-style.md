## Ingredients

- one piece of beef from the hip side
- lard for larding
- four carrots
- four onions
- two cloves
- parsley
- chives
- veal knuckle
- bay leaf
- thyme
- salt
- pepper
- four cups of water
- one glass of good brandy

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef from the hip side
2. Make holes in it with a knife and introduce strips of lard into these holes
3. Place in a casserole with four carrots, four onions, two cloves, parsley, chives, veal knuckle, bay leaf, thyme, salt, pepper, and four cups of water
4. Leave to boil for four to five hours, depending on the size of the piece
5. When half-cooked, add a glass of good brandy
6. Serve with the vegetables
