## Ingredients

- yam root
- whey
- watercress
- lettuce
- sugar
- bitter almonds
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel, wash and cut a yam root into small pieces
2. Cook it in water for two hours
3. Remove and reduce to a paste
4. Put a portion of milk whey on the fire with watercress and lettuce
5. Let this mixture boil for half an hour
6. Strain through thick cloth
7. Return to the fire
8. Mix in the yam paste, sugar, some crushed bitter almonds and a little nutmeg
9. Let it boil for one more hour
10. Serve
