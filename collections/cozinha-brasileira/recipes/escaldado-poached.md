## Ingredients

- one spoonful of fat
- one bottle of hot water
- salt
- some onion leaves
- eggs according to number of servings
- manioc flour or cornmeal

## Directions

1. Fry one spoonful of fat until it stops crackling
2. Pour one bottle of hot water, salt, and some onion leaves into it
3. When this broth is boiling, add a portion of eggs according to the number of people to be served
4. When the egg whites are cooked and the yolks soft, remove with a skimmer and place on a separate plate
5. Make a porridge with this broth using manioc flour or cornmeal, placing the poached eggs on top
