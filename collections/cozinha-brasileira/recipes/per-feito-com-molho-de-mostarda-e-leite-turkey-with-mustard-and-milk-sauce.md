## Ingredients

- turkey pieces
- wheat flour
- butter
- milk
- cornstarch
- sugar
- English mustard powder
- white wine

## Directions

1. Fry the turkey pieces, coated in wheat flour, in butter on both sides
2. When well cooked and browned, serve with the following sauce: boil two cups of milk with a pinch of cornstarch and two spoons of sugar
3. Remove from heat and mix in two spoons of English mustard powder and one glass of white wine
