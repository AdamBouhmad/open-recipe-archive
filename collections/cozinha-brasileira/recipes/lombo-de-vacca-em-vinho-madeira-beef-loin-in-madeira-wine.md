## Ingredients

- beef loin
- lard pieces
- beef slices
- veal slices
- onions
- aromatic herb sprigs
- Madeira wine, half a bottle
- beef broth, half a glass
- Spanish sauce, one good glass

## Directions

1. Prepare the loin, rolled and tied as previously described
2. Place it in a casserole in the bottom of which have been placed some pieces of lard, slices of beef and veal, onions and a sprig of aromatic herbs
3. Cover the loin with some lard slices
4. Pour over it half a bottle of Madeira wine and half a glass of beef broth
5. Let it simmer briefly, then cook over gentle heat for about two hours
6. When cooked, pass the broth through a silk sieve
7. Pour a good glass of Spanish sauce over it and add the strained juice
8. Reduce everything to the consistency of beef broth essence and pour over the loin already placed in the serving dish
