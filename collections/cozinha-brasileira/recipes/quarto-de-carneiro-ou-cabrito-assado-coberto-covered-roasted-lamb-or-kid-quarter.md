## Ingredients

- fat lamb or kid quarter
- salt
- bread crumbs
- salad

## Directions

1. If the quarter is very fat, do not lard it
2. Spit roast it covered with paper untied, seasoned only with salt
3. Once cooked, remove the paper
4. Moisten the quarter well with the sauce that dripped
5. Cover it with bread crumbs
6. Give it a few more turns on the spit so this coating takes on a good color
7. Serve with some salad
