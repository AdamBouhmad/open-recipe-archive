## Ingredients

- tayoba
- water
- salt
- wheat flour
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- fat

## Directions

1. Cut a tayoba root cooked in water and salt
2. Coat in wheat flour seasoned with salt and ground pepper
3. Pass through beaten eggs and fry in fat
4. When fried, pour the remaining egg on top and serve
