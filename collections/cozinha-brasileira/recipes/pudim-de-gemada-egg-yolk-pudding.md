## Ingredients

- five egg yolks
- five spoons of wheat flour
- half a bottle of milk
- salt
- two spoons of butter
- one quarter of peeled and crushed almonds
- one quarter of sugar
- one spoon of orange flower water

## Directions

1. Beat five egg yolks with five spoons of wheat flour, half a bottle of milk, salt and two spoons of butter
2. Boil everything, stirring continuously
3. When cooked, add one quarter of peeled and crushed almonds mixed with one quarter of sugar and one spoon of orange flower water
4. Mix everything together
5. Place in a buttered mold
6. Bake in the oven
7. Serve covered with sugar and cinnamon
