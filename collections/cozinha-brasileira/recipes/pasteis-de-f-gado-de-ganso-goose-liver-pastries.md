## Ingredients

- goose
- fubá (corn meal)
- water
- dried cogumelos (mushrooms)
- cogumelos (mushrooms), weight equal to liver
- dried roscas (hard biscuits), ground
- sugar
- nutmeg
- cardamom
- salt
- three tablespoons goose fat
- one cup white wine
- pastry dough

## Directions

1. Place the goose in a cage slightly larger than itself
2. Make balls with one tablespoon of fubá, water, and a little dried mushroom powder
3. Make the goose swallow a portion of these balls three times daily, increasing the dose day by day
4. Give no other food or even water
5. After twenty-four to thirty days, the goose is fattened and the liver is the size of a plate, sometimes weighing four pounds
6. Chop the liver with a weight of mushrooms equal to its own weight
7. Mix in ground dried biscuits, sugar, nutmeg, cardamom, and a little salt
8. Sauté in three tablespoons of goose fat, adding one cup of white wine
9. Let simmer for one hour near the fire
10. Make a pastry crust and fill it with this mixture
11. Finish cooking in the oven
12. Can also be eaten cold
