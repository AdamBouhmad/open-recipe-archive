## Ingredients

- mate
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take a portion of mate and place it in a bowl
2. Pour water over it and stir
3. Give each guest the bowl with the proper metal straw, which is inserted into the liquid
4. Each guest drinks as they wish
5. Always present a sugar bowl when serving mate, as some prefer it with sugar and others without
