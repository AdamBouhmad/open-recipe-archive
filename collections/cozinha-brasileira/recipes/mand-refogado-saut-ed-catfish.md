## Ingredients

- fresh catfish
- lard
- chopped onion
- corn flour
- water
- salt
- parsley
- marjoram
- two egg yolks
- one cup of lemon juice
- cassava flour

## Directions

1. Take fresh catfish, remove the entrails and cut into steaks
2. Sauté in lard with chopped onion
3. Dust with corn flour and add half a bottle of water, salt, parsley and marjoram
4. Let boil for one hour
5. Incorporate two egg yolks beaten in one cup of lemon juice
6. Boil a little more and serve with cassava flour broth
