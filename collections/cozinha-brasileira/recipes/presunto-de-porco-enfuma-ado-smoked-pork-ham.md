## Ingredients

- a pork leg from wild boar
- salt (substantial amount)
- peppers
- onions
- cloves
- red wine

## Directions

1. Trim a pork leg from wild boar and place it in a brine with substantial salt, peppers, onions, cloves and red wine
2. Leave it in this brine for ten to fifteen days, having placed a piece of board over the quarter and a large weight on top
3. After this time, remove it, dry it well, and expose it to the sun for one day
4. Wrap it in cloth, hang it in smoke for fifteen to twenty days
5. After this time, it serves both to eat it cold and raw, as well as to eat it cooked or roasted
