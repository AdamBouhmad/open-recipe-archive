## Ingredients

- one bottle of milk
- salt
- three spoons of sugar
- four egg whites
- egg yolks
- sugar
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Boil one bottle of milk with salt and three spoons of sugar until reduced to half
2. Add four well-beaten egg whites, continuing to beat
3. Add the egg yolks
4. Let boil for ten minutes
5. Pour onto plates and let cool
6. Cover with sugar and cinnamon powder
7. Serve
