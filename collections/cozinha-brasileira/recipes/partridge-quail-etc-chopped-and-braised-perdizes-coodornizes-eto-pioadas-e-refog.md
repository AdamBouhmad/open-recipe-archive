## Ingredients

- birds roasted on a spit
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- two peppers
- one tablespoon of butter
- handful of breadcrumbs
- one large peeled cucumber without core
- one cup of wine
- slices of peeled lemon

## Directions

1. Roast the birds on a spit
2. remove the bones and cut the meat into pieces
3. add salt, parsley, chopped onion, two peppers, one tablespoon of butter, a handful of breadcrumbs, one large peeled cucumber without core, and boil everything with one cup of wine
4. add some slices of peeled lemon and serve
