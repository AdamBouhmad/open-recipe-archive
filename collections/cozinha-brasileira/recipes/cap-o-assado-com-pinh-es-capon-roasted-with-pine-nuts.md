## Ingredients

- one capon
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- garlic
- cloves of India
- parsley
- onion leaves
- half pound of ham
- half pound of lamb meat
- three cooked eggs

## Directions

1. Rub the capon with salt and pepper, wrap it in a cloth dampened with vinegar
2. Add a clove of garlic mashed, some cloves of India, chopped parsley, and onion leaves
3. After four hours, remove it and fill with half pound of ham, half pound of lamb meat, three cooked eggs
