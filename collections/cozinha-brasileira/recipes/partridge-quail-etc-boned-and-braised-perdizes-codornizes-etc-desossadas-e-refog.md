## Ingredients

- birds roasted on a spit
- onion
- parsley
- one clove of garlic
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- one chalice of white wine
- a pinch of polvilho

## Directions

1. Take the birds roasted on a spit
2. remove their bones and chop the meat with onion, parsley, one clove of garlic, salt and pepper
3. braise this chopped mixture in butter
4. moisten it with one chalice of white wine in which a pinch of polvilho has been dissolved
5. serve on toasted bread
