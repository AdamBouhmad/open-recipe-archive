## Ingredients

- one mutton tongue, skin removed, cut in long slices
- butter
- two spoons of wheat flour
- four glasses of white wine
- two egg yolks
- one handful of raisins
- one dozen peeled and chopped almonds
- one spoon of sugar
- salt
- nutmeg
- thyme

## Directions

1. Turn the slices in hot butter and add two spoons of wheat flour, letting them brown.
2. When the tongue is dark colored, add four glasses of white wine in which two egg yolks have been dissolved.
3. Add a handful of raisins, a dozen peeled and chopped almonds, one spoon of sugar, a little salt, nutmeg and thyme.
4. Cook over coals.
5. Serve with vegetables.
