## Ingredients

- hare
- two spoons fat
- salt
- pepper cloves
- nutmeg
- six tomatoes
- one chopped onion
- one spoon wheat flour
- handful of chosen and washed carapicus
- one cup hot water
- one glass wine
- orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut the hare meat into pieces
2. Sauté in two spoons of fat with salt, pepper cloves, nutmeg, six tomatoes, one chopped onion, one spoon of wheat flour, and a handful of chosen carapicus washed in hot water
3. When everything is impregnated with fat, add one cup of hot water, one glass of wine, and a little orange juice
4. Boil everything for one hour near the fire
5. Serve
