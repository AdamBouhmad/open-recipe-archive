## Ingredients

- turtle eggs
- salt
- fat
- half a bottle of milk
- two spoons of sugar
- one spoon of cornstarch

## Directions

1. Take a portion of turtle eggs, beat with salt and as much fat as needed; fry, and when ready, add half a bottle of boiled milk, two spoons of sugar and one spoon of cornstarch
2. Let boil, stirring continuously, and serve
