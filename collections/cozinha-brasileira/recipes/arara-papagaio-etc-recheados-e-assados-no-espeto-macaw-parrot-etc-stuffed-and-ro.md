## Ingredients

- one bird (arara, papagaio, etc.)
- the bird's liver
- half a quarter of bacon
- two cooked eggs
- one onion
- parsley
- salt
- hot peppers
- olives
- a handful of bread crusts, grated
- brandy
- thin strips of bacon

## Directions

1. Chop the bird's liver with half a quarter of bacon, two cooked eggs, one onion, and parsley.
2. Mix with salt, hot peppers, olives, and a handful of grated bread crusts moistened with brandy.
3. Stuff the bird with this mixture and sew the opening.
4. Lard the breast with thin strips of bacon.
5. Spit the bird, wrap it with thin slices of bacon, and roast over regular heat.
6. When cooked, remove the bacon layer and turn it a few more times until the roast has taken on a good color.
7. Serve with some compote.
