## Ingredients

- clean fish
- fat
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- pepper
- fat

## Directions

1. Clean some fish, pass through fat, wrap in breadcrumbs, place in a covered casserole, and sprinkle with salt and pepper, a little fat; place the casserole over coals, cover with coals and let roast; when roasted, serve
