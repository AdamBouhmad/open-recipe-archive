## Ingredients

- quinces, apples or pears
- twine
- paper

## Directions

1. Tie a piece of twine to the stem of each fruit that you want to pick, cutting the fruit above the knot
2. Place each fruit in a paper wrapper, leaving the ends of the twine tied to the stem of each fruit
3. Close the wrapper, hanging the fruits in an aired room
4. These fruits are conserved as well as possible, becoming even more flavorful with time
