## Ingredients

- fresh veal meat, cut into small pieces
- pork fat
- chayote squash, peeled and chopped
- water
- wheat or corn flour
- salt
- onion leaves
- pepper
- orange juice
- brazas

## Directions

1. Cut fresh veal meat into small pieces and sauté them in pork fat with chopped peeled chayote squash
2. Add a cup of water, two spoonfuls of wheat or corn flour, salt, onion leaves, pepper, and a little orange juice
3. Let cook covered over hot coals for two hours and serve
