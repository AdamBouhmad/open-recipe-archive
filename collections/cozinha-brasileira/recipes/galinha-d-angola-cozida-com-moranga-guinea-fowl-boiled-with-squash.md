## Ingredients

- one guinea fowl
- water
- salt
- one squash
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. After plucking and cleaning the bird, boil it in water and salt with one squash cut into pieces with seeds removed
2. When cooked, remove the pieces of squash, place them on a plate with the skin facing down
3. Cut the bird into pieces and place them on the squash, drizzling melted butter over all and dusting with sugar
4. Place the plate in the oven, let it roast for half an hour, and serve
