## Ingredients

- leftover roasted loin
- tubaras
- beef broth
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover roasted loin into small slices.
2. Cut the tubaras into an equal amount of small slices.
3. Place everything in a casserole with one cup of broth and another cup of white wine.
4. Let it simmer for half an hour over moderate heat.
5. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste.
6. Serve as is.
