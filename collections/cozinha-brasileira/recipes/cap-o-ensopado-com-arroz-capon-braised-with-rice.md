## Ingredients

- one capon, cut into pieces
- four spoons of fat
- half plate of chosen and washed rice
- one bottle of hot water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Brown the capon pieces in four spoons of hot fat
2. Remove them and heat the fat well, then add half plate of chosen and washed rice
3. Stir until all the rice is well coated in fat and heated
4. Add one bottle of hot water, salt, parsley, onion leaves and the capon pieces
5. Let everything boil well once, then let it dry near the fire
6. To serve, place the rice on a plate with the capon pieces on top, and cover everything with grated cheese
