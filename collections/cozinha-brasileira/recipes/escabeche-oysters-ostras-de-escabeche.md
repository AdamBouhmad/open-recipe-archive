## Ingredients

- oysters, opened
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- white wine

## Directions

1. Take opened oysters and heat them over hot coals to scald them in their own water
2. Remove them and place in a pan
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and nutmeg
4. Pour white wine over them so that it covers them
5. Keep them to be served cold
