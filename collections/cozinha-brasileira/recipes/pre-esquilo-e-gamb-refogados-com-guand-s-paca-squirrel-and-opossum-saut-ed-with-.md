## Ingredients

- one paca, squirrel or opossum, cut in quarters
- sour orange juice
- comarí peppers
- salt
- cornmeal
- one cup of water
- one glass of brandy
- half a dozen tomatoes
- pigeon peas
- parsley leaves
- onions

## Directions

1. Cut one of these animals in quarters and moisten them with sour orange juice, comarí peppers and salt
2. Coat them in cornmeal and fry on both sides
3. Add one cup of water, one glass of brandy and half a dozen tomatoes, letting it boil for an hour until cooked
4. On the other hand, boil a portion of pigeon peas in water with salt, parsley leaves and onions
5. When cooked and the water reduced, add them to the meat with the sauce
6. Let it boil a little more and serve
