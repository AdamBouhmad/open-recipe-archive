## Ingredients

- half a pound of white rice
- meat broth
- pulp of ten tomatoes
- a little butter

## Directions

1. Cook half a pound of white rice in meat broth
2. When sufficiently cooked, add the pulp of ten tomatoes, fried a little in butter, passed through a sieve, and cooked in a little broth
3. Let everything boil for another half hour and serve
