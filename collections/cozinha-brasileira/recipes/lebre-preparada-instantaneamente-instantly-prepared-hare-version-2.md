## Ingredients

- one still-warm hare
- four ounces of bacon
- seasoning herbs
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- one and a half bottles of strong red wine
- six spoonfuls of butter
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Cut the still-warm hare into pieces and combine with blood in a large vessel.
2. Add four ounces of bacon, a bunch of seasoning herbs, onions, salt, pepper, and one and a half bottles of strong red wine.
3. Place over brisk fire so the flames surround the vessel; at first boil the wine will catch fire (add a little brandy if desired for quicker effect).
4. After the wine burns off, wrap pieces in six spoonfuls of butter, then in wheat flour.
5. Return to vessel and reduce the sauce.
6. Ready in half an hour.
