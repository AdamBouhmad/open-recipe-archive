## Ingredients

- beef loin
- lard
- butter
- carrots
- onions
- bay leaf
- aromatic herbs
- chives
- cloves
- good broth

## Directions

1. Prepare and stuff a beef loin with lard and tie it in a roll, using string to maintain its shape
2. Butter a casserole and place the loin in it with carrots, onions, bay leaf, aromatic herbs, chives, two cloves and a spoonful of good broth
3. Cover with a sheet of paper oiled with butter and let it simmer
4. Place over gentle heat both above and below; allow it to take color, leaving it to cook the necessary time
5. Remove the vegetables and fat, and let it cook a little longer until the broth becomes thick, to baste the loin
6. Place in a serving dish and pour tomato sauce over it
