## Ingredients

- half a quarter of bacon
- one yam peeled and chopped
- one cup of meat broth
- one cup of white wine
- parsley
- pepper
- nutmeg
- one egg yolk
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Fry half a quarter of bacon
2. When the fat is melted, remove the cracklings and add one peeled and chopped yam
3. Stir and add one cup of meat broth, one cup of white wine, parsley, a little pepper and nutmeg
4. Boil until the yam is cooked, incorporating it with one egg yolk dissolved in one spoon of lemon juice, and serve
