## Ingredients

- a portion of tapir meat
- milk serum
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- ginger
- pepper
- mustard seeds
- one canteen of milk
- one tablespoon of sugar
- two egg yolks
- one small teaspoon of cornstarch
- toasted bread slices

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of tapir meat in milk serum with salt, parsley, onion leaves, ginger, pepper, and whole mustard seeds
2. When cooked, remove from the fire
3. In another pan, boil one canteen of milk with one tablespoon of sugar, two egg yolks, and one small teaspoon of cornstarch
4. Place the meat in this cream
5. Let the whole boil for a short time
6. Serve over toasted bread slices
