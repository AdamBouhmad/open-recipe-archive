## Ingredients

- pork meat
- palm heart
- fat
- hot water
- salt
- onion
- six camari peppers

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of pork meat into small parts, which are braised with a finely chopped palm heart and scalded in very hot water, with everything well saturated with fat.
2. When the palm heart is in circumstances to stick to the bottom of the pan, add a bowl of hot water, salt, an onion, half a dozen camari peppers.
3. Let it boil until the water dries up and serve.
