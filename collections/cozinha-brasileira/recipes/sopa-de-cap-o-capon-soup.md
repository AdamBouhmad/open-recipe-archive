## Ingredients

- 1 capon
- a piece of veal
- a portion of beef liver
- salt
- large onions
- cooked capon meat
- wheat flour
- butter
- egg yolks
- sugar
- peppers

## Directions

1. Make a strong broth with the capon, veal, and beef liver
2. Add necessary salt
3. Take large onions and let them simmer a little in the broth
4. Remove the centers of the onions
5. Fill the onion cavities with a stuffing made from cooked and minced capon meat, wheat flour, butter, egg yolks, sugar, and peppers
6. Let the prepared onions simmer in a little broth
