## Ingredients

- veal meat
- bacon slices
- carrots
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- parsley
- chervil
- broth
- brazas or oven

## Directions

1. Place the veal meat on bacon slices, some carrots, salt, pepper, onions, parsley, chervil and a few cups of broth, and roast over hot coals or in the oven
