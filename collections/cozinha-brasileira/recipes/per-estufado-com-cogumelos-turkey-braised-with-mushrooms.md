## Ingredients

- turkey pieces
- two handfuls of mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- marjoram
- parsley
- two glasses of wine
- two tablespoons of fat
- two cups of meat broth or water
- one tablespoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Take the turkey pieces, two handfuls of mushrooms, salt, pepper, marjoram, parsley, two glasses of wine, two tablespoons of fat and two cups of meat broth or water
2. Put everything in a pan and seal the lid
3. Place over moderate heat and leave to boil for three to four hours
4. Then add one tablespoon of vinegar and serve
