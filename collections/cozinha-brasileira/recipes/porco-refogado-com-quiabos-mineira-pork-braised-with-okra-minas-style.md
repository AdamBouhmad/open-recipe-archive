## Ingredients

- pork meat
- fat
- hot water
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- garlic clove
- cornmeal
- okra

## Directions

1. Take a piece of pork meat, cut it into very small parts, which are scalded, left to cool, and then fried with little fat until they become browned.
2. At this point add two cups of water, salt, parsley, onion, a clove of garlic, a spoonful of cornmeal, and a portion of okra cut into thin slices.
3. Let boil for half an hour, and when the sauce is almost dry and the meat is cooked, serve.
