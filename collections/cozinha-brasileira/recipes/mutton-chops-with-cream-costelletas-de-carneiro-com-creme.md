## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- milk
- salt
- one bay leaf
- two cloves
- one palm heart cut in long pieces

## Directions

1. Put the beaten and trimmed chops in a pan with boiling milk.
2. Add salt, a bay leaf, two cloves, and palm heart cut in long pieces.
3. Simmer over moderate heat.
4. When cooked, remove the chops and palm heart and place on a platter.
5. Thicken the milk with four egg yolks and pour over the chops.
6. Serve.
