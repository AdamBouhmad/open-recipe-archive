## Ingredients

- snails
- butter
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- nutmeg
- water
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Melt two tablespoons of butter
2. Mix in a handful of bread crumbs, pepper, grated nutmeg and add the snails
3. Stir and add one cup of water and two egg yolks diluted in one glass of lemon juice
4. After boiling once more, serve
