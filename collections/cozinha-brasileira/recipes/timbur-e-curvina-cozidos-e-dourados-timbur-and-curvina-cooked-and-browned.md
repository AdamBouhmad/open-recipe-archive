## Ingredients

- fish
- water
- salt
- bay leaf
- onion
- manioc flour or breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Clean a fish, boil it in water, salt, a bay leaf, and a cut onion
2. Once cooked, remove the skin of the fish with the scales, place on a plate
3. Having fried a portion of manioc flour or breadcrumbs until copper colored, pour over the fish, which is served with manioc flour pirão
