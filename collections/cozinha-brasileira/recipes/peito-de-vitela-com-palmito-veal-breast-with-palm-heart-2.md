## Ingredients

- veal breast meat
- lettuce leaves
- salt
- parsley
- palm heart, chopped
- a little fat
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Cut the veal breast meat into equal pieces
2. Blanch the meat
3. Cook with some lettuce leaves, salt and parsley
4. Once cooked, brown the chopped palm heart with a little fat
5. Add meat broth to it
6. Just before serving, place the meat in a casserole, give it another boil, and serve
