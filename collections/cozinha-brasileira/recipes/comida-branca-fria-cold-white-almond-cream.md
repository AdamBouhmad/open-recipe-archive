## Ingredients

- almonds prepared as for comida branca
- isinglass
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare the almonds as already described for comida branca and pass through a fine sieve.
2. Take isinglass broken into pieces and beaten in a cup of water; let the isinglass cook for two and a half hours and mix it with the cream which should be lukewarm.
3. Pour into a vessel or mold that is placed in water to cool.
