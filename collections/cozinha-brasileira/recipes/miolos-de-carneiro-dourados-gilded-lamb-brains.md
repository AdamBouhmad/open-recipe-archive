## Ingredients

- lamb brains
- hot water
- two spoonfuls of butter
- one onion
- chopped parsley
- salt
- ground pepper
- grated cheese
- slices of bread
- eight beaten eggs
- sugar
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Scald the brains in hot water; after chopping, fry them in two spoonfuls of butter, one onion, chopped parsley, salt and ground pepper with an equal amount of grated cheese
2. Pour this fried mixture on the plate over slices of bread fried in butter, cover with grated bread and finally with eight beaten eggs
3. Place the plate in the oven to toast
4. Serve covered with sugar and ground cinnamon
