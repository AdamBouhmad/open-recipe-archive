## Ingredients

- nuts (chestnuts, pine nuts, etc.)
- lard
- white wine
- grated cheese
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook a quantity of these fruits
2. Peel them and crush them in a mortar
3. Sauté this mixture in melted lard
4. Immediately add two cups of white wine, grated cheese and the juice of a lemon
5. Boil for about a quarter of an hour and serve
