## Ingredients

- lamb or kid meat
- sweet olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- garlic
- onion leaves
- bay leaves
- marjoram
- one bottle Malmsey wine
- two egg yolks
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Marinate the meat in sweet olive oil, salt, pepper, parsley, garlic, onion leaves, bay leaves, and marjoram for twelve hours
2. Place over fire with one bottle Malmsey wine
3. Leave over coals for six hours
4. Thicken the sauce with two egg yolks
5. Add lemon juice
6. Serve the meat with this sauce
