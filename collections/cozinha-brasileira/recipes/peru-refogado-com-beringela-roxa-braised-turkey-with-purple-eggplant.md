## Ingredients

- turkey
- one pound of minced salt pork
- parsley
- onion
- pepper
- cloves
- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- one chicara of red wine
- six purple eggplants
- one tablespoon of sugar
- three egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the turkey into pieces and place in a pan with one pound of minced salt pork, parsley, onion, pepper, and cloves
2. When the salt pork is fried, remove the turkey pieces and the rinds
3. Add one tablespoon of wheat flour to the fat
4. When cooled, add one chicara of red wine
5. Add six purple eggplants that have been parboiled in hot water, one tablespoon of sugar, and three egg yolks
6. Return to the fire
7. When simmering, add the turkey meat and finish cooking near the fire
8. Serve
