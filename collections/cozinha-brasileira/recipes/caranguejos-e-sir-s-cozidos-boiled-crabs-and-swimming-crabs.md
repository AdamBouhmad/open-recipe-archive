## Ingredients

- portion of crabs
- one bottle of white wine
- piece of butter
- three cut onions
- bay leaves
- salt
- peppers

## Directions

1. After washing well a portion of crabs, place in a casserole with one bottle of white wine, a piece of butter, three cut onions, bay leaves, salt and peppers
2. Let boil for half an hour and serve after draining and cooling
