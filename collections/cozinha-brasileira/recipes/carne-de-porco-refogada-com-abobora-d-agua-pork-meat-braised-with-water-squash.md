## Ingredients

- one pork roast from wild pig
- whey from milk
- portion of water squashes cut in small pieces
- one tablespoon fine cornmeal
- one bottle water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- ginger

## Directions

1. Soak a pork roast from wild pig in whey for two days.
2. Remove and braise a portion of water squashes cut in small pieces in the same fat.
3. Add one tablespoon of fine cornmeal and finally one bottle of water.
4. Return the meat and let it boil with salt, parsley, onion leaves, and a little ginger.
5. Serve.
