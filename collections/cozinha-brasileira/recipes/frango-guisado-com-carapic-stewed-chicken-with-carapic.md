## Ingredients

- one chicken
- quarter pound of bacon
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- cloves of India
- onion
- a handful of carapicús
- juice of one bitter orange
- one spoon of tapioca
- one spoon of sugar
- three egg yolks
- one glass of wine or water

## Directions

1. Cook chicken cut into pieces with chopped bacon, salt, parsley, pepper, cloves of India, and onion
2. When cooked, remove it and strain the broth, reducing it by half
3. Add a handful of carapicús, juice of one bitter orange, one spoon of tapioca, one spoon of sugar, and three egg yolks mixed in one glass of wine or water
4. Bring the sauce to a boil, add the chicken pieces, finish cooking, and serve with simple cornmeal mush or with the sauce
