## Ingredients

- mutton, cut into pieces
- fat, one spoon
- salt
- cloves
- nutmeg
- milk, a few cups
- four egg yolks
- milk, half cup

## Directions

1. Sauté the mutton cut into pieces in one spoon of fat with little salt, cloves and nutmeg
2. Then add a few cups of milk and boil near the fire until the meat is cooked
3. Add four egg yolks dissolved in half a cup of milk
4. Let it boil a little more and serve
