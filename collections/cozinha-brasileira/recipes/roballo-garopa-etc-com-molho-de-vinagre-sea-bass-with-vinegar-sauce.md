## Ingredients

- Sea bass
- Ten egg yolks
- Half bottle of water
- One teacup of vinegar
- Salt
- Grated nutmeg
- Two spoons of butter
- One spoon of capers

## Directions

1. Cook the fish as indicated
2. Keep it hot in its broth until serving time
3. Beat ten egg yolks with half bottle of water, one teacup of vinegar, salt, grated nutmeg, two spoons of butter and one spoon of capers
4. Stir over fire until it reaches consistency
5. Serve with the fish and boiled peeled potatoes
