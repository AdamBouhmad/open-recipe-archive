## Ingredients

- good piece of beef from the breast and rib section, without removing the hide
- salt
- garlic
- butter

## Directions

1. Take a good piece of beef from the breast and rib section without removing the hide; pass over it a little salt ground with garlic and butter; place it on a spit, exposing it to an ardent fire.
2. The meat becomes well toasted on the outside and inside barely cooked with a little blood still present. This is how most backlands people prepare their churrasco; some, however, roast it in the oven when they have one at their disposal, which rarely happens in those desert regions.
3. Serve at table without sauce, if desired, or then with a spicy sauce.
