## Ingredients

- puff pastry dough
- butter
- pulp of peaches
- apples
- sugar syrup
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Place in a well-buttered form a layer of dough
2. Place on the bottom layer another of peach pulp
3. Place on this one of apples, and so on until filling the form, with the last layer being dough
4. Take to the oven, remove and turn over onto a serving dish
5. Cover with sugar syrup and cinnamon, and serve
