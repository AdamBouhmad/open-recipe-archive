## Ingredients

- one duck
- breadcrumb crumb
- duck liver, chopped
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- one onion
- two cloves

## Directions

1. After plucking, cleaning and washing, fill the duck with breadcrumb crumb mixed with the chopped duck liver, all moistened with white wine, seasoned with salt, pepper, one onion and two cloves
2. Roast it on a spit, at first over moderate fire so the excess fat has time to melt, and then over livelier fire so the roast takes the proper color
3. When cooked, serve with salads
