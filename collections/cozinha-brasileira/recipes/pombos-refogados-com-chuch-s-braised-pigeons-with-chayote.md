## Ingredients

- small pigeons
- butter
- chuchús, peeled and split and blanched
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- two glasses of white wine

## Directions

1. Open the small pigeons in the middle and fry them (always turning them) in hot butter over the fire
2. When they have taken on good color, add a portion of chuchús peeled, split, and blanched
3. Add salt, parsley, onion leaves, and two glasses of white wine
4. Cover the pan and let it cook for half an hour over moderate fire
5. Serve afterwards
