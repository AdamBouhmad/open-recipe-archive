## Ingredients

- beef leg
- water
- salt
- carrots
- onions
- clove
- cabbage leaves
- brandy
- wheat flour
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take the beef and leave it to boil for five hours over good fire, having added to the water salt, carrots, onions, clove, the chopped leaves of a cabbage, and a glass of brandy
2. When the beef is cooked, remove it from the fire as well as the seasonings, arranging everything symmetrically on the plate
3. Leave the broth to thicken, adding at the end a little wheat flour and vinegar
4. Leave this sauce over the beef and vegetables before serving
