## Ingredients

- veal breasts
- water
- ham
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion leaves
- wheat flour
- broth
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place the veal breasts in cold water on the fire
2. Give them a boil, then remove and let cool
3. Cut into pieces that are not too large
4. Fry with chopped ham, a little salt, pepper, parsley, onion leaves, half a spoon of wheat flour
5. Add a cup of water or broth and let cook
6. When cooked, remove and pass the sauce through a sieve
7. Add three egg yolks, return to the fire for five to six minutes
8. Serve
