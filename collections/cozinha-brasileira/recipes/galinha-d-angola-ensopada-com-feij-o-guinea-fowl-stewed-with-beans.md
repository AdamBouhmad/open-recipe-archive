## Ingredients

- one guinea fowl
- fat
- beans or fresh fava beans
- water
- salt
- one orange

## Directions

1. Cut one bird into pieces; braise them in fat
2. When cooked, remove them, and braise in the same fat a portion of beans or fresh fava beans cooked in water and salt
3. Add the pieces of the bird and the juice of one orange; boil everything for half an hour near the fire and serve
