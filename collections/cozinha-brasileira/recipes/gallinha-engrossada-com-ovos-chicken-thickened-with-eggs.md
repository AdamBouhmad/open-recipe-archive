## Ingredients

- chicken pieces
- fat
- one bottle of water
- one glass of white wine
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- pepper
- lemon juice
- three egg yolks
- a pinch of starch
- one tablespoon of sugar
- juice of one lemon
- one glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Braise the chicken pieces in fat and when browned, add one bottle of water, one glass of white wine, salt, parsley, onion leaves, pepper, and lemon juice
2. When cooked, add three egg yolks beaten with a pinch of starch, one tablespoon of sugar, the juice of one lemon, and one glass of white wine
3. Simmer a little longer
4. Serve
