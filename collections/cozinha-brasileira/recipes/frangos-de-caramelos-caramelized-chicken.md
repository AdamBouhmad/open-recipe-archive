## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- nutmeg
- parsley
- sugar
- sauce from roasting
- glass of red wine

## Directions

1. Season a chicken with salt, nutmeg and chopped parsley
2. Rub it well with these seasonings and roast on the spit, covered with paper
3. When roasted, dissolve one pound of sugar in the sauce that dripped down
4. Add a glass of red wine to it and place the chicken in this broth
5. Let it boil a little and serve, sprinkling it with cinnamon
