## Ingredients

- repolho (cabbage)
- barril de vinagre or vinho (barrel that was for vinegar or wine)
- sal (salt)
- bagas de zimbro (juniper berries)
- gengibre (ginger)

## Directions

1. Chop a cabbage and place it in a barrel that was for vinegar or wine, in layers three fingers thick, and on top of this a layer of salt, with different spices, such as juniper berries and ginger
2. Cover it then with a lid that fits snugly in the barrel, and place a heavy weight on top so that the cabbage is well compressed, and place the barrel in a cool place
3. After eight to ten days, it will be ready for use
