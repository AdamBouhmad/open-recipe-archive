## Ingredients

- four pounds mutton, cut into pieces
- fat, one spoon
- water, one bowl
- carapicú, handful
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- cornstarch, one spoon
- juice of one terra orange

## Directions

1. Cut four pounds mutton into pieces and sauté in one spoon fat without letting them brown
2. Add one bowl of water, a handful of carapicú, salt, parsley, year-round onion leaves, one spoon cornstarch and the juice of one terra orange
3. Let boil over coals for two hours and serve with mineiro cornmeal mush
