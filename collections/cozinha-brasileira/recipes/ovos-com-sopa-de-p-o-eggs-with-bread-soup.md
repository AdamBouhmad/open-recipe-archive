## Ingredients

- breadcrumb
- half a bottle of milk
- salt
- ground cinnamon
- lemon zest
- half a spoonful of butter
- a dozen eggs
- a spoonful of melted butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of breadcrumb in half a bottle of milk with salt, ground cinnamon, lemon zest, and half a spoonful of butter.
2. When the bread is broken down, add a dozen well-beaten eggs, mix everything, and pour into a casserole containing a spoonful of melted butter.
3. Place on the fire or over coals, let boil until cooked.
4. Turn the casserole onto a plate, cover with sugar, and serve.
