## Ingredients

- Beef broth
- Onions
- Cloves
- Bay leaves
- Herbs for seasoning
- Peppers
- Wine
- Sardines
- Capers
- Small onion
- Crackers

## Directions

1. Take four cups of good beef broth, four onions, some cloves, bay leaves, a bouquet of seasoning herbs and four to six peppers
2. Let it boil for half an hour
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Add two cups of wine, some chopped sardines, some spoonfuls of capers, a chopped small onion and four spoonfuls of crushed crackers
5. Let it boil another half hour
6. Serve
