## Ingredients

- white beans
- butter
- wheat flour
- onion
- vinegar
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of white beans.
2. In another part, fry two spoonfuls of butter, two of wheat flour and a chopped onion.
3. Add two spoonfuls of vinegar and stir.
4. Add the cooked beans and broth, salt and parsley.
5. Let it boil and serve.
