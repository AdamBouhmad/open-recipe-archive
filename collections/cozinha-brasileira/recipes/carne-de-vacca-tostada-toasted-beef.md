## Ingredients

- bacon cut into small pieces
- bread crumbs
- mushrooms
- onion
- parsley
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- meat broth
- cognac or white wine
- butter

## Directions

1. Fry bacon cut into small pieces.
2. Upon it scatter a little bread crumbs, mushrooms cut into small pieces, onion, parsley, one clove of chopped garlic, salt and pepper.
3. Add slices of beef which are then covered with mushrooms, onions, parsley, salt, pepper and bread crumbs.
4. Moisten these slices with meat broth and braise over gentle fire.
5. If desired to toast them, place over lively fire; otherwise serve with some sauce.
6. One can also, instead of bacon, add a little butter and include before cooking a little cognac or white wine.
