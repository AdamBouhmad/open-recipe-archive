## Ingredients

- pigeon meat and giblets
- parsley
- onion
- pepper
- cinnamon
- sugar
- bacon slices
- bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove the meat and giblets from the birds and chop very well with parsley, onion, pepper, cinnamon, and sugar
2. Line a pan with bacon slices, place the chopped mixture on buttered bread slices
3. Cover the pan and cook with coals below and above
4. Serve
