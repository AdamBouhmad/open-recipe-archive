## Ingredients

- dozen lettuce plants
- water
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- clove
- bay leaves
- onion
- peas
- meat broth
- bread

## Directions

1. Wash a dozen lettuce plants, let them drain well, and after splitting them in half without removing the leaves, place them carefully in a casserole containing three to four cups of water with pepper and salt
2. Let them boil on moderate heat for two hours, then add parsley, clove, bay leaves, onion
3. When this mixture is dry, pour it into a tureen containing fresh peas cooked in meat broth
4. Finally add toasted bread cut into pieces and pour meat broth over them
