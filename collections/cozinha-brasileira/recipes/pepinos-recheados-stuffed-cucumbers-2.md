## Ingredients

- six large cucumbers
- one quarter pound of mutton meat
- one quarter pound of fresh bacon
- one chopped onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- two egg yolks
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- vinegar
- two spoonfuls of fat
- one glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Take six large cucumbers and pierce at the base so the seeds come out through the hole
2. Make a hash of one quarter pound of mutton meat, one quarter pound of fresh bacon, one chopped onion, parsley, salt, pepper, two egg yolks, and one spoonful of wheat flour
3. Fill the cucumbers with this stuffing and close the hole again
4. Scald the cucumbers with vinegar
5. Place in a casserole with two spoonfuls of fat and one glass of white wine
6. Put in the oven, let it boil and serve
