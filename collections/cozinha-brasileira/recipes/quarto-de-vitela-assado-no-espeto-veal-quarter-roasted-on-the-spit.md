## Ingredients

- veal quarter
- water
- salt
- pepper
- garlic clove
- parsley
- thyme
- chive leaves
- marjoram
- lemon juice
- paper
- butter
- meat sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the veal quarter for five minutes, remove it from water and let it cool
2. Lard it gently
3. Soak it for six hours in salt, pepper, a garlic clove, parsley, thyme, chive leaves, marjoram, all chopped, and a little lemon juice
4. Spit it covering it with paper greased with butter
5. Once roasted, remove the paper and serve with some meat sauce or any other sauce
