## Ingredients

- one quarter of bacon
- one onion, chopped
- one pound of mutton, chopped
- one pound of mutton, chopped
- breadcrumb softened with milk
- four egg yolks
- nutmeg
- salt
- parsley, chopped
- one spoonful of wheat flour

## Directions

1. Cut the bacon into pieces and fry it a little with a chopped onion and a pound of chopped mutton meat
2. Remove from the heat and add another pound of chopped mutton meat, breadcrumb softened with milk, four egg yolks, nutmeg, salt, chopped parsley and one spoonful of wheat flour
3. Form balls with this mixture and fry them in butter or lard
