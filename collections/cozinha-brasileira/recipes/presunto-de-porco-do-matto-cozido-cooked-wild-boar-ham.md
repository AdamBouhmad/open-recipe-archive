## Ingredients

- one quarter of wild boar
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- marjoram
- lemon juice
- ginger
- water
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Place a quarter of the wild boar in a brine made of salt, pepper, cut onion, marjoram, lemon juice, ginger, and a little water
2. Leave in this brine for three days, turning every twelve hours
3. Wrap in cloth and cook in the same brine for five to six hours with some potatoes over a very active fire
4. When cooked, let cool, remove the cloth, and serve cold
