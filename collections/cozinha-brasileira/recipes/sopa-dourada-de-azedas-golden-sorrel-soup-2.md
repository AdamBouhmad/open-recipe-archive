## Ingredients

- a portion of sorrel
- one head of lettuce
- a little chervil, finely chopped
- a little fat
- a portion of meat broth
- eight egg yolks
- a portion of cold broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as sorrel soup
2. Before pouring the soup over the bread, thicken the broth with eight beaten egg yolks mixed in a cup of cold broth
3. Bring to a boil once more
4. Pour over toasted bread slices
