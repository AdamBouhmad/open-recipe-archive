## Ingredients

- one fish cut into steaks the thickness of a finger
- a little chopped lard
- some onions
- chopped parsley
- salt
- peppers
- one garlic clove
- three to four cloves
- two cups of water
- two egg yolks
- one cup of wine
- capers (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the fish steaks, a little chopped lard, some onions, chopped parsley, salt, peppers, one garlic clove, three to four cloves and two cups of water in a pot and bring to boil
2. Cover and when cooked, remove it and strain the broth thickened with two beaten egg yolks and one cup of wine, and serve
3. If capers are available, add two to three spoons of them
