## Ingredients

- veal loin
- bacon
- broth
- ham
- parsley
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- carrots
- lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the veal loin until flat, wrapped in a strong cloth
2. Boil for five minutes to tenderize
3. Lard it in this state
4. Place in a pan with bacon, broth, a little ham, parsley, onion, salt, pepper, and carrots
5. When cooked, strain the sauce and let it thicken
6. Return the loin to the vessel and let it cook over coals until serving time
7. Before serving, add some slices of lemon
