## Ingredients

- one bird (arara, papagaio, etc.)
- wine and garlic marinade
- salt
- hot pepper juice (pimenta comari)
- parsley
- chopped onion leaves
- cassava flour
- two spoonfuls of fat
- two cups of water
- one glass of local orange juice
- one dozen eggplants
- the bird's liver, chopped
- a little chopped bacon
- grated cheese
- bread crumb
- wine
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the bird into pieces and marinate in wine-and-garlic with salt, hot pepper juice, parsley, and chopped onion leaves for four to five hours.
2. Coat in cassava flour and sauté in two spoonfuls of fat.
3. Add two cups of water and one glass of local orange juice.
4. Prepare one dozen eggplants stuffed with a mixture made from the bird's liver, a little chopped bacon, grated cheese, and bread crumb moistened with wine and sugar.
5. Add the stuffed eggplants to the pan.
6. Let boil for half an hour near the fire and serve.
