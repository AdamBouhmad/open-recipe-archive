## Ingredients

- one capon
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- one clove of garlic, crushed
- cloves
- parsley, chopped
- onion leaves
- half pound of ham
- half pound of mutton meat
- three cooked eggs
- one onion
- pine nuts or chestnuts
- butter
- paper

## Directions

1. Rub the capon with salt and pepper, wrap it in a cloth moistened with vinegar
2. Add to it a crushed clove of garlic, some cloves, chopped parsley and onion leaves; let it sit for four hours
3. Remove the cloth and fill the capon with a mixture of half pound of ham, half pound of mutton meat, three cooked eggs, one onion, salt and pepper, all finely chopped
4. Place on a spit and roast wrapped in paper, anointed with butter
5. Meanwhile, cook a portion of pine nuts or chestnuts in water; once cooked, shell them
6. Serve the capon on a plate with the pine nuts or chestnuts arranged around it
