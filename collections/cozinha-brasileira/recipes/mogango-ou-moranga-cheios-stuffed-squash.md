## Ingredients

- ripe squash
- water
- beef
- salt
- garlic
- peppers
- vinegar
- lard
- olives
- sausage

## Directions

1. Take a ripe, dry squash
2. Open a hole at the bottom so the seeds come out through there
3. Cook it whole in water until very well cooked
4. Separately, chop with a knife a quantity of beef
5. Season with salt, garlic, peppers and vinegar
6. Sauté in very hot lard
7. Add some olives and pieces of sausage and let them cook
8. When cooked, fill the squash with this mixture and pieces of sausage
9. Line a frying pan with plenty of lard, place the filled squash in it and roast in the oven
10. When roasted, serve
