## Ingredients

- a portion of fresh green fava beans
- one spoonful of fat
- some chopped onion leaves
- one cup of water
- salt
- parsley
- a little lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a portion of fresh green fava beans
2. Fry one spoonful of fat with some chopped onion leaves
3. Add one cup of water and the fava beans
4. Boil for half an hour
5. Add salt, parsley and a little lemon juice or vinegar
6. Serve
