## Ingredients

- three spoons of English mustard sauce
- three spoons of vinegar
- three spoons of water
- one spoon of butter
- three egg yolks
- two spoons of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix well three spoons of English mustard sauce, three of vinegar, three of water, one of butter, three of egg yolks, and two of sugar
2. Place over coals and heat, stirring constantly, until it begins to boil
3. Serve with meats of all kinds
