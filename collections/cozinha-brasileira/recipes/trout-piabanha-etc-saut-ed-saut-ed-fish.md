## Ingredients

- clean fish
- water
- salt
- white wine
- one clove of clove
- bay leaves
- one onion
- tarragon
- salt
- pepper
- one spoon of fat
- one spoon of corn meal
- lime juice

## Directions

1. After cleaning the fish, boil in water and salt; remove, dry and place to cook in white wine with one clove, bay leaves, one onion, a little tarragon, salt and pepper; when cooked, melt one spoon of fat, stir, add one spoon of fine corn meal until it takes color; add the fish and broth; and having boiled a little, serve with a little lime juice
