## Ingredients

- bacon, sliced
- parsley
- small onions
- half a bay leaf
- one glass of white wine
- small whole onions
- carrots, sliced
- lean bacon
- salt
- broken Indian pepper

## Directions

1. Take some bacon cut into slices and mash it with parsley, small onions and half a bay leaf, all finely chopped
2. Fill the beef heart with this prepared bacon
3. Place in a casserole a glass of white wine, small whole onions, carrots cut into slices of lean bacon, salt, and broken Indian pepper
4. Put the heart in the casserole, cover carefully with hot coals on top, placing it over low fire
5. Let it cook slowly for five to six hours and serve
