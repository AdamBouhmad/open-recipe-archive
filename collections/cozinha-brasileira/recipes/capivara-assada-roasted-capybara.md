## Ingredients

- one capybara loin
- aguardente
- cloves
- parsley
- onions
- ginger
- marjoram
- thyme
- salt
- peppers
- juice from land orange or lemon juice

## Directions

1. After disinfecting the capybara loin, place it to soak for six hours in aguardente, cloves, parsley, onions, ginger, marjoram, thyme, salt, and peppers
2. Thread it onto a spit and roast over an open fire, moistening it with juice from land orange
3. When cooked, serve
