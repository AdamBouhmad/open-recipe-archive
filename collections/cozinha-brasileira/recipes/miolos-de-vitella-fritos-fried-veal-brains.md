## Ingredients

- veal brains
- dried breadcrumbs or crackers
- salt
- pepper
- onion leaves
- butter

## Directions

1. After cleaning and cooking the brains as described for cold brains
2. Coat in a layer of ground dried breadcrumbs or crackers, salt, pepper, and chopped onion leaves
3. Fry in butter over moderate heat
