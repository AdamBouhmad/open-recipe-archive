## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- salt
- pepper
- wheat flour
- butter
- four glasses of white wine
- one onion
- one bunch of parsley
- one spoon of toasted wheat flour

## Directions

1. Beat the chops and trim them into a regular shape.
2. Season with salt and pepper and coat in wheat flour.
3. Brown on both sides in butter.
4. Add four glasses of white wine, sliced onion, and a bunch of parsley.
5. When cooked, remove the parsley and add one spoon of toasted wheat flour to thicken the sauce.
6. Add one spoon of vinegar and serve.
