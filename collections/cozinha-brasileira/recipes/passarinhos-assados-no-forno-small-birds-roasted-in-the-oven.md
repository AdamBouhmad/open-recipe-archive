## Ingredients

- one dozen small birds
- two spoonfuls of fat
- one spoonful of fine cornmeal or wheat flour
- one glass of wine
- salt
- peppers
- slices of peeled lemon

## Directions

1. Braise a dozen small birds in two spoonfuls of fat, dust with one spoonful of fine cornmeal or wheat flour, one glass of wine, salt, peppers and some slices of peeled lemon
2. Let them roast over coals until cooked, and serve
