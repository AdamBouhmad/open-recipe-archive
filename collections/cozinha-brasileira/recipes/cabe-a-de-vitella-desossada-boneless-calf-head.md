## Ingredients

- calf head
- calf brains
- calf tongue
- calf or chicken meat
- wheat flour
- eggs
- raisins
- beef broth
- white wine
- carrots
- herbs for seasoning
- pepper
- cloves
- lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the head well and open from the underside and remove the bones, except for the upper part of the skull, without damaging the outer skin
2. Cut the brains, eyes, and tongue with some calf or chicken meat and make a mass of these offals with three spoonfuls of wheat flour, three eggs and some raisins
3. Fill the head with this mass, giving it its original shape, sewing the openings with string
4. Cook in beef broth to which half a bottle of white wine, carrots, a bundle of herbs for seasoning, pepper, and two cloves are added
5. When cooked, remove and let the broth thicken by adding a spoonful of wheat flour or cornstarch and the juice of a lemon
6. Remove the string from the head, place on a platter and serve when the sauce has been poured over it
