## Ingredients

- goose pieces
- fat
- water
- salt
- pepper
- one onion
- parsley
- marjoram
- fern sprouts
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Take the pieces of goose, pass them in hot fat, and cook them, adding water, salt, pepper, one onion, parsley and marjoram.
2. When almost cooked, pour into the broth a portion of washed fern sprouts, and after two boils, remove them and pour over a platter covered with breadcrumbs.
3. Place the pieces of goose on top, pouring over everything a portion of butter, in which a finely chopped onion should have been fried; and serve thus.
