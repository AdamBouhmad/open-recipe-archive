## Ingredients

- one cooked chicken
- two handfuls of breadcrumbs
- a quarter of washed raisins
- two spoons of butter
- five egg yolks
- nutmeg
- clove
- sugar

## Directions

1. After the chicken is cooked, fill it with the following mixture: take two handfuls of breadcrumbs, a quarter of washed raisins, two spoons of butter, five egg yolks, nutmeg, clove and sugar.
2. Mix everything well and fill the chicken.
3. Place it in a pan with a little fat and roast until it is a good color, and serve with salad.
