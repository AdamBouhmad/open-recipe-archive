## Ingredients

- eggplants
- cheese
- breadcrumbs
- cooked egg yolks
- butter
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook some eggplants
2. Split them lengthwise so the pieces do not separate
3. Remove the pulp which is mashed with cheese, breadcrumbs, cooked egg yolks, butter, cinnamon and a little sugar
4. Fill the eggplants with this mixture
5. Tie the two halves and fry them in butter
6. Place them on a plate and serve sprinkled with sugar and cinnamon
