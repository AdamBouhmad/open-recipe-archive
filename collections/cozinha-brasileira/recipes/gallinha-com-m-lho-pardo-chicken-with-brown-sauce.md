## Ingredients

- chicken blood
- one glass of vinegar
- two tablespoons of fat
- one bottle of water
- salt
- one onion, cut
- a little grated nutmeg
- one tablespoon of fine manioc flour

## Directions

1. When killing the chicken, collect its blood in a plate with one glass of vinegar
2. Pluck and cut the chicken into pieces
3. Braise the pieces in two tablespoons of fat
4. Add one bottle of water, salt, the cut onion, a little grated nutmeg, the blood dissolved with vinegar, and one tablespoon of fine manioc flour
5. Let everything simmer
6. Serve; if the sauce is too sharp, add one tablespoon of sugar
