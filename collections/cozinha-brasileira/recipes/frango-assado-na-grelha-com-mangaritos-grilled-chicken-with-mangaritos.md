## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- garlic juice
- vinegar
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- mangaritos

## Directions

1. Take the chicken and cut it into four pieces
2. Soak the pieces with salt, chopped parsley, pepper, a little garlic juice and a spoonful of vinegar
3. Wrap in breadcrumbs and roast on the grill, dripping a little butter on it from time to time
4. Turn to roast on both sides at the same time
5. Fry some mangaritos in butter and after cutting them in slices, serve them around the roasted chicken pieces
