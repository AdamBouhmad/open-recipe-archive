## Ingredients

- one water squash
- milk
- salt
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- two spoonfuls of butter
- parsley
- onion

## Directions

1. Boil a peeled and chopped water squash in milk and salt
2. When cooked and the milk is almost dry, add one spoonful of wheat flour fried in two spoonfuls of butter, parsley and onion
3. Once it has boiled a few times, serve
