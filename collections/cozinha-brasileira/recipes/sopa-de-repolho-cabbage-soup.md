## Ingredients

- one head of cabbage
- hot water
- slices of beef
- bacon
- one clove
- two carrots
- one large onion
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Blanch one head of cabbage in hot water and let drain
2. Separate its leaves and place them in a pan containing slices of beef covered with bacon, along with one clove, two carrots, and one large onion
3. After cooking, remove the cabbage with the meat slices; place everything in a tureen over sliced bread
4. Strain the broth and pour it into the tureen over the cabbage
