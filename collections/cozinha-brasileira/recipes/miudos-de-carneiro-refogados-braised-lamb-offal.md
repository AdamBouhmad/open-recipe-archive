## Ingredients

- lamb lungs
- liver
- heart
- kidneys
- feet
- water
- salt
- two spoonfuls of butter
- two spoonfuls of wheat flour
- one spoonful of sugar
- one onion
- two cups of red wine
- meat broth
- one dozen okras
- cornmeal mush or tapioca flour

## Directions

1. Take the lungs, liver, heart, kidneys and feet of the lamb, wash and clean everything and scald in hot water
2. Then cook in water and salt; when cooked, remove the bones from the feet and cut all the meat into small pieces
3. On the other hand, fry two spoonfuls of butter with two of wheat flour, one spoonful of sugar and one cut onion until they become dark copper color
4. Add two cups of red wine, the same amount of broth and one dozen okras cut into rounds; add the cooked offal and boil everything for half an hour over coals
5. Serve with cornmeal mush or tapioca flour porridge
