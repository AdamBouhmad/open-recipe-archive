## Ingredients

- ham
- spinach
- sliced bread
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. After removing salt and cooking the ham as previously explained, place it for a short time in a mild oven to dry the surface, having previously rounded it
2. Serve on a platter with sautéed spinach at the bottom, surrounded by toasted bread slices
3. Top with Spanish sauce
4. Alternatively, serve it over a stew of carrots, asparagus tips, guandú, peas, cabbage, broad beans, mangalós, fern sprouts, lentils, or chickpeas
