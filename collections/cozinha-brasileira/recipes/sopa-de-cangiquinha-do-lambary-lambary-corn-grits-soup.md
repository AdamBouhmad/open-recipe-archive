## Ingredients

- corn grits
- salt
- butter
- two egg yolks
- bread, fried
- raisins

## Directions

1. Boil corn grits until quite thick
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Season with salt and butter
4. Add two beaten egg yolks
5. Add pieces of fried bread and some raisins
