## Ingredients

- small birds
- salt
- bananas
- bread crumbs
- sugar
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Season the small birds with salt, fry them with peeled bananas split in half
2. When cooked, dust with a portion of grated bread
3. Place them on a plate, cover with a layer of sugar and ground cinnamon, and serve
