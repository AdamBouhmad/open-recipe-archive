## Ingredients

- leftover lamb
- one tablespoon cornmeal
- one teaspoon cornstarch
- one onion
- parsley
- salt
- two wine glasses
- same amount water or meat broth
- sugar
- two egg yolks
- yam stems, peeled and boiled
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cut leftover lamb into pieces and fry until browned with one tablespoon cornmeal, one teaspoon cornstarch, one onion, parsley, and salt
2. Add two wine glasses of wine, same amount of water or meat broth, sugar, and two beaten egg yolks
3. Boil for one hour over coals
4. Serve the sauce over peeled, scalded yam stems boiled in water and salt for at least one hour until they lose their bitter taste
5. Cover with a layer of grated cheese
