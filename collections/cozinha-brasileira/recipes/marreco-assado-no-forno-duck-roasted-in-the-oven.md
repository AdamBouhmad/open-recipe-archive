## Ingredients

- duck liver
- two cooked eggs
- handful of carapicú
- one onion
- salt
- pepper
- bacon slices
- one glass of white wine
- clove of India
- parsley
- salad or pulp pasta

## Directions

1. Chop the duck liver with two cooked eggs, a handful of carapicú and one onion; mix with salt and pepper and fill the duck
2. Place it in the oven in a pan lined with bacon slices, one glass of white wine, salt, clove of India, parsley
3. Let it boil until the meat is cooked and serve with some salad or pulp pasta
