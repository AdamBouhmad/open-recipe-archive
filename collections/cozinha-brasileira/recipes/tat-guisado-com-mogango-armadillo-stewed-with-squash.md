## Ingredients

- one armadillo
- water
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- onion leaves
- one squash, cut and cleaned
- fat
- one minced onion
- cornmeal
- one cup of broth
- half a dozen tomatoes

## Directions

1. Boil an armadillo in water with salt, peppers, parsley, onion leaves, and a cut and cleaned squash
2. When the armadillo is cooked, remove and cut into pieces
3. Braise in fat with a minced onion and dust with cornmeal
4. Add one cup of the broth in which the armadillo was boiled and half a dozen tomatoes
5. Serve over the pieces of squash
