## Ingredients

- palm heart
- asparagus
- water
- salt
- melted fat

## Directions

1. Cut a palm heart into pieces half a palm in length; for asparagus, remove the white part from below; boil either of these vegetables in water and salt for a quarter of an hour; pour out the water and dry the vegetables with a towel; place in a pan, pour melted fat over the top, and keep.
