## Ingredients

- oysters
- butter
- onion
- parsley
- pepper
- nutmeg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Open two or three dozen oysters and place their meat and water in a pan
2. Heat until almost boiling
3. Place in a fireproof dish with two tablespoons of butter, some chopped onion, parsley, pepper and grated nutmeg
4. Heat to melt the butter and add the oysters
5. Cover with a layer of bread crumbs, cover the pot, place coals on top
6. When the bread has a good color, serve
