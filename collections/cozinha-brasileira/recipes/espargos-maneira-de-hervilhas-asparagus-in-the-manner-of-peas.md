## Ingredients

- asparagus shoots
- water
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- wheat flour
- meat broth
- nutmeg
- sugar
- three egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take asparagus shoots and cut into small pieces
2. Place in a casserole with boiling salted water
3. When they have boiled once, remove with a skimmer and let drain
4. Afterwards place in cold water, being careful not to crush them
5. Half an hour before serving, let drain and sauté with a little butter, salt and broken Indian pepper
6. Sprinkle with a good pinch of wheat flour, moisten with a little broth and add salt and peppers (if lacking, use grated nutmeg and sugar)
7. Let boil, thicken the sauce with three egg yolks and serve
