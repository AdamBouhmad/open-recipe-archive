## Ingredients

- eight pounds veal meat
- four pounds pork fat
- four pounds pork meat
- salt
- saltpeter
- pepper
- cloves
- marjoram
- garlic
- casing

## Directions

1. Cut all meat finely and pass through a sieve
2. Season with salt, a little saltpeter, pepper, cloves, marjoram, and crushed garlic
3. Leave in the vessel until the next day
4. Fill the casings and hang in smoke
5. Serve raw or fried in butter
