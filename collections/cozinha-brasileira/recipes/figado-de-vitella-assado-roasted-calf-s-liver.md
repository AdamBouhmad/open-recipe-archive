## Ingredients

- calf liver
- wheat flour
- one egg
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into slices
2. Pass it first in wheat flour, then in beaten egg, and afterwards again in wheat flour
3. Roast it on the grill and serve with melted butter
