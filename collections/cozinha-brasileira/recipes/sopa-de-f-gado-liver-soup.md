## Ingredients

- a piece of cooked liver
- 1/2 jar white wine
- 2 jars water
- lemon peels
- cinnamon
- raisins
- sugar
- salt
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Take a piece of cooked liver and pass it through a sieve by repeated friction
2. Add to it half a jar of white wine, 2 jars of water, lemon peels, cinnamon, raisins, sugar, and a little salt
3. Bring it to a boil
4. Thicken it with some egg yolks
