## Ingredients

- Fish
- Sea water or well salted water
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Bay leaves
- Good sweet oil

## Directions

1. Clean the fish by removing the scales
2. Scald with sea water or well salted water
3. Let cool in the same water
4. Put in a pan with cloves, pepper, nutmeg and bay leaves
5. Pour good sweet oil over it, with the fish completely covered
6. Keep for later use
7. Large fish may be preserved this way, but they should be cut into steaks before being prepared
