## Ingredients

- one bird (arara, papagaio, etc.)
- water
- salt
- a portion of pine nuts
- breadcrumbs
- one chopped onion
- two spoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Boil the bird in water and salt with a portion of pine nuts.
2. When cooked, remove the bird and separate the meat from the bones, cutting it into pieces.
3. Dust the meat with breadcrumbs and one chopped onion.
4. Peel and crush the pine nuts into a paste and fry in a casserole with two spoonfuls of butter.
