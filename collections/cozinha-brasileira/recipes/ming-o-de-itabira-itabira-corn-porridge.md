## Ingredients

- a good portion of butter
- one bowl of cornmeal
- water
- a little sugar
- cinnamon
- anise

## Directions

1. Fry a good portion of butter with one bowl of cornmeal
2. When it turns cinnamon color, add water and boil for one hour with a little sugar, cinnamon, and anise
3. Finally add one more bowl of milk
