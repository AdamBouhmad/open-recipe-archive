## Ingredients

- a piece of tapir meat
- salt
- water
- onion
- parsley
- pepper
- cloves
- ginger
- three tablespoons of fat
- two tablespoons of wheat flour
- one onion, minced
- one cup of strained broth
- another cup of milk cream
- a portion of sapucaia nuts, cut

## Directions

1. Take a piece of tapir meat and boil it with salt, water, onion, parsley, pepper, cloves, and ginger
2. When cooked, remove the meat and sauté in three tablespoons of fat with two tablespoons of wheat flour and one minced onion
3. Add one cup of strained broth and another cup of milk cream, and a portion of cut sapucaia nuts
4. Let it boil and serve
