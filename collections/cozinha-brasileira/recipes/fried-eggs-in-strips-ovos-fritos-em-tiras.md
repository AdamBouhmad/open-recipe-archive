## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- a little brandy
- salt
- peppers
- sardines
- little fat
- chopped salad

## Directions

1. Beat eight egg yolks with a little brandy, a little salt and peppers
2. Pour into a pan over coals until hardened; then cut into strips the thickness of a finger
3. Place these strips interspersed with sardines in a pan with little fat; let heat, and serve with chopped salad
