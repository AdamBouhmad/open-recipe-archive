## Ingredients

- portion of lobsters
- water
- salt
- handful of breadcrumbs
- milk
- one spoon of butter
- two egg yolks
- one spoon of sugar
- nutmeg
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Boil a portion of lobsters in water and salt
2. When cooked, remove the tail and shield
3. Fill the shield with a mixture made from a handful of breadcrumbs softened in milk and kneaded with one spoon of butter, two egg yolks, one spoon of sugar, a little grated nutmeg and wheat flour enough to make a paste
4. Shell the tails afterwards
