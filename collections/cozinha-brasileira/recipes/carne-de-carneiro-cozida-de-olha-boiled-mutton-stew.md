## Ingredients

- mutton with bones
- water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- small cabbage, split
- cassava root, split
- sweet potatoes
- pumpkin pieces
- lemon juice
- six comarí peppers, crushed
- mustard powder

## Directions

1. Take a piece of mutton with bones, soak in cold water for some time, then place on fire with cold water, salt, parsley, onion leaves, a small split cabbage, split cassava root, sweet potatoes and pumpkin pieces
2. Let everything boil until well cooked
3. Strain the broth, remove the fat and reduce to four cups
4. Mix in lemon juice, six crushed comarí peppers and mustard powder
5. Serve this sauce together with the stewed meat
