## Ingredients

- one snake, cut into pieces
- two spoons of fat
- one chopped onion
- one spoon of wheat flour
- one cup of water
- salt
- parsley
- peppers
- a little grated nutmeg
- two egg yolks
- one cup of wine

## Directions

1. Cut a snake into pieces and stew with two spoons of fat and a chopped onion
2. Sprinkle with one spoon of wheat flour and one cup of water, salt, parsley, peppers, and a little grated nutmeg
3. Leave to boil near the fire until cooked
4. Incorporate the broth with two egg yolks dissolved in a cup of wine and serve
