## Ingredients

- lamb or kid quarter
- thin strips of bacon
- cloth
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaves
- clove
- grated ginger
- paper
- butter
- wine
- fat
- small potatoes
- wheat flour
- milk
- eggs

## Directions

1. Take the lamb or kid quarter, lard it on the outside with thin strips of bacon
2. Wrap it tightly in cloth and soak for twenty-four hours in vinegar seasoned with salt, pepper, bay leaves, clove, and grated ginger
3. Spit roast it covered with buttered paper, or braise it in a tightly covered pan with two spoonfuls of fat and three glasses of red wine
4. Serve either way with its own sauce, first removing the coating, with small potatoes or cakes made of wheat flour, milk, and eggs
