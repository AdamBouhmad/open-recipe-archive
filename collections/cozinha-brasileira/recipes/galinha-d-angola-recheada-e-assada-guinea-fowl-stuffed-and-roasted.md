## Ingredients

- liver and giblets of the bird
- half a quarter of bacon
- two cooked eggs
- one onion
- one garlic clove
- peppers
- one slice of bacon

## Directions

1. Chop the liver and giblets of the bird with half a quarter of bacon; mix with two cooked eggs cut into small pieces, one onion, one garlic clove and peppers
2. Stuff the bird with this mixture, wrap it in a slice of bacon, roast it on a spit, moistening it with a sauce made of vinegar and butter
3. When cooked, remove and serve with its own defatted sauce
