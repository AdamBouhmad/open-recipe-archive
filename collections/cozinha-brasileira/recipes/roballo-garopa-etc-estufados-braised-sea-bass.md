## Ingredients

- Sea bass
- White wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Celery
- Melted butter
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Clean the fish
2. Boil it in white wine with salt, pepper, parsley and celery
3. Remove and let cool
4. Pour melted butter on it and wrap with a layer of breadcrumbs
5. Place on a plate and put in the oven until it takes good color
6. Serve
