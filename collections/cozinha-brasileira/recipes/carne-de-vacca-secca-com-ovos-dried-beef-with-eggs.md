## Ingredients

- dried beef
- eggs
- lard
- onions
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the dried meat for two or three hours.
2. Remove and let it cool.
3. Shred this cooked meat and mix it with eggs that should be well beaten to mix well.
4. Fry in lard with chopped onions, salt and pepper.
