## Ingredients

- paca meat separated from bones
- half a pound of bacon
- salt
- peppers
- parsley
- onions
- garlic
- grated ginger
- some cassava flour
- two eggs
- hot fat
- wheat flour
- sugar
- hot water
- selected raisins
- sapucaia nuts

## Directions

1. Separate the meat from the bones, chop it with half a pound of bacon, salt, peppers, parsley, onions, garlic, grated ginger, a little cassava flour and two eggs
2. Form this mixture into cakes, which are passed in hot fat
3. Remove the cakes, and in the fat add one tablespoon of wheat flour and one of sugar, heating them to brown
4. At this time add two cups of hot water, a portion of selected raisins and the same amount of sapucaia nuts cut up
5. Boil a little, and put the cakes in this sauce
6. Let it boil for one more hour, and serve
