## Ingredients

- a little butter
- purified salt

## Directions

1. Put a little butter in a pot
2. After it is melted and a deposit has formed at the bottom of the pot which is very clear, pass it through a fine sieve
3. Mix in purified salt and beat lightly with a fork
4. Serve hot in a bowl. It is the true Dutch sauce, and is served with fish
