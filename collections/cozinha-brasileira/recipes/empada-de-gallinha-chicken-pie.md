## Ingredients

- chicken pieces
- melted fat
- one cup of meat broth
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- clove
- onion
- half a garlic clove
- half a palm heart, chopped and scalded
- quarter pound of wheat flour
- half a pound of cow butter
- three eggs
- half a spoonful of sugar
- one egg yolk

## Directions

1. Take the chicken pieces and sauté them in melted fat
2. Add one cup of meat broth, salt, pepper, bay leaf, clove, onion, half a garlic clove and half a palm heart, chopped and scalded in hot water
3. In another place make a dough with a quarter pound of wheat flour, half a pound of cow butter, three eggs, half a spoonful of sugar
4. Form a box with this dough, or using a mold, line the interior walls of it with this dough, well buttered
5. Place the chicken and cooked palm heart inside, make a lid with the same dough, cover it and place it in the oven
6. When cooked, remove it and coat the exterior cap with the broth of a beaten egg and return it to the oven for some time
7. This egg serves to make the cap glossy, and this is called gilding the cap
