## Ingredients

- otter or giant otter
- sour orange juice (laranja da terra azeda)
- salt
- parsley
- onions
- pepper
- nutmeg
- butter or fat
- paper

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the otter.
2. After cleaning and washing, soak in sour orange juice, salt, parsley, onions, pepper, and nutmeg for twenty-four hours.
3. Wrap in paper spread with butter or fat and spit-roast over moderate fire.
4. When the meat is roasted, serve.
