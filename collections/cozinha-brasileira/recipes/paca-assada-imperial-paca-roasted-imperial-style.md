## Ingredients

- one paca
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- parsley
- manjerona
- two bottles of red wine
- bacon
- mushrooms
- bacon
- palm heart
- pitted olives
- breadcrumbs
- one onion
- salt
- pepper
- fat

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the paca and put it in a brine made with salt, pepper, onions, parsley, manjerona and two bottles of red wine for twelve hours
2. Lard it with bacon and fill it with mushrooms, bacon, palm heart, some pitted olives, breadcrumbs, one onion, salt and pepper, all finely chopped
3. Wrap it in paper greased with fat, put it on a spit and roast it on low heat until cooked
4. Remove the paper, increase the heat, give it a few more turns so the paca browns, and serve with the sauce that remained in the dripping pan
