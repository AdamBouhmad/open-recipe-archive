## Ingredients

- a large piece of fish without bones
- a portion of shrimp
- a quarter of a palm heart
- a handful of mushrooms
- parsley
- one onion
- peppers
- salt
- one cup of wine
- two hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Take a large piece of fish without bones, a portion of shrimp, a quarter of a palm heart, a handful of mushrooms, parsley, one onion, peppers, and salt
2. Boil everything together with one cup of wine
3. When cooked, add two chopped hard-boiled eggs
4. Place this dough in the pie box and bake in the oven, serve cold or hot
