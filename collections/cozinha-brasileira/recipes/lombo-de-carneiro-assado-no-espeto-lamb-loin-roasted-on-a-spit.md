## Ingredients

- lamb loin
- fine strips of bacon
- fine pepper
- salt
- garlic
- butter
- white wine
- one onion
- parsley

## Directions

1. Lard the loin with fine strips of bacon wrapped in fine pepper, salt and pounded garlic
2. Place it on a spit, pouring on it from time to time a spoonful of the sauce that dripped under the spit in a casserole in which were placed one spoonful of butter, two glasses of white wine, one onion and a little chopped parsley
