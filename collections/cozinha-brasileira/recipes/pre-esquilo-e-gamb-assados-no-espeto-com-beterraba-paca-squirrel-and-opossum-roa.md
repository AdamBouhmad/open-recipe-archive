## Ingredients

- one paca, squirrel or opossum
- salt
- pepper from peppers and tomatoes
- egg yolks
- breadcrumbs
- butter, melted
- beets
- vinegar
- fat, melted
- comarí peppers

## Directions

1. Take one of these animals; after removing the head, rub the meat with salt, pepper juice and tomatoes
2. Thread it on a spit and roast it; when almost cooked, brush it with egg yolks, coat it in breadcrumbs, and finish roasting it, moistening with melted butter until it takes on a good color
3. Serve with beet salad prepared as follows: cook beets in salted water, peel and cut them into thin slices; mix one cup of vinegar, three spoons of melted fat, a little salt and some comarí peppers
