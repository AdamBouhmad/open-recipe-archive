## Ingredients

- fish cut into pieces
- milk
- salt
- parsley
- grated cheese
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces and boil in milk, salt, and parsley
2. When cooked, place it in the mold, filling the gaps with grated cheese and small pieces of butter
3. Bake in the oven and serve hot
