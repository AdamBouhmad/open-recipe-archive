## Ingredients

- beef meat and bones
- salt
- onion
- Indian pepper
- nutmeg
- small potatoes
- cloves

## Directions

1. Make a broth of beef meat and bones
2. Add a little salt, onion, Indian pepper and nutmeg
3. Remove the foam from time to time
4. When everything is cooked, add some peeled small potatoes
5. Once these are cooked, add some cloves
6. Give it one final boil
7. Serve
