## Ingredients

- repolho fermentado (fermented cabbage)
- agua (water)
- toucinho enfumatado (smoked bacon)
- linguiça (sausage)
- vinho branco (white wine)
- pimenta (pepper)

## Directions

1. Place a portion of fermented cabbage soaked in cold water
2. After an hour, drain it and place it in a pan with a pound of smoked bacon cut into pieces, a sausage, a cup of white wine, two cups of water, and as much pepper as is needed
3. Let it boil over moderate heat and serve
