## Ingredients

- hare
- salt
- pepper
- hen's blood
- bacon lard
- two spoons melted butter
- half spoon English mustard
- one glass red wine

## Directions

1. Rub the hare with salt, pepper, and hen's blood, exposing it to the flame to dry the blood
2. Lard it with bacon
3. Roast it on the spit
4. Make a sauce with two spoons of melted butter, half spoon of English mustard, and one glass of red wine
5. Use this sauce to baste the hare while roasting
6. Serve
