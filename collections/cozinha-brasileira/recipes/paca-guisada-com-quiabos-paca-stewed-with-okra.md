## Ingredients

- one whole paca
- water
- salt
- peppers
- sprig of mint
- ginger
- two tablespoons pork fat
- one chopped onion
- broth
- juice of one terra orange
- portion of young whole okra

## Directions

1. Boil the whole paca in water with salt, peppers, a sprig of mint, and ginger.
2. When cooked, separate the meat from the bones.
3. Fry the meat in two tablespoons of pork fat with one chopped onion.
4. Add the strained and reduced broth, juice of one terra orange, and a portion of young whole okra.
5. Let it boil for a quarter of an hour and serve.
