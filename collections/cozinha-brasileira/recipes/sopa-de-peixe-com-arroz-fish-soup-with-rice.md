## Ingredients

- rice
- peas
- green beans
- carrots
- onions
- parsley
- celery
- tomatoes
- butter
- fish broth or vinegar

## Directions

1. Put rice to cook with some peas, green beans, carrots, onions, parsley, celery and tomatoes
2. When everything is cooked, add a little butter and let boil over a low fire until almost dry
3. Add fresh fish broth, or then one or two spoons of vinegar
