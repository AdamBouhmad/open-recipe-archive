## Ingredients

- piece of lean beef
- ham or pig's leg
- chicken
- butter or fat
- water
- onion
- parsley
- two cloves

## Directions

1. Take a piece of lean beef, ham (or pig's leg), and chicken; fry all this chopped over a gentle fire in butter or fat for half an hour; then add a little water, onion, parsley, and two cloves, and let it cook four hours, skimming well; pass through a fine sieve and keep to make sauces.
