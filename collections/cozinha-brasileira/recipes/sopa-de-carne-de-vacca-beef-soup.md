## Ingredients

- a good piece of beef, well fatty or containing bones
- cold water
- salt
- cabbage leaves
- parsley
- thyme
- onions
- macaroni, vermicelli or sago (optional)
- bread toasted or cooked rice

## Directions

1. Choose a good piece of beef that is well fatty or contains bones
2. Break the bones into small pieces and place everything with cold water and salt on the fire
3. Carefully skim the froth several times
4. After cooking for two or three hours, strain it
5. Add cabbage leaves, parsley, thyme and onions tied in a cloth which should be removed when serving
6. Add macaroni, vermicelli or sago (if desired) after these have been soaked in cold water for about one hour
7. Pour the clear broth over toasted bread or cooked rice when serving at table
