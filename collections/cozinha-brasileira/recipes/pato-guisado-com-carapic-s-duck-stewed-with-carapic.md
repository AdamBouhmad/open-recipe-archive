## Ingredients

- duck, cut in pieces
- half a pound of bacon
- water
- salt
- pepper
- marjoram
- portion of carapicú, chosen and blanched
- one spoonful of vinegar
- one spoonful of breadcrumb crumb
- breadcrumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the duck in pieces and boil them with half a pound of bacon, water, salt, pepper and marjoram until cooked
2. Remove from fire, strain the broth and boil in it a portion of chosen and blanched carapicú, adding one spoonful of vinegar with one spoonful of bread crumb
3. Wrap the meat in breadcrumbs and fry it in plenty of butter
4. When the carapicú is cooked and the broth has reduced, serve on a platter, placing the fried duck pieces on top
