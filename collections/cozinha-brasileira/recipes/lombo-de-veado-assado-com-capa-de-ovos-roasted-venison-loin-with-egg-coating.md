## Ingredients

- venison loin
- salt
- lemon juice
- onion slices
- parsley
- cloves
- ginger
- bacon
- breadcrumbs
- four egg yolks
- one tablespoon of sugar
- cinnamon powder
- lemon juice
- white wine

## Directions

1. Rub the loin with salt and lemon juice
2. Cover it with slices of onion, parsley, cloves and ginger
3. Wrap it and put it on the spit
4. When roasted, remove it from the spit and remove the bacon that wrapped it as well as the seasonings
5. Place in a large casserole and cover with the following sauce: a handful of breadcrumbs, four egg yolks, one tablespoon of sugar, cinnamon powder, a little salt, the juice of one lemon, white wine as much as needed to form a thick porridge
6. Put it thus in the oven and serve with some compote or salad
