## Ingredients

- one or more rusks
- sugar
- hot water
- butter (optional, for patients who can tolerate it)

## Directions

1. Break one or more rusks into pieces
2. Place them in a bowl with a few spoonfuls of sugar
3. Pour hot water over them
4. Cover with a plate
5. Let sit for a quarter hour before serving
6. If the patient can tolerate butter, add one spoonful to the bowl before pouring the water
