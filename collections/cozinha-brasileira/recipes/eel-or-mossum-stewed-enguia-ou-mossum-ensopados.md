## Ingredients

- Two to three eels
- White wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Bay leaves
- Two onions
- Carapicús
- Three egg yolks
- One cup of orange or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the skins from two to three eels and cut into pieces
2. Cook in white wine, salt, pepper, cloves and bay leaves
3. Remove cooked pieces and place on a plate kept warm by the fire
4. In another part, fry two halved onions and chopped carapicús, salt and pepper, without browning
5. Add the strained broth and finish cooking
6. Thicken with three beaten egg yolks mixed with one cup of orange or lemon juice
7. Pour over the fish and serve hot
