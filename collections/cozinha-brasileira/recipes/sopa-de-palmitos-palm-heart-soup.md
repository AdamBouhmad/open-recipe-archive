## Ingredients

- palm heart, finely chopped and blanched
- fat
- salt
- tomatoes
- a little fine cornmeal
- water
- arrowroot
- cold water

## Directions

1. Blanch finely chopped palm heart in hot water
2. Sauté in fat with salt, tomatoes, and a little fine cornmeal
3. Add necessary water
4. Dissolve one spoonful of arrowroot in two cups of cold water
5. Stir the soup and gradually add the arrowroot water little by little
