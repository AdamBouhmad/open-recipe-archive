## Ingredients

- one chicken, cut into pieces
- half a pound of melted bacon
- four to six cups of water
- salt
- parsley
- onion leaves
- two dozen okras, cut
- four comari peppers
- one spoon of fine cornmeal

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into pieces and brown them in melted bacon.
2. Add water, salt, parsley, onion leaves, cut okras, comari peppers, and cornmeal.
3. Let them boil until cooked, and serve with cornmeal mush.
