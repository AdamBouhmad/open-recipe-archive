## Ingredients

- pork blood
- salt
- caul fat, cut into small pieces
- garlic, chopped
- onion, chopped
- parsley
- chives, chopped
- marjoram
- thyme
- winter savory
- piri-piri pepper
- cloves
- pepper from India
- cinnamon, finely ground
- half pound of refined sugar
- cassava flour or cornmeal, sifted

## Directions

1. Prepare the blood in a vessel in which you will have put a little salt, and stir so it does not clot
2. Cut the caul fat into small pieces
3. Finely chop the garlic, onion, parsley, chives, marjoram, thyme, winter savory and piri-piri pepper
4. Add to this cloves, pepper from India, finely ground cinnamon and half pound of refined sugar
5. Thicken the blood with cassava flour or sifted cornmeal
6. Proceed in the manner already explained
