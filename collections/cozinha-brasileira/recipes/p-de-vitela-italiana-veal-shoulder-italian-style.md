## Ingredients

- veal shoulder
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley
- onion
- beef butter
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Lift carefully the skin covering the shoulder, leaving it attached at the bone end
2. Lard it, seasoning with salt, pepper, nutmeg, chopped parsley and onion
3. Cover it again with its skin, securing with stitches
4. Roast in the oven with a little beef butter
5. When cooked, place on a platter, pour a little butter over it, and cover with a layer of grated cheese
6. Serve with its own sauce
