## Ingredients

- breasts from a dozen small birds
- butter
- wheat flour
- remainder of the small birds
- one glass of white wine
- two cups of water
- half a dozen tomatoes

## Directions

1. Fry the breasts of a dozen small birds in butter
2. On the other hand, melt a little butter, mix one spoonful of wheat flour, the rest of the small birds, one glass of white wine, two cups of water, and half a dozen tomatoes
3. Let it boil for half an hour
4. Then place the fried breasts in this sauce, boil a little more, and serve
