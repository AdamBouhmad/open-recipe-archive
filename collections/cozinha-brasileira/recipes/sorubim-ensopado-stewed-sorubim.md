## Ingredients

- postas of sorubim fish
- water
- salt
- parsley
- marjoram
- pepper
- three egg yolks
- one chicara of lemon juice
- two tablespoons of manioc flour
- one tablespoon of fat

## Directions

1. Take some steaks of the fish, wash them, and boil them in water, salt, parsley, marjoram and pepper
2. Once cooked, remove the broth, and mix three beaten egg yolks with one chicara of lemon juice, two tablespoons of manioc flour and one tablespoon of fat
3. Boil until it reduces to five or six chicaras of an incorporated sauce, pour the fish inside, heat and serve
