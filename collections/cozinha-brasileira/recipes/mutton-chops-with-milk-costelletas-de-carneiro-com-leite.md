## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- milk
- salt
- whole Indian peppers
- parsley
- onion leaves
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Put the chops in already boiling milk with salt, whole Indian peppers, parsley, and onion leaves.
2. When cooked, place the chops on a plate buttered with cow butter.
3. Cover with a portion of breadcrumbs and then with four beaten eggs.
4. Bake in the oven and when the eggs are cooked, serve.
