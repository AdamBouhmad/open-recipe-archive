## Ingredients

- butter
- spirits of wine
- warm water
- crystallised salt

## Directions

1. Heat a portion of spirits of wine, wash the butter with it by kneading it well; pour out the liquid and wash it afterwards with warm water in which crystallised salt has been dissolved; afterwards wash the butter with plenty of water; and if salted again, it will be like fresh.
