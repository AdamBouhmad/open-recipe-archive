## Ingredients

- calf brain
- water
- salt
- wheat flour
- pepper
- mustard sauce
- butter

## Directions

1. Let the brain cool after being cooked in water and salt.
2. Cut it into slices that should be covered with wheat flour, pepper, salt and mustard sauce.
3. Fry it in butter on both sides.
