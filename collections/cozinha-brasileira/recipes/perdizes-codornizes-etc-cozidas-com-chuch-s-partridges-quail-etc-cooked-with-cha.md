## Ingredients

- partridge, quail, or similar bird
- half a quarter pound of bacon
- salt
- parsley
- peppers
- marjoram
- onion leaves
- ginger
- half a garlic clove
- coriander
- chayotes
- arrowroot
- bitter orange juice

## Directions

1. Boil the bird with half a quarter pound of bacon, salt, parsley, peppers, marjoram, onion leaves, ginger, half a garlic clove, and a little coriander
2. When cooked, remove the bird and strain the broth
3. Return the bird cut into pieces to the pan with a portion of chayotes and a small spoon of arrowroot dissolved in a glass of bitter orange juice
4. Boil for another half hour and serve
