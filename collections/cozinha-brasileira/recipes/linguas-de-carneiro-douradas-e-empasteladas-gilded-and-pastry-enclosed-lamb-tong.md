## Ingredients

- lamb tongues
- one dozen sardines in barrels or fresh
- brandy
- six egg yolks
- two egg whites
- one cup of milk
- two spoonfuls of wheat flour or fine cornmeal
- one spoonful of sugar
- nutmeg
- butter
- lemon
- salad

## Directions

1. Boil the tongues until cooked, peel them and split them into four strips lengthwise
2. Take one dozen sardines which are placed on a plate, covered with brandy, and the plate placed over a vessel containing boiling water to heat it; add brandy and let it burn until it extinguishes on its own
3. Mix six egg yolks and two egg whites well beaten, one cup of milk, two spoonfuls of wheat flour or fine cornmeal, one spoonful of sugar and a little nutmeg
4. Put a little butter in the bottom of a casserole and fry it, pouring into it half of the mixture just prepared and let it fry
5. Place on this fried mixture, after removing the casserole from the fire, the strips of tongues and the sardines broken into pieces with some slices of peeled lemon without seeds, all in alternating order
6. Pour the rest of the mixture on top, cover the casserole and place it to roast in an oven or over coals, also covering the lid with coals
7. Serve with some salad
