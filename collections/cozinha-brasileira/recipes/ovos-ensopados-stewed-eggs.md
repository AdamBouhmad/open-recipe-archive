## Ingredients

- eggs
- water
- one spoonful of wheat flour
- one spoonful of butter
- one spoonful of vinegar
- one glass of wine
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Break eggs into a pot of boiling water, carefully dropping them into the water so they remain whole.
2. Cook until done.
3. Place the cooked eggs on a plate and let them drain.
4. Prepare a sauce with one spoonful of wheat flour, one of butter, one spoonful of vinegar, one glass of wine, salt, a little sugar, and cinnamon.
5. Let the sauce simmer for a few minutes.
6. Pour the sauce over the eggs and serve.
