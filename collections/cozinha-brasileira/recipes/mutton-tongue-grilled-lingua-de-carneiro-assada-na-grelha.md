## Ingredients

- one mutton tongue
- sweet olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- garlic water
- pickled cucumbers

## Directions

1. Boil the tongue to remove the thick skin that covers it.
2. Split lengthwise.
3. Soak in sweet olive oil with salt, pepper and garlic water.
4. After one hour, grill it.
5. Serve with pickled cucumbers.
