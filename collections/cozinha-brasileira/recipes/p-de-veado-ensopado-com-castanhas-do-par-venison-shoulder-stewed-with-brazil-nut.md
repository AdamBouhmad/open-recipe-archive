## Ingredients

- meat from two venison shoulders
- fat
- one tablespoon of wheat flour
- one bowl of water
- salt
- some pimento peppers
- one onion
- parsley
- marjoram
- portion of Brazil nuts
- one egg yolk
- one glass of sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from the two venison shoulders and then cut it into small pieces and braise in hot fat
2. Stir and add one tablespoon of wheat flour, one bowl of water, salt, some pimento peppers, one onion cut up, parsley, marjoram and a portion of Brazil nuts
3. Let cook over moderate heat until everything is cooked
4. Remove the meat and nuts and reduce the broth, incorporating it with one egg yolk and one glass of sour orange juice and serve
