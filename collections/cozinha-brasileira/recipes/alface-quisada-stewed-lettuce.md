## Ingredients

- lettuce
- water
- salt
- butter
- wheat flour
- one egg
- vinegar
- one cup of white wine
- one cup of water

## Directions

1. Take a portion of lettuce, chop and boil it in water for five minutes
2. Remove and drain it
3. Fry, in another pan, two spoonfuls of butter with half a spoonful of wheat flour and before this begins to brown, add one egg dissolved in one spoonful of vinegar, one cup of white wine, and another of water
4. Place the lettuce in this broth, boil it over coals
5. Serve
