## Ingredients

- four pigeons
- lemon juice
- salt
- two tablespoons of butter
- one glass of wine
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- one cup of cream
- four tablespoons of sugar
- juice of one sweet orange
- one glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Take four pigeons and rub them with lemon juice and a little salt
2. Put them to cook with two tablespoons of butter, one glass of wine, cinnamon, and nutmeg
3. When cooked, remove them and mix with one cup of cream, four tablespoons of sugar, the juice of one sweet orange, and one glass of white wine
4. Let the mixture boil once and serve with the pigeons
