## Ingredients

- barb and crumatá fish
- water
- salt
- parsley
- pepper
- one cup of vinegar
- two tablespoons fat
- one onion
- fish broth
- two egg yolks
- small portion of peeled and cut chuchús

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish, boil in water, salt, parsley, pepper and one cup of vinegar
2. When cooked, remove the fish
3. Fry two tablespoons of fat with one onion, add the fish broth
4. Mix two beaten egg yolks, boil and add a small portion of peeled and cut chuchús
5. Cook and serve around the fish
