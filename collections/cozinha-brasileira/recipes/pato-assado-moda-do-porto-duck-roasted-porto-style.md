## Ingredients

- duck
- bacon slices
- equal parts wine and water
- salt
- parsley
- one onion
- cloves
- one bay leaf
- pinch of cumin
- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon wheat flour

## Directions

1. Place the duck in a pan belly-side down on bacon slices
2. Cover with equal parts wine and water
3. Add salt, parsley, one onion, cloves, one bay leaf, and a pinch of cumin
4. Let simmer over moderate heat until cooked
5. Strain the broth and add one tablespoon of butter mixed with one tablespoon of wheat flour
6. Let reduce to two cups and serve with the duck
