## Ingredients

- a piece of beef or pork liver, finely chopped
- the same amount of finely chopped mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- water
- a portion of carapicús
- a pinch of cornstarch
- one tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix a piece of finely chopped beef or pork liver with the same amount of finely chopped mushrooms, salt and pepper
2. Stuff the squabs with this mixture, selecting ones no more than ten to fifteen days old
3. Boil with water and a portion of carapicús
4. Serve the carapicús afterwards with the squabs
5. Pour over them the reduced sauce incorporated with a pinch of cornstarch and mixed with one tablespoon of vinegar
