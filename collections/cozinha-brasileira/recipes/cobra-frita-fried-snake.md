## Ingredients

- one snake, cut into pieces
- sour orange juice
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- cloves
- ginger
- fat
- one chopped onion
- breadcrumbs
- boiled yam root or cassava

## Directions

1. Place the snake cut into pieces in a marinade of sour orange juice, salt, parsley, onion, cloves, and ginger
2. After six hours, fry it in plenty of fat with a chopped onion and a portion of breadcrumbs
3. Serve over cooked yam or cassava root boiled in water and salt
