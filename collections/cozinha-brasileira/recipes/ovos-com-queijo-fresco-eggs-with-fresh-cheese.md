## Ingredients

- slices of fresh cheese
- fat or oil
- beaten eggs
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Fry slices of fresh cheese in fat, and when well fried, pour beaten eggs on top, and let fry without stirring until the eggs are cooked.
2. Turn the casserole onto a plate, and cover with sugar and cinnamon powder.
