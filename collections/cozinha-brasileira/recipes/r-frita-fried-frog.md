## Ingredients

- frog legs with skin removed
- salt
- sour orange juice
- garlic
- peppers
- nutmeg
- bread crumbs
- fat or butter

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the frog legs and rub with salt
2. Soak in sour orange juice, garlic, peppers, and nutmeg for three hours
3. Remove and coat in bread crumbs
4. Fry in plenty of fat or butter and serve with salads or compotes
