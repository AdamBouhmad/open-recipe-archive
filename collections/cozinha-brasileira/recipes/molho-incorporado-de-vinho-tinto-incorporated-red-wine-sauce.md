## Ingredients

- Red wine
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Sugar
- Egg yolks
- Cornstarch

## Directions

1. Boil half a bottle of red wine with some cloves, cinnamon and sugar
2. Thicken with two egg yolks and a spoonful of cornstarch
3. Serve with puddings
4. This sauce needs only one boiling
