## Ingredients

- one goose
- prunes and apples, peeled
- salt
- marjoram
- bananas
- sweet potatoes
- cassava
- potatoes or salad for serving

## Directions

1. Take the cleaned goose and fill it with peeled prunes and apples, adding salt and marjoram.
2. In the absence of prunes and apples, one may use peeled bananas, sweet potatoes, or peeled cassava cut into pieces.
3. Place this in a pan and roast in the oven or over embers.
4. Serve with potatoes or salad.
