## Ingredients

- the breast, gizzard, liver of one chicken
- half a quarter of bacon
- two egg yolks
- one onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- one cup of white wine

## Directions

1. Mince the breast, gizzard, and liver of one chicken with half a quarter of bacon, two egg yolks, one onion, parsley, salt and pepper
2. Simmer a little, adding one cup of white wine until it reduces
3. Fill the crusts with this mixture and fry the pastries in fat
