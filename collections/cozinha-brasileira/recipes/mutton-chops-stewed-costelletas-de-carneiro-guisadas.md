## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- salt
- fat
- water
- onion leaves
- parsley
- Indian peppers
- two egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Beat the chops and season with salt.
2. Fry on both sides in a little fat.
3. Drain the fat and add two glasses of water, onion leaves, parsley, and Indian peppers.
4. Let boil and serve with its own sauce, thickened with two egg yolks and lemon juice.
