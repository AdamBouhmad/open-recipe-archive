## Ingredients

- bananas
- water
- butter or lard
- sugar
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Boil a quantity of bananas in water with their skins
2. Peel them, split them in half and fry them in butter or lard
3. Sprinkle with sugar and grated cheese and serve
4. Note: if the bananas are very ripe, they do not need to be boiled; peel them and fry them directly
