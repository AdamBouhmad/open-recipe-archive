## Ingredients

- pig's tails
- guandú
- carrots
- onions
- bundle of aromatic herbs
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Place pig's tails in brine for eight days
2. Place them in a vessel with guandú, carrots, onions, a bundle of aromatic herbs, water, and salt
