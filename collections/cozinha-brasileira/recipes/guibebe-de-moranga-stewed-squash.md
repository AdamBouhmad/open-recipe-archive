## Ingredients

- one squash or any pumpkin
- water
- salt
- melted fat
- pepper
- one glass of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the squash into pieces
2. Remove the seeds
3. Cook the pieces in water and salt
4. When cooked, scrape the flesh
5. Sauté in melted fat with salt, pepper and one glass of vinegar
6. Boil a little more and serve
