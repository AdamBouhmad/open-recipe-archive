## Ingredients

- turkey pieces
- water
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- parsley
- onion
- mangaritos
- lard
- wheat flour
- butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take the turkey pieces and cook them in water, salt, pepper, cloves, parsley and onion with a portion of mangaritos, and fry everything in four spoons of lard (if the mangaritos are large, cut them into slices)
2. Pass the broth through a cloth, and mix it with one spoon of wheat flour
3. Fry it in a little butter and lemon juice; reduce it to few cups, and pour it over the meat and the fried mangaritos, placed on a plate, to then be taken to the table
