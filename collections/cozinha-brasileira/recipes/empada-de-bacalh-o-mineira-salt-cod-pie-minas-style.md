## Ingredients

- salt cod
- aguardente
- white wine
- peppers
- onions
- parsley
- mushrooms
- salt
- vinegar
- eggs

## Directions

1. Boil the salt cod for half an hour in aguardente
2. Remove, chop it, and after chopping, boil it in white wine with peppers, onions, and parsley
3. Let the broth dry out
4. Mix in a handful of mushrooms boiled in water, salt, vinegar, and some chopped hard-boiled eggs
5. Place this dough in the mold, bake and serve
