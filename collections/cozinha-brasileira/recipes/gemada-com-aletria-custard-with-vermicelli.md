## Ingredients

- six eggs
- three spoonfuls of sugar
- one bottle of milk
- vermicelli cooked in water
- sugar
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs with three spoonfuls of sugar
2. Mix in little by little one bottle of milk
3. Place on the fire and boil
4. Add the vermicelli cooked in water and removed with a skimmer to drain well
5. Let boil until it thickens
6. Place in plates
7. Cover with sugar and cinnamon powder
8. Serve
