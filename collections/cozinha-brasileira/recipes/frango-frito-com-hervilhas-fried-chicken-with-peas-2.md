## Ingredients

- one chicken
- water
- salt
- onion leaves
- fresh peas
- two spoons of lard
- breadcrumbs
- one spoon of wheat flour

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken, cut into pieces, in water with salt and onion leaves
2. When cooked, boil fresh peas in the same water
3. In another part, fry the chicken pieces coated in breadcrumbs in two spoons of lard
4. When browned on both sides, remove them
5. In the same lard, add one spoon of wheat flour, mix well
6. Immediately add the peas with all the broth
7. Let them finish drying and serve with the peas around the chicken pieces
