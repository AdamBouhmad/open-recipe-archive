## Ingredients

- grated cheese
- bread, sliced
- beef stock
- two cups of cream

## Directions

1. Place a layer of grated cheese at the bottom of a tureen
2. Cover with a layer of bread slices
3. Alternate layers of cheese and bread until the tureen is full
4. Pour beef stock mixed with two cups of cream over the top, enough to cover the last layer of bread
5. Place the tureen in the oven
6. Let sit for one hour, adding a little broth from time to time as it dries out
