## Ingredients

- a portion of peas
- water and salt
- a little butter
- one glass of fatty milk or milk cream for each bottle of peas
- three spoons of clean sugar
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook in water and salt a portion of peas, and when cooked, drain in a sieve
2. In another place, put in a saucepan a little butter, which melts over the fire
3. Add the peas, adding little by little one glass of fatty milk or milk cream for each bottle of peas
4. Add three spoons of clean sugar, remove from fire and thicken with two egg yolks
