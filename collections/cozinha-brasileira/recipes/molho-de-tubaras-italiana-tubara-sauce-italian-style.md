## Ingredients

- four or six tubaras
- one spoon of sweet olive oil
- one onion
- a little minced parsley
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- bay leaves
- some slices of lemon
- one chalice of white wine
- two spoons of sauce substance

## Directions

1. Fry four or six tubaras in one spoon of sweet olive oil with one onion and a little minced parsley, salt, pepper, cloves, bay leaves and some slices of lemon
2. After ten minutes, add one chalice of white wine and two spoons of sauce substance
3. Leave to boil near the fire for half an hour
4. Before serving, remove the lemon slices, parsley, onion, and finally the fat
5. Serve
