## Ingredients

- one chicken, cut in pieces
- water
- salt
- seasonings
- hot fat
- roasted mutton meat, chopped
- cooked ham, chopped
- breadcrumbs
- six egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken pieces in water with salt and seasonings
2. Braise it afterwards in hot fat
3. Place it on a plate over a layer of chopped roasted mutton, cooked ham, and breadcrumbs
4. Cover with the same chopped mixture
5. Pour six egg yolks over it
6. Finish cooking in the oven
