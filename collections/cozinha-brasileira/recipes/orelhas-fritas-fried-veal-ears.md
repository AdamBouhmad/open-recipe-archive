## Ingredients

- veal ears
- ham slices
- herb bouquet
- salt
- pepper
- carrot
- cabbage leaves
- wine
- water
- egg yolks
- sugar
- lemon
- cooked ham
- eggs
- wheat flour
- raw egg
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the well-cleaned ears with ham slices, herb bouquet, salt, pepper, a carrot, cabbage leaves, equal parts wine and water
2. Remove them when cooked
3. Strain and thicken the broth
4. Before serving, add two egg yolks, a little sugar, and lemon juice
5. Fill the ears with a stuffing made of cooked and chopped ham, eight cooked and chopped eggs, a little wheat flour, and a raw egg
6. Fry in butter
7. Serve with the prepared sauce
