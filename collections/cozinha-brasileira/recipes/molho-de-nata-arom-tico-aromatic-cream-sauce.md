## Ingredients

- Rich milk
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Lemon zest
- Vanilla
- Salt
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Let some cups of rich milk boil with sugar, cinnamon, lemon zest, a little vanilla and a tiny bit of salt
2. Finally, thicken with two to three egg yolks
