## Ingredients

- lamb kidneys
- butter
- salt
- one minced onion
- breadcrumbs
- mustard powder

## Directions

1. Remove fat from kidneys and scald in hot water
2. Split down the middle
3. Fry in butter over high heat, seasoning with salt and minced onion
4. Before serving, sprinkle with breadcrumbs and mustard powder
