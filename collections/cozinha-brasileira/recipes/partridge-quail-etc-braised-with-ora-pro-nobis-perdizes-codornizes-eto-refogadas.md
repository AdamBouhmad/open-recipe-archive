## Ingredients

- birds
- fat
- one onion
- one tablespoon of fine fubá
- two cups of water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- portion of ora-pro-nobis
- juice of one land orange

## Directions

1. Take the birds and cut them into pieces
2. braise them in little fat, turning them on both sides to brown
3. add one chopped onion, one tablespoon of fine fubá, two cups of water, salt, pepper, parsley, a portion of ora-pro-nobis and the juice of one land orange
4. boil for another half hour and serve with fubá angá from the mill
