## Ingredients

- veal feet
- sour orange
- water
- salt
- eggs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the veal feet and rub with sour orange to make them white
2. Cook in water and salt
3. When cooked, remove the bones from the feet
4. Thicken the broth with beaten eggs, add lemon juice
5. Let the feet boil a little more in this sauce and serve
6. They can also be fried or braised in sauce after cooking
