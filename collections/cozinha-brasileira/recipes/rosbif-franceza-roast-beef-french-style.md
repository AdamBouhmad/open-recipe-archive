## Ingredients

- beef loin with ribs
- bacon
- butter
- potatoes
- beef broth
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- fresh butter
- meat substance

## Directions

1. Take a good piece of beef loin with ribs, covered with fat.
2. Remove the meat from the kidney side and carefully separate the loin.
3. Saw the rib bones.
4. Insert bacon through the edge where the ribs were cut.
5. Remove the membrane from the loin and cover it with the fat from the kidney side.
6. Tie it with a cord so it does not fall.
7. Four hours before serving, spit the meat, tie it with cords at both ends, and cover with buttered paper.
8. Roast it with a pan underneath to catch the dripping fat, which is used to baste the meat with a brush from time to time.
9. Half an hour before serving, remove the paper and cords and return to the fire to brown.
10. Baste with a bit of meat substance.
11. Place on a plate and surround with potatoes boiled in beef broth with salt, pepper, nutmeg, and fresh butter.
12. Let the butter drain and return the potatoes to the fire to toast, or arrange potatoes braised in butter.
13. When serving, place some meat substance on top and pour the sauce from the roasting pan into a tureen, being careful to remove excess fat.
