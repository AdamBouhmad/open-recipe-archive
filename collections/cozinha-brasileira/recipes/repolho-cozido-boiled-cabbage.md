## Ingredients

- cabeça de repolho (head of cabbage)
- carne de porco salgada (salted pork)
- linguiças or presunto (sausages or ham)
- cenouras (carrots)
- cebola (onion)
- raiz de aipo (celery root)
- salsa (parsley)
- pimenta (pepper)
- noz moscada (nutmeg)
- agua (water)

## Directions

1. Cut a head of cabbage into four pieces and blanch it
2. Place it in a pot with a piece of salted pork, sausages or ham, some carrots, an onion, a celery root, parsley, a little pepper, nutmeg and enough water
3. Let it boil for an hour, and then place the cabbage on a plate, the meat on top with its broth, and serve
