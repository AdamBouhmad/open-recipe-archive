## Ingredients

- a portion of beans
- water and salt
- vinegar
- hot lard
- Indian pepper or comaris

## Directions

1. Take a portion of beans, remove the ends, and cook whole with only water and salt
2. When well cooked, remove with a skimming spoon and put on a plate
3. Serve putting on top a sauce of vinegar, very hot lard, and plenty of Indian pepper or comaris
