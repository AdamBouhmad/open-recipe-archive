## Ingredients

- a portion of mangalós
- water and salt
- a dozen tomatoes
- parsley
- onion leaves
- ground pepper
- lard
- one spoon of sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Cook a portion of mangalós in water and salt
2. When cooked, pass through a sieve
3. Fry a dozen tomatoes, parsley, onion leaves, ground pepper and lard
4. Add the mangalós purée and one spoon of sour orange juice
5. Stir, put on a plate, and serve with fried sausages
