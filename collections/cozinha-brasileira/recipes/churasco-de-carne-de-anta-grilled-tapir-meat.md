## Ingredients

- a good portion of tapir meat with skin attached
- salt
- lemon juice
- comaris peppers

## Directions

1. Cut a good portion of tapir meat, leaving the skin attached to the meat
2. Rub it with plenty of salt
3. Place it in the oven to roast
4. Serve with the skin serving as a platter, accompanied by a sauce of lemon juice and comaris peppers
