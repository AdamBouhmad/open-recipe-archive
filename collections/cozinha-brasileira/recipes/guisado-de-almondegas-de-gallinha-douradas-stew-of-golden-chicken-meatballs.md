## Ingredients

- prepared and fried meatballs
- three cups of milk
- three egg yolks
- more than one cup of milk
- two cloves
- one spoonful of sugar
- one glass of wine

## Directions

1. When the meatballs are made and fried, place them to simmer in the following sauce
2. Boil three cups of milk and add three beaten egg yolks, diluted in more than one cup of milk
3. Add to them two cloves, one spoonful of sugar, and one glass of wine
4. Give it another boil and serve with the meatballs
