## Ingredients

- four young pigeons
- water
- parsley
- onion leaves
- salt
- pepper
- a portion of mushrooms
- one palm heart cut into pieces
- wheat flour
- one cup of broth
- two egg yolks
- three tablespoons of cream

## Directions

1. Cook four young pigeons in water containing parsley, onion leaves, salt, pepper, a portion of mushrooms, and one palm heart cut into pieces
2. After everything boils for a quarter of an hour, transfer to a casserole after dusting with wheat flour and moistening only with one cup of broth
3. Let it boil over coals for a quarter of an hour
4. Serve on the plate, pouring the sauce over it, to which are added at this time two egg yolks beaten with three tablespoons of cream
