## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- water
- salt
- half pound of veal
- one onion
- nutmeg
- one bottle of milk
- bread crumbs
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the rabbit in water and salt, remove the bones, chop the meat very finely with half pound of veal, one onion, salt, and grated nutmeg
2. On another part, bring one bottle of milk to boil with a portion of bread crumbs and a little salt, letting it reduce to one third
3. Add the chopped meat at this time, and as soon as it heats serve it
