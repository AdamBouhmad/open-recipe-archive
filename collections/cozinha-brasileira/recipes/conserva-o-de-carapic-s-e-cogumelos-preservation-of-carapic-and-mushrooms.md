## Ingredients

- carapicú
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Select and clean the carapicú
2. Place in a pan with water and much salt and bring to a boil
3. After boiling, remove and dry in the sun or oven and keep in a sack for when needed
4. When using them, soak for six hours in warm water, then boil them, and when drained they are ready to use
