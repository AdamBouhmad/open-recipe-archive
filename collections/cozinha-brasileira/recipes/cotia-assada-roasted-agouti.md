## Ingredients

- one agouti
- salt
- peppers
- tomato juice
- bacon strips
- onions
- parsley
- garlic
- half cup of water
- one spoonful of orange juice

## Directions

1. After removing the skin of the agouti and cleaning it, rub it with salt, peppers and tomato juice, leaving it in this seasoning for six hours so the meat becomes well impregnated
2. Place it in a pan over strips of bacon with sliced onions, parsley and one clove of garlic, moistening it with half cup of water and one spoonful of orange juice
3. Roast it over a moderate fire and serve it with its defatted sauce
