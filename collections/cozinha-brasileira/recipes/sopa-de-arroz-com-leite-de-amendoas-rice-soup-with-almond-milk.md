## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- salt
- lemon peel
- bay leaves
- 1 quarter pound sweet almonds, shelled
- 1 half quarter pound sugar
- 1 half bottle water

## Directions

1. Cook rice in water with very little salt
2. Add a little lemon peel and bay leaves, being careful not to heat too strongly
3. Pound one quarter pound of shelled sweet almonds with half a quarter pound of sugar
4. Add water gradually, about half a bottle
5. Strain by pressing, and add this milk to the rice soup
6. Add more sugar if necessary
