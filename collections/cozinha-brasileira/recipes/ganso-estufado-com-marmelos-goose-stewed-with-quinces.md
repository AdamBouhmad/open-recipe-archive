## Ingredients

- one goose
- quinces, cut in two or four pieces
- salt
- cumin
- marjoram
- cassava roots, peeled and cut into pieces
- one cup of water
- parsley
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Fill the goose with quinces cut in two or four pieces (according to its size), with a little salt, cumin and a little marjoram.
2. Place the goose in the pan, and around it, some peeled cassava roots cut into pieces, with one cup of water, salt, parsley, and lemon peel.
3. Cover the pan tightly and allow to boil, at first over gentle fire to let the goose fat melt, and then over very active fire, for two to three hours.
4. Serve whole with the cassava around it.
