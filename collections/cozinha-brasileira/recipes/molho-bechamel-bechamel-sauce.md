## Ingredients

- one spoon of fat
- one spoon of tapioca starch
- salt
- pepper
- half a bottle of milk, cooked

## Directions

1. Fry one spoon of tapioca starch in one spoon of fat
2. Immediately add salt, pepper, and gradually half a bottle of cooked milk
3. Let boil a little and serve
