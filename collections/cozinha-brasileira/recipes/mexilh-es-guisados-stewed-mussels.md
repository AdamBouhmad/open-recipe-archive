## Ingredients

- carapicús
- onion
- parsley
- garlic
- olive oil
- white wine
- pepper
- nutmeg
- mussels

## Directions

1. Chop finely a handful of carapicús, one onion, a little parsley and one garlic clove
2. Put in a pot with two or three tablespoons of olive oil, sauté and add one cup of white wine, pepper and grated nutmeg
3. Boil a little
4. After the mussels have boiled in their own water, place the chopped mixture on a plate with the mussels in their shells and serve
