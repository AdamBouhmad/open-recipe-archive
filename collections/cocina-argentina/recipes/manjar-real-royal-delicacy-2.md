## Ingredients

- four pounds of sugar
- a pound and a half of ground almonds
- half a bottle of milk
- three ounces of wheat starch

## Directions

1. Make a high-point syrup with four pounds of sugar
2. Let it cool slightly and add a pound and a half of ground almonds
3. Meanwhile, dissolve and cook three ounces of wheat starch in half a bottle of milk
4. When thick and cooked, mix with the almond syrup
5. Return to the heat and allow to reach the proper point
