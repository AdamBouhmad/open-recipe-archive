## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- grated cheese
- one or two sauces

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower as if for white sauce.
2. Place it in a porcelain vessel and turn it out onto the serving dish, whose bottom is covered with a layer of grated cheese.
3. Place the dish on the stove with gentle heat underneath, and covering the surface of the cauliflower with another layer of grated cheese, cover it with its lid or with a sheet, and place hot coals and live embers on top so that the gentle heat on the bottom cooks without burning, and on the surface the cheese melts, adhering to the cauliflower.
4. Serve it accompanied by one or two sauces to the taste of the diners.
