## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Two onion heads
- Celery branches
- Salt
- Flour
- Butter
- Bird stock
- Bird blood
- Vinegar
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the squabs with two onion heads, celery branches, and salt.
2. Prepare a sauce by making a roux with flour and butter, then add the bird stock and the blood (collected in a cup with a tablespoon of vinegar to prevent coagulation).
3. Season the sauce with salt and pepper.
4. Place the cooked squabs in a serving dish with the celery and peeled onion heads.
5. Pour the sauce over the squabs.
