## Ingredients

- Two dozen puff pastry pieces (hojaldra)
- Cream, beaten with sugar and cinnamon
- Very ripe strawberries

## Directions

1. Bake two dozen puff pastry pieces on a baking sheet in the oven until lightly golden.
2. When done, remove from the oven while still hot.
3. Place one dozen on the serving dish.
4. Spread one full spoonful of cream (beaten with sugar and cinnamon) on each piece.
5. Cover with the other dozen puff pastry pieces.
6. Cover the top with a layer of very ripe strawberries covering the entire dish.
7. Serve.
