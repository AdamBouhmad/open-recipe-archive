## Ingredients

- Six mature lemon peels, cut very thin
- Water, quantity corresponding to three glasses
- Pieces of good cinnamon
- Sugar, sufficient amount to sweeten well
- Two egg whites
- A pinch of nutmeg powder
- One bottle of good red wine (Burgundy or Bordeaux)

## Directions

1. Cut the lemon peels very thin and place them in infusion for two hours in the quantity of water corresponding to three glasses, together with pieces of good cinnamon and sufficient sugar to sweeten well.
2. Strain through a sieve.
3. Beat two egg whites and mix them with a pinch of nutmeg powder.
4. Pour a bottle of good red wine (Burgundy or Bordeaux) over all of this.
5. Freeze using the customary procedure.
