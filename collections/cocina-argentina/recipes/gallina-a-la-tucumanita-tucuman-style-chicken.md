## Ingredients

- A hen
- Blood from the hen
- Salt
- Onions
- Tomatoes
- Bacon
- Chorizo
- Offal
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Pepper
- Oregano
- Cumin
- Butter
- Broth
- One dozen peanuts
- One dozen sweet almonds
- Bread crumb

## Directions

1. Cut the neck of the hen and catch the blood in a cup
2. Pluck, open, wash, and portion the hen, cooking it with a little salt
3. Make a seasoning with onions, tomatoes, bacon, and chorizo, which will cook with the hen, along with the offal and hard-boiled eggs: chop everything very finely and season with pepper, oregano, a pinch of cumin, and three tablespoons of butter
4. Pour part of the broth over this seasoning and add the hen pieces, cooking over low heat for half an hour
5. Meanwhile, toast and grind one dozen peanuts and the same number of sweet almonds
6. Grind with them a piece of bread crumb soaked in liquid and the blood that was collected
7. Mix together
