## Ingredients

- Macaroni
- Cheese, grated
- Bread crumbs, finely crumbled
- Cheese, grated
- Butter, melted

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni.
2. Spread grated cheese on the bottom of a heat-resistant platter.
3. Place the cooked macaroni over the cheese.
4. Even the surface and scatter over the macaroni a mixture of finely crumbled bread crumbs and grated cheese in equal parts.
5. Pour melted butter over this mixture.
6. Place in the oven with fire on top of the lid.
7. It is ready when the cheese on the surface has taken on a good color.
8. Serve in the same platter in which it was made.
