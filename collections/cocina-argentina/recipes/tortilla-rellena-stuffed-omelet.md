## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- white onion
- garlic
- cumin
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat together the whites and yolks of the eggs needed for the omelet; season this mixture with pepper and salt, and have ready a finely chopped filling seasoned with chopped white onion, pepper and a little garlic and cumin.
2. Butter the bottom and sides of a pan, put it on moderate heat, and pour in half of the beaten egg.
3. When cooking is evident on the surface, arrange the filling on top; spread it, and pour all the rest of the beaten egg over it. Increase the heat, and after also buttering it, put the lid on and flip it, so that the lid is over the fire and the omelet finishes cooking and browning.
4. Serve in the pan, placed on a platter, with the edges hidden with sprigs of parsley.
