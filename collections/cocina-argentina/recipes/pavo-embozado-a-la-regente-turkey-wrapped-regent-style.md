## Ingredients

- One turkey
- Two dozen beaten eggs
- Three loaves of fat, grated
- Half a pound of cheese, grated
- Turkey blood and giblets
- One ounce of ground parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Four tablespoons of olive oil
- Matambre (meat roll)
- Onions
- Garlic
- Vinegar
- Olive oil
- Oregano
- Cumin
- Butter
- Tomato juice
- Fine herbs
- Olive oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Toasted bread slices
- Consomé

## Directions

1. Prepare the turkey in the customary manner
2. Stuff it with a mixture of two dozen beaten eggs, three grated loaves of fat, half a pound of grated cheese, the cooked and chopped blood and giblets of the turkey, one ounce of ground parsley, salt, pepper and four tablespoons of olive oil, mixing everything when filling
3. Wrap it in a matambre that has been marinated for eight hours in a marinade of ground onions and garlic, vinegar, olive oil, pepper, salt, oregano and cumin
4. With strong thread and a thick needle, sew the edges of the matambre from top to bottom over the turkey's loin, covering it entirely
5. Coat the matambre with butter and put it in a hot oven
6. When the matambre has taken on a dark golden color, bring it to the oven door to turn the turkey to the other side and put it back in
7. When this side is equally browned, remove it from the oven, remove the matambre, coat it with butter, and put it back in the oven to brown for a moment and finish cooking
8. Serve it in slices over a sauce of tomato juice, chopped fine herbs, oil, vinegar, pepper and pieces of bread toasted in boiling consomé
