## Ingredients

- A piece of cod, well washed and desalted
- Butter, seasoned with pepper
- One cup of cream, or in its absence, good milk
- Bread crumbs
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Boil a piece of cod in water, well washed and desalted, and remove the skin and spines.
2. Make a white sauce of butter seasoned with pepper; mix in one cup of cream, and in its absence, good milk.
3. Add the cod to this sauce and let it boil so that it thickens.
4. Then even out the surface and cover it with a layer of bread crumbs.
5. Over this bread crumbs pour melted butter.
6. Another layer of bread crumbs with melted butter on top, allow it to brown in the kitchen oven or in a portable stove.
7. Serve very hot, with small slices of bread fried in butter around it.
