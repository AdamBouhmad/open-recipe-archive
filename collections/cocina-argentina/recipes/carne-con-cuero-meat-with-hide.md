## Ingredients

- piece of meat with hide
- linen sack
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the piece of meat in two waters and dry it carefully with a napkin, both meat and hide
2. Place it well extended in a linen sack proportioned to the size of the piece, so that it receives direct contact with the earth from all sides
3. Fold the mouth of the sack and sew it with fine stitches
4. Dig a hole in the garden half a meter deep, with width and length proportioned to the size of the sack
5. Place the sack in the hole with the meat facing down and the hide facing up
6. Fill the hole again and press the surface, leaving it for three hours
7. Extract the meat with the same care with which it was buried
8. Season lightly with salt
9. Take it to the fire where it will be roasted by experts until cooked through, resulting in exquisite meat with hide, tender as if it had been cooked for many hours
