## Ingredients

- tender lettuce hearts
- cooked beef
- cooked potatoes
- onion
- parsley
- roasted garlic clove
- vinegar
- pure olive oil
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- parsley branches
- black olives

## Directions

1. Chop very finely the tender lettuce hearts, cooked beef, cooked potatoes, onion, and parsley.
2. Add one roasted garlic clove, well ground.
3. Incorporate all of this and season with the necessary good vinegar, pure olive oil, pepper, salt, and a light sprinkling of nutmeg.
4. Arrange in the salad bowl with an edging of parsley branches and black olives.
