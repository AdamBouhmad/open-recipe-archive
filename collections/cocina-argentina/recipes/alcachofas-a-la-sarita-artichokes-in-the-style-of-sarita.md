## Ingredients

- artichokes
- cold cooked meat
- chorizo
- raisins
- hard-boiled eggs
- celery
- parsley
- breadcrumbs
- oil

## Directions

1. After cooking the artichokes, remove the leaves of the first row and cut off the top heart with a knife.
2. Round them well and remove the central part.
3. Prepare a filling of cold cooked meat, crumbled chorizo, raisins, chopped hard-boiled eggs, celery, parsley, and breadcrumbs, all finely chopped.
4. Fill the artichokes and arrange them in a pan that resists heat.
5. Pour half a spoonful of oil over each one, cover the filling with half a hard-boiled egg, and cook them covered in steam.
