## Ingredients

- Corbina or other fish, freshly caught
- Garlic
- Onion
- Pepper
- Oregano
- Salt
- Vinegar, one tablespoon
- Yellow corn flour
- Lard

## Directions

1. Descale the fish and wash it with three waters, coat it internally and externally with a compound of garlic, onion, pepper, oregano and salt, all ground, and moistened with one tablespoon of vinegar.
2. Leave it in this marinade for two hours.
3. Then cut it into crosswise pieces; coat it in yellow corn flour and fry it in lard; first over not very lively fire to allow the fish to cook; then raising the heat so that it browns.
