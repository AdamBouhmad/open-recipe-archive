## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a liter of rich milk
- two ounces of butter
- four ounces of sugar
- half a teaspoon of cinnamon powder
- ten ounces of bread crumbs
- zest of the skin of one lemon
- four eggs
- one large tablespoon of good cognac
- raisins from Málaga
- cinnamon candies

## Directions

1. Boil three-quarters of a liter of rich milk. When it has boiled, add two ounces of butter and four ounces of sugar, with half a teaspoon of cinnamon powder.
2. Mix with ten ounces of bread crumbs and the zest of the skin of one lemon (that is, the zest of the skin, not the pith).
3. Beat four eggs and add them, together with one large tablespoon of good cognac.
4. Butter a mold, arrange the pudding in it, and place it in the oven, where it should cook for one hour.
5. Serve studding it with raisins from Málaga and cinnamon candies.
