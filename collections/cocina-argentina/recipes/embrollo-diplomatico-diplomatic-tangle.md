## Ingredients

- eighteen egg yolks
- two egg whites, well beaten
- a large glass of milk
- a glass of Port wine
- zest of the skin of a lemon
- cinnamon
- sugar
- a small piece of vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix and beat all ingredients together
2. Pour into a buttered mold
3. Bake in the oven
