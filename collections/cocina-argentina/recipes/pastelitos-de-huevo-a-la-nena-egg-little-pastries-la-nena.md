## Ingredients

- puff pastry dough
- butter
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- thick cream of raw milk
- lard

## Directions

1. Make a puff pastry dough, and well stretched with a rolling pin, cut it into 8-centimeter squares, which are laid out on a tablecloth.
2. In a pan placed over a brisk fire, put a piece of butter.
3. When the butter, already melted, is very hot, break eggs over it, sprinkling them with salt and pepper.
4. From the moment the white turns opaque, take the egg carefully with two spoons, and place it on the square of puff pastry, covering it with another, after having poured over the egg 1 tablespoon of thick cream of raw milk.
5. The lower and upper edges of the little pastry are sealed by moistening them inside with a finger dipped in milk.
6. They are fried in lard for lunch. But, to serve them at dinner, it is better to cook them in the oven.
