## Ingredients

- sesos (brains)
- sal (salt)
- pimienta (pepper)
- pan frío (cold bread)
- huevo batido (beaten egg)
- especias (spices)
- mantequilla (butter)

## Directions

1. Cook the brains and remove fibers and membranes
2. Season with salt and pepper, and place between slices of cold bread
3. Wrap in beaten egg conditioned with spices and fry in hot butter over moderate heat
4. When they brown, serve very hot
