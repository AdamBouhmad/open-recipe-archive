## Ingredients

- 1 liter of good milk
- 3 eggs
- sugar
- almonds
- coconut
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 glass of good grape brandy or cognac

## Directions

1. Cook 1 liter of good milk with 3 eggs and sweeten with toasted sugar
2. Grind almonds and coconut, dissolve with a little of the same milk, pass through a sieve
3. Pour on top 6 well-beaten egg yolks, one for every 3 cups
4. Beat everything very well with a chocolate whisk
5. Remove from fire at once
6. Add 1 glass of good grape brandy or, failing that, good cognac
7. Serve after beating well and making foam
