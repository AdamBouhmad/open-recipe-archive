## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Squab giblets
- Beaten eggs
- Toasted bread
- Almonds
- Raisins from Málaga
- Pepper
- Salt
- Cumin
- Butter

## Directions

1. Pluck the squabs very carefully to remove the hidden quills close to the skin
2. Empty them, wash them and clean them well
3. Stuff them with a mixture of their cooked and chopped giblets, beaten eggs, toasted and grated bread, ground almonds, Málaga raisins, pepper, salt, cumin, and a piece of butter
