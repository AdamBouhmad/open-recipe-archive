## Ingredients

- piece of veal, rib, sirloin or leg
- vinegar
- olive oil
- two heads of onion
- one head of garlic, ground
- pepper
- salt
- oregano
- cumin, pulverized

## Directions

1. Place the piece of veal, rib, sirloin or leg in a wooden, earthen or porcelain vessel for six hours
2. Marinate it in a mixture of vinegar, olive oil, two heads of onion and one of garlic ground, pepper, salt, oregano and pulverized cumin
3. Dry it and place it on the grill or in a well-heated oven, as cooking should be rapid
4. Serve with salad
