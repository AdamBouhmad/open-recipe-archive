## Ingredients

- forty egg yolks
- two pounds of ground sugar
- half a pound of ground almonds
- half a pound of sugar
- two sponge cakes of twenty centimeters
- half a pound of cream

## Directions

1. To well-beaten egg yolks, mix two pounds of ground sugar, half a pound of ground almonds with half a pound of sugar, two sponge cakes of twenty centimeters, equally ground, and half a pound of cream.
2. Once all incorporated, stir and beat until it forms a smooth paste.
3. Arrange it in a mold buttered with butter and place it in the oven.
