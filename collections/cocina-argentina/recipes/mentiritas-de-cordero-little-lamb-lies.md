## Ingredients

- young lamb ribs
- cooked potato
- three eggs
- parsley
- celery
- pepper
- salt
- pork fat from fresh bacon
- olives
- pickled cucumbers

## Directions

1. Peel and scrape the lamb ribs well with a knife.
2. Grind the meat and mix with an equal quantity of cooked and ground potato.
3. Add three eggs, parsley, celery, pepper, and salt.
4. Incorporate everything well by stirring with a spoon.
5. Divide into portions according to the number of cleaned ribs.
6. Place the cleaned bone in the middle of each portion, shaping them like ribs.
7. Roll them in beaten egg, then in breadcrumbs.
8. Fry in pork fat extracted from fresh bacon.
9. Serve without sauce, with garnishes of olives and pickled cucumbers.
