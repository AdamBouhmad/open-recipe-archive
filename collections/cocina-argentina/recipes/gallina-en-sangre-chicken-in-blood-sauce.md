## Ingredients

- One chicken
- Salt
- Onions
- Tomatoes
- Bacon
- Chorizo
- Chicken giblets
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Pepper
- Oregano
- Cumin
- Three tablespoons butter
- One dozen peanuts
- One dozen sweet almonds
- Piece of bread crumb soaked
- Chicken blood

## Directions

1. Cut the neck of the chicken and collect the blood in a cup
2. Pluck, open, wash and butcher the chicken and cook with a little salt
3. Make a dressing with onions, tomatoes, bacon and chorizo, which will have cooked with the chicken, as well as the giblets and hard-boiled eggs: everything finely chopped and seasoned with pepper, oregano, a pinch of cumin and three tablespoons of butter
4. Pour part of the broth over this dressing and add the chicken pieces, and cook over low heat for half an hour
5. Meanwhile, toast and grind one dozen peanuts and as many sweet almonds; grind with them a piece of soaked bread crumb and the blood that has been collected
6. Mix all this together, and a short time before removing the pot from the fire, add this final dressing; stir with the spoon, let it come to a half boil for a moment, and serve
