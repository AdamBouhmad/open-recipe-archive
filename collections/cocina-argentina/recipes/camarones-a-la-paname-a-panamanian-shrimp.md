## Ingredients

- three dozen shrimp
- salt
- aromatic herbs, a bunch
- white onion, finely chopped
- tomato, finely chopped
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fish three dozen shrimp with a small net
2. Cook the shrimp in salted water seasoned with a bunch of aromatic herbs
3. Once cooked, peel the shrimp
4. Take the tails and place them in a serving dish
5. Season with salt, pepper, finely chopped white onion and tomato, bread crumbs, and oil and vinegar
6. Mix and stir everything together carefully and cover until the moment of serving
