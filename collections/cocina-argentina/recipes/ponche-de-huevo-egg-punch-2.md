## Ingredients

- twelve egg yolks
- juice of two lemons
- one glass of Jamaican rum or cognac
- one liter of hot brewed tea, sweetened with sugar
- six egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat together twelve egg yolks and the juice of two lemons
2. Add, little by little and without ceasing to beat, one glass of Jamaican rum or cognac if rum is unavailable
3. Mix into one liter of hot brewed tea already sweetened with sugar
4. After incorporating everything well, beat with a molinillo
5. Add six egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
6. The action of the molinillo in the comforting liquid will create delicious foam in each cup
