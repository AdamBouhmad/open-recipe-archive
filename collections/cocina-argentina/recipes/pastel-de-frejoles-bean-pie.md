## Ingredients

- 1 pound beans (porotos)
- butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup milk
- fresh bacon
- 2 tablespoons pure olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- 1 clove garlic
- puff pastry

## Directions

1. Soak 1 pound of beans in cold water for ten hours.
2. Carefully remove the skin covering them; wash them, give them a boil, and grind them in a mortar.
3. Place them in a plate or deep dish with a piece of butter, and stir and beat with 1 tablespoon of flour and 1 cup of milk.
4. Fry fresh bacon in small pieces, which is mixed into the dough. Give it a final beating by adding 2 tablespoons of pure olive oil, and season with salt, pepper, and 1 clove of ground garlic.
5. Prepare a mold lined with butter, making sure the bottom has a thick layer of butter; pour the dough on top, over it a very thin puff pastry, and put it in the oven until the pastry has browned.
