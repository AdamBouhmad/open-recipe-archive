## Ingredients

- eggs
- cream or milk
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- onion
- lard
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Break the desired number of eggs - keeping in mind that two fill a plate. Having broken the eggs, separate in different vessels the yolks and whites, and beat them: the yolks just enough for them to break down; the whites to stiff peaks: that is, until they become light and consistent foam.
2. Mix them in a cup of raw cream, or good milk, salt to taste, pepper, and a clove of garlic and a slice or two of onion, ground in the mortar.
3. Put in a pan a piece of lard on high heat. When the lard is boiling, lightly mixing the yolks, the whites in foam and the milk, stir and fry.
4. Since the cooking of this omelet is very quick, due to its lightness, a moment later it is necessary to divide it in a cross with the tip of a knife, and with two forks turn each of the four parts so that they finish frying.
5. When serving it, give it its shape, making the action of the knife disappear with a relief of sliced potatoes.
