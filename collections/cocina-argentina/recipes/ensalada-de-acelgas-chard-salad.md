## Ingredients

- chard
- water
- salt
- onion heads
- broth
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- bread

## Directions

1. Wash the chard and boil it in plenty of water without salt, with two onion heads.
2. When the chard is so cooked that it falls apart at the touch, remove it from the fire, drain it, and after removing the stems, break it apart very well with a spoon until it is reduced to a paste, which is thinned by adding one tablespoon of broth.
3. Chop the onion that has been cooked finely, mix it with the chard, and season it as a salad with the oil, vinegar, and pepper, which should have been previously beaten in a cup. It should be noted that this salad requires more oil than vinegar.
4. Toast very thin slices of bread on the grill, and on each one place one tablespoon of chard, arranging them with good taste in a small salad bowl.
