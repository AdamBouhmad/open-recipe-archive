## Ingredients

- milk to fill a serving dish
- ground almonds and coconut, dissolved in milk
- cloves
- cinnamon
- anise
- six egg whites

## Directions

1. Sweeten the quantity of milk necessary to fill a serving dish to taste
2. Season the milk with ground almonds and coconut, dissolved in milk and passed through a sieve
3. Heat to a boil, adding at the same time cloves, cinnamon, and anise enclosed in a piece of gauze tied with string
4. Once it boils, remove from the heat and immediately pour into the serving dish
5. Add six egg whites that have been beaten to stiff peaks, having them ready beforehand
6. Stir quickly, but only with two turns of the spoon, and let the foam that forms in the moment reappear
7. Let it cool and serve
