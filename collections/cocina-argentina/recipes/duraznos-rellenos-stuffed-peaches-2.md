## Ingredients

- peaches
- water
- ground cooked meat
- hard-boiled eggs, chopped
- Corinth raisins
- white onion, finely chopped
- tomato juice
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- cumin
- broth

## Directions

1. Boil the peaches in a regular quantity of water. Give them two short boils, remove from water and let cool.
2. Cut a small round piece from the side of the stem, and by pressing and using the tip of a knife, extract the pit, filling them with ground cooked meat, chopped hard-boiled eggs, and Corinth raisins.
3. In a heat-resistant dish, prepare a sauce made of finely chopped white onion, tomato juice, butter, pepper, salt, a pinch of cumin and half a cup of broth.
4. Place the stuffed peaches in the dish, taking care to close the filling with the small round pieces that were removed, and arrange them in a group, one next to the other, so they cook without accident.
5. Cover the dish and place coals on top, cook slowly over low heat for half an hour.
6. Serve in the same dish on a platter.
