## Ingredients

- Duck
- Duck giblets (liver, gizzard, heart)
- Stock
- Butter
- Four eggs
- Grated bread
- Milk
- Salt
- Pepper
- Almonds
- Málaga raisins
- Olives
- Roasted tomato juice
- Parsley
- Peanuts
- Cream or good milk
- Four cooked egg yolks
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Debone the duck carefully, keeping it whole.
2. Cook the giblets (liver, gizzard, heart) in the cooking stock.
3. Chop the cooked giblets very finely, along with a piece of cooked meat.
4. Melt and warm a piece of butter, beat it until foamy.
5. Mix in four eggs, grated bread, milk, salt, pepper, almonds, and Málaga raisins.
6. Combine this mixture with the chopped meats, add olives, and fill the duck with this stuffing.
7. Sew the duck closed with a few stitches to prevent the filling from spilling out.
8. Place in the oven.
9. Prepare a sauce: either made from roasted tomato juice strained through a sieve, with ground parsley and ground peanuts from the mortar, seasoned with oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper; or made from parsley, grated toasted bread, cream or good milk, four cooked and ground egg yolks, and the necessary seasoning of oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
10. Arrange the cooked duck in its serving dish and pour the sauce over it.
11. Remove the stitches holding the filling in place.
