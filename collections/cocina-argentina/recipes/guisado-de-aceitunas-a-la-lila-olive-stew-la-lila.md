## Ingredients

- black olives, the meatiest
- butter
- three onion heads
- parsley
- capers
- flour
- good broth
- pepper
- salt
- one glass of Champagne wine
- pure oil

## Directions

1. Soak the black olives in warm water for half an hour
2. Wash them also with warm water and pit them carefully, keeping them whole
3. Make a sauce with three onion heads, parsley, and capers very finely minced and cooked in butter
4. When the onion begins to brown, sprinkle and stir in one tablespoon of flour, then three tablespoons of good broth to which pepper and salt have been added
5. Pour in one glass of Champagne wine
6. A moment later, mix in the olives
7. Let it boil for five minutes
8. When transferring to the serving dish, add a few drops of pure oil
