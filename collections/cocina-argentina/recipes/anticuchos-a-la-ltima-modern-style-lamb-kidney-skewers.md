## Ingredients

- lamb kidneys
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- garlic
- mirasol chili pepper (or red chili pepper)
- fresh bacon
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash the kidneys in cold water.
2. Prick them with a thick needle and soak them for half an hour in water seasoned with salt.
3. Place them in a marinade of oil and vinegar, pepper, minced garlic, and powdered mirasol chili pepper (or red chili if mirasol is not available), for two hours.
4. After marinating, split the kidneys in half.
5. Mix them with very thin slices of fresh bacon.
6. Thread them on a thin wooden skewer.
7. Place over a strong fire, turning constantly.
8. Brush continuously with butter mixed with a spoonful of marinade using a feather.
9. Cook quickly and serve very hot.
