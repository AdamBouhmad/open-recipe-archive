## Ingredients

- One quarter leche (unit) of milk
- One quarter leche of cream
- Sugar to taste
- Sponge cake slices (bizcochuelos)
- Natural cream for moistening
- Candied fruits for decoration

## Directions

1. Dissolve cream in a quarter portion of milk and sweeten to taste.
2. Beat with a hand mill (molinillo) until much foam forms.
3. Arrange a layer of sponge cake slices moistened with natural cream on a plate.
4. Spoon the foam by spoonfuls over the cake layer.
5. Serve decorated with candied fruits.
