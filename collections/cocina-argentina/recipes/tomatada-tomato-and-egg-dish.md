## Ingredients

- Ripe tomatoes
- Onion heads
- Butter
- Pepper
- Salt
- Beef broth, good quality, 1 cup
- Raw cream, 1 cup
- Vinegar, 2 tablespoons
- Good olive oil, 1 tablespoon
- Bread, toasted on grill, 3-4 slices
- Eggs, 1 per person
- Parsley sprigs for garnish

## Directions

1. Cook together ripe tomatoes and onion heads.
2. Remove seeds from the tomatoes and cut into regular pieces; cut the onions into slices.
3. Place them in an enameled pot to season over low heat for a while with a piece of butter, pepper and salt, stirring for ten minutes.
4. Pour over them one cup of good broth and one cup of raw cream.
5. Stir again; add two tablespoons of vinegar and one of good oil.
6. Submerge three or four slices of bread toasted on a grill in this sauce.
7. Break eggs over this as if to fry them, regularly one per person.
8. Cover the pot and leave it on the fire for one minute.
9. Bring to the table in the same vessel, which should be placed on a platter, festooned with parsley sprigs to cover the edges.
