## Ingredients

- thin slices of cold bread
- fresh cheese or grated Gruyere cheese
- fried chorizo, sliced
- seasoned broth
- onions, finely chopped
- garlic clove, ground
- pepper
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cut very thin slices of cold bread and toast them on the grill
2. Layer the toasted bread in an enameled porcelain skillet
3. Over each bread layer, place a layer of fresh cheese slices or grated Gruyere and fried chorizo slices
4. Over each of these layers, pour two kitchen spoonfuls of broth seasoned with a sauce of finely chopped onions, one ground garlic clove, and pepper
5. When the skillet is filled with these layers, cover with a layer of grated cheese and fried chorizo slices
6. Cover with a lid and cook over low heat
7. Just before serving, break as many eggs on top as there are people at table
8. Once the eggs are cooked, serve in the same skillet placed on a tray, with parsley branches adorning the edges
