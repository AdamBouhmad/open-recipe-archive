## Ingredients

- Purple corn, kernels removed from the cob
- Purple corn cobs (maslos)
- Water, regular quantity
- Sour cherries (guindas)
- Pineapple rinds or ananas peels
- Lemons, two, cut in pieces
- Sugar
- Cinnamon sticks
- Whole cloves
- Pineapple slices
- Cherries, two to three dozen

## Directions

1. With the kernels removed, place purple corn with its cobs to cook in a regular amount of water, with sour cherries, pineapple or ananas rinds, and two lemons cut in pieces
2. Let cook until the corn bursts open like a flower
3. Remove from heat and strain through thin gauze
4. Let cool and season with sugar, pieces of cinnamon, whole cloves, pineapple slices and two or three dozen cherries
5. Place to ferment in glass or clay vessels for three days
6. After this time, this confection has become the most exquisite beverage of its kind
7. Serve by adding new seasoning of sugar and cinnamon
