## Ingredients

- eggs
- milk
- salt
- parsley
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Break the number of eggs according to the size you wish to give the omelet. Beat separately the yolks and the whites: the latter to stiff peaks, the former as for sponge cake.
2. Meanwhile, put a good piece of butter in a pan on the fire. When it begins to boil, quickly mix yolks and whites; stir so that they incorporate at the same time as you season with salt and chopped parsley, and fry.
3. Since its lightness causes rapid cooking, turn it from bottom to top, lifting it with the blades of two knives, and arrange on this cooked surface a layer of cream, which a moment later will be covered by folding the omelet in the shape of a turnover.
4. Serve with cooked potatoes, placed around as reliefs alternating with sprigs of parsley.
