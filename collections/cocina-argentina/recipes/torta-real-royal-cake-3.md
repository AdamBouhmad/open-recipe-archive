## Ingredients

- puff pastry dough
- dulce de leche (milk preserve)
- peach preserve
- cherry or strawberry preserve

## Directions

1. Make a puff pastry dough, and cut three molds from it the size of the dish in which the preparation is to be made.
2. Spread one at the bottom.
3. Place it in the oven, and once cooked remove it; place another and put it equally in the oven, from where, once ready, remove it to cook the third.
4. On the puff pastry at the bottom of the dish, spread a layer of dulce de leche or milk preserve; on top of this, another of peach preserve, and after this, the second puff pastry, on which arrange a layer of cherry or strawberry preserve, and a layer
