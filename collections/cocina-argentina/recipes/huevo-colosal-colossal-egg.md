## Ingredients

- two prepared bladders, one smaller than the other
- eggs

## Directions

1. Take two prepared bladders, inflated and dried, one smaller than the other
2. Break eggs, separating the whites from the yolks, until there is enough yolk to fill the small bladder
3. Fill the small bladder with yolks and, once well sealed in its round form, cook it in boiling water
4. When the yolk is hard, remove it from the water and, breaking the bladder, extract the yolk in the form it has taken, and place it inside the large bladder, which has previously been filled with whites
5. The yolk remains in the middle of the white
6. Close it also in its round form and place it in boiling water
7. When the white has acquired the necessary hardness in its cooking, break the bladder, from which comes out a colossal egg, which looks beautiful on the table, placed on a spicy sauce, with decorations of parsley and lettuce hearts, bathed in vinegar and oil, and seasoned with spices
