## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt
- vinegar
- oil
- pepper
- cream puree

## Directions

1. Lightly boil the asparagus in salted water without cooking them too much, to preserve their most delicate part.
2. Tie them in small bundles that represent one serving.
3. Marinate them for two hours in a mixture of vinegar and oil seasoned with salt and pepper.
4. When serving, transfer them to a dish of cream puree.
