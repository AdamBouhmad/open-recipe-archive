## Ingredients

- Dried beans
- Onion head
- Fresh bacon without salt, a piece
- Pork fat, fresh, half a pound
- Onion heads, 2, finely chopped
- Garlic cloves, 2, ground
- Salt

## Directions

1. Soak the dried beans in cold water for twenty-four hours, by which time the skin will have loosened; remove it carefully.
2. Wash them very well and place them to cook in cold water with an onion head and a piece of fresh bacon without salt.
3. Heat half a pound of fresh pork fat together with two finely chopped onion heads, two ground garlic cloves, and salt; when this sauce is golden, add the beans which should be almost falling apart, as they must be cooked for at least three hours.
4. Stir well until they come to a boil again, and stirring constantly, let them fall apart completely and reach the smoothness and consistency of a purée.
5. Cut the bacon into pieces and add it to the beans.
