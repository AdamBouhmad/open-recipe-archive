## Ingredients

- sábalo (a fish)
- brine
- butter
- fine herbs
- pepper
- nutmeg
- breadcrumbs
- white wine
- lemon

## Directions

1. Scale, clean, and empty the sábalo, then place it in brine in an earthenware pot for one hour, turning it occasionally so that it salts evenly
2. Select a fire-resistant serving dish and place a layer of butter and finely chopped fine herbs on it
3. Place the sábalo on this layer, dusting it on both sides with pepper and nutmeg
4. Cover with a layer of breadcrumbs, moisten with a bottle of white wine
5. Add another layer of breadcrumbs and pour melted butter over the sábalo, which should not be fully covered by the wine
6. Place in the oven, opening it occasionally to check the cooking and baste it with its own broth
7. Serve in the same dish in which it was roasted, accompanied by lemon halves for those who wish to season it with juice
