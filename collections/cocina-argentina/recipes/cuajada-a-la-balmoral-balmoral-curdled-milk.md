## Ingredients

- good fresh milk, preferably the last milk from the cow's udder
- the purple part of 2 thistle flowers, ground fine
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Prepare the thistle flower parts, finely ground in a mortar and enclosed in thin gauze
2. Milk the cow and use the milk while still warm with natural body heat
3. Put the milk in a deep dish
4. Stir the milk with the thistle flower bundle for two minutes, squeezing it and stirring in all directions
5. Let the milk rest after two minutes
6. Test the coagulation by moving the container; when the milk does not move when shaken slightly, the curdled milk is ready
7. Serve in bowls with a generous sprinkling of sugar and cinnamon
8. Alternatively, pour the milk into cups immediately after adding the thistle flower
