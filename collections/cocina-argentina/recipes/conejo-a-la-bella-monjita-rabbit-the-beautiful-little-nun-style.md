## Ingredients

- rabbit
- vinegar
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- oregano
- cumin
- garlic
- butter
- bacon
- flour
- Porto wine or sweet wine
- small onions
- pickled gherkins
- olives
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the rabbit with all cleanliness requirements and cut it into four quarters
2. Place the quarters for one hour in an adobo of vinegar, oil, salt, pepper, oregano, a pinch of cumin and one crushed garlic clove
3. After removing from the adobo, dry it well with a napkin and fry it in butter together with small pieces of bacon the size and shape of dice
4. When the bacon has browned, remove it, leaving the rabbit to finish frying
5. Once the rabbit is fried, return the bacon to the pan; add two tablespoons of flour and mix it well to incorporate it
6. After one minute of boiling, pour in half a bottle of Porto wine or another sweet wine
7. Cook over high heat with seasoning of salt and pepper and four or five small onions until the sauce has reduced and thickened
8. Serve with pickled gherkins alternating with olives around the edges of the platter and sprigs of parsley on the surface
