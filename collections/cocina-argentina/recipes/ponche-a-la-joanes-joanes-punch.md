## Ingredients

- pieces of sugar
- lemon peel
- hot water
- juice of two lemons
- one bottle of good cognac
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Rub pieces of sugar on the peel of ripe lemons
2. Use these to sweeten the hot water in the punch bowl
3. Add the juice of two lemons
4. Add one bottle of good cognac
5. Serve hot in cups, placing two thin slices of lemon in each cup
