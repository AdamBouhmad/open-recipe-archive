## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- butter
- fine herbs
- onions
- clove spice
- pig liver
- fresh bacon
- mushrooms
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Kill the suckling pig in the same manner as a pig, but extract all the blood so the meat retains its natural whiteness.
2. Plunge it in boiling water for half a minute to remove the hair.
3. Extend it on the kitchen table and scrape it with a knife until it is clean.
4. Rub it with rough linen and wash it repeatedly.
5. Remove the legs and when cleaning the interior, leave the kidneys.
6. Fill the interior with a mixture of butter, finely chopped fine herbs, onions studded with clove spice, the cooked and chopped liver of the suckling pig mixed with fresh bacon and cooked mushrooms, seasoned with salt.
7. Secure the thighs with larding needles and spit it on the spit.
8. Once well heated and beginning to roast, sprinkle it three or four times with salt-seasoned water.
9. Then baste it with oil from time to time so the skin toasts well and crisps when chewed.
10. Serve very hot, straight from the spit to the table.
11. Serve various sauces separately according to the taste of the diners.
