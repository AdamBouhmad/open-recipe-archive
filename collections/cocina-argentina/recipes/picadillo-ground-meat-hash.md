## Ingredients

- A piece of beef from the ham section
- Two or three onion heads
- A bunch of parsley and spearmint
- Juice of one roasted tomato passed through a sieve
- Two or three ounces of pork fat from fresh bacon, fried
- Broth
- Vinegar
- Some almonds
- Raisins from Málaga
- A pinch of cumin
- Egg slices for garnish

## Directions

1. Wash the beef and cook it with two boils
2. Remove from heat, dry with a napkin, and grind in a mortar or with a stone
3. Shred it and chop it finely with a knife
4. Grind and shred again
5. Chop the onions and parsley with spearmint according to quantity
6. Add the tomato juice and put everything in an enameled iron or porcelain pot with the pork fat
7. Cook until the onion turns golden
8. Place the ground meat in this sauce and add a few tablespoons of broth, a little vinegar, some almonds and Málaga raisins, and a pinch of cumin
9. Cook over moderate heat
10. After half an hour of cooking, remove from heat but keep warm
11. If it has dried too much, add two tablespoons of broth
12. Serve with egg slices as garnish
