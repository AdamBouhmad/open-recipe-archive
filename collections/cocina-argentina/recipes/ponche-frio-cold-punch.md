## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound of powdered sugar
- 1 lemon, cut in slices
- 1 cup of rum, Jamaica brandy or cognac
- 1 bottle of good white wine

## Directions

1. Mix in a punch bowl and, well covered, let infuse for half an hour before drinking
