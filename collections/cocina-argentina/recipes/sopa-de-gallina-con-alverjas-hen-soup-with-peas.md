## Ingredients

- hen
- salt
- celery
- garlic clove
- parsley
- lettuce leaves
- raw peas
- two ounces of butter
- two tablespoons of flour
- bread fried in butter
- hen meat

## Directions

1. Cook the hen with very little salt, celery, and one garlic clove until tender
2. Grind together parsley, lettuce leaves, and raw peas
3. Put two ounces of butter and two tablespoons of flour in a saucepan and melt them over fire, stirring
4. Add the vegetable mixture and let rest for ten minutes at low heat
5. Pour the hen broth over this mixture and let it boil
6. The soup should be somewhat thick
7. Strain into a soup tureen over pieces of bread fried in butter and pieces of hen meat
