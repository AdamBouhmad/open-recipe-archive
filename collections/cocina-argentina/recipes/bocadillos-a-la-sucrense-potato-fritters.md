## Ingredients

- two dozen chuños (frozen potatoes)
- three onion heads
- one tomato
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- cumin
- aji powder
- butter
- eight egg yolks
- pork fat

## Directions

1. Soak two dozen chuños for two days.
2. Wash them thoroughly in several waters and cook them.
3. Once well cooked, squeeze them and grind them.
4. Cook a sauce in butter made with three onion heads, a seeded tomato, and a handful of parsley.
5. Grind everything finely and season with salt, pepper, cumin, and a bit of aji powder if desired.
6. Mix the potato paste and sauce vigorously so they incorporate well.
7. Add a beaten mixture of eight egg yolks.
8. Fry by spoonfuls in pork fat, flattening the mass with the back of the spoon so it takes the shape of a fritter.
9. Serve with a garnish of candied eggs.
