## Ingredients

- 16 egg yolks
- 3/4 liter very sweetened milk
- 4 to 6 orange leaves
- 4 ounces ground almonds, dissolved in milk
- sponge cake crumbs
- egg, beaten
- butter for frying
- sugar and cinnamon for coating

## Directions

1. Pour the egg yolks into the very sweetened milk and pass through a sieve
2. Put on fire and when it begins to boil, add four to six orange leaves
3. When thickened, mix in the ground almonds dissolved in milk and passed through a sieve
4. Let it reach a high point of thickening and pour into a dish or deep bowls over sponge cake crumbs
5. Let cool and cut with a knife tip into thin slices
6. Dip each slice in beaten egg and then in sponge cake crumbs
7. Fry in butter
8. Coat with sugar and cinnamon and serve
