## Ingredients

- dozen eggs
- butter
- mushrooms
- flour
- roast juice
- white wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. With a dozen beaten eggs, make an omelet fried in butter, being careful to lift it with two forks so that it cooks evenly.
2. When half cooked, remove part of the center, being careful not to perforate it. Fill this hollow with mushrooms previously prepared as follows:
3. Make a sauce of butter with a spoonful of flour dissolved in a little roast juice; half a glass of white wine, and the juice of a lemon. Cook the necessary mushrooms, cut into small pieces, in this sauce.
4. Once cooked, remove the mushrooms with a skimmer and fill the omelet, folding it over itself to cover the filling. After stirring, finish cooking it. Serve it, sprinkled with the sauce in which the mushrooms have cooked.
