## Ingredients

- fish
- red wine vinegar
- onion slices
- garlic clove
- pepper
- lard
- breadcrumbs
- nutmeg
- hard-boiled eggs
- broth
- puff pastry
- butter
- green olives
- shrimp tails

## Directions

1. Place the fish for eight hours in a marinade of red wine vinegar, onion slices, one ground garlic clove and whole pepper. This is understood after it has been washed and the bones removed.
2. Then chop very finely, fry in lard, mix in breadcrumbs, a little nutmeg, chopped hard-boiled eggs and broth in very small quantity so it does not become watery.
3. Arrange all this in a dish that can resist the heat of cooking; covering the filling with puff pastry, put in the oven, taking care to place on the pastry a paper spread with butter to prevent it from burning, removing it five minutes before taking the pastry from the oven.
4. Serve with garnishes of green olives and shrimp tails.
