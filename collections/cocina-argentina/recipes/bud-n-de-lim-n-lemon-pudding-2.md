## Ingredients

- three-quarters cup of rich milk
- 2 ounces of butter
- 4 ounces of sugar
- half teaspoon of powdered cinnamon
- 10 ounces of bread crumbs
- zest of the peel of 1 lemon
- 4 eggs
- 1 large tablespoon of good cognac
- butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Boil three-quarters cup of milk
2. When it has boiled, add 2 ounces of butter and 4 ounces of sugar with half a teaspoon of powdered cinnamon
3. Mix with 10 ounces of bread crumbs and the zest of lemon peel
4. Beat 4 eggs and add them along with 1 large tablespoon of good cognac
5. Butter the mold, place the pudding in it, and put in the oven at moderate heat to cook for one hour
