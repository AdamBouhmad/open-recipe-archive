## Ingredients

- corn (choclo), grated and well ground
- broth, one cup
- onion, abundant
- tomatoes
- garlic, a little
- pepper, a little
- yellow chili, well ground, much
- salt
- poached chicken pieces or pork meat
- white corn flour, one or two tablespoons if needed

## Directions

1. Grate the corn and grind it very well in a mortar
2. Add one cup of broth and strain through a thin cloth, squeezing very well to extract the juice
3. Make a sauce with abundant onion, tomatoes, a little garlic and a little pepper, much well ground yellow chili and salt
4. Once cooked, add the corn and pieces of poached chicken or pork meat
5. Boil everything together until it thickens
6. If it has not thickened enough, add one or two tablespoons of white corn flour, diluting it in broth, stirring constantly so it does not stick
7. When you know by the taste that it is cooked, remove from fire and send to the table
