## Ingredients

- One pejerrey fish
- Salt
- One onion head
- Peppercorns
- Bread crumbs dissolved in milk
- Four eggs, whites and yolks
- Butter
- Pepper
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cook a pejerrey with salt, an onion head, and some peppercorns
2. Once cooked, carefully remove the bones and spines, shredding the fish well with your hands to find any remaining spines
3. Mix with bread crumbs dissolved in milk and the whites and yolks of four eggs beaten separately: the whites whipped to stiff peaks, the yolks beaten with one ounce of unmelted butter
4. Stir and combine, seasoning with pepper and salt
5. Butter the pudding mold, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, fill with the prepared mixture, and place in the oven
6. Serve with hard-boiled eggs cut in half and thin slices of bread toasted on the grill
