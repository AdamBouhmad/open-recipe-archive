## Ingredients

- one quarter of a liter of good milk
- half a pound of sugar
- six egg yolks
- cinnamon powder
- three dozen slices of sponge cake
- cream
- egg threads
- white candies

## Directions

1. Prepare a quarter of a liter of good milk, which after a boil, let it cool.
2. Add half a pound of sugar and six egg yolks half-beaten, seasoning with cinnamon powder.
3. Cut three dozen slices of sponge cake, soak them in that milk, and arrange a layer of them at the bottom of a dish.
4. On this layer of sponge cake, spread another of cream, on which pour a strong sprinkling of sugar and cinnamon.
5. Then another layer of sponge cake, and another of cream, alternating until the dish is filled with one of sponge cake, and place it in the oven for a quarter of an hour, with moderate heat.
6. Serve with reliefs of egg threads and white candies.
