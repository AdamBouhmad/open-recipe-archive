## Ingredients

- pork fat
- pieces of bacon
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- pepper
- fava beans
- salt
- bay leaf
- oregano
- mint
- thyme
- ham
- blood sausage
- half a small cup of anise liqueur

## Directions

1. Put pork fat in a pot. When it is hot, add pieces of bacon, garlic, onion, and parsley in good quantity.
2. When this is sautéed, add a sprinkling of pepper; then add the fava beans and sufficient salt.
3. Let them cook on low heat for one hour, with the pot covered by a deep plate in which you will put water, a bay leaf, oregano, mint, and thyme. The plate itself should be covered, and from time to time drops of the water it contains should be added to the stew.
4. After one hour of cooking, add ham and blood sausage cut into small pieces, and half a small cup of anise liqueur.
5. Let it season thus for half an hour, and serve. This is an extremely appetizing dish.
