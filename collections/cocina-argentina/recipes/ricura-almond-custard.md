## Ingredients

- 1 pound of almonds, finely ground
- 1 pound of sugar
- 2 cups of water
- 6 eggs, beaten

## Directions

1. Make a syrup in high point with 1 pound of sugar and 2 cups of water
2. Pour the ground almonds into the boiling syrup, stir
3. Add the beaten eggs to the mixture
4. Fill a sponge cake with this paste, carefully hollowing out the center
5. Place in a regularly heated oven
