## Ingredients

- Rice
- Water
- Gruyère cheese, grated
- Four egg yolks
- One dozen almonds
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Toast the rice, then wash it well and cook in unsalted water
2. Once well cooked, drain in a napkin to extract the water
3. Mix with grated Gruyère cheese, four egg yolks, one dozen finely ground almonds, pepper, and very little salt because of the cheese
4. Stir and mix everything very well
5. Place in the oven at moderate heat
