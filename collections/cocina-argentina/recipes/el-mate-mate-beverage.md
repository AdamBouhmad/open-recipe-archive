## Ingredients

- yerba mate
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil water with three boilings in a clay or enameled iron kettle
2. In the mate already filled with yerba alone, and when the water gives its first boil, pour two spoonfuls of cold water through the bombilla
3. Press without stirring, and let the yerba become moistened, so that the action of cold water draws out its aroma
4. When the water from the kettle has given three boils, extract from the mate the cold water that remains after moistening the yerba by carefully tilting the mate and pressing it on the other side with the bombilla
5. Add sugar, and after this pour the hot water calmly
6. When serving, move the bombilla around but without lifting it
