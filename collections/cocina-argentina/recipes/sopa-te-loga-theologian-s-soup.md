## Ingredients

- turkey meat
- chicken meat
- squabs
- beef
- lamb
- head and feet of meat
- handful of chickpeas
- salt
- bread, cut into dice shape
- pork fat or butter
- carrots
- green peas
- cabbage
- onions
- salt and hot water
- bird giblets (livers, hearts, gizzards, and ovaries)
- hard-boiled eggs, sliced

## Directions

1. Make broth with turkey meat, chicken, squabs, beef, lamb, head and feet, and a handful of chickpeas, seasoned with salt to taste and cooked slowly in a covered pot
2. Place diced bread pieces, fried in pork fat or butter, in the soup tureen beforehand
3. Cook carrots, green peas, and cabbage separately in a separate broth
4. Soak onions in salt and hot water
5. Chop all the bird giblets (livers, hearts, gizzards, and ovaries) into small pieces
6. Add slices of hard-boiled eggs
7. Pour the very hot broth over all this
8. Cover the tureen again and let it rest for ten minutes before serving
