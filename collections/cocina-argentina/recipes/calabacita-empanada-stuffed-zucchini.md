## Ingredients

- calabacitas (zucchini)
- tomates (tomatoes)
- cebollas (onions)
- ajos (garlic)
- ají verde (green chili)
- mantequilla (butter)
- sal (salt)
- pimienta (pepper)
- azafrán (saffron)
- culantro seco (dried cilantro)
- vinagre fuerte (strong vinegar)
- pasas de uva (raisins)
- almendras tostadas (toasted almonds)
- aceitunas negras (black olives)

## Directions

1. Peel the zucchini and chop them with tomatoes, onions, garlic, and green chili
2. Coat in sufficient butter and salt
3. Once cooked in that sauce, add a little pepper, saffron, and dried powdered cilantro, all dissolved in a little strong vinegar, with raisins, toasted almonds, and black olives
