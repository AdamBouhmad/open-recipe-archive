## Ingredients

- 4 pounds of sweet potatoes
- 1/4 of milk
- 5 pounds of sugar
- 12 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
- 2 cups of Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Cook 4 pounds of sweet potatoes
2. Peel them and dissolve in 1/4 of milk, strain through a sieve
3. Mix with syrup made in high point with 5 pounds of sugar
4. Stir and cook over fire until it reaches the point of dulce de leche
5. Remove from fire and add 12 egg yolks, 1 teaspoon of cinnamon, and 2 cups of Sherry wine
6. Beat together and pour spoon by spoon over the papilla, stirring constantly to prevent the egg from breaking when cooking
7. Return to fire, stirring always, and when it begins to boil, remove from fire and pour into dessert dishes
8. Beat much so that it whitens
