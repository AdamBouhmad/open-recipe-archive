## Ingredients

- one pound of very well ground almonds
- one pound of sugar
- two glasses of water
- six eggs

## Directions

1. Make a high-point syrup with one pound of sugar and two glasses of water
2. In this boiling syrup, add one pound of very well ground almonds, stir and add the beaten eggs
3. Fill a sponge cake with this paste by hollowing out the center carefully
4. Place in the oven at regular heat
