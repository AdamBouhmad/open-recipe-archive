## Ingredients

- avocados at their first sign of ripeness
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- tender lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Select avocados at their first sign of ripeness.
2. Remove the skin and cut them into thin vertical slices.
3. Arrange them in layers in the salad bowl, pouring over each layer by spoonfuls the oil and vinegar beaten with salt, followed by a light sprinkling of pepper.
4. If to be served at a table, place an avocado at the center as a garnish, over tender lettuce leaves.
