## Ingredients

- pork tripe and intestines
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- onion
- tomato
- garlic
- oregano
- pepper
- lettuce hearts
- radishes

## Directions

1. Well washed, scraped and prepared, cook the pork tripe and intestines in lightly salted water
2. Once cooked, chop them finely and place them for two hours in an adobo of oil and vinegar, onion, tomato, garlic, oregano, salt and pepper
3. Serve with lettuce hearts basted in oil and vinegar seasoned with salt and pepper, alternating with radishes cut in quarters
