## Ingredients

- thick slices of white onion heads
- hard-boiled eggs
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- French mustard

## Directions

1. Cut onion heads into thick slices and cook them with a boil of half an hour.
2. Drain the water so they remain dry and can be removed from the pot without losing their shape.
3. Arrange them in layers in the salad bowl, alternating with hard-boiled eggs cut in half.
4. Pour over everything by spoonfuls a mixture of oil and vinegar beaten with pepper and a spoonful of French mustard.
