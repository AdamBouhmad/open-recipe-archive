## Ingredients

- peaches
- water
- cooked meat, chopped
- hard-boiled eggs, chopped
- Corinth raisins
- white onion, finely chopped
- tomato juice
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- cumin
- beef broth, half cup

## Directions

1. Peel the peaches delicately, taking care not to remove flesh, and place them in a regular amount of water to boil.
2. Give them two short boils, remove from water and let them cool.
3. Cut a round piece from the side where the stem is, and pressing them and using the knife point, extract the pit through this opening.
4. Fill them with picadillo of cooked meat, chopped hard-boiled eggs, and Corinth raisins.
5. Prepare a sauce previously cooked and composed of finely chopped white onion, tomato juice, butter, pepper, salt, a pinch of cumin and half a cup of broth.
6. Place the stuffed peaches in an oven-safe dish with this sauce, taking care to close the filling by placing the small rounds removed to extract the pit.
