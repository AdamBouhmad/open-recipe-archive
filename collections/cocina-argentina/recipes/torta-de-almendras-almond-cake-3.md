## Ingredients

- One pound pulverized sugar
- One pound peeled almonds, finely ground
- Twelve eggs
- Half ounce cinnamon
- Butter for the mold
- Six egg whites
- Half pound pulverized sugar for the glaze
- Cinnamon for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Beat the twelve egg yolks and two egg whites.
2. Continue beating until thickened, then mix in the sugar and beat again.
3. Add the ground almonds and stir, beating with two spoons to incorporate both substances while sprinkling with half an ounce of cinnamon.
4. Butter a mold or heat-resistant dish and place the batter in it.
5. Bake in a hot oven until cooked.
6. Remove from the oven and coat with a meringue made from six egg whites and half pound of pulverized sugar, sprinkled with cinnamon.
7. Return to a warm oven to dry the glaze.
