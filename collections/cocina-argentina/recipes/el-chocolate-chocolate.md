## Ingredients

- Chocolate, previously grated, one ounce per cup
- Water or milk

## Directions

1. Place one ounce of previously grated chocolate in each cup and fill it with the water or milk in which it is to be made
2. Pour into the chocolate pot and place on the fire, stirring constantly with the beater to mix it
