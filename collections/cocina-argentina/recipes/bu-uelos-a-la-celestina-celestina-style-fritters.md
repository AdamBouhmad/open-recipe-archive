## Ingredients

- One pound flour (flor de harina)
- Salt
- Five ounces butter
- Twenty egg yolks and eight egg whites, beaten
- Butter for frying
- Red wine syrup
- Cinnamon for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of flour with a pinch of salt and five ounces of butter, stirring constantly during cooking.
2. When it begins to sound when stirred, remove from heat and let cool.
3. When lukewarm, mix twenty beaten egg yolks and eight beaten egg whites into the cooked mixture.
4. Drop the batter by spoonfuls into boiling butter, one spoonful per fritter.
5. As each fritter cooks and browns, remove it from the butter and group them together on a dish under a folded cloth to keep warm.
6. Serve drizzled with red wine syrup and sprinkled with cinnamon.
