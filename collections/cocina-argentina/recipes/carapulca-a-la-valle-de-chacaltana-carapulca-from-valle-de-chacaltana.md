## Ingredients

- One pound of dried potato, lightly toasted and ground
- Half a pound of well-fatty pork
- Half a pound of pork fat
- Two large onions
- Two tomatoes
- One clove of garlic
- Ten ground chili peppers, or one teaspoon of paprika if no chili is available
- Salt to taste
- Half a pound of roasted peanuts, finely ground
- Pork broth

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of dried potato, lightly toasted and ground, in water
2. Separately cook half a pound of well-fatty pork
3. Heat half a pound of pork fat until boiling and add two finely chopped large onions, two finely cut tomatoes, one clove of garlic, and ten ground chili peppers (or one teaspoon of paprika and salt to taste)
4. Cook the sauce well
5. Add the cooked dried potato and the pork cut into pieces
6. Boil everything together for a while
7. Before removing from heat, add half a pound of roasted peanuts, finely ground and dissolved in a small cup of pork broth
8. Optional: add poultry meat such as turkey, chicken or squabs, and grated butter rosquillas
