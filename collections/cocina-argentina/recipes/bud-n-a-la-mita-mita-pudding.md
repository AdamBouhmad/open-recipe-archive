## Ingredients

- one liter of milk
- five ounces of sugar
- a small piece of vanilla
- zest of the skin of one lemon
- two small sticks of cinnamon
- seven ounces of starch dissolved in milk
- seven egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put a liter of milk to boil with five ounces of sugar, a small piece of vanilla, the zest of the skin of one lemon, and two small sticks of cinnamon.
2. When it has come to a boil, remove it from the fire, pass it through a sieve, and return the milk to the fire, adding, little by little, seven ounces of starch dissolved in milk.
3. Stir, bringing it to a boil for ten minutes.
4. Let it cool, and add seven well-beaten egg yolks.
5. Place it on the fire for a moment, without stopping beating, and empty it into the pudding mold to cool.
