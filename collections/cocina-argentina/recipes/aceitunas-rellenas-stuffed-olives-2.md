## Ingredients

- large green olives
- toasted bread, grated
- fresh cheese
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, ground
- white broth with white wine
- butter
- white onion, chopped
- parsley
- pickled gherkins
- cumin
- tomato

## Directions

1. Use the largest green olives. Cut the lower extremity and through there, with the tip of a sharp knife, extract the pit without damaging the shape of the fruit.
2. Fill with a sauce made of grated toasted bread, fresh cheese and hard-boiled egg yolks, broken down and kneaded with oil and vinegar, seasoned with salt, pepper and ground parsley.
3. Cover the filling with the cut piece, securing it with very thin small stakes made from thin cane strips.
4. Cook in good broth saturated with white wine; once cooked in one boil, remove from the broth and give them another boil in a sauce made of butter, white onion, parsley and pickled gherkins: all very finely chopped and seasoned with pepper, salt, a little cumin, oil, vinegar and one tablespoon of tomato.
