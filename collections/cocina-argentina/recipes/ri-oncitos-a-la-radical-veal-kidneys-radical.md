## Ingredients

- veal kidney
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- flour
- white wine
- parsley
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the veal kidney lengthwise into two halves.
2. Cut crosswise into very thin slices.
3. Place them with butter in a pan and fry, seasoning with salt and pepper.
4. Once fried, add a cup of broth with flour dissolved in it.
5. Add half a glass of white wine and a bit of chopped parsley.
6. Let cook for a quarter of an hour.
7. Serve with lemon juice.
