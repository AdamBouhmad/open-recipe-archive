## Ingredients

- Pieces of pork adobo: ribs or sirloin
- Pork fat for frying
- Puff pastry sheets

## Directions

1. Cut regular pieces of pork adobo, either ribs or sirloin.
2. Flatten them one by one by pounding them with a heavy object: the pestle of a mortar or pounding tool.
3. Fry them in pork fat in an enameled porcelain pan or glazed earthenware pan.
4. Once cooked, arrange them in a serving dish between two layers of puff pastry.
5. Place in an oven that should be at strong heat due to the short cooking time required.
