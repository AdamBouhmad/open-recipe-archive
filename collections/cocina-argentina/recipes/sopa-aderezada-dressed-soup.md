## Ingredients

- bread
- bacon
- ham
- poultry giblets
- hard-boiled eggs
- grated cheese
- parsley
- whole pepper
- butter
- broth
- hard-boiled eggs for garnish
- artichoke hearts
- fried small birds

## Directions

1. Cut bread into thin slices and toast them carefully on a grill
2. Chop bacon, ham, poultry giblets, and hard-boiled eggs with grated cheese, parsley, and whole pepper
3. Season with butter and cook over fire for ten minutes
4. In a casserole, layer toasted bread and chopped bread, alternating until the casserole is half full
5. Mix the sauce with the broth and pour it over the bread
6. Place over fire until it comes to a boil
7. Serve with slices of hard-boiled eggs, artichoke hearts, and fried small birds as garnish
