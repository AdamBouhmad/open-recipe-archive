## Ingredients

- shrimp shells
- one tablespoon of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- broth
- bread crumbs
- bread crumbs
- egg yolk
- nutmeg
- butter
- shrimp tails

## Directions

1. Place shrimp shells, except those from the head, in the oven to toast well
2. Crush them until they become powder
3. In a pan, melt one tablespoon of butter over fire
4. When melted, mix in the shrimp shell powder along with one tablespoon of flour and stir with a few tablespoons of broth
5. Remove from fire, add broth and bread crumbs
6. Return to fire and let boil
7. Strain through a sieve and if too thick, add more broth
8. When serving, add meatballs made by beating one tablespoon of butter and three eggs until foamy, adding salt and nutmeg, and gradually mixing in bread crumbs to form a thick paste; cook these in separate broth
9. Also add peeled shrimp tails to the soup
