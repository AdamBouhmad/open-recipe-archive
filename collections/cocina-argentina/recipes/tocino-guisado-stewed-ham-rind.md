## Ingredients

- ham rind, cut into small pieces
- butter
- white onion
- tomatoes
- parsley, finely chopped
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the ham rind into small pieces
2. Give them a boil in a sauce made of butter, white onion, tomatoes and finely chopped parsley
3. Break eggs over the top
4. Season with salt, pepper and one tablespoon of vinegar
5. Stir until the egg is cooked and serve
