## Ingredients

- pork fat
- pieces of bacon
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- pepper
- fava beans
- salt

## Directions

1. Put pork fat in a pan and heat it.
2. Add pieces of bacon, garlic, onion and parsley in good quantity.
3. When this is sautéed, add a sprinkling of pepper and then the fava beans and sufficient salt.
4. Let them cook slowly for one hour with the pan covered with a deep plate.
