## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Onions
- Two tomatoes
- Two tablespoons oil
- Two tablespoons red vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Half a glass of spirits
- Fresh bacon strips

## Directions

1. In a glazed earthenware or iron cooking vessel, place onions cut into quarters, two deseeded tomatoes, two tablespoons of oil, and two tablespoons of red vinegar.
2. Add salt, pepper, and half a glass of spirits.
3. Arrange the partridges on top, surrounded above and below with strips of fresh bacon.
4. Cover tightly so that steam does not escape.
5. Do not stir, but shake frequently to prevent burning on the bottom.
