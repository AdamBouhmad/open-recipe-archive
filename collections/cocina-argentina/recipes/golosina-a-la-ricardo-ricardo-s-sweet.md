## Ingredients

- half pound of sweet almonds
- a dozen bitter almonds
- 1 tablespoon of sugar
- zest of the peel of 1 lemon
- half pound of powdered sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 6 egg whites

## Directions

1. Peel, wash and clean the almonds
2. Grind them finely in a marble mortar, frequently adding 1 tablespoon of sugar to prevent the almond paste from becoming oily
3. When the paste is very fine, add the zest of lemon peel, half pound of powdered sugar, and 6 egg yolks
4. Mix everything by beating for half an hour
5. Beat 6 egg whites separately and well
6. Add them at the moment of placing the paste in a mold for the oven, which should be at moderate heat
