## Ingredients

- one liter of good vinegar
- four heads of garlic, ground
- pepper
- cumin, ground
- one head of onion, finely chopped
- oregano powder
- salt
- two bay leaves
- pork loin or ribs
- butter

## Directions

1. In a porcelain or terra-cotta vessel, pour one liter of good vinegar, four heads of ground garlic, pepper, ground cumin, one finely chopped head of onion, oregano powder, salt, and two bay leaves
2. Mix well, stirring with a wooden spoon, and submerge the meat pieces in this mixture, turning them in it
3. Cover the vessel and keep the meat in this adobo for three hours, then hang it to air dry in a cool, shady place with air circulation
4. Place the adobo in an oven-safe dish with butter at the bottom and roast in the oven
