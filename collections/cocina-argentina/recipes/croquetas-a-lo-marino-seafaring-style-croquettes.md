## Ingredients

- chicken meat, roasted in oven, finely minced
- butter
- flour, one tablespoon
- milk, one glass
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- egg yolks, beaten
- bread crumbs
- hard-boiled eggs, halves
- black olives

## Directions

1. Mince finely the roasted oven chicken meat and fry in a pan with butter
2. When half fried, add one tablespoon of flour and stir constantly so it does not burn
3. Pour in one glass of milk and season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg
4. When everything is cooked, incorporated, and in a mass, let it cool
5. Form balls the size of an egg, roll them in beaten egg yolks, then in bread crumbs
6. Fry in butter until golden
7. Place on a serving dish, alternating with halves of hard-boiled eggs and black olives
