## Ingredients

- one beer bread
- butter
- quince jelly
- five egg yolks
- five egg whites
- five ounces of sugar
- milk

## Directions

1. Cut a beer bread into slices. Spread half of these with butter, and the other half with quince jelly.
2. Butter the pudding mold and arrange the slices in layers: one with butter over another with jelly.
3. Beat five egg yolks and the whites with five ounces of sugar and enough milk to cover the slices and fill the mold.
4. Pour over the slices, let soak for two hours, and place in the oven.
