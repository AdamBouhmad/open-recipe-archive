## Ingredients

- a dozen eggs
- a piece of meat from a stew pot
- roasted peanuts
- parsley
- white onion
- pepper
- salt
- a pinch of cumin
- muscatel raisins
- butter
- half a glass of sweet wine
- egg for coating
- breadcrumb shavings
- cheese shavings

## Directions

1. Boil a dozen eggs in water.
2. Remove a piece of cooked meat from a stew pot, cool it in water, dry it, and grind it in a mortar.
3. Grind the roasted peanuts.
4. Chop parsley and white onion.
5. Mix the ground meat with the peanuts, parsley, and onion, seasoning with pepper, salt, a pinch of cumin, and muscatel raisins.
6. Place a piece of freshly churned butter in a pan and heat until it begins to boil.
7. Add the prepared meat mixture to the butter, then add half a glass of sweet wine.
8. Cook for ten minutes and remove from heat.
9. Cool the mixture in a serving dish.
10. Peel the boiled eggs and cut them in half.
11. Remove the yolks and replace them with the meat filling.
12. Close the egg halves and wrap them in beaten egg thickened with breadcrumb and cheese shavings.
13. Fry them in butter that is already boiling in the pan.
14. Serve on a porcelain platter.
