## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters milk
- 2.5 pounds sugar
- 4 ounces starch
- 18 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 6 ounces coconut, ground and moistened with milk
- 4 tablespoons hot milk
- 2 drops lemon essence
- powdered and sifted sugar for coating

## Directions

1. Cook the milk with sugar and starch, stirring constantly with a thin cane
2. When thickened, remove from fire and pour in the half-beaten egg yolks by the spoonful, adding a tablespoon of cinnamon
3. Return to fire, always stirring so it does not stick to the bottom
4. After a good boil, remove from fire
5. Prepare the coconut milk: grind the coconut very fine, moisten with milk, pass through strong cloth to extract all the juice, and mix with four tablespoons of hot milk
6. Pour the coconut preparation into the thickened milk off the fire, stirring vigorously to incorporate
7. Add two drops of lemon essence
8. Pour into a flat dish and let cool
9. When the surface no longer adheres to the touch of a finger, cover with a layer of powdered and sifted sugar
10. Pass a very hot metal sheet over the surface to caramelize it
