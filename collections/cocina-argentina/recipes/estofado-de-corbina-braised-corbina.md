## Ingredients

- Corbina (white fish), cut into regular pieces and washed repeatedly in salted water
- Butter
- Onion, sliced into rounds
- Pepper
- Roasted tomatoes, seeded, peeled and ground
- Garlic, one clove, ground
- Oregano, ground
- Good vinegar, half a bottle
- Water, half a bottle
- Pickled gherkins
- Black olives

## Directions

1. On the bottom of an enameled pot, place over a layer of butter another layer of onion slices with a light dusting of pepper.
2. Over this place a layer of corbina chunks with another light, very light dusting of pepper, and continue thus until done.
3. Once done, spread over all a sauce of roasted tomatoes that have been seeded, peeled and ground, onion slices, pepper, one clove of garlic ground, oregano likewise ground, and half a bottle of good vinegar and half of water.
4. Set to cook over low heat. When the onion is well cooked, the braised dish is ready.
5. Serve with garnishes of pickled gherkins and black olives.
