## Ingredients

- two pounds turtle meat
- three ounces butter
- four garlic cloves, ground
- two ounces roasted peanuts, ground
- onion, sliced
- tomato juice
- parsley, chopped
- salt
- four cups good broth
- one glass wine
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash two pounds of turtle meat well
2. Place it in an enameled pot
3. Fry with three ounces of butter, adding four ground garlic cloves, two ounces of roasted and ground peanuts, sliced onion, tomato juice, chopped parsley and corresponding salt
4. When everything is well fried, add four cups of good broth and a glass of wine
5. Season and let it boil until well cooked
6. Sprinkle with pepper in the soup tureen when serving
