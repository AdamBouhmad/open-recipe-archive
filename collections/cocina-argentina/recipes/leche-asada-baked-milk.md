## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar to sweeten well
- 30 egg yolks
- a handful of wheat flour
- pieces of cinnamon
- live coals

## Directions

1. Fill a baking dish with milk and the necessary sugar to sweeten it well
2. Pass 30 egg yolks through a sieve and add to the milk
3. Add a handful of wheat flour and pieces of cinnamon
4. Put a pot of water on active fire
5. When the water is boiling, place the prepared milk dish on top of the pot as a lid
6. Stir with a spoon as if making mazamorra
7. When it comes to a boil, cover the dish with a metal sheet and place a layer of live coals on top while maintaining active fire under the pot of boiling water
8. When cooking is calculated to be done, uncover the dish and pierce the preparation with a straw; if it comes out dry, it is ready to serve
