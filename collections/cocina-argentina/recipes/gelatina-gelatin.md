## Ingredients

- 2 beef feet, peeled, hooves removed and washed
- 1 fat hen, prepared
- water to cover
- 6 eggs
- 1 glass of good sherry wine
- 1 ounce of powdered cinnamon
- sugar to taste
- juice of 2 sweet oranges
- a few drops of lemon essence

## Directions

1. Cook the beef feet and hen in enough water to cover them for twenty-four hours, being careful to remove fat and add water as the broth reduces
2. When the meat separates from the bones, remove from heat, let cool slightly, and strain through a cloth, pressing to extract the juice
3. Mix the juice with 6 beaten eggs (yolks and whites together), 1 glass of sherry wine, 1 ounce of powdered cinnamon, sugar to taste, juice of 2 sweet oranges, and a few drops of lemon essence
4. Return to the heat and cook slowly for one hour until reduced to about 2 liters
5. Place a piece of bombazine cloth suspended over a receiving vessel to serve as a filter, and pour the hot juice through it by spoonfuls, allowing the egg whites and cinnamon to settle on top so the juice passes through drop by drop and comes out well clarified
6. Place the vessels containing the gelatin in the shade and open air for several hours to gel and set
