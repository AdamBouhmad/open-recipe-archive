## Ingredients

- dried prunes
- whole peeled almonds
- dried peach halves
- preserved cherries
- Málaga raisins
- whole cloves
- cinnamon sticks
- sugar
- water
- 1 ripe pineapple
- ripe peaches
- golden oranges
- 3 tablespoons of Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Cook dried prunes, whole peeled almonds, dried peach halves, preserved cherries, Málaga raisins, whole cloves, and cinnamon sticks with sugar and sufficient water as for compote
2. Remove from fire and let cool
3. Choose a ripe pineapple, peel it, and cut into thin slices
4. Choose the best peaches and oranges of deep golden color and very fresh, peel and cut into small slices
5. Prepare a deep glass or porcelain vessel and arrange by layers: first one of fruit, over it a dusting of sugar and cinnamon, sprinkling with 3 tablespoons of the best Sherry wine, and another layer of compote, and another of fruit, with equal dusting of sugar and cinnamon, and sprinkled with wine
6. The last layer must be of fruit, then fill the container with wine until the fruit is covered
7. Leave from one day to the next, soaked in liquor, fruits and compote, and serve in glasses
8. Used for lunch
