## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- bread slices
- sugar
- cinnamon
- clove
- strong tea infusion
- good milk

## Directions

1. Spread fresh butter on bread slices that will cover the bottom of the soup tureen
2. Sprinkle sugar, cinnamon, and a very small amount of clove on top
3. Pour a very strong tea infusion and double the quantity of good milk over it
