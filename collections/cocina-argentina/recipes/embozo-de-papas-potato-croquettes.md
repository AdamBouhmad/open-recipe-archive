## Ingredients

- papas (potatoes)
- cuatro huevos batidos (four beaten eggs)
- mantequilla (butter)
- sal (salt)
- harina (flour)
- especias (spices)
- cebolla (onion)
- tomate (tomato)
- perejil (parsley)
- carne cocida (cooked meat)
- almendras (almonds)
- pasas de uva (raisins)
- maní tostado y molido (roasted ground peanuts)
- huevos duros (hard boiled eggs)

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes unpeeled, then clean and grind them, making a mass
2. Add four beaten eggs, butter, and salt in necessary measure
3. Mix well. If the mass is loose, add flour bit by bit until it reaches sufficient consistency
4. Meanwhile, cook in butter conditioned with spices, onion, tomato, parsley, and finely chopped cooked meat
5. Add almonds, raisins, roasted ground peanuts, and hard boiled eggs sliced
6. Stir constantly, and when the onion is cooked, remove from fire
7. Cut the potato mass into proportional pieces, extend slightly, place sauce in the center with a spoon, wrap with the mass, and give it the enlarged shape of a potato, being careful to flour the hands well to prevent sticking
8. Fry in butter, turning from one side to the other so they brown evenly
9. Serve without sauce, with parsley sprigs and mint leaves as garnish
