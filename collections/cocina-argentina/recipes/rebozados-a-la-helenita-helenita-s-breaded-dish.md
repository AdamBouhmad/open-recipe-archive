## Ingredients

- mantequilla (butter)
- pan rallado (grated bread)
- queso rallado (grated cheese)
- una, dos o tres cucharadas de crema (one, two or three spoonfuls of cream)
- leche (milk)
- las yemas de dos huevos (yolks of two eggs)
- pimienta (pepper)
- sal (salt)
- huevos (eggs)

## Directions

1. Place a piece of butter in a plate; mix grated bread and cheese with it, one, two or three spoonfuls of cream, or lacking that, good milk, the beaten yolks of two eggs, pepper, and very little salt because of that which the cheese contains
2. Mix all this very well, beating it, and place over fire in an enameled pot over a layer of butter spread on the bottom of the pot
3. When cooking begins to manifest on the surface, break over it as many eggs as desired, taking care that they fall in their form, one beside the other, covering the frying paste
4. Sprinkle pepper, a little salt, and grated cheese over them; cover the pot with its lid, spreading embers and coals over it
5. When the cheese and eggs have taken on a light golden color and the whites have turned white, serve in the same pot, on a serving platter, hiding the edges with sprigs of parsley and mint
