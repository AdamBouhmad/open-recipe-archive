## Ingredients

- beets
- vinegar
- water
- oil
- pepper
- salt
- pickled gherkins
- green olives

## Directions

1. Cook the beets, cut them into thin slices, and soak them in a mixture of vinegar and water for two hours.
2. Drain the water and vinegar, leaving them dry, and pour over them the necessary oil and vinegar, which should have been previously beaten together with a sprinkling of pepper and salt.
3. Incorporate everything and arrange in the salad bowl with an edging of pickled gherkins and green olives.
