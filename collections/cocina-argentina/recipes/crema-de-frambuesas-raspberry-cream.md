## Ingredients

- 16 grams powdered gum arabic
- 125 grams ground sugar
- 1/4 liter milk
- raspberries
- sponge cake biscuits

## Directions

1. Put the powdered gum arabic and ground sugar in a quarter liter of milk and let the gum and sugar dissolve
2. Grind raspberries and pass through a sieve, mixing this juice with the milk
3. Beat the milk strongly and collect the foam as it forms, converting it into cream
4. Arrange the cream in a pyramid on a dish, surrounded by sponge cake biscuits with their lower ends cut so they stand upright
