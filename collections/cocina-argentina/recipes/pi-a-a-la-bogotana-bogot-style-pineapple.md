## Ingredients

- one pineapple
- cinnamon
- clove
- ground sponge cake
- four eggs
- butter
- one glass of Burgundy wine
- cinnamon
- clove
- toasted and ground sugar

## Directions

1. Cook a pineapple; once cooked, peel it, grind it, and mix with powdered cinnamon and clove and ground sponge cake in the quantity necessary to form a paste with the pineapple. Wrap this with four well-beaten eggs, and pour into a buttered mold and cook in the oven. Serve over a sauce made with one glass of Burgundy wine, mixed and boiled with a seasoning of powdered cinnamon and clove and toasted and ground sugar.
