## Ingredients

- rice, one ounce per person
- warm water
- good meat broth
- onions, thinly sliced
- tomato
- whole pepper
- garlic clove, ground
- bell pepper
- oregano
- parsley, chopped
- pork fat or butter
- salt
- fresh cheese slices
- hard-boiled eggs, sliced
- fried sausage, sliced

## Directions

1. Calculate one ounce of rice per person
2. Wash the rice with warm water, rubbing it well
3. Cook it slowly in good meat broth
4. Make a sauce with thinly sliced circular onion slices, tomato, whole pepper, one ground garlic clove, bell pepper, chopped oregano and parsley, and the corresponding pork fat or butter and salt
5. When the onion has browned after coming to a boil, add broth from the pot and put it on the fire
6. When it begins to boil, add the rice already cooked beforehand
7. When serving, place fresh cheese slices, hard-boiled egg slices, and fried sausage slices in the tureen
