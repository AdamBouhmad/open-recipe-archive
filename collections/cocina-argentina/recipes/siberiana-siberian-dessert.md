## Ingredients

- one pound of almonds, blanched and soaked in cold water
- milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Grind very well in a mortar one pound of blanched almonds that have been soaked in cold water
2. Once ground, mix with milk and stir well
3. Strain through a clear cloth, pressing strongly so the almond releases all its juice
4. Grind the resulting pomace again, and mixing it with more milk, strain again through linen in another portion of milk
5. Combine all the strained liquid, sweeten appropriately, place on the heat and stir until it has boiled for five minutes
6. Once boiled, pour into a large porcelain cup, and before serving, place it between ice for one hour
