## Ingredients

- one fat hen
- potatoes
- carrots
- turnips
- green beans
- rice, soaked
- good broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare a fat hen by plucking it very carefully, opening it, washing it repeatedly, as well as its offal, and sprinkling a little salt on it
2. Cut potatoes, carrots, and turnips into slices; mince green beans
3. Put all this with a handful of soaked rice in a pot or cooking vessel, along with a good quantity of good broth and the pieces and offal of the hen
4. Cover very well so the aroma does not escape and cook over good heat until the meat, on the hen's leg, falls apart when pressed
5. Then the cazuela is ready and is served
