## Ingredients

- One fat four-month-old chicken
- Salt
- Water
- Chicken gizzard
- Chicken liver
- Chicken heart
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Kill a fat four-month-old chicken by cutting off its head
2. Collect the blood, which with the gizzard, liver and heart will be cooked with two boils in lightly salted water
3. Chop everything finely and season it to serve as filling for the chicken
4. Pluck and wash the chicken, dry it by pressing it in a napkin
5. Coat it entirely with a feather in a mixture of oil and vinegar beaten with pepper, salt and ground parsley
6. Roll it in bread crumbs and place it on the grill or roaster with a good fire, but not too lively
7. When the breading begins to crust, turned once or twice, coat it with the oil and vinegar mixture, and put it back on the fire, increasing the heat
8. Do this up to three times, in which you will let it take on a golden toast, a sign of being ready to serve
9. Coat it very lightly with the mixture again when sending it to the table
