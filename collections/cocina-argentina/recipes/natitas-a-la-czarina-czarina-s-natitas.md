## Ingredients

- slices of sponge cake
- heavy cream
- butter
- cinnamon
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Soak slices of sponge cake in heavy cream and arrange on a dish previously buttered, alternating with layers of whipped cream dusted with cinnamon and powdered sugar. The bottom layer should be sponge cake and the top layer should be cream. With a thick dusting of sugar, place in a well-heated oven for fifteen minutes.
