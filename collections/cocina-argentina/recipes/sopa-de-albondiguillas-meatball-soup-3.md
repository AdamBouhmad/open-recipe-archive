## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one cup milk
- two ounces butter
- a pinch of salt
- a pinch of pepper
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Break four eggs in a bowl and beat them with one cup of milk, two ounces of butter, a pinch of salt and a pinch of pepper
2. Mix wheat flour into this beaten mixture until a consistent dough forms
3. Form small meatballs the size of an olive from the dough
4. Coat them in flour
5. Fry in butter
6. Place the meatballs in the soup tureen and pour very hot broth over them
