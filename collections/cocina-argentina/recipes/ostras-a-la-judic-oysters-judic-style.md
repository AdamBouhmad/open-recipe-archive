## Ingredients

- Three dozen oysters
- Butter, one piece
- Fine herbs
- Mushrooms, chopped
- Pure olive oil, one tablespoon
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Bread crumbs, hard and grated
- Lemon, one, juiced

## Directions

1. Open three dozen oysters; collect the water they contain, strain it through cloth, and once heated and about to boil, place the oysters in it for four minutes.
2. Remove them from the water immediately, drain them, and place them in a dish that can resist the action of fire, with a piece of butter and a seasoning of fine herbs and chopped mushrooms, one tablespoon of pure olive oil, and a strong seasoning of pepper and nutmeg.
3. Cover all with a layer of hard bread crumbs, grated, and keep the dish over gentle fire and covered with a portable stove provided with lively heat.
4. When the bread crumbs have taken on a golden color, remove the dish from the heat and serve, sprinkling the oysters with the juice of one lemon.
