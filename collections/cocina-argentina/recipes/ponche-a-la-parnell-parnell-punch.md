## Ingredients

- 1/2 bottle of rum
- 2 lemon peels
- sugar
- 1 liter of sweetened tea

## Directions

1. Infuse for one hour in 1/2 bottle of rum the peels of 2 lemons after rubbing them on a piece of sugar
2. Prepare at the same time an infusion of 1 liter of tea sweetened to taste, with the piece of sugar rubbed with lemon included
3. Remove the peels that are infusing and mix the rum to the tea infusion which should be boiling
