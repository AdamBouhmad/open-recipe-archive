## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- truffles
- ground cooked meat
- hard boiled eggs
- melted butter
- fine bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the tomatoes in half widthwise; remove the seeds; carefully scoop out the pulp; pass it through a sieve and incorporate it into a filling prepared with truffles, minced cooked meat, and chopped hard boiled eggs.
2. Fill the tomatoes with this preparation.
3. Put melted butter on the bottom of a heat-resistant baking dish; place the tomatoes in this dish with a thick layer of very fine bread crumbs.
4. Complete the cooking on low heat with hot coals in the dish cover and coals underneath.
