## Ingredients

- potatoes
- hard-boiled eggs
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- garlic

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes, remove their skin, wash them, and cut them into slices, alternating with hard-boiled eggs cut in the same manner.
2. Season with the proper oil, vinegar, and salt, previously beaten together with pepper, one roasted onion head and one roasted garlic clove, washed and ground in the mortar.
