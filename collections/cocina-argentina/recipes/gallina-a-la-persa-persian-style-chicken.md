## Ingredients

- One fat chicken
- Sweet, juicy and fragrant apples
- Bread
- Butter
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare a fat chicken by killing it the day before
2. When emptying it, be careful not to remove the interior fat
3. Choose very sweet, juicy and fragrant apples; chop them finely, mix them with very finely chopped bread pieces, and fry them in butter
4. During frying, add a little sugar, very little, just enough to increase the sweetness of the apple
5. When this has cooked well, stuff the chicken, coat it with butter and roast it for a long time, frequently removing it from the fire to baste it with its own juice, alternating with coatings of butter
