## Ingredients

- onions, twenty, cut into dice
- butter, half a pound
- chicken fat and roasted oven chicken meat
- bread, equal quantity to the chicken, well soaked in cream and then squeezed
- egg yolks, six raw
- salt
- pepper, whole grain and ground
- pure cream, one glass
- sheep intestines, previously inflated and dried

## Directions

1. Cut twenty onions into dice and after washing in boiling water, cook in a pan with half a pound of butter
2. Mince and grind together the chicken fat and roasted oven chicken meat with an equal quantity of bread that has been well soaked in cream and then squeezed
3. Dissolve everything with six raw egg yolks, salt, whole and ground pepper, and one glass of pure cream
4. Add all of this to the pan in which the onions are frying
5. After one minute of boiling, stirring to mix the ingredients, remove from heat and let cool
6. Once cooled, fill the previously inflated and dried sheep intestines with this mixture using the same procedure as for making sausages
