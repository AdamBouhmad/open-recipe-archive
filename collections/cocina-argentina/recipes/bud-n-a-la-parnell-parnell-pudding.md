## Ingredients

- one cup of milk
- three cups of ground and sifted sugar
- four cups of wheat flour
- one cup of butter
- four eggs
- one tablespoon of soda
- one tablespoon of cream of tartar
- one small glass of aniseed liqueur
- one teaspoon of powdered cinnamon
- zest of the skin of one lemon
- half a pound of currants
- a little dried lemon preserve, crumbled

## Directions

1. Beat together: one cup of milk, three cups of ground and sifted sugar, four cups of wheat flour, one cup of butter, four eggs, one tablespoon of soda, one tablespoon of cream of tartar, one small glass of aniseed liqueur, one teaspoon of powdered cinnamon, the zest of the skin of one lemon, half a pound of currants, and a little dried lemon preserve, crumbled.
2. Once well beaten and incorporated, place it in a mold buttered with butter and put it in the oven, which should be very hot.
