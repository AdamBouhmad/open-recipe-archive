## Ingredients

- Two fat chickens
- Salt
- Two whole onion heads
- Two dozen peppercorns
- Bundle of fine herbs
- String
- Chicken giblets
- Chicken gizzard
- Chicken liver
- Chicken heart
- Chicken tripes
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Four hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Kill two fat chickens
2. Wash and clean them well, inside and outside, and place them in a terra cotta pot or enameled iron pot, well submerged in water lightly seasoned with salt, together with two whole onion heads, two dozen peppercorns, and a bundle of fine herbs wrapped in string and pressed when throwing into the pot
3. Boil this cooking over high heat for three hours, adding boiling water little by little as it diminishes with prolonged cooking
4. The giblets, gizzard, liver and heart should cook together with the chickens
5. Clean the tripes, empty them, wash in three waters and cut into four centimeter pieces; once well cleaned inside, which will have been stirred, coat them with a beaten mixture of oil, vinegar, pepper and salt, and stirred again, roast them on the grill
6. When the meat of the leg or breast yields to finger pressure, the chicken is ready
7. Remove them from the pot and stuff them, adding to their giblets four hard-boiled eggs
8. The broth from this cooking is excellent, especially for children
