## Ingredients

- green corn cobs with grain full of sweet milk
- salt to taste
- a very little sugar
- good quantity of pork lard fried with ají
- corn husks
- water
- split cane sticks

## Directions

1. Grate the fresh corn, then grind it in a stone mortar.
2. Once well ground, season with salt to taste, a very little sugar, and a good quantity of pork lard fried with ají and previously strained.
3. Mix everything, stir and beat with a spoon.
4. On two corn husks placed in opposite directions for each huminta, place three spoonfuls of the paste in the center.
5. Fold and tie with twine.
6. To cook in a pot: fill one-third with water, cross split cane sticks over the water surface, arrange the humintas one on top of another, and boil for two hours.
7. Drain the water and serve the humintas in their wrappings.
