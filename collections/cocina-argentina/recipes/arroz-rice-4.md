## Ingredients

- four ounces of lard
- a small piece of chopped onion
- one clove of garlic
- salt
- one pound of rice, washed in several waters
- cold water

## Directions

1. Fry the chopped onion, garlic clove, and salt in four ounces of lard until the sauce is well cooked
2. Add one pound of rice that has been washed in several waters to remove the flour it generally contains, and mix it with the lard
3. Immediately, before the rice cooks, add cold water until the rice is covered
4. Cover very well and cook over low heat for half an hour
5. The rice will then be grainy and very well cooked
