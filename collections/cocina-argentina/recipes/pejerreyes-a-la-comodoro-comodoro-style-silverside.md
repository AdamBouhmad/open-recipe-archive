## Ingredients

- Onion heads
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Tomatoes, seeded
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Butter
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Pejerreyes (silverside fish)
- Water
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pickled onions
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Mince very finely onion heads, hard-boiled eggs and seeded tomatoes; coat with bread crumbs, season with salt, and fry in butter.
2. Once fried, dust with pepper and cumin, and use this to stuff the silverside that will have been cleaned, scaled, and with their spines removed.
3. Arrange them in a fish cooking vessel with water and salt, and covered with its lid, give them one hour of boiling over good heat.
4. Remove them carefully from the fish vessel and transfer them to the one that is to take them to the table.
5. Add oil, vinegar and pepper, and serve with garnishes of pickled onions and sprigs of parsley.
