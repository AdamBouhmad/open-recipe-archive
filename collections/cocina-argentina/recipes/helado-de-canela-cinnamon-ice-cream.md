## Ingredients

- Good cinnamon pieces, well broken
- Water, necessary quantity for the ice cream
- One ounce of ground cinnamon
- Sugar
- A few drops of cinnamon essence

## Directions

1. Cook pieces of good cinnamon, well broken, for one hour in the quantity of water necessary for the ice cream.
2. Add one ounce of ground cinnamon, enclosed in a small bag of flannel or gauze.
3. Remove from the fire and strain through a sieve.
4. Add sugar to taste.
5. When placing it to freeze, add a few drops of cinnamon essence to increase its perfume.
