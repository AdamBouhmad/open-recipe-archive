## Ingredients

- Shrimp, alive
- Salt
- Bundle of fine herbs
- Butter
- Two onion heads, finely chopped
- Parsley
- One garlic clove, well crushed
- Tomato juice, strained and seasoned with salt and pepper
- Shrimp broth
- Two egg yolks
- Grated Parmesan cheese
- Half a teaspoon of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the shrimp in water seasoned with salt and a bundle of fine herbs.
2. Remove the shrimp from the pan and separate the heads from the tails.
3. Peel the tails very well, wash them, and arrange them in a serving dish.
4. Grind the heads in a mortar or pestle and dissolve the resulting paste in a little of the cooking broth, then strain through a sieve.
5. In a pan, fry butter and cook the finely chopped onion heads, parsley, well-crushed garlic clove, and the tomato juice seasoned with salt and pepper.
6. When the onion has taken on color, add the head broth, two egg yolks, and a regular amount of grated Parmesan cheese, ensuring the sauce becomes thick.
7. Add half a teaspoon of powdered sugar to mitigate the saltiness of the cheese.
8. Pour the boiling sauce over the dish containing the shrimp tails and serve hot.
