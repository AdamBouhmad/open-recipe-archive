## Ingredients

- wine
- water
- ice
- sugar
- lemon
- cinnamon
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Prepare a sangria mixture with the traditional ingredients of wine, water, ice, sugar, lemon, cinnamon, and nutmeg
2. Freeze in an ice cream maker
