## Ingredients

- Six eggs, whites and yolks
- Butter
- Onion
- Garlic
- Tomato
- Rice, cooked in unsalted water
- Gruyère cheese, grated
- Two dozen fresh almonds, ground
- Milk
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Beat together the whites and yolks of six eggs
2. Heat butter in a pan that has been previously fried with onion, garlic, and tomato, then strained through a sieve to remove all these ingredients
3. Pour the beaten eggs into the hot butter and stir well while cooking, seasoning with salt and pepper
4. Cook the rice in unsalted water and drain it in a napkin to extract all water
5. Mix the cooked rice with grated Gruyère cheese to season, and add two dozen fresh almonds ground and moistened while grinding with a few drops of milk
6. Butter the pudding mold
7. Spread a layer of the rice and almond mixture on the bottom
8. Add a layer of the scrambled egg on top
9. Alternate layers, ensuring the last layer on top is rice
10. Brush lightly with a small amount of butter and place in the oven
11. When the pudding takes on a deep golden color, it is properly cooked and can be served
