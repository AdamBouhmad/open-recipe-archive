## Ingredients

- Flour
- Bread yeast
- Hot salted water
- Lard from fried bacon
- Ham, sliced thin
- Black olives, pitted

## Directions

1. Form the flour into a mound on a table and make a hollow in the center.
2. Dissolve a piece of bread yeast in hot lightly salted water and pour it little by little into the hollow while stirring, forming a stiff dough.
3. Knead the dough, gradually adding lard extracted from fried bacon, until it becomes soft and smooth.
4. Cover with a folded cloth and let it rest for one hour.
5. Divide into equal portions and dust with flour above and below, then stretch with a rolling pin into thin wheels of desired size on a cloth.
6. Cut thin slices of ham with a very sharp knife.
7. Place two ham slices separated by a layer of pitted black olives on half of each wheel of dough.
8. Fold the dough over the filling and seal the edges with a crimp.
9. Place on baking sheets in the oven and remove when golden brown, cooking quickly.
