## Ingredients

- flour
- cold water
- salt
- sugar
- suet
- egg yolks
- squabs
- butter
- white onion
- pepper
- cumin
- Malaga raisins
- vinegar
- hard-boiled eggs
- olives

## Directions

1. Take one pound of flour and knead it by adding little by little cold water lightly saturated with salt and sugar.
2. Make a stiff dough, which will be softened by kneading it with both hands on a table. Once softened a bit, mix in it a small quantity of suet and the yolks of two eggs.
3. Knead again with the suet and egg, then extend with a rolling pin until it is thin.
4. Then spread over that entire flattened surface, by means of a feather or with the hand, melted suet; sprinkle flour over that suet, fold in two and pass the rolling pin again, extending and thinning the dough, and again spread suet and sprinkle flour, up to six times.
5. Then turn the dish or vessel in which it is to be arranged over the dough; take the measure and cut around the dough with the tip of a knife.
6. Filling the dish, arrange the squab filling which will be prepared as follows: Wash the squabs and their offal, give them a boil, and after letting them cool, cut them apart; setting aside the offal, put the pieces and give them another boil in a sauce of butter, chopped white onion, pepper, a little cumin, Malaga raisins, half a tablespoon of vinegar, sufficient salt and the offal, which will be arranged between piece and piece, as well as olives and hard-boiled eggs in slices.
7. With the filling arranged and the edge of the dish buttered, cover with the puff pastry and put in the oven, after spreading the surface of it very lightly with butter and sprinkling sugar over it.
