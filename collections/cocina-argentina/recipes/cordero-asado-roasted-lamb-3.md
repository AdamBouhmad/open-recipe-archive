## Ingredients

- lamb at half nursing age
- vinegar
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- oregano
- cumin
- two heads of onion
- two cloves of garlic, ground

## Directions

1. Prepare a lamb at half nursing age
2. Skin it, open it and wash it
3. Place it for three hours in a marinade of vinegar, oil, salt, pepper, oregano, cumin, two heads of onion and two cloves of garlic ground
