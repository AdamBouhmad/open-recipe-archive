## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Chicken broth
- Flour
- Butter
- Cognac
- Gruyere cheese

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens
2. With their broth, make a sauce as follows: take one tablespoon of flour, which is dissolved in butter and add the necessary broth to regulate the thickness
3. Arrange the chickens in pieces on a platter
4. Pour a good glass of cognac into the sauce, pour it over the pieces
5. Grate Gruyere cheese over it, so as to cover them, and put them in the oven, from where they are removed when the cheese has browned
