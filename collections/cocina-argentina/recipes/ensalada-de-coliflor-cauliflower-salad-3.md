## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- water
- onion
- parsley
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the cauliflower well and boil it with much water that covers it entirely in a pot.
2. When the foam subsides, remove it from the fire; drain the water, and without removing it from the pot so that it does not lose its shape, wash it in water that is warmer than lukewarm so that it does not harden.
3. Extract it carefully from the pot and cut off the lower branches.
4. Chop these branches very finely, together with one onion head and a bunch of parsley.
5. Mix, beating together, the necessary oil and vinegar with pepper and salt; pour part of it over the chopped mixture, which is stirred and arranged on the bottom of the salad bowl.
6. Next, place the cauliflower in the center, taking care to plant it well, and bathe it with the rest of the oil and vinegar mixture.
