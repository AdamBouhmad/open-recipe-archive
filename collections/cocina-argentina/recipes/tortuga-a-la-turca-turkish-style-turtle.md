## Ingredients

- turtle meat
- vinegar, one bottle
- oil, two or three tablespoons
- salt
- pepper
- oregano
- garlic, half a head, ground
- onions, two heads, sliced
- green chiles, four, deseded and cut into small pieces
- English mustard or yellow chile sauce
- roasted tomato juice, strained
- sour orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut the turtle meat into thin slices and place in a marinade for six hours
2. Prepare the marinade in a wooden, ceramic, or enameled earthenware vessel: combine one bottle of vinegar, two or three tablespoons of oil, salt, pepper, oregano, half a head of ground garlic, two heads of sliced onions, and four green chiles, deseded and cut into small pieces
3. Arrange the turtle meat slices in this marinade, submerge them well, and let them cure for the stated time
4. After curing, drain and fry in an enameled pan, due to the vinegar from the marinade
5. After five minutes of boiling over a lively fire, the frying is done and should be served with piquant sauce, either English mustard or yellow chile, but always in roasted tomato juice passed through a sieve and with sour orange juice
