## Ingredients

- One and a half dozen spinach leaves, cleaned
- Water
- Bread crumbs soaked in milk
- Six egg yolks
- Butter
- Four egg whites
- Salt and pepper
- Toasted bread or bread fried in butter

## Directions

1. Cook a dozen and a half clean spinach leaves in unsalted water
2. Once well cooked, mash with a spoon until it resembles a paste
3. Mix in bread crumbs soaked and mashed in milk
4. Add six egg yolks beaten with a piece of butter and four whites beaten to stiff peaks
5. Combine well, season with salt and pepper
6. Butter the pudding mold and sprinkle with breadcrumbs
7. Fill with the mixture and place in the oven
8. Serve with toasted bread on the grill or fried in butter
