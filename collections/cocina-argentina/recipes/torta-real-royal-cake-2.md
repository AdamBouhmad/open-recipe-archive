## Ingredients

- Puff pastry dough
- Dulce de leche or manjar blanco (milk candy)
- Peach preserves
- Sour cherry or strawberry preserves
- Peeled almonds, ground and seasoned with sugar and cinnamon
- Lemon preserves
- Syrup at high point
- Candied fruits for decoration
- Angel hair (spun sugar) for decoration

## Directions

1. Make a puff pastry dough and cut three molds the size of the dish in which the confection will be made.
2. Spread one layer in the bottom of the dish and bake in the oven until cooked; remove and place another layer, baking it equally; remove when ready and bake the third layer.
3. On the bottom puff pastry layer, spread a layer of dulce de leche or manjar blanco.
4. Over this, spread a layer of peach preserves.
5. Place the second puff pastry layer on top.
6. Arrange a layer of sour cherry or strawberry preserves and a layer of almond paste (peeled almonds, ground and seasoned with sugar and cinnamon).
7. Add another layer of lemon preserves.
8. Top with the third puff pastry layer.
9. Coat the top with syrup at high point and bake in the oven for a quarter hour at moderate heat.
10. Decorate with candied fruit reliefs and angel hair or spun sugar.
