## Ingredients

- Veal loin (sirloin), 4 centimeters thick
- Olive oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Salt
- Oregano
- Cumin
- Garlic, ground
- Bread crumbs soaked in cream
- Hard-boiled egg yolks
- Beef marrow
- Parsley
- Eggs, beaten
- Butter
- Lettuce hearts
- Olive oil and vinegar for dressing

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of veal loin 4 centimeters thick and as long as needed according to the number of sandwiches required.
2. Let it marinate for two hours in a marinade of oil and vinegar seasoned with pepper, salt, oregano, cumin, and ground garlic.
3. Cut into slices 2 centimeters thick and lightly pound each slice with the flat of a knife, laying them on a cloth to receive the filling.
4. Make the filling by grinding together in a mortar bread crumbs soaked in cream, hard-boiled egg yolks, beef marrow, and parsley, seasoned with salt and pepper.
5. Spread a spoonful of this filling on one slice of meat and cover with another, brushing the meat edges with beaten egg to prevent the filling from coming out.
6. Wrap each sandwich in paper greased with butter and roast on a grill, or in the oven if permitted.
7. Serve arranged in a crown around the edge of the platter, with a small cluster of lettuce hearts dressed in oil and vinegar, seasoned with salt and pepper, in the center.
