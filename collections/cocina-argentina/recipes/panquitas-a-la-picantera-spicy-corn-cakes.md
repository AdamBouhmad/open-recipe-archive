## Ingredients

- fresh corn cobs, grated and ground
- pork lard
- salt
- ground red ají, to desired degree
- green onion, finely chopped
- ground cumin
- corn husks or panc
- water
- split cane sticks

## Directions

1. Grate and grind the fresh corn well.
2. Knead with plenty of pork lard, salt, and ground red ají with water to the desired degree.
3. Cook in pork lard finely chopped green onion, sprinkle a little ground cumin over it, and mix with the corn, beating it very well so that all ingredients incorporate.
4. Wrap in corn husks, placing two spoonfuls for each husk.
5. Fold and tie with twine.
6. Bake in the oven, or cook in a pot with little water, arranged in piles on cane sticks crossed over the boiling water surface, being careful to cover the pot mouth to conserve the necessary steam for cooking.
