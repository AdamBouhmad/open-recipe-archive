## Ingredients

- puff pastry with powdered sugar mixed into the layers with flour and butter
- 6 bananas, sliced thin
- 1/2 medium pineapple, sliced thin
- 6 peaches cut into slices with pits
- 12 Tours plums
- 6 cherries, fresh and raw
- juice of 3 oranges
- 24 grape grains
- 2 pounds ground sugar
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 1 glass good Priorat wine

## Directions

1. Prepare a puff pastry with powdered sugar mixed into its layers
2. Cut bananas and pineapple into thin slices
3. Blanch the peaches cut into slices with their pits and the Tours plums with two boilings
4. Mix all the fruits, both blanched and raw, with the orange juice
5. Add the ground sugar, cinnamon, and a glass of good Priorat wine
6. Cover well and let marinate for two hours
7. Arrange this mixture in a porcelain baking dish resistant to fire
8. Cover with the puff pastry
9. Bake in a moderate oven so the pastry cooks delicately
10. Decorate as desired
