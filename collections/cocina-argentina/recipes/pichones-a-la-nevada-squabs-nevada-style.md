## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Butter
- Olives
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Almonds
- Raisins
- Salt
- Pepper
- Puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the squabs.
2. Fill them with olives, halved hard-boiled eggs, almonds, and raisins, seasoning with salt and pepper.
3. Fry the filled squabs in butter.
4. Once fried, wrap each squab in puff pastry dough.
5. Place in the oven for rapid cooking of the pastry.
6. Serve hot, bringing from oven to table.
7. This dish can also be served as an excellent cold dish.
