## Ingredients

- cold bread
- milk
- 1 chicken
- ham
- pepper
- flour
- 1 clove garlic
- butter
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. After scraping the crust from cold bread with a knife, cut it into slices that are soaked in milk.
2. Cook a chicken, cut it into pieces, and chop its meat very well with a piece of ham. Season with pepper, flour and 1 clove of ground garlic. Stir and mix, fry in butter.
3. Beat egg yolks, coat the bread slices with them, and fry them well browned.
4. Then, between two of these fried slices place the chicken and ham filling, coat again in egg, and fry.
5. Serve hot at lunch; and as a cold dish it is very pleasant.
