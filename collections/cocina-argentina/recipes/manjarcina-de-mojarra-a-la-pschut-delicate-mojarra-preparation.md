## Ingredients

- mojarras (small fish)
- salt
- water
- butter
- pure olive oil
- grape vinegar
- broth or stock
- mushrooms
- onion
- parsley

## Directions

1. Open, clean, and scale the mojarras well, then wash with well-salted water
2. Finish opening them with three strikes on the back with the handle of a mortar
3. Cut off the tail and head
4. Arrange them in a pan with skin side down with a proportionate amount of melted butter and four tablespoons of pure olive oil
5. Place the pan over high heat, stirring the mojarras once or twice so they cook evenly
6. Once cooked, remove them from the pan
7. In the same pan (which should be earthenware or iron enameled with porcelain), pour half a glass of grape vinegar, a cup of broth or good stock, a dozen mushrooms cut into pieces, onion slices, a tablespoon of finely chopped parsley, salt and pepper
8. Reduce this sauce over high heat
9. When serving, place the mojarras in a serving dish, add half a cup of melted butter to the sauce, and pour it boiling over the fish
