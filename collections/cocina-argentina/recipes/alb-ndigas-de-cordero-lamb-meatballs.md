## Ingredients

- Meat from lamb leg
- Bacon
- Parsley
- One clove of garlic
- Bread soaked in milk and ground
- Black pepper
- A little cumin
- Salt to taste
- Eggs
- Flour
- Boiling broth

## Directions

1. Chop the meat from the lamb leg together with bacon, parsley, and one clove of garlic
2. Add bread soaked in milk and ground, pepper, a little cumin, salt to taste, and eggs
3. Mix and knead to make a mass
4. Shape into balls the size of hen eggs
5. Coat them in flour so they do not fall apart
6. Cook them in boiling broth for about fifteen minutes at a vigorous boil
7. Remove from heat when done
