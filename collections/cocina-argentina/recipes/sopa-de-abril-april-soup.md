## Ingredients

- carrots
- lettuce
- meat
- chorizo
- tender fava beans
- onions
- oregano
- parsley
- salt
- tomato juice
- pork fat or butter
- broth
- bread, thinly sliced and toasted on grill

## Directions

1. Chop carrots and lettuce in equal quantities
2. Add equally chopped meat and chorizo, and tender fava beans
3. Cook seasoned with onions, oregano, parsley, salt to taste, and tomato juice in pork fat or preferably butter
4. When it comes to a boil, pour good broth over it
5. Cook for half an hour
6. Place a layer of thin bread slices toasted on the grill in the bottom of the soup tureen
7. Fill with the soup
8. Let it rest a moment and serve
