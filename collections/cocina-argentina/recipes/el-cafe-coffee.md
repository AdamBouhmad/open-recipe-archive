## Ingredients

- Coffee, ground and sifted twice
- Water, cold
- Water, hot

## Directions

1. Half an hour before serving, with the coffee already between the first and second sifting, add three spoonfuls of cold water, intended to moisten it and prepare the aroma for the second emission of boiling water, which ruins it when falling on dry powder
2. Next, pour the hot water slowly and in three emissions, to allow the aroma to exhale
