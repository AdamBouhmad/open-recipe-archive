## Ingredients

- four-month-old pork, cleaned and gutted
- garlic, ground separately
- pepper
- walnuts
- peanut
- almonds
- salt
- oregano
- good red wine, two bottles
- vinegar, two bottles
- water
- myrtle branch
- laurel leaves
- rosemary

## Directions

1. Debone the pork by starting with the ribs, loin, and then the remaining bones of legs and arms, leaving the head intact
2. Leave it in vinegar for twenty-four hours
3. Previously grind separately garlic, pepper, walnuts, peanut, almonds, salt and oregano
4. Sprinkle the necessary salt over the pork
5. Coat with ground garlic and sprinkle pepper
6. Spread the mass of peanut, walnuts and almonds over it
7. Roll it up tying with double twine from one end to the other
8. Tie the whole thing in a circle with the same twine
9. Place in a deep enameled porcelain pot or terra-cotta pot with two bottles of good red wine, two of vinegar, enough water to cover everything, and a bunch of myrtle, laurel leaves and rosemary
10. When a straw can be easily inserted into the meat, it is ready
11. Remove from fire and serve, or store after cooling it for several hours in a fresh, shady place
