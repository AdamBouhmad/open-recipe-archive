## Ingredients

- four ounces of toasted and ground sesame seeds
- one pound of ground almonds
- three pounds of sugar
- one ounce of powdered cinnamon
- half an adarme of ground clove
- a pinch of ginger
- two ounces of fresh butter
- one glass of virgin or bee honey
- a pinch of ground nutmeg
- three pounds of toasted bread, finely grated

## Directions

1. Make a high-point syrup with three pounds of sugar mixed with the honey. Remove from heat to incorporate the grated bread and butter. Return to heat and stir until perfectly mixed. Remove from heat again to mix in the cinnamon, clove, nutmeg, and ginger, allowing it to rest for one hour. Then add the sesame seeds and almonds. Place on heat and bring to the desired consistency according to taste.
