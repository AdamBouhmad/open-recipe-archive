## Ingredients

- six to twelve glasses of orange or lemon sorbet
- water sweetened with burned sugar
- cinnamon
- powdered nutmeg
- rum, cognac, or grape brandy

## Directions

1. Pour six to twelve glasses of orange or lemon sorbet into a punch bowl
2. Add water sweetened with burned sugar, cinnamon, and powdered nutmeg to taste
3. Add rum, cognac, or grape brandy in desired quantity
4. Stir to incorporate everything
5. Cover the punch bowl with a folded napkin and let infuse for one hour
6. When serving, place a small piece of snow in each cup
