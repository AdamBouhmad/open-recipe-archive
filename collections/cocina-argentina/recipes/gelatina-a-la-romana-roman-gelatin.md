## Ingredients

- 6 lamb feet, cleaned with care
- 2 veal feet, cleaned with care
- water to cover
- 1 cup of bitter almond milk (made from peeled, washed bitter almonds ground very finely, mixed with a little water and strained through gauze)
- sugar to taste
- 4 drops of heliotrope essence

## Directions

1. Cook the lamb and veal feet in sufficient water to cover them, skimming and degreasing carefully and thoroughly until they fall apart
2. The gelatin is ready to set at this point
3. Strain through a fine cloth
4. Mix in 1 cup of bitter almond milk
5. Add sugar to taste and 4 drops of heliotrope essence
6. Stir to incorporate everything thoroughly
7. Pour into cups or glasses to serve
