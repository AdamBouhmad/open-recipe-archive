## Ingredients

- Doves
- Beef stock
- Butter
- Grated bread
- Buttered paper
- Yellow chili sauce or English mustard

## Directions

1. Cook the doves in good beef stock until well done.
2. Remove from the stock and let cool.
3. Dry them, coat with butter, and roll in grated bread.
4. Wrap in buttered paper.
5. Place in the oven until golden.
6. Serve with yellow chili sauce or English mustard.
