## Ingredients

- Duck
- Piece of veal sirloin
- Fresh bacon
- Parsley
- Scallions
- Thyme
- Laurel
- One garlic clove
- Pepper
- One clove spice
- Salt
- Cognac
- Cooked parsley branches for garnish

## Directions

1. Debone the duck and mince it finely.
2. Mince together with a piece of veal sirloin, fresh bacon, parsley, scallions, thyme, laurel, one garlic clove, pepper, one clove spice, and salt to taste.
3. Grind everything very well and mix and incorporate into the minced meats.
4. Line a terrine with a well-fitting lid with strips of fresh bacon.
5. Over this layer of bacon, spread the minced meat mixture.
6. Add more pieces of bacon on top, sprinkle with one glass of cognac.
7. Cover with strips of bacon, and adjusting the lid carefully, place in the oven to cook for four hours.
8. Serve in the terrine, hidden in a serving dish with branches of parsley.
