## Ingredients

- liquid pork blood
- salt
- biscuit or crackers with water
- three dozen almonds, peeled
- three dozen walnuts
- a handful of roasted peanuts
- one head of garlic
- oregano
- pepper
- green onion, finely chopped
- one cup of raw cream
- three eggs, beaten
- fresh bacon, finely chopped
- sausage casings of regular thickness
- wire loop
- string

## Directions

1. Take liquid pork blood and mix with a little salt to prevent coagulation
2. Grate biscuit or crackers with water in proportion to the quantity of blood
3. Peel and grind three dozen almonds, three dozen walnuts, and a handful of roasted peanuts, along with one head of garlic, oregano, and pepper
4. Pulverize everything in a mortar and mix with the blood
5. Add finely chopped green onion, one cup of raw cream, three beaten eggs, and plenty of fresh bacon, finely chopped
6. Mix everything thoroughly, stirring well in all directions
7. Using a small wire loop at the opening of the casing and a spoon, stuff the filling into casings of regular thickness
8. Tie the ends and separations with string
9. Boil, taking care to prick the casing in various places with a pin to prevent it from bursting from the heat
10. Hang with the same precautions as the sausage
