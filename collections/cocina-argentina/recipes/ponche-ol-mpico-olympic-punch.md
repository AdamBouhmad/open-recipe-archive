## Ingredients

- 1 Panama or Brazil coconut, or 4 dozen Paraguay coconuts
- milk
- 2 dozen peeled and washed almonds
- 1 liter of boiling cream of milk
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grate first and then grind in a mortar a Panama or Brazil coconut, or failing this, 4 dozen Paraguay coconuts
2. While grinding in the mortar, add little by little half spoonfuls of milk, at the same time as, every four spoonfuls, 2 dozen peeled and washed almonds, which are ground with the coconut
3. Once ground, strain in a sieve of strong cloth
4. Press well to make them release their thickest juice, which is mixed with 1 liter of boiling cream of milk, and seasoned with sugar and cinnamon
