## Ingredients

- Lamb legs, cleaned and peeled
- Ground meat mixture with raisins, crushed almonds, ground roasted peanuts, salt, and pepper
- Pork fat
- Butter
- Ground veal
- Two tablespoons of finely chopped fine herbs
- Flour
- Warm water
- Lemon
- Butter
- Lean ham, chopped into small pieces
- Veal, chopped into small pieces
- Two chicken legs, chopped into small pieces
- Salt, pepper, and spices
- Bay leaf
- Two carrots
- Two onions
- Six hard-boiled egg yolks, well ground
- Milk

## Directions

1. Clean the lamb legs and carefully remove the upper bone with a pointed knife without tearing the meat.
2. Fill the hollow with a picadillo seasoned with raisins, crushed almonds, roasted and ground peanuts, salt, and pepper.
3. Stuff the legs and sew them well closed to prevent the filling from escaping.
4. In a pan, melt a piece of pork fat and a piece of butter.
5. Cook ground veal and two tablespoons of finely chopped fine herbs in this mixture.
6. After two boils, add one tablespoon of flour, one cup of hot water, and a lemon cut into thin slices.
7. Stir well and after two more boils, pour this preparation over the legs arranged at the bottom of a pan.
8. Let them cook for four hours.
9. Remove from heat and drain the legs, remove the stitching thread, place them in a serving dish.
10. To prepare the sauce: bring to a slow boil in a pan 250 grams of butter, 25 grams of lean ham, 500 grams of veal, and two chicken legs, all chopped into fine pieces.
11. Season with salt, pepper, spices, a bay leaf, two carrots, and two onions.
12. When the meats have taken on color in the butter, add half a dozen well-ground hard-boiled egg yolks, stirring vigorously to blend the mixture.
13. Gradually pour in one liter of milk, stirring constantly, and let it cook at a slow fire for one hour, stirring always.
14. Pass through a fine sieve and pour hot over the stuffed legs at the moment of serving.
