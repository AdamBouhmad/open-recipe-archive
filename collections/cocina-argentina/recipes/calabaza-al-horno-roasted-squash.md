## Ingredients

- squash
- salt
- three eggs
- grated cheese
- sauce cooked in pork fat fried with pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the squash and cook it in salted water.
2. Once cooked, dry it in a cloth; mash it with a spoon, crushing and stirring it alternately.
3. Mix in three beaten eggs, grated cheese, and sauce cooked in pork fat fried with pepper.
4. Arrange in a baking dish and place in the oven.
