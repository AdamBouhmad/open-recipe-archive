## Ingredients

- Pork ribs, prepared and pounded
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs, grated
- Fine herbs, chopped
- Salt, pepper, and nutmeg
- Butter for wrapping
- English mustard sauce
- Hard-boiled egg yolks
- Broth

## Directions

1. After preparing and pounding the pork ribs, butter them and roll them in grated breadcrumbs mixed with chopped fine herbs, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
2. Wrap them in paper spread with butter and cook them in a roasting pan, turning them so the paper does not burn.
3. Serve with a spicy sauce made from English mustard thickened with hard-boiled egg yolks dissolved in broth.
