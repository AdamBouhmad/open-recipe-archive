## Ingredients

- butter
- onions, minced
- garlic, ground
- oregano
- salt to taste
- water
- potatoes
- milk
- fresh cheese
- eggs
- fish without scales or bones
- two dried and toasted chiles

## Directions

1. Cook minced onions in butter with very little ground garlic, very little oregano, and salt to taste, frying the sauce well
2. Add a small cup of water; when boiling, add peeled potatoes until lightly cooked
3. Pour in the desired quantity of milk and bring to a boil
4. When the potatoes are well cooked, add a good piece of minced fresh cheese and break as many eggs as there are people at table
5. While the chowder cooks, fry pieces of fish without scales or bones and add to the chowder
6. Also add two dried and toasted chiles, which give it an exquisite flavor
7. If during boiling the milk reduces, add more, as the first condition of chowder is that it have much broth
8. This dish is served only at lunch
