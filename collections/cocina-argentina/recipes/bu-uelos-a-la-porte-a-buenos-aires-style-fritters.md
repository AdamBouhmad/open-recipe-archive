## Ingredients

- Good apricots
- Grape brandy
- Pulverized sugar
- Lemon zest
- Two handfuls flour
- One glass milk
- Butter
- One small glass brandy
- One and a half egg whites, beaten and made into foam
- Salt
- Butter for frying
- Sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Select good apricots, cut them in half, and remove the pits.
2. Dissolve pulverized sugar in quality grape brandy and soak the apricot halves in this mixture along with lemon zest cut into pieces for two hours.
3. Remove the apricot pieces from the brandy and dry them.
4. Make the fritter batter: dissolve two handfuls of flour in a glass of milk, add a piece of butter, stir and beat well, then add a small glass of brandy little by little.
5. When ready to fry, add one and a half egg whites (beaten and made into foam) to the batter along with a pinch of salt.
6. Wrap each apricot piece in the batter, place it on a spoon, and fry in boiling butter.
7. If convenient, place the fritters briefly in the oven so the sprinkled sugar caramelizes, rather than serving them directly from the pan with sugar sprinkled on top.
