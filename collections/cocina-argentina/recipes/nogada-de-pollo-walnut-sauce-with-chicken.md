## Ingredients

- chicken
- walnuts
- milk
- onion heads
- garlic
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- broth
- pickled cucumbers
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Roast a good chicken on a grill with bright heat, turning it carefully so it does not burn
2. Bone it and prepare it in the same sauce and walnut milk as the cod
3. Follow the same preparation method as Nogada de Bacalao, using the prepared chicken instead of cod
4. Serve with the same garnishes
