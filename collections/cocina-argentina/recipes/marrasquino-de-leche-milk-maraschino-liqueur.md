## Ingredients

- 1500 grams alcohol
- 1000 grams distilled water
- 500 grams sugar
- 2 grams mace
- 4 grams cloves
- 1.50 grams grated lemon zest
- 0.50 centigrams anise
- 30 bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Mix all these ingredients together
2. Place in infusion in a crystal or porcelain vessel for eight days
3. Filter
