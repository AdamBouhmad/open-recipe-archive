## Ingredients

- slices of sponge cake
- Burgundy wine
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak slices of sponge cake in Burgundy wine and arrange in layers in a heat-resistant porcelain dish. Each layer should be lightly dusted with sugar and cinnamon. Once filled, place the dish in the oven at moderate heat for half an hour.
2. This dessert is very pleasant served hot or cold, and more than justifies its name.
