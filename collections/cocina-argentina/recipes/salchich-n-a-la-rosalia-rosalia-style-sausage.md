## Ingredients

- pork meat, finely chopped
- pork bacon, finely chopped
- vinegar
- garlic, ground
- salt
- pepper
- cumin
- peanuts, ground
- almonds, ground
- walnuts, ground
- bread, pulverized
- oregano, chopped
- parsley, chopped
- thick sausage casing
- string

## Directions

1. On a board, chop pork meat and bacon very finely with a machete and place in a terra-cotta or wooden vessel to macerate in vinegar for twenty-four hours
2. Drain the vinegar until the meat is dry, then mix well with ground garlic, salt, pepper, cumin, peanuts, almonds, walnuts, pulverized bread, and chopped oregano and parsley
3. Mix everything very well with a spoon and stuff into thick sausage casings tied at intervals, forming a horseshoe shape
4. Boil in water for two hours, then remove from fire, dry with a cloth, and let dry for a few hours in a cool place before storing
