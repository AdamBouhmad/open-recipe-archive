## Ingredients

- Pork fat, 4 ounces
- Onion, a small piece, chopped
- Garlic clove, 1
- Salt
- Rice, 1 pound, washed
- Water, cold

## Directions

1. Fry a small piece of chopped onion, one garlic clove, and salt in four ounces of pork fat.
2. When this sauce is well cooked, add one pound of rice which should be washed several times until stripped of the flour it generally has.
3. Mix it with the fat and immediately, before the rice cooks, pour cold water over it until it is covered.
4. Cover very well and cook over low heat for half an hour; the rice will then be fluffy and well cooked.
