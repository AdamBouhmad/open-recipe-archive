## Ingredients

- eggs, eight
- lamb kidneys, two, cooked
- mushrooms, four, cooked
- asparagus, one dozen, cooked
- cheese, grated
- bread, cold and grated
- pure olive oil
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Break eight eggs and beat them partially
2. Cook two lamb kidneys, four mushrooms, and one dozen asparagus
3. Cut into small pieces the kidneys, mushrooms, and the asparagus tips
4. Mix with the eggs, add grated cheese and grated cold bread
5. Fry everything in pure olive oil and serve over tomato sauce
