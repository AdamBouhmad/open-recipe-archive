## Ingredients

- sábalo (a fish)
- salt
- water
- butter
- fine herbs
- pepper

## Directions

1. After scaling, opening, and cleaning the sábalo, soak it for one hour in well-salted water
2. Mix butter with a handful of finely chopped fine herbs and proper seasoning of salt and pepper
3. Fill the interior of the sábalo with this mixture
4. Make two lengthwise cuts along the backbone on the back and place the herb-butter mixture inside these cuts as well
5. Wrap the fish in paper spread with butter and cook it on a grill over moderate heat
6. When well cooked, remove from paper and serve, sprinkled with lemon juice, accompanied by a sharp sauce of either mustard with tomato juice or oil and vinegar with green chili peppers
