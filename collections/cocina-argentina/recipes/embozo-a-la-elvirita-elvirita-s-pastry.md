## Ingredients

- una libra de harina (one pound of flour)
- media libra de grasa de vaca, chancho, o mantequilla (half pound beef fat, pork fat, or butter)
- sal (salt)
- azúcar (sugar)
- leche (milk)
- carne de solomo (sirloin meat)
- pasas de uva (raisins)
- huevos duros (hard boiled eggs)
- aceitunas (olives)
- pimienta en polvo (powdered pepper)
- ajo molido (ground garlic)

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour, half pound of beef fat, pork fat, or butter; sufficient salt to taste, and equal quantity of sugar
2. Stir all together and add milk little by little, kneading with both hands to incorporate
3. Make a mixture of sirloin meat, raisins, chopped hard boiled eggs, olive pieces, powdered pepper, sufficient salt and sugar to taste, and a small pinch of ground garlic if desired
4. Extend the mass with a rolling pin and make the pastry, either in the form of a cake or empanada
5. Bake in an oven with strong heat for quick cooking
