## Ingredients

- Pork fat
- Breadcrumbs
- Pasta
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Coat the pudding mold entirely with pork fat and sprinkle breadcrumbs on top
2. Place a layer of pasta in the bottom
3. Break an egg in the center over the pasta and sprinkle generously with breadcrumbs
4. Add another layer of pasta and another egg, continuing to alternate layers until the mold is full
5. Close the mold and place in a water bath in a pot with water filled halfway, bringing to a boil over good heat
6. Cover and place hot ashes and coals on top of the lid
7. After one hour of cooking, the pudding is ready
8. Turn it out onto the serving dish and pour the desired sauce over it
