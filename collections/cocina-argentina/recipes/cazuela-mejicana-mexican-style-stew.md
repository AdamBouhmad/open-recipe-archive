## Ingredients

- one good hen, cut into small pieces
- pork, from chest or ribs
- ham with its bacon
- one sausage or chorizo
- a little beef
- potatoes
- onion
- tomato
- white cabbage hearts
- pork fat
- green chiles
- onion heads
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- oregano
- huacatay
- thyme
- bay leaf
- cooked potatoes
- fresh cheese
- egg yolks
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut into small pieces one good hen, pork from the chest or ribs, ham with its bacon, one sausage or chorizo, and a little beef
2. Cut potatoes, onion, tomato, and white cabbage hearts into small pieces
3. Fry green chiles and sliced onion heads in pork fat
4. Mix in the minced meats, salt, and pepper
5. When half fried, add two ladlefuls of broth and the vegetables, oregano, huacatay, thyme, and bay leaf
6. Bring to a boil, adding broth if it thickens
7. In the final boil, add cooked potatoes, well broken up, and strips of fresh cheese
8. Before serving, place at the bottom of the tureen a mixture of cooked egg yolks beaten and mixed with oil and vinegar
