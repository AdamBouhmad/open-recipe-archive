## Ingredients

- lamb at mid-lactation stage
- vinegar
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- oregano
- cumin
- two onion heads
- two cloves of garlic
- lamb liver
- lamb tongue
- hard-boiled eggs
- two dozen toasted and ground peanut kernels
- almonds
- raisins
- olives
- butter
- pickled gherkins
- lettuce hearts
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Skin, open, and wash the lamb.
2. Place it in a marinade of vinegar, oil, salt, pepper, oregano, cumin, two onion heads, and two cloves of garlic, minced, for three hours.
3. While the lamb marinates, prepare the stuffing with minced cooked liver and tongue of the lamb; finely chopped hard-boiled eggs; two dozen toasted and ground peanut kernels; almonds, raisins, and olives.
4. Put all of this in a pan over the fire with a piece of butter, seasoned with pepper and salt.
5. Stir for five minutes to incorporate it, and remove from the fire.
6. Remove the lamb from the marinade, pat it dry, and stuff it with this mixture.
7. Sew it closed with a needle and strong thread.
8. Spread it with butter, and place it in a buttered pan.
9. Put it in a moderately hot oven to cook slowly.
10. Serve with pickled gherkins and lettuce hearts seasoned with oil and vinegar.
11. The head and legs of the lamb, which have also been marinated, should be cooked slowly and served in tomato sauce.
