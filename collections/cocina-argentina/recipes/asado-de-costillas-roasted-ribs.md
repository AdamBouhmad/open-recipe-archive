## Ingredients

- cross-cut piece of cow ribs, quadrilateral in shape
- cold water
- broth conditioned with salt
- garlic and onion, ground
- tomato sauce
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- green chiles, sliced

## Directions

1. Cut across the rib cage of a cow a quadrilateral piece of the size needed
2. Wash it with cold water and after drying it, turn it in a plate of broth seasoned with plenty of salt and a pinch of ground garlic and onion
3. Let it drain and extend it on the grill on the bone side, over a very lively fire
4. When the bones are toasted, prepare another bed of equally lively coals
5. Transfer the grill to the new bed of coals, quickly turning the piece to the meat side, which is already almost cooked through the ribs
6. With five more minutes of fire it is ready to serve on a liquid tomato sauce, seasoned with oil, vinegar, pepper, salt and sliced green chiles
