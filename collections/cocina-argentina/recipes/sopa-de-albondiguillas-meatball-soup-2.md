## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one cup of milk
- two ounces of butter
- a pinch of salt
- a pinch of pepper
- wheat flour
- butter for frying
- hot broth
- pepper
- one beaten egg

## Directions

1. Break four eggs into a bowl and beat them with one cup of milk, two ounces of butter, a pinch of salt, and a pinch of pepper
2. Mix wheat flour into this mixture until a consistent dough forms
3. Shape the dough into small meatballs the size of an olive
4. Roll the meatballs in flour and fry them in butter
5. Place the meatballs in a soup tureen and pour hot broth over them, to which pepper and a beaten egg have been added
