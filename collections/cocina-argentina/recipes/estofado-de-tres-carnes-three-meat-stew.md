## Ingredients

- a piece of beef from the chest
- a piece of pork
- a hen
- a leg of mutton or pork, preferably the latter
- fresh bacon without salt
- a bouquet of aromatic herbs composed of parsley, basil, mint or lemon balm, rosemary and bay leaf, around green onion leaves
- two cloves of garlic, chopped
- two onion heads
- two dozen Malaga raisins
- two dozen almonds
- peppercorns
- a piece of whole cinnamon
- a pinch of cumin
- a tablespoon of salt
- a tablespoon of sugar
- two ounces of pork fat from fresh bacon, fried with paprika
- a quarter of legitimate priorato wine, lightly saturated with water

## Directions

1. Prepare a piece of beef from the chest, a piece of pork, and a hen; add if desired a leg of mutton or pork, preferring the latter
2. After properly washing with lukewarm water, place them in an enameled iron pot lined with slices of fresh unsalted bacon
3. Between the meat place a bouquet of aromatic herbs composed of parsley, basil, mint or lemon balm, rosemary and bay leaf, all around green onion leaves; tie the bouquet with string to prevent it from scattering
4. Place also between the meat two chopped cloves of garlic and two onion heads
5. Add two dozen Malaga raisins, two dozen almonds, some peppercorns, a piece of whole cinnamon, a pinch of cumin, a tablespoon of salt, a tablespoon of sugar and two ounces of pork fat extracted from fresh bacon, fried with paprika to give it color
6. Pour over all this a quarter of legitimate priorato wine, lightly saturated with water
7. Seal the pot hermetically and place it on a strong fire for the first boil, then for five hours at the coals over a low fire, being careful not to open it; instead only remove it frequently from the fire and without opening it, stir it to prevent it from sticking to the bottom
8. When serving, take care to remove the pieces with two forks to prevent them from falling apart
9. After placing the meat and hen in the center, pour the sauce over them circularly and place on the surface any garnishes desired, whether slices of eggs or pickled gherkins
