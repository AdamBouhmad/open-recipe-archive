## Ingredients

- a piece of bread yeast from a bakery
- two tablespoons of wheat flour
- ten tablespoons of powdered sugar
- six tablespoons of butter
- one pound of flour
- a sufficient quantity of cream
- ground almonds
- syrup mixed with Jamaica rum

## Directions

1. Take a piece of bread yeast from a bakery and dissolve it in two tablespoons of wheat flour; stir very well and, covering the mixture with a cloth, let it ferment for two hours.
2. Then, knead this yeast with ten tablespoons of powdered sugar, six of butter, one pound of flour, and a sufficient quantity of cream to make of this mixture a soft paste.
3. Take a mold of a beautiful shape, cover the bottom with a layer of ground almonds, fill it with the paste, and let it rest for four hours.
4. Bake in a very hot oven.
5. Upon removing it from the mold, sprinkle it with syrup mixed with Jamaica rum.
