## Ingredients

- pork loin
- chili pepper
- garlic
- cumin
- pepper
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- bitter orange juice
- onions
- pork fat
- sweet potato

## Directions

1. Choose the fattest part of the pork loin and cut it into pieces the size and shape of steaks.
2. Immerse each piece in a marinade composed of powdered chili pepper, garlic, cumin, pepper, and salt, with a little oil and vinegar.
3. Remove to a platter and cover for two hours to marinate well.
4. Separately, make the sauce: in bitter orange juice, place thinly sliced onions, well-ground chili pepper, and salt, and let it pickle for four hours.
5. Fry the pork loins in very hot pork fat until they brown.
6. Place them on a platter and pour the sauce with the orange juice over them.
7. Arrange boiled sweet potato pieces as garnish.
