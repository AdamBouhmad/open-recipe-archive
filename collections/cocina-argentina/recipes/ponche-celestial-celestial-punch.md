## Ingredients

- 9 lemon peels
- 1/2 bottle of cognac
- juice of 30 lemons
- 2 bottles of cognac
- 4 pounds of powdered sugar
- 4 bottles of distilled water
- 1 bottle of boiling milk

## Directions

1. Cut the peels of 9 lemons so thin that they are transparent, put them in 1/2 bottle of cognac, and let soak 24 hours
2. Mix the juice of 30 lemons with 2 bottles of cognac, 4 pounds of powdered sugar, 4 bottles of distilled water, and 1 bottle of boiling milk, add to the entire previous infusion, stirring much to prevent coagulation
3. Then, let cool and pass through a flannel strainer
4. Put in a vessel and let ferment for 10 days
