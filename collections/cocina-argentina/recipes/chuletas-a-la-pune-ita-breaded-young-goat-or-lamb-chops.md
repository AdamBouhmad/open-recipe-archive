## Ingredients

- young goat or lamb chops
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- milk
- egg yolks
- breadcrumbs
- egg

## Directions

1. Flatten the chops one by one with a meat mallet.
2. Moisten them with broth and season with salt and pepper.
3. Place them in a heat-resistant dish brushed with butter and put them in the oven without letting them cook completely.
4. Remove them and let them cool.
5. In boiling milk, melt butter and add flour dissolved in milk.
6. Add two egg yolks to this cream.
7. Without letting the cream cool, roll the chops in it and then in breadcrumbs.
8. Once cold, brush them with egg, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, and fry at the moment of serving.
