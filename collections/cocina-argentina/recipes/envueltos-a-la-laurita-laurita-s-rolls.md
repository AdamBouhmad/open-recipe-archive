## Ingredients

- good sirloin veal, one piece
- bread crumbs, one handful
- fresh bacon
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- beef marrow
- egg yolks, beaten, with one egg white
- bread crumbs, grated
- canned or marinated onions and green chiles
- tomato juice sauce

## Directions

1. Choose a good piece of sirloin veal and cut it crosswise into thin slices, which are then flattened with a mallet
2. Grind in a mortar one handful of bread crumbs, fresh bacon, parsley, salt, pepper, and beef marrow
3. Spread two tablespoons of this mixture on each slice of veal and cover with another prepared slice
4. Wrap in a beaten mixture of egg yolks with one egg white, roll in bread crumbs
5. Toast on a grill over moderate heat
6. Serve with relief of canned or marinated onions and green chiles, and separately accompanied with tomato juice sauce
