## Ingredients

- Lamb tripe, cleaned
- White onion, chopped
- Garlic, ground
- Oregano
- Pepper powder
- Olive oil
- Vinegar
- Lettuce leaves
- Lettuce hearts
- Olive oil and vinegar for dressing

## Directions

1. Wash the lamb tripe thoroughly several times, scrape it, and cook it.
2. Chop it very fine and place it in a platter to marinate with a mixture of chopped white onion, ground garlic, oregano and pepper powder, oil and vinegar.
3. Let it marinate for two hours.
4. Serve in a platter on lettuce leaves, with lettuce hearts dressed in oil and vinegar on top.
