## Ingredients

- leg of lamb, minced
- bacon
- parsley
- one clove of garlic
- bread soaked in milk and ground
- pepper
- a little cumin
- salt to taste
- eggs
- flour
- breadcrumbs
- cream
- onions
- vinegar
- oil
- hard-boiled eggs
- pickled gherkins

## Directions

1. Mince the meat from the leg with bacon, parsley, and a clove of garlic
2. Add bread soaked in milk and ground, pepper, a little cumin, salt to taste, and eggs
3. Mix and work into a dough, forming it into the shape of hen eggs
4. Cook in boiling broth, rolled in flour so they do not fall apart
5. After a quarter hour of vigorous boiling, remove from heat and place on the serving dish
6. Coat with a sauce made of breadcrumbs dissolved in cream, cooked and sliced onions, pepper, vinegar, and oil
7. Garnish with hard-boiled eggs cut in half and pickled gherkins
