## Ingredients

- Thin slices of beef from the ham section
- Salt and pepper
- Vinegar
- Pure olive oil
- Broth
- Bread slices
- Sauce used for picadillo

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices of beef from the ham section
2. Lightly grill them until they begin to release bloody juice
3. Half-grind them in a mortar
4. Chop them finely with a sharp knife
5. Put them in a pot with the picadillo sauce
6. Add grilled bread slices soaked in broth seasoned with pepper, vinegar, and pure olive oil
7. Cook over low heat for one hour
8. Remove and leave on the coals until time to serve
9. This dish is preferably used for dinner
