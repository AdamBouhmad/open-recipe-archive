## Ingredients

- Charqui (dried beef)
- Onions, chopped
- Bread, sliced
- Potatoes, fried
- Water, boiling
- Olive oil
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Roast and grind the charqui (dried beef) in a mortar until shredded.
2. Arrange it in a platter by layers, alternating with chopped onions and slices of bread and fried potatoes.
3. Pour boiling water over this, then drain it.
4. Pour oil and vinegar over the top, seasoning everything with salt and pepper.
5. Serve with halved hard-boiled eggs around the edges.
