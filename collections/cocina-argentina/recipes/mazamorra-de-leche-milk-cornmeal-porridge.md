## Ingredients

- milk
- cinnamon
- sugar
- anise
- flour
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Season the milk with cinnamon, sugar and anise
2. Put on fire, and after two boils, remove, pass through a sieve, and add the flour that has been dissolved in milk
3. Return to fire and stir constantly until the flour has cooked and thickened
4. Remove again from fire, and add, without ceasing to stir, 3 well-beaten egg yolks
5. Leave on fire for one minute and serve on plates, sprinkling with sugar and cinnamon
6. Serve cold
