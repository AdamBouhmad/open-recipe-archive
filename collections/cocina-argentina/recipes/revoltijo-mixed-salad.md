## Ingredients

- beef
- ham
- cabbage
- onion heads
- potatoes
- carrots
- green beans
- green pickled peppers
- radishes
- olives
- hard-boiled eggs
- bread fried in butter
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- lettuce hearts and leaves

## Directions

1. Cook all ingredients well, then chop finely the beef, ham, cabbage, onion heads, potatoes, carrots, green beans (stripped of their film), green pickled peppers, radishes, olives, hard-boiled eggs, and bread fried in butter.
2. Mix and blend everything well, pouring over it by spoonfuls the oil and vinegar beaten with pepper and salt.
3. Once prepared, arrange the salad as a beautiful mosaic, placing as garnish four tender lettuce hearts at the center of the salad bowl, and leaves of four others around the edges, all bathed in the mixture of beaten oil and vinegar.
