## Ingredients

- 1 pound peeled and ground walnuts
- breadcrumbs from a criollo bread, grated
- 12 egg yolks
- 1/2 pound powdered sugar
- slices of sponge cake
- 1 cup sweetened milk
- cinnamon
- 4 tablespoons ground walnuts
- 3 egg whites
- 1/2 pound powdered sugar for meringue
- gold threads or spun eggs in syrup
- candies for decoration

## Directions

1. Mix the ground walnuts with the grated bread crumbs, egg yolks, and powdered sugar
2. Beat the mixture well
3. Prepare a layer of sponge cake slices soaked in nogada (sweetened milk sprinkled with cinnamon and mixed with ground walnuts passed through a sieve)
4. Arrange the walnut paste on this base
5. Bake in a strong oven for about fifteen minutes
6. Remove from the oven and coat with a frosting made from three egg whites and half a pound of powdered sugar beaten to meringue point
7. Return to a warm oven so the frosting dries
8. Decorate with gold threads or spun eggs in syrup, alternating with candies
