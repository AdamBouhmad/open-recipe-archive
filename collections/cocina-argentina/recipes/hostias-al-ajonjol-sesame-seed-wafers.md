## Ingredients

- half a pound of toasted and ground sesame seeds
- half a pound of toasted whole sesame seeds
- two pounds of ground almonds
- one pound of toasted and ground hazelnuts
- three ounces of powdered cinnamon
- one adarme of ground clove
- three pounds of bee honey
- three pounds of grated toasted bread
- half an ounce of grated nutmeg
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a syrup from sugar, mix it with the bee honey, bring to an alfajorado point, add the previously mentioned ground ingredients, and allow to boil until it reaches a high point. Then remove from heat and pour over wafers, spreading a layer of the mixture on one wafer and covering with another, as done with a sandwich.
