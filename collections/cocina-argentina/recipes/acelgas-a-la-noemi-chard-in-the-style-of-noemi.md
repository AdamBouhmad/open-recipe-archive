## Ingredients

- chard
- butter
- one spoonful of flour
- a pinch of sugar
- pepper
- salt
- one cup of good milk
- lemon juice
- bread fried in butter

## Directions

1. After cooking, grind the chard, and once well ground, fry it in butter with a spoonful of flour, a pinch of sugar, pepper and salt.
2. Once fried, pour over it a cup of good milk and let it cook until it reduces to a thick sauce.
3. Squeeze a little lemon juice into it and serve with slices of bread fried in butter on top as garnish.
