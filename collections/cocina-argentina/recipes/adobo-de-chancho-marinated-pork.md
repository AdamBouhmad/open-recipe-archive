## Ingredients

- one liter of good vinegar
- four clove heads of garlic, ground
- pepper
- cumin, ground
- one onion head, finely minced
- oregano powder
- salt
- two bay leaves
- pork loin or ribs

## Directions

1. In a porcelain or terracotta vessel, pour one liter of good vinegar
2. Add four clove heads of garlic ground, pepper, cumin also ground, one finely minced onion head, oregano powder, salt, and two bay leaves
3. Grind well, stirring with a wooden spoon
4. Submerge the pieces of meat in this mixture, turning them in it
5. Cover the vessel and keep the meat in this adobo for three hours
6. Hang it to air-dry in a cool, shaded place with a current of air
7. Roast this adobo in the oven; it is a delicious dish
