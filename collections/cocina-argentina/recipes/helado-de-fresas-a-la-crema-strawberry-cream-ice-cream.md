## Ingredients

- milk
- cinnamon
- sugar
- very ripe strawberries
- essence of strawberries

## Directions

1. Have cold milk ready, then cook it with a light boil, seasoned with cinnamon and enough sugar to sweeten it well
2. Place very ripe strawberries in a dish and mash them with two spoons
3. Beat them further to finish breaking them down
4. Mix them with the milk and pass through a sieve
5. Add a few drops of strawberry essence to enhance the perfume
6. Place in an ice cream maker to freeze
