## Ingredients

- quadrilateral piece of meat, three centimeters thick, from the sirloin or hip of beef or lamb
- salt
- live coals

## Directions

1. Cut the meat in a quadrilateral shape with three centimeters of thickness from the sirloin or hip of beef or lamb
2. Clean it of hides, nerves and fats
3. Wash it in cold water and dry it with care
4. Give it a very slight seasoning of salt
5. Strike the surface with a mortar
6. Extend it over a bed of well-fanned live coals
7. At the same time, prepare another bed of lively coals nearby
8. When the edges of the churrasco begin to pale, quickly turn it over and extend it on the raw side, hastening to remove from the other side the coals adhering to it
9. This short operation is enough for the churrasco to be ready
10. Serve without sauces, as sauce would remove the appetizing flavor given by the immediate contact with fire
