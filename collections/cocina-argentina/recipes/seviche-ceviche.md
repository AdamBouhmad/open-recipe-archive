## Ingredients

- One corbina (white fish)
- Juice of 30 sour oranges
- Onion heads, sliced very thinly
- Ground ají (chili pepper)
- Pepper
- Salt
- Oregano
- Ground cumin

## Directions

1. Select a beautiful corbina, a fish with white and appetizing flesh; descale it, open it, remove the spines and wash it repeatedly with cold water.
2. Cut it into regular square chunks like large dice.
3. Previously prepare a sauce made with the juice of 30 sour oranges, onion heads cut into very thin slices, plenty of ground ají, pepper, salt, oregano, and ground cumin.
4. Place the corbina chunks on a platter, pour the sauce over them, toss each piece of corbina in the sauce, and let them pickle in the strong juice of the sour orange—which serves as cooking—for eight hours, and serve immediately.
