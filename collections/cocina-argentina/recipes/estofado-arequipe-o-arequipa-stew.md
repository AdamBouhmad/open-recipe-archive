## Ingredients

- A piece of the best beef
- Lamb loin
- Half a chicken
- A slice of bacon
- A chorizo
- Onion heads cut in half
- A sprig of parsley
- A handful of soaked chickpeas
- A large piece of butter
- Two cloves of garlic
- Ground and whole black pepper
- One ounce of chocolate
- One bottle of red wine
- Half a bottle of water
- Six tablespoons of good vinegar
- Salt in small quantity
- A little cumin

## Directions

1. Cut all the meat into large pieces and arrange in the pot
2. Add the onion heads, parsley, chickpeas, butter, garlic, pepper, chocolate, wine, water, vinegar, salt, and cumin
3. Cover the pot well and bring to a boil over low heat for four hours without uncovering it
4. Shake the pot from time to time to prevent it from sticking to the bottom
5. When ready to serve, uncover and enjoy the delicious aroma
