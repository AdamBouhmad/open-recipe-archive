## Ingredients

- fat chicken
- butter
- onion, chopped
- salt
- white wine, one glass
- flour, three tablespoons, fried in butter
- fried sausage pieces
- small meatballs of meat and egg, spiced and fried
- asparagus tips
- puff pastry

## Directions

1. Cut up a fat chicken and fry in a pan with butter
2. Once fried, add a little chopped onion, a little salt, and one glass of white wine and continue cooking for five minutes
3. Arrange the pieces on a serving dish and strain the broth in which it cooked, adding three tablespoons of flour fried in butter to thicken it
4. On the dish, alternating with the chicken pieces, place fried sausage pieces and small meatballs of meat and egg seasoned with pepper and fried, as well as asparagus tips, and pour the sauce over
5. Cover this filling with a layer of puff pastry and place in the oven, which should be at high heat for rapid cooking
