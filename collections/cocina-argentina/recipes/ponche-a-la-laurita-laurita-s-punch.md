## Ingredients

- four spoonfuls of powdered sugar
- cinnamon
- one glass of water
- one glass of wine or other liquor

## Directions

1. Put four spoonfuls of powdered sugar with cinnamon into a bottle
2. Add one glass of water and one glass of wine or other liquor
3. Chill thoroughly
4. Cork the bottle and shake strongly until the liquid has turned to foam
5. Serve immediately
