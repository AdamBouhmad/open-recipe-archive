## Ingredients

- rabbit
- salt
- chili pepper
- cumin
- pork lard
- poultry
- mushrooms
- garlic
- fresh bacon
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Prepare the rabbit as described and open it completely
2. Extend the rabbit face down and strike it three times from top to bottom along the spine with a pestle to stretch it well
3. Season with salt and, if desired, with a little chili pepper and ground cumin
4. Fry it in pork lard over high heat with a stone or other weight on the loin to flatten it so it fries better
5. Turn it from one side to the other two or three times, and when it has taken the golden color this type of frying requires, remove it from the fire and drain all the grease
6. Prepare a filling of chopped poultry, mushrooms, and one crushed garlic clove
7. Cover the bottom and sides of a baking dish with thin slices of fresh bacon
8. Spread half of the filling on top, place the rabbit on top, sprinkling it with pepper and grated nutmeg
9. Fill the dish with the rest of the filling, covering it all with a thin slice of bacon
10. Put the lid on and seal its edges with paste to prevent steam from escaping
11. Place it in the oven where it must remain for three hours
12. Serve cold
