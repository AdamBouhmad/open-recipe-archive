## Ingredients

- Chickpeas, well washed
- Water
- Molasses (chancaca), ground
- Purple beans, one dozen raw
- Cloves, one dozen

## Directions

1. Soak well-washed chickpeas in cold water for 24 hours
2. Drain the soaking water and grind in a mill or mortar, very well ground
3. Place to cook with plenty of water over high heat for three hours
4. Strain through thin, strong cloth, pressing to extract the bulk of the juice
5. Regrind the remaining bagasse and mix with the juice already strained, pass through the strainer again
6. Combine with the rest and place in an earthen vessel to ferment, sweetening it with ground molasses and adding with it one dozen raw purple beans and one dozen cloves
7. Fermentation reaches proper point in five days in summer and eight days in winter
