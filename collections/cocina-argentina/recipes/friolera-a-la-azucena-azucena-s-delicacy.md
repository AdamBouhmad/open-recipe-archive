## Ingredients

- three eggs
- six tablespoons of flour
- one cup of heavy cream
- a pinch of salt
- six tablespoons of powdered sugar
- a dozen cherries, washed but with their pits
- cinnamon
- butter
- sugar and cinnamon for serving

## Directions

1. Break three eggs into a bowl and add six tablespoons of flour, dissolving it with one cup of heavy cream to form a clear batter. Add a pinch of salt, six tablespoons of powdered sugar, and a dozen well-washed cherries with their pits still intact. Well mixed and dusted with cinnamon, arrange in a buttered baking tin with edges, and place in a well-heated oven after scattering small pieces of butter over the surface of the batter.
2. Serve hot with a dusting of sugar and cinnamon.
