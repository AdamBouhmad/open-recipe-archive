## Ingredients

- one spoonful of rice flour
- six very fresh egg yolks
- sixteen spoonfuls of powdered sugar
- one liter of cream
- one cup of coconut milk
- one cup of almond milk

## Directions

1. Dissolve one spoonful of rice flour in six very fresh egg yolks
2. Add sixteen spoonfuls of powdered sugar and one liter of cream to this mixture, stirring vigorously
3. Place this preparation in a pot and cook over low heat for a quarter hour, stirring constantly for half an hour with a wooden spoon
4. Once cooking is complete, allow the cream to cool
5. Add one cup of coconut milk and one cup of almond milk, both extracted by grinding in a mortar, diluted in a little milk, and strained through a strong cloth sieve that allows pressing and extracting all the juice
6. Stir well to incorporate the milk into the cream
7. Place in an ice cream maker to freeze
