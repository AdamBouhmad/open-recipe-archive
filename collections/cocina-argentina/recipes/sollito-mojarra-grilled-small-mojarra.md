## Ingredients

- sollitos (small whole fish)
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- fine herbs
- bitter orange juice

## Directions

1. Clean and season with salt and pepper
2. Wrap in paper spread with butter and roast on grills
3. Once cooked, remove the paper and fill with butter mixed with finely chopped fine herbs, bitter orange juice, salt and pepper
4. Serve hot as they come from the grills
