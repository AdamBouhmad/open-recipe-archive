## Ingredients

- Egg yolks
- Butter

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks lightly and place them inside a fine cloth such as linen or clear gauze.
2. Squeeze them out in a thin stream into hot butter in a saucepan, held high above.
3. Two people should perform this operation: one strains the eggs and another uses two forks to spread them so they fry in separate strands.
4. Remove the fried strands with the forks as they cook and place on a napkin that absorbs the butter and leaves them golden and dry.
5. Use to garnish salads and lean dishes.
