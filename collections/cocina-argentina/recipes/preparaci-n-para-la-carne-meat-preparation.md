## Ingredients

- meat: beef or poultry
- salt
- thin cloth napkin
- twine

## Directions

1. Wash and prepare the piece of meat: beef or poultry, enclose it in a thin cloth napkin, which will be gathered at its ends, tying it with twine, leaving the meat inside loose and ample but well sealed.
2. Dig a hole in clean, humid earth, for example in a garden or vegetable patch, and bury the meat thus sealed in the napkin for three hours.
3. Then remove it carefully so that no earth enters it, dusting the napkin with a feather duster before untying the twine.
4. Place the meat on a plate, season it with necessary salt, and extend it on the grill, or give it the required destination.
5. By this operation, the meat obtains an exquisite flavor and becomes so tender that a brief boil is sufficient for its perfect cooking, as well as ten minutes of lively fire on the roaster.
