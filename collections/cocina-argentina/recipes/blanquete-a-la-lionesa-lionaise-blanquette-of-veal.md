## Ingredients

- veal breast
- onions
- carrot
- salt
- whole pepper
- water
- butter
- flour
- parsley
- cebolleta (scallion)
- nutmeg
- ground pepper
- milk

## Directions

1. Cut regular square pieces from the veal breast.
2. Place them in a pan with two onions, one carrot, salt, and whole pepper.
3. Cover the meat with water and bring to boil, removing the foam.
4. After two boils needed to skim, place the pan to simmer slowly in half the broth in which it has cooked.
5. Use the other half of the broth for the sauce by passing it through a sieve.
6. In a dish put four ounces of butter, one spoonful of flour, a small amount of chopped parsley, a scallion cut into pieces, salt, pepper, a small grating of nutmeg, and the carrot that has cooked with the meat, cut into rounds.
7. Place over heat and cook until the onion and parsley soften.
8. Add some of the broth passed through the sieve, which should be kept warm near the fire.
9. Add then one spoonful of ground pepper, and after a boil of ten minutes, add a glass of good milk.
10. Stir well, add the pieces of veal and let cook for a quarter of an hour, time necessary for it to be ready to serve.
