## Ingredients

- four pounds of sweet potatoes
- a quarter portion of milk
- high-point syrup

## Directions

1. Cook four pounds of sweet potatoes
2. Peel them and dissolve in a quarter portion of milk
3. Strain through a sieve and mix with high-point syrup
