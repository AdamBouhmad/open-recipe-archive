## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Fine herbs
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Toasted bread soups in boiling consommé
- Giblets (cooked and chopped)
- Beaten eggs
- Toasted and grated bread
- Ground almonds
- Málaga raisins
- Salt
- Cumin
- Butter

## Directions

1. Pluck the squabs carefully, removing all hidden feathers close to the skin.
2. Clean and empty them thoroughly, then rinse well.
3. Prepare the filling by combining the cooked and chopped giblets, beaten eggs, toasted grated bread, ground almonds, Málaga raisins, pepper, salt, cumin, and a piece of butter.
4. Cook this mixture in a frying pan over fire for ten minutes, stirring constantly to incorporate all ingredients.
5. Fill the squabs with this mixture.
6. Season the filled squabs with salt and pepper, then coat with butter.
7. Wrap each squab in buttered paper.
8. Place in a well-heated oven until cooked.
9. Prepare the sauce by grinding parsley and mixing it with juice from a tomato roasted over hot coals and strained through a sieve.
10. Season the sauce with salt, pepper, a few drops of vinegar, and a good amount of pure olive oil.
11. Serve the squabs with the parsley sauce.
