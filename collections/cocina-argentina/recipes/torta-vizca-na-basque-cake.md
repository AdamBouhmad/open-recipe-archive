## Ingredients

- Thirty egg yolks, well beaten
- One soup plate of cream
- Four ounces ground sugar
- Half a medium-sized sponge cake (bizcochuelo), ground
- Butter for the mold
- Egg white batter for dipping
- Butter for frying
- Syrup
- Almonds, raisins, and candied fruits for decoration

## Directions

1. Mix the thirty well-beaten egg yolks with one soup plate of cream and four ounces of ground sugar, adding half a ground medium-sized sponge cake.
2. Beat the mixture together until it thickens.
3. Place in a buttered mold and bake in the oven until cooked.
4. Remove from the oven and cut into slices.
5. Dip the slices in a batter made from egg whites, then fry them in butter.
6. Roll the fried slices in syrup and arrange them on a dish in a wheel shape, with the slices as spokes and decorations of almonds, raisins, and candied fruits.
