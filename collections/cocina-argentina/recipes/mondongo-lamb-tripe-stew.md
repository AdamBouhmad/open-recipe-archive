## Ingredients

- the thickest part of lamb belly
- hot water
- one or two onion heads, depending on quantity
- pork fat
- a clove of garlic, ground
- juice of a tomato passed through a sieve
- pepper
- cumin
- four tablespoons of broth
- bread crumb softened in milk
- two egg yolks, beaten
- vinegar and oil
- a glass of heavy cream or good milk

## Directions

1. Take the thickest part of the lamb belly and wash it very well several times with hot water, scraping it each time with a knife
2. Wash until all odor is lost
3. Give it three boils, changing the water and washing it again in each change
4. Cook until it falls apart under pressure
5. Chop into small pieces and place in a sauce prepared as follows:
6. Chop very finely one or two onion heads depending on quantity and fry in a pan with pork fat, a clove of ground garlic, the juice of a tomato passed through a sieve, a little pepper and a little, very little cumin
7. When the onion is lightly golden, add four tablespoons of broth and let it boil a little more
8. Mix in a proportionate quantity of bread crumb softened in milk, to which two beaten egg yolks and a little vinegar and oil have been previously mixed
9. Incorporate the chopped tripe to the sauce and give it half a boil
10. When serving, still in the pan, mix in a glass of heavy cream or good milk
