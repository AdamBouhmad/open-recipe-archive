## Ingredients

- one pound of wheat flour
- half a pound of butter
- eight eggs, separated
- a small glass of cognac
- cinnamon
- sugar to taste
- plum preserve

## Directions

1. Mix and beat very well one pound of wheat flour and half a pound of butter
2. Beat the egg yolks and whites separately
3. When they reach meringue point, mix yolks and whites together
4. Add them to the flour mixture and beat very well with a small glass of cognac, cinnamon, and sugar to taste
5. Prepare a plum preserve and spread a layer of it on the bottom of a heat-resistant porcelain dish
6. Pour the dough on top, spread it in the shape of a cake, and place in a very hot oven, as cooking should be rapid
7. Serve in the same dish, without relief decorations
