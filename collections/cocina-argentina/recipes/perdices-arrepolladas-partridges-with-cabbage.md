## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Onions
- Two carrots
- Celery
- Parsley
- Fresh bacon
- Well-made sausage
- Cabbage
- One liter of stock
- Nutmeg
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the partridges.
2. Place them in a cooking vessel with onions, two carrots, celery, parsley, fresh bacon, a well-made sausage, and a good cabbage that has been soaked in boiling water and well wrung out.
3. Pour one liter of stock seasoned with nutmeg over everything.
4. Let simmer for three hours.
5. To serve, place the cabbage at the bottom of the serving dish, arrange the partridges on top, alternating with pieces of sausage and bacon cut into small pieces.
