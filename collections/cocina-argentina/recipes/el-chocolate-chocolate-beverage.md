## Ingredients

- 1 ounce of chocolate, previously grated, per cup
- water or milk

## Directions

1. Place one ounce of grated chocolate in each cup and fill it with the water or milk in which it is to be made
2. Pour into the chocolate pot and place over fire, stirring constantly with the whisk to mix it
3. When it begins to boil, remove from fire; beat for two minutes, and return to fire, beating and stirring always
4. When with the second boil it has risen to the edge of the container, remove from fire again; beat for another two minutes and return to fire again
5. When, rising further, the third boil occurs, remove from fire, beat and serve if possible in cups similar to jicaras which preserve its heat and creamy foam
