## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- pickled onions
- pepper
- fresh bacon
- butter
- onions, halved
- half bottle of red wine
- half bottle of vinegar
- water
- rosemary branch

## Directions

1. Make deep incisions in the fleshy parts and insert a filling of pickled onions, pepper, and fresh bacon
2. Cook in an enamel porcelain pot with butter, halved onions, pepper, half a bottle of red wine, half a bottle of vinegar, water to cover the meat, and a bunch of rosemary
3. Bring to a gentle boil until the liquid has evaporated and the meat is golden
