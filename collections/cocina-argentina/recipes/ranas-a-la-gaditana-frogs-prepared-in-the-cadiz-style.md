## Ingredients

- Frog thighs
- Butter
- Flour
- Mushrooms, cut into small pieces
- Fine herbs, chopped
- Salt and pepper
- Half a glass of white wine
- Good broth
- Two or three egg yolks, beaten

## Directions

1. Briefly blanch frog thighs in boiling water for one minute.
2. Cool them in ice water for one minute.
3. Dry them and fry them slowly in butter.
4. Dust them with flour using a spoon, add a handful of mushrooms cut into small pieces, chopped fine herbs, salt, and pepper.
5. Add half a glass of white wine and some spoonfuls of good broth.
6. When the sauce has reduced, the cooking is complete.
7. Remove the pan from the heat and arrange the frog thighs with the mushrooms in a serving dish.
8. Remove the fine herbs from the sauce with a skimmer.
9. Add two or three beaten egg yolks to the sauce and pour it very hot at the moment of serving.
