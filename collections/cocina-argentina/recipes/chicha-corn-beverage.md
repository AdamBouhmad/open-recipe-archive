## Ingredients

- Corn flour, defatted
- Water, sufficient amount

## Directions

1. Cook defatted corn flour (that is, with the grain oil removed) in plenty of water.
2. As it cooks, the oil of the grain will separate and rise to the surface.
3. After each operation, remove two glasses of the broth and deposit them in a clay vessel, the same as the one in which the flour cooks.
4. For each one removed, add two jugs of hot water kept at hand.
5. When in the prolonged cooking and transfer of the liquid the flour has disappeared from the bottom of the pot, remove it from the fire.
6. Combine all the broths and let them cool.
7. Place in a clay pot or jar, cover very well, and leave to ferment.
