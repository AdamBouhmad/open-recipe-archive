## Ingredients

- dozen eggs
- lamb kidneys
- butter
- fine herbs
- roast juice
- Jerez wine
- salt
- pepper
- hot sauce or mustard sauce or tomato sauce with green chilies

## Directions

1. Beat twelve eggs to foam, and put half in a pan with butter, to cook as an omelet on moderate heat, turning it so that it cooks evenly.
2. In the meantime, prepare the filling by cutting lamb kidneys into small pieces and cooking them in butter, fine herbs finely chopped, four spoonfuls of roast juice, and half a glass of Jerez wine: all with seasoning of pepper and salt.
3. Next, spread the filling over the omelet, and pour the rest of the beaten egg over it, adding a piece of butter, and covering the pan with its lid, on which will be placed hot ash and embers.
4. Once the omelet is cooked, serve it with hot sauce, either mustard or tomato with green chilies.
