## Ingredients

- A piece of beef from the loin or ham
- Six eggs
- Six peeled and skinned walnuts
- A piece of toasted bread, grated
- One ounce of butter
- Pepper
- Almonds
- Málaga raisins
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Tomato sauce, liquid

## Directions

1. Parboil a piece of beef from the loin or ham
2. Chop it finely, then grind it in a mortar; shred it, remove nerves and skin, and grind and shred again
3. Season with pepper, one tablespoon of vinegar and salt, and let it marinate for one hour
4. Beat six eggs
5. Grind six peeled and skinned walnuts
6. Grate a piece of toasted bread
7. Mix everything together, stirring very well, and add one ounce of butter, pepper, almonds, and Málaga raisins
8. Butter the pudding mold on the bottom and sides and sprinkle with breadcrumbs
9. Fill with the prepared mixture and place in an oven at moderate heat
10. Serve with liquid tomato sauce
