## Ingredients

- Water, one arroba
- Rice, one pound
- Roasted peanuts, one pound
- Walnuts, one pound
- Sweet almonds, four ounces
- Toasted corn, one handful
- Fennel, one small bunch
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Orange leaves

## Directions

1. Cook in one arroba of water: one pound of rice, one pound of roasted peanuts, one pound of walnuts, four ounces of sweet almonds, all well cleaned and ground, adding one handful of toasted corn and one small bunch of fennel
2. When it has cooked sufficiently, pass through a strainer and place to ferment, seasoned with sugar, cinnamon, clove, and orange leaves
3. Three days of fermentation are sufficient to produce a delicious beverage
