## Ingredients

- 12 ripe, healthy quinces for cooking
- 12 raw quinces
- water

## Directions

1. Cook 12 ripe, healthy quinces
2. Remove the seeds from 12 raw quinces with their surrounding gum and soak them in a cup with a little hot water
3. When the cooked quinces begin to fall apart and the cooking water has reduced, remove from heat and let cool enough to handle
4. Extract the seeds from the cooked quinces
5. Make a syrup from the cooking water and strain it through cloth
6. Rub the soaked seeds to extract all their gum and strain it well
7. Mix the seed gum with the syrup
8. Return to heat and cook until the point of coagulation is reached, which is known when the bubbles of the boil crack as they burst
9. Serve in cups
