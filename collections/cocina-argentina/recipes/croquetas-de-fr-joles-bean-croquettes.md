## Ingredients

- Cooked beans, skins removed, ground
- Six eggs, beaten
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Lemon zest scrapings
- Three tablespoons flour
- Butter for frying

## Directions

1. Cook the beans, remove their skins, and grind them in a mortar.
2. Beat six eggs and mix them into the bean paste along with sugar, cinnamon, lemon zest scrapings, and three tablespoons of flour to make the mixture somewhat compact.
3. Heat a piece of butter in a pan over high heat until it boils.
4. Drop the paste by spoonfuls into the hot butter.
5. When one side is golden, flip to brown the other side and complete frying.
6. Serve hot.
