## Ingredients

- squash
- tomatoes
- onions
- garlic
- green chili
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- saffron
- dried cilantro
- strong vinegar
- raisins
- toasted almonds
- black olives
- puff pastry

## Directions

1. Peel the squash and chop it with tomatoes, onions, garlic and green chili, and coat in sufficient butter and salt.
2. Once cooked in this sauce, add a little pepper, saffron and dried cilantro, ground to powder, all dissolved in a little strong vinegar, with raisins, toasted almonds and black olives.
3. Place this filling between two squares of puff pastry, which are roasted in the oven, or fried in butter.
