## Ingredients

- asparagus
- boiling water
- fresh butter
- cream or good milk
- one spoonful of flour
- salt
- pepper
- three egg yolks
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Submerge the asparagus in boiling water and place them in a pan over low heat with very fresh butter.
2. Sauté them in the butter without allowing them to brown.
3. Add to this sauté one cup of cream or good milk.
4. Add one spoonful of flour dissolved in milk, salt and pepper, and cook them until the sauce is well reduced.
5. At the moment of serving, remove the pan from the heat and complete the sauce by pouring in three beaten egg yolks, stirring them in to incorporate.
6. Serve accompanied by a mixture of oil, vinegar, salt and pepper.
