## Ingredients

- hen
- salt
- onion slices
- two tablespoons of flour
- white wine

## Directions

1. Cook the hen with salt to taste and onion slices
2. When the broth is ready, remove the fat from the surface
3. In this fat, dissolve two tablespoons of flour
4. Beat well and add the necessary broth for the soup
5. Add the pieces of hen and after a light boil, add a glass of good white wine before serving
