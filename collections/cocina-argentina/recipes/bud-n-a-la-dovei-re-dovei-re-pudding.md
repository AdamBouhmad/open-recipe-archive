## Ingredients

- veal
- raw bacon
- white onion
- four eggs
- butter
- parsley
- thyme
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Chop very finely a piece of parboiled veal, and an equal piece of raw bacon, ground. Chop and fry white onion. Beat separately whites and yolks of four eggs.
2. When the whites are stiff, mix everything; add a spoonful of butter, chopped parsley and thyme, season with salt and pepper, and stir and beat well, until forming a paste.
3. Grease the entire mold with lard, sprinkle breadcrumbs on top, and place a layer of paste on the bottom. Over it, in the center, break an egg, and on top a strong sprinkling of breadcrumbs, and another layer of paste and another egg, and so on until filling the mold, which is closed and placed in a water bath, in a pan that with water up to the middle will be boiling on good heat. Cover it, and place hot ash and embers on the lid.
4. After one hour of cooking, the pudding is ready. Turn it out onto the platter to take it to the table, after pouring over it the sauce you wish.
