## Ingredients

- dorado (a large fish)
- salt
- vinegar
- flour
- lard
- oil
- pepper
- onion heads
- tomatoes
- parsley
- green chili peppers
- olives
- pickled vegetables
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Scale, open, and wash the dorado, then cut it into cross-sectional pieces five centimeters thick
2. Place the pieces in a wooden, terra-cotta, or porcelain-enameled vessel filled with salted water strongly saturated with vinegar for one hour
3. Coat the dorado pieces in flour and fry them in lard in an enameled pan
4. Cook the head in an identical pot with salted water saturated with vinegar
5. Arrange the fried pieces in a circle on a serving platter with the head in the center
6. Pour over the whole a sauce made of oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, sliced onion heads, chopped tomatoes and parsley, and strips of green chili peppers
7. Garnish with olives and pickled vegetables between sprigs of fine herbs
