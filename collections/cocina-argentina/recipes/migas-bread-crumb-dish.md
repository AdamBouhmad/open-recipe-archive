## Ingredients

- half a pound of pork fat
- two large onion heads, finely minced
- four cloves of garlic, ground
- one tablespoon of paprika
- salt to taste
- five medium breads soaked in milk
- half a pound of fresh cheese, minced
- six egg yolks, lightly beaten
- olives
- pieces of boiled yuca

## Directions

1. Heat half a pound of pork fat and fry the finely minced onions, ground garlic, paprika, and salt to taste
2. When the onion has taken on color, add five medium breads soaked in milk, lightly squeezed, and broken up, stirring continuously to prevent burning
3. When it comes to a boil, add half a pound of minced fresh cheese
4. When everything is well incorporated and a moment before removing from the heat, add six lightly beaten egg yolks
5. Serve with garnishes of olives and pieces of boiled yuca
