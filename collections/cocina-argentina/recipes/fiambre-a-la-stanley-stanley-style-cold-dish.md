## Ingredients

- fat chicken
- ham slices
- sausage pieces, fried
- hard-boiled eggs, halves
- olives
- beef broth, one liter
- puff pastry

## Directions

1. Stuff a fat chicken with thin slices of ham, pieces of fried sausage, halves of hard-boiled eggs, and olives
2. Cook in one liter of beef broth at a rolling boil for one hour
3. Let cool and drain
4. Wrap in puff pastry and roast in the oven
5. This dish is not only a cold dish but also figures as an exquisite morsel when served hot, accompanied separately with parsley or tomato sauce
