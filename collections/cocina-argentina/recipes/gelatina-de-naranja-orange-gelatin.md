## Ingredients

- oranges for juice
- orange peels
- sugar for syrup
- isinglass or Japanese gelatin leaf

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice from oranges through a silk sieve
2. Make a syrup by boiling orange peels in water
3. Strain the syrup
4. Add the isinglass or Japanese gelatin leaf and the orange juice
5. Prepare the gelatin and pour it into orange peel halves cut crosswise
6. Surround with sugar
