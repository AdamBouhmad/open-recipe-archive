## Ingredients

- one liter of good milk
- one pound of sugar
- twelve egg yolks
- six egg whites, well beaten
- zest of the skin of two lemons
- one small glass of Port wine (or curacao)

## Directions

1. Mix one liter of good milk with one pound of sugar and place on the heat
2. When it begins to boil, add twelve egg yolks and six well-beaten egg whites, and the zest of the skin of two lemons
3. Stir this mixture over a lively fire until a green syrup appears on the surface
4. Pour into a pudding mold that has been previously buttered
5. Place in the oven with moderate heat and rapid cooking
6. At the moment the mass begins to pull away from the sides of the pudding mold, sprinkle a small glass of the best wine, using Port wine as the measure (curacao is more recommended for this pudding)
7. Cool and serve
