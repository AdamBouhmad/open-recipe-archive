## Ingredients

- lettuce
- vinegar
- oil
- pepper
- salt
- black olives
- onions

## Directions

1. Chop the outer leaves very finely, leaving the hearts intact, which are arranged forming a small grove in the center of the salad bowl.
2. Beat together one cup of vinegar and four tablespoons of good oil with a sprinkling of pepper and salt until completely mixed.
3. Place the chopped lettuce mixed in this mixture in the salad bowl with its arrangement of hearts.
4. Top with black olives and, if desired, onions cut in thin slices.
