## Ingredients

- Potatoes as needed
- Olive oil
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Ground chili pepper to taste
- Fresh, tender lettuce leaves
- Onions
- Black pepper
- Oregano powder
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Fried fish pieces
- Cooked shrimp
- Fresh cheese slices
- Sweet potatoes or yams
- Olives
- Radishes
- Lettuce hearts

## Directions

1. Cook the necessary potatoes and peel them, removing their skin
2. Grind them as if making purée
3. Mix the potato mass with plenty of olive oil, vinegar, salt, and ground chili pepper to taste
4. Line the serving dish with a layer of fresh, tender, well-washed lettuce leaves
5. Spread the potato mixture on these leaves in the form of a cake
6. Prepare a sauce by cutting onions into very thin rounds and pickling them for three hours in vinegar, olive oil, black pepper, powdered oregano, and chili if desired
7. Place these pickled onions on top of the causa
8. Garnish with hard-boiled eggs cut into slices, pieces of fried fish or cooked shrimp, fresh cheese slices, sweet potatoes or yams cut into small pieces, olives, and radishes
9. Scatter lettuce hearts as if they were bushes in a meadow
