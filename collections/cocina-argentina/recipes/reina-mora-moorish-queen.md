## Ingredients

- thirty egg whites, beaten until fluffy
- two pounds of ground almonds
- three biscuits
- four pounds of powdered sugar
- butter
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix and knead, making a paste of thirty egg whites beaten until fluffy, two pounds of ground almonds, three biscuits, and four pounds of powdered sugar
2. When the paste is united and smooth, place it in a mold with a beautiful form, buttering the bottom and sides with a layer of butter
3. Sprinkle with cloves and cinnamon, and place in the oven
4. Serve hot or cold
