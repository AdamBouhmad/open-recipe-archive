## Ingredients

- mantequilla (butter)
- una cabeza de cebolla (one onion head)
- un tomate (one tomato)
- un diente de ajo (one garlic clove)
- huevos (eggs)
- pimienta (pepper)
- sal (salt)
- pan (bread)

## Directions

1. Mince very finely one onion head, one tomato, and one garlic clove, and fry them in butter until the onion takes on a light golden color
2. Remove from the fire and pass through a sieve
3. Return to the pan and break the eggs over it, sprinkling pepper and salt over each one, and serve from the moment the whites have turned white
4. Serve with very thin, golden toasted bread slices arranged in a circle at the center of the plate, with one slice served per egg
