## Ingredients

- cod
- vinegar
- water
- garlic
- green onion leaves
- walnuts
- milk
- onion heads
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- oil
- broth
- pickled cucumbers
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Soak the cod for ten hours in cold water saturated with vinegar, stirring three times
2. Wash it again with three waters, then cook for one hour in water saturated with vinegar with one garlic clove and green onion leaves unpicked
3. Remove from heat, wash again with two waters, cut into small pieces, remove bones and spines, and place in a dish to infuse in water with vinegar
4. Break walnuts and drop them in boiling water briefly to loosen the skin, remove the skin by washing in cold water until white, then grind well
5. Mix the ground walnuts with boiling milk, passing them through cheesecloth or linen cloth and pressing well so the thick walnut juice comes out with the milk
6. Make a sauce with cooked onion heads separated leaf by leaf (unpicked), one ground garlic clove, pepper, a little breadcrumbs, salt to taste, and butter
7. Mix, bring to a boil briefly, add five tablespoons of vinegar and three of oil, mix in the cod, add three tablespoons of broth, and boil for fifteen minutes, stirring gently
8. Remove from heat, mix in the walnut milk, and serve garnished with pickled cucumbers and hard-boiled egg slices
