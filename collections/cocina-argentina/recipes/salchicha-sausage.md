## Ingredients

- pork meat, finely chopped
- bacon, finely chopped
- vinegar
- four heads of garlic, ground
- pepper
- cumin, ground
- onion
- oregano powder
- salt
- two bay leaves
- sausage casings

## Directions

1. Chop the pork meat very finely and chop bacon as well
2. Place in a wooden tub or terra-cotta pot to marinate for twenty-four hours with the same mixture as for the adobo: vinegar, ground garlic, pepper, ground cumin, chopped onion, oregano powder, salt, and bay leaves
3. Stuff the mixture into sausage casings, tied at intervals of a third
4. Hang to dry in a shady, well-ventilated place, taking care not to hang in the kitchen where smoke would impart an unpleasant odor
