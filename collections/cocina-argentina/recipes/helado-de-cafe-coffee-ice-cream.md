## Ingredients

- two liters of good milk
- one cup of natural cream
- half pound of coffee, carefully roasted and ground

## Directions

1. Mix two liters of good milk with one cup of natural cream
2. Bring to a boil for ten minutes with half a pound of coffee that has been carefully roasted, ground, and enclosed in a fairly large flannel bag so the coffee can soak into the liquid through the fabric
3. Remove the milk from the fire and squeeze the flannel bag well three or four times after submerging it again
4. Empty the milk into another container
5. Sweeten strongly and allow to cool
6. Pass to an ice cream maker to freeze
